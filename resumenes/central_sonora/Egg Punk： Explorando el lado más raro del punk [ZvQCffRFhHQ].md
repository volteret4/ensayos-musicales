## curiosities
**Formation and Style (2013)** : The Conjets formed in 2013 in Indiana, playing an ultra-accelerated, lo-fi punk rock with "martian" vocals. Their sound blended the energy of hardcore punk with influences from Power Pop and New Wave. "Una de estas bandas fue de conjets, formada en 2013 en Indiana, una banda que tocaba a Punx Rock Low Fy, ultra acelerado, con voces marcianas, que tenía mucho de ese hardcore punk, pero también de Power Pop y New Wave."
**Distinctive Sound** : The band's sound is characterized as resembling "gods reproduced at 45 revolutions per minute, on a record player that urgently needs a needle change," conveying a raw, high-speed, and somewhat distorted quality. "la banda suena como dios reproducidos a 45 revoluciones por minuto, en un tocalisco es que necesita un cambio de hoja urgentemente."
**Foundational Role in Eckpank** : The Conjets are considered a foundational band of the Eckpank genre, with their first LP, released by Tequeton Traejer, seen as a cornerstone of the movement. They were also positioned at the extreme end of the Eckpank spectrum in an influential 2018 Instagram diagram. "Sello que dito el primer LP de Comhets, considerado la pidrangular del género y que sigue irritando algunas de las bandas más interesantes de la nueva ola de la que ahora os voy a hablar."

# genre - Punk

## curiosities
**Birth and Democratization (Mid-1970s)** : Punk was born in the mid-1970s in the United Kingdom and the United States as a direct response to overly commercial and sentimental music. With a rebellious and anti-establishment attitude, it uniquely democratized music, enabling any young person who could play three chords to form a band. "el género conseguió lo que no había hecho ningún género hasta entonces, democratizar la música."
**Global Spread and Substyles** : In the years following its emergence, the punk movement extended virtually worldwide, leading to the development of various substyles such as Oi!, Psychobilly, and Riot Grrrl. "Durante los siguientes años, este movimiento se extendió prácticamente por todo el mundo, derivando en varios opestilos, como el hoy, el Psycho Billy, o el Riot Girl."

# genre - Ekpank

## curiosities
**Origin and Early Characteristics (Mid-2010s)** : Ekpank originated in the US Midwest in the mid-2010s, initially as bands playing fast, hard-hitting punk with a "clownish" attitude and "nerd" aesthetic. These musicians enjoyed hardcore punk but didn't identify with the serious, aggressive image of traditional punk. They embraced insecurity, introversion, and fun, playing in basements and releasing ultra-limited music on cassettes. "A mediados de la década de 2010, empezaron a surgir en el medio huesté de Estados Unidos una serie de bandas con ese sonido rápido y contundente, pero con una actitud, digamos, más payazá y con una estética más nerd."
**Internet and Meme-Driven Naming (2017-2018)** : The subgenre gained its name from Instagram memes, starting in 2017. A 2018 diagram by "meme le escribo," positioning bands on a spectrum from Chain Punk to Ekpank, inadvertently gave the movement a definitive name and set it against more serious punk. This turned Ekpank into a trendy internet underground label, transforming it from a local to a global phenomenon. "esta publicación, para bien o para mal, había puesto un nombre definitivo al movimiento y la habían enfrentado con el panc más serio y distorsionado."
**Sound Evolution** : The sound of Ekpank evolved to become even more playful, incorporating frenetic rhythms, spasmodic and playful guitars, and more nasal or "martian" vocals. This contrasts sharply with the seriousness of hardcore punk. "El sonido evoluciono hacia un estilo aún más juguetón, abrazando ritmos frenéticos, tarras espasmódicas y juguetonas y voces más nasalísimas cianas."
**Aesthetic Evolution** : The aesthetic of Ekpank became markedly more absurd, featuring surreal collages, deliberately ridiculous lyrics, and an attitude that celebrates the bizarre above all else. This was molded through internet memes, forums, and platforms like Bandcamp and YouTube. "Mientras que su estética se volvió más marcadamente absurda, compartadas sus realistas, letras deliberadamente ridículas y un actitud que celebra valorizarro por encima de todo."
**Global Expansion and New Generation** : Although similar groups existed worldwide, the coining of the term Ekpank sparked a new generation of bands that identified with the label. The scene moved beyond its Midwest origins to become a global micro-genre, with notable vibrant scenes emerging in Australia and Spain. "El ekpank se había convertido en la etiqueta de moda de la underground de internet y pasó de ser un fenómeno local a un global."
**Pandemic's Role in Development (Late 2010s)** : Towards the end of the 2010s, particularly during the pandemic, many rising punk artists leveraged isolation and the "duit dueltzerve" (do-it-yourself) nature of the genre. They recorded at home with simple equipment like a drum machine and a poorly tuned guitar, allowing for increasingly experimental and interesting sonic explorations. "muchos panks en auge aprovecharon la reclusión y la naturaleza, duit dueltzerve del género, para grabarse en sus casas y experimentar con ese sonido."
**Revolutionary Impact** : Ekpank, without explicitly aiming to save or reinvent punk, has become an almost accidental revolution within the genre. It has revitalized the primitive, visceral, and energetic spirit of rock and roll through the language of the internet, connecting with new generations in an unprecedented way. "El Eckpank no pretende salvar al punk de reinventarlo, pero de forma casi accidental se ha convertido en una pequeña revolución dentro del genero."

# curiosity

**Instagram Meme Origin of Eckpank (2017)** : In 2017, an Instagram meme posted by the account "meme le escribo" visually depicted Peter Parker from Sam Raimi's Spider-Man movie removing his glasses to reveal two contrasting worlds. One side showed aggressive punk aesthetics with chains and leather, while the other was a colorful collage of a peace symbol, a clown, a drawing of Kit Haring, and an egg. This meme is credited with giving rise to the strange subgenre of Eckpank. "En 2017, un meme de Instagram dio lugar al subgenero más extraño del punk."
**Spectrum Diagram and Eckpank Naming (2018)** : A year after the initial meme, in 2018, Instagram user "Chanax va a trabajar Rose va a trabajar 2000" sent a publication to "meme le escribo" which was then shared. This publication featured a diagram showing various bands on a spectrum between Chain Punk and Ekpank, popularizing the comparison between traditional, "jargoleta" punk and a new, more colorful, ironic, and strange type of punk, which began to be called Ekpank. This inadvertently provided a definitive name to the movement. "En ella, vemos un diagrama con varias bandas en un espectro entre Chain Punk y Ekpank."
**Midwest Scene Consolidation (2016)** : By 2016, a distinct scene had consolidated in the US Midwest. It consisted of bands that played catchy and fast songs with spasmodic guitars, strange sounds, and "mongoloid" lyrics, directly contrasting with the seriousness of hardcore. These bands embraced insecurity, introversion, and, above all, fun. "En 2016 se había consolidado una escena en el medio-weste de bandas con canciones pegadizas y rápidas, guitarras espasmodicas, sonidos extraños y letras mongoloides que se contraponía con la sirida del hardcore, bandes que abrazaban la inseguridad, la introversión y sobre todo la diversión."
**Internet as a Shaping Force for Eckpank** : The Eckpank aesthetic and sound were largely molded through the internet, leveraging memes, forums, and platforms such as Bandcamp and YouTube. This online environment facilitated its unique development and spread. "En internet se fue moldeando a través de memes, foros y plataformas como banca a mi youtube."
**Global Spread and Regional Scenes of Eckpank** : The Eckpank scene evolved beyond its Midwest origins to become a global phenomenon, with particularly vibrant and interesting scenes currently thriving in Australia and Spain, alongside the ongoing North American presence. Australia's scene has adopted and reinvented the genre with a unique local touch, while Spain's scene, including pioneers like Pútrido and foundational bands like Los Valencianos Finales, is considered one of the healthiest globally. "La escena Kpang Español Actual, junto a la Australia en la norteamericana, es la que goza de mejor salud a nivel global."
**Eckpank Revitalizing Rock and Roll** : These bands, operating through the unique language of the internet, have successfully revived the most primitive, visceral, and energetic spirit of rock and roll. When many considered punk dead once again, it was reborn through Eckpank in an unexpected and utterly absurd form, connecting with new generations in an unprecedented manner. "estas bandas han logrado revivir el espíritu más primitivo, visceral y energético del rock and roll, y todo ello desde el lenguaje propio de Internet."
