## curiosities
**Origin and Characteristics** : Rally House is a genre that combines elements of 90s House, PlayStation 1 video game aesthetics, and the dirty, addictive sound of Funk, all infused with a sense of digital nostalgia. It has a very clear sound and a recognizable aesthetic, despite lacking a formal manifesto. It is considered controversial by purists but possesses the ingredients for digital-era success, accumulating many views and having a viral name. "un genero que probablemente haría tirarse de los pelos a los más puristas de la música electrónica, pero que tiene todos los ingredientes para triunfar en la era digital."
**Sound Defining Elements (2022)** : Starting in 2022, the characteristic compressed and saturated piano stabs, which are central to the Rally House sound, began to gain popularity. Although the genre's aesthetic and overall sound were not fully consolidated at this point, its distinctive sound was clearly taking shape. "A partir de 2022, comenzaron a popularizarse los característicos, pianos e estaps, comprimidos y saturados que definen el sonido del rally House, aunque por entonces ni la estética ni el sonido estaban del todo consolidados, el sonido ya estaba tomando forma."
**Aesthetic Definition (November 2023)** : In November 2023, the track "Malvoroclub" by PX RKX played a pivotal role in definitively shaping the genre's aesthetic. This was achieved through the integration of imagery featuring real cars from vintage magazines and visual elements sourced from PlayStation 1 era racing games. "En noviembre de 2023, el productor PX RKX subió el track Malvoroclub, el cual, finalmente, definió la estética con imágenes de coches de reales de revistas antiguas y visuales de juegos de carreras de la era de PlayStation 1."
**Genre Naming (January 2024)** : Three months after the aesthetic was defined, in January 2024, producer Prod.MT officially named the genre with his track "RKX". This marked a significant milestone in the solidification and recognition of Rally House. "el productor Prod.MT, Bautizor Final Genero, con su track RKX, todo y que decirlo, un temazo."
**Digital Era Success Factors** : Rally House is well-suited for success in the digital era due to its clear sound, recognizable aesthetic, and viral name, allowing it to accumulate many views across online platforms. This contrasts with older genre formation tied to specific physical locations. "Tiene una estética tan reconocible y hasta un nombre tan viral."
**Digital Nostalgia Connection** : The appeal of Rally House lies in its ability to evoke a memory of what House music once was, which the listener's mind attempts to reconstruct. It channels an unnamed emotion that the internet and algorithms have transformed into an aesthetic, tapping into a screen-mediated past rather than a real one. "Porque no se las suena House, suena un recuerdo de lo que fue el House y que tu mi amor ya intenta reconstruir, a una emoción sin nombre que internet y algoritmo han convertido en estética."
**Evolution and Expansion** : Since its inception, Rally House has shown a dynamic nature, with numerous producers exploring and expanding its possibilities. Far from being a rigid, closed formula, many artists are actively seeking new directions and broadening the genre's scope. "el lojo es de quedarse como una fórmula cerrada, muchos de ellos ya están ampliando las posibilidades del género y buscando nuevos caminos."
**Vocal Characteristics** : The genre incorporates distinct vocal styles, often featuring heavily compressed, robotic male voices reminiscent of rapping, or more melodic, sung female vocals. These female vocals are frequently in Portuguese, a direct influence inherited from Brazilian Funk. "Suelen ser voces robóticas masculinas, muy comprimidas, en plan rafeadas, o voces femininas más cantadas y melodicas, muchas veces en portugués, herencia del fonc Brasileno."
**Piano Stab Sound** : The piano stabs in Rally House are heavily compressed and saturated, engineered to sound as if they originate from a 'ripped' PlayStation 1 video game. This distinct, somewhat raw sound quality, rather than sounding "Q3" (presumably "cute"), contributes a "guapo" (cool) flow to the tracks. Many songs within the genre utilize the same sound sample for these piano stabs. "este piano se comprimi satura hasta sonar como si saliera de un videojuego de PlayStation 1 más ripeado, y el lejos de sonar Q3 le da un flow bastante guapo a las canciones."
**Visual Aesthetic Inspiration** : The visual aesthetic of Rally House draws directly from classic PlayStation 1 driving games. Many of its videos incorporate fragments from titles such as Koalima Rally, Tokaturinkar, Rice Rice, R-Type 4, Glanturismo, and Difforispy. "Muchos videos sus enfragmentos de títulos como Koalima, Rally, Tokaturinkar, Rice Rice, R-Type 4, Glanturismo, Difforispy, or Rally."

## curiosities
**Old School House Characteristics** : Old School House, particularly from the late 1980s and early 1990s, is characterized by a classic 4x4 beat at around 120 bpm and the iconic "piano stab." This piano stab is a short, dry chord that, despite being melodic, functions primarily as a rhythmic rather than harmonic element. Key labels from this era included Tracks Records from Chicago and Strilly Riding and Lyrgos Records from New York. "Hablamos del clásico beat 4x4 en un tempo alrededor de 120 bpm y el mitico pianito del House, el llamado piano stab. Ese acorde corto y seco, que aunque sea melodico, se utiliza más como un recorso ritmico que armonico."
**Influence on Rally House** : Nineties House music is identified as one of the three major pillars from which Rally House directly draws its sound and aesthetic. Its echoes, particularly the classic 4x4 beat and piano elements, are fundamental to the new genre. "Ecos del House 90ero... tres grandes pilares de los que me vi directamente, el House o el Escúl."

## curiosities
**Origin and Early Platforms (2000s)** : Funk emerged during the 2000s, primarily on platforms like SoundCloud and YouTube. Its sound was influenced by 90s Memphis Rap, incorporating elements of jazz, trap, and the original funk genre, from which it takes its name. "El fonc surgió durante la década de los 2000, sobre todo en plataformas como Soundcloud y YouTube, influenciado por el rap de Memphis de los años 90, con elementos de jazz, trap y fang."
**Sound Characteristics** : Funk is known for its dark sound, oversaturated bass, powerful basslines, slowed vocals, and a distorted 808 cowbell, a classic sound from Southern Rap. The entire production is oversaturated to the point of "picar en rojo" (clipping into the red), making it instantly recognizable even in a one-second clip, optimized for platforms like TikTok. "sus sonidos muy característicos, bajos potentes, voces ralentizadas y ese cobel de la rola, 8-8, clásico del rap suréño, distorsionado hasta sonar sucio. Todo en conjunto, sobresaturado hasta picar en rojo, para que sea reconocible en un extracto de un segundo, pensado para sonar en plataformas como TikTok."
**Usage in Internet Culture** : Funk tracks are widely used in various forms of internet culture, including memes, gym edits, chat leagues, absurd memes, and "shitposts." This ubiquitous presence helped spread its distinctive sound across online communities. "Se usaba para muchos tipos de memes, Edith de Jim, Liga Chat, memes Absurdos y muchas hitpost."
**Resurgence and TikTok Influence (2019)** : From 2019 onwards, Funk experienced a significant resurgence, largely driven by the momentum of TikTok and a dedicated fan community. During this period, the genre began to mutate and branch out into various sub-genres. "A partir de 2019, mi gracia es al impulso de TikTok, y una comunidad de fans muy fiel, el género vivió en renacer, y como ha sido un monstruo mutante se tratara, empezó a ramificarse en varios sub-generos."
**Global Producer Presence** : The fascinating aspect of Funk is the global distribution of its artists. Producers of Funk can be found worldwide, with examples cited from Canada, the United States, France, and Russia, demonstrating its widespread international reach. "lo fascinante del FONG es que estos artistas provienen de todo el mundo, podés encontrar productores de FONG en Canadá, Estados Unidos, Francia o incluso Rusia."

## curiosities
**Characteristics and Link to Rally House** : Funk House is a softer and more danceable variant that combines the spirit of Drift Funk with the rhythmic structure of House music. This sub-sub-genre represents a "missing link" with Rally House, featuring heavy basslines, nocturnal atmospheres, echoes of Southern Rap, and the characteristic 4x4 rhythm of House. "una variante y mucho más suave, y podríamos decir, bailable, donde el espíritu del Drift Fonc se mezca con el ritmo del house. En ese momento, ahí donde encontramos ese eslabón perdido con el Real y House, un sub-sub-genero que a un abajo es gordos, atmósferas nocturnas, ecos del rap suereño y el ritmo 4x4 característico del house."
**Producer Transition to Rally House** : Many producers who started their careers in Funk House eventually transitioned and began making their first forays into the Rally House genre. This migration highlights Funk House's role as a direct predecessor and stylistic bridge. "Muchas productores de este sub-genero se pasarán, guardan sus pinitos en el Real y House."

**Death Mill Channel** : The Death Mill channel produced a video explaining the phenomenon of Rally House, which served as a reference point for understanding this new genre. "Hace poco en un video del canal Death Mill que explicaba el fenómeno del Real y House"
**PlayStation 1 Sound and Aesthetic** : The first PlayStation console significantly influenced Rally House through its compressed sound and aesthetic. Due to the need to fit music, graphics, effects, and voices onto a single CD, audio was heavily compressed, frequencies were cut, or short, raw samples were used. The resulting dense, saturated, and somewhat noisy sound is now associated with the retro energy and charm of that era, directly inspiring Rally House. "Aquellos videojuegos tenían que meterlo todo en un único CD. Música, gráficos, efectos, voces... El resultado era un sonido de ensos, saturado y ambinudo rujoso. Que hoy asociamos con la energía y encanto retro de esa época."
**Digital Nostalgia Concept** : Digital nostalgia is a core concept that defines new internet-born genres like Rally House. It is not a nostalgia for a real past, but rather for a past mediated by screens, such as video games or low-resolution textures, evoking feelings tied to digital experiences. "en realidad no es nostalgia de un pasado real, sino de un pasado mediado por pantallas, menos de videojuegos o texturas en bajar resolución."
**New Genre Formation Paradigm** : The way musical genres are born is changing, shifting from origins in physical spaces like neighborhoods or clubs to emerging on digital platforms like playlists, forums, or memes. These new genres are created, named, and disseminated through screens, lacking a specific geography or physical scene, driven instead by a global obsession with finding and replicating emotional sounds. "Ahora ya no nacen en un barrio o una cena de clubs, sino en una playlist, foros o incluso mms."
