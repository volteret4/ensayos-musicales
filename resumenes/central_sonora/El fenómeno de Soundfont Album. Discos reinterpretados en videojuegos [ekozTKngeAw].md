# instrument - Soundfont

## curiosities
**Essential Element for Video Game Sound Reversions** : A soundfont is described as the essential element that enables musical reinterpretations to convincingly emulate the characteristic sounds of a video game. This is achieved by using sound sets extracted from various consoles or their iconic games. "El elemento esencial que hace que todas estas reversiones suenen como sacadas de un videojuego, es el Soundfond."
**Definition and Musical Application** : A Soundfont, also known as "Fountain of Sound," is a comprehensive collection of expanded sounds designed to imitate musical instruments. It serves as a versatile sound palette that can be utilized within a synthesizer or a preferred digital audio workstation (DAW) like Ableton. "El Soundfond o Fountain de Sonido, es una colección de sonidos ampliados que imitan instrumentos musicales. Es como una paleta de sonidos que se puede utilizar en un sintetizador o tu dao preferido."
**SF2 Format and Plugin-Based Loading** : Soundfonts are typically found in the SF2 format. They are loaded into music production software via a plugin that simulates a synthesizer, allowing musicians to assign these distinctive sounds to individual tracks within a MIDI composition. "Estos soundfonds, que suelen estar en formato, S, F, E, 2, se cargan en un programa de producción musical, a través de un plugin que simula un citetizador."

# instrument - MIDI File

## curiosities
**Digital Score, Not Actual Sound Content** : A MIDI file fundamentally does not contain actual recorded sound. Instead, it holds a set of instructions that direct a computer on precisely what notes to play, which instrument to use, at what volume, and at what specific moment, effectively functioning as a digital musical score or blueprint. "Un Archivo Middits no contiene sonido real, sino instrucciones que le dicen al ordenador que no te has tocado, con que instrumento a que volumen y en que momento, es como una partitura digital."
**Prerequisite for Soundfont Application** : A MIDI file of a song is a mandatory prerequisite for applying a video game soundfont to that particular track. It serves as the underlying "partitura digital" that the soundfont's instrument palette will then interpret. "Para poder aplicar el soundfont de cualquier videojuego a una canción, primero tienes que disponer del Archivo Middits de dicha canción."

# instrument - SPC 700 chip

## curiosities
**Super Nintendo Sound Processing Unit** : The Super Nintendo console incorporated the SPC 700 chip, which was instrumental in its audio capabilities. This chip provided eight distinct audio channels and introduced the ability to reproduce more realistic samples compared to earlier console sound hardware. "La super Nintendo, con su chip, SPC 700, por ejemplo, y contaba con ocho canales de audio, y la posibilidad de reproducir Samplez, algo más realistas."
**Enhanced Audio Capabilities and Effects** : The presence of the SPC 700 chip significantly enhanced the Super Nintendo's sound potential, enabling composers to craft richer and more atmospheric game environments. It also allowed for the incorporation of advanced audio effects such as echo and reverb, features extensively utilized in iconic games like Chrono Trigger and Donkey Kong Country. "Esto permitió a los compositores crear ambientes más ricos y usar efectos como Echo y Reverb, cosa que aprovecharon juegos como Chrono Traeger o Donkey Kong Country."

**Early Video Game Music Reproduction (Pre-CD Era)** : Before the widespread adoption of CDs in consoles like the PlayStation, video game music could not be reproduced as full audio streams due to severe storage limitations on cartridges. Instead, consoles relied on dedicated sound chips that generated music in real-time based on simple instructions and predefined sound sets. Games only needed to store the musical score, which the hardware then interpreted live, saving significant space. "Hasta la llegada del CD en consolas como Play Station, la música de los videojuegos no se reproducía con un asximo de audio, porque básicamente no cabían los cartuchos. En lugar de eso, las consolas usaban chips de sonido que generaban la música en tiempo real, aparte de instrucciones simples y sonidos predefinidos. Los juegos, tan solo tenían que cargar con la partitura que el hardware interpretaba tiempo real, y así conseguí anabrar mucho espacio."
**Limitations of Early Console Sound Architecture** : Early console sound hardware was significantly constrained, forcing composers to work with very limited audio waveforms, such as square, triangle, and noise waves. Furthermore, the number of simultaneous audio channels was extremely low, restricting the complexity and richness of the music produced. "todo esto empilca a trabajar con formas de audio muy limitadas, como ondas cuadradas, triangular eso de ruido, y con muy pocos canales de audios simultáneos."
**NES Sound Capabilities and Melodic Style** : The NES console featured a mere five sound channels: two square wave channels primarily for melodies, one triangular wave channel for bass, one noise channel for percussion, and one channel for very compressed, often unintelligible, sound samples. This channel limitation was the reason NES video game music was predominantly melodic, as it couldn't utilize many voices simultaneously. "la NES solo contaba con cinco canales de sonido, dos de onda cuadrada, que se solean destinadas a las melodías, uno de onda triangular para los bajos, uno de ruido para la percusión, y uno de muestras de sonido, o sea, a Samplez. Samplez muy, muy comprimido, vamos, que no se entendía nada. De ahí, que la música de los videojuegos de NES, fue ser muy melodica, ya que no podía utilizar muchas voces a la vez."
**Super Nintendo Sound Characteristics and JRPG Association** : Generally, the Super Nintendo console offered a softer and more melodic sound palette, characterized by "fake reverbs." This distinctive sound quality was often associated with and became iconic for Japanese Role-Playing Games (JRPGs). "la Super Nintendo, parlo general, tiene un sonido más suave y melodico, con reverts falsos, la cual la social jrpg."
**Mega Drive Sound Characteristics and Bass Emphasis** : In contrast to the Super Nintendo's softer sound, the Genesis Mega Drive featured a more "hoarse" audio profile. A key characteristic of its sound was a pronounced emphasis on bass frequencies, which gave its music a distinct sonic identity. "Mientras que Mega Drive tiene un sonido más afónico, donde los bajos tienen más importancia..."
**PlayStation vs. Nintendo 64 Sound (Next Generation Differences)** : The PlayStation and Nintendo 64, both next-generation consoles, had a crucial difference in their music reproduction. PlayStation, with its CD-ROM format, could play high-quality audio. Nintendo 64, however, continued using cartridges with less storage, forcing developers to rely on MIDI files and compressed samples. This resulted in a more limited yet distinct and nostalgic sound for N64 games, while PlayStation offered acclaimed orchestral soundtracks. "Mientras que Play Station ya podía reproducir música en alta calidad, gracias a su formato de CD, la Nintendo 64 utilizaba cartuchos, que tenían mucho menos espacio de almacenamiento. Esto obligó a los desarrolladores del Nintendo 64, a seguir usando Archivos MIDI y muestras comprimidas para ahorrar memoria, como resumen de la música. Como resultado, muchos juegos del Nintendo 64 tienen un sonido más limitado, con instrumentos virtuales más simples, cosa que hizo que tuviese un sonido muy característico, que he visto hoy con mucha nostalgia."
**PlayStation Orchestral Soundtracks with Compression** : The PlayStation was renowned for delivering high-quality, acclaimed orchestral soundtracks, a capability greatly facilitated by its CD-ROM format. However, despite this advantage, some games, notably Final Fantasy titles, still opted to use compressed music to conserve valuable storage space. "En Play Station en cambio, podimos disfrutar de mandas sonoras orquestales, hallabandas, reputadas, gracias a la CD. Pero es cierto que algunos juegos siguen usando música comprimida para ahorrar espacio, como es el caso de la música de los Final Fantasy."
**Technical Limitations Fostering Creativity in Game Music** : The severe technical limitations that early video game composers faced, rather than being an obstacle, actively sharpened their creativity. This environment compelled them to innovate, ultimately transforming these technical restrictions into a unique and recognizable aesthetic style in itself. "Todas estas limitaciones con las que trabajaban los compositores, les hizo agodizar su creatividad, y terminaron creando un estilo propio. En vez de ser un obstáculo, convirtieron esa restricción técnica en una estética en sí misma."
**Midi Slaps as Precursor to Soundfont Albums** : The popularity of soundfont albums, though a recent explosion, can be traced back almost a decade to a practice known as Midi Slaps. This initial form of musical production was considered less creative, involving the simple replacement of instruments in a popular song's MIDI file with sounds from another source. "su origen se debe rastrear casi una década atrás, en una práctica conocida como Midi Slaps. El Midi Slaping es una práctica considerada poco creativa de producción musical, que consiste en sustituir los instrumentos de un archivo midi de una canción popular, por otra fuente de sonido."
**Initial Perception and Evolution of Midi Slaps** : Midi Slaps were initially often perceived as lazy, annoying, or a mere technical joke, merely recycling songs with minimal reinterpretation and editing. However, starting around 2022, this trend began to be viewed differently as more elaborate versions emerged. "Mucha gente lo veía como algo vagó o molesto. Una especie de broma técnica que reciclaba canciones sin reinterpretarlas realmente. Pero, con el tiempo, y especialmente a partir de 2022, esta tendencia empezó a verse de otra forma."
**Evolution of Soundfont Versions into Coherent Atmospheres** : Over time, soundfont versions became significantly more refined and elaborate. Creators moved beyond simply changing MIDI instruments, striving instead to construct a coherent atmosphere. This involved careful consideration of how vocals, effects, and spatial dynamics would sound, aiming for a complete adaptation to the specific sound rules of an old console. "Con el tiempo empezaron a aparecer versiones más trabajadas. No se limitaban a cambiar los instrumentos de un midi, sino que buscaban construir un atmósfera coherente, pensando en cómo sanaría las voces, los efectos, el espacio, como si el disco enteros tuviera adaptado a las reglas de sonido de una consola antigua."
**Birth of the Soundfont Album Phenomenon** : This more deliberate, thoughtful, and expertly executed approach, driven by clear artistic intention, significantly improved the perception of soundfont creations. This enhanced artistic quality is credited with giving birth to the popular soundfont album phenomenon. "Esta forma de hacer más cuidada, pensada y trabajada, con una intención clara, hizo que empezase a verse con mejores ojos, y ahí es donde nace este fenómeno del Soundphone de álbum."
**Undertale (2015) - Influencing Retro Sound Perception** : The launch of the video game Undertale in 2015 was a key factor in the soundfont phenomenon's rise. Its creator, Toby Fox, composed the soundtrack using simple virtual instruments similar to those of the Super Nintendo. Despite their technical limitations, these sounds conveyed profound emotional depth, demonstrating to many, especially young people, that an orchestra wasn't required to evoke strong emotions. Undertale became a viral hit, and its music fundamentally shifted perceptions of retro sounds. "El primero es el lanzamiento del videojuego Undertale, en 2015. Su creador, Toy Fox, compuso la banda sonora con instrumentos virtuales simples, muy parecidos a los de consolas como super Nintendo. Aunque técnicamente eran muy limitados, esos sonidos lograron transmitir una enorme carga emocional, y demostraron a mucha gente, especialmente gente joven, que no hacía falta un arque estar para emocionar. Undertale se volvió un fenómeno viral, y su música ayudó a cambiar la percepción sobre los sonidos retro."
**Nintendo Gigaleak (2020) - Enabling High-Fidelity Soundfont Recreation** : The Nintendo Gigaleak in 2020, a massive unauthorized release of confidential company information, was a critical factor for the soundfont movement. Among the leaked files were original sound samples from classic games like Super Mario 64 and Ocarina of Time. This unprecedented access allowed fans for the first time to work with clean, uncompressed versions of sounds that had defined a generation, enabling much higher fidelity soundfont recreations. "El segundo punto es el Gigalig de Nintendo de 2020, una fritaración masiva de información confidencial de la compañía. Entre los archivos había prototipos, herramientas internas, documentación, y lo más importante para este fenómeno. Samples de sonido originales de juegos clásicos como Super Mario 64 o Karen Off Time. Por primera vez, los fans podieron acceder a las versiones limpias, y sin compresión, de sonidos que marcaron a una generación. Esta filtración no fue el origen de los soundphones, pero sí que permitió recrear esos sonidos con mayor fidelidad."
**Soundfont Album Popularization in 2022** : By the year 2022, the convergence of three key factors—the cultural impact of Undertale, the technical resources provided by the Nintendo Gigaleak, and the pervasive influence of the YouTube algorithm—collectively propelled the unique soundfont genre from a niche interest for connoisseurs to widespread popularity across the internet. "Con estos tres elementos, finalmente en el año 2022, este género tan peculiar pasó de un nicho para entendidos a popularizarse en la red."
**Creative Approaches to Soundfont Album Production** : When creating soundfont albums, artists often pursue distinct creative paths: some meticulously aim to replicate the original album's spirit with the highest possible fidelity, while others deliberately exaggerate the soundfont's inherent limitations to imbue the music with a fresh and novel character. "Hay quienes buscan replicar el espíritu original del disco con la mayor finalidad posible, y otros que buscan exagerar las limitaciones del soundfont para darle un carácter nuevo."
**Sonic Translation and Emotional Resonance** : The ultimate outcome of a soundfont reinterpretation is a "sonic translation" where an album, perhaps by Nirvana, Daft Punk, or Radiohead, is transformed to sound as if it had been originally released in a cartridge format for the Nintendo 64. This transformation not only evokes nostalgia but also triggers a strange and profound emotional sensation, akin to the music having traversed a parallel dimension. "El resultado es una especie de traducción sonora, donde un album de Nirvana, Daphpanko, Radiohead, suena como si hubiera sido lanzado en un formato de cartucho para Nintendo 64. Y esa transformación no solo despierta la nostalgia, también provoca una sensación extraña y emocional, como si la música hubiera viajado a través de una dimensión paralela."
**Album Cover Reinterpretation Using 3D Models** : Artists engaged in creating soundfont albums often extend their creative reinterpretations to the album covers themselves. They commonly use 3D models captured in software like Blender, adapting them to fit within the visual context of the original album artwork, thereby completing the aesthetic transformation. "Estos artistas también interpretan las portadas de los discos con el imaginario de los juegos. Suelito marcapturas de modelos 3D en Blender, que adaptan dentro del contexto de las portadas originales."
**The Phenomenon of Double Nostalgia** : A significant part of the allure and charm of these reinterpreted soundfont albums lies in what is termed "double nostalgia." This refers to the simultaneous connection listeners feel to the original song or album—a piece of music that deeply marked them—and the specific video game sounds that transport them directly back to childhood afternoons spent in front of a console. "Partida del encanto de estos discos reinterpretados está en lo que podríamos llamar una doble nostalgia. Por un lado está la conexión con la canción original, un tema que te marcó, un disco que escuchaste mil veces. Y por otro, está sonido en el videojuego que nos lleva directamente a la tarde de infancia delante de la consola."
**Emotional Power of Double Nostalgia** : The fusion of nostalgia for the original song and the nostalgic sound of video games creates a potent emotional effect. This combination connects two distinct sets of memories simultaneously, activating an emotion that, while difficult to articulate, is very easy and powerful to experience. "Al combinarlos, el efecto es potente, ya que te conecta con dos recuerdos a la vez y esa fusión activa una emoción difícil de describir, pero muy fácil de sentir."
