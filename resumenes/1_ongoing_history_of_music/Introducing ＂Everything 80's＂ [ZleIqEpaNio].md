# curiosity

**Cassette Tape Recording Format** : Cassettes were a widely used analog magnetic tape recording format designed for both audio recording and playback. Although the technology began to emerge in the 1960s, its peak cultural impact and widespread adoption as a dominant medium for audio consumption occurred throughout the 1970s and 1980s. "Cassettes were an analog magnetic tape recording format for audio recording and playback, although the birth and growth of the cassette began in the 1960s, its cultural moment took place during the 1970s and 1980s."
