# artist - Marilyn Manson

## curiosities
**Rib Removal Rumor – Denied; Floating Ribs Can Be Surgically Removed but Manson Did Not Have It Done** : One of the most enduring Marilyn Manson rumors is that he had his lowest ribs removed to facilitate auto-erotic self-gratification. Floating ribs (numbers 11 and 12) do not extend all the way around to the breastbone and can be surgically removed — a procedure sometimes done for women alongside tummy tucks to create an hourglass figure. The same rumor has been attributed to Cher, Pamela Anderson, Kate Moss, Janet Jackson, and Britney Spears. However, Manson categorically did not have this done. The rumor persists purely because he has never worked to dispel the myth, preferring to let the mystery stand. "Because Manson is so weird in the first place, it's understandable why people would believe this rumor."

# artist - David Bowie

## curiosities
**Naked in Bed with Mick Jagger – Angela Bowie's 1990 TV Claim, Denied Then Partially Walked Back** : On May 4, 1990, David Bowie's ex-wife Angela appeared on the Joan Rivers Show, newly freed from a 10-year gag order after their divorce. She stated on national television: "I caught him in bed with Mick Jagger — and yes, they were naked." Bowie was a professed bisexual during the 1970s. Mick Jagger immediately dismissed the claim as "rubbish," and Bowie's lawyer sent an open letter stating that "any implication that there was ever a gay affair between Mick Jagger and David Bowie is an absolute fabrication." Within a week, Angela was qualifying her statement. By her 1993 autobiography, the account had evolved: she had walked in, assumed they had been having sex, and that was her honest belief, but she had no proof. An additional complication: Angela may have had her own affair with Jagger during the marriage, possibly inspiring the Rolling Stones song "Angie" — or, in another rumor, that "Angie" referred to David himself using a nickname. "Did they spend the night together? Yes. Were they naked? Apparently. Was it sexual? Who knows?"

## curiosities
**Pandrogyny Project – Matching Breast Implants on Valentine's Day 2005 with Life Partner Lady Jaye** : Genesis P-Orridge, founder of Throbbing Gristle (who coined the term "industrial music" with their 1977 debut *The Second Annual Report*) and later Psychic TV, entered into a relationship with Psychic TV keyboardist Lady Jaye Breyer that was itself conceived as an ongoing performance art piece. The goal was to make the two people look increasingly alike through shared wardrobes, matching haircuts, permanent eyeliner, lip injections, oral surgeries, cheek implants, and ultimately matching breast implants, received together on Valentine's Day 2005. Their shared identity was to be called Ms. Breyer P-Orridge — a merged being making a statement about the oneness of humanity and the obsolescence of traditional gender roles. Genesis retained his original genitalia; Lady Jaye declined to have anything added. Lady Jaye died on October 9, 2007, from a previously undiagnosed heart condition possibly connected to her long-term battle with stomach cancer. "Their goal was to merge their identities into a single being."

## curiosities
**Death (November 22–23, 1997) – Ritz Carlton Sydney, Belt, No Suicide Note; Coroner: Consistent with Auto-Erotic Asphyxiation** : The night before Michael Hutchence's death began normally: a dinner with his father and stepmother at a restaurant called the Flapper of India, where he seemed fine. He returned to room 524 at the Ritz Carlton Double Bay, Sydney. Ex-girlfriend Kim Wilson (inspiration for "Suicide Blonde") came to the hotel with her boyfriend; they drank daiquiris, champagne, and beer in the bar before going to Hutchence's room. They left around 4am. Over the following hours, Hutchence made multiple calls but reached only answering machines. Early that morning he went downstairs looking disheveled to mail some letters — the last time anyone saw him alive. A maid found his body around noon on Saturday, November 23. He appeared to have gone into a violent rage — prescription pills scattered across the room, a broken hand from hitting the wall, facial cuts, furniture thrown — before fastening a leather belt to the bathroom door and hanging himself. There was no suicide note. The official coroner's verdict was death consistent with auto-erotic asphyxiation, a practice that kills an estimated 500 to 1,000 people per year worldwide. Hutchence had acknowledged in a late interview trying to achieve "the ultimate sexual high," and his mother confirmed he had spent time with sadomasochists in the preceding months. "The coroner says is consistent with auto-erotic asphyxiation, a death by misadventure."

## curiosities
**Sex Tape with Scott Stapp of Creed (1999, Florida) – Red Light District Attempted to Sell; Lawsuit Prevented Release** : In 2005, a company called Red Light District — the same distributor behind the Paris Hilton tape — came into possession of approximately 45 minutes of video footage from 1999, shot on tour in Florida, featuring Kid Rock and Creed singer Scott Stapp being pleasured by a series of groupies in a motor home. "Kid Rock and Scott Stapp have had a falling out."
