## curiosities
**Extreme Stage Antics (late 1960s - early 1970s)** : Iggy Pop was known for an extreme stage act in the late 1960s and early 1970s. This included cutting himself with broken glass, rubbing raw meat all over his body, spreading a jar of peanut butter on himself, dropping his pants, and stage diving into the crowd. "part of his stage act was to cut himself up with broken glass and then rub raw meat all over his body."
**Stoogh Manor Residence** : Iggy Pop lived at Stoogh Manor, a rented house in Detroit, which he shared with his bandmates. Any female who entered the house was asked to donate her panties to a collection stapled to the kitchen counter. "he was living at Stoogh Manor, a rented house he shared with his bandmates in Detroit."
**First Marriage Ceremony** : Iggy Pop's first marriage ceremony took place on the front lawn of Stoogh Manor. The groom, Iggy Pop, wore a Nazi uniform, and the bride was a woman nicknamed "Potato Girl." "the ceremony happened on the front lawn of Stoogh Manor with a groom wearing a Nazi uniform."
**Drug-Fueled Behavior** : A significant portion of Iggy Pop's extreme behavior was fueled by large quantities of various drugs, including LSD, Coke, and PCP. "a lot of this was powered by very large quantities of drugs, including LSD, Coke and PCP."
**Gorilla Incident with Elton John (early 1970s)** : In the early 1970s, Elton John, who had started his own record label, flew to an Iggy Pop gig in Atlanta with the intention of signing him. Elton John decided to surprise Iggy on stage by dressing up in a gorilla costume, but Iggy, being heavily strung out on PCP, believed it was a real gorilla and had an intense freak out. This incident led Elton John to leave without signing a record contract. "Elton John decided to surprise Iggy by dressing up in a gorilla costume and joining Iggy on stage."
**Post-Stooges Decline (1974)** : In 1974, after his band The Stooges had broken up, Iggy Pop's life spiraled into a period where he was described as a "babbling, wandering junkie" prone to blackouts. He had no money or place to live, and the low point came when the LAPD found him hiding under the counter of a burger joint. "In 1974, his band, the stooge's head, broken up and Iggy was reduced to a babbling, wandering junkie prone to blockouts."
**Mental Hospital Stay** : Following his discovery by the LAPD, Iggy Pop was brought before a judge who offered him a choice between jail and a mental hospital. He chose the mental hospital, and his only visitor at the LA mental treatment center was his new friend, David Bowie. "When he was brought before a judge, he gave him a choice. Jail or a mental hospital."
