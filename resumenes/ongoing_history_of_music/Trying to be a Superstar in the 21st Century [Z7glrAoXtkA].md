## curiosities

# curiosity

**Music Industry Transformation (Post-2000)** : Since 2000, metrics like album sales and chart positions have become less relevant for defining superstardom.
**Album Sales Decline** : Album sales have been dropping at double-digit rates, with digital albums dropping 25% annually, leading to the belief that 20-million-selling albums are a thing of the past.
**End of Traditional Gatekeepers** : The internet has removed the power of traditional gatekeepers (labels, radio, print media) to manufacture consensus, shifting power to the individual fan.
**Consumer Choice and Thinner Attention** : Unlimited choice has led to less agreement on which acts deserve attention, causing public interest to be spread much thinner.
**Heritage Acts' Impact** : The longevity of first and second-generation rock bands (Rolling Stones, Paul McCartney, etc.) creates a crowded marketplace where new acts must compete with legends for money and attention.
**Shrinking Royalties and the Touring Treadmill** : Shrinking royalty checks have forced even heritage acts to tour continuously, while the decimation of the musician "middle class" has forced newer artists into an exhausting cycle of recording and touring.
**The Death of the Album Concept** : The rock album as a sequenced, cohesive whole is dying; in streaming, individual tracks are consumed via playlists, and "deep cuts" receive almost zero traction.
**Streaming Payouts and Marketing Focus** : Streaming pays only a fraction of a cent per play, and because revenue is concentrated in pop and hip hop, labels have shifted marketing dollars away from guitar-based music.
**Streaming's Effect on Songwriting** : To avoid being skipped before the 30-second payout threshold, songs are becoming "front-heavy." Average instrumental intros have shrunk from 17 seconds in the 80s to under 5 seconds today, with more hooks jammed into the first half-minute.
**Spotify Skip Rates** : Data shows 25% of songs are skipped in the first five seconds, and 35% are skipped before 30 seconds pass.
