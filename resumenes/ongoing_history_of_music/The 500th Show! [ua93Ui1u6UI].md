## curiosities
**Death (April 8, 1994)** : Kurt Cobain died by suicide, found on Friday, April 8, 1994, in his Seattle home with a shotgun wound to the head and a suicide note nearby. He was 28 years old. "Kurt Cobain is in the news again. A body has been found in his Seattle home...Police in Seattle say the body of a man in his 20s has been found with a shotgun wound in the head at the home of Kurt Cobain of Nirvana."
**Previous Drug Overdose (March 1994)** : The month before his death, Kurt Cobain was hospitalized in Rome for a drug overdose, specifically from painkillers and champagne. He was recuperating from this overdose prior to his death. "Last month, the late singer of Nirvana was hospitalized in Rome for a drug overdose...He had been recuperating from last month's overdose of painkillers and champagne."
**Discovery of Body** : The body of Kurt Cobain was found by an electrician who was doing work at his Seattle home on April 8, 1994. The electrician saw the body through a window, leading police to break into a cottage above a detached garage where they found the body and a suicide note. "Police say the body was found this morning by an electrician who had been doing some work at the home. He saw the body through a window and police had to break into the cottage above a detached garage where they discovered the man and a suicide note."
**Initial Identification Delay** : There was a period of initial uncertainty regarding the official identification of the body found at Kurt Cobain's home, though various sources quickly reported it as him. "Well, still no official ID on the body found in Kurt Cobain's Seattle home, but various sources are reporting the body is that of Kurt Cobain."
**Mother's Warning and Disappearance** : Kurt Cobain's mother stated he had been missing for six days prior to his death. She had previously warned him not to "join the stupid club of other rock stars who had died early." "His mother says Cobain has been missing for six days and she says the last time she spoke with her son, she told him not to join the stupid club of other rock stars who had died early."
