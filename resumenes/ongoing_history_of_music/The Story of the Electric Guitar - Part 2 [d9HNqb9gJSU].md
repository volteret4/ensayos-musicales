# curiosity

**Music Prior to Electric Guitar** : For centuries, music largely focused on pure and clear frequencies from instruments, with any added "oomph" achieved through techniques like playing fortissimo, but within limits. The prevailing dogma was to avoid overdoing things. "For centuries, music was nice and clean.
