# curiosity
**Pervasive Ageism in Music Industry** : The music business often exhibits ageism, with a strong bias against older artists trying to break in for the first time, perceiving them as "too old," "out of touch," or past their prime, unless they are already established with numerous hits. This makes breakthroughs at older ages, such as in one's 30s or later, even more impressive. "The music business smacks of ageism. There's almost no way for you to break in for the first time if you're older. The bias is that you're just too old. Your time has passed. You're out of touch."
