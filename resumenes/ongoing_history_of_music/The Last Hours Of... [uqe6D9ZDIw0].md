**Carabiner in Suicide** : A carabiner, described as a type of shackle with a shaped latch and spring-loaded catch, was used by Chris Cornell in his suicide. An exercise band was attached to it, and the carabiner was jammed tightly into a doorframe, leaving indentation marks. "The band had been attached to a carbonyl, which is a type of shackle. And I know you've seen these things. They used shaped latches that have a spring-loaded catch."
