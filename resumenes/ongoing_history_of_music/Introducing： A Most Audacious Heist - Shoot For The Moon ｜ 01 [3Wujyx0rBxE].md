# curiosity

**"A Most Audacious Heist" Podcast Production Details** : This podcast is described as a gripping true crime story, filled with ambition, risk, and obsession, following the exploits of a 19th-century Empress and a 20th-century thief whose fates are intertwined by the CC Star diamond. Episode 1, titled "Shoot for the Moon," is hosted by Seren Jones, written and produced by Emma Jane Kirby, with research by Hannah Tava-Colorsade. Sound Design and Mix Engineering are by Toby Matamoll and Louis Nank-Manel. The podcast is a Blanchard House production for Curious Cast, with creative director Rosie Pye, head of content Lawrence Grisel, and executive producers Amika Shautino-Nolan, Lawrence Grisel, Dileva Lasquez, and Chris Duncombe. It is inspired by the book "Stealing CC Star" by Jennifer Bauer's Barney. "This is a most audacious heist. Episode 1. Shoot for the Moon."
