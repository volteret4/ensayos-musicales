## curiosities
**Formation After Sex Pistols Concert** : Peter Hook and his friend Barney decided to form a band after seeing the Sex Pistols play in Manchester, inspired to make similar noise. Barney got a guitar, and Peter found a bass. "After seeing the sex pistols play in Manchester, he and his friend Barney decided that they could make that kind of noise too."
**Makeshift Amplifier Solution** : Lacking money for an amplifier, Peter Hook and Barney ingeniously wired their instruments through the tone arm of a turntable in a family's console stereo, which provided enough sound for their early efforts. "He neither had any money for an amplifier so they figured out how to wire their gear through the tone arm of the turntable in a console stereo and buy one of their families."
**Unconventional Bass Technique Origin** : Peter Hook, inspired by punk's DIY ethos, initially played bass without formal training. His bass was slung too low, his stance was wrong, and his amp was underpowered. To be heard over loud drums and guitars during pub rehearsals, he started playing parts much higher on the fretboard, which inadvertently advanced the sound of rock and roll. "So to cut through the din, he started playing his parts far, far higher on the fretboard than normal."
