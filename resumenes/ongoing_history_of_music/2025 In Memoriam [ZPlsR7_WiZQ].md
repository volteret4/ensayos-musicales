# curiosity

**Musician “Mass Extinction” Period (2015–2016)** : The deaths of major figures such as Scott Weiland, Lemmy Kilmister, David Bowie, Prince, Glenn Frey, Keith Emerson, and George Michael marked the beginning of a sustained period of high-profile musician deaths.

**Jefferson Airplane Same-Day Deaths (2016)** : Multiple members of Jefferson Airplane died on the same day, illustrating the clustering of losses during this period.

**Pink Floyd Lyric Reference – Mortality Theme** : The lyric “Shorter of breath and one day closer to death” from _Dark Side of the Moon_ was used to frame the increasing rate of musician deaths.
