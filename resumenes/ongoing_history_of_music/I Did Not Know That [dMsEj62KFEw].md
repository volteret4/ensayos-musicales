## curiosities
**Origin of the Term "Gig"** : The word "gig," used to refer to a live music event, originated in the late 19th and early 20th century. Linguists suggest the term derived from musicians traveling between towns by horse and cart, with the cart itself being called a "gig." Upon reaching a town, the musicians would use this cart as their stage, hence the phrases "going to a gig" or "playing a gig." "When the band reached the next town, they would use the gig as their stage."
