# instrument - Bass Amplifier
## curiosities
**Sun Musical Equipment Company's Pioneering Role** : The first high-powered concert bass amplifiers were developed by Norm Sonholm (bass player for The Kingsmen) and his brother, who founded the Sun Musical Equipment Company out of frustration with existing amp technology. Their gear was later used by legendary bands such as The Who, The Rolling Stones, Jimi Hendrix, and Cream.

# instrument - Roland TR-808
## curiosities
**Accidental Success from Defective Transistors** : The Roland TR-808, a foundational drum machine for hip-hop and electronic music, achieved its iconic sound partly due to the use of defective transistors. These transistors, rejected for other devices due to being "too noisy" and having "too much inherent distortion," were repurposed as noise oscillators, which are crucial for drum machine construction.
