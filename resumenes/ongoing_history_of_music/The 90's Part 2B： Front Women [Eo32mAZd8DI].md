# artist - Ace of Cups

## genres
Rock

## curiosities
**Early Female Rock Band (1967)** : An all-female rock band from San Francisco that wrote their own songs, had a frontwoman who could "shred like a man," and a female drummer. This lineup was considered radical even in 1967, challenging prevailing norms about women in rock music. "An all-girl rock band? A bunch of women who wrote their own songs, went a checkout front that could shred like a man and a girl playing drums? Even in 1967, this was radical."
**Jimmy Hendrix's Support** : Jimi Hendrix was greatly impressed by the band's musical talent and invited them to open for a couple of his shows, signaling a significant early endorsement for the all-female group. "Hendrix was so impressed by their chops that he invited them to open a couple of his shows."
**Breakup Due to Sexism (1972)** : Despite their talent and early recognition, the band broke up by 1972, attributed to the re-emergence of old sexist attitudes in the music industry that dismissed the idea of female rock bands. "The Ace of Cups gave it a good shot, but by 1972, they'd broken up."
