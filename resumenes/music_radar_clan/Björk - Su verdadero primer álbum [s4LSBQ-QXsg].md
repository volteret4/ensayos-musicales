## albums
**Björk (1977) - Debut Album** : Recorded when she was 11 years old and released in 1977, just after she turned 12, this album features Björk singing and playing the flute. It primarily consists of covers of artists like The Beatles and Stevie Wonder, but also includes some of her own compositions and piano playing. The musical accompaniment was mainly provided by her stepfather and 2-3 of his musician friends. Her mother, Gipee, created the album artwork. The album sold 7,000 copies in Iceland, a country with 230,000 inhabitants at the time, achieving platinum status. "Acabó siendo disco de platino, porque con tampoco la fio vender 7.000 copias será francamente complicado."
**5 Albums (c. 1980-1985) - Various Independent Groups** : Between the ages of 15 and 20, Björk was a member of approximately 8 to 9 independent groups, and she featured on five of their released albums during this period. "de 5 de ellos editaron discos en los que Björk figura."
**7 to 10 Albums (c. 1980-1985) - Icelandic Local Music** : In a period of prolific local music production in Iceland, Björk is credited, either through playing or singing, on approximately 7 to 10 albums from various independent groups she was involved with after turning 15. "no creo que son 7, 3, 7 y 10 álbumes en los que Björk figura de una u otra manera en los títulos de crédito, tocando o cantando."

## songs
**Sonrisas y Lágrimas Fragments (c. 1968)** : Björk's first known recording, made when she was 3 years old, features her interpreting several fragments from "Sonrisas y Lágrimas," also known in South America as "La Novicia Rebelde" (The Sound of Music). "la primera grabación que tenemos en Bjork, es cuando ella tenía 3 años y en ella interpreta varios fragmentos de sonrisas y lágrimas o como se le conoce en Sudamérica la novice rebelde."

## curiosities
**Childhood Musical Influences** : As a child, Björk listened to artists such as Sparks, Yoko Ono, and the psychedelic works of The Beatles, particularly *Sgt. Pepper*, information gleaned from interviews with her stepfather. "Ella de niña ya escuchaba los Sparks, por ejemplo, escuchaba Yoko Ono o escuchaba incluso la parte más psicodélica de los Beatles de Sadián Piper y toda esta parte."
**Early Audiovisual Material Generation** : Björk has been generating audiovisual material since the age of 3, with many recordings available from when she was 7 to 9 years old, showcasing her participation in school plays, home performances, and self-recorded theatrical works. "es una persona que lleva generando material audiovisual desde los 3 años."
**Christmas Story Narration (c. 1970)** : At age 5, Björk narrated a Christmas story for a school function, an early example of her creative output. "un cuento de Navidad que ella narra cuando tenía apenas 5 años una función escolar."
**Early Radio Performance (c. 1976)** : At age 11, a school recording by Björk caught the attention of Icelandic National Radio, which subsequently invited her to perform live, singing and playing the flute. "fue a parar por casualidad a la radio nacionalizlandesa. Que terminó poniendo ahí, terminó llevándola ahí a tocar con su flauta y a cantar a la radio nacionalizlandesa."
**Lenient First Album Contract** : Her family initially hesitated to allow an 11-year-old Björk to record an album, but they proceeded after one small Icelandic record label offered a "permissive" and "relaxed," rather unprofessional, contract. "uno de los contratos de una de las pequeñas escograficas era bastante permisivo y bastante relajado, poco profesional."
**Mother's Album Artwork** : Björk's mother, Gipee, designed and created all the artwork for her 1977 debut album, contributing to its homemade feel. "su madre Gipee fue la que hizo la portada hizo todo el rasgortístico del álbum."
**Collector's Item Status of Debut Album** : The 1977 album *Björk* has become a highly sought-after "holy grail" for collectors of independent and alternative music, with copies currently valued between $400 and $700. "se ha convertido uno de los santos griales del coleccionismo de la música independiente y alternativa."
**Contract Termination and Grand Piano Purchase** : After her 1977 debut, Björk became disenchanted with the record label and broke her contract, which had initially allowed for two more albums. With the money she received, she purchased her first grand piano. "se quedó bastante desencantada con la disco gráfica, acabó rompiendo el contrato con la disco gráfica por el que podía haber grabado dos álbumes más. Sabemos que con el dinero se compró su primer piano de cola."
**Extensive Group Participation (Age 15-20)** : From age 15, Björk spent approximately five or six years participating in eight or nine different independent groups, experimenting across various musical genres. "Björk pasó desde los 15 años, unos 5 o 6 años en las que estuvo en unos 8 o 9 grupos."
**Early UK Concerts with Precursor Group (c. 1984-1985)** : Around 1984-1985, when Björk was about 20 years old, a precursor group to The Sugarcubes, which included most of the future Sugarcubes members, performed their first concerts in the United Kingdom, engaging in collaborations and small performances. "dieron sus primeros conciertos en el rino unido haciendo colaborando en presentaciones, haciendo pequeñas actuaciones."
**Solo Debut After The Sugarcubes (1993)** : Björk launched her solo career and released her debut album in 1993, following six or seven years of work with The Sugarcubes. "en 1993, después de 6, 7 años, trabajando con Osocarquius, Björk empiezan su trabajo en solitar arranca el debut en 1993."
**VH1 Top 10 Best Female Artists Ranking** : Björk is listed at number 29 on VH1's famous list of the 10 best female artists in history. "Bjork está de número 29 en una famosa lista que hizo VH1 de las 10 mejores artisas femininas de la historia."
**Rolling Stone Top Singers Ranking** : Björk is ranked number 60 on *Rolling Stone* magazine's list of great voices and singers. "este depuesto número 60 en la lista de las grandes voces y cantantes que hizo la revista Rolling Stone."

# artist - The Sugarcubes

## member of

## curiosities
**Precursor Group Formation** : Prior to their official formation, a group featuring "practically all the members" of The Sugarcubes performed their first concerts in the United Kingdom around 1984-1985, doing collaborations and small performances. "con su grupo anterior del que estaban prácticamente todos los miembros de Osocarquius como otro nombre. Ya dieron sus primeros conciertos en el rino unido haciendo colaborando en presentaciones, haciendo pequeñas actuaciones."
**Meteoric Rise in Independent Music** : The release of their first album marked the beginning of a "tremendous meteoric career" in independent music. "salía el primer disco de Osocarquius, y con él ya empezaba una carrera tremenda meteorica en la música independiente."
**International Success and Influence** : The Sugarcubes achieved "enormous success," quickly leaving Iceland to become a significant reference point in both the United States and the United Kingdom, maintaining their influence to this day. "tuvieron un enorme éxito, salieron de Islandia muy rápido, y fueron un enorme referente en Estados Unidos, en el rino unido, me recero hoy en día."
**Collector's Item Status** : Albums by The Sugarcubes are considered "authentic collector's items" and are highly valued in the market. "Los discos de Osocarquius son una auténtica pizza de coleccionismo, y están verdaderamente cotizados."
