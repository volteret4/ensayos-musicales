## albums
**Castig Windu Unreleased Album (1994) - The Stolen Discogs/eBay Saga** : In 1994, Aphex Twin, operating under aliases such as Castig Windu, was developing material for a potential album and proceeded to press approximately 5,000 vinyl copies as a test. Ultimately, he decided against a formal release, and the project was abandoned. Years later, after the record label where he had worked changed locations, one of these vinyls was acquired by an individual through unstated means (implied theft from the "único que había en la discográfica"). This unique copy surfaced for sale on Discogs in January 2014, advertised as the "only existing copy" of his unreleased work, sparking significant collector interest. "Lo curioso es que 1994, 20 años atrás Apex Twin estaba trabajando en material y hizo una prueba para convertir eso en un álbum. Llego a trabajar a juntar muchos materiales, algunas bases, muchas cosas y lo llegó a prensar en 5.000."
**Castig Windu Unreleased Album - Contents and Live Usage** : The unreleased Castig Windu album contained a variety of tracks. Some of these songs had previously appeared in various Aphex Twin compilations throughout the 1990s, surprising those who acquired the album. A significant portion consisted of practically unfinished songs or tracks in a "precarious" state, along with numerous rhythmic bases and conceptual ideas intended for future albums. Aphex Twin had often used material from these vinyls, particularly other samples he kept, in his live DJ sets ("pinchadas") over the years, with many of these performances existing as bootlegs. "Las que ya habían sido editadas nos llevamos la sorpresa porque habían sido editadas en distintos recobulatorios de Apex Twin durante los 90. Muchas de ellas son canciones prácticamente no terminadas o en un estado un tanto precario y luego hay muchos sonidos de bases rítmicas y de ideas que iban a ser discos."

## curiosities
**Overall Story - Thefts, Secret Discs, Crowdfunding, Videogames** : The convoluted history surrounding a specific Aphex Twin unreleased album is notable for encompassing a wide array of dramatic elements, including thefts, the emergence of secret albums, a successful crowdfunding campaign, and even a connection to the world of video games. "Tiene robos, discos secretos, tiene creofondin, tiene videojuegos, tiene de todo. Es una de mis historias preferidas de un disco."
**Genius and Alias Use** : Described as a prodigious genius and an entire style unto himself, Aphex Twin has been a "shadow genius" in electronic music for the past two decades. He is renowned for constantly changing his name, utilizing over 15 recognized aliases, including Castig Windu, and releasing material under more than six of them. His propensity for not confirming his own material or identity often leads to rumors, making him a "very complicated" and elusive figure in music. "Es ese genio de la electrónica que desde los 90 es prácticamente un estilo."
**Album Production Style** : Aphex Twin is unique in that he has never created a traditional, "solid" album intentionally designed as such. Instead, much of his published output consists of compilations of various songs he has developed over time, rather than cohesive, planned album projects. "nunca he hecho un disco como tal. Ha publicado un montón de material en el cual ha recopilado un montón de canciones que él ha ido trabajando pero nunca ha diseñado un disco. Solido como tal pensado como disco."
**SoundCloud Activity (Since 2015)** : Since 2015, Aphex Twin has maintained an anonymous SoundCloud account, identified by the username User 4, 8, 7, 3, 6, 3, 5, 3, 0, 1, where he consistently uploads new material. As of now, this account hosts approximately 178, nearing 200, tracks that have not been officially released elsewhere, with "100 temas" specifically mentioned as published in recent years. Although he has never officially confirmed his ownership of the account, it is widely accepted as his, and he has subtly referenced it in interviews. All content on this SoundCloud page is available for free download. "desde el 2015 Apex Twin tiene una cuenta de SoundCloud en el que él va subiendo material que va haciendo de manera completamente anónica."

# curiosity

**The Unreleased Aphex Twin Album Crowdfunding (2014)** : In January 2014, following the appearance of a rare, unique copy of an unreleased Aphex Twin vinyl (under his alias Castig Windu) for sale on Discogs, an individual initiated a large-scale crowdfunding campaign. The objective was to pool resources to purchase this "very expensive jewel" that had never been found before, digitize it into MP3 format, and then distribute the download link to all contributors. The campaign successfully raised $13,000 in record time, enabling the group to acquire the vinyl, rip it to MP3, and share it with everyone who participated. "una persona decidió empezar una enorme campaña de Cloud Founding para que la gente pusiese dinero y entre todos juntar el dinero suficiente para comprar esa carísima joya nunca antes encontrada y así poder sacarlo en archivo de MP3 y mandar el linda descargar todas las personas que pusieron dinero para poder pagarlo."
**Resale of the Aphex Twin Unreleased Album (2014)** : After successfully purchasing the unique Aphex Twin vinyl from Discogs for approximately $12,000-$13,000 through crowdfunding, and once the news of its existence and distribution had gained public attention, the crowdfunding group decided to auction the physical pressing on eBay. This resale event saw the album fetch a significantly higher price, selling for $46,000. "lo que hicieron fue su gastar en eBay después de que toda esta noticia y se salida la luz, esa misma prueba de prensado y operata que habían comprado en Discox por casi 12, 13 mil dólares."
**Philanthropic Use of Crowdfunding Surplus (2014)** : The crowdfunding campaign for the unreleased Aphex Twin album initially gathered more funds than required for the purchase. Following the resale of the album for $46,000 on eBay, which resulted in a substantial profit, the group took the commendable step of returning all initial financial contributions to the participants. The remaining surplus funds were then generously donated to Doctors Without Borders. "Muy loablemente, primero lo que hicieron fue de volver a todas las personas que participaron el dinero que pusieron en su día para el Cloud Founding y el resto acabó siendo donado a médicos sin fronteras."
**Markus Persson's Purchase of Unreleased Aphex Twin Album** : Markus Persson, widely recognized as the developer behind the popular video game Minecraft, acquired the only known original copy of an unreleased Aphex Twin album. He paid $46,000 for this rare vinyl through an eBay auction, making him the ultimate owner of this unique musical artifact that had previously been at the center of a crowdfunding and resale saga. "Él acabó pagando 46 mil dólares por la única copia original conocida de este disco nunca he ditado de Apex Twin"
