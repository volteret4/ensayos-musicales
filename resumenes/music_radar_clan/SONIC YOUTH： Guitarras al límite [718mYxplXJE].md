## albums
**Confusion Is Sex (1983) - Debut Album** : Sonic Youth's first album, released in the same year as Swans' debut. At the time, it was largely misunderstood, and some professional critics even questioned the band's ability to play their instruments. Today, it is considered a masterpiece of experimentation, recognized as being too advanced for its audience in 1983. "Hoy en día están consideradas dos obras maestras de la experimentación, pero en su día como siempre."

**Bad Moon Rising (1985) - Second Album** : Released shortly after the marriage of some of the band's members, this album, like its predecessor, was not understood by the public upon its release. "justo poco después de que se casaran los imprimiños de Son Youth que son matrimonio."

**EVOL (1986)** : This album, released in the second half of the 80s, contributed to the improvement of Sonic Youth's guitar sound and helped them gain an important name within the underground scene. Despite this, the band struggled financially during this period. "siguen mejorando todo el sonido de guitarra de Son Youth, siguen dándoles un nombre importante en la estenante band."

**Sister (1987)** : Another album from the second half of the 80s that continued to refine Sonic Youth's guitar sound and establish their reputation in the underground. The band, however, barely managed to sustain themselves financially, relying heavily on numerous low-paying concerts. "siguen mejorando todo el sonido de guitarra de Son Youth, siguen dándoles un nombre importante en la estenante band."

**Daydream Nation (1988) - Breakthrough Album** : This album marked a significant success for Sonic Youth, catapulting them out of underground circles. While the speaker notes it's a "master album" with "epic moments," they also express that it might not be substantially better than earlier works like *Confusion Is Sex* or *Sister*. Its success led to two major changes: the band gained greater cachet, allowing them to live comfortably from their music, and they signed with Geffen Records. "Redding Nation es el éxito que los catapulta y los saca un poco de esos círculos underground."

**Goo (1990) - Geffen Records Debut** : One of four "masterpiece" albums released by Sonic Youth on Geffen Records, marking a highly influential period for the band. The cover art for this era is particularly notable in rock history. "En Giffen sacaron cuatro discos que son cuatro cañonazos, que son Go, Dirty, Experimental, Project Set Trash a No Star y Washington Machine."

**Dirty (1992) - Geffen Records Release** : Considered by the speaker to be, by far, Sonic Youth's best album. It is one of the four highly regarded "masterpiece" albums released during their time with Geffen Records, contributing to an "incredible" era for the band. "para mí Dirty es con diferencia de mejor discos que hicieron sonichos, es una auténtica locura."

**Experimental Jet Set, Trash and No Star (1994) - Geffen Records Release** : This album is part of the quartet of "masterpiece" records Sonic Youth released while signed to Geffen Records, which proved to be a highly influential and defining period in their career. "En Giffen sacaron cuatro discos que son cuatro cañonazos, que son Go, Dirty, Experimental, Project Set Trash a No Star y Washington Machine."

**Washing Machine (1995) - Geffen Records Release** : The last of the four "masterpiece" albums released by Sonic Youth on Geffen Records, which represented an incredibly important and productive phase for the band. "En Giffen sacaron cuatro discos que son cuatro cañonazos, que son Go, Dirty, Experimental, Project Set Trash a No Star y Washington Machine."

**SYR Series Albums (Various Years) - Experimental Phase** : After their time with Geffen, Sonic Youth founded their own label, SYR (Sonic Youth Recordings), and began releasing a series of experimental albums. These albums, including *SYR2*, *SYR3*, *SYR4*, *SYR7*, and *SYR8*, marked a direct departure from traditional music, focusing on sound experimentation, artistic improvisation with guitars and instruments, and generating new sounds. They are difficult for many to digest and received poor critical reviews as they weren't structured songs, but are highly valued by those interested in instrumental discovery and new sounds. The band also incorporated collaborations with significant figures from jazz and symphonic experimental music into these projects. "los discos, la famosa saga de discos, si resón y lluv, solamente ya ni sean discos de música, son discos de experimentación de sonido."

## curiosities
**Early 80s Origins** : Sonic Youth emerged in the early 1980s, sharing the same city, influences, and almost the same timeline as Swans. They knew each other, often played concerts together, and nearly collaborated on projects, even releasing their first albums in the same year. "Empezaron en la misma ciudad al mismo tiempo, se conocían, tenían las mismas influencias."

**Guitar Experimentation Focus** : From their inception, Sonic Youth was obsessed with generating new sounds using guitars, dedicating nearly the first ten years of their career to exploring different playing techniques. They significantly pushed the boundaries of guitar experimentation. "dedican casi los primeros 10 años de su carrera a buscar maneras de tocar la guitarra."

**Affordable Gear and Innovative Techniques** : Unlike many rock bands that fetishize expensive vintage gear, Sonic Youth consistently used relatively affordable and accessible equipment. Their value came from how they played, tuned, and utilized these instruments in unprecedented ways. They employed unconventional methods like using drumsticks to pluck or strike strings, applied completely different tunings from normal ones, and incorporated atonal and arhythmic structures. "Sonic Youth nunca ha usado material especialmente costoso, especialmente fetichista."

**Pioneers of Guitar Pedal Chains** : Sonic Youth were arguably the "kings" of extensive guitar pedal chains, stacking multiple pedals (often five or more) to create unique and complex soundscapes. This technique became highly influential, with much of the guitar sound in the late 90s, 2000s, and even today bearing their sonic imprint. "Sonic Youth posiblemente fueron los regles de esas tiras interminables de pedales de guitarra."

**Guitar Destruction as Tuning** : The band went as far as destroying and breaking guitars to extract different sounds, considering the destruction itself almost a form of tuning. They also experimented with strings not originally intended for guitars to generate novel sounds. "incluso a veces habían llegado a destruir y arromper el material como las guitarras para incluso sacar material distinto porque la destrucción de las guitarras lo consideraban como casi en la manera afinarras."

**Lasting Impact on Guitar Sound** : Sonic Youth made an unparalleled contribution to the evolution of guitar sound in music history, with their experimental techniques and tonal innovations influencing countless artists who followed. "Creo que muy pocas personas en la historia han aportado más al sonido de guitarra como el que ha aportado Sonic Youth."

**Consistent Career Path** : In contrast to bands like Swans, which radically changed their style over time, Sonic Youth maintained an incredibly solid and consistent career. For 30 years, they steadfastly defended their vision of experimental rock, continuously improving and innovating within their own framework without losing their roots. "La carrera son Youth es una de esas carreras increíblemente solidar y corrente."

**Early Critical Doubt** : Upon the release of their first album, *Confusion Is Sex*, professional critics harbored serious doubts about whether the band members even knew how to play their instruments, reflecting how advanced and unconventional their sound was for the time. "tenían serias dudas de que esa gente supiese tocar la guitarra."

**Early European Success** : Sonic Youth consistently enjoyed significant success and positive critical reception in Europe from their very first day. This early recognition across the Atlantic actually influenced their eventual success and acceptance in America, a trajectory also seen with bands like Moby. "Son Youth desde el primer día en Europa. Siempre tuvo muy buena crítica, muy buena cojida."

**Financial Struggles Before Breakthrough** : Before the success of *Daydream Nation*, Sonic Youth struggled financially. They extensively toured, playing many concerts with low impact and little pay, simply to make a living from their music. "Daba muchísimos conciertos de poca repercusión de poco dinero entonces estaban todo el viajinando dando conciertos para poder vivir de ello."

**Geffen Records Partnership and Grunge Influence** : Signing with Geffen Records, which was crucial to the grunge movement in the late 80s, was a profoundly important development for Sonic Youth. Their presence at Geffen meant that aspects of their unique guitar sound were incorporated into grunge albums being made there, while Sonic Youth, in turn, absorbed fresh ideas from younger grunge bands, helping to update and revitalize their sound throughout the 1990s. "la entrada de Sonidjuz a Giffen Records hace que muchísimas partes del sonidjuz se incorporen en los discos de granchos que se estaban haciendo en Giffen y por el otro lado también Sonidjuz se nutrió mucho de muchas ideas frescas de gente del grancho que estaba trajando en Giffen."

**Paradoxical Early 90s Career** : Despite the early 1990s being described as potentially the worst stage of their career by some metrics, Sonic Youth experienced one of their best creative moments thanks to their collaboration and album releases with Geffen Records during this same period. "aunque son durante los primeros de los 90, tuvo la peor etapa de su carrera con diferencia a peor de su carrera Sonidjuz tuvo el mejor momento de su carrera gracias a que entraron en Giffen."

**Post-Geffen Experimental Phase** : After their influential period with Geffen, Sonic Youth entered a new and complex experimental phase, founding their own record label, SYR (Sonic Youth Recordings). This label became the outlet for extremely experimental, improvisational albums that pushed boundaries far beyond conventional music, challenging listeners who sought traditional songs. "funda su propia discográfica S e I G R sonichos Recordings, con la que empieza a sacar unos discos experimentales, que son una locura, una hida de olla."

**Band Dissolution and Members' Divorce** : The band is currently dissolved, a consequence of the divorce between its two main members. They reportedly do not have a good relationship and have not given interviews together. One of the members (she) has released an autobiography that has received positive critical comments. "la banda está disuerta, los dos miembros han divorciado y no terminan de tener buena relación ni siquiera han entrevistado juntos, ella ha sacado una biografía interesante."

# genre - Grunge

## curiosities
**Geffen Records as a Gestational Hub** : The grunge movement fundamentally developed at Geffen Records in the late 1980s, making the label a crucial entity for defining the sound of rock in the United States. "en Giffen se gesta el movimiento grancho en los finales de los 80."

**Influence from Sonic Youth** : The entry of Sonic Youth into Geffen Records led to significant parts of their distinctive guitar sound being incorporated into the grunge albums produced at the label. This cross-pollination enriched American rock, explaining why the guitar sound of the grunge movement sometimes felt very close to Sonic Youth's. "muchísimas partes del sonidjuz se incorporen en los discos de granchos que se estaban haciendo en Giffen."

# label - Geffen Records

## curiosities
**"Mother" of the Grunge Movement** : Geffen Records is described as the "absolute mother" of the grunge movement, playing an immensely significant role in shaping the sound of American rock. The grunge movement itself developed within the label in the late 1980s. "Giffen que es la madra absoluta del movimiento grancho y este movimiento discográfico es muchísimo más importante de lo que parece para definir el sonido del rock de Estados Unidos."

**Cross-Pollination with Sonic Youth** : The signing of Sonic Youth to Geffen Records was highly influential. Sonic Youth's sound permeated the grunge albums being made at Geffen, and conversely, Sonic Youth absorbed fresh ideas from the younger grunge bands also working there, which helped to update their sound significantly during the 1990s. This interaction profoundly nourished American rock. "ese tan de en que hizo que Sonidjuz se entreme clase con bandas del grancho en la discográfica Giffen, nutrió muchísimo todo el rock americano."

**Relevant Discographic Movement** : The interactions and developments at Geffen Records, particularly concerning the grunge movement and Sonic Youth, represent one of the most relevant and impactful discographic movements in the history of rock music. "este es increíblemente relevante, son unos los movimientos discográficos más relevantes del rock."
