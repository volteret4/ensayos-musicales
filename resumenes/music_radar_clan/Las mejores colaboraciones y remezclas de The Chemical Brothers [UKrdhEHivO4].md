# artist - The Chemical Brothers

## genres
Electronic

## albums
**Surrender** : This album features the collaboration "Let Forever Be" with Noel Gallagher, a song described as one of the best The Chemical Brothers have ever produced. "Let Forever Big, que estaba en el Surrender, que era la canción de los que Michael Brothers en la que cantaba Noel Gallagher."
**Cambuizas** : This album includes "The Test", an 8-minute collaboration with Richard Ashcroft. This track is highlighted for its length, power, and significant popularity in its day. "se hicieron con Richard Ascroff en el Cambuizas cuando sacaron de test."
**First Compilation Album** : This compilation features an exclusive extra song, "The Golden Path," a collaboration with The Flaming Lips, which was not released on any of their regular studio albums. "está en el primer recopilatorio que sacaron los que Michael Brothers, que tenía una canción extra que habían hecho con los Flaming Leaps de Golden Path."
**Latest Album** : This album includes collaborations such as "Wide Open" with Beck and "Under the Neon Lights" with St. Vincent. It was noted for potentially being less electronic in sound compared to their previous works. "su último disco al mejor más tan tan electrónico."

## songs
**All Rights Reserved** : This song is a collaboration with Klaxons and was ranked as the tenth best collaboration mentioned. "all rights reserved que cantaban con los claxons."
**Wide Open** : Featured on The Chemical Brothers' latest album, this track is their sole collaboration with Beck. Beck's performance on the song is praised as fantastic, making it one of the album's best. "la única colaboración que tienen con beck en la que beck está fantástico y nos traen uno de los mejores temasos del último disco."
**Under the Neon Lights** : This song is a collaboration with St. Vincent, also from The Chemical Brothers' latest album. St. Vincent's vocals on the track are highlighted as fantastic. "la voz de son Vincent está fantástica también este trabajo de los chemicals Brothers."
**Word, YVG** : This collaboration with Bez (Bezorton in the transcript) is noted as one of The Chemical Brothers' first important partnerships and is described as a fantastic song. "una de las primeras colaboraciones importantes de los que Michael Brothers se aprecipe de todo Word, YVG, con Bezorton, una de las primeras personas con las que colaboraron, una canción fantástica que hacían con Bezorton."
**The Boxer** : A collaboration with Tim Burgess, this track was one of The Chemical Brothers' most frequently played songs during its time. "uno de los temazos que más sonaron de los que Michael Brothers en la época."
**The Golden Path** : This song is a collaboration with The Flaming Lips. It was an exclusive extra track released on The Chemical Brothers' first compilation album and was not found on any of their other records. "una canción extra que habían hecho con los Flaming Leaps de Golden Path."
**Auto-esquentro** : This collaboration with Bernard Sumner of New Order was described as an "awesome" and "great track." It includes the lyric: "Sometimes I feel that I am just under the storm, whoever's running deep right through my door. You make it far and far and on the grind, you always get there." "esta colaboración que hicieron Bernard Samer de los New Order, con los que Michael Brothers fue la caña, fue un temazo. Sometimes I feel that I am just under the storm, whoever's running deep right through my door. You make it far and far and on the grind, you always get there."
**Kelle o Kelle** : A powerful collaboration featuring Kele, the lead singer of Bloc Party. This track is singled out as one of The Chemical Brothers' most potent songs. "una de las canciones más potentes que hicieron los que Michael Brothers."
**Let Forever Be (1999)** : This acclaimed collaboration with Noel Gallagher is from The Chemical Brothers' album *Surrender*. Described as "tremendous" and one of their best songs, it's also noted for its "awesome" music video and stems from a close, long-standing friendship between the artists. "es tremenda, con diferencia una de las mejores canciones que hicieron los que Michael Brothers."
**The Test** : An 8-minute collaboration with Richard Ashcroft, found on The Chemical Brothers' album *Cambuizas*. This "very long, very powerful, tremendous song" was highly popular in its day and is considered The Chemical Brothers' best collaboration due to its perfect blend of electronic and rock music, emblematic of British indie. "sin duda es la mejor colaboración que han hecho los que Michael Brothers, porque es que está en el camino exacto entre la electrónica y la música rock, la sígabritamica."

## curiosities
**Collaborative Prowess** : The Chemical Brothers are known for their extensive history of collaborations with a wide array of artists, including prominent figures like Noel Gallagher, Richard Ashcroft, Bez, The Charlatans, and St. Vincent, making them ideal for bridging genres. "han colaborado con Noel Gallagher con Richard Astro con Bezorton con los charlatans han colaborado con todo el mundo Consum Vincent."
**Remix Expertise** : In addition to their collaborations, The Chemical Brothers are highly recognized for their excellent remixes of tracks by other important UK artists. "no solo son colaboraciones, también han hecho remézclas buenísimas, gente importantísima del reino unido."
**Enduring Friendship with Oasis** : The Chemical Brothers have maintained a very close relationship with Oasis, which has led to multiple collaborations and well-received remixes, such as their version of "Falling." "la relación de oasis y de los que Michael Brothers siempre ha sido cercanísima."
