## albums
**Donbillie de Truth (2005) - Critical Success Despite Fragmentation** : This album received very good reviews and acceptance, with some critics even stating it was the best material Oasis had released in years since their "golden age." Despite its success, the recording process was completely fragmented, with members composing material separately and only coming together for the recordings, functioning more as a group of musicians with a common goal rather than a cohesive band. "incluso llegaron a decir que fue lo mejor que ha sacado oasis en años después de su época dorada."
**Diger Un Sol (likely Dig Out Your Soul) - Divergent Songwriting & Internal Strife** : This album received quite good reviews, notable for featuring significantly less material from Noel Gallagher and a considerable amount from Liam Gallagher, alongside contributions from non-Gallagher members, a previously "unthinkable" situation. The recording process was particularly complicated due to internal band issues, including Liam Gallagher's abrupt departure during sessions to get married without informing anyone. "la propia grabación del diger un sol fue demasiado complicada, tenemos ya a esa neidot en la cual liamgala guerrilla en plena grabación, se larga y desaparece para volver a casarse sin decirle nada nadie."
**Hit En Que Mis Fue (likely Heathen Chemistry) - Shifting Songwriting Dynamics** : Recorded around 2001, this album marked a significant shift in Oasis's creative process, as Noel Gallagher did not compose all the material for the first time. Material from Liam Gallagher began to appear on the album, indicating a change in the band's songwriting distribution and a perceived decline in Noel's interest in composing solely for the band. "no el Galagher por primera vez no componen todo el material de oasis empieza a ver material de Liam Galagher."

## curiosities
**MTV Unplugged Absence (1996)** : Oasis was invited to perform for MTV Unplugged, a common honor for major 90s bands. Liam Gallagher, however, did not attend, claiming throat problems, forcing Noel Gallagher to perform the entire concert, providing vocals. This event was widely seen as "the beginning of the end" for the Gallagher brothers' relationship. "eso ya fue como el principio del fin de la relación de los hermanos Galagher."
**"Disaster Tour" (2002)** : The band's tour in 2002 was notorious for a series of unfortunate events, including arrests, traffic accidents, and continuous concert cancellations, which led to it being described as an "authentic disaster." "tenemos esa famosa gira del desastre de oasis en el que hay arrestos, hay accidentes de tráfico, hay continuas cancelaciones de conciertos acaba todo hecho un auténtico desastre."
**Member Departure and Replacement (2004)** : In 2004, a member referred to as "A Languay," who was described as "quite important within the first trajectory of Oasis," abandoned the band. This individual was subsequently replaced by Zak Starkey, the son of Ringo Starr. "en el 2004 a Languay abandona la banda que era una persona además bastante importante dentro de la primera trayectoria de oasis y es reemplazado por nada menos que por el hijo de Ringo Star."
**Glastonbury Performance Disaster (2004)** : Oasis's concert at Glastonbury in 2004 was an "authentic disaster" where the band performed "quite badly." The audience ended up booing them, and critics "destroyed them" with negative reviews, signaling that the band was "practically made into pieces." "fue un auténtico desastre, la banda tocó bastante mal, la gente acabó abuchándolos, la crítica los destrozó y ya era un poco un síntoma de que esa banda estaba prácticamente hecha pedazos."
**Fragmented Internal Relations (2005-2006)** : During an extensive and successful tour between 2005 and 2006, the members of Oasis, particularly the Gallagher brothers, "practically didn't speak to each other." They often gave interviews separately and would only interact on stage for performances, with Noel Gallagher even joking about not knowing if Liam would show up for a concert. "no el gala guerrilla llegaba a bromear diciendo que a veces iba al concierto sin saber si lean gala guerr iba a aparecer en el concierto porque no sabía nada de él."
**Liam's Unannounced Marriage During Recording (during Diger Un Sol)** : During the recording sessions for the album Diger Un Sol, Liam Gallagher abruptly "took off and disappeared" to get married without informing anyone in the band. This act was seen as "one of the straws that broke the camel's back" regarding the escalating internal conflicts. "liamgala guerrilla en plena grabación, se larga y desaparece para volver a casarse sin decirle nada nadie, ya es como una de las gotas que colma el vaso."
**Official Breakup at Rock en Seine (August 29, 2009)** : The band officially separated on August 29, 2009, at the Rock en Seine festival in Paris. Minutes before their scheduled performance, organizers announced that Oasis "no longer exists," following a violent physical altercation where Liam Gallagher, reportedly very drunk and accompanied by his new wife, "came to blows" with Noel. "en el cual minutos antes de salir a Waysys tienen que dar una nota, los organizadores diciendo la famosa nota de novel gala guerr, Waysys ya no es que no existe."
**Controversial Post-Breakup Award Acceptance (2010)** : In 2010, Liam Gallagher accepted an award for Oasis for "the best album of the last 30 years" alone. During his speech, he thanked everyone who had been involved with Oasis but notably omitted his brother, Noel. He then famously threw the award into the audience, a moment described as "one of the peak moments" in Liam's life, marking the beginning of an "active hatred" between the brothers. "ni siquiera se lograece a su hermano ni siquiera lo menciona fue muy polémico, para el final coger el premio y lanzar al público y tirarlo."

## curiosities
**Struggling Solo Career** : Liam Gallagher's solo career has been described as "somewhat regular," contrasting with Noel's success. His previous project, PDI, "practically ended up ruined," and there is uncertainty about the success of his current solo material. "Liam Galagher le ha ido un poco regular, ha tenido el proyecto de PDI que hay que prácticamente ha quedado arruinado."
**Refusal to Perform at MTV Unplugged (1996)** : Liam declined to perform with Oasis at their MTV Unplugged concert, citing throat problems, which led to Noel taking over vocals and was identified as a key moment in the deterioration of the brothers' relationship. "Liam Galagher no fue porque no quiso, alegó que tenía problemas de garganta y al final fue no el Galagher el que hizo el concierto poniendo su voz."
**Relationship with Nicole Pelton (2001)** : Around 2001, Liam Gallagher began a relationship with Nicole Pelton, which reportedly led to a "very bad relationship with the rest of the group" (Oasis). "Liam Galagher se separa empieza su relación con Nicolás Pelton, la cual tiene bastante mala relación con el resto del grupo."
**Increased Songwriting Contribution (during Hit En Que Mis Fue)** : For the first time, Liam Gallagher started contributing his own material to Oasis albums, such as on "Hit En Que Mis Fue," which demonstrated a shift in songwriting dynamics away from Noel being the sole composer. "empieza a ver material de Liam Galagher incluso tenemos algún material de Liam Galagher, tiene no está mal."
**Mid-Recording Marriage (during Diger Un Sol)** : During the recording of the "Diger Un Sol" album, Liam Gallagher left the studio without telling anyone to get married, an act cited as a major point of conflict and a "last straw" for the band. "liamgala guerrilla en plena grabación, se larga y desaparece para volver a casarse sin decirle nada nadie."
**Drunken Altercation and Oasis Breakup (August 29, 2009)** : Liam arrived "totally drunk" at the Rock en Seine concert in Paris with his new wife, leading to a physical fight ("llegaron a los puños") with Noel Gallagher minutes before their performance, which directly caused the announcement of Oasis's immediate dissolution. "liamgala grabía llegado totalmente borracho a ese concierto de parís, con su nueva esposa, había sido en desastre llegaron a los puños."
**Controversial Award Acceptance (2010)** : When Oasis received an award for "best album of the last 30 years," Liam Gallagher accepted it alone, famously thanking everyone involved except Noel, and then throwing the award into the audience. This act was seen as a "very controversial" and "peak moment" in his life, marking the intensification of his feud with Noel. "ni siquiera se lograece a su hermano ni siquiera lo menciona fue muy polémico, para el final coger el premio y lanzar al público y tirarlo."
**Formation of Bedi (May 2010)** : In May 2010, seemingly "rebounding" from the Oasis breakup, Liam Gallagher announced he would continue his career with former Oasis members under the new band name "Bedi." "en mayo de 2010 yo creo que he rebotado a liamgala guerr anuncia que sigue su carrera con los miembros de Waysys aunque ahora se va a llamar Bedi."
**Self-Funding and Producer Hiring for Bedi (2010)** : Liam Gallagher founded his own record label specifically for the Bedi project and spent a "fortune" to hire Steve Lillywhite, one of the most important and expensive producers in the UK, known for working with U2, to ensure a high-profile comeback. "liamgala guerr funda su propia discográfica para este nuevo proyecto y no solo eso sino que coge a Steve Lily Wipe que es uno de los productores más importantes y más caros del reino unido que ha trabajado con gente como youtube, en el que se gasta una auténtica millonada para hacer un poco una vuelta por todo lo alto."
**Personal Management Issues** : Despite his musical endeavors, Liam Gallagher is described as someone who "doesn't even know how to manage himself," "doesn't even know how to handle his affairs," and continues to face "many anarchy problems." This suggests that his solo career might be "doomed to disaster," not due to musical quality but due to his lack of self-management. "es una persona que no sabe ni autogestionarse, no sabe ni llevar sus asuntos, es una persona que sigue con muchos problemas de anarquía."

## curiosities

"al final fue no el Galagher el que hizo el concierto poniendo su voz."
**Decreased Songwriting Contribution (during Diger Un Sol)** : On the album "Diger Un Sol," Noel Gallagher contributed "very little material" compared to his previous role, where he would compose "100% of the material," indicating a notable shift in his creative output for Oasis. "tenemos aquí ya muy poco material de novel gala guerr, bastante pero no como antes que el 100% del material de novel gala guerr."
**Independent Material Development** : Even while still part of Oasis, Noel Gallagher was already working on new material, but he explicitly stated that it was "not for Waysys" (Oasis), signaling his intention to pursue projects outside the band. "incluso a no el gala guerrilla tenemos entrevistas en la que nos dice que está trabajando en material pero no para Waysys."
**Joking about Liam's Unreliability (2005-2006)** : During a successful tour, Noel Gallagher would joke that he sometimes went to concerts "without knowing if Liam Gallagher was going to appear" because of their minimal communication and strained relationship off-stage. "no el gala guerrilla llegaba a bromear diciendo que a veces iba al concierto sin saber si lean gala guerr iba a aparecer en el concierto porque no sabía nada de él."
**First Solo Concerts (February 2010)** : Noel Gallagher held his first solo concerts in February 2010, shortly after the official breakup of Oasis, although his solo album would be released several months later. "justo en febrero de 2010 tenemos ya los primeros conciertos en no el gala guerr como solista aunque su material tardaría un poco."
