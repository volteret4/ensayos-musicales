## albums
**Blue Lines (1991) - Pioneering Trip Hop Album** : Released in 1991, this album is credited with marking the beginning of an entirely new musical style: trip hop. It stands out because, unlike grunge, rock, or rap, it provides a definitive starting point for its genre. Widely considered one of the most important albums in music history, it is praised for its complexity and completeness, blending electronic music with pop, reggae, jazz, hip hop, soul, and funk. "nunca nada había sido como ese disco."
**Protection** : Geoff Barrow and Tricky worked on parts of this album after their contributions to *Blue Lines*. "trabajó con ellos simplemente en blue lines y parte en el protection."

## songs
**Unfinished Sympathy (1991)** : Described as a masterpiece and a pinnacle of music, this song is remarkably simple yet deeply rich, encompassing jazz, soul, and funk influences. It features Shara Nelson's voice, which evokes jazz singers and the intense blues lyrics of Aretha Franklin. Astonishingly, its intense base rhythm was created using a glass and a spoon. "la base de ancínice simpatí está hecha con un vaso de cristal y una cuchara."
**Safe from Harm (1991)** : This track is a notable blend of soul and funk, underpinned by electronic techno bases. "esa mezcla entre el sol y el funky con esas bases de electrónica tecno."

## curiosities
**Trip Hop Originators** : Massive Attack is widely acknowledged as the "parents" of trip hop, a genre that blends pop and electronic music. Their debut album, *Blue Lines*, released in 1991, is recognized as the definitive starting point for this new style, a rare instance of a genre having a clear inaugural record. "ellos fueron los padres del trip hop."
**Profound Reggae Influence** : Reggae music is cited as one of Massive Attack's most significant influences, particularly evidenced by the very slow tempos found in their music. Some of their songs are direct adaptations or versions of classic 1970s reggae tracks. "Massive Attack tiene una de sus grandes influencias en el riggy tenemos ese canciones de Massive Attack que directamente son versiones de canciones de música riggy de los 70 clásicas."
**Group Formation and Evolution** : The group initially formed as a reaction, or "rebound," following the disintegration of the Wild Bunch movement, resulting in a larger, less cohesive collective. Over time, key contributors like Shara Nelson, Tricky, and Andy Vowles progressively departed, eventually reducing the group to its current two-member formation. "el grupo era un grupo bastante más grande que no era excesivamente cohesionado porque se fundó por rebote tras la desolección del movimiento wildbunch y se haran el sonabándolo el grupo triki a poco más trabajo con ellos está bajón poco más en el protection pero acabaría desarrollando su carrera independiente incluso andio booz también acababa abandonando el grupo hasta quedarse en la formación que tenemos hoy en día de sólo dos miembros."
**Disappearance of Fusion Style** : As the group's membership decreased due to various departures, the characteristic style of intricate blending and fusion of numerous genres, for which Massive Attack was known, gradually faded. Despite this, their early work remains a pinnacle of musical innovation. "este estilo de tanta mezcla de tanta fusión de tantos estilos es algo que a medida que ha ido reduciendo su grupo ha ido poco a poco desapareciendo."

# genre - Trip hop

## curiosities
**Unique Origin Point** : Unlike many other genres such as grunge, rock, or rap, which lack a single definitive "first album," trip hop uniquely has a clear starting point with Massive Attack's *Blue Lines* in 1991. "el blue lines que salió en 1991 que marca el pistoletazo de salida de todo un nuevo estilo de música."
**Defining Sound of the 90s** : Trip hop emerged as one of the major pillars and key protagonists of 1990s music, both within the pop landscape and broader musical trends. "el trip hop ha sido uno de los grandes pilares y uno de los protagonistas de la música que hemos tenido en los 90."
**Musical Characteristics** : At its core, trip hop features very simple electronic bases, drawing inspiration from Detroit techno, characterized by repetitive and monotonous two or four-bar rhythms that establish distinct beats. It uniquely incorporates the novel influence of very slow reggae music, contributing to its tranquil, listenable, and commercially accessible nature. "todas sus bases son bases electrónicas muy sencillas seredadas del techno de Detroit y tiene esos dos compases esos cuatro compases muy repetitivos muy monótonos que dan unos ritmos muy marcados pero tienes esa influencia tan novedosa de la música riggy de tiempos muy lentos."
**Lasting Influence** : The genre's pioneering work, particularly by Massive Attack, served as a direct source of inspiration for many subsequent artists, including contemporary acts like DX, Gorillaz, DJ Shadow, Portishead, and Moby. "hoy en día tenemos a DX, tenemos a gorillas y tenemos a mucha gente que bebe directamente de las fuentes del trip hop que tuvimos en los 90."

# genre - Electronic Music

## curiosities
**80s Evolution and Limitations** : During the 1980s, electronic pop and new wave music were characterized by their imaginative and fun qualities. However, they were generally considered to lack musical sophistication and complexity, often prioritizing the "idea" or concept over technical quality. "era muy imaginativa y era muy divertida pero musicalmente no era lo más sofisticado ni lo más complejo que había."
**Late 80s Decline** : By the late 1980s, the electronic pop formula became "quite exhausted," leading to a significant decrease in quality material after 1987. Major electronic groups like Kraftwerk were also experiencing a downturn in their creative output, reflecting a general slump in electronic music production. "a final de los 80 esa forma la ya estaba bastante agotada ya prácticamente a partir del 87 ya tenemos muy poco material de calidad de electrónica pop tan famoso que se ha nacido en los 80."
**Reinvention by Trip Hop** : The arrival of Massive Attack's *Blue Lines* in 1991, with its complex fusion of electronic elements with pop, reggae, and jazz, marked the "starting shot" for the reinvention of modern electronic music, which previously seemed an "unthinkable" blend to critics in the 80s. "el pisto letazo de salida de la reinvención de la música la itónica moderna."

# curiosity

**"First Album" Phenomenon in Music History** : Unlike many established genres such as grunge, rock, or rap, where the exact first album is often debated or unknown, trip hop stands out for having a clear and recognized starting point with Massive Attack's *Blue Lines* in 1991. "nunca tenemos un punto de partida me refiero no sabemos con certeza cuál fue el primer disco de grunch que se hizo en el mundo no sabemos con certeza cuál fue el primer disco de rap ni tampoco cuál fue el primer disco de rock pero sin embargo tenemos la suerte de tener este disco el blue lines que salió en 1991 que marca el pistoletazo de salida de todo un nuevo estilo de música."
