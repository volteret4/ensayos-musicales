# genre - House music
## curiosities
**Early Arrival in Manchester (1986)** : House music arrived in Manchester around 1986, two or three years before the rest of the United Kingdom. Theories for its arrival include Tony Wilson bringing vinyls from US trips, Happy Mondays members bringing LPs from New York/Chicago, or vinyls arriving from Amsterdam and Ibiza alongside certain drugs. "es en 1986 cuando se produce el gran cambio cualitativo de sonido Manchester porque se incorpora y llega al rino nido en la música House, sabemos además con mucha certeza que el movimiento House de música llega a Manchester antes que al resto del reino unido"
**Integration into Manchester Sound (1984-1986)** : Between 1984 and 1986, the Manchester movement began incorporating the fast and highly danceable basslines of House music. This fusion led to a new, more danceable, agile, fun, and "discoteca-oriented" vision for Manchester music. "el movimiento Manchester empieza a incorporar esos sonidos en la música House, esas vas rápidas y súper bailables de la música House y nace ahí entre 1984 y 1985, 86, esa nueva visión y ese núcleo duro de la música Manchester mucho más bailable, mucho mas agil, mucho más divertida y sobre todo mucho más discotecera"
**Evolution to Acid House in the UK (1990s)** : The initial fun, disco-oriented, and accessible House music brought from Chicago was eventually superseded by Acid House in the UK during the 1990s. Acid House is described as harder, more radical, and "bastillera," effectively "destroying" the earlier, more lighthearted House sound that had integrated with indie music. "nacía el Aisid Jados en el reino unido, el Jados más duro y más radical que se acababa cargando ese es el Jados divertido, discotequero, fácil que tenía y que se habían traído de Chicago para convertirlo en la música Bastillera que se le hais y Jados durante los 90."

# venue - Hacienda
## curiosities
**Founding and Role in Manchester Transition (1982)** : Hacienda was a nightclub founded by Tony Wilson in Manchester in 1982. Although it didn't directly initiate the Manchester movement, it was a central protagonist and likely spearheaded the transition from Post-Punk to the Manchester sound, playing a key role in the local band scene and early House music adoption. "también funda la discoteca la cienda en 1982 en Manchester. La cienda no inicia el movimiento Manchester, aunque es protagonista absoluta pero sí que posiblemente inicia la transición a ese movimiento."
**Pivotal for House Music in Manchester** : Hacienda was the venue where the first House music vinyls from Chicago began to play in Manchester, thanks to Tony Wilson's vision and DJs like John da Silva, who established the House sound with "Ibiza nights." It was key in introducing these sounds to the city before the rest of the UK. "esos primeros vinilos en música House que invadieron al Reino Unido dos o tres años antes ya estaban en Manchester y ya estaban sonando en la cienda, gracias a DJs como John da Silva o co, gracias a la visión de Tony Wilson"

# curiosity
**The Manchester Movement (Madchester) - Timeline and Characteristics** : The Manchester movement emerged between the Post-Punk era of the early 1980s and the Britpop/more commercial music of the 1990s. Its diffuse beginnings are situated between the mid-1980s (1985-1986) and 1991-1992, with a core period from 1986 to 1991. The sound was distinctively more joyful, danceable, agile, and electronic than Post-Punk, featuring happier lyrics and faster rhythms. "Esos uno de esos movimientos que salió entre el postpack de primeros de los 80 y el Britpop y la música un poco más comercial de los 90. Ahí en medio tenemos pues el import, tenemos el su case y una de las cosas yo creo que más movidas y que más gancho tenía a la era ese sonido Manchester."
**Tony Wilson's Foundational Role** : Tony Wilson, a Manchester native, music critic, and founder of Factory Records and the Hacienda nightclub, is regarded as the "absolute father" of the Manchester movement. His vision and initiatives were instrumental in fostering the scene and discovering key bands. "el protagonista del movimiento es Tony Wilson. Tony Wilson, si el mismo Tony Wilson que había fundado Factory Records y que había descubierto a Joey Division es el padre absoluto del movimiento Manchester."
**Early Gestation of the Manchester Sound (1982-1984)** : The core of the Manchester sound began to develop rapidly within the city between 1982 and 1984, driven by the dynamic interaction between the Hacienda nightclub and a vibrant local scene of bands, even before these groups gained widespread fame. "en esa ciudad entre la cienda y este movimiento local de bandas ya había un movimiento muy agile, yo creo que ya se había gestado el núcleo duro del sonido Manchester entre la cienda y todas estas bandas locales"
**Golden Age and UK Explosion (1986-1988)** : The Manchester sound underwent a major qualitative shift in 1986 with the integration of House music, ushering in its "golden age." The movement then exploded and expanded across the entire United Kingdom in late 1987 or early 1988, fueled by rapid developments in 1988, including significant band publications and the endorsement of John Peel. "ya en 1986 aparece la gran época dorada del movimiento Manchester y a final de ese 87 justo en el verano de 1987, tal vez primeros 88 es cuando ya explota el movimiento Manchester y sale de Manchester para expandirse en todo el Reino Unido"
**John Peel's "Something is Happening in Manchester" Article (1988)** : Around 1988, the influential John Peel, described as a "great visionary," published an article in the New Musical Express titled "Something is happening in Manchester." This article was pivotal in making the burgeoning Manchester scene visible and exciting to a wider audience. "John Pill, el gran visionario del Reino Unido, se iba y decía la grandísima frase en un artículo para la new musical express algo está pasando en Manchester, ese era el título de un artículo que yo creo que ya sea visible a todo el mundo que Manchester pasaba algo muy interesante"
**The Love Story of Electronic and Indie Music** : The Manchester movement fostered a "curious love story" between mass-market electronic music and indie music. This fusion produced a highly danceable indie sound with electronic bases, creating a "golden age of danceable music" and serving as a foundation for genres like Trip Hop. "gracias a este movimiento Manchester, no hace una incuriosa historia de amor entre la música electrónica de gran consumo y la música indie que nos deja ese indie super bailable con bases electrónicas"
**Decline due to Major Label Commercialization** : Following its explosion in popularity around 1988, the Manchester movement was "literally destroyed" by major multinational record labels. These labels, in their pursuit of commercial success, rapidly produced the sound, often with questionable quality, by pressuring bands to become as commercial as possible, thereby eroding the movement's authentic, underground, and "carefree" essence. "después de esa explosión de popularista en 88 ya en las grandes multinacionales a devorarlo todo, ya explote el movimiento Manchester y literalmente se lo carga."
**Transition to Britpop and "Death by Its Own Success"** : By the early 1990s, the Manchester movement's essence had eroded, leading to a transition towards Britpop. The movement "died within its own success" as the new Britpop wave became heavily dominated by multinational corporations and mass consumption, losing its original spirit. "se moría dentro de su propio éxito al movimiento Manchester."
**Unclear Reference to Electronic/Indie Collaborators** : The transcript describes "those great electronic groups, almost indie, that collaborate with many indie people" who brought about a golden age of danceable music. The specific names are garbled in the transcript as "los que en el bro del esfad pollo es límito a esta gente," making identification unclear, but highlighting a key aspect of the era's musical fusion. "tenemos a esos grandes grupos electrónicos casi indiscuélaboran con mucha gente indie pues los que en el bro del esfad pollo es límito a esta gente que nos trajo una época dorada en música bailable."
