# genre - Techno

## curiosities
**Historical Significance** : Techno is recognized as one of the most important musical movements in history, representing a major musical revolution. It has had a significant impact on music and beyond, transforming many things forever. "el techno es uno de los movimientos musicales más importantes de la historia es una de las grandes revoluciones musicales que hubo en la historia."
**Musical Definition - Structure and Tempo** : Techno music is characterized by a simple structure, typically ranging between 120 and 150 BPM, though most often around 120 BPM. It employs a 4x4 rhythmic structure with a 16-step sequence. "el techno realmente es una estructura muy sencilla es entre 120 y 150 p.m. pero casi siempre son alrededor de 120 p.m. una estructura de 4x4 una secuencia de 16 pasos."
**Musical Definition - Core Elements** : The fundamental elements of techno include a strong and deep kick drum, complemented by a snare, a clap, and a bass line. The bass line may not always be a traditional bass but can be a repeated low sound, often manipulated with pitch effects. Techno frequently incorporates many effects, and typically features one or two simple, looping melodic lines, as its true essence lies in its rhythmic base. "el secreto lo realmente increíble del techno está aquí en su base ahí está el verdadero corazón de la música techno en su base."
**Primitive Brain Connection to Rhythm** : There is a unique and primitive connection between the human brain and constant rhythmic bases or percussion. Neurological studies have revealed this connection to be deeply ingrained, possibly even in our DNA, explaining why the first form of music invented was percussion and how children instinctively move to a constant rhythm. "hay una relación muy primitiva dentro de nuestro cerebro que conecta con las bases rímias constantes y el tecno llevó esa conexión a unos nuevos niveles que nunca nadie los había llevado."
**Emotional Transmission and Psychological Impact** : Techno is highly effective at transmitting specific states of mind and emotions. It is frequently used in cinema and television to convey feelings like stress, anxiety, speed, tension, struggle, and during chases, due to its ability to connect with anxious states. "se usa muchísimo el tecno para poder transmitir ciertos estados de ánimo y ciertas emociones en las películas estados estrés de ansiedad de velocidad de tensión de pelea de lucha persecuciones."
**Marketing Applications and Consumer Behavior** : Beyond artistic expression, techno has extensive applications in marketing. Its capacity to transmit sensations is leveraged in fashion, where it's a protagonist on runways (like in a recent Chanel collection), generating excitement. Retail stores and brands use techno music to create a state of excitement and anxiety that encourages increased purchasing. "usan la música techno para generar ese estado de excitación y de ansiedad que hace que la gente compre mar."
**Fitness and Dopamine Secretion** : Techno music is commonly played in gyms because it impacts heart rate and stimulates dopamine secretion, thereby exciting individuals during workouts. The constant rhythmic base also aids in synchronization with exercise machines. "el técno os lo ponen en el gimnasio casi siempre porque sabemos que inciden el ritmo cardíaco pero también la secreción de dopamina, con lo cual los ponen técnos para excitaros en el gimnasio."
**Influence in Industrial Documentaries** : The rhythmic nature of techno makes it a frequent choice for background music in documentaries focusing on machinery and industrial processes, enhancing the depiction of mechanical movement and efficiency. "muchas documentales de maquinarias e industriales ponen música techno de fondo cada vez que ponen a máquinas."
**Ubiquitous Unconscious Consumption** : People are constantly exposed to techno music in various contexts without realizing it, because its physical and psychological effects on the body are well-understood and intentionally utilized. "os ponen música techno todo el tiempo sin que os veis cuenta precisamente porque saben que hace efectos físicos sobre vuestro cuerpo."
**Origins and European Explosion** : Techno was born in Detroit but subsequently traveled to Europe, where it dramatically expanded into an immense variety of electronic music movements. "El techno es nacen de Troy pero viene Europa y en Europa explota en una cantidad ingente de movimientos electrónicos."
**Defining Moment in Electronic Music** : The emergence of techno is considered a pivotal moment in electronic music, comparable to The Beatles' impact on pop music, marking a clear "before and after" point in the genre's history. "el nacimiento del techno es el equivalente al nacimiento de los Beatles para la música pop, como marca el antes y después en la música electrónica."
**Widespread Musical Influence Post-1985** : Techno profoundly changed the understanding of music and its influences have spread across nearly all musical styles created after 1985. It led to a significant increase in the prominence of rhythmic bases across various genres. "ha tenido influencias en toda la música postura en 1985."

# genre - Hip Hop

## curiosities

"Muchísimo hip hop moderno de casi finales de los 90 cambia, las bases clásicas del Breakbeat por bases muchísimo más técnico."
