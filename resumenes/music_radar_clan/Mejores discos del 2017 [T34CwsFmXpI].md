# artist - Arca

## genres
Electronic

## albums
**Arca (2017) - Accessible Experimentation** : For those who followed Arca from the beginning, this album might be considered the least strong. However, its electronics are less aggressive, less experimental, and less rough, and Arca also sings on it, making it a much more accessible work. While previous works, especially the first, were technically superior and more respected in electronic circles, this album potentially brings experimental electronic culture to a broader audience. "este que ya la electrónica no está agresiva no están experimentando no están rudo también canta nos ha dejado un trabajo muchísimo más accesible."

## curiosities
**Gateway to Experimental Electronic Culture** : This album is considered truly interesting for its potential to bring difficult, intellectual, and experimental electronic sounds to a wider, mainstream audience. "tal vez por la repercusión que puede llegar a tener este trabajo es un trabajo verdaderamente interesante."

# artist - Wolf Alice

## albums
**Visions of a Life (2017) - Coherent Eclecticism** : This album is highly eclectic, even more so than their debut. It incorporates a wide range of styles, sometimes echoing the hard and aggressive tones of Courtney Love or PJ Harvey, sometimes the classic singer-songwriter, and at other times the depressive feel of Lou Reed. Despite mixing many styles, time changes, and influences, the album achieves a truly coherent, uniform, and logical sound. "es como un mejón en muchísimos estilos hay muchísimos cambios de tiempo en las canciones hay muchísimos cambios de estilo hay muchísimas influencias y sin embargo a pesar de tener todo eso metido dentro del disco suena de una manera verdaderamente coherente es una uniforme y es un alógico."
**First Album (Year Not Given) - Fantastic Debut** : Wolf Alice's first album was fantastic, setting a high standard for their work.

## curiosities
**Diverse Influences and Comparisons** : The album's sound has been compared to the aggressive styles of Courtney Love and PJ Harvey, the introspective nature of classic singer-songwriters, and the depressive undertones of Lou Reed, all integrated into a unified whole. "a veces nos recuerda pues ese toque tan duro y tan agresivo pues que podía tener corny lozo que podía tener cuña adrina a veces nos recuerda caja una canta autora clásica a veces tiene ese toque tan depresivo que podría tener incluso lurrid."
