# artist - Kit Koala

## genres
electrónica
jazz
downtempo

## albums
**Injatio (2000) - Experimental Scratching** : This album, released in 2000, was considered late for a work purely dedicated to scratching. Kit Koala blends electronics with jazz and downtempo, creating a unique style that combines classic old-school scratching with distinct experimental methods. The album showcases incredible new ways of working with scratching. "Kit Koala nos hace esa electrónica en mezclada con el jazz con el down tenido con todas estas formas y hace una suya y además combina ese scratching más clásico de la vieja escuela con unas maneras distintas, hacer el scratching que nos deja cosas increíbles."
**Carpatán el Syndrome - First Album Mention** : This is mentioned as Kit Koala's first album, preceding the experimental work done in 2000. It is noted that by 2000, it was already quite late to release an album solely dedicated to classic scratching. "se acaba sobre el primer disco Carpatán el Syndrome, es curioso porque ya en el 2000 era como muy tarde para hacer un disco muy dedicado al scratching."

## songs
**Ner Ball** : This track from Kit Koala's 2000 album highlights his innovative scratching techniques, demonstrating how he converts scratching into more than just a sound.
**Drank Trampet** : On this track, Kit Koala transforms scratching into an instrument, specifically mimicking a trumpet sound. This technique is described as a new way of playing the trumpet rather than traditional scratching, going beyond the established norms of classic scratching. "sobre todo en Drank Trampet hace el scratching con un sonido de trompeta que es como que es como una nueva manera de tocar la trompeta no son tan del scratching clásico sino como una nueva trompeta hace un trabajo verdaderamente increíble que se sale de los canones que teníamos dentro de lo que es el scratching clásico."

## curiosities
**Scratching as an Instrument** : Kit Koala is noted for converting scratching not merely into a sound effect, but almost into a full-fledged instrument. This experimental approach is particularly evident in tracks like "Drank Trampet." "Kit Koala convierte el scratching no tanto en un sonido sino casi en un instrumento."

# artist - PPDLUX

## albums
**Super Sound (1999)** : Released in 1999, this album gained fame because one of its songs was featured in a Levi's advertisement. It showcased PPDLUX as a great DJ artist with innovative scratching techniques, featuring simple scratching with a very dry equalization. "PPDLUX en 1999 sacaba Super Sound, sobre Mardisco, se hacía famoso porque una de las canciones salía en un anuncio de Levis si no os recuerdo mal."
**Beatitude (2003)** : This follow-up album, released in 2003, features the renowned track "Salami Fiber." The album further demonstrated PPDLUX's unique approach to scratching, particularly with its speed-scratching mastery. "luego en el disco siguiente en el Beatitude, creo que era el 2003, yo con el día de acá el 2003, hace la famosa canción Salami Fiber que es una obra maestra del scratching de velocidad."

## songs
**3 times a player** : This opening track from "Super Sound" features a very simple scratching technique combined with a super dry equalization, which produced a fantastic and distinct sound. "arrancaba con el primer tema 3 times a player en el que hacía un scratching muy sencillo pero con una ecualización súper seca que nos dejaba un sonido fantástico."
**Before you live** : Another track from "Super Sound" where PPDLUX employed fantastic accelerated scratching, creating a sound completely different from classic scratching. This revealed his artistry as a DJ. "en el Before you live hacían un scratching acelerado fantástico que nos dejamos un sonido totalmente distinto a lo que entendemos por el scratching clásico y descubriamos que entre el PPDLUX había un gran artista de DJ."
**Salami Fiber (2003)** : Featured on the "Beatitude" album, this song is recognized as a masterpiece of speed scratching, showcasing exceptional velocity and precision in its execution. "hace la famosa canción Salami Fiber que es una obra maestra del scratching de velocidad."

## curiosities
**Levi's Commercial Appearance** : A song from PPDLUX's 1999 album "Super Sound" was famously used in a Levi's advertisement, contributing to the album's recognition. "se hacía famoso porque una de las canciones salía en un anuncio de Levis si no os recuerdo mal."

# artist - DJ Cantar

## genres
Japanese Hip Hop

## concerts

## instruments

## albums
**EP (2017) - Two Songs** : In 2017, DJ Cantar released a small EP featuring two songs, one of which was a collaboration titled "City Lights." This release continued to showcase his unorthodox and masterful scratching style. "este 2017 sacaba un pequeño EP con dos canciones, una de ellas era City Lights con una colaboración."

## songs
**City Lights (2017)** : This song, a collaboration from his 2017 EP, demonstrates DJ Cantar's unique ability to manually mark time using scratching. His performance on this track further cemented his reputation as an unorthodox genius of scratching. "en esa canción vamos DJ Cantar DJ Cantar marca los tiempos manualmente con el scratching que es una pasada pero es que vamos, este hombre es un genio de la scratching poco ortodoxo vamos."

## curiosities
**First Japanese DMC World DJ Champion** : DJ Cantar made history in 2002 by becoming the first Japanese artist to win the prestigious DMC World DJ Championship, a highly respected global competition for the greatest DJs. He had participated in several finals before achieving this victory. "DJ Cantar fue muy famoso porque fue el primer japonés en ganar el mundial de DJs, el DMC World."
**Historic 2002 DMC Performance** : His 6-minute performance at the 2002 DMC World DJ Championship is considered legendary and a turning point in music history, particularly the last 25 seconds which seemed physically impossible at the time. He utilized a completely different approach to turntablism, using various needles and heads on each deck and manipulating the non-sound parts of vinyl records (like the center) for noise equalization at incredible speeds. "recuerdo esos 6 minutos de DJ Cantar que pasaran a la historia de la música sin duda... y es que hacía una manera completamente distinta de trabajar los platos, él trabajaba con distintas agujas y distintos cabezales en cada uno de los platos para ajustar mejor el sonido a lo que quería hacer, trabaja con partes del disco de vinilo que no son sonido como por ejemplo la parte central para acualizar ruidos y esa acualización a velocidad de bértico que yo creo que jamás en la vida había visto nada parecido dejó a todo el mundo con la boca abierta en el mundial del 2002."
**Inspiration for Japanese DJs** : DJ Cantar's groundbreaking victory at the 2002 DMC World DJ Championship paved the way for a new generation of great Japanese DJs to become prominent figures in the global DJ scene. "a partir de ahí comenzó una nueva estela de grandes DJs japoneses en el 10 y World que hoy en día son como grandes parte de los grandes protagonistas."

# artist - Mr. Oizo

## genres
underground experimental electronica (French scene)

## albums
**Analo-guon-s-attaque (1999)** : Released in 1999, this album is credited with inaugurating the French underground experimental electronica scene. Mr. Oizo's work on the album, especially his equalization and time-marking with scratching, was considered incredibly wild and innovative. "en 1999 se acaba analo-guon-s-attaque, un disco que es la leche yo creo que era uno de los tíos que inauguraba la estena underground experimental electrónica francesa de la que luego vendría toda la agenación de Vanguard Records."

## songs
**Analo-guon-s-attaque (1999) - Title Track** : The title track from his 1999 album showcases Mr. Oizo's incredible equalization of scratching, which produces a super penetrating sound. This track is highlighted as a significant example of the album's experimental sound design. "desde la canción que da nombre el disco analo-guon-s-attaque, cualiza el Scratchin de una manera increíble que nos ha un sonido como súper penetrante."

## curiosities
**Pioneer of French Experimental Electronica** : Mr. Oizo is recognized as one of the key figures who launched the underground experimental electronica scene in France in the late 1990s, a movement that later influenced artists associated with Vanguard Records. His album "Analo-guon-s-attaque" was pivotal in establishing this new sound. "creo que era uno de los tíos que inauguraba la estena underground experimental electrónica francesa de la que luego vendría toda la agenación de Vanguard Records."
**Revolutionary Equalization and Timing** : His work on the 1999 album "Analo-guon-s-attaque" is characterized by insane equalization and precise time-marking through scratching, creating a distinct and innovative sound that was highly experimental for its time. "Mr. Oizo hace unos trabajos de cualización de marcar los tiempos con el Scratchin que son una puñetera locura."

# artist - Yeop Barrow

## member of
PortiGer

## instruments
Turntable
Mixing console

## albums
**PortiGer - First Album** : This debut album by PortiGer features incredible and unprecedented scratching moments, exemplified in tracks like "Doom" and "Wondering Star." At the time, many believed Yeop Barrow's scratching involved complex post-production due to its seemingly impossible nature. "recordamos el primer disco de PortiGer que nos deja momentos increíbles de un Scratchin completamente jamás visto como puede ser en la canción de Doom o como puede ser en la canción de Wondering Star."
**PortiGer - Second Album** : The second album from PortiGer escalated the level of scratching experimentation, featuring astonishing moments in songs such as "Cowboys," "Elisium," and "Onlea U," which left listeners questioning how such sounds were produced with a turntable. "al día segundo disco de PortiGer que tenía esos momentos Scratchin tan increíbles como puede ser en el Cowboys o como puede ser en el Elisium o en el Onlea U que era como ya un nuevo nivel."
**Rosalind Leif and New York (Live Album) - Live Revelation** : This live album from PortiGer was a pivotal release, as it allowed audiences to witness Yeop Barrow performing his seemingly impossible scratching techniques in real-time. It demonstrated that he achieved these complex sounds using only a turntable and a mixing console, pushing classic scratching to unprecedented levels. "después salió el primer disco en directo de PortiGer el Rosalind Leif and New York en el cual veíamos por primera vez como Yeop Barrow estaba haciendo todo eso y lo estaba haciendo en directo y lo único que usaba ese hombre era un plato y una mesa de mezclas como todos."

## songs
**Doom** : A track from PortiGer's first album, noted for its incredible and never-before-seen scratching, showcasing Yeop Barrow's innovative approach.
**Wondering Star** : Featured on PortiGer's debut album, this song contains remarkable scratching moments that challenged perceptions of what was possible with turntables.
**Cowboys** : This track from PortiGer's second album presented new levels of incredible scratching, contributing to the band's reputation for experimental sound.
**Elisium** : A song from PortiGer's second album, highlighted for its astonishing scratching techniques that pushed the boundaries of the art form.
**Onlea U** : From PortiGer's second album, this track is described as reaching a new level of scratching complexity, causing listeners to marvel at Yeop Barrow's turntable mastery.

## curiosities
**Most Unorthodox DJ and Scratcher** : Yeop Barrow, the creative leader of PortiGer, is regarded as the most unorthodox DJ and scratcher ever. He is considered a genius and a "messiah of the turntable" due to his unconventional methods. "el que yo creo que es con diferencia el DJ y Scratchin menos ortodoxo que jamás ha existido que es John Barrow de PortiG el líder creativo de PortiGer es ese hombre trabajando los platos, es un genio, yo creo que es el mesías del plato y sobre todo el mesías del plato por ortodoxo."
**Invention of New Scratching Method** : He invented a completely new way of working with scratching. Initially, many listeners of PortiGer's first two albums believed his unique sounds were a result of complex post-production, involving equalization and computerization, because producing such sounds directly with a turntable and hand seemed physically impossible. "Él inventa una manera completamente nueva de trabajar el Scratchin hasta tal punto que yo creo que muchos cuando habían salido los dos primeros discos de PortiGer pensábamos que el Yeop Barrow no hacía Scratchin realmente sin ocasiones Scratchin normal y luego llevaba un complejo proceso de cualización y informatización después que le llevaba a hacer ese sonido porque hacer ese sonido directamente con el plato y la mano era físicamente imposible."
**Live Performance Revelation** : The live album "Rosalind Leif and New York" conclusively demonstrated that Yeop Barrow achieved his groundbreaking scratching techniques in real-time during performances, using only a turntable and a mixing console. This revealed his visionary experimentation, pushing classic scratching to unimagined new levels. "nos dábamos cuenta de que este hombre había llevado el Scratchin clásico a los nuevos niveles que nadie jamás había imaginado y yo creo que Yeop Barrow a lo mejor de la manera clásica del Scratchin no es el mejor Scratcher pero de la manera apocor to doxa yo creo que es el gran visionario y el gran experimentador del Scratchin."

# instrument - Turntables

## curiosities
**As a Sound Instrument** : The turntable is described as an instrument that, when manipulated with hands through scratching, can generate new sounds, evolving beyond its primary function into a tool for musical creation. "mover el plato con las manos y convertir el plato y también lo un instrumento para generar sonidos nuevos."
**Advanced Customization by DJ Cantar** : DJ Cantar's innovative use of turntables involved working with distinct needles and heads (cartridges) on each turntable. This customization allowed him to precisely adjust the sound to his creative intentions, contributing to his physically impossible-looking performances. "él trabajaba con distintas agujas y distintos cabezales en cada uno de los platos para ajustar mejor el sonido a lo que quería hacer."
**Yeop Barrow's Unorthodox Mastery** : Yeop Barrow, creative leader of PortiGer, is considered a "messiah of the plato" (turntable) for his unorthodox and completely new methods of working with scratching. He achieved complex sounds with just a turntable and a mixing console, challenging the perceived limits of the instrument. "el mesías del plato y sobre todo el mesías del plato por ortodoxo él inventa una manera completamente nueva de trabajar el Scratchin."

## curiosities

"trabaja con partes del disco de vinilo que no son sonido como por ejemplo la parte central para acualizar ruidos."

# curiosity

**Evolution of Scratching** : While classic scratching, established and perfected in the 1980s and 90s with dedicated albums, focused on precision and virtuosity, a new wave of experimental scratching emerged. This new approach moved beyond traditional paths, producing incredible and seemingly impossible sounds through less conventional methods. "siempre ha habido como esos grandísimos puristas el scratching, el scratching además yo creo que ya fue como descubierto y perfeccionado muy pronto con lo cual tampoco la gente se ha salido mucho de esos caminos clásicos el scratching... el scratching tan clásico dando su camino para entrar en un camino de experimentación y que nos dejó un scratching que era mejor independientemente de que sea tan preciso, tan virtuoso y entró en maneras muy poco todos las hacer el scratching y nos ojo un sonido que parece increíble y parecién posible que alguien puede hacer."
