**Debate Over Musical Validity** : Trap music is currently facing significant criticism, with many people creating videos and writing articles to declare it regrettable, useless, and not contributing to music, even outright stating that it is "not music." # curiosity

**Historical Pattern of Criticizing New Genres** : The argument that a new music style is monotonous, simple, repetitive, and lacks creativity is not new. This assertion has been made whenever a new movement has appeared over the last 50 years, often targeting genres that later become foundational. "este argumento no es nuevo a soledarse en muchos casos y cada vez que ha ido apareciendo un nuevo movimiento durante los últimos 50 años se ha hecho esta afirmación."
**Widespread Problem of Problematic Lyrics** : Misogynistic, violent, or otherwise inappropriate lyrics are not exclusive to Trap music but are a pervasive issue across many established music genres. This suggests a broader societal problem that should prompt reflection on how such messages are accepted in music generally, rather than singling out one genre. "este comportamiento en las letras vejatorias hacia las mujeres y otros colectivos o que hacen apología de la violencia por desgracia no es nuevo."
**Reasons for Rejection of New Music** : The intense rejection and categorical statements against new music styles, such as Trap, are often rooted in three common issues: a narrow perspective and limited knowledge of music history, a significant ignorance of the specific genre's diversity, and the "narcissistic fallacy." "creo que detrás del gran rechazo que hay del trap o sobre todo esa afirmación es tan categorica que hace alguna gente para decir que el trap no es música me parece que realmente están hechas en base a tres cosas."
**Narcissistic Fallacy in Music Criticism** : The "narcissistic fallacy" is described as a common cognitive bias where individuals conclude that if they do not like a particular type of music, it cannot be considered music, because if it were music, they would inherently like it. This fallacy prevents people from granting the benefit of the doubt to things they dislike. "la falacia narcisista que es bastante conocida que es a mí me gusta la música a mí no me gusta el trap consecuencia el trap no puede ser música porque de ser música me gustaría."
