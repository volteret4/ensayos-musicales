## albums
**Everyday Robots** : This is one of the solo albums released by Damon Albarn, recognized as a relatively known work following his extensive career with various bands and projects. It was released after his album "Dr Dee." "Antes de Every Day Robots Daemon Alban ya había hecho un album que era Doctor D."
**Dr Dee** : An album by Damon Albarn, which is considered fairly well-known among his followers and preceded his solo album "Everyday Robots." "Daemon Alban ya había hecho un album que era Doctor D que esta mende hace unos años."
**Son's from Wonderland (2016)** : This album is Damon Albarn's musical interpretation of "Alice in Wonderland," specifically composed for an artistic theatre play in the United Kingdom in 2016. It is regarded as one of his significant musical contributions. "la interpretación musical que había hecho Daemon Alban de Alice en el país de las maravillas en este disco que es Son's from Wonderland."
**Monkey: Journey to the West** : An opera composed by Damon Albarn, which draws inspiration from monkeys. This particular fascination developed during his initial involvement with the Gorillaz project. "Daemon Alban hizo una ópera sobre monos... inspirado un poco en los monos, en el furor sobre los monos que le dio cuando empezó con Gorilla."
**Mali Music (2002)** : An album dating from 2002, born from Damon Albarn's journey through Africa in search of new sounds. He traveled to Mali, where he met and collaborated with numerous local musicians, serving as the producer and the central figure for the project. The album integrates African drum rhythms and drum machines popular in the region and is considered a "secret gem" in his discography. "Daemon Alban solo se va a recorrer África en Búsqueda de Sonidos Daemon Alban es un grandísimo seguidor de los ritmos de tambores africanos, lo que sigo no hace como el África andab Viajo a Mali y conocí a muchísimos músicos locales con el que cabría haciendo este disco en el cual es el productor."

## curiosities
**Invented Collaboration Group** : Space Monkeyz was not a traditional band but an entity invented by Damon Albarn and his friends specifically for their collaboration with Gorillaz on the "Laika Come Home" album. They focused on creating music with Caribbean, African, and Rige rhythms. "Space Monkage no existe Es un grupo que se inventó también Daemon Alban con unos amigos que hacían música caribeña, africana y de ritmos Rige con el que hizo este disco."
**One-Time Project** : Space Monkeyz was a one-off project that only existed for its collaboration with Gorillaz on "Laika Come Home" and did not continue to create music thereafter. "no volvieron a existir nunca más."
