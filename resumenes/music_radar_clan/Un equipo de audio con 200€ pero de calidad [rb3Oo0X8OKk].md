# instrument - Tocadiscos (Turntable)

## curiosities
**Second-hand Purchase Recommendation** : For beginners, it is recommended to start with a good quality second-hand turntable. These can be found for less than 100 euros and provide a decent audio experience without a significant initial investment. "hoy por fin de segunda mano podéis comprar tocariscos de muy buena calidad que están por menos de 100 euros."
**Age Limit for Used Turntables** : It is advisable to avoid purchasing turntables manufactured before 1990 (older than 25-30 years). Older models may have non-standard components, making repairs challenging and finding compatible capsules difficult. "Yo no compré a tocariscos antes de 1990 porque os podéis meter en tocariscos que no se han estandar, hay mucho tocariscos de los 80 que no es muy bueno."
**Integrated Preamplification Requirement** : A crucial feature for a simplified beginner setup is a turntable with integrated preamplification. This allows for direct connection to active speakers, eliminating the need for a separate preamplifier, and can be identified by "phono/line" switches or dual output jacks on the back. "yo ya solo compraría tocariscos que tengan preamplificación integrada."
**Recommended Models for Beginners (Second-Hand)** : Several specific turntable models are suggested as good quality, affordable options for beginners looking to spend around 100 euros or less on the second-hand market. These include the Technics SL-D2 (often available for about 100 euros), Pioneer PL-90 (90s models), various Sony models, and the Audio-Technica LP60 (which can be found for approximately 60 euros second-hand). "teneis desde el técnico de los 90, el DSE 2 hay muchos DSE 2 que se están vendiendo por 100 euros."
**Durability and Ease of Component Replacement** : The fundamental design of turntables is considered robust and well-established. The primary components subject to wear, such as the capsule and headshell, are typically easy to replace, contributing to the longevity of the equipment. "El fundamento del tocariscos es bastante precario, lo conocemos hace muchísimos años y casi todo el desgazil de terriero del tocariscos está centrado en la cápsula y el capazal que es muy fácil de cambiar."

# instrument - Altavoz (Speaker)

## curiosities
**New Purchase Recommendation** : Speakers are generally recommended to be bought new, as it is difficult to detect subtle issues in used units, such as hairline cracks in the membranes, which are not easily visible or repairable. "yo los altavoces sí los compraría nuevos."
**Active Speaker Requirement** : Active speakers are essential for a simple entry-level setup because they incorporate built-in amplification. This allows them to be connected directly to a pre-amplified turntable, simplifying the overall audio system and avoiding the need for an external amplifier. "Lo otro es unos altavoces activos, esto lo conectáis directamente al tocariscos y funciona perfecto."
**Edifier R1700BT Model Details** : The Edifier R1700BT active speakers are highlighted for their excellent quality-price ratio, typically costing around 150 euros. They feature Bluetooth connectivity, effective bass and treble controls, separate woofer and tweeter drivers for distinct frequency ranges, and a wooden cabinet construction. "Estos que son los que usan el otro vídeo son unos edificiarlos, R10700 con Bluetooth, están en torno a los 150 euros, me parece uno de los que tienen mejor relación calidad precio al mercado."
**Budget-Friendly Edifier Alternative** : A more affordable Edifier speaker model, priced around 100-120 euros, is also mentioned as a viable option. Although it lacks Bluetooth and has a less refined cabinet design for sound dispersion, it still offers a fantastic quality-price ratio and good power output. "edicier también tiene unos, no recuerdo mi nombre pero os pondré abajo en la descripción, que están en torno a los 100, 120 euros, no tiene Bluetooth, la caja no es irregular, no dispresa también o es sonido, pero también tiene una relación que vea la precio fantástica y tiene muy buena potencia."
**Dual Input Sensitivities** : Quality speakers, such as the recommended Edifier models, often include separate audio inputs like "AUX" for general audio equipment and "PC IN" for computers. These inputs are designed with different power sensitivities to accommodate the varying output levels from sound cards and dedicated audio devices, allowing multiple sources to be connected simultaneously. "tienen distintas sensibilidades de potencia, porque las tarjetas gráficas y los equipos sonidos no trabajan a misma potencia."
**Separate Drivers (Woofer & Tweeter)** : For optimal sound reproduction, particularly across different frequency ranges, it is considered crucial for a speaker to feature separate drivers. This includes a woofer dedicated to bass (graves) frequencies and a tweeter for treble (agudos) frequencies. "yo nunca me compraría un altavoz que no los tenga separados."
**Wooden Cabinet Importance for Sound Quality** : The material of the speaker cabinet significantly impacts sound quality, with wooden cabinets being superior to plastic. Wood offers better resonance characteristics, preventing the hollow, "egg-shell" sound often associated with plastic enclosures. Even more affordable agglomerated wood cabinets are preferable to plastic, while higher-end options use veneered or multi-layered wood for enhanced performance. "las cajas de madera son lo mejor que hay para la calidad de sonido."
**Non-Symmetrical Cabinet Design for Resonance Control** : High-quality speakers frequently feature non-symmetrical cabinet designs with inclined sides and irregular internal shapes. This construction is vital for minimizing internal sound resonances, as it prevents sound waves from repeatedly reflecting between parallel surfaces (a "ping-pong" effect). This design improves sound dispersion and reduces unwanted coloration, especially in the bass frequencies. "si os acordáis el sonido entra dentro y cuando la caja es perfectamente cúbica tiene un problema, fijáis, imagina que tenemos una caja donde los lados son paralelos y entre el audio, cuando las cajas tienen un lado completamente paralelos, el sonido se queda rebotando de una otra, hace como una especie de timpón y el sonido queda generando resonaciones de una caja otra."
**Speaker Power Measurement (RMS)** : Speaker power is commonly quantified in three ways: power per individual speaker (combining bass and treble power), total power of the entire system, and RMS (Root Mean Square). RMS indicates the average power output during real-world use, accounting for the non-uniform power emission across various frequencies and music types. An RMS value exceeding 50 watts is generally considered adequate for home audio use. "el RMS en marca el grado medio de potencia que tienen los altavoces en uso real, sinceramente un RMS de más de 50 batidos para casa yo creo que está bastante bien."

**Beginner Audio Equipment Budget** : For individuals starting to build a home audio system, a budget ranging from 200 to just over 500 euros is sufficient to acquire a functional system with decent sound quality, typically comprising a second-hand turntable and new active speakers. "con 200 euros por ejemplo 200 y poco euros os podéis comprar algo para empezar que está bastante bien."
**Gradual Upgrade Approach for Audio Systems** : It is advised for beginners to initially purchase basic audio equipment and then progressively upgrade individual components, such as the amplifier or turntable capsule, as their understanding and interest in audio technology advance. This approach is preferred over making a large initial investment in an expensive system. "Es mejor empezar por un equipo básico y ir mejorándolo o ir comprando cosas de más calidad según te va gustando o según vas conociéndolo."
**Risk of Overspending for New Audio Enthusiasts** : Investing a large sum, such as 800 euros, into a high-end audio system when first starting out or for typical home listening is considered inefficient. Beginners often face challenges in properly setting up, equalizing, and fully utilizing powerful equipment designed for larger venues, leading to wasted potential. "gastarte 800 euros en un equipo de sonido cuando estás empezando o cuando tienes 69 años me parece tirar el dinero."
**Importance of Preamplification for Simplified Setup** : A key recommendation for streamlining an entry-level audio setup is to pair a turntable featuring integrated preamplification with active speakers. This combination facilitates a direct connection between the components, thereby eliminating the complexity and additional cost of a separate preamplifier. "Si no tiene preamplificación, tendréis que conmembrar un preamplificador entre uno y otro y ya va a ser un lío."
