**Extended Play (Unspecified Year) - Pre-Grunge Phase** : Melvins recorded an extended play (EP) before their first full-length album, which is described as being "a bit strange" and not yet grunge music, leaning more towards "slash metal" (likely thrash/sludge metal). This early work shows the band's initial style before evolving into a more grunge-like sound. **Gluey Porch Treatments (1987) - Early Grunge Defining Album** : Recorded after Melvins moved to Seattle and extensively mingled with local bands, this album is identified as potentially the first grunge album because it includes core elements defining grunge. It marked an evolution from their earlier thrash/hardcore style, incorporating a noisy, "macarra" (thuggish) sound with elements of classic rock and introspective, furious lyrics that prefigured Nirvana, Pearl Jam, and Soundgarden. The album's release on a local Seattle label, likely Sub Pop, solidified its place in the nascent grunge scene. "en este disco Bluey Porch Whitmans de 1987 tenemos todavía una estén a muy jar cores Lack Metal que tenías ese unido tan ruido suitando Macarra que todavía arrastraban algunas partes del Rock de la música gran de Seattle pero que ya se va a derezando con la música, con alguna parte de los edques de disco, alguna parte del Rock más clásico no olvidamos que Pell Yam siempre tuvieron pues una versión muy del Rock clásico dentro de la música granch y a la vez las letras de Melvin's en 1987 no recordan muchas letras en Irvana, no recordan a veces incluso a letras de Pell Yam, no recordan algunas letras de Soundgarden, entonces yo creo que se hay un punto que da el pistoletazo de salida, a lo que sería el movimiento granch es cuando de Melvin's se van a Seattle y graban este disco."

## curiosities
**Move to Seattle and Scene Integration** : After forming, Melvins moved to Seattle and deeply integrated with the local music scene. This mixing with other bands was crucial for their stylistic evolution and connection to the emerging grunge sound. "cuando se vencia a Tel de Melvin's se mezcla muchísimo con todas las bandas locales y con ese video."
**Connections to Nirvana** : The drummer for Melvins played drums on Nirvana's first demo. Buzz Osborne, Melvins' singer, introduced Dave Ball (who became Nirvana's drummer) to the other members of Nirvana. Melvins also frequently played concerts with Nirvana and members of Pearl Jam. "el batería de Melvin's fue el batería que tocó la batería en la primera maqueta de Irvana o por ejemplo Bussos Pong, que es el cantante de Melvin's fue el que introdujo a Dave Ball el que sería el batería de Irvana a los miembros de Irvana."
**Evolution of Style** : Melvins evolved their style significantly around 1987, moving away from a thrash/hardcore sound. This evolution was influenced by the "rabioso" (furious) and introspective nature of Seattle bands, contrasting with the more "macarra" (thuggish) rock from places like Washington and California which were laying the groundwork for new punk bands like Blink-182 and Limp Bizkit. "Melvin se evolucionaron bastante su estilo a lo que sería su disco de 1987 porque abandonan ese estén a Tanis Lack Bang Harcord en la que estaba metido."

# genre - Grunge

## genres
Rock
Indie Rock

## curiosities
**Diffused Origin** : Grunge did not have a clear, distinct beginning or a simple break from previous styles like some other musical movements. It is described as a very diffuse, complex, and dispersed movement, making its precise delimitation difficult. "La música granch no es ese estilo, es un estilo muy difuso que nos cuesta mucho de limitar."
**Continuum, Not Inflection Point** : The transition from hardcore punk or "slag" (thrash/sludge metal) to grunge was not marked by a clear inflection point. Instead, it was a gradual appearance of early bases of grunge within various rock movements of the late 1980s. "no tiene un punto de inflexión que podamos decir claramente aquí es donde termina el hardcore punk o por ejemplo el slag y empieza el sonido del granch."
**Local and General Characteristics** : Grunge is best defined by two non-musical characteristics: its local origin in Seattle and the general similar age range of its main protagonists. "El granch posiblemente fue un movimiento muy local y muy general, son las dos cosas que mejor delimitan este movimiento."
**Generation X Identity** : The key figures of grunge were mostly of similar age, largely belonging to Generation X, described as "the furious generation" and "the first failed generation" in many years, which contributed to its particular character. "el granch está muy delimitado generalmente en lo que conocemos como la generación en X, la generación rabiosa con el mundo un poco, la primera generación fracasada que hayamos tenido muchos años."
**Seattle Origin** : Grunge was fundamentally a local movement born in Seattle, even though it later conquered the entire United States and the world. Its genesis was strongly tied to two or three independent record labels in Seattle, primarily Sub Pop. "fue un movimiento local de Seattle, el granch conquistó el mundo entero y conquistó Estados Unidos entero pero lo que está claro es que nací en Seattle."
**Early Bands** : Early grunge bands included Nirvana, Alice In Chains, Pearl Jam, and Soundgarden. "Las primeras bandas de granch como Nirvana o Alice In Change como Pearl Jam, Sound Garden."
**Community of Fury** : The Seattle scene was not just a limited group of specific bands but a collective of generally furious people, contributing to the introspective and raw nature of the music. "no era una escena de limitada de bandas concretas, era un grupo de gente generalmente muy rabiosa."
**Lack of Consensus on "First Album"** : There is no professional consensus among rock historians on which album marks the definitive start of grunge music or the inflection point between 80s indie rock and grunge. "ni siquiera entre los historiadores profesionales al rock, hay un consenso de cuál es el punto de inflexión que delimita el final del rock indie de los 80 y el inicio del granch."
**Evolution Post-1988** : The evolution of grunge became clearer from 1988 onwards, with many publications from Sub Pop being officially considered grunge. "a partir de 1988 la evolución del granch es mucho más fácil porque ya en su pop tenemos muchas publicaciones ya que las conseguimos casi oficialmente granch."
**Mainstream Jump Post-1989** : After 1989, particularly with the release of Nirvana's *Bleach* and later albums by Pearl Jam and Alice In Chains, the grunge movement transitioned from Sub Pop to Geffen and became widely known globally. "Justo después ya todo el movimiento granch, saldría de su pop a Giffen y la parte más andegranada un pura se quedaría en su pop."
