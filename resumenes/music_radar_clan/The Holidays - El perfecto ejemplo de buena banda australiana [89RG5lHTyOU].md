## albums
**Los Paradise (2010) - Debut Album** : This was the band's first album, released in 2010. It is described as very good, especially for those who enjoy 80s Synth-Pop, and is presented as a worthwhile listen to understand current Australian music. "los Paradas es un disco que francamente la le da pena por lo menos escuchar, conocer y ver lo que se está cocinando en australia."
**Latest Album (2014)** : The band released their latest album relatively recently in 2014 and continues to be active in the music scene. "el último disco hace bastante poco del 2014, y yo creo que puede que sea la típica banda que promete y que va a acabar seguido de australia."

## curiosities
**Australian Popularity and Awards** : The Holidays are quite popular in Australia, where they have won many awards and received multiple nominations in the "Mercurio Australiano". They have also produced several good music videos. "había tenido un montón de premios en australia, y ahora se refiraba en el mercuría australiana, había estado nominados varias veces, tienen varios videoclímpicos bastante buenos y de hecho en australia son bastante populares."
**Musical Style** : Their sound is characterized as modern, clean, polished, well-worked, and easy to listen to. It evokes a retro electronic and synthesizer influence, drawing comparisons to groups like MGMT and appealing particularly to fans of 80s Synth-Pop. "es el típico grupo un poco de S.A.N.P. que te recuerda un poco a MGMT o a estos grupos de poco entre electrónicos y desintitizadores que tiene se ha ido en tanto retro pero que hace una música muy limpia, la verdad es que está muy pulida, está muy bien trabajada, es fácil de escuchar."
**International Reach Challenge** : Despite their quality and popularity within Australia, The Holidays exemplify the challenge faced by many Australian bands, as their music struggles to be distributed internationally and remains largely unknown outside their home country. "es una pena que toda esta música buena que se hace australia se queda por el camino."
**Active Status** : The band remains active, having released their most recent album in 2014. "es una banda que sigue en activo con lo cual el último disco hace bastante poco del 2014."
