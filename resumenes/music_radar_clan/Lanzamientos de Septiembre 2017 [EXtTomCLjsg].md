# genre - Trip Hop
## curiosities
**Decline in Early 21st Century** : The trip hop movement is characterized as having "died out a bit" in the early 21st century, with only a few artists like Tricky still actively representing the genre. "el movimiento trip hop ha quedado ahí un poco muerto a primeros del siglo XXI."

# curiosity
**September as a Peak Release Month** : September, similar to April and May, is consistently identified as one of the busiest months for new music releases, aligning with the end of summer which is an opportune time for selling records. "septiembre al igual que abril y mayo suelen ser de los meses más calientes en lanzamientos musicales."
**Post-Summer Album Sales Strategy** : The period immediately following summer is considered an excellent time for selling records, which contributes to the concentration of numerous new music releases in September. "Acaba el verano y es un momento muy bueno para vender discos."
**Universal Friday Release Day** : It is a standard practice that all new music releases are scheduled for Fridays. "todos los lanzamientos de música se hacen siempre los viernes."
**Intense September Release Schedule** : September is recognized as one of the most intense months for music, featuring a high volume of potentially relevant releases. "septiembre siempre es uno de los meses más intensos en música."
