# artist - Noel Gallagher

## member of
Oasis

## genres
Electronic (influence)

## albums
**Council Skies (2023)** : Released a month prior to the review, this album received generally very good critical reviews. It was described as more experimental and marked the first time Noel Gallagher did not produce himself, instead collaborating with David Holmes, a "very old school British electronic artist." The album successfully innovates Noel Gallagher's sound while still appealing to fans of his classic style, evolving his music without losing his loyal audience and attracting new listeners. It incorporates experimental elements not as electronic music itself, but through the use of noises, loops, and samples from various sources, such as a woman with scissors, a telephone ringing, or car sounds. These unexpected elements are integrated based on a solid understanding of his core sound. The album's structure begins with a long instrumental piece, filled with saturated sounds and noises, designed to prepare the listener for the blend of mixed influences and his classic sound. While the instrumental aspects and production are highly praised, the lyrics are noted as weaker and more "banal" compared to his previous work, suggesting a focus on sound over lyrical complexity. "ha conseguido evolucinar su sonido sin perder al público fil que tenía."
**Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds (2011)** : This album, Noel Gallagher's first solo effort, featured "super cool" and "very interesting" lyrics. His solo projects were generally better received from the outset compared to Liam Gallagher's. "en el primer disco de High Fly inversas de letras eran como super chulas a mí me encantaban eran como muy interesantes."

## songs
**Council Skies (2023) - Lead Single** : This advance track for the album of the same name was a very risky and groundbreaking release, described as a "leap into the void" for Noel Gallagher's established sound. "esa canción que toca con el piano que creo que es una canción extra que es como critete de sincha que es como mira ahora que has empezado en este camino esto podría haber sido una buena cara veo un buen final de un disco anterior."

## curiosities
**Post-Oasis Career Trajectory** : Following the dissolution of Oasis, Noel Gallagher embarked on a solo career that is described as more linear and simpler than that of his brother, Liam. He maintained strong connections with the artistic community and musical establishment within the United Kingdom. "después de la muerte de lo así se ha tenido una carrera como más lineal y más sencilla."
**Songwriting Talent** : Noel Gallagher was consistently regarded as the "great songwriting talent" within Oasis. However, in the later stages of the band's existence, he experienced a loss of creative leadership, sharing it more extensively with his brother and other band members. "Él fue al mejor siempre el gran talento compositor aunque sí que es verdad que en la última parte de Basis él perdió mucho, liderado creativo."
**Critical Perception as Overrated** : Noel Gallagher's name frequently appears on lists categorizing "most overrated artists or guitarists" in music history. "no el Galagher saben muchas listas de los artistas o guitarristas más sobrevalorados de la historia."
**Public Persona Shift (2023)** : In 2023, Noel Gallagher's public persona underwent a noticeable shift, characterized by him frequently making "unfortunate comments" on a wide array of subjects. This behavior stood in contrast to Liam Gallagher's more composed demeanor during the same period. "no el Galagher que no para hacer comentarios es afortunados sobre cualquier cosa que se lo ocurre."
**Ibiza House Sale** : Noel Gallagher sold his house in Ibiza after James Blunt purchased the adjacent property. He stated in an interview that he "couldn't stand being his neighbor" and preferred to sell his house. "vendió su casa de Ibiza y dijo una entrevista que vendía su casa de Ibiza porque la de lado la covia comprado James Blunt y no soportaba ser el vecino de James Blunt."
**Musical Influences in Council Skies** : Noel Gallagher's "Council Skies" album maintains strong ties to classic influences from the "old school," including The Who and Paul Weller, yet manages to sound fresh rather than like past hits. "sigue teniendo muchísimas de esas influencias clásicas pues el de Huda, allá donde Paul Gueller todo está vieja escuela."
**Production Collaboration with David Holmes** : For the "Council Skies" album, Noel Gallagher worked with David Holmes, an "old school British electronic artist," as his producer. This marked the first time Gallagher did not produce his own work. Holmes's production was praised for its "super accurate" integration of new sounds without them appearing forced. "la producción de David Holmes está super acertada porque con sí hacer ese encaje natural un poco de todos esos sonidos sin que son effortados."
**Lyrical Quality of Council Skies** : The lyrical quality on "Council Skies" is considered somewhat weaker and more "banal" compared to Noel Gallagher's earlier work, including his first High Flying Birds album, where the lyrics were described as "super cool" and "very interesting." This suggests a greater focus was placed on the album's sound and the incorporation of new elements rather than lyrical complexity. "la pena es que las letras han mejor de este disco en el galager se han que a un pelín más flojillas las letras o así estuvo tempradas de letras realmente buenas."
