# artist - Nirvana

  ## curiosities
    **Kurt Cobain's Death Announced** : On the night of April 8, 1994, Kurt Cobain, the singer of the rock group Nirvana, was found dead. He was 27 years old, lying on the floor of his house with a note written by him beside him before his suicide. His death in 1994 became one of the most incredible and strange stories in music, giving rise to an enormous "black legend" surrounding his supposed suicide. "A noche encontraron muerto a Kurt Cobain, que era el cantante del grupo de rock Nirvana."
    **Ideological Figure** : Kurt Cobain was seen as an ideological figure and the visible head of a youth movement that sought escapism through music and expressed disdain for American politics. "Iba de ideólogo, era la cabeza visible de un movimiento juvenil que busca en la música la evasión y hace frente a su desprecio por la política norteamericana."
    **Last Concert in Germany** : Nirvana's last known stage appearance was on March 1, 1994, in Germany. This concert marked the final public trace of Nirvana on a stage. "Y empezamos con el último concierto que era Nirvana, que es el 1 de marzo de 1994 en Alemania."
    **Severe Laryngitis in Germany** : After the last concert in Germany, Kurt Cobain was diagnosed with severe laryngitis. Instead of returning to the United States, he traveled to Rome for treatment. "Tras ese concierto, a Kurt Cobain se le diagnostica una lángitis muy grave."
    **Rome Overdose Incident (March 4, 1994)** : While in Rome, taking medication, Kurt Cobain was found on the morning of March 4, 1994, in his hotel room with Kurt Ylov, having suffered a massive overdose and fallen into a coma. The overdose was reportedly caused by sedatives and champagne. "la mañana siguiente, el 4 de marzo de 1994, Kurt Cobain aparece en la habitación de su tal con Kurt Ylov como una enorme sobredosis y en coma."
    **Kurt Ylov's Suicide Attempt Claim** : A few days after the Rome overdose, Kurt Ylov stated in an interview that the incident was a suicide attempt by Kurt Cobain, a claim he never accepted. This marked the beginning of a very strange period for him. "Kurt Ylov a los pocos días a una entrevista había dicho que había sido un intento de su suicidio de Kurt Cobain, pero él nunca lo había aceptado."
    **Seattle Police Call (March 18, 1994)** : On March 18, Kurt Ylov called the police to their Seattle home, claiming Kurt Cobain had locked himself in a room with a weapon and intended to commit suicide. Police found Kurt locked in a room but he had no weapon and no suicidal intent, stating he was merely tired of arguing with Kurt Ylov. "el 18 de marzo está en siete en su casa, Kurt Ylov llama la policía."
    **Strained Relationship with Kurt Ylov** : Kurt Cobain had a very bad relationship with Kurt Ylov, characterized by frequent arguments, and he had attempted to leave her multiple times. He told police he had locked himself in the room because he was tired of arguing and didn't want to see her. "Él había tenido una fuerte discusión con Kurt Ylov, tenía una relación muy mala, discutían a menudo, Kurt Cobain había intentado dejar la dos de las veces."
    **Police Remove Weapons and Sedatives** : Following the March 18 incident, police preventatively removed all weapons and strong sedatives from Kurt Cobain's house. Kurt Ylov later admitted to police she had not seen him with a weapon or heard him explicitly state suicidal intent, and that he was not in a depression at that moment. "Preventivamente la policía se llevó todas las armas de casa y todas las pastillas y carmanches fuertes que había dentro de la casa de Kurt Cobain."
    **Drug Intervention (March 25, 1994)** : On March 25, Kurt Ylov organized a drug intervention for Kurt Cobain, attended by Nirvana band members, Geffen record label executives, and two of his best friends, to address his heroin problem. Kurt Cobain was very negative about the process, and initially denied having a major addiction, instead claiming Kurt Ylov was the one with the problem. "el 25 de marzo, o cosas más tarde, Kurt Ylov decía hace una especie de intervención de las drogas para Kurt Cobain."
    **Heroin Reduction During Pregnancy** : Kurt Cobain significantly reduced his heavy heroin use after Kurt Ylov became pregnant. He was quite impacted by the pregnancy and his consumption dropped considerably from his previously high levels. "cuando Kurt Ylov se quedó embarazada, Kurt Cobain se quedó bastante impactado y bien continuó con la droga Aeroina, él redujo enormemente el consumo de Aeroina que tenía el enorme"
    **Agreement to Enter Rehab** : After an eight-hour intervention filled with arguments, Kurt Cobain finally agreed to return to a detox center in Los Angeles, where he had been previously, seemingly to be left in peace and avoid further controversy. "después de más de ocho horas solando juntos, de peleando, de todas estas cosas, finalmente Kurt Cobain un poco para que le dejen en paz y para que no genera más polémica, decide y acepta volver al centro de sintoxicación"
    **Admission to Los Angeles Detox Center (March 30, 1994)** : Kurt Cobain officially entered the Los Angeles detox center on March 30, 1994. Reports from the center were positive, indicating he was doing well, his health was improving, he had a positive attitude, played guitar often, and received visitors. "El 30 de marzo, unos días después, ingresa oficialmente en el centro de los áncieles de desintoxicación."
    **Visit from Daughter and Nanny (April 1, 1994)** : On April 1, Kurt Cobain received a visit from his young daughter and her nanny at the detox center. He was happy with his daughter, and the nanny noted a significant improvement in his condition. "el 1 de abril, él recibe una visita de su hija pequeña con la niñera que tenía."
    **Aggressive Call and Rehab Escape (April 1, 1994)** : On the night of April 1, conflicting reports suggest Kurt Cobain received a very aggressive phone call from Kurt Ylov at the detox center, which may have contributed to his escape. Regardless, he escaped the center, took a plane, and flew to Seattle. "hay quien dice que esa misma noche recibe una llamada muy agresiva del cornylofa al centro de sintoxicación. Se a lo que fuese ese día, Curco Vence escapa del centro de sintoxicación, se cojo un avión y se va a hacerte."
    **Encounter with Mike McCann** : On the plane to Seattle, Kurt Cobain unexpectedly sat next to Mike McCann, a member of Hansen Roces. McCann noted Kurt's improved appearance but also his palpable concern. "se sienta al lado en el avión, sin haberlo planeado con Mike McCann, que es el member de Hansen Roces, el cual reconoce que, reconoce a verlo visto, reconoce que le había sorprendido lo mejorado y lo bien que estaba curco vende después de los problemas con el avión y acá había tenido, pero también reconoce a verlo visto muy preocupado."
    **Return to Seattle and Estrangement from Kurt Ylov** : Upon arriving in Seattle, Kurt Cobain did not return to Kurt Ylov. He was seen moving around various places in Seattle, not appearing drugged or in a worsening state, but maintaining almost no contact with her. "aunque vuelve a Seattle, él no vuelve con cornylo. Él está moviéndose de un lado para otro en Seattle y se le ve continuamente por varios sitios de Seattle, a Silvembon estado en un momento se ve drogado ni se ve que en peora, pero él no tiene prácticamente una relación con cornylof."
    **Discovery of Kurt Cobain's Body (April 8, 1994)** : On April 8, 1994, an electrician installing a security device found Kurt Cobain dead on the floor of his home, which he shared with Kurt Ylov. Initially disbelieving he was dead, the electrician found a suicide note, then called the police who certified his death. "el 8 de abril de 1994 cuando un electricista entra en casa de Córkovend y de Córnilof y se encuentra Córkovend en el suelo muerto."
    **Official Date of Death (April 5, 1994)** : The autopsy revealed Kurt Cobain died on April 5, 1994. This date is significant as it was two days after Kurt Ylov hired a private detective to find him and the day before she reported to the police that he intended to die by gunshot. "la torce revela que Córkovend murió el 5 de abril."
    **Kurt Ylov's Absence and Suspicious Behavior** : Police found Kurt Ylov's behavior suspicious immediately after the discovery. Despite living in the house, she claimed she wasn't there and did not return until after the police arrived, without providing a clear justification for her absence. "Córnilof vivía en esa casa hasta justo el prédio antes de Córkovend va a aparecer muerto y ella no vuelve a entrar sorprendentemente sin encontrar justificación hasta después de que aparezca la policía."
    **High Doses of Sedatives and Heroin** : The autopsy revealed Kurt Cobain had enormous doses of sedatives and heroin in his body, which police found incompatible with reported sightings of him in good spirits and not relapsing on heroin. "Córkovend tiene un enorme dosis de ansiolíticos y de Eroina en el cuerpo."
    **Police Theory of Homicide and Staged Suicide** : Police developed a theory that Kurt Cobain likely ingested a strong dose of sedatives (voluntarily or involuntarily) while nearly unconscious, followed by a strong dose of heroin administered before the gunshot. Upon failure of the overdose to be fatal, a shot was fired. Forensic and police experts believed it was physically impossible for him to shoot himself with that amount of heroin, and questioned the logic of injecting heroin while already nearly unconscious. "lo más probable es que a Córkovend se la ha llevado una fuerte dosis de ansiolíticos de manera voluntaria o involuntaria cuando estaba en un estado de casi en inconsistencia."
    **Issues with Suicide Weapon and Fingerprints** : Police were suspicious due to the complicated position of the weapon and the complete absence of Kurt Cobain's fingerprints on it, making the suicide theory difficult to accept. "no aparecía ninguna huella rectilar de Córkovend en el arma, aparte que aparecía una posición bastante complicada, entonces era un proceso bastante difícil de dirigerir."
    **Doubts About Suicide Note Authorship** : While some affirmed the suicide note could only have been written by Kurt Cobain, a key detail was that it wasn't signed by him but by "Boddah," his imaginary childhood friend. Calligraphers gave mixed opinions: some denying it was his handwriting, others suggesting it could be his in an altered, depressed, or drugged state. Ultimately, no one could definitively confirm it was his handwriting, it lacked his fingerprints, and wasn't his normal writing style. "la carta de espiedad Córkovend no afirmó Córkovend. Así no que la femó, Voda, que era el amigo imaginario que había tenido Córkovend en la infancia, tela."
    **Strange Thematic Content of Suicide Note** : The content of the suicide note was deemed very strange, as it didn't align with Kurt Cobain's recent behavior. It contained expressions of love for Kurt Ylov and his daughter and urged them to be strong, which was incompatible with his attempts to leave her and his recent detestation of her. "la temática era muy rara, Córkovend ya no se oportaba Córkovend, de hecho no se estaba viendo, él había inventado de jala varias veces y acababa diciendo lo mucho que quería Córkovend, que adoraba a ella y a su hija"
    **Kurt Ylov's Behavior at Vigil** : At a vigil for Nirvana fans, Kurt Ylov made a spectacle of herself, publicly weeping and dramatically reading from Kurt Cobain's suicide note, particularly parts referencing his love for her. She also threw his personal t-shirts to the grieving fans, which was described as a truly shameful display, especially as she had reportedly taken all of his money. "se aprece Córkov como una vida prácticamente despectáculo, leyendo frases y fragmentos de la nota de sucio Córkovend, los que hacía referencia a lo mucho que quería Córkov, la cual por cierto, se ha agoncado con todo el dinero de Córkovend."
    **Edom Hock's Allegations and Mysterious Death** : An artist named Edom Hock, who knew both Kurt Cobain and Kurt Ylov (being closer to Ylov), claimed in an interview months after Cobain's death that Kurt Ylov had offered him $50,000 to kill Kurt Cobain, which he refused. Three days later, Edom Hock was found dead under suspicious circumstances, with no apparent cause for assassination, having no financial, drug, or conflict problems. "un artista que se llamaba Edom Hock, que eran conocidos de los dos, pero era más amigo del Córnilov y no tenía mucho éxito, en un entrevista una vez dijeros, pocos meses que Córnilov la había ofrecido 50.000 dólares por matar a Córkovend y te lo había rechazado."
    **Kurt Ylov's Stepfather's Accusation** : Months later, Kurt Ylov's stepfather publicly stated in a US music magazine his conviction that she either actively killed Kurt Cobain or directly ordered his assassination. "el padre Astro de Córnilov dijo en una entrevista, una revista musical en Estados Unidos, espero que no me maten por decir esto, pero estoy completamente convencido de que Córnilov mató o activamente a Córkovend o que directamente encarbó su asesinato."
    **Private Detective's Confession** : Years after Kurt Cobain's death, the private detective hired by Kurt Ylov before his death also claimed she had commissioned his murder. The detective implied the motives included her inability to accept him leaving her, his impending sobriety while she remained an addict, and his status as her sole financial support. "el detective privado que Córnilov había contratado, fue otra de las personas que dijo que Córnilov había encargado en asesinato del Córkovend."
    **Case Closed as Suicide with Lingering Doubts** : For four or five years, Kurt Cobain's suicide was investigated as a possible murder before being officially closed as a suicide. However, significant doubts persist due to the lack of his fingerprints on the suicide note or weapon, the unconfirmed authorship of the note, the absence of records of prior suicide attempts, and Kurt Ylov's "premonitory" behavior in the months leading up to his death. Many still believe she ordered or committed the murder to prevent him from leaving, taking their daughter, and to continue accessing his money. "Durante años el suicidio de Córkovend estuvo investigado como posíla asesinato, hasta finalmente cuatro o cinco años después se acabó archivando el caso como un suicidio de Córkovend, aunque deja a borde a ver tan asenorme, y les ayuda a que suscita el que no haya aparecido sus vuelvas ni en la carta, ni en el arma, a que nunca se ha podido acreditar la autoría de su carta, de suicidio, el que no haya constancia de raderos intentos en suicidio y sobre todo a todo el premio enitorio comportamiento, había tenido Córnilofe en dos meses antes"

# curiosity
  **Ongoing Debate about Kurt Cobain's Death** : Even after the official closure of Kurt Cobain's death case as a suicide, many people today still believe Kurt Ylov either ordered his assassination or directly pulled the trigger herself. This belief persists because they think she wanted to prevent him from leaving her, from taking their daughter, and to continue living off his money. "aún hoy en día mucha agenda de piensa que fue Córniloble, que encaruela asesinato o la que directamente apretó el gatillo y mató a Córkovend, para que no la dejase, para que no le quita esa niña y sobre todo para día de hoy seguir viviendo el dinero del Córkovend."
