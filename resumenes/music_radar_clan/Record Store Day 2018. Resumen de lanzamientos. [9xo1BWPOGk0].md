# curiosity

**Record Store Day 2023 Trend: Live Albums** : Unlike last year's focus on 7-inch singles, this year's Record Store Day sees a notable trend towards live albums. Artists are widely choosing to release either unreleased, re-edited, or famous bootleg live albums, aiming to engage their fanbases. "este año destacan, los álbumes en directo, este año todo el mundo ha optado por lanzar álbumes en directo, inéditos o reditados o bootlegs famosos, un poco para enganchar los fans."
**Vinyl Production Saturation (Previous Year)** : The previous year's Record Store Day experienced saturated vinyl factories due to important anniversaries and major releases, which led to a bottleneck in 12-inch production. As a result, many 7-inch and 10-inch vinyl records were released instead. This year, there is less saturation in 7-inch production. "teníamos las fábricas de vinilos saturadas, acabó saliendo muchísimo vinilo de siete y diez pulgadas, precisamente porque la producción de 12 pulgadas estaba saturada."
