# artist - Radiohead

## albums
**The Bends** : The cover art for the album *The Bends* was created by Stan Lee Donwood, who received the commission as a personal favor from his university friend, Thom Yorke. The success of this initial work led Donwood to become the primary graphic artist for all subsequent Radiohead projects. "le encarga el trabajo de la portada de Depends. El trabajo fue un éxito y hizo también las portadas de todos los singles y a partir de ahí él hizo todo el trabajo que tiene que ver con Radiohead."
**The King of Limbs** : During the presentation of *The King of Limbs*, Thom Yorke and Stan Lee Donwood were photographed together distributing free newspapers in the street. This public appearance was crucial in officially debunking the long-standing rumor that Donwood was merely a pseudonym for Yorke. "repartido periódicos gratuitos en la calle cuando presentarán el King of Limes. Era una de las fotos que desmintió oficialmente que Thom Yorke y Stan Lee Donwood no eran la misma persona."

## curiosities
**Pablo Jonei Reference** : Stan Lee Donwood is credited with creating all the graphic art for Radiohead, with the specific mention that this work was not done by "Pablo Jonei," indicating a distinction between Donwood's role and any other potential contributors to the band's visual identity. "él es el artista que ha hecho todo el arte gráfico de Radiohead que no ha sido el Pablo Jonei."
**Members Facilitated Contact for Interview** : To arrange an interview with the reclusive artist Stan Lee Donwood, a reporter from Rolling Stone had to contact members of Radiohead, who then helped facilitate a multi-step process to obtain Donwood's email address. "se puso un contacto con gente del grupo Radiohead que le puso un contacto a su vez con otra gente que le podía poner en contacto con el artista y después de un tiempo invertido consiguió un correo electrónico."
**Extensive Audiovisual Work** : Radiohead is notable for the extensive audiovisual work surrounding the group, which goes beyond just album covers to include a rich history behind their posters, t-shirts, and a multitude of other incredible audiovisual materials. "una de las cosas más audiciantes que tiene Radiohead desde todo el trabajo audiovisual que hay alrededor del grupo, no sólo las portadas de sus discos, sino toda la historia que hay detrás de sus postres, de sus camisetas y ha hecho un montón de deberes audiovisuales con material increíble."
**Award-Winning Websites** : Radiohead's websites, designed by Stan Lee Donwood, have garnered numerous awards, showcasing the innovative and critically acclaimed digital presence of the band. "Ha tenido webs que han ganado un montón de premios."
**Corrais Mobile Application** : Radiohead, in collaboration with Stan Lee Donwood, designed a mobile application, referred to as "los hechos a Corrais," which blended image, sound, and an "astral journey" experience, ultimately being hailed as an "authentic work of art." "llegaron incluso juntos a diseñar una aplicación de móvil que mezclaba imagen, sonido y una especie de viaje astral, los hechos a Corrais, la famosa aplicación que hicieron juntos que fue un auténtico obra de arte."

# artist - Stan Lee Donwood

## member of
Radiohead (collaborator)
Thom Yorke (collaborator on solo work)
Atoms for Peace (collaborator)

## albums
**The Bends** : Stan Lee Donwood's first major work for Radiohead was designing the cover for *The Bends*, a commission given to him by Thom Yorke as a personal favor while Donwood's own career was struggling. The success of this artwork marked the beginning of his extensive collaboration with the band. "por hacerlo un favor personal a su amigo Thom Yorke le encarga el trabajo de la portada de Depends. El trabajo fue un éxito."
**Atoms for Peace Album** : Stan Lee Donwood was responsible for all the graphic work related to the album by Atoms for Peace, extending his creative partnership beyond Radiohead to Thom Yorke's other musical projects. "él ha hecho también todo el trabajo gráfico de los trabajos en solitario que ha hecho Thom Yorke, tanto de A.R.I.C.E. como el álbum de Atoms for Peace."

## curiosities
**London Art Exhibition (September 2002)** : In September 2002, an art exhibition featuring Stan Lee Donwood's work was inaugurated in London, showcasing his distinctive graphic art, particularly his contributions to Radiohead's visual identity. "En septiembre del 2002 en Londres se inauguró una exposición de arte muy especial, porque el protagonista de la exposición era Stan Lee Donwood."
**Enigmatic Public Figure** : Stan Lee Donwood is known for his enigmatic nature, consistently avoiding public appearances and even his own exhibition openings, which contributes to him being seen as more mysterious than Radiohead itself. "es una persona la que nunca se le ve y que nunca está en público."
**Ranked Among Great Graphic Artists** : Stan Lee Donwood is regarded as one of the great graphic artists in music over the last 20 years, highly sought after for interviews due to his significant influence on the visual aspect of contemporary music. "uno de los grandes artistas gráficos que tienen que ver con la música de los últimos 20 años."
**Email-Only Contact Method** : Stan Lee Donwood is notoriously difficult to contact, as he does not answer the telephone and can only be reached via email, making communication with him a unique and challenging endeavor. "no solo no se le ven público sino que tampoco contesta el teléfono, solo se puede poner en contacto con el mediante su correo electrónico."
**Email Interview Published (September 2004)** : After 1.5 years of effort, Stan Lee Donwood surprisingly agreed to an interview conducted entirely via email, even requesting the questions in advance. This interview, focusing on Radiohead's art, was published in September 2004 by Rolling Stone (UK) and subsequently translated and published by numerous magazines worldwide, becoming a significant event in the music world. "en septiembre justo dos años más tarde acababan publicando una entrevista de Stan Lee Donwood sobre el art de Radiohead y fue todo un acontecimiento. Esa entrevista fue traducida mucho si dié más y publicada muchísimas revistas por todo el mundo."
**Doubts About His Existence** : For many years, Stan Lee Donwood was so reclusive and enigmatic that people even doubted his real existence, leading to widespread and plausible rumors that he was actually Thom Yorke operating under a pseudonym for all of Radiohead's graphic work. "se llegó incluso a dudar de que existiese realmente. Hoy en día sabemos que en el sasis sino que es una persona real es un amigo personal de Thom Yorke, pero durante muchos años había el grandísimo rumor y con mucho sentido de que era el propio Thom Yorke que actúa con el Seidónimo de Stan Lee Donwood, el que hacía todo el trabajo gráfico del grupo."
**Pseudonym, Not Real Name** : Stan Lee Donwood is not the artist's real name; it is a pseudonym he uses for his professional work, contributing to his mysterious persona. "que no es un hombre real."
**University Friendship with Thom Yorke at Oxford** : Stan Lee Donwood and Thom Yorke met and became friends while they were university classmates at Oxford, a relationship that later proved pivotal for Donwood's career in graphic design. "eran compañeros de universidad en Oxford. Ahí es como se conocieron."
**Comprehensive Radiohead Graphic Work** : Following the success of *The Bends* cover, Stan Lee Donwood took on all graphic work for Radiohead, designing not only album covers but also singles, posters, badges, t-shirts, and even the band's websites, becoming an integral part of their visual brand. "él hizo todo el trabajo que tiene que ver con Radiohead. Como os dije él hace hasta los postres, él hace las chapas, él hace las camisetas, él diseñó hasta las webs del grupo."
**Recognized Artist in Music and Art** : Today, Stan Lee Donwood is a truly recognized artist, appreciated for his contributions both within the music industry and in the broader art world, reflecting the high quality and impact of his creative output. "Hoy en día él es un artista verdaderamente reconocido tanto en la música como en el arte y el trabajo que hace es increíble."
**Disproved Pseudonym Rumor with Thom Yorke Photo** : A significant photograph emerged depicting Stan Lee Donwood alongside Thom Yorke distributing free newspapers for *The King of Limbs* release, which officially disproved the persistent rumor that Donwood was merely a pseudonym for Yorke. "Era una de las fotos que desmintió oficialmente que Thom Yorke y Stan Lee Donwood no eran la misma persona."
**Graphic Work for Thom Yorke's Solo Projects** : Stan Lee Donwood has extended his graphic design work beyond Radiohead to Thom Yorke's solo projects, referred to as "A.R.I.C.E.," further cementing their artistic partnership. "él ha hecho también todo el trabajo gráfico de los trabajos en solitario que ha hecho Thom Yorke, tanto de A.R.I.C.E."
**Osso Logo Designer** : Stan Lee Donwood designed the famous "osso" logo, which is said to have an incredible story behind its creation, highlighting his influence on iconic imagery associated with Radiohead. "él diseñó también el famoso logo de losso que tiene una historia increíble."
**Co-Designed Corrais Mobile Application** : Stan Lee Donwood, along with Radiohead/Thom Yorke, co-designed a mobile application known as "los hechos a Corrais," which integrated image, sound, and an "astral journey" experience, acclaimed as an "authentic work of art." "llegaron incluso juntos a diseñar una aplicación de móvil que mezclaba imagen, sonido y una especie de viaje astral, los hechos a Corrais, la famosa aplicación que hicieron juntos que fue un auténtico obra de arte."
