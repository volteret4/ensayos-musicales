# instrument - Turntable

## curiosities
**Audio-Technica AT-LP3 (2017) - Best Affordable Turntable** : This model was selected by De Bayern The Factory as the best turntable of 2017 in the affordable category, replacing the AT-LP120 which had held the spot for several years. It is more economical, costing between 200-210 euros compared to the AT-LP120's 250 euros. While it foregoes some features like a height regulator, deemed less important for home use, it boasts a slightly better built-in phono preamplifier than the LP120. "es la mejor compra que se pueda hacer desde el punto de vista económico."
**Lenco L-3808 - Unique Direct Drive** : Priced around 200 euros, this turntable stands out for being the only one in its category to feature a chassis inspired by the popular Technics MK series. Although direct drive motors are often not preferred by audiophiles, the Lenco's motor is highlighted as exceptionally good. It is described as the quietest direct drive motor with the least vibration. Its less powerful nature makes it better suited for home listening rather than DJ scratching, offering a superior direct drive option for desktop use. "es posiblemente el motor de traición directa económico mejor que hay para tener de sobre mesa en casa."
**Pro-Ject Essential 3 - European-Made Affordable Option** : Approaching 300 euros, this model is considered one of the most controversial platters due to the diverse criteria used in its selection, which extends beyond mere sound quality to include manufacturing, components, aesthetics, and industrial design. It is recommended as the most economical option not manufactured in China, being produced in Europe. This makes it the top budget choice for those seeking a non-Chinese turntable. "la mejor opción económica que puede tener de un tocado que no sea chino."
**Dual MTR-75 - Solid Construction in Affordable Range** : This turntable is presented as a curious case, given that Dual's low-end models typically receive poor reviews. Despite this, the MTR-75 is aesthetically, functionally, and sonically almost identical to the Audio-Technica AT-LP3, though it is slightly more expensive. It offers good value for money, includes a decent preamplification system, and features a solid construction that minimizes vibration. "los duales básicos no son encerras mejores."
**Epsilon Omega 100 - French Design with External Motor** : Priced just over 300 euros, this model was unfamiliar to the speaker. It is notable for being one of the few affordable turntables (under 500 euros) that does not incorporate components from Audio-Technica, Rega, or Pro-Ject, instead utilizing its own proprietary parts. Manufactured in Europe, specifically France, it is praised for its aesthetically pleasing industrial design. It features a classic external motor positioned on the side, rather than an internal motor with an internal belt, making it the most economical option for turntables with an external belt drive. "la opción más económica de los tocas discusos de cuerda externa que te puedes comprar."
**Nad 558 - Mid-Range with Excellent Cartridge** : Costing over 450 euros, this turntable is well-regarded and comes equipped with an excellent Ortofon OM10 cartridge. It features a crystal platter, which is often controversial in non-high-end models because crystal, unless tempered, is considered poor for vibration absorption. Despite the typical issues with non-tempered crystal platters, this model is recommended. "lleva una cápsula, una ortofón OM10, que es una cápsula excelente."
**Sony PS-HX500 - Best for Digital Conversion** : Retailing for under 500 euros, this turntable is specifically recommended as potentially the best option on the market for converting vinyl to digital music formats, such as MP3. It includes a built-in digital transformation system and comes with proprietary software that is particularly effective for transferring vinyl records to digital systems. "posiblemente sea la mejor opción del mercado para pasar música digital."
**Thorens T203 - Swiss-Made Industrial Design Marvel** : This Swiss-made turntable is priced over 600, nearly 700 euros. It is lauded as a marvel of industrial design. While its sound quality is considered good but not incredible, it is recognized for its robust construction, featuring an aluminum tonearm, which is increasingly popular. Its primary recommendation stems from its exceptional industrial design. "principalmente porque lo considera una maravilla del diseño industrial."
**FanFirmGate - Rare Industrial Design Gem** : Also priced over 600, almost 700 euros, this turntable is described as difficult to find and rarely purchased. Despite its obscurity, it is considered an industrial design jewel and an authentic marvel. It features a distinct motor system: an external motor with an external belt, positioned at the bottom left corner near the regulators, reminiscent of classic industrial designs from older turntables. "una pequeña joya del diseño industrial."
**Epsilon Omega Cien - Carbon Fiber with Versatile Connectivity** : Priced at 750 euros, this turntable boasts carbon fiber construction. It maintains a three-speed playback system (33, 45, and 78 RPM), a feature often omitted in modern turntables. It incorporates a fantastic classic motor system, enhanced by its carbon fiber build. It is described as one of the most affordable options to include an Ortofon 2M Red cartridge. Furthermore, it offers Bluetooth connectivity and particularly good USB connections, making it an excellent choice for use as an interface for digital supports or with Bluetooth/USB-enabled speakers. "la mejor opción que te puedes comprar"
**Pro-Ject The Classic - Modern Dual 1019 Reproduction** : Costing over 1000 euros, this turntable is celebrated not only for its fantastic sound quality, carbon tonearm, and extremely thick platter designed to eliminate vibration, but also for being a reproduction of the classic Dual 1019 from 1965. The Dual 1019 is considered one of the best turntables ever manufactured. The Pro-Ject The Classic features a wooden chassis and a metal plinth, making it aesthetically marvelous and a faithful reinterpretation of the classic Dual turntables from the 1960s. "una reinterpretación de esos tocaliscos clásicos que fabricaba dual en los años 60."
**Edwards Audio TT2 SE - Audiophile Favorite with Tempered Glass** : Popular among audiophiles, this model is priced between 1250 and 1300 euros. It features a fantastic preamplification system and an exceptionally thick tempered glass platter, which is highly effective for vibration control. Additionally, it incorporates ceramic coating, a feature that is gaining popularity. It is considered a fantastic option in its price range. "una opción fantástica."
**Rega Planar 6 - Sound Quality Focused Design** : Priced at 1500 euros, this model comes from Rega, a brand renowned for its high-quality products. It is described as incredibly simple yet absolutely focused on delivering superior sound quality. It is considered to offer one of the best sound qualities among turntables priced under 2000-3000 euros. "una de las mejores calidad de sonido de los platos que están por menos de 2 mil 3 mil euros."
**TA-2000R - German Technical Marvel** : This German-made turntable, priced around 5500 euros, is described as a technical marvel and a "fetishist" item. It features Aeria Audio preamplification and an MC2 cartridge, indicating a significant leap in quality. It represents "otros niveles de calidad."
**Origin Live (2009) - Top-Tier Rebuilt Sound Quality** : Valued at over 6000 euros, this model was unfamiliar to the speaker. It is a popular turntable, not new to the market (originally from 2009), but its components have been rebuilt and remade. It is considered the best in terms of sound quality available for purchase. "lo mejor que se puede comprar como calidad de sonido."

## curiosities
**De Bayern The Factory - Vinyl Publication** : This entity is highlighted as one of the most important publications in the world of vinyl. It compiled a list of the 15 best turntables on the market in 2017, categorizing them into three groups: affordable, mid-range, and high-end, selecting the top 5 from each category. The publication is noted for not being particularly fond of Technics turntables or their chassis designs. "nunca han sido muy fans de los técnix ni de las carcasa técnix."
**Turntable Categories - 2017 Market Guide** : The 2017 guide to the best turntables categorized models into three distinct groups based on price and features: "affordable" (under 355 euros), "mid-range" (from 300 to 780 euros), and "high-end" (from 1000 euros upwards), addressing different budget levels.
**Crystal Platters - Vibration Challenges** : Crystal platters are often a contentious feature in turntables, particularly in non-high-end models. This is primarily because they typically do not use tempered glass, and standard crystal is considered the worst material in terms of vibration absorption. However, some models are recommended despite having crystal platters.
**Direct Drive Motors - Audiophile Concerns** : Direct drive motors are generally not the preferred choice for audiophiles due to potential vibration issues. However, certain manufacturers like Lenco have developed direct drive motors that are an exception to this rule, achieving low noise and minimal vibration.
**78 RPM Speed - Declining Feature** : The ability to play records at 78 RPM is a classic turntable feature that many modern turntables are now omitting. Only specific models, like the Epsilon Omega Cien, continue to maintain this three-speed functionality (33, 45, and 78 RPM).
**Beginner Turntable Recommendation - Audio-Technica AT-LP3** : For individuals new to vinyl, the Audio-Technica AT-LP3 is specifically recommended as the ideal starting turntable. It is favored for its affordability and ease of use for home listening.
**Pro-Ject Fan Recommendation - Essential 3** : For enthusiasts who are particularly fond of the Pro-Ject brand, the Pro-Ject Essential 3 is highlighted as a model that aligns with their preferences and offers strong value within its category.
**Rega Components in Other Brands - Quality Influence** : Even if a turntable is not a full Rega model, some manufacturers incorporate Rega components into their designs. This indicates Rega's influence and reputation for quality extends beyond its own branded products.
**Technics MK Chassis Inspiration - Lenco L-3808** : The Lenco L-3808 is noted for being the sole turntable that mounts a chassis inspired by the iconic Technics MK series, a design widely recognized as the most popular turntable chassis. This design choice differentiates it in the market.
