## albums
**Previous Album (2012)** : This album, released in 2012, was the band's work preceding their latest release. It contributed significantly to the band's early critical and sales success. "salí en el 2012 su anterior trabajo."
**First Album - Bestselling Record for Label** : DXX's debut album achieved tremendous critical and sales success. It holds the record as the best-selling album in the history of their label, Young Turks, which is characterized as a very small record company. "De hecho el primer álbum de DXX es el álbum más vendido de la historia de esta discográfica."
**Two First Albums - Tremendous Success** : The band's initial two albums were met with immense critical acclaim and significant sales success, establishing a high benchmark for their subsequent releases and contributing to high audience expectations. "ha tenido un éxito de crítica y un éxito venta tremendo con sus dos primeros álbumes."
**New Album (January) - Disparate Critical Reception** : Released in January, this album has been subject to a significant disparity of opinions from both critics and the public. While many important music critics have awarded it high scores, others have given it very low marks, describing it as "absolutely boring." The album was recorded over more than two years, spanning three different cities and two countries, and consists of 10 songs. "acerca del nuevo disco de DXX que salía en enero, hace muy poco y estamos leyendo la escrítica, se estamos escuchando el disco y todos nos estamos haciendo a pregunta en tanto de extraña y es, es realmente bueno el disco de DXX es tan malo como lo cuentan el último disco que han sacado."

## curiosities
**10-Year Career Mark** : The group is nearing its tenth anniversary, marking a decade since their formation and the start of their career. "va a hacer ahora 10 años."
**Slow Release Frequency** : DXX maintains a release schedule of one album approximately every 4-5 years, a pace considered to be a very long wait compared to the majority of bands. This infrequent output cultivates heightened expectations for significant evolution and exceptional quality in their music. "va a ritmo de un disco de 4-5 años, yo creo que se ha hecho muchísimo esperar."
**High Expectations for Evolution** : Due to their infrequent album releases, which sometimes amount to only two albums in an entire decade, there is a strong expectation for a particularly robust, aggressive evolution and an incredible level of quality in their musical output. Audiences do not anticipate the slower, more typical evolution seen in other groups from a band that takes five years to produce an album. "Yo creo que todos nos esperamos una cierta evolución especialmente fuerte agresiva y una especie de increíble calidad dentro de la música que hacen porque para tomarse cinco años para hacer un álbum es lo menos que te puedes esperar."
**Second Album Lacked Evolution** : A notable criticism directed at DXX's second album, despite its overall quality, was its perceived lack of substantial evolutionary progression from their preceding work. "Uno de los grandes problemas que la chacábamos al segundo disco de DX6 a pesar de su normal de calidad era precisamente que no era excesivamente volutivo."
**New Album Recording Process** : The latest DXX album underwent an extensive recording period lasting over two years. This process involved sessions in three distinct cities across two different countries, indicative of a demanding and high-level production effort. "es un álbum que se ha grabado durante más de dos años y en tres ciudades distintas de dos países."
**Jamie XX's Dominant Influence** : Over the years, DXX has increasingly been perceived as Jamie XX performing with a pair of vocalists. His significant professional evolution during the band's five-year hiatus has profoundly shaped the sound of DXX's current music, particularly influencing the background instrumentation. "Creo que ya hoy en día todos hemos asumido que DXX es que empieza a convertirse en Jamie XX con un par de cantantes."
**Oliver and Romi's Creative Stagnation** : While Jamie XX's contributions to the background sound of DXX's music have evolved enormously, the creative input from Romi and Oliver, specifically concerning their guitars and lyrics, has not shown significant progression. Their guitar sounds are described as very similar to those on their previous two albums, and their lyrical themes and writing style remain largely unchanged. "la parte de la que han hecho ellos no ha evolucionado especialmente. Marafiro las guitarras son muy parecidos a lo que han sido los otros dos trabajos y las letras son muy similares tan del temática como en la manera de escribir o como la manera de expresar sus sentimientos."
**Lyrical Maturity Discrepancy** : When DXX began their career, the members were in their early twenties, making their lyrics about heartbreak and adolescent struggles understandable. However, now in their thirties, an anticipated level of maturity in their lyrical content or emotional expression is notably absent from their latest album. "DXX era muy joven es cuando empezaron su carrera, tenía un poco más de 20 años, entonces esas letras y esa manera trágica hablar del desamor o de estos problemas que puede tener una persona postada al escente eran comprensibles pero ahora que tienen 30 años ya se despresopó en un grado de madurez en las letras o en la manera de expresar los sentimientos que no se ve especialmente en el álbum."
**Album Quality vs. Expectations** : Despite the inherent quality of the new album, certain aspects are criticized as unacceptable or as failing to meet the high demands placed on a group that releases an album only every five years, especially one that takes two years to produce a mere 10 songs. "Lo único que espero es no tener que hacer otros 5 años a para dónde nos lleva es un algún concierto de las cosas reprochables entiendo algunas críticas negativas que está teniendo pero lo que es la calidad del álbum yo creo que es una gran calidad lo que no quita que no tenga otros aspectos que no se le pueden permitir o que se le tienden a exigir a un grupo que va a hacer un álbum cada 5 años y sobre todo que se tira dos años para hacer un álbum de 10 canciones."
**Aggressive Media Marketing** : There is a perception that the media attention surrounding DXX's new album is somewhat "prefabricated," largely orchestrated by their small record label, Young Turks. The label, which considers DXX a significant revenue generator, is seen as intentionally creating an "enormous snowball" of buzz around the band. "Otra cosa que está reprochando mucho es un poco la repercusión mediática que es un poco casi prefabricando alrededor del álbum de 16 o discográfica que es muy pequeña ha convertido a DXX como en la vacalechera que les da un montón de dinero."
**Overpriced Limited Edition Release** : An expensive and unusual limited edition of the new album, priced at nearly 50 euros or dollars, was produced in such vast quantities that it began to be discounted by 10 dollars within just a month of its release. This marketing decision is highlighted as part of the label's aggressive promotional tactics. "esa carísima y extraña edición limitada que han hecho que cuesta casi 50 euros o casi 50 dólares dependiendo de dónde estéis y la cual se han fabricado tantísimas unidades que a día de hoy apenas un mes después de que salir el álbum en algunos sitios ya empieza a estar rebajada 10 dólares."
**Potential Transformation to Mass Consumption** : There is an open question regarding whether DXX is undergoing a transformation from an independent group to one focused on mass consumption. This kind of transition is relatively common for independent bands, with varied outcomes regarding success. "no sabemos si es una transformación del grupo gran consumo, estas transformaciones de grupos independientes a tener gran consumo, gracias a veces son relativamente comunes, hay que le funcionar bien, hay a quien no le ha funcionado tan bien, pero bueno son enormemente respetables."

# label - Young Turks

## curiosities
**Small and Successful Label** : Young Turks is characterized as a very small record label that historically generates little revenue. However, they made an enormous discovery with DXX, which has become a significant asset for them. "es la discográfica que está detrás de DXX es una escográfica muy pequeña que hace muy poco dinero y que ha sido un descubriiente enorme para ellos."
**DXX as "Cash Cow"** : DXX has become the primary "cash cow" for Young Turks, providing them with substantial financial returns. The first DXX album holds the record as the best-selling release in the label's history. "ha convertido a DXX como en la vacalechera que les da un montón de dinero al final ya un turc, es la discográfica que está detrás de DXX es una escográfica muy pequeña que hace muy poco dinero y que ha sido un descubriiente enorme para ellos."
**Aggressive Marketing for DXX Album** : The label is noted for generating an "enormous snowball" of media buzz around DXX and its new album. This included an expensive and unusual limited edition release, priced at nearly 50 euros or dollars, which quickly saw price reductions of 10 dollars barely a month after its launch due to overproduction. "Creo que están generando un enorme bola de nieve en torno al grupo y también ciertas cosas que no terminamos entender como por ejemplo esa carísima y extraña edición limitada que han hecho que cuesta casi 50 euros o casi 50 dólares dependiendo de dónde estéis y la cual se han fabricado tantísimas unidades que a día de hoy apenas un mes después de que salir el álbum en algunos sitios ya empieza a estar rebajada 10 dólares."

# curiosity

**Disparity of Critical Opinion on New Albums** : It is uncommon to observe such a significant disparity of opinions regarding a new album, as exemplified by DXX's latest release. While numerous critics are awarding very high scores, an equal number of prominent music critics are issuing very poor ratings, often describing the album as "absolutely boring." "es curioso porque no suede darse tan a menudo semejante de disparidad de opiniones, porque está leyendo muchas crícitos, que están dando puntaciones verdaderamente altas, pero en otro lado tenemos un montón de otros críticos musicales importantes, que les están dando notas verdaderamente pésimas, que están diciendo que son discos absolutamente aburridos."
**Expected Evolution for Infrequent Bands** : For music groups that release albums very infrequently, perhaps only two in an entire decade, there is a heightened expectation from the audience for a strong, aggressive evolution and exceptional quality in their music. A slower, more typical evolutionary pace, common for other bands, is not anticipated from artists who dedicate five years to producing an album. "Una evolución más lenta y normal que te puedes esperar en otra clase de grupos, a este tipo de grupos es algo que presuponois que no va a hacer."
**Transformation from Independent to Mainstream** : The transition of independent music groups towards a mass consumption audience is a relatively common phenomenon within the industry. While some groups navigate this transformation successfully, others do not, yet these shifts are generally viewed as respectable. "estas transformaciones de grupos independientes a tener gran consumo, gracias a veces son relativamente comunes, hay que le funcionar bien, hay a quien no le ha funcionado tan bien, pero bueno son enormemente respetables."
