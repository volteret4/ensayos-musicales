## curiosities
**Most Important White Rap Group** : The Beastie Boys, while not the first white rap group in history, were undoubtedly the most important, leaving a significant impact on music. "no fueron el primer grupo de rap blanco de la historia, pero sin duda fueron los más importantes."
**Pivotal Influence in Music (30-40 Years)** : The Beastie Boys have been extremely important figures in music over the last 30 to 40 years, extending their significance beyond just the rap genre. "fueron gente importantísima en los últimos 30 a 40 años de música."
**Market Opening and Musical Evolution** : Their music, particularly their first album, was pivotal not only for rap but for music in general, as they opened a new market and contributed to a huge evolution within music history. "abrieron un nuevo mercado y supusieron una enorme evolución dentro de la historia de la música."
**Wealthy New York Origin** : The Beastie Boys were initially described as four wealthy individuals from New York, who were passionate about music, explored various styles, and were initially struggling to find their direction. "eran cuatro chales pudidos en Nueva York, eran muy aficinados en la música, seguían muchos estilos y querían triunfar y estaban un poco dando palos de ciego."
**Genre Experimentation Before Hip Hop** : Before fully committing to hip hop, the band experimented with various genres, releasing records in punk, rock, and practically everything they could think of. "Hicieron discos de pan, hicieron discos de rock, hicieron discos de prácticamente todo lo que se le ocurrió hasta que decidieron probar en el hip hop."
**Non-Purist Approach to Rap** : Ironically, because they were not huge fanatics of rap, they adopted a non-purist stance, which allowed them to freely mix and "destroy" rap with other elements without concern. "como ellos no eran unos grandes fanáticos del rap, tampoco era muy purista, si tampoco les importaba mucho mezclarlo, destrozarlo con cualquier cosa."
**Opened New Market via Non-Purism** : This lack of deep knowledge and especially the absence of purism concerning rap ultimately opened a new market for the music industry, leading to significant innovation. "esa falta de conocimiento profundo sobre el rap y sobre todo esa falta de purismo sobre el rap es lo que habría un nuevo mercado para industria música."
**Pioneers of Musical Fusion** : Their fundamental importance stems from their fusion of rap with other musical styles, including electronic music, alternative music, Bossa Nova, and country music, opening rap to a vast stylistic blend that continues to influence contemporary music. "la fusión que hacen con otros estilos de música, como la electrónica, como la música alternativa, como la voz anoba, como la música Country"
**Talented Musicians Beyond Rap** : The Beastie Boys are recognized as great musicians in their own right, not merely as rap singers, which enabled their wide-ranging musical fusions. "ellos al final son grandes músicos aparte de cantantes de rap"
**Influence on Trip Hop and Modern Groups** : They were a significant influence on the early generations of Trip Hop, particularly the Bristol scene, impacting groups like Massive Attack, and are considered essential for understanding contemporary acts like Gorillaz. "fueron una importante influencia dentro de las primeras generaciones del Trip Hub, de Bristol, de gente como Massive Attack y sobre todo hoy en día no podemos entender a grupos como Gorillas"
**Bridge Between Rap and Alternative Music** : Despite being widely associated with rap, the Beastie Boys are fundamentally positioned halfway between rap and independent alternative music. "los bistiboys están a medio camino entre el rap y la música alternativa independiente."
**Diverse Non-Rap Discography** : Many people are unaware that the Beastie Boys have released numerous non-rap albums, ranging from fully instrumental works (almost akin to jazz) to records blending Bossa Nova and country music. "tienen un montón de discos que no son de rap, tienen desde discos completamente instrumentales, casi de música ya, a discos que mezclan voz anoba, música Country"
**Champions of Artists' Rights** : Within the music industry, the Beastie Boys played a very important role in the fight for artists to claim their rights and understand the allocation of royalties. "los bistiboys han sido muy importantes en la lucha de los artistas por reclamar sus derechos."
**Investigation into Royalty Allocation** : A significant part of their career was dedicated to investigating how artists' royalties were unfairly managed and what portion of the money they generated actually reached their pockets. "pasaron mucha parte de su carrera defendiendo un poco, intentando indagar sobre cómo se asesinaban los royalties o los artistas y qué parte del dinero que ellos producían y va para dar a sus bolsillos."
**Legacy of Royalty Recovery** : Thanks to the Beastie Boys' efforts, many artists today have recovered substantial amounts of money from their earnings. "muchos artistas han recuperado muchísimo dinero, gracias a los bistiboys"
**Conflict with Multinational Label** : They spent their lives in conflict with a major multinational corporation, Grandergian Records, over artist rights and royalties. "ellos se pasaron subida enfrentados a una de las grandes multinacionales, Grandergian Records"
**Founders and Patrons of Independent Label** : They eventually formed their own independent record label, which released important alternative music groups, establishing them as significant patrons of alternative music throughout the 1990s. "acabaron formando su propia discografía independiente de la que salieron importantes grupos de música alternativa, ellos han sido grandes mecenas de la música alternativa durante los 90."
**Four Albums on Rolling Stone's Greatest List** : As a testament to their influence and quality, the Beastie Boys have no less than four albums featured on Rolling Stone's famous list of the 500 greatest albums in music history, a feat surpassing many other groups. "los bistiboys tienen nada menos que cuatro álbumes, que es más de lo que tienen muchísimos grupos"
**Essential for Understanding Influenced Artists** : Their work is a crucial source for understanding other significant artists, such as Massive Attack, DJ Shadow, and Gorillaz. "una fuente muy importante para entender mucha música importante que si escucha gente que sigue este blog, cómo puede ser más hipotáquia, cómo puede ser diyeisciado, cómo puede ser gorila"
**Shared Style with Gorillaz** : Listening to a Beastie Boys album and then a Gorillaz album reveals that they share the same, albeit somewhat evolved, musical style. "escuchas un disco de los bistiboys, escuchas un disco de gorila, si te das cuenta de que es lo mismo, es una versión un poco volucinada del mismo estilo."
**Musical Geniuses and Fusion Pioneers** : They are considered some of the great geniuses of the last 40 years of music, creating complex works that extend far beyond rap, and are recognized as pioneers of musical fusion that began in the mid-90s and was crucial throughout the decade, producing some of the best albums in music history. "los bistiboy son uno de los grandes geniuses, los últimos 40 años de música... uno de los padres de la fusión musical que empezó a mitad de los 90"

# genre - Rap

## curiosities
**Lack of Early Evolution** : The initial six to eight years of rap music, spanning until the mid-1980s, were characterized by a limited amount of evolution, with the genre maintaining a highly purist style. "Los primeros seis, siete, ocho años del rap hasta mitad de los ochenta son muy pocos volutivos."
**Purist Origins in New York** : Early rap was a very purist musical style, largely concentrated within specific ghettos of New York, reflecting a localized and unmixed approach to the genre. "El rap era un estilo muy purista en unos getos muy concentrados en Nueva York."
**1996 as a Turning Point** : The year 1996 marked a significant turning point for rap music, largely due to collaborations (like franty MC with Eros Mith) and the emergence of rap fusion albums, like the first album by Beastie Boys. "se producen en enorme punto de inflexión en 1996"
