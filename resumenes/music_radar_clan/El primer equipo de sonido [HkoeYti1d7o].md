# instrument - Tocadiscos

## curiosities
**Source of Sound Quality** : The turntable is the fundamental component from which the sound quality originates. If the turntable is not good, the rest of the sound chain (preamplifier, amplifier, speakers) will only be able to maintain the initial quality and prevent loss, but it will never improve it. "el tocatiscos es de donde arrastra la calidad de sonido."

**Preamplifier Integration** : Turntables can come in two main types: those with an integrated preamplifier and those without one. This distinction is crucial for connecting it correctly to the rest of the sound system. "Tenemos los tipos de tocatiscos, que son los tocatiscos con preamplificador y sin preamplificador."

**Audio-Technica LP60 Model** : This specific turntable model is mentioned as an example of a turntable that includes an integrated preamplifier, simplifying the setup process for users. "audio técnica en el LP60, incluye preamplificador."

**Audio-Technica LP120 Model** : This specific turntable model is mentioned as an example of a turntable that does *not* include an integrated preamplifier, meaning an external preamplifier or an amplifier with built-in preamplification would be required. "en el LP120, no incluye preamplificador."

# instrument - Preamplificador

## curiosities
**Role in Sound Chain** : The preamplifier is a necessary component in the sound system chain, specifically for turntables, and cannot be omitted. It performs an initial amplification step before the main amplifier. "si el tocatiscos no tiene el preamplificador es el amplificador, el que tiene que tener el preamplificador, pero no puede desaparecer."

**Cost-Effectiveness for Quality** : Despite being considered by some as the most "dispensable" part of the sound chain, acquiring a good external preamplifier is recommended for those seeking true sound quality. They are quite economical, with good models available for around 30-40 euros, and help prevent quality loss during the initial amplification stage. "Es bastante económico y no suele hacer una gran transformación. Yo creo que la parte más prescindible de todo, pero que si queréis tener un equipo realmente de calidad deberías comprarlo. Porque por 40 euros, 30 euros os podéis comprar uno bueno y hace esa primera amplificación donde muchas veces se pierde cierta calidad de sonido."

**Connection Inputs** : Amplifiers that include preamplification feature distinct audio inputs for turntables, typically labeled "fono" (phono) and "line." The "fono" input is designed to pass through preamplification, which is necessary for turntables, whereas the "line" input does not. It is critical to connect the turntable to the correct "fono" input for proper functioning. "Los amplificadores que llevan preamplificación tienen una entrada distinta para los tocatiscos. Entonces, vais a ver que detrás tenéis dos entradas de audio distintas, una que pone line y una que pone fono, una no pasa por preamplificación, la otra sí pasa por preamplificación."

# instrument - Amplificador

## curiosities
**Main Function and Importance** : The amplifier is crucial for sound boosting and powering the speakers, performing the largest part of the sound potentiation. A good amplifier significantly contributes to the overall sound quality. "El amplificador es importante que sea bueno porque hace la mayor parte de la potenciación del sonido."

**Cost vs. Power for Home Use** : When purchasing an amplifier, a higher price often correlates with greater power, which may be unnecessary for typical home use. It's advised against spending excessively on monstrous power levels that would only be needed in settings like a disco. "tenés en cuenta que el mayor coste de la amplificación es la potencia y si tú vas a tener tu amplificador en tu casa no necesitas una potencia monstruosa en tu amplificador."

**Second-Hand Purchase Recommendation** : Buying a second-hand amplifier is a highly recommended and cost-effective strategy. An excellent amplifier can be obtained for significantly less money; for example, a 25-year-old amplifier purchased for 100-120 euros can be fantastic. "El amplificador siempre es pensado que es el modelo perfecto para comprarte uno de segunda mano. Yo he metirado muchos años con un amplificador que tenía 25 años de segunda mano y era fantástico y me costó 120 euros 100 euros."

**Affordable Quality for New Amplifiers** : It is possible to purchase very good quality new amplifiers without spending excessively. Around 200 euros can secure a good amplifier, and about 250 euros can buy an "excellent" one. "con 200 euros ya te puedes comprar un amplificador muy bueno, bastante bueno. Y con 250 euros te compras un pepinaco de amplificador."

**Genre-Specific Performance** : Amplifiers can be optimized for different musical genres. Some are better for rock music, while others excel with electronic and digital sounds, allowing users to tailor their equipment to their specific listening preferences. "Hay amplificadores que son mejores para la música rock, hay amplificadores que son muy buenos para el sonido de el electrónica y digital."

**Complexity for Beginners** : Highly powerful and complex amplifiers, while appealing, often come with numerous control dials (e.g., 8 wheels) that can be overwhelming and difficult for beginners to understand and utilize effectively, potentially leading to a suboptimal user experience despite the high cost. "compro un amplificador muy potente y muy complejo, está muy bien hasta que tienes 8 ruedas de control del sonido que no sabes cómo usar."

# instrument - Altavoces

## curiosities
**Active vs. Passive Types** : Speakers are categorized into two main types: passive and active. Passive speakers solely reproduce sound and require an external amplifier. Active speakers, however, have an integrated amplifier within them. "Tenemos dos tipos de altavoces, los altavoces activos y pasivos. Los altavoces pasivos simplemente reproducen sonido, no sirven para otra cosa. Sin embargo, los altavoces activos incluyen un amplificador dentro."

**Identification of Active Speakers** : Active speakers can often be identified by the presence of a volume control, even if it's small, as this indicates the inclusion of an internal amplifier. They are also typically specified as "active" or "passive." "los altavoces activos que tienen amplificador, siempre tienen control de volumen. Y aunque al mejor no tengan amplificación grande, si tiene un control de volumen, siempre tienen por pequeña que sea una pequeña amplificación."

**Flexibility of Active Speakers** : If good active speakers with an integrated amplifier are purchased, they offer flexibility. Should the user later decide to connect an external amplifier, they can simply choose not to use the internal amplifier of the active speakers. "un nosbueno salga a voces con amplificador. Si tú mañana le conectas, un amplificador a este notebook de igual puedes no usar el amplificador de los altavoces."

# curiosity

**Fundamental Sound System Components** : A complete sound system typically consists of four interconnected devices: a turntable, a preamplifier, an amplifier, and speakers. These components must be connected in a specific sequence—turntable to preamplifier, preamplifier to amplifier, and amplifier to speakers—to ensure proper sound reproduction. "Un equipo de sonido se basa en cuatro aparatos que van conectados y es un tocatiscos que va conectado a un preamplificador del que hablamos en otro día, que va conectado a un amplificador y que ese amplificador va conectado a los altavoces."

**Importance of Component Connection Order** : Respecting the correct connection cycle of the four sound system components (turntable, preamplifier, amplifier, speakers) is critical. Failure to follow this order can result in no sound, very poor sound quality, or unusual audio output. "El ciclo del equipo de sonido de estos cuatro aparatos es importante respetarlo, si no complices estos cuatro pasos, puede que no suene nada, puede que suene muy mal o que suene muy raro."

**All-in-One Sound Systems** : The simplest configuration for a sound system is an all-in-one unit that integrates all components, including speakers. While generally not of very high quality, these units are often misrepresented as "horrible" and can be a suitable starting point for beginners. "El primer sistema más sencillo es un aparató todo incluido, que tiene todos estos aparatos en el mismo, incluso los altavoces. Estos aparatos no suelen ser de muy buena calidad, pero no son tan horrible muchas veces cómo te los venden en muchos sitios, tampoco son el fin del mundo si quieres empezar, no son tan mala opción."

**Common Beginner Configuration (Turntable, Amplifier with Preamp, Speakers)** : A widely adopted setup for beginners involves a turntable connected to an amplifier that has a built-in preamplifier, which then connects to speakers. This setup requires careful attention to connecting the turntable to the correct "fono" input on the amplifier. "la más común del tocatiscos con un amplificador y con unos altavoces. Además, como la mayoría intentáis tener tocatiscos que son buenos con lo cual muchos ya no llevan preamplificador, intentáis conectar directamente el amplificador."

**Basic Configuration (Turntable with Preamp, Active Speakers)** : A straightforward and effective system for beginners, or those primarily using a turntable, consists of a turntable with an integrated preamplifier (like an Audio-Technica LP60) directly connected to active speakers (which have their own amplifier). This setup fulfills all four necessary steps of the sound chain in a simplified manner. "Es una buena configuración para la gente que está empezando o que directamente solo va a usarlo para conectar un tocatiscos. Es una buena opción para no gastarte mucho dinero."

**Advanced Configuration (Turntable with Preamp, Good Amplifier, Speakers)** : A more complex but higher-quality setup involves a turntable with an integrated preamplifier connected to a dedicated, high-quality amplifier, which then drives the speakers. This allows for better sound potentiation and overall fidelity. "tú puedes coger tu tocatiscos con preamplificación integrada, comprarte un buen amplificador y eso conectarlo a los altavoces."

**Gradual Equipment Upgrades** : Instead of making a large initial investment in expensive equipment, it is recommended to start with a reasonably priced and functional system. Components can then be gradually upgraded or added as the user gains experience and identifies specific needs for improving the sound quality to their personal preference. "no es tanto gastarte 2.000 euros en un equipo, sino honestamente, empieza con un equipo que esté relativamente bien, que sea relativamente asequible y vete comprandole piezas o añadí en los pasos según tú vayas sintiendo que necesitas mejorarlo."

**Avoiding Overpriced Equipment** : There is a tendency among some "sound snobs" to recommend overly expensive and complex audio equipment, often suggesting investments of 2000 euros or more. This is often unnecessary for home use, as much of the cost goes towards excessive power or features that a typical user won't need or know how to use. "no os preocupáis tanto por comprar grandes aparatos costosos en contra de lo que dice mucho flipado del mundo del sonido que se cree Mozart."
