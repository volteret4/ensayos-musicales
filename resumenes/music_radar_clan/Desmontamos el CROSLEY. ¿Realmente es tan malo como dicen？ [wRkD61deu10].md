# instrument - Tocadiscos (Turntable)

## curiosities
**All-in-One Turntable Stylus Pressure**: All-in-one turntables typically feature a very high stylus pressure because they often lack essential components like counterweights, which can result in pressures destructive to vinyl records. "estos aparatos de todo incluido siempre tienen un peso muy elevado porque no llevan pues contra pesos u otras cosas."
**Internal Components for Sound Processing**: A functional all-in-one turntable should ideally integrate a preamplifier, an amplifier, and speakers within its design to properly process and output sound. "dentro de este crossley deberíamos generar todo el equipo de sonido del toque a veces el pre amplificador y del amplificador y altavoces porque sin eso esto no funciona."
**Cable Quality and Isolation for Sound Integrity**: High-quality audio equipment, including turntables, relies on thick, insulated cables to transmit sound effectively, as exposed or inferior cables are a significant source of interference and loss of sound quality. "si tú ya no usas unos cables gruesos y de calidad como los que llevan los buenos aparatos y encima no los aislas vas a tener un montón de interferencias."
**Cable and Cartridge Vibration Impact**: Vibrations originating from internal cables and the cartridge within a turntable can severely degrade the overall sound quality and even risk scratching the vinyl record. "el temblor del cable salida y la cápsula madre va a ir desbarsoversen el vinilo y va a absorberse en el sonido. Entonces todo este temblor va a destopar un montón de calidad de sonido. Incluso puede rallar el disco del vinilo."
**Quality Variance Among All-in-One Turntables**: Not all "all-in-one" turntables are inherently poor quality; some models are considered "relatively good" and can serve as acceptable starting points for beginners, highlighting that the issue lies with specific problematic models rather than the entire category. "El problema no son los tocado de todo, incluido. El problema es este tocado de todo, incluido que no solo es un desastre para cuidar tus discos de vinilo sino que esto es imposible que son indecentemente."

# instrument - Aguja (Stylus)

## curiosities
**Critical Importance of Stylus Pressure**: The pressure exerted by the stylus on a vinyl record is a critically important specification for any turntable, directly impacting both sound reproduction and record longevity. "la forma de trabajar de la aguja la presión de la aguja es importante."
**Damaging Effects of Excessive Stylus Pressure**: When stylus pressure is excessively high, it can cause significant and irreparable damage to vinyl records, particularly affecting older pressings. "Esa presión que es hacerse sobre el disco de vinilo es muy importante porque cuando es demasiado elevada dañan los discos de vinilo sobre todo los discos antiguos."
**Comparative Stylus Pressure in All-in-One Units**: Other all-in-one turntables, distinct from the Crossley, typically operate with a stylus pressure ranging from 3.6 to 3.8 grams, which is considered a more normal and less damaging level for vinyl records. "hay otros aparatos todo en uno que los son un crossley que trabajan a 3,6,3,8 que ya es otra cosa."

# instrument - Disco de Vinilo (Vinyl Record)

## curiosities
**Increased Vulnerability of Older Vinyl Records**: Older vinyl records are particularly susceptible to damage from high stylus pressure compared to their modern counterparts. This is attributed to industrial manufacturing differences that historically resulted in a "whiter" or softer material composition. "dañan los discos de vinilo sobre todo los discos antiguos porque aunque son del mismo material que hoy en día son más blancos por ciertas razones industriales."

# instrument - Altavoces (Speakers)

## curiosities
**Substitutability of Built-in Speaker Power**: The wattage of a turntable's built-in speakers is not the most critical factor, as external speakers can be connected to significantly improve the audio output, even if the integrated speakers are of poor quality. "La potencia de los altavoces nos da un poco igual porque al final tú le puedes conectar tus altavoces y son a muy mal."
