# artist - Burial

## curiosities
**Ghost Identity and Anonymity** : Burial is widely known as a reclusive and enigmatic artist, often referred to as a "ghost." He has never given a concert, conducted an interview, or spoken publicly, and his physical appearance remained unknown for many years. This extreme anonymity led to widespread speculation that he might be a pseudonym for other famous artists. "Burial es un fantasma nadie conoce a Burial nadie sabe nada de Burial no da conciertos no da entrevistas físicamente no habla no salen la prensa nadie sabe nada de Burial."
**Initial Misattributions** : Due to his anonymity, there was significant speculation about Burial's true identity. He was thought by some to be Autechre (X-Twin), Norman Fath-El-Deane, or 'V', a prominent figure in British dubstep. "se la atribuía que posiblemente Burial podía ser a X-Twin se pensó que podía ser normal como Faddeux Lim se pensaba que podía ser el V que es uno de los grandes nombres del Daphsett británico."
**Revealing Photo** : After much speculation, Burial eventually sent a low-quality photo of himself, taken with a mobile phone, to dispel the rumors. This revealed him to be a young man from South London, the epicentre of the late 90s and 2000s electronic movements like dubstep. "un día Burial nos mandaba una foto suya hecha así con el móvil cutremente y nos damos cuenta de por primera vez teníamos una foto de Burial."
**Hobbyist Musician with a Day Job** : Despite being a highly influential figure in 21st-century music, Burial does not pursue music professionally. He holds a regular job, and many of his family, colleagues, and acquaintances are unaware of his renowned musical career, which he treats as a hobby. "muchos de sus familiares compañeros de trabajo y que gente que lo rodea no saben que es un referente de la historia del siglo XXI tiene su propio trabajo es sólo hace esto como hobby no se dedica profesionalmente a esto."
**Absence of Live Performances** : Burial has consistently maintained his commitment to anonymity by never performing live. He has never been seen physically at any public musical event. "ni lo ves en ningún sitio ni el no ha dado jamás un concierto en nunca se la ha visto físicamente en ningún sitio."
**South London Underground Origins** : Burial emerged from the vibrant underground electronic scene of South London, a hub for innovative electronic music starting in the first half of the 90s and continuing into the 2000s, encompassing genres like darkstep, rammamba, 2-step, and UK garage. "Burial nace en la estena del South London de electrónica underground."
**Broad Stylistic Impact** : While often categorized as dubstep, Burial's style is complex and difficult to neatly pigeonhole, fitting broadly into the underground electronic movement. He didn't invent radical new sounds but profoundly reinterpreted music, breaking stylistic canons that influenced even charts like those of NME for 15 years and beyond. "burial cambia por completo rompe muchísimos canones estilísticos que trascienden a que incluso a las listas de la de la antivio la niumus musical expres."
**Alchemy of Sound Design** : Lacking a professional studio or a vast library of sounds, Burial became a "sound alchemist," forced to create his own unique sonic palette. He records everyday sounds like cars and people talking on the subway, then processes and reinterprets them into complex sonic textures. "burial se convierte en el gran alquimista de los sonidos burial no tiene un estudio ni tiene una gran base de la que puede sacar sonidos entonces se ve obligado a fabricar sus sonidos."
**Innovative Sound Processing Techniques** : Burial employs a sophisticated and unconventional approach to sound manipulation. He takes audio tracks, such as a voice, and applies extreme acceleration to some parts while drastically slowing down others, then joins these disparate segments with abrupt cuts to create complex, fractured soundscapes. "coge una pista de audio como voy a ser la de violense y acelerar unos trozos mientras ralentizan el extremo otros trozos mientras pasa velocidad ultra rápida el siguiente trozo y lo junta todo haciendo cortes."
**Revolutionary Rhythmic Disruption** : Burial is celebrated for pioneering the "ruptura del ritmo" (rupture of rhythm), defining himself as a "great rhythmic artist." Unlike conventional electronic music that relies on synchronized loops and samples, Burial intentionally desynchronizes his sounds and uses inexact loops, creating a complex, characteristic rhythm that features subtle spatial gaps and timing dislocations. "burial introduce la gran revolución de la ruptura del ritmo burial muchas veces se define como la rítmica como el gran artista rítmico."
**Profound Emotional Depth** : Despite his music's seemingly simple structure of fast basslines and slow, processed voices, Burial achieves a remarkably complex and passionate emotional depth. He conveys profound, often hard-to-identify sensations, setting him apart from the generally less sentimental dubstep, 2-step, and UK garage genres of his time. "consigue transmitir muchísimos sonidos muy profundos y sobre todo muchísimas sensaciones como muy profondas y difíciles de identificar sí que es verdad que el dapster y el tuster y el y toda esta parte del yucaygaras no era muy cargada en ese sentido sentimentalmente pero burial sí lo es."
**Intuitive Visual Approach to Rhythm** : Burial employs a unique, intuitive, and visual method for constructing his rhythmic foundations. He consciously seeks a "visual aesthetic" within audio waveforms, often shaping his rhythmic bases based on how the peaks and valleys of the audio appear visually, aligning his technical work with a personal visual preference. "él busca una estética visual dentro de las ondas de audio entonces él en muchos casos trabaja las bases rítmicas visualmente de que le gusten visualmente cómo hace los picos ibaillés del audio."
**Evocative Song Definitions** : Burial defines his songs not by conventional themes but by highly specific, emotionally intense yet mundane feelings. He describes them as sensations like "being alone one night in a McDonald's" or "the feeling when it starts raining but it's not cold," creating complex, relatable, yet hard-to-pinpoint emotional states. "él define sus canciones pues como esa sensación que tiene pues se unicionando un día sólo en un McDonald's de noche o esa sensación que tienes cuando está empezando a llover pero no hace frío define sus canciones como momentos como emocionalmente intensos a la vez cotidianos y muy difíciles identificar."
**Scarcity of Post-Album Releases and Merchandise** : Since his full-length albums, Burial has primarily released singles and EPs, which are often exceptionally difficult to acquire. Additionally, official merchandise like T-shirts is virtually non-existent, reflecting his continued reclusive nature. "Burial ha seguido siendo un fantasma no vuelto a sacar discos y ha ido sacando singles y epés que son francamente complejos de comprar a veces yo los tengo todos pero me he llevado años poder tenerlos."
**Influence on Contemporary Artists** : Burial is regarded as a central figure in the evolution of numerous artists, directly influencing the rhythmic approaches and soundscapes of figures such as Thom Yorke (Radiohead), Björk, Arca, Lorde, Nicolas Jaar, James Blake, and Bon Iver. These artists themselves often acknowledge Burial as a pivotal influence. "burial es el protagonista absoluto de la evolución de todas estas bandas no lo digo lo dicen ellos mismos más refiero hoy en día yo creo que es difícil encontrar entrevistas de Tom York en las que él no menciona burial."

## albums
**Untrue (Year Not Specified) - Best 21st Century Album** : Considered by the speaker to be the best album of the 21st century, "Untrue" is described as magical and a masterpiece of modern music. It showcases Burial's unique blend of simple rhythmic structures with profound emotional depth. "el mejor disco que ha habido en el siglo XXI es este Andrew de Burial este disco es mágico."
**Untrue (Year Not Specified) - Video Game Samples** : This album extensively features samples from video games, particularly over 30 distinct sounds from "Metal Gear Solid," including weapon reloads, intros, and gunshots. These elements are reprocessed by Burial to create entirely new sonic textures. "en el antrús se han recogido como más de 30 sonidos que tienen sacados el pues por ejemplo el arma que se recarga o cuando hay una intro o cuando hay un disparo."
**Untrue (Year Not Specified) - Subliminal Sound Integration** : The first track of "Untrue" serves as a prime example of Burial's technique of integrating almost subliminal, diffused, low, and desynchronized sounds into his music, which are barely audible but contribute significantly to the overall atmosphere. "la primera pista del antrú que es casi no se oye es como un ejemplo de todos esos sonidos que le mete burial de fondo a las canciones."

# artist - Thom Yorke

## curiosities
**Burial's Influence on Rhythmic Approach** : Thom Yorke became a dedicated follower of Burial after 2006 and reinterpreted his own method of working with music, particularly focusing on rhythmic bases inspired by Burial's style. He has publicly stated that his album "The King of Limbs" and his solo career, including "Tomorrow's Modern Boxes," are directly influenced by Burial's rhythmic innovations. "Tom York después el 2006 se convierte con auténtico seguidor de burial y reinterpreta casi su manera de trabajar la música a partir de las bases ritmias de burial haría un disco prácticamente inspirado en las bases ritmias de burial que es el King of Limbs."

# artist - Björk

## curiosities
**Burial's Influence on Rhythmic Approach** : Björk also reinterpreted her approach to constructing rhythmic bases after being exposed to Burial's work, particularly his innovative rhythmic ruptures and desynchronization techniques. She, like Thom Yorke, was inspired by the unique soundscapes of albums like "Untrue." "vioork reinterpreta su manera de trabajar las bases ritmias a partir de la entruda burial."
**Sampled by Burial** : Burial has sampled Björk's vocals in his own work, reinterpreting her singing in his characteristic processed style. "esta es villosa cantando y este es burial coge en la villa se veis esto es increíble o sea este es ásier cantando en concierto este es ásier pues reinterpretado por burial."

# genre - IDM

## curiosities
**Intellectual but Un-danceable** : IDM (Intelligent Dance Music) emerged as an intellectual counterpart to more intense electronic music, featuring artists like Autechre (Textuín), Tosca, and Boards of Canada (The Tumbles). # curiosity

**South London / Brixton Underground Electronic Scene (Mid-90s to Mid-2000s)** : From the mid-1990s to the mid-2000s, London's South London and Brixton areas fostered a vibrant underground electronic music movement, building on pure techno. This scene, which included genres like darkstep, rammamba, 2-step, and UK garage, was characterized by clandestine parties held in abandoned factories and semi-closed-off areas, many of which have now been converted into hipster restaurants. "nace en Londres concretamente en el south London y en el bricley en esas dos zonas de Londres un movimiento electrónico que se consolida que venía a coger evoluciones del techno más puro y ahí nacía todo el movimiento electrónico en el grande south London."
**Non-Professional Artists in South London Scene** : Many influential figures within the South London underground electronic scene, such as Ozoorton Health and V (a prominent dubstep artist, not the rapper), did not pursue music professionally. They maintained day jobs and released their material through completely independent record labels. "esos nombres importantes que teníamos en esa estena como ozorton health o comodel bi que no es el bi el rapero el bi de hecho no se dedican profesionalmente a esto ellos tenían sus trabajos y luego por las noches pues se pinchaban todo el son del ground el material que se acaban los acaban en discográficas totalmente independientes."
**Electronic Music Production Conventions** : Traditional electronic music adheres to precise canons, primarily based on synchronized loops and samples that are exact divisions of one another, ensuring a highly coordinated sound. Producers often rely on automatic synchronization tools in processing machines to guarantee perfect alignment of all loops. "la electrónica siempre tiene como unos canones muy precisos la electrónica se basa en lúfcia en sample que se coordinan todos unos con otros y que son como unos divisiones exántagos de los otros con lo cual la música es como muy sincronizada."
**Manchester Scene's Influence on Electronic Music** : The "Manchester scene" (likely referring to Madchester) played a role in the evolution of electronic music, after which electronic sounds increasingly fused with indie movements, indicating a significant cross-pollination of genres. "después del match ester entonces arraba de la electrónica se mezclo mucho con los movimientos indis."
