# artist - The Chemical Brothers

## members

## albums
**Surrender (1999) - Most Successful Album** : This album is widely considered the band's most successful and, in terms of quality, virtually their best work. Its significant success led to almost all its tracks being viable as singles, with three official singles released in 1999 achieving immense popularity and heavy rotation. "su álbum de más éxito y prácticamente en el mejor calidad de manera un ámime."

## songs
**Music Response (1999)** : This particular track is an unconditional staple in all Chemical Brothers' tours and appears across their discography, sometimes as a full song, sometimes as a sampled element. Ed Simons, known as "the inexpressive active member" of The Chemical Brothers, has consistently identified it as one of the best pieces they have ever created and states he is still not tired of it today. Despite the band's affection for the song and its universal acclaim, Virgin Records initially harbored significant doubts about its commercial viability as a single. It was eventually released as a fourth single approximately 1.5 years after the "Surrender" album had been on the market, prompted by the continued strong performance of the three earlier singles. "El mío sí Respons es una pieza que para los que ya he sido a conciertos de los Chemical Prodex suena incondicionalmente en todas sus giras, en todos sus discos, a veces suena cocacción, a veces suena cocosampleado y que incluso éximos más conocido como el activo inexpresivo de los Chemical Prodex siempre había definido como una de las mejores piezas que ha hecho y que hoy en día no se ha casado de ella."

## curiosities
**Initial Doubts for Music Response Single** : Despite the band's appreciation for "Music Response" and its high regard among the public, their record label, Virgin Records, expressed considerable doubts regarding its commercial potential as a single. They were unsure if the track was sufficiently commercial to warrant a single release. "tenían ciertas dudas, sobre todo en la Virgy, de quien dependía su disco gráfica, tenían bastantes dudas y hasta que punto era una canción suficientemente comercial para compartir el Singer."
**Music Response Promo Edition Success** : Prior to its official commercial release, a promotional edition of "Music Response" was extensively distributed, primarily to professional DJs and production companies. This promo achieved significant success, being utilized for various production tasks, sampling purposes, and as background music in nightclubs, largely attributed to its easily sampleable nature which made it highly adaptable for remixes. "antes de sacarse en single en IP se sacó en un edición promo que se distribuye sobre todo por DJs profesionales y por productoras para que usas en este single, para hacer trabajos de producción, para sampliar o para usarme fondo, para echar en discotecas y tuvo muchísimo áxipo, la verdad es que sus sampliadores es muy fácil y se presta mucho a este tipo de remetras."
**Distinctive Music Response Promo Vinyls** : Numerous vinyl copies of the professional promotional edition of "Music Response" were released to professional DJs, major clubs, and producers for the purpose of creating remixes. The vast majority of these copies, however, were destroyed due to continuous and heavy play. These promo records are distinguishable by their lack of most standard references found on commercial releases, and by featuring unique labels described as "obce de obra emocional para vivir a su venta," likely indicating their non-commercial, promotional nature. "Es fácil de reconocerlo porque no solo no tiene la mayoría de las refrenses, los que tienen disco que semente, sino que tiene las famosas etiquetas de disco como obce de obra emocional para vivir a su venta."
**Music Response Promo as Highly Sought-After Collector's Item** : The promotional vinyl edition of "Music Response" is currently considered a highly prized collector's item. Finding copies in good condition, particularly with their original cardboard sleeves intact, is noted as being exceptionally difficult. "Hoy en día es una pizza bastante bastante cotizada."
**UK Single Chart Disqualification for Music Response** : The commercial single release of "Music Response" included five songs: the main track, two remixes, and two previously unreleased compositions. However, the United Kingdom's music industry, a crucial market, refused to classify it as a single. This was because the UK's technical definition for a single on the charts limits releases to a maximum of three songs, leading to its classification as an EP. Consequently, it was barred from the single charts and single format sections, contributing to its low sales as many potential buyers were unaware of its release due to its absence from chart listings. "El problema fue que el reino nido que es el de nace, el nido de crece, toda la música prácticamente se negó a considerar un single porque el reino nido al parecer técnicamente solo consideró el single cuando tiene solo tres canciones y este disco yo tenía cinco."
**Complex Composition Credits for Music Response** : "Music Response" is notable for having a large number of official composers, despite most of its samples being crafted by The Chemical Brothers. This complexity arises from the inclusion of tiny, nearly imperceptible fragments from an unsuccessful song by an obscure artist named Manicol, who had previously collaborated with Misielio. The presence of these specific fragments legally mandated crediting Misielio as a co-author of "Music Response." "es una de las canciones que más compositor estiene a nivel oficial porque aunque casi todo el sample ha hecho por los cómicas roles, son unos pequeños trozos prácticamente imposibles de reconocer de una canción de una artista realmente no conocida en la manicol que colaboraba con misielio y de teranamigas entonces misielio."
**Ed Simons as "inexpressive active member"** : One of the members of The Chemical Brothers, identified as "éximos" (likely Ed Simons), is famously known as "el activo inexpresivo" (the inexpressive active member) of the group. "éximos más conocido como el activo inexpresivo de los Chemical Prodex"

# curiosity

**United Kingdom Single Chart Rules** : In the United Kingdom, the official definition for a single, for charting purposes, stipulates that a release can contain a maximum of three songs. Releases featuring more than three tracks are typically categorized as EPs, which renders them ineligible for inclusion in the official singles charts. "el reino nido al parecer técnicamente solo consideró el single cuando tiene solo tres canciones y este disco yo tenía cinco."
