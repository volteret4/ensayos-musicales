## albums
**Debut Album (2010)** : James Blake released his first album, which garnered significant critical acclaim for its unique approach to electronic music. Critics considered his proposal groundbreaking and original upon its release. "Saca su primer disco y la crítica alucinó."
**Second and Third Albums** : Following his debut, James Blake released two more albums, bringing his total to three. Each subsequent album demonstrated an improvement and refinement of his distinctive style. "En total tiene tres discos y cada uno es mejor que la anterior."

## songs
**Limit to Your Love (2010)** : This electronic and slow version of Feist's song caused a significant stir among electronic music critics. Despite its apparent simplicity, featuring a single vocalist with piano and a rhythmic base, the track is incredibly complex, incorporating extensive sound processing. Its rhythmic foundation is a slow-motion rendition of a Dubstep rhythm from the early 2000s UK electronic scene, transforming a typically aggressive base into something entirely new. "Esta canción hizo muchísimo revuelo en la crítica de la electrónica."
**A Modern Soul** : This song, which initially appears to be a simple track of a person playing piano with two background sounds, is in fact remarkably complex. It features multiple piano tracks and nearly five distinct tracks of James Blake's own voice, all subjected to extensive sound processing to achieve its clean and pure sound. "Tiene varias pistas de piano, tiene casi cinco pistas distintas de su propia voz y todo asulta y todo tiene un enorme procesado de sonido, desde su voz al piano."
**Unnamed Single (2018)** : Released in 2018, this single is highlighted as a technical marvel in sound processing. It pushes the boundaries of pitch shifting and playback speed variation to unprecedented extremes, continuously manipulating dozens of audio tracks. Instruments and choirs constantly change playback speeds, resulting in a terrifyingly complex yet fascinating auditory experience. "Esta canción yo creo que nadie jamás nunca había llevado la variación de las velocidades de reproducción del pitch sweet thing a unos extremos cangrantes."

## curiosities
**Early Classical Training** : From a young age, James Blake received a very classical musical education, studying solfège and piano and mastering various instruments. This strong classical background later influenced his unique electronic interpretations. "Desde joven tiene una formación muy clásica de música, la estudia sorfeo, estudia piano, toca muchos instrumentos."
**Pioneer of a New Electronic Style (2010)** : In the early 2010s, James Blake began developing a highly personal interpretation of electronic music that captured significant critical attention. His work was seen as the discovery of a new musical style and a novel approach to music creation, blending intimate singer-songwriter traditions with electronic sounds and very slow tempos. "En los primeros de esta década en el 2010 empieza a hacer una interpretación muy personal de la música electrónica que llama muchísimo la atención a la crítica."
**Influence on Contemporary Music** : James Blake is regarded as one of the most influential figures in contemporary music, including mainstream popular music. His slow, intimate electronic style has particularly impacted artists like Bon Iver and Nicolas Jaar, who belong to the same generation. "Yo creo que son uno de los tíos que más se ha influenciado la música actual hoy en día incluso la música de gran consumo."
**Kanye West's Endorsement (2012)** : In 2012, James Blake's popularity surged significantly after Kanye West publicly declared him his favorite artist. This endorsement instantly turned many into James Blake fans, greatly amplifying his public profile. "Otro punto que desató su popularidad fue en el 2012 cuando Cani West dijo que era su artista preferido y ahí todo el mundo se puso loco."
**Extensive Collaborations** : Following his rise to prominence, James Blake has collaborated with numerous renowned artists due to his unique sound processing abilities. His collaborators include Kendrick Lamar, Beyoncé, Chance the Rapper, Brian Eno, Nicolas Jaar, Bon Iver, and Jack White. "Él ha colaborado con todo el mundo porque todo el mundo está procesado yo creo que así con colaborar con él."
**Masterful Sound Processing** : James Blake is hailed as a "sound wizard" and a "culinary master in musical form" for his unparalleled ability to process sound. He applies intricate, subtle, and elegant ultra-processing techniques to create sounds that are meticulously perfect, almost impossible, yet appear natural and organic. "Él es capaz de ultraprocesar el sonido de una manera muy elegante."
**Voice as an Instrument** : James Blake employs highly complex methods to process his voice, often layering his vocals five or six times with different treatments. These processed vocal tracks can sound like instruments, female choirs, or variations in playback speed, achieving a unique texture that sometimes masks the origin of the sound. "A veces parece que son instrumentos cuando es su voz ultra procesada, a veces parece que son coros de mujeres y es su voz procesada."
**Reimagining UK Electronic Rhythms** : A characteristic element of James Blake's style is his use of rhythmic bases heavily influenced by early 2000s UK electronic movements such as Dubstep, Trance, and Drum and Bass. He takes these often aggressive bases and reinterprets them in a completely new way, creating extremely slow, distinct rhythmic foundations that revitalize a genre previously thought to be exhausted. "Casi toda la base arrímica que usa el sonido elitónico. La toma de los movimientos británicos en primeros el 2000 del Duffstep, del Trustet, del Dragon Base."
**King of Pitch Shifting** : James Blake has become known as "the king of pitch shifting" for his sophisticated manipulation of audio speeds. He masterfully twists playback velocities, mixes different speeds, and creates wonderful effects across all his audio tracks. "Él es capaz de coger su música, coger todas sus pistas de audio, retorcer las velocidades de reproducción de manera complejísima, mezclar distintas velocidades de reproducción y generar efectos maravillosos."
**Alchemy of Natural Sound** : Unlike a trend towards increasingly artificial sounds in electronic music, James Blake pursues the opposite extreme: processing sound to make it appear more natural and organic. He is seen as an "alchemist of sound" who brings audio processing to new levels of perfection, creating a perspective that is increasingly influencing others and may become a more common trend, as listeners seek organic sounds amidst artificiality. "James Blake ha conseguido ir al extremo completamente puesto, procesar cada vez más el sonido para que cada vez parezca más natural y más orgánico."

# genre - Electrónica

## curiosities
**Intimate and Slow Style** : A new style of electronic music emerged in the early 2010s, characterized by its slow tempos and intimate qualities, often combining elements of singer-songwriter traditions. James Blake is credited with defining this specific subgenre within electronic music. "Un nuevo estilo de electrónica tan lenta y tan intimista, es casi un cantato de electrónico."
**Sound Processing Evolution** : While much of electronic music increasingly leans towards artificial and synthetic sounds, there is a developing trend, notably championed by James Blake, that seeks to process sound to make it appear more natural and organic. This perspective might become a more common direction in the future as a reaction against overly artificial sounds. "Cada vez procesamos más el sonido y estamos yendo a sonidos que son cada vez más artificiales. James Blake ha conseguido ir al extremo completamente puesto, procesar cada vez más el sonido para que cada vez parezca más natural y más orgánico."

# genre - Dubstep

## curiosities
**Reinterpretation by James Blake** : James Blake has uniquely reinterpreted Dubstep, taking its rhythmic bases from the early 2000s UK scene and slowing them down significantly to create entirely new and distinct sounds. This approach is seen as a novel interpretation of a genre that was once considered exhausted. "Cuando uno vise el Duffstep, piensan esto. Pero sin embargo James Blake consigue hacer una reinterpretación totalmente nueva y hacer las basas rímpicas como estas."

# instrument - Voice

## curiosities
**Complex Processing as an Instrument** : The human voice, particularly in James Blake's music, is subjected to complex processing, often layered multiple times with varying treatments. This technique transforms the voice into something resembling other instruments, female choirs, or sounds manipulated by different playback speeds, effectively making the voice a highly versatile and processed instrument. "James Blake procesa su voz de una manera complejísima siempre."

# curiosity

**The Shift Towards Organic Sound Processing** : A growing perspective in music production, exemplified by James Blake, involves intensely processing sounds not to make them artificial, but to achieve a heightened sense of naturalness and organic quality. This approach suggests a future trend where increasingly artificial and synthetic sounds become less appealing, driving a search for more natural sounds even within highly processed audio. "Ha conseguido ir al extremo completamente puesto, procesar cada vez más el sonido para que cada vez parezca más natural y más orgánico."
