# artist - Staff Sergeant Barry Sadler

## genres
Counter-Cultural March

## songs
**Ballad of the Green Berets (1966)** : This song was the Billboard year-end number one for 1966 and was recorded by Staff Sergeant Barry Sadler, an army special forces member moonlighting as a singer. It resonated with Americans who were wary of the direction of 1966 popular culture and still supported the Vietnam War. Stereo Gum's Tom Bryan called it the "worst number one he's ever written about," and its success felt counter-cultural given other popular songs of the year like "96 Tears" and "When a Man Loves a Woman." "Recorded by an army special forces staff sergeant moon lighting as a singer, Ballad of the Green Berets was a hit with Americans who didn't like the trajectory of 1966 popular culture and were still in favor of the Vietnam War."
