# curiosity
**Billboard Hot 100 Launch (1958)** : The Billboard Hot 100 chart was launched in 1958, leading Billboard to retroactively declare official Songs of the Summer for every year since its inception. "Now that the chart Bible has gone back in time to declare an official song of summer for every year since the hot 100 was founded."
**Hot 100 First Two Songs of Summer (1950s)** : Due to the Hot 100's launch in 1958, there were only two Billboard Songs of the Summer for the 1950s: Domenico Modugno's "Volare" in 1958 and Paul Anka's "Lonely Boy" in 1959. "because the hot 100 launched in 1958, there are only two Billboard songs of the summer for the 50s."
**Moon Landing and Woodstock Summer (1969)** : The summer of 1969 was a historically significant period in America, notably marked by both the moon landing and the Woodstock music festival. "America, in the summer of moon landing and woodstock, we could have done better."
**Summer of Love (1967)** : The year 1967 is historically referred to as the "Summer of Love," a period during which The Doors' signature hit "Light My Fire" reached number one. "Because their signature hit reached number one in the summer of love."
**Pop's Greatest Years (1984)** : The year 1984 is highlighted as one of "Pop's greatest years," marked by Prince's "When Doves Cry" becoming a top hit and the Song of the Summer. "and the top hit of one of Pop's greatest years."
**Music Business Strategy for Song of the Summer (2010s)** : In the 2010s, with Billboard officially tracking the "Song of the Summer" chart, many contenders for the title seemed "premeditated" to peak during the hot months. The advent of digital downloads and streaming further facilitated fans' ability to "cast their votes" and influence the outcome. "Now that the music business knew there was a chart to top and a contest to be won, many of these contenders seemed premeditated to win the title, especially now that digital downloads and streaming made it easier than ever for fans to cast their votes."
**Pandemic Impact on Songs of Summer (2020-2021)** : The pandemic in 2020 and 2021 led to the Billboard Songs of the Summer for those years—Dababy's "Rockstar" and BTS's "Butter"—being "mostly consumed indoors" rather than in traditional outdoor summer settings. "We were plagued by a pandemic in 2020. And 2021. Which meant that Debayby's rock star featuring Roddy Rich and BTS's Butter were mostly consumed indoors."
**Song of the Summer Meme Comeback (2024)** : The "song of the summer meme" experienced a notable comeback in 2024, driven by the latest hits from artists such as Post Malone, Morgan Wallen, Kendrick Lamar, Shaboozey, and Sabrina Carpenter. "Nonetheless, I am pleased that the song of the summer meme has made a comeback in 2024."
**Competition in Pop Music** : The vigorous competition among artists for Song of the Summer contenders is seen as a positive force, being "good for the business of pop." "The competition is good for the business of pop, my favorite sport."
**Enduring Songs of Summer** : Several past Songs of the Summer winners, including hits by The Stones, The Doors, Donna Summer, Prince, The Police, Brandy and Monica, Rihanna, and Beyoncé, have demonstrated significant endurance, lasting well beyond their initial fleeting summer moment.
