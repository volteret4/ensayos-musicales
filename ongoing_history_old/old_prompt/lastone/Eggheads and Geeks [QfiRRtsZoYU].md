## albums

## curiosities
**Multi-Disciplinary Career** : Eno has established himself as a musician, composer, record producer, music theorist, philosopher, filmmaker, writer, lecturer, and conceiver/constructor of art installations displayed globally.
**Invention of Ambient Music Genre** : Credited with inventing the entire concept and genre of ambient music, which formed the foundations for numerous electronic music genres.
**Extensive Producer Credits** : Has produced records for notable artists including David Bowie, U2, Talking Heads, and Coldplay.
**Mentorship of Daniel Lanois** : Discovered and mentored the Canadian musician and producer Daniel Lanois.
**Development of Oblique Strategies** : Developed "Oblique Strategies," a method designed to encourage inspiration and out-of-the-box thinking in creative projects.

**Creator of the Bloom iPhone Application** : Developed an application called Bloom for the iPhone.
**Advocacy for BBC Funding** : Has petitioned on behalf of the BBC for increased funding.
**Acting Role in British Sitcom No Lest** : Appeared in an acting role in the British sitcom "No Lest."
**Composer of Nokia Ringtones** : Wrote ringtones for Nokia phones.
**Composer of Windows 95 Startup Sound** : Composed the iconic startup flourish for Windows 95, reportedly created on a Mac computer.
**Origin of Ambient Music from Car Accident** : In 1975, a car accident left him bedridden for months; unable to adjust the volume of quiet music, he conceptualized music having properties like color or light, leading him to experiment with synthesizers to create "sound scapes" he termed ambient music.
