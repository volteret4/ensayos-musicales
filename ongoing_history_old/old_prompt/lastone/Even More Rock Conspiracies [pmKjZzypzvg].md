# artist - Ohio Players

## members
Billy Beck

## songs
**Love Rollercoaster (1975)** : This 1975 hit became the subject of an urban myth due to a "weird sound" heard between 2 minutes 32 seconds and 2 minutes 36 seconds. In the pre-internet era, rumors spread that the sound was a woman being murdered live in the studio and then looped into the mix, or that she was killed specifically for the sessions. Other versions claimed it was a girl dying from a rollercoaster fall, or a woman named Esther Cordet being scalded by heated honey she posed with for album artwork, or even a rabbit killed outside the studio. The truth is that the sound was singer Billy Beck's "weird, inhaling scream," which a radio DJ joked sounded like someone dying, causing the rumor to spread. The Ohio Players never denied the story, as the rumor reportedly helped sell more records.

Even the bed I slept in in Los Angeles was in the shape of a coffin, a huge double bed shaped like a coffin. My whole life was spinal tap at that time."
