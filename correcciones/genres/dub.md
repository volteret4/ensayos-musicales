# genre - Dub

## curiosities
**Gorillaz Debut Album Component** : Dub was included as one of the diverse musical influences that shaped the sound of the Gorillaz's debut album. "The music was rather experimental, more trip-hop, some alt-rock, bits of Brit-pop, little dub, little reggae, and a bunch of hip-hop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Influence on Bass Playing Style** : Dub, a Jamaican style of music, heavily influenced bass players such as Jah Wobble, who adopted its slow, low characteristics and deep, deep grooves into his playing. "Jaw's playing was heavily influenced by dub that Jamaican style of playing that could be slow and low with a deep, deep groove." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kbFfT0pKchI
**Influence on Public Image Limited** : Dub music was a significant influence on Johnny Lydon's creative direction for Public Image Limited, contributing to their experimental sound. "He got deep into reggae and dub." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
