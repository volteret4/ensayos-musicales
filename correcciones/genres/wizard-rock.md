# genre - Wizard Rock

## curiosities
**Harry Potter Inspiration** : Wizard rock is a subgenre composed of bands entirely inspired by the Harry Potter series of books and films. "A whole bunch of bands inspired by Harry Potter." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6snk0m4200
**Rockstock Festival** : The wizard rock scene is substantial enough to host its own dedicated festival, known as "Rockstock," which famously includes a silent 'W' at the beginning of its spelling. "That scene is big enough to have its own festival, which they call Rockstock, which is spelled with a silent W at the beginning." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6snk0m4200
