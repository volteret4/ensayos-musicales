# genre - Techno punk

## curiosities
**Hybrid Post-Punk Genre** : Techno punk was an interchangeable label for a danceable post-punk sound that incorporated diverse influences like New Wave, Kraut Rock, CBGB punk, funk, rap, Jamaican dub, and disco, characterized by its rhythmic grooves.
