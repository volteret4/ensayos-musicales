**The Tragically Hip's Original Sound** : The Tragically Hip developed a distinctive sound described as "Blue Z, blue color, guitar-driven rock," which set them apart from the prevalent hair metal or synth-heavy music of their time. This genre emerged from their shift from covering songs to writing their own material, notably influenced by artists like Van Morrison. "Blue Z, blue color, guitar-driven rock.
