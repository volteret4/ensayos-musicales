# genre - Psychoacoustics

## curiosities
**Application to Digital Audio** : Professor Dieter Selzer, an expert in psychoacoustics, influenced Carlheinz Brandenburg's work on digital audio compression. Selzer's dream of a "digital jukebox" likely spurred the initial research into how music could be transmitted efficiently, considering the human perception of sound.
