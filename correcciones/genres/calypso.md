# genre - Calypso

## curiosities
**Ancient Roots** : Calypso is noted to have roots dating back "hundreds of years." This emphasizes its long-standing presence and evolution as a traditional musical form. "Both sounds date back hundreds of years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
**Descent from French Music and African Slave Culture** : Calypso music descended from a form of French music that was "co-opted by African slaves working on the plantations." This historical lineage highlights its complex cultural synthesis rooted in colonial history and slave experiences. "descended from, believe it or not, a form of French music that was co-opted by African slaves working on the plantations." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
**Origin in Trinidad and Tobago** : Calypso is a Caribbean rhythm that originated in Trinidad and Tobago. It serves as one of the key Caribbean influences in the hybrid sound of ska. "Calypso, which came out of Trinidad and Tobago, and descended from, believe it or not, a form of French music that was co-opted by African slaves working on the plantations." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
