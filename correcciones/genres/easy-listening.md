# genre - Easy Listening

## curiosities
**Definition and Purpose** : Easy listening music, as exemplified by the Andrew Loog Oldham Orchestra's arrangements of rock songs in the 1960s, involved "watering down" original tracks. This genre was specifically created to appeal to audiences who might be offended by rock and roll, making the music more palatable.
