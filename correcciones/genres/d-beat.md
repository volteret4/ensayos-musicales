# genre - D-Beat

## curiosities
**Definition and Origins** : D-Beat is a derivative of UK Hardcore, named after a distinctive drum pattern central to many of its songs. It emerged from an amalgamation of British punk (like The Sex Pistols) and the New Wave of British Heavy Metal bands from the early 1980s, with Motorhead being a significant influence.
