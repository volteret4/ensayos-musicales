## curiosities
**David Bowie's Incorporation (1997)** : Jungle music was one of the contemporary dance genres that captured David Bowie's interest and was subsequently featured on his 1997 album, *Earthling*. This artistic choice was viewed by some as an important recognition of the genre by a mainstream artist.
