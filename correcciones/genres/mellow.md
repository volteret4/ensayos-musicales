# genre - Mellow

## curiosities
**Musical Trend (Early 1970s)** : "Mellow" was the prevailing term used in the early 1970s to describe a soft music trend. This trend saw many fans abandoning the acid rock, funk, and R&B of the 1960s in favor of artists like James Taylor, Bread, Olivia Newton-John, Paul Simon, The Eagles, and Fleetwood Mac, contributing to a "cancer of Mellow" that irritated certain groups.
