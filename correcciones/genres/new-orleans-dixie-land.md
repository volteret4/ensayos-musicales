# genre - New Orleans Dixie Land

## curiosities
**1960s Revival** : New Orleans Dixie Land jazz experienced a revival in the UK around the same time folk music was booming in the US. English hipsters, dissatisfied with the new rock and roll, embraced traditional jazz that had been popular 60 years prior. "And suddenly New Orleans Dixie Land was in vogue. Things lasted until about 1965 before it all died away." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gkLMwrthZ9s
