# genre - Acid house raves

## curiosities
**Influence on Madchester Attitude** : The attitude of the acid house raves from the mid-1980s, characterized by huge parties where people consumed ecstasy and danced for extended periods, heavily influenced young Manchester musicians. They sought to combine this attitude with "proper guitars." "Many young musicians had co-opted an attitude based on the acid house raves of the middle ladies, these huge parties where people dropped a tab of E and danced forever." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSvoppf1dGY
