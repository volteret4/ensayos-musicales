# genre - Gothic Fiction

## curiosities
**Foundation of Gothic Horror** : Established by stories like Polidori's "The Vampire" and Shelley's "Frankenstein," conceived during the "year without a summer" in 1816, a period of global cooling caused by the Mount Tambora eruption. This type of horror has influenced novels, movies, comic books, fashion, and music. "These two stories imagined during the year without a summer caused by the biggest volcanic eruption in 1300 years, created the foundation of gothic fiction, a type of horror that endures today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
