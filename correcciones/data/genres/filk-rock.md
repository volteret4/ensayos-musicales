"FILK music is so enduring that it has its own awards program called the Pegasus Awards. And there's a FILK Hall of Fame." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6snk0m4200
**Name Origin and Definition** : FILK (F-I-L-K) rock is a genre whose name originated from a typo (I being next to O on the keyboard). It consists of folk songs specifically favored by fans of science fiction, horror movies, and fantasy. "The name itself is a typo, I is next to O on the keyboard. These were folk songs, favored by certain fans of science fiction and horror movies and fantasy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6snk0m4200
