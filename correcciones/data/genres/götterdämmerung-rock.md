# genre - Götterdämmerung Rock

## curiosities
**Alternative Name for Krautrock** : "Götterdämmerung Rock" was one of the terms, alongside "Deutsch German Rock," "Über Rock," and "Teutonic Rock," used to describe the distinct German music before "Krautrock" was coined by the British and widely adopted. "While most music people initially refer to this music as Doitrach German Rock, it was also called Uber Rock, Tutonic Rock, and Gaudedemmer Rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
