# genre - Movie Soundtracks

## curiosities
**Early LP Content (Late 1940s)** : When Columbia Records introduced the 12-inch LP in June 1948, the new format was initially reserved for "serious high-brow music," which included movie soundtracks, along with classical music, Broadway show tunes, and jazz. This focus required comprehensive liner notes to educate and inform listeners about the music.
