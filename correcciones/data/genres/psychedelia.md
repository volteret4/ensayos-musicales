"They had an indie pop sound sprinkled with various bits of psychedelia." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo
**Modern Psychedelic Material** : Dreampop is described as overlapping with anything resembling modern psychedelic material, showcasing a connection between the genres. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
