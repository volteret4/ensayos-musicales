# genre - Antifolk

## curiosities
**Early Beck's Sound** : Before his mainstream breakthrough with "Loser" in 1994, Beck's early music was frequently categorized as Antifolk. These indie records were often described as confounding and unusual, sharing a close relationship with Freak Folk, and incorporating elements of the Tweetpop aesthetic in their sound and approach.
