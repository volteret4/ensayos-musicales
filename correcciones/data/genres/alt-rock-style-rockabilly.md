# genre - Alt-Rock Style Rockabilly

## curiosities
**Brief Early 80s Popularity and Lack of Revival** : Alt-rock style rockabilly was a significant genre in the very early 1980s, popular for about 18 months with bands like the Stray Cats. However, it subsequently faded out and, unlike many other genres, has never experienced a full-blown revival in the alt-rock sense or with the same style and scope. "The only genre I can really think of at this point is maybe alt-rock style rockabilly." "It was really big in the very early 80s with bands like the Stray Cats, but then it just kind of went away." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HSvgRyGiOpE
