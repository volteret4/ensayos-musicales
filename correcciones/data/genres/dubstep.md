# genre - Dubstep

## curiosities
**Genre Transformation Through Remixes** : Remixes can transform a song originally written for one genre into another, with dubstep serving as an example of a genre into which many familiar songs have been remixed, turning them into "bangers." "And for example, of any familiar songs you've heard that have been remixed into dubstep bangers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
**Integration into Rock Music** : Dubstep influences were purposefully integrated into USS's production, particularly evident in the song "This Is The Best." This coincided with a period when the success of bands like Imagine Dragons was opening a "lane" for electronic-infused rock. "Ash night or both like huge dubstep fans and so that made it into the production of it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N-3eddy4J7g
