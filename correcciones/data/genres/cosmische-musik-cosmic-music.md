# genre - Cosmische Musik (Cosmic Music)

## curiosities
**Emergence of Space Rock** : The name "Cosmische Musik" or "Cosmic Music" came into use around 1971 to describe new sounds emerging from German musicians, characterized as a form of psychic space rock. This development was influenced by the pervasive idea of space in late 1960s culture, including the space race and moon landings. "It was called Cosmish Music, or Cosmic Music. That name came into use sometime in about 1971, and was kind of a psychic space rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
