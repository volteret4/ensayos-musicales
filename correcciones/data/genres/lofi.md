# genre - Lofi

## curiosities
**Authenticity and Realness** : To many, the sonic opposite of polished or slick, which Lofi embodies, "equates to authentic and real," appealing to listeners seeking a raw, unadulterated musical experience. "It can be the sonic opposite of polished or slick, and to many people that equates to authentic and real." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
**Late 80s Indie Rock Affiliation** : By the late 1980s, the Lofi label was being applied to many indie rock bands who occasionally ventured into what was termed "Ram Shackle Territory," even if their recordings were not strictly "home-made." "By the late 80s, the Lofi label was being affixed to many indie rock bands who occasionally veered into, let's call it, Ram Shackle Territory." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
**Modern Popularity** : The Lofi approach is "more popular than ever" today, with artists recording albums wherever they choose, often using laptops and software like GarageBand, demonstrating its lasting influence and accessibility. "These days, the Lofi approach is more popular than ever with artists recording albums wherever they want, using a laptop and garage band or something similar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
**Opposite of High-Fi** : Lofi is defined as the "opposite of high-fi," representing a recording style that intentionally embraces lower fidelity, which gained significant prominence in the 1990s. "Lofi is obviously the opposite of high-fi." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
**Recording Environment and Sound** : Practitioners of Lofi prefer making recordings in "grittier, less high-tech surroundings," such as a studio with less than state-of-the-art gear or facilities, a garage, or a bedroom, resulting in a "grittier low-tech sound." "Lofi practitioners prefer to make recordings in grittier, less high-tech surroundings, producing a, well, a grittier low-tech sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
**Term Coined in 1986** : Although Lofi-style recordings existed for decades, the term itself was coined in 1986 by a DJ from a community FM station in New Jersey, who hosted a show dedicated to home recordings. "but it wasn't until 1986 when a DJ from a community FM station in New Jersey coined the term when he hosted a show dedicated to home recordings." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
