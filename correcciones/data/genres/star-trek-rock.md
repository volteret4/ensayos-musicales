# genre - Star Trek Rock

## curiosities
**Inspiration and Language** : Star Trek Rock refers to a niche area of music dedicated to bands whose entire inspiration comes from the Star Trek franchise. Some bands within this subgenre even perform their songs in Klingon, the fictional language from Star Trek.
