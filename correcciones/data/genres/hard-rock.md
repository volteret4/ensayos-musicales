# genre - Hard Rock

## curiosities
**"Satanist" Branding due to Association with Crime** : Hard rock, along with metal, was a genre often viewed with suspicion and branded as "Satanist" by those who distrusted it, particularly after its association with serial killers like Richard Ramirez. The public reaction was fueled by the perception that the music could inspire evil. "for a time was branded as Satanists by people who distrusted hard rock and metal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FtGJHhqXcDI
**Alice in Chains Vocal Harmonies** : Alice in Chains was noted for having the best hard rock harmonies since the first version of Van Halen, specifically those between David Lee Roth and Michael Anthony. "Best hard rock harmony since, I don't know, David Lee Roth and Michael Anthony in the first version of Van Halen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vLZla5h4xKQ
**Grammy Award for Linkin Park** : Linkin Park's debut album, Hybrid Theory, was nominated for three Grammys, winning one in the category of Best Hard Rock Performance for their song "Crawling." "winning one for Crawling in the category of Best Hard Rock Performance." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM
**Increased UK Chart Presence (2010s)** : Hard rock, particularly groups from indie labels, saw an increased presence on the UK charts during the 2010s. "Heavy metal and hard rock, again, groups drawn largely from indie labels, saw an increased presence on the charts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Influence on Alt Hip Hop** : The band Oxymorrons from Queens, New York City, are noted for their sound which incorporates a "Hard Rock Twist" into Alternative Hip Hop. "and you can call them Alternative Hip Hop with a Hard Rock Twist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1NNvxgx8uA
**Long Hair as a Standard** : By the mid-1970s, long hair became a standard requirement in some quarters of music, particularly within hard rock and metal. This tradition continues today, cementing long hair as an expected aesthetic for these genres. "Long hair became standard in some quarters. Hard rock and metal often required it. That's a tradition that continues today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bferkEWvJg0
**Shift in UK Market Dominance** : This increased chart presence marked a shift from a long period where American metal and hard rock bands, with few exceptions like Accept and Scorpions, largely dominated the British market. "And this was a nice change from a long stretch where American metal and hard rock bands pretty much had the British market to themselves, with a couple of bands like Accept and Scorpions thrown in." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**The Runaways as Pioneers** : The Runaways are considered the first all-female hard rock band in history to gain international attention. Their presence, led by Sandy West on drums, challenged gender norms in the male-dominated hard rock genre. "We can safely call them the first all-female hard rock band in history to receive international attention." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY
**Velvet Revolver Grammy Award (2004)** : Velvet Revolver won a Grammy Award for Best Hard Rock Performance for their song "Fall To Pieces" from the album "Contraband." "I hit the contributor to VR winning a Grammy Award for Best Hard Rock Performance." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
