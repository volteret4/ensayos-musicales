# genre - Foxtrot

## curiosities
**Criticism in 1920s** : In 1920, an editorial in the *Daily Mail* condemned the Foxtrot, stating that it, along with the One-step, "Expressor Audit Ugly Manners" and represented a "modern hiltor skelter." The article expressed a desire for "civilized dancing" like waltzing to return and linked the Foxtrot to "uncontrollable manners" and even divorce cases. "Graceful dancing is dead in ballrooms. We conclude that Foxtrot and one step, Expressor Audit Ugly Manners, our modern hiltor skelter, they show the wheel coming again full circle, back to the Polynesian round the campfire with the Tom Tom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L0j9iDuHQVA
