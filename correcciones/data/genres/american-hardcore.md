# genre - American Hardcore

## curiosities
**Intensification of Moshpits** : As American hardcore gained prominence, the intensity of slam dancing evolved into moshing. Participants, predominantly men, would gather near the stage and engage in frenzied, often violent, movements. This phenomenon was potentially first observed among Orange County punks in the late 1970s at clubs like The Cuckoo's Nest in Costa Mesa. "As American hardcore took off, things got even more intense. People, and almost always guys, would get close to the stage and basically lose their minds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bferkEWvJg0
