# genre - Lost Wave

## curiosities
**Canadian Roots** : A notable curiosity of Lost Wave songs is that a significant number of them appear to have roots in Canada, a phenomenon that has yet to be explained. "Now, you've probably noticed by now that for some reason a lot of Lost Wave songs have roots in Canada." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw
**Defining Characteristics** : Lost Wave refers to a category of songs that exist but are nameless, unclaimed, and missing from the musical record, often dubbed the "Jane and John Doe's of the music world." It is not considered a true genre because the songs don't need to share any sonic commonalities; their defining trait is simply being lost. "It's not really a genre because all these songs don't need to have anything sonically in common." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw
**The Challenge of Identification** : Identifying Lost Wave songs has become a serious game for music fans, with significant prestige awarded to anyone who successfully cracks these "cold cases." This challenge is often referred to as "Challenge accepted." "However, much glory in a claim will go to anyone who correctly identifies any of them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw
