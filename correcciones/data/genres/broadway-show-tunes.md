# genre - Broadway Show Tunes

## curiosities
**Early LP Content (Late 1940s)** : When Columbia Records introduced the 12-inch LP in June 1948, the format was initially reserved for "serious high-brow music," including Broadway show tunes, alongside classical music, movie soundtracks, and jazz, requiring extensive liner notes to inform the audience.
