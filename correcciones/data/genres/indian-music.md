# genre - Indian Music

## curiosities
**32-Note Scale System** : Indian music employs a 32-note scale system, which contrasts significantly with the 12-note scale used in Western music. While this unique tuning may sound "out of tune" to unaccustomed Western ears, it is considered exciting and deeply resonant within its cultural context. "Indian music, they use 32 notes. And to us, it sounds out of tune, but to them it feeds them. It gives them excitement." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mjQBaX_3oL4
