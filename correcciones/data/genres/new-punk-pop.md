# genre - New Punk Pop

## curiosities
**Emergence After 2010 with Darker Tone** : Starting sometime after 2010, a "new generation of pop punk bands" emerged, referred to as "new punk pop" (spelled "new" with a "U" and "U-Mot" or "nu-"). This contemporary iteration of the genre was noted for its slightly "darker" sound compared to its 1990s predecessors. "Starting sometime after 2010, we began to see a new generation of pop punk bands.
