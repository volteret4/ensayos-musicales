# genre - Psychedelic

## curiosities
**Hapshash and the Colored Coat's "Heavy Metal" Album** : The 1967 album "The Human Host and the Heavy Metal Kids" by Hapshash and the Colored Coat, despite its title, is described as having a psychedelic musical style rather than resembling what would later be known as heavy metal. "But if we listen to the record, it's more psychedelic than aliens." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8GU8ZpSgY8
**Rickenbacker Sound Preference (1960s)** : The characteristic "jangle" or "chime" sound of Rickenbacker guitars appealed to artists experimenting with the new psychedelic sounds of the 1960s. "the unique sound of the Rickenbacher made it a favorite among... those into the new psychedelic sounds of the 1960s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d9HNqb9gJSU
