# genre - Country-swing

## curiosities
**Early Distortion Use** : Electric bluesmen and country-swing guitarists in the 1940s were among the first to experiment with distorted guitar sounds. Junior Barnard, a country-swing guitarist with Bob Wills and the Texas Playboys, deliberately pushed his Epiphone Emperor archtop and a vacuum-tube amp beyond their limits in 1945 to achieve a gritty, growly sound on his 1946 recording "Rolly Polly." "Electric bluesmen, as far back as the 1940s, began to use distorted guitar sounds to add some extra rawness to their playing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iGav9uJwA_w
