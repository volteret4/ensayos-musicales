# genre - Black metal

## curiosities
**Violence and Criminal Activity** : The black metal genre has been associated with various violent and criminal acts perpetrated by its members. Examples include Gawl of Gorgoroth being jailed for torture, Faust of Emperor serving 14 years for murder, and Vargh Vikernes of Burzum receiving 21 years for stabbing a rival band member 23 times.
