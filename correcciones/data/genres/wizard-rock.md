**Inspiration and Festival** : Wizard Rock is a subgenre of nerd rock entirely inspired by the Harry Potter book series. The scene is sufficiently large and dedicated to have its own festival, which is known as "Wrockstock." "There was Wizard Rock, a whole bunch of bands inspired by Harry Potter. That scene is big enough to have its own festival, which they call Rockstock, which is spelled with a silent W at the beginning." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8lQv8eMJ868
