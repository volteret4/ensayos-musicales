# genre - Schlager music

## curiosities
**Rejected by German Youth** : Schlager music was the dominant form of contemporary German music that emerged from the 1920s, 30s, and 40s. Characterized as cheesy, inoffensive easy listening, sweet, sentimental, and boring, it was vehemently rejected by young German protesters in the late 1960s who saw its tradition as connected to a past they sought to transcend.
