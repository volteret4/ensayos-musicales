# genre - International Songs

## curiosities
**RCA's Sky Blue Vinyl Coding** : RCA used sky blue vinyl to denote international songs as part of its experimental genre-based color-coding system for 7-inch singles. The system was introduced with the debut of the 45 RPM format but did not persist. "International songs were sky blue." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VlIErdSSVOM
