## curiosities
**Christian Metalcore Identity** : Christian metalcore is a genre that As I Lay Dying from San Diego fits into, characterized by pro-Christian themes in their music. Members of such bands, like Tim Lambesis, often profess strong Christian beliefs, though lyrics might not always be explicit about God or Jesus. "For years, they've made music with pro-Christian themes and with all the members professing to be very good Christians themselves time and time again." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6xygY-npCr4
**Crossover with Third Wave Emo** : During the third wave of emo, there was a significant crossover and fragmentation, with many bands being classified as metalcore alongside emo, punk, pop punk, and post-hardcore. "There was, in short, a crossover between proper emo, if you'll allow me to use that term, an emo-like bands, who were probably best classified as punk, pop punk, post-hardcore, and metalcore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
