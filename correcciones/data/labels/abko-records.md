# label - Abko Records

## curiosities
**Allan Klein's Ownership of Early Rolling Stones Catalog** : Abko Records, run by Allan Klein (who succeeded Andrew Loog Oldham as The Rolling Stones' manager), owned the rights to all Rolling Stones material released before 1970. This ownership became a critical factor in the "Bittersweet Symphony" lawsuit, as The Verve had not secured sampling permission from Abko, despite their initial agreement with Decca Records.
