# label - JBL

## curiosities
**Speakers in the Wall of Sound (1973)** : JBL speakers were a fundamental component of The Grateful Dead's revolutionary "Wall of Sound," unveiled in 1973. The massive system incorporated 568 JBL speakers, which were essential to achieving its unprecedented volume and clarity across vast distances. "The wall contained hundreds of speakers, 568 JBLs, and about 50 electro voice tweeters, to be specific." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
