# label - Corwood Industries

## curiosities
**Corwood Industries – Over 50 Albums, Anonymous P.O. Box, One of Rock's Great Mysteries** : Founded by Jandek (believed to be Sterling Richard Smith) in Houston, Texas. The label has released more than 51 albums since 1978 with no conventional promotion or distribution, communicating only through an anonymous P.O. box.
