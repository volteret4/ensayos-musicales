# label - Swan Song

## curiosities
**Led Zeppelin's Vanity Label** : Swan Song was a special subsidiary label created by Atlantic Records in the 1970s for Led Zeppelin. It served as a "vanity label," allowing the major label to cater to a prominent artist while offering a semblance of independent operation or increased creative control. "When Led Zeppelin ruled the world in the 1970s, their label Atlantic created a special subsidiary for them called Swan Song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
