## curiosities
**Attempt at Ticket Sales (Late 1970s)** : In the late 1970s, PhotoMat, known for its film development kiosks, attempted to leverage its network of 4,000 kiosks to sell concert tickets. The company planned a $1 service charge and insisted on having access to all inventory for any given show.
