# label - Compute Ticket

## curiosities
**Demise (1970)** : Despite its rivalry with TRS, Compute Ticket ultimately failed and ceased operations by the spring of 1970. "But in the end, Compute Ticket didn't last, and by the spring of 1970, it was dead." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
**Early Computerized Ticketing Rival** : Compute Ticket, originally Tele-Ticket, was a rival to Ticket Reservation Systems Inc. (TRS) in the early computerized ticket-selling market. It was powered by "giant IBM 360-50 mainframes" and also levied service charges of 35 cents in Los Angeles and 50 cents in New York. "It was powered by a couple of giant IBM 360-50 mainframes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
