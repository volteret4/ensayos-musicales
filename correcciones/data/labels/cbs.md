## curiosities
**The Clash's Controversial Signing (1977)** : On January 26, 1977, CBS Records signed The Clash to a major label deal valued at 100,000 pounds, a figure significantly higher than other competing offers. This decision sparked widespread condemnation within the British punk community, who viewed it as a "sell out" for a band previously seen as ideologically pure and anti-establishment, leading to protests throughout the UK.
