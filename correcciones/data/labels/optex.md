# label - Optex

## curiosities
**Stage Construction for Downsview Park Concert** : Optex was the company contracted to build the stage at Downsview Park for the Radiohead concert on June 16, 2012. The stage's collapse resulted in the death of a drum tech, and Optex was a central figure in the subsequent inquest, which found the roof design to be outdated.
