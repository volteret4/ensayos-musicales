# label - BC Rich

## curiosities
**Acrylic Construction** : The distinctive, wild shapes of BC Rich guitars, including models like the Warlock, Seagull, Stealth, Eagle, Mockingbird, and Bitch, were made possible by their construction entirely from acrylic. This material allowed for unconventional and visually striking body designs. "All of them were made completely of acrylic, which is why they can assume such wild shapes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**Famous Users** : BC Rich guitars have been favored by a diverse range of prominent musicians, including Prince, Eric Clapton, Slash, Geezer and Tony from Black Sabbath, various Kiss guitarists (including Paul Stanley), and James Hetfield of Metallica. The brand also made an appearance in the film *Spinal Tap*, with characters Derek Smalls and David St. Hubbins playing them. "Other BC Rich players include Prince, Eric Clapton, Slash, Geezer and Tony from Black Sabbath, any number of kiss guitars, including Paul Stanley. James Headfield of Metallica is used one." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**Hair Metal Popularity** : BC Rich guitars became highly sought-after instruments for hair metal bands, perfectly suiting the visually extravagant demands of 1980s MTV music videos. Their aggressive, pointy designs were ideal for making a bold visual statement. "If you were in a hair metal band, this is exactly the kind of outrageous guitar you wanted for your MTV video." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**Wild and Crazy Designs** : BC Rich guitars are renowned for their wild and outrageous designs, often resembling medieval weapons rather than traditional instruments, particularly their "warlock line." Their aesthetics pushed boundaries far beyond designs like Fender's Flying V from 1958. "Wow, these things look more like medieval weapons than guitars, especially their warlock line of instruments. If Fender's Flying V from 1958 looked strange, BC Rich took things about 20 steps further." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
