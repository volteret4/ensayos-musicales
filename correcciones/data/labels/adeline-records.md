# label - Adeline Records

## curiosities
**Billie Joe Armstrong's Ownership** : Adeline Records is an indie label based out of the Bay Area of California that issued the album Money Money 2020 by the group The Network in 2003. Significantly, the label is owned by Green Day's frontman, Billie Joe Armstrong, a key piece of evidence linking The Network to Green Day despite Armstrong's public denials.
