# label - Dali

## curiosities
**Kyuss Signing and Name Change** : Dali, an indie label, signed Kyuss and re-released their 1987 demo in 1990. The label insisted that the band shorten their name from "Sons of Kyuss" to simply "Kyuss." "That dates back to 1987 and was re-released in 1990 after they got a deal with an indie label called Dali. That's when they also shortened their name to just caes, because the record label insisted." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG1TQuhcf1o
