# label - Roadrunner Records

## curiosities
**Financial Role of Hit Bands** : Roadrunner Records benefited significantly from Nickelback's success, with millions in album sales for the label directly subsidizing the releases of a diverse range of metal bands, including Opeth, Killswitch Engage, and Mastodon. Additionally, these profits provided crucial "seed money" for Billy Talent's efforts to establish a presence in the US market through their deal with Roadrunner. "When Nickelback was selling millions for roadrunner records, that money subsidized releases for a ton of metal bands like Opef and Kill Switch Engage and Mastodon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Zniri9uSlc
**Last-Minute Release of Billy Talent 3** : Following Warner US's decision to drop "Billy Talent 3" after it was leaked, Roadrunner Records stepped in to release the album. Ron Berman, a long-time fan, facilitated this last-minute deal. However, the quick turnaround meant they didn't have sufficient time to properly set up the release, and it came out "months after the release date," contributing to the record being "sabotaged." "so we ended up going to now roadrunner records because Ron Berman was a fan for a long time and they put out the record and it was like a last minute thing so they didn't have a lot of time to set it up right." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GT_u0eFDlk8
