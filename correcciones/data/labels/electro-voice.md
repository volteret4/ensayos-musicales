# label - Electro Voice

## curiosities
**Tweeters in the Wall of Sound (1973)** : Electro Voice tweeters were integrated into The Grateful Dead's "Wall of Sound" in 1973. Approximately 50 of these tweeters complemented the hundreds of JBL speakers to provide specific frequency distribution within the immense, 75-ton sound system. "The wall contained hundreds of speakers, 568 JBLs, and about 50 electro voice tweeters, to be specific." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
