# label - Warner Bros. Records

## curiosities
**Extravagant Album Promotion (1990s)** : In 1998, Warner Bros. Records demonstrated the lavish budgets available for promotion in the 1990s by hosting a global listening party for R.E.M.'s *Up* album in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The label flew in journalists and radio and music channel personnel from around the world to listen to the record, charging all associated costs back to the band. "When Warner Bros. was prepping the release of REM's up album in 1998, they held a listening party in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r8AazBVoS7g
**Seymour Stein's Executive Role** : Seymour Stein, founder of Sire Records, eventually became a vice president at Warner Bros. Records. "He ended up as a vice president at Warner Bros. Records eventually." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcYKZ5ovoy4
