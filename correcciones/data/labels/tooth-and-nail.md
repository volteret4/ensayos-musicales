# label - Tooth and Nail

## curiosities
**Artist Autonomy** : The label offered bands the freedom to create their desired music without feeling pressured to convert non-believers or to cater to the sensibilities of "frightened parents." "Tooth and Nail allowed the bands to make the music they wanted with no attempt to either A. Convert non-believers or B. Please frighten to parents." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
**Attracting Major Label Interest** : Major labels closely monitor bands signed to Tooth and Nail, especially when these bands demonstrate the ability to draw significant crowds, such as a thousand people, to their shows. "These days when Tooth and Nail signs a band, the major label is watched very, very closely. Hey, anytime a band they've never heard of can attract a thousand people to a show, they're going to pay attention." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
**Foundation and Purpose (1993)** : Tooth and Nail is a Christian-owned, Seattle-based record label established in 1993 by Brandon Ebel. Ebel founded the company after observing that talented punky and grungy indie bands in the Pacific Northwest could not secure deals with mainstream labels due to their religious connections, nor with traditional Christian labels because their sound was considered "too heavy." "Now Tooth and Nail is a Christian-owned Seattle-based record label that specializes in contemporary sounding Christian rock. The company was founded in 1993 by a guy named Brandon Ebel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
**Founding and Mission** : Tooth and Nail is a Christian-owned, Seattle-based record label established in 1993 by Brandon Ebel.
