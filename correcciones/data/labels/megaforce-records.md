# label - Megaforce Records

## curiosities
**Launchpad for Metal Icons** : Megaforce Records was a metal label launched in 1982 by Marsha Zazula and her husband John. It quickly gained prominence in the metal community by releasing the first records for influential bands such as Metallica and Anthrax, establishing itself as a key player in the genre's early development.
