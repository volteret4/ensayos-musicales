# label - Distortion Records

## curiosities
**Releasing Unknown Artists** : Distortion Records is a label known for releasing music by "Unknown Mystery 60s Group," which came from a reel-to-reel tape found in a flea market. They have issued these songs across multiple volumes. "Eventually this tape made its way to a label called Distortion Records which released the songs under the name Unknown Mystery 60s Group." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw
