# label - VH1

## curiosities
**Co-Premiere of "Black or White" (1991)** : VH1 was among the channels that simultaneously broadcast the premiere of Michael Jackson's "Black or White" music video in November 1991, reaching an audience of 500 million people worldwide. This made VH1 a key player in the cultural event surrounding the video's release.
