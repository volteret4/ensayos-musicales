# label - Pop Tones

## curiosities
**Alan McGee's New Label (2000)** : Pop Tones was the new label founded by Alan McGee, who previously founded Creation Records, by the year 2000. McGee actively sought out and signed The Hives, which subsequently led to their significant major label deal.
