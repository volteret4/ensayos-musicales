# label - HMV Records

## curiosities
**Last UK 78 RPM Releases (November 1960)** : HMV Records continued to produce 78 RPM records in the UK until November 1960. Ray Charles's classic, catalog number POP742, is identified as the last 78 RPM record released by HMV in the UK. "In the UK, it's known that HMV records was pumping out 78s until November of 1960." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
