**Dropping The Stooges and Demanding Refund** : Elektra Records eventually dropped The Stooges due to the band's escalating drug problems and chaotic live performances. The label went further by demanding an $11,000 refund on an advance they had previously given to the band. "So Electra dropped them. Actually, they more than dropped them. They demanded a refund of $11,000 on an advance they had given the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8
**Nuggets Compilation Release (1972)** : Elektra Records was persuaded by guitarist Lenny Kay to release the "Nuggets" compilation in 1972. This double album, which collected material from 1964 to 1968, played a crucial role in memorializing and codifying the entire Garage Rock era. "He was a big fan of this raw and sometimes sloppy rock, and was able to convince electro records to release a collection of material issued between 1964 and 1968." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
**Rejection of First Album Mix** : Elektra Records rejected the initial mix of The Stooges' self-titled debut album. The label president, Jack Holtzman, personally stepped in to remix the record before its release. "The first mix was rejected by the record label. The lecture president, Jack Holtzman, came in to do it himself." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8
**Signing of The Stooges** : Elektra Records, through talent scout Danny Fields, signed The Stooges despite the label's bosses being "absolutely stupified" by the band's unconventional sound. Fields was known for signing influential hippie bands like The Doors. "He, for example, was the guy who signed the doors. While in Detroit, to check out another bank called the MC5, Danny heard about the stuages. And even though his bosses were absolutely stupified by them, they agreed to sign the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8
**They Might Be Giants Album Release** : Elektra Records released They Might Be Giants' album `Flood` in 1990. "they released an album entitled Flood on Electro Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
