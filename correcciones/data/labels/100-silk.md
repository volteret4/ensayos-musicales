# label - 100% Silk

## curiosities
**Concert at Ghost Ship Warehouse** : The house music label 100% Silk organized the concert that was taking place on the second floor of the Ghost Ship warehouse in Oakland, California, when it caught fire on December 2, 2016. This event led to the deaths of 36 people in the illegally converted industrial building.
