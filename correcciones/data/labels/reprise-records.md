# label - Reprise Records

## curiosities
**Concept Album Strategy** : Reprise Records released two major alt rock concept albums, Green Day's *American Idiot* and My Chemical Romance's *The Black Parade*, within a few years of each other, suggesting a potential label strategy to capitalize on the success of the format. "And like Green Day, my chemical romance was on the reprise label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc
**Founded by Frank Sinatra** : Reprise Records was established in 1960 by Frank Sinatra, who at that time was recognized as the biggest star of the 20th century. Green Day later signed a record deal with this label. "reprise records, the label created by of all people Frank Sinatra in 1960, who was the biggest star of the 20th century to that point." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
