# label - Rendezvous Records

## curiosities
**Partnership with Alan Freed (1950s)** : Rendezvous Records, a major record store in Cleveland run by Leo Mintz, played a pivotal role in the early days of rock and roll. Mintz's observation of white teenagers buying R&B records led him to suggest that his friend, radio announcer Alan Freed, play them on his show. Mintz later purchased airtime for Freed on WJW, supplying the R&B records that would become the foundation of Freed's "Rock and Roll" programming.
