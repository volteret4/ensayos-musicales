# label - SEATS (Southeastern Advanced Ticketing Service)

## curiosities
**Licensing Select-A-Seat Software** : SEATS was a company that licensed Select-A-Seat's software, helping to spread computerized ticketing across nearly a dozen major American cities. The acronym stood for Southeastern Advanced Ticketing Service.
