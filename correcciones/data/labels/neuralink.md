## curiosities
**Implanted Chips for Music Consumption** : Elon Musk's company, Neuralink, is developing implanted chips with the promise of enabling individuals to consume music directly through a chip embedded in their heads. This technology aims to beam music directly into the auditory cortex, potentially rendering wireless Bluetooth headphones obsolete.
