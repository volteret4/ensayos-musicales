## curiosities
**Sister Label and Distribution Partnerships** : Anti-Records operates as a sister label to Epitaph Records. It provides marketing assistance to independent artists, such as Wilco's DBPM Records, and has a distribution deal with Republic Records, which is a sublabel of the Universal Music Group.
