# label - Gentlemen of the Road

## curiosities
**Artist-Owned Label for Debut Album (2009)** : Mumford & Sons established Gentlemen of the Road as their own business entity in 2009, taking the name from Michael Chabon's 2007 novel. This indie label was a prerequisite for them to self-finance and release their debut album, *Sigh No More*, which was subsequently licensed by Island Records. "They called the company Gentlemen of the Road, taking it from the title of a 2007 novel by Michael Shabon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P1RyOchn7pk
