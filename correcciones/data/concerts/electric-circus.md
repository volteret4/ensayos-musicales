# concert - Electric Circus

## curiosities
**'Short Circuit' Compilation Album** : The Electric Circus was the venue for the recording of the 10-inch record "Short Circuit: Live at the Electric Circus," which featured a song by Warsaw, indicating its role in showcasing emerging bands. "They even placed a song on a long, forgotten 10-inch record until the short circuit live at the Electric Circus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w
**Warsaw's First Gig Venue** : The Electric Circus, a club in Manchester, was the venue for Warsaw's (later Joy Division) first ever gig on May 29, 1977. Despite mixed reviews, the performance garnered positive mentions in music papers. "Warsaw played their first gig on May 29th, 1977, and a Manchester club called the Electric Circus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w
