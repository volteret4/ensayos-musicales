# concert - Gold Dollar

## curiosities
**The White Stripes' First Gig Venue (August 14, 1997)** : The Gold Dollar, a bar located in Detroit, hosted The White Stripes' inaugural public performance on August 14, 1997. This event marked their initial immersion into the Detroit and Michigan underground indie scene. "This seemed to work so they booked a gig at a place called the Gold Dollar, a bar in Detroit. And the date for that was August 14, 1997." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
