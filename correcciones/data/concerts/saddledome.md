# concert - Saddledome

## curiosities
**Robert Plant Tour Venue** : The Saddledome in Calgary was one of the "hockey rinks" where The Trews performed while opening for Robert Plant on his North American tour. It was also the venue where Robert Plant offered an indirect apology to The Trews for his earlier criticism. "that night in in Calgary at the Saddle Dome. He's like, you know, Canada has always had such a rich history of songwriting." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
