## curiosities
**First Public Address System Demonstration (December 24, 1915)** : On December 24, 1915, San Francisco City Hall was the venue for the first public demonstration of the Magnavox public address system. A crowd of 100,000 people gathered to hear amplified Christmas music and holiday speeches, marking a significant early application of microphone, amplifier, and loudspeaker technology for large public gatherings. "On December 24th, 1915 their Magnavox system was used for the first time. They had a mic, an amp, and a 34 inch speaker set up at San Francisco City Hall." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
