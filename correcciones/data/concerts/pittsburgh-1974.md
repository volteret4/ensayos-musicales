# concert - Pittsburgh 1974

## curiosities
**Neil Peart's First Gig with Rush** : Neil Peart's inaugural performance with Rush took place on August 14th, 1974, in Pittsburgh. At this concert, Rush served as the opening act for both Uriah Heep and Manfred Mann. "Neil's first gig with the band was August 14th, 1974 in Pittsburgh." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B0CZ1deEgic
