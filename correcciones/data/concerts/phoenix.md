# concert - Phoenix

## curiosities
**The Rolling Stones Secret Show** : The Phoenix in Toronto was the venue for a rumored "secret show" by The Rolling Stones in Summer 2005. The Trews opened this highly anticipated and last-minute gig, finding The Rolling Stones soundchecking on stage when they arrived. "we went to the Phoenix and it was sound check. And we walked in and there was the rolling stones, you know, sound checking on the stage at the Phoenix." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
