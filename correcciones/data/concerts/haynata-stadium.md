# concert - Haynata Stadium

## curiosities
**Abdulaziz Stati Concert Stampede (Rabat, Morocco, May 23, 2009)** : A free concert by Abdulaziz Stati at Haynata Stadium in Rabat, Morocco, on May 23, 2009, attracted approximately 70,000 attendees. As other local concerts concluded, the massive influx of people converging on the stadium resulted in a stampede, which caused a wire fence to collapse, trapping eight individuals. This incident ultimately led to 11 deaths by asphyxiation and nearly a dozen injuries.
