# concert - Torquay Jug Pub

## curiosities
**Ziggy Stardust Debut Venue (February 10, 1972)** : The Torquay Jug Pub in London was the location for the formal debut of David Bowie's Ziggy Stardust character on February 10, 1972. Despite low attendance due to Bowie's then-minor star status, it was a pivotal moment in the character's history.
