# concert - Station Night Club

## curiosities
**Deadly Pyrotechnics Fire (Warwick, Rhode Island, February 20, 2003)** : The Station Night Club in Warwick, Rhode Island, was the scene of a horrific fire on February 20, 2003, during a Great White performance. Pyrotechnics ignited flammable acoustic foam in the walls and ceilings, engulfing the club in flames within about a minute and filling it with thick black smoke within two. The resulting panic to escape killed 100 people and injured 230. "That was awful.
