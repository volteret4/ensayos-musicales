## curiosities
**Malcolm McLaren's Punk Fashion Epiphany (1975)** : In 1975, while on a trip to New York for a fashion fair, Malcolm McLaren visited CBGBs to immerse himself in the local music scene. It was there he encountered Richard Hell, whose poverty-driven habit of securing ripped clothing with safety pins mistakenly inspired McLaren, leading him to adopt this look and introduce it to his London store, "Sex," where it became a foundational element of punk fashion.
