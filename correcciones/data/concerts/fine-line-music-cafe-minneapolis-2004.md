# concert - Fine Line Music Cafe, Minneapolis (2004)

## curiosities
**The Pixies' First Reunion Show (2004)** : On April 13, 2004, The Pixies played their first show since their 1992 Vancouver performance with U2. This gig at the Fine Line Music Cafe in Minneapolis marked the start of their reunion tour, occurring 11 years, two months, and 30 days after Black Francis' fax announcing the band's breakup. "On April 13, 2004, the Pixies played together for the first time since that U2 show in Vancouver in 1992. The gig was at a club in Minneapolis." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
