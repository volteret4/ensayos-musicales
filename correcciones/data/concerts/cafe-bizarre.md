# concert - Cafe Bizarre

## curiosities
**The Velvet Underground's Challenging Residency (December 1965)** : In December 1965, this Greenwich Village, New York City club hosted The Velvet Underground's first regular gig, where they were contracted for six sets per night, six nights a week. However, the band's avant-garde and unconventional music frequently drove patrons away, leading to their being fired multiple times for causing customers to leave, until their opportune discovery by Andy Warhol. "Unfortunately for the owner, the band's music ten to do chase away patrons instead of luring them in and the band was fired at least once for driving away customers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
