# concert - Hyde Park

## curiosities
**Red Hot Chili Peppers Record Attendance and Gross (2004)** : In the summer of 2004, Hyde Park in London hosted the Red Hot Chili Peppers over three nights, drawing a record attendance of 245,000 people. These concerts generated a gross of $17 million, setting a significant record for the venue.
