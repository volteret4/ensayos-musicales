# concert - Route 91 Harvest Festival

## curiosities
**Impact on Festival Security Protocols** : The tragic events at the Route 91 Harvest Festival in Las Vegas in 2017 led to a significant overhaul of security protocols across festivals globally, particularly in the United States. This horrific incident forced event organizers to implement new measures, fundamentally changing the security landscape and attendee experience at US festivals. "But this awful event set a lot of security protocols in place that are now used by festivals around the world, especially in America." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xid8CZf4oYo
**Site of Deadliest Mass Shooting in US History (2017)** : On October 1, 2017, the Route 91 Harvest Festival in Las Vegas, held at the Las Vegas Village across from the Mandalay Bay Hotel, became the scene of the deadliest mass shooting by a lone gunman in American history. While Jason Aldean was on stage around 10:00 PM, 64-year-old Stephen Paddock opened fire from room 134 on the 32nd floor of the Mandalay Bay Hotel, continuing for 10 awful minutes starting at 10:05 PM. The attack resulted in 58 deaths (with two more later), 413 gunshot wounds, and at least 867 injuries. Paddock was found dead of a self-inflicted gunshot wound, and a large arsenal of 24 firearms and thousands of rounds of ammunition were found in his room. Despite extensive investigation, his motivations remain unknown. "This remains the deadliest mass shooting by a lone gunman in American history." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xid8CZf4oYo
