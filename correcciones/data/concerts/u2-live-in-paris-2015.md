# concert - U2 Live in Paris (2015)

## curiosities
**Part of Innocence + Experience Tour Under Live Nation Deal** : U2's "Live in Paris" concert in 2015 was a component of their Innocence + Experience Tour. This specific gig was highlighted as falling under the extensive 12-year partnership deal the band struck with Live Nation in March 2008, which covered various aspects of their career, including touring.
