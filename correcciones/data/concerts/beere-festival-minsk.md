# concert - Beere Festival (Minsk)

## curiosities
**Worst Rock Concert Tragedy (1999)** : The Beere Festival, a rock concert in Minsk, Belarus, on May 30, 1999, is recorded as the worst rock concert tragedy of all time. Under sunny skies, up to 10,000 people gathered, but a sudden downpour of rain and hail caused hundreds to rush for shelter in a nearby subway station. A person, believed to be one of the young women leading the way in high heels, slipped on the wet concrete stairs, leading to a pile-up where people were trampled and suffocated. The disaster resulted in 50 concert-goers and 3 policemen dying, with 42 of the victims being teenage girls.
