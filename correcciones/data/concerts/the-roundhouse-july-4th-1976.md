# concert - The Roundhouse (July 4th, 1976)

## curiosities
**Introduction of US Punk to the UK** : Located in Camden, London, this gig was England's "first proper exposure to US style punk rock" through the Ramones. Despite a near cancellation by their European record company, the Ramones delivered an "explosive" performance to an audience of about 2,000, profoundly influencing a generation of British punk musicians. "The roundhouse in Camden, which is on the north side of London, held about 2,000 people." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
