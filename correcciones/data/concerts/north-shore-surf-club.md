# concert - North Shore Surf Club

## curiosities
**Nirvana's First Gig with Dave Grohl (1990)** : On October 11, 1990, the North Shore Surf Club in Olympia, Washington, hosted Nirvana's debut live performance with their new drummer, Dave Grohl. This concert was a significant milestone, completing the lineup that would soon rise to become the biggest rock band of the 1990s.
