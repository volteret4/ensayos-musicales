# concert - Us Festivals

## curiosities
**Fixed Location Predecessor** : The Us Festivals were noted as previous festivals in the US that were "rooted in one place," distinguishing them from the traveling format of Lollapalooza. "There had been other festivals in the US. Of course, there was Monterey, there was Woodstock, there was Ultimonts, the Us Festivals, there was something called the Gathering of the Tribes. But all of them were rooted in one place." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
