# concert - Winnipeg 1975

## curiosities
**Rush's First Canadian Show with Neil Peart** : The first Canadian concert featuring Neil Peart as Rush's drummer occurred in Winnipeg on January 15th, 1975. This show took place shortly before the recording of their *Fly By Night* album later that month. "The first Canadian show together was in Winnipeg on January 15th, 1975." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B0CZ1deEgic
