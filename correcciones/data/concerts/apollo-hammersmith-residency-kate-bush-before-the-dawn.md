# concert - Apollo Hammersmith residency (Kate Bush, Before the Dawn)

## curiosities
**Kate Bush's Historic Return to Stage (2014)** : Kate Bush staged a highly anticipated 22-show residency titled "Before the Dawn" at the Apollo Hammersmith in London, running from August 26th to October 1st, 2014. Tickets sold out in minutes for her first full live shows in decades, attracting fans globally to multimedia events featuring elaborate costumes, magicians, dancers, puppets, and 3D animation. "That is until March 2014 when she announced a 22-show residency at the Apollo Hammersmith in London.
