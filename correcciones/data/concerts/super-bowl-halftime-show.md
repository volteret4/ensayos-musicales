**Janet Jackson/Justin Timberlake Incident (February 1, 2004)** : The 2004 Super Bowl halftime show in Houston became infamous due to the incident where Justin Timberlake briefly exposed Janet Jackson's nipple. This highly controversial moment was the most replayed event on DVRs at the time and contributed to the public's desire for easier online video sharing, influencing YouTube's creation.
