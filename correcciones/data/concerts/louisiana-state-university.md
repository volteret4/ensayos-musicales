# concert - Louisiana State University

## curiosities
**Canceled Gig and Future Fulfillment** : Leonard Skinnerd had a concert scheduled at Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge for October 20, 1977. This gig was canceled due to the plane crash. Fourteen years later, the band made good on the promise, performing for about 100 people who had held onto their tickets from the original date. "The band did make good on that canceled show at Louisiana State University. About 100 people who hung onto their tickets for the gig on October 21, 1977 were able to see the band play 14 years later." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dgzF_KBSQWM
