# concert - US Festival (second)

## curiosities
**Massive Ticketing Challenge for Ticketmaster (1983)** : The second US Festival, a large event in San Bernardino, California, underwritten by Steve Wozniak, presented a significant opportunity and challenge for Ticketmaster in early 1983. Ticketmaster, despite not yet having its own terminals, secured the exclusive contract and successfully sold approximately 670,000 hard tickets for the festival. "It became the sole seller of tickets for the second us festival, a giant event in San Bernardino California underwritten by Steve Wozniak of Apple computers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
