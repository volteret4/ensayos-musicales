# concert - The Kingdom (Burlington)

## curiosities
**Early "Smells Like Teen Spirit" Play (1991)** : On a Thursday night following its CD single release in August 1991, Nirvana's "Smells Like Teen Spirit" was played at The Kingdom (potentially called Energy at the time) in Burlington for an alternative dance night. The song's impact was evident as attendees already knew all the words. "We played the CD single at the kingdom in Burlington. We might have been called energy at the time I can't remember, but we played it on the Thursday night for an alternative dance night. Everybody knew all the words already." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bI6TTA-fn7c
