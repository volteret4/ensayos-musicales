# concert - Monterey Pop

## curiosities
**Advances in Festival Amplification (1967)** : Monterey Pop in 1967 is recognized as one of the festivals where significant advances in sound systems for large-scale musical events began to be made. These developments were crucial in addressing the growing demands for amplified music at outdoor festivals and improving the listening experience for large audiences. "Some advances were made for festivals like Monterey Pop in 1967 and Woodstock in 1969." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**First Modern Music Festival** : Monterey Pop, held in San Francisco in the summer of 1967, is considered the first modern music festival, recognizable in the format known today. It paved the way for other major festivals like Woodstock and Glastonbury. "Now, the first modern music festival, the thing that would be recognizable to us today, would probably be Monterey Pop, which was held in San Francisco in the summer of 1967." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
