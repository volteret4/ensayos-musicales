# concert - Shea Stadium

## curiosities
**Acoustic Challenges (1965)** : Shea Stadium presented significant acoustic challenges during The Beatles' concert in August 1965. The venue lacked a proper concert sound system, forcing the band's audio to be routed through the stadium's public address system, originally designed for announcements, resulting in notoriously poor sound quality.
