# concert - Reliant Stadium

## curiosities
**Live Album Recording Location** : Reliant Stadium in Houston was the venue where Linkin Park's live album and DVD were primarily recorded on August 2, 2003. Although the audio for the release was taken exclusively from this performance, video footage was also captured the following night in Dallas.
