# concert - Indian Ocean tsunami benefit

## curiosities
**Catalyst for Alice in Chains Reunion (Boxing Day 2004)** : Three members of Alice in Chains (Jerry Cantrell, Mike Inez, and Sean Kinney) were asked to play a benefit for the victims of the Indian Ocean tsunami on Boxing Day 2004, agreeing to perform with guest singers. This event was a pivotal moment that "started the reunion" process for the band. "The thing that started the reunion was the Indian Ocean tsunami on Boxing Day 2004." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yR3xc3ra5tE
