# concert - Elton Mont Speedway Free Festival

## curiosities
**Historical Significance (1969)** : Held on Saturday, December 6, 1969, at a racetrack in Northern California, this festival is noted for taking place before the era of professional concert security. It is infamously remembered for the death of Meredith Hunter during The Rolling Stones' set, which "formally killed the peace and love vibe of the 1960s" and prompted promoters to begin hiring professional security companies for crowd control.
