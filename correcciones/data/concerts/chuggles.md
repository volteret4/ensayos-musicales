# concert - Chuggles

## curiosities
**Early Trews Venue** : Chuggles was one of three main bars in Antigonish, Nova Scotia, where The Trews (then One-eyed Trouser) frequently played cover gigs for years during the late 1990s. It was a regular haunt for the band in their hometown. "We mostly played at Chuggles." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
