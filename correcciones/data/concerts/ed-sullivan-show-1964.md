# concert - Ed Sullivan Show (1964)

## curiosities
**George Harrison's Gretsch Country Gentleman Performance (February 1964)** : George Harrison played a Gretsch Chet Atkins Country Gentleman model during The Beatles' appearance on The Ed Sullivan Show in February 1964, which led to a massive increase in orders for that specific guitar model. "George Harrison played a Gretch Chad Atkins country gentleman model for the group's Ed Sullivan appearance in February 1964." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d9HNqb9gJSU
**John Lennon's Rickenbacker 325 Performance** : John Lennon famously played his Rickenbacker 325 model during The Beatles' 1964 appearance on The Ed Sullivan Show, an event watched by 73 million people. "That's the guitar, 73 million people saw in play when the Beatles appeared on the Ed Sullivan show in 1964." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d9HNqb9gJSU
**The Beatles' Appearance and Guitar Sales Impact** : The Beatles' appearance on The Ed Sullivan Show in 1964 caused a massive surge in orders for both Epiphone Casino guitars (played by John Lennon) and Gretsch Chet Atkins Country Gentleman models (played by George Harrison), significantly boosting sales for both brands. "And the second the Beatles appeared on the Ed Sullivan show in 1964, orders came flooding in." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d9HNqb9gJSU
