# concert - The Spoke in London

## curiosities
**Live Debut of "Not Necessary"** : This student pub at Western University in London was the venue where The Tragically Hip first played "Not Necessary" live as an encore. Gordon Sinclair reportedly picked up an acoustic guitar moments before going on stage to show the band how to play the song they had never performed before. "I remember being in a dressing room at the spoke in London, the student pub at Western. And we had finished our set and they were calling for an encore and we said, let's play this song necessary." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G9H9q7BYqEA
