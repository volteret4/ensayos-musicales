## curiosities
**Concert Venue in Tacoma, Washington** : A 14-year-old girl, referred to as Paulie, was hitchhiking her way home from a concert at this venue in Tacoma, Washington, in June 1987 when she was kidnapped at gunpoint by serial rapist Gerald Frant. This incident later inspired Nirvana's song "Polly."
