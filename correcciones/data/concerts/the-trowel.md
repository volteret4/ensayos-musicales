# concert - The Trowel

## curiosities
**Buffalo Venue Promoted by Dave Taylor** : The Trowel was one of several "big venues" in Buffalo, New York, that was promoted by Dave Taylor, who eventually became The Trews' manager. This connection helped The Trews secure shows in Buffalo. "Dave Taylor, a promoter at a Buffalo, New York, who promoted like three or four venues, like big venues, like the trowel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
