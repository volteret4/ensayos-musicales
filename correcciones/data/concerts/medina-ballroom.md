# concert - Medina Ballroom

## curiosities
**Location of Scott Weiland's Final Planned Show** : The Medina Ballroom was the venue where Scott Weiland was scheduled to perform on December 3rd, 2015, the night he died on his tour bus parked nearby. "He wanted to rest out before that night's show at the Medina Ballroom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r6vVw66-gmI
