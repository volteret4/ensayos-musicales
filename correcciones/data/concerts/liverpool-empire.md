# concert - Liverpool Empire

## curiosities
**Kraftwerk's Transformative Performance (September 11, 1975)** : Kraftwerk's concert at the Liverpool Empire on this date had a profound impact on Andy McCluskey of OMD, transforming his life and serving as a major inspiration for the band's electronic direction. "I went to go and see Kraftwerk play at the Liverpool Empire on September the 11th, 1975. And that transformed my life." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0fQQubt9UTI
