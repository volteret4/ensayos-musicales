# concert - Rose Bowl

## curiosities
**Music for the Masses Tour Finale (June 6, 1988)** : The Rose Bowl in Pasadena was the site of the grand finale of Depeche Mode's "Music for the Masses" tour on June 6, 1988. The band successfully filled the stadium with 60,000 people, a pivotal moment that was documented by a live album and a concert film, solidifying their breakthrough into superstardom.
