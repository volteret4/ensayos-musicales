# concert - Variety Theater (Cleveland)

## curiosities
**Structural Damage from Extreme Volume (1986)** : A Motörhead concert at the Variety Theater in Cleveland in 1986 reportedly hit 130 decibels, a volume so intense that it "caused structural damage" to the venue. This incident underscores the physical impact that extremely loud live music can have on performance spaces.
