# concert - Point Theatre

## curiosities
**The Smashing Pumpkins Tragedy (May 11, 1996)** : This venue in Dublin was the site of a tragic incident during a Smashing Pumpkins concert on May 11, 1996. Seventeen-year-old Bernadette O'Brien died after being crushed in the mosh pit, prompting Billy Corgan to stop the show. The event, attended by 7,000 people, led to increased crackdowns and legal actions against moshing.
