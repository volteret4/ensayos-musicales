# concert - Coachella Festival

## curiosities
**The Stooges' Reunion Performance (2003)** : The Stooges officially regrouped and performed at the Coachella Festival in California on April 27, 2003. This marked their first performance together after a hiatus of 29 years, two months, and 19 days since their last gig in February 1974.
