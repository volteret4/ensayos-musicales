# concert - Emo's (Austin)

## curiosities
**Black Keys Live Recording Venue** : Emo's in Austin, Texas, was the venue where The Black Keys performed on October 24, 2003, a show that was later released as the official bootleg *Live in Austin*, recorded during their *Thickfreakness* tour. "It was recorded like a boot leg, but was released with the approval of the block keys. It's simply called Live in Austin, and dates all the way back to a show they played in October 24, 2003, at a place called Emo's, when the band was touring the Thick Freakness album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
