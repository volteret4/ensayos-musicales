# concert - David Bowie World Convention

## curiosities
**Inaugural Convention (2022)** : Liverpool was chosen to host the first-ever David Bowie World Convention from June 17th to 19th, 2022. This three-day event was held to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Bowie's iconic "Ziggy Stardust" album, bringing together fans for a comprehensive celebration of his work.
