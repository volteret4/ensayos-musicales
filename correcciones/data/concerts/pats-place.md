# concert - Pats Place

## curiosities
**Antigonish Bar** : Pats Place was one of the three main bars in Antigonish, Nova Scotia, where The Trews (as One-eyed Trouser) played cover gigs in the late 1990s. Its quality as a venue varied, sometimes being good and sometimes bad. "Pats Place was kind of like the, I don't know, sometimes it was good, sometimes it was bad." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
