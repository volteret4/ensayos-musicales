# concert - Rock am Ring

## curiosities
**Major European Festival Performance** : Billy Talent played at the Rock am Ring festival in Europe, a significant moment highlighting their success there, particularly in Germany. The band members recall performing in front of a massive crowd of 10,000 people at this event, which was a testament to their growing European fanbase. "I remember seeing you play rock and ring for the first time. Yeah. And thinking, how many? Okay, this guy used to get me coffee and he's seeing it in front of how many people are in 10,000." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GT_u0eFDlk8
