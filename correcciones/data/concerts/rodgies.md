# concert - Rodgies

## curiosities
**Weezer's First Gig with Permanent Name** : Weezer played their first gig under their permanent name at Rodgies on March 19, 1992. The opportunity arose because the club needed more bands to fill out that night's bill. Dog Star, featuring Keanu Reeves on bass, was the headliner that evening. "The day they moved into the house, they got an offer to play at a club called Rodgies, which needed more bands to fill out that night's bill." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DoJa8jcrlUA
