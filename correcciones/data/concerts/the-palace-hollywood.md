# concert - The Palace (Hollywood)

## curiosities
**Ramones' Farewell Performance** : This venue hosted the Ramones' official final gig on August 6, 1996, marking the end of their 22-year career. "until the official end of the band on August 6, 1996 after a gig at the palace in Hollywood." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
**Ramones' Final Concert Venue (August 6, 1996)** : The Palace in Hollywood was the venue for the Ramones' 2263rd and final planned concert on August 6, 1996. "After playing exactly 2262 shows, it was determined that the 2263rd gig would be their last, and would happen at a venue called the Palace in Hollywood." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
