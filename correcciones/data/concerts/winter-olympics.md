# concert - Winter Olympics

## curiosities
**Dave Grohl Meets Jim Craig** : Years after Dave Grohl's childhood admiration for Jim Craig, the 1980 "Miracle on Ice" Olympic goalie, Grohl eventually met his hero at a Winter Olympics event. This encounter added a unique twist to the inspiration behind Grohl's song "My Hero." "I'm not sure if Dave actually connected with Jim Craig the Olympic goalie but they did meet at the Winter Olympics years later which is a whole twist to the my hero thing right." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PHLwjD-L724
