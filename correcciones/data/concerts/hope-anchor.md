# concert - Hope & Anchor

## curiosities
**Joy Division's First London Gig** : Joy Division played their first ever gig in London at the Hope & Anchor on December 27, 1978. It was during the return journey from this cold and flu-ridden show that Ian Curtis suffered his first confirmed epileptic seizure in the band's van. "The members of Georgia Division were in their van on their way home from their first ever gig in London at a place called the Hope & Anchor." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w
