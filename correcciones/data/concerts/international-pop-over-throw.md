# concert - International Pop Over Throw

## curiosities
**Annual Festival in Chicago (Since 1997)** : The "International Pop Over Throw" is an annual festival hosted in Chicago that has been running since 1997. It features bands primarily within the Powerpop vein, serving as a living legacy to the genre and specifically inspired by Material Issue's album of the same name.
