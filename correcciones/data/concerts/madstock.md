# concert - Madstock

## curiosities
**Madness Reunion and "Earthquake" (August 18, 1992)** : The Madstock festival in Finsbury Park, London, on August 18, 1992, was significant as the first performance by the band Madness in eight years, drawing over 36,000 attendees. During their set, the intense dancing of the crowd, particularly during "One Step Beyond," generated vibrations that registered on the Richter scale, earning the event the nickname "the great Madness earthquake."
