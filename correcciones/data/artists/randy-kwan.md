# artist - Randy Kwan

## member of
- Headstones

## curiosities
**Co-writer of "Cemetery"** : Co-wrote the Headstones' song "Cemetery" with Hugh Dillon, having been in Dillon's typing class during high school. "Randy Kwan, who wrote cemetery with me back in the day, who was in my typing class." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
**Early Role in Sean Penn Atenshry** : He was initially "driving" the pre-Headstones band Sean Penn Atenshry, and jammed with Mark Gibson and Trent Carr at a house on Carlson before Hugh Dillon joined. "So it was Randy driving it and, you know, we're drinking a lot and having a lot of fun." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
