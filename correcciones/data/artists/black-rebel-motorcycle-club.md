## curiosities
**Family Collaboration** : Michael Been, Robert's father, worked as a soundman for Black Rebel Motorcycle Club at a festival in Brussels. He died there from a fatal heart attack on August 19th, 2010. "Dad and son worked together for quite a bit with Black Rebel Motorcycle Club right to the end. On August 19th of 2010, Dad was working as a soundman for his son's band, a festival in Brussels. When he had a fatal heart attack, he died right there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Formation and Continuity** : Black Rebel Motorcycle Club was formed in 1998 by Robert Been and continues to be an active group today. Robert Been initially used the name Robert Turner to distance himself from his father's musical legacy, Michael Been of The Call. "His break came with a band called Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, a great group formed in 1998 and one that continues today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
