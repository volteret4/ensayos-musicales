# artist - Billy Ward and the Dominoes

## songs
**60 Minute Man (1951)** : This 1951 recording is cited as a potential source for Alan Freed's popularization of the term "Rock and Roll." The song contains the lyric "I rock them, roll them all night long," which at the time was African American slang for engaging in sexual activity.
