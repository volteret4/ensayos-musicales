## curiosities
**Post-Peak Technopop Persistence** : The Communards, a spin-off from Bronski Beat, were highlighted as a group that, like their parent band, continued to produce music with strong Technopop DNA even after the genre's peak popularity declined in the mid-1980s. "Bronski beat and their spin off the Comming Arts stood their ground." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
