## curiosities
**Brother's Death (March 2007)** : Matt Sorum's younger brother passed away from brain cancer in the first week of March 2007, marking a rough start to the year for Velvet Revolver. "Former Matt Sorum's little brother died of brain cancer the first week of March." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
**Cool and Friendly Demeanor (2004)** : At a press conference for Velvet Revolver's "Contraband" album, Matt Sorum, along with Duff McKagan and Slash, were noted as being "cool, all very friendly, all hanging out together," contrasting with Scott Weiland's detached presence. "The three guns and roses guys were cool, all very friendly, all hanging out together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
**Shared Rehab Experience with Scott Weiland (Pre-2003)** : Scott Weiland trusted Matt Sorum partly because they had been in rehab together, contributing to the initial chemistry and comfort within Velvet Revolver. "Besides, he'd been in rehab with Matt Sorom so he trusted him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
**Velvet Revolver Formation (2003)** : Matt Sorum, as one of the three core Guns N' Roses members, was instrumental in forming Velvet Revolver alongside Duff McKagan and Slash. "It was Duff, Slash, and drummer Matt Sorom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
