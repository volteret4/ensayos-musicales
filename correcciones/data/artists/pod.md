## curiosities
**Atlantic Records Signing** : The band's substantial fanbase, cultivated through performances in Christian-friendly clubs across the U.S., ultimately led to their discovery and signing by Atlantic Records. P.O.D. became one of the pioneering bands to successfully cross over from the "Christian Underground" into the mainstream "Heathen Rock" world. "Eventually and thanks to the network of fans POD had amassed in the Christian friendly clubs across the US they were discovered and signed by Atlantic Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
**Born Again Members** : All members of P.O.D. are born again Christians, a fact that is not immediately apparent from their appearance, which includes tattoos and long hair. "All the members of the band are born again." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Criticism from within Christian community** : P.O.D. has been subject to condemnation by some individuals who believe that Christian bands, regardless of their message, are perverting Christianity by playing rock music. **Early Career & Manager** : The band's manager previously operated a Christian rock club in San Diego. P.O.D. formed in 1992 and initially built their career by performing at youth group concerts in churches and community centers across the country. They independently sold 40,000 copies of three self-released EPs. "When they started up in 1992, POD began by playing all the youth group concerts at churches and community centers in their area and then across the country." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
