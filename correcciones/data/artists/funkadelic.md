# artist - Funkadelic

## albums
**Standing on the Verge of Getting It On (1974)** : Released in 1974, this album contains the song "Alice in My Fantacies." "This is Funkadelic from a 1974 album called Standing on the Verge of Getting It On." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xo5DRSW95QQ

## songs
**Alice in My Fantacies (1974)** : Featured on Funkadelic's 1974 album "Standing on the Verge of Getting It On." The main guitar riff from this song was later appropriated by Tom Morello and used in Rage Against The Machine's track "Bombtrack." "And the main riff was caught from a song called Alice in My Fantacies by a band called Funkadelic." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xo5DRSW95QQ

## curiosities
**Giant of Rock Music** : Funkadelic is recognized as one of the significant figures in the broader landscape of rock music. "I can teen a turner, funk a delix, Stevie Wonder, Bob Marley, Prince or Slash." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Influence on Living Color** : Funkadelic served as an inspiration for the all-black funk metal band Living Color, alongside other artists like James Brown and Run DMC. "They were inspired equally by James Brown, Funkadelic, Run DMC, Slion the Family Stone, and Led Zeppelin Van Halen, and the Clash." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
