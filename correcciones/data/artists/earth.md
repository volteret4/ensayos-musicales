## albums

## songs
**Divine and Bright** : This song features early vocals from Kurt Cobain, who recorded them as a favor to Earth's bandleader and his close friend, Dylan Carlson, prior to the release of Nirvana's "Nevermind." The track was unavailable for years due to Carlson's addiction but was reissued in 2010 on the album "A Bureaucratic Desire for Extra Capsular Extraction." "As a favor to Dylan, a pre-never-mind Kurt offered up some vocals on a song called Divine and Bright that disappeared for years because Dylan was an addict and couldn't hold it together. In 2010, a collection of recordings from that time was reissued on an album called A Bureaucratic Desire for Extra Capsular Extraction, and Divine and Bright is included." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0D4wGWI-_z8

## curiosities
