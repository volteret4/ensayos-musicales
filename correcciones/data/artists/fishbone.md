## curiosities
**$1.4 Million Settlement for Stage Diving Injury (2014)** : In 2014, Fishbone was required to pay $1.4 million to a woman who sustained a broken collarbone when the band's Angelo Moore stage-dived on top of her. This incident underscored the serious legal and financial consequences of stage diving for bands and highlighted the need for greater audience safety. "That same year, fishbone had to pay $1.4 million to a woman who was hurt with a band's Angelo Moore staged over on top of her, breaking her collarbone." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Booked for Content Pivot (1996)** : Fishbone was one of the bands booked for the 1996 Warped Tour as the festival pivoted its content from broad-range alternative to focus on punk and other music that complemented skate culture, following its sponsorship by Vans. "This meant booking fishbone, the muddy, muddy boss tones, no effect, 3-11 penny wise, and this weird new group from San Diego called Blink 182." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4

**Pioneers of Genre Blending (1979)** : Fishbone formed in 1979 in South Central LA with four black members, becoming highly important for blending punk and funk, along with ska, hard rock, soul, and elements of hip hop and rap. "They were formed in 1979, four black guys from South Central LA. Their stuff was socially conscious, anti-racist, clever, witty, and basically a lot of fun." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
