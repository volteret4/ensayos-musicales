# artist - Darryl Bata Montey

## curiosities
**Devotional Tour Replacement (1993)** : Darryl Bata Montey joined Depeche Mode as a keyboardist, replacing Andy Fletcher for the last 39 shows of the second leg of the Devotional Tour after Fletcher suffered a nervous breakdown. He celebrated his 30th birthday during the tour with a three-day party.
