# artist - Lana Del Rey

## genres
- Dreampop (adjacent)
- Whisper Pop

## albums
**Born to Die (2012) – Breakthrough Album** : Lana Del Rey broke through in 2012 with this album after several years out of the spotlight. It was characterized by a noir and femme-fatale aesthetic, with storytelling and moodiness that evoked various subtle emotions, exemplifying the whisper pop style. "It was all very noir and rather femme-fatale with its storytelling and moodiness, evocative of many different subtle emotions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
