## curiosities
**Graceful Departure from Blink 182 (2022)** : Upon the announcement of Tom DeLonge's return to Blink 182, Matt Skiba handled the situation "like a pro." He was aware the announcement was forthcoming, accepted the circumstances gracefully, and extended his congratulations and thanks to the band members for the opportunity he had been given. "Matskiba took it like a pro. He knew the announcement was coming. He accepted the situation gracefully and congratulated the guys and thanked them for the opportunity of being in the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaGUC4av1R4
**Last Show with Blink 182 (2020)** : Matt Skiba's final performance with Blink 182 occurred on January 18, 2020, in Los Angeles, just before the global COVID-19 pandemic caused widespread shutdowns. His absence from the song "Quarantine," released later that year, was noted. "And that also turned out to be the last show with Mads Keeba." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaGUC4av1R4
**Not Sober During "Nine" Production (2019)** : During the period surrounding the creation of Blink 182's *Nine* album, Matt Skiba was not sober, paralleling a similar struggle experienced by Mark Hoppus at the time. "Same thing with Mads Keeba." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaGUC4av1R4
**Replaced Tom DeLonge in Blink 182 (2015)** : In March 2015, Matt Skiba was brought in from Alkaline Trio to replace Tom DeLonge in Blink 182. After performing three initial gigs, starting March 18, 2015, he was made an official member of the band. "Mark and Travis had no plans to kill Blink so they brought in Matt Skiba from Alkaline Trio." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaGUC4av1R4
