# artist - Bill Black's Combo

## concerts
- Maple Leaf Gardens (Toronto, 1964)

## curiosities
**Opening Act for The Beatles (1964)** : Bill Black's Combo served as an opening act for The Beatles' concert at Maple Leaf Gardens in Toronto on September 6, 1964. However, like the other supporting acts, they were met with boos from the impatient crowd, who were exclusively eager to see The Beatles perform. "As the opening bands, which included Bill Black's combo...hit the stage to crowd-bood, they only wanted one band, and one band only." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2IDbX5rNT1E
