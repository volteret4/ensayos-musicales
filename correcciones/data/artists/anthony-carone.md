## curiosities
**College Education** : After high school, Anthony Carone attended Humber College. "When I graduated high school, I went to Humber College, kept playing in bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Early Life and Musical Background** : Grew up in Mississauga, specifically Streetsville, which had a notable punk rock music scene. He played guitar in punk rock bands while simultaneously taking piano lessons. "Yeah. I came into the picture a little later, but when I was younger, I grew up in Mississauga in Streetsville, nice little punk rock music scene there. And I played in guitar and some punk rock bands all the while taking piano lessons." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Meeting Tim Oxford and Joining Arkells (2012)** : Anthony met Tim Oxford while playing bass in a cover band. Tim later called Anthony during the middle of his Warped Tour with Il Scarlett in 2012, inviting him to join Arkells as their keyboardist, as their previous one was returning to school. Anthony learned the material on the road and joined them two days after returning from tour. "So Tim calls me in the middle of that tour and goes, you know, our keyboardist is going back to school and we need someone to come in." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Warped Tour Appearance** : Anthony Carone played on the Warped Tour with the band Il Scarlett. He returned from the tour directly to jam with Arkells, still dressed in his Warped Tour attire, including a shaved head and bandana, finding it aesthetically surprising that Arkells accepted him. "I went on Wharp tour and in the middle of that Wharp tour. Who were you playing with for a while? Il Scarlett. Oh, cool. I was with them for a couple of years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
