# artist - The Box Tops

## members
- Alex Chilton (lead singer)

## songs
**The Letter (1967)** : This song was a smash single in the summer of 1967, featuring a 16-year-old Alex Chilton as the lead singer. The success of this track preceded Chilton's formation of Big Star after The Box Tops broke up.
