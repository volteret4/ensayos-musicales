## curiosities
**Playing an Actual B3 in the 90s** : Rob Collins of The Charlatans UK was noted for playing an actual Hammond B3 organ in the early 1990s. This usage marked a resurgence of the B3 sound, which had previously been considered outdated during the punk era.
