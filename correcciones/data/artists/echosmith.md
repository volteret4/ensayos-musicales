## curiosities
**Formation and Early Performances** : Echosmith was formed in early 2009 by the three Sierota brothers and their sister Sydney. They began playing together at home as children and eventually moved on to performing at farmers markets in their native California. "When Echo Smith was formed in early 2009, there were three brothers and a sister, Sydney, Noah, Graham and Jamie, Ceroda. They all started playing together at home when they were kids and eventually moved to playing farmers markets around where they grew up in California." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
**Jamie Sierota's Departure** : The original lineup, consisting of three brothers and one sister, remained intact until the end of 2016, when Jamie Sierota left the band. This change meant the band was "downgraded to two brothers and a sister" after having achieved a platinum single with "Cool Kids" in 2014. "That lineup stayed intact through to the end of 2016, which means this is how things were when they had a platinum single with this song, Cool Kids in 2014. It used to be three brothers and a sister, but they've since been downgraded to two brothers and a sister since 2016." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
