# artist - Alabama 3

## songs
**Woke Up This Morning (1997)** : Released in 1997, this song initially performed poorly as a single and slipped into obscurity. However, it was later discovered by producer David Chase, who selected it as the theme for his HBO mob series, "The Sopranos." Despite initial considerations for different theme songs per episode, HBO opted for consistency due to cost, leading Chase to offer Alabama 3 $40,000 US for the song, a deal they accepted, making the song synonymous with the show. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfie4YYncxk
