# artist - Organization

## albums
**Tone Float (1970)** : This album, released in 1970, represented the experimental stage of German rock, primarily utilizing traditional instruments. The music was considered challenging and did not chart, leading to the group's dissolution, but its members went on to form the highly influential band Kraftwerk.
