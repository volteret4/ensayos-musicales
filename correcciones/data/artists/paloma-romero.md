**Early Punk Drummer and Quick Learning** : Paloma Romero, a Spanish-born drummer, became a key figure in the early UK punk scene. Inspired by the movement, she learned to play drums in just one week before joining The Slits, an all-female band she co-founded with two 14-year-olds. "The whole scene inspired her to learn how to play the drums. She says she practiced for a whole week to learn how to play them. And then she joined an all-female band with a couple of 14-year-olds and they became the slits." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY
