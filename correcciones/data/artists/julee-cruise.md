## curiosities
**Association with David Lynch and Twin Peaks** : Julee Cruise gained prominence in the 1990s through her extensive musical contributions to director David Lynch's Twin Peaks projects, with her music featured throughout the TV series. "Julie had a nice run in the 1990s thanks to her association with director David Lynch and specifically his Twin Peaks projects. Her music was all over those TV series." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sdPeii-8Ysw
**Broadway Career** : Beyond her music, Julee Cruise also performed on Broadway as both an actor and singer, including a notable role portraying Janis Joplin. "Julie also appeared on Broadway as an actor and singer, including a role where she played Janice Chaplin." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sdPeii-8Ysw
**Poor Health and Depression from Systemic Lupus** : By 2018, Julee Cruise was in poor health due to systemic lupus, an autoimmune disease that caused significant pain and eventually left her unable to walk or stand, leading to a deep depression. "By 2018 she was in poor health as a result of systemic lupus, an autoimmune disease that caused her much pain. Soon she became unable to walk or stand, sending her into a deep depression." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sdPeii-8Ysw
**Suicide and Final Moments** : Julee Cruise died by suicide on June 9th at the age of 65, with her husband playing "Rome," her favorite B-52's song, during her final moments.
