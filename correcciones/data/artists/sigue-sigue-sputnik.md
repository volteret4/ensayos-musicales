# artist - Sigue Sigue Sputnik

## labels
- EMI Records

## albums
**Flaunt It (1986) – Debut Album with Advertisements Embedded Between Tracks** : Sigue Sigue Sputnik built their entire career on selling out as an art form, with music secondary to hype, image, and money-making. They signed to EMI without even submitting a demo tape — only a video of science fiction film clips. Their main slogan was "Fleece the World." Before the album's release they auctioned advertising space between the tracks, with studio line from L'Oréal and *ID* magazine buying space on the external artwork and commercials embedded within the record itself. Several band members had almost zero musical ability. The album underperformed and the band broke up after their second release.
