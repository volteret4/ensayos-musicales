# artist - Ike Turner

## member of
- Jackie Brenston and his Delta Cats

## members
- Willie Kitsert (guitar)

## songs
**Rocket 88 (1951)** : Recorded around March 1, 1951, this song features accidental guitar distortion after Ike Turner's band's guitar amp, belonging to Willie Kitsert, was damaged during a drive to Memphis, Tennessee. One account suggests it fell off the car, another that it was dropped from the trunk. The speaker cone burst, and newspaper was wadded and jammed into the enclosure as a makeshift repair. The resulting sound from this damaged amp was used for the recording, making it potentially the first rock and roll song if guitar distortion is the guideline. "And the sound that came out of this amp was pretty cool." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iGav9uJwA_w

This song was considered an early, "frightening" example of rock and roll, blending rhythm and blues with hillbilly and country music.
