## curiosities
**Culinary Skills** : Farrell is noted for his cooking abilities, particularly his skill with barbecue. "Guys, also quite the cook, especially when it comes to the barbecue." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YcdidjWaIqE
**Nickname Origin** : Dave Farrell acquired the nickname "Phoenix" because he has two prominent phoenix tattoos on his back. "They called him Phoenix because he has two big Phoenix tattoos on his back." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YcdidjWaIqE
**Rejoining Linkin Park (2002)** : Farrell officially rejoined Linkin Park on bass in December 2002, just before the recording of their second album, *Meteora*. His return was made possible by the time secured through the release of the *Reanimation* album. "the reanimation album bought Lincoln Park enough time to record a second CD, and the good news is that it allowed Dave Farrell to rejoin the group on base." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YcdidjWaIqE
**Temporary Departure from Hybrid Theory** : In 1999, Farrell temporarily left Hybrid Theory to tour the Christian circuit with his former band, Tasty Snacks. This absence necessitated the hiring of two session bass players for Linkin Park's debut album, *Hybrid Theory*, with Brad Delson filling in for any unplayed parts. "Didn't help that Dave, the bass player, left a tour of the Christian circuit with Tasty Snacks that year." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YcdidjWaIqE
