## curiosities
**Amazing Dolores Day** : Her local popularity was so significant that the city declared the day of her album's release as "Amazing Dolores Day." "By this time, she was such a local treasure that the city declared the day of its release Amazing Dolores Day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Background and Career Change** : The Amazing Dolores was originally a former model and owned a beauty and charm school in Mudlick, West Virginia. At the age of 40, she decided to shift her career focus to music. "She was a former model and the owner of a beauty and charm school. When she was 40, she decided to pivot into a music career." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Charleston Sensation** : She became a local sensation in and around Charleston, known for her distinctive appearance which included gold, leopard print clothing, and a giant beehive hairdo. Her performances also featured strange, stream-of-consciousness ramblings with apocalyptic religious themes. "The Amazing Dolores became something of a sensation in and around Charleston with her penchant for gold, leopard print clothing, a giant beehive hairdo, and weird stream of consciousness ramblings of an apocalyptic religious nature." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Current Status** : As of the discussion, she was believed to still be alive but no longer performing actively. "As far as I know, she's still around, although she doesn't seem to perform much anymore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Fan and Album Support** : Stan Lynch, the drummer for Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers, was a fan and provided assistance in recording her 1994 album, *Stop Messin with My Mind*. "She had a fan in Stan Lynch, the drummer for Tom Petty in the Heartbreakers, and he helped her record an album in 1994 called Stop Messin with My Mind." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
