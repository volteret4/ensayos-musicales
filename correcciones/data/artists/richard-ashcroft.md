# artist - Richard Ashcroft

## member of

## songs
**Bittersweet Symphony (1997)** : As the lead member of The Verve, Richard Ashcroft negotiated the initial sample use for "Bittersweet Symphony." However, due to Allan Klein's lawsuit regarding the sample, Ashcroft was forced to sign over all songwriting credits and rights to the entire song, receiving only $1,000 and no royalties for over 20 years until the rights were returned to him in May 2019.
