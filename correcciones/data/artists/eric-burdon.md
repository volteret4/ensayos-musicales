# artist - Eric Burdon

## curiosities
**"The Eggman" Nickname Origin** : Eric Burdon, the leader of The Animals, acquired the nickname "the Eggman" after he disclosed details of an unusual oral sex act involving raw eggs, which he learned from his Jamaican girlfriend, Sylvia. This anecdote later found its way into The Beatles' song "I Am the Walrus," with John Lennon adopting the moniker.
