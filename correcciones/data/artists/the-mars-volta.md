## curiosities
**Part of 2010s Progressive Rock Resurgence** : The Mars Volta was included in the broad category of bands participating in the 2010s comeback of progressive rock. This resurgence was characterized by an "attitude and an approach to instruments and arrangements," encompassing various experimental and complex styles like math rock and djent. "This was a big tent, including everyone from Biffy Cliro, to Dance Gavan Dance to the Mars Volta." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HSvgRyGiOpE
