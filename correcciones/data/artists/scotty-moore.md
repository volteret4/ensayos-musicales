# artist - Scotty Moore

## instruments

## curiosities
**Use of Echo and Reverb** : Scotty Moore, a guitarist in Elvis Presley's band, was known for using echo and reverb in his sound, an element admired by East Bay Ray of the Dead Kennedys.
