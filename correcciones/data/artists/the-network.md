## curiosities
**Suspected Green Day Identity** : The Network, despite denials from Billie Joe Armstrong, is widely suspected to be a pseudonymous project of Green Day. Evidence includes Armstrong's ownership of their label, Adeline Records, biographical lyrical references on their album Money Money 2020, visual similarities (such as "The Snew's nose" to Tré Cool's), the band's website being registered to Green Day Inc., and their single live performance opening for Green Day on October 13, 2005.
