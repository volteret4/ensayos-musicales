## curiosities
**Name Conflict with German Band** : Josh Homme's Gamma Ray project was short-lived as a potential long-term band due to a name conflict. A German power metal band already held the "Gamma Ray" name and initiated legal threats, forcing Homme to abandon the moniker for his new project. "His first thought was to resurrect gamma ray, but there was a problem. A German power metal band had dibs on that name and started making noises about legal action." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG1TQuhcf1o
