# artist - Bruno Walter & Philharmonic Symphony Orchestra of New York

## albums
**Mendelssohn's Concerto and E-Miner for Violin and Orchestra Opus 64 (1948) – First Consumer LP** : This performance was the first LP ever manufactured for consumer use. It was released to the public on June 28, 1948, shortly after the official unveiling of the long-playing record format.
