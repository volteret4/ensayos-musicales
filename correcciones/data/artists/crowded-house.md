## curiosities
**Farewell Concert for Charity (1996)** : Sensing their run was ending, Crowded House decided to split in grand style. On November 24, 1996, they performed a large farewell concert on the steps of the Sydney Opera House to raise money for the Sydney Children's Hospital, which was experiencing a cash crunch. "So on November 24th of 1996, Crowded house did their part by performing a giant farewell gig on the steps of the famous Sydney Opera House." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**International Acclaim and Fanbase** : Led by singer Neil Finn, the band achieved global recognition and a series of best-selling albums between 1985 and 1996, garnering fans worldwide, including Eddie Vetter of Pearl Jam. "They found fans around the world. Eddie Vetter of Pearl Jam just loved these guys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Origins in Split Enz** : Crowded House emerged from the successful New Zealand band Split Enz. "Crowded house grew out of split hands, one of the most successful New Zealand bands of all time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Paul Hester's Post-Band Career (2005)** : After the breakup, former drummer Paul Hester became a TV star in Australia, notably playing Paul the cook in The Wiggles, a children's band. "Former German Paul Hester, who had become something of a TV star in Australia. In fact, you know, if you have kids and they're into the Wiggles, Paul the cook was played by Paul Hester." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Reunion Triggered by Tragedy and Anniversary (2007)** : The funeral of Paul Hester, combined with the upcoming 10-year anniversary and DVD release of their Sydney Opera House performance, prompted a reconnection among the surviving members. On January 22, 2007, Crowded House officially announced their reunion after 10 years, one month, and 28 days apart. "The funeral coupled with the 10 year anniversary of the Sydney Opera House performance, the DVD was coming out to see. A reconnection began." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Tragic Death of Paul Hester (2005)** : Paul Hester suffered from personal problems and deep depression, leading him to hang himself on March 26, 2005. "Paul hanged himself on March 26th of 2005." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
