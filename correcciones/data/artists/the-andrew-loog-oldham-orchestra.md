# artist - The Andrew Loog Oldham Orchestra

## members
- Andrew Loog Oldham (manager)
- David Whitaker (orchestrator/arranger)

## albums
**The Rolling Stones Songbook (1966)** : Released by Andrew Loog Oldham, then manager of The Rolling Stones, this album featured orchestral instrumental arrangements of Stones songs for the easy-listening crowd. It contained a version of "The Last Time" with a six-second string figure arranged by David Whitaker that was central to The Verve's "Bittersweet Symphony" lawsuit. "In 1966, he released the Rolling Stones songbook under the name The Andrew Lou Geoldem Orchestra, and it featured this version of the 1965 Stone Song The Last Time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
