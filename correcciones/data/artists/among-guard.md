## curiosities
**Rivers Cuomo's Early Band** : This was one of Rivers Cuomo's very first bands, a "glammy metal group," where he performed under the name Peter Kits. The band played maybe six shows before Peter reverted to Rivers, and the group moved to Los Angeles, eventually changing their name to Zoom and breaking up in 1990.
