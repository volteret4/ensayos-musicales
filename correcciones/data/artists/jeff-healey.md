## curiosities
**Residency Offer to The Trews** : Jeff Healey saw The Trews open for him in Halifax in the summer of 2001 and was impressed. He subsequently offered them a weekly residency as the house band at his new establishment, Jeff Healey's bar, on Bathurst and Queen in Toronto, performing every Thursday. "He really liked us. He said, look, I'm starting a bar called Healey's. You guys give me my house band every Thursday." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
