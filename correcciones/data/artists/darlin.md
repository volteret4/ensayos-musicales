## albums
**Cindy So Loud / Darlin' (1992) – Full Rock Band; Two Songs; Melody Maker Review Killed the Band** : Darlin' was a full-on rock band featuring Guy-Manuel on guitar and Thomas on bass. They recorded a total of four songs (including their own composition "Cindy So Loud" and a cover of the Beach Boys' "Darlin'"), played two gigs, and lasted six months. A British music paper (Melody Maker) reviewed them saying they played "daft punky trash," and the band called it a day. The third member, Laurent Boutonnat, went on to work with Phoenix.
