## curiosities
**Band Recruiting Strategy at McMaster** : On his first day at McMaster University, Max Kermann proactively sought out potential band members. He met Mike DeAngeles by asking about his favorite bands and instruments. On the second day, Nick Dica approached him because of his Sam Roberts Band T-shirt, and Max quickly recruited him for bass. "I did immediately start profiling people on campus and seeing, this is like the kind of person that would want to be in a band with me. And on the first day I met Mike and on the second day I met Nick." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Chief Lyricist and Song Starter** : Max Kermann is responsible for starting Arkells' songs and is the band's chief lyricist. However, the songwriting process involves substantial collaborative editing from the other band members. "Yeah, I mean, I start the songs, but the more we do it, the more there's a pretty good heavy dose of editing going on from the guys where I go, is there a better way to say this? Or is this part clear?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Early Life and Education** : Grew up in downtown Toronto. Began playing guitar between ages 15 and 16. Attended McMaster University in Hamilton to study Political Science, primarily to meet potential bandmates rather than for academic interest. "Yeah, I grew up in downtown Toronto and I played in a couple of high school bands and I had a good time doing that. I learned how to play guitar when I was 15, 16 years old." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
