**Airport and Airline Incidents** : Wes Scantlin has a notorious record of problematic behavior at airports and on airlines. On September 4, 2012, a dispute with a flight attendant over alcohol on an American Airlines flight from Boston to LA led to an emergency landing in Austin, Texas, where he was arrested. In early 2015, at Denver International Airport, he rode a baggage carousel into a restricted area. Later that same year, he was arrested for disorderly conduct at Milwaukee airport. In 2016, he was denied boarding a flight to Louisville, Kentucky, due to extreme intoxication. In 2017, he was arrested at LAX for attempting to board a flight with a BB gun and has since been banned from the airport unless traveling for work.
