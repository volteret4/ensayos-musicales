**Musical Style and Contribution to The Cramps** : Poison Ivy, real name Christie Wallace, was the guitarist and co-writer of all original songs for The Cramps, a New York City-based band known for its unique blend of punk, psychobilly, garage, and horror movie aesthetics. She also occasionally provided vocals and, despite not being a virtuoso, was an inspirational figure in a challenging music scene.
