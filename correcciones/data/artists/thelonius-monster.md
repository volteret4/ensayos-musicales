**Brief Membership of John Frusciante (October 1988)** : Thelonius Monster, an L.A. punk band, offered John Frusciante a gig in October 1988. Frusciante briefly became a member, with legend stating he was "three hours into his membership" when the Red Hot Chili Peppers called to offer him their guitarist position, which he accepted instead.
