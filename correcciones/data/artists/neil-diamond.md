# artist - Neil Diamond

## curiosities
**Affected by Sony BMG Rootkit (2005)** : Albums by Neil Diamond were among those shipped with the Sony BMG Rootkit malware (XCP and MediaMax) installed. This software was surreptitiously placed on users' computers when they attempted to rip CDs, compromising system security without their consent. "The XCP malware came on albums ranging from Neil Diamond to Frank Sinatra to Celine Dion to Cindy Lopper." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vJszsJQpINQ
**John Wayne Gacy's Musical Preference** : John Wayne Gacy, a serial killer with at least 33 victims, was noted to be a fan of Neil Diamond, among other artists. This detail provides insight into the musical preferences of a notorious criminal.
