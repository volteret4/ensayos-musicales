## curiosities
**Top of the Pops Performance (July 6, 1972)** : The Spiders from Mars performed with David Bowie on Top of the Pops on July 6, 1972. Guitarist Mick Ronson was dressed in a gold one-piece, and shared a moment of public affection with Bowie (Bowie draped his arm around Ronson's shoulders) that was highly shocking for the era.
