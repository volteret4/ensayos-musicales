# artist - Oz Beldon

## curiosities
**Highest Ground-Based Gig (Date Not Specified)** : Oz Beldon, the founder of the organization Music for Children, holds the record for the highest gig performed with feet still on the ground. He delivered a 40-minute show for a group of hikers on the summit of Mount Mera in the Himalayas, at an impressive altitude of 21,825 feet.
