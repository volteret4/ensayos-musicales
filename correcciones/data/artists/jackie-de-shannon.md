# artist - Jackie De Shannon

## concerts
- Maple Leaf Gardens (Toronto, 1964)

## curiosities
**Opening Act for The Beatles (1964)** : Jackie De Shannon was one of the opening acts for The Beatles' concert at Maple Leaf Gardens in Toronto on September 6, 1964. She, along with the other support acts, was booed by the audience who were impatient and only wanted to see The Beatles. "As the opening bands, which included...the Celterie Jackie De Shannon hit the stage to crowd-bood, they only wanted one band, and one band only." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2IDbX5rNT1E
