## curiosities
**Judgment Night Collaboration with Faith No More (1993)** : Boo-Yaa T.R.I.B.E. collaborated with rock band Faith No More for the 1993 *Judgment Night* soundtrack. This pairing was considered radical at the time due to the limited crossover between rock and hip-hop. The project was a major catalyst for the creation of rap rock and nu metal, and helped bridge the divide between rock and hip-hop fans.
