## curiosities
**Departure from Pulp** : Russell Senior was a guitarist and violinist for Pulp, joining in 1980 and staying through their Britpop peak. He eventually quit the band, feeling annoyed that singer Jarvis Cocker (referred to as "Jarvis Pulp" in the transcript) was receiving all the attention. "But then he soured on the whole thing and was greatly annoyed that Singer Jarvis Pulp was getting all the attention. So he quit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Post-Music Writing and Antique Dealing** : Since leaving Pulp, Senior has pursued a career in writing books and musicals, dedicating most of his attention to these creative endeavors. He is also noted to have a side hustle as a dealer in antique glassware, and lives in Sheffield. "Russell has devoted most of his attention to writing books and musicals.
