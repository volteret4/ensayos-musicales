## curiosities
**First All-Female Band to Sign Major Record Deal** : Ginger Bianco kept time for Goldie and the Gingerbreads, an all-girl band formed in 1962. They were trailblazers as the first all-female band to sign a major record deal, paving the way for future female groups.
