## curiosities
**Beastie Boys Influence (Early Hip-Hop)** : The band began as a hip-hop group, heavily influenced by the attitude and style of the Beastie Boys. "Bloodhound Gang began as a hip-hop group influenced by the attitude of the Beastie boys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
**King of Prussia, Pennsylvania Origin** : The Bloodhound Gang originated in King of Prussia, Pennsylvania, located in the Philadelphia area. "They're from King of Prussia, Pennsylvania, which is in the Philadelphia area." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
**Morph into Satirical Alt-Rock** : Over time, The Bloodhound Gang transitioned into an alt-rock band, specializing in satire that leaned heavily into "sophomore college type humor." "but the longer they stuck around the more they morphed into an odd sort of alt rock band specializing in satire built around sophomore college type humor." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
**Philosophy of Offence** : From their inception, the band deliberately aimed to offend people, operating under the philosophy, "let's hurt everyone's feelings to make us feel better about ourselves." "from the very beginning they had absolutely no trouble offending people. In fact, their philosophy was, let's hurt everyone's feelings to make us feel better about ourselves." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
