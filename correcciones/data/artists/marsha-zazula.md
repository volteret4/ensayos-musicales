**Founding Megaforce Records** : In 1982, Marsha Zazula, along with her husband John, launched Megaforce Records, a metal-focused label. It became highly influential in the genre by releasing the debut records for seminal bands like Metallica and Anthrax, establishing itself as a key player in the genre's early development.
