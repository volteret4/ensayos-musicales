## curiosities
**Origin of "The Tragically Hip" Name** : The phrase "the tragically hip," which the band adopted as their name, was a throwaway line found in a VHS tape full of skits released by Mike Nesmith, formerly of The Monkees. "The tragically hip, which was a throwaway phrase in a VHS tape full of skits, released by Mike Nessmith, formerly of the monkeys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oMBmdfrA47Q
**Source of "The Tragically Hip" Name (1981)** : The name "The Tragically Hip" was taken from a line in a 1981 video collection of music and comedy produced by Mike Nesmith, a former member of The Monkees. The specific line was part of a skit about fundraising for "poor yuppies" to sustain their lifestyles of Lamborghinis and cocaine. "That was stolen from a line in a 1981 video collection of music and comedy by Mike Nessmith, ex of the monkeys. The skit had to do with raising money for poor yuppies to keep them in Lamborghini's and cocaine. They were the so-called tragically hip." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PnLdgohA5gY
