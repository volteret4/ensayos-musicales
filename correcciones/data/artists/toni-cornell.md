# artist - Toni Cornell

## songs
**Duet with Father (2018) – Pre-Death Recording** : In 2018, just in time for Father's Day, Toni released a duet she had recorded with her father, Chris Cornell, before his death. She was 13 years old when the recording was made. "In 2018, Tony released a duet she sang with her father before he died. This was released just in time for Father's Day that year." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc

## curiosities
**Addiction and Suicide Education Mission** : Following her father Chris Cornell's death in 2017, Toni embarked on a public mission to educate people about addiction and suicide. "After Chris died in 2017, Tony went on a mission to educate the public against things like addiction and suicide." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**Involvement with Chris's Foundation** : Toni is actively involved with Chris Cornell's foundation, which is dedicated to helping homeless, poor, and neglected children. "She's also part of Chris's foundation that works to help homeless, poor and neglected children." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**Preserving Father's Memory** : Along with her older sister Lillian, Toni keeps her father's memory alive by performing his songs in both online and live settings. "Both of them have been keeping their fathers' memory alive by performing a songs both online and live." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
