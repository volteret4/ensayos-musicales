# artist - Breezer

## albums
**The Lost Tapes Volume One (N/A) – AICIS Album** : This album was released under the name AICIS, an AI version of Oasis created by the British band Breezer. The music itself was composed by Breezer, but the lead vocals were generated by AI to sound like Liam Gallagher, a project undertaken out of a desire for an Oasis reunion. "Now the music comes from this band they're called Breezer but this is an AI-generated version of Liam Gallagher on lead vocals and damn it's pretty good." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QFtS8fl553Y

## curiosities
**Creation of AI-Oasis "AICIS"** : The British band Breezer created an artificial intelligence version of Oasis, dubbed AICIS, because they were "tired of waiting for an Oasis reunion." Breezer composed the music, and AI generated lead vocals in the style of Liam Gallagher for their album "The Lost Tapes Volume One," a project that even received approval from Liam Gallagher himself. "A British band got so tired of waiting for an Oasis reunion that they brought in some artificial intelligence. They created an AI version of Oasis called AI CIS and released an album called The Lost Tapes Volume One." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QFtS8fl553Y
