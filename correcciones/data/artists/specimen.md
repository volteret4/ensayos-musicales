## curiosities
**Founders of The Batcave Club** : Members of Specimen founded The Batcave club in Soho, London, in July 1982, which became the central headquarters for the burgeoning goth scene, characterized by its theatrical, performance art-like approach and specific aesthetic. "it was called the Batcave. And it was founded by members of a group called Specimen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
**Radical First Single Release** : Specimen's first single was uniquely released on video, a seriously radical move for 1982, demonstrating their theatrical and performance art-like approach to music. "For example, their first single wasn't released on record or cassette. They issued it on video, a seriously radical thing to do for 1982." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
**Spreading the Goth Gospel** : Specimen, through their Batcave club, played a significant role in spreading the goth culture, with Batcave nights franchising to other UK clubs and even to New York, establishing a consistent look, feel, and sound. "The Batcave people did a lot, probably more than anyone, to spread the gospel of Goth in those early days. And again, the heart of it all was specimen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
