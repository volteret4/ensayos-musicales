# artist - Leon Theremin

## curiosities
**Co-creation of the Rhythmicon (1932)** : Theremin collaborated with American composer Henry Cowell to create the Rhythmicon, unveiled as a prototype in 1932. "He eventually got hooked up with Russian inventor Leon Thereman... And together they created something they called the Rhythmicon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
**Inventor of the Theremin** : Leon Theremin is recognized as the inventor of the Theremin, a ghostly hands-free electronic device considered one of the early ancestors of the modern synthesizer. "Russian inventor Leon Thereman, the inventor of that ghostly hands-free electronic device, the Thereman, that was one of the early ancestors of the modern synthesizer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
