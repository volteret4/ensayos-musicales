## curiosities
**Early Categorization in the 1980s** : Rick Springfield, like Tom Petty and Greg Kihn, was occasionally grouped into the early alternative, new wave, or Powerpop categories in the early 1980s. This classification was a result of the broad tendency to label any succinct, energetic rock song as Powerpop during a period of genre fluidity. "This happened due to a tendency at the time to categorize all short, sharp rock songs as PowerPop.
