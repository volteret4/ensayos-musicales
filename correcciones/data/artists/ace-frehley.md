**Supported Donald Trump** : Ace Frehley was listed as one of the musicians who showed support for Donald Trump, making him an exception among many artists who declined to perform. "Outside of Kid Rock, Ted Nugent, three doors down and ace freely." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**Supportive Crew** : The crew working for Ace Frehley during his US tour with The Trews were noted for being "super accommodating and really sweet." This support was particularly appreciated by Colin McDonald, who was suffering from a severe bronchial infection at the time. "their crew was always really cool with us. They were super accommodating and really sweet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
**US Tour with The Trews** : Ace Frehley toured the United States with The Trews as his opening act for a six-week trek, performing in House of Blues venues across the country. The audiences for these shows were predominantly "die-hard Kiss fans" who were not necessarily open to new music. "We ended up doing like six week trek, opening for Ace Freyly at House of Blues all over America." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
