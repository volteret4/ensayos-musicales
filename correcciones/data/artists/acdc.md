**Four Lead Singers – Bon Scott Died 1980, Brian Johnson Made Back in Black, Axl Rose Subbed in 2016** : AC/DC has had four lead singers. Dave Evans was the original, present from the band's 1973 formation through their glam-rock phase. Bon Scott replaced him after being discovered working as the band's chauffeur; he recorded seven albums cementing AC/DC's reputation but died of acute alcohol poisoning on February 19, 1980. Brian Johnson replaced him in time for *Back in Black* (1980), which has sold at least 50 million copies. When Johnson developed serious ear problems in 2016, Axl Rose of Guns N' Roses sang for approximately two dozen shows; Johnson subsequently received treatment and rejoined. "The swap worked way better than anyone could have dreamed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0F22NTnQzQ
