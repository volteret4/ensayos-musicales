## songs
**Texarkana Baby (1949)** : Released in March 1949, "Texarkana Baby" by Eddie Arnold was among the very first 45 RPM singles ever issued. This 7-inch single format was RCA Records' direct response to Columbia Records' introduction of the LP. "And amongst the first 45's ever released was this from Eddie Arnold called Texarkana Baby." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
**Texarkana Baby (1949) – First Official 7-inch Single** : This country and western song, released by RCA on March 31, 1949, holds the distinction of being the very first official 7-inch single. Notably, its initial pressing was on green vinyl, following RCA's brief attempt at color-coding records by genre. "The first official release was this, Texar Cana Baby by a country in Western singer named Eddie Arnold." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VlIErdSSVOM
