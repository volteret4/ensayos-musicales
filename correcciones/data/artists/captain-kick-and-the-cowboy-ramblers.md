## curiosities
**Large Bluegrass Ensemble** : Winston Marshall's Bluegrass group featured up to 16 members. They performed covers of artists ranging from The Grateful Dead, Neil Young, and Dexys Midnight Runners to Shania Twain, and played at a small 40-person bar in London called The Bosens Locker. "His sort of Bluegrass group was called Captain Kick and the Cowboy Ramblers, and it had up to 16 members." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P1RyOchn7pk
