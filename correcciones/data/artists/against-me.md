## curiosities
**Diverse Approach to Punk Revival** : Against Me!, featuring Laura Jane Grace, was cited as an example of the wide variety of approaches within the mid-decade punk revival of the 2010s. This revival embraced Garage Punk and Post Punk styles, influenced by previous punk eras while not being pure 70s, 80s hardcore, or 90s punk. "There was a wide variety of approaches from Laura Jane Grace and against me to the riot girl sensibilities of the reformed Slater Kinney..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HSvgRyGiOpE
**Source of Rage** : Against Me! is noted for being a "good source of rage" in their musical expression, suggesting their songs frequently embody strong anger and rebellious sentiment. "So is against me." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0ARYt4sYT90
