## curiosities
**Band Name Evolution** : The group initially performed as The Farriss Brothers. When the Farriss family moved to Perth, the other band members stayed together under the name The Vegetables. By 1979, everyone reunited in Sydney and began performing as INXS, adopting a name aligned with the trend of "license plate names" popular at the time. "This became the Ferris brothers, and when the Ferris family moved to Perth, the other guys of the band stuck together under the name, the vegetables. But then everybody was back together in Sydney by 1979, where they started performing under the name in excess." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko

**Death (November 22–23, 1997) – Ritz Carlton Sydney, Belt, No Suicide Note; Coroner: Consistent with Auto-Erotic Asphyxiation** : The night before Michael Hutchence's death began normally: a dinner with his father and stepmother at a restaurant called the Flapper of India, where he seemed fine. He returned to room 524 at the Ritz Carlton Double Bay, Sydney. Ex-girlfriend Kim Wilson (inspiration for "Suicide Blonde") came to the hotel with her boyfriend; they drank daiquiris, champagne, and beer in the bar before going to Hutchence's room. They left around 4am. Over the following hours, Hutchence made multiple calls but reached only answering machines. Early that morning he went downstairs looking disheveled to mail some letters — the last time anyone saw him alive. A maid found his body around noon on Saturday, November 23. He appeared to have gone into a violent rage — prescription pills scattered across the room, a broken hand from hitting the wall, facial cuts, furniture thrown — before fastening a leather belt to the bathroom door and hanging himself. There was no suicide note. The official coroner's verdict was death consistent with auto-erotic asphyxiation, a practice that kills an estimated 500 to 1,000 people per year worldwide. Hutchence had acknowledged in a late interview trying to achieve "the ultimate sexual high," and his mother confirmed he had spent time with sadomasochists in the preceding months. "The coroner says is consistent with auto-erotic asphyxiation, a death by misadventure." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GIUTQhcmR5s

**John Hughes Film Exposure (1980s)** : INXS's music was heavily mined for the soundtracks of John Hughes' 1980s films. These carefully curated soundtracks were instrumental in exposing numerous North American audiences to various alternative rock acts, especially those from the UK. "So many people learned of bands like The Specials and Spandovalet and Billy Idle and General Public and Wang Chung and Yellow and New Order and the Psychedelic Furs and the Smiths and Echo and the Bunyman, Susanne Vagan in excess the Jesus and Mary chain, Flesh for Luluo and so many others from those soundtrack albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
**Last Public Sightings and Communications (November 1997)** : On the night of Friday, November 21, 1997, Michael Hutchence had an Indian dinner with his father and stepmother, appearing to have a good time despite his father's unease. He returned to Room 524 at the Ritz Carlton Hotel in Sydney around 10:30 PM, joking with girls in the elevator. He then went on a "last bender," making calls to former girlfriends Kim Wilson (who visited with her current boyfriend until 4 AM) and Michelle Bennett (who rushed to the hotel after a worried call but found no answer). He also had two calls with Bob Geldof around 5 AM about a court injunction, including a loud 20-minute argument, and a desperate-sounding call with Paula Yates at 5:38 AM. His last sighting was asking a front desk clerk to mail two letters—one to Paula and one to a friend in Hong Kong. "Sometime after that, Michael went down to the front desk and asked the clerk to mail two letters, one to Paula and the other to a friend in Hong Kong. That was the last time anyone saw him alive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8

Despite several attempts to continue with different singers, the public remained indifferent, leading to the band officially calling it quits. "and of course the apparent accidental suicide of Michael Hutchins in that Sydney hotel room in November of 1997. **Relationship Turmoil and Self-Medication (November 1997)** : Michael Hutchence was experiencing intense personal turmoil due to his relationship with UK TV presenter Paula Yates, the ex-wife of Bob Geldof, especially concerning a dispute over children. He was described as not being in a good place and was self-medicating with drugs, including heroin, which he and Paula were both using. "The turmoil with Paula and Bob was peaking over children. She was expecting. He was self-medicating." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8
**Suicide (November 1997)** : Between 9:55 AM and 10:30 AM on Saturday, November 22, 1997, Michael Hutchence, depressed, drunk, and sleep-deprived, entered a violent rage, throwing furniture, punching a wall, breaking his hand, and scattering five different prescription pills. He then hanged himself with a leather snakeskin belt from a doorknob in his hotel room. A housekeeper discovered his body at 11:50 AM. No suicide note was found. "depressed, drunk, and sleep deprived, Michael had gone into a violent rage, throwing furniture around, breaking his hand, punching a wall, and scattering five different prescription pills all over the place. Sometime between 955 AM and 1030 AM, he hanged himself with a leather snake skin belt from a doorknob." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8
