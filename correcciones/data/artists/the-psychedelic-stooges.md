## curiosities
**Extreme Contrast to Mainstream and Hippie Bands** : The Stooges, particularly through Iggy Pop's antics, presented a "crazy" and extreme alternative that starkly contrasted with mainstream acts like The Beatles and The Rolling Stones, and was also distinct from the prevailing hippie groups. "This wasn't anything mainstream bands like the beetles or the stones we're doing. And the hippie groups, certainly weren't into this kind of thing either." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
**First Proper Public Performance (March 1968)** : The band's first official public performance took place in March 1968. The opening act for this show was unusual: a food processor (blender with water) roaring through the PA system for about 15 minutes. "The first proper public performance came in March of 1968. The opening act for this show was a food processor." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
**First Show (Halloween, Year Not Specified)** : Iggy Pop formed The Psychedelic Stooges in the summer of 1967, and their first show was a private party held on Halloween. The performance was reportedly so poor that their manager quit and returned to his job as a school teacher. "they played their first show at a private party that Halloween. It wasn't very good, so bad that their manager quit and went back to his job as a school teacher." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
**Iggy Pop's Unusual Stage Attire** : During their first public performance, Iggy Pop appeared on stage wearing a nightshirt, golf shoes, white mime makeup, and a large afro wig made from tinfoil. "Iggy was wearing a night shirt and golf shoes and white mime makeup and a big afro wig made out of tinfoil." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
