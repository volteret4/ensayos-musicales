# artist - Tim Curry

## curiosities
**Johnny LaGuardia Role in Times Square (1980)** : Tim Curry returned to the screen in the 1980 movie *Time Square*, playing a hip radio DJ named Johnny LaGuardia. This role further cemented his connection with alternative culture, as the film explored the nascent punk rock and new wave era in New York City. **Rocky Horror Picture Show (1975) Performance** : Tim Curry gained significant recognition in the alternative music world for his role in *The Rocky Horror Picture Show*. His portrayal of a heavily made-up, transvestite homosexual mad scientist was considered "way too weird" for most people in the 1970s, contributing to his status as a darling in the alternative scene a few years after the film's initial release.
