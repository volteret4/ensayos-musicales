## curiosities
**Brother and Sister Myth** : A persistent myth claimed that Jack and Meg White were brother and sister, which is entirely false. Jack Gillis (his birth name) was the tenth of ten children from a Catholic family in Detroit, with no sister named Meg, while Meg White was from Gross Point Farms with one sister, Heather, and no brothers. This false narrative was deliberately cultivated by Jack White in early interviews, where he referred to Meg as his "little sister," even after their marriage certificate was posted online in 2001. "When you see a band that's two pieces, husband and wife, boyfriend and girlfriend, you think, oh, I see. When their brother and sister, you go, oh, that's interesting. You care more about the music, not the relationship, whether they're trying to save their relationship by being in a band together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ
