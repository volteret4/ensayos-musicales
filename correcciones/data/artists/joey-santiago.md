## curiosities
**Surf Influence on Guitar Writing** : Joey Santiago, guitarist for The Pixies, was fascinated by how surf compositions could convey feeling and imagery without lyrics. This fascination stuck with him, and now when writing melodic guitar parts, his brain automatically gravitates towards 60s surf music. "Now when he's writing for guitar, he says that when it comes to all the melodic parts, his brain will go right to 60s surf." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
