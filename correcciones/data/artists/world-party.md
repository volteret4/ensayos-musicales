## albums
**Private Revolution (1986) - Influenced Debut** : This debut album, released in 1986, served as Carl Wallinger's primary vehicle for songwriting. It featured alt-rock songs that showcased influences from a wide range of artists, including The Beatles, Bob Dylan, The Beach Boys, The Rolling Stones, Prince, and Van Morrison.
