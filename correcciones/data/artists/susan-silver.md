# artist - Susan Silver

## curiosities
**Atmosphere Artist Management (2005-2011) – Post-Divorce Venture** : After her divorce from Chris Cornell, Susan Silver established a new company called Atmosphere Artist Management in 2005. This venture, however, was later shut down in 2011 for unspecified reasons. **Co-Manager of Alice in Chains – Partnership with Kelly Curtis** : Susan Silver co-managed Alice in Chains with Kelly Curtis through their shared management company. When Mother Love Bone collapsed and transformed into Pearl Jam, Kelly Curtis shifted his focus to become Pearl Jam's full-time manager, leaving Susan to manage Alice in Chains independently. "When Mother Lovebone started to get hot, Kelly wanted to spend more time with them. **Divorce from Chris Cornell (2004) – Followed by Lawsuit** : Susan Silver and Chris Cornell divorced in 2004, after a relationship that spanned the rise of Soundgarden and Alice in Chains, Soundgarden's breakup, Cornell's initial solo career, and his struggles with addiction, as well as the death of Alice in Chains singer Lane Staley. Their divorce was followed by a contentious lawsuit initiated by Cornell. "She and Chris divorced in 2004." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**First Management Client (1981) – The U-Men** : Susan Silver began her career in artist management by managing The U-Men, a Seattle group formed in 1981 that predated the widespread Grunge movement by a couple of years. She also managed Screaming Trees before taking on Soundgarden. "Silver got her start managing the U-Men, a Seattle group that predated the whole Grunge thing by a couple of years in 1981." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**Lawsuit by Chris Cornell – Alleged Royalties Diversion and Withholding of Items** : Following their 2004 divorce, Chris Cornell sued Susan Silver for a million dollars, accusing her of diverting Soundgarden royalties to the other band members and withholding valuable items such as a guitar, Grammy Awards, demo tapes, personal recordings, diaries, and journals. The suit used strong terms like "fraud, oppression, and malice" in its documentation, and it was eventually settled. "Chris sued Susan for a million dollars, claiming that she diverted Soundgarden royalties away from him and to the other three band members, words like fraud, oppression, and malice reused in the documents." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**Marriage to Chris Cornell (September 1990) – Manager and Wife** : Susan Silver, who was already managing Soundgarden, began dating Chris Cornell and they married in September 1990. Their relationship lasted through significant periods in the rise of both Soundgarden and Alice in Chains, as well as Chris's early solo career and personal struggles. "She and Chris started dating, which probably had its awkward moments with the rest of the band, and then they got married in September of 1990." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
