## curiosities
**Boston Origin and Cabaret Sound** : This two-piece group from Boston has a unique sound and look, described as if Siouxsie and the Banshees were performing in East Berlin cabarets in 1932. "There are two piece groups out of Boston and they look and sound like what might have happened had Suzy and the Banshee's been playing the Cabaret's and East Berlin in 1932." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=evlGhfUe6QQ
**Goth Enough for Tours** : The Dresden Dolls' "kind of sort of Goth" style was sufficiently Goth-aligned to earn them an invitation to tour with Nine Inch Nails, demonstrating their acceptance within the broader alternative scene. "Goth enough to be invited on tour with Nine-Hitch Nails" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=evlGhfUe6QQ
