## curiosities
**Cats in Windows - Photo Book** : Max Knight is a photographer known for a photo book aptly titled *Cats in Windows*. **Sigh No More Album Cover Photographer** : Max Knight was the photographer for Mumford & Sons' *Sigh No More* album cover, which was shot in the summer of 2009. The complex eight-hour shoot involved challenges such as a busy street, sun reflections, and disturbances from a nearby pub. "The photographer's name was Max Knight, a guy who coincidentally has a photo book called Cats in Windows, which is just like it sounds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P1RyOchn7pk
