## curiosities
**First Live Performance with Electric Guitar and Amplifier** : In 1932, jazz player Jack Miller performed a gig at Grommand's Chinese Theatre in Los Angeles using a brand new Rickenbacker electro guitar, which was considered the first true electric guitar equipped with a pickup. He documented this pioneering experience in a series of articles for the jazz magazine Downbeat. This performance spurred other musicians to adopt amplified lead guitar within a year, allowing them to compete with the loud horns of Big Band Orchestras.
