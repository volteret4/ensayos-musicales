# artist - Herbie Flowers

## instruments
- Fretless electric bass
- Stand-up double bass

## curiosities
**Iconic Bassline and Payment** : Herbie Flowers was the session player responsible for creating the famous sliding bass line on Lou Reed's "Walk on the Wild Side." He layered two distinct bass lines—one from a stand-up double bass and another from a fretless electric bass—to achieve the distinctive sound. For his work, Flowers was reportedly paid $30. This iconic bass line has since been sampled countless times in hip-hop and dance songs globally. "For his trouble. Herbie was paid 30 bucks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4
