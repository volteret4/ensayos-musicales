# artist - The Surley Young Bucks

## members
- Dan Griffith (had some songs)

## genres
- Country

## songs
**Costin (2007)** : This song was performed at The Surley Young Bucks reunion show in 2007, a year after Arkells officially started. Bits of Arkells' future sound could be heard in this track. "You can certainly hear bits of our Kells and songs like this. It's called Costin." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA

## curiosities
**2007 Reunion Show** : Despite Arkells having already started, The Surley Young Bucks held a reunion show in 2007, where the song "Costin" was performed. "Welcome everyone to the Surley Young Box reunion show. Yeah. Thank you for joining us. This is a special evening. It's a special evening. Thanks for remembering." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Pre-Arkells Project** : This was a band that some future Arkells members, including Dan Griffith, were involved with before Arkells became a "going concern." It was described as a "half band cover band" (referencing The Band) with a country-styled musical approach. "Surley Young Box, a pre-Archelles band, recorded when they had a reunion show in 2007, but a year after the Archelles started as a going concern." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
