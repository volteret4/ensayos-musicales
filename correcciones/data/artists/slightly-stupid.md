# artist - Slightly Stupid

## albums
**Dabbington** : This track was part of an experimental pressing made from cannabis resin. After the resin record proved impractical, the song was subsequently released on regular vinyl as an individual track. "And the track is called Dabbington." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B2g4QSREzpw

## curiosities
**Cannabis Resin Record Experiment** : Slightly Stupid experimented with pressing a record using cannabis resin, with the conceptual idea of listening to it and then melting it down to smoke. This experiment was highly impractical, costing $6,000 worth of resin for a single record and yielding very poor sound quality on a turntable. Only two copies made of bubble hash resin were ever produced, ultimately leading the band to smoke the resin and release the track "Dabbington" on regular vinyl. "a record that was pressed out of bubble hash resin, only two copies were ever made, but it wasn't an interesting experiment that was supposedly designed to help the planet in some sort of way." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B2g4QSREzpw
