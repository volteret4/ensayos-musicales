# artist - YouTube

## albums
**How to Dismantle an Atomic Farm (2009) - First iTunes LP** : This album was identified as the very first iTunes LP, making it the debut digital album to be released with interactive extras. The iTunes LP format, codenamed Cocktail, was introduced in the fall of 2009 by Apple's iTunes Store, aiming to integrate interactive album artwork and other supplementary materials. "The first ever iTunes LP, the first digital album filled with these extras was YouTube's How to Dismantle an Atomic Farm." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KaIp6KVn-co
