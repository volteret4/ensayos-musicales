# artist - Jorge Garcia

## curiosities
**Hurley Album Cover Model** : Jorge Garcia, who played the character Hurley in the TV series "Lost," is featured on the cover of Weezer's "Hurley" album. The image is a cropped picture of Rivers Cuomo hugging Garcia in the green room of the late-night talk show "Lopez Tonight," where both were guests.
