# artist - The Sham Rocks

## curiosities
**Pseudonym for American Clover** : The Sham Rocks was a pseudonym adopted by the American band Clover when they were working in the UK. This was done to avoid legal trouble, as there was already an existing British band named Clover. Under this name, they famously backed Elvis Costello on his debut album, My Aim Is True, although they remained uncredited. "This is because there was already a British band called Clover, so the American Clover tried to use the name, the Sham Rocks, to avoid getting in trouble." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E
