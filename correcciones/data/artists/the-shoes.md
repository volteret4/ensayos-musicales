## curiosities
**New Wave Powerpop** : The Shoes are mentioned as one of the bands that defined the new wave brand of Powerpop. Their sound reflects the post-punk era, taking the energetic, melodic, and hook-driven characteristics of Powerpop and blending them with new wave sensibilities, showcasing punk's influence.
