# artist - Massive Attack

## members
- Adrian "Tricky" Thaws
- Grant "Daddy G" Marshall
- Horace Andy

## genres
- Trip Hop
- Trip-Hop

## albums
**Mezzanine (1998)** : This 1998 album is highlighted as a personal favorite among Massive Attack's "long list of excellent records" and features the song "Angel." "My personal favorite is Mezzanine from 1998, which features this song called Angel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1NNvxgx8uA
**Mezzanine – DNA Encoded Audio** : Massive Attack released a special edition of their *Mezzanine* album, which came in a can of spray paint. This unique format contained audio that was encoded in DNA. "It came in a can of spray paint containing audio that was encoded in DNA." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
**Misanine (1998)** : This 1998 album featured the song "Teardrop." "Massive Attack and Teardrop from their 1998 album Misanine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bQz8R0gbXc

## songs
**Angel (1998)** : Featured on the 1998 album "Mezzanine," this song was primarily written by Daddy G and includes guest vocals from Jamaican roots singer Horace Andy. "My personal favorite is Mezzanine from 1998, which features this song called Angel. Daddy G was the main songwriter, and the guest vocalist is Horus Andi, a Jamaican root singer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1NNvxgx8uA
**Teardrop (1998)** : From their 1998 album "Misanine," this song gained significant mainstream recognition by being used as the theme song for the TV show "House." "Massive Attack and Teardrop from their 1998 album Misanine. And yes, that was used as the theme song for the TV show House." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bQz8R0gbXc

## curiosities
**Acknowledged Influence of Public Image Limited** : Massive Attack is listed among the bands that acknowledged the importance and influence of Public Image Limited. "Nine-inch nails, massive attack, manic street preachers, ministry, primal scream, they all acknowledged the importance of public image limited." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Founding Members' Heritage** : Two of the three main members of Massive Attack are Black. Adrian "Tricky" Thaws has a Jamaican father and an English and Guyanese mother, while both of Grant "Daddy G" Marshall's parents are from the West Indies. "Adrian Tricky Thaws and Grant Daddy G. Marshall. Tricky's father was Jamaican while his mother was both English and Guyanese, and both of Daddy G's parents are from the West Indies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1NNvxgx8uA
**Greatest Trip Hop Act** : Massive Attack was cited as "probably the greatest of them all" among the Trip Hop acts. "and probably the greatest of them all. Massive Attack." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bQz8R0gbXc
**Legendary Albums at Olympic** : Massive Attack, among numerous other artists, recorded legendary albums at Olympic Studios. "Oasis, U2, the Arctic Monkeys, the Killers, the Pesh Mode, Moracy, Pink Floyd, Massive Attack, the Spice Girls, Adele and dozens and dozens of others made legendary albums at Olympic." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
**Pioneers of Trip-Hop** : Massive Attack is recognized as one of the all-time best trip-hop bands and has been actively making music since 1988, producing a substantial catalog of excellent records. "As Tripop bands go, Massive Attack is one of the all-time best. They have been a going concern since 1988 and have a long list of excellent records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1NNvxgx8uA
