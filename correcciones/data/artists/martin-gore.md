## curiosities
**Chief Songwriter for Depeche Mode** : Martin Gore has been the primary songwriter for virtually all of Depeche Mode's material, consistently credited on albums from A Broken Frame in 1982 to the present day. "The chief songwriter for 99% of Depeche Mode's existence has been Martin Gore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Devotional Tour Behavior and Arrest (1993)** : During the Devotional Tour, Martin Gore reportedly drank vast amounts of beer and often stripped naked. He relied on very strong sleeping pills to get any sleep. On November 3, 1993, he was arrested in Denver for playing loud music in his hotel room at 4 AM and spent the night in jail. "Martin Gore would allegedly drink vast amounts of beer and then strip naked." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
**Panic Attacks and Seizures (1993)** : Martin Gore experienced severe panic attacks, leading him to believe he was dying, and he really should have been taking it easy. In November 1993, ahead of five nights at the Great Western Forum in Los Angeles, he suffered a grand mal seizure during a business meeting at the Sunset Marquis Hotel. This was his second such attack, attributed to stress, alcohol, and drugs, in addition to numerous minor panic attacks. "Then came a grand mall seizure in the middle of a business meeting at the Sunset Marquis Hotel in Los Angeles in November 1993, a head of five nights at the Great Western Forum." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
**Sobriety and Continued Music Career (Late 1990s-Present)** : Martin Gore achieved sobriety midway through the recording of the "Ultra" album, which eased the rest of the recording process, particularly the vocal tracks. He continues to record and tour with Dave Gahan, maintaining a sober lifestyle. "Halfway through the recording of this record, Dave got clean and sober." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
**Songwriting Dominance and Personal Challenges (Early 1990s)** : Martin Gore became the sole songwriter for Depeche Mode, which created tension with Alan Wilder. He claimed to be autistic and indulged heavily in partying and drinking, often withdrawing into video games like "Sonic the Hedgehog." Becoming a father for the first time in June 1991 added further stress. "He wanted to contribute more as a songwriter, but that had pretty much become the sole domain of Martin Gore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
