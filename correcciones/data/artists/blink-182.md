**Nine (2019) – Eighth Studio Album** : Released on September 20, 2019. Mark Hoppus counted it as their ninth studio album, including *Buddha* (1994). It features 15 songs, many built around rhythms by Travis Barker with hip-hop influences, loops, and electronics, a new sound for the band. Collaborators included Miley Cyrus and Feral Williams, with a goal for a cross-format top 40 hit, though this was hindered by the COVID-19 pandemic. The album's lyrics were darker, reflecting Mark Hoppus's depression, anger over mass shootings, and political views. It received excellent reviews and reached number three on the American top 200 and was a top 10 rock album in Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, and Austria. "Nine was released on September 20th, 2019." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaGUC4av1R4
**One More Time (2023) – Reunion Album** : This album was released on October 20, 2023, after recording sessions began in January 2022. Travis Barker self-produced the album in his home studio. It was profoundly influenced by Mark Hoppus's battle with cancer, the band's rekindled relationships, and a sense of maturity. The recording was kept secret to ensure the reconciliation with Tom DeLonge would work. It includes 17 tracks, plus 10 bonus tracks, and a "cancer song" titled "You Don't Know What You've Got." The album debuted at number one on the Billboard Top 200, aided by 11 different vinyl versions, and charted highly internationally. "One more time is an interesting album for Blink 182 because it was obviously affected by Mark's cancer, relationships, friendships, and becoming a little more mature." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaGUC4av1R4
