**Early Retirement from Touring (1979)** : Kate Bush famously retired from extensive touring in 1979 at the age of 20, after releasing only two albums. Her decision was influenced by the exhausting nature of touring and, significantly, by the tragic death of her lighting director in a fall during a warm-up show for "The Tour of Life." "First, she found the whole ordeal exhausting. Second, during a warm-up show for that road trip, which was called The Tour of Life, her lighting director was killed in a fall. And that really soured things." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak

**Historic London Residency (2014)** : After decades of minimal live appearances, Kate Bush announced a surprise 22-show residency at the Apollo Hammersmith in London in March 2014. Titled "Before the Dawn," the concerts, running from August 26th to October 1st, sold out within minutes and featured elaborate multimedia events with costumes, magicians, dancers, puppets, and 3D animation. "That is until March 2014 when she announced a 22-show residency at the Apollo Hammersmith in London. It began on August 26th and ended on October 1st." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
