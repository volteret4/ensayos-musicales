**For What It's Worth (1966) – Written About Sunset Strip Riot at Pandora's Box Club; Sampled 36+ Times** : "For What It's Worth" was written about a riot that broke out to protest the closing of a Sunset Strip club called Pandora's Box in 1966. It remains one of the most popular 1960s counterculture songs and has been sampled at least three dozen times by artists including House of Pain, Everlast, Chumbawamba, and Butthole Surfers. It has been covered by everyone from Cher to David Cassidy to Ozzy Osbourne to Rush to Public Enemy to Stevie Nicks.
