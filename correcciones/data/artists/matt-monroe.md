## songs
**From Russia with Love (1963) – Closing Credits Song** : Matt Monroe, an English singer often compared to Frank Sinatra, performed the song "From Russia with Love" that played over the closing credits of the 1963 James Bond film. While John Barry's instrumental was the title theme, Monroe's vocal performance provided a distinct musical element for the film's conclusion.
