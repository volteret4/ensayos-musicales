## curiosities
**Roskilde Cancellation (2000)** : The Wases (The Verve) cancelled their set at the Roskilde Festival on June 30, 2000, due to concerns that the organizers did not have adequate safety measures or their "act together" amidst heavy rain and muddy conditions. "Both the Wases and the Pet Shop boys had canceled their sets because they didn't think organizers had their act together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7v1REnqKuXE
