# artist - Jake Holmes

## songs
**Dazed and Confused (1967)** : Jake Holmes, a folk singer and later a writer of commercial jingles, originally wrote this song. Jimmy Page heard Holmes perform it in 1968 when Page was still with The Yardbirds. Later, Page reworked the song for Led Zeppelin, leading to a successful claim by Holmes that Led Zeppelin's version was plagiarized. "But before Jake Holmes got into the advertising game in jingles, he was a folk singer and wrote Dazed and Confused in 1967." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w

## curiosities
**Commercial Jingles Writer** : Before his folk singing career and "Dazed and Confused," Jake Holmes became a writer of commercial jingles, notably for the US Army's "Be All You Can Be" campaign and Dr. Pepper. "He's not a blues singer, but a writer of commercial jingles. Remember the US Army's Be All You Can Be? We do more before 9am than most people do all day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
