# artist - The Innocent

## members
- Trent Reznor (keyboards)

## albums
**Living in the Street (1985) – Debut Album** : Released in 1985, this album featured a 19-year-old Trent Reznor on keyboards, though he did not contribute vocals or songwriting. The album later became a source of embarrassment for Trent after he achieved fame. Legend suggests he attempted to buy up and destroy all remaining copies, sending his sister to acquire them. "Nevertheless, this band remains something of an embarrassment for Trent." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M3nUQtja0qc

## curiosities
**Bond Jovi-ish Band (1985)** : Trent Reznor played keyboards in this "Bond Jovi-ish" band in 1985, which lasted only three months. "After that was a stint playing keyboards in a bond jovish band called The Innocent, which lasted all three months." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DKdF8NgF5Bw
