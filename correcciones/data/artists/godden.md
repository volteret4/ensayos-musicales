# artist - Godden

## curiosities
**Early Screamo Influence** : Godden was mentioned as one of the influential bands in the "deep head early first-screamo stuff" that members of Alexis on Fire listened to. This niche genre was part of a strong underground scene. "I was kind of more the deep head early first-screamo stuff like where I was listening to bands like Sasha, Love Lost, Penoffa, Godden, these are all different." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
