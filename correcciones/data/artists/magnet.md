## albums
**The Simple Life (2007)** : Magnet's 2007 album, "The Simple Life," was debuted by Evan Johnson through a concert performed on an airplane. The album achieved commercial success, hitting number one on the Norwegian record charts in its first week of release. "He debuted his album The Simple Life by performing it on an airplane flying from Oslo to Reykjavik in Iceland at a flying altitude of 40,000 feet. Apparently this doesn't work because the album hit number one on the Norwegian record. And it's first week in the stores." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T1XSJIm0Nh4
