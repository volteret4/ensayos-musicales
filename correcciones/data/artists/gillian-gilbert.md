## curiosities
**"Blue Monday" Composition & Accidental Rest** : As keyboardist for New Order, Gillian Gilbert meticulously wrote out the entire sequence for "Blue Monday" by hand on paper. She accidentally included an extra rest in her notation, causing her program to be slightly out of time, but this "mistake" was intentionally kept for the extra tension it added to the arrangement. "Meanwhile, Jillian Gilbert, the keyboardist, wrote out the entire sequence by hand on paper. **Synthesizer Automation** : Gillian Gilbert's synthesizers became an integral part of New Order's evolving electronic setup. A sequencer was used to automate keyboard parts and trigger sounds on her synthesizers, contributing to the band's distinctive new sound.
