# artist - John William Polidori

## curiosities
**The Vampire (1816 Novella)** : Penned during the Villa Diodati gathering in the "year without a summer," this story about undead bloodsuckers was written 80 years before Bram Stoker's "Dracula," establishing an early foundation for gothic horror.
