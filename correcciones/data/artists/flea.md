## curiosities
**Chess Enthusiast** : Flea, the bassist for the Red Hot Chili Peppers, is an avid chess player. When the band is on tour, he frequently challenges individuals to games, including grandmasters who are invited backstage specifically to play him. "When the chili peppers are on the road, he'll often challenge people to games, including grandmasters who are invited backstage for the purpose of playing flea." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CgDCaUxbQDU
**Creative State Before Reunion** : Anthony Kiedis observed that in the band's previous incarnation (before John's return), Flea was not "feeling his best self" and was not "on fire" musically. This perceived lack of enthusiasm from Flea was a factor that led Anthony to start contemplating John's potential involvement. "I knew that Flea was in a mood in a disposition where he wasn't feeling his, his, his best self in the previous incarnation. For whatever reason, it just like he wasn't, he wasn't on fire." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Early Tattoo Adopter** : Flea was an early adopter of tattoos, frequently visiting specific shops in Los Angeles and having a "regular guy" named Bob Roberts for his ink. Bob Roberts was a "very, very famous guy" and a hard artist to get an appointment with. "I remember many years ago talking to flea about tattoos and he was an early adopter and he had all these places in LA that he would go and he had his regular guy. And he would, you know, whenever he needed something cut stopper and wanted something new, you know, he would go to this guy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
**Immediate Offer to John Frusciante** : Within seconds of John Frusciante suggesting a jam, Flea quickly moved beyond that idea and directly asked John if he wanted to rejoin the Red Hot Chili Peppers. This direct approach fast-tracked the reunion discussion from a casual musical get-together to a full commitment. "It was within seconds after I suggested that Flea was like, you ever think about being in the Red Hat Chili Peppers again?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Inspired Creative Contribution** : In the band's collaborative songwriting process, Flea was often inspired by John Frusciante's musical contributions. When John would bring a "gem" (ideas, chord progressions, arrangements) to practice, Flea would respond by bringing his own "gem" to the table, fostering a natural back-and-forth creative flow. "It's like every time John would bring a gem into practice, you know, flee would feel like, okay, I'll bring a gem." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Messy Divorce During Band Turmoil (1992)** : During John Frusciante's first departure from the Red Hot Chili Peppers in 1992, Flea was also experiencing significant personal stress, going through a "messy divorce." This personal struggle was part of the broader challenges the remaining band members faced. "flea was going through a messy divorce." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Musical Evolution with Frusciante** : Flea's bass playing style evolved significantly under John Frusciante's influence, becoming "more melodic" and moving away from his previous emphasis on "finger picking or popping and slapping." This shift paralleled Anthony Kiedis's more melodic singing approach. "Flea's bass playing evolved too. He wasn't finger picking or popping and slapping as much. He was becoming more melodic too, and that's John's influence." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Observations on John Frusciante's Musicality** : Flea described John Frusciante as a "very musical guy" who diligently studied synthesizers and background harmonies from 1950s doo-wop and 1960s pop groups. He emphasized Frusciante's innate drive to create music. "I mean, he's a very musical guy. He works really hard on study of synthesizers and the study of background harmonies, you know, from like pop groups from the 60s and do lap groups from the 50s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Pivotal Role in Frusciante Joining RHCP (1988)** : Flea played a key role in John Frusciante joining the Red Hot Chili Peppers. After a jam session with Frusciante at Flea's apartment, Flea was so impressed that he immediately called Anthony Kiedis, stating, "this kid has got to be in our band." "But the chemistry with Flea was good. They held a jam session just the two of them at Flea's apartment, and once that was over, Flea called Anthony and said, this kid has got to be in our band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Pre-Anthony Jam with John** : Unbeknownst to Anthony Kiedis, Flea had already jammed with John Frusciante before both Flea and Anthony simultaneously brought up the idea of John's return. This pre-existing musical interaction gave Flea a basis for his conviction in John rejoining. "And he's like, yeah, I've actually jammed with him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Prioritized Commitment Over Initial Jam** : Flea focused on John Frusciante making a firm decision to *join* the band rather than first testing their chemistry with a casual jam session. The first time the reunited lineup played together was in rehearsal about a month after John agreed to rejoin. "Flea went right past that. He was just like, he just, he wanted to concentrate on the topic of whether or not I was going to actually join." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Reaching Out to Frusciante for Reunion (1998)** : Flea was instrumental in convincing John Frusciante to rejoin the Red Hot Chili Peppers in 1998. He visited Frusciante at his house and "broached the subject" of him coming back, expressing his personal desire for the reunion. His garage was subsequently set up as a rehearsal space for the reunited band. "And Flea went by the house for a visit and broached the subject above John rejoining the band. Nothing would make me happier, he said." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Recipient of Jam Suggestion** : John Frusciante mentioned to Flea that Chad Smith had expressed missing playing with them and suggested a jam session. This conversation took place while John and Flea were in John's kitchen. "I didn't really talk about it until, Flea and I were standing in my kitchen and Chad had said something to me when I saw him at Flea's wedding." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Simultaneous Reunion Discussion with Anthony** : Flea and Anthony Kiedis remarkably approached each other at the same time to discuss John Frusciante's return, both having independently been thinking about it. Flea had a "more important" thing to tell Anthony, which turned out to be the same subject. "Basically, we were both saying the exact same thing at the exact same time, which was what do you think about John?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
