**Musical Family Background** : Thundercat, whose real name is Stephen Lee Bruner, hails from a highly musical family. His father was a professional drummer who played for iconic Motown acts such as The Supremes, Gladys Knight, and The Temptations, while his mother was a professional musician who played the flute and percussion. "Thundercat comes from a very musical family. His dad played drums for the Supremes and Gladys Knight and the Temptations and other Motown acts.
