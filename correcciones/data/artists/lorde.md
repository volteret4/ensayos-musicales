## curiosities

This theory was fueled by alleged old interviews where she supposedly preferred the name "Ella" and stated her age as 45. The TV show *South Park* even incorporated this theory into a story arc, portraying Lorde as a 45-year-old geologist named Randy Marsh. "The people who believe in this theory point to some old interviews allegedly given by Lord, where she says she prefers to go by the name of Ella and that she's actually 45 years old." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg
