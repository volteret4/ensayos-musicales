# artist - Wings

## songs
**Live and Let Die (1973) – Worldwide Hit** : Paul McCartney and Wings performed "Live and Let Die" for the 1973 James Bond film. This track marked a significant shift for the Bond franchise by introducing a rock song as a title theme, becoming a major worldwide hit and is considered by some to be the best Bond theme ever. The song's success was global and set a new standard for future Bond themes. "This became a major hit worldwide and if you ask me this is still the best Bond theme ever." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo

## curiosities
**Album Art Designed by Storm Thorgerson** : Wings' album covers were among the many iconic designs created by the renowned Storm Thorgerson and his partner Aubrey Powell. Their joint efforts contributed to the visual representation of this post-Beatles project.
