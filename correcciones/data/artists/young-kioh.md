# artist - Young Kioh

## curiosities
**Producer of "Old Town Road" Beat** : Young Kioh, a Dutch producer, created the beat for Lil Nas X's "Old Town Road" based on Nine Inch Nails' "34 Ghosts 4." Lil Nas X purchased this beat online for $30. Young Kioh was unaware of the beat's use until the song became a massive hit. Through a quiet negotiation, he received songwriting credits, a share of the profits, and shared in the Grammy wins for the song. "He found a Dutch kid by the name of Young Kioh, who had created a beat based on a track called 34 Ghosts 4." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
