## curiosities
**Early Life, Education, and Career Path** : Grew up in London, Ontario, and attended McMaster University. He initially planned to become a professor, studying Political Science and Philosophy. However, as the band started playing more, it became increasingly difficult for him to consider leaving music for an academic career, ultimately leading him to a life in music by accident. "I went to school with kind of designs on being a prof. And so I wanted to kind of stay in school. But then I kind of met these guys and everything kind of just happened by accident for me." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Meeting Max Kermann** : Nick Dica met Max Kermann on the second day of university, approaching him because Max was wearing a Sam Roberts Band T-shirt. After confirming his interest in music and ability to play bass, Nick was immediately invited to join the band. "And then the next day Nick introduced himself to me because I was wearing a Sam Roberts band T-shirt. And Nick said, I like Sam Roberts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Most Serious Student** : Nick was considered the most serious student among the band members during their time at McMaster, often having notes that Max would borrow. Max would frequently meet Nick at an ice cream shop to borrow school-related materials, knowing it would put Nick in a good mood. "Nick was the most serious. We still have notes for sure. When Max, I remember when Max needed to borrow stuff, school related, like notes or something, he always asked to meet me at the ice cream shop because he knew that would put me in a good mood." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
