## curiosities
**Departure from Rage Against The Machine** : De la Rocha left Rage Against The Machine due to a "creative dispute, maybe a political dispute," as he wished to pursue a musical direction that diverged from the other band members' vision. "he left the band over a creative dispute, maybe a political dispute. He wanted to take the band in one direction while the other guys didn't want to go there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Struggles with Solo Direction** : According to Trent Reznor, de la Rocha faced difficulty in deciding on a clear musical direction for his solo work, coupled with the pressure of making an "important statement" after leaving "one of the best bands of the 90s." "Over time I think it was a matter of Zach not knowing what direction to go. I know the feeling of fear he's in a difficult position of leaving one of the best bands of the 90s, and he wants to make an important statement when he makes it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Unreleased Album with Trent Reznor** : De la Rocha worked on a solo album with Trent Reznor of Nine Inch Nails, reportedly recording up to 20 songs. However, Reznor stated that "that album will never see the light of day" due to de la Rocha's indecision about the musical direction and his desire to make an important statement after leaving a prominent band. "I know for a fact that he worked on an album with Trent Resner of Nineteen Snails. There may have been up to 20 songs recorded for this CD, but as Trent told me directly, and I quote, that album will never see the light of day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
