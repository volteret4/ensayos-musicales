# artist - Jeremy Steig

## songs
**Howlin' For Judy (1970)** : This 1970 instrumental featured a flute sample that became the basis for the Beastie Boys' 1994 song "Sure Shot." The Beastie Boys' unauthorized use of this sample led to a legal case that was eventually settled, with Steig stating it felt like "he won the lottery."
