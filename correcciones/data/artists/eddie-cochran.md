## curiosities
**Influence on The Sex Pistols** : Eddie Cochran wrote songs about teenage boredom. These tracks were later covered by later generations of punk bands, specifically mentioning the Sex Pistols, indicating his lasting influence on rebellious youth culture. "Eddie Cochrane, who songs about teenage boredom were later covered by sedentions of the sex pistols." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
