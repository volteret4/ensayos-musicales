**Wave of Mutilation (UK Surf Mix) (1989) – Not a Remix but a Completely Different Recording; 800 Promo Copies** : The Pixies' standard version of "Wave of Mutilation" appears on Doolittle (1989) — loud, fast, screaming. The UK Surf Mix is a slower, more languid, almost tranquil completely different recording, mislabeled as a remix. It first appeared on a one-sided 12-inch promotional record of only 800 copies, then on the B-side of the "Here Comes Your Man" single, then on the soundtrack to the Christian Slater film Pump Up the Volume. For some fans, it is the superior version. A comprehensive collection of Pixies B-sides is available on The Complete B-Sides (released March 2001). "Depending on your view of the Pixies, you may consider this to be the superior version of Wave of Mutilation." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-7KiHetQxMQ
