# artist - Herb Madison

## curiosities
**Co-writer of a 1949 Song** : Herb Madison co-wrote a song in 1949 with Carl Sigmund. This song was later recorded by Guy Lombardo and Canadians in 1950, becoming a hit, and then covered by Bing Crosby, Doris Day, Prince Buster, and The Specials.
