# artist - Tony DeFries

## curiosities
**Managerial Support for Bowie's Image (Late 1970s)** : Tony DeFries became David Bowie's manager in the late 1970s and was highly supportive of Bowie's innovative artistic endeavors, including his experimental approach to rock music and his decision to wear a dress in public.
