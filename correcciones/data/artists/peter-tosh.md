## curiosities
**Inspiration for 2 Tone Mascot** : An old photograph of reggae star Peter Tosh served as the direct inspiration for the black and white rude boy image created by Jerry Dammers. This image later became known as Walt Japsco, the unofficial mascot of Dammers' 2 Tone record label and the broader Ska movement. "In fact, it was based on a photo, a very old picture of reggae star Peter Tosh." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mhQ36Y-c9fM
**Inspiration for The Specials' Logo** : Peter Tosh, a Jamaican musician and former member of Bob Marley's Wailers, was the real-life individual whose photograph inspired the stylized Jamaican rude boy character, Walt Japsco, featured in The Specials' band logo. "Walt was actually based on a photograph of Jamaican musician Peter Tosh, who was a former member of Bob Arley's Whalers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
