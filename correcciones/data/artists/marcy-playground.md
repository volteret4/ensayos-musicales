## curiosities

**Formation and Origin** : Marcy Playground originated in Minneapolis in 1994, several years before achieving widespread fame with their breakthrough hit. "They came out of Minneapolis in 1994." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d_uDHds2XGQ
**John Wasneyac's Life in Canada** : John Wasneyac, the singer and leader of Marcy Playground, has resided in Canada for an extended period, is married with four sons, and is a partner in a recording studio in Vancouver, which he reportedly acquired using the financial proceeds from the hit song "Sex and Candy." "The singer and leader, John Wasneyac, has been living in Canada for quite some time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d_uDHds2XGQ
