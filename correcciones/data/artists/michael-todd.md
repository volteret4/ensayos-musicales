## curiosities
**Arrest and Departure from Coheed and Cambria (July-August 2011)** : After the Walgreens robbery, Michael Todd was tracked down via the taxi company and arrested on charges of possession and armed robbery, held on $10,000 bail. The band's keyboard player filled in on bass for that night's show, and Todd officially left the band on August 4, 2011. "Todd left the band on August 4." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Legal Sentence and Cancer Diagnosis (Post-2011)** : Michael Todd was sentenced to one year of home confinement and three years of probation, which included mandatory drug counseling sessions. Life became further complicated when he was diagnosed with cancer in 2012, though he later recovered and has pursued solo projects. "He was sentenced to one year's home confinement and three years of probation in order to attend drug counseling sessions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**OxyContin Addiction and Armed Robbery (July 10, 2011)** : As the bassist for Coheed and Cambria, Michael Todd developed an addiction to OxyContin. On July 10, 2011, during a tour stop in Mansfield, Massachusetts, he took a taxi to a Walgreens in Adelboro, where he robbed the pharmacist of six bottles of OxyContin by threatening them with a bomb via a message on his phone. "He got hooked on OxyContin." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
