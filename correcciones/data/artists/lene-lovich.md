**Diverse Artistic Background** : Born in Troy, Lene Lovich pursued an education in art school in England, where she worked as a sculptor, actress, and cabaret performer. She also played the saxophone and contributed her screams for post-production on horror films, showcasing her eclectic talents before her music career took off. "She was born into Troy, but she ended up in art school in England working as a sculptor actress and a cabaret performer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=clPuV7ezrIc
