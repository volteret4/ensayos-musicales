## curiosities
**Last Tour and Final Gig (December 2015)** : The Wildabouts embarked on a tour at the end of 2015. On December 1, 2015, they played what would be Scott Weiland's final gig at Adelaide Hall in Toronto. Scott's third wife, Jamie, hoped he would get clean once this tour concluded. "On December 1st, 2015, he and The Wild Abuts pulled into Toronto...It would be his last gig." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
**Scott Weiland's Erratic Gigs (2015)** : YouTube videos of The Wildabouts' gigs in 2015 hinted at Scott Weiland's erratic behavior on stage, raising concerns about his sobriety. "YouTube videos of Wild About Gigs hinted something erratic." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
**Scott Weiland's Last Band** : The Wildabouts was the final band that Scott Weiland was a part of before his death on a tour bus in 2015. "And finally, there's Scott's last band, The Wild abouts, and his death on that tour bus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r6vVw66-gmI
**Tour Bus Death (December 3, 2015)** : Scott Weiland died on The Wildabouts' tour bus on December 3, 2015, in Bloomington, Minnesota. He was found unresponsive by the band's tour manager in the back bedroom of the bus. "On Thursday, December 3rd, 2015, The Wild Abuts Tour Bus was in the parking lot of a country in and sweet hotel in Bloomington, Minnesota." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
