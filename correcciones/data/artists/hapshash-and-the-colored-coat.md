# artist - Hapshash and the Colored Coat

## albums
**The Human Host and the Heavy Metal Kids (1967) – Early "Heavy Metal" Reference** : This obscure 1967 album contains the term "heavy metal" in its title, making it one of the earliest non-scientific uses of the phrase. However, the music on the record is described as more psychedelic, rather than fitting the later characteristics of the heavy metal genre. "Okay, band, album, heavy metal.
