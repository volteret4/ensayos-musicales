## curiosities
**Glamour in Pop Music Videos** : NSYNC's expensive music videos contributed to the return of glamour in pop music during the mid to late 1990s. Their visual style stood in contrast to the unpolished, authentic aesthetic favored by the alternative rock scene at the time.
