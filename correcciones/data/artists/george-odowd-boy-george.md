# artist - George O'Dowd (Boy George)

## curiosities
**Blitz Club Origin** : George O'Dowd, who later achieved international fame as Boy George, started his career working as a cloakroom attendant at London's Blitz Club. This club was a crucial gathering point for the fashion-forward Blitz kids in 1979 and 1980, serving as a cradle for the New Romantic movement.
