## songs
**Change (1989)** : Kino, with their singer Soi, released the song "Change" (translates from Russian) around 1989 during the last years of the Soviet period. This song quickly became a political rallying cry amidst the significant social and political changes occurring in 1989, 1990, and 1991. The band, like others, was allegedly part of a CIA operation to import subversive rock into the USSR, with claims that Soi was given songs written by Hollywood songwriters.
