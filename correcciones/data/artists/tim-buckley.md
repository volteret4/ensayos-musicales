## curiosities
**Critical Acclaim vs. Commercial Recognition** : Although critics highly praised his albums, Tim Buckley's talent was not widely recognized until after his death in 1975. At the time of his passing, he was deeply in debt, owning only the clothes he wore, a guitar, and an amplifier. "Critics loved his albums, but he wasn't recognized for the talent that he was until well after he died in 1975 of what appears to be a heroine overdose at age 28. He was very deep in debt, and at the time of his death, owned nothing more than the clothes on his back, a guitar, and an amplifier." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Early Life and Marriage** : Tim Buckley began his career as a folk singer in the 1960s. He met Mary Guibert in French class in high school in 1964 and married her in 1965. **Musical Evolution and Self-Destructive Behavior** : After his divorce, Tim Buckley dedicated himself full-time to music, exploring a wide range of genres including folk, psych, jazz, funk, and soul.
