# artist - Marcus Mumford and his Mary Men

## members
- Marcus Mumford
- Ted Dwane
- Winston Marshall

## curiosities
**Precursor to Mumford & Sons (2007)** : This group, consisting of Marcus, Ted, and Winston, performed at the bar Communion, where Ben Lovett would occasionally join them on stage. By October 2007, they began recording together, which ultimately led to their transformation into Mumford & Sons. "Meanwhile, Marcus, Ted and Winston had coalesced in a group called Marcus Mumford and his Mary Men." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P1RyOchn7pk
