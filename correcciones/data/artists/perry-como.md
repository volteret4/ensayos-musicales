## curiosities
**Transition from Big Band Singer to Solo Star (1942)** : Perry Como was one of several big band singers who leveraged a loophole in the 1942 musicians' strike. Since singers were not bound by the union's recording ban, they were able to pursue solo careers, which transformed them into independent stars and personalities.
