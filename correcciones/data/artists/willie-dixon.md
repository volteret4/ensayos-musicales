# artist - Willie Dixon

## songs
**Bring It On Home (Year Unspecified)** : Led Zeppelin's song "Bring It On Home" was ruled highly derivative of an earlier song by Willie Dixon. "The songs Bring It On Home and The Lemon Song were ruled as highly derivative of earlier songs by Willie Dixon and Halen Wolf." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
**You Need Love (1962)** : The lyrics of Led Zeppelin's 1969 hit "Whole Lotta Love" were deemed too close to this song. Willie Dixon was eventually granted a co-writing credit for "Whole Lotta Love" in 1985, 23 years after its initial release. "And the lyrics to Whole Lot of Love, Big Song from 1969, were way too close to a 1962 Willie Dixon song entitled You Need Love." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
