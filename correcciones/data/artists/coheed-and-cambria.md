## albums
**Concept Album Series** : Coheed and Cambria exclusively release concept albums, all of which are installments in a continuous storyline called "The Armory Wars." This saga is set in "Heaven's Fence," a Federation comprising 78 planets connected by a special energy source known as the Keywork. "Their records are all installments in the storyline of the Armory Wars which takes place in Heaven's Fence, a Federation of 78 Planets connected by the Keywork, a special sort of energy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc
**Year of the Black Rainbow (2010)** : This album, released in 2010, was offered in a Deluxe version. This special edition included a 352-page novel, which was written by the band's singer, Claudio Sanchez, further expanding the conceptual universe. "The band's 2010 album, Year of the Black Rainbow, also came in a Deluxe version which included a 352 page novel written by singer Claudio Sanchez." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc

## curiosities
**Band Expulsion and Sentencing** : Following his arrest, Michael Todd was immediately removed from Coheed and Cambria. At his trial, he received a sentence of one year of home confinement, three years of probation, and a requirement for extensive drug treatment. The leniency in his sentence was partly influenced by his recent diagnosis with testicular cancer. "Todd was immediately kicked out of the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6xygY-npCr4
**Comic Book and Graphic Novel Creator** : Claudio Sanchez, the lead singer and guitarist for Coheed and Cambria, ventured into the world of comic books in 2008 with two issues of "The Bag.OnLine Adventures." He later progressed to graphic novels, most notably the sci-fi series "The Amory Wars," which intricately details the story of the 78 planets of Heaven's Fence and the "key work," the energy beams that bind everything together. "He started in the world of comic books in 2008 with two issues of something called the bag online adventures." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
**Contemporary Concept Album Artists** : Coheed and Cambria is mentioned as one of the contemporary acts currently releasing concept albums. **Escape and Arrest** : After obtaining the drugs, Michael Todd fled the Walgreens, ran across a field behind the building, and jumped into a cab. His intention was to make that night's gig in nearby Mansfield, Massachusetts. However, security cameras at the pharmacy and witness identification of the cab company allowed authorities to track the cab to the Coheed and Cambria tour bus in the venue's parking lot, where Todd was arrested before the band took the stage. "Before the bank had hit the stage that night, Todd was arrested." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6xygY-npCr4
**Fan Scamming and Financial Troubles** : Despite a lenient sentence for the robbery, Michael Todd later admitted to scamming fans of Coheed and Cambria. He accepted money for band merchandise that he did not actually own or possess, further contributing to his financial problems, particularly in light of his ongoing cancer treatments. "But then, he admitted to scamming fans of the band by accepting money to sell co-heat and Cambria merchandise that he didn't actually own." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6xygY-npCr4

**Walgreens Robbery Attempt** : In July 2011, while on tour with Soundgarden, Coheed and Cambria's bass player Michael Todd was arrested for attempting to rob a Walgreens drug store in Massachusetts. He entered the store and showed the pharmacist a note on his Blackberry stating, "I have a bomb, give me some oxycontin or else." When no OxyContin was available, he demanded 30-milligram Percocets plus Oxycodone. "He was arrested for trying to rob a Walgreens drug store in Massachusetts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6xygY-npCr4
