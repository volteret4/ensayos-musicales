## albums
**Electric Warrior** : This album was recorded at Trident Studios. "T-Rex did Electric Warrior in the building." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
**Electric Warrior (1971)** : Released in September 1971, this album elevated the sound and attitude established by "Ride a White Swan." It was characterized as fun, funny, cartoony, fantastical, and provocatively naughty, making it a significant album that rocked with a distinct glam rock style and sound, and is considered the first glam rock album. "Electric Warrior, I think we can say, was the first glam rock album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw

## songs
**20th Century Boy (1973)** : A major glam rock hit released in the spring of 1973. "a major glam rock hit from the spring of 1973." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
**Bang a Gong (Get It On) (1971)** : A major hit single from Mark Bowen and T-Rex in the UK, which also became a radio hit for FM rock stations in North America. This song helped define the sound of the *Electric Warrior* album. "Bang a gong, a major hit single from Mark Bowen and T-Rex in the UK, and a radio hit for FM Rock stations in North America." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
**Ride a White Swan (1970)** : Released in October 1970, this song became a significant British hit in late 1970 and early 1971, accidentally defining the sound of glam rock. T-Rex performed it twice on Top of the Pops. "Not only was it a hit, but it accidentally found the sound for glam rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
