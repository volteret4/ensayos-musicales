# artist - Nico

## member of
- The Velvet Underground

## labels
- Beggars Banquet

## instruments
- pump organ

## albums
**The Marvel Index (1968)** : Released after Nico left The Velvet Underground to go solo, this album was characterized as stark and very dramatic. Nico also adopted a completely black wardrobe with bright red hair and played an old pump organ, elements recognized as gothic precursors. "Later, when Nico left the Velvet's to-go solo, she released a 1968 album called The Marvel Index. It was stark, very dramatic." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI

## songs
**Frozen Warnings (1968)** : A track from "The Marvel Index," this song is highlighted as potentially belonging to "the first goth album" due to its stark, dramatic sound and Nico's accompanying dark aesthetic and instrumentation. "Here's a bit of a song called Frozen Warnings. The first goth album, maybe." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI

## curiosities
**Artist on Beggars Banquet** : Nico was an artist featured on the Beggars Banquet roster, contributing to the label's diverse portfolio of acts. "And God bless Nico, but thank God for the cold." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
**Inclusion for Visual Interest** : Nico, a German model, was encouraged to be part of The Velvet Underground by Andy Warhol, primarily to add "visual interest" to the band. "Warhol became The Velvet's mentor and chief patron, encouraging them to hire on a German model named Nico for visual interest." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
