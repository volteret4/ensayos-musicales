# instrument - Leslie Speaker

## curiosities
**Rotating Horn for Doppler Effects** : The Leslie speaker is a large external speaker, often used with Hammond B3 organs, that features a rotating horn. This unique design creates cool Doppler effects by allowing players to increase or decrease the speed at which the horn rotates, adding a distinctive tonal characteristic. "And the best known of all these external cabinets was the Leslie speaker, which had a rotating horn, without getting too complicated, you can get some pretty cool Doppler effects by increasing and decreasing the speed at which the horn rotates." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
