# instrument - Yamaha SK line

## curiosities
**Passion Pit's Preferred Synth** : The Yamaha SK line (including the SK15, SK20, and SK30) is favored by Passion Pit, with the SK30 used extensively on their album "Manners." These synths are described as expressive and modeled after ARP instruments. "We love the Yamaha SK line. 15, 20, 30. We use the 30 all over Manners. And those are really expressive synths that are modeled after the ARPs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng
**SK30's CS80 Chip** : The SK30 model contains a CS80 chip, a component typically found in much more expensive synthesizers, making the SK30 a highly valued instrument (one was acquired for free). "Their chip inside of them is... The SK30 is a CS80 chip, which is a lot more expensive synthesizer than the SK30." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng
