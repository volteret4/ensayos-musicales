# instrument - Record Groove

## curiosities
**Living Stereo Groove Technology (Late 1950s)** : "Living Stereo" records featured a revolutionary V-shaped groove, with each side cut at a 45-degree angle. This design allowed for two separate and distinct soundtracks to be impressed into a single groove. During playback, a specialized stereo pickup with two ceramic bars would read the impressions from both sides simultaneously, reproducing the sound from the left and right sections of an orchestra through separate speakers. "Two separate and distinct sound tracks. One from one side of the orchestra or one speaker system. Another track from the other side of the orchestra for the second speaker system. Yet both are coming from one record, one groove, picked up by one stylus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8BZMo41DjlY
