# instrument - Epiphone Hawaiian Guitar

## curiosities
**1935 Sale and Lap-Played Style** : In 1935, Epiphone introduced its first electric guitars, which were Hawaiian guitars designed to be played lying across the lap. These initial models retailed for approximately one hundred dollars. "In 1935, the first epiphone electrics went on sale. They were Hawaiian guitars.
