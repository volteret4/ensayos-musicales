# instrument - 3M Machine

## curiosities
**Pioneering Digital Recording to Tape** : Roy Cooder's 1978 album "Bop to You Drop" utilized a special 3M machine that recorded digital signals directly to tape. This represented an early experimental step towards digital recording, even though subsequent mixing and mastering were still analog.
