# instrument - Upright Bass

## curiosities
**Traditional Trag Jazz Instrument** : The upright bass is a traditional instrument used in Trag Jazz, a revival of Dixieland Jazz. Trag Jazz music was explicitly made with upright bass, among other brass and rhythm instruments.
