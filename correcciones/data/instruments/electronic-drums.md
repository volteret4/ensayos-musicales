# instrument - Electronic Drums

## curiosities
**Modern Emulation in Software** : While standalone electronic drum units are less common today, their sounds are widely emulated via software and programs such as Pro Tools, Logic, and GarageBand, allowing musicians to access vintage sounds and many others. "Instead these drum amulators are emulated themselves via software and programs like Pro Tools, Logic, and GarageBand." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
**Pads Triggering Sounds** : Electronic drums are characterized by pads that are physically struck with sticks, with the impact configured to trigger various sounds. "These are pads that you actually hit with a stick. That impact can be set to trigger all kinds of sounds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
