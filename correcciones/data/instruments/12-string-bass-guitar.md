# instrument - 12-string bass guitar

## curiosities
**First Appearance in "Surrender" (1978)** : The 12-string bass guitar made its apparent debut in music on Cheap Trick's 1978 song "Surrender." It was played by bassist Tom Peterson, who felt the song required three times the strings of a standard four-string bass. "And like I said earlier, that's apparently the first song to ever feature a 12 string bass guitar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
