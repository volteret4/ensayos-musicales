# instrument - LinnDrum

## curiosities
**Cheaper, More Stable Successor (1982)** : Released in 1982, the LinnDrum was a more affordable and stable model than its predecessor, the LM-1. Its popularity compelled studio drummers to purchase and learn to program Linn units for self-preservation in recording sessions. "Another cheaper and more stable model called the Lynn drum was released in 1982." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
**Widespread Adoption** : The LinnDrum was highly sought after and used by numerous prominent artists, including Fleetwood Mac, Depeche Mode, Stevie Wonder, Michael Jackson, ABC, and Devo, and contributed most of the drum sounds to Prince's album *Purple Rain*.
