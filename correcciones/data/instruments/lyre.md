# instrument - Lyre

## curiosities
**Accompaniment for Ancient Lyric Poetry** : The lyre was an instrument commonly used to accompany lyric poetry in ancient times. Sappho, a Greek poet from Lesbos, wrote lyric poetry specifically intended to be sung in company by someone playing a lyre, demonstrating its integral role in the performance of early poetic and musical works.
