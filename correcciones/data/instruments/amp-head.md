# instrument - Amp Head

## curiosities
**Separate from Speaker Cabinets** : The concept of an "amp head" being separate from the speaker cabinets was a novel approach introduced by Marshall. This design allowed for greater flexibility, enabling musicians to string together multiple heads and cabinets. "The amplifier was separate from the speaker cabinets. It's known as a head, which was a new way of looking at things." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
