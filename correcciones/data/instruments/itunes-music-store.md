## curiosities
**Most Downloaded Song and Album on Opening Day** : On January 9, 2001, the opening day of the iTunes Music Store, U2's "Vertigo" was the most downloaded song, while Beck's "Sea Change" was the most downloaded album. These statistics highlight the immediate popularity of these artists and the new digital storefront. "We do know that at the end of that first day, January 9th, 2001, this song from U2 was downloaded more than any other. And just to finish off that thought, the most downloaded album that day was C changed by Beck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
**Opening Day (January 9, 2001)** : The iTunes Music Store officially launched on January 9, 2001. Due to the high volume of tens of thousands of people accessing the store simultaneously, it is impossible to definitively determine the absolute first song downloaded. "The star went online on January 9th, 2001 and while it is impossible to determine what song was first." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
