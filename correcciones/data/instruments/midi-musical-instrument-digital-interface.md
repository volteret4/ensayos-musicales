# instrument - MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface)

## curiosities
**Post-MCL Technology** : MIDI was a later development compared to earlier, more complex systems like the Fairlight's Music Composition Language. It simplified the process of connecting and controlling musical instruments and computers. "The early days before MIDI, you know what MIDI is, musical instrument digits, interface." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mjQBaX_3oL4
**Standardized Communication Protocol (1983)** : Introduced in June 1983, MIDI provided a common language for different musical instruments and devices to communicate with each other, regardless of manufacturer. This standard meant that any MIDI-equipped synthesizer could now interact with any MIDI drum machine or sequencer, enabling a single musician to produce the sound of an entire orchestra on stage. "And in June of 1983, we got it. Musical instrument digital interface or MIDI for short." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
