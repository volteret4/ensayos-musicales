# instrument - Oberheim DMX

## curiosities
**Competitor to Linn Machines** : The Oberheim DMX was a notable drum machine available in the market, providing competition to Roger Linn's units and becoming a significant instrument in its own right. "the Oberheim DMX" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
**Stephen Morris's Use for "Blue Monday"** : New Order drummer Stephen Morris acquired an Oberheim DMX and used it to sample his own bass drum, programming the initial patterns that would form the basis of their hit song "Blue Monday." "He'd recently acquired an Oberheim DMX drum machine. He sampled himself playing the bass drum on his kit and then loaded that sound into the DMX." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
