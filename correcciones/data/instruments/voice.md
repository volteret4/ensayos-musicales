## curiosities
**Natural Vocal Pitch Nuance** : For a human voice to sound natural and not artificial, it should not consistently hit perfect pitch. Instead, slight deviations, often described as being "a bit below the perfect pitch," are preferred, as perfect pitch can make a voice sound like a machine or a musical instrument rather than a human voice. "you don't want to be hammering it out into the perfect pitch, because then it's going to sound like a machine, right? Like a musical instrument, not a voice anymore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mjQBaX_3oL4
