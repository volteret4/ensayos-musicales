# instrument - BC Rich Guitars

## curiosities
**Acrylic Construction for Shapes** : BC Rich's ability to create models with extreme, unconventional body shapes—such as the Warlock, Seagull, Stealth, Eagle, Mockingbird, and Bitch—was due to their construction primarily from acrylic. This material allowed for innovative and visually dramatic designs. "All of them were made completely of acrylic, which is why they can assume such wild shapes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**Wild Designs for MTV Era** : BC Rich emerged as a company specializing in guitars with "wild and crazy" designs, which became particularly popular during the 1980s music video era, especially for hair metal bands. Their instruments, like the Warlock line, were visually striking, resembling "medieval weapons" and taking radical guitar aesthetics far beyond earlier designs. "Wow, these things look more like medieval weapons than guitars, especially their warlock line of instruments. If Fender's Flying V from 1958 looked strange, BC Rich took things about 20 steps further." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
