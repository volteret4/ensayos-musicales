# instrument - Stand-up Double Bass

## curiosities
**Predecessor to Electric Bass (Pre-1950s)** : Before the 1950s, bass notes in music were primarily produced by the stand-up double bass, a large, bulky acoustic instrument often taller than the musician playing it. While it provided the necessary low end, its size and lack of amplification made it less practical as music styles, particularly rock and roll, demanded louder and more portable instruments, leading to its eventual replacement by the electric bass guitar in rock music.
