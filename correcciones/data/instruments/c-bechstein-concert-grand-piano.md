# instrument - C. Bechstein concert grand piano

## curiosities
**Played by Rock Legends** : This particular piano has been graced by numerous legendary musicians, including Paul McCartney on "Hey Jude," Freddie Mercury on "Killer Queen" and other Queen tracks, and early Elton John songs like "Saturday Night's Alright for Fighting," and virtually every other piano recording made at Trident. "This is the piano that we hear Paul McCartney playing on Hey Jude. Freddie Mercury is playing on the Queen song Killer Queen and a bunch of other Queen songs. A bunch of early Elton John has played on this piano, like Saturday nights all right for fighting." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
**Trident Studios' Legendary Piano** : A handmade C. Bechstein concert grand piano, over 100 years old, is a prized possession at Trident Studios in London. It is known for its exceptionally bright sound, possibly due to its century-old hardened hammers, and its slightly stiff keys, which require a more forceful playing style. "One of Rock's most famous pianos lives in that building. It's a handmade C. Beckstein concert grand that's well over 100 years old." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
