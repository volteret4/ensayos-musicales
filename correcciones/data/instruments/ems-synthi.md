# instrument - EMS Synthi

## curiosities
**Keyboardless Synthesizer** : The EMS Synthi was a keyboardless synthesizer that Kraftwerk utilized, particularly featured on Side 1 of their 1974 "Autobahn" album. It contributed to the unique, all-electronic sound the band developed during this pioneering period. "It featured the mini-moke, a keyboard less synth by a company called EMS, a model called the Synthy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
