# instrument - Sinclair

## curiosities
**Pioneering Digital Keyboard for Remixing (1980s)** : The Sinclair was a sophisticated keyboard device introduced in the 1980s that, alongside the Fairlight, transformed remixing capabilities. It enabled operators to digitally store, surgically excise, manipulate, and implant musical material, as notably used by the Pet Shop Boys. "It began with two sophisticated keyboard devices, the Fairlight and the Sinclair. The new keyboards allowed operators to surgically excise a piece of musical material stored digitally, manipulated, and then implanted wherever they chose." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
