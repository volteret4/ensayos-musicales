# instrument - Fender Rhodes piano

## curiosities
**Used in "Blurred Lines"** : The Fender Rhodes piano was one of the instruments used in Robin Thicke and Pharrell Williams' "Blurred Lines." Its presence was noted during the plagiarism trial against Marvin Gaye's estate, although the jury ultimately focused on the "feel and sound" rather than specific instrumentation.
