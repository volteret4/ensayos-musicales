# instrument - Hard Drive

## curiosities
**Digital Recording Medium** : A hard drive is a digital recording medium. When music is recorded directly to a hard drive, it is considered a digital recording, represented by the "D" in Spars Codes (e.g., DDD). "If the music went directly to a hard drive, then that's digital, hence D." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
