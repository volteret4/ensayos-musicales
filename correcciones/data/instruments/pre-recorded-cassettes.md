# instrument - Pre-recorded Cassettes

## curiosities
**Simultaneous Market Entry (1966)** : Pre-recorded cassettes first appeared in 1966, with a large number of titles released on the same day. This simultaneous market entry means there was no single "first" pre-recorded cassette title.
