## curiosities
**Introduction by Fender Guitar Company (1951)** : In 1951, Leo Fender's guitar company quietly launched the Precision Bass, a revolutionary solid-body electric bass guitar. Designed to be lightweight and portable, it was worn around the neck, featured four thick strings, and had frets for precise note placement, distinguishing it from the bulky, fretless acoustic stand-up double bass. Its introduction was a bold move before the rise of rock and roll, with early models bundled with a 26-watt amp and 15-inch speaker to create a market for it.
