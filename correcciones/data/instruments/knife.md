# instrument - Knife

## curiosities
**Knife as a Musical Instrument in Blues** : One of the earliest and most effective methods to create "blue notes" or "bent notes" in blues music was to use a knife on the fretboard of a guitar to bend the strings. This technique allowed musicians to produce sounds outside conventional scales, a practice Pythagoras would have disapproved of, and subsequently spread from blues to jazz, R&B, and rock, becoming an integral part of modern music.
