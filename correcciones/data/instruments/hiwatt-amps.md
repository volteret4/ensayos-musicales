# instrument - Hiwatt Amps

## curiosities
**Contribution to Modern Amp Sound** : Along with Vox and Marshall, Hiwatt played a pivotal role in the evolution of the modern guitar amplifier. These three British companies collectively established the beloved, powerful sound that became characteristic of electric guitar music. "The upshot is that between high watt, Marshall, and Vox, we have the modern guitar amp, and that lovely, powerful sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**High Wattage Output** : Hiwatt amplifiers, founded by Dave Reeves, were designed to deliver exceptional volume, significantly surpassing their competitors. While Marshall amps pumped out 100 watts, Hiwatt models were rated at 200 watts, and later even 400 watts, making them highly attractive to musicians seeking maximum power.
