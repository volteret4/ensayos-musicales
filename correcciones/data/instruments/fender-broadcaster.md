# instrument - Fender Broadcaster

## curiosities
**Legal Renaming to Telecaster** : The Broadcaster, which sold for $170, was Fender's follow-up to the Esquire. However, upon its release, another musical instrument company was already using the name "Broadcaster" and threatened legal action, leading Fender to rename the guitar the Telecaster. "The Esquire was followed by the Broadcaster, which sold for $170. But when it went on sale, it turned out that another musical instrument company was using the word Broadcaster and threatened to sue. And that's when the guitar was renamed the Telecaster." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
