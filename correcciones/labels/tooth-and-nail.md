# label - Tooth and Nail

## curiosities
**Founding and Mission** : Tooth and Nail is a Christian-owned, Seattle-based record label established in 1993 by Brandon Ebel. He founded it to sign "punky and grungy indie bands" from the Pacific Northwest that were overlooked by mainstream labels due to their religious affiliations and rejected by Christian labels for being too heavy. "The company was founded in 1993 by a guy named Brandon Ebel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Major Label Scrutiny** : Currently, major record labels closely monitor bands signed by Tooth and Nail, particularly those that draw large crowds to their shows, recognizing the label's ability to identify talent. "These days when Tooth and Nail signs a band, the major labels watch very, very closely." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Notable Bands** : Besides MXPX, P.O.D. once recorded for Tooth and Nail. Other bands associated with the label include The Juliana Theory, Zao, Starflyer 59, Further Seems Forever, Mae, and Anberlin. "POD, once recorded for them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Supporting Heavy Christian Music** : The label's purpose was to allow these bands to create the music they desired without pressure to convert non-believers or appease concerned parents. "Tooth and Nail allowed the bands to make the music they wanted with no attempt to either A, convert non-believers or B, please frighten to parents." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
