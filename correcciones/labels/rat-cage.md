# label - Rat Cage

## curiosities
**Origin and Early Releases** : Rat Cage was initially a record store in the East Village owned by Dave Parsons, who then decided to start a record label of the same name. This label was responsible for releasing some of the Beastie Boys' earliest works, including the LP *Pollywog Stew* (1982) and the "Cookie Puss" EP (1983). "He already owned a record store in the East Village called Rat Cage, but he was also thinking of starting up a record label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l1jPP1FhGo8
