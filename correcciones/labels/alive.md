# label - Alive

## curiosities
**First Black Keys Label** : This Los Angeles-based indie label was the only one to respond positively to The Black Keys' initial demo, leading to the release of their debut album, *The Big Come Up*. It marked the band's sole release with Alive Records. "Except one, a company that of Los Angeles called Alive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ooNAHswiT5Q
**First Black Keys Signee** : Alive, a Los Angeles-based company, was the only indie label to sign The Black Keys after they sent out their initial demo. It became the label for their debut album, *The Big Come Up*, and their only release with Alive.
