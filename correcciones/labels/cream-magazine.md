## curiosities
**Early "Punk" Term Usage (1970-1971)** : Cream, a Detroit-based music magazine, played a role in the early usage of the term "punk" in music criticism. In December 1970, critic Lester Bangs referred to Iggy Pop as "that stooge punk." In May 1971, Dave Marsh called Question Mark and the Mysterians "purveyors of punk rock" in the magazine.
