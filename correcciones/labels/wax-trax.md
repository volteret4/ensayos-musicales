# label - Wax Trax

## curiosities
**Home of Industrial Music** : Wax Trax of Chicago emerged around 1990 as a specialist indie label for heavy industrial music, serving as the home for bands such as Ministry (fronted by Al Jourgensen), Front 242, and Front Line Assembly. "This is where we can talk about wax tracks of Chicago. It appeared around 1990. This was the home of Ministry Front II IV II, Front Line Assembly and Dozens More." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCtQqVBtCaI
**Industrial Music Label** : Wax Trax was identified as one of the labels where industrial music thrived, serving as a key outlet for the genre's intense and aggressive sound before it broke into the mainstream. "Industrial lived on labels like wax tracks and mute and strange European imprints." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
