# label - Ampex

## curiosities
**Magnetic Tape Recording Standard (Post-WWII)** : Ampex became a leading company in recording technology after receiving a $50,000 investment from Bing Crosby (worth over half a million today). Working with Jack Mullen, who brought German Magnetophone technology to the US, Ampex set the standard for both audio and video recording on magnetic tape. This development was crucial for the spread of multi-track recording and the modern recording industry.
