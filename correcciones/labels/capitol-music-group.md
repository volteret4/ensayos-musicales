# label - Capitol Music Group

## curiosities
**Universal Music Group Division and Imprints** : Capitol Music Group functions as a major division within the Universal Music Group structure. This division encompasses several well-known imprints, including Capitol Records, Virgin Records of America, Astral Works, Caroline, and Motown, alongside many others.
