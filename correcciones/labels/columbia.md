# label - Columbia

## curiosities
**Innovator of Double-Sided Discs (1910)** : In 1910, Columbia introduced and demonstrated its "Double Disc Records," which featured music on both sides, offering two different selections for a price slightly above that of a single-sided record. These records were playable on various machines, including Columbia Graphophones and Victor Talking Machines, and were promoted as providing double value. "The purpose of this record is to demonstrate the Columbia Double Disc Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
**Major Label Signing in Seattle** : Columbia was one of the major record labels that actively sought out and signed bands from the developing indie scene in Seattle, including Alice in Chains. "Major record labels had heard about the developing indie scene and were in town snapping up some of the bigger bands. Allison Chains was signed by Columbia." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCtQqVBtCaI
**Depeche Mode Signing** : Columbia Records signed Depeche Mode for the *Delta Machine* album, following the band's long career with Mute Records. This move to a major multinational label did not significantly impact the band's sound or reception for fans. **Major Label Benchmark** : Columbia Records is mentioned as an example of a "great record company" for which a 20,000-pound recording budget would be considered relatively small for a professional production. This highlights the substantial nature of that budget for an independent band like Joy Division. "Relmente, 20 mil libras era relativamente poco dinero para una grabación profesional de una gran discografía, como podía ser la Colombia." ← Music Radar Clan > Especial JOY DIVISION： 2. **Vinyl Production Prioritization** : Columbia Records, facing high international demand for vinyl production, strategically delayed the release of Depeche Mode's "Where Is The Revolution" vinyl single. This decision was made to ensure that sufficient units of the *Spirit* album could be manufactured and released first, with the single's vinyl scheduled for April 28th after album production was secured. "Colombia con ánimo de asegurar que se iban a poder fabricar suficientes unidades del álbum Spirit ha tenido que demorar la producción del single Welles y Revolución en Vinilo un poco de tiempo para dar prioridad al álbum." ← Music Radar Clan > Depeche Mode - Spirit (informacion) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bxhxzNTGclQ&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← columbia
