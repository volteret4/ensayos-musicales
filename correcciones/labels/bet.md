# label - BET

## curiosities
**Co-Premiere of "Black or White" (1991)** : BET participated in the simultaneous premiere of Michael Jackson's "Black or White" music video in November 1991, which was viewed by 500 million people globally. This collaborative broadcast across multiple networks underscored the immense cultural significance of the video.
