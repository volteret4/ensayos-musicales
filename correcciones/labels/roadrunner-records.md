# label - Roadrunner Records

## curiosities
**Financial Role of Hit Bands** : Roadrunner Records benefited significantly from Nickelback's success, with millions in album sales for the label directly subsidizing the releases of a diverse range of metal bands, including Opeth, Killswitch Engage, and Mastodon. Additionally, these profits provided crucial "seed money" for Billy Talent's efforts to establish a presence in the US market through their deal with Roadrunner.
