## curiosities
**Purchase of Layne Staley's Condo (April 1997)** : The LaRusta Trust, which was part of the Alice in Chains Management Company, purchased a three-bedroom, 1500 square foot condo for Layne Staley in Seattle's University District in April 1997. The cost was $262,000, and Staley used "John LaRusta" as an alias to keep his name out of public records.
