# label - Mint Records

## curiosities
**CUB's Label** : Mint Records signed the Vancouver-based band CUB, known for their distinctive "cuddlecore" genre. This genre was characterized by melodic music, jangly guitars, and a pronounced childlike quality. The label supported CUB's unique artistic expression, which also involved their playful live performances such as playing in pajamas and bringing gifts for fans. "A perfect example of this is CUB of Vancouver band signed to mint records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
**Vancouver Indie Label History (1991-Present)** : A Vancouver-based independent record label founded in 1991, dedicated to providing an outlet for up-and-coming local artists. It achieved decent, albeit modest, success before gaining more widespread attention with artists like The New Pornographers. "They'd been fighting the good fight since 1991, trying to be an outlet for up and coming local artists." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=raRCZ0Y5up8
