# concert - Longhorn Ballroom, Dallas

## curiosities
**Sex Pistols Gig with Sid Vicious – January 10, 1978** : The Longhorn Ballroom in Dallas hosted a Sex Pistols show on January 10, 1978. Sid Vicious was reportedly "in fine form" that night, despite three of his bass strings being broken, likely because a roadie was playing his parts backstage. "Here's a radio commercial for the January 10th 1978 show at the Longhorn Ballroom in Dallas. ... Oh, Sid was in fine form that night in Dallas." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8
