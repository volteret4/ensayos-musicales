# concert - The Tonight Show with Johnny Carson

## curiosities
**"Holy Grail" for Comedians** : Getting an appearance on *The Tonight Show with Johnny Carson* on NBC was considered the "holy grail" for comedians, offering significant national exposure. "Johnny Carson was the host that was sort of the holy grail for comedians was to get on the Tonight Show with Johnny Carson." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6O5TCTO-go4
**Springboard for 90s Sitcom Stars** : The show served as a major springboard for several comedians who would eventually transition from stand-up to starring in their own TV sitcoms in the 1990s, including Jerry Seinfeld and Ellen DeGeneres. "The tonight show on NBC was a huge springboard for several comedians who would eventually make the leap from stand-up to TV sitcoms in the 90s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6O5TCTO-go4
