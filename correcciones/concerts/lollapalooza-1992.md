# concert - Lollapalooza 1992

## curiosities
**Impact on Alternative Music** : By the time Lollapalooza returned exactly one year after its debut in 1991, alternative music was no longer an "alternative to anything" but was well on its way to supplanting the mainstream. This rapid shift was largely attributed to the rise of grunge. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Red Hot Chili Peppers' Performance with Arik Marshall** : The Red Hot Chili Peppers performed at Lollapalooza in 1992 with Arik Marshall as their guitarist, following John Frusciante's first departure. The festival was one of the "big festival dates" mentioned as part of the tour where Marshall was brought in as a temporary replacement. "While the Chili Pepper's regrouped, eventually bringing in a guy named Eric Marshall for a bit... Here's the band performing at Lola Palusio later back here." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
