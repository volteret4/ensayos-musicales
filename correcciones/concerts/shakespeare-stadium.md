# concert - Shakespeare Stadium

## curiosities
**Amplification Challenges for The Beatles (August 1965)** : In August 1965, The Beatles performed at Shakespeare Stadium using a PA system with four All-Tech amplifiers, each producing 175 watts. Despite this being considered loud, the crowd's noise reached 135 decibels, double the system's output. This event highlighted the inadequacy of existing PA technology for large rock concerts and spurred demand for more powerful sound reinforcement. "When The Beatles played Shakespeare Stadium in August 1965, all they had behind them was four all-tech amplifiers, each generating 175 watts of power, through the same PA system that the announcer used for baseball games." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
