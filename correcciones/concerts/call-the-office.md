# concert - Call the Office

## curiosities
**The Tragically Hip Live Performance (1988)** : A live recording of The Tragically Hip was captured at Call the Office in London on March 3, 1988. This performance notably featured a version of "New Orleans is Sinking" approximately 18 months before its release on their debut album, "Up To Here." "Here they are at Call the Office in London. The date is March 3rd, 1988, which means this performance happened about 18 months before this song appeared on their debut album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oMBmdfrA47Q
