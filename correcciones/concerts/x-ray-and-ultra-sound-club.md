# concert - X-ray and Ultra Sound Club

## curiosities
**Pivotal Headstones Showcase** : This Toronto club, managed by Ivan Matt Sell, hosted a crucial showcase for The Headstones where key industry figures like Cam Carpenter and Joe Bamford were invited. The unexpected presence and endorsement of Gord Downie and Paul Langlois from The Tragically Hip at this event were instrumental in The Headstones securing a record deal with MCA. "Ivan Matt Sell. Yeah, who ran the X-ray and Ultra Sound Club, remember that? And then she booked us there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
