# concert - Joshua Tree National Park

## curiosities
**Inspiration for Album Concept** : The area around Joshua Tree National Park, in the California desert, served as a key inspiration for the concept of Queens of the Stone Age's album *Songs for the Deaf*, which is imagined as a soundtrack to a drive through this region. "This is supposed to be taken as a soundtrack of a drive from Los Angeles through to Joshua Tree National Park out in the desert, while flipping through small town radio stations along the way..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc
