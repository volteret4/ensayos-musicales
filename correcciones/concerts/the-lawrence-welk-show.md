# concert - The Lawrence Welk Show

## curiosities
**First Televised Guitar Smashing** : In 1956, Rockin' Rocky Rockwell performed on "The Lawrence Welk Show" and concluded his rendition of Elvis Presley's "Hound Dog" by smashing his acoustic guitar. This event is believed to be the earliest televised instance of an artist intentionally destroying an instrument. "That's when a guy named Rockin' Rocky Rockwell performed a not-so-respectful version of Elvis Presley's Hound Dog. And when he finished, he smashed his acoustic guitar to bits." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
