# concert - Concert for Nelson Mandela

## curiosities
**Peter Buck's Flight Incident (2001)** : On April 21, 2001, Peter Buck of REM was scheduled to play at a concert for Nelson Mandela at Trafalgar Square in London. However, a pre-flight incident on British Airways Flight 48, where he reacted unusually to a combination of Ambien and red wine, resulted in his arrest at Heathrow Airport, jeopardizing his participation.
