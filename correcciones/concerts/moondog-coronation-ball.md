# concert - Moondog Coronation Ball

## curiosities
**First Rock Concert (1952)** : The Moondog Coronation Ball, held in Cleveland on March 21, 1952, and organized by radio DJ Alan Fried, is widely considered to be the "first ever rock concert." The event was plagued by awful sound, with thousands of screaming attendees struggling to hear bands that had almost no sound reinforcement, only basic instruments, mics, and a few stage amplifiers.
