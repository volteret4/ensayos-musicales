# concert - Spike Island

## curiosities
**The Stone Roses' Legendary Outdoor Gig (May 27, 1990)** : Spike Island, a peninsula on the Mersey River surrounded by chemical factories and power plants and a reclaimed chemical dump, was the site of a legendary outdoor gig by The Stone Roses on May 27, 1990. Although the band's performance was considered "average or even below average," it attracted their biggest crowd (28,000-30,000 people). The event became iconic as a "meeting of the minds" for the "Madchester kids" and "rave kids," and was heavily hyped by the British music press, who declared it the start of the 90s.
