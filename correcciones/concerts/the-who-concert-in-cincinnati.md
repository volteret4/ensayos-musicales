# concert - The Who concert in Cincinnati

## curiosities
**Impact on Safety Regulations (1979)** : The Who concert in Cincinnati on December 3, 1979, was the site of a tragedy where 11 people were trampled to death due to a stampede for general admission spots. This event prompted the enactment of new rules globally, including limiting ticket sales based on venue square footage, establishing guidelines for ushers and security, and developing evacuation plans.
