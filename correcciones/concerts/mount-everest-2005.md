# concert - Mount Everest (2005)

## curiosities
**Highest Gig on Ground (5,545 meters)** : In November 2005, a British charity organized a group of amateur musicians to perform above Everest base camp on Mount Everest, at an altitude of 5,545 meters. This fundraiser for Nepalese children is considered the highest gig ever performed while still touching the ground. "A British charity organized a group of amateur musicians on Mount Everest. It was a fundraiser for Nepalese children. The gig in November 2005, was staged above Everest base camp and an altitude of 5,545 meters." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T1XSJIm0Nh4
