# concert - Performance Studio

## curiosities
**Site of Early Ramones Rehearsal** : On January 24, 1974, five days after Douglas Colvin and Johnny Cummings bought their instruments, the Ramones held a "more formal rehearsal" at the Performance Studio. Plans were made here for their first public gig on March 30th. "On January 24th, 1974, five days after Douglas and Johnny smoked that joint and bought the guitars, they had a more formal rehearsal, and a place called the Performance Studio." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
