## curiosities
**Early Mass Event Sound Challenges (1967)** : The Monterey International Pop Festival in June 1967, held in San Francisco, was the "first truly massive music event" that required specialized sound reinforcement. Despite efforts to amplify the sound for over 90,000 people using "amplifiers and speaker boxes cobbled together," the audio quality was "pretty bad." However, the experience highlighted the need for improved festival sound systems.
