# concert - Wintergarden Ballroom, San Francisco

## curiosities
**Final Sex Pistols Show with Sid Vicious – January 14, 1978** : This venue hosted the last-ever Sex Pistols show featuring Sid Vicious on January 14, 1978. It was a chaotic performance that culminated in Johnny Rotten's famous parting words, marking the band's on-stage breakup. "Before we go any further, let's go back to the final sex pistol show at the Wintergarden Ballroom in San Francisco on January 14, 1978." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8
