# concert - Moon Dog Balls

## curiosities
**Successful Concert Series by Allan Freed** : Disc jockey Allan Freed, under his "Moon Dog" persona, hosted "Moon Dog Balls" in Cleveland during the 1950s. These concerts were "insanely successful," demonstrating the popularity of his radio program playing Black R&B records for white youth and his initial cultural impact. "On the air, he referred to himself as the Moon Dog, and through these concerts called Moon Dog Balls, which were insanely successful." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
