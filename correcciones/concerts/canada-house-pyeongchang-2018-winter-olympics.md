# concert - Canada House (Pyeongchang 2018 Winter Olympics)

## curiosities
**Impromptu Performances for Athletes** : Arkells performed three "house party" style shows at Canada House during the last five days of the 2018 Pyeongchang Winter Olympics. The band traveled last-minute, flying coach without instruments, and had to play on the ground because the stage was only large enough for drums. This timing allowed them to play for Olympians who had finished their events and were "ready to party," creating a much livelier atmosphere than if they had performed earlier in the Games. "we did like three shows at the Canada House like every night and it was a party." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kKNn01M8GG4
