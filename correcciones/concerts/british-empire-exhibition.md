## curiosities
**Early Large-Scale Amplification Event (1925)** : The British Empire Exhibition in 1925 featured an early large-scale use of amplification technology, where King George V addressed 90,000 people. This event showcased the continued advancements in public address systems following their initial invention and demonstration, setting precedents for large public speeches and performances.
