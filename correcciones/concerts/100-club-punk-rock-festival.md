# concert - 100 Club Punk Rock Festival

## curiosities
**Britain's First Official Punk Festival (September 20-21, 1976)** : Located on Oxford Street in London, the 100 Club, previously known as a jazz venue, hosted Britain's first official punk rock festival over two nights. Featuring bands like the Sex Pistols, The Clash, and The Damned, the event drew lines around the block, though only about 600 people could enter. Despite mixed performance quality, it solidified a community and attracted record company and music press interest, making punk rock "no longer a secret" in the UK.
