# concert - Lollapalooza Festival

## curiosities
**Bringing Rap to White Kids** : The festival played a key role in introducing rap to white audiences by booking prominent acts such as Ice-T, Ice Cube, George Clinton, Cypress Hill, and A Tribe Called Quest. "The La La Paloza Festival helped bring rap to white kids by booking mainstayjacks like Ice Tea, Ice Cube, George Clinton, Cypress Hill, and a tribe called Quest." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bQz8R0gbXc
**Creation Inspired by The Pixies' Performance** : The Lollapalooza Festival, conceived by Perry Farrell, was indirectly inspired by The Pixies' headlining performance at the 1990 Reading Festival. The enthusiastic reaction of 30,000 fans singing along to "Debaser" was the catalyst that convinced Farrell his idea for an annual North American festival of new bands would be successful. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z9T9wi0vEvc
**Inclusiveness for House of Pain** : House of Pain, emerging from LA in the early 90s, was swept into the "inclusiveness" of the original Lollapalooza years, which helped their rise. "House of Pain came out of LA in the early 90s and were swept into the inclusiveness that came during the original Lollapalooze years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bQz8R0gbXc
**Premier Platform for Alternative Nation (1990s)** : For a significant portion of the 1990s, Lollapalooza served as the "premier live event" for disseminating the "gospel" of the alternative nation, establishing itself as a crucial cultural and musical platform. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z9T9wi0vEvc
