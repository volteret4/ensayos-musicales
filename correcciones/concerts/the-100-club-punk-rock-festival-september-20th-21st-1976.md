# concert - The 100 Club Punk Rock Festival (September 20th & 21st, 1976)

## curiosities
**Britain's First Official Punk Rock Festival** : Held on Oxford Street in London, this festival was a direct consequence of the Ramones' influential gigs and is recognized as Britain's first official punk rock festival. The venue, previously known for jazz, hosted bands like the Sex Pistols, The Clash, and The Damned, drawing extensive queues despite limited capacity (around 600 people). Although performances were not top-tier, the event successfully "coalesce[d]" a punk community and attracted significant interest from record companies and the music press, ending punk rock's secrecy in the UK.
