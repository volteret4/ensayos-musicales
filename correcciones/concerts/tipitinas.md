# concert - Tipitina's

## curiosities
**Blind Melon's New Orleans Show (October 21, 1995)** : Tipitina's, a club on Napoleon Avenue in New Orleans, was scheduled to host Blind Melon for a show on October 21, 1995. This was the city where frontman Shannon Hoon would die of a cocaine overdose before the performance. "Early on October 21st, the band arrived in New Orleans to play a club show at a place called Tipatina's Undepollion Avenue." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
