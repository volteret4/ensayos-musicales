# concert - PNE Forum

## curiosities
**The Smashing Pumpkins Fatality (September 24, 2007)** : The PNE Forum in Vancouver was the location of a Smashing Pumpkins concert on September 24, 2007, where 20-year-old Kenny Leung died after being pulled from a mosh pit, unconscious. This incident, while not officially linked to moshing by autopsy, further fueled demands for stricter controls and zero-tolerance policies regarding aggressive crowd behavior.
