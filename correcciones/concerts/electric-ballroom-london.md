# concert - Electric Ballroom, London

## curiosities
**The Vicious White Kids' Sole Gig – Late August 1978** : The Electric Ballroom was the venue for the one and only concert by Sid Vicious's band, The Vicious White Kids, in late August 1978. The band, including Rat Scabies and Glenn Matlock, played nine songs, repeating them three times over three sets. "Sid put together a band and played one show at the Electric Ballroom in London in late August of 1978." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8
