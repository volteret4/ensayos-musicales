# concert - U2 Achtung Baby Tour (1992)

## curiosities
**End of Tour and Pixies' Burnout** : The tour concluded in Vancouver on April 25, 1992, leaving The Pixies "fried" and leading to their decision to take a one-year break, which ultimately preceded their breakup. "By the time everything ended in Vancouver on April 25, 1992, the band was fried." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Heightened Internal Tensions for The Pixies** : The tour with U2 exacerbated internal tensions within The Pixies, particularly due to bassist Kim Deal's boyfriend, Jim Greer, a Spin Magazine writer, documenting their experiences, which did not go over well with the band. "It just ramped up the tensions even higher." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**The Pixies as Opening Act and Audience Disconnect** : The Pixies served as an opening act for U2 during their "Achtung Baby" tour in 1992. Despite U2's respect for them, The Pixies struggled to connect with U2's audience, often performing to arenas and stadiums that were less than a quarter full. "Everything reached a crescendo during the band's stint warming up for you too during the octangue baby tour in 1992." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
