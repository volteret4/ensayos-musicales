# concert - NAC (St. Catharines)

## curiosities
**Early Sold-Out Show** : Alexis on Fire played a sold-out show at the NAC in St. Catharines, which was a significant achievement for the band at the time, even if it only held around 100 people. This performance led Michael McCarty of EMI Music Publishing to take interest in the band. "And he brought, he convinced Michael to come down to St. Catherine's one night and, and see us play. I think we played at the NAC, maybe that night." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
