# concert - Las Vegas Show (August 12, 1999)

## curiosities
**Emotional Performance Before Jail** : This concert was described as one of Stone Temple Pilots' finest shows, with Scott Wyland delivering an "insanely great" performance, fully aware that he had to turn himself in the next day for a jail sentence. He told his bandmates it was an honor to spend his "last night of freedom" with them. "Man, he was insanely great. Just really. It was one of the bands' finest shows I think." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TDwagoyMvck
