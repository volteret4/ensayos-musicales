# concert - Cleveland Arena

## curiosities
**Venue for First Major Rock Concert (1952)** : The Cleveland Arena hosted the Mundoq Coronation Ball on March 21, 1952, which is regarded as the first major rock concert. The venue, designed for 10,000 people, was overwhelmed by 20,000 attendees, leading to a riot and the show's premature shutdown.
