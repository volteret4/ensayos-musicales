# concert - British Airway 767 charity gig

## curiosities
**Highest Airplane Concert (43,000 feet)** : This charity event, featuring Tony Hadley of Spandau Ballet and Kim Wilde, took place aboard a chartered British Airway 767. The aircraft reached an altitude of 43,000 feet, establishing a record for the highest concert performance aboard an airplane.
