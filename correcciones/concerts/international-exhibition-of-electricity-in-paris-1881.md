## curiosities
**Debut of the Tiatrophone** : The Tiatrophone, a pioneering streaming system, was demonstrated by Clement Adler at the International Exhibition of Electricity in Paris in 1881. This event showcased the innovative technology that allowed remote listening to live performances.
