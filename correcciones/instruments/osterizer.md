# instrument - Osterizer

## curiosities
**Iggy Pop's Homemade Instrument** : The Osterizer was a homemade instrument created and played by Iggy Pop during The Stooges' early professional gigs in 1968. It consisted of a vacuum cleaner attached to a blender, but he soon gave it up due to the difficulty in amplifying its sound. "And was the singer playing a vacuum cleaner? Yes, yes it was. And was it attached to a blender? And yeah, it was. It was a homemade instrument that Iggy called the Osterizer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8
