# instrument - MOOG Synthesizer

## curiosities
**Inspiration for Polyvox** : The MOOG synthesizer was the inspiration for the Soviet-era Polyvox synthesizer. An electronic engineer in Kiev, deeply impressed by the MOOG's sound but having never seen one, set out to build his own version based on theoretical knowledge. "And he wanted to build a MOOG. I loved the sound of the MOOG. He knew the theory of how a MOOG was built. Had heard what they sound like but had never seen one." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RWbKqqW78yg
