# instrument - X-ray Film

## curiosities
**Rotten Genza Dat (Bone Records)** : X-ray film, discarded from hospitals and found in dumpsters, was repurposed as a material for bootleg records in the Soviet Union. These "Rotten Genza Dat" or "bone records" were thick enough to be etched with music, though they could only be recorded on one side and wore out quickly. They were highly illegal but sold for about one ruble, 80% less than official records that were often unavailable. "Music discarded X-ray film found in dumpsters outside hospitals. They were just thick enough to be turned into records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YeNWDtN_sHE
