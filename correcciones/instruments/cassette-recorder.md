# instrument - Cassette Recorder

## curiosities
**Recording Device (1979)** : In Utah, during Christmas time in 1979, a small boy used a cassette recorder to sing and record his voice. He found joy in hearing himself on tape and in the experience of having an audience for his performance. "a small boy is singing into a cassette recorder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vFVnhksrFao
**Tool for Preserving Memory** : The cassette recorder served as a crucial tool for a frightened child to document a painful and shocking secret in his own language, ensuring that these "last words" and "grainy flecks and wisps" of his memory were preserved before he would forget them. "There's something really important the child needs to say, something he wants put on record in his own language." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vFVnhksrFao
