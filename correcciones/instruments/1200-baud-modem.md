# instrument - 1,200-baud modem

## curiosities
**BAS Terminal Connectivity (1974)** : In December 1974, BAS (Bay Area Seating Service) operated its 24 terminals using telephone lines with 1,200-baud modems for connectivity. This technology, while functional, was noted for not being particularly quick.
