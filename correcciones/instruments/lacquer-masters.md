**Supply Chain Disruption (2020)** : In early 2020, a fire at a California plant that produced 80% of the world's lacquer masters led to significant production delays and higher prices for new vinyl albums, highlighting a vulnerability in the vinyl manufacturing supply chain. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
