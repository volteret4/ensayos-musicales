## curiosities
**Dee Dee Ramone's Purchase** : On January 23, 1974, Douglas Colvin (Dee Dee Ramone) bought a Danelectro bass for $50 at Manny's Guitar Center on 48th Street, coinciding with Johnny Ramone's guitar purchase.
