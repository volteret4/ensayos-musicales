## curiosities
**Bono's Composition for Humanitarian Mission** : Bono used a Spanish guitar to compose part of the melody for "Mothers of the Disappeared" during a humanitarian mission in Ethiopia. The original composition was a short song designed to teach young children about personal hygiene. "He had written a little diddy on Spanish guitar designed to teach young children about personal hygiene." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VR5Fncdc2kQ
