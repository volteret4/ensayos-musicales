# instrument - Dat tape

## curiosities
**Theme Song Composition** : The opening sound of the ongoing history show's theme song, which features a "rewind through time kind of thing," was created by scrubbing a dat tape backwards. "the opening is just a dat tape scrubbing backwards through a whole bunch of stuff because ongoing history, just a rewind through time kind of thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4xxgPX-aR90
