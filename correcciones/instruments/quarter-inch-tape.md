## curiosities
**Early Radio Production** : For the first 250 episodes of the show, the speaker's voice track was recorded onto quarter-inch tapes. These tapes were then physically edited using razor blades and grease pencils. "He would come into the studio in 83, Kennedy, Roza, Craig would work in those nights or I was working come up with the script and we record them on a quarter inch tape." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4xxgPX-aR90
