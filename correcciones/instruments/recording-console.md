## curiosities
**eBay Purchase for "Rubber Factory"** : The Black Keys acquired a recording console on eBay, which was used for their *Rubber Factory* album. This console had a history, having been previously owned by the sound engineer for Loverboy, though the band ultimately considered it "crap" and left it behind. "They bought a recording console on eBay, which happened to have been owned by the guy who Winston Soundford lover boy. **Sound City Studios Console at 606 Studios** : The control room at Foo Fighters' 606 Studios eventually acquired the renowned recording console from the famed Sound City Studios after its closure. This console boasts a rich history, having been used by iconic bands such as Nirvana (for "Nevermind"), Red Hot Chili Peppers, Rage Against the Machine, Slipknot, and Tom Petty, before finding its new home with the Foo Fighters.
