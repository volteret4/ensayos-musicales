# instrument - Whammy Bar

## curiosities
**Evolution from Vibrola** : The modern whammy bar on the Fender Stratocaster was an improvement on the earlier Vibrola concept, which was originally developed by Doc Kauffman in the 1940s. "And the Stratocaster had the first modern whammy bar, which was an improvement on the Vibe Rolla, the Doc Hoffman had come up with back in the 1940s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
