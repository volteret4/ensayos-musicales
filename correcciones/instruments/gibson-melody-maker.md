# instrument - Gibson Melody Maker

## curiosities
**Historical Ownership and Significance** : Joan Jett's main guitar for many years was a white Gibson Melody Maker, which she purchased from Eric Carmen after The Raspberries disbanded. This specific guitar was famously used on The Raspberries' hit "Go All The Way" and featured prominently on many of Joan Jett's own successful songs. "For the longest time, Joan Jett's main guitar has been a white Gibson melody maker.
