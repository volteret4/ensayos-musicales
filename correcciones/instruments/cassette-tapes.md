## curiosities
**Network for Bootlegging Western Music** : As cassettes became prevalent, music fans in the USSR spent countless hours dubbing copies of smuggled Western music. By the 1980s, an informal, coast-to-coast bootlegging network spanned all 11 time zones of the USSR. This widespread distribution exposed millions of young people to Western rock and helped them learn English, making them more susceptible to Western culture.
