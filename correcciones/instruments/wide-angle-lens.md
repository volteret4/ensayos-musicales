# instrument - Wide-angle lens

## curiosities
**Used for Oasis Album Cover** : Photographer Michael Spencer Jones utilized a wide-angle lens during the photoshoot for Oasis's "Definitely Maybe" album cover. This specific lens was necessary to capture all five band members within the unusually narrow confines of Paul "Bonehead" Arthurs' living room in Didsbury, Manchester. "Spencer had to use a wide angle lens to get everybody in the shot." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tbKuJR0cPs
