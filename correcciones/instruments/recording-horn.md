# instrument - Recording Horn

## curiosities
**Early Fade-Out Simulation** : In an 1895 recording called "The Spirit of 76" featuring a marching band, a fade-out effect was achieved by having the band physically march away from the acoustic recording horn. As they moved further away, their sound naturally became quieter, simulating a fade. "As the song winds down, the band marches away from the acoustic recording horn. The further they get from that recording horn, the quieter they become and thus fade out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
