# instrument - Phono Autograph

## curiosities
**First Device for Recording Sound Visually (1860)** : Invented by French stenographer Edward-Léon Scott de Martinville in 1860, the Phono Autograph was designed to visualize sound by inscribing waveforms onto paper. It created the first audio recordings, though it lacked a playback mechanism until 2008 when laser scanning enabled their reproduction.
