# instrument - Singing Arc

## curiosities
**First Purely Electronic Musical Instrument (1899)** : Invented in 1899 by physicist William Dutdle, the Singing Arc was the first purely electronic musical instrument. Dutdle discovered that the coil and capacitor of a common carbon arc lamp emitted sound and, through experimentation, learned to control its oscillations with a keyboard to alter pitch. "And the Singing Arc, which is what he called this thing, became the first purely electronic musical instrument." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
