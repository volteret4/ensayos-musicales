# instrument - 10-inch, 78 RPM discs

## curiosities
**Standard Record Format (Pre-1938)** : Prior to the advent of album cover artwork, all commercial music releases were produced on 10-inch, 78 RPM discs, with each side typically containing a single song. These individual discs were commonly packaged together in rudimentary book-like bindings to form what were then termed "phonograph albums." "In those days, all music came on 10-inch, 78 RPM discs.
