**Pioneering All-Female Rock Band** : Fanny was an excellent all-female rock group from California that achieved significant milestones, including a major label deal with Warner. They were the third all-female group to secure such a deal, following Goldie and the Gingerbreads and The Pleasure Seekers. "With DeBure, they ended up with a major label deal with Warner, making them the third all-female group behind Goldie and the Gingerbreads and the Pleasure Seekers to have that kind of deal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY

**Victims of Industry Sexism** : Despite their talent and championship by figures like David Bowie, Fanny's career was hindered by the "old boys club" in the music industry. They were not given due credit as musicians and songwriters, instead being pressured to conform to expectations of being "cute" and "sexy," which they refused.
