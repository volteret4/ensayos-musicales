## curiosities
**Crash 'n' Burn Club (Summer 1977) – First Punk-Only Venue in Canada, Shut Down by Liberal Party Complaints** : The Diodes formed in late 1976 in Toronto and opened their first show supporting the Talking Heads in January 1977. That summer, they converted their rehearsal space at 15 Duncan Street in downtown Toronto into Crash 'n' Burn, the first punk-only club in Canada. Numerous young bands from Canada and the US played there over several months until the club's closure in the fall — the cause being that the Liberal Party of Canada had an office upstairs and kept complaining to the landlord about the noise and the strange people outside. The Diodes were the first Canadian band to sign with a major label, inking a deal in August 1977 and releasing their self-titled debut album in October.
