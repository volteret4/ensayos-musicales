## albums
**Get The Knack (1979) – Rapid Success Story** : Released on June 11, 1979, after being recorded in two weeks for $18,000, "Get The Knack" sold 1 million copies by June 24, becoming Capitol's fastest-selling debut album since "Meet the Beatles" in 1964 and generating astounding profits. ## curiosities
**Backlash from Rapid Success** : Despite their phenomenal commercial success, The Knack experienced severe backlash due to how quickly they became popular. However, their success demonstrated to other record labels that signing New Wave bands could be a profitable strategy during an economic downturn. "And even though the NAC experienced some severe backlash because they became so successful so fast, other record labels realized that their way out of the economic doldrums might just be to sign more new wave bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYsV3ozbk4M
**Reality Bites Soundtrack Contribution (1994)** : The Knack contributed to the soundtrack for *Reality Bites*, a key film in the "slacker culture" movement released in 1994.
