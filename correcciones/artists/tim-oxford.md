## curiosities
**Early Life and Musical Development** : Born in Quebec and grew up in Newmarket, Ontario. He decided to pursue music at a young age, starting on guitar but admitting he played it "very badly" for a couple of years before switching to drums around age 12, becoming "all in" immediately. "Yeah, I was born in Quebec, grew up in New Market, Ontario, and I did not go to McMaster. I wanted to be a musician at a pretty young age. So I started playing guitar very badly for a couple years and then moved over to the drums at about 12." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Entry into Arkells and Professionalism** : Tim Oxford joined Arkells after attending audio school and networking through various pub gigs. He was introduced to the band by their previous keyboard player who mentioned they needed a drummer. His professionalism and working equipment (unlike the previous drummer's broken hi-hats) immediately impressed the band. He initially recorded a 5-song EP with them, some of which was done in his basement facilities. "Then when we met Tim, it was like, whoa, this guy is really professional because he was a very accomplished drummer, but his equipment also worked." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Home Recording Studio** : Tim Oxford not only proved to be an accomplished drummer but also possessed recording skills from audio school and had facilities in his basement, which the band utilized for early recordings. "Yeah, because here's a drummer who's not only a great drummer, but also went to school for recording and had facilities in his basement." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Initial Hesitation about Joining Arkells** : Despite his professionalism, Tim Oxford initially had reservations about joining Arkells. After his first tryout, he told his partner, Sarah, that he probably wouldn't go back, but changed his mind quickly when they began recording together shortly after. "If I'm being totally honest, I went home and I remember Sarah being like, hey, my lady saying, hey, how to go? You were trying out with that band or whatever. And I was like, I don't think I probably won't go back initially." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
