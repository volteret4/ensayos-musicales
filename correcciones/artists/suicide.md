# artist - Suicide

## members
- Alan Vega

## curiosities
**"Punk Masses" Gigs** : Inspired by Lester Bangs' December 1970 reference to Iggy Pop as a "stooge punk" in Cream Magazine, Alan Vega and his band Suicide began calling their gigs "punk masses," an early adoption of the term "punk" in a performance context. "He was in a band called Suicide, and they took that cream reference and started calling their gig punk masses." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VYi3r0G-ne4
**CBGB Scene Band** : Suicide was one of the bands featured in the burgeoning CBGB scene, alongside Blondie, The Talking Heads, and The Heartbreakers, as the club became a central hub for new music. "CBGB was becoming the center of a new scene featuring bands with names like Blondie and Suicide and The Talking Heds and a television spin-off band called The Heartbreakers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VYi3r0G-ne4
