# artist - The Dead Weather

## concerts
- Twilight Soundtracks

## songs
**Hanging From The Heavens (Unknown Year)** : This song serves as the A-side of a 7-inch record, which bears the catalog number TMR001. The record was acquired from Jack White's Third Man Record Store in Nashville. "The A side is the deadweathers hanging from the heavens." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VlIErdSSVOM

## curiosities
**Twilight Soundtracks Contribution** : The Dead Weather contributed to the various *Twilight* soundtracks that accompanied the film series. These soundtracks featured a nice selection of tunes, mostly album cuts and outtakes, from a range of artists spanning different genres. "The Paramore, Lincoln Park, Collective Soul, Death Cab for Cutie, Tom York, the Killers, Editors, Metric, Block Keys, Dead Weather, Beck, Vampire Weekend, Florence in the Machine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
