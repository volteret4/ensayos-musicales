## curiosities
**Berkeley College of Music Education** : Furthered his musical education by studying bass at the prestigious Berkeley in Boston. "You went to Berkeley, right? Yeah, eventually. Berkeley, Aboston, yeah, to study bass." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
**Musical Education Through Trent Carr** : With few other friends, White spent weekends at Trent Carr's downtown Toronto home, receiving his "musical rock and roll education." Carr would play him records by bands like Led Zeppelin and The Who, inspiring White to practice bass alone throughout the week. "He'd play me like, let's out on a pink floor and the who and all these bands. And then I'd go home and play bass all week by myself and then come back and show him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
**Scarborough Upbringing** : Grew up in Scarborough, Ontario, which he characterized as a "cultural wasteland" due to a lack of local musical scene or friends outside of Trent Carr. "I grew up in Scarborough, which was a cultural wasteland." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
**Trent Carr's Influence on Becoming a Bassist** : Became a bass player because his childhood friend Trent Carr advised him to. When White expressed interest in buying a guitar, Carr told him he should get a bass instead, a suggestion White readily accepted. "The only reason I'm a bass player is because I was, I've known Trent since I was really young... Trent said, no, you should get a bass." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
