## curiosities
**Coachella Hologram Performance (2012)** : In 2012, a virtual Tupac Shakur made a striking appearance at Coachella during a set by Dr. Dre and Snoop Dogg. Though marketed as a hologram, it was technically an advanced projection using Pepper's Ghost techniques, appearing "awfully lifelike" and marking a pivotal moment in the development of simulated live performances.
