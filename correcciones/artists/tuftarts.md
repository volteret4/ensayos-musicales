## curiosities
**Shared Bills with Ramones** : In 1975, Tuftarts performed on bills with the Ramones at CBGB. The band was also described as being "sort of a direct descendant of sniper," Joey Ramone's old band, indicating a lineage within the punk scene. "Other times, they'd be on a bill with a talking heads or blondey or a band called Tuftarts, which, if you want to get technical about it, was sort of a direct descendant of sniper, Joey's old band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
