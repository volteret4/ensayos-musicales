## curiosities
**Influence on Green Day** : Operation Ivy was a band highly revered by a young Billy Joe Armstrong, who would later go on to form Green Day, highlighting a significant lineage within the Bay Area punk scene. "they devolved out of a band called Operation Ivy, a group that was revered by a young Billy Joe Armstrong, who of course was in Green Day" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gkLMwrthZ9s
**Operation Ivy (1987–1989) – Skacore Pioneers; Dissolved into Rancid** : Operation Ivy formed in 1987 and, like the Bosstones, embraced the blend of ska and hardcore known as skacore. They held it together for a couple of years and a couple of releases before dissolving and coalescing into a new band — Rancid, which became the first of the new wave of skacore bands to break out big. "Operation Ivy, they were formed in 1987 and like the muddy, muddy Boston's they were into that blend of Skah and Hardcore known as Skahcore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yqo-YDJR0nM
