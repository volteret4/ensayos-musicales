## curiosities
**Decline in the 1970s** : Despite her prominence in the late 1960s with Jefferson Airplane, the 1970s saw a decline in her musical force, possibly due to alcohol and drug abuse. She went from lead vocalist to being largely relegated to harmonies and "oh babies" in Jefferson Starship's 70s music. "But the 70s seemed to just swallow Grace Slick or maybe it was the alcohol in the drugs." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 7 - Women Who Rocked the 70s | http://www.ftr70.com
