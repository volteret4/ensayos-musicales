## curiosities
**Death and Legacy** : Bruce Fairbairn passed away on May 17, 1999, at his home in Vancouver at the age of 49. Before his death, he established Little Mountain Sound in Vancouver, building it into one of North America's greatest recording studios. "On May 17, 1999, he was found dead at his home in Vancouver. It was just 49. But before he passed away, Bruce built his Vancouver recording studio, which is called Little Mountain Sound by the way, into one of the greatest recording studios in North America." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkSKxm_U9Yc
**Early Career and Transition to Producer** : Bruce Fairbairn started as a trumpet player in the Canadian 1970s band Prism. He began producing Prism's records in 1978 because the band could not afford an external producer, quickly gaining a reputation for his production skills. "He began as a trumpet player and a Canadian band from the 70s called Prism because the group couldn't afford a real producer, Bruce began producing Prism's records in 1978." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkSKxm_U9Yc
**Extensive Production Success** : Throughout the 1980s and 1990s, Fairbairn produced mega-platinum albums for numerous major rock bands, including Aerosmith, AC/DC, Bon Jovi, INXS, Kiss, Loverboy, Poison, The Scorpions, and Van Halen, accumulating close to 100 million album sales. "Throughout the 80s and 90s, he produced mega platinum albums for Erasmith and ACDC and Bon Jovi and Inaccess and Kiss and Leverboy and Poison, The Scorpions and Van Halen and a ton of others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkSKxm_U9Yc
**Mentor to Bob Rock** : Bruce Fairbairn mentored Bob Rock, who started as an assistant producer at Fairbairn's Little Mountain Sound studio and later became his protégé. "Eventually, Bob started taking on his own clients and now his resume reads like some kind of who's who. ... And he got to where he is today thanks to the guidance and mentoring he received from another Canadian Superproducer, the late Bruce Fairburn." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkSKxm_U9Yc
