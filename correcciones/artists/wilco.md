## songs
**Record Labels (2011) – Nick Lowe Cover** : This song is a cover of a Nick Lowe track from 1991. Wilco released their version in 2011 on DBPM Records, their own independent company. They received marketing assistance from Anti-Records, which is a sister label to Epitaph Records, and Anti-Records has a distribution deal with Republic Records, a sublabel of the Universal Music Group.
