# artist - Neil Diamond

## curiosities
**John Wayne Gacy's Musical Preference** : John Wayne Gacy, a serial killer with at least 33 victims, was noted to be a fan of Neil Diamond, among other artists. This detail provides insight into the musical preferences of a notorious criminal. "John Wayne Gacy... was into Elton John, Bob Dylan, Neil Diamond, and Aureo Speedwagon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FtGJHhqXcDI
**Affected by Sony BMG Rootkit (2005)** : Albums by Neil Diamond were among those shipped with the Sony BMG Rootkit malware (XCP and MediaMax) installed. This software was surreptitiously placed on users' computers when they attempted to rip CDs, compromising system security without their consent. "The XCP malware came on albums ranging from Neil Diamond to Frank Sinatra to Celine Dion to Cindy Lopper." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vJszsJQpINQ ← neil-diamond
**Bubblegum Music Songwriter** : Neil Diamond is recognized as one of Pop's best writers, and he contributed his songwriting talents to the Bubblegum Music genre. His involvement highlights the quality of songwriting within Bubblegum music. "Some of Pop's best writers wrote songs for Bubblegum, Neil Diamond comes to mind." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 30 - The Sweet Sound of 70s Bubblegum Music | https://seventies.libsyn.com/030-bubblegum
**Zero DB Initiative Signatory** : Neil Diamond is among the artists who signed the Zero DB initiative, established in 2008 by the charity Reprieve. This initiative encourages condemnation of music as torture and advocates for governments to enforce international treaties related to prisoner treatment, such as the Convention Against Torture. "The Sinees include Tom Morello, average against the machine, members of REM, rise against Pearl Jam, Trent Reznor, Limp Biscuit, system of a down, disturbed the estate of Prince, the Chili Peppers, Eminem, Marilyn Manson, Neil Diamond, Dr. Dre, and a host of others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1wdsGt07Gg ← neil-diamond
