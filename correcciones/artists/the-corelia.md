## albums
**Divorce at High Noon (1997)** : This record was released in 1997 by The Corelia, a band Alex Kapranos (then known as Alex Huntly) was part of in the mid-90s. The style and structure of their music, particularly the unexpected and dramatic tempo changes, foreshadowed characteristics later found in Franz Ferdinand's work. ## songs
