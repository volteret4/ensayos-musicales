**Desert Session Projects – Produced by Eleven Members** : Josh Homme's Desert Session projects received production assistance from Elaine Johannes and Natasha Schneider of the band Eleven, following their work on Chris Cornell's solo album. This collaboration brought Johannes and Schneider into contact with members from various other prominent bands, including A Perfect Circle, Nine Inch Nails, Danzig, Marilyn Manson's band, and PJ Harvey. **Early Polka Guitar Lessons** : At the age of nine, when Josh Homme first began learning to play the guitar, he took polka lessons. This unexpected musical foundation precedes his later fame as a guitarist known for his distinct rock style.
