## curiosities
**Asteroid** : Eric Clapton has a piece of space rock, an asteroid, named after him. "Eric Clapton, Brian Wilson, ZZ Topp, Yes, Bruce Springsteen, and Jimmy Page are among those with their own pieces of space rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMPLplhDk8I
**Early Adopter of Wah-Wah Pedal** : Eric Clapton, described as a "hot new player from the UK," was one of the two influential guitarists Henry Goldrich convinced to purchase a wah-wah pedal at Manie's Music in the mid-1960s. His adoption of the pedal was instrumental in shaping his playing and the broader sound of guitar-based rock and roll. "One was up and coming guitarist named Jimmy Hendrix. And then there was Eric Clapton, a hot new player from the UK. They each bought, okay, this thing sounds cool. Give me one." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
