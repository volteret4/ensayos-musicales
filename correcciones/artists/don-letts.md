## curiosities
**Pioneer DJ in Early Punk Scene** : Don Letts, a member of Big Audio Dynamite with Mick Jones of The Clash, was deeply involved in the early punk scene as a DJ. He played dub and reggae records in clubs, introducing these new genres and sounds to the pioneers of the punk era, marking a significant first in musical cross-pollination.
