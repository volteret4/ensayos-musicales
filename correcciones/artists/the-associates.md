## curiosities
**Alan Rankine Death (January 2, 2023) – Heart Disease, Age 64; Launched Snow Patrol, Belle & Sebastian, Biffy Clyro via Electric Honey Records** : Alan Rankine co-founded the Associates with Billy MacKenzie in Dundee, Scotland in 1979 — evolving out of post-punk bands and following a Bowie-like direction. A former competitive tennis player (too short at 5'8" to go professional), Rankine also worked as a production collaborator with the Cocteau Twins. After the Associates broke up, he became a lecturer at Stow College in Glasgow teaching music production, and helped students set up a record label called Electric Honey, which launched Snow Patrol, Belle & Sebastian, and Biffy Clyro. He was diagnosed with heart disease in the 2020s and died at home with family on January 2, 2023, at age 64.
