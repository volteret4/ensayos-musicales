## curiosities
**Lou Reed's Earliest Band** : The Shades was Lewis Allen Reed's (Lou Reed's) earliest band, and they focused on the Doo-wop genre popular at the time. "Lewis's earliest band was The Shades and they were into the duop of the day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
**Lou Reed's First Band with a Recording (1958)** : The Shades were a Dubock group that Lou Reed was a part of in 1958 when he was 14 years old, marking his entry into the recording business. This high school band stood out by releasing a single, which included Lou's first recorded performance. "What made The Shades different was the fact that they recorded a single in 1958." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zWNzlSxrj6s
