**Grizzled Veteran Participant** : The Alarm, a Welsh band whose origins trace back to the mid-1970s, was one of the "grizzled veterans" who participated in the Warped Tour. "The alarm, the Welsh band, who's would go back to the middle of the 70s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Mike Peters Death (2025) – Cancer Activist and Everest Base Camp Concert** : Mike Peters, energetic frontman of Welsh band The Alarm, died in 2025. A multiple cancer survivor, he founded the Love Hope Strength Foundation for cancer research and patient support. In 2007 he led an expedition to Everest Base Camp for the highest-altitude concert ever performed on land, raising cancer research funds. "In 2007, he led an expedition to Everest Base Camp for the concert at the highest altitude ever performed on land." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZPlsR7_WiZQ
