**Early Career and Persona** : Susan Dietrich, known as The Space Lady, is from Pueblo, Colorado. For many years, she was a street musician, frequently performing in cities like Boston and San Francisco. She was easily recognizable by her unique outfit: a silver plastic helmet adorned with white wings and a flashing light. She initially played the accordion. "She wore a silver plastic helmet adorned with white wings and a flashing light on top. And she played the accordion." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Musical Evolution** : In the 1980s, she transitioned from playing the accordion to using a battery-powered synthesizer for her performances. "In the 80s, she moved from the accordion to a battery-powered synthesizer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
