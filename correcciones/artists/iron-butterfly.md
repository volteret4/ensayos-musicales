# artist - Iron Butterfly

## albums
**In-A-Gadda-Da-Vida (1976) - First Platinum Album Award Recipient** : Iron Butterfly was awarded the first Platinum Record for their album "In-A-Gadda-Da-Vida" in 1976. This award was introduced when the RIAA reformalized its sales certifications, with Platinum signifying sales of one million copies in the United States.
