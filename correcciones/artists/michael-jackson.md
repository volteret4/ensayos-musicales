**Black or White (1991)** : The music video for "Black or White" premiered in November 1991 and was a massive cultural event, seen simultaneously by 500 million people across MTV, VH1, BET, and the new fourth TV network, Fox. Directed by Hollywood director John Landis, the video came with a Dolby surround mix at a time when most TVs were still in mono, and its clever morphing visual effects were widely discussed for weeks or months. Its total cost was about $4 million in 1991, equivalent to about $9.2 million today, and it helped the *Dangerous* album sell 32 million copies. "When the video for Michael Jackson's Blocker White premiered in November 1991, it was seen simultaneously by 500 million people thanks to MTV, VH1, BET, and the brand new fourth TV network, Fox." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7IDbqdIw3WU
