## curiosities
**First Commercial CD Release (1982)** : On August 17, 1982, Claudio Arrau's performance of Chopin waltzes became the first commercial compact disk ever produced. Arrau was personally invited to the German pressing plant to press the start button for the manufacturing of his disks, marking a historic moment for the new format. `"The first commercial compact disk was produced on August 17, 1982.
