## curiosities
**First Rock and Roll Era Musician Murdered** : Sam Cooke, a renowned soul singer, was murdered on December 11, 1964. He sustained a gunshot wound to the chest when Bertha Franklin, the manager of the Hossie End of Motel on South Central Los Angeles, claimed he attacked her, asserting it was in self-defense. Despite her claim, many questions about the case persist even today.
