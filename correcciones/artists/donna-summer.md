## songs
**I Feel Love (1977)** : Produced by Giorgio Moroder, "I Feel Love" was an extraordinarily big hit in 1977. While rooted in disco, the song was distinctive for its robotic and futuristic sound, driven by Moroder's experimentation with new synthesizers. It proved to be extremely inspirational for musicians exploring synths and electronic music.
