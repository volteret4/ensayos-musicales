## curiosities
**Pioneer of Bebop Jazz** : Sonny Rollins was among the musicians who developed bebop, a complex and experimental form of jazz focusing on virtuosity and improvisation. This style represented a departure from the rigid structures of big band jazz and appealed primarily to fellow musicians rather than dancers.
