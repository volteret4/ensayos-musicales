## concerts
- **Dingwalls (July 6th, 1976, London)** : The Damned were present at this Ramones concert, which, along with The Roundhouse show, provided critical inspiration for them to become the first British punk band to release a record. "the damn who would become the first British punk band to release a record"
- **The 100 Club Punk Rock Festival (September 20th & 21st, 1976, London)** : The Damned performed at Britain's first official punk rock festival. This event was crucial for establishing the UK punk scene, attracting both a devoted youth audience and industry professionals. "when people heard about these shows featuring the sex pistols and the clash and the dammed and a bunch of others, the lines extended around the block."
- **The Roundhouse (July 4th, 1976, London)** : The Damned attended this Ramones gig, where they were exposed to US-style punk rock and inspired by the performance that ignited the British punk scene. "the damn who would become the first British punk band to release a record"

## songs
**New Rose (1976)** : "New Rose" by The Damned was released on October 22nd, 1976, and holds the distinction of being the first punk rock single ever released in the UK. It was also the inaugural release from Stiff Records, which was dedicated to promoting DIY punk. "New Rose from the damned. The first punk rock single ever released in the UK, October 22nd, 1976." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
