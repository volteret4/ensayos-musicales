## curiosities
**Contemporary Goth-ish Sound** : Interpol, hailing from New York, is considered one of the best-known contemporary bands that, while "Goth or at least Goth-ish," seeks to preserve the spirit of 1979 Goth. "Interpol, one of the best known of the contemporary Goth or at least Goth-ish bands that seek to keep alive the spirit of 1979." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=evlGhfUe6QQ
**Formation and Early Comparisons** : Interpol formed in New York in 1997 and, upon their emergence, were immediately likened to influential bands from two decades prior, such as Joy Division, Television, The Doors, and Lou Reed, establishing their sound within a specific lineage of alt rock. "They had come together in New York in 1997 and were immediately compared to bands we'd been around 20 years earlier, Joy Division, Television, even the Doors and Lou Reed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo
**Indie Rock Revival (2000s)** : Interpol is mentioned as one of the "fresh sounding bands" that contributed to the Indie Rock Revival in the early 2000s, fostering a renewed public interest in diverse musical offerings. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
**Influence on Vestal Watch Design** : Interpol's music played a significant and direct role in the design process for Vestal watches. The band's tracks were reportedly listened to on a continuous loop for a couple of months by Vestal designers while they were developing new watch models, indicating a deep artistic connection. "And by the way, Interpol's music is a big part of the design process at Vestel. They listened to the band's music on a loop for a couple of months when they were putting together these watches." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mhQ36Y-c9fM
**Part of Indy Rock Revival** : Interpol was explicitly named as a band that, alongside The Strokes, The White Stripes, the Yeah Yeah Yeahs, The Vines, The Hives, and Arcade Fire, was a firm part of the Indy Rock Revival in the late 2003/early 2004 period. "The strokes were firmly part of the Indy Rock Revival that also included the white stripes, the yay-yayas, interpole, the vines, the hives, and this new band called Arcade Fire." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jEfofyobGiI
**Post-Cure Influence** : Interpol was cited as a group influenced by The Cure, reflecting the enduring legacy of the Goth band in contemporary music. "if you're looking for bands influenced by them we can turn to groups like Interpol, Smashing Pumpkins, The Killers, Nine-H Nails, My Chemical Romance, and so many more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
**Vintage Custom-Tailored Suits** : Interpol adopts a distinctive formal look by acquiring vintage suits and having them custom-tailored for each member. While the suits may not perfectly match, their collective formal appearance is a key part of their visual identity. "Other bands into the uniform look, Interpol, who buy vintage suits and have them custom tailored. They don't necessarily match, but they all look very formal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mhQ36Y-c9fM
