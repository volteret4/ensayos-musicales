## genres

## songs
**Good Rockin' Tonight (1947)** : Roy Brown, a Black blues singer from Louisiana, released this hit song in 1947. Along with Wynonie Harris's version, this track helped move the notion of "rockin'" as a musical concept deeper into the public consciousness.
