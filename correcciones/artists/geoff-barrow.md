# artist - Geoff Barrow

## member of
- Portishead
- Massive Attack

## curiosities
**Early Work with Massive Attack** : Geoff Barrow worked with Massive Attack on their seminal album *Blue Lines* and also contributed to parts of *Protection* before later founding the band Portishead. "Jeff Barrow que después montaría portis jez que trabajó con ellos simplemente en blue lines y parte en el protection." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack - Blue Lines. Un antes y un después en la música. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ik7PQ2X1o3M&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← geoff-barrow
**Founded Portishead** : Barrow was so profoundly amazed by the sound being created in the studio during the *Blue Lines* sessions that he was inspired to found Portishead shortly thereafter. "Jeop Barrow quedó tan alucinado con el sonido que se estaba gestando en ese estudio, que fundó Portishead un poco más adelante." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack y El Origen del Trip Hop en Bristol. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bpOr4KKm5rM&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← geoff-barrow
**Production Assistant during Blue Lines** : Geoff Barrow was a young and exceptionally talented production assistant during the recording of Massive Attack's *Blue Lines*. At the time, he was also a musician just beginning his career.
