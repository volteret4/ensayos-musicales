# artist - John Lindell

## songs
**Stay, the Second Time Around (1980s)** : This song was given the placeholder name "On the Roof" during a prolonged search, estimated to be from the 1980s based on its instrumentation. It remained unidentified until 2013 when a Swedish radio station played it, leading to its identification. "The actual name of the song is Stay, the second time around by a sweet name John Lindell." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw

## curiosities
**Unaware of Search** : John Lindell, the artist behind "Stay, the Second Time Around," had long moved on from music by the time his song was identified in 2013. He had no idea that so many people had been actively searching for his track. "He had long moved on from music and had no idea that so many people were concerned about finding a song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw
