# artist - Sasha

## curiosities
**Early Screamo Influence** : Sasha was one of the bands mentioned by Dallas Green as an influence for the early "screamo" sound that helped inform Alexis on Fire's distinct style, part of a collection of lesser-known acts that band members sought out in their youth. "I was kind of more the deep head early first-screamo stuff like where I was listening to bands like Sasha, Love Lost, Penoffa, Godden, these are all different." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
