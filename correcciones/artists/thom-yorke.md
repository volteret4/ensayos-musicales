## albums
**The Eraser (Year not specified, but likely 2006)** : This solo album was constructed from small musical ideas and sounds Yorke had collected on his laptop during various writing sessions, including moments while on tour with Radiohead in hotel rooms. He and Radiohead producer Nigel Godrich sorted these accumulated bits, arranged them into pieces, and then Yorke wrote melodies, lyrics, and added vocals, with some drums and guitar later. The album was primarily computer-generated and minimalist, but performed well commercially. "The eraser album was constructed from bits and pieces gathered off the floor during various writing sessions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**A Moon Shaped Pool** : This album, part of Yorke's solo career, eventually became available on Spotify despite his initial resistance.
