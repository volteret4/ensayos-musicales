## songs
**Rock Around the Clock (1954) – Cultural Impact** : Bill Haley and His Comets' 1954 recording of "Rock Around the Clock" gained immense cultural significance, particularly after being featured over the opening and closing credits of the 1955 movie *Blackboard Jungle*. Initially, its release did not cause much stir, but its association with the film, especially in Britain in 1956, ignited a phenomenon of rebellious audience behavior in theaters, including dancing in aisles, seat carving, fighting, and turning firehoses on each other. This led to widespread condemnation of rock and roll music.
