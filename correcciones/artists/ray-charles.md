**Early Rock and Roll Piano Hero** : Ray Charles was an early pioneer of rock and roll, considered one of the "piano heroes" who helped define the genre in its formative years. His piano style was instrumental in blending various musical influences, including jazz and rhythm and blues, into rock and roll. "Instead, the early pioneers were piano heroes, Little Richard, Jerry Lee Lewis, Fats Domino, Ray Charles, Huey Piano Smith." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g
