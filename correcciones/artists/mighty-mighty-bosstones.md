# artist - Mighty Mighty Bosstones

## curiosities
**Mighty Mighty Bosstones – Formed 1983 Boston; Invented Skacore by Mixing Two-Tone with Hardcore; Major Label by 1993** : The Mighty Mighty Bosstones came together in 1983 when a group of guys became obsessed with the Two-Tone sound despite some members playing in hardcore bands influenced by Motorhead and AC/DC. This combination of Two-Tone ska with American hardcore became known as skacore — annoying to ska purists and initially baffling to hardcore kids, but eventually it clicked. By 1993 they had a major label record deal and a signature look involving plaid suits. "The Boss Tones took two-tone style Skah and made it with American hardcore to create something that was eventually called Skahcore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yqo-YDJR0nM
