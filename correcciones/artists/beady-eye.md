## curiosities
**Formation Post-Oasis Split** : After Oasis dissolved, Liam Gallagher formed Beady Eye, retaining the other former Oasis members Gem Archer, Andy Bell, and drummer Chris Sharrock. Beady Eye was the first of the Gallagher brothers' post-Oasis projects to release new music. "When Oasis broke up, it was Liam who retained custody of the rest of the band. The guitarist, Gammart, Jury Nandy Bell, along with drummer Chris Shorock stayed with him. He called the band BDI and they were the first out of the gate with new music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bB7GAUM63Y
**One More Album Before Dissolution** : Beady Eye released one additional album after "Different Gear Still Speeding" before Liam Gallagher decided to fold the band and launch a full-on solo career. "There was one more BDI album before Liam folded that tent with an idle launching of a full-on solo career." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bB7GAUM63Y
