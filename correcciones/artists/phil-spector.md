# artist - Phil Spector

## curiosities
**Wall of Sound Production Method** : Phil Spector was known for his immersive production through his "Wall of Sound" methods, developed in the early 1960s. These techniques typically resulted in upbeat, high-energy, and danceable songs. When working with George Harrison, however, he adapted this method to a more subdued style. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
