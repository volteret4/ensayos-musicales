# artist - Nat King Cole Trio

## albums
**King Cole Trio (1945)** : This album, issued by Capitol Records in 1945, was an "old school" album containing four 78 RPM records tucked into sleeves. It was one of the biggest early successes demonstrating the value of album artwork, spending most of its year at number one on the charts. Its commercial performance convinced the industry of the power of decorated packaging. "One of the biggest early successes was the King Cole trio. It was an album from that King Cole, issued by Capital Records in 1945. It was an old school album with 478 RPM records tucked into the sleeves. When it spent most of its year at number one on the charts, everybody was convinced about the value of artwork." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pwebM3TfSdg
