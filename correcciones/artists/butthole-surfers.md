## curiosities
**Alleged Introduction to Heroin** : Gibby Haynes of the Butthole Surfers was reportedly the individual who introduced Scott Wyland to heroin. "The first of those junkies was Gibby Haynes of the Butthole Surfers, the guy who apparently introduced Scott to smack in the first place." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TDwagoyMvck
**Dog Named Mark Farner** : Gibby Haynes, a member of the Butthole Surfers, owned a pit bull named Mark Farner, honoring the guitarist from Grand Funk Railroad. "Gibby Haynes and the Butthole Surfers had a pit bull named Mark Farner after the guy in Grandfell Grille Road." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**Exodus Recovery Center Stay (Spring 1994)** : Gibby Haynes was present at the Exodus Recovery Center in Marina del Rey during spring 1994, at the same time Scott Wyland was considering checking into the facility for detox, leading to Wyland's decision not to check in. "There are two major junkies already on the premises and that will not help you kick your habit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TDwagoyMvck
**Lollapalooza 1991 Lineup** : Butthole Surfers were part of the lineup for the first Lollapalooza festival in the summer of 1991. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Lollapalooza 1991 Performer** : The Butthole Surfers performed at the first Lollapalooza tour in the summer of 1991, which, by drawing upwards of 20,000 attendees for a lineup of non-mainstream bands, signaled a shift in music tastes towards alternative acts. "Along with founding band Jane's addiction, the line up included Suzy and the band sheets, living color, iced tea and body count, butthole surfers, Rawens band and some weirdo group that was never going to go anywhere called Nine-inch Nails." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2wZydttn4Cs
**Tour with Stone Temple Pilots** : Stone Temple Pilots opted to tour with the Butthole Surfers in June and July of 1993, turning down an offer to open for Aerosmith due to genre differences. It was during this tour that their singer, Gibby Haynes, allegedly introduced Scott Weiland to heroin. "So they turned that opportunity down in favor of a tour with, wait for it, the Butthole Surfers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r6vVw66-gmI
