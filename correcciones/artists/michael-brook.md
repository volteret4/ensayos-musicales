## curiosities
**Inventor of Infinite Sustain Guitar** : Michael Brook, hailing from a family of scientists, invented and constructed the "infinite sustain guitar" in Chinatown, Toronto. He developed this instrument after experimenting with E-Bows and built three units: one for himself, one for The Edge of U2, and one for Daniel Landwom. "the infinite sustain guitar was born in Chinatown in Toronto by an old friend, a Michael Brook." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L03Yk1SHRNM
