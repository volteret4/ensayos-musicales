## curiosities
**First British Technopop Billboard Top Ten Hit** : A Flock of Seagulls achieved a significant milestone by being the first British Technopop band to break into the top ten of the Billboard singles chart, likely during the winter of 1981-82. This success was attributed not only to the song itself but also heavily to the extensive MTV play of its video and singer Mike Score's distinctive swoopy haircut. "As far as anyone can tell, this song was the first hit by a British Technopop band to reach the top ten of the Billboard singles chart. The song is fine, but it was MTV play of the video and singer Mike Scors' swoopy haircut that really made that happen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
