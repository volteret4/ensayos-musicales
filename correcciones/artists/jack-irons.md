## curiosities
**Drummer for The Wallflowers** : Jack Irons, a former drummer for the Red Hot Chili Peppers and Pearl Jam, was one of the musicians who cycled through the lineup of The Wallflowers. "People to cycle through the band have included former chili peppers and pro jam drummer Jack Irons." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Quitting Red Hot Chili Peppers (1988)** : Jack Irons, the drummer for the Red Hot Chili Peppers, quit the band following the death of guitarist Hillel Slovak from a heroin overdose in June 1988, stating he "couldn't take it anymore." His departure left Flea and Anthony Kiedis to decide the band's future. "drummer Jack Irons couldn't take it anymore, so he quit, leaving just flea and anthony to do something, or not." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
