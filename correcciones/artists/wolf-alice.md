## curiosities
**Advocacy for Women in Rock** : Ellie Rowsell is a vocal advocate for increasing female representation in rock and roll, stating that "There are many aspects of life that need more women in them and rock and roll is certainly one." She actively encourages more women to embrace being rock stars. "This woman not only loves to rock out, but she also wants to share the experience. ... She wants more women to dare to be rock stars. Here's a quote from Ellie. There are many aspects of life that need more women in them and rock and roll is certainly one." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Inspiring Young Musicians** : Ellie actively inspires young women to play guitar; for example, at one gig, a 15-year-old girl named Jade played her guitar part on stage and "wowed the audience." Rowsell notes that every night, a young woman approaches her, crediting her for inspiring them to pick up the guitar. "And so when Gignette came around, a 15-year-old girl named Jade came up on stage, took Ellie's guitar and wowed the audience. ... Ellie says that every night some young woman comes up to her and says, hey, it's because of you I'm playing the guitar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
