**First All-Female Hard Rock Band with International Attention** : The Runaways, anchored by 15-year-old Sandy West on drums, are identified as the first all-female hard rock band in history to gain international attention. The group featured future stars like Joan Jett, Lita Ford, and Mickey Steele, along with Cherie Currie and Jackie Fox. "We can safely call them the first all-female hard rock band in history to receive international attention." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY
**Mismanagement and Industry Sexism** : The Runaways faced significant challenges due to poor management and marketing, including controversial advertising slogans like "Jailbait on the run." The prevailing sexist attitude that "girls couldn't rock" ultimately contributed to their downfall, alongside internal issues and drug problems. "Unfortunately, The Runaways were managed and marketed badly. Jailbait on the run was one advertising slogan that was used. And the sexist attitude that girls couldn't rock doomed them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY
**Sandy West's Early Death** : Sandy West, the drummer for The Runaways, died in 2006 at the age of 47 from lung cancer. Her early passing marked a tragic end for a pioneering figure in hard rock drumming. "And then Sandy died in 2006 of lung cancer at the age of 47." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY
