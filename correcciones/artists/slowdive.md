## curiosities
**Example of Shoegaze-leaning Dreampop** : Slowdive is highlighted as a band that exemplifies how Dreampop can "leak into the Shoegaze world." Their music is characterized by atmosphere, textures, reverb, and echo, but with a potentially more serious attitude and increased volume compared to quieter Dreampop acts, bridging the two genres. "Yes, Dreampop can be quiet like the cocktail twins and Mazzy Star, but it can also leak into the Shougay's world and touch on groups like Slow Dive and My Bloody Valentine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
**Post-Rock Evolution** : Initially a shoegaze band, Slowdive's later material began to incorporate elements of post-rock, demonstrating an overlap between the two genres. "Slow dive, definitely Shoegaze, but their later stuff was also very post-rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
**Shoegaze Band Example** : Slowdive was mentioned as an example of a Shoegaze band, a loud, fuzzy, buzzy form of guitar rock that emerged from the Thames River Valley in the very late 80s and early 90s. "There were bands like Slow Dive, Chapter House, Curve, Lush, My Bloody Valentine, Adorable, The Catherine Wheel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
