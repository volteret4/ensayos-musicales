## curiosities
**Rabat Concert Stampede (May 23, 2009)** : A free concert by Abdulaziz Stati in Haynata Stadium in Rabat, Morocco, on May 23, 2009, attracted approximately 70,000 people. As other nearby performances concluded, the large influx of attendees converging on the stadium resulted in a stampede, during which a wire fence collapsed, trapping eight individuals. This incident ultimately led to 11 deaths by asphyxiation and nearly a dozen injuries. "The attraction was a free concert by Abdulaziz Stati." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
