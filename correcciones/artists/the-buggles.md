# artist - The Buggles

## songs
**Video Killed The Radio Star** : This song was notably played as MTV, after 44 years and 5 months since its launch on August 1, 1981, shut down the last of its global music video-only channels at 6 am GMT on December 31, 2025. The song marked both the beginning and the end of MTV's pure music video era. **Video Killed the Radio Star (1981) - First Video on MTV** : "Video Killed the Radio Star" by The Buggles was the inaugural music video aired on MTV when the channel launched in 1981. This iconic broadcast ushered in a new era of music consumption and presentation, profoundly impacting the industry.
