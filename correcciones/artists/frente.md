# artist - Frente!

## genres

## albums
**Marvin the Album (International Version)** : A special international reissue of Frente!'s debut album, created after the original domestic version went platinum in Australia. This international version reordered songs, subbed out old ones for new, and featured only two songs from the Australian release. Three songs, including their cover of "Bizarre Love Triangle," appear exclusively on this version. "The album was reissued with the songs reordered and you went subbed out for old ones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcF5JMhBj2M

## songs
**Bizarre Love Triangle (1992)** : Frente!'s cover of New Order's song, released in 1992, was included on the international version of their debut album, "Marvin the Album." It did not perform well in the UK or on American singles charts, but was a top 10 hit at Alternative Radio and a sizable alt-rock hit in Canada. It offered a "quick bit of sweetness" as Grunge music was exploding in late 1992, serving as a counterpoint to the prevailing sound. As of February 2026, it had 37.5 million Spotify plays and 3.7 million YouTube views, totaling 41.3 million, with a One Hit Wonder Power Rating of 3.69 out of 10. "One of those is their cover of New Order's bizarre love triangle." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcF5JMhBj2M
