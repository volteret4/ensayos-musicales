# artist - Terry Marshall

## curiosities
**Name in JTM45** : Terry Marshall is Jim Marshall's son, and his initial is represented by the "T" in the iconic JTM45 amplifier model name. This signifies a family connection to the groundbreaking amplifier's origin. "Well, the J is for Jim, the T is for his son Terry, and the M is for Marshall, of course." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
