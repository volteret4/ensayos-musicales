## curiosities
**Genuine Musical Genius** : Grandmaster Flash is recognized as one of the "genuine stars, genuine musical geniuses" of the early rap and hip-hop scene, whose contributions were not always taken seriously by the wider music market. "It didn't matter that there were genuine stars, genuine musical geniuses. One DMC, Curtis Blow, Africa Babata, Grandmaster Flash, LL Cool J, the scene and the sounds were just not taken seriously." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l1jPP1FhGo8
**Pioneers of Rap/Hip Hop** : Grandmaster Flash was a key figure who emerged from the nascent rap and hip hop scene on the streets of New York, where DJs developed techniques of "turntableism" by chopping and manipulating records to create new sounds for MCs to rhyme over. "This is the culture that gave us run DMC and Grandmaster Flash." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
