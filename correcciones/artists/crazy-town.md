## songs
**Butterfly (2001) - Sampled Hit** : This song was Crazy Town's massive hit in 2001, reaching number one on several charts, including the Billboard Hot 100, and charting top 10 in Canada, the UK, and Australia. It sold over a million copies and was one of the first one-hit wonders of the 2000s. The song was based on a sample from the Red Hot Chili Peppers' track "Pretty Little Ditty," leading to all four members of the Chili Peppers receiving songwriting credit. "It was based on a sample from a Red Hot Chili Pepper song called Pretty Little Ditty, which explains why all four members of the Chili's get a songwriting credit. It hit number one on a series of charts, including the Billboard Hot 100, and was also a top 10 hit in Canada, the UK, and Australia. It sold over a million copies, and was one of the first one hit wonders of the 2000s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HBS24NwhrE8

## curiosities
**Ozzfest Ejection (Reason for)** : The band was reportedly kicked off the Ozzfest tour due to frontman Shifty Shellshock's excessive drinking. "Things got so bad at one point that he managed to get his band kicked off the Oz Fest tour for being too much of a drunk." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HBS24NwhrE8
**Tour Van Collision with Moose** : The rap-rock group Crazy Town narrowly avoided serious injury when their tour van collided with a moose near Sudbury on Halloween night. The impact totaled the van and killed the moose, with neither surviving. "The van was totaled, so was the moose. Neither survived." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
