## curiosities
**Use of Devil's Triad** : Beethoven, a seminal classical composer, is cited among numerous others who incorporated the "devil's tri tone" (flattened fifth) into his compositions. This demonstrates that dissonant chords, often later associated with "evil" or "devil's music," were historically employed by revered classical musicians for expressive purposes.
