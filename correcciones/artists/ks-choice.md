## songs
**Not An Addict (1995)** : A song that started as a local hit in Belgium, then became regional, and spread internationally through 1996 after being picked up by American label 550 Music (part of the Sony Empire). It caught fire in North America at the end of the Grunge era, though five of the album's six singles went nowhere in North America. The lyrics were written by Sarah Bettens, better known as Sam in the 90s, and are primarily about addiction, specifically cigarettes, though Bettens was also experimenting with acid and mushrooms at the time. As of February 2026, it had approximately 27 million Spotify plays and 7.5 million YouTube views, totaling 38.4 million, with a One Hit Wonder Power Ranking of 3.36 out of 10. "Yes, it's about addiction, primarily cigarettes, actually." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcF5JMhBj2M
