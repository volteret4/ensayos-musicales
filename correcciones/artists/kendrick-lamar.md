**To Pimp a Butterfly (2015) - Defining Album of the 21st Century** : This album is widely considered by critics to be the most important album of the 21st century. Its gestation began in 2013 during Kenny West's famous Jesus tour, which Kendrick Lamar participated in. The creation of the album involved a complex idea from Kendrick Lamar, who dedicated two years to traveling the world to find the right collaborators. He acted as a "master of ceremonies," bringing together over 100 artists, with 65 officially credited, to contribute to his clear vision for the album. The album transcends traditional rap, incorporating Jazz, Funk, deep Soul, Avant-Garde, and Reggae, synthesizing 50 years of musical genius into just over an hour of music.
