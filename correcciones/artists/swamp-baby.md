## songs
**Who the Hell Do You Think You Are (1996)** : This song was credited to Hugh Dillon (who played the character Joe Dick) and Swamp Baby, and it won a Genie Award for Best Achievement in Music, Original Song. The music was created by Swamp Baby based on lyrics found in the book upon which the movie "Hardcore Logo" was based. "The tracking question was, who the hell do you think you are." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yl2poVWguu0

## curiosities
**Formation for "Hardcore Logo" Movie** : Swamp Baby was a real Toronto band specifically assembled for the 1996 movie "Hardcore Logo" by Peter Moore of the Cowboy Junkies. They wrote music for the film based on the lyrics found in Michael Turner's book, which served as the movie's source material. "Swamp Baby was real. They were a Toronto band pulled together just for the movie by Peter Moore of the Cowboy Junkeys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yl2poVWguu0
**Limited Public Output** : Beyond their contributions to the "Hardcore Logo" soundtrack, there is no other acknowledged music released by Swamp Baby. "Is there anything else out there by them? Not that anyone is willing to admit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yl2poVWguu0
