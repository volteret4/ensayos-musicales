## curiosities
**Precursor to Oasis (1991)** : Rain was the band Liam Gallagher was in prior to the formation of Oasis. After a Rain gig in 1991, Liam and his bandmates invited Noel Gallagher to become their manager, but Noel instead joined as lead guitarist, leading to the band's metamorphosis into Oasis.
