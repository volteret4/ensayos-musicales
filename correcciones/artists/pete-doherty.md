**Dolby Cafe Breakfast Challenge (August 21, 2018)** : On August 21, 2018, in Cliftonville, Margate, the "very skinny" Pete Doherty, who is six feet three inches tall, successfully completed the Dolby Cafe's "eating challenge" breakfast.
