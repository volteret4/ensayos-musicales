## curiosities
**Times Square Soundtrack Contribution (1980)** : Suzi Quatro contributed to the soundtrack for the 1980 movie *Time Square*. This soundtrack was significant for being among the earliest compilations to feature the nascent alternative music of the day, which included punk rock and new wave.
