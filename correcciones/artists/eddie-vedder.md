**Legal Dispute over "Hard Sun" Cover (2010)** : Despite not being legally required to ask for permission to cover a song, Eddie Vedder was sued by the original composer, Gordon Peterson, for allegedly not seeking permission and for "eroding the integrity" of the song by changing some lyrics. The case went to court until April 2010, when a judge ruled that Vedder did not materially infringe on Peterson's song. "The case wound its way through the courts until April 2010 when a judge ruled that Eddie did not materially infringe on Peterson's song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
