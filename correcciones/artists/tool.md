**Lachrymology – Tool's Invented Pseudo-Science of Crying** : Tool promotes a fake philosophy called "lachrymology" (the study of crying), supposedly derived from a 1940s book called *A Joyful Guide to Lachrymology* by one Ronald P. Vincent — a former crop duster turned philosopher after his wife was "dismembered in a bizarre snowplow accident." The only source for the existence of this book and its author is Tool themselves, who have never confirmed or denied the hoax. The core thesis — that humans cannot evolve emotionally, spiritually, or mentally without experiencing pain, and that releasing repressed trauma (possibly through crying) improves mental, spiritual, and physical condition — has been elaborated by fans into something resembling Freudian psychoanalysis.
