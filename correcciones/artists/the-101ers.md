## curiosities
**London Flat** : The members of The 101ers resided together in a flat located at 101 Walter Tinteres in London. "Bunch of guys lived in a flat and 101, Walter Tinteres in London. They played in a group which they called appropriately enough the 101ers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
**Nashville Club Gig (Pre-Clash)** : The band had a gig at a club called Nashville where the Sex Pistols performed as the opening act. This event proved pivotal as their guitar player, Joe, was so impressed by the Sex Pistols' attitude and energy that he subsequently left The 101ers to form The Clash. "One night they had a gig at a club called Nashville." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
