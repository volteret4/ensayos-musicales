# artist - Timbaland

## albums
**Loose (2006) – Producer for Nelly Furtado** : Timbaland produced Nelly Furtado's "Loose" album, released in June 2006. The song "Do It" from this album became the subject of plagiarism accusations. "Then, in June 2006, Nelly Furtado releases her loose album, which was produced by Timberland." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYD_dIvYY5o

## songs
**Block Party (2005)** : This original ringtone, created by Timbaland for MTV in January 2005, sounded similar to a cover of Jan Tempest Suni's "Asad Jazzed Evening" posted online by a Norwegian programmer. "One of his ringtones is called Block Party. It sounds a lot like the cover done by the Norwegian guy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYD_dIvYY5o
**Do It (2006)** : This song, from Nelly Furtado's "Loose" album (produced by Timbaland in 2006), quickly drew accusations of plagiarism due to its similarities to Jan Tempest Suni's "Asad Jazzed Evening." "Almost immediately, some people began to hear similarities between the song Do It and Sunni's Asad Jazzed Evening, which is when the accusations of plagiarism began to fly." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYD_dIvYY5o

## curiosities
**Creation of MTV Ringtones (2005)** : In January 2005, Timbaland was commissioned to create original ringtones for MTV, one of which, "Block Party," bore a resemblance to a cover of Jan Tempest Suni's "Asad Jazzed Evening."
