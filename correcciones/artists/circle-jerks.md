## albums
**Group Sex (Year Unknown)** : This album from the Circle Jerks featured 14 songs that were completed in just 15 minutes and 25 seconds, demonstrating the characteristic brevity of early hardcore punk. The opening track itself was only 23 seconds long. "The first Circle Jerks album was called Group Sex, 14 songs that were done in 15 minutes in 25 seconds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEHkKfmmDiI
**Red Tape (1980) – Mosh Pit Classic** : This album, released in the fall of 1980, is referred to as a "Mosh Pit Classic." It was one of the groups featured in Penelope Spheeris's film "The Decline of Western Civilization," showcasing moshing to their music. "56 seconds of the Circle Jerks' Mosh Pit Classic Red Tape from the fall of 1980." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
