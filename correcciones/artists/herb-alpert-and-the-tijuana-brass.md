# artist - Herb Alpert and the Tijuana Brass

## curiosities
**Commissioned Sound System** : Herb Alpert and the Tijuana Brass, alongside The Beach Boys, commissioned a specialized sound system for their tours. This initiative was part of a broader trend among popular bands to address the limitations of existing amplification for live music performances in the mid-1960s. "The Beach Boys commissioned a specially built sound system for their tours, so did her bellbird and the Tijuana brass." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Second Custom Concert Sound System (1965)** : In 1965, Herb Alpert and the Tijuana Brass contracted Harry McHughan sound systems for their tour. This initiative is noted as establishing what appears to be the second dedicated concert sound system for touring artists, following The Beach Boys' earlier efforts.
