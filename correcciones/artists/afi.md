## curiosities
**924 Gilman Street Alumni** : AFI are listed among the alumni of 924 Gilman Street, the important all-ages punk club in West Berkeley, California. Due to the club's policy against major label bands, AFI, having achieved mainstream success, are no longer welcome there. "Gilman Street alumni like Green Day and the offspring and AFI and Primus and even bad religion aren't really welcome anymore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKHdTSfDd6E
