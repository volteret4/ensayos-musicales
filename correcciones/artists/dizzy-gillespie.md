## curiosities
**Pioneer of Bebop Jazz** : Dizzy Gillespie was instrumental in the creation of bebop, an experimental and complex jazz style that prioritized musical virtuosity and improvisation. This new direction in jazz was made by musicians for musicians and marked a clear break from the popular big band format. "Charlie Parker, Sonny Rollins, Dexter Gordon, Miles Davis, Dizzy Gillespie, Thelonius Monk.
