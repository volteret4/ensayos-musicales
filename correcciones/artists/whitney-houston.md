## curiosities
**Pop Dominance (Mid-1980s)** : Alongside Huey Lewis, Whitney Houston was instrumental in propelling pop music to the top of the cycle by mid-1984, leading to a period where rock seemed to have "blown itself out." "Huey Lewis and Whitney Houston propelled pop to the top of the cycle." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
**Posthumous Hologram Tour (2020)** : Two years after other hologram tours began, a Whitney Houston hologram tour was launched, featuring a live band, dancers, and more. This illustrated the increasing sophistication and production value applied to virtual performances of deceased artists, aiming to recreate a full concert experience. "Two years later, there was a Whitney Houston hologram tour that came with a live band, dancers, and more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
