## curiosities
**Pioneering Industrial Hip Hop Sound** : The Bomb Squad, Public Enemy's production team, was crucial in developing a sound in the mid-1980s that incorporated hip hop elements into beat-heavy keyboard and drum machine music, laying groundwork for Industrial Hip Hop.
