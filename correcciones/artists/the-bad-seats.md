## songs
**Release the Bats (Year Unspecified)** : This song was on heavy rotation at The Batcave club and, like Bauhaus's "Bela Lugosi's Dead," is considered a quintessential goth track. It was released a couple of years before its heavy rotation at the Batcave. "Here's a song that was on heavy rotation from the DJ booth during those Batcave nights. The Batcave was an Australian singer with a band called The Bad Seats. He'd released this song a couple of years earlier." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
