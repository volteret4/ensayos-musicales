# artist - Indio

## members

## labels

## albums
**Big Harvest (1989)** : This album was issued in 1989 on NM Records in Canada, featuring "Hard Sun" as its main single. It later went out of print and was mostly forgotten until Eddie Vedder's cover made the song popular again, leading to its reissue decades later. "In 1989, he issued a record on NM Records in Canada called Big Harvest, and its main single was a song called Hard Sun." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## songs
**Hard Sun (1989)** : Released as the main single from his 1989 album *Big Harvest*, this song became a top 10 hit in Canada. Its original lyrics included the line "When I stay to pillage her." "And its main single was a song called Hard Sun, and it became a top 10 single." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## curiosities
**Departure from Music Industry** : Gordon Peterson, who went by the name Indio, chose to walk away from the music industry after the success of *Big Harvest* and "Hard Sun" in 1989. He reportedly disliked how the industry operated, how it treated people, and how it made him feel personally. "He didn't like how it worked, how it treated people, how it made him feel. So he quit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Lawsuit against Eddie Vedder (2010)** : Gordon Peterson sued Eddie Vedder for covering "Hard Sun," claiming Vedder did not get permission and "eroded the integrity of the song" by altering some lyrics. Despite the lawsuit, a judge ruled in April 2010 that Vedder had not materially infringed on the song. "But Peterson then sued Eddie for a not getting permission to cover the song and be quote eroding the integrity of the song by changing some of the lyrics." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
