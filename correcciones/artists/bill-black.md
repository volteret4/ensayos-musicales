## curiosities
**Initial Dislike of the Precision Bass** : Bill Black, the bass player for Elvis Presley, initially harbored a strong dislike for the Fender Precision Bass. He reportedly struggled to master the instrument and found it difficult to play. "Then came Bill Black, the bass player for Elvis Presley, although it is said that he initially really, really hated the thing because he just couldn't quite get the knack of playing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
