## members

## songs
**GrooveJet (2000) - First Song Loaded into a Prototype iPod** : The Italian producer Spiller's track "GrooveJet" was the very first song transferred to a prototype iPod in Cupertino in the year 2000. This occurred during the development phase of Apple's iconic portable music device. "The track is GrooveJet and it was the first song transferred to a prototype iPod in Cupertino that year." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
**Groovejet (If This Ain't Love) (2000)** : Released in 2000 by Cristiano Spiller and his friends, this single was named after a nightclub in Miami. According to those present on the Apple campus in Cupertino, California, this song was the very first to be transferred from CD to MP3 and then onto a prototype iPod. "According to those who were there on the Apple campus in Cupertino, California, this song was the first to be transferred from CD to MP3 to a prototype iPod." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fhq9WVeiE_U
