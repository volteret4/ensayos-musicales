# artist - Diana Meltzer

## labels

## curiosities
**"The Woman with the Golden Ears" in A&R** : Meltzer transitioned into A&R, where she gained a reputation as "The Woman with the Golden Ears" due to her exceptional ability to identify and sign hit acts. Her keen ear led her to sign prominent bands such as Creed, Evanescence, Alter Bridge, Finger 11, Drowning Pool, and Bright Eyes. "And because she was so good at picking hit acts, she became known as the Woman with the Golden Ears." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oRR7N-YUcRE
**Founding Wind-Up Records** : Diana Meltzer co-founded Wind-Up Records with her late husband, Alan. Her path to label ownership began with a background in fashion modeling, followed by running a record store in Connecticut, which gave her insight into the retail side of music and artist needs. "Together with her late husband, Alan, she founded Wind Up Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oRR7N-YUcRE
