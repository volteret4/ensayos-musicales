## curiosities
**Recording Antics at Sun Label** : Billy Lee Riley was a rockabilly singer who recorded for the Sun label in Memphis in the 1950s, much like Elvis. He pushed rock and roll to extremes, with people recalling how he would literally swing upside down from the rafters, screaming into the microphone during recording sessions.
