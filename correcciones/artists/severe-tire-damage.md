# artist - Severe Tire Damage

## songs
**Carcinoma (1993)** : This track from the album "Who Cares" represents the sound of Severe Tire Damage, the band recognized for performing the first documented musical netcast on the internet. "Here is what Severe Tire Damage sounded like. This is a track called Carcinoma, from an album entitled Who Cares." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso

## curiosities
**First Documented Musical Netcast (June 24, 1993)** : On June 24, 1993, Severe Tire Damage performed a gig on the patio of Xerox's Palo Alto Research Center in California, which was broadcast onto the Internet Multicast Backbone. This landmark event was seen and heard by "proto-internet gigs" as far away as Australia, despite terribly slow video and low-fi audio. "The earliest performance that anyone can document is an event that happened on June 24th, 1993." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
**Opening for The Rolling Stones' Netcast (1994)** : In November 1994, Severe Tire Damage strategically played sets before and after The Rolling Stones' 20-minute netcast at the Cotton Bowl, realizing the channel was open. This clever move meant they simultaneously opened for the Stones and had the Stones open for them. "So before and after the stones 20 minute netcast, Severe Tire Damage played sets." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
