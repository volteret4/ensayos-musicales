## songs
**Institutionalized (1983)** : This song is the title track from their 1983 self-titled debut album. It features lyrics that depict a scenario of a young person feeling unheard and misunderstood by their family, culminating in a mother screaming the child's name after being ignored. The song is highlighted as a great example of California hardcore, and simultaneously shows the beginnings of trash metal and skate punk. "And I just like staring at the wall thinking about everything that I was thinking about, nothing. And then my mom came in, and I didn't even know if she was there. She called my name, and I didn't hear her, and then she started screaming, my, my.
