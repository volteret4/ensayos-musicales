## curiosities
**Early Life and Music Career** : Richard Coles began his musical journey as a choir boy and later moved into theater. He became the keyboardist for the "groundbreaking gay positive band" Bronski Beat in the early 80s, and then followed singer Jimmy Somerville to form The Communards, where he also played keyboards. "Growing up, he was a choir boy before moving on to theater." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Transition to Priesthood and Retirement** : While working in music, acting, writing, and radio hosting, Coles was simultaneously studying theology. He was ordained a priest in the Anglican Church and served as a vicar until his retirement in 2022, marking a complete career shift from rock star to clergyman. "He was ordained a priest in the Angle Concerts and served as a vicar until his retirement in 2022." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
