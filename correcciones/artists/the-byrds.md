## albums
**Sweetheart of the Rodeo (1968)** : This album, featuring Gram Parsons, was so controversial within the country music establishment that it led to the band being booed off the stage at the Grand Ole Opry. It represented a bold cross-genre experiment that challenged traditional boundaries.
