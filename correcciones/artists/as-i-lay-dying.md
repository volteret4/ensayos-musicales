## curiosities
**Arrest and Conviction** : The "hitman" Lambesis hired turned out to be an undercover police officer, leading to his arrest on May 7, 2023. He was subsequently tried and convicted before any harm could come to Megan. "But then the hitman he hired turned out to be an undercover cop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Attempted Murder-for-Hire (May 7, 2023)** : Tim Lambesis, frontman of the San Diego metalcore band, hired what he believed was a hitman to kill his estranged wife, Megan, reportedly due to a dispute over his desire to focus on record producing versus her wanting him to tour for income. Lambesis offered $20,000 for the act. "The story is that he hired a hitman to kill Megan, his estranged wife." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Incarceration and Band Reformation** : Lambesis was sentenced to six years in prison. While he was incarcerated, As I Lay Dying continued under a different name with another vocalist. Upon his release on probation in December 2016, the original group reformed and has since been touring and recording. "He was sentenced to six years in prison." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Professed Christianity** : Despite the attempted murder-for-hire, all members of As I Lay Dying, including Tim Lambesis, publicly profess to be practicing Christians. "All the members in As I Lay Dying, including Lembesis, profess to be practicing Christians." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
