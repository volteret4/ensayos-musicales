## curiosities
**Departure from Faith No More** : Jim Martin was the guitarist for Faith No More during their successful period in the late 1980s and early 1990s. However, he was fired from the band in 1993 and has not returned since. "Jim Martin was the guitarist with Faith No More during their run in the late 80s and early 90s. But by 1993 things weren't going so great so he was fired and hasn't been back since." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Giant Pumpkin Grower** : After his music career, Jim Martin developed a passion for competitively growing giant pumpkins at his home in Castro Valley, California, starting in 2000. In 2005, he grew a pumpkin weighing 1,087 pounds and was once named one of the five best giant pumpkin growers in America. "Instead, what he's done is developed a passion for, wait for it, growing giant pumpkins at his home in Castro Valley, California." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
