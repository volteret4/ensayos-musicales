# artist - DJ A.M.

## curiosities
**Death by Drug Overdose** : Following the traumatic plane crash that he survived with Travis Barker, DJ A.M. tragically died from a drug overdose. His death deeply affected Travis Barker, setting him back for months during a critical period of his own recovery and creative work. "when Travis found out that DJA am died of a drug overdose, that set him back months." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q-QP7ePxkQE
**Fatal Plane Crash (2008)** : DJ A.M. was one of two survivors, along with Travis Barker, of a catastrophic Learjet 60 plane crash on September 19, 2008, in Columbia, South Carolina. He had convinced Travis Barker to take the chartered private jet after a show. "Travis and AM were the only two people to survive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q-QP7ePxkQE
**Live DJing and Drumming Project** : DJ A.M. collaborated with Travis Barker on a unique musical project that blended live drums with DJ mixing. DJ A.M. would spin classic songs on his turntables, while Travis Barker augmented the grooves with live drumming, creating an innovative performance style that they shared online. "Their idea was to combine a DJ's mixing with live drums. DJ A.M. would spin classic songs and his decks and Travis would augment the groove." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q-QP7ePxkQE
