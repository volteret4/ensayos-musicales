## curiosities
**Departure from Roseanne** : Due to a "burning hatred" from Rosanne Barr, who was angered by his sole "created by" credit and felt the show was based on her life, Williams faced constant pushback and tantrums. The situation became unworkable, forcing executive producers to choose between him and Barr, leading to Williams being asked to leave in the middle of the first season. "Williams was asked to leave in the middle of the first season." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6O5TCTO-go4
**View on Stand-up Sitcoms** : He told Vulture in 2013 that a fully realized stand-up routine is like a "home run," providing an "entire world and a constellation of characters," thereby solving three challenges at once: a concept, jokes, and a star. "A fully realized stand-up routine is like a home run. You're getting an entire world and a constellation of characters." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6O5TCTO-go4
