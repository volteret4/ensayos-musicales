**Formation and Unique Sound** : Sparks was formed in Los Angeles in 1971 by brothers Ron and Russell Mael, known for their quirky rock style. Their music was distinct not only for Russell Mael's unique voice but also for their clever and sophisticated lyrics, traversing a wide range of genres including glam rock, art rock, chamber pop, electronic dance, and power pop.
