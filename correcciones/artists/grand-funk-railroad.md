## curiosities
**Major International Rock Act (Early 1970s)** : Grand Funk Railroad was surprisingly one of the biggest international rock acts in the early 1970s, despite their perceived obscurity today, at a time when much of rock music was considered tired. "The biggest international rock acts in the early 1970s were Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd, The Rolling Stones, Elton John, and as unbelievable as it sounds today, Grand Funk Railroad." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VYi3r0G-ne4
**Member Honored by Gibby Haynes' Dog** : Mark Farner, a member of Grand Funk Railroad, was the namesake for a pit bull owned by Gibby Haynes of the Butthole Surfers, reflecting the cross-band appreciation. "Gibby Haynes and the Butthole Surfers had a pit bull named Mark Farner after the guy in Grandfell Grille Road." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
