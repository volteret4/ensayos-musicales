## curiosities
**Band Philosophy and Independence** : Eli's band, Inhaler, formed in 2012 when all four members were still in their teens. The group is explicitly uninterested in receiving help from famous relatives, and vice versa. An anecdote illustrates this: when their Airbnb in London was unavailable, Eli called his mom for hotel suggestions, to which she jokingly replied, "well, there's always a park bench." They ended up staying with a friend. "Eli's band is called Inhaler. There are four pieces that started up in 2012 and all four members weren't even in their teens. No one in the group is interested in getting any help from famous relatives and vice versa. Eli tells a story about going to London and finding out that their Airbnb wasn't available. So Eli phoned mom and said, uh, do you know any good hotels in London? And mum replied, well, there's always a park bench. They ended up finding a friend and sleeping on the couch." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Full Name and Late Introduction to Music** : Eli Husson's full name is Elijah Bob Patricia's Guggy Q Husson. He is the third child of Paul and Allison Hewson (Bono). Despite his father's fame, Eli didn't get into music until he was 13 and was unaware of his father's unique status until age 10. "His full name is Elijah Bob Patricia's Guggy Q Husson. He is the third child of Paul and Allison Husson. Paul Husson, better known as Bono. Now, even though dad is one of the biggest rock stars in the known universe, Eli didn't get into music until he was 13. He didn't know that there was anything special about his dad until he was 10." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
