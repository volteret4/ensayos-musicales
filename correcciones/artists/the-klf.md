## curiosities
**Building a Pyramid from Human Ashes** : The KLF, a distinctive music and art collective, is engaged in a project to construct a pyramid in Liverpool using the cremated remains of 34,592 deceased individuals. People can have their ashes formed into a brick to be added to this structure, with a reservation cost of £99. "The KLF, the weird music and art collective, is building a pyramid out of thirty four thousand five hundred ninety two dead people." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**People's Pyramid Project (2018)** : The KLF conceived of building a pyramid constructed from the remains of 34,592 deceased individuals. Construction on this "People's Pyramid" began on November 23rd, 2018, in the Liverpool area, with individuals able to reserve a spot for 99 pounds. "came up with the idea of building a pyramid made of the remains of 34,592 dead people." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
**Public Money Burning (1994)** : The KLF is known as one of the most subversive experimental bands in British music history, notably for publicly burning one million pounds in cash in 1994.
