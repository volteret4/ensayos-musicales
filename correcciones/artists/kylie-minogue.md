## songs
**Where the Wild Roses Grow (1995)** : Kylie Minogue contributed guest vocals to this 1995 song by Nick Cave and The Bad Seeds, which appeared on their album "Murder Ballads." Her participation was highlighted as an "odd pairing" due to her established image as an international pop superstar whose songs typically encourage dancing, differing significantly from Nick Cave's thematic focus.
