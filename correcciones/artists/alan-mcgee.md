# artist - Alan McGee

## curiosities
**Founder of Creation Records** : Alan McGee, a Scotsman, founded Creation Records in 1983, which carved out an important niche in the early 1980s indie movement. "It was founded by a Scotsman named Alan McGee in 1983." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
**Key Discoveries and Financial Outcome** : McGee's significant early contribution was signing The Jesus and Mary Chain. He later discovered and signed Oasis, another band with "brawling, hard to deal with brothers." Despite Creation Records facing money issues, including a near-bankruptcy due to a costly My Bloody Valentine record, McGee eventually sold the label to Sony. "McGee's best early contribution was signing the Jesus and Mary Chain." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
