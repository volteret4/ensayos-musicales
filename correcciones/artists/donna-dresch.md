## curiosities
**Brief Stints with Grunge and Indie Bands** : For a period, Donna Dresch played with "the guys," including a stint as a member of the Seattle grunge band Screaming Trees and touring Australia playing bass for Dinosaur Jr. These experiences highlight her diverse musical background before forming Team Dresch. "Donna was a member of Seattle Grunge Ben Screaming Trees for a bit and toured Australia playing bass for dinosaur junior." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qss5kC2FUx0
**Zine Publisher and LGBTQ+ Advocate** : Donna Dresch had her own zine in the late 1980s and early 1990s, and became a vocal proponent for LGBTQ issues "long before we used the term, LGBTQ." Her indie label, Chainsaw, was named after her zine.
