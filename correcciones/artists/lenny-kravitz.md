## curiosities
**Alias Romeo Blue** : Before finding mainstream success, Kravitz experimented with various musical phases, including performing under the alias "Romeo Blue," which was not successful. "By 1988, he had gone through several musical phases, including a stint going by the name Romeo Blue, which didn't go very well." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Bidding War and Name Reversion** : His unique music generated a five-way bidding war among record labels. Upon signing with Virgin Records, he agreed to revert to his real name, a decision noted as a positive move. "When he agreed to sign with Virgin, he also agreed to go back to his real name, which was a good move." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Diverse Musical Exposure** : His musical influences spanned jazz and classical, alongside rock legends like The Who, Led Zeppelin, KISS, Pink Floyd, Jimi Hendrix, and The Rolling Stones. "But along with jazz and classical, he was exposed to the who and Led Zeppelin, KISS, and Pink Floyd and Jimmy Hendrix in the Rolling Stones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Early Musical Aspirations and Training** : Kravitz desired to be a musician from the age of five and performed with the California Boys Choir as a child, later singing with the Metropolitan Opera in New York. "When he was a kid, he performed with a California boys choir and even sang with Metropolitan Opera in New York." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Family Background** : Lenny Kravitz is the son of actress Roxy Roker, known for her role as Helen Willis on "The Jeffersons," and Sy Kravitz, an NBC News producer and jazz promoter. He is also a cousin of TV weatherman Al Roker. "Lenny is the son of Roxy Roker, the actress who played Helen Willis on the TV show The Jefferson's. And before you ask, yes, he is a cousin of TV weatherman, Al Roker." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Father's Jazz Connections** : His father, Sy Kravitz, was a jazz promoter who counted Ella Fitzgerald, Miles Davis, and Duke Ellington as personal friends. "And a sometimes jazz promoter who listed Elefits Gerald, Miles Davis, and Duke Ellington as personal friends." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Preference for Analog Recording** : Lenny Kravitz often opts for traditional analog recording methods, utilizing "real to real tape, analog recording consoles, and take after take after take until they nail their parts." This approach stands in contrast to modern digital trickery, allowing for natural imperfections to contribute to the music's beauty. "You don't have to be as good as some of the people from the old analog days when you couldn't fix glaring mistakes with digital trickery." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Zniri9uSlc
**Successful Real Estate Ventures** : In addition to his ongoing music career, Lenny Kravitz has achieved significant financial success in real estate, renovating properties in locations such as New York, the Bahamas, Miami, Paris, and Toronto. "Today, he still performs in records, but is making gazillions of dollars in the real estate business by renovating properties in New York, the Bahamas, Miami, Paris, and Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Toronto Condo Design** : Lenny Kravitz is involved in designing condos for the Toronto real estate market, similar to Pharrell Williams. "Lenny Kravitz isn't the only artist to get into designing condos for the Toronto market." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
