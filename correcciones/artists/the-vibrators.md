## songs
**Disco and Moscow (1980)** : "Disco and Moscow" was a seven-inch single released by the English punk band The Vibrators in the fall of 1980. This release coincided with a period when the Cold War was intensifying. The band is not known to have had any connections with the KGB or any other secret police organizations from the Soviet bloc.
