**Another Way to Die (2008) – Vocals for First Bond Duet** : Alicia Keys supplied the vocals for "Another Way to Die," a duet with Jack White for the 2008 James Bond film "Quantum of Solace." This marked the first duet in the history of Bond themes. The video was shot in Toronto while Keys was promoting her film "The Secret Life of Bees." "Keys supplied the vocals." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo
