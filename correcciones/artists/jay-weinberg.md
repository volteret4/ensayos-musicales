## curiosities
**Bruce Springsteen & The E Street Band Fill-in** : After graduating high school in 2008, Jay filled in as drummer for Bruce Springsteen's E Street Band when his father, Max Weinberg, had a scheduling conflict with the Conan O'Brien show. "So, who did Springsteen call the fill-in? Son Jay." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**Dismissal from Madball** : Jay was fired from the hardcore band Madball mid-tour in Canada because he was considered not a good fit for the group. "But he got fired midway through a tour of Canada because it was felt that he wasn't a good fit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**High School Band Engagements** : During his mid-teens, while still in high school, Jay performed with punk bands like The Used and The Bouncing Souls, followed by stints in metal bands Chaosus and Sadie May. "By the time he was in his middle teens, Jay was gigging with punk bands like The Used in the Bouncing Souls." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**Joining Slipknot (2013)** : In 2013, following Jordy Jordison's departure from Slipknot, Jay auditioned and was secretly hired as his replacement, with the news kept from fans for over a year. "Jay auditioned and was hired as his replacement, although that was kept a secret from fans for over a year." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**Musical Taste Development** : Jay Weinberg's musical preferences were significantly heavier than his father's, Max Weinberg, gravitating towards metal. "his tastes tended to be a lot harder than what his father was into." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**Ozzfest 2000 Inspiration** : In 2000, his father took Jay to an Ozzfest show, where seeing the band Slipknot solidified his desire to play metal and drums. "Way back in 2000, Dad took Joey to an Osfest show, an event that included a new band called Slipknot." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
**Self-Taught Drummer** : Jay started playing drums at age 14, largely self-taught and often using his father's equipment, though his father provided some assistance. "So, at age 14, and usually Dad's gear, he started to play. And he was mostly self-taught, although Dad helped out a little." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
