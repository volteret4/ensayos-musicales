## curiosities
**Attempt to Influence 1987 UK Election** : Red Wedge was a collective of musicians in the UK dedicated to engaging young people in politics. Their primary objective was to facilitate a shift from the conservative government to a Labour government in the 1987 election. Despite their significant efforts and the participation of numerous notable artists, their campaign ultimately failed, and Mrs. Thatcher secured another victory.
