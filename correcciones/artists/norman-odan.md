# artist - Norman O'Dan

## curiosities
**Inspiration for Ziggy Stardust** : Norman O'Dan, a Texas proto-psychobilly singer also known as The Legendary Stardust Cowboy, was a big fan of space travel and admired by David Bowie. He was one of the two people who inspired the name for Bowie's alien rock star character, Ziggy, in *The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and The Spiders from Mars*. "And that name was inspired by two people, Bowie's new friend Iggy Pop, and a Texas proto-psychobilly singer named Norman O'Dan, who went by the handle of the legendary Stardust Cowboy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dm5N6yDRxXQ
