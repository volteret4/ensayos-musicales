## curiosities
**Motown Staff Producer and Credit for Oasis's "Step Out"** : Henry Cosby was a staff producer at Motown and a co-writer of Stevie Wonder's 1966 hit "Uptight Everything's All Right." Due to the similarities between that song and Oasis's "Step Out," Cosby was granted co-writing credit for "Step Out" and was entitled to at least a quarter of the proceeds from any version of the song.
