## albums
**Suede (1992) – Self-Titled Debut** : This self-titled album was released in 1992 and marked the first signs of what would become Britpop. The music celebrated the great eccentric English rock of the 70s and 80s, influenced by artists like David Bowie, Roxy Music, and T-Rex. "It's hard to describe how different that song sounded to English ears." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5q5EnZj1FwE
**Suede (1993) - Debut Album** : Released on March 29, 1993, this self-titled album became the fastest-selling debut album in UK history. It was a key release that kickstarted the Britpop revolution, capturing the attention of the British music press. "And when their self-titled first album came out on March 29, 1993, it became the fastest selling debut album in the history of the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSvoppf1dGY
