## curiosities
**Bo Diddley Beat** : The "Bo Diddley beat" is cited as an example of a rhythm and beat that is "not protected" by copyright, illustrating that specific rhythmic patterns can be used by multiple artists without legal consequence. "That's why you can have bow diddly. And Iggy Pop. And you too. And Jet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
**Giant of Rock Music** : Bo Diddley is recognized as one of the significant figures in the broader landscape of rock music. "Where would we be with a bow diddly and little Richard and Jimmy Hendrix?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Popularizer of the "Bo Diddley Beat"** : Bo Diddley is renowned for using a particular rhythm so effectively that it became known as the "Bo Diddley beat." Although the beat itself originated at least 50 years before his time, he was instrumental in introducing it to rock and roll, influencing numerous artists from Iggy Pop to Joan Jett. "Bow didn't invent that beat. It's history goes back at least 50 years, but he was the one who introduced it to rock and roll." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
