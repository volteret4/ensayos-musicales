## curiosities
**Dave Grohl's All-Time Favorite** : The Bad Brains are identified as a legendary DC band and one of Dave Grohl's all-time favorites. Their influence is evident as they appeared as guest musicians on the Foo Fighters' song "Feast or Famine," recorded near Grohl's childhood home in Arlington, Virginia.
