## curiosities
**Early Pogoing at UFO Club (1966)** : According to followers of The Pink Floyd, people at their "freak out gigs" at the UFO Club in London in 1966 sometimes "Pogwood in place." This predates the popularization of the Pogo during the British punk rock explosion in the mid-1970s.
