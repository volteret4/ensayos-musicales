# artist - Jackie Brenston and the Kings of Rhythm

## songs
**Rocket 88 (1951)** : Released under this name, the song features a fuzzed-up guitar sound played by Willie Kitsert, resulting from a damaged guitar amplifier where the speaker cone had burst. The band, led by Ike Turner, improvised a repair with wadded newspaper, creating a distinctive sound that some consider the birth of rock and roll due to its early use of guitar distortion.
