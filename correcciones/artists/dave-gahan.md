# artist - Dave Gahan

## member of
- Depeche Mode

## albums
**Paper Monsters (2003)** : Released on June 3, 2003, this was Gahan's first solo project after 22 years with Depeche Mode. The album was described as "surprisingly solid" and remained within Depeche Mode's musical territory. Its creation was a slow process, aided by a friend named Knox Chandler. "By June 3rd, 2003, it was ready. Dave released an album called Paper Monsters." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo

## curiosities
**Initial Apprehension for Solo Endeavor** : Gahan admitted that the idea of undertaking a solo project on his own "really kind of freaked him out." "The thought of doing it all really kind of freaked him out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Long Incubation Period for Solo Debut** : It took Dave Gahan 22 years to release his first solo album while being a member of Depeche Mode. "It took him 22 years to get around to it. But at least he did it, right?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Motivation for Solo Work After Near-Death Experiences** : After a period of "recovery and resurrection" following multiple "near-death experiences in the 1990s," Gahan felt compelled to "test myself in a positive way" by recording his own album with his own songs, despite initial trepidation. "But after Dave's recovery and resurrection following a series of near-death experiences in the 1990s, you see thought, you know, maybe it's time I tested myself in a positive way and recorded my own album with my own songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Vampire Conspiracy Theory** : The theory that Dave Gahan is a vampire arose in the early 90s due to his wasted appearance from heroin and cocaine addiction, his belief in his own "rock god hype," and his commissioning of a coffin-shaped bed for pre-show naps on tour. Further fueling the rumor was an alleged incident where he bit a journalist on the neck backstage, along with bizarre behaviors like painting his entire house black. Pictures of a "vampiric looking" Gahan with fangs circulated as the public internet was emerging. Gahan himself stated in a 2001 interview that he "definitely could have been a vampire in my own head," confirming he slept in a huge double coffin-shaped bed in Los Angeles, describing his life at the time as "Spinal Tap." "I definitely could have been a vampire in my own head. Even the bed I slept in in Los Angeles was in the shape of a coffin, a huge double bed shaped like a coffin. My whole life was spinal tap at that time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg
