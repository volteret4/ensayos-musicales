# artist - TLC

## curiosities
**"No Scrubs" Cover on Teal Album** : TLC's song "No Scrubs" was covered on Weezer's Teal Album, marking the only exception to the album's rule of covering songs released before 1994, because the band simply thought it was "cool." "TLC's No Scrups. It made the cut because the band decided that it was, well, it was just cool." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
