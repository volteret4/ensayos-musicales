## curiosities
**Syringe and Heroin Injection** : Syringes were used by both Kurt Cobain and Layne Staley in their final hours for injecting heroin. Cobain injected $100 worth of Mexican black tar heroin into his arm before pulling the trigger of a shotgun, while Staley was found with a syringe sticking out of his leg and another loaded one in his hand.
