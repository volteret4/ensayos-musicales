# instrument - ARP Odyssey

## curiosities
**Synthesizer in Krautrock** : The ARP Odyssey was a synthesizer similar to the Mini-Moog that found use in Krautrock, specifically mentioned as part of the instrumentation for Kraftwerk's 1974 "Autobahn" album, contributing to its pioneering electronic sound. "There was an ARP Odyssey, which was similar to the mini-moke." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
**Used in Rush's 2112** : The ARP Odyssey keyboard synthesizer was utilized for the "trippy synth sounds" featured in the "Overture" section of Rush's *2112* album. These parts were played by Hugh Syme, who was also responsible for the band's artwork. "It starts with trippy synth sounds played on a keyboard called an ARP Odyssey." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B0CZ1deEgic
