# instrument - Guitar Amp

## curiosities
**Influence on Early Rock and Roll Sound** : In early March 1951, during a recording session for Jackie Brenston and His Delta Cats, a guitar amp was damaged, resulting in a loose speaker cone. Lacking funds for repairs, the band jury-rigged it with newspaper, creating a distinctive "buzz" that was captured on their song "Rocket 88." This buzz has led many to consider "Rocket 88" as the first rock and roll song, making the broken amp's sound a pivotal, accidental contribution to the genre's origins.
