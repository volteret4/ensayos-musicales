# instrument - Echo Machine

## curiosities
**Cheap Effect for "Bela Lugosi's Dead"** : A cheap echo machine was one of the only special effects used in the recording of Bauhaus's debut single, "Bela Lugosi's Dead," contributing to its atmospheric sound. "There were no special effects other than a cheap echo machine and the sound Daniel could get out of his amplifier." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
