## curiosities
**Standard Size and Capacity** : Commercial real-to-reel stereo tapes typically measured seven and a half inches in diameter. A standard 1200-foot reel, played at seven and a half inches per second, offered an impressive two hours of uninterrupted music, a revolutionary capacity compared to 78 RPM records.
