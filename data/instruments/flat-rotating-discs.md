# instrument - Flat Rotating Discs

## curiosities
**Early Recorded Music Format (Early 20th Century)** : Flat rotating discs represented the very beginning of the recorded music industry in the early 20th century. Initially, these discs were sold individually in plain packaging with no adornment and could hold approximately 3.5 minutes of music per side, necessitating multiple discs for longer performances like symphonies, which were then sold together in "record albums."
