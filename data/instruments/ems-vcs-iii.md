# instrument - EMS VCS III

## curiosities
**Primitive Keyboard-less Synthesizer** : The EMS VCS III was a primitive synthesizer, notable for lacking a keyboard, which Pink Floyd used on the instrumental track "On the Run" from their 1973 album *Dark Side of the Moon*. This instrument allowed the band to explore new sonic possibilities in the early 1970s. "If you go back to Dark Side of the Moon and the Instrumental Track on the Run, we hear primitive synthesizer, one without a keyboard called an EMS VCS III." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
