# instrument - Plate Reverb

## curiosities
**Experimentation at The Bathhouse Studio** : Arkells extensively experimented with an "old plate reverb" available at The Bathhouse studio during the recording of "Michigan Left." The band members, being "23 year olds or whatever," were able to stay up until 5 AM, creatively using various studio equipment and effects, including the plate reverb, given they were living in the same building as the studio. "They have every guitar amp you can imagine. Yeah, so we definitely, and a lot of, you know, we got really into like throwing things through delays or, you know, a gated reverb on a snare drum. Like different things that we didn't have the luxury of time due to budgetary restraints on the first record." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kKNn01M8GG4
