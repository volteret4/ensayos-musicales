## curiosities
**On-Stage Smoothie Ritual** : Starting around 2008, Ash from USS adopted a unique on-stage ritual of preparing and sharing protein and electrolyte smoothies during live performances. This practice quickly became a signature element of their shows, often involving him pouring smoothies for members of the audience. "it just became this thing that we did at every show." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
**Smoothie Era's Fiery End in Singapore** : The distinctive "smoothie era" of USS's live shows met a dramatic end during a performance in Singapore. Due to incompatible voltage (220 volts vs. North American 120 volts), the on-stage blender caught fire, sparking and smoking. Ash was mildly electrocuted during the incident, which he described as the "grand finale" of this memorable stage act. "the blender actually caught on fire and started sparking and smoking and I got mildly electrocuted on stage and that was like the grand finale of the smoothie era." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
