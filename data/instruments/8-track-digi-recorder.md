# instrument - 8-track digi recorder

## curiosities
**The Big Come Up Recording Equipment** : This digital recording device was used to record The Black Keys' debut album, *The Big Come Up*, in the unfinished basement of Patrick Carney's parents between January and March of 2002. "It was called The Big Come Up and was recorded in Patrick's parents' unfinished basement in Akron using an 8-track digi recorder between January and March of 2002." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ooNAHswiT5Q
