# instrument - Ampex 200 real-to-real tape recorder

## curiosities
**Development with Bing Crosby's Investment (1940s)** : The Ampex 200 reel-to-reel tape recorder was perfected by Major Jack Mullin, largely thanks to a $50,000 (over $600,000 in today's money) investment from radio star Bing Crosby. Crosby sought to use the machine to prerecord his East Coast radio show, eliminating the need for a second live broadcast for the West Coast. "And the result was the Ampex 200 real-to-real tape recorder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk
**Revolutionizing Music Recording** : The Ampex 200 reel-to-reel tape recorder marked a turning point in music production. For the first time, recorded performances could be edited, and with Les Paul's work, overdubbing became possible. This shifted the purpose of recording sessions from merely capturing live performances to improving upon reality and creating sounds impossible to perform live.
