## curiosities
**Listener Adjustment in Early Stereo** : In the early experimental phase of stereo mixing, engineers sometimes employed extreme panning, expecting listeners to use their stereo system's balance controls. The idea was that users would adjust these controls to find their preferred or most pleasing stereo mix.
