## curiosities
**Discovery by Major Jack Mullin (Post-WWII)** : After World War II, Major Jack Mullin discovered several German magnetic tape machines at a secret broadcast post while helping to clear up after the Nazis. He obtained permission to bring this gear back to the US, as German patents had been invalidated by the war. "It was only after the war when Major Jack Mullin, who was helping mop up after the Nazis, discovered a couple of these magnetic tape machines at a secret broadcast post." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk
**German Secret Technology (WWII)** : During World War II, the Germans developed and kept magnetic tape recording technology a secret. They used it to confuse the Allies by broadcasting orchestral performances for hours without breaks and simultaneously broadcasting speeches by Hitler from different cities, creating the illusion of non-stop or ubiquitous presence. "The Nazis confused the Allies during the war by broadcasting long performances by orchestra's hours and hours and hours without anybody taking a break." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk
**Introduction to the US (Post-WWII)** : Major Jack Mullin disassembled the discovered German magnetic tape machines, shipped them to the US, and established a company in San Francisco to exploit this new technology. This brought magnetic recording to America, eventually leading to its widespread adoption in broadcasting and music. "So, Mullin disassembled everything, shipped it home, and set up a company devoted to exploiting this new technology based out of San Francisco." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk
