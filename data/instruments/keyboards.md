## curiosities
**80s-Style Revival (Mid-2000s)** : In 2006 and 2007, bands like The Bravery and The Killers were credited with reintroducing the sound of 80s-style keyboards into rock music. This marked a departure from the guitar-heavy dominance of 1990s grunge and punk, bringing a new sonic variety to the alt-rock scene. **Alternative Dance Basis** : Keyboards were a fundamental component of Alternative Dance, utilized extensively alongside drum machines, loops, and samples to create its electronic-driven sound. **Centrality in 80s Alt Rock Dance Music** : Keyboards were central to the "keyboard bass bands" of the 1980s, such as Depeche Mode and New Order, whose music alternative kids danced to before grunge's guitar dominance. This electronic, dance-oriented attitude saw a revival in the 2000s. "25 years earlier, long before Grunge guitars took over, alternative kids danced to keyboard bass bands like Depeche Mode, New Order, OMD, Durand Iran, the Human League, Eurasia, Eurythmics, and so many others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo ← keyboards
