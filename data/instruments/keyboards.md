# instrument - Keyboards

## curiosities
**80s-Style Revival (Mid-2000s)** : In 2006 and 2007, bands like The Bravery and The Killers were credited with reintroducing the sound of 80s-style keyboards into rock music. This marked a departure from the guitar-heavy dominance of 1990s grunge and punk, bringing a new sonic variety to the alt-rock scene. **Alternative Dance Basis** : Keyboards were a fundamental component of Alternative Dance, utilized extensively alongside drum machines, loops, and samples to create its electronic-driven sound. **Billy Preston's Self-Taught Mastery** : Billy Preston, a highly versatile and influential musician, was entirely self-taught on the keyboards. His innate talent allowed him to play with legendary artists such like The Beatles, Ray Charles, Little Richard, and Sly and the Family Stone, and tour with The Rolling Stones. **Centrality in 80s Alt Rock Dance Music** : Keyboards were central to the "keyboard bass bands" of the 1980s, such as Depeche Mode and New Order, whose music alternative kids danced to before grunge's guitar dominance. This electronic, dance-oriented attitude saw a revival in the 2000s.
