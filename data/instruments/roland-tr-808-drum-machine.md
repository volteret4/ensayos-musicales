# instrument - Roland TR-808 Drum Machine

## curiosities
**Mainstream Introduction through "Brass Monkey"** : The Roland TR-808 Drum Machine was introduced to a wider audience, including millions of non-rap and non-house music listeners, through its prominent use in the Beastie Boys' song "Brass Monkey," the fourth single from their album "Licensed to Ill." "This was the fourth, and it introduced millions of non-rappers and those not into house music to the sounds of the legendary Roland TR-808 Drum Machine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cYwk-B3qwwk
**Mass Introduction via "Brass Monkey" (1986)** : The Roland TR-808 drum machine was introduced to millions of non-rappers and individuals not previously exposed to house music through its prominent use in the Beastie Boys' song "Brass Monkey." This track made the distinctive sounds of the 808 widely accessible and recognizable. "It introduced millions of non-rappers and those not into house music to the sounds of the legendary Roland TR-808 drum machine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l1jPP1FhGo8
