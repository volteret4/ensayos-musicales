# instrument - Solid-body Guitar

## curiosities
**Solution to Feedback** : Solid-body guitars, such as the Gibson Les Paul, were developed as a direct solution to the feedback problems prevalent in hollow-body designs when played loudly. Their dense construction prevented unwanted resonance, allowing for higher volume without interference.
