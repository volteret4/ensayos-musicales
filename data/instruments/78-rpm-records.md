# instrument - 78 RPM records

## curiosities
**Poor Sound Quality (Pre-Late 1940s)** : For the initial 60 years of the recorded music industry, the sound quality of 78 RPM records played on gramophones was notably poor compared to live music. These early recordings were considered "terrible" due to the limitations of technology available at the time to accurately capture audio.
