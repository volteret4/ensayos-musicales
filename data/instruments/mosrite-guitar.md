## curiosities
**Johnny Ramone's Purchase** : On January 23, 1974, Johnny Cummings (Johnny Ramone) purchased a blue Mosrite guitar for $50 at Manny's Guitar Center on 48th Street, a pivotal moment in the formation of the Ramones.
