# instrument - DJ programs

## curiosities
**DJ Transition (Pre-2008)** : Prior to 2008, some "turntablist DJs" had not yet moved to modern DJ programs like Serrato or CD decks. They were noted as one of the last bastions keeping vinyl active before its significant resurgence. "The only people keeping vinyl alive were blood-eyed purists and turntableist DJs who had yet to make the transition to CD decks and DJ programs like Serrato." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNqqLDpUuZY
