# instrument - Slingerland

## curiosities
**Neil Peart's Drum Manufacturer** : Slingerland was the company that manufactured the double bass drum kit Neil Peart brought to his Rush audition in 1974. This kit was noted for its unusually small bass drums. Slingerland was one of many different drum manufacturers Neil Peart used throughout his extensive career. "The whole thing was made by a company called Slingerland." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B0CZ1deEgic
