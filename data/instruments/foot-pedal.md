# instrument - Foot pedal

## curiosities
**Ludwig and Ludwig Patent in 1909** : A major breakthrough occurred in 1909 when Ludwig and Ludwig, a company based in Chicago, patented the foot-operated bass drum pedal. This design was so effective and revolutionary that it remains fundamentally the same as what is found on modern drum kits today. "But it wasn't until 1909 that the foot-operated bass drum pedal was patented by Ludwig and Ludwig out of Chicago." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WL3zEWJJNuw
**Second Major Innovation for Drummers** : The foot pedal was the second significant invention aimed at consolidating multiple percussion roles into a single drummer. It allowed a drummer to maintain a beat on the bass drum using their foot, thereby freeing both hands to play other instruments. The first foot pedals emerged in the 1890s. "The second invention was the foot pedal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WL3zEWJJNuw
