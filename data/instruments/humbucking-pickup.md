# instrument - Humbucking pickup

## curiosities
**Noise Cancellation Technology** : The humbucking pickup, featuring two coils wrapped around a magnet, is designed to cancel out noisy interference, such as buzz originating from nearby light fixtures or other electrical devices, thus providing a cleaner sound.
