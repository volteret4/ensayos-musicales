# instrument - Triode vacuum tube

## curiosities
**Foundation of Audio Amplification** : The triode vacuum tube was indirectly invented by Lee DeForest after observing a gas flame "shiver" in the presence of electromagnetic signals. This invention was capable of taking any electrical signal, including sound from a microphone, and making it larger. DeForest had a working version by 1912, leading to his first audio amplifier prototype by 1914, which was crucial for boosting electrical signals in various technologies.
