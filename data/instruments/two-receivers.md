# instrument - two receivers

## curiosities
**Requirement for WPJ Stereo** : To listen to WPJ's experimental stereo broadcasts in 1925, consumers needed two separate radio receivers. One receiver was tuned to the frequency carrying the left channel, and the other to the frequency for the right channel, enabling the dual-channel audio experience. "You'd have to have two receivers at the other end, one tune to the frequency for the left and then another for the right." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbSmBrhNACg
