# instrument - Control Data 1700

## curiosities
**Early Computer Ticketing System (1965)** : The Control Data 1700 was the machine used by Ticket Reservation Systems Inc. (TRS) and its partner Jack Quinn for their pioneering computerized ticketing service, starting in 1965. Its operating system was specifically rewritten to handle ticket sales.
