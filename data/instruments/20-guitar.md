# instrument - 20-guitar

## curiosities
**Monty Norman's "James Bond Theme" Adaptation (1962)** : Monty Norman adapted the melody for the "James Bond Theme" from his earlier work, "Good Sign, Bad Sign," by moving it to a "20-guitar." This specific instrument was part of his original vision for the iconic theme, before John Barry's orchestral arrangement.
