## curiosities
**Initial Threat and Eventual Decline (2000s-2010s)** : MP3 file sharing was initially perceived as "evil and illegal" and threatened the music industry, with predictions that no one would ever pay for music again. However, by the 2010s, streaming services mitigated this, and the market shifted, with millions paying for access. The poor audio quality of MP3s also prompted a slow move towards high-resolution audio.
