## curiosities
**Contribution to Rhythmicon (1930s)** : Leon Theremin also helped develop the Rhythmicon in the 1930s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Touchless Electronic Instrument (Early 1920s)** : Invented by Soviet physicist Leon Theremin 20 years after the Singing Arc, this instrument could be played without being touched. Coils inside its box generated an electrical field; waving one hand through this field produced a tone, and waving the other hand changed its pitch. When played correctly, it mimics a "warbling female opera singer" and was notably used for the theme in the sci-fi film "The Day the Earth Stood Still." It was experimented with by groups like The Beach Boys and Led Zeppelin in the 1960s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
