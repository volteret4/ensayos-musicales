# instrument - Modular console

## curiosities
**Connection to The Who and Led Zeppelin** : Records by influential bands such as The Who and Led Zeppelin are highly likely to have had their music processed through this exact hardware, underscoring its historical significance in rock music production. "If you have records by the Who and Led Zeppelin, there is a very good chance the music went through that exact hardware." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
**Olympic Studios Console in Calgary** : One of the renowned modular consoles, utilized at Olympic Studios during the late 1960s and early 1970s, is now preserved at the National Music Center in Calgary, Alberta. "If you ever find yourself at the National Music Center in Calgary, you should be able to find one of the famous modular consoles used at Olympic studios in the late 60s and early 70s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
