# instrument - Drum Machine

## curiosities
**Evolution from Rhythm Machines (1960s-1970s)** : Rhythm machines evolved into drum machines in the 1960s and 1970s, with companies like Roland, Multivox, and Bentley selling increasingly sophisticated devices that were popular with new German electronic bands. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**First Album Use (1972)** : The first rock band to use a drum machine for an entire album was Kingdom Come in 1972. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Flesh and Blood Drummers' Concerns (1980s)** : The proliferation of drum machines in the 1980s, especially after the introduction of the Roland TR-808, made "flesh and blood drummers really nervous." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**On-Stage Use (Techno Pop)** : In techno pop, rhythm tracks were often pre-recorded and played back on stage using reel-to-reel tape machines, or the drum machines themselves were used on stage, as with Echo & The Bunnymen and The Sisters of Mercy. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
