## curiosities
**Revolutionizing Audio Playback (Summer 1948)** : The introduction of the 33 1/3 RPM LP in the summer of 1948 drastically changed the audio landscape, allowing for up to 22 minutes of audio per side. This innovation was a direct response to the limitations of 78 RPM records and was eagerly adopted by movie studios and Broadway producers, leading to a boom in longer-form soundtrack and cast recording releases.
