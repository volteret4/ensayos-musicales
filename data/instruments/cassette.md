# instrument - Cassette

## curiosities
**Analog Sound of Debut Album** : The Wu Tang Clan's debut album *Enter the Wu-Tang (36 Chambers)* had a distinctive sound that resembled an "underground demo tape from a cassette." This was due to its production without means or resources, resulting in a raw, impure, unclean, and "contaminated" sound, which ultimately made it very interesting and characteristic of the group's best work. "sin embargo el disco en el butanclan sonaba a maqueta andernal grauma de un cassette. **Lack of Professional Promotional Material** : Cassettes did not feature the same abundance of professional promotional material that was never released commercially, a factor that makes 1990s promotional CDs particularly valuable to collectors. **Limited Collector Value for Singles** : Unlike Compact Disc singles, cassette singles never gained significant collector's value. **Music Consumption Before Digital** : Before the digital age, listening to a song required owning the CD or being fortunate enough for it to be programmed on the radio and then capturing it with a cassette recorder. This highlights the limited and physical nature of music consumption before the internet. "o tener la suerte de que la programaran en algún radio y capturarla con un cassette." ← Por qué ya no elegimos nuestro tono de llamada？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PVZrB26sBlQ
**Rapid Growth Post-Walkman** : The cassette format saw an almost explosive development after the introduction of the Walkman. This contrasted with the Compact Disc's initially slow and progressive evolution. **Recording Medium for Collaborative Project** : Cassettes were utilized to record the initial ideas and early rehearsals for the collaborative album between Björk and Dirty Projectors. This choice underscored the raw, intuitive, and low-production approach taken during the project's creation. "se juntaron y grabaron en casetes un poco las los primeros ensayos las primeras pizzas que tenían que ni eran cáncenas terminadas ni eran cáncenas bien definidas." ← Music Radar Clan > Björk y su curioso proyecto con los Dirty Proyectors | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Q1WS6Cu3eI&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Slow Evolution Due to Patent Issues** : The audio cassette experienced a very slow evolution and nearly failed due to Philips' rigorous patent on the system. This served as a cautionary tale for Sony and Philips when designing the licensing model for the Compact Disc.
