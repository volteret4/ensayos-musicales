# instrument - Gated Reverb

## curiosities
**Experimentation on "Michigan Left"** : Arkells explored new recording techniques and effects, such as "a gated reverb on a snare drum," during the month-long sessions for "Michigan Left" at The Bathhouse studio. This was a luxury not afforded during the budget-constrained recording of their previous album, "Jackson Square," allowing for more adventurous sound design. "we got really into like throwing things through delays or, you know, a gated reverb on a snare drum. Like different things that we didn't have the luxury of time due to budgetary restraints on the first record." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kKNn01M8GG4
