## curiosities
**Four-on-the-Floor Beat** : Session drummer Earl Young pioneered the "four-on-the-floor" beat, striking the bass drum on every beat to accelerate Harold Melvin & The Blue Notes' "The Love I Lost," a technique that became fundamental to the emerging disco sound.
