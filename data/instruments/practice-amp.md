# instrument - Practice Amp

## curiosities
**Julian Casablancas' Vocal Technique** : For the recording of "Is This It," Julian Casablancas sang his vocals through a tiny practice amp. This technique was intentionally used to achieve a specific "low-fi effect" for his voice, contributing to the album's raw and gritty sound.
