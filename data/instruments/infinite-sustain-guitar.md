# instrument - Infinite Sustain Guitar

## curiosities
**Invention and Purpose** : This unique guitar was invented and built by Michael Brook in Chinatown, Toronto. It was designed to create a cyclical, sustained sound, inspired by E-Bows. Three were constructed: one for Brook, one for U2's The Edge (used notably on "With or Without You"), and one for Daniel Landwom.
