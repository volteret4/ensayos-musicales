# instrument - Korg 700S

## curiosities
**Early DIY Synthesizer** : The Korg 700S was the specific synthesizer purchased by Daniel Miller, a film editor, to record his influential "Warm Leatherette" single in his bedroom in 1978. This relatively rudimentary piece of gear played a pivotal role in the early, minimalist sound of Technopop, demonstrating that complex musical experience wasn't required for creative output. "So he saved up some money from his job as a film editor and bought a Cork 700S and a four track reel to reel machine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
