# instrument - CDJ

## curiosities
**Compact Disc Turntable Concept** : The CDJ concept emerged in 2001, representing a DJ turntable designed for compact discs. While not the very first of its kind, the Pioneer CDJ-1000 quickly became the industry standard. **DJ Industry Standard** : Pioneer's CDJ series (referred to as "CD Jotamil") is credited with making Pioneer an absolute protagonist in the DJ world, widely considered the best CD player for DJing in history. "una de las razones que lo hizo protagonista absoluto del trabajo del DJ fue el CD Jotamil, que era el reproductor de CDS para pinchar mejor que ha habido en la historia" ← Music Radar Clan > LO MEJOR DEL SONAR 2018 (y no son los conciertos) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZzXvhYpV1DU&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← cdj
**Initial DJ Resistance** : DJs were initially reluctant to switch from vinyl to compact discs and MP3s around the early 2000s. This resistance was partly attributed to "clasismo," as vinyl had become a status symbol after its perceived "death," and partly because early CDJs offered few compelling reasons to switch.
