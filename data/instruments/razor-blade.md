# instrument - Razor blade

## curiosities
**Early Audio Editing Tool** : For the first 250 episodes of the show, voice tracks recorded on quarter-inch tapes were manually edited down using a razor blade, alongside a grease pencil, in a real-time analog process. "Then Craig and I would get out a razor blade and tape and a grease pencil. And we would edit the voice track down in real time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4xxgPX-aR90
**Tool for "For Real" Incident (May 15, 1991)** : Richie Edwards used a razor blade to carve the words "for real" into his forearm during an interview with NME reviewer Steve LeMack at the Norwich Art Center. This act of self-mutilation was a dramatic response to questions about the band's authenticity. "I mean, who carries a razor blade with him? And he carved the words for real into his forearm." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCwgvHg86Oc
