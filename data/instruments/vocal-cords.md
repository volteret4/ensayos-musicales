# instrument - Vocal Cords

## curiosities
**Age-Related Changes** : With age, vocal cords lose flexibility, becoming thinner and stiffer. This stiffness reduces their ease of vibration, making it difficult, if not impossible, to hit high notes which require extreme vibration. This leads to singers having less control over their vocal performance. "The vocal cords themselves lose flexibility. They get thinner and become stiffer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjwRApoJhA
**Gendered Vocal Changes with Age** : As vocal cords stiffen with age, some men experience an increase in vocal pitch, while women may experience the opposite effect. This is due to the reduced flexibility and vibration capacity of the vocal cords. "Some men find their voices increase in pitch. Women may find the opposite." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjwRApoJhA
