# instrument - Singing Arc

## curiosities
**First Purely Electronic Instrument (1899)** : Invented by physicist William Duddell in 1899, the Singing Arc was discovered when the coil and capacitor of a carbon arc lamp produced sound. Duddell learned to control the oscillations in the circuit with a keyboard to alter its pitch, making it the first purely electronic musical instrument. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
