## curiosities
**Sales Decline in the 2010s** : Digital album sales experienced a sharp decline in the 2010s. After selling 118 million units in the United States in both 2011 and 2012, sales crashed by 67% to just 39.3 million by the decade's end. This reflects the rapid shift from ownership to access-based streaming. "As the 2010s began, the recorded music industry was still very bullish on selling digital albums... Again, if we look at the United States, 118 million digital albums were sold in both 2011 and 2012. But then things began to drop, and by the time the decade ended, just 39.3 million were sold." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNqqLDpUuZY
