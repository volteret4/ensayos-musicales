## curiosities
**RCA's Response to the LP (1949)** : RCA Records introduced the 7-inch, 45 RPM single in March 1949 as a direct competitor to Columbia's newly launched LP format. "Texarkana Baby" by Eddie Arnold was among the first 45s to be released. "RCA Records responded to Columbia's LP with the 7 inch 45 RPM single." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
