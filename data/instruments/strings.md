## curiosities
**Remote Recording for "One Tree Hill"** : The string parts for U2's "One Tree Hill" were recorded by Toronto musicians Dick Paul and Adele Armin at Grand Avenue Studios in Hamilton. The recording session was directed remotely by Daniel N. Waw and The Edge through a six-hour trans-Atlantic phone call. "Dick Paul and Adele Armin of Toronto recorded their parts at Grand Avenue Studios in Hamilton. They were directed by Daniel and the edge over the course of a six hour, Trent Atlantic phone call." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VR5Fncdc2kQ
