## curiosities
**Auto-Tune Integration** : Some guitar amplifiers are now manufactured with auto-tune technology built directly into them. This feature offers real-time pitch correction, allowing a musician to play an incorrect note and have it "corrected before it comes out of the speaker," effectively perfecting the output before it reaches the audience. "For example, there are guitar amplifiers with auto-tune built-in, which means you can play a bad note and have it corrected before it comes out of the speaker." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Zniri9uSlc
