# instrument - Sound System

## curiosities
**Origins and Use in Jamaica** : Portable sound systems were built by entrepreneurial individuals in Jamaica specifically for playing music at "high volumes on the street and at parties and festivals." These systems were central to the competitive music scene and the emergence of Rude Boys.
