# instrument - Earbuds

## curiosities
**Bluetooth Limitations** : Currently, wireless earbuds, when connected via Bluetooth, do not possess sufficient bandwidth to accurately transmit true lossless audio signals. This technical constraint means that despite advancements in audio file formats, the final sound quality delivered to wireless earbuds remains subject to compression. "Bluetooth connections for wireless headphones and earbuds don't have enough bandwidth to properly transmit true lossless signals." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v7DXXu_1fvM
**Poor Quality Audio Consumption** : Earbuds are explicitly criticized as offering the "worst" audio quality for listening to music, despite their widespread use, especially among younger generations. "Ear buds are the worst." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lGNrJgwQjgg
