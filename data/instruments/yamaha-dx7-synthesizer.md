# instrument - Yamaha DX7 synthesizer

## curiosities
**Ubiquitous Sound of the 1980s** : The Yamaha DX7 was the first instrument to fully leverage FM Synthesis technology, earning it the distinction of being the "most important, most popular, and most ubiquitous electronic instrument of the 1980s." Its widespread adoption by virtually every band utilizing synthesizers contributed significantly to the uniform sound prevalent in much of the decade's music, regardless of genre. "And the first company to really take advantage of this was Yamaha, specifically with this model known as the DX7. The DX7 has been called the most important, most popular, and most ubiquitous electronic instrument of the 1980s. Almost every band that used synths had a DX7 somewhere in the mix." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TsiYLGGSJ2U
