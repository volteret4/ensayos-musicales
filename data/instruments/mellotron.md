# instrument - Mellotron

## curiosities
**Description and Sound** : The Mellotron is described as a "taped piano" that, when played as a piano chord, can produce sounds like an actual recorded flute or even a string arrangement. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ocMHRLQMQY0
**Famous Uses** : The Mellotron is most famously known for its background flute sound on Led Zeppelin's "Stairway to Heaven." It was also used by Brendan O'Brien on the Red Hot Chili Peppers' "Blood Sugar Sex Magik" album, particularly on the song "Breaking the Girl," and later on Billy Talent's "Saint Veronica" and "White Sparrows." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ocMHRLQMQY0
