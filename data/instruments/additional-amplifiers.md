## curiosities
**Enhancing Binaural Transmission (1933)** : During EMI's 1933 binaural broadcast of the Philadelphia Orchestra, the quality of the telephone line transmission was significantly improved by incorporating additional amplifiers. These boosted the frequency range, resulting in sound comparable to modern FM radio.
