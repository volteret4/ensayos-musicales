## curiosities
**High-Margin Format Post-1965** : The rise of the album format, characterized by high-margin LPs, became a pivotal factor after 1965, enabling musicians to generate significant wealth from record sales. This format surpassed the 45 RPM record in terms of profitability and popularity. **Introduction of the Long-Playing Album (June 1948)** : The LP album was first introduced in June 1948 but was initially considered too costly for ephemeral pop music; consequently, record labels primarily utilized LPs for classical recordings, Broadway shows, and jazz.
