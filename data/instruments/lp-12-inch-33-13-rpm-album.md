# instrument - LP (12-inch, 33 1/3 RPM album)

## curiosities
**Introduction and First Release (1948)** : The 12-inch, 33 1/3 RPM album, known as the LP, was launched by Columbia Records in 1948, replacing the 78 RPM record as the industry's primary format. The first LP featured the Philharmonic Symphony Orchestra of New York performing Mendelssohn's Violin Concerto in E Minor.
