# instrument - Smartphones

## curiosities
**Critical Mass and Music Consumption Shift (2010s)** : Smartphones achieved "critical mass" in the 2010s, becoming central to music consumption. They integrated functions previously handled by separate devices like iPods, serving as computers, communicators, gaming units, cameras, and links to vast libraries of cloud-based music. "But in the 2010s, smartphones achieved critical mass. There are computers, our communicators, our gaming units, our cameras, and are linked to tens of millions of songs in the cloud." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNqqLDpUuZY
**Dominant Device for Music Listening (2010s)** : By the 2010s, the majority of music listening occurred on smartphones. This represented a shift from dedicated portable music players, as smartphones consolidated multiple functions including access to tens of millions of songs in the cloud. "when it comes to listening to music, we do most of it from our phones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNqqLDpUuZY
