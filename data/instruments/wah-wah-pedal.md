# instrument - Wah-wah pedal

## curiosities
**Mechanical Precursors and Rock Innovation** : The wah-wah effect had been mechanically reproduced by brass players as far back as the 1920s. However, it was truly brought into rock music in 1967, with Jimi Hendrix and Eric Clapton releasing records featuring the pedal within days of each other, marking its innovation in guitar effects.
