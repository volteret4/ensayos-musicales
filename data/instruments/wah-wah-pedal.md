# instrument - Wah-Wah Pedal

## curiosities
**Origins and Popularization** : While the wah-wah effect was mechanically reproduced by brass players as far back as the 1920s, it was incorporated into rock sound in 1967. Jimi Hendrix and Eric Clapton both released tracks featuring the pedal within days of each other in the spring of 1967. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
