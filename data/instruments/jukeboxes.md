**Music Dissemination & Economic Impact (Pre-1937)** : Jukeboxes played a crucial role in spreading new genres like R&B, country, and rock and roll. Their immense popularity was so significant that a coin shortage in 1937 was attributed to the sheer number of coins being fed into these machines by the public.
