# instrument - iPod

## curiosities
**First Song Loaded (2000)** : During the development phase of the iPod by Apple, the track "GrooveJet" by Italian producer Spiller was the first song transferred onto a prototype device in Cupertino in 2000. This represented an early step in the creation of portable digital music players. "The track is GrooveJet and it was the first song transferred to a prototype iPod in Cupertino that year." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
**Influence on Podcasting Name** : Apple's iPod, a personal audio device introduced in 2000, played a significant role in the coinage of the term "podcasting." Journalist Ben Hammersley, in a 2004 article for The Guardian, combined "iPod" with "broadcasting" to create the neologism, effectively linking the device to the emerging on-demand audio format. "He combined Apple's iPod with broadcasting." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uEe_HLUay5M
**Portability Revolution (Early 2000s)** : The iPod, along with other portable digital devices, revolutionized music consumption by allowing users to carry thousands, or even tens of thousands, of songs with them everywhere. This unprecedented portability, combined with instantaneous track access, was a key factor in the widespread adoption of compressed file formats like MP3s, despite their reduced audio fidelity. "With portable digital devices like the iPod, you could take thousands, tens of thousands of songs with you everywhere." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GlcOoINI7EQ
