# instrument - Eight track mobile unit

## curiosities
**Used for Live Recordings on Warp Tour** : During Weezer's nine-date stint on the Warp Tour in summer 2000, an eight-track mobile unit was utilized to capture some of their live performances and soundtrack recordings. "They had an eight track mobile unit capturing some of their performances, but most of what we got were soundtrack recordings." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DoJa8jcrlUA
