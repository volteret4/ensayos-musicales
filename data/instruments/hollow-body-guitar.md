# instrument - Hollow-body Guitar

## curiosities
**Prone to Feedback** : Hollow-body guitars were the most common type of guitar produced at a certain point but were susceptible to feedback issues when played too loudly. This limitation spurred the development of solid-body electric guitars to mitigate such acoustic problems.
