# instrument - Snare drum stand

## curiosities
**First Significant Drum Innovation** : The introduction of a dedicated stand for the snare drum, appearing around 1898, marked the first significant innovation in the development of the modern drum kit. This allowed the snare to be played independently and more easily by a single drummer alongside other instruments.
