# instrument - Oscillators

## curiosities
**Wonky Behavior of Mini-Moog Oscillators** : The Mini-Moog's distinctive sound was partly due to its three oscillators, devices that control voltage in sound, not syncing up properly. This "wonky" behavior, while a technical imperfection, serendipitously enabled the Mini-Moog to produce wild and crazy sounds. "It wasn't very reliable when it came to staying in tune and holding settings because engineers couldn't get the things three oscillators, the devices that controlled voltage in the sound to sync up properly." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
