## curiosities
**Thomas Edison's Recording Medium** : Thomas Edison's "talking machine," the phonograph, introduced in 1877, utilized cylinders as its primary recording medium. These cylinders could only capture audio in a monophonic format, with all spatial attributes of a performance being lost. "Thomas Edison's cylinders and Emil Berliner's rotating discs could only capture and record the audio that came at the recording horns." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbSmBrhNACg
