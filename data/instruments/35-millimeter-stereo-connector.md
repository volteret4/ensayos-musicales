# instrument - 3.5 millimeter stereo connector

## curiosities
**Descendent of Quarter-Inch Plug** : The ubiquitous 3.5 millimeter stereo connector, used for line-in and line-out sockets on computers and countless other devices today, directly descended from George Koi's 1878 design for the quarter-inch funnel plug. This smaller connector adapted the durable, quick-connect principles established by Koi for modern portable electronics and audio equipment.
