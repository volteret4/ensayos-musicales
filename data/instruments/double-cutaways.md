# instrument - Double Cutaways

## curiosities
**Enhanced Fretboard Access** : Double cutaways are specific dips cut into the body of an electric guitar, like the Gibson ES-335 and Fender Stratocaster, that allow players to easily reach the frets closest to the body. This design greatly improves playability for higher notes on the fretboard. "Those are the dips cut into the body that allow you to play those frets closest to the body." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
**Extended Fret Range** : The double cutaways on the ES-335 were revolutionary for their time, enabling players to reach all the way up to the 22nd fret. This extended range was a significant advantage that no other guitar offered at that point. "With the ES-335, players can reach all the way up to the 22nd fret, something that no other guitar offered at the time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
