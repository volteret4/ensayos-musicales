# instrument - Remington C-11 shotgun

## curiosities
**Purchase by Dylan Carlson for Kurt Cobain (March 30, 1994)** : The Remington C-11 shotgun that was later found at the scene of Kurt Cobain's death was purchased by his friend, Dylan Carlson, on March 30, 1994. Kurt, claiming he needed a new gun for "protection" after police confiscated his previous collection, provided Carlson with $308.17 to cover the cost of the shotgun and a box of shells from Stan Baker's Sports.
