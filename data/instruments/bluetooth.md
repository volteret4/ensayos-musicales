# instrument - Bluetooth

## curiosities
**Limitation for High-Res Audio** : The current Bluetooth standard poses a limitation for high-resolution audio transmission on devices like the iPhone because it lacks the necessary bandwidth to accommodate the large data files of formats such as FLAC. This means that even if a phone supported high-res files, a physical wired connection to headphones would be required to experience the full quality. "Part of the problem is that it relies on Bluetooth to connect to headphones, and the current Bluetooth standard just doesn't have enough bandwidth to accommodate all the data of, say, a high-res, flack file." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GlcOoINI7EQ
