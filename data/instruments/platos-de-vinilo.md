**Used as Instruments in Turnabilism** : Vinyl turntables were innovatively utilized by DJs as musical instruments within the turnabilism movement, rather than just for playing pre-recorded music. This pioneering approach allowed for the creation of unique sounds and techniques like scratch and breakbeat, which became fundamental to the instrumental aspect of hip hop.
