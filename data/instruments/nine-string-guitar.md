# instrument - Nine-String Guitar

## curiosities
**Used in Complex Math Rock** : Nine-string guitars, along with eight-string guitars, were noted as instruments that signaled "really complex math rock" in the 2010s progressive rock comeback. Their use indicated bands pushing the boundaries of musical complexity and technical virtuosity.
