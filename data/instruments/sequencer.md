# instrument - Sequencer

## curiosities
**Automating Keyboards and Bass Lines** : The sequencer, described as a "new robotic device," was adopted by New Order to automate keyboards and trigger Gillian Gilbert's synthesizers. Bernard Sumner also utilized it to sequence Peter Hook's bass line, which allowed entire songs to be played by machines, foundational to their innovative sound. "they got a sequencer, this new robotic device that could automate keyboards and had it trigger something on Jillian Gilbert synthesizers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY ← sequencer
**Device for Orchestrating Other Devices** : A sequencer is a technological development that enables other musical devices to perform specific actions at precise times. It essentially tells instruments what to do and when, allowing for automated and complex musical arrangements without continuous human intervention. "The final big technological development was the sequencer, a device that could tell other devices what to do and when." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0 ← sequencer
**First 36-Step Sequencer (Crabwork/Matt Rangweacher)** : Crabwork collaborated with the German sound company Matt Rangweacher to construct the very first 36-step sequencer in existence. The development of this pioneering machine was so financially demanding that Ralph Hütter resorted to mortgaging his own house to cover the costs. "Craf Work desarrolla el primer sintanoma con Matt Rangweacher, la empresa de sonido alemana con la que construyeron el primer secuenciador de 36 pasos que existía." ← Music Radar Clan > Quienes eran Kraftwerk realmente？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9zIi8kqDI7g&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← sequencer
