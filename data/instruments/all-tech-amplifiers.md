# instrument - All-tech amplifiers

## curiosities
**Beatles' Shakespeare Stadium Equipment (1965)** : At The Beatles' August 1965 concert at Shakespeare Stadium, their sound system consisted of four All-Tech amplifiers, each delivering 175 watts of power. These amplifiers, used with the standard baseball PA system, were considered "crazy, crazy loud" for their time, yet were overwhelmed by the 135-decibel crowd noise, underscoring the limitations of amplification technology for large rock concerts. "all they had behind them was four all-tech amplifiers, each generating 175 watts of power, through the same PA system that the announcer used for baseball games." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
