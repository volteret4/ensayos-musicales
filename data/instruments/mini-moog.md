# instrument - Mini Moog

## curiosities
**Portable and Affordable Synthesizer (1971)** : Introduced by Dr. Robert Moog in 1971, the Mini Moog revolutionized music by being portable (a little bigger than a suitcase) and, crucially, much cheaper than its predecessors, making advanced synthesis technology accessible to a new generation of musicians, especially those coming of age in the punk era. "The big break came in 1971 when Dr. Mogue showed up again, this time with a new synthesizer design he called the Mini Mogue. This one invention made much of today's music possible." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zSFhhEaiYwE
