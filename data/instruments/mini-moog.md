# instrument - Mini-Moog

## curiosities
**Catalyst for Gary Numan's Electronic Shift** : In the spring of 1979, Gary Numan's discovery and impromptu playing of a Mini-Moog synthesizer left in a studio dramatically changed his musical direction. This encounter inspired him to transition from guitar-based punk to electronic punk, leading to the creation of his massive hit "Are 'Friends' Electric?". "Gary Newman, previously a big fan of guitars, started playing with the new synthesizer someone had left in a studio, was a mini-mog." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
