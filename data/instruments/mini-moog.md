# instrument - Mini-Moog

## curiosities
**Portable and Affordable Synthesizer (1970)** : Introduced by Dr. Robert Moog on January 24, 1970, the Mini-Moog (or mini-Mogue) was small, portable, powerful, and, at $1,200, reasonably cheap. Within three years of its introduction, 300 units were being sold every month, providing rock musicians with their first viable alternative to guitars, pianos, and drums. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
