# instrument - Whammy Pedal

## curiosities
**Jack White's Use for Bass Sound** : Jack White utilized a whammy pedal, set to transpose the sound down one octave, to create the distinctive bass-like riff for "Seven Nation Army" on his K Archtop guitar. "And the reason it sounds like a bass guitar is because it's funneled through a whammy pedal set to transpose the sound down one octave." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVIswnZYFkU
**Tom Morello's Use** : Tom Morello frequently uses a whammy pedal, notably on songs like "Bulls on Parade," to achieve his unique sounds. "He's playing the Arm the Homeless guitar through a whammy pedal on Bulls on Parade." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVIswnZYFkU
