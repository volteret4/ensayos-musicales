## curiosities
**"Down In It" Video Production Mishap** : For aerial shots in the "Down In It" music video, a camera was attached to a balloon due to budget constraints. A gust of wind tore the balloon and camera from its moorings, causing it to drift 150 miles east of Chicago to Burr Oak, Michigan. It was found by a farmer and mistakenly given to the police, who, upon reviewing the footage of a staged murder, believed it was evidence of a real gang or cult killing, leading to an FBI investigation that lasted a year. "The video director required some aerial shots but since there was no money in the budget for a crane and a helicopter and all that sort of stuff, a camera was attached to a balloon and sent aloft." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DKdF8NgF5Bw
