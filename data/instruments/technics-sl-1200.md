# instrument - Technics SL-1200

## curiosities
**Gold Standard for Direct Drive** : The Technics SL-1200 series is widely regarded as the gold standard for direct-drive turntables. It is prized for its powerful motor, durable tone arm, and intuitive pitch control, designed to withstand extensive use and abuse. "The gold standard for direct drive turntables, as far as I'm concerned, is the family of techniques SL-1200s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IIJySSo276k
**Historical Impact and Museum Display** : Introduced in 1972, the SL-1200 achieved such popularity and cultural significance that an example is now displayed in the London Science Museum as a piece of technology that shaped the world. Its rapid acceleration (0 to 33 1/3 RPM in 0.7 seconds) and resilience were fundamental to the development of hip-hop and rap. "The SL-1200 was first introduced in 1972, and was so popular that an example is on display at the London Science Museum as an example of a piece of technology that shaped our world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IIJySSo276k
**Indestructible Turntable for DJs** : The Technics SL-1200 turntable, even a 2010 model, is renowned for its durability and is considered "pretty much indestructible," especially by those who have deejayed in clubs. Many expect it to be the last turntable they would ever need to purchase due to its robust construction. "This turntable is pretty much indestructible. If you've ever deejated a club, you'll know what I'm talking about." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VlIErdSSVOM
**Production Halt and Revival** : Technics manufactured the SL-1200 in large quantities until 2010, when they ceased production, deeming it no longer cost-effective. However, the subsequent resurgence of vinyl records led to a dramatic increase in prices for used 1200s, prompting Technics to reintroduce the model in 2016, though at a significantly higher cost—two to three times its original price. "Techniques cranked them out by the thousands until 2010, when they decided that it was just too much trouble, and the return on investment just wasn't worth it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IIJySSo276k
**Revolutionizing DJ Mixing (1972)** : The Technics SL-1200 turntable, introduced in 1972, became highly prevalent and played a crucial role in elevating record mixing to a "whole new level" for DJs. Its advanced capabilities supported the growing demand for seamless transitions and creative mixing on the dance floor. "And once very speed turn tables became more prevalent, like the amazing techniques SL-1200, which was introduced in 1972, mixing records went to a whole new level, whole new direction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0FYq5MBdHw4
