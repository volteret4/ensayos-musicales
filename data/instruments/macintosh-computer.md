# instrument - Macintosh computer

## curiosities
**Trent Reznor's Sampling Tool** : In 1994, Trent Reznor utilized a Macintosh computer to manipulate beats from Iggy Pop's "Nightclubbing." He loaded the waveform, turned it upside down, and added effects to create a new sound. "In 1994, Trent Rezner took those beats, loaded them into a Macintosh computer, turned the wave form upside down, added a few extra effects, and got this." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OxctiPr0l-8
