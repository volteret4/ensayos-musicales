## curiosities
**Growth of Market with Rock and Roll** : As rock and roll gained popularity, a significant market for drum sticks emerged, driven by the increased demand from new drummers. "A big market grew for drum sticks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WL3zEWJJNuw
**Vic Firth's Revolution in Manufacturing (1963)** : Vic Firth, founded in Boston in 1963, revolutionized drum stick manufacturing by developing sticks with a variety of consistent weights, balances, and feels across a series of carefully matched models, rather than just producing generic sticks. This innovation fundamentally changed how drum sticks were made and how drummers selected them, making stick choice as personal as selecting golf clubs or hockey sticks.
