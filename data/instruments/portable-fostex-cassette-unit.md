# instrument - Portable Fostex cassette unit

## curiosities
**Recording of Nebraska Album** : Bruce Springsteen famously recorded his entire "Nebraska" album between 1981 and 1982 using only a portable Fostex cassette unit. This choice underscored the possibility of creating impactful, high-quality albums outside of expensive studio environments, utilizing minimalist and accessible home recording equipment.
