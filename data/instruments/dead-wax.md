# instrument - Dead Wax

## curiosities
**Identification of Collectible Vinyl** : The "dead wax" refers to the space on a vinyl record between where the grooves run out and the center label. It can contain inscriptions, such as "Why don't you trade those guitars for shovels?" on the collectible Nirvana "Bleach" singles, which helps to authenticate valuable copies and differentiate them from counterfeits. "So look at the dead wax, which is that space between where the grooves of the record run out and the label of the center." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tbmssCZ05ro
