# instrument - Reebok's Real to Real tape machine

## curiosities
**Tom Molten's Use for First Extended Remix** : In the early 1970s, Tom Molten meticulously spliced together a long, unbroken version of a song on a Reebok's Real to Real tape machine to create what is potentially the first-ever extended remix for dance floors. He achieved this by adding eight or sixteen bars to the track. "Tom went home and carefully spliced together a long, unbroken version of a song on our Reebok's Real to Real tape machine, adding eight bars here, and there's 16 bars there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
