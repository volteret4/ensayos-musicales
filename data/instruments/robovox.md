# instrument - Robovox

## curiosities
**Florian Schneider's Custom Build** : The Robovox was a specialized piece of equipment built by Florian Schneider of Kraftwerk. He created it to electronically treat vocals in a distinctive way, adding to the band's innovative and futuristic sound, as heard on their 1974 "Autobahn" album.
