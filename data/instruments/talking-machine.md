# instrument - Talking Machine

## curiosities
**First Sound Capture Device (1877)** : The talking machine, first demonstrated by Thomas Edison in 1877, was among the earliest machines capable of capturing sound. Its technological limitation of recording for only up to three minutes directly influenced the standard length of popular songs that followed.
