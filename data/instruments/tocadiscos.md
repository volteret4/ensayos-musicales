**Preamplifier Integration** : Turntables can come in two main types: those with an integrated preamplifier and those without one. This distinction is crucial for connecting it correctly to the rest of the sound system. "Tenemos los tipos de tocatiscos, que son los tocatiscos con preamplificador y sin preamplificador." ← Music Radar Clan > El primer equipo de sonido | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HkoeYti1d7o&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← tocadiscos
**Source of Sound Quality** : The turntable is the fundamental component from which the sound quality originates. If the turntable is not good, the rest of the sound chain (preamplifier, amplifier, speakers) will only be able to maintain the initial quality and prevent loss, but it will never improve it.
