# instrument - Boom microphone

## curiosities
**Kingsmen Vocal Recording Technique** : During the recording of "Louis Louis" by The Kingsmen, a single boom microphone was suspended over the entire band. Singer Joe Eli, notably wearing braces, had to crane his neck back and sing almost directly into this microphone, which contributed to the unintelligible quality of his vocals on the track. "Second, singer Joe Eli, who was wearing big, heavy braces on his teeth, had to crane his neck way back and sing almost directly up into the single boom microphone hanging over everybody that was used for the session." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
