# instrument - Recording Console

## curiosities
**Sound City Studios Console at 606 Studios** : The control room at Foo Fighters' 606 Studios eventually acquired the renowned recording console from the famed Sound City Studios after its closure. This console boasts a rich history, having been used by iconic bands such as Nirvana (for "Nevermind"), Red Hot Chili Peppers, Rage Against the Machine, Slipknot, and Tom Petty, before finding its new home with the Foo Fighters. "The control room eventually acquired the recording console from the famed Sound City Studios that went out of business. That console has been used by Nirvana. Never mind what's made on it.
