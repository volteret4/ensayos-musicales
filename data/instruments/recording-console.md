# instrument - Recording console

## curiosities
**eBay Purchase for "Rubber Factory"** : The Black Keys acquired a recording console on eBay, which was used for their *Rubber Factory* album. This console had a history, having been previously owned by the sound engineer for Loverboy, though the band ultimately considered it "crap" and left it behind. "They bought a recording console on eBay, which happened to have been owned by the guy who Winston Soundford lover boy. But it was crap, and they ended up just leaving it in the factory when they were done." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
