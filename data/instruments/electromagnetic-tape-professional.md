# instrument - Electromagnetic Tape (professional)

## curiosities
**Superior Frequency Range** : Professional electromagnetic tapes, distinct from consumer cassette tapes, possess a significantly wider frequency range, often exceeding that of compact discs. These specialized tapes are utilized in high-quality recordings to capture a much broader audio spectrum.
