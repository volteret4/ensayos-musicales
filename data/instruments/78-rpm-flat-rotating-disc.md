# instrument - 78 RPM flat rotating disc

## curiosities
**Increased Recording Capacity and Song Length Standardization** : Invented by Emil Berliner, this format increased recording capacity slightly beyond the Edison cylinder, allowing for approximately three minutes of music per song. This limitation significantly standardized the length of popular music tracks to around three minutes, a convention that largely persists today. "An Emil Berliner came along with this flat rotating disc that spun at 78 RPM, capacity increased a little bit. You now had around three minutes for a song before you ran into space on the side of one of his records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
