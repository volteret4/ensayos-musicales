# instrument - Broken Bottle

## curiosities
**Broken Bottle as a Musical Instrument in Blues** : A broken bottle served as an improvised but effective tool for blues musicians to create "blue notes" or "bent notes" on a guitar fretboard. Used as an alternative when a knife was unavailable, this method allowed for string bending and the production of sounds outside conventional scales, contributing significantly to the distinctive sound of blues, jazz, R&B, and rock.
