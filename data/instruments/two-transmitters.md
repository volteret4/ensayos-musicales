## curiosities
**WPJ's Stereo Broadcast Method** : In 1925, American radio station WPJ experimented with stereo broadcasting by employing two separate transmitters. Each transmitter was dedicated to one channel (left or right) and broadcast at a distinct frequency, requiring listeners to use two receivers.
