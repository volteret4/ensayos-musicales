# instrument - Arm the Homeless Electric Guitar

## curiosities
**Tom Morello's Custom Main Guitar** : This is Tom Morello's most famous guitar, a custom instrument he named "Arm the Homeless Electric" after carving the slogan into its body in the early 1990s. He bought the original wood body from a Hollywood luthier in 1986 and then replaced all its components to his liking. "His most famous is probably a custom job that he calls his Arm the Homeless Electric. He calls it this because he carved that slogan, Arm the Homeless, into the body of a guitar sometime in the early 1990s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVIswnZYFkU
