**Pioneering Digital Keyboard for Remixing (1980s)** : The Fairlight was one of two sophisticated keyboard devices that emerged in the 1980s, revolutionizing remixing. It allowed operators to digitally store, surgically excise, manipulate, and implant musical material, offering unprecedented control for remix artists like Frankie Goes to Hollywood and New Order. "It began with two sophisticated keyboard devices, the Fairlight and the Sinclair. The new keyboards allowed operators to surgically excise a piece of musical material stored digitally, manipulated, and then implanted wherever they chose." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
