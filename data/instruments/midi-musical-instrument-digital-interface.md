# instrument - MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface)

## curiosities
**Global Communication Standard** : Originating from a paper titled "Universal Synthesizer Interface," MIDI became the worldwide standard for electronic instrument communication. It allowed a single cable to carry 16 channels of information, enabling one controller to trigger events on up to 16 different devices simultaneously. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
**Industry Standard for Communication (June 1983)** : Introduced in June 1983, MIDI provided a common language and industry standard for electronic musical instruments. This allowed synthesizers, drum machines, and sequencers from different manufacturers to communicate with each other, making it possible for a single musician to produce the sound of an entire orchestra on stage. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
