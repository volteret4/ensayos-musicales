# instrument - MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface)

## curiosities
**Standardized Communication Protocol (1983)** : Introduced in June 1983, MIDI provided a common language for different musical instruments and devices to communicate with each other, regardless of manufacturer. This standard meant that any MIDI-equipped synthesizer could now interact with any MIDI drum machine or sequencer, enabling a single musician to produce the sound of an entire orchestra on stage.
