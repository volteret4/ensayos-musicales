## curiosities
**DJ Transition (Pre-2008)** : Before 2008, a subset of "turntablist DJs" had not yet transitioned to CD decks or digital DJ programs like Serrato. These purists were among the few groups actively keeping vinyl alive during a period when its extinction seemed imminent. "The only people keeping vinyl alive were blood-eyed purists and turntableist DJs who had yet to make the transition to CD decks and DJ programs like Serrato." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNqqLDpUuZY
