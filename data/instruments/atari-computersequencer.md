# instrument - Atari (computer/sequencer)

## curiosities
**First Computer Sequencer** : The Atari was utilized by Chris Berkett as a computer sequencer, described as the first of its kind, during the production of Buffy St. Marie's "Coincidence and Likely Stories." "And I played all their MIDI files into the keyboard, the same keyboard she had in my studio. And we called it all that on the 2024 track tape and then then to do the vocals and everything else I had to take a flight case full of like 1524 tracks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mjQBaX_3oL4
