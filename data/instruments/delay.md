# instrument - Delay

## curiosities
**Creative Use at The Bathhouse Studio** : During the recording of "Michigan Left" at The Bathhouse, Arkells experimented late into the night with various effects, including a "weird delay that they had hanging around." Living in the studio building allowed them to use the equipment freely, often staying up until 5 AM to explore different sounds and percussion. "we were up till five in the morning sometimes with a weird delay that they had hanging around, like, or making weird percussion sounds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kKNn01M8GG4
