## curiosities
**Creative Use at The Bathhouse Studio** : During the recording of "Michigan Left" at The Bathhouse, Arkells experimented late into the night with various effects, including a "weird delay that they had hanging around." Living in the studio building allowed them to use the equipment freely, often staying up until 5 AM to explore different sounds and percussion.
