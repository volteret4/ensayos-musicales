**First Songs Ripped for Piracy (1996)** : In 1996, a group known as Compressed to Audio (CDA) began ripping CDs and making them available for free online for illegal file sharing purposes. Metallica's "Until It Sleeps" is believed to be the first song ripped and uploaded, closely followed by Rage Against the Machine's "Bulls on Parade" and Soundgarden's "Black Hole Sun."
