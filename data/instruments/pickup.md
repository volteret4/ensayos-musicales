## curiosities
**Function and Design** : A pickup is a device designed to convert the vibrations of a guitar's strings into electrical energy for amplification. It fundamentally consists of a wire coiled around a magnet. While research into pickups began in the 1890s, George Beechum was the first to successfully adapt the technology for use with the guitar. "The answer was a device known as a pickup, which essentially is a wire coiled around a magnet.
