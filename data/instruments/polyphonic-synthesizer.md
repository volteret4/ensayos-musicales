# instrument - Polyphonic Synthesizer

## curiosities
**Advancement in Sound Texture** : Polyphonic synthesizers significantly advanced electronic music in 1980-81 by allowing multiple notes to be played simultaneously, unlike earlier monophonic models. This innovation enabled a much "thicker" sound and richer textures in Technopop compositions, eliminating the need to layer individual notes on separate tape tracks to form chords. "The sounds thickened up thanks to new polyphonic synthesizers. You could only play one note at a time on the old synths, the monophonic ones, so if you wanted to build up a chord, you needed to layer each new note on a new track on the tape machine. Not with the polyphonic machines." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
