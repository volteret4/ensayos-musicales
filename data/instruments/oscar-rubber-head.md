# instrument - Oscar (rubber head)

## curiosities
**EMI's Binaural Experiment Device (1933)** : Oscar was the name given to a rubber human head accurately carved by EMI engineers for a binaural sound experiment on April 27, 1933. Microphones were placed where the ears would be, designed to capture sound more like human hearing for transmission.
