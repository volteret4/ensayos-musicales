**TR-808 Iconic Sound Rooted in Defective Transistors** : The Roland TR-808 drum machine, fundamental to the development of hip-hop and electronic music, owes part of its distinctive sound to transistors that were rejected for use in other devices because they were considered "too noisy." The defects became the defining characteristic.
