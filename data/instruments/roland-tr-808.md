# instrument - Roland TR-808

## curiosities
**Defining Acid House Rhythms** : The Roland TR-808 drum machine played a crucial role in the development of Acid House, providing the rhythmic backbone for its distinctive sound. Its inclusion was essential for creating the genre's specific production aesthetics. "Acid House with its bass lines from Roland TV 303 synthesizers and TR808 drum machines." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0FYq5MBdHw4
**Drummers' Concern (Early 1980s)** : In the early 1980s, the introduction of drum machines like the Roland TR-808 caused concern among drummers, who feared these new technologies would lead to job displacement. This sentiment mirrored the anxieties of other musicians facing technological advancements. "Drummers were concerned that newly introduced drum machines like the Roland TR-808 were going to put them out of work." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ak6OuOUkRrQ
**TR-808 Iconic Sound Rooted in Defective Transistors** : The Roland TR-808 drum machine, fundamental to the development of hip-hop and electronic music, owes part of its distinctive sound to transistors that were rejected for use in other devices because they were considered "too noisy." The defects became the defining characteristic. "Its distinctive sound is partly due to defective transistors that were rejected for other devices for being 'too noisy.'" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
