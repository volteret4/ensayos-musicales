# instrument - Roland TR-808

## curiosities
**Accidental Success from Defective Transistors** : The Roland TR-808, a foundational drum machine for hip-hop and electronic music, achieved its iconic sound partly due to the use of defective transistors. These transistors, rejected for other devices due to being "too noisy" and having "too much inherent distortion," were repurposed as noise oscillators, which are crucial for drum machine construction. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
**Analog Drum Machine (1980s)** : Roland unveiled the TR-808, an analog drum machine with a unique sound that inspired many musicians, contributing to the nervousness of human drummers in the 1980s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
