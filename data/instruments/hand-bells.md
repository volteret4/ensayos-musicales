# instrument - Hand Bells

## curiosities
**First Musical Instrument Played in Space (1965)** : Along with a harmonica, hand bells were stowed aboard Gemini 6 by Wally Schirra and Thomas Stafford and used to perform "Jingle Bells" on December 7, 1965. This performance represents one of the first times musical instruments were played in space. "On December 7, 1965, Wally Shira and Thomas Stafford pulled out a harmonica and some hand bells that they'd stowed away aboard Gemini 6 and performed jingle bells for the planet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
