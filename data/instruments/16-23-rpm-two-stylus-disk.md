# instrument - 16 2/3 RPM two-stylus disk

## curiosities
**EMI's Unsuccessful Stereo Attempt** : EMI experimented with a two-stylus disk designed to play at 16 2/3 RPM as a potential solution for stereo records. However, this format ultimately proved to be unworkable and failed to become a viable commercial option for stereo LPs.
