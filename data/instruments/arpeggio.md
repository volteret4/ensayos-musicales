# instrument - Arpeggio

## curiosities
**Central to "Stairway to Heaven" Lawsuit** : An arpeggio, defined as playing a chord by plucking its individual notes, was the specific guitar figure in Spirit's instrumental "Taurus" that Randy California claimed Jimmy Page ripped off for Led Zeppelin's "Stairway to Heaven," leading to a protracted legal battle.
