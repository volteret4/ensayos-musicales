## curiosities
**Apple Macintosh (1984) – Music Industry Adoption** : The Apple Macintosh computer was introduced on January 22nd, 1984, during Super Bowl 18, accompanied by the iconic "Big Brother" commercial directed by Ridley Scott. It was officially unveiled on January 24th, 1984, by Apple Computer. The Mac quickly became popular with musicians, especially after the development of the Pro Tools software by DigiDesign, which made it standard equipment in both recording studios and live performance settings. Its increasing use led Apple and third-party software companies to continuously tailor products to musicians' needs.
