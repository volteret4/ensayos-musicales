# instrument - Styla-phone

## curiosities
**Use in "Space Oddity"** : David Bowie utilized a device called a styla-phone in the recording of "Space Oddity." This instrument was reportedly introduced to him by his friend Mark Bowlin, though another legend suggests it was an "addictive one."
