**100% Intrinsic Noise Elimination** : The digital format offered a groundbreaking advantage by enabling, for the first time, the complete elimination (100%) of intrinsic noise. This inherent background noise had been an unavoidable characteristic of analog systems like vinyl and magnetic tapes. **Initial "Eternal" Claim** : Digital systems, notably compact discs, were initially believed to be intrinsically eternal supports, immune to degradation over time simply by being stored. It was commonly understood that a CD would function perfectly even after 200 years, offering a stark contrast to magnetic tapes.
