**Prominence in Ska** : The trombone is highlighted as a significant brass instrument in traditional ska music. Its "big" presence, along with the saxophone and trumpet, is a key component of the genre's sound. "Traditionally, ska also featured lots of brass instruments. Trag Jazz music was explicitly made with trombones, among other brass and rhythm instruments.
