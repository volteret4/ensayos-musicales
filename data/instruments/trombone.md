# instrument - Trombone

## curiosities
**Prominence in Ska** : The trombone is highlighted as a significant brass instrument in traditional ska music. Its "big" presence, along with the saxophone and trumpet, is a key component of the genre's sound. "Traditionally, ska also featured lots of brass instruments. The trombone is big, so it was the saxophone, maybe the trumpet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
**Traditional Trag Jazz Instrument** : The trombone is a traditional instrument used in Trag Jazz, a revival of Dixieland Jazz. Trag Jazz music was explicitly made with trombones, among other brass and rhythm instruments. "That meant music made with trumpets, the trombone, the clarinet, the banjo, upright bass and drums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
