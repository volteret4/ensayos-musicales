## curiosities
**Eclipsed 8-track and Cassette (1980s)** : The compact disc format eventually eclipsed both the 8-track and cassette. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
**Optical Recording Technology** : The CD is based on optical recording and playback technology patented by Jim Russell in 1970. Russell's invention digitized music into binary code to prevent the wear and tear associated with physical contact in vinyl. Sony and Philips later co-developed the commercial format after purchasing licenses for Russell's patents. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNiL63fY1Y8
