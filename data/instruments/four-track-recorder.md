# instrument - Four-track recorder

## curiosities
**Used by John Frusciante for Demos** : During his period of addiction and depression after leaving the Red Hot Chili Peppers, John Frusciante used a four-track recorder to make demos for his solo material. These demo tapes were among the few musical possessions that survived his house fire in 1996. "He preferred to focus on painting, attempting to write screenplays and messing around with a four-track recorder making demos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
