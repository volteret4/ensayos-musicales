# instrument - Four-track recorder

## curiosities
**DJ Squeaky's Essential Production Tool** : DJ Squeaky used a four-track recorder as part of his basic setup to produce the early records for foundational Memphis rap artists, underscoring the scene's raw, DIY approach to music creation. "Con un grabador de cuatro pistas, un sampler SP122, la caja de ritmos VOS DR660 y algún viejo teclado Roland, yo forma los primeros discos de la escena." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
**Used by John Frusciante for Demos** : During his period of addiction and depression after leaving the Red Hot Chili Peppers, John Frusciante used a four-track recorder to make demos for his solo material. These demo tapes were among the few musical possessions that survived his house fire in 1996. "He preferred to focus on painting, attempting to write screenplays and messing around with a four-track recorder making demos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc ← four-track-recorder
