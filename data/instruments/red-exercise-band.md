# instrument - Red exercise band

## curiosities
**Used in Chris Cornell's Suicide (May 18, 2017)** : Chris Cornell was found with a red exercise band around his neck, which had been attached to a carabiner jammed tightly into the doorframe of his hotel room. This band was the means by which he committed suicide by hanging. "Inside Cornell was found laying on the floor, blood running down his mouth and a red exercise band around his neck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
