## curiosities
**Raw Recording Technique** : For the recording of "Is This It," The Strokes intentionally used only three microphones to capture the album's sound. This minimalist approach was a deliberate choice to achieve a "gritty raw sound," contributing to the unprofessional aesthetic that initially concerned their US label, RCA. "The whole thing was recorded using just three microphones to get that gritty raw sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jEfofyobGiI
