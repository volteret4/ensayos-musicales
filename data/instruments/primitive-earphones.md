# instrument - primitive earphones

## curiosities
**Early Use with Tiatrophone** : Primitive earphones were used by subscribers of Clement Adder's Tiatrophone system in 1881 to listen to live opera and theater transmissions from their homes. These early devices provided a reasonably accurate sonic representation of the audience's experience. "People in their homes using these direct lines could be then connected and using primitive earphones here are reasonably accurate sonic representation of what the audience was hearing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbSmBrhNACg
