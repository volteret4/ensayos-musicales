**Jeff Mills' Mastery and Signature Sound** : Jeff Mills gradually polished and improved his production skills, becoming a master in the use of the Roland TR-909 drum machine. This instrument became a signature part of his sound, often incorporated into his famous 3- and 4-deck DJ sessions and even in orchestral performances. **Professional Drum Machine** : The Roland TR-909, released shortly after the TR-808 (within two years), is widely considered the first professional drum machine capable of complex functions. It became instrumental in allowing DJs to generate their own rhythmic sequences and sounds, marking a qualitative leap in DJing.
