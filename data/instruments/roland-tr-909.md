**Professional Drum Machine** : The Roland TR-909, released shortly after the TR-808 (within two years), is widely considered the first professional drum machine capable of complex functions. It became instrumental in allowing DJs to generate their own rhythmic sequences and sounds, marking a qualitative leap in DJing.
