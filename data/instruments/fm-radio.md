**Rise of FM Radio and Album Tracks (1960s)** : In the 1960s, FM radio, initially a low-rated and low-revenue segment, evolved as station owners allowed programmers and DJs to play album tracks beyond the Top 40 singles, which proved highly popular and signaled to the recorded music industry that albums, with their higher profit margins, represented the future.
