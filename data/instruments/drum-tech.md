## curiosities
**Scott Johnson's Death at Downsview Park Stage Collapse** : Scott Johnson, the drum tech for Phil Selba of Radiohead, tragically died on June 16, 2012. He was crushed by a 5,000-pound video monitor when a stage collapsed at Downsview Park in Toronto, just before a Radiohead soundcheck, an event that led to a comprehensive inquest and safety recommendations.
