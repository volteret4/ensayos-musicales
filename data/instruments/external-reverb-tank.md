## curiosities
**Fender's Licensed Technology** : In 1961, Fender, a manufacturer of both guitars and amplifiers, began selling an external reverb tank, a technology they had licensed from Hammond. "In 1961, Fender, which also made amps as well as guitars, sold what was known as an external reverb tank.
