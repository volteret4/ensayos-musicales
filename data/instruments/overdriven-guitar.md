**Neil Young's Signature Sound** : Neil Young's career in the 1970s was characterized by the sound of an "obnoxiously overdriven guitar grinding under a simple melody." This calculated sloppiness and use of distortion had a particular appeal, especially to young musicians in Seattle, making it a foundational influence on the grunge sound.
