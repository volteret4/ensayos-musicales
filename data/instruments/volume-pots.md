# instrument - Volume Pots

## curiosities
**Adam Jones's Meticulous Tone Control** : Tool guitarist Adam Jones is exceptionally particular about his guitar's volume pots, often going through ten different ones with his guitar tech to find one that feels "just the right way" for him. He utilizes the guitar's volume knob instead of the amp to achieve specific "slightly hollow-ish" tones. "he'll go through when he has his guitar tech replaced the volume pots in his guitar. He'll go through 10 of them before it'll feel just the right way that he likes it to feel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
