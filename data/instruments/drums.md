# instrument - Drums

## curiosities
**Alan Cross's Personal Connection** : Alan Cross, the podcast host, plays the drums and bought his first kit with money earned from teaching lessons at a drum shop. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Ancient Women Drummers** : Historical evidence from 3,000 years ago in Egypt, Mesopotamia, and Ancient Greece shows that women were humanity's first formalized drummers. They used drums to induce trance states during rituals. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
**Establishes and Holds the Beat** : The drummer is responsible for establishing and holding the beat of a band. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CVPPymc_qrk
**Gord Downey's Performance on "Pasco Submarine" (2003)** : Gord Downey notably played the drums himself on his song "Pasco Submarine," featured on his album "The Battle of the Nudes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Masculinization for Warfare** : Over centuries, the role of drumming shifted from women to men as the instrument was adopted for military purposes to lead armies into battle. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
**Motown Hired Local Drummers** : Many Motown acts in the 1960s touring live would hire local drummers to fill out their band, rehearsing quickly before shows. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Mushroom as Drummer (2025)** : In 2025, a Manchester outfit called Bionic and the Wires successfully used a mushroom as a drummer by converting its tiny electrical bleats into MIDI signals that controlled robot arms. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
**Neil Peart's Influence on Taylor Hawkins** : Neil Peart of Rush was Taylor Hawkins' drumming idol ("my guy on the wall"). Taylor admits to "ripping off" Peart's drum fills for Foo Fighters songs, specifically "crazy fills" and sections where the drums become a "big part of the song" (e.g., in "Winnebago" and "Times Like These"). ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
**Perceived as a "Man's Job"** : Prior to Maureen Tucker joining The Velvet Underground in 1965, playing drums was largely considered a man's job, making her a pioneer. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Prominence in Rush's "2112"** : Taylor Hawkins noted that Rush's "2112" was the first time he heard music where drums were so prominently featured. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
**Sen-e Takachi's Stick Breaking Habit** : Japan's first female Prime Minister, Sen-e Takachi, a metalhead and drummer, used to require four pairs of drumsticks for every gig during her university metal band days because she would break them all. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
**Stereotypes and Contributions** : Drummers often receive a "bad rap" and are the subject of jokes, but they have made very significant, often overlooked, contributions to music, altering the course of rock music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Tendonitis in Drummers** : Alan White, Oasis drummer, suffered from severe tendonitis in his hands and arms in 2000, a condition described as "a very common thing for drummers," leading to a show cancellation. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bB7GAUM63Y
**Zach Starky's First Kit** : Zach Starky received his first drum kit from his godfather, Keith Moon. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Zach Starky's Only Lesson** : Ringo Starr gave his son Zach Starky exactly one drum lesson. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
