**Custom Signature Guitar Design** : This custom signature guitar was designed specifically for St. Vincent (Annie Clark), making her one of the few individuals and potentially the only woman to have her own signature model. Described by St. Vincent as having a "retro-future" aesthetic, it is notably lightweight, comfortable for both standing and sitting play, available in various colors, and engineered for "tone flexibility" with three mini-humbuckers and a "convoluted, but very cool pickup configuration."
