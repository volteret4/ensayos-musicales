# instrument - Carabiner

## curiosities
**Used in Chris Cornell's Suicide (May 18, 2017)** : A carabiner, described as a "type of shackle" with shaped latches and a spring-loaded catch used to clip items to a belt, was found jammed tightly into a doorframe in Chris Cornell's hotel room. A red exercise band, which Cornell used to hang himself, was attached to this carabiner, leaving indentation marks. "The band had been attached to a carbonyl, which is a type of shackle. And I know you've seen these things. They used shaped latches that have a spring-loaded catch." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
