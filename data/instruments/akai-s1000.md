# instrument - Akai S1000

## curiosities
**Early Affordable Sampler** : The Akai S1000 is mentioned as one of the first affordable samplers that revolutionized music production, especially for hip-hop and electronic music. Its availability allowed DJs and producers to extensively manipulate drum breaks like the "Amen Break." "When sampling technology came along using gear like the Akai S1000, one of the first affordable samplers, DJs and producers started messing with Coleman's drum break again and again and again." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VnXBJqKwkrw
