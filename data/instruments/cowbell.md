# instrument - Cowbell

## curiosities
**"Suzy Q" Signature Drum Element** : The drum part for Dale Hawkins' "Suzy Q" featured a distinctive and ear-catching cowbell. This element originated during a soundcheck when Hawkins heard AJ Tomanello playing his drum and cowbell simultaneously, and the part was subsequently copied by Ronnie Lewis for the released version of the song. "That drum part was played by Stan Lewis's 15-year-old brother, Ronnie, on the new version, but he's closely copying the part that AJ Tomanello played on the demo." ← Episode 63： ＂Susie Q＂, by Dale Hawkins | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4vqj1DcXOfw
**Joke Inclusion in "Feeling This"** : In Blink 182's song "Feeling This," a cowbell was initially included as a joke, but it sounded good enough that the band decided to keep it in the final recording. "The cowbell was used as a joke, but it turned out okay so they kept it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0 ← cowbell
