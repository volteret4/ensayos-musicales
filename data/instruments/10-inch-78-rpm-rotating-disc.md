# instrument - 10-inch 78-RPM rotating disc

## curiosities
**Standard Format (Improved Audio)** : The 10-inch 78-RPM rotating disc became the standard recording format, significantly improving audio quality compared to earlier methods. Although better, it still couldn't fully replicate the sound of live music and held a maximum of four minutes of music per side.
