**Impact on Music Fidelity (Late 1982)** : The introduction of the Compact Disc (CD) in late 1982 revolutionized music listening, as consumers perceived its sound quality as unprecedentedly realistic and perfect. This led to billions of dollars being spent by consumers eager to experience the promised "musical perfection" of compact discs in their homes and cars. **Symbol of a Past Era** : The CD represents a bygone era of the music industry where physical album sales were the primary source of revenue and profit for artists. The phrase "post-CD world" denotes the current digital landscape characterized by declining physical sales.
