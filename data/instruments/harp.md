# instrument - Harp

## curiosities
**Performance at Bank of Montreal** : The harp was played by Toronto musician Joanne Jordan at an event held in the historic Bank of Montreal building, before it transitioned into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1993. During her performance, while playing the harp and appreciating the architectural beauty of the Great Hall's dome and stained glass, Jordan experienced a supernatural encounter, witnessing a ghostly apparition. "She was playing the harp and joined the beauty of the great hall's dome and the magnificent stained glass just above her." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TbQVjQPExYo
**Unconventional Use in Modern Music** : Tom Munger, a harpist in Florence + The Machine, began with classical training but diverged by incorporating jazz and rock styles, along with effects pedals, into his playing. This demonstrates a modern, experimental approach to an instrument traditionally associated with classical music, making it "not your average harpist." "And Tom experimented with adding all kinds of effects pedals to his instruments. He is not your average harpist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=24NeCYqlVco
