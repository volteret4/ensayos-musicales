# instrument - Electrophone

## curiosities
**British Version of the Tiatrophone** : The Tiatrophone system, which provided live music streaming over telephone lines, was introduced in the United Kingdom under the name Electrophone. This demonstrates the international adoption and branding of Clement Adler's pioneering technology.
