# instrument - Tascam 4-track

## curiosities
**Demoluca Recording Gear** : John Gallant owned a Tascam 4-track recorder, which was a rare piece of equipment at the time, and it was used to record Pezz's first 4-song demo, "Demoluca," in Ian D'Sa's parents' basement in 1994. "He used to have a foretrack and back then, there was a hard thing to find and he had a task in foretrack. So we recorded our first four-song demo in my parents' basement and we called it Demoluca." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
