## curiosities
**Drum Beat Production by Tim** : Drummer Tim utilizes Pro Tools to process and loop drum beats for the band's songwriting. He records practice sessions using various tools like mics, phones, or Zoom recorders, then cuts these recordings into loops and organizes them into folders to share with the other band members for creative inspiration. "I'll take that and I'll just cut up, like, you know, make loops out of these beats and then I'll just dump them all in a folder and send them to these guys so they can, you know, if they're ever sitting around holding a guitar or whatever they're doing, they can pull them up and just start going through them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kKNn01M8GG4
