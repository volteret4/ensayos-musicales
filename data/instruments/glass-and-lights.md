## curiosities
**Core of Pepper's Ghost Illusion** : Glass and lights are the fundamental components used to create the "Pepper's Ghost" illusion, a technique dating back to the late 19th century. This method projects a 3D image onto a stage, which, while not a true hologram, forms the technological root for many modern "hologram" performances by artists.
