# instrument - Marshall Amplifier

## curiosities
**Inventor of Marshall Amplifiers** : Originally a London drum teacher and music store owner, Jim Marshall began building custom amplifiers in the early 1960s. He responded to customer demand for louder gear that could cut through the noise of larger rock and roll audiences. His amplifiers were known for being thick and loud, with a modular "stack" design that became synonymous with the sound of powerful rock music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNiL63fY1Y8
**The Marshall Stack** : Developed by Jim Marshall, this modular configuration involves stacking amplifier guts and controls in one box on top of speaker cabinets. It became a visual and sonic staple of loud rock music and is used by artists including Slash, Oasis, Sum 41, and Pop Evil. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNiL63fY1Y8
