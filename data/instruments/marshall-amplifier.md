# instrument - Marshall Amplifier

## curiosities
**Detailed Specifications for Iggy Pop and The Stooges** : Iggy Pop and The Stooges' rider included extremely detailed and humorous specifications for Marshall amplifiers. This included three Marshall VBA bass amplifiers and three JCM 800 single channel master volume Marshall Amp Heads (100 watt, model 2203), requiring recent testing (within "living memory, preferably that of a goldfish"). It specified "six knobs" (Presence, Bass, Middle, Treble, Pre, and Post-Game) and two inputs (high and low impedance), explicitly forbidding dual-channel reverb heads or foot switches. "The actual model number of the amplifiers is 2203." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z2hvUDtBxho
