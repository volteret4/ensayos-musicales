# instrument - Acoustic Horn

## curiosities
**Central to Early Acoustic Recording** : The acoustic horn was a crucial component of early recording sessions, particularly before 1925. Musicians would gather around this large horn, which funneled sound to a diaphragm that vibrated, causing a stylus to cut grooves into a rotating disc, thereby creating a master recording without the use of microphones.
