## curiosities
**Nazi Magnetic Tape Recorder** : The Magnetophone was a German magnetic tape recording machine discovered by US Army Signal Corps member Jack Mullen near Frankfurt just before the end of World War II. These suitcase-sized devices used reels of magnetic tape to store and play back audio with remarkable fidelity, allowing German radio to broadcast hours-long speeches and continuous dance music, baffling Allied intelligence.
