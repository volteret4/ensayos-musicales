# instrument - Fuzz box

## curiosities
**Distinctive Sound of "Spirit in the Sky"** : The distinctive fuzz tone heard in Norman Greenbaum's "Spirit in the Sky" originated from a fuzz box that was built directly into his Fender guitar. This gear choice was instrumental in giving the song its unique sound.
