# instrument - Stems

## curiosities
**Definition and Use in Remixing** : "Stems" are defined as the individual musical elements (e.g., drums, vocals, bass) that layer together to create a full song. Access to these stems is crucial for remixing, allowing producers to "surgically excis[e] bits of the song and making copies" to extend its length or manipulate parts, akin to adding "breadcrumbs to some hamburger to make the meat go further." "It also helps if you have access to stems of a song. These are the individual musical elements that layered together make up the song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
**Origin of Stems** : Stems can be obtained from the original multi-track recording of a song or, in later developments, digitally recreated. "You can either get stems from the original multi-track recording or you can digitally recreate them for yourself, but that came later." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
