## curiosities
**Introduction to Hawaii** : The Spanish guitar was introduced to the Kingdom of Hawaii by Spanish and Mexican sailors prior to its annexation by the United States in 1893. Its presence there led to its repurposing by native Hawaiians into what became known as the Hawaiian guitar.
