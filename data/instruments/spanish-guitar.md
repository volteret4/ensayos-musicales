# instrument - Spanish guitar

## curiosities
**Flamenco Inspiration in Rock** : The Spanish guitar, with its inherent connection to flamenco, served as the opening sound for Love's album *Forever Changes*, directly inspired by flamenco records. "El disco Forever Changes de la banda de Los Ángeles Love empieza con una guitarra española inspirada en el flamenco." ← Cómo el Flamenco Influyó a Músicos Extranjeros (Y Quizá No Lo Notaste) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ReRvRfbHec
**Introduction to Hawaii** : The Spanish guitar was introduced to the Kingdom of Hawaii by Spanish and Mexican sailors prior to its annexation by the United States in 1893. Its presence there led to its repurposing by native Hawaiians into what became known as the Hawaiian guitar. "something called the Spanish guitar, which had been introduced to Hawaii by Spanish and Mexican sailors before the annexation." ← Ongoing History of New Music > Connections | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
