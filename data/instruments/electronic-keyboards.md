## curiosities
**Innovation in Flamenco** : Electronic keyboards were incorporated into Camarón de la Isla's album *La Leyenda del Tiempo*, a landmark in flamenco history. This instrumental addition, alongside funk and disco influences, marked a significant departure from traditional flamenco, initially drawing controversy from purists.
