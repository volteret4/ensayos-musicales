# instrument - Stick

## curiosities
**Glüleg's Carlos Alonzo's Instrument** : The "stick" is a distinct instrument played by Glüleg's singer Carlos Alonzo, characterized by a long fretboard with either 10 or 12 strings. It is described as "not the easiest thing to play," indicating its unique and challenging nature.
