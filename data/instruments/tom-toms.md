# instrument - Tom-toms

## curiosities
**Modern Definition and Mounting Options** : In modern terminology, drums that feature either an open-ended shell or a skin covering the bottom of the shell are referred to as tom-toms. These drums can be conveniently mounted on stands or positioned directly on the floor.
