# instrument - Motion Capture Suits

## curiosities
**ABBA Voyage Technology** : Motion capture suits are an essential component of the ABBA Voyage residency in London. They were worn by the band members to record their movements, which were then used with advanced projection technology to create lifelike "Abbatars" for the stage show, allowing the living members to "perform" without physically touring. "Through the use of motion capture suits and some really cool projection technology, it really does look like the four members of Abba are there on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
