## curiosities
**Sun Musical Equipment Company's Pioneering Role** : The first high-powered concert bass amplifiers were developed by Norm Sonholm (bass player for The Kingsmen) and his brother, who founded the Sun Musical Equipment Company out of frustration with existing amp technology. Their gear was later used by legendary bands such as The Who, The Rolling Stones, Jimi Hendrix, and Cream. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
