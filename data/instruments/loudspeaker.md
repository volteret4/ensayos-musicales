# instrument - Loudspeaker

## curiosities
**British Invention, Integrated by Magnavox** : The loudspeaker, a British invention, was integrated with microphone technology and Lee DeForest's amplifier tubes by Edwin Jensen and Peter Prittum of Magnavox between 1911 and 1915. This collaboration led to the creation of the first successful public address system, capable of amplifying sound for large audiences.
