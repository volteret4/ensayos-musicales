## curiosities
**Larry Hinkel's Makeshift Percussion** : For the HG Hancock band, drummer Larry Hinkel creatively assembled his percussion setup using common household items: a laundry basket for the bass drum, kitchen pots for cymbals or toms, and knitting needles as drumsticks. "Larry cobbled together a jumpsuit consisting of a laundry basket, some pots from the kitchen, and a couple of mom's knitting needles for sticks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA
