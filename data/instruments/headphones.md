**Noise-Canceling Headphones Origin (1978)** : The concept for noise-canceling headphones emerged in 1978 when Amar Bose, the founder of Bose speakers, was dissatisfied with the headphones provided to him on an airline flight. Before becoming available to the public, this technology was first adopted by the Air Force, Army, and aviation professionals.
