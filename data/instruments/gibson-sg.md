# instrument - Gibson SG

## curiosities
**Design and Variants (SG Jr., SG Custom)** : The Gibson SG is a solid body guitar that comes in several variants, ranging from the more affordable SG Jr. to the Deluxe SG Custom, appealing to a wide range of players. "SG stands for Solid Guitar, and there are many, many SGs out there. They come in four variants, the cheaper SG Jr., and all the way to the Deluxe SG Custom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d9HNqb9gJSU
**Les Paul's Name Transfer and Continuous Production (1961)** : In 1961, Les Paul's name was temporarily transferred to this new solid body design, which was dubbed the SG. The SG, an acronym for Solid Guitar, has been in continuous production ever since its introduction. "In 1961, Les Paul transferred his name to a new solid body design called the SG for a couple of years until his contract with the company ran out. The SG has been in production continuously ever since." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d9HNqb9gJSU
