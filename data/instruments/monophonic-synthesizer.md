# instrument - Monophonic Synthesizer

## curiosities
**Early Synthesizer Limitation** : Early synthesizers were typically monophonic, meaning they could only play one note at a time. To create chords, musicians had to painstakingly layer each individual note onto a separate track on a tape machine, a labor-intensive process that was revolutionized by the advent of polyphonic synthesizers. "You could only play one note at a time on the old synths, the monophonic ones, so if you wanted to build up a chord, you needed to layer each new note on a new track on the tape machine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
