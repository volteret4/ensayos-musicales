# instrument - Fender Telecaster

## curiosities
**Precursor to the Stratocaster** : The Fender Telecaster is recognized as the precursor to the Stratocaster. It was a one-pickup electric guitar and holds the distinction of being the world's first mass-produced and commercially successful electric guitar.
