# instrument - Recorders

## curiosities
**Early Fan Bootlegging Tool** : Recorders were used by fans, including a young Eddie Vedder, to sneak into concerts and capture live performances. This practice allowed fans to "relive the concert experience over and over again at home," influencing Pearl Jam's later decision to adopt a bootleg-friendly policy and release official live recordings. "Eddie Vedder remembers sneaking in recorders into shows when he was a kid, just so he could relive the concert experience over and over again at home." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
