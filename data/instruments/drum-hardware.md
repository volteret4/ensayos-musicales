# instrument - Drum hardware

## curiosities
**Mid-1950s Standardization and Refinement** : By the mid-1950s, innovation in drum hardware began to slow down, with manufacturers largely focusing on refining and improving the existing standard drum kit setup. This saw the common four-piece kit (snare, bass, mounted tom, floor tom) evolve into a five-piece with two mounted toms, and sometimes a second floor tom was added, along with changes in cymbal selection and arrangement.
