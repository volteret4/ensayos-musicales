## curiosities
**Neanderthal Bone Flute Discovery** : An ancient flute made from the femur of a bear was discovered in Slovenia. This flute, featuring four holes corresponding to the notes Do, Re, Mi, and Fa of the modern diatonic scale, was crafted by a Neanderthal and could be up to 82,000 years old. This discovery suggests music creation predates Homo sapiens. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BHN6db7m3DM
