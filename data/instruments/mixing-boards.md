## curiosities
**Advancement in Electrical Recording** : Mixing boards became an integral part of electrical recording studios after the introduction of magnetic tape technology. They allowed engineers unprecedented control over audio signals, facilitating the cutting, editing, and eventually multi-track and sound-on-sound recording that led to the modern recording industry.
