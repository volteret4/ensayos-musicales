# instrument - Organs

## curiosities
**Madchester Sound Component** : "Swirling organs" were a distinctive element of the Madchester sound, alongside jangly guitars, contributing to its psychedelic and indie rock aesthetics. "And the result was a clash of jangly guitars and swirling organs and indie rock aesthetics in a beat that you could actually dance to." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSvoppf1dGY
**Use in Blink 182 Recording** : Blink 182 utilized old organs during the recording of their self-titled 2003 album as part of their experimental approach to sound. "They used Polynesian Camel and Bals, turntables for scratching and looping, and old organs and cellos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0
