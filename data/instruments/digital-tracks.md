**Sales Decline in the 2010s** : Sales of individual digital tracks plummeted during the 2010s. From 1.3 billion tracks sold in the United States in 2012, sales fell to approximately a quarter of that by the end of the decade, indicating a dramatic shift away from purchasing individual songs towards streaming.
