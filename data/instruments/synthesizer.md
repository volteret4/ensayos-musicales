# instrument - Synthesizer

## curiosities
**"Punk" Approach to Playing** : Many musicians adopting synthesizers had zero formal musical training, but found it enjoyable to experiment and "scroll around" with them to produce cool, novel sounds that had not been heard before.
**Widespread Adoption (c. 1980)** : Became widely introduced around 1980, as the gear became cheap enough, portable enough, and powerful enough for everyday musicians to acquire and use.
