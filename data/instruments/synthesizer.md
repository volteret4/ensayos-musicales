## curiosities
**ARP 2500 in Close Encounters of the Third Kind** : The famous synthesizer notes featured in the movie *Close Encounters of the Third Kind* were produced using an ARP 2500 keyboard. This specific synthesizer is now on display at the National Music Center in Calgary. "The keyboard used was something called an ARP 2500. That very synth is on display at the National Music Center in Calgary." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
