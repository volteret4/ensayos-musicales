# instrument - Wawa pedal

## curiosities
**Introduction and Impact on Rock Music** : The wah-wah pedal was introduced by Henry Goldrich at Manie's Music in the mid-1960s. He convinced influential guitarists like Jimmy Hendrix and Eric Clapton to buy one, leading to its rapid adoption. The pedal dramatically changed how these artists played and influenced the entire direction of guitar-based rock and roll, becoming a ubiquitous effect in countless songs across genres including Metallica, Bob Marley, and Pink Floyd.
