# instrument - Teleharmonium

## curiosities
**Massive Early Electronic Instrument** : The Teleharmonium was an early electronic musical instrument known for its immense size and weight, exceeding 200 tons. Its massive scale required it to be transported between locations on railway cars, making it impractical for widespread use. "some were just too damn big, the Teleharmonium, which was one of these instruments, weighed over 200 tons, and it had to be moved from place to place on railway cars." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
