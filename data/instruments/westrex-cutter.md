# instrument - Westrex cutter

## curiosities
**Key to Stereo LPs (1957)** : In 1957, Westrex developed a revolutionary cutter that could carve a unique 45-degree angled groove, shaped like a valley, into vinyl. This innovation was the crucial element that enabled the creation of widely compatible and effective stereo LPs.
