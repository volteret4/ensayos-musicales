# instrument - Fender Esquire

## curiosities
**Prototype and Price** : The Esquire was a prototype solid-body Spanish-style guitar developed by Leo Fender by 1949, inspired by a design by Paul Bigsby. It was relatively affordable, priced at $140, and was noted for its good sound. "And by 1949, he had a prototype he called the Esquire. And $140, it was relatively cheap, and it sounded good." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
