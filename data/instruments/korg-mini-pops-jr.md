# instrument - Korg Mini Pops Jr.

## curiosities
**"Echo" Nickname and Band Name Influence** : Famously used by Echo & the Bunnymen, this unit acquired the nickname "Echo" within the band, a name that subsequently contributed to the band's own identity. "It is the Echo in Echo in the Bunnyman." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
**Affordable Drum Machine** : The Korg Mini Pops Jr. was a tiny and affordable drum machine, selling for less than $150. "tiny unit called the Corg Mini Pops Jr., which sold for less than 150 bucks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
