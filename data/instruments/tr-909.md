# instrument - TR-909

## curiosities
**Iconic Early Techno Gear** : The TR-909 (Roland TR-909) was one of the programmable drum machines used by early techno artists. Although it is now a very expensive and sought-after collector's item, it was initially an affordable piece of equipment, enabling young musicians in Detroit to create music.
