# instrument - TR-909

## curiosities
**Core of Gabber Sound** : The classic kick drum sound from the TR-909 rhythm box was a fundamental element in the creation of Gabber music. Producers would significantly accelerate this sound to 180-190 or even 200 BPMs and heavily distort it, transforming it into the genre's characteristic "sonic hammer." "cogieron el clásico bombo de la caja de ritmos TR-9-09, lo aceleraron hasta los 180, 190, o incluso 200 BPMs. Y lo distorsionaron hasta convertirlo en un martillo sónico." ← Qué fue el GABBER y por qué asustaba a Europa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0mnM_wO4eFY
**Iconic Early Techno Gear** : The TR-909 (Roland TR-909) was one of the programmable drum machines used by early techno artists. Although it is now a very expensive and sought-after collector's item, it was initially an affordable piece of equipment, enabling young musicians in Detroit to create music.
