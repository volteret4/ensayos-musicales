# instrument - Pioneer CDJ-1000

## curiosities
**Advanced Features for DJs** : The CDJ-1000 offered complex and advanced functionalities that were not possible with traditional turntables. These included looping capabilities, the ability to mark beats from 4 to 16 counts for loop creation, and various effect modules like echoes. These features provided a strong incentive for DJs to transition from turntables to CDJs. **Setting the CDJ Standard** : The Pioneer CDJ-1000, released in 2001, is considered the most important CDJ in history, defining compact disc DJing similar to how the Technics SL-1200 defined vinyl DJing. While not the first CDJ, it offered advanced features that gave DJs compelling reasons to switch from vinyl.
