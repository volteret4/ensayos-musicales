## curiosities
**Direct Recording Technique** : For *Sgt. Pepper's*, The Beatles adopted a direct recording system for many instruments, particularly electric ones. This involved connecting the instruments directly via cable, bypassing microphones, which led to much higher quality and a more professionalized recording method that later became standard.
