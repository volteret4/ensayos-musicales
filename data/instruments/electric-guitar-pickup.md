## curiosities
**Elvino Ray's Contribution to Invention** : Elvino Ray, Win Butler's grandfather and an employee of the Gibson Guitar Corporation, made significant contributions to the invention of what became known as the modern electric guitar pickup. A prototype of his work is displayed at the Jimmy Hendrix Museum in Seattle, recognizing his pioneering role.
