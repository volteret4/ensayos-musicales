# instrument - real-to-reel tape machines

## curiosities
**Stereo Recording Capability** : After further development, real-to-reel tape machines became capable of recording in stereo. This was achieved by dedicating half of the tape's width to the left channel and the other half to the right, marking a major advancement in multi-channel audio capture.
