# instrument - Farfisa organ

## curiosities
**Signature Garage Rock Sound** : The Farfisa organ, characterized as a "thin sounding organ," was a common and distinctive instrument frequently used in Garage Rock bands. Its unique timbre contributed to the genre's basic and raw sound.
