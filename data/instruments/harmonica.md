# instrument - Harmonica

## curiosities
**Bono's Playing on U2 Track** : Bono plays the harmonica on the U2 song "Trip Through Your Wires," track 9 from The Joshua Tree. "Bono plays harmonica" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VR5Fncdc2kQ
**First Musical Instrument Played in Space (1965)** : On December 7, 1965, astronaut Wally Schirra used a harmonica to perform "Jingle Bells" aboard Gemini 6. This marked the first instance of a musical instrument being played in space. "On December 7, 1965, Wally Shira and Thomas Stafford pulled out a harmonica and some hand bells that they'd stowed away aboard Gemini 6 and performed jingle bells for the planet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
