# instrument - Tambourine

## curiosities
**Papal Ban and Religious Condemnation** : The tambourine, along with other rhythmic instruments, was banned from "all of Christendom" by John Paul III. This prohibition was based on the belief that rhythm and music could lead to "heathenism," reflecting a broader historical suppression of percussion in religious contexts.
