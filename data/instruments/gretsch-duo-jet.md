# instrument - Gretsch Duo Jet

## curiosities
**Used on "Black Hole Sun"** : The Gretsch Duo Jet was the specific guitar used by Chris Cornell during the recording of Soundgarden's song "Black Hole Sun." It was run through a Marshall JMP-50 half-stack and a Mesa Boogie Dual Rectifier Solo half-stack, combined with a Leslie cabinet, to achieve the track's distinctive sound. "It's that last guitar we hear on this song, running through a Marshall JMP-50 half-stack, which provide the higher frequencies, as well as a Mesa Boogie dual rectifier solo half-stack for low-end grunt. The two amps were used in tandem, along with a Leslie cabinet, to get that distinctive warbling sound at the beginning." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
