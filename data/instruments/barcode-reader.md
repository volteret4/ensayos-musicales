## curiosities
**SoundScan Implementation (May 25, 1991)** : With the introduction of SoundScan on May 25, 1991, barcode readers at cash registers began to accurately count each record sale as it happened. This system provided precise, one-by-one sales data, replacing previous estimation methods. "Instead of guessing how many records were sold, a barcode reader at the cash register counted the record as being sold as it was being paid for, so sales were counted one by one." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rf1tFWQg51Y
