**Revolutionary DVS System** : Final Scratch is a system created by Richie Hawtin that revolutionized DJing by allowing DJs to import MP3 or computer-based sound files to turntables equipped with special vinyl records. These records, instead of containing music, simply used fixed rhythms and measures to control the sound loaded from the computer, effectively making digital files playable on traditional turntables. "Final Scratch es el sistema que nos permite importar sonido, de MP3 o del ordenador, a platos que tienen un vinilo que simplemente usa ritmos, que simplemente tiene compases fijo que reproducen el sonido que cargas es el ordenador." ← Music Radar Clan > HISTORIA Y EVOLUCIÓN DEL DJ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VtfJyysUI7A&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← final-scratch ← final-scratch
