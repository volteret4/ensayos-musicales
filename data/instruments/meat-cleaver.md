# instrument - Meat cleaver

## curiosities
**Purchased for Self-Harm in Amsterdam** : While on tour in Amsterdam, Richie Edwards slipped away to a store and purchased a meat cleaver. The suspicion among his bandmates was that he intended to use it to chop off his fingers to avoid having to go on stage and perform. "He also slipped off to a store to buy a meat cleaver and the suspicion was that he wanted to chop off a few fingers so he didn't have to go on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCwgvHg86Oc
