## curiosities
**Michael Jackson's Use on "Thriller" (1982)** : Michael Jackson utilized EMU gear on his iconic *Thriller* album. At the time, a $10,000 EMU machine allowed for sampling any sound, provided the sample did not exceed two seconds in length.
