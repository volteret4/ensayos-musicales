# instrument - Acetate Disc

## curiosities
**Test Pressing for Auditions and Demos** : Acetates are described as "test pressings" used for auditions, such as the Beatles' 10-inch acetate demo for EMI Records, and for circulating demos to record companies, as with The Velvet Underground's Scepter Studios recordings. "It's a 10-inch acetate, a test pressing in other words, and is a one-of-a-kind thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tbmssCZ05ro
