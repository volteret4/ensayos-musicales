# instrument - Orchestra

## curiosities
**Essential Element of Bond Themes (All Eras)** : A "big orchestral arrangement" is identified as a major point of continuity and an essential element across nearly all James Bond themes. Producers believe that without this, the theme would not sound "properly Bond" and could be mistaken for any generic action movie. This requirement persisted through the 90s and 2000s, influencing tracks like Billie Eilish's "No Time to Die" which used a 70-piece orchestra. "There has to be some kind of big orchestral arrangement. That's the major bit of continuity." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo
