## curiosities
**High-Res Audio Codec** : FLAC (Free Lossless Audio Codec) is mentioned as an example of a lossless compression codec used for high-resolution audio files. To experience the full data quality of a FLAC file on a mobile device, a physical wired connection to headphones would be required due to Bluetooth's bandwidth limitations. "You'd need to physically connect your headphone to the lightning or USB port with a wire." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GlcOoINI7EQ
