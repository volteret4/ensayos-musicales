**Initial Dismissal as Non-Musical** : In the early 1980s, when scratching first emerged with the concept of the DJ, many "old-fashioned critics" claimed it was not music. They argued that it was simply moving a record from one side to another and "destroying it," reflecting a resistance to new forms of musical expression.
