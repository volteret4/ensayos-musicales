# instrument - Pitch Wheel

## curiosities
**Guitar-like Note Bending** : The Mini-Moog synthesizer included a feature called a pitch wheel, which allowed players to bend notes in a manner similar to how guitarists bend strings. This innovation provided synthesists with a new level of expressive control previously uncommon in electronic instruments.
