# instrument - Turntables

## curiosities
**78 RPM Speed Setting (1960s-1980s)** : Many turntables sold through the 1960s, 70s, and 80s included a speed setting to allow playback of old 78 RPM records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
**Musical Instruments for DJs** : In the hands of skilled DJs, turntables and mixers transcended their original purpose to become genuine musical instruments, capable of creating entirely new music from spare parts, akin to an orchestra but without the need for expensive studio equipment like the Fairlight. This technique is now known as "turntablism." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
