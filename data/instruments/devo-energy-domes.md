# instrument - Devo Energy Domes

## curiosities
**Bob Casale's Urn** : These distinctive "weird flower pot things" were famously worn on the heads of Devo members. Following Bob Casale's death in 2014, his cremated remains were placed in a 3D printed urn designed to look exactly like one of these Devo Energy Domes. "Those by the way are called Devo Energy Domes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
