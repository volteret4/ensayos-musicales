# instrument - Roland TR-77

## curiosities
**Coexistence with Human Drummers** : The Roland TR-77 was a drum machine known to coexist alongside human drummers, as exemplified by Echo & the Bunnymen who used it after transitioning from the Korg Mini Pops Jr. "who by the way coexisted with another unit called the Roland TR-77." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
