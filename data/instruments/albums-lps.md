# instrument - albums (LPs)

## curiosities
**Dominant Format and Shelving Investment** : Albums, specifically 33 1/3 RPM LPs, were a dominant music format requiring significant shelving investment in record stores, alongside 45s, 8-tracks, and cassettes. Sam Goody initially focused solely on selling new LPs, which retailed for about $4.85 each. "Since it's been a lot of money investing in shelving, first for 45s and albums, then they needed new shelving for 8 tracks and cassettes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
