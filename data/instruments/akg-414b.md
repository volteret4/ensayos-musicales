# instrument - AKG 414B

## curiosities
**"Nothing Compares 2 U" Vocal Recording (1990)** : Engineer Chris Birkett used his "go-to mic," an AKG 414B, to record Sinead O'Connor's vocal for "Nothing Compares 2 U." She flawlessly sang the entire vocal in one take and then perfectly double-tracked it using the same microphone. "I put up my go-to mic with 414B, AKG, which I use all the time. And she nailed the whole vocal in one take." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=afuqjWl1JyI
