**Discontinuation Due to Streaming (2010s)** : Apple's decision to stop making iPods, one of the most successful consumer electronic devices ever, was a direct consequence of the rise and dominance of streaming services in the 2010s. The shift from owning music on a dedicated device to accessing it via the cloud rendered the iPod largely obsolete. **Pre-Smartphone Portable Devices (Pre-2010)** : Before 2010, people typically used separate portable devices like iPods for listening to music, often because they still relied on flip phones. The advent of smartphones later consolidated these functions.
