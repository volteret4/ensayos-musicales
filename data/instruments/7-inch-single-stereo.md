# instrument - 7-inch single (stereo)

## curiosities
**Introduction (May 1959)** : Stereo 7-inch singles first emerged in May 1959, bringing the new two-channel audio format to the popular single record market. This development contributed significantly to the rapid obsolescence of the older 78 RPM singles.
