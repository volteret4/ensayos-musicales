# instrument - 33 1/3 RPM vinyl record

## curiosities
**Long-Playing Mono Format (1948)** : The long-playing 33 1/3 RPM vinyl record was introduced in 1948, offering extended playback times. However, for a significant period after its introduction, all recordings on this format were still monophonic, preceding the widespread availability of stereo LPs.
