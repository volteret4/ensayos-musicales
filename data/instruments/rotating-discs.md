# instrument - rotating discs

## curiosities
**Emil Berliner's Recording Medium** : Emil Berliner's recording system utilized rotating discs as its medium, distinct from Thomas Edison's cylinders. Similar to cylinders, these discs could only capture monophonic audio funneled through recording horns, leading to a loss of spatial information and poor audio quality. "Thomas Edison's cylinders and Emil Berliner's rotating discs could only capture and record the audio that came at the recording horns." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbSmBrhNACg
