# instrument - microphones

## curiosities
**Placement in Oscar the Rubber Head** : As part of EMI's 1933 binaural sound experiment, microphones were strategically placed within the ear positions of a specially carved rubber human head named Oscar. This arrangement was designed to capture sound in a manner analogous to human hearing.
