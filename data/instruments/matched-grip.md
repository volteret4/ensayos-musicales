# instrument - Matched grip

## curiosities
**Ringo Starr's Popularization and Impact** : Ringo Starr favored the "matched grip" for holding drum sticks, where both sticks are held like clubs. This technique allowed drummers to "attack their instrument" with greater energy and a more primal feel. Following Ringo's prominent appearances on television (like The Ed Sullivan Show) and in movies during the mid-1960s, almost every subsequent rock drummer adopted the matched grip, making it the dominant stick-holding technique in rock music.
