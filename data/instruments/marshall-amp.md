# instrument - Marshall Amp

## curiosities
**"Father of Loud" Legacy** : Jim Marshall, the founder of Marshall Amplification, earned the moniker "Father of Loud" for his groundbreaking work in creating powerful amplifiers. Marshall amps, distinctive with their name in white script on the front, became essential for achieving the high volume required by hard rock and heavy metal. **Custom Build for Pete Townshend (1962)** : In 1962, Jim Marshall crafted a bespoke amplifier for Pete Townshend of The Who. Townshend specifically requested an amp "bigger and louder than the American Fender amps that he had been using" because he wanted "not to hear the crowd," a demand that significantly advanced amplification technology for live music.
