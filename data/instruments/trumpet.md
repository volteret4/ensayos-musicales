# instrument - Trumpet

## curiosities
**First Instrument for Bassists** : The trumpet can sometimes be a musician's first instrument before they transition to another, such as the bass, contributing to a diverse musical background. "In fact his first instrument wasn't bass, it was the trumpet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CVPPymc_qrk
**Prominence in Ska** : The trumpet is mentioned as one of the brass instruments that traditionally feature heavily in ska music. Its presence, alongside trombone and saxophone, contributes to ska's characteristic sound. "Traditionally, ska also featured lots of brass instruments. The trombone is big, so it was the saxophone, maybe the trumpet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
**Traditional Trag Jazz Instrument** : The trumpet is a traditional instrument used in Trag Jazz, a revival of Dixieland Jazz. Trag Jazz music was explicitly made with trumpets, among other brass and rhythm instruments. "That meant music made with trumpets, the trombone, the clarinet, the banjo, upright bass and drums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
