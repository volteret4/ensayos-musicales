# instrument - Remington 11 Shotgun

## curiosities
**Purchase and Use by Kurt Cobain (March 30 - April 5, 1994)** : A Remington 11 shotgun was purchased for Kurt Cobain by his friend Dylan on March 30, 1994, for $308.37, as Cobain claimed he needed it for protection. Cobain used this shotgun to commit suicide in the greenhouse at his home in the early morning of April 5, 1994, after injecting a significant amount of heroin.
