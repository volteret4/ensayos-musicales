# instrument - Rockman

## curiosities
**Small Box, Big Sound** : Invented by Tom Scholz of Boston, the Rockman was a compact device designed to provide the full "fuzz, distortion, and sustain" associated with a large, loud amplifier, but within a small box. This allowed players to achieve powerful, overdriven tones at low volumes, making it ideal for quiet practice or small spaces. "Tom Schoetz, the leader of Boston and a graduate of MIT, invented something called the rock man, which gave the player all the fuss and distortion and sustain of a Big Am playing loud, but in a small box that could be enjoyed quietly." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iGav9uJwA_w
