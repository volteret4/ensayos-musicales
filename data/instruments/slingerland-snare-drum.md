# instrument - Slingerland Snare Drum

## curiosities
**Source for Linn LM-1 Sample (1930s)** : A vintage Slingerland snare drum from the 1930s was specifically used to create one of the snare samples for the groundbreaking Linn LM-1 drum computer. "One of the snare sounds, for example, was an old slinger Lynn from the 1930s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0
