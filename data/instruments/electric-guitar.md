## curiosities
**Carl Perkins' "Triple String" Technique (1956)** : On "Matchbox," Carl Perkins utilized a unique "Triple String" guitar break technique, playing all three top strings at the same time with a pick. This was typically done finger-style, but Perkins adapted it and used it to assert his guitar prowess during the session, explicitly aiming to outshine Jerry Lee Lewis.
