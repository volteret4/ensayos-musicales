# instrument - Real Simple Syndication (RSS)

## curiosities
**Enabling Online Content Delivery** : In 2000, software developer Dave Weiner innovated a method to attach audio files to Real Simple Syndication (RSS), a format designed for sharing online content. This technological advancement was crucial because it allowed users to receive content directly without having to visit a specific website, forming a fundamental backbone for the early distribution and growth of podcasts.
