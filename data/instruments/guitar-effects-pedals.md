# instrument - Guitar effects pedals

## curiosities
**Central to Shoegaze Sound** : Guitar effects pedals were a crucial element in creating the characteristic shoegaze sound, enabling guitarists like Robin Guthrie of the Cocteau Twins to explore new sounds and My Bloody Valentine to create "layers and layers of feedback" and "near-trance-like soundscapes." The extensive use of these pedals meant musicians had to "keep their eyes on the foot pedals they were using," contributing to the "shoegazing" stage aesthetic. "Rarely had so many people use so many effects pedals in the guitars to create such an impenetrable atmosphere of sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vEHdoUlGurY
