# instrument - Drum Kit

## curiosities
**Chad Smith's 308-Piece Kit (2006)** : Chad Smith of the Red Hot Chili Peppers holds the Guinness World Record for performing with the biggest drum set, a massive 308-piece kit. This extraordinary setup, manufactured by Pearl Drums, was used for a special one-off performance in a music store in 2006, not for touring.
