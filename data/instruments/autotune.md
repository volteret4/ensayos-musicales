**Integral to Early Spanish Trap** : The use of Auto-Tune was a fundamental point for early Spanish trap artists, who produced music on cheap computers with a lot of it. This usage sparked controversy but ultimately reshaped music production and consumption. **Origin and Exaggerated Use in Trap** : Auto-Tune was originally created to correct vocal pitch. However, trap artists deliberately exaggerated its effect, deforming the human voice in a peculiar way to achieve a strident, narcotic-like sound that became a signature element of the genre. "El autotune se había creado para correcir tonos a la hora de cantar, pero si los exageras, deforma la voz humana de una forma muy peculiar y así lo soltrab para que sonar al estridente y sin arcóticas." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Ubiquitous Post-Trap** : After trap's influence, Auto-Tune became widespread, permeating commercial pop and indie music, cementing its place as a common production tool despite initial resistance.
