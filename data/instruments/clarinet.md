## curiosities
**Role in IMS's "Hello World" (N/A)** : The classical composition "Hello World," created and notated entirely by the IMS computer program from the University of Melaga, was specifically written for clarinet, violin, and piano. This demonstrates an early instance of a computer program composing for specific acoustic instruments without human musical input. "It was a classical composition for clarinet, violin, and piano." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QFtS8fl553Y
**Traditional Trag Jazz Instrument** : The clarinet is a traditional instrument used in Trag Jazz, a revival of Dixieland Jazz. Trag Jazz music was explicitly made with clarinets, among other brass and rhythm instruments. "That meant music made with trumpets, the trombone, the clarinet, the banjo, upright bass and drums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
