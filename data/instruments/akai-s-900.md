## curiosities
**Affordable Sampler Breakthrough (1986)** : Released by Akai in 1986, the S-900 was a significant breakthrough in making sampling more accessible. This "little sampler" could store a 12-second sample and cost only $1,000, representing a shift towards more powerful, smaller, and cheaper technology. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
