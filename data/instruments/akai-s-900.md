# instrument - Akai S-900

## curiosities
**Affordable Sampler Breakthrough (1986)** : In 1986, the Japanese company Akai released the S-900, a significant breakthrough in making sampling technology more accessible. This sampler had enough memory to store a 12-second sample and was priced at only a thousand dollars, making it significantly more affordable than previous options like the Fairlight.
