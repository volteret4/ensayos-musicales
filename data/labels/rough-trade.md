# label - Rough Trade

## curiosities
**C86 Cassette Contributor (1986)** : One of the indie labels from which bands were selected to contribute to the NME's C86 cassette collection in 1986.
**Home to Major Artists** : Was notably home to The Smiths, who were recognized as the biggest indie band of their era.
**Part of Beggars Group** : Operates as one of the labels within the "solar system" of the Beggars Group.
