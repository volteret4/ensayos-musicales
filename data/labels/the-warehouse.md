# label - The Warehouse

## curiosities
**Depeche Mode Riot Incident (March 20, 1990)** : One of The Warehouse's stores in Los Angeles was the site of an infamous riot on March 20, 1990, during an autograph session for Depeche Mode's *Violator* album. Thousands of fans overwhelmed security, prompting a "tactical alert" by the LAPD and the deployment of 20 police units. The chaotic event led to injuries, arrests, and damage, including to a Fox News microwave van, and forced the band to depart early.
