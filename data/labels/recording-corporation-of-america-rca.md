# label - Recording Corporation of America (RCA)

## curiosities
**1957 Promotional Film** : In 1957, RCA issued a promotional film featuring Tchaikovsky's "Romeo and Juliet" to showcase the advancements in electronic recording. The film highlighted how modern technology allowed "great music" to be enjoyed in homes, depicting various listener types ("Smith" characters) who all sought the "truest, most lifelike reproduction of the original music possible," illustrating the era's pursuit of high fidelity.
