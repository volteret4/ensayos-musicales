# label - Odion Records

## curiosities
**Release of the First Record Album (1909)** : Odion Records released a four-disc set of Tchaikovsky's "Nutcracker Suite" in April 1909, pioneering the concept of packaging multiple records together as an "album." This collection, reminiscent of a photo album, retailed for 16 shillings, a considerable sum at the time.
