# label - Helium 3

## curiosities
**ISRC Registration** : Helium 3, distributed by Warner Music Group, is identified as the likely right holder that registered the ISRC (A-H-T) for Muse's song "Dig Down". This illustrates the role of labels in managing international standard recording codes.
