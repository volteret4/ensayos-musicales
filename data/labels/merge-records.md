# label - Merge Records

## curiosities
**Best-Selling Album: Funeral** : Arcade Fire's *Funeral* became Merge Records' best-selling album at the time, indicating its immense commercial success for an independent release and for the label. "Funeral had been the best selling album ever." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Continued Success: Neon Bible** : Arcade Fire's *Neon Bible* album surpassed the sales of *Funeral*, becoming an even greater commercial success for Merge Records and further solidifying their relationship. "And Neon Bible did even better." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Discovery of Arcade Fire (2003)** : Merge Records first took notice of Arcade Fire after their 2003 debut EP, *Us Kids No*, and subsequently requested a full album from the band, initiating their long-standing partnership. "that release attracted the attention of merge records, which was an indie label based out of Durham, North Carolina. They liked what the band was doing, and they asked for a full album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Independent Label for Arcade Fire** : Merge Records is an independent record label based in Durham, North Carolina, and is the label to which Arcade Fire is signed. Despite its indie status, it has been the home for critically acclaimed and globally recognized albums such as "The Suburbs." "Arcade Fire is an independent band from Montreal that records for an indie label in Durham, North Carolina." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ir2FfLQJNtY
**Independent Label in Durham, NC** : Merge Records is an independent label based in Durham, North Carolina, founded by members of the 90s indie band SuperChuck. It is the label for bands like The Arcade Fire. "They record for merge records, an independent label set up in Durham, North Carolina by a couple of members of a 90s indie band called SuperChuck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=__7iEl3lH8g
**Indie Label Background** : Merge Records is an independent label based out of Durham, North Carolina, notably run by members of another indie band called SuperChunk. "merge records, an indie label based out of Durham, North Carolina, and run by members of another indie band called SuperChunk" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Indie Label Success (Post-2004)** : Founded in 1989 by members of SuperChunk in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, Merge Records struggled for years. Its work began to pay off significantly in late 2004 when the Montreal-based Arcade Fire, a Merge act, gained popularity, becoming one of the best-selling independent artists of 2005. "They struggled for years, and it wasn't until late 2004 that this work really began to pay off. That's when a Merge Act, the Montreal-based Arcade Fire, began to take off." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
