# label - Merge Records

## curiosities
**Independent Label for Arcade Fire** : Merge Records is an independent record label based in Durham, North Carolina, and is the label to which Arcade Fire is signed. Despite its indie status, it has been the home for critically acclaimed and globally recognized albums such as "The Suburbs." "Arcade Fire is an independent band from Montreal that records for an indie label in Durham, North Carolina." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ir2FfLQJNtY
**Indie Label Success (Post-2004)** : Founded in 1989 by members of SuperChunk in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, Merge Records struggled for years. Its work began to pay off significantly in late 2004 when the Montreal-based Arcade Fire, a Merge act, gained popularity, becoming one of the best-selling independent artists of 2005. "They struggled for years, and it wasn't until late 2004 that this work really began to pay off. That's when a Merge Act, the Montreal-based Arcade Fire, began to take off." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
