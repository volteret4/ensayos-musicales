# label - Two Tone

## curiosities
**Founding and Ethos** : Founded in March 1979 by Jerry Dammers of The Specials with £700 borrowed from friends and family, the label's name reflected the black and white suits worn by 1960s mods and skinheads, symbolizing the band's peaceful, multi-racial makeup. "In March 1979, the Specials borrowed 700 pounds from family and friends to form an independent record label that Jerry Damors called Two Tone. This was after the black and white suits worn by mods and skinheads in the 60s and after the peaceful, multi-racial makeup of the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
**Success in Post-Punk Britain** : Two Tone had a successful run over several years as ska became one of the biggest trends in post-punk Britain, also signing other groups like The Selector, Madness, and The Beat. "Two Tone ended up having a pretty good run over the next few years as Scott became one of the biggest trends in post-punk Britain." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
