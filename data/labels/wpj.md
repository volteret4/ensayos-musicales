## curiosities
**WPJ New Haven Stereo Experiment (1925)** : WPJ, an American radio station located in New Haven, Connecticut, was at the forefront of stereo broadcasting experiments in 1925. The station utilized two separate transmitters, each broadcasting a distinct frequency for the left and right audio channels.
