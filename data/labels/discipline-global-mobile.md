# label - Discipline Global Mobile

## curiosities
**Founded by Robert Fripp to Reclaim Rights (Late 1980s)** : Robert Fripp established Discipline Global Mobile (DGM) in the late 1980s. This was a direct response to his discovery that Island Records was defrauding him of his material rights, with the label created specifically to pursue extensive legal actions to recover King Crimson's catalog.
