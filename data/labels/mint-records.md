# label - Mint Records

## curiosities
**CUB's Label** : Mint Records signed the Vancouver-based band CUB, known for their distinctive "cuddlecore" genre. This genre was characterized by melodic music, jangly guitars, and a pronounced childlike quality. The label supported CUB's unique artistic expression, which also involved their playful live performances such as playing in pajamas and bringing gifts for fans. "A perfect example of this is CUB of Vancouver band signed to mint records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
**Early Struggles and Breakthroughs** : The label experienced "a real struggle" in its initial years but gained momentum when its instrumental act, Huevos Rancheros, received a Juno nomination for Best Alternative Album. Further success came with The New Pornographers' debut album, *Mass Romantic*, in November 2000, which won an Alternative Juno. "The first years were a real struggle. But then things started to pick up. When one of their acts, the instrumental group Huevos Rancheros, received a Juno nomination for Best Alternative Album and then came the new pornographers. That first record won the alternative Juno." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q4dEV1yn3OY
**Founding and Mission (1991)** : Mint Records was founded in 1991 by "a couple of campus radio guys" with the explicit goal of providing a voice to up-and-coming acts in the lower BC mainland, drawing inspiration from Sub Pop's similar mission. "Mint had been around since 1991 when a couple of campus radio guys decided they needed their own record label. Their whole goal was very much like what we saw with Subpop. They needed to give a voice to the up and coming acts in the lower BC mainland." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q4dEV1yn3OY
**Vancouver Indie Label History (1991-Present)** : A Vancouver-based independent record label founded in 1991, dedicated to providing an outlet for up-and-coming local artists. It achieved decent, albeit modest, success before gaining more widespread attention with artists like The New Pornographers. "They'd been fighting the good fight since 1991, trying to be an outlet for up and coming local artists." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=raRCZ0Y5up8
