**Fiona Apple's Major Label Deal** : Sony Music signed Fiona Apple to a major label deal by the time she was 20. Her demo cassette reached the label through an unconventional route: it was passed to the babysitter of a music publicist, who then forwarded it to Sony, leading to her contract. "That publicist then passed it on to Sony Music, and by the time Fiona was 20, she had a major label deal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g ← sony-music
**London Headquarters Knife Fight (2018)** : In November 2018, the headquarters of Sony Music in London, England, was stormed by police, leading to the evacuation of the building and the closure of surrounding streets. The incident stemmed from a fight between two chefs in the catering department, who attacked each other with knives, resulting in injuries but no fatalities. "In November 2018, police stormed the headquarters of Sony Music in London, England." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO0K_N7Ys7I ← sony-music
**Option to Buy Further Share (2006)** : In 2006, Sony retained an option to purchase an additional 50% of Michael Jackson's remaining share in ATV Music, which would have granted Sony 75% ownership of The Beatles' publishing, then valued at $1 billion. "Sony retained an option to buy 50% of Jackson's remaining share. So that means Sony would own 75% of the Beatles publishing.
