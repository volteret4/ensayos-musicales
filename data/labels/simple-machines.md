# label - Simple Machines

## curiosities
**Cassette-Only Label Experiment (1992)** : This label, run out of a house in Arlington, Virginia, by members of the band Sudami, operated as an experiment to exclusively release material on cassette. Their strategy involved dubbing new cassettes (typically five at a time) as demand dictated, aiming to ensure their releases would never go out of print. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**Release of Dave Grohl's "Pocket Watch" (1992)** : Simple Machines released Dave Grohl's solo album "Pocket Watch" under his alias "Late" in 1992, on a cassette-only basis, and later unsuccessfully lobbied him to release it on CD. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
