# label - Apple Computers

## curiosities
**Underwriting US Festival (1983)** : The second US Festival in San Bernardino, California, was a large event underwritten by Steve Wozniak, a co-founder of Apple Computers. Ticketmaster secured the contract to be the sole ticket seller for this massive event.
