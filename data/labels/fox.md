**Highest-Ever Primetime TV Ratings (1991)** : The premiere of Michael Jackson's "Black or White" video in November 1991, broadcast simultaneously across multiple networks including the then brand new fourth TV network, Fox, gave Fox its highest-ever primetime TV ratings in its young history. This event highlighted the immense power of major music video releases to attract audiences.
