# label - Atlantic Records

## curiosities
**Eugenius Record Deal** : Atlantic Records signed the Scottish band Eugenius to a major label deal. This deal was secured following Kurt Cobain's public endorsement of the band after they opened for Nirvana on their 1991 European tour, leading to the release of two well-reviewed albums.
