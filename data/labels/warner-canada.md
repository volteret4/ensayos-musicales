# label - Warner Canada

## curiosities
**Continued Canadian Rights** : After a "bidding war," Billy Talent signed a split deal with Atlantic Records for global and US distribution, but Warner Canada retained their rights for the band's releases in Canada. This ensured their continued presence and support in their home country. "Canada, Warner Canada would still have us in Canada." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GT_u0eFDlk8
**First Right of Refusal Deal** : Warner Canada expressed early interest in Billy Talent and signed a deal granting them the "first right of refusal" on the band's demos. This arrangement proved to be both "a blessing and a curse" as it complicated later negotiations with other labels during a bidding war for the band. "Warner Canada, we decided to sign this deal with Warner Canada. They had the first right of refusal at the demos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GT_u0eFDlk8
