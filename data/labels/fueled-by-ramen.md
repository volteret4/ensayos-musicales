# label - Fueled By Ramen

## curiosities
**Signing of 21 Pilots (April 2012)** : This indie label, based in New York, signed 21 Pilots in April 2012. The decision was made after observing the massive indie fanbase the group had built and sampling material from their first two albums. A key condition of the deal required 21 Pilots to discontinue their *Regional At Best* album. "After hearing about the massive indie fan base the group had built and sampling material from these two albums, the band was signed in April 2012 by Fueled By Ramen, an indie label based out of New York." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8sAxFvktx-A
