# label - Toast Hawaii

## curiosities
**Andy Fletcher's Record Label** : Toast Hawaii is the name of the record label founded by Andy Fletcher of Depeche Mode. He sold his interest in the Gasconi restaurant to dedicate more time to this label, alongside his occasional DJ gigs. "He sold it to spend more time on his own record label, which he calls Toast Hawaii." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WIFxAaReLn0
