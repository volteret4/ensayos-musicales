# label - Third Man Records

## curiosities
**Innovative Vinyl Pressings** : This record label is renowned for its unique and unconventional special edition vinyl releases. These often feature records with two halves containing wild colored oil inside, and similar pressings have incorporated materials such as blood, hair, urine, marijuana leaves, razor blades, gunpowder, and even cremated remains.
