**Juan Atkins' Independent Venture** : Founded by Juan Atkins after the split of Cybotron, Metroplex became his personal record label. It was a vital platform for releasing not only his own music as Model 500 but also for supporting and showcasing other like-minded techno producers. "Para el elamente, establece su propio sello discográfico, Metroplex, donde no solo lanzaría su propia música, sino también la de otros productores afines." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw
