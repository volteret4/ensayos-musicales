# label - Geffen Records

## curiosities
**"Mother" of the Grunge Movement** : Geffen Records is described as the "absolute mother" of the grunge movement, playing an immensely significant role in shaping the sound of American rock. The grunge movement itself developed within the label in the late 1980s. **Cross-Pollination with Sonic Youth** : The signing of Sonic Youth to Geffen Records was highly influential. Sonic Youth's sound permeated the grunge albums being made at Geffen, and conversely, Sonic Youth absorbed fresh ideas from the younger grunge bands also working there, which helped to update their sound significantly during the 1990s. This interaction profoundly nourished American rock.
