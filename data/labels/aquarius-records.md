# label - Aquarius Records

## curiosities
**First Interest in Sum 41** : A representative from Aquarius Records attended a Sum 41 show at the Elmacombo, becoming the first label to express significant interest in the band before any other major labels. Despite a later bidding war, Sum 41 ensured Aquarius retained the Canadian distribution rights for their music. "somebody from Aquarius Records was there. And this was kind of before we had any real attention from any other labels." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6L4xKBFEYkE
**Well-Known Canadian Independent Label** : Aquarius Records is described as a prominent Canadian independent record label with a history stretching back to the 1970s, known for working with artists such as April Wine and Rough Trade. "Aquarius is this well-known Canadian independent record label that goes all the way back to the 70s and leaves." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6L4xKBFEYkE
