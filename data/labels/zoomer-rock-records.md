# label - Zoomer Rock Records

## curiosities
**Gavin Rossdale's Personal Label** : This is Gavin Rossdale's own record label, established for releasing his music. Bush's 2014 album, "Man On the Run," was released under this imprint, signifying increased artist independence. "It's on my label, Zoomer Rock Records. And your personal label?
