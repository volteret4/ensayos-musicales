# label - Robert Mapplethorpe's Independent Label

## curiosities
**Patti Smith's Debut Single (1974)** : On June 5, 1974, Patti Smith released her debut seven-inch single, featuring "Hey Joe" and "Piss Factory," on Robert Mapplethorpe's "tiny independent label." One thousand copies were issued, and the B-side, "Piss Factory," quickly drew attention.
