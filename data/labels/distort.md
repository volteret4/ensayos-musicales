# label - Distort

## curiosities
**Aggressive Music Champion** : Distort Records was started by Greg, a show promoter in Toronto who was a significant champion of aggressive music, booking bands like Devin Townsend and Strapping Young Lad. Distort released Alexis on Fire's self-titled debut album. "Greg, who ran to start, he loved aggressive music. And he was a show promoter in Toronto, too, at the time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
**Indy Label with Universal Distribution Deal** : Distort is an Indy label that records bands like Alexis On Fire. Recognizing its own limitations in distribution, Distort subcontracts all its distribution requirements to Universal. This strategic partnership enables their artists to access broader markets while the label retains its independent artistic control and identity. "Here's an Indy band. They record for an Indy label called Distort, but Distort doesn't have the distribution umph that they need to get their bands records to all the places that they have to be." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
