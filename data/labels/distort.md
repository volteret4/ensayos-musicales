# label - Distort

## curiosities
**Aggressive Music Champion** : Distort Records was started by Greg, a show promoter in Toronto who was a significant champion of aggressive music, booking bands like Devin Townsend and Strapping Young Lad. Distort released Alexis on Fire's self-titled debut album. "Greg, who ran to start, he loved aggressive music.
