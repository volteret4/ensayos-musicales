# label - Rolling Stone magazine

## curiosities
**Early "Punk" Usage Publication** : Rolling Stone magazine published Lester Banks' review of an MC5 album on April 6, 1969. This review contained a significant early use of the word "punks" in a musical context, with Banks describing "a bunch of 16-year-old punks on a meth power trip," marking a key moment in the term's association with rock music. "In a review of an album by a Detroit group called the MC5, published in Rolling Stone magazine on April the 6th of 1969, he managed to use this phrase.
