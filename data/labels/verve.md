# label - Verve

## curiosities
**Founding and Early History (1956)** : Verve Records was founded in 1956 as an independent jazz label. It demonstrated a willingness to experiment with music by signing acts like The Velvet Underground, who were considered too strange for mainstream rock and roll. "Verve, an independent jazz label founded in 1956, that decided to take a chance on the Velvet's, thanks largely to the group's connection to Andy Warhol." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
**Velvet Underground Signing (Post-Demos)** : After circulating their Scepter Studios demos to various record companies who all passed on signing them, The Velvet Underground eventually signed with Verve label, where they re-recorded the material. "Eventually, the velvet signed with Vervelable, rerecorded everything they'd done to that point, and completely forgot about those two acetates." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tbmssCZ05ro
