# label - Computer Applications Incorporated

## curiosities
**Early Computer Ticketing Involvement** : This New York-based computer company employed Harvey Dubner, a hardware specialist who was instrumental in the creation of the first computer-controlled communication service for ticketing, Ticket Reservation Systems Inc. (TRS). The company specialized in selling large, expensive mainframe computers, costing hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars. "The first person to come up with the idea of selling and keeping track of concert tickets with a computer seems to be Harvey Dubner, a hardware specialist at a New York computer company called Computer Applications Incorporated." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
