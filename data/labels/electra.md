# label - Electra

## curiosities
**Refusal by Daniel Johnston** : Electra Records offered Daniel Johnston a major record deal, but he famously refused it. His stated reason was that Metallica, who were signed to Electra, were "obviously possessed by Satan," illustrating how his unique and often delusional worldview impacted his career decisions. "Electra came calling with a major deal, but Daniel refused to sign. Why? Well, because Metallica was on Electra and they were obviously possessed by Satan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c8CD_FcWcg8
