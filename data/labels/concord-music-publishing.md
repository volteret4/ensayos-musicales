# label - Concord Music Publishing

## curiosities
**Acquisition of Imagine Dragons Catalog** : Concord Music Publishing acquired the entire music catalog of Imagine Dragons up to 2020 for $100 million. "Imagine Dragons, parted with all the music they'd written to that point. It all went to a company called Concord Music Publishing. And like I said, how much did they get? $100 million." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pe40SqDrpZ0
**Imagine Dragons Catalog Acquisition (2020)** : Concord Music Publishing purchased the majority stake in Imagine Dragons' music catalog (everything released up to and including the `Origins` album) in August 2020 for an assumed price of over 100 million US dollars. The company made this investment believing Imagine Dragons is a safe bet with a long lifespan, ensuring a return on investment through steady revenue from sales, streaming, radio play, commercials, movie soundtracks, video games, TikTok, and licensing. "Concord Music Publishing bought the Imagine Dragons catalog for something more than a hundred million US dollars back in 2020." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GmYlTwI_TQQ
