# label - Concord Music Publishing

## curiosities
**Imagine Dragons Catalog Acquisition (2020)** : Concord Music Publishing purchased the majority stake in Imagine Dragons' music catalog (everything released up to and including the `Origins` album) in August 2020 for an assumed price of over 100 million US dollars. The company made this investment believing Imagine Dragons is a safe bet with a long lifespan, ensuring a return on investment through steady revenue from sales, streaming, radio play, commercials, movie soundtracks, video games, TikTok, and licensing.
