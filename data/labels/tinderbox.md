# label - Tinderbox

## curiosities
**Release of 21 Pilots Double Album (2011)** : Tinderbox released a unique hard copy edition in 2011 that bundled both *Regional At Best* and the first self-titled *21 Pilots* album. The primary purpose of this packaged release was to attract the attention of other record labels. "Plus, there was also a second hard copy edition through a label called Tinderbox. It featured both Regional At Best and a copy of the first self titled album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8sAxFvktx-A
