# label - Dino Lone Records

## curiosities
**Arkells Partnership and EP Re-release (Late 2007)** : Dino Lone Records signed Arkells around late 2007, agreeing to re-release their *Deadlines EP* with a flyer placed in the City and Colour record, which was distributed across Canada. This partnership was formed under a tight deadline, leading to Arkells frantically changing their name from Charlemagne to Arkells in 45 minutes before the flyer went to print. "We had just started working with DinoLone records and they were going to, and we told Joel, we said, we're going to graduate in three months. And then after that, we're all yours. And he agreed to that. And he said, we're going to put a flyer for your EP. We're going to re-release your deadlines EP." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
