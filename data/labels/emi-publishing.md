# label - EMI Publishing

## curiosities
**Connecting Nora Jones to Blue Note** : An employee from EMI Publishing played a pivotal role in connecting Nora Jones with Blue Note Records. Her husband, JC Hopkins, was a musician who played with Nora's bassist, Lee Alexander, leading her to hear Nora perform at her 21st birthday jazz brunch. She then arranged a meeting between Nora and Bruce Lentval, the head of Blue Note Records. "Her husband, my bass player played with her husband. And I also had done a gig where I ended up doing a gig with him. And so they came.
