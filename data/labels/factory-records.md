# label - Factory Records

## curiosities
**Manchester-Based Independent Label** : Factory Records was an independent label based out of Manchester, which was not considered a "cool place to be" at the time compared to London. It was the label for archetypical British indie band Joy Division. "They recorded it for factory, an independent label based out of Manchester when Manchester really wasn't much of a cool place to be for anything." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
**Tony Wilson's Role** : Tony Wilson, associated with Factory Records, was a proponent of the theory that rock and pop music are engaged in a death struggle, playing out in cycles. His involvement lends credibility to historical observations about the cyclical nature of music trends. "That theory contends that rock and pop music are locked in a death struggle that plays itself out like clockwork every 13 years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-CT7yYXonIk
