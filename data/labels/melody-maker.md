# label - Melody Maker

## curiosities
**"Oh, You Pretty Thing" Article (January 22, 1972)** : This British music weekly published a front-page article in its January 22, 1972 issue titled "Oh, You Pretty Thing," featuring David Bowie. The article detailed Bowie's effeminate, camp image and his use of sexual ambiguity, which was seen as a deliberate act of "shocking outrage" for pop music. "He gave an interview to Melody Maker, one of the big British music weeklies. The title of the article was, oh, you pretty thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
