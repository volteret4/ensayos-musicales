# label - Live Nation

## curiosities
**Financial Impact of Lawsuits on Safety** : Live Nation has faced substantial financial repercussions from lawsuits related to festival disasters, such as the Astroworld Festival. These significant costs, as exemplified by the experience of Live Nation and Travis Scott, serve as a major incentive for the music industry to implement and improve crowd control, security, and safety measures. **Involvement in Downsview Park Inquest** : As the promoter for the Radiohead concert at Downsview Park, Live Nation was a key party in the extensive and complex inquest that followed the stage collapse on June 16, 2012, which resulted in the death of drum tech Scott Johnson. The inquest scrutinized the outdated stage design and safety protocols.
