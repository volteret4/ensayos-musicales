# label - Bing Crosby Enterprises

## curiosities
**Bing Crosby Enterprises VL System** : Bing Crosby Enterprises introduced a stereo system called VL, which was an acronym for Vertical and Lateral. This system attempted to record both left and right channels within a single record groove by tracking both vertical and lateral movements of the stylus.
