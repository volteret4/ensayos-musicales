# label - Ampex

## curiosities
**Ampex and the Magnetophone** : Ampex was founded by a US Army Major who disassembled Nazi magnetophones and brought them to America. The company was then established to commercialize this new magnetic recording system, bringing tape technology to a broader market. "A major in the US Army had then disassembled and sent them back to America, where he established a company called Ampex that offered this new commercial recording system." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbSmBrhNACg
**Magnetic Tape Recording Standard (Post-WWII)** : Ampex became a leading company in recording technology after receiving a $50,000 investment from Bing Crosby (worth over half a million today). Working with Jack Mullen, who brought German Magnetophone technology to the US, Ampex set the standard for both audio and video recording on magnetic tape. This development was crucial for the spread of multi-track recording and the modern recording industry. "Ampex then set the standard for both audio and video recording on tape." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
