## curiosities
**Investment in Ticketmaster (1980s)** : The Hyatt hotel chain, controlled by the Pritzker family, played a crucial role in Ticketmaster's growth by investing in the company. This investment stemmed from Hyatt's need for an untainted ticket seller to manage operations at the Superdome in New Orleans. "Another big break came when the high at hotel chain run by the Pritzker family got a contract to run the superdome in New Orleans. They talked to Ticketmaster and they liked the company enough to invest in it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
