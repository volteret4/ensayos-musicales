# label - Paramount

## curiosities
**Shellac Records** : In 1930, Paramount released Tommy Johnson's "Alcohol and Jake Blues" on shellac, a material that was very brittle and could easily smash like china. "When this was released on the Paramount label in 1930, we were still using Shalak to make records. They were very brittle and it could smash like China, even if he just looked at it wrong." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tbmssCZ05ro
