# label - Quality Records

## curiosities
**"Guess Who?" Stunt** : In 1965, Quality Records devised a clever stunt to get Canadian radio stations to play a garage rock single by Chad Allen and the Expressions. They sent out a 7-inch single with "Guess Who?" on the label instead of the band's name, successfully tricking stations into playing what they thought was a hot new British invasion band. "So the label Quality Records came up with a way to punk these stations. They sent around a seven inch single with the title of the song on the label. But where the name of the band should have been was just guess who?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
