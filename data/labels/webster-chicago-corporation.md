# label - Webster Chicago Corporation

## curiosities
**First 45 RPM Adapters** : The Webster Chicago Corporation was the first company to produce 45 RPM adapters. Their initial inserts were made of solid zinc and were difficult to fit into the large 45 hole, and even harder to remove without risking damage to the record. "The first company to make such a thing was the Webster Chicago Corporation." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
