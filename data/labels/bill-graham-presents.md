# label - Bill Graham Presents

## curiosities
**Evolution in the 1970s Rock Market** : As rock music became a massive business in the 1970s, Bill Graham Presents scaled up its operations to meet the demands of an exploding fan base, increased radio play, and larger tours booking bigger venues. This growth necessitated better ticketing and financial management systems. "When rock became big business in the 1970s, Graham knew he had to become big too." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
**Focus on Hippie Bands and General Admission Trick** : Founded by Bill Graham, the company initially promoted "hippie bands" in San Francisco at venues like the Fillmore and Winterland. Graham employed a peculiar trick with general admission tickets: he would collect untorn tickets at the door, put them in a basket, and then resell them at the box office once the basket was full. "His company, Bill Graham Presents, started with the hippie bands of San Francisco in medium-sized venues like the Fillmore and the Winterland." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
