# label - Verve Records

## curiosities
**Release of The Velvet Underground & Nico** : Verve Records, a division of Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, was the label responsible for finally releasing The Velvet Underground & Nico in March 1967, after significant delays due to censorship and Warhol's demanding production requirements, such as the peelable banana sticker.
