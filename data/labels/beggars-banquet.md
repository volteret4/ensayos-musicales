# label - Beggars Banquet

## curiosities
**Pioneering Independent Label** : Beggars Banquet was listed as one of the small independent labels that worked outside the major label system during the rise of indie music. "that included rough trade for a D, beggars banquet, epitaph, SST, and many others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
**Support for Gothic Bands** : Beggars Banquet was described as one of the "small nimble indie labels" that regularly appeared in post-punk England, signing artists like Bauhaus and releasing their debut single, "Bela Lugosi's Dead." "Bauhaus and Bella Logosi's dead, at least on beggars banquet, another one of those small nimble indie labels that popped up almost daily in post-punk England." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
