# label - Harry McHughan Sound Systems

## curiosities
**Second Dedicated Concert Sound System (1965)** : Harry McHughan Sound Systems was contracted by Herb Alpert and the Tijuana Brass for their 1965 tour, providing what is considered the "second concert sound system" specifically designed for touring artists. This represented an early step in the professionalization of live sound.
