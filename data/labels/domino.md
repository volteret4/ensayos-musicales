# label - Domino

## curiosities
**2005 Breakthrough** : Experienced a major breakthrough in 2005 when the Arctic Monkeys, a young and internet-savvy band, signed with them. This period also saw Franz Ferdinand, another Domino artist, achieve significant chart success, elevating the label's stature. "Their big break came in 2005 when a very young, very erudite, very internet-conscious band out of Sheffield came to their attention." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=raRCZ0Y5up8
**Dominant British Independent Label** : Domino is identified as one of the independent labels that dominate much of the British rock scene today. "much of the British rock scene is dominated by independent labels like Domino and Cooking Vinyl and Go Disks, Rough Trade, XL Recording and dozens and dozens more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=__7iEl3lH8g
**London Indie Label (1993-Present)** : Founded in London in 1993, this independent label struggled for the remainder of the 1990s, being marginalized as it and its roster were not part of the then-dominant Brit-pop scene. Despite challenges, it maintained enough credit and success to continue operating. "Domino, a London-based label founded in 1993 that just got out of the long for the rest of the decade." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=raRCZ0Y5up8
**Signing of Arctic Monkeys** : Domino Records won the bidding war for the Arctic Monkeys after their online demo phenomenon attracted numerous labels. Domino subsequently signed the band and oversaw the re-recording of many of their demos for their official debut album. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YvcnHKI4oIw
