# label - Drag City Records

## curiosities
**Release of Death's Lost Recordings** : Drag City Records played a pivotal role in the rediscovery of the band Death by releasing their seven long-lost songs from 1975 on an album titled "For All the World to See," bringing their pioneering proto-punk sound to a wider audience decades later. "One thing led to another and those old master tapes from 1975 made their way to a label called Drag City Records, who released those seven songs commissioned by Columbia on an album entitled For All the World to See." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cy5SCQV0HAY
