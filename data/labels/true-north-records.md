**Founding and Canadian Focus** : True North Records was established by Bernie Finkelstein, who was active in Toronto's Yorkville scene. The label was "unabashedly Canadian" and, aided by Cancon quotas, successfully launched artists like Bruce Cockburn and Rough Trade to legitimate hit status.
