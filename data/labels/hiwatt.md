## curiosities
**Adoption by Major Bands** : Hiwatt's powerful amplifiers attracted a roster of influential bands, with Pete Townshend famously defecting from Marshall to Hiwatt for its higher wattage. Other notable users included Pink Floyd, The Moody Blues, Fleetwood Mac, Genesis, Heart, and Jethro Tull. "P-tounds and defected from Marshall to high watt for this very reason. **Contribution to Modern Guitar Amps** : Hiwatt, along with Marshall and Vox, played a crucial role in the development and popularization of the modern guitar amplifier, shaping the powerful and beloved sounds characteristic of guitar rock. "The upshot is that between high watt, Marshall, and Vox, we have the modern guitar amp, and that lovely, powerful sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**Focus on Volume** : Reeves initially sold his amplifiers directly to musicians, with a key selling point being their exceptional volume. He named his amps "Hiwatt" to reflect their high wattage output. "At first he sold directly to musicians, promising them plenty of volume. He called his amps high watt because, well, they had a lot of watts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**Founding by Dave Reeves (1966)** : Hiwatt, another British amplifier firm, was founded by electronic engineer Dave Reeves in 1966. His entry into amplifier manufacturing was serendipitous, beginning after he was laid off and used his severance pay to transition from repairing electronics to making them. "This one, founded by an electronic engineer named Dave Reeves. The best thing that ever happened to him was being laid off in 1966, with the severance money he moved from repairing electronics to making them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
**High Wattage Output** : Hiwatt amplifiers quickly surpassed Marshall's 100-watt output, with models rated at 200 watts and later even 400 watts. This significant power attracted guitarists seeking maximum volume. "And while Marshall pumped out 100 watts, high watts were rated at 200, then 400." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fQCOj3_zy54
