# label - Technics

## curiosities
**Inventors of Direct Drive Turntables** : Technics is credited with inventing the direct-drive motor and the professional DJ turntable. Their SP10 model, released in 1970, was the first direct-drive turntable in history, followed by the iconic SL-1200 in 1972.
