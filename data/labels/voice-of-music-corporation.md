# label - Voice of Music Corporation

## curiosities
**Inventors of Famous 45 Adapter** : This company, based in Benton Harbor, Michigan, employed James L.D. Morrison, who invented the most famous design for the 45 adapter – the three-arm, swastika-like device. The patent for this adapter was filed in 1951, and tens of millions of these were in circulation by the 1960s. "It was invented by James L.D. Morrison for the Voice of Music Corporation of Benton Harbor, Michigan, and that patent was filed in 1951." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VlIErdSSVOM
