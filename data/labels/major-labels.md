## curiosities
**Distribution Deals with Indie Labels** : Major labels have distribution deals with many indie labels, allowing them to participate in the broader music market. "In many cases, these acts came from the world of indie labels who had distribution deals with the major labels." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Industry Dominance (Traditional Wisdom)** : Traditional wisdom holds that the recorded music industry is dominated by major labels. "Traditional wisdom says that the recorded music industry is dominated by the major labels." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Reduction to Three Companies** : Over 25 years, the number of major labels was reduced to just three: Universal (the biggest), Sony, and Warner Music. "over the last 25 years their number has been whittled down to just three companies, the Universal, the biggest, Sony and Warner music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
