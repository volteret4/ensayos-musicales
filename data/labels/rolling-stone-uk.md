# label - Rolling Stone (UK)

## curiosities
**Stan Lee Donwood Interview (September 2004)** : The Rolling Stone magazine in the UK was "completely revolutionized" by the prospect of interviewing Stan Lee Donwood, as it was considered a major event in music for such a reclusive artist to grant an interview. The interview, conducted entirely by email after initial refusal to give questions in advance, was finally published in September 2004.
