# label - Zamba

## curiosities
**Acquisition by BMG** : In 2002, Zamba was acquired by BMG. This meant that Volcano and all the artists on its roster, including Tool, returned to the ownership of the company that had originally owned Zoo Entertainment. "In 2002, Zamba was bought by BMG, which returned Volcano, and all the other artists on the roster, back to the company that had owned it when it was still called Zoo Entertainment." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
**Acquisition of Volcano** : In the spring of 1998, Zamba purchased Zoo, which was subsequently renamed Volcano. Zamba became the parent company of Volcano, and thus Tool, until Zamba itself was acquired by BMG. "In the spring of 1998, Zoo was bought by Zamba, whereupon Zoo's name was changed to Volcano." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
