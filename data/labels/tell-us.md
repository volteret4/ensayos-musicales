**Broadcasting Audio Improvement** : Tell Us, Steve Church's company, was dedicated to enhancing audio quality for broadcasting purposes. Church's appreciation for Fraunhofer's Layer 3 led his company to manufacture and license Zeffer audio streaming conversion boxes, operating on MPEG Layer 3, to the National Hockey League, initiating a significant improvement in broadcast sound. "Steve was looking for ways to improve the quality of audio when it came to broadcasting, radio and such.
