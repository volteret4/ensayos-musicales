# label - Sire Records

## curiosities
**"Don't Call It Punk" Marketing Campaign (Late 1970s)** : Launched a deliberate marketing campaign to promote its new signings under the banner "Don't Call It Punk," explicitly defining them as American New Wave to circumvent punk's negative baggage in North America.
**North American Distribution** : Served as the distributor for The Smiths' music in North America.
**Seymour Stein's Vision** : Label head Seymour Stein loved the new music emerging from punk and post-punk scenes, actively signing acts like The Ramones, The Dead Boys, The Talking Heads, The Pretenders, The Undertones, and Echo & The Bunnymen.
