# label - Nothing Records

## curiosities
**Trent Reznor's Label** : This was Trent Reznor's label. It released 12 Rounds' album "My Big Hero." Trent noted that the label was "dying," which affected its ability to properly support releases. "He and Trent first crossed paths in the late 90s when a 12-round record called My Big Hero was released through Trent's Nothing Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DKdF8NgF5Bw
