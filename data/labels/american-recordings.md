# label - American Recordings

## curiosities
**Major Label for Outsider Artist** : American Recordings, founded by Rick Rubin, provided major label distribution for some of Wesley Willis's material. This achievement represents a rare instance of an outsider musician gaining significant mainstream industry support. "He even picked up a record deal with Rick Rubin's American recordings, which means he actually had major label distribution for some of his material." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c8CD_FcWcg8
