# label - SST

## curiosities
**First Release (1979)** : Released Black Flag's EP "Nervous Breakdown" in 1979 as its inaugural record. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Growth with Hardcore Popularity** : The label significantly increased its output as hardcore music gained popularity and became more widespread during the Ronald Reagan administration. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Hardcore Spreader** : Beyond releasing Black Flag's records, SST was instrumental in spreading hardcore music from groups like The Minutemen, The Meat Puppets, and dozens of other acts. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Origin as Electronics Company (1966)** : Founded by guitarist Greg Ginn in 1966 when he was just 12 years old, initially operating as a mail-order business for electronic components. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Transformation into Record Label (1978)** : Ginn transformed the company into a record label in 1978 specifically to distribute material from his band, Black Flag, as no other labels were willing to sign them. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
