# label - Multinational major record label

## curiosities
**Contradiction with Rage Against the Machine's Politics** : Rage Against the Machine, a left-wing collective, faced inherent contradictions working with a "multinational major record label." This arrangement required them to adhere to label expectations such as touring, promotion, interviews, and releasing singles with edited expletives for radio and video play, which often clashed with their radical political message.
