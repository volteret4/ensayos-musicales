# label - Audio Eagle Records

## curiosities
**Patrick Carney's Indie Label** : This indie label is owned by Patrick Carney, and it notably signed the band Drummer, whose members are all drummers from other bands. "Patrick Carney was watching an indie band called Drummer, who was signed to his audio-eagle records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ooNAHswiT5Q
**The Black Keys Live Recording (2003)** : This venue in Austin, Texas, hosted a Black Keys show on October 24, 2003, during their *Thickfreakness* tour. This performance was officially released as the live album *Live in Austin*, notable for its initial release as a podcast. "It's simply called Live in Austin, and dates all the way back to a show they played in October 24, 2003, at a place called Emo's when the band was touring the Thick Freakness album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ooNAHswiT5Q
**The Black Keys' Performance Slot** : Following the positive critical reception of their third album, *Rubber Factory*, The Black Keys earned a performance slot at the Lollapalooza festival. "more good reviews, a slotted Lollapalooza..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ooNAHswiT5Q
