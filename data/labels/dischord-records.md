# label - Dischord Records

## curiosities
**Founded by Ian MacKaye and DIY Ethic** : Dischord Records was founded by Ian MacKaye and Minor Threat as part of their serious do-it-yourself (DIY) ethic. The label became a platform for Minor Threat's own material and eventually for other hardcore bands. "They had a serious do-it-yourself ethic, founding a record label called Discord that would eventually release material by other hardcore bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Policy of Low Record Prices** : A key principle of Dischord Records was a strict policy of keeping record prices low and affordable, making music accessible to their fanbase.
