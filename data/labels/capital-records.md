## curiosities
**Signing and Dropping of Virtual Rapper FM Mecha (2022)** : Capital Records signed virtual rapper FM Mecha in August 2022 and released the single "Florida Water." However, the label subsequently dropped FM Mecha due to significant backlash and criticism from the public, who viewed the character as a caricature of Black culture created by non-Black individuals.
