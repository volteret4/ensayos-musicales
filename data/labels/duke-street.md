# label - Duke Street

## curiosities
**Indie Label with Factor Funding** : Duke Street was an indie label that had access to Factor funding, which helped enable bands like Chalk Circle to record their debut single "April Fool" in 1986. This demonstrates how Factor's support extended to independent labels, fostering a new generation of Canadian musicians.
