# label - VH1

## curiosities
**Co-Premiere of "Black or White" (1991)** : VH1 was among the channels that simultaneously broadcast the premiere of Michael Jackson's "Black or White" music video in November 1991, reaching an audience of 500 million people worldwide. This made VH1 a key player in the cultural event surrounding the video's release. "it was seen simultaneously by 500 million people thanks to MTV, VH1, BET, and the brand new fourth TV network, Fox." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7IDbqdIw3WU ← vh1
**Major Eurodance Disseminator** : VH1, in conjunction with MTV and Viva, was instrumental in the dissemination of Eurodance music. Its heavy rotation of music videos contributed significantly to the genre's widespread popularity on television during the 1990s.
