## curiosities
**Issue of Oversized Records (1904)** : In 1904, the British company NeoPhone issued records that were an extraordinary twenty-one inches wide. This highlights the early lack of standardization in record sizes, as the industry experimented with larger formats to increase capacity, despite the challenge of finding compatible turntables.
