# label - Creation Records

## curiosities
**C86 Cassette Contributor (1986)** : One of the indie labels from which bands were selected to contribute to the NME's C86 cassette collection in 1986.
**Early Breakthroughs (1983-1984)** : Its first release was by The Legend in the summer of 1983, with a significant breakthrough occurring in November 1984 with a single by The Jesus and Mary Chain, which brought considerable attention to the label.
**Financial Ruin from "Loveless"** : The extensive two-year production process for My Bloody Valentine's album "Loveless" literally bankrupted the label.
**Founding Vision (1983)** : Founded by Scotsman Alan McGee in 1983, driven by his disillusionment with the prevalent synth-based music of the time and a desire to release material that captured the "promise of the Sex Pistols."
