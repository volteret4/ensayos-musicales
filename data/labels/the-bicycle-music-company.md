# label - The Bicycle Music Company

## curiosities
**Acquisition of Nine Inch Nails Masters (2010)** : In March 2010, The Bicycle Music Company acquired the rights to the master recordings of Nine Inch Nails' "Pretty Hate Machine." This acquisition resolved long-standing legal issues that had prevented Trent Reznor from accessing the masters and the album from being in print, subsequently allowing him to remaster the album. "But that changed in March of 2010 when the rights to the master recording were acquired by a company called the Bicycle Music Company. They allowed Trent access to these masters so he could remaster them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=__7iEl3lH8g
