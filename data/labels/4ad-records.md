# label - 4AD Records

## curiosities
**Distinct Visual Identity and Adventurous Artists** : Similar to Factory Records, 4AD Records dedicated efforts to cultivating a distinct visual identity for the label. It was known for signing artists who exhibited an "adventurous approach to music," such as Modern English. "Like factory records, for AD worked on creating and maintaining a distinct visual identity." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
**Founding and Relationship with Beggars Banquet** : 4AD Records was founded in 1977 and was initially conceived as the "experimental side" or "lab" for Beggars Banquet. The original idea was that if an artist found success on 4AD, Beggars would absorb them, a concept that only happened with the band Bauhaus before 4AD charted its own independent course. "For AD was founded in 1977 and is actually related to Beggars Banquets. They were conceived as the more experimental side, the lab, if you want, for Beggars." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
