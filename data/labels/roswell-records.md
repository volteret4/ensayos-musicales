# label - Roswell Records

## curiosities
**Dave Grohl's Own Label for Protection (1994)** : Roswell Records was Dave Grohl's self-established record company, created to "protect his interests" and "retain ownership of his music." This decision came after his Pre-Foo Fighters demos were bootlegged and he received numerous record deal offers, drawing on his negative experience with royalty issues during his time in Nirvana. He informed potential business partners that they would "have to go through my own record company." "Dave's label was called Roswell Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**UFO-Inspired Name** : The name "Roswell" for Dave Grohl's record label was taken from the town in New Mexico that was allegedly the site of the infamous UFO crash in 1947. This choice reflects Dave Grohl's long-standing fascination with UFOs and science fiction.
