# label - Wax Trax!

## curiosities
**Influence on Pretty Hate Machine** : The Wax Trax! label scene in America significantly influenced the musical style of Nine Inch Nails' debut album, "Pretty Hate Machine." This influence, along with harder-edged synth electronic music, shaped the sound that Trent Reznor was exploring at the time.
