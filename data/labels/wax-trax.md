# label - Wax Trax

## curiosities
**Home of Industrial Music** : Wax Trax of Chicago emerged around 1990 as a specialist indie label for heavy industrial music, serving as the home for bands such as Ministry (fronted by Al Jourgensen), Front 242, and Front Line Assembly.
