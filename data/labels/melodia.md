# label - Melodia

## curiosities
**Soviet State Recording Company** : Melodia was the state-sanctioned recording industry in the USSR. Initially, it exclusively recorded artists approved by the state, typically "official vocal instrumental assemblies" rather than unofficial amateurs. "Now there was a state sanctioned recording industry in the USSR. The label was called Melodia, and at first it would only record artists approved by the state." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YeNWDtN_sHE
**State-Sanctioned Bootlegging** : By 1980, with supervision from the Kremlin, Melodia began to bootleg recordings by Western artists such as The Beatles and The Rolling Stones. This act of intellectual property theft paradoxically aligned with the CIA's objective of distributing Western music and soft power into the Soviet population. "By 1980, under supervision from the Kremlin, state recording company Melodia, started bootlegging recordings by the Beatles and the Rolling Stones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NyKvAhO7MHE
