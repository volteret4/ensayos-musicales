# label - Righteous Babe Records

## curiosities
**Artist-Owned Template** : This indie label was founded by Ani DiFranco at age 19, taking inspiration from 1970s punk and alternative artists who sought creative and financial control. Righteous Babe Records refined this model, becoming a template for hundreds of future artist-owned and operated labels. "And her righteous babe records became a template for hundreds of artists owned and operated labels in the future." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zZM9ajwYr9E
**Pioneering Independent Success (1990s)** : Ani DiFranco established and managed Righteous Babe Records entirely herself from the beginning of her career, achieving significant success by deliberately going against the major label-dominated music industry culture of the 1990s. This independent approach solidified her status as a hero among her fans. "from the very beginning, she did everything herself through her own label, Righteous Babe Records. ... Ani wanted to be independent. She would have nothing to do with that major label culture and turned out to be very successful in swimming against it, which alone makes her a hero in the eyes of her fans." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
