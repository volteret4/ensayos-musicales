# label - RCA

## curiosities
**Lou Reed's "Metal Machine Music" Release (1975)** : RCA released Lou Reed's experimental double album "Metal Machine Music" in the summer of 1975, not out of artistic support, but as a retaliatory move against Reed who was trying to get out of his contract. Reed was contractually obligated to deliver more albums and delivered this avant-garde work of noise and feedback, which RCA, in an "in-your-face" response, put out to the public.
