# label - Rascal Records

## curiosities
**Hollywood-Based Operation** : Rascal Records was a label based in Hollywood. It originated from a company that initially existed only on paper and was owned by a relative of the band DA. "Rascal records did exist. They were in Hollywood." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw
**Limited Releases** : The label released a few other singles besides "Ready and Steady" by DA, but it never issued any other recordings from DA. "And yes, Rascal did release a few other singles, but nothing ever again, from DA." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw
