## curiosities
**Lionel Mapelson – The Godfather of Bootlegging (Early 20th Century)** : Lionel Mapelson, librarian at the Metropolitan Opera in New York City, covertly recorded complete opera performances in the early twentieth century using two-minute wax cylinders. These recordings are considered the origin point of musical bootlegging.
