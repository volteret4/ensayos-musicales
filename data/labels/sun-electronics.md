# label - Sun Electronics

## curiosities
**Collaboration for First Large-Scale Rock Sound System (1963)** : In 1963, Sun Electronics was contracted by The Beach Boys to build what became the first large-scale sound system specifically engineered for a rock concert environment. This marked a significant milestone in the development of dedicated live sound technology for the rock genre.
