# label - Jamaican National Radio

## curiosities
**Platform for Shuffle Music (late 1950s-early 1960s)** : Jamaican National Radio regularly featured "Shuffle" music, a blend of various influences, throughout the late 1950s and early 1960s. This made the radio a crucial platform for the dissemination and popularization of this evolving sound. "This musical stew became known as Shuffle and was often heard on Jamaican National Radio throughout the late 1950s and into the early 1960s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
