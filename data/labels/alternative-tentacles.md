# label - Alternative Tentacles

## curiosities
**American Hardcore Export** : By fostering relationships with other indie labels globally, Alternative Tentacles successfully exported American hardcore music to new territories worldwide. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Dead Kennedys' Self-Created Label** : Formed by The Dead Kennedys after they were unable to find any other entity to believe in and release their music, initially with the sole purpose of putting out a single. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**First Release (1979)** : Released The Dead Kennedys' self-produced single "California Über Alles" in 1979 as its first record. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Full-time Business** : After enjoying the initial experience of self-release, the band decided to develop Alternative Tentacles into a full-time business. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
