**Reluctance to Sign Sid Baret for Solo Career** : M.Y. Records was hesitant to back Sid Baret's solo career because, despite his talent, they considered him "crazy" and, more significantly, unpredictable. This made him a challenging artist for the label to manage for recording purposes.
