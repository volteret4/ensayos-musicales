# label - Warp Records

## curiosities
**Associated with Advanced Technology in IDM** : IDM artists on Warp were often noted for using advanced technologies and computers in their electronic music production, setting a standard that Boards of Canada diverged from with their vintage gear. **Benchmark for Electronic Sound** : Warp Records is referenced as a benchmark for classic British electronic music, particularly in the IDM genre. John Frusciante's 2013 electronic album, "Outsides," was described as sounding almost like a Warp release, aligning him with the label's aesthetic and influential artists such as Autechre, Aphex Twin, and Clark. **Hub of 90s IDM** : Warp Records was a central label for the "hard core" of the IDM movement in the 1990s, featuring prominent artists like Autechre, Aphex Twin, and The Future Sound of London, as well as Boards of Canada.
