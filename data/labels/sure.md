## curiosities
**SM58 Directional Microphone (1966)** : The Sure SM58, introduced in 1966, was a pioneering directional microphone. Bill Hanley, the "Father of Festival Sound," was among the first to widely employ such microphones, recognizing their ability to "greatly cut down on distortion and feedback," significantly improving live sound quality.
