## curiosities
**Skate Punk Label Reputation** : Cargo Music had a reputation for signing skate punk bands, which made Blink-182, with their 1995 album _Cheshire Cat_ released on the label, a perfect fit for the Warped Tour's shift towards punk and skate culture.
