# label - Grand Avenue Studios

## curiosities
**Recording of "One Tree Hill" Strings** : Dick Paul and Adele Armin, Toronto-based musicians, recorded their string parts for U2's "One Tree Hill" at this studio in Hamilton. The session was directed remotely by Daniel N. Waw and The Edge via a six-hour trans-Atlantic phone call. "Dick Paul and Adele Armin of Toronto recorded their parts at Grand Avenue Studios in Hamilton." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VR5Fncdc2kQ
