# label - Fire Records

## curiosities
**Releases of Last US 78 RPM Records (June 1960)** : Fire Records released two songs in June 1960, Buster Brown's "Fanny May" (a blues song) and Bobby Marshan's "Something On Your Mind" (a soul track), which are considered among the last 78 RPM records officially released by a record company in America. "Both recorded on fire records, both of which came out in America sometime in June of 1960." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
