# label - Fire Records

## curiosities
**Last 78 RPM Releases in US (1960)** : Fire Records released "Fanny May" by Buster Brown and "Something On Your Mind" by Bobby Marshan in June 1960, which were among the last 78 RPM records officially released by a record company in America. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
