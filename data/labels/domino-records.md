# label - Domino Records

## curiosities
**Arctic Monkeys Signing** : Domino Records signed the Arctic Monkeys after the band had already amassed a massive online following through their innovative use of MySpace. This demonstrated a shift in the music industry, where bands could build significant fan bases and generate label bidding wars even after making their music freely available online. **Key Role in Classic Rock Revival** : Domino Records was a very important record label during the classic rock revival movement. It is known for granting significant creative freedom to its artists, as exemplified by the contractual agreement with Arctic Monkeys. "Es una discográfica que fue muy importante en el todo este rival del rock clásico. **Limited Vinyl Release** : Domino Records, referenced as "dígilo, mi no" in the transcript, undertook the release of a small, limited vinyl edition of the collaborative project between Björk and Dirty Projectors. This occurred approximately one year after the project's initial digital release, with the specific aim of generating additional funds for the marine conservation cause. "Lo digo todo, mi no, que es la discográfica que moda los transcernina, que moda los kilzas, y nada, muchísimos artistas indiquizos, una pequeña edición de este proyecto un poco para ayudar a recordar más dinero al proyecto." ← Music Radar Clan > Björk y su curioso proyecto con los Dirty Proyectors | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Q1WS6Cu3eI&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
