# label - Domino Records

## curiosities
**Arctic Monkeys Signing** : Domino Records signed the Arctic Monkeys after the band had already amassed a massive online following through their innovative use of MySpace. This demonstrated a shift in the music industry, where bands could build significant fan bases and generate label bidding wars even after making their music freely available online. It is known for granting significant creative freedom to its artists, as exemplified by the contractual agreement with Arctic Monkeys.
