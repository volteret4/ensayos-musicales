# label - Domino Records

## curiosities
**Arctic Monkeys Signing** : Domino Records signed the Arctic Monkeys after the band had already amassed a massive online following through their innovative use of MySpace. This demonstrated a shift in the music industry, where bands could build significant fan bases and generate label bidding wars even after making their music freely available online. "By the time they signed with Domino Records, the band had a massive online following thanks to MySpace." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
**Support for Vinyl** : Domino Records, Franz Ferdinand's main label, is praised for being "really cool" and understanding of artists' desires for physical media, particularly vinyl. They are described as "vinyl nuts" themselves. "Like, Domino is on main label and they're really cool with that stuff. They get it. They're vinyl nuts too." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RWbKqqW78yg
