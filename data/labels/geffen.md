# label - Geffen

## curiosities
**Inspired Weezer Song** : Rivers Cuomo attended the opening of a new phase of the Hollywood Bowl, which featured renovations. While looking through the program, he saw pictures of Wilson Phillips, inspiring the Weezer song "Beverly Hills." "Rivers Quomo came up with the idea for this song after attending the opening of a new phase of the Hollywood Bowl." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
**Preceded Bus Accident** : Weezer had just played a show in Toronto on December 6, 2009, as part of their "Ratitude" tour, before their tour bus hit a patch of ice in New York and crashed. "They just played a show in Toronto, and we're headed back into the States." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
**Released Death to False Metal** : Weezer returned to Geffen for the release of "Death to False Metal," after "Hurley" was released on Epitaph Records. "For the next record, they were back on Geffen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
