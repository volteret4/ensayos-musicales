## curiosities
**Catalog Acquisition Company** : Roundheal was mentioned as one of the companies involved in the "gold rush" for artists' music catalogs in the late 2010s. These companies, including Primary Wave and Hypnosis Song Fund, acquired music rights for long-term lucrative propositions, offering artists upfront financial security and tax advantages.
