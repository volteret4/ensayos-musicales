# label - Fiction Records

## curiosities
**Cult Hero Single Release** : Fiction Records released the experimental seven-inch single "I'm a Cult Hero" by the pseudonymous band Cult Hero in 1979. Despite the single's prompt disappearance from sight, its release served the secret purpose of Robert Smith (of The Cure) testing out new bassist Simon Gallup. "The single came out on fiction records and promptly disappeared from sight." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E
