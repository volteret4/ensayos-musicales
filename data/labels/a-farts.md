# label - A Farts

## curiosities
**Limited Releases and "Gustav Rael" Style** : A Farts was a record label founded by Peter Culman in Germany during the 1990s. The label was known for producing hundreds of works, none of which ever exceeded sales of 1,000 units. The music released under this label was described as having "ese rollo en el ronque de Gustav Rael," indicating a specific, perhaps niche, aesthetic.
