# label - Beserkley Records

## curiosities
**Early Independent Alternative Label** : Beserkley Records was listed among the small, independent, niche record companies that emerged in the late 1970s and early 1980s, providing "an alternative to the majors." Jonathan Richman, described as a prototypical alternative artist from the 1970s, recorded for this specific label.
