**Marianne Faithfull Interview (2004)** : *Mojo* magazine published an interview with Marianne Faithfull in its August 2004 issue, controversially titled "Sé quién mató a Jim Morrison" (I know who killed Jim Morrison). This article is noted for shedding light on Jim Morrison's final months and is available online for public access.
