# label - Trauma

## curiosities
**Independent Label Origins for Bush** : Bush initially signed with Trauma, an independent label based in the Valley. Trauma utilized Interscope as their distributors, establishing an early hybrid model for the band with both independent and major label affiliations. "I signed to a label called Trauma so I've had an independent label in the valley and Indusco really were basically my distributors." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lGNrJgwQjgg
