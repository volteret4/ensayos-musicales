## curiosities
**First Combined Phone and MP3 Player (1999)** : In 1999, Samsung released the SPH M2100, which holds the distinction of being the first mobile phone to integrate MP3 playback capabilities. This innovation, introduced within a year of the first dedicated MP3 player, stimulated a rapid acceleration in the development of converged mobile devices. "Now, you may be surprised to learn that the first combination mobile phone and MP3 player came out in, wait for it, 1999.
