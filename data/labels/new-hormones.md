# label - New Hormones

## curiosities
**Self-Established by Buzzcocks** : New Hormones was established by Pete Shelley and Howard Devoto of the Buzzcocks in 1977, due to the lack of interest from existing labels in releasing punk music. It cost the band 500 pounds to record and release their EP "Spiral Scratch" on this label. "The Buzzcocks called their label New Hormones. It cost them all of 500 pounds to record and release Spiral Scratch." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
