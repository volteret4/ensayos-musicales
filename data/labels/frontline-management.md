# label - Frontline Management

## curiosities
**Acquisition by Ticketmaster (2008)** : In 2008, Ticketmaster acquired Frontline Management, an artist management company whose clients included major acts like Aerosmith and Jimmy Buffett. This acquisition was part of Ticketmaster's strategy for vertical integration within the concert industry, aiming to strengthen its position by owning key elements of the music business. "And that's why in 2008, ticket master bought an artist management company called Frontline Management." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8q2bRZyHnHE
**Role in Live Nation Entertainment** : Following the merger of Ticketmaster and Live Nation, Frontline Management became part of the new Live Nation Entertainment entity. Its role was to steer tours managed by Frontline clients not only into Live Nation's owned amphitheaters but also into other venues that had ticketing contracts with Ticketmaster, thereby enhancing vertical integration. "And Frontline Management was there to steer some tours into not only those amphitheaters, but to venues with which ticket master had contracts to sell tickets." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8q2bRZyHnHE
