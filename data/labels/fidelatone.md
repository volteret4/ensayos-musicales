# label - Fidelatone

## curiosities
**Early 45 RPM Adapter Design** : Fidelatone was an early manufacturer of 45 RPM adapters. While their solution was not significantly better than existing designs, their idea was later adapted by Capitol Records for their "optical center" adapter.
