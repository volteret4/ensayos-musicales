# label - SRO Anthem

## curiosities
**Big Wreck Version 2.0 Signing** : When Big Wreck reformed as Version 2.0 and began recording, they signed with SRO Anthem. "But we were now with SRO Anthem and, you know, Ray and Andy were basically like, this is Russia's, yeah, I imagine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QIA7V4PxCdM
**Creative Freedom Granted** : SRO Anthem, represented by Ray and Andy, offered Big Wreck significant creative freedom for their "Albatross" album, in contrast to previous label experiences. They encouraged the band to pursue their artistic vision without imposing commercial expectations for a "hit sound." "And they were like, well, now that we got you away from those people, do what you want. Go do what you want to do." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QIA7V4PxCdM
