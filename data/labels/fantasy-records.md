# label - Fantasy Records

## curiosities
**Copyright Ownership of CCR Songs** : Fantasy Records, run by Saul Zaentz, came to own the copyrights for songs written by John Fogarty for Credence Clearwater Revival. This ownership was a result of John Fogarty giving them up to escape his contract with Zaentz, leading to an antagonistic relationship. "This guy ran Credence's record label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYD_dIvYY5o
**Liner Note Advocate Ralph Gleason** : Fantasy Records executive Ralph Gleason was a notable proponent of detailed liner notes, often using them to express his opinions on albums and discuss the social or political significance of the music. "There was an executive named Ralph Gleason from fantasy records who was big into liner notes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KaIp6KVn-co
