# label - Fantasy Records

## curiosities
**Copyright Ownership of CCR Songs** : Fantasy Records, run by Saul Zaentz, came to own the copyrights for songs written by John Fogarty for Credence Clearwater Revival. This ownership was a result of John Fogarty giving them up to escape his contract with Zaentz, leading to an antagonistic relationship.
