# label - Virgin Megastores

## curiosities
**Canadian Presence and Airport Locations** : Canada had only one Virgin Megastore, located in Vancouver, and long-standing plans for a Toronto location at Yonge and Dundas ultimately fell through. Additionally, Virgin operated airport locations under the name "Virgin Books and Music," spanning from Winnipeg to Shanghai. "There was just one Megastore in Canada, and it was in Vancouver." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
**Decline and Current Status** : Virgin music stores have become a shadow of their former selves, with all of the Megastores now closed. A few smaller Virgin stores may still be found across the Middle East. "All of the Megastores are gone, but you still might be able to find a few smaller stores across the Middle East." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
**Demise by 2009** : Virgin Megastores, once a large retail presence, ceased to be "mega or anything" by 2009, reflecting the rapid decline of physical music consumption and the chain record store model. "Virgin Megastores were no longer mega or anything by 2009." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZzIqIydZTlA
**Founding and First Megastore (1971 - 1979)** : The first Virgin store was opened in January 1971 by Richard Branson, who would go on to found Virgin Records the following year. The company experienced rapid growth, leading to the opening of its first "Megastore" in 1979 on Oxford Street near Tottenham Court Road in London, situated just a few blocks from HMV's flagship store. "The first Virgin store opened in January 1971 by Richard Branson, who had later found Virgin Records a year later." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
**Global Expansion (Peak Period)** : Virgin Megastores expanded extensively worldwide, with 125 stores in the UK, 35 in France, and 23 in the US, including its iconic location in Times Square, as well as large stores in Los Angeles and San Francisco. The chain's international presence also included Australia, Japan, Greece, Hungary, Italy, Spain, Germany, the Netherlands, 32 stores across the Middle East, five in Morocco, and even a store on Reunion Island in the Indian Ocean. "Virgin stores sprouted up everywhere." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
