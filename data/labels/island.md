# label - Island

## curiosities
**Global Ska Promotion** : Island Records was a new label run by Englishman Chris Blackwell, which released Desmond Dekker's "The Israelites," Ska's first worldwide hit, in 1969. "Big hits on a new label called Island run by an Englishman named Chris Blackwell." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WX2GlpMNg_8
**Signing Aggressive Bands** : Island Records became active in signing bands from the aggressive music scene, such as Thrice and Thursday, during a period when the music industry as a whole was seeking new sounds beyond the declining pop music trend. "And then that was what kind of when Island came in and signed Thrice in Thursday and this whole like the entire music industry was looking for something else." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
