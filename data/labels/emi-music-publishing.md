# label - EMI Music Publishing

## curiosities
**Early Support for Alexis on Fire** : EMI Music Publishing, run by Michael McCarty, played a crucial role in Alexis on Fire's early career. McCarty gave the band a small advance for a publishing deal, which, though seemingly unusual for their genre, enabled them to purchase a van and record their first album for free after hours in the company's writing room. "So the guy who used to work, who used to run EMI Music Publishing Michael McCarty, do we started talking to us just about, you know, whatever." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
