## curiosities
**Acquisition of Sound Jam and iTunes Launch** : In 2000, Apple acquired a product called Sound Jam, which was software designed for CD-ripping and MP3 playback, fulfilling Steve Jobs's ambition for the company to deeply engage with the music sector. This acquisition led to the software being renamed iTunes, which was then officially introduced on January 9, 2001.
