# label - Independent Labels (British, 1980s)

## curiosities
**Business Acumen Issues** : Many great British indie labels of the 1980s, such as Factory, Two-Tone, and Rough Trade, were poor at business. This led to significant financial problems, including bankruptcies and severe debt, despite their cultural impact. "This highlights a problem with many of those great British indie labels of the 80s. They were crap at doing business." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
**Role in British Music Innovation (1980s)** : Dozens of independent labels emerged in Britain throughout the 1980s, acting as a major catalyst for musical innovation. These labels were "nimble and quick," enabling them to get music into stores and bands on tour far more rapidly than major labels, thereby driving the development of British indie culture. "Another thing that spurred innovation in British music through the 80s were the dozens and dozens and dozens of independent labels that popped up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
