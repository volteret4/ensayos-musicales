# label - Columbia Records

## curiosities
**First Art Director Hire (1938)** : In 1938, Columbia Records in New York made a groundbreaking move by hiring Alex Steinweiss, a 23-year-old graphic designer, as the music industry's first-ever art director. This pivotal decision initiated the concept of distinctive album cover artwork.
