# label - Free World Entertainment

## curiosities
**Temporary Name for Volcano** : At one point during Tool's legal disputes, Volcano Entertainment changed its name to Free World. This was one of several confusing name changes the label underwent, contributing to the band's difficulties in navigating their contractual obligations.
