## curiosities
**Founding and Physical Format Hegemony** : Founded by Bernliner, the Victor company quickly established itself as a "mother absolute" in the realm of physical music formats, making pivotal contributions to the development of sound recording and early gramophones. Victor's influence grew further after its merger with RCA, collectively driving significant disc improvements until the mid-1970s.
