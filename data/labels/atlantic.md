**Creating a Vanity Subsidiary** : In the 1970s, the major label Atlantic Records created a special subsidiary called Swan Song specifically for Led Zeppelin. This practice illustrates how major labels establish "vanity labels" or "boutique labels" for their important artists, offering perceived indie street cred or enhanced creative control. "When Led Zeppelin ruled the world in the 1970s, their label Atlantic created a special subsidiary for them called Swan Song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
