# label - Maestro

## curiosities
**First Commercial Fuzz Tone (Unspecified Year)** : The Maestro Fuzz Tone FZ1 is recognized as, to the best of anyone's knowledge, the first commercially available fuzz box. Its introduction followed Grady Martin's accidental discovery of distorted guitar sounds and subsequently paved the way for a new category of effects pedals.
