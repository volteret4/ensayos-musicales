## curiosities
**Censorship Allegations by Rage Against the Machine** : NBC, as the broadcaster of Saturday Night Live, was accused by Rage Against the Machine of censorship. This occurred after the band's performance on the show was cut short following their protest against guest host Steve Forbes and rumored plans to denounce NBC's parent company, General Electric, for its role in weapons manufacturing and war crimes.
