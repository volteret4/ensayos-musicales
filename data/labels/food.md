# label - Food

## curiosities
**Blur Discovery and Renaming** : Food, an indie label, discovered the band Seymour and successfully persuaded them to change their name to Blur, contributing to their rise as one of the most important new bands in the UK by 1994. "Then there was a group called Seymour, who attracted the attention of an indie label called Food. They convinced them to change their name to Blur." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q4dEV1yn3OY
**Britpop Success with Tiny Label** : Food was a "tiny little label" that signed Blur, enabling them to achieve mainstream success during the Britpop scene of the mid-1990s. This case highlights how, in the UK, even small independent labels could launch major acts into the national spotlight due to the specific characteristics of the British music industry. "People forget that blur made it big while on a tiny little label called Food." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
