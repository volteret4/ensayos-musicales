# label - Vox Amplifiers

## curiosities
**Surpassed by Marshall Amplifiers** : Vox amplifiers, notably used by The Beatles, were surpassed in popularity in the UK by Marshall amplifiers within 18 months of Marshall's first live performance. This marked a significant shift in the British music scene's preferred amplification. "Within 18 months, Marshall's were the most popular amps in all of the UK, even blowing away Vox amplifiers, which were used by the Beatles." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
**Unveiling of the Gibson Les Paul** : On June 24, 1952, a special event for musicians was held at the Waldorf Astoria Hotel in New York, where the first Gibson Les Paul guitar was officially unveiled. This event marked the public debut of one of rock's most iconic instruments. "The first Gibson Les Paul was unveiled on June 24, 1952, at a special event for musicians at the Waldorf Astoria Hotel in New York." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
