# label - Black Swan

## curiosities
**Harlem-Based Record Label** : Black Swan was a record label based in Harlem. Blues singer Trixie Smith recorded for this label in September 1922. "Trixie Smith, a blues singer originally from Atlanta, did some work for a label called Black Swan, which was based in Harlem." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
