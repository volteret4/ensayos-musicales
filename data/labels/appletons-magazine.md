## curiosities
**John Philip Sousa Article (1906)** : In 1906, Appleton's magazine published an article by John Philip Sousa titled "The Metis of Mechanical Music." This article served as a platform for Sousa to express his strong opposition to recorded music and warn of its predicted negative impact on musical taste and development.
