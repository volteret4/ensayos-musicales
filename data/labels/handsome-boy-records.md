# label - Handsome Boy Records

## curiosities
**Early Supporter** : Jeff Rogers, who operated Handsome Boy Records, saw Pezz perform at Elvis Mondays at the Old El Mocambo and continued to support them. "When he saw us, Jeff Rogers also saw us and they kept, Jeff had handsome boy records at the time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Early Supporter of Pez** : Jeff Rogers, who operated Handsome Boy Records, became an early supporter of the band Pez (later Billy Talent) after seeing them perform at "Elvis Mondays" nights. "Jeff Rogers also saw us, and they kept, Jeff had handsome boy records at the time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8
