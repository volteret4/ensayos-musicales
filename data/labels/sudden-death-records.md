# label - Sudden Death Records

## curiosities
**Founded by D.O.A.** : Sudden Death Records was founded by the band D.O.A. as their own indie label to release their music. This mirrored the common strategy among hardcore bands of creating their own distribution channels. "By the way, DOA also had to form their own indie label to get their music out there and they called it Sudden Death Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Founding by DOA** : Sudden Death Records was an independent label founded by the Canadian band DOA to release their music. "DOA also had to form their own indie label to get their music out there and they called it Sudden Death Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEHkKfmmDiI
