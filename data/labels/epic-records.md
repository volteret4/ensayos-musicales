# label - Epic Records

## curiosities
**Rage Against The Machine Contract** : Epic Records successfully signed Rage Against The Machine following a bidding war among major labels. The agreement was secured because Epic acceded to all of the band's demands, including maintaining full creative control, recognizing that their extensive worldwide distribution and marketing power were crucial for the band to convey its political message effectively. "Epic was the major that agreed to all of Rage's demands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xo5DRSW95QQ
