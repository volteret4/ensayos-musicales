# label - Decca

## curiosities
**Early Large Label** : Decca is mentioned as one of the bigger record labels in the early days when all labels were considered "Indie" before industry consolidation led to the rise of only a few major players. "They were some that were bigger than others like Deca and London and RCA and EMI, but there were literally thousands of small labels that did their own thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
**Request for William Shatner Album (1968)** : Decca Records approached William Shatner, then a young actor gaining success from Star Trek, to record an album. This request led to Shatner creating "The Transformed Man" in 1968. "Well, Deca asked me to make an album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
