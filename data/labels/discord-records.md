# label - Discord Records

## curiosities
**Fugazi's DIY Label** : Discord Records was Fugazi's own label, through which they handled all aspects of their music production and release, reflecting their strong DIY ethos and contempt for the mainstream record industry. "So they did everything on their own, through their own label, which they called Discord." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QtMI00T5jw
**Spiritual Home of Emo** : Discord Records became recognized as the "spiritual home" for the early emo scene, particularly due to its association with Fugazi, who set the ethical and musical standards for emo core. In 1988, this emo world was small, primarily confined to the Washington D.C. area and bands signed to Discord. "Their label, Discord Records, became the spiritual home to the emo world. Now, remember this world wasn't very big in 1988. It was confined mostly to the Washington DC area and mostly to bands on the Discord label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
