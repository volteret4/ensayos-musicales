# label - Network Records

## curiosities
**Specialist Indie for Heavy Music** : Network Records, based in Vancouver, was founded by the band MOVE when they couldn't find a label for their music. It became a specialist indie label that filled the gap for heavy music, finding bands like Skinny Puppy. Later, it also helped nurture artists like Sarah McLachlan and gave the Barenaked Ladies a new lease on life.
