# label - Network Records

## curiosities
**Declined Offer to Sloan** : Network Records offered Sloan a record deal, which the band seriously considered due to the rarity of such offers for Halifax bands. However, they ultimately declined in favor of signing with Geffen Records, which they perceived as a more significant opportunity. "And in the meantime, we have been offered a deal by Network Records, which when that was coming off the facts machine, it was hard not to just sign it right away because nothing happens like that to bands from Halifax, you know, let alone Canada, especially for DGC, but for a band from Halifax." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mbic-70ZhpE
**Fierce Indie Label** : Network Records is described as one of Canada's most fierce indie record labels, with which Sarah McLachlan reinvented herself as a solo singer-songwriter. "She reinvented herself as a solo singer-songwriter with network records, one of the country's most fierce indie record labels." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zZM9ajwYr9E
**Sandbox Album Release** : Network Records released Sandbox's 1996 album "Bionic." "It was a single from a 1996 album released on network records called Bionic." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUQ2vZzmRiI
**Sandbox Record Deal** : Vancouver's Network Records signed Sandbox after receiving their demos, leading to the release of their debut album, *Bionic*, in 1995. This deal established Sandbox's entry into the music industry. "Sandbox was from New Glasgow, Nova Scotia, and after sending out some demos, they nailed down a deal with Vancouver's network records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
**Specialist Indie for Heavy Music** : Network Records, based in Vancouver, was founded by the band MOVE when they couldn't find a label for their music. It became a specialist indie label that filled the gap for heavy music, finding bands like Skinny Puppy. Later, it also helped nurture artists like Sarah McLachlan and gave the Barenaked Ladies a new lease on life. "Network Records of Vancouver, a label founded by a band called MOVE when they couldn't find anyone to touch their stuff." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCtQqVBtCaI
