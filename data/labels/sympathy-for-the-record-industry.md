# label - Sympathy for the Record Industry

## curiosities
**Esteemed Indie Label** : Sympathy for the Record Industry was a label held in high esteem by the indie community. They signed The White Stripes within two years of their formation and later also signed The Von Bondis, highlighting their role in the early 2000s Garage Rock revival. "Within two years, the White Stripes had a deal with a label called Sympathy for the Record Industry, which was held in very high esteem by members of the indie community." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
