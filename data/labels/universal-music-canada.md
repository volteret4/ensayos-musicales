# label - Universal Music Canada

## curiosities
**Storage of Master Tapes** : Universal Music Canada maintains a storage facility where master tapes, including the 65 reels of two-inch tape recorded for The Tragically Hip's *Road Apples* album, are kept. The process of retrieving these tapes was described as complex and disorganized, as they were "spread all over the place" and not consistently labeled. "Johnny's made a number of trips to the storage place where Universal has all these things." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G9H9q7BYqEA
