# label - Calvin Klein

## curiosities
**Almost a Warped Tour Sponsor** : Calvin Klein was initially considered as a potential title sponsor for the Warped Tour after its financially difficult first year. However, a meeting with their representatives was canceled due to a blizzard, which stranded their rep for 24 hours, allowing Vans to step in and secure the sponsorship before rescheduling could occur. "For a while, it looked like the title sponsor was going to be Calvin Klein, but again fate intervened." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
