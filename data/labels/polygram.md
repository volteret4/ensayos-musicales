**Acquisition of Independent Labels** : Polygram, facing a crisis similar to Sony's in the early 1990s, initiated a process of acquiring several independent record labels. **Acquisitions of Independent Labels (1989)** : Polygram acquired both Island Records and A&M Records in 1989. These acquisitions were significant in consolidating independent labels into larger corporate structures, eventually leading to Polygram morphing into Universal, one of today's three major labels.
