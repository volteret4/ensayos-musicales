# label - Polygram

## curiosities
**Acquisitions of Independent Labels (1989)** : Polygram acquired both Island Records and A&M Records in 1989. These acquisitions were significant in consolidating independent labels into larger corporate structures, eventually leading to Polygram morphing into Universal, one of today's three major labels.
