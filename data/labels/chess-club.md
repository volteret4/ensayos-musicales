# label - Chess Club

## curiosities
**Boutique Indie Support for Early EPs** : Chess Club, a boutique label based in London, was responsible for issuing Mumford & Sons' first three indie EPs: *Self-titled or Lennie Your Eyes* (2008), *Love Your Ground* (2008), and *The Cave and the Open Sea* (2009). All these EPs were released on limited edition 10-inch vinyl. "The first EP is either self-titled or called Lennie Your Eyes, both are completely acceptable, and it was issued by a boutique label out of London called Chess Club." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P1RyOchn7pk
