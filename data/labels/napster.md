# label - Napster

## curiosities
**Massive Copyright Violations** : Lars Ulrich's legal team identified 1.4 million copyright violations involving 95 Metallica songs by approximately 350,000 users.
**Publicity Stunt** : A list of ~350,000 users trading Metallica files, spanning 60,000 pages, was publicly displayed in front of TV cameras as part of Metallica's lawsuit against the service.
**Target of Metallica Lawsuit (2000)** : Sued by Metallica in April 2000 after unreleased Metallica files were discovered on the platform.
