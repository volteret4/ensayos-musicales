# label - Interscope Records

## curiosities
**Blink-182's Departure (2012)** : Blink-182 formally split from Interscope Records in October 2012. This decision allowed the band to release music independently, providing them with complete creative freedom and eliminating external interference in their projects. "They left Interscope Records in October 2012." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q-QP7ePxkQE
**Fred Durst as Executive** : Fred Durst, known for his role in Limp Bizkit, later became a major executive at Interscope Records, demonstrating a unique career trajectory from artist to industry leader. "Fred has since become a major executive at Interscope Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Somq2MoRi5Q
**Rescue of Trent Reznor** : Producer Jimmy Iovine brought Trent Reznor into the fold of Interscope Records after his "suffocating deal" with TVT Records was resolved, allowing Trent to record his second album without further dispute. "After Trent was rescued from the suffocating deal with TVT records producer Jimmy Iovine brought him into the fold of interscope records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DKdF8NgF5Bw
