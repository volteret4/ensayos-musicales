# label - Pioneer

## curiosities
**CDJ Innovation** : Pioneer played a pivotal role in the evolution of DJ technology by introducing the CDJ line, specifically the CDJ-1000 in 2001. This machine, a compact disc version of a DJ turntable, became a standard for digital DJing. **First Car CD Player (1984)** : Pioneer was the first manufacturer to introduce a CD player for cars, the CDX-1, in 1984.
