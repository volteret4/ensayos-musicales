**R.E.M.'s Multinational Era (Post-1987)** : R.E.M. transitioned to Warner Music after experiencing "problems and certain disagreements" with their previous independent label, IRS Records, around 1987. From this point forward, R.E.M. consistently remained with multinational labels, releasing what many consider their "great trilogy" of albums, which are revered as some of the finest in US music history.
