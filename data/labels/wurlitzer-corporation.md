## curiosities
**Early Organ and Jukebox Manufacturer (1853)** : The Wurlitzer Corporation began manufacturing pipe organs in 1853, later expanding into jukeboxes in the 20th century, and then into electronic organs in the 1950s. **Introduction of the Side Man (1950s)** : In the 1950s, Wurlitzer introduced the Side Man, a standalone rhythm box, to distinguish their electronic organs. This device used vacuum tubes and a clock to generate bass drum, snare, tom-tom, and cymbal sounds, with tempos ranging from 34 to 150 BPM.
