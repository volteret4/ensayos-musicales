# label - Creation

## curiosities
**Oasis's Indie Label** : Creation was the "small indie label" that Oasis impressed with their swagger, leading to their debut album recording. "After impressing the owner of a small indie label called Creation with their swagger, Oasis recorded a debut album that was at once new and familiar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSvoppf1dGY
**Oasis's Rise on a "Has-Been" Label** : Creation, described as a "formerly has been" label, became the home of Oasis, who then grew into one of the biggest bands in the world during the Britpop era. This demonstrates the capacity of indie labels, even those with past struggles, to achieve global prominence, particularly within the UK music scene. "Meanwhile, Oasis became one of the biggest bands in the world while part of the roster of a formerly has been label called Creation." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
