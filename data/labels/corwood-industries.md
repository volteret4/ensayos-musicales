# label - Corwood Industries

## curiosities
**Corwood Industries – Over 50 Albums, Anonymous P.O. Box, One of Rock's Great Mysteries** : Founded by Jandek (believed to be Sterling Richard Smith) in Houston, Texas. The label has released more than 51 albums since 1978 with no conventional promotion or distribution, communicating only through an anonymous P.O. box. "Since 1978, Jandek has released no fewer than 51 indie albums on his own label called Corwood Industries." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dt7mb4yyuWM
**Jandek's Label with Limited Information** : Corwood Industries is the label through which Jandek's releases started appearing in 1978, notable for seemingly having only a post office box and providing no biographical information or liner notes for his prolific output. "These releases started appearing in 1978 on the Corwood Industries label, which only seemed to have a post office box." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oGscVtrdER0
**Self-Run Label for Prolific Artist** : Corwood Industries is Jandec's self-owned label, through which he has independently released at least 85 albums since 1978. This independent operation highlights his reclusive nature and complete artistic control over his prolific output. "I know that there are at least 85 Jandec albums that have been released since 1978 and they have all been released on his own Corwood Industries label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c8CD_FcWcg8
**Shared Phone Number with Corporation** : The phone number for Corwood Industries is shared with the "Sterling Smith Corporation," which is hinted to be a stocks and securities company. This connection contributes to the ongoing mystery surrounding Jandec's financial support and potential real identity. "Now there were hints that the Sterling Smith Corporation, a company that shares a phone number with Corwood Industries, is a stocks and securities company." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c8CD_FcWcg8
