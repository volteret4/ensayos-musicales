# label - Record Label (for Queens of the Stone Age)

## curiosities
**Dislike of `Songs for the Death`** : The record label for Queens of the Stone Age expressed strong disapproval of the album `Songs for the Death` from the moment they first heard the demos, consistently sending negative feedback and notes. "Except there was almost nothing the record label liked about the album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BvhjsTUIS2o
