# label - 102.1 The Edge

## curiosities
**"Hollowpoint in Hyperbole" Awards (2008)** : In 2008, USS's song "Hollowpoint in Hyperbole" won "Best New Song," and the band itself won "Best New Band" from 102.1 The Edge. This recognition, spurred by a dare from a friend to record their best song, significantly elevated the band's profile. "in 2008 when our song hollow points in hyper hyperbole we won best new song and we won best new band on a dare from one of our closest friends Brian McKenzie." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
**"Laces Out" Single Delivery (2009)** : In January 2009, Jay Parsons personally delivered the master copy of "Laces Out," the first single from USS's *Customization* album, to Martin Streak at 102.1 The Edge. This delivery was made under tight deadline pressure to ensure the song's timely addition to radio rotation. "I said ash we need the song we need the master copy of laces out because we got to deliver it to one of two point one the edge and at the time Martin Streak rest in peace was the point man." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
**Discovery of "Welding the C-Drive" (2008)** : In January 2008, 102.1 The Edge began discussing USS's independently released debut EP, *Welding the C-Drive*, which had come out in summer 2007. The EP was brought to the station by Barry Taylor, and the staff were reportedly "very taken aback by the maturity of this record for something that was independent and a first time out," leading to significant radio play. "it was in January of 2008 that we got the call Ash in Texas me and cold Lake Alberta that one on two point when the edge was talking about our album well in the C drive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
**Jay Parsons' "The Military Mixtape"** : In 2017, Jay Parsons was given a radio show called "The Military Mixtape" on 102.1 The Edge, airing on Saturday nights. This was a significant personal achievement for Jay, as it allowed him to DJ and find new tunes on a station he and Ash grew up listening to, and which had supported USS. "Fast forward to 2017 um 102.1 the edge uh came on board and uh gave me the military mixtape and that's on Saturday nights." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N-3eddy4J7g
**New Music Search Winners (Pre-2000s)** : This radio station hosted a "New Music Search" competition which was won by the band Head, who notably beat out other well-known Canadian acts like Hayden and Lowest of the Low. "they had won the new music search...they beat Hayden, maybe the lowest of the low." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
