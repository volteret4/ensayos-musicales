**Role in Alt-Rock Scene (Mid-1980s)** : Independent record stores were vital to the growth of the alternative scene in the mid-1980s. They distinguished themselves from larger chains by consistently stocking "more of the weird stuff," thereby catering to and attracting fans of non-mainstream music.
