# label - Gramophone Company

## curiosities
**Original Owner of HMV Label** : The Gramophone Company was the original owner of the HMV label, which stood for "His Master's Voice," and it also operated the first HMV stores in the UK. The company later merged with another recorded music manufacturer to form EMI.
