# label - Sun Studios

## curiosities
**Historic Recording Location for "Rocket 88"** : Sun Studios in Memphis was the location where Jackie Brenston and his Delta Cats recorded "Rocket 88" on March 2nd, 1951. This session, overseen by producer Sam Phillips, is historically significant as it potentially yielded the first rock and roll record due to an accidental bass distortion.
