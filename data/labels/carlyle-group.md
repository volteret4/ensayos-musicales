# label - Carlyle Group

## curiosities
**Acquisition and Flip of Ticketron (1990-1991)** : The Carlyle Group, a private equity firm based in Washington, D.C., acquired Ticketron in 1990 when the company was in disarray. They held onto Ticketron's assets for approximately a year before "flipping" them to Ticketmaster, leading to Ticketmaster's market dominance. "And the buyer was the Carlyle group, a private equity firm founded in Washington, D.C.
