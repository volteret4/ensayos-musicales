# label - Twin/Tone Records

## curiosities
**Home to The Replacements** : Twin/Tone Records was the label for The Replacements during their early career when they were still very much a hardcore act before evolving into a power pop band. "They recorded for a label called Twin Tone" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Signed The Replacements** : The Twin/Tone label, a local independent label based in Minneapolis, signed The Replacements. This signing allowed the band to release their music and gain an audience, contributing to the burgeoning Minneapolis indie scene. "They signed to a local indie label called Twin Tone and put up music like this." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XjTWcy8RHEs
