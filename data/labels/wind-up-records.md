# label - Wind-Up Records

## curiosities
**Co-founded by Diana Meltzer** : Wind-Up Records was co-founded by Diana Meltzer and her late husband, Alan. Meltzer, known as "The Woman with the Golden Ears," was instrumental in signing many successful acts to the label, including Creed, Evanescence, Alter Bridge, Finger 11, Drowning Pool, and Bright Eyes.
