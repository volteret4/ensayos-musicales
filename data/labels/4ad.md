# label - 4AD

## curiosities
**Adventurous UK Indie Label** : 4AD is described as an adventurous indie label based out of the UK. It was the label that first picked up The Pixies when they couldn't find an American label interested in their experimental noise. "As they couldn't find an American label interested in their brand of experimental noise, the group was first picked up by 4AD, that adventurous indie label out of the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCtQqVBtCaI
**Pioneering Independent Label** : 4AD was a small independent label that specialized in working outside the major label system. It notably signed the Scottish Dreampop band Cocteau Twins in 1979. "One of the earliest examples of Dreampop was Scotland's Cocktoe twins, who arrived from the scene in 1979, signing to the indie label 4AD." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
