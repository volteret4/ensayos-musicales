# label - 4AD

## curiosities
**Avant-garde Focus** : Initially conceived as a platform to test out more avant-garde acts, with the intention that successful artists would "graduate" to the main Beggars Banquet label.
**Cult Following** : Developed a reputation for "weirdness" that garnered a significant and loyal fanbase, leading many to purchase records solely based on the 4AD label, often without prior knowledge of the band, fostering a cult-like adherence.
**International Success with Pixies** : Signed the American band Pixies, who went on to become one of the most influential American indie bands, after they failed to secure a record deal in the United States.
**Pioneering Sampling Project (1987)** : Its studio project MARRS created the seminal track "Pump Up the Volume" in 1987, a landmark record recognized for its innovative sampling techniques and profound impact on dance music.
