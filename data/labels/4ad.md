# label - 4AD

## curiosities
**Avant-garde Focus** : Initially conceived as a platform to test out more avant-garde acts, with the intention that successful artists would "graduate" to the main Beggars Banquet label. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Behind M|A|R|R|S** : 4AD, described as an "adventurous British indie label," was the company behind the M|A|R|R|S project. Its founder, Ivo Watts-Russell, assembled musicians and DJs from his label to create the track "Pump Up the Volume." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Cult Following** : Developed a reputation for "weirdness" that garnered a significant and loyal fanbase, leading many to purchase records solely based on the 4AD label, often without prior knowledge of the band, fostering a cult-like adherence. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Goth and Related Music (N/A)** : A record label that specialized not only in Goth but also in something closely related. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RvzvcACkYqk
**International Success with Pixies** : Signed the American band Pixies, who went on to become one of the most influential American indie bands, after they failed to secure a record deal in the United States. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Pioneering Sampling Project (1987)** : Its studio project MARRS created the seminal track "Pump Up the Volume" in 1987, a landmark record recognized for its innovative sampling techniques and profound impact on dance music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
