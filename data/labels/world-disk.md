# label - World Disk

## curiosities
**Promotion of Domestic Jamaican Music** : World Disk was Clement "Coxsone" Dodd's company, which played a significant role in promoting the new and evolving domestic sound of Ska in Jamaica by recording local acts instead of just playing imported records. "His company was called World Disk, which went a long way in promoting the new evolving domestic sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WX2GlpMNg_8
