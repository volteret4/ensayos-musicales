# label - Small Wonder

## curiosities
**Risk-Taking Indie Label** : Small Wonder was an indie label that took a chance on Bauhaus's debut single, "Bela Lugosi's Dead," despite its unusual length of over nine and a half minutes, which would normally be considered career suicide for a first single. "an indie label called Small Wonder decided to take a chance." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
