# label - Computer Ticket Service

## curiosities
**Predecessor to Ticketmaster** : Formed by programmers and computer salesmen inspired by Select-A-Seat's technology, Computer Ticket Service was the initial name for the company that would eventually become Ticketmaster. Co-founder Albert Leffler sought a more memorable name for the new venture. "They formed a company called Computer Ticket Service, but co-founder Albert Leffler didn't like that name." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE
