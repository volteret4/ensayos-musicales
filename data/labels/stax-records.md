# label - Stax Records

## curiosities
**Rapid Catalog Expansion** : To quickly establish a new catalog after its split from Atlantic Records, Stax Records released an unprecedented 27 albums all at once. This strategy provided numerous artists, including Isaac Hayes, with the opportunity to create and release music, with Hayes notably insisting on doing it "his way." "Stacks records had split from Atlantic records and needed a catalog so they released 27 albums all at once to give them this quick catalog of music." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 22 - Power to the People - The Music of the Black Power Movement | https://seventies.libsyn.com/022-power-to-the-people-62520-11-01-am
**Signing of Delaney & Bonnie (1968)** : In 1968, Delaney & Bonnie signed with the legendary Stax Records in Memphis, which influenced their music with a distinct soul sound. "In 1968 Delinean Bonnie signed with the legendary stacks records and Memphis and you can hear that soul influence in their music." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 59 - Bands and Booze on the Festival Express of 1970 | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Split from Atlantic Records** : Stax Records underwent a significant transition when it split from Atlantic Records. This separation necessitated a rapid strategy to rebuild its music catalog independently.
