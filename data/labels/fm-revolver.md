# label - FM Revolver

## curiosities
**Unsanctioned Re-release and Band Attack (1989-1990)** : This indie label, after having once signed The Stone Roses, re-released the 12-inch for "Sally Cinnamon" in late 1989 with a "tacky video" without the band's permission, which prompted the band to attack the label's offices on January 30, 1990, causing $50,000 in damages and leading to their arrest. "It all started when FM revolver, an indie label that had once signed the roses, decided that they wanted their piece of the hype." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MN97R6cxQVw
