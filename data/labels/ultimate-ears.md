# label - Ultimate Ears

## curiosities
**Founding of In-Ear Monitor Company (Early 2000s)** : After inventing in-ear monitors for Alex Van Halen, Jerry Harvey founded Ultimate Ears, a company dedicated to manufacturing these specialized devices for other performers. The company faced challenges in convincing musicians to adopt the technology due to initial concerns about cost ($700-$900 a pair) and appearance. "So well, the Jerry founded a new company called Ultimate Ears and started making these things for everyone." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
**Impact on Performer Experience (Early 2000s)** : Ultimate Ears, and similar systems, started appearing around 2000, initially referred to as "ear worn monitors." Despite initial resistance, performers who tried them found the "difference was so dramatic that they never went back to using stage monitors ever again," highlighting their superior performance and hearing protection. "Usually though, when a performer tried out a set, the difference was so dramatic that they never went back to using stage monitors ever again." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
