# label - Roulette Records

## curiosities
**Hip-Pocket Records Releases (1966)** : Roulette Records was among the labels that released singles on Hip-Pocket Records in 1966, collaborating with Filco and Ford. These inexpensive, bendable plastic discs were sold for $0.69 at Ford dealerships and Woolworth stores, featuring artists such as Ben Morrison. `"They got Atlantic, Mercury, and Roulette records to release singles this way, selling them for $0.69 at four dealerships and through Woolworth stores."` ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OuFvfZJKcpc
