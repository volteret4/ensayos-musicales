# label - Hib-Tone

## curiosities
**Independent Label for R.E.M.** : R.E.M. released music through Hib-Tone, one of the independent labels that supported college rock bands in their early careers. "then they put up music through two independent labels, one called Hibton and the other called IRS." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
**R.E.M.'s First Record Label** : Hib-Tone was the initial record label for R.E.M., where they released their very first records as a no-name indie band from the Deep South before moving on to IRS Records. "Their first records were released on a label called Hib-Tone." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AymMybE33zU
