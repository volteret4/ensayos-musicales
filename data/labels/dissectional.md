# label - Dissectional

## curiosities
**Parent Company Succession** : Initially, Dissectional's parent company was Zamba. Following Zamba's acquisition by BMG in 2002, BMG became the parent. Then, in 2008, BMG was acquired by Sony, meaning Dissectional and any future Tool releases would fall under the Sony Music Entertainment umbrella for distribution. "The parent company was Zamba. But then remember that in 2002, Zamba was sold to BMG, which at the time was one of the major labels." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
**Tool's Co-Owned Sub-Label** : Dissectional was established as Tool's own sub-label, created under the umbrella of Volcano 2. This venture was set up as a joint company where Tool had direct input and a say in how things operated, a mechanism intended to avoid future disputes and provide the band with greater control over their releases. "was structured to give Tool its own sub-label, which they called dissectional." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
