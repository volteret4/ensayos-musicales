# label - A&M Records (Canada)

## curiosities
**Package to Donna Halper** : A&M Records' Canadian arm played a role in Rush's breakthrough by having promoter Bob Roper send a package containing Rush's record to DJ Donna Halper at WMMS Cleveland. The package noted that no label, including A&M Canada, was interested in signing the band.
