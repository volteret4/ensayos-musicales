# label - JC Koss Television rental company

## curiosities
**Funding for Portable Record Player Innovation** : John Koss initially founded the JC Koss Television rental company in 1953, with the idea of renting TVs to hospital patients. The success of this venture provided him with sufficient capital to pursue his interest in creating a truly portable record player in the 1950s, a product that did not widely exist at the time and eventually led to the development of consumer headphones.
