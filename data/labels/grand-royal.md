# label - Grand Royal

## curiosities
**Artist-Founded Label** : Grand Royal was a private label established by the Beastie Boys themselves, using profits from "License to Ill," through which they signed other acts like Luscious Jackson. "The Beastie Boys moved to LA. They set up their own label called Grand Royal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9_BTe15NooI
**Beyond Music - Magazine and Mailing List** : As part of the expanding "Beastie Empire," Grand Royal diversified beyond just a record label, also launching its own magazine and a mailing list for fans. "Grand Royal also started a magazine and a mailing list." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9_BTe15NooI
**Business Failure and Closure** : The label eventually accumulated too many debts and went out of business in 2001, marking a significant exception to the otherwise successful "Beastie Empire." "The biggest exception was the band's record label, Grand Royal. It ran up to many debts, and went out of business in 2001." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9_BTe15NooI
