# label - Odeon

## curiosities
**Pioneering Record Bundles (1909)** : In 1909, Odeon, a German company, was the first to introduce the practice of bundling multiple records together. These bundles, consisting of 10-inch, 78 RPM discs packaged in bound book-like formats, served as the direct precursor to what would become known as record albums.
