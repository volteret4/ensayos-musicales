# label - LSD

## curiosities
**Unconventional Marketing Plan** : LSD, a label based in Vancouver, devised an unusual marketing strategy to include an actual tab of LSD with every seven-inch single release from Lowest of the Low's *Halusagini* album. Although this plan was never executed, it provides insight into the company's unconventional approach. "one of the marketing plans the label came up with was to include an actual tab of LSD with every seven-inch single release from the record. That never happened, but it'll give you an idea of what kind of company it was." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
