## curiosities
**AudioSphere's First Commercial Stereo Recordings (1954)** : In May 1954, AudioSphere made history by releasing the first-ever commercial stereo recordings. These recordings were presented on real-to-reel tapes and captured classical performances at an Italian music festival.
