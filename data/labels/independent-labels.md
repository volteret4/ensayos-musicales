**Swans' Early Releases and Departure** : Swans began their career releasing their first albums, *Filth* and *Cop*, on independent labels. These releases had minimal repercussion and sales. The band would later leave independent labels to sign with a major label following the unexpected popularity of their "Love Will Tear Us Apart" cover.
