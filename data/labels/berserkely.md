# label - Berserkely

## curiosities
**Early Alternative Label** : Berserkely was a tiny label that released Jonathan Richman and The Modern Lovers' "Roadrunner" in 1971. It is cited as one of the first "alternative companies," providing a crucial "career alternative" for bands that could not secure deals with major record labels. "Berserkely was one of their early alternative companies which made Jonathan Richmond and the Modern Lovers one of the first alternative bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
