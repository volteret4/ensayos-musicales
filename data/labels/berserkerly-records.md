# label - Berserkerly Records

## curiosities
**Early "Alternative" Tag** : Berserkerly Records is considered one of the first indie labels to be tagged with the term "alternative," indicating its role in promoting music outside the mainstream. "One of the first indie labels tagged with the term alternative seems to be Berserkerly Records, a company founded by members of a Bay Area band called Earthquake in 1973." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
**Focus on Power Pop** : Much of the music released by Berserkerly Records fell into the Power Pop genre, with Jonathan Richman and the Modern Lovers being one of its most influential acts. "Much of what Berserkerly releases was of the Power Pop variety." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
**Founding (1973)** : Berserkerly Records was founded in 1973 by members of the Bay Area band Earthquake. The founders were frustrated with the business practices of larger labels and sought to operate more effectively on their own. "Berserkerly Records, a company founded by members of a Bay Area band called Earthquake in 1973." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
**Operations (Approx. 10 years)** : The label operated for about ten years, navigating the complexities of the music business independently. "And for about ten years, they muddled their way through the process." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
