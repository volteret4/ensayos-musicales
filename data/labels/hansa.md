# label - Hansa

## curiosities
**Bowie and Iggy Pop Recording Studio in Berlin** : Hansa Studio, specifically Studio 2, was the recording location for David Bowie and Iggy Pop's projects during their time in Berlin. Located about 500 yards from Potsdamer Platz, near the Berlin Wall, it was where Bowie produced Iggy's "The Idiot" and recorded his own albums "Low" and "Heroes." "They found a studio called Hansa, about 500 yards from Potstember Plats, which is where the Berlin Wall ran through the city and still does in sections." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2FyAZGUI_mk
