# label - Abkco

## curiosities
**Copyright Acquisition of "Bitter Sweet Symphony"** : Abkco, the company that administers the works of The Rolling Stones, gained full copyright to The Verve's song "Bitter Sweet Symphony" after the band lost a legal challenge over excessive sampling from a Stones-related cover. Abkco subsequently licensed the song to Nike for a commercial in 1998, against The Verve's wishes. "Abco sued, got the rights to bittersweet symphony, and immediately licensed it to Nike." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DYtsWfguqGo
