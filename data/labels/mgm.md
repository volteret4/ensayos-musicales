**Blackboard Jungle Release (1955)** : MGM released the film *Blackboard Jungle* in 1955. This movie was significant for featuring Bill Haley and the Comets' "Rock Around the Clock" in its opening titles, marking the first time a rock and roll song was used in a Hollywood feature film.
