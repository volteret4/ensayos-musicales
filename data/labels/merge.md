# label - Merge

## curiosities
**Early Struggles and Arcade Fire Breakthrough** : The label faced "rocky" times for approximately a dozen years until Arcade Fire's *Funeral* album came along, which led to a significant explosion in the label's fortunes and global recognition. "It was rocky for about a dozen years, but then Arcade Fire comes along with Funeral, and things just blew up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q4dEV1yn3OY
**Founding (1989)** : Merge Records, an indie label based in Durham, North Carolina, was established in 1989 by two members of the band Superchunk. They founded it because they faced difficulties in finding a major label or subsidiary to release their own music. "Their label was an Indie from Durham, North Carolina called Merge. The company was established in 1989 by two people from a band called SuperChunk, and theirs was the same story that we've heard again and again. They couldn't find any major or major label subsidiary to release their music, so they just created their own company and did it themselves." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q4dEV1yn3OY
**North Carolina Indie Label (1989-Present)** : A North Carolina label that had been struggling since its founding in 1989. Its fortunes significantly improved after nurturing Arcade Fire, whose massive online following and subsequent offline success boosted Merge's profile. "Merge had been struggling along since 1989, before they encountered Arcade Fire, which thanks to music blogs and file sharing, built a massive online following that eventually moved into the offline world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=raRCZ0Y5up8
