# label - Baldwin Radio Company

## curiosities
**Profits Used for Religious Promotion** : The Baldwin Radio Company, founded by Nathaniel Baldwin, manufactured headphones for the US Navy. Despite significant government contracts making a lot of money, the company ultimately went bankrupt in 1924. Profits from the factory were controversially channeled into promoting a "very fundamentalist version of the Church of Latter-day Saints," an offshoot that strongly believed in polygamy, which Baldwin himself also practiced.
