## curiosities
**Acquisition of BMG** : In 2008, Sony acquired BMG, leading to the creation of a new combined entity, initially known as Sony BMG, and later as Sony Music Entertainment. This acquisition brought Tool's Dissectional label, through its various parent companies, under the ultimate umbrella of Sony. "But then in 2008, BMG was bought by Sony, creating a new company called Sony BMG." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
**Future Distribution of Tool's Music** : It is assumed that if and when Tool releases a new album, it will be distributed through Sony or one of its subsidiaries, given Sony Music Entertainment's current ownership of the Dissectional label. "this means that Tool's Disexional label is now under the umbrella of Sony, which means that if and when Tool releases new album, it will be distributed through Sony, or one of their subsidiaries." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
**Major Label Patron of Volcano** : Sony Records served as the major label patron for Volcano Entertainment, meaning it held significant influence over the label that housed Tool for a period. "Volcano's major label patron was Sony Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
