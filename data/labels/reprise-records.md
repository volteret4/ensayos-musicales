# label - Reprise Records

## curiosities
**Founded by Frank Sinatra (1960)** : Created by Frank Sinatra in 1960.
**Major Label for Green Day** : Signed Green Day, who released their album Dookie through the label.
**Success with Concept Albums (2000s)** : Following the significant success of Green Day's American Idiot, Reprise Records also released My Chemical Romance's The Black Parade, suggesting a strategy to capitalize on the renewed popularity of concept albums.
