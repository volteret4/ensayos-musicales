# label - A&M Records

## curiosities
**Sex Pistols "God Save the Queen" Release and Destruction (1977)** : A&M Records signed the Sex Pistols for a mere five days in 1977, during which time approximately 25,000 copies of their "God Save the Queen" 7-inch single were pressed. Following the band's dismissal due to other artists' objections, these singles were ordered destroyed, though several dozen were "liberated" and became highly valuable collectibles. "But in that five days, A&M had pressed up something like 25,000 copies of God Save the Queen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zWNzlSxrj6s
