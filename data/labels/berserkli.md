# label - Berserkli

## curiosities
**Early Regional Alternative Label** : Berserkli was an example of a small regional record label that provided a home for artists whose music was considered too challenging for major multinational labels but still had an audience, thus contributing to the rise of "alternative" music. "These artists found homes with small regional record labels with names like Berserkli and Rounder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=__7iEl3lH8g
