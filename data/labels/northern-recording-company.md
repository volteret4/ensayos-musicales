# label - Northern Recording Company

## curiosities
**First African-American Woman-Owned Label** : The Northern Recording Company, based out of Detroit, was the first record label to be owned and operated by an African-American woman, founded with a modest $85. "She was called the Northern Recording Company and was based out of Detroit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XjQiZqpmQlE
**First Record Label Owned by a Woman** : The Northern Recording Company was founded in 1958 by Sadie DuPouy (Johnny May Matthews), who borrowed $85 to start the venture. Based in Detroit, it holds the distinction of being, as far as records show, the first record label owned by a woman, marking a significant and pioneering step in the industry. "In 1958, she borrowed $85 to form a label called Northern Recording Company. And as far as I can tell, that made her the first woman to ever own her own record label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oRR7N-YUcRE
