# label - Pickwick Records

## curiosities
**Budget Album Production (1960s)** : Pickwick Records in Manhattan was a label that specialized in producing budget albums, sold for about a dollar in department and furniture stores. This "prefabricated crap" was a common and profitable business model in the 1960s. "This kind of prefabricated crap was very common back then and Pickwick made a lot of money doing this." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
