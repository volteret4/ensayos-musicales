# label - Reciprocal Recording

## curiosities
**Early Grunge Recording Studio** : Reciprocal Recording was the studio used by Bam Bam for their first record, the "Villains" EP, in 1984, making them the first Grunge band to record there. The studio later became a significant hub for the grunge movement, utilized by iconic bands such as Nirvana, Alice in Chains, Soundgarden, The Melvins, and Mudhoney.
