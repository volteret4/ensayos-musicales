# label - Virgin Label

## curiosities
**God Save the Queen Pressing Issues** : The Virgin Label encountered significant difficulties in getting the Sex Pistols' "God Save the Queen" pressed. This was primarily because some workers at the manufacturing plant were so deeply offended by the song's content that they refused to perform their duties.
