# label - Warner Music Group

## curiosities
**Attempt at Digital Liner Notes (2008)** : In 2008, Warner Music Group launched an initiative to integrate liner notes into digital music downloads on iTunes. They added interactive booklets to 75 albums, but the project ultimately failed because the content relied on Flash, which Apple discontinued support for due to security vulnerabilities. "Back in 2008, Warner Music Group tried to bring liner notes to digital downloads on iTunes when they added interactive booklets to 75 albums on iTunes. The problem was the content used flash and Apple stopped supporting that because of a security flaw." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
