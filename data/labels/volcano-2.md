## curiosities
**Joint Company for Tool's Control** : Following the resolution of initial lawsuits and Q Prime's acquisition of a 50% stake in Volcano (then owned by Zamba), a new company named Volcano 2 was formed. This entity was specifically structured to establish Dissectional as Tool's own sub-label. It was set up as a joint company, granting Tool an active say in its operations to prevent future disagreements and ensure greater band control.
