# label - Alltec

## curiosities
**Voice of the Theater Series (1968)** : Alltec was the manufacturer of the "Voice of the Theater" series cabinets, which were a key component of The Grateful Dead's early sound system designed by Bob Hyal in 1968. This system, while advanced for its time, was eventually deemed insufficient by the band. "They'd been using a system designed by a guy named Bob Hyal in 1968 using some Alltec cabinets, this was their famous Voice of the Theater series." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
