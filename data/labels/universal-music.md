## curiosities
**Marketing Representative Iber Hamilton** : Iber Hamilton, a marketing representative at Universal Music, was closely involved with The Matthew Good Band. He was the individual in the panda suit at Edgefest 1999 who attempted a stage dive, inspiring the artwork for the "Loser Anthem" EP. "Now, the guy in the panda outfit is this guy named Iber Hamilton who works at Universal Music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-s0gwXY_CZQ
