# label - Rough Trade Records

## curiosities
**British Indie Label Influence** : A London-based independent record label known for its significant role in breaking new music, particularly since the punk era in the 1970s. They provided an early platform for bands like The Strokes. "The strokes started by sending a demo to Rough Trade Records in London, and they agreed to release an EP called The Modern Age." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=raRCZ0Y5up8
**Maintaining Physical Locations** : Rough Trade Records was highlighted as one of the few music retailers that continued to operate multiple physical locations, with two in London, one in Bristol, one in Nottingham, one in Liverpool, and two in New York, contrasting with the general mass extinction of chain stores. "They have two locations in London, one in Bristol, another in Nottingham, one in Liverpool, and a couple of New York locations." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZzIqIydZTlA
