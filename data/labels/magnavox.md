# label - Magnavox

## curiosities
**Early Public Address System (December 24, 1915)** : The American company Magnavox, founded by Edwin Jensen and Peter Prittum, developed an early public address system. Between 1911 and 1915, they integrated a microphone with a British loudspeaker and Lee DeForest's amplifier tubes. Their system, comprising a mic, an amp, and a 34-inch speaker, was publicly demonstrated for the first time on December 24, 1915, at San Francisco City Hall, allowing 100,000 people to hear amplified Christmas music and holiday speeches.
