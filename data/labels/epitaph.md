# label - Epitaph

## curiosities
**"Biggest Selling Indie Band" Claim** : At one point, Epitaph was home to a band (unnamed in the transcript) that became the biggest selling indie band in the history of the universe. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Bad Religion's Self-Created Label (1981)** : Established by the band Bad Religion because no other record labels were interested in releasing their music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**First Release (February 1981)** : Its first release was Bad Religion's self-titled EP in February 1981. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Growth Beyond Hardcore** : Over time, the company expanded its focus beyond solely hardcore punk. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Uncommon Radio Play** : It was generally uncommon for bands on the independent label Epitaph to have their singles picked up by mainstream radio stations. This made The Offspring's "Smash" gaining traction on LA radio a "sheer fluke" and "even weirder" when it spread to other stations. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
