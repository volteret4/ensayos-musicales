# label - Epitaph

## curiosities
**"Biggest Selling Indie Band" Claim** : At one point, Epitaph was home to a band (unnamed in the transcript) that became the biggest selling indie band in the history of the universe.
**Bad Religion's Self-Created Label (1981)** : Established by the band Bad Religion because no other record labels were interested in releasing their music.
**First Release (February 1981)** : Its first release was Bad Religion's self-titled EP in February 1981.
**Growth Beyond Hardcore** : Over time, the company expanded its focus beyond solely hardcore punk.
