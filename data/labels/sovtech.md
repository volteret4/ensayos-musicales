# label - Sovtech

## curiosities
**Big Muff Production Collaboration** : While the Big Muff pedal was stamped with the Electroharmonics name, Sovtech, a company based in the USSR, was also involved in its production. This collaboration suggests a recognition of their expertise in creating fuzz effects.
