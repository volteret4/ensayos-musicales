# label - XL Recordings

## curiosities
**Adele's Indie Label** : XL Recordings is the home label for Adele, and is considered an indie artist, making her the only indie artist to have sold more records than The Offspring's *Smash*. "Yes, Adele is an indie artist. Her home label is XL, part of the Bigger's Bank of the Group, part of the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QtMI00T5jw
**Conventional Release of In Rainbows (2007)** : XL Recordings handled the conventional release of Radiohead's album "In Rainbows" after its initial pay-what-you-want digital experiment. This release contributed to the album's total sales exceeding 3 million copies. "More people bought the album when it was released conventionally through Excel recordings." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=19Z_AAEy8Bc
**Home to Adele** : XL Recordings is the label for globally successful artist Adele, making her a certifiable indie artist due to XL's affiliation with the Beggars Group. "She records for XL, so she's an indie artist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
