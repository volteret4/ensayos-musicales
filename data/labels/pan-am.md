## curiosities
**Flight 103 Bombing (1988)** : Pan Am Flight 103, a 747 en route from London to New York, was bombed over Lockerbie, Scotland, on December 21, 1988, in one of the worst incidents of airline terrorism. A bomb, secreted inside a cassette player by a Libyan agent, detonated at 31,000 feet and 500 miles per hour, disintegrating the plane and killing all 243 passengers, 16 crew members, and 11 people on the ground.
