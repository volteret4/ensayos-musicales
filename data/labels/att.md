# label - AT&T

## curiosities
**Co-development of the First Speaker** : AT&T was involved in a co-development project with General Electric in 1921, which successfully produced the first speaker. This device, featuring a paper coil, was a foundational invention for sound reproduction from electrical signals. "And that happened in 1921, when a co-development project between General Electric and AT&T resulted in the first speaker, a paper call that reproduced sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
