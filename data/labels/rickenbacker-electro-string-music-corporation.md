# label - Rickenbacker Electro String Music Corporation

## curiosities
**Pioneering Electric Guitar Sales** : Rickenbacher's Electro String Music Corporation is noted as potentially the very first company to sell electrified guitars, though other companies soon followed suit. Their first model was the A25 in 1932.
