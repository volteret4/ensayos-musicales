# label - Rounder

## curiosities
**Early Independent Alternative Label** : Rounder was listed as one of the small, independent, niche record companies that began to emerge in the late 1970s and early 1980s. These labels were crucial in providing "an alternative to the majors" by signing and distributing groups that embodied the creative and attitudinal spirit of punk. "These labels, which had names like Epitaph, ST, Berserkerly, Rounder, Sire and dozens of others, provided an alternative to the majors." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
**Early Regional Alternative Label** : Rounder was an example of a small regional record label that provided a home for artists whose music was considered too challenging for major multinational labels but still had an audience, thus contributing to the rise of "alternative" music. "These artists found homes with small regional record labels with names like Berserkli and Rounder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=__7iEl3lH8g
