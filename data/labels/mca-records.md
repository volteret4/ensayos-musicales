# label - MCA Records

## curiosities
**Canceled Gig and Future Fulfillment** : Leonard Skinnerd had a concert scheduled at Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge for October 20, 1977. This gig was canceled due to the plane crash. Fourteen years later, the band made good on the promise, performing for about 100 people who had held onto their tickets from the original date. "The band did make good on that canceled show at Louisiana State University. About 100 people who hung onto their tickets for the gig on October 21, 1977 were able to see the band play 14 years later." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dgzF_KBSQWM
**Dream Performance Destination (1977)** : Leonard Skinnerd harbored a long-standing dream of performing at Madison Square Garden. This aspiration was on the verge of being realized, as the band was scheduled to play at the venue several weeks into their tour leg that began on October 13, 1977. "The next leg of the tour began on October 13th in statesboro, Georgia, and would eventually see the band realize their dreams by playing Madison Square Garden several weeks down the road." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dgzF_KBSQWM
**Last Performance Before Crash (October 19, 1977)** : On October 19, 1977, Leonard Skinnerd performed at the Greenville Memorial Auditorium in Greenville, South Carolina. This concert tragically marked their final performance before the fatal plane crash occurred the very next day. "And on October 19th, Leonard Skinner performed at the Greenville Memorial Auditorium in Greenville, South Carolina." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dgzF_KBSQWM
**Street Survivors Cover Change (1977)** : After the tragic plane crash in October 1977, Steve Gaines' widow, Teresa Gaines, formally requested that MCA Records withdraw the original cover of the *Street Survivors* album, which depicted the band surrounded by flames. In response, the album was reissued with a plain black cover. "Teresa Gaines, Steve's widow, asked that MCA records withdraw that version. It was reissued and the band appears on a plain black cover." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dgzF_KBSQWM
