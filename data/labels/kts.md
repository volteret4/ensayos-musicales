# label - KTS

## curiosities
**Bootleg Label Characteristics** : KTS was a renowned bootleg label known for two main things: super high-quality live recordings, which they obtained from unknown sources, and a wide selection of studio recordings that were never intended for official release. The label operated in offshore locations like Italy, Singapore, and Indonesia, where copyright laws were less stringent. "They were renowned for two things.
