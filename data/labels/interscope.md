# label - Interscope

## curiosities
**Audioslave Co-Release (First Album) – Partnership with Epic** : Interscope Records, Chris Cornell's label, collaborated with Epic Records (Rage Against the Machine's label) for the joint release of Audioslave's debut album. This was part of a negotiated deal to navigate the band members' existing contractual obligations. "The first album would be released by both Epic, Rage Against the Machine's label, and Interscope, Chris's label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**Audioslave Release (Third Album for US) – Continued Involvement** : Interscope Records managed the US release of Audioslave's third album, while Epic handled the international release. This arrangement concluded the complex label deal that had been negotiated for the supergroup's releases. "then the third album would be Interscope for the US and Epic for the rest of the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**Audioslave Sole Release (Second Album) – Exclusive Distribution** : Interscope Records held exclusive distribution rights for Audioslave's second album, as part of the label compromise worked out during the band's formation. This followed a joint release for their debut and preceded a split arrangement for their third. "The second album would be Interscope only..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**Jimmy Iovine's Influence** : Jimmy Iovine is recognized as a skilled marketer and played a supportive role in Bush's recordings. He also co-founded Beats with Dr. Dre, driven partly by concerns over the poor quality of available headphones. "Which in the Iveane, that took a care of quite a bit of it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lGNrJgwQjgg
**Rescuing Nine Inch Nails from TVT** : Interscope Records played a crucial role in liberating Nine Inch Nails from their restrictive and litigious contract with TVT Records. Trent Reznor initially viewed the move as "the lesser of several evils" due to his perception of Interscope's image at the time (e.g., Marky Mark), but later appreciated having a label president who, as a former producer, understood the challenges of making records. "They rescued us from TBT, it was a lesser of several evils, and at the time my vision of the inner scope was Mark E. Mark, it was like, Christ, one TVT, and another." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M3nUQtja0qc
**Support for Artists** : Interscope Records, particularly with Jimmy Iovine, provided substantial financial, emotional, and musical backing to Bush over the years. Gavin Rossdale began recording his "Sea of Memories" album while still under Interscope's care. "I had the benefit, the backing and the financial support, emotional support, musical support of the team at Indusco that always helped me greatly over the years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lGNrJgwQjgg
