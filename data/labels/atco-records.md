# label - Atco Records

## curiosities
**Run by Margo Netts in the 1980s** : Margo Netts, a music executive since 1970, ran Atco Records for the majority of the 1980s. During her leadership, she oversaw campaigns for a diverse roster of artists, including Stevie Nicks, Pete Townshend, ABBA, and Hall & Oates, solidifying her influence in the music business.
