# label - International Business Machines (IBM)

## curiosities
**Renaming from Hollerith's Legacy (1924)** : By 1924, the Computing Tabulating Recording Company, which originated from Herman Hollerith's pioneering work in data tabulation, became known as International Business Machines, or IBM for short. This renaming solidified its identity as a major player in business technology.
