# label - Rhapsody

## curiosities
**Evolution of Listen.com** : Rhapsody was the name adopted by Listen.com in 2001, after the company initially launched in 1999 as the first modern streaming service. It subsequently evolved into the legitimate Napster. "It changed its name to Rhapsody in 2001 and is now known as Napster, but this is a completely legitimate and legal Napster unlike the original." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Y6nHw5xx88
**First Legitimate Streaming Service (2001)** : Rhapsody is identified as the first legitimate streaming music service, launched in 2001. It pioneered the legal streaming model, paving the way for the eventual dominance of streaming in the music industry, which by the end of the 2000s saw dozens of services emerge, led by Spotify. "The first legitimate streamer was Rhapsody in 2001." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
