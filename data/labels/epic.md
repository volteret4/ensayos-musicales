# label - Epic

## curiosities
**Audioslave Co-Release (First Album) – Partnership with Interscope** : For Audioslave's debut album, Epic Records, which was Rage Against the Machine's label, collaborated with Interscope Records (Chris Cornell's label) for a joint release. This arrangement was part of a complex compromise to accommodate both artists' existing contracts. "The first album would be released by both Epic, Rage Against the Machine's label, and Interscope, Chris's label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**Audioslave Release (Third Album for Rest of World) – Continued Involvement** : Epic Records continued its involvement with Audioslave for their third album, handling its release for the rest of the world, while Interscope managed the US release. This demonstrated an ongoing, albeit split, relationship with the supergroup. "then the third album would be Interscope for the US and Epic for the rest of the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
