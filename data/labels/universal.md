# label - Universal

## curiosities
**Largest Major Label** : currently the biggest of the three major recorded music companies. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Lorde's Success Strategy** : signed Lorde and employed very intensive promotional marketing, including multiple remixes and promotional campaigns, to move her across several genres and formats, her career having begun as an alternative artist. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Major Record Label** : One of the three major multinational entities that dominate the music industry, controlling the majority of the world's music trade and its associated revenue. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Pressplay Venture (2000-2003)** : Universal was involved in Pressplay, a joint venture online music store launched around 2000. It was criticized for being difficult to use, having restrictions, and being loaded with DRM safeguards, leading to its closure in March 2003 after about two years. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
