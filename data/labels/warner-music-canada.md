# label - Warner Music Canada

## curiosities
**Jen Hurst's Role** : Jen Hurst became the Junior A&R girl at Warner Music Canada after her previous label dissolved. Her prior belief in Pezz, coupled with positive reviews, led to the band being at the top of her list for potential signings, culminating in their record deal. "But the label she was working for ended up falling apart. And then she became the junior A&R girl at Warner Music, Canada." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Make Music Matter Distribution Partner** : Warner Music Canada serves as a distribution partner for music produced by participants in the Make Music Matter program. Songs written and recorded in therapeutic sessions are mixed by volunteer mixers and then released through this major label. "We release them through Warner Music Canada." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
**Support for Billy Talent** : Warner Music Canada is Billy Talent's record label and was fully supportive of drummer Aaron Solowoniuk during his struggle with MS. They also provided significant assistance in organizing the band's interviews. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ocMHRLQMQY0
