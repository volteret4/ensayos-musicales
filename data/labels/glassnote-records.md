# label - Glassnote Records

## curiosities
**Home to Notable Indie Artists** : Glassnote Records is an indie label that is home to several prominent artists, including Phoenix, Mumford & Sons, and Chvrches, demonstrating its standing in the independent music scene. "This is the indie label that's home to Phoenix and Mumford & Sons and Churches." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**The Strumbellas' Breakthrough Deal** : Daniel Glass, who heads Glassnote Records, signed The Strumbellas after hearing samples of their third album and seeing them live. This deal was pivotal for the band, leading to a worldwide release of their "Hope" album. "But then they came to the attention of Daniel Glass at Glass Note Records. The strombellas manager made contact, sent them samples of the band's third album and waited by the phone. But then it rang. We liked the record and Daniel Glass is flying to Toronto to see the band live. Everybody got along and the deal was struck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
