# label - Record Labels

## curiosities
**Album-Centric Contracts** : still use albums as the "currency of the recorded music industry," basing contracts and awards on them, despite the shift to singles in streaming.
**Diminished Power in Streaming Era** : lost significant power as "cultural gatekeepers" in the streaming era, unable to create consensus about good music or promote artists to superstar status.
**Greed in Vinyl Resurgence** : became "a little greedy" during the vinyl resurgence, selling new vinyl at "ridiculous prices," such as Daft Punk's *Random Access Memories* for $45.
**Post-Grunge Proliferation** : piled on after grunge became popular, leading to record sales "through the roof."
**Role in 80s Alt-Rock** : were part of the "outsider" scene in the 80s, reveling in their non-mainstream status.
