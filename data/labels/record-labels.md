# label - Record Labels

## curiosities
**Profit Margins on Albums (Post-1965)** : Record labels found albums "very attractive" after 1965 because the "profit margins were so much higher on albums than they were on singles," which contributed to the music industry's shift away from singles to focus on album releases.
