# label - Joke Records

## curiosities
**Founding by The Middle Class** : Joke Records was an independent label founded by the band The Middle Class to release their debut 1979 EP, "Out of Vote," because their short, aggressive music was not suitable for commercial radio or major labels. "So The Middle Class formed their own indie label called Joke Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEHkKfmmDiI
**Self-Released The Middle Class EP** : Joke Records was formed by The Middle Class to release their 1979 debut EP, "Out of Vogue," because commercial radio and major labels showed no interest in their hardcore sound. This highlights the early adoption of the indie label strategy in hardcore. "So the Middle Class formed their own indie label called Joke Records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
