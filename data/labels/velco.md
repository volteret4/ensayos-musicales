# label - Velco

## curiosities
**Manufacturer of Airline Guitars** : Velco was the company responsible for building the Airline Rezo Glass guitar model. This model, famously used by Jack White, was constructed from fiberglass, an innovative material at the time, which allowed for distinctive, non-traditional body shapes.
