## curiosities
**Acclaimed Bird Illustrator** : After leaving the music scene in the early 90s, Robert Dean moved to Costa Rica to decompress and pursue his love of wildlife. He became an acclaimed illustrator of birds, closely observing and painting the birds of the jungle, and now lives in Monteverdi, Costa Rica, as a professional ornithology writer and artist. "He's become an acclaimed illustrator of birds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Departure from Japan** : Robert Dean served as the lead guitarist for Japan's first three albums in the late 1970s and early 1980s. He left the band as they began exploring more electronic sounds, a direction that pushed him aside. "Robert was the band's lead guitarist for the first three albums but then started getting pushed aside as Japan started exploring more electronic sounds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
