## curiosities
**Telegenic New Wave Band** : Flock of Seagulls was highlighted as one of the "Telegenic New Wave bands" that achieved stardom with the advent of music videos on MTV (1981) and MuchMusic (1983). Their visual appeal played a significant role in showcasing music in a new light during this era.
