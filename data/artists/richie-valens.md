# artist - Richie Valens

## curiosities
**Plane Crash Death (1957)** : On February 3, 1957, a day famously known as "the day the music died," Richie Valens was killed when the small plane he was a passenger in went down in a cornfield outside of Mason City, Iowa. He died alongside fellow musicians Buddy Holly and The Big Bopper. "February 3rd, 1957 has gone down in history as the day the music died." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
