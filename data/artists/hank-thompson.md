## curiosities
**Honky Tonk Singer and Rockabilly Precursor** : Hank Thompson was a honky tonk singer who performed a "paired down version" of Bob Wills' western swing style. His music incorporated the rhythms and instrumentation of western swing but emphasized vocals and non-instrumental solos. Thompson was one of several artists whose music served as a precursor to rockabilly. **Mentor to Wanda Jackson** : Thompson discovered Wanda Jackson when she was 14 during a TV audition. Impressed by her voice, he invited her to perform with his band, even though she was inexperienced. He couldn't take her on tour due to her schooling but mentored her on stage whenever he played in Oklahoma, teaching her how to perform with other musicians. He also invited her to appear on his local TV show, which eventually led to her own TV show.
