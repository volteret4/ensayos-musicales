## albums
**Kerosene Hat (1993)** : This 1993 album presented itself as containing 12 tracks, but it famously harbored multiple hidden songs. If allowed to play beyond the listed tracks, the CD counter would advance to track 15 for "High Desert Biker Meth Lab" and then continue all the way to track 69, where the full version of "Euro Trash Girl" was hidden. "In 1993, they released an album called Caracene Hat. It looked like it contained 12 tracks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J_VEOIwavtg

## songs
**Euro Trash Girl (1993)** : The full version of this song was hidden as track 69 on Cracker's 1993 album, "Kerosene Hat." Originally a throwaway track, its discovery led to it being released as a single, demonstrating an instance of an unlisted song gaining commercial success. "The full version of Cracker's Euro Trash Girl, hidden as Track 69, on their 1993 album Caracene Hat. Once it was discovered, it was turned into a single." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J_VEOIwavtg
**High Desert Biker Meth Lab** : This unlisted song is hidden on Cracker's 1993 album, "Kerosene Hat". Despite the album appearing to have 12 tracks, if the CD continued playing, the counter would pass tracks 13 and 14 before settling on "High Desert Biker Meth Lab" at track 15, completely unmentioned in any official documentation. "Before it settled on a song called High Desert Biker Meth Lab at Track 15, it wasn't listed anywhere." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J_VEOIwavtg
