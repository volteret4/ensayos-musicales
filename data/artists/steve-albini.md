# artist - Steve Albini

## curiosities
**Champion Poker Player** : Steve Albini, renowned as a producer for bands like Nirvana and the Pixies, is also a champion poker player. He was taught to play by his great-grandmother as a child and won his first World Series of Poker Title in a seven-card stud tournament in Las Vegas, beating 30 other players and taking home $106,000.
