**Asteroid 110393 Ramstein** : An asteroid, officially named 110393 Ramstein, has been designated in honor of the band Rammstein. This asteroid is about six kilometers in size, not very bright, and located somewhere between Mars and Jupiter. It was discovered by Jean-Claude Merlin at an observatory in Lucre-Sau, France. "The official name of their rock is 110393 Ramstein." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMPLplhDk8I
