## curiosities
**Orange County Gigs** : Soy DeSant began playing regular gigs around Orange County, establishing themselves in the local music scene during Scott Weiland's teenage years. "And the band also began playing some regular gigs around Orange County." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r6vVw66-gmI ← soy-desant
**Scott Weiland's Post-Punk Band** : After being inspired by an art rock band, Scott Weiland formed his own post-punk band named Soy DeSant. The French name, meaning "a style of one's own," was a nod to Depeche Mode, one of Weiland's favorite bands. "That inspired Scott to form his own post punk band that he called Soy DeSant, which translated means a style of one's own." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r6vVw66-gmI ← soy-desant
