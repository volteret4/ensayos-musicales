## curiosities
**Early Ambition as Singer-Songwriter** : Prior to the formation of The Black Keys, Dan Auerbach aspired to be a singer-songwriter performing in local bars. His need for a demo tape to secure out-of-town gigs led him to seek Patrick Carney's help, which inadvertently initiated their collaboration. "We had this idea of being a singer-songwriter who played the bars in the area, but the no one would give him any out of town gigs without a demo." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I ← dan-auerbach
**Influence of Junior Kimbrough** : In high school, Dan Auerbach was a devoted fan of blues singer Junior Kimbrough. Listening to Kimbrough's music, notably while "smoking a lot of dope," was a pivotal experience that inspired Auerbach to commit to playing guitar full-time. "Dan was a big fan of this guy in high school. In fact, listening to Kimbrough, while smoking a lot of dope, helped him decide to pursue the guitar full time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I ← dan-auerbach
**Producer Work** : Dan Auerbach has extended his musical career to producing for other artists, including Dr. John and a group named Hacienda. "He did a record by Dr. John, and another by a group called Hacienda." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I ← dan-auerbach
**Producer of the Year Grammy** : Dan Auerbach was awarded a Grammy for Producer of the Year, alongside The Black Keys' other Grammy wins for their album *El Camino*. "Dan are back one producer of the year." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I ← dan-auerbach
