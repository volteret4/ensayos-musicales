# artist - Sylvia Robinson

## labels
- Sugar Hill Records

## curiosities
**Discovery of The Sugar Hill Gang** : Sylvia Robinson discovered Big Hank, a member of The Sugar Hill Gang, through a tip from her son at Krispy Krust Pizza in New York. She then recruited the other two members by driving around and asking rappers if they were interested in recording rap over the music of Chic's "Good Times." This unconventional method led to the formation of the group and the recording of "Rapper's Delight" in just 14 minutes. **Founder of Sugar Hill Records** : Sylvia Robinson founded Sugar Hill Records with the intention of recording a rap record. She faced challenges in finding rap artists willing to be recorded, as rap was primarily viewed as a live performance art, and many doubted its commercial viability on vinyl. Her perseverance led to the discovery and recording of The Sugar Hill Gang. **Musical Background** : Sylvia Robinson was a member of the duo Mickey and Sylvia in the late 1950s, known for their song "Love Is Strange." Her past experience in the music industry gave her insight into creating popular music.
