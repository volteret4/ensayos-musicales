## curiosities
**Pioneers of Rap/Hip Hop** : Run-D.M.C. emerged from the street culture of New York where quick-handed DJs and rhyming MCs competed. This culture, often referred to as turntableism, laid the foundation for groups like Run-D.M.C. to create new music from manipulated record sounds.
