## curiosities
**Celiac Disease and Tragic Death** : Joe C also suffered from celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder that primarily impacts the small intestine and causes a severe intolerance to gluten, requiring strict avoidance of wheat, barley, rye, and related foods. Complications from celiac disease ultimately led to his death on November 6, 2000, at the young age of 26, as he passed away in his sleep while visiting his parents. "And that's what killed him on November 6, 2000 at the age of 26, complications of celiac disease." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u5NLt6mkmrk
**Dwarfism and Multiple Health Issues** : Joe C, known as Kid Rock's hypeman and standing at three feet nine inches, was a dwarf who grappled with a multitude of medical issues throughout his life. These included kidney failure, which necessitated daily dialysis, and a regimen of up to 65 pills per day to manage his various conditions.
