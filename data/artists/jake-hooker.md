# artist - Jake Hooker

## curiosities
**Songwriter for "I Love Rock and Roll"** : Jake Hooker is credited as the writer of the song "I Love Rock and Roll." The future rights to this song were part of a financial bond, similar to Bowie Bonds, allowing Hooker to receive upfront payment while the bondholders managed its exploitation through licensing.
