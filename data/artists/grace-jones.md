**Nightclubbing (Post-1979) - Post-Disco Reinvention** : This album is regarded as possibly the most passionate and significant work of Grace Jones's career. Emerging after the anti-disco riots, it marked a profound reinvention of her sound, shifting from her disco roots to an entirely new electronic style. **European Disco Influence** : Grace Jones drew extensively from the distinct European version of disco, which differed significantly from the pure, Gloria Gaynor-influenced Afro-American disco that struggled to penetrate Europe.
