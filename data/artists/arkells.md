# artist - Arkells

## labels
- Amazon Music (commissioned exclusive)

## songs
**Pub Crawl (2020)** : A Christmas song commissioned as an exclusive for Amazon Music. It is based on the band's tradition of catching up with friends during Christmas after long tours by gathering at a pub, pooling credit cards, and drawing them to determine who pays the tab before moving to the next pub. The song was leaked to bars across Canada and some in the US in November 2020, where fans were encouraged to request it from bartenders. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nYPGQiDL5bw
