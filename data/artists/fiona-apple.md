## curiosities
**Court Watch PG Volunteer** : She volunteers for an organization called Court Watch PG, which monitors bail hearings and holds individuals within the judicial system accountable. "She's also a volunteer for an organization called Court Watch PG, which keeps tabs on bail hearings and holds people in the judicial system accountable." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zZM9ajwYr9E ← fiona-apple ← fiona-apple
**Discovery of Jazz and Style Development** : After initial classical music training, Fiona Apple discovered jazz through artists such as Ella Fitzgerald and Billie Holiday, often playing along with their records on the piano. This exposure profoundly influenced her, leading to a distinctive musical style that blends classical, jazz, and pop elements. "She said classical music, but eventually discovered jazz through the music of Elephant's Gerald and Billy Holiday. Fiona would sit the piano playing along. Her style became a mashup of classical jazz and pop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g ← fiona-apple
**Early Songwriting and Musical Adaptability** : Fiona Apple started composing her own songs at the young age of eight. She possessed a unique talent for converting guitar chord charts into sheet music specifically for the piano, demonstrating her innate musical aptitude and versatility. "Like Tori Amus, she was composing her own songs at an early age, eight in her case. She had this knack of being able to take chord charts for guitars and turn them into sheet music for the piano." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g ← fiona-apple
**Major Label Deal through Unconventional Means** : Fiona Apple secured her major label deal with Sony Music by the age of 20 through an unusual path: she passed a demo cassette to the babysitter of a music publicist, who then forwarded it to the label. This unconventional method underscores the raw talent that caught the industry's attention. "She started making demo recordings and eventually passed a cassette to the babysitter of a music publicist. That publicist then passed it on to Sony Music, and by the time Fiona was 20, she had a major label deal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g ← fiona-apple
