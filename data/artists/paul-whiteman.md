## curiosities
**Big Band Leader** : Paul Whiteman was a prominent band leader during the Big Band era of jazz, leading large orchestras of ten or more musicians. These leaders were the stars, with all other musicians and singers functioning as employees under their rigid direction. "These were orchestras of ten or more people led by a big name, Paul Whiteman, Tommy Dorsey, Benny Goodman, and so on." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk ← paul-whiteman
**King of Jazz Title** : Whiteman earned the title "King of Jazz" by leading a large, 35-musician, all-white band, which contrasted with typical jazz bands of 8-10 improvising musicians. His band played from written charts, essentially cleaning up jazz and making it more palatable for polite society.
