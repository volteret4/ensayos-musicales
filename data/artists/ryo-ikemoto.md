# artist - Ryo Ikemoto

## curiosities
**Audiovisual Performance with Kode9 (Sónar Festival)** : Ryo Ikemoto (referred to in the transcript as "Rio Jimorimoto" and "Coilmoreimoto"), a legend in anime and video games, collaborated with Kode9 on a hybrid concert and interactive audiovisual projection at Sónar. This performance seamlessly integrated Ikemoto's animation and video game experience with Kode9's music, creating a sophisticated and "classic" approach to combining visuals and sound.
