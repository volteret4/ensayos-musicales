## curiosities
**Norwegian Ice Musician** : Terje Innsung set is a Norwegian musician renowned for his innovative experiments with musical instruments made exclusively from ice. He reportedly believes that Swedish glacier ice yields the best quality trumpets. "He's a Norwegian musician who experiments with musical instruments made out of ice. Apparently Swedish glacier ice makes the best trumpet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0D4wGWI-_z8
