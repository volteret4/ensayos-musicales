## curiosities
**Album Output** : The band released a total of eleven albums throughout their career. "No means no, was around for 11 albums" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Genre Blending and Influence** : NoMeansNo occupied a unique space by blending elements of hardcore and metal. Their sound contained elements that would later define post-hardcore, influencing bands like At the Drive-In, Rollins Band, Alexisonfire, Billy Talent, Deftones, and Silverstein. "They ended up occupying a space somewhere between hardcore and metal. If you listen carefully to what they did, you can hear elements of what would become known as post-hardcore, a sound that would later birth groups like at the drive-in, Rollins Band, Alexis on Fire, Billy Talent, The Death Tones and Silverstein." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Origins and Relocation** : NoMeansNo started in Victoria, British Columbia, and later relocated to Vancouver. "And then there was no means no, who started in Victoria and later moved to Vancouver." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Van Theft & Polish Mafia (Poland)** : While on tour in Poland one Christmas, NoMeansNo had their rented van stolen after a show in Warsaw, containing about $10,000 worth of merchandise. With local police being unhelpful and the Dutch rental company concerned about insurance coverage outside Holland, a fan connected them with the Polish Mafia. Band member Rob Wright met two heavily armed men in a church basement and negotiated the van's return for 2000 German marks. The next day, the van and all its contents were recovered from a barn outside Warsaw. However, the Warsaw police later demanded another 2000 German marks for operating outside legal channels. "The next day the van was given the name of a village about 100 kilometers outside of Warsaw and told to wait in the town square." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgN0vBIugLc
