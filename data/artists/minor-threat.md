## curiosities
**Documented Moshing in Film** : Minor Threat was among the bands whose live performances, featuring moshing, were documented in Penelope Spheeris's film "The Decline of Western Civilization." This film played a role in the global spread of moshing culture. "The bands ranging from the Ramones to the dead canadies to DOA to minor threat." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Predecessor to Embrace** : Ian MacKaye was a member of this hardcore band before he was inspired to leave and form Embrace, a proto-emo group. "Everyone was inspired to leave his hardcore band called Minor Threat to form a proto-emo group called Embrace." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
