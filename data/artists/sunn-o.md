## curiosities
**Ritualistic Drone Metal Development** : Sunn O))) significantly advanced Drone Metal into a more ritualistic territory. Their approach often removes vocals and sometimes even drums, focusing on minimalist soundscapes where notes are sustained for minutes, creating tectonic vibrations and almost stopping musical time. Live, they amplify this ceremonial feel by performing in tunics and enveloped in fog, making their stage presence an extension of the sound itself.
