## songs
**Mighty Mighty (1974)** : This song, from their 1974 album *Open Our Eyes*, features the chorus "we are people of the mighty, mighty people of the sun." It achieved commercial success, reaching number 29 on the Billboard Hot 100 and number 4 on the Billboard Hot Soul Charts. However, some radio stations refused to play it, believing it was associated with the Black Power movement, as noted by the band's founding members. Laurie Janelle Dance, an Assistant Professor of Sociology, recalled that listening to the song in the 70s helped her heal by declaring "black was beautiful," directly challenging the dominant culture's historical depiction of African Americans as inferior.
