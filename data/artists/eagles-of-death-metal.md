## curiosities
**Bataclan Massacre Eyewitness Account** : Jesse Hughes, the singer for Eagles of Death Metal, provided a powerful account of what he witnessed from the stage during the Bataclan Massacre on November 13, 2015. He recounted seeing acts of immense bravery, with people shielding loved ones from bullets, rather than any cowardice. This quote underlines the tragic heroism amidst the terror. "I didn't just see people getting murdered. I saw people jump up and jump in front of bullets for the ones they love and give their life. I never saw a single coward that night. I only saw bravery." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xid8CZf4oYo
**Return to Paris After Attack (2016)** : Following the horrific Bataclan Massacre, Eagles of Death Metal defiantly returned to Paris on February 16, 2016. The band performed again in the city to finish their interrupted show and to pay tribute to the 90 victims, including their merchandise manager, Nick Alexander, who died in the attack. "On February 16, 2016, the band returned to Paris to finish the show and to pay tribute to those who died, including the group's merch manager, Nick Alexander." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xid8CZf4oYo
