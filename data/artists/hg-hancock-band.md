# artist - HG Hancock band

## members
- Dave Grohl
- Larry Hinkel

## curiosities
**Breakup due to Parental Divorce** : The band dissolved when Larry Hinkel's parents divorced, leading to his relocation to Maryland with his father. "The first, again the HG Hancock band, broke up when Larry's parents divorced and he moved Maryland with his father." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA ← hg-hancock-band
**Dave Grohl's First Band** : Formed around 1981, this was Dave Grohl's first band, a Grade 5 bedroom project with his school friend Larry Hinkel, where Dave played guitar. "This was around the same time as the formation of Dave's first band. He had a school buddy named Larry Hinkel and they named their Grade 5 Bedroom Project the HG Hancock band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA ← hg-hancock-band
**Makeshift Drum Kit** : Larry Hinkel, the drummer, used an improvised drum kit consisting of a laundry basket, kitchen pots, and knitting needles for sticks. "Larry cobbled together a jumpsuit consisting of a laundry basket, some pots from the kitchen, and a couple of mom's knitting needles for sticks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA ← hg-hancock-band
**Unusual Naming Convention** : The band's name, H G Hancock, was Dave Grohl's idea, derived from the initials of Hinkel and Grohl, and their gym teacher's surname, a choice inspired by the naming of Lynyrd Skynyrd. "Well H for Hinkel, G for Grohl and Hinkel because, well, that was the name of their gym teacher. It was Dave's idea." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA ← hg-hancock-band
