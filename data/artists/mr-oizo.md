## genres

## curiosities
**Pioneer of French Experimental Electronica** : Mr. Oizo is recognized as one of the key figures who launched the underground experimental electronica scene in France in the late 1990s, a movement that later influenced artists associated with Vanguard Records. His album "Analo-guon-s-attaque" was pivotal in establishing this new sound.
