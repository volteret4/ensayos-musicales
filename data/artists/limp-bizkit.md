## members

## curiosities
**Jessica Malachik Tragedy and Lawsuits (2001)** : At the Big Day Out Festival in Australia in 2001, 16-year-old Jessica Malachik was crushed in the pit and died five days later from a heart attack related to asphyxiation. The coroner cited singer Fred Durst for "alarming and inflammatory comments" when EMS people tried to rescue Jessica. Her parents filed a civil suit against Limp Bizkit, and the band was subsequently sued by their own insurance company, which stated such lawsuits were not covered by their policy.
**Woodstock '99 Incitement** : Encouraged the crowd to "go nuts" during their performance at Woodstock '99.
