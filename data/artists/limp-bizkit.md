**Big Day Out Tragedy and Lawsuits (2001)** : As headliners at the Big Day Out festival in Sydney on January 21, 2001, Limp Bizkit's singer Fred Durst had pre-show concerns about inadequate security barriers, which he discussed multiple times with the promoter. Despite assurances, a massive crush occurred towards the stage during their first song, resulting in approximately 30 injuries and the death of 15-year-old Jessica Milicic, who died five days later from a heart attack caused by asphyxiation. An investigation closed in November 2002 without charges, but new security rules were implemented. Milicic's parents filed a civil suit against Limp Bizkit in November 2004, and the band was also sued by their own insurance company over legal fees, leading to speculation that these events contributed to their subsequent quiet period. "So, is it any wonder we haven't heard anything from Limpbiscat since then?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7v1REnqKuXE
