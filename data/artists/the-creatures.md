**Divorce (August 2007)** : After a relationship that spanned approximately 25 years, with 15 years of marriage, Siouxsie Sue and Budgie publicly announced their divorce in August 2007. While Siouxsie never remarried, Budgie did and has two children. "And sadly in August 2007, after so many years, they publicly announced their divorce. **Side Project and Marriage (1991)** : Siouxsie Sue and Budgie formed The Creatures as their own side project due to their growing closeness within Siouxsie and the bandsheets. They married in 1991 and subsequently relocated to a gothic-style residence in the South of France. "The two grew so close that they had their own side project called The Creatures. And in 1991, they got married and moved into an appropriately gothic place, a castle-type thing in the South of France." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Dhh5_PDhd0
