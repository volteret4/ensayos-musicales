## curiosities
**102.1 New Music Search Winners** : The band Head, featuring Noah Mints, achieved significant recognition by winning the 102.1 New Music Search competition. They notably outperformed other prominent Canadian bands such as Hayden and Lowest of the Low. Their distinct style of writing very personal songs was a major influence on Ash. "they had won the new music search, the one, the one of the, one of the two point one new music search they beat Hayden, maybe the lowest of the low." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
**International Touring and Label Bankruptcy** : The band signed with IRS Records, a label also known for R.E.M., and embarked on international tours. However, their label's bankruptcy led to the band's decline and eventual breakup a couple of years later. "They landed a deal with IRS records, the same label that gave us REM, and they also toured internationally. But then IRS went bankrupt and the band started to founder and after a couple of more years they broke up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Opening for Stone Temple Pilots** : Early in their career in the 1990s, Head played a club show in Toronto as an opening act for the then-unknown American band, Stone Temple Pilots. "And remember seeing them play a club show in Toronto in the opening act with some unknown American band called Stone Temple Pilots." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
