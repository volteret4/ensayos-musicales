# artist - Cerrone

## curiosities
**European Disco Rhythmic Foundation** : Cerrone is cited as an example of European disco artists whose music was characterized by strong, electronic rhythmic bases, which formed a crucial pillar for the foundation of techno music. **Influence on US Electronic Music** : Cerrone's work, along alongside Giorgio Moroder, significantly influenced Afrika Bambaataa's instrumental developments and mixtapes, helping to bring European disco elements to the US and lay the foundation for Detroit Techno and Chicago House.
