# artist - Geoff Barrow

## member of
- Massive Attack
- Portishead

## curiosities
**Accumulating Film Soundtracks and Samples** : Applying his acquired knowledge, Barrow began actively collecting film soundtracks and sampling their rhythms and textures, which would become a cornerstone of his future work. "Me empezó a comular mandas sonoras y samplear sus límicas y texturas." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Dislike for "Trip Hop" Label** : Geoff Barrow, along with Tricky and 3D, expressed a strong dislike for the "Trip Hop" categorization of their music. "Jo Farrow, Tricky o 3D, detestaban la tiqueta TripHop." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Early Studio Work on Blue Lines** : Geoff Barrow worked in the studios where Massive Attack recorded *Blue Lines*, though not in a prominent role; he primarily changed reels and connected cables. "Yo varro a trabajar con los estudios donde se grabó blue lines." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Early Work with Massive Attack** : Geoff Barrow worked with Massive Attack on their seminal album *Blue Lines* and also contributed to parts of *Protection* before later founding the band Portishead. "Jeff Barrow que después montaría portis jez que trabajó con ellos simplemente en blue lines y parte en el protection." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack - Blue Lines. **Founded Portishead** : Barrow was so profoundly amazed by the sound being created in the studio during the *Blue Lines* sessions that he was inspired to found Portishead shortly thereafter. "Jeop Barrow quedó tan alucinado con el sonido que se estaba gestando en ese estudio, que fundó Portishead un poco más adelante." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack y El Origen del Trip Hop en Bristol. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bpOr4KKm5rM&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← geoff-barrow ← geoff-barrow
**Observational Learning from Massive Attack** : Despite his minor role, Barrow meticulously observed Massive Attack's creative process, noting how they manipulated tempos, equalized bass, and transformed vinyl fragments into intricate sound textures. "Tuvo una sinta de primera fila para ver cómo trabajaban masifatac, cómo jugaban con los tempos, cómo cualizaban los bajos y cómo convertían fragmentos de vinilos en texturas sonoras." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Production Assistant during Blue Lines** : Geoff Barrow was a young and exceptionally talented production assistant during the recording of Massive Attack's *Blue Lines*. At the time, he was also a musician just beginning his career.
