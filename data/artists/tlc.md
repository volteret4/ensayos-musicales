# artist - TLC

## songs
**Waterfalls (1995)** : This song was the winner of the 1995 Billboard Song of the Summer. Although the Atlanta Girl Group trio may have recorded songs considered superior, this hit, with seven weeks at number one, became their longest-running and signature track. It is remembered for both its "beachy music video" and its "storytelling cautionary lyrics." "The song that dominated the summer of 1995 was TLC's Waterfalls." ← Hit Parade Music History and Music Trivia > Song(s) of the Summer Edition Part 2 | https://shows.acast.com/hit-parade/episodes/695d4ef50c30a1408dc292e4

## curiosities
**"No Scrubs" Cover on Teal Album** : TLC's song "No Scrubs" was covered on Weezer's Teal Album, marking the only exception to the album's rule of covering songs released before 1994, because the band simply thought it was "cool." "TLC's No Scrups. It made the cut because the band decided that it was, well, it was just cool." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM ← tlc
**1999 Singles Chart Dominance** : TLC was a prominent act on the singles charts in 1999, reflecting their continued success and influence in popular music.
