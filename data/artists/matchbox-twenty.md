## songs
**Bent (2000)** : This song was the winner of the 2000 Billboard Song of the Summer. It is described as a "fairly forgettable rocker" that benefited from lead singer Rob Thomas's higher profile after he co-wrote and sang the Santana smash "Smooth." ## curiosities
**First-Person Lyrical Approach** : Matchbox Twenty was cited as an example of a Post-Grunge band that utilized a "first person approach" in their lyrics, a characteristic that helped define the genre's move towards more direct and accessible songwriting.
