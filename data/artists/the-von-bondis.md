## curiosities
**Detroit Origins and White Stripes Connection** : The Von Bondis were also from Detroit and had a fluctuating relationship with The White Stripes, sometimes as friends, rivals, or enemies. The band's main members were Jason Stolstheimer, a bartender, and Marcy Bowen, who worked in a hair salon. They started as The Baby Killers in 1997, changing their name to The Von Bondis by 2000, and were signed by Sympathy for the Record Industry, similar to The White Stripes. "They were also from Detroit, and were alternately friends and rivals and enemies of the White Stripes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
**UK Success and Jack White Co-Production** : Like The White Stripes and The Strokes, The Von Bondis initially found significant attention in the UK. They toured with The White Stripes and other young Garage Rock bands, and Jack White notably co-produced their first album, "Lack of Communication." "It just like the Stripes in the Strokes, the first territory to pay close attention was the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
