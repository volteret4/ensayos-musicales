## curiosities
**Kurt Cobain's Acknowledged Influence** : The Raincoats were an all-female post-punk band often named by Kurt Cobain as a significant influence, making them an initial inspiration for the Riot Grrrl movement. Their work was cited alongside other pioneering female artists in punk and post-punk. "They were the raincoats, an all-female post-punk band that were often named Jack by Kurt Cobain, Kim Gordon of Sonic Youth, goth queen Suzy Su, Chrissy Hine to the pretenders, Exine Servenka of the LA punk band X." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qss5kC2FUx0
