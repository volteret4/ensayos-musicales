## curiosities
**Kurt Cobain's Acknowledged Influence** : The Raincoats were an all-female post-punk band often named by Kurt Cobain as a significant influence, making them an initial inspiration for the Riot Grrrl movement. Their work was cited alongside other pioneering female artists in punk and post-punk.
