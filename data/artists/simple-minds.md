# artist - Simple Minds

## songs
**Don't You (Forget About Me)** : This song is noted for being loaded with vocables, particularly at its conclusion, featuring many "la la la" sounds. The use of these singing placeholders is considered perfect for the song. "Simple minds taking us out of don't you forget about me, with many la la la, the singing placeholder known as a vocable." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjwRApoJhA
