## albums
**Doors and Windows (Multimedia CD-i Disc)** : This release by the Crenbury's was a CDI (Compact Disc Interactive) disc, containing both music and multimedia content. It was designed to be playable in both computers and CD players, though users were advised to turn down their CD players for Track 1 as it could produce ear-piercing static. Attempts to rip the disc to a computer were reportedly blocked by the technology. "A Crenbury's disc called Doors and Windows. This is a CDI disc, meaning that it contains all sorts of multimedia content as well as music. This one could be played in a computer and a CD player. But you need to turn down your CD player for Track 1 because it just might read it as ear piercing static." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BHkJFA0LMro ← crenburys

## curiosities
**Interactive Multimedia Album Release (September 26, 1990s)** : "Doors and Windows" was announced by Island Records and Philips Media as a technological breakthrough in multimedia entertainment. This CDI disc offered fans an intimate, interactive look at the band, featuring multimedia content alongside music, and was set to arrive in stores worldwide on September 26th. "Island Records and Philips Media announced a technological breakthrough in multimedia entertainment. Doors and Windows, an intimate, interactive look at one of the most successful new groups in music today. Arriving in stores worldwide on September 26th, Doors and Windows offers a unique opportunity for fans to get better acquainted with the Crenbury." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BHkJFA0LMro ← crenburys
