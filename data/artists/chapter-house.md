# artist - Chapter House

## genres
- Shoegaze

## curiosities
**Influence on Album Title** : This English group provided Radiohead with a Jerky Boys tape, which ultimately inspired the title "Pablo Honey" for Radiohead's debut album. "Radiohead was given a jerky boys tape by another English group called Chapter House." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**Shoegaze Band Example** : Chapter House was mentioned as an example of a Shoegaze band, a genre characterized by lots of distortion, feedback, buried vocals, and a disarming amount of melody, often described as immersive, semi-psychedelic, and dreamy. "There were bands like Slow Dive, Chapter House, Curve, Lush, My Bloody Valentine, Adorable, The Catherine Wheel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
