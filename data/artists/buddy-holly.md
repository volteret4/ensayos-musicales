## curiosities
**Iconic Stratocaster Use** : Buddy Holly is frequently depicted holding a Fender Stratocaster in photographs, indicating his prominent use of the instrument and its association with his image. "Pretty much every picture I've seen a buddy Holly holding a guitar as him holding a Strat." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
**Plane Crash Death (1957)** : On February 3, 1957, a day famously known as "the day the music died," Buddy Holly was killed when the small plane he was a passenger in went down in a cornfield outside of Mason City, Iowa. He died alongside fellow musicians Richie Valens and The Big Bopper. "February 3rd, 1957 has gone down in history as the day the music died." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
