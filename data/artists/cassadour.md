## curiosities
**Kingston Origins and Family Ties** : Cassadour is a band from Kingston, Ontario, whose members met at Queen's University and began performing gigs locally. The band includes Boris Baker, son of Rob Baker (guitarist for The Tragically Hip), as their bass player. For a period, Angus Fay, nephew of The Tragically Hip's drummer Johnny Fay, was the group's drummer. "Cassadour, her bandit of Kingston, Ontario. Members met at Queen's University and started playing gigs around town. To complete things, note that the bass player is Boris Baker, the son of tragically hip guitars, Rob Baker. And for a while, the group's drummer was Angus Fay, nephew of hip drummer Johnny Fay." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
