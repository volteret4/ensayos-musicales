## curiosities
**Co-Founder of Crabwork** : Florian Schneider, together with Ralph Hütter, founded Crabwork as a personal project. Their musical partnership began before 1970, with documented recordings from that year showing them performing as a duo, forming the original core of the band. **Obsession with Kling Klang Studio** : Florian Schneider, alongside Ralph Hütter, developed an intense, almost unhealthy obsession with the Kling Klang Studio. Their dedication led them to virtually live in the studio, consuming meals and sleeping there, and it was widely observed that the studio had "dominated their personality" to a "sickly" degree.
