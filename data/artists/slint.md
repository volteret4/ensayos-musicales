## albums
**Spiderland (1991)** : This album is considered legendary among post-rock fans and laid key foundations for the post-rock approach to soundmaking, finding an audience despite the band having broken up by its release. "There were only two Slint albums, but the one that every post-rock fan talks about is Spiderland, which was released in 1991." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY

## curiosities
**Billy Corgan's Admiration** : Billy Corgan of The Smashing Pumpkins highly admired Slint; he gushed about them for almost 15 minutes in a conversation, and considered being in a band with a Slint member "a dream come true." "In fact, when Billy Corrigan broke up the smashing pumpkins, he recruited Slint guitarist David Peio for this short-lived band called Zwan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
**Formation and Influence (1986-1994)** : Formed in Louisville, Kentucky, in 1986, Slint spent eight years laying key foundations for the entire post-rock approach to sound-making and is considered legendary in this musical world. "Slint was formed in 1986, and over eight years laid some of the key foundations of the whole post-rock approach to soundmaking." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
**Influence on The Smashing Pumpkins** : Slint's sound had an obvious influence on The Smashing Pumpkins, evident when playing songs from both bands back-to-back. "We heard how slint influenced the smashing pumpkins, and that influence is pretty obvious when you play songs from both bands back to back." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
**Recommended Post-Rock Band** : The band is explicitly listed as a recommendation for those wanting to explore post-rock further. "If you want to go deeper, look into bands like Talk Talk, Slint, Barxide Coses, Tortoise, Magwai, Stereolab, Godspeed you, Black Amper, we always keep coming back to them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
