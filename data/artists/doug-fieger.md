# artist - Doug Fieger

## curiosities
**"My Sharona" Lyrical Perspective (2012)** : In 2012, Doug Fieger told Rolling Stone that "My Sharona" was written from the perspective of a 14-year-old boy, despite him being 25 and Sharona being 17 at the time of its inspiration. "Sharona was 17. I was 25 when I wrote that song. But the song was written from the perspective of a 14-year-old boy. **Relationship with Sharona Alperin** : Doug Fieger and Sharona Alperin toured together for a couple of years, during which time Sharona described their relationship as "magical." However, she eventually ended the romantic relationship when she was 21 due to Fieger's possessiveness, though they remained friends until his death in 2012.
