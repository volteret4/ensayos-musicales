# artist - Pythagoras

## curiosities
**Architect of Mathematical Music Theory (c. 570 BC)** : A Greek mathematician who used math and science to organize and define musical sounds, effectively distinguishing melody from noise. He is considered foundational to music history for creating a system to define how notes relate to one another. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
**Pythagorean Tuning and Ratios** : Pythagoras developed a tuning system based on mathematical ratios, such as 3:2, derived from experiments with strings. He discovered that a string half the length of another produces a pitch exactly double (an octave), a discovery that dictated Western scales, instrument tuning, and composition until the early 1500s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
