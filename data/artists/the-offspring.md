**Smash (1994) – Biggest Selling Independent Album** : Released on April 15th, 1994, on the independent label Epitaph, this album initially received little fanfare. It first gained traction among skateboarders and snowboarders. Through a stroke of luck, its first single was picked up by an LA radio station, an uncommon occurrence for Epitaph bands, and then spread to other stations. The label invested $5,000 for a music video, which MTV subsequently began playing, causing the album's sales to skyrocket. "Smash," which was originally expected to sell 60,000 copies over its entire lifespan, began selling that many copies every couple of days. Ultimately, it sold over 12 million copies, making it the biggest selling independent record of all time. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
