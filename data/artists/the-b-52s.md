## curiosities
**"Dorky Bands" Category (Early 1980s)** : Identified as one of the "dorky bands" within the New Wave genre. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
**DIY Instrumentation and Image** : To achieve their specific sound, their guitarist sometimes replaced lower guitar strings with bass strings. Visually, they cultivated a unique look using 1950s thrift-shop clothing and bouffant hairdos, which inspired their name. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYsV3ozbk4M
**Formation and Philosophy** : The band formed over cocktails in a Chinese restaurant in Athens, Georgia, with the specific goal of countering the seriousness of rock music with silliness. They were a mixed-gender and multi-sexual band that rehearsed in the blood-letting room of an abandoned mortuary. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYsV3ozbk4M
**Impact on Kurt Cobain** : While initially dismissed by some as a novelty act, their 1980 performance on _Saturday Night Live_ reportedly transfixed a young Kurt Cobain. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYsV3ozbk4M
**Osama Bin Laden's Fandom** : Pictures of The B-52s were found on hard drives during the raid on Osama Bin Laden's compound in Pakistan, suggesting he was a fan of the band, prompting speculation about him dancing to songs like "Love Shack" or "Rock Lobster." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
**Ricky Wilson's Death from AIDS (October 12, 1985)** : Ricky Wilson, the guitarist for The B-52s, became the first notable musician to die from AIDS on October 12, 1985. He had been experiencing illness since 1983 and was very sick for most of 1985, suffering from Kaposi's Sarcoma, infections, and pneumonia. Due to the significant stigma surrounding AIDS patients and gay men, Ricky kept his condition a secret, even from his sister and bandmate, Cindy Wilson, who only learned of his diagnosis three days before his death. He passed away at the age of 32 at Sloan Kettering Hospital in New York and was buried in Okeneh Hill Cemetery in Athens, Georgia. His tombstone bears the inscription, "The Breeze of Grace is always blowing. Set your sail to catch that breeze." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Somq2MoRi5Q
