**Ricky Wilson's AIDS-Related Illness and Death (October 12, 1985)** : Ricky Wilson, the guitarist for The B-52s, became the first notable musician to die from AIDS on October 12, 1985, just nine days after actor Rock Hudson's passing. He had been ill since 1983 and experienced severe health issues throughout most of 1985, including Kaposi's sarcoma, infections, and pneumonia. Due to the pervasive stigma associated with AIDS and gay men at the time, Wilson kept his condition private, with his sister and bandmate, Cindy Wilson, only learning of his diagnosis three days before his death. He passed away at Sloan Kettering Hospital in New York at the age of 32. "Ricky Wilson became the first notable musician to die of AIDS." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Somq2MoRi5Q
