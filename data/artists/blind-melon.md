## curiosities
**Band Name Origin** : The name "Blind Melon" was chosen either from a Cheech and Chong skit or from a term guitarist Roger Smith's father used to describe neighborhood stoners. "Blind Millen was chosen as their name, from either a Cheach and Chong Skip, or from a term guitarist Roger Smith's father used for neighborhood stoners." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8
**Connection to Guns n' Roses** : Shannon Hoon's sister was friends with Axl Rose during high school in Indiana, which led to Shannon singing backup vocals on a couple of Guns n' Roses songs and appearing in the "Don't Cry" video. "Shannon ended up in the Guns and Roses orbit because his sister had been friends with Axel Rose back in high school in Indiana." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8
**Drug Counselor on Tour** : Due to Shannon Hoon's worsening drug problems and the birth of his daughter, a drug counselor was assigned to accompany him on tour to monitor his well-being. "It was agreed that a drug counselor would accompany Shannon on the next tour." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8
**Extensive Touring Schedule (1995)** : Blind Melon undertook tours with prominent artists such as Neil Young, Lenny Kravitz, and The Rolling Stones, indicative of their rising profile in the mid-1990s. This touring activity, however, also occurred during a period where frontman Shannon Hoon was struggling with severe drug and alcohol issues. "There were tours with Neil Young and Lenny Kravitz in the Rolling Stones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
**Fatal Drug Binge (October 1995)** : On October 20, 1995, after a problematic show at Numbers club in Houston, Shannon Hoon engaged in an all-night drug binge and had to be coerced onto the tour bus for the 350-mile drive to New Orleans. He continued using cocaine throughout the night on the bus and, immediately upon arrival in New Orleans, left to acquire more. Early on October 21st, he crept back to a bunk on the bus. Around noon, sound tech Lyle Eves found him unresponsive, and an ambulance was called, but it was too late. "Shannon had an all-night drug binge and eventually had to be corral for the tour bus ride to the next show in New Orleans." "He was unresponsive. An ambulance was called, but it was too late." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8
**Naked Urination on Vancouver Audience – October 31, 1993, Pacific Coliseum** : On Halloween 1993, Blind Melon were supporting Lenny Kravitz at a sold-out Pacific Coliseum in Vancouver (13,000 people). Singer Shannon Hoon stripped naked for the final three songs, then urinated on the audience. The band attempted to escape on their tour bus, but police cruisers blocked the exit. Hoon climbed onto the roof of the bus and began screaming at the officers. It took 15 minutes to talk him down; he was then arrested and charged with committing an indecent act. The promoter spent the following weeks apologizing to those who had been urinated upon. "Which kind of gives a whole new meaning to the title of this song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fMVGuHjJp9U
**Shannon Hoon's Overdose Death (1995)** : Lead singer Shannon Hoon died on October 21, 1995, from a massive cocaine overdose on the band's tour bus. The bus was parked in a parking lot outside the Hotel Intercontinental in downtown New Orleans. The night before, Blind Melon had played a "bad show" in Houston, with Hoon "buzzed on Coke" for most of the gig, and he continued using his stash on the bus to New Orleans. He was last seen around 10 AM after having breakfast, before creeping back into guitarist Christopher Thorne's bunk, where he was found unresponsive shortly after noon. "The official coroner's verdict? Heart failure brought about by a massive cocaine overdose. The date was October 21, 1995." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgN0vBIugLc
