# artist - Aretha Franklin

## curiosities
**Backstage Rider – Pre-Show Cash Demand** : In addition to canapés and ginger ale, Aretha Franklin's rider included a demand for $25,000 in cash, in addition to her usual performance fee, which had to be provided before she went on stage. She often kept this purse, containing the cash, on top of her piano in full view to keep an eye on it. "She demanded $25,000 in cash. This is in addition to her usual performance fee. And she had to have it before she went on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z2hvUDtBxho

**Death in 2018** : Aretha Franklin was one of the iconic musicians who passed away in 2018. "Aretha Franklin." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sdPeii-8Ysw
**Recording at Fame** : Aretha Franklin recorded at Fame Recording Studios, known for being a place where black artists could work undisturbed during a period of significant racism and discrimination. "Wilson Pickett, Aretha Franklin, Edda James. They all recorded there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk ← aretha-franklin
**Recording at Muscle Shoals** : Aretha Franklin also recorded at Muscle Shoals Sound Studio. "More Rolling Stones, more Aretha Franklin, Paul Simon, Leonard Skinnerd." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk ← aretha-franklin
