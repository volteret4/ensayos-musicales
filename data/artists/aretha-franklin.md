## curiosities
**Backstage Rider – Pre-Show Cash Demand** : In addition to canapés and ginger ale, Aretha Franklin's rider included a demand for $25,000 in cash, in addition to her usual performance fee, which had to be provided before she went on stage. She often kept this purse, containing the cash, on top of her piano in full view to keep an eye on it. "She demanded $25,000 in cash. This is in addition to her usual performance fee.
