# artist - Matt Rangweacher

## curiosities
**Collaboration on First 36-step Sequencer** : Matt Rangweacher is identified as a German sound company that collaborated with Crabwork in the construction of the first 36-step sequencer ever created. This project was so significant that Ralph Hütter mortgaged his house to fund its development.
