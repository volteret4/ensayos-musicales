## songs
**Jamaica Mistaica** : A song written by Jimmy Buffett about the January 16, 1996 incident where his plane carrying Bono was shot at by Jamaican police. The chorus includes the lines: "come back, come back, back to Jamaica. Don't you know we made a big mistake? We'd be so sad if you told us goodbye and we promised not to shoot you out of the sky." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
**Jamaica Mistaica (1996)** : He wrote this song about the January 16, 1996, incident at Negril Airport, Jamaica, where police mistakenly opened fire on his Grumman H.U. 16 C plane "Hemisphere Dancer" with Bono and others onboard. The chorus includes the lines: "Come back, come back, back to Jamaica. Don't you know we made a big mistake? We'd be so sad if you told us goodbye and we promised not to shoot you out of the sky." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
