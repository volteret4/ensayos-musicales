# artist - Nitty Gritty Dirt Band

## genres
- Soft Rock

## concerts
- **1977 Soviet Union Tour** : The Nitty Gritty Dirt Band was selected over the Doobie Brothers and the soft rock band America to become the first American band to tour the Soviet Union in 1977. This tour, championed by President Jimmy Carter who believed rock and roll could undermine communism, was a wild success among young people in Russia and various Soviet republics, with demand for tickets overwhelming the KGB. "In 1977, the nitty-gritty dirt band was chosen over the Dubie brothers, and the soft rock band America, must have been their name, to be the first American band to tour through the Soviet Union."

## curiosities
**First American Band to Tour Soviet Union (1977)** : In 1977, the Nitty Gritty Dirt Band was chosen over more conventional acts like the Doobie Brothers and America to be the first American band to tour the Soviet Union. This tour, endorsed by President Jimmy Carter, aimed to undermine communism through rock and roll. The tour proved immensely successful among young people in Russia and other Soviet republics, generating such high demand for tickets that it overwhelmed the KGB.
