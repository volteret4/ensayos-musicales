# artist - The Weakerthans

## curiosities
**Mike DeAngeles' Favorite Band** : The Weakerthans, a band from Winnipeg, were Mike DeAngeles's favorite band when he met Max Kermann on the first day of university. Their album had just been released, and Mike had recently seen them in Hamilton, leading to his obsession with their music. "He said, yeah, I'm into this band. You probably haven't heard of them. They're from Winnipeg. They're called The Weaker Thens. And they were my favorite band at the time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
