**Fatherly Advice to Zach Starkey** : Ringo Starr gave his son, Zach Starkey, only one drum lesson and tried to dissuade him from a music career, suggesting he pursue a profession like a doctor or lawyer instead. "Dad gave him exactly one lesson before he tried to dissuade his son about getting into show business. Be a doctor, be a lawyer, don't be a musician. Yeah, that didn't work." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk

**Japanese Applesauce Commercial (1996)** : In 1996, Ringo Starr traveled to Japan to participate in an advertisement for a brand of applesauce. The anecdote suggests that "Ringo" reportedly means "applesauce" in Japanese. "In 1996, Ringo Star went to Japan to take part in an ad for a brand of applesauce. By applesauce, apparently that's what Ringo means in Japanese." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
