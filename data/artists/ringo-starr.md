# artist - Ringo Starr

## curiosities
**Attempt to Dissuade Son from Music** : Ringo gave Zach only one drum lesson and tried to dissuade him from pursuing a career in show business, advising him to be a doctor or lawyer instead of a musician.
**Father of Zach Starky** : Ringo Starr is the father of drummer Zach Starky.
**Godfather of Zach Starky** : Keith Moon of The Who was Zach Starky's godfather.
