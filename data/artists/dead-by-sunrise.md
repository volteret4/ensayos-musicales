## albums
**Out of Ashes (2009) - Debut Album** : The debut album for Dead by Sunrise was released in 2009. The group was formed by Chester Bennington to release songs he had written that were not stylistically suitable for Linkin Park. "A Dead by Sunrise album would eventually come out in 2009." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIlCaKCvylI

## songs
**The Morning After** : This was an early song written by Dead by Sunrise. It was explicitly noted as "definitely not Lincoln Park material, but certainly very Chester," showcasing its distinct sound compared to his main band. "It's called The Morning After. Chester Bennington with Dead by Sunrise in a song called The Morning After." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIlCaKCvylI

## curiosities
**Chester's Addiction Battle (2017)** : In June 2017, Chester Bennington confided in his friend Ryan Schuck about his struggles related to this side project, revealing that he was enduring "an hour by hour battle with addiction" just months before his death. "But in June 2017, he did tell his friend Ryan Schuck of his side project, Dead by Sunrise, and he was suffering, and this is a quote, an hour by hour battle with addiction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qQm7u3W13eM
**Origin as Snow White Tan** : Chester Bennington's side project was initially known as "Snow White Tan," a name that was recognized as a possible reference to David Bowie and his Ziggy Stardust persona, before eventually changing to Dead by Sunrise. "his own side project, which was first called Snow White Tan. Notice the reference to Bowie and Zee Stardust, and then Dead by Sunrise." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIlCaKCvylI
