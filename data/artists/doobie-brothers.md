# artist - Doobie Brothers

## curiosities
**Considered for Soviet Tour** : The Doobie Brothers were considered as potential candidates for the first American band to tour the Soviet Union in 1977 but were ultimately passed over in favor of the Nitty Gritty Dirt Band. This initiative was part of a US effort to use rock and roll to undermine communism through cultural exchange.
