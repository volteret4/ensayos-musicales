# artist - Frankie Goes to Hollywood

## instruments

## songs
**Relax (1984)** : The distinct sound on this song would not have been possible without the use of a Fairlight Computer Musical Instrument. The snare drum sound in the song is a sample from a Led Zeppelin record; when the drummer hit the snare in the studio, it triggered this manipulated sample in the Fairlight. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
