**Two Tribes (1984) – Cold War Protest Song; Video Featured Reagan vs. Chernenko Wrestling Match** : "Two Tribes" was one of the most popular anti-war protest songs of the 1980s. The video featured a wrestling match between Ronald Reagan and Soviet leader Constantine Chernenko, and it was released at a moment when the US and USSR had come dangerously close to nuclear war. In 1983 alone: NORAD ran a training tape on Soviet attack on the live systems causing a false alarm; a Soviet fighter shot down Korean Air Lines Flight 007; a Soviet early warning system falsely detected multiple US ICBM launches, with one submarine commander — Lieutenant Colonel Stanislav Petrov — unilaterally deciding not to launch a retaliatory strike.
