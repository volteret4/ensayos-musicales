## curiosities
**Alleged Saxophone Playing** : It is claimed that he played the saxophone, and another story suggests he jumped up and down on the spot while doing so. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Associate of Billy Idol** : Billy Idol (William Broad) used to hang around with Sid Vicious. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Pogo Dance Originator (Legend, 1970s)** : Often cited as the inventor of the Pogo dance during the original punk rock days in the UK in the 1970s. Legends suggest he started it either as a way of mocking non-punks at Sex Pistols shows or because a tight leather poncho restricted his dance movements to just jumping up and down. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
