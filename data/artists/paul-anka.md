# artist - Paul Anka

## curiosities
**Flow to America** : Paul Anka was among the ambitious and talented Canadian musicians who flowed south to seek their fortune in America. This was a common trend when domestic acceptance and popularity for Canadian artists often only came after achieving success in the US.
