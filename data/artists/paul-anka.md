# artist - Paul Anka

## curiosities
**Flow to America** : Paul Anka was among the ambitious and talented Canadian musicians who flowed south to seek their fortune in America. This was a common trend when domestic acceptance and popularity for Canadian artists often only came after achieving success in the US. "ambitious, talented Canadian musicians flowed south to seek their fortune in America." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
**Pre-1970 Exodus to US** : Paul Anka, a promising Canadian artist before 1970, moved to the United States because the nascent Canadian music industry offered limited opportunities to make a living. "Neil Young, Jody Mitchell, Paul Anke, Leonard Cohen, all of our biggest and most promising artists beat a path for the United States." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2kYleeMnl8 ← paul-anka
