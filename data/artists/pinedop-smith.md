## curiosities
**First Musician Shot – Chicago Dance Hall** : Pinedop Smith, a Boogie-Woogie piano player from Chicago, was the first musician known to have been shot. In 1929, just as he was about to enter a recording session, he was fatally shot during a fight at a dance hall. It is speculated that he might not have been the intended victim of the shooting.
