# artist - System of a Down

## songs
**BYOB (2005) – Anti-Iraq War Protest Song; Stands for "Bring Your Own Bombs"** : "BYOB" from the 2005 album Mezmerize was inspired by the March 2003 invasion of Iraq in search of weapons of mass destruction that never existed. System of a Down has been very active in criticizing war, especially with regard to the plight of their Armenian people and the Armenian Genocide. "BYOB, which in case you didn't know, stands for Bring Your Own Bombs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YsNRNKvd1GI
