## instruments

## curiosities
**Pioneer of Double Bass Drums on Record** : Louis Bellson made history in 1952 by being the first drummer to use a drum kit equipped with double bass drums on a recording. "Then there was another drummer named Louis Belson, who in 1952 made the first recording using a kit with double bass drums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WL3zEWJJNuw
