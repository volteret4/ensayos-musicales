# artist - David Myers

## curiosities
**"Art Context" Podcast Guest** : He was the debut guest on the "Art Context" podcast. During his appearance, he discussed the "philosophy behind his approach to art," his extensive body of work, and "larger world issues," including Black Lives Matter. He also offered his perspective as a "white director coming up in black music world." "It was talking about the philosophy behind his approach to art, the work he's done, and as well digging into some of the larger world issues out there. Like we have a whole talk about Black Lives Matter on that podcast. And being a white director, it is perspective coming up in black music world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUQ2vZzmRiI ← david-myers
