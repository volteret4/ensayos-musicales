## curiosities
**Connection to "Never Say Never Again" Theme (1983)** : Herb Alpert, co-founder of A&M Records, is connected to the "Never Say Never Again" theme through his marriage to Lani Hall, who sang the song. This familial tie links a major record label figure to one of the less-remembered Bond themes. "She was married to her Belpert, the A and A and M records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo ← herb-alpert
**Encounter with Gino Vannelli** : Herb Alpert, at A&M Records, was directly approached by Gino Vannelli, who audaciously bypassed security to plead for a chance in music. Alpert responded by giving Vannelli the opportunity he sought. "Herb Alpert turned around and said, hey, okay, what is it? And he said, just give me a chance. And he did. Herb Alpert gave Gina Vannelly the chance that he was looking for." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 45 - Oh, Canada! Canada's Rock Music Industry in the 70s | https://www.ftr70.com/ ← herb-alpert
