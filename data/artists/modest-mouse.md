**Isaac Brock's Difficult Youth** : Isaac Brock, the frontman, experienced a turbulent childhood, including being homeschooled for a period. His mother left his father for his father's brother, and their house subsequently flooded, leading her to move into her husband's trailer. With no space for Isaac, he stayed on the second floor of the flooded house until authorities intervened. "Family life was a little messed up for Isaac. He was homeschooled for a while but then his mum left his father for his father's brother than the house flooded which meant that she had to move into her husband's trailer. But there was no space in the trailer for Isaac so he stayed at the flooded out house on the second floor until the cops made a move." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
