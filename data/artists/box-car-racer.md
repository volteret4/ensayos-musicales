## members

## genres
- Punk

## albums
**Box Car Racer (2002) – Self-Titled Concept Album** : Released on May 21st, 2002, this was Tom DeLonge's side project, initially conceived to satisfy his desire to play dirtier, more aggressive punk while recovering from back surgery. It was not intended to be a real band. The album is a concept album that explored themes of the end of the world, conspiracies, religion, the apocalypse, and the Masons. It started acoustically but became louder and more electric during its creation. Produced by Jerry Finn, who also worked with Blink 182. "They released an album, a concept album, that dealt with the end of the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0

## curiosities
**Hidden CD Artwork Messages** : The CD artwork for the "Box Car Racer" album contains hidden messages. For example, a sequence "LNW-1301" is the longitudinal location of the Twin Towers/World Trade Center. "If you have a copy of the CD, there are all kinds of hidden messages in the artwork." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0
**Name Origin** : The name "Box Car Racer" was shared with a previous band Travis Barker was in before Blink 182. The common story that it alluded to the B-52 bomber that dropped the atomic bomb on Nagasaki is incorrect; its true origin is Travis's old band. "The real origin goes back to Travis's old band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0
**Origin and Purpose** : Box Car Racer was Tom DeLonge's project, driven by his need to play more aggressive punk music and to occupy himself while recovering from back surgery. He did not envision it as a permanent band. "This was Tom's product and his chance to exercise his need to play punk that was dirtier and more aggressive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0
**Travis Barker's Involvement** : Travis Barker joined Tom DeLonge on drums for Box Car Racer, a decision that deeply upset Mark Hoppus of Blink 182, who felt betrayed. Travis only sang on a couple of tracks. "But then he asked Travis to be his drummer, which was okay for Travis, but it greatly pissed off Mark." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0
