**Don't Worry Baby (January 1964 / Released March 1964)** : Recorded in January 1964, this song was first released on the "Shutdown Volume 2" album in March, and then famously as the B-side to "I Get Around". The girlfriend's role as a supportive, "supernaturally good woman" who understands his fears speaks to male insecurity and the inability to live up to traditional masculine roles, providing an emotional counterpoint to the bravado of the A-side, "I Get Around."
