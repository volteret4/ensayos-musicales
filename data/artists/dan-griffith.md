## curiosities
**Education and Entry into Arkells** : Dan Griffith attended McMaster University with Max Kermann, Mike DeAngeles, and Nick Dica. He joined Arkells a little after the initial trio started jamming, becoming their keyboard player until he left to go back to school. "So Dan went to Mac with us. Okay. And he joined the band a little after the three of us started jamming and with our original drummer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA ← dan-griffith
**Involvement with The Surley Young Bucks** : Dan Griffith was also involved with another band called The Surley Young Bucks, which was described as a half-original, half-cover band playing country-styled music. He had a few songs within this group. "There was another band called the SIRLY Young Bucks, which everybody wants to go dig in and there's some stuff on the internet. It's kind of like a half band cover band. Like the band? The band. Like the band. Yeah. And kind of like just like country sort of styled music. And Dan had a few songs and there was some like put some original songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA ← dan-griffith
