## curiosities
**Band Longevity and Reunions** : The group remained active until 1995, briefly reformed in 2004, and has been continuously back together since 2008, maintaining their blend of punk rock and laugh-out-loud satire. "The group lasted until 1995, reformed briefly in 2004, and have been back together since 2008." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
**DIY Indie Beginnings** : The band developed as a strictly do-it-yourself project and an indie act, initially self-releasing a series of cassettes before putting out their debut album. "Like Camp R, they evolved as a strictly do-it-yourself project and as an indie act. **Philadelphia Origin (1983)** : The Dead Milkmen formed in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, around 1983, at roughly the same time Camper Van Beethoven was starting. "They were formed in Philadelphia around the same time as Camp Riven Beethoven was starting in Pennsylvania in 1983." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
**Satirical Hardcore Punk** : While some classified them as a comedy act, The Dead Milkmen were more accurately described as satirical. They were rooted in the local hardcore scene but distinguished themselves by incorporating a sense of humor, offering a refreshing contrast to the often serious and dour American hardcore style. "The Dead Milkman were far more punk-leaning. They were part of the local hardcore scene, but differentiated themselves by showing a sense of humor." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
