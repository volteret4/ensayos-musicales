**Lighting Roadie Turned Band Member** : In 1976, while working as a lighting roadie for The Doobie Brothers, Bobby LaKind was observed playing congas on stage. The band was impressed by his skills and invited him to assist in the studio. His contributions were so successful that he became a full member of the band in 1979.
