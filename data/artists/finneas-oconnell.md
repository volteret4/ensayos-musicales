# artist - Finneas O'Connell

## songs
**No Time to Die (2020) – Co-writer and Producer** : Finneas O'Connell co-wrote and recorded the theme song for "No Time to Die" with his sister, Billie Eilish. They composed and recorded the track over three days on a tour bus in late 2019, with a full 70-piece orchestra added in England. His collaboration with Eilish resulted in her becoming the youngest ever Bond theme artist and the song winning a Grammy.
