## songs
**Still Loving You** : This song is cited as a prime example of a ballad found on metal albums, illustrating the "twin musical threads of sonic aggression and acoustic intimacy" within the genre. Such ballads were strategically used to slow the pace, engage the audience, and by contrast, intensify the impact of the louder sections.
