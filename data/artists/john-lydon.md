# artist - John Lydon

## member of
- Sex Pistols

## labels
- Pan Am (airline involved in incident)

## songs
**Death Disco (1988)** : This Public Image Limited song is highlighted due to John Lydon's near-death experience in the Lockerbie bombing. He and his wife, Nora, were supposed to be on Pan Am Flight 103 on December 21, 1988, but missed it by minutes after his wife took too long to pack, leading to a fight and traffic delays to Heathrow. "This gives his excuse to play public image limited's Death Disco, president." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg

## curiosities
**Declined Plastic Beach Participation** : John Lydon was approached to contribute to the Gorillaz album "Plastic Beach" but declined, stating he had "better things to do." "Then again, Johnny Liden and Tom Weitz also said they had better things to do." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Near-Miss Lockerbie Bombing (1988)** : John Lydon, of the Sex Pistols and Public Image Limited, came very close to dying in the bombing of Pan Am Flight 103 over Lockerbie, Scotland, on December 21, 1988. He and his wife, Nora, had seats on the flight but missed it by minutes due to his wife taking too long to pack, which caused a big fight, and then getting stuck in traffic on the way to Heathrow Airport. "Johnny Leiden with public image limited and death disco. He came very, very close to dying in the Lockerbie bombing in December of 1988." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
