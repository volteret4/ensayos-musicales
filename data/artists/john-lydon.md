# artist - John Lydon

## curiosities
**Childhood Meningitis (1963)** : At age seven, Lydon contracted spinal meningitis, likely from playing in rat-infested puddles in North London. He fell into a coma for seven months and spent a full year in the hospital, which resulted in the loss of four years of his early memories. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Formation of the Rotten Persona** : The illness left Lydon with a permanent spinal curvature and a piercing stare, and it changed his personality into that of a fierce contrarian. These traits directly informed his "Johnny Rotten" identity and his role as the frontman for the Sex Pistols. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Sex Pistols Audition** : Lydon secured his position in the Sex Pistols at the "Sex" clothing shop on King's Road by singing Alice Cooper's "I'm 18" on the shop's jukebox. Malcolm McLaren was captivated by Lydon's DIY "I Hate Pink Floyd" T-shirt and his distinctive look. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
