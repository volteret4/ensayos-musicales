# artist - Bionic and the Wires

## curiosities
**Cross-Species Music Making with Fungi** : This Manchester-based group conducted experiments where they plugged mushrooms, fungi, and other plants into sensors that picked up their tiny electrical bleats. These signals were converted into MIDI language, translated into motor signals, and used to move robot arms, including one experiment where a mushroom served as the drummer. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
