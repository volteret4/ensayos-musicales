## curiosities
**Pioneer of Electronic Keyboards in Art Rock** : Jean-Michel Jarre was recognized as an art rock artist who enthusiastically adopted and integrated electronic keyboards into his musical output, contributing to the evolving sound of the genre. **Dreampop Influence** : Jean-Michel Jarre is cited as an influence for dreampop, contributing to its ambient and electronic characteristics and helping to shape its distinct, cleaner sound away from guitar-heavy distortion.
