## curiosities
**Co-Management with Kelly Curtis** : Alice in Chains were initially co-managed by Kelly Curtis. However, Susan Silver took over much of the day-to-day management when Kelly Curtis became preoccupied with his other, increasingly popular band, Mother Love Bone. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Discovery of Body and Estimated Death Date (April 19, 2002)** : An anonymous 911 call on Friday, April 19, 2002, led police to his condo. At 5:41 pm, police kicked in the door and found his body on the couch, surrounded by drug paraphernalia including syringes and crack pipes. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
**Extreme Physical Deterioration (Post-1996)** : His body became an emaciated shell, dropping to about 80 pounds. He suffered from rotted teeth, liver failure, constant vomiting, and loss of bowel control, using drugs primarily to alleviate dope sickness rather than to get high. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
**Favorite Recording Spot** : Robert Lang Studios north of Seattle was a favorite spot for Alice in Chains. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
**Isolation and Public Sightings (Post-1996)** : He was rarely seen, with drugs delivered to his door. His only public sightings were occasionally at a neighborhood bar called The Rainbow, where he would sit alone, eyes closed, and nod off without ordering or drinking anything. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
**Key Grunge Band** : contributed to Alt Rock coalescing around hard guitar rock with "riff-ready songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Last Performance and Hermit-Like Existence (July 3, 1996 - April 2002)** : Layne Staley gave his last performance with Alice in Chains on July 3, 1996, after which he withdrew from everyone, living a hermit-like existence in a condo on the 4500 block of 8th Avenue Northeast in Seattle's University District. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
**Record Deal Secured by Susan Silver** : Susan Silver was instrumental in securing Alice in Chains their record deal. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Sole Management by Susan Silver** : Susan Silver became Alice in Chains' sole manager after Kelly Curtis became fully committed to managing Pearl Jam, leaving him no time for Alice in Chains. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Susan Silver's Enduring Involvement** : Susan Silver remains involved with Alice in Chains, describing them as being "like brothers to me." She notes that they have collectively experienced both the greatest and worst moments in life. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Unrecognizable Remains and Cause of Death (April 2002)** : Staley's body was in such a state that it was unrecognizable, requiring 24 hours for positive identification by the King County Medical Office. The coroner estimated his time of death as April 5, 2002, which tragically coincided with the date Kurt Cobain had killed himself. The autopsy determined the cause of death was an overdose from a speedball (a mixture of cocaine and heroin). ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
