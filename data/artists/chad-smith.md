# artist - Chad Smith

- SOS All Stars

## instruments

## curiosities
**Biggest Drum Set Performance (2006)** : Chad Smith holds the Guinness Book of World Records title for performing with the biggest drum set, a monstrous 308-piece kit. This one-off performance in 2006 was for Pearl Drums, a manufacturer he endorses, and was done as a favor to a friend who owned a music store, rather than for touring. "Chad played a stupidly-large drum kit that consisted of 300 and 8 pieces." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zWNzlSxrj6s
**Catalyst for John Frusciante's Reunion** : Chad Smith played a pivotal role in initiating the discussion for John Frusciante's return to the Red Hot Chili Peppers. At Flea's wedding, Chad told John that he missed playing with him and suggested that he, John, and Flea should jam sometime. "Chad had said something to me when I saw him at Flea's wedding. And he was like, he misses playing with me and him and Flea should jam sometime." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Joining Red Hot Chili Peppers (December 1988)** : Chad Smith auditioned for the Red Hot Chili Peppers and secured the drummer position in December 1988, just weeks after John Frusciante joined as guitarist. This completed the lineup that would become known as the classic Red Hot Chili Peppers, debuting on "Mother's Milk" by early 1989. "And that's when Chad Smith auditioned and got the job in December just a few weeks after John signed up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Refusal to Stay at The Mansion (1991)** : During the recording of "Blood Sugar Sex Magik" at the historic mansion at 351 Laurel Canyon, Chad Smith was "allegedly too freaked out by the vibes of the place" and refused to stay there during the recording sessions, unlike the other three band members. "Chad Smith was allegedly too freaked out by the vibes of the place so he refused to stay there during sessions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
