## curiosities
**Arrest for Murder (Early January 2023)** : Fio Lendiel, also known as Milo Stone, was arrested in early January 2023 and charged with the murder of his girlfriend, Alex. Alex was last seen on December 6, 2023, and reported missing on December 12th after failing to board a scheduled trip to Hawaii. Her remains were later found in a park in Berkeley, California, and Lendiel's Toyota pickup was discovered in front of his house. "In early January 2023, he was arrested and charged with Alex's murder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Mike Patton's Original Band** : Before joining Faith No More in January 1989, Mike Patton was the singer for his own group, Mr. Bungle. He had met Faith No More members in 1986. "Mike was in his own group called Mr. Bungle, and had met Faith No More sometime in 1986." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vLZla5h4xKQ
