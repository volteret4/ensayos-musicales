## curiosities
**$100,000 Record Deal and Drug-Induced Insanity (Early 1970s)** : In the early 1970s, after receiving a $100,000 record deal advance, Iggy Pop checked into the Beverly Hills Hotel and consumed such vast quantities of cocaine, heroin, speed, weed, and angel dust that he became certifiably insane. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
**Early Career as a Drummer** : Started in the mid-1960s as the drummer for the Detroit band The Iguanas, playing gigs around Michigan and recording several songs. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Early Stage Diving (1969)** : Documented as stage diving with The Stooges as far back as 1969. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Foundations of Modern Punk Rock** : After his drumming career, he started writing songs, moved on to The Stooges, collaborated with David Bowie, and "took a lot of LSD," which collectively laid the groundwork for modern punk rock. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Godfather of Punk Reputation** : Known as the "Godfather of Punk." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Management by Main Man** : Iggy Pop was a client of Tony DeFries' management company, Main Man, during a period when many of its artists "orbited" around David Bowie. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Motown Session Drummer** : Hired locally by Motown acts like The Shangri-La's, The Temptations, and The Four Tops for live gigs, demonstrating an ability to pick up almost any song within seconds. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Neuropsychiatric Institute Confinement (Early 1970s)** : Following his drug-induced breakdown, Iggy Pop was found homeless and given the choice of jail or a mental institution; he chose to spend time in the Neuropsychiatric Institute in Los Angeles, where David Bowie was his only visitor. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
