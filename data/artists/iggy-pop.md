## albums
**Lust for Life (1977)** : Title track from his 1977 album.

## songs
**Lust for Life (1977)** : The drum beat for this song was inspired by the theme song of a German news program that Iggy Pop and David Bowie watched while living in Berlin, trying to "straighten out their lives."

## curiosities
**Early Career as a Drummer** : Started in the mid-1960s as the drummer for the Detroit band The Iguanas, playing gigs around Michigan and recording several songs.
**Early Stage Diving (1969)** : Documented as stage diving with The Stooges as far back as 1969.
**Foundations of Modern Punk Rock** : After his drumming career, he started writing songs, moved on to The Stooges, collaborated with David Bowie, and "took a lot of LSD," which collectively laid the groundwork for modern punk rock.
**Godfather of Punk Reputation** : Known as the "Godfather of Punk."
**Motown Session Drummer** : Hired locally by Motown acts like The Shangri-La's, The Temptations, and The Four Tops for live gigs, demonstrating an ability to pick up almost any song within seconds.
