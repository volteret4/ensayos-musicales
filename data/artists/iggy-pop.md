# artist - Iggy Pop

## curiosities
**Move to West Berlin with David Bowie (Post-1976)** : Iggy Pop, seeking sobriety, moved to West Berlin with David Bowie after Bowie's period of intense drug addiction and paranoia. They both aimed to clean up and dry out in the city. "It wasn't until he and Iggy Pop had a moment of clarity and decided that they needed to move to West Berlin to clean up, get sober and dry out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
