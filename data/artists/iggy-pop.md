**Elton John Gorilla Suit Incident – Richards, Atlanta, October 1973** : In October 1973, Iggy Pop and the Stooges were playing at Richards in Atlanta at the same time Elton John was touring America. Elton — engaged in a semi-friendly rivalry with David Bowie, who was a known Stooges supporter — decided to crash the show dressed in a full gorilla suit to surprise Iggy on stage. A photographer from Detroit music magazine *Creem* was tipped off. No one warned Iggy that this was planned, and no one warned Elton that Iggy was heavily intoxicated on speed that night. When Elton bounded onto the stage in the gorilla suit, Iggy genuinely believed it was a real gorilla, was terrified, and fled the stage. The incident was captured on film. After Elton removed the gorilla head and multiple people calmed Iggy down, the show resumed and the two performed several songs together — Iggy bare-chested, Elton still in the suit. "Iggy thought it was an actual gorilla. And this freak out is captured on film." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7lpjI8F2Apk
