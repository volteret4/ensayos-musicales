# artist - Avril Lavigne

## genres
- Pop
- Pop Punk (debated)

## curiosities
**"Pop Punk Queen" Nickname** : Held the nickname of "Pop Punk Queen" for a while.
**"Too Pop for Alternative" Criticism** : Was considered too pop to be alternative by some, and alt-rock radio stations sometimes refused to play her music, claiming her fans were "too young" and lacked "true spirit of punk."
