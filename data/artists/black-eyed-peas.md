## songs
**I Gotta Feeling (2009) - Most Purchased Digital Track of 2010s** : "I Gotta Feeling" by the Black Eyed Peas was the most purchased digital track of the 2010s, with over 9 million downloads. This demonstrates a significant commercial success for the track in the early part of the digital sales era before the market shifted predominantly to streaming. "The most purchased digital track of the decade was, I got a feeling by the Black Eyed Peas, which was bought and downloaded a little over 9 million times." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNqqLDpUuZY ← black-eyed-peas
