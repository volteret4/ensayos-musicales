# artist - Moose

## curiosities
**Shoegaze Naming Origin** : A review of a show by Moose in the British music magazine _Sounds_ is credited with coining the term "shoegaze." The observation that band members stared at the floor, specifically their shoes, rather than engaging with the audience during performances led to the derogatory term, which was later re-appropriated by the bands themselves.
