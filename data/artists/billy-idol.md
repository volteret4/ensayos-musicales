# artist - Billy Idol

## members
- William Broad

## songs
**Dancing With Myself (1980)** : Original version released in the fall of 1980 as part of Generation X (known as Gen X by then), later remixed and re-released when he went solo.

## curiosities
**Name Change** : Changed his name from William Broad to Billy Idol.
**Sex Pistols Associate** : Was an acolyte of The Sex Pistols, following them everywhere and deeply involved in the original punk rock scene.
