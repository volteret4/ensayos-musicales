## songs
**Cult of Personality (1988)** : This song was released by Living Colour in 1988, marking their emergence as a band that integrated metal, funk, punk, hip-hop, jazz, and alt-rock. The band was known for combining their fresh musical package with direct commentary on politics and race. ## curiosities
**Groundbreaking Funk Metal Sound** : Living Colour, a band of four Black men from New York City, created a funk metal sound that laid the groundwork for many groups of the 1990s. Their influence was felt across bands like Rage Against the Machine, 311, Primus, and even Korn. "The resulting funk metal laid the groundwork for many groups of the 1990s, ranging from rage against the machine to 311 to primus to even corn." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
