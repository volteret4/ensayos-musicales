**Tainted Love (1981) – Gloria Jones Cover** : Soft Cell's 1981 release of "Tainted Love," a single from their "Non-Stop Erotic Cabaret" album, was a massive hit. It entered the charts on four different occasions over the following decade and sold millions of copies. The recording itself was "banged off for a day and a half." This version was a cover of Gloria Jones' 1965 B-side, which was written by Ed Cobb in 1964. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
