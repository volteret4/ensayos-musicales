# artist - Paul Epworth

## songs
**Skyfall (2012) – Co-writer & Music Theorist** : Paul Epworth co-wrote Adele's "Skyfall" theme and meticulously researched previous Bond themes for months. His analysis revealed that a variation of a "minor ninth" chord was a common element across all successful Bond themes. This discovery was key in helping him and Adele craft a song that was both modern and deeply rooted in the franchise's musical tradition. "And the co-writer of the song, a guy named Paul Epworth, spent months listening to themes from the past." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo
