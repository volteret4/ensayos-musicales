**If the Kids Are United (1978) – Oi Punk Anthem; Working Class, Uncompromising** : "If the Kids Are United" is a 1978 single from Sham 69, one of the most successful English punk bands of their time. Sham 69 were an Oi band — a punk subculture some called "real punk" — entirely devoted to the working class and refusing to compromise on any level.
