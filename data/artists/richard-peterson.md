# artist - Richard Peterson

## curiosities
**"My Second Album" Hidden Track** : Richard Peterson, a friend of the Stone Temple Pilots and an admirer of Johnny Mathis, performed a hidden track titled "My Second Album" on the band's "Purple Album". This comedic piece was intentionally included to poke fun at the common practice among bands of incorporating hidden tracks at the end of their CDs.
