# artist - Richard Peterson

## curiosities
**"My Second Album" Hidden Track** : Richard Peterson, a friend of the Stone Temple Pilots and an admirer of Johnny Mathis, performed a hidden track titled "My Second Album" on the band's "Purple Album". This comedic piece was intentionally included to poke fun at the common practice among bands of incorporating hidden tracks at the end of their CDs. "The performer is a guy named Richard Peterson, a friend of the band, and a big fan of Johnny Mathis, which explains why the song goes like this." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J_VEOIwavtg
