# artist - Roy Cooder

## albums
**Bop to You Drop (1978)** : Released in 1978, this album by Roy Cooder is considered an early attempt at an all-digital rock album. It was recorded using a special 3M machine that captured digital signals to tape, however, digital mixing boards were not yet available, so the mixing and mastering processes were still analog.
