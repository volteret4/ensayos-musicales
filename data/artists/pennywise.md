## albums
**Unknown Road (1993)** : Released in 1993 by this Southern California band, this record sold approximately 100,000 copies by indie standards. Its success cemented Pennywise as a significant force within the skate punk world and was one of the first indications of something special happening with the genre. "And it cemented them as a force in the Skate Punk world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=44dXy0StjIQ

## curiosities
**Booked for Content Pivot (1996)** : Pennywise was one of the bands brought on board for the 1996 Warped Tour, aligning with the festival's new direction to highlight punk and music that resonated with skate culture, following its sponsorship by Vans. "This meant booking fishbone, the muddy, muddy boss tones, no effect, 3-11 penny wise, and this weird new group from San Diego called Blink 182." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Financial Support for Blink-182 Tour** : When Blink-182 received an invitation to tour Australia but lacked the financial means to go, the members of Pennywise generously purchased their plane tickets. "So the guys in the band Pennywise bought them their tickets." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eljIIqCu5gU
