## curiosities
**Discovery and Formation in England** : Jimi Hendrix was discovered at 22 years old in Greenwich Village, New York. He was subsequently invited by the manager of The Animals to audition for a new group being formed in England, which led to the formation of The Jimi Hendrix Experience. "Jimmy's 22 years old and was discovered in Greenwich Village, New York and invited by the manager of the animals to audition for a new group being formed in England." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8q2bRZyHnHE
