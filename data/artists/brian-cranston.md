# artist - Brian Cranston

## curiosities
**Songwriter's Guild Membership for Humming Royalties** : While acting in "Malcolm in the Middle," Brian Cranston frequently hummed and whistled songs. To ensure he received performance royalties for these instances, he joined a songwriter's guild. He would then use the royalty checks to throw parties for the show's crew. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
