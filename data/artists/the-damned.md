# artist - The Damned

## venues
- 100 Club
- The Cuckoo's Nest

## curiosities
**Attendance at Ramones London Gigs (July 1976)** : Members of The Damned were among the key figures transformed by seeing The Ramones perform in London on July 4th and 6th, 1976.
**First British Punk Record Release** : The Damned would become the first British punk band to release a record.
**Performance at 100 Club Punk Rock Festival (September 20 & 21, 1976)** : The Damned performed at Britain's first official punk rock festival at the 100 Club on Oxford Street, London.
