## curiosities
**Attendance at Ramones London Gigs (July 1976)** : Members of The Damned were among the key figures transformed by seeing The Ramones perform in London on July 4th and 6th, 1976. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
**First British Punk Record Release** : The Damned would become the first British punk band to release a record. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
**Performance at 100 Club Punk Rock Festival (September 20 & 21, 1976)** : The Damned performed at Britain's first official punk rock festival at the 100 Club on Oxford Street, London. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
**Vampire Persona (Punk Era)** : Dave Vanian, the singer of the punk band The Damned, was known for sometimes claiming to be a vampire, an interesting detail that aligns with the horror and macabre themes later adopted by goth culture. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
**Warped Tour Veteran** : An English punk band from the 1970s that joined the Warped Tour as "seriously grizzled veterans." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
