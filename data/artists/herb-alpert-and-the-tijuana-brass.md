# artist - Herb Alpert and the Tijuana Brass

## curiosities
**Commissioned Sound System** : Herb Alpert and the Tijuana Brass, alongside The Beach Boys, commissioned a specialized sound system for their tours. This initiative was part of a broader trend among popular bands to address the limitations of existing amplification for live music performances in the mid-1960s.
