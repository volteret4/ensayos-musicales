**Defining Sound of "Música Blanca" at Barraca (1981)** : The Fall was a foundational group in establishing the "música blanca" sound, which was introduced by DJs Juan Santamaría and Carlos Simó at Barraca in 1981. This new musical direction aimed to provide an alternative to the widespread funk and disco, characterized by its exotic and previously unheard rhythms. "música blanca para distinguirse del fanco disco dominantes de la época, y que estaba definida por grupos como de Pech Mode, de Kyu, y un division o de Fall." ← Cuando Valencia fue la capital mundial del Clubbing | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ckJgog8P_og
