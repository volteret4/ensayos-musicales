## curiosities
**924 Gilman Street Alumni** : Primus is identified as an alumnus of 924 Gilman Street, the influential all-ages punk club in West Berkeley, California. The club's rule prohibiting major label bands means that Primus, despite their roots there, is no longer welcome to perform. "Gilman Street alumni like Green Day and the offspring and AFI and Primus and even bad religion aren't really welcome anymore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKHdTSfDd6E
