## curiosities
**Aggressive and Versatile Playing Style** : Les Claypool is known for "attacking a bass" with a style that can be both rocking and super funky. He employs various techniques including strumming strings, slapping strings, and tapping them with his fingers. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Diverse Bass Collection** : Claypool uses standard four-string basses, a six-string bass when the need arises, and a variety of stand-up basses. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Guest Vocalist** : Tom Waits provided a guest vocal performance on the track "Tommy the Cat." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Metallica Offer** : There is a story that he was offered the job of bassist in Metallica after the death of Cliff Burton, but he reportedly did not get the job because, as quoted, he "was too good." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Multi-Instrumentalist and Vocalist** : Live, he performs all these playing techniques while simultaneously singing lead vocals. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Unique Instrumentation and Effects** : Some of his guitars feature a whammy bar, allowing him to bend notes in unusual ways. He also processes his notes through a series of processors and distortion pedals. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
