# artist - Joey Armstrong

## instruments
- drums

## albums
**Berkeley's On Fire (2018) – Second Album with SWMRS** : Released under the name SWMRS.
**Don't Be a Dick (2011) – Debut Album with Emily's Army** : Produced by his father, Billy Joe Armstrong.
**Drive North (2016) – First Album with SWMRS** : Released under the band's new name, SWMRS.
**Lost at 17 (2013) – Second Album with Emily's Army** : Produced by his father, Billy Joe Armstrong.

## songs
**Berkeley's On Fire (2018)** : The title track and first single from SWMRS' 2018 album.

## curiosities
**Emily's Army Formation and Name Changes** : His first group was known as Emily's Army, which went through several name changes before becoming SWMRS.
**Inspired by School of Rock** : Joey decided to form his own band with his friend Cole after seeing the movie "School of Rock" with Jack Black.
**Named After Joey Ramone** : Joey Armstrong was named after Joey Ramone.
**Son of Billy Joe Armstrong and Adrian Nesser** : Joseph Marciano Armstrong, known as Joey, was born on February 28th, 1995, the day after his parents' first wedding anniversary.
**Temporary Member of SWMRS** : His younger brother, Jacob Danger Armstrong, temporarily appeared in the group.
