## songs
**You Showed Me (1969)** : The Turtles, a 60s group, released "You Showed Me" in 1969. A portion of this song was later sampled by De La Soul for their track "Transmitting Why From Mars" on the *3 Feet High and Rising* album, leading to a significant lawsuit. ## curiosities
**Lawsuit against De La Soul (1989)** : In 1989, The Turtles sued De La Soul for "stealing" a portion of their 1969 song "You Showed Me" for the track "Transmitting Why From Mars" from De La Soul's *3 Feet High and Rising* album. This resulted in a $1.7 million lawsuit against De La Soul and their record company, which was settled out of court, sending a clear message: "Do not sample without permission."
