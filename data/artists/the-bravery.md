# artist - The Bravery

## curiosities
**80s-Style Keyboards** : Along with The Killers, The Bravery were instrumental in reintroducing the sound of 80s-style keyboards into rock music in the mid-2000s. This marked a shift towards greater sonic variety beyond the grunge and punk influences that had dominated the preceding era.
