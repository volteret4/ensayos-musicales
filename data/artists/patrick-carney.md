## curiosities
**Drummer Project (Bass Player)** : Patrick Carney joined the indie band Drummer, signed to his own Audio Eagle Records label, where he played bass in the studio. This project was unique in that all its members were drummers from various other bands. "Patrick joined them in the studio where he played bass." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Early 4-track Recorder Owner** : Patrick Carney's ownership of a 4-track recorder played a crucial role in the initial stages of The Black Keys' formation. It enabled him and Dan Auerbach to record their first demo, which was essential for Auerbach's aspirations as a singer-songwriter. "Since Patrick had a 4 track recorder, he asked Patrick if he would help him make one of these demos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Producer Work** : Patrick Carney has also undertaken production work, including producing part of The Sheepdogs' 2012 debut record, as well as albums for Tennis and Tobias Gesso Jr. "He produced part of the Sheep Dogs, 2012 debut record along with albums by tennis and Tobias Gesso Jr." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
