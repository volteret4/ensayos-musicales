## curiosities
**21st Century Tweetpop Descendant** : Arlo Parks is presented as a 21st-century artist working within the Bedroom Pop genre, which is described as a direct descendant of Tweetpop. Her music, like others in the genre, is often dreamy, introspective, sensitive, and vulnerable, a style made possible by accessible modern recording technology. "We wouldn't have artists like Girl in Red or Beba Dooby or Arlo Parks, Rex Orange County, and Sockermummy if it wasn't for the various flavors of Tweet Pop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g ← arlo-parks
