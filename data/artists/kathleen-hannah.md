**"Smells Like Teen Spirit" Inspiration (1991)** : One night in 1991, after going out drinking with Kurt Cobain, Kathleen Hannah commented that he smelled "sweet, almost like a particular type of deodorant marketed to teens." Cobain, who never wore deodorant, was inspired by her remark to use "Smells Like Teen Spirit" as the title for a new song.
