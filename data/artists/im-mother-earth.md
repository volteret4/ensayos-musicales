## curiosities
**Breakup (Post-2003)** : The band broke up after the release of their final record "Quick Silver Meat Dream" in 2003, with members pursuing other projects. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Brian Bern's Post-Band Career (Post-2003)** : Brian Bern became a radio personality in Halifax and unsuccessfully auditioned to be the new singer for Stone Temple Pilots. He is also currently involved in the world of cryptocurrency. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Christian Tana's Post-Band Career (Post-2003)** : Christian Tana continued in the music industry, working with his brother's record label, serving as general manager of an event venue in Peterborough, and working as an event planner for a theater in Coburg, Ontario. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Early Success & Bidding War (Early 1990s)** : After recording demos and playing gigs, the band became the subject of a bidding war before releasing their debut album. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Edwin's Solo Career and New Band (Post-2003)** : After leaving the band, Edwin pursued a solo career, releasing three records. In 2008, he joined Mike Turner (ex-Our Lady Peace) and Jeff Burrows (The Tea Party) to form the band Crash Karma. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Formation (1990)** : The band was formed in 1990 when brothers Jag and Christian Tana met vocalist Edwin at a shared rehearsal space. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Jag Tana's Post-Band Career (Post-2003)** : Jag Tana started a record label, produced several acts, and worked on TV projects. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Reunions (2012, 2016)** : The band reunited in 2012 with Brian Bern as singer, a reunion that lasted for approximately three years. A series of reunion dates across the country occurred in early 2016. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Vocalist Change (Post-Scenery and Fish)** : Original vocalist Edwin decided to leave the band after the release of "Scenery and Fish" and was replaced by Brian Bern. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
