## albums
**Come On Over** : This album by Shania Twain achieved extraordinary success, selling 40 million copies worldwide and earning it the distinction of being the eighth best-selling album of all time. Its massive global sales positioned Twain as one of three Canadians with the biggest-selling albums in the 1990s, demonstrating the unparalleled international impact of Canadian music. "And Shania Twain's come on over sold 40 million worldwide, making it the eighth best-selling album of all time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo ← shania-twain
**The Woman in Me** : Shania Twain's album "The Woman in Me" was the next album by a woman to surpass Lynn Anderson's record of 14 consecutive weeks at number one on the country album chart, doing so 25 years later. "The album was number one on the country album chart for 14 weeks in a row and the next woman to do better than that was Shenaya Twain with the woman in me and that was 25 years later." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 23 - Women of Country Music in the 70s | https://seventies.libsyn.com/023-country-women-72820-2-45-pm
