# artist - Adriana Salatano

## songs
**Prisencolinensinainciusol (1972) – Gibberish Hit That Proved Europeans Would Buy Any American Music** : Italian singer Adriana Salatano released this song composed entirely of nonsense syllables designed to sound like American English. Salatano created it to prove that European audiences would purchase any music from the United States regardless of meaning. It became a genuine hit, reaching number two in Belgium and entering the top ten in Italy and the Netherlands.
