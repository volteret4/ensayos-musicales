# artist - Adriana Salatano

## songs
**Prison Cooling and Cena in Scullio (1972)** : An Italian song written and performed by Adriana Salatano, with lyrics entirely made of gibberish that sound like American English but mean nothing. Salatano created the song to prove that Europeans would buy any music from America, and it successfully charted in several countries, reaching number two in Belgium and top 10 in Italy and the Netherlands. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
