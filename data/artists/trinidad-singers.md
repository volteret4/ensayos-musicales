# artist - Trinidad Singers

## songs
**Superstar (1969)** : The Trinidad Singers provided backing vocals for Murray Head on the single "Superstar," released in the fall of 1969. This song, connected to the *Jesus Christ Superstar* musical, reached the Billboard Hot 100 in January 1970.
