# artist - Catherine Davis

## songs
**Carol of the Drum (1941) – Later known as Little Drummer Boy** : Catherine Davis, a classical composer, wrote "Carol of the Drum" in 1941, purportedly basing it loosely on an untraced Czech song. By the end of the 1950s, after numerous recordings, its title became "Little Drummer Boy."
