# artist - k.d. lang

## songs
**Surrender (1997) – Closing Credits Song** : k.d. lang's song, originally submitted as "Tomorrow Never Dies," was ultimately renamed "Surrender" and used over the closing credits of the 1997 James Bond film. This decision came after Sheryl Crow's version was chosen for the opening title sequence. **Tomorrow Never Dies (1997) – Rejected Title Theme** : k.d. lang initially won the tender to sing the theme for the 1997 James Bond film "Tomorrow Never Dies" with her song also titled "Tomorrow Never Dies." However, Sheryl Crow later submitted her own song, and the producers, unable to choose between them, ultimately opted for Crow's version for the opening sequence.
