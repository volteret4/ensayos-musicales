**Jarvis Cocker's Artistic Influence** : Scott Walker's work deeply influenced Jarvis Cocker, particularly in Pulp's later career. Cocker "deepened" his interest in Walker's charismatic classics, which ultimately led him to develop a "modern experimental" singing style and move completely away from the Brit-Pop sound.
