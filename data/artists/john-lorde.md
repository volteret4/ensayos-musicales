## songs
**Highway Star (1972)** : In this 1972 Deep Purple track, John Lorde innovatively ran his Hammond organ through a Big Muff pedal. This demonstrated that fuzz boxes and distortion pedals were not limited to guitars and could be used to add growl to any electrical signal.
