## curiosities
**Pioneer of EDM Resurgence** : David Guetta was part of the previous generation of DJs who slowly resurrected and relaunched EDM when it was "quite dead." This generation gradually built momentum and experience. **Revival of Eurodance Formula (USA)** : David Guetta was among the artists who achieved number one status on the charts in the United States by adopting a musical formula that bore strong structural similarities to Eurodance. His success contributed to the genre's unexpected spiritual resurrection in the US, about a decade after its decline in Europe. **Strategic Career Management** : He is credited with having significant experience ("bagaje muy importante") before he began filling stadiums. As a "smart guy," he strategically disengaged from "grand promotion" to control his own career, which allowed him to survive the pressures that later affected younger artists. "cuando la vidieta empezó a llenar estadios era un tío que llevaba un bagaje muy importante y como tío listo que es lo primero que hizo fue descogarse de la gran promoción y de ser que así el dueño de su propia promoción de espectáculos, ni eso es lo que prácticamente la permitió sobrevivir." ← Music Radar Clan > Lo que la muerte de AVICII representa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9W8u_1LE1ys&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← david-guetta ← david-guetta
**Superstar DJs and EDM Movement** : David Guetta is identified as an EDM hero, embodying the shift in music where performers with laptops became the new rock and roll figures, drawing massive crowds to their shows globally.
