## albums
**My Beauty (1999) – Solo Album That Sold 500 Copies Due to Controversial Cover** : Singer Kevin Rowland insisted on posing for the cover of his solo album wearing heavy makeup, panties, and stockings. The image alienated his fanbase so thoroughly that the record initially sold just 500 copies. It has since sold only around 20,000 total, and many of those purchases are attributed to curiosity about the cover image.
