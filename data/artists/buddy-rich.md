# artist - Buddy Rich

## curiosities
**Drum Kit Standardization Efforts** : Along with Gene Krupa, Buddy Rich played a crucial role in standardizing how drum kits were constructed and set up. He developed a drum kit configuration that was very similar to Krupa's influential 1948 setup. "Buddy Rich developed something very similar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WL3zEWJJNuw
**Frequent Drum Battle Competitor** : Buddy Rich was Gene Krupa's most frequent competitor in popular drum battles, which were held on stage, on television, and on record. These competitions significantly contributed to popularizing drumming. "His most frequent competitor was his friend Buddy Rich." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WL3zEWJJNuw
