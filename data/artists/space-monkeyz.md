## curiosities
**Invented Collaboration Group** : Space Monkeyz was not a traditional band but an entity invented by Damon Albarn and his friends specifically for their collaboration with Gorillaz on the "Laika Come Home" album. They focused on creating music with Caribbean, African, and Rige rhythms.
