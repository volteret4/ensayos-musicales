## curiosities
**Overcoming Injury to Master Guitar** : Django Reinhart was a guitarist who suffered severe burns to his hand, leaving him with only two workable fingers. Despite this debilitating injury, he managed to become a master of the guitar, serving as an inspiration to Tony Iommi after Iommi's own hand accident.
