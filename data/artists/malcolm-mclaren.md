# artist - Malcolm McLaren

## curiosities
**Encounter with Johnny Rotten (1975)** : In 1975, Malcolm McLaren's clothing shop served as a significant location in music history. It was here that he encountered Johnny Rotten, who was wearing an "I Hate Pink Floyd" t-shirt, leading to Rotten's consideration for the lead singer role in the Sex Pistols. "Johnny Rotten walked into Malcolm McLaren's clothing shop in 1975.
