## curiosities
**Beneficiary of Radio Star Maker** : Metric's career was furthered by the funding available from the new Radio Star Maker program, introduced in 2000. This program, along with Factor, was instrumental in supporting emerging and established Canadian talent. "Soon this cash was instrumental in helping further the careers of metric, broken social scene, Alexis on fire, city and color, and many more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
**Indie Rock Revival (2000s)** : Metric is mentioned as one of the "fresh sounding bands" that emerged during the Indie Rock Revival in the early 2000s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
**Polaris Music Prize Nomination (2006)** : Metric was one of the bands nominated for the 2006 Polaris Music Prize, an award aimed at recognizing artistic merit over commercial success, though the prize was ultimately awarded to Final Fantasy. "The winning album was... By Final Fantasy... which won out over records by Broken Social Scene, The New Prenogpers, and Metric." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZzIqIydZTlA
**Twilight Soundtracks Contribution** : Metric contributed to the various *Twilight* soundtracks that accompanied the film series. These soundtracks featured a nice selection of tunes, mostly album cuts and outtakes, from a range of artists spanning different genres. "The Paramore, Lincoln Park, Collective Soul, Death Cab for Cutie, Tom York, the Killers, Editors, Metric, Block Keys, Dead Weather, Beck, Vampire Weekend, Florence in the Machine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
