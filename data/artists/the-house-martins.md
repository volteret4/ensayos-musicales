## curiosities
**Hugh Whitaker Gasoline, Firebombing, and Axe Attack on Former Business Partner** : After the House Martins broke up, drummer Hugh Whitaker invested his life savings with a partner named James Hughitt in a car dealership. By 1993, Hughitt's mismanagement had left Whitaker completely broke. Whitaker responded by pouring gasoline into Hughitt's mailbox at least twice; when this produced no results, he firebombed his house; when that also failed, he attacked him with an axe, hacking him up significantly. Whitaker served a six-year sentence for assault. He now plays in local bands in Leeds and stays away from the car business.
