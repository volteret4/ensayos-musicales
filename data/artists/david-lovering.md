## curiosities
**Magician and Scientific Phenomenalist** : After The Pixies' initial breakup, David Lovering rebranded himself as "the scientific phenomenalist." His act involved performing magic tricks rooted in science and physics, leveraging his knowledge of electrical engineering. He joined the Magic Castle, an LA nightclub specializing in magic, and later became part of a trio called "The Unholy Three." His performances often featured complex machines he built. He continues to perform magic alongside his duties with The Pixies, following their 2004 reunion. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
