**"10,000 Days" Layered Song Experiment** : Tool engineered a unique "do-it-yourself, three-dimensional song" from their "10,000 Days" album. This complex audio experience is achieved by simultaneously playing the title track "10,000 Days" (11:13) with "Wings for Marie" (6:11), and then immediately adding "Viginti Tres" (5:02) as "Wings for Marie" concludes, while the title track continues. The precise timing, where the combined duration of "Wings for Marie" and "Viginti Tres" matches "10,000 Days", ensures that tempo, key, flow, breakdowns, and transitions perfectly align, creating a new, multi-movement composition. "Take three separate songs from tools 10,000 days album, layer them on top of each other, and you get a brand new multi-movement song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J_VEOIwavtg
