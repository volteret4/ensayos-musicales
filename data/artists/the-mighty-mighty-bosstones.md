## albums
**Don't Know How To Party (1993)** : This third album was a breakthrough for The Mighty Mighty Bosstones, featuring a Ska track with prominent horns that became a major alt-rock radio hit. The band was formed in 1983, originally inspired by the Two Tone movement, but also incorporated hard rock influences from bands like ACDC, Motorhead, and Social Distortion. "The Mighty Mighty Boss Tones blew up in 1983 with their third album Don't Know How To Party. **Third Wave Pioneers** : Formed in 1983 in Boston, The Mighty Mighty Bosstones were early practitioners of the third wave of Ska, initially inspired by the Two Tone movement but also incorporating hard rock influences from bands like ACDC, Motorhead, and Social Distortion. They became prominent in the late 1980s and early 1990s by fusing Ska with hardcore elements. "The Mighty Mighty Boss Tones. They were formed in 1983 and inspired by the whole two-tone thing, at least originally." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WX2GlpMNg_8
**Third Wave Ska Band** : The Mighty Mighty Bosstones were identified as a band from the "third wave" of Ska, which arrived in the 1990s. This wave saw significant popularity with bands like No Doubt, Sublime, Reel Big Fish, Goldfinger, and Dance Hall Crashers. "Third wave Scott arrived in the 1990s, no doubt, muddy, muddy boss tones, sublime, real big fish, gold finger, dancehall crashes and so many more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HSvgRyGiOpE
