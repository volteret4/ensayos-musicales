## songs
**(I Can't Get No) Satisfaction (1965)** : Released in the summer of 1965, this song became an instant number one hit, largely due to Keith Richards' distinctive guitar riff. The band's decision to overrule Richards' desire for a horn section and instead record his guitar part through a Maestro Fuzzbox pedal on May 12, 1965, proved transformative. The track's success caused Maestro Fuzz Tone pedals to fly off the shelves, cementing the role of distortion in rock music. "When I can't get no satisfaction, was released in the summer of 1965, it was an instant hit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iGav9uJwA_w
