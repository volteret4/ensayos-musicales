## curiosities
**John Hughes Film Exposure (1980s)** : Yello's music was included on the carefully selected soundtracks of John Hughes' 1980s films. These soundtracks were highly influential in exposing many people, especially in North America, to a variety of alternative rock bands, particularly those from the UK. "So many people learned of bands like The Specials and Spandovalet and Billy Idle and General Public and Wang Chung and Yellow and New Order and the Psychedelic Furs and the Smiths and Echo and the Bunyman, Susanne Vagan in excess the Jesus and Mary chain, Flesh for Luluo and so many others from those soundtrack albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
**Technopop Descendant** : Listed as one of the artists who descended from the original Technopop scene. "There was Bronsky beat, aha, yellow, Elfaville, Frankie Ghost of Hollywood. They all descended from the original Tecno Pop scene." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng
