# artist - Christopher Cerf

## curiosities
**Composer of Torture Music** : Christopher Cerf, the composer of the theme song for "Sesame Street" and the "Meow Mix" theme, is an artist whose music was used as a torture device. He also signed the Zero DB petition, an initiative condemning the use of music for torture. "That also includes Christopher Surf, the composer of the theme song for Sesame Street, and also the composer of the Meow Mix theme, which was also used as a torture device." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1wdsGt07Gg
**Zero DB Initiative Signatory** : Christopher Cerf, known for composing the theme songs for "Sesame Street" and "Meow Mix" (both of which were used as torture devices), signed the Zero DB petition. This initiative, part of the charity Reprieve, was founded in 2008 to condemn the use of music for torture.
