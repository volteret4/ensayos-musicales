## curiosities
**Early German Electronic Musicians** : Tangerine Dream was among the German groups that began creating electronic music in the early 1970s, seeking to establish a unique musical identity separate from the prevailing British and American rock and roll. This development was significantly aided by the experimental electronic studio built by West German Radio, which had exposed German musicians to electronic sounds for nearly two decades. **German Electronic Music Pioneers** : Tangerine Dream was part of a movement of German (specifically West German) artists in the 1960s who sought to create a distinct musical identity by focusing on electronic sounds, rather than imitating American or British styles. "They had names like Tangerine Dream and Amundool." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
