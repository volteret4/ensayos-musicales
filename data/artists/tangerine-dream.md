**Early German Electronic Musicians** : Tangerine Dream was among the German groups that began creating electronic music in the early 1970s, seeking to establish a unique musical identity separate from the prevailing British and American rock and roll. This development was significantly aided by the experimental electronic studio built by West German Radio, which had exposed German musicians to electronic sounds for nearly two decades.
