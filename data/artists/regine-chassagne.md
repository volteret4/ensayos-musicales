# artist - Regine Chassagne

## member of
- Arcade Fire

## curiosities
**Birthplace and Family History** : Regine Chassagne was born in Montreal to parents who had emigrated from Haiti in 1962, seeking refuge from the dictatorship of Papa Dr. Valier. Her family history includes the tragic Jeremy Vespers massacre of 1964, where some of her relatives were killed, emphasizing the context of her parents' move. "She was born in Montreal to parents from Haiti. They had moved to Quebec in 1962 to escape the craziness that was the dictatorship of Papa Dr. Valier." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Marriage to Win Butler (2003)** : Regine Chassagne and Win Butler, after meeting in 2001 and forming an early version of Arcade Fire, were married in 2003. "They got married in 2003." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**McGill Jazz Voice Student** : Regine Chassagne met Win Butler in Montreal in 2001 while she was studying jazz voice at McGill University, a pivotal encounter for the formation of Arcade Fire. "Regina and Wins met in 2001 while Regina was studying jazz voice at McGill." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Multi-Instrumentalist Skills** : Win Butler was deeply impressed by Regine Chassagne's ability to play a wide array of instruments, including keyboards, organ, accordion, drums, and xylophone, which prompted him to invite her to join his band. "Wins was really impressed the way she could play just about any instrument, keyboards, organ, accordion, drum, cellophone." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
