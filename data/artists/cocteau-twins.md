## songs
**Pearly-Dewdrops' Drops (1984)** : Described as the band's biggest single from 1984, showcasing Elizabeth Fraser's distinctive "singing in tongues" style where she uses a made-up, unrecognizable language, emphasizing sonic textures over lyrical meaning. "From 1984, it's Curly Do Drops Drops." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4

## curiosities
**Influence on Sigur Rós** : The Cocteau Twins are cited as very important in the dream pop influences for Sigur Rós. "Dreampop, the Cocktoe twins are very important here, along with the Verve and anything Radiohead did with OK Computer and Beyond." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
