# artist - Pat Hare

## songs
**I'm Gonna Murder My Baby (1954)** : On this 1954 song, Pat Hare achieved a warm, fuzzy sound by turning his amplifier's volume knobs all the way to the right to make the amp "scream." This was part of a broader trend of guitarists experimenting with ways to get distorted sounds in the years following the accidental discovery of fuzz.
