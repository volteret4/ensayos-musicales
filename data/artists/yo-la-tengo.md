## curiosities
**Founding and Shared Interests (1984)** : Ira Kaplan and Georgia Hubley, who formed the indie group Yo La Tengo in 1984, first met at shows and record shops in New Jersey. Their bond was solidified when they discovered a mutual fandom for the New York Mets baseball team. "Ira Kaplan and Georgia Hubley would see each other at the same shows at the same record shops in New Jersey. And when they realized they were both fans of the New York Mets, poor guys, that sealed the deal. Yolotengo started in 1984" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Dhh5_PDhd0
