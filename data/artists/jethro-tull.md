## curiosities
**Anti-Streaming Stance** : Jethro Tull refuses to have any of their discography on Spotify, and generally opposes any digital support, including digital downloads and YouTube. They believe that their albums are global components that should be heard as a whole, and any fragmentation or partial listening out of context destroys the integrity of their work. **Protection of Artistic Integrity** : The band actively pursues its rights on YouTube, not primarily for royalty concerns, but to prevent individual song fragments from being heard outside the context of the full album. Their singer has stated that vinyl is the perfect format, requiring listeners to engage with half an hour of music at a time, preventing arbitrary skipping.
