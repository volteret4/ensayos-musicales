## curiosities
**Influence on Punk and Post-Punk** : Although Jonathan Richman and the Modern Lovers never achieved significant record sales, they were highly influential to early punk bands and many post-punk groups such as the Sex Pistols, R.E.M., Violent Femmes, Dinosaur Jr., The Cars, The Pixies, and the Ramones. This impact stemmed from Jonathan Richman being a big Velvet Underground fan. "Jonathan Richmond and the Modern Lovers never sold a lot of records, but they were very important to the early punks and many of the post-punk bands like the Sex Pistols and REM and the Violent Femmes and Dinosaur Jr. and the Cars and the Pixies and the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk ← jonathan-richman-and-the-modern-lovers
