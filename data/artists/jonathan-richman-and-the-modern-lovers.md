## songs
**Roadrunner (1971)** : The song "Roadrunner" was recorded in 1971 and later released in 1976 on the Berserkerly label. It is described as being about a muscle car, not a cartoon bird, and became an enduring and influential track from the band. ## curiosities
**Influence on Punk and Post-Punk** : Although Jonathan Richman and the Modern Lovers never achieved significant record sales, they were highly influential to early punk bands and many post-punk groups such as the Sex Pistols, R.E.M., Violent Femmes, Dinosaur Jr., The Cars, The Pixies, and the Ramones. This impact stemmed from Jonathan Richman being a big Velvet Underground fan. "Jonathan Richmond and the Modern Lovers never sold a lot of records, but they were very important to the early punks and many of the post-punk bands like the Sex Pistols and REM and the Violent Femmes and Dinosaur Jr. and the Cars and the Pixies and the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dDcD8kg0wEk
