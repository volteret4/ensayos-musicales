# artist - Jeff

## curiosities
**A&R Executive at Zomba and Warner Brothers** : Jeff served as a VP of A&R at Zomba Music, where he first heard about Chester Bennington and facilitated his audition for the band Zero. He later became a VP at Warner Brothers Records, where he played a crucial role in Linkin Park signing their record deal in late 1999, despite Warner's previous rejections of the band. "A VP of ANR at Zomba Music had heard about Chester. He called him up and asked him if he'd be interested in auditioning for this band called Zero." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM
