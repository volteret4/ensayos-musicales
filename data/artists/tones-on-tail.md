**Go (Year not specified) - B-side Hit** : This song was the B-side of a 12-inch single for a forgotten track called "Lions." "The song was the B side of a 12-inch single from a forgotten song called Lions. It was a nonsense track thrown together at the last second because they needed a song for the B side. **Go! (1984)** : This track is primarily what the band is remembered for, ranking at number 50 on a list of alt-rock one-hit wonders. As of February 2026, it accumulated just under 10 million combined plays on Spotify and views on YouTube (9,991,205), giving it a one-hit wonder power score of 1.17. The song has been widely used in popular culture, appearing in TV shows such as Beverly Hills 90210, Rick and Morty, Stranger Things, the movie Gross Point Blank, and a Ford TV commercial. "Go! From Tons on Tale from 1984, about the only thing the band is remembered for." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_qxdftwrX3E
**You the Night and the Music** : This song was one of the tracks considered and tested by the Arnold World Wide advertising agency for the 2000 Volkswagen "Milky Way" commercial. **Musical Appeal and Audience** : While the band released other singles, none achieved the widespread recognition of "Go!", especially within Alt Rock radio and dance clubs that catered to an audience with a preference for dark clothing. "But nothing was anywhere as big as the one we're about to hear, especially on Alt Rock Radio and in Dance Clubs, especially the kind that catered to people wearing a lot of black clothing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_qxdftwrX3E
**Reconstitution as Love and Rockets** : After Tones on Tail ceased activity, the group was reconstituted under the name Love and Rockets, which featured the members of Bauhaus with the exception of singer Peter Murphy.
