**Iowa Plane Crash Fatality** : Ritchie Valens died early in 1959 in an infamous Iowa plane crash, an event that also claimed the lives of J.P. Richardson (The Big Bopper) and Buddy Holly. "that also took the lives of Buddy Holly and Richie Valens." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 2 - Countryish Music of the 1970s (Encore) | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Plane Crash Death (1959)** : Ritchie Valens was killed in a small plane crash in a cornfield outside Mason City, Iowa, on February 3, 1959. This tragedy, which also claimed the lives of Buddy Holly and Big Wopper, is historically referred to as "the day the music died." "Buddy Holly, Richie Val, and Big Wopper were all killed when their small plane went down in a cornfield outside of Mason City, Iowa." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo ← ritchie-valens
