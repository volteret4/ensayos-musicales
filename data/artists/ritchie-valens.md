**Plane Crash Death (1959)** : Ritchie Valens was killed in a small plane crash in a cornfield outside Mason City, Iowa, on February 3, 1959. This tragedy, which also claimed the lives of Buddy Holly and Big Wopper, is historically referred to as "the day the music died."
