## curiosities
**Marriage to Chrissie Hynde** : Jim Kerr, the singer of Simple Minds, was married to Chrissie Hynde of The Pretenders at the time his band was being pressured to record "Don't You (Forget About Me)" for *The Breakfast Club* soundtrack. Hynde's personal appeal to the band was instrumental in convincing them to record the song, which became their biggest hit.
