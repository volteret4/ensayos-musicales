## curiosities
**Band Formation (1974)** : Dee Dee Ramone and John Cummings (later Johnny Ramone) got drunk after work one day in 1974 and decided to form a band. Neither guy knew the first thing about music, but they wanted to make it, even though they couldn't play a note on their new instruments. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg

**Dee Dee Ramone's Playing Style** : His playing consisted of long runs of the same notes. He was considered one of the most primitive and basic bass players in history, but his spirit was what counted. His approach inspired many young, alienated, and disenfranchised kids. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Dee Dee Ramone's Role** : Dee Dee Ramone became the main songwriter and lyricist for the Ramones for 15 years. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Early Influential Band** : The Ramones are identified as one of the early influential bands that helped define the "new music scene," alongside the Sex Pistols and Blondie. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
**Early Punk Ethos** : The appeal and genius of the Ramones was showing everyone that anyone could do what they were doing, requiring only the guts to do it. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg

**Final Show at The Palace (1996)** : The Ramones' last concert was on August 6, 1996, at The Palace in Hollywood, marking their 2263rd gig after playing exactly 2262 shows.
