# artist - Ramones

## members
- Joey Ramone
- Johnny Ramone

## venues
- CBGB
- The Cuckoo's Nest
- The Palace, Hollywood

## songs
**Anywhere You Wanted (Dave Clark 5 cover) (1996)** : The last song the Ramones ever played live, performed on August 6, 1996, at The Palace in Hollywood, with Eddie Vedder joining on stage.

## curiosities
**Bands for Moshing** : Listed among the bands to which audience members would mosh, as depicted and influenced by "The Decline of Western Civilization" movie.
**Final Show at The Palace (1996)** : The Ramones' last concert was on August 6, 1996, at The Palace in Hollywood, marking their 2263rd gig after playing exactly 2262 shows. The "long farewell" tour included dates on Lollapalooza alongside Metallica and Soundgarden.
**Joey Ramone's Illness (1996)** : At the time of their final performances, Joey Ramone was suffering from lymphoma, which made dealing with the rigors of performing increasingly difficult.
**Recognition by Alternative Bands (1990s)** : The Ramones were recognized by 1990s alternative bands like Pearl Jam, Green Day, and Nirvana as pioneers who influenced their music.
**Special Guest at Final Show (1996)** : Eddie Vedder, a good friend of guitarist Johnny Ramone, joined the band on stage for their last song, "Anywhere You Wanted."
**Unceremonious Exit (1996)** : After their final song, all band members simply walked away from the stage without saying goodbye.
