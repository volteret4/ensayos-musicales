# artist - Gory Carter

## songs
**Rock Away (1949)** : This 1949 recording by Gory Carter showcases a noticeable fuzz in his guitar sound, predating Chuck Berry's similar sounds by half a decade. Along with Junior Barnard's work, this example represents a deliberate attempt to make the guitar sound more raw.
