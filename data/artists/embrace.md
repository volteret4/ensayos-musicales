**Musical Style and Equipment** : Embrace's musical style featured loud guitars, prioritizing strumming over complex riffing. Their preferred equipment included Gips, Les Paul, or SG guitars, which were played through Marshall Tube amps, with a strict rule against using transistor amplifiers. "The guitars were loud, but there was less riffing and more strumming. Favorite guitars were Gips and Les Paul's or SG's, played through Marshall Tube amps. No transistors allowed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
**Revolution Summer Participation** : Embrace was a part of what is remembered as the "Revolution Summer" in the mid-1980s, a period when D.C. hardcore bands aimed to break free from the limiting dogma of traditional hardcore. "They became part of what is now remembered as the Revolution Summer when DC hardcore bands tried to break out of the dogma that had limited what hardcore could be." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
