# artist - Depeche Mode

## genres
- New Wave
- Synth Pop

## albums
**Violator (1990) – Riot-Inducing Album** : an autograph session for this album release in March 1990 led to 25,000 people rioting outside a Los Angeles record store, signaling a shift in alternative music's popularity.

## songs
**Dreaming of Me (1981)** : Their debut single, released in early 1981. It sounded very different from original New Wave but was classified as such, exemplifying the emerging Synth Pop genre.

## curiosities
**Butch Vig Remix Production** : Butch Vig produced a remix record for the group.
**Featured on Alt-Rock Playlists** : their music was found on radio playlists for late 1986 alt-rock stations.
**Influence on 2000s Revival** : Was a band rediscovered and mined for inspiration by early 2000s New Wave revivalists, including The Bravery.
**MTV Exposure (Early 1980s)** : Mentioned as a New Wave band that was successful due to MTV exposure.
**Riot for Album Release** : in March 1990, 25,000 people rioted outside a record store in Los Angeles during an autograph session for their *Violator* album, an unusual occurrence for an alternative band at the time.
