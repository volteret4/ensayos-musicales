## curiosities
**Claimant in "Stairway to Heaven" Lawsuit** : Randy California, the lead guitarist for Spirit, and later his estate after his death, claimed for years that Jimmy Page plagiarized Spirit's instrumental "Taurus" for the iconic guitar arpeggio in Led Zeppelin's "Stairway to Heaven." He sought a share of the estimated $500 million net worth of "Stairway to Heaven" through a contentious lawsuit that went all the way to the Supreme Court in 2020 before being ultimately dismissed.
