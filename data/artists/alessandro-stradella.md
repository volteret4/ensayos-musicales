## curiosities
**Early Musician Murder – Stabbed in Genoa** : Alessandro Stradella, an Italian classical composer active in the mid-1600s, was a significant star and influential with his six operas, 170 contadas, and numerous instrumental compositions. On February 25, 1682, he was found stabbed to death in a public square in Genoa. No one was ever convicted for his murder.
