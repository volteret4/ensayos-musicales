# artist - Maureen Tucker

## genres
- Alternative

## instruments
- Bass drum
- Cymbals
- Drums
- Felt mallets

## curiosities
**Joining The Velvet Underground (1965)** : Was the last person to join The Velvet Underground in 1965, after their first drummer quit and moved to Nepal (where he later died of malnutrition).
**Nickname** : Known as "Mo."
**Nigerian Drum Virtuoso Inspiration** : Stated that her unique drumming style and setup were inspired by a Nigerian drum virtuoso.
**Pioneering Female Drummer** : Broke significant gender barriers by becoming one of the first women drummers in a rock band, starting in 1965, when drumming was predominantly considered a "man's job." Although initially unnoticed, she was later recognized in retrospect as a musical pioneer.
**Unorthodox Drumming Style** : Played drums in an unconventional manner, standing up behind a non-traditional kit, using felt mallets instead of sticks. She laid the bass drum on its side and played it with mallets, rather than using a foot pedal, and "hardly used symbols" (cymbals).
