## curiosities
**Awareness of Bowie's Illness** : Donnie MacCalson was among the very few individuals, along with Tony Visconti, who was aware of David Bowie's terminal illness during the recording of Black Star. This knowledge was kept from most other musicians and collaborators. He spent countless hours in nearly interminable sessions, contributing significantly to the album's complex sound.
