## curiosities
**Concert at 42,080 Feet (2010)** : On June 28, 2010, James Blunt performed a concert on a Boeing 767 that took off from Stansted Airport in England. The plane reached a height of 42,080 feet during his performance, setting a new record for the highest gig, approximately two miles higher than the previous record held by Jamiroquai.
