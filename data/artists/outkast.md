# artist - Outkast

## songs
**Hey Ya (Year Not Specified)** : Outkast's song "Hey Ya" inadvertently created a problem for the camera company Polaroid. The lyric that encourages people to "shake it like a Polaroid picture" led many to believe that shaking or waving their photos would help them develop faster. However, Polaroid had to issue a public statement clarifying that shaking or waving can actually damage the image quality. "There's a line that encourages people to shake it like a Polaroid picture, which is what some people insist on doing while waiting for the photo to develop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO0K_N7Ys7I
