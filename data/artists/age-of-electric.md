## curiosities
**Origin and Location** : The band Age of Electric originated from Regina and Lanigan, Saskatchewan, establishing their regional roots in the Canadian music scene. The Dahle brothers, Ryan and Kurt, were key members. "the band was from Regina and Lannigan's Saskatchewan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
**Post-Breakup Member Activities** : Following Age of Electric's original run, members pursued various projects: the Dahle brothers formed Limblifter, Todd Kerns became the bass player and backup vocalist for Slash in The Conspirators, and three other members joined Matthew Good's backup band. These transitions highlight the interconnectedness of Canadian musicians. "Todd Kerns from Age of Electric has been working as the bass player in Back of Vocalist for Slash, yes, as in Guns and Roses Slash, in his band The Conspirators." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
**Reunion and Re-issue** : Age of Electric had a reunion show in Calgary in 2015, indicating a return to live performances. In 2017, their acclaimed album *Make a Pest a Pet* was re-issued, suggesting a renewed interest in their catalog.
