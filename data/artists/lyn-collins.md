## songs
**Think (About It) (1972)** : Released in 1972 on James Brown's People Records, this song was written by James Brown himself. It achieved commercial success, reaching number 9 on the Billboard R&B chart. More significantly, "Think (About It)" has been sampled over 3,000 times, making Lyn Collins the third most sampled artist mentioned. Its distinct funk sound is evident in later hip hop tracks, such as "It Takes 2" by Rob Base and DJ E-Z Rock (1988) and "Motown Philly" by Boyz II Men.
