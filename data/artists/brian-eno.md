**Origin of Ambient Music from Car Accident** : In 1975, a car accident left him bedridden for months; unable to adjust the volume of quiet music, he conceptualized music having properties like color or light, leading him to experiment with synthesizers to create "sound scapes" he termed ambient music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
