**Origin of Ambient Music (Car Accident)** : The genesis of ambient music for Brian Eno came after a 1975 car accident left him bedridden for several months. Unable to adjust the volume of very quiet "heart music" playing, he experienced an epiphany, realizing that music could possess properties similar to color or light in creating a harmonious and balanced sensory experience. "In 1975, he got into a car accident, and this led him bedridden for several months." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
