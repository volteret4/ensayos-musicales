## songs
**Got to Give It Up (1977)** : A 1977 song whose estate successfully sued Robin Thicke over similarities in his song "Blurred Lines." The court case relied on a "note by note" comparison of the musical notation, leading to a jury finding of infringement and an award of $7.2 million. "Marvin Gaye's 1977 song got to give it up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4cZfegexwwk

## curiosities
**Influence on Early Tragically Hip** : The Tragically Hip's early setlists largely consisted of blues rock standards, including material from Marvin Gaye. "Early stuff from the stones, material from the doors, the yard birds, Marvin Gaye, Otis Redding, Ben Morrison, that kind of thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnz_Ycd_q7s
**Killed by His Father** : Marvin Gaye was shot and killed by his father, Marvin Gaye Senior, on April 1, 1984. The incident occurred when Marvin Gaye attempted to intervene in a fight his father was having with his mother. His father, who may have been mentally impaired due to a brain tumor, was responsible for the fatal shooting. "About a year later, April 1, 1984, Marvin Gaye was shot by his father when he tried to intervene in a fight his father, Marvin Gaye's senior, was having with his wife, Marvin's mother." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q6dAX6HCjgw
