# artist - Marvin Gaye

## genres
- Soul/R&B (implied)

## songs
**Got to Give It Up (1977)** : This song was central to the "infamous blurred lines trial" where Robin Thicke's "Blurred Lines" was accused of sounding too similar. A jury found that "Blurred Lines" infringed upon "Got to Give It Up," leading to an award of $7.2 million to Gaye's estate. "They believe that blurred lines felt, and that's an important word, felt too much, like Marvin Gaye's 1977 song got to give it up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE

## curiosities
**Killed by His Father** : Marvin Gaye was shot and killed by his father, Marvin Gaye Senior, on April 1, 1984. The incident occurred when Marvin Gaye attempted to intervene in a fight his father was having with his mother. His father, who may have been mentally impaired due to a brain tumor, was responsible for the fatal shooting. "About a year later, April 1, 1984, Marvin Gaye was shot by his father when he tried to intervene in a fight his father, Marvin Gaye's senior, was having with his wife, Marvin's mother." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q6dAX6HCjgw
