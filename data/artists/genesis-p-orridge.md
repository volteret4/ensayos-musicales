# artist - Genesis P-Orridge

## curiosities
**Pandrogyny Project – Matching Breast Implants on Valentine's Day 2005 with Life Partner Lady Jaye** : Genesis P-Orridge, founder of Throbbing Gristle (who coined the term "industrial music" with their 1977 debut *The Second Annual Report*) and later Psychic TV, entered into a relationship with Psychic TV keyboardist Lady Jaye Breyer that was itself conceived as an ongoing performance art piece. The goal was to make the two people look increasingly alike through shared wardrobes, matching haircuts, permanent eyeliner, lip injections, oral surgeries, cheek implants, and ultimately matching breast implants, received together on Valentine's Day 2005. Their shared identity was to be called Ms. Breyer P-Orridge — a merged being making a statement about the oneness of humanity and the obsolescence of traditional gender roles. Genesis retained his original genitalia; Lady Jaye declined to have anything added. Lady Jaye died on October 9, 2007, from a previously undiagnosed heart condition possibly connected to her long-term battle with stomach cancer.
