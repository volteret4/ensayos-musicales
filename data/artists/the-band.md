## songs
**The Weight (1968)** : Written by Robbie Robertson for the album `Music From Big Pink`, this song features cascading lines of melody combined with "mock serious lyrics" that bring an Old Testament character face-to-face with a 1970 rock musician. It was notably heard in the movie `Easy Rider` and performed live during the 1970 Festival Express, with Arkansas native Levon Helm on lead vocals. "Robbie Robertson's The Weight, written for the group's first capital album, Music From The Big Pink in 1968, and heard, and by the way, in the movie Easy Rider, cascading lines of melody, combined with mock series lyrics, to bring an old testament character face to face with a 1970 rock musician." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 59 - Bands and Booze on the Festival Express of 1970 | https://www.ftr70.com/
