# artist - Rick James

## labels
- Hypnosis Song Fund (as owner of catalog)

## songs
**Importance as a Songwriter, Arranger, Producer** : Rick James is highlighted not just for "Super Freak," but as a serious player, songwriter, arranger, and producer, whose influence was significant for artists like Prince. The Hypnosis Song Fund, as catalog custodians, feels a responsibility to celebrate him as one of the great artists of all time. "Rick James is much more than just Super Freak, right? If you go back and you listen to Prince, Prince will tell you how important Rick James was and Prince will tell you that, you know, you don't get to Prince without Rick James being there first. Rick James was a serious player. He was a serious songwriter. He was a serious arranger. He was a serious producer. You know, Rick James was the real deal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pe40SqDrpZ0
**Minor Birds Membership in Toronto** : Rick James was in Toronto with the Minor Birds, alongside Neil Young, 13 or 14 years before the release of "Super Freak," demonstrating an early connection to the Canadian music scene. "Rick James and obviously Rick was in Toronto with the minor birds in Neil Young, you know, 13 or 14 years before Super Freak." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pe40SqDrpZ0
**Super Freak (Year)** : This song has been interpolated into Nicki Minaj's "Super Freaky Girl" (a US number one hit) and MC Hammer's "U Can't Touch This." The Hypnosis Song Fund, as custodians of Rick James' catalog, aims to ensure his legacy is celebrated and his music continues to generate income through new uses in movies, TV, video games, samples, and interpolations. "Nicki Minaj's Super Freaky Girl, which is an interpolation of Rick James Super Freak, which obviously was also interpolated into MC Hammer's. You can't touch this." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pe40SqDrpZ0
