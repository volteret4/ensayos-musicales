## songs
**Bring the Noise (1991) - Anthrax Collaboration** : Public Enemy's 1987 track "Bring the Noise" received a popular metal update in 1991 through a collaboration with speed metal band Anthrax. This fusion proved to many that a "middle ground" existed between guitar-based rock and full-on rap, characterized by aggressive sound and heavy beats, which was popular in car stereos and clubs. "they teamed up with Public Enemy for a metal update of their 1987 track Bring the Noise." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PqOJ3YiFgYE
