## curiosities
**Battle of Bands Win Led to Bob Rock's First Ever Production Credit** : The band started as the K-Tels in 1978 in Vancouver but were threatened with legal action by the cheap record company K-tel International and renamed themselves Young Canadians. Frontman Art Bergman would remain a presence in Canadian alt-rock for decades, ultimately being made a Member of the Order of Canada in 2020. The band won a local Battle of the Bands contest that awarded them studio time at a new facility called Little Mountain Sound. The engineer assigned to these unknown contest winners was a young man named Bob Rock — it was the first time Rock ever produced anything. He went on to produce Metallica, Mötley Crüe, Bon Jovi, The Cult, the Tragically Hip, and many others.
