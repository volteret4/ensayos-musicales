## curiosities
**Broadway Musical Character** : John Bechtel is the touring keyboard player for Ministry and has also played with Fear Factory, Killing Joke, and Prong. He is a character in both the graphic novel *Funhouse* and its subsequent Broadway musical adaptation, which were created by his sister, Alice Bechtel. "John is actually a character in both, but Alice is best known for creating the Bechtel Test, which is the test that measures the representation of women in fiction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
