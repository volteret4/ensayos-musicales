## curiosities
**Death (1941)** : His groundbreaking career was cut short when he died of tuberculosis on March 3rd, 1941, at the age of 25. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Joining Benny Goodman (1939)** : Christian’s impact was solidified when he joined Benny Goodman's Big Band in 1939, where he was given the platform to improvise and solo. His work created the foundations for the role of the modern lead guitarist. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**The First Guitar Hero** : A Black musician from Oklahoma, Christian is recognized as the world's first guitar hero for transforming the electric guitar from a rhythm-only instrument into a lead solo instrument. He began innovating with guitar solos during jam sessions as early as 1931, eventually acquiring a new electric model in 1936. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
