## curiosities
**Background Vocals for "Spirit in the Sky"** : The Stovall Sisters were a gospel trio from Oakland, California, who provided the distinctive background vocals for Norman Greenbaum's hit song "Spirit in the Sky." "Also, in the background, the background singers, that's actually a gospel trio.
