# artist - Purvis Staples

## curiosities
**Facilitator of Mainstream Transition** : Purvis Staples, son of Pop Staples and brother to Mavis Staples, was instrumental in helping The Staples Singers transition from a traditional gospel sound to a more mainstream style, blending R&B, folk, and pop. However, he was not an active member of the group during their major hit-making period in the 1970s.
