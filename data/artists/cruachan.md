## curiosities
**First Celtic Metal Band** : Cruachan is identified as arguably the first Celtic metal band, formed in Dublin in 1992. "If we must point to the first Celtic metal band, we should probably go with Cruahan. They were formed in Dublin in 1992." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Influences and Style** : The band drew inspiration from the British metal band Skyclad and the folk stylings of Horslips, then incorporated death metal elements into their sound. This fusion created a sound described as "the darkest, heaviest, drinkingest pub music you've ever heard." "They were formed in Dublin in 1992, taking cues not only from a British metal band called Skyclad, but also the folk styleings of horse-lips. Then they added a little death metal to the mix, because you know, why not. What does that even sound like? Well, like the darkest, heaviest, drinkingest pub music you've ever heard." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
