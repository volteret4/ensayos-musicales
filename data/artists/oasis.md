## curiosities
**Entourage Involved in Air Rage (1998)** : A 30-member entourage, including Oasis, was with Liam Gallagher during his March 1998 Cathay Pacific air rage incident, leading the captain to threaten their removal.
**Entourage Involved in Air Rage Incident (March 1998)** : The band and their 30-member entourage were on the Cathay Pacific flight from Hong Kong to Perth, Australia, during Liam Gallagher's air rage incident, leading to a captain's threat of diversion.
**Influenced by The Beatles** : Oasis is described as a band heavily influenced by The Beatles.
**Sales Comparison** : The Cranberries' albums sold similarly or better than records by Oasis. Elastica's debut was the fastest-selling debut since the first Oasis album.
**Signed by Alan McGee** : Signed by Creation Records founder Alan McGee a couple of years after My Bloody Valentine's "Loveless" album was completed. Their detailed story is slated for a future program.
**Zach Starky as Drummer** : Zach Starky, son of Ringo Starr, joined Oasis in 2004 as an employee for their drumming duties.
