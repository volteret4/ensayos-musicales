## curiosities
**"Tommy" as Spiritual Awakening** : Roger Daltrey, decades after its release, reflected that *Tommy* came out at a critical time in America when youth were severely impacted by the Vietnam War and conscription. He viewed the album as a "spiritual awakening" that deeply resonated with people and captured their imaginations due to its profound musical and thematic connections. "And it came out at a time particularly in America where the youth of America were really being, you know, hammered by the Vietnam War. Conscription being sent off to Vietnam. So I think it was kind of spiritual awakening in some way.
