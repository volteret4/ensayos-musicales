## curiosities
**Proposed Reformation (2017)** : On July 19, 2017, Chester Bennington sent an email to Matt Sorum, the former drummer of Guns N' Roses, expressing his enthusiasm for reforming Kings of Chaos, which he described as an "all-star covers band." This communication occurred less than 24 hours before Chester's death. "On July 19, he sent an email to Matt Sorum, the former drummer with guns and roses, saying that he would like to reform kings of chaos and all-star covers band.
