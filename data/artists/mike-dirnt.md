## curiosities
**Origin of Stage Name** : Mike Dirnt's stage name is directly derived from his Fender Precision Bass. His real name is Michael Ryan Pritchard, and he acquired the moniker "Dirnt" because he would frequently make a "Durant, Durant, Durant" sound while playing air bass and walking around school. "Mike Durant's real name is Michael Ryan Pritchard.
