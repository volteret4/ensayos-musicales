## curiosities
**Parody and Copyright Precedent (1956)** : Dickie Goodman was taken to court for copyright infringement for his 1956 novelty song "Flying Saucer," but a judge ruled it was parody and therefore a brand new composition, allowing him to continue having hits using this method until 1975.
