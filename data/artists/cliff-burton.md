## curiosities
**Ace of Spades Bunk Selection (September 27, 1986)** : The events leading to Cliff Burton's death began with the band drawing cards to decide bunk assignments on the tour bus. Cliff drew the Ace of Spades, giving him first choice, and he chose the bunk Kirk Hammett had been using, making the incident a tragic twist of fate. "Cliff drew the Ace of Spades which was the high card so he got to choose his bunk first." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Crushed by Bus in Sweden (September 27, 1986)** : During the bus accident, Cliff Burton was thrown through a window and the bus rolled on top of him. While trapped, rescue crews attempted to use a winch and crane to lift the bus, but the cable slipped, crushing him a second time, which was definitively fatal. "As a winch and crane were used to raise the bus off Cliff, the cable slipped and he was crushed a second time and that most certainly killed him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Fatal Tour Bus Accident (September 27, 1986)** : Cliff Burton died on September 27, 1986, in Sweden during Metallica's tour for "Master of Puppets." After drawing the Ace of Spades to choose his bunk on the bus, he slept in Kirk Hammett's usual spot. The bus hit black ice around 7 a.m., causing the driver to lose control, resulting in the bus leaving the road and rolling onto its side. "He was the one who was killed and then awful tour bus accident in Sweden back on September 27, 1986." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
