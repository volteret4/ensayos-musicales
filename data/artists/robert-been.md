# artist - Robert Been

## curiosities
**Adopted Stage Name Robert Turner** : Initially used the name Robert Turner to avoid association with his famous father.
**Breakthrough with Black Rebel Motorcycle Club** : His career breakthrough came with the formation of Black Rebel Motorcycle Club in 1998, a group that continues to be active.
**Collaborated with Father** : Robert worked extensively with his father, Michael Been, who served as a soundman for Black Rebel Motorcycle Club until his death.
**Filled in for Father at Reunions** : After Michael Been's death, Robert filled in for his father during some The Call reunions.
**Reverted to Original Name** : He later reverted to calling himself Robert Been.
**Son of Michael Been** : Robert Been, born August 22nd, 1978, is the son of Michael Been from the band The Call.
