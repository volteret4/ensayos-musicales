## curiosities
**Collaboration with Father** : Robert Been and his father, Michael Been, worked together extensively with Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, right up until Michael's death. Michael was serving as a soundman for the band at a festival in Brussels when he suffered a fatal heart attack on August 19th, 2010. "Dad and son worked together for quite a bit with Black Rebel Motorcycle Club right to the end. And I mean the real end. On August 19th of 2010, Dad was working as a soundman for his son's band, a festival in Brussels. When he had a fatal heart attack, he died right there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Identity and Band Formation** : Born on August 22nd, 1978, Robert Bean initially used the name Robert Turner in music to avoid association with his father, Michael Been. His career breakthrough came with the band Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, formed in 1998 and still active today. He later reverted to using his birth name, Robert Bean. "On August 22nd 1978, he had a son named Robert Bean. When he got into music, he called himself Robert Turner, just so people wouldn't associate him with his father. His break came with a band called Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, a great group formed in 1998 and one that continues today. He's since reverted to calling himself Robert Bean." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
