# artist - Mick Ronson

## member of
- David Bowie

## curiosities
**Collaboration with David Bowie** : As David Bowie's Spiders From Mars guitarist, Mick Ronson collaborated with Bowie to produce Lou Reed's 1972 album, *Transformer*. Their work helped Reed find a new sound, demonstrating Ronson's influence beyond his guitar playing. "Bowie and his spiders from Mars guitarist, Mick Ronson, helped Lou find a new sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4
**Death (April 1993)** : Mick Ronson, who was David Bowie's guitarist and Morrissey's producer, died in April 1993 from cancer. "Mick Ronson, David Bowie's guitarist and Morrissey's producer, Cancer April 1993." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ua93Ui1u6UI
**Top of the Pops Performance (July 6, 1972)** : As guitarist for The Spiders from Mars, Mick Ronson performed with David Bowie on Top of the Pops on July 6, 1972, wearing a gold one-piece costume. His relaxed demeanor and the public display of affection from Bowie (an arm draped around his shoulders) were notable and impactful. "Onstage with him was his guitarist, Mick Ronson, dressed in a gold one piece." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
