# artist - The Icicle Works

## genres
- Alt Rock

## songs
**Birds Fly (Whisper to a Scream) (1983)** : This song initially reached number two on the British charts upon its UK release. However, for its North American debut, the American label insisted on changes, including a remix that removed a spoken-word part, a shortening of the band's name to "Icicle Works," and a reversal of the title to "Whisper to a Scream Birds Fly" for the US market, where it charted at number 37 on the Top 40. In Canada, the original mix, title, and band name were retained, and the song reached number 19 on the charts. It has accumulated 18.4 million combined Spotify plays and YouTube views, with a one-hit wonder score of 2.26 out of 10. "In the UK, the song was called Birds Fly Whisper to a Scream. But for the US, it had to be Whisper to a Scream Birds Fly. And this worked sort of. The song reached number 37 in the US on the top 40 charts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_qxdftwrX3E

**Record Label Interference** : The band's American record label significantly intervened with their hit single, demanding a remix, shortening the band's name from "The Icicle Works" to "Icicle Works," and reversing the song's title for the US release. "A remix followed, which removed a spoken word bit in the original. The group's name had to be shortened from V. Isicle Works to just Isicle Works. And for some weird marketing reason, I have no idea why the title was reversed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_qxdftwrX3E
