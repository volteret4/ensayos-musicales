**Boy with Uke (Charlie Yang) – Korean-American; Anonymous on TikTok for 2 Years Before Revealing Identity** : Boy with Uke is the stage name of Charlie Yang, a Korean-American musician who began posting music to TikTok anonymously, performing in a mask. He maintained this for two years before revealing his face and real name on an Instagram post, saying that performing in a mask had given him confidence at first but had become a hindrance.
