## curiosities
**Influence on Dan Auerbach** : Junior Kimbrough, a blues singer, significantly influenced Dan Auerbach in high school. His music was a pivotal inspiration that led Auerbach to dedicate himself to playing the guitar full-time, ultimately leading to The Black Keys' tribute album, *Chulahoma*. "Dan was a big fan of this guy in high school.
