## curiosities
**Filled in for James Hetfield Twice** : John Marshall has twice stepped in to perform for James Hetfield of Metallica when Hetfield was unable to play. One instance occurred after Hetfield broke his arm skateboarding, and another when he was injured by ill-timed pyrotechnics. "He filled in twice for Hetfield. Once after Hetfield broke his arm while skateboarding and another time when he was badly synched by some ill-time pyro." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
**Guitar Tech for James Hetfield** : John Marshall is the dedicated guitar tech for James Hetfield of Metallica. He previously played guitar for the band Metal Church before taking on this role. "John Marshall is the guitar tech for Metallica's James Hetfield." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
