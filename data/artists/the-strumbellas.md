## curiosities
**Craigslist Ad for Members** : Simon Ward, the songwriter, used Craigslist to find additional musicians for the band, leading to the recruitment of two members who were not originally from Lindsay. "The other two came aboard after Simon placed an ad on Craig's list looking for more players and we can say that the strombellas officially came together in 2008." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Discovery by Daniel Glass** : The band came to the attention of Daniel Glass, who heads Glassnote Records. Their manager sent samples of the band's third album, leading Glass to fly to Toronto to see them perform live, ultimately resulting in a record deal. "But then they came to the attention of Daniel Glass at Glass Note Records. The strombellas manager made contact, sent them samples of the band's third album and waited by the phone. But then it rang. We liked the record and Daniel Glass is flying to Toronto to see the band live. Everybody got along and the deal was struck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Early Career Struggles and Toronto Trips** : After officially forming in 2008, The Strumbellas spent a couple of years "slugging it out," making numerous trips from Lindsay to Toronto to play in local bars and clubs. This period helped them gain experience and produce early recordings. "They slugged it out for a couple of years making the trip down to Toronto countless times to play the bars and clubs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Initial Recognition and Sales Figures** : Before their major breakthrough, The Strumbellas released one EP and two well-regarded albums. They received a couple of Juno nominations in the Roots and Traditional category, with sales typically around 15,000 copies per album. "There was one EP and two well regarded albums but no real notoriety other than a couple of Juno nominations in the Roots and Traditional category and sales per album were at the 15,000 copy level." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Lindsay, Ontario Roots - Population 20,354** : The Strumbellas' roots are in Lindsay, Ontario, a town of 20,354 people located northeast of Toronto. Four of the six band members originate from this area. "Next we're going to move to Lindsay Ontario, a place of 20,354 souls northeast of Toronto. There lie the roots of the strombellas." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
