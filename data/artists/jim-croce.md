**Bad, Bad Leroy Brown (1973)** : This song was the winner of the 1973 Billboard Song of the Summer. It is described as "goofy, slap happy, and sturdy" and was notable for being the only song of the summer from the first half of the 70s that was not a ballad, giving it a distinct advantage with its tempo.
