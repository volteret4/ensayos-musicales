## songs
**Nobody Does It Better (1977) – Title Theme** : Carly Simon performed "Nobody Does It Better" as the title theme for the 1977 James Bond film. This song was part of a series of Bond themes predominantly sung by women during the Roger Moore era, characterized by a more mature, adult contemporary sound. **You're So Vain** : In this well-known song, Carly Simon writes about a specific individual, who was later revealed to be Warren Beatty. This contributed to the 70s trend of singer-songwriters incorporating autobiographical elements into their music.
