## curiosities
**Artisanal Approach to Sampling** : J Dilla's music is grouped with that of Cut Chemist and DJ Shadow as exemplifying an artisanal, pure craftsmanship approach to the sampling process. This refers to the manual, ear-driven method of creating tracks with limited technology, which gave their music a distinctive, raw quality marked by subtle imperfections. **Manual Sampling Techniques** : J Dilla's work is referenced as an example of 90s instrumental hip hop where samples were triggered manually with fingers, known as "finger drumming." This technique led to characteristic minor desynchronizations and timing issues, similar to those found in DJ Shadow's music, because artists had to adjust sample timings by ear. "esto nos pasa en, por ejemplo, en discos de JDL, o en muchos discos de hip hop instrumental que se hacían con Sample que todo se hacía disparando a mano con los dedos, el finger, graming, todos los samples que iba a disparando a mano entonces." ← Music Radar Clan > DJ SHADOW - Endtroducing...
