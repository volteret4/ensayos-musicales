# artist - Bédito

## curiosities
**Challenging LGBTQ+ Stereotypes with Nude Magazine Cover** : Bédito, the singer of Debosi, is openly lesbian and comfortable with her identity. She was particularly revolutionary for appearing completely naked on a magazine cover. This action challenged the media's tendency to stereotype LGBTQ+ individuals, especially by showing that not all non-heterosexual people conform to the "handsome, fit, fantastic life" image often sold by the press, instead presenting a normal homosexual woman without those physical stereotypes or complexes. She spoke freely about her identity and made visible that not all parts of the homosexual community or non-heterosexual individuals fit into the stereotypes propagated.
