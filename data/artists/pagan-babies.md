## songs
**Best Sunday Dress** : This song is attributed to the band Pagan Babies, which featured Courtney Love and Cat Bjelland. It is speculated that this track "may or may not have been part of any live set by Sugar Baby Doll," suggesting a continuity of material between the two short-lived groups. "That's best Sunday dress from their band Pagan Babies, which I think managed to stay together for about 20 minutes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qss5kC2FUx0

## curiosities
**Post-Sugar Baby Doll Collaboration** : After Sugar Baby Doll broke up in late 1985, Courtney Love and Cat Bjelland stayed together to form a new group called Pagan Babies. This band was another "pre-Riot Grrrl band" and, despite its brief existence, represents a link in the early collaborations of these influential musicians. "But when the band broke up in late 1985, Courtney and Cat stayed together long enough to be in a new group called Pagan Babies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qss5kC2FUx0
