**Steve Mackey Death (March 2, 2023) – Undisclosed Illness, Age 56; Produced Florence and the Machine's Lungs; Appeared in Harry Potter** : Steve Mackey joined Pulp in 1989 and was their bassist through the glory years of the 1990s Britpop era before leaving around 2002. He became a producer and remix artist, working with Cornershop, The Kills, and The Horrors, and co-produced Florence and the Machine's debut album *Lungs*. He also worked as a music director and sound designer for institutions including the Louvre, the Museum of Modern Art, and a Shanghai museum. Harry Potter fans may know him as one of the Weird Sisters in *Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire* — a group that also featured Jarvis Cocker of Pulp and Johnny Greenwood and Phil Selway of Radiohead. When Pulp announced a reunion in early 2023, Mackey did not want to participate — in retrospect, probably because he was already ill. His death was announced March 2, 2023, having been in hospital since Christmas 2022 with an undisclosed condition. He was 56. "In retrospect, that's probably because he was ill." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-JPLLJyOid8
