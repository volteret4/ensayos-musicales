## albums
**Different Class (1995)** : This album was released about a month after Oasis's *Morning Glory* and prominently explored the notion of class in British society. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg
**His 'n' Hers (1994)** : The band's fourth album, released in 1994, was a "breakthrough" after languishing in obscurity for over a decade. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg

## songs
**Common People (1995)** : The first single from *Different Class*. It became a hit, based on Jarvis Cocker's experience with a fellow student, Sophia Focca, at St. Martin's College of Art and Design. Focca, from a well-off family, seemed to be "interested in slumming with her school kids," an attitude Jarvis embellished for artistic purposes. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg

## curiosities
**Long Road to Success (1978-1994)** : Founded by Jarvis Cocker in 1978, the band "languished in obscurity for a decade and a half," barely a "footnote to British music history" until their 1994 breakthrough album. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg
**Post-Britpop Status (Late 1997)** : As Britpop officially ended, the band "went on an indefinite hiatus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg
