**Death by Suicide (April 5, 1994)** : Kurt Cobain died in the early morning of Tuesday, April 5, 1994, by suicide via shotgun, preceded by a massive heroin overdose. His body was found approximately two weeks later, on April 8. His family and friends were aware he was in trouble, especially after a previous suicide attempt at a hotel in Rome. On March 25, 1994, an intervention was held at his Seattle home with his wife Courtney Love, bandmates Chris Novoselic and Dave Grohl, touring guitarist Pat Smear, and members of Nirvana's management and legal team, lasting five hours. He seemed convinced to seek help and flew to LA on March 30 to check into the Exodus Recovery Center.
