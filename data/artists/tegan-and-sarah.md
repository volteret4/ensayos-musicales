## curiosities
**Awards and Honors** : Throughout their career, Tegan and Sarah have received numerous accolades, including multiple Juno Award wins and several shortlistings for the Polaris Music Prize. They were also honored with the prestigious General Governor's Performing Arts Award. "Along the way, they've been nominated for multiple June Awards, winning several. They've been shortlisted for the Polaris Music Prize a couple of times." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11UsTJQyQds
**Early Career and Name Change** : Starting at age 15, Tegan and Sarah began writing songs and performing. By 1998, they were playing professional gigs as "Sarah and Tegan," but reversed the order of their names to "Tegan and Sarah" to clarify they were a duo, as the original name caused confusion, sounding like a solo performer named "Sarah and Tegan." "So reversing the order of their names made it clear that we were dealing with the duo, Tegan and Sarah." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11UsTJQyQds
**Early Touring Challenges** : Their journey to success involved humble beginnings, with early tours consisting of traveling from gig to gig on a Greyhound bus. "They've come a long way since those first tours when they went from gig to gig on a Greyhound bus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11UsTJQyQds
**Ice Cream Sandwich Collaboration** : In a unique initiative supporting same-sex marriages, Tegan and Sarah partnered with a maker of ice cream sandwiches to raise awareness and funds for the cause. "They got together with the maker of some ice cream sandwiches that supported same sex marriages." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11UsTJQyQds
**Industry Recognition and Collaborations** : Their talent garnered attention from prominent musicians; Jack White was such a fan that The White Stripes recorded one of their songs. They also developed friendships with members of Death Cab for Cutie and embarked on tours with acclaimed artists such as Neil Young, Weezer, The Black Keys, Cake, Bryan Adams, and Sious and Colour. "Jack White was such a fan that the white stripes recorded one of their songs. They became friends with members of Death Cab for Cutie." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11UsTJQyQds
**LGBTQ+ Activism and Philanthropy** : Both openly gay and married, Tegan and Sarah are passionate activists in the LGBT community. They established the Tegan and Sarah Foundation, which advocates for "economic justice, health and representation for LGBTQ girls and women." Their philanthropic efforts include supporting various causes and movements across borders and donating proceeds from ticket and merchandise sales. "There's the Tegan and Sarah Foundation, which fights for, and here's the quote, economic justice, health and representation for LGBTQ girls and women." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11UsTJQyQds
