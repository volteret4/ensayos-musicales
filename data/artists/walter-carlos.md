# artist - Walter Carlos

## instruments
- Synthesizer

## albums
**Switched-On Bach (1968) – All-Electronic Classical Album** : This album was recorded by classical musician Walter Carlos using synthesizers, notably without a symphony orchestra, which was considered "heresy" at the time. Carlos collaborated with Dr. Bob Moog to perfect the synthesizer's sounds and control, and the album went on to sell more than a million copies. "And the result was an all-electronic 1968 album switched on Bach, which sold more than a million copies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0

## curiosities
**A Clockwork Orange Score (Early 1970s)** : Walter Carlos composed the all-electronic score for Stanley Kubrick's movie *A Clockwork Orange*. The profits generated from these bold synthesizer experiments later funded a sex change operation. **Sex Change and New Identity** : After achieving success with pioneering electronic music and film scores, Walter Carlos underwent a sex change operation, funded by the profits from these ventures.
