**Professional Wrestling Involvement** : Billy Corgan's involvement in professional wrestling began in 2001 when he attended an ECW card and, after being insulted, was invited to hit a wrestler with a guitar. Ten years later, he founded his own league, Pro Resistance Wrestling. This evolved into work with Non-Stop Action Wrestling in 2015, where he became president in 2016. He left that league to purchase the National Wrestling Alliance (NWA) in 2017, one of the oldest pro wrestling leagues (founded 1948), where he also serves as president, effectively reviving the organization. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
