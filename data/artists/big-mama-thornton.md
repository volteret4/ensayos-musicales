## curiosities
**Annoyed by Lieber's Direction** : Big Mama Thornton was reportedly annoyed at Jerry Lieber for instructing her on how to sing "Hound Dog," one of the very first songs written by Lieber and Stoller. This incident highlights Lieber's characteristic approach to directing lead vocalists.
