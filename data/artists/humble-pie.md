## curiosities
**"Heavy Metal-Laden Crap Rock Band" Review (1970)** : In a November 12, 1970, review in Rolling Stone, writer Mike Saunders described the English band Humble Pie as a "noisy, unmelodic, heavy metal-laden crap rock band." This early print usage of "heavy metal" in a musical context was critical and not yet a formal genre descriptor.
