# artist - Stryper

## genres
- Christian Metal
- Heavy Metal

## curiosities
**Acronym Meaning** : The band's name, STRIPER, serves as an acronym for "Salvation Through Redemption, Yielding Peace, Encouragement, and Righteousness." This acronym was humorously described as "about a strain and acronym as you're ever going to find." "By the way, Striper stands for salvation through redemption, yielding peace, encouragement, and righteousness, which is about a strain and acronym as you're ever going to find." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
**Distinctive Stage Show** : Stryper was known for their unique stage presence, which included dressing in "bumblebee spandex" outfits and dramatically firing Bibles into the audience using "specially designed cannons." "They all dressed in bumblebee spandex and fired bibles into the crowd using specially designed cannons." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
**Mainstream Breakthrough** : Stryper, an Orange County band, is credited with successfully breaking Christian rock into mainstream consciousness. "It took an orange county band called Striper to break through into mainstream consciousness." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
**Musical Style and Audience** : Their sound was characterized as a blend of Van Halen and Def Leppard. Despite their explicitly wholesome Christian message, Stryper successfully attracted the attention and following of metalheads. "Striper sounded a lot like a cross between Van Halen and Def Leopard. And despite their wholesome message, they attracted the attention to metalheads." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6cbL0zLQeIs
