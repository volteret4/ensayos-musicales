# artist - Tori Amos

## curiosities
**Alleged Inventor of Pumpkin Spice Latte (1995)** : A 1995 review of Tori Amos playing in Seattle (Starbucks' home city) quotes her telling the crowd, "You all have your Starbucks things. Well, I have one that tastes like pumpkin pie. It's my own invention. It's my contribution to Halloween, like a little witch warmer." This precedes Starbucks' official launch of the Pumpkin Spice Latte in 2003, leading to the theory that Amos invented the popular beverage. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
