# artist - Fifth Column

## genres
- Pre-Riot Girl

## albums
**To Sir with Hate (1985)** : This was the first album released by the Toronto-based band Fifth Column, from which the song "Where Are They Now?" originates. The band was known for its DIY approach, releasing material on cassette and publishing its own fanzine. "The first Fifth Column album was entitled To Sir with Hate." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qss5kC2FUx0

## songs

## curiosities
**Toronto DIY Pioneers** : Formed in Toronto in the early 1980s, Fifth Column embraced a strong do-it-yourself ethos, self-releasing music on cassette and publishing their own fanzine. Their fanzine often included cassette compilations featuring other like-minded bands, making them a significant pre-Riot Grrrl influence from Canada. "In the early 80s there was a band called Fifth Column, they were formed in Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qss5kC2FUx0
