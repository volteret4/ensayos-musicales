# artist - The Velvet Underground

## albums
**Unreleased Acetate (1966)** : In April 1966, an acetate was secretly made after hours at Scepter Studios in New York City, containing material that would later appear on The Velvet Underground's debut record but with different recordings. This unique item was lost for years until Montreal record collector Warren Hill discovered it at a New York flea market in Chelsea, purchasing it for 75 cents. After an initial high bid of $155,000 on eBay fell through, it was successfully sold to a high-end dealer in 2014 for $25,200. Recordings from this "crackly and rough" acetate have since been made publicly available.
