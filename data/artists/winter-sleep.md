## curiosities
**Hamilton Local Act and Influence** : Winter Sleep was an indie rock band that played regularly in Hamilton venues such as the Casbah and Underground around 2006, a period described as the "Golden Age" for indie rock. Their guitar-driven style was a musical influence on Arkells' early sound, notably on songs like "The Boss Is Coming." "So every week at the club it seemed like at the Casbore, the Underground there was bands like Winner Sleep or Joel Plasket, Constantine, Stars, Sam Roberts, Arcade for all of these bands just coming through Hamilton that we got to go." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA ← winter-sleep
