# artist - Cher

## songs
**Believe (1998)** : This groundbreaking song is notable for its extensive and exaggerated use of the Auto-Tune effect, pushing it to its maximum setting to create a distinctive "robotic sound." "Believe" was instrumental in popularizing Auto-Tune not merely as a subtle pitch correction tool, but as a prominent and overt artistic effect, subsequently influencing numerous artists to explore its creative capabilities. "But then along came Share and the song Believe in 1998, in which the auto-tune effect was maxed out, resulting in this robotic sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uEe_HLUay5M
**Half-Breed (1973)** : Cher released this single in 1973, which directly addressed her claimed one-eighth Cherokee heritage and the experience of being of mixed descent. "She even had a single in 1973 called Halfbreed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
