## songs
**Mama Told Me Not To Come** : Randy Newman wrote this song, which Three Dog Night turned into their first number one hit. Newman recorded his own version a couple of years after Eric Burden included it on his solo album, "Eric Is Here," singing it "earnestly" to convey the story of a straight-laced guy at a wild party. He admitted he didn't initially like Three Dog Night's rendition until the royalty checks started coming in.
