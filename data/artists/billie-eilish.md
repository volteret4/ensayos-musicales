## songs
**No Time to Die (2020) – Youngest Bond Theme Artist** : Billie Eilish, along with her brother Finneas O'Connell, wrote and recorded the theme song for the James Bond film "No Time to Die" over three days on a tour bus in late 2019. It was recorded in England with a 70-piece orchestra. The song was released on February 13, 2020, to coincide with a postponed movie release, but the film faced further delays due to COVID-19, leaving the song without a concurrent film for 18 months. Eilish was 18 at the time, making her the youngest person ever to record a Bond theme. The song later won a Grammy Award for Best Song, Written for Visual Media. "She and Brother Finneas wrote and recorded it over three days on a tour bus in late 2019." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo
