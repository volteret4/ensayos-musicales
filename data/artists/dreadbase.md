# artist - Dreadbase

## curiosities
**"Bittersweet Symphony" Remix (mid-90s)** : Dreadbase is a DJ/producer whose remix of The Verve's "Bittersweet Symphony" in the mid-90s was a life-changing song for Ash. This particular remix profoundly inspired Ash's vision for USS, motivating him to integrate melody and simplicity into electronic music, aiming to "bring melody to the robot" and "bring a heart to the robot." "He did this remix of Bittersweet Symphony in the mid 90s and I heard that and I said that's what we're that's what I need to do." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
