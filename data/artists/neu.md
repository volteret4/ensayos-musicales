**Pioneers of Motorikbeat** : Formed in 1971, the group Neu! (German for "new") pioneered the Motorikbeat, a distinctive straight 4/4 rhythm that conveys a feeling of movement, often associated with driving on Germany's motorways. This approach became popular in the German underground and influenced artists in Britain and pre-CBGB New York.
