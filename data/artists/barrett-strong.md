## songs
**Jamie (Year unknown)** : Barrett Strong co-wrote this song with Mickey Stevenson and originally recorded it. After Strong left Motown, the label reused his backing track for Eddie Holland to record new vocals, turning it into a top 30 hit. This success for Holland must have been "galling" for Strong, who had left partly due to a lack of hits. **Money (That's What I Want) (1959)** : Recorded by Barrett Strong for the Tamla label in 1959, this song was written by Janie Bradford and Berry Gordy. It became the first hit for the new Motown Records in 1960 and has been covered many times, notably by The Beatles in 1963.
