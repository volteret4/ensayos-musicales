# artist - Barrett Strong

## labels

## songs
**Money (That's What I Want) (1959)** : Recorded by Barrett Strong for the Tamla label in 1959, this song was written by Janie Bradford and Berry Gordy. It became the first hit for the new Motown Records in 1960 and has been covered many times, notably by The Beatles in 1963.
