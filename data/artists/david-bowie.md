**"Bowie Quits" Concert (July 3, 1973)** : At the Hammersmith Odeon in London, Bowie staged a theatrical "retirement" for his Ziggy Stardust character, announcing that it was "the last show that we'll ever do." This event was captured by documentary filmmaker D.A. Pennebaker. Despite the dramatic announcement, Bowie did not actually quit, returning to the stage for a TV special less than two months later and continuing his recording and touring career.
