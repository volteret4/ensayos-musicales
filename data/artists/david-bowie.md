## albums
**Blackstar** : David Bowie's final album is interpreted by some conspiracy theorists as confirming his "anointment" of Kanye West, due to the album's title "Blackstar" aligning with Kanye's identity as a "Black Star." Theorists claim the album is "loaded with all kinds of coded messages," citing the lyric "I'm a Black Star, not a gangster" as an obvious reference to Kanye. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg
**Blackstar (2016)** : Released on Friday, January 14, 2016, two days before his death. The album is described as excellent and contains many references to mortality and his impending death. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
**Hunky Dory (1971) – Earnings Dispute** : This album's earnings, dating back to 1971, were included in the financial settlement terms when David Bowie sought to terminate his contract with manager Tony DeFries and Main Man in April 1975. Main Man was to receive half of Bowie's earnings from this album through an upcoming live album. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Live in Santa Monica '72 (2008) – From Bootleg to Official Release** : Originally recorded during an October 20, 1972 performance at the Santa Monica Civic Auditorium during the _Ziggy Stardust_ tour, this recording circulated as a bootleg for decades. After legal disputes and semi-official releases, it was finally issued as an official album on June 3, 2008. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OrHtPVBjXw4
**Santa Monica 72 (2008) – Live Album Release** : This live album started as a live radio broadcast, subsequently morphed into a bootleg, then received a semi-official release, before finally achieving full legal release in 2008. The record's complex journey caused considerable difficulties for Bowie, his manager Tony DeFries, and others involved. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Station to Station (1976)** : A platinum-selling album that David Bowie claims to have no memory of recording, writing, releasing, or performing due to severe drug use. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
**The Next Day (2013)** : reached number one on the charts, making it his most successful album in 30 years. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Ziggy Stardust (1972) – The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars** : Cited as one of the most significant rock releases of all time, commonly found in rock fans' collections. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VR5Fncdc2kQ
**Ziggy Stardust and The Spiders for Mars (1972) – Seminal Album** : This album was a huge seller in England through late 1972 and into 1973, complete with major hit singles, propelled by Bowie's Ziggy Stardust alter-ego. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
**Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars (1972)** : This album features one of the most iconic album covers of the 1970s, depicting David Bowie posing on Heddon Street in London, an area near Regent Street and Oxford Circus. Conspiracy theories suggest this 1972 image was taken to predict the rise of Kanye West. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg

## songs
**Five Years (1972)** : This song from the *Ziggy Stardust* album predicts the emergence of a "star man" destined to save humanity from doom. Conspiracy theorists connect this prophecy to Kanye West, noting the album's release on June 16, 1972, and Kanye's birth on June 8, 1977, which is described as "almost five years later to the day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg
**Lazarus (2016)** : released as a single prior to his death, upon close inspection, it showed that Bowie knew the end was near. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k

**Harsh Separation Terms (1975)** : The terms of David Bowie's separation from Tony DeFries in April 1975 were reportedly so severe that Bowie broke down and cried at the conference table, remaining in shock for a week. The agreement stipulated that Main Man would receive half of Bowie's earnings from all albums, retroactively from *Hunky Dory* (1971) up to an upcoming live album, and DeFries was guaranteed 16% of Bowie's gross earnings for seven subsequent years, through to 1982. Bowie signed these terms due to his intense desire to leave the contract. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
