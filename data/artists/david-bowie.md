# artist - David Bowie

## albums
**Heroes (1977) – Berlin Trilogy Album** : This album, released in 1977, is part of David Bowie's "Berlin trilogy" of records. The entire record was made in Studio Two of Hansa Studios, a location about 150 yards from the Berlin Wall, which added to the album's distinct atmosphere. "The whole record was made in Studio Two of Hansa Studios, which was maybe 150 yards from the Berlin Wall." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk
**Station to Station (1976) – Cocaine-Induced Amnesia** : Bowie had absolutely no memory of recording this album in 1976, a period during which he was heavily addicted to cocaine and alcohol, leading to extreme paranoia and a significant decline in his physical and mental health. "Bowie had no memory at all of recording the station to station album in 1976." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU

## songs
**Heroes (1977) – Multiple Inspirations** : This powerful title track from Bowie's 1977 album features artsy lyrics about two lovers finding optimism amid a world collapsing into death, including references to swimming with dolphins. Its meaning is layered, drawing inspiration from producer Tony Visconti's embrace with German singer Antonia Moss by the Berlin Wall, the Otto Mueller painting "Lovers Between Garden Walls," Alberto Denti di Pagano's 1956 short story "A Grave for a Dolphin," and a dream Bowie had about swimming with dolphins followed by a day with his girlfriend Claire Shenstone in East Berlin, watching soldiers. Bowie confirmed seeing Visconti and Moss, and Shenstone claimed their day directly inspired elements like dolphins, the wall, and gun shooting. "So which is true? All of them, apparently." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk
**Heroes (1977) – Verbatim Lyric Fragment** : This fragment appears to be a partial lyric or an echoing of the song's theme. "I can remember the man, the standing by the wall, the fire." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk

## curiosities
**"A Grave for a Dolphin" Story Connection** : The reference to swimming with dolphins in "Heroes" lyrics has a connection to a 1956 short story by Alberto Denti di Pagano, titled "A Grave for a Dolphin." This story told of an Italian soldier and a girl from Somalia involved in a doomed relationship during World War II. "Bowie remembered a short story written in 1956 by Alberto Denti di Pagano, entitled A Grave for a Dolphin." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk
**Attitude Towards Experimentation** : David Bowie's work helped trace the experimental attitude that defined post-rock, following The Velvet Underground's initial steps. "Then we trace this attitude towards experimentation through things like crowd rock, David Bowie, public image limited, and the no wave seen in New York." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
**Claire Shenstone's Day Out with Bowie** : During the recording of the *Heroes* album, Bowie spent a day with his then-girlfriend, model, and artist Claire Shenstone. The night before, Bowie had dreamt he was swimming with dolphins. The next day, they visited East Berlin and observed soldiers marching, an experience which Shenstone claims directly inspired the song's lyrics, specifically the references to dolphins, standing by the wall, and gunshots. "Shenstone claims that the song's lyrics were directly inspired by that day they spent together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk
**Cocaine Addiction and Paranoia in Los Angeles (1974-1976)** : Between 1974 and 1976, Bowie was deeply entrenched in cocaine and alcohol addiction, a state exacerbated by his move to Los Angeles, the "cocaine capital of North America." This led to severe cocaine-induced paranoia, a common reaction among users, with Bowie's focus on witches. "Between 1974 and sometime in 1976, Bowie was an absolute mess." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Encounter with Jimmy Page and Occult Beliefs (Mid-1970s)** : Bowie's paranoia regarding witches stemmed from an encounter with Led Zeppelin guitarist Jimmy Page in a Manhattan townhouse. Page was known for his deep involvement in the occult, and Bowie believed that this association had put his soul in danger. "This resulted from an encounter with Led Zeppelin guitarist Jimmy Page in a Manhattan townhouse." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU

**Hansa Studios' Historical Significance** : Studio Two of Hansa Studios, where David Bowie recorded his *Heroes* album, has a unique historical background. The building was originally a concert hall that was later used by the Gestapo as a ballroom during World War II. It was then repurposed into a recording studio by a pair of brothers in 1962. "The building was a former concert hall used by the Gestapo during World War II as a ballroom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk
**Hollywood Exorcism and Satanic Visions (Mid-1970s)** : While renting a house in Hollywood during his drug-fueled paranoia, Bowie reportedly observed Satan "doing the breaststroke" in his pool. This led him to fly a "white witch" from New York to perform an exorcism on the house. "After observing Satan doing the breaststroke and the pool of his rented Hollywood house, he had a white witch fly out from New York to perform an exorcism." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Late Touring Career Rider – Minimalist Demands** : In the late stages of his touring career, David Bowie became remarkably undemanding. His rider simply asked for a fruit bowl and that his dressing room be maintained at a specific temperature, between 14 and 18 degrees Celsius. "All he asked for was a fruit bowl and that he required his dressing room to be kept at a temperature between 14 and 18 degrees Celsius." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z2hvUDtBxho
**Move to West Berlin for Sobriety (Post-1976)** : Following his period of intense drug addiction and paranoia, Bowie, along with Iggy Pop, made a conscious decision to move to West Berlin to get sober, clean up, and dry out, seeking clarity from his drug-induced state. "It wasn't until he and Iggy Pop had a moment of clarity and decided that they needed to move to West Berlin to clean up, get sober and dry out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Obsession with Hitler and Nazi Sympathies (Mid-1970s)** : Bowie developed a dangerous obsession with Hitler and the Third Reich during his drug-addled period, frequently discussing Nazis in interviews. In 1976, he controversially told Playboy magazine that Hitler was one of the first rock stars, and was seen giving what appeared to be a Nazi salute to a crowd at a London train station. "And if that wasn't enough, Bowie had become obsessed with Hitler and the Third Reich." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Obsession with Witches and Bodily Fluids (1974-1976)** : During his period of intense drug use and paranoia, Bowie became convinced that "witches" were trying to steal his "precious bodily fluids." To prevent this, he began saving all his urine in a fridge and maintained a strict diet of bell peppers and milk to keep his "essence pure," leading his weight to drop below 90 pounds. "Specifically, he got it into his mind that witches, that's as specific as we can get, were out to steal Bowie's precious bodily fluids." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**Otto Mueller Painting Influence** : David Bowie was inspired by a painting titled "Lovers Between Garden Walls" by Otto Mueller. He and Iggy Pop encountered this artwork in a Berlin museum around the time *Heroes* was being recorded, adding another layer of artistic influence to the song's lyrical themes. "Bowie and his buddy Iggy Pop saw in a Berlin museum around this time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk
**Visconti and Moss Inspiration for "Heroes"** : Producer Tony Visconti provided a long-standing explanation for the lyrics of "Heroes," stating that David Bowie saw him embracing German singer Antonia Moss by the Berlin Wall. Visconti, from the West, and Moss, from the East, were depicted as hating the divided city but insisting on kissing by the wall as bullets flew from guard towers. Bowie later confirmed seeing the couple. "We've been told that one of the lovers is from the west, which is Visconti, and the other from the east, which is Moss." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DAjcCk9xYWk
