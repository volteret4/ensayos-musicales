**Boston Mosh Pit Lawsuit (1997)** : During Everclear's performance at The Paradise club in Boston on November 13, 1997, three members of the New England Patriots – quarterback Drew Bledsoe, backup quarterback Scott Zolak, and offensive lineman Max Lane (who weighed 305 pounds) – were in the mosh pit. Max Lane arced off the stage and landed on 23-year-old Tamika Messier, crushing her and causing severe neck, shoulder, and arm injuries, including two herniated discs and three fused vertebrae. Messier filed a $1.2 million lawsuit on December 10, 1997, against the club, Lane, Bledsoe, and Everclear, which was settled in March 1999, with Everclear contributing to the settlement and subsequently facing higher liability insurance premiums.
