## curiosities
**Tamika Messier Lawsuit (1997-1999)** : A lawsuit was filed against the band, the venue (The Paradise), Drew Bledsoe, Scott Zolak, and Max Lane after offensive lineman Max Lane stage-dived from their stage at The Paradise in Boston on November 13, 1997, landing on and severely injuring 23-year-old Tamika Messier. The case settled for $1.2 million in March 1999, with Everclear contributing to the settlement, which significantly impacted their liability insurance premiums. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
