# artist - Tim Carlton

## curiosities
**Opus Number One Co-Creator (1989)** : Tim Carlton, along with Derek Deal, recorded "Opus Number One" in 1989 for Cisco's Call Manager system. This six-minute piece is considered the most famous on-hold music ever created and was notably featured in a Bud Light Super Bowl commercial in 2023. "It's probably the most famous on hold music ever created." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8GU8ZpSgY8
