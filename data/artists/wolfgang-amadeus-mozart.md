## curiosities
**Pain Management Study with Ibuprofen** : Researchers at the University of Utah conducted a study revealing that listening to Mozart while taking ibuprofen significantly enhances pain relief. While ibuprofen alone reduced inflammation by 70%, the combination with Mozart music further decreased inflammation from 70% to 90%, suggesting a notably greater reduction in pain. Though currently tested on mice, these findings could lead to new human pain management methods if replicated in human trials.
