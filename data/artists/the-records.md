# artist - The Records

## genres

## curiosities
**New Wave Powerpop** : The Records are listed among the artists who contributed to the new wave take on Powerpop. They exemplify the integration of catchy melodies and sharp guitar work with the energy and attitude influenced by punk, marking Powerpop's entry into the alternative rock sphere.
