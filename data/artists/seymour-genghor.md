# artist - Seymour Genghor

## curiosities
**Painter and Audiovisual Director for "El Rabidud"** : Seymour Genghor, a personal friend of Royce Immorce and a well-known painter in the United Kingdom, was responsible for all the audiovisual creative work for Royce Immorce's solo album, "El Rabidud." His contributions included designing videoclips and personally painting Royce Immorce for the album's cover art, a painting that genuinely exists.
