## curiosities
**Nine Inch Nails as Opener** : In the spring of 1990, Nine Inch Nails opened for Peter Murphy during a tour. This provided an early touring platform for Trent Reznor's band, during which they performed a cover of a Queen song and experienced growing frustrations with their record label, TVT. "In the spring of 1990, Trent went on the road as the opener for Peter Murphy and one of the songs in the nine-inch nail set list at that time was this cover of an old song by Queen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M3nUQtja0qc
**Solo Career** : Following the breakup of Bauhaus, Peter Murphy embarked on a solo career. "indirectly gave us Peter Murphy" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
