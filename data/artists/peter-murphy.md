## curiosities
**Continued Loyal Fanbase** : Peter Murphy continues to maintain a loyal fanbase for his solo work. "Peter Murphy continues to have his loyal fans." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
**Devout Catholic Upbringing** : Peter Murphy was raised in a devout Catholic family, an aspect of his background that shaped his early life. "raised in a devote Catholic family." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Distinctive Appearance** : Murphy was characterized by his elegant, impossibly thin physique and prominent cheekbones, contributing to his distinctive visual identity. "elegant, impossibly thin, great cheekbones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Financial Struggles in Turkey** : By the mid-1990s, Peter Murphy faced financial difficulties in Turkey, as the costs of full-time Arabic study, combined with earnings from translating books and composing music for his wife's dance company, proved insufficient compared to his potential income from reuniting with his former bandmates. "Peter Murphy was running out of money in Turkey." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Iconic Maxell Commercials** : In 1981, Peter Murphy was chosen to appear in TV commercials for Maxell Cassettes, receiving 10,000 pounds for two shoots. He became the recognizable figure in the enduring magazine ads depicting a person sitting in a chair being "blown away" by music from a stereo, an image that remained in use for over 15 years. "The guy in the chair? That's Peter Murphy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
**Nine Inch Nails as Opener** : In the spring of 1990, Nine Inch Nails opened for Peter Murphy during a tour. This provided an early touring platform for Trent Reznor's band, during which they performed a cover of a Queen song and experienced growing frustrations with their record label, TVT. "In the spring of 1990, Trent went on the road as the opener for Peter Murphy and one of the songs in the nine-inch nail set list at that time was this cover of an old song by Queen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M3nUQtja0qc
**Nine Inch Nails as Opening Act** : During his tour to promote the "Deep" album, Peter Murphy introduced thousands of people to a then-little-known band from Cleveland, Nine Inch Nails, who served as his opening act. "he introduced thousands of people to his opening act. Little band out of Cleveland called Nine-inch Nails." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Pneumonia and Band Division** : Peter Murphy became severely ill with pneumonia just before Bauhaus was scheduled to record their next album. This led to the rest of the band recording "Burning from the Inside" without him, a process that revealed they could work effectively independently and contributed to the internal split of Bauhaus into two factions: Peter Murphy and everyone else. "And then Peter got very sick with pneumonia, just as the group was scheduled to start recording their next album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
**Post-Bauhaus Projects and Dali's Car** : After Bauhaus splintered on July 5, 1983, Peter Murphy briefly formed the band Dali's Car, which released one album before he embarked on a solo career. "Peter Murphy briefly formed a band called Dali's Car, which lasted for one album before he went solo." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
**Relocation to Turkey - Study of Islam** : In the spring of 1990, funded by the success of his "Deep" album, Peter Murphy moved his family to Turkey, where he began an intensive study of Islam and eventually converted to the faith, becoming a Muslim. "he began to study Islam, eventually becoming a Muslim." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Solo Career** : Following the breakup of Bauhaus, Peter Murphy embarked on a solo career. "indirectly gave us Peter Murphy" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
**Support for Nine Inch Nails** : Peter Murphy introduced thousands of people to the then-emerging band Nine Inch Nails from Cleveland, who served as his opening act during his tour to promote the "Deep" album. "he introduced thousands of people to his opening act. Little band out of Cleveland called Nine-inch Nails." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
**Withdrawal to Turkey and Islam** : In the early 1990s, Peter Murphy began to withdraw from Western society, moving his family to Turkey after the success of his 1990 album "Deep." There, he dedicated himself to studying Islam and eventually converted, financing his full-time Arabic studies and other pursuits through earnings from his music and other ventures, though these paled in comparison to potential reunion earnings. "In the spring of 1990, he released an album that sold enough copies to allow him to move his family to Turkey, where he began to study Islam, eventually becoming a Muslim." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
