## curiosities
**21st Century Tweetpop Descendant** : Beabadoobee is cited as a 21st-century artist whose Bedroom Pop music is a direct descendant of Tweetpop. This contemporary style, characterized by dreaminess, introspection, sensitivity, and vulnerability, is facilitated by the widespread availability of inexpensive and powerful recording technology, allowing for high-quality music production in intimate settings.
