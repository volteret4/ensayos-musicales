# artist - Julio Iglesias

## curiosities
**Soviet Blacklist - Neo-Fascism** : Julio Iglesias was listed on the USSR's 1985 "not recommended music" list, surprisingly categorized under "Neo-Fascism." This unexpected inclusion demonstrates the broad and sometimes inexplicable reach of Soviet censorship efforts to control cultural influences during the Cold War.
