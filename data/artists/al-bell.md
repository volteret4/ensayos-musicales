## curiosities
**Songwriting Credit for "I'll Take You There"** : Al Bell took the soul songwriting credits for "I'll Take You There," a hit song by The Staples Singers, a decision that reportedly displeased Mavis Staples, who had mostly ad-libbed the lyrics during recording.
