# artist - Young Kio

## curiosities
**Producer of "Old Town Road" Beat** : Young Kio, a Dutch record producer, created the beat for Lil Nas X's "Old Town Road" by manipulating a sample from Nine Inch Nails' instrumental track "34 Ghosts 4" and selling it for $30 on his online store, where Lil Nas X purchased it. His real name is Kioa Rokima. "He stumbled upon a track called 34 Ghosts 4, an instrumental from the 2008 Nine-H Nails album Ghost 1-4." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhdsufCUC2w
