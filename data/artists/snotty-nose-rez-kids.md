## curiosities
**Haisla Nation Origins** : The duo Snotty Nose Rez Kids are members of the Haisla Nation in British Columbia, having grown up just south of Kitimat, a location that informs their perspective and music. "Their Snottie Knows Res Kids, members of the Heistler Nation in BC, they grew up just south of Kitimat." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA ← snotty-nose-rez-kids
**Polaris Music Prize Finalists** : They were short-list finalists for the Polaris Music Prize in 2018 with their album "The Average Savage," connecting them to a prestigious Canadian music award. "This duo also has a Polaris Music Prize connection.
