## curiosities
**Conquering the Charts - British Blues Rock** : The Yardbirds are mentioned as one of the acts, alongside John Mayall's Bluesbreakers, that were conquering the charts during a good time for blues rock bands in England. This indicates their significant influence and success in the genre. **Early R&B Exploration** : The Yardbirds were among the British bands in the 1960s who attempted to play R&B, which aligned with the musical tastes of the Mod subculture. **Influence on Early Tragically Hip** : The Tragically Hip's early setlists largely consisted of blues rock standards, including material from The Yardbirds. **Jimmy Page Hearing "Dazed and Confused"** : While Jimmy Page was a member of The Yardbirds, he heard Jake Holmes perform his song "Dazed and Confused" in 1968, which he later reworked for Led Zeppelin, leading to a plagiarism dispute.
