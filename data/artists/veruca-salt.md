# artist - Veruca Salt

## members
- Louise Post (singer, songwriter)
- Nina Gordon (singer, songwriter)

## labels
- Geffen
- Minty Fresh

## albums
**American Thighs (1994)** : The band's debut record, released in the fall of 1994. The title is taken from a line in the AC/DC song "You Shook Me All Night Long."

## songs
**Seether (1994)** : The lead single from "American Thighs," a version of the song previously released on Minty Fresh. Written by Nina Gordon. Its pick-up by MTV led to the band's breakthrough.

## curiosities
**Band Name Inspiration** : The South African band Seether is named after Veruca Salt's song "Seether."
**Inspiration from Grunge Success** : Like many bands, they were inspired by the success of groups like Pearl Jam and Nirvana to start making their own music.
**Rapid Ascent** : Signed quickly due to labels actively seeking their style of music and their "damn catchy songs." They were approached by Minty Fresh for a single after only a couple of shows.
**Songwriting Approach** : Nina Gordon and Louise Post handled all the songwriting, with the rule that "whoever wrote the song got to sing it," rather than a collaborative approach.
**Touring with Hole** : The success of their single led Hole to sign them to open a tour.
