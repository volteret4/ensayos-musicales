# artist - Lionel Hampton

## instruments
- Fender Precision Bass (P-Bass)

## curiosities
**Early Adoption of Precision Bass** : Lionel Hampton's band is believed to be the first to incorporate the Fender Precision Bass into their lineup, with Monk Montgomery as their bassist. This marked a significant early adoption of the electric bass in a professional setting. "As far as we can tell, Lionel Hampton's band was the first to incorporate the P-Base. It appears that his guy was named Monk Montgomery." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
