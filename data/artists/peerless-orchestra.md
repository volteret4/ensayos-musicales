# artist - Peerless Orchestra

## curiosities
**Early Wax Cylinder Recording (1896)** : The Peerless Orchestra's medley of hit songs was recorded in 1896 on a wax cylinder, serving as a demonstration of Thomas Edison's phonograph technology. This early recording exemplifies how music could "now be captured and enjoyed wherever and whenever anyone wished" thanks to Edison's invention.
