# artist - Taj Khrishlow

## curiosities
**Co-Host of Architects of Art Podcast** : Taj Khrishlow is a co-host of the new podcast "Art Katex, the Architects of Art," which explores the creative journeys and processes of music video directors. "We have Taj Khrishlow and Director X. And we want to give you a bit of an introduction to what this new podcast is all about." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kbFfT0pKchI ← taj-khrishlow
**Founding Fella Production Company** : Taj Khrishlow, along with Director X, founded the production company Fella, which achieved success with the music video for "Topstar" by DJ Khaled, Drake, and Justin Bieber. "We broke our production company, Fella, with this music video for DJ Khaled, Drake and Bieber called Topstar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kbFfT0pKchI ← taj-khrishlow
**Pioneering Canadian Directors** : Taj Khrishlow is one of two individuals from Brampton, Ontario, who became internationally recognized for creating art through music videos. "And there were two kids from Brampton, Ontario that went out to make art that broke out to the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kbFfT0pKchI ← taj-khrishlow
