# artist - Katie Melua

## concerts
- **Troll A Gas Rig (2006) – Lowest/Deepest Concert Ever** : On October 1, 2006, British singer Katie Melua and her band performed for 20 oil rig workers on the Stay Oil's Troll A Gas Rig. This 30-minute gig took place 303 meters (nearly 1,000 feet) below sea level, making it the world record holder for both the lowest and deepest concert ever performed, as the rig is submerged under the ocean. "On October 1st, 2006, she and her band played a show for some oil rig workers 303 meters, which is almost a thousand feet below sea level. The show was on the Stay Oil's Troll A Gas Rig."
