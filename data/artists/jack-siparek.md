## curiosities
**Mother Suggested "The Trews"** : Jack Siparek's mother, Jean, was instrumental in the band's final name change in 2002. Facing a cease and desist letter from another band named "Trouser," Jean called the band and suggested "The Trews," explaining it meant "Scottish pants" and offered a solution to their urgent need for a new name. "Jack's mother, Jean called us and said, you guys should call yourselves the trouser because the trues mean the same thing as trousers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
