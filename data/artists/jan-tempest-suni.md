## songs
**Asad Jazzed Evening (2000)** : This track was recorded by Finnish musician Jan Tempest Suni in August 2000. It won an electronic arts festival in Helsinki and was later covered by a Norwegian programmer, eventually becoming central to plagiarism accusations against Timbaland and Nelly Furtado. "In August 2000, a musician from Finland named Jan Tempest Sunni recorded a track called Asad Jazzed Evening." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYD_dIvYY5o

## curiosities
**Norwegian Programmer's Cover (2002)** : Around Christmas 2002, a Norwegian programmer covered Jan Tempest Suni's "Asad Jazzed Evening" with permission and posted the result online. This cover later became a reference point in plagiarism accusations against Timbaland. "Then, about two years later, this would be around Christmas of 2002, a Norwegian programmer covered the song with permission and posted the result online." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYD_dIvYY5o
**Plagiarism Allegations Against Timbaland and Nelly Furtado (2006)** : Jan Tempest Suni's "Asad Jazzed Evening" (2000) became the basis for plagiarism accusations when Nelly Furtado's song "Do It," produced by Timbaland, was released in 2006 and sounded similar. "Almost immediately, some people began to hear similarities between the song Do It and Sunni's Asad Jazzed Evening, which is when the accusations of plagiarism began to fly." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYD_dIvYY5o
