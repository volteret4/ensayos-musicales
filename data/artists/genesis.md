**Foxtrot (1972)** : This album is regarded by the critic as potentially the first great work and major record by Genesis. It represented a distinct and personal branch of progressive music, differentiating Genesis from other bands through its unique progressive style, rich instrumental sections, and the prominent use of flutes. It also marked the beginning of Peter Gabriel's charismatic and theatrical stage persona, as he started to discover himself and become "pletórico" in his performance.
