**Peter Gabriel Left 1975; Phil Collins Replaced Him After 40 Auditions; Ray Wilson Later Filled In** : Peter Gabriel was Genesis's founding singer from 1967 until his departure in 1975, which nearly ended the band. After approximately 40 auditions, they chose their own drummer Phil Collins, whose tenure extended the band's commercial reach considerably. In 1996–2000, when Collins was occupied with his solo career, a singer named Ray Wilson fronted the band before Collins returned. "You know, that worked out okay, didn't it?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0F22NTnQzQ
**Phil Collins as Drumming Hero** : Phil Collins, particularly during his time with Genesis, was one of Taylor Hawkins' drumming heroes. Collins' influential style in the progressive rock genre contributed to Hawkins' own musical development. "and Phil Collins when he was with Genesis." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA
**Post-Peter Gabriel Catalog Sale for $300 Million** : Genesis sold their catalog of material recorded after Peter Gabriel's departure for $300 million. "Genesis and all their post-Peter Gabriel material, $300 million" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pe40SqDrpZ0
**Reunion for Touring** : Genesis was one of many bands from past decades that reunited in the 21st century, a trend largely motivated by financial incentives. With declining CD sales impacting artist revenues, returning to the touring circuit offered a lucrative way to appeal to nostalgic fan bases and fill large venues.
