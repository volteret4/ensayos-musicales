## curiosities
**Album Art Designed by Storm Thorgerson** : Genesis's album covers were among the many iconic designs created by the renowned Storm Thorgerson and his partner Aubrey Powell. Their collaborations significantly contributed to the visual identity of major progressive rock bands. "That includes Pink Floyd, Black Sabbath, Led Zeppelin, ACDC, the police, Genesis..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tbKuJR0cPs
**Contribution to "Monty Python and the Holy Grail"** : Genesis, along with Led Zeppelin, provided financial support for the film "Monty Python and the Holy Grail" after Pink Floyd encouraged them to contribute to the project, which was facing funding challenges. "They later got Led Zeppelin and Genesis to kick in a few Bob and the movie was made." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**Peter Gabriel Left 1975; Phil Collins Replaced Him After 40 Auditions; Ray Wilson Later Filled In** : Peter Gabriel was Genesis's founding singer from 1967 until his departure in 1975, which nearly ended the band. After approximately 40 auditions, they chose their own drummer Phil Collins, whose tenure extended the band's commercial reach considerably. In 1996–2000, when Collins was occupied with his solo career, a singer named Ray Wilson fronted the band before Collins returned. "You know, that worked out okay, didn't it?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0F22NTnQzQ
