# artist - Matt Zvielandr

## curiosities
**Missed Pan Am Flight 103 (1988)** : Matt Zvielandr, who was the number one ranked tennis player in the world at the time, was scheduled to fly to New York on Pan Am Flight 103 on December 21, 1988. For an unspecified reason, he did not board the flight, thereby avoiding the Lockerbie bombing.
