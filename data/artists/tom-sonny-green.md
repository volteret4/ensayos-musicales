**All-port Syndrome Diagnosis and Symptoms** : Tom Sonny Green, the drummer for the British band Alt-J, lives with All-port syndrome, a rare genetic condition that primarily affects the kidneys, causing high levels of protein in urine and eventual end-stage renal disease. Additionally, the syndrome leads to hearing loss due to inner ear abnormalities and can affect the eyes, causing extreme sensitivity to light. "This is a rare genetic condition that starts by affecting the kidneys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u5NLt6mkmrk
**COVID-19 Vulnerability and Illness** : Due to All-port syndrome and the immunosuppressant drugs required for his kidney transplant, Tom Sonny Green is classified as extremely vulnerable to COVID-19, necessitating rigorous health vigilance. Despite his carefulness, he contracted a severe case of COVID in the summer of 2021, which led to a week-long hospitalization and left him debilitated for four months. "Having all-port syndrome classifies him as being extremely vulnerable to COVID, so he's had to be vigilant about staying healthy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u5NLt6mkmrk
