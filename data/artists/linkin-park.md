**Hybrid Theory (2000) – Debut Album** : Released on October 20, 2000, this album was a monumental success, selling five million copies in its first year and over 30 million worldwide. It topped charts in New Zealand (number one), and reached number two in the US, Germany, and Australia, also charting in the top ten in Canada, Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, Hungary, Norway, Scotland, Sweden, and Switzerland. The album earned three Grammy nominations, winning one for "Crawling" in the Best Hard Rock Performance category. Its success was attributed to the band's strong music, Mike Shinoda's rapping, and their firm stance against various "stupid suggestions" from label representatives regarding their image and sound, such as removing rap elements or altering their logo. "That record was released on October 20th of 2000." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM
