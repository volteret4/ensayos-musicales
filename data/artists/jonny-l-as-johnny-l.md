## albums
**Sawtooth (1997) - Double Vinyl Re-edition (December 8, 2017)** : A re-edition of Jonny L's "Sawtooth," referred to as "So Truth" in the transcript, is set for release on December 8th. Originally from 1997, this album is considered one of the most important in Drum and Bass culture. It was initially released as a series of 10-inch records, making it difficult and expensive to collect all parts. This new double vinyl compilation is hailed as a "great legend of Drum and Bass for electronic music."
