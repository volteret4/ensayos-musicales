## songs
**Licensed to Kill (1989) – Title Theme** : Gladys Knight was brought in to sing the title theme for the 1989 James Bond film "Licensed to Kill." The song, co-written by Narada Michael Walden, had a very 80s sound and production, becoming a hit in Europe and reaching number 79 in Canada, though it did not chart in America.
