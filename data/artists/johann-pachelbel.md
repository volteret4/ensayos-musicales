## genres

## songs
**Canon in D (1680-1706)** : Composed sometime between 1680 and 1706, this piece stands as Pachelbel's sole major hit, despite him having composed a vast quantity of music during his lifetime. Unless one is deeply immersed in classical music, it is unlikely they would be able to name another of his works. ## curiosities
**Classical One-Hit Wonder** : Johann Pachelbel is considered a classical one-hit wonder, with his "Canon in D" being his only widely recognized piece, despite a prolific career spanning many compositions. He is often cited alongside other classical composers like Jeremiah Clark, Samuel Barber, and Thomas Albinoni who are known predominantly for a single popular work.
