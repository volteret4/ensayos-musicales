# artist - Ataxia

## members
- Fugazi members
- John Frusciante

## albums
**First Ataxia Album (Early 2000s)** : This album, a collaboration between John Frusciante and members of Fugazi, is described as fantastic. It includes the track "The Curtains," which is particularly praised as the best song of that period. The album features very charismatic home recordings with poor sound quality, contributing to its raw, singer-songwriter feel, and is almost an acoustic work, distinct from the expected guitar-driven sound. **Second Ataxia Album (Late 2000s)** : Released during a period of transformation for John Frusciante, this album was generally less favored than Ataxia's debut. The speaker explicitly states a preference for the first album, indicating a perceived drop in quality or appeal for the second release.
