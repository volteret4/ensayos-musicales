# artist - Chromio

## albums
**Drive Time (2011) – The World's Smallest Album** : In 2011, Montreal band Chromio claimed to release the "world's smallest album," titled "Drive Time." It featured 55 songs, with the entire compilation lasting only 183 seconds, consisting of a series of sounds from Nokia phones and their navigation systems. While not a traditional album, they did issue a red vinyl 5-inch single that included multiple versions of two songs, with only 40 copies made, making them potentially collectible.
