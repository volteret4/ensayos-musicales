**Musical Evolution** : The Waterboys' sound evolved over time, starting with their "Big Music" phase, then transitioning into a more folk-oriented style, and later returning to a rock sound. "The Water Boys sound evolved over the years. The Big Music phase came at the beginning of their career. **The Big Music Concept** : Mike Scott's vision for the band was to fuse the traditional folk music of the British Isles with rock. He coined the term "The Big Music" to describe their sound, which he defined as "a mystical celebration of paganism. It's exolding of the basic and primitive divinity that exists in everything," essentially meaning anthemic. "Mike liked to call what the band did The Big Music, which is shorthand for, and this is a quote, a mystical celebration of paganism. It's exolding of the basic and primitive divinity that exists in everything, which I guess is just another way of saying, anthemic." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
