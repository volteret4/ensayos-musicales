## curiosities
**The Toasters – Founded by British Expat Rob Hingley in New York; Most Important American Ska Band of the 1980s; Founded Moon Ska Records** : The Toasters were the most important American ska band of the 1980s. Founder Rob Hingley was a Brit who moved to New York only to find almost no one knew about the British ska scene, so he formed a band based on Two-Tone traditions. Beyond touring and recording, Hingley also established Moon Ska Records, which eventually became the largest and most important independent ska label in America, distributing dozens of bands across the continent. Moon Ska Records had a business until 2000, though some European operations survived.
