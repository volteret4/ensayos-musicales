# artist - Nick Lowe

## genres
- Powerpop
- Pub Rock

## albums
**Labor of Lust (1979)** : This album, released in the summer of 1979, achieved gold record status in Canada. It featured his hit single "Cruel to Be Kind" and showcased Lowe's significant contribution to the Powerpop genre, highlighting his ability to create commercially successful tracks. "The album was called Labor of Lust and that became a gold record in Canada." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho

## songs
**Cruel to Be Kind (1979)** : This song was a hit single for Nick Lowe in the summer of 1979, appearing on his gold-certified album "Labor of Lust" in Canada. It exemplifies Lowe's Powerpop style, which has been highly influential, earning him recognition from contemporary artists like Alex Turner of the Arctic Monkeys. "Nick Lowe with Cruell to Be Kind from the summer of 1979." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho

## curiosities
**British Pub Rock Roots** : Nick Lowe emerged from the British pub rock scene in the early 1970s, which served as a foundational stage for his distinctive Powerpop sound. His background in this scene informed his songwriting, leading to both his own hit singles and tracks covered by other prominent artists. "One of the British Godfathers of this sound was Nick Lowe, a guy who came out of the British pubroxene of the early 70s..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
**Family Connection to Johnny Cash** : Nick Lowe was Johnny Cash's father-in-law, a notable personal connection that links him to another iconic figure in music history, and also explains how Cash came to cover some of Lowe's tracks. "Johnny Cash by the way was Nick's father-in-law." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
**Songwriting Influence** : Nick Lowe is a highly influential songwriter, not only having hit singles of his own but also seeing several of his tracks become hits for other artists, including Elvis Costello and Johnny Cash. His talent is recognized by contemporary musicians, with Alex Turner of the Arctic Monkeys citing him as a favorite songwriter. "If you ask Alex Turner of the Arctic Monkeys who his favorite songwriter of all time is, he will tell you that one of them has got to be Nick Lowe." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
