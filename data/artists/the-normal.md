## songs
**Warm Leatherette (1978)** : This single was recorded by Daniel Miller in his bedroom using a Korg 700S synthesizer and a four-track reel-to-reel machine. Released in November 1978, the song became a surprise hit, selling 30,000 copies despite Miller's lack of formal musical experience. It was described as minimalist, futuristic, and possessing a distinct attitude that differed from traditional rock. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
