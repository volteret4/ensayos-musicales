# artist - Jackson Pollock

## curiosities
**Chelsea Hotel Resident & Incident** : Painter Jackson Pollock was a resident at the Chelsea Hotel for a period. During a luncheon organized by art collector Peggy Guggenheim at the hotel, he became intoxicated and vomited on the dining room carpet. Someone facetiously suggested cutting out and hanging the piece of carpet, predicting it would one day be worth millions.
