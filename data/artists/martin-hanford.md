# artist - Martin Hanford

## curiosities
**Illustrator of Album Artwork** : Martin Hanford, known as the creator of the *Where's Waldo* books, began his career as an illustrator for album artwork. An example of his earlier work can be found on the front cover of The Vapors' album, *Magnets*, where a hidden assassin is said to be present.
