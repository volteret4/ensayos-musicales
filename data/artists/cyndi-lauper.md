# artist - Cyndi Lauper

## curiosities
**"I Want My MTV" Campaign** : Cyndi Lauper was one of the prominent artists who appeared in promos for the "I Want My MTV" campaign. This highly effective campaign encouraged people to call their local cable companies to demand that they carry the new channel, significantly expanding MTV's reach and power across the US. **Affected by Sony BMG Rootkit (2005)** : Albums by Cyndi Lauper were among those shipped with the Sony BMG Rootkit malware (XCP and MediaMax) installed. This software was surreptitiously placed on users' computers when they attempted to rip CDs, compromising system security without their consent.
