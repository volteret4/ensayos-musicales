# artist - Cyndi Lauper

## curiosities
**Affected by Sony BMG Rootkit (2005)** : Albums by Cyndi Lauper were among those shipped with the Sony BMG Rootkit malware (XCP and MediaMax) installed. This software was surreptitiously placed on users' computers when they attempted to rip CDs, compromising system security without their consent. "The XCP malware came on albums ranging from Neil Diamond to Frank Sinatra to Celine Dion to Cindy Lopper." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vJszsJQpINQ
