**Comic Book Artist and Writer** : Gerard Way began creating comics at 16, with his first work, "On Raven's Wings," seeing two issues published. By 2001, he was interning as a sketch artist at Cartoon Network in New York and pitched an animated TV series called "The Breakfast Monkey," which was not picked up. The experience of living through 9/11 directly led him to pursue music, resulting in the formation of My Chemical Romance. In 2007, he started a new mini-series, "The Umbrella Academy," which became a major success and was adapted into a popular Netflix series. He also worked with the Marvel Universe on Spider-Man, where he created the character Penny Parker. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
