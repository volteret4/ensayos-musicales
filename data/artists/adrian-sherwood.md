# artist - Adrian Sherwood

## curiosities
**Producer for Ministry's "Twitch" (1986)** : In 1986, Al Jourgensen collaborated with Adrian Sherwood to produce the album "Twitch." Jourgensen was drawn to Sherwood's signature style of heavy percussion, electronic rhythm, distortion, and tape loops. "He jumped at the chance to work with Adrian. And the result was the 1986 album entitled Twitch." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zSFhhEaiYwE
**Radical Remixes for Depeche Mode** : Adrian Sherwood created "pretty radical remixes" for Depeche Mode songs like "People Are People" and "Master and Servant," characterized by heavy percussion, electronic rhythm, distortion, and tape loops. These remixes were highly influential, particularly on Al Jourgensen of Ministry. "Adrian had created some pretty radical remixes for Depeche Mode, most notably for people are people and master and servant." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zSFhhEaiYwE
