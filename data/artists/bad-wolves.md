## songs
**Zombie (2018) – The Cranberries Cover** : Bad Wolves, an LA-based metal supergroup formed in 2017, recorded their version of The Cranberries' 1994 song "Zombie" in November 2017 while gathering material for their debut album. The cover featured a few lyrical changes, most notably a reference to drones, and was heavier than the original but remained largely true to it. The band's label, 11-7, had an employee named Dan Wait who was a friend of The Cranberries' singer Dolores O'Riordan; he suggested sending her the recording for her blessing. Dolores not only loved the version but offered to come to a studio in London on January 15, 2018, to contribute "sweetening" like guitar or background vocals. She left an excited voice message the night before, but was found dead the next morning. Despite the tragedy, Bad Wolves released the song, promising all profits to Dolores' three children, with the first check amounting to $250,000. This cover became the first rock song to be declared platinum in 2018. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
