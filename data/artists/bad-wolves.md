## albums
**Debut Album (2017-2018) – Material Gathering** : The band was formed in 2017 and immediately began gathering material for their first album, recording their version of "Zombie" in November 2017. "They were formed in 2017 and set about gathering material for a debut album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## songs
**Zombie (2018)** : This version was recorded in November 2017, featuring a slightly heavier sound than the original and minor lyrical changes, notably a reference to drones. Dolores O'Riordan approved of their rendition and offered to contribute to the final version, but tragically died the day before the scheduled studio session. The band released it, promising all profits to O'Riordan's three children, with the first check amounting to $250,000. "The first check was for $250,000." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## curiosities
**Dolores O'Riordan's Approval and Tragic Death** : Dolores O'Riordan, lead singer of The Cranberries, not only loved Bad Wolves' version of "Zombie" but offered to join them in London to add guitar or background vocals, leaving an excited voice message the night before the planned session. Tragically, she was found dead the next morning, January 15, 2018, having passed out in a bath after heavy drinking. "At the night before, she left a voice message, saying how excited she was about the project, and that she'd see everybody the next day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**First Rock Song to go Platinum in 2018** : Bad Wolves' cover of "Zombie" achieved platinum status in 2018, marking it as the first rock song to do so that year. "Our first story involves the first rock song to be declared platinum in 2018." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Music Video Details** : The music video for Bad Wolves' "Zombie" features a woman in gold who resembles Dolores O'Riordan from the original 1994 video, though it is not her. The numbers she smears from a glass, $1,15,18, represent January 15, 2018, the day Dolores O'Riordan died. "And the numbers she smears from the glass are $1,15,18. The day Dolores died." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
