# artist - Jim James

## albums
**Tribute To (Re-edition) (December 8, 2017)** : A re-edition of Jim James' album, phonetically referred to as "Trybo 2," is set for release on December 8th. This album, originally released some years prior, was a tribute to George Harrison following his death, featuring covers and interpretations of Harrison's songs. While not a commercial success initially, it developed a considerable cult following in subsequent years.
