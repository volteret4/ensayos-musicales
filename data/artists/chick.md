# artist - Chick

## members
- Mariah Carey (as writer, producer, backup singer, video director)

## albums
**Someone's Ugly Daughter (1995)** : This grunge album was secretly recorded by Mariah Carey under the band name Chick in 1995, during a period when she felt burned out on pop music. Carey wrote, produced, sang backup vocals, and directed the videos for the project, which is described as good and typical of angsty female-fronted rock from the 1990s. "The album was called Someone's Ugly Daughter. Mariah wrote, produced and sang backup. She also directed video." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ ← chick
