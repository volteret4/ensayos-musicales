## curiosities
**Attempted Bowie Bonds** : James Brown was one of several artists who attempted to replicate David Bowie's financial innovation, the Bowie Bonds, by securitizing his own music royalties. "Other artists tried the same thing. James Brown, the songwriting team of Holland Dozier Holland, the Isle of Brothers, Ashford and Simpson, even the guy who wrote songs like I Love Rock and Roll for Joan Jett, and another songwriter who wrote songs for Rod Stewart and Tupac." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yq2A_o9-ulM
