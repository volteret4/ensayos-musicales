## curiosities
**21st Birthday Party Destruction (August 23, 1967)** : Keith Moon, the "king of hotel room demolition," celebrated his 21st birthday on August 23, 1967, at the Holiday Inn in Flint, Michigan, resulting in a "crazy party" that left the hotel a "disaster." Legend has it that a Lincoln Continental, driven by Keith Moon, ended up in the swimming pool, initiating the "rock and roll cliche." The hotel damage amounted to $24,000 in 1967, which is approximately $200,000 today.
