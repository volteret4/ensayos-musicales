# artist - Gloria Jones

## songs
**Tainted Love (1965)** : Written in 15 minutes by Ed Cobb in 1964, Gloria Jones released "Tainted Love" in May 1965 as the B-side to "My Bad Boys Come In Home," which was intended to be the big single. Both songs failed commercially, and the record disappeared. However, in 1973, British club DJ Richard Seerling discovered it while vacationing in the U.S. and began playing it in Northern Soul clubs, where it became a dance floor sensation. Word of its popularity reached Jones, who re-recorded the song, but this attempt also failed. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## curiosities
**Car Accident with Mark Bowlin (1977)** : On September 16, 1977, Gloria Jones was driving Mark Bowlin's (of T. Rex) Mini home after a night out when the car went out of control over a small bridge, hitting a fence post and then a tree. Mark Bowlin, who owned the car but never learned to drive due to a fear of dying in a car accident, was killed instantly. Jones survived the crash. Bowlin had other cars, including a Rolls-Royce, which he had lent to another band that night. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
