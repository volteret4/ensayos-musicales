# artist - Fisher Z

## songs
**The Worker (1979)** : This song was released as a single in 1979, notable for its special picture disc featuring an optical illusion of a rotating train. The illusion, which linked to the song's lyrics, was visible only when the record spun on a turntable, creating a cool visual effect.
