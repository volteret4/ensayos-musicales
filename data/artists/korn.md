## curiosities
**Dreadlocks in 1990s Rock and Metal** : Jonathan Davis of KoRn notably wore dreadlocks throughout the 1990s, contributing to the hairstyle's popularization among musicians in the rock and metal genres. "And then through the 90s, dreads appeared on everyone from Rob Zombie and Zach from Raging Us the Machine to Jonathan Davis of Corn." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q3zkJX8S4Xc
**Influence from Body Count** : KoRn is part of the lineage of bands influenced by Body Count, connecting them to the origins of the nu-metal movement. "So basically, you can draw a line from Body Count, right through corn, slipknot, and limp biscuit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Tattoo Admiration by Darren Fyfer** : The members of KoRn, specifically Fieldy and Jonathan (presumably Jonathan Davis), are admired by Goldfinger drummer Darren Fyfer for their "super into" and "really nice tattoos." "All the guys in court. All those guys are super into like fieldy and Jonathan. They got some really nice tattoos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
