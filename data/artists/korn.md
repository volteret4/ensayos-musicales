**Active Rock Radio Success (2010s)** : one of the two most successful survivors of the new metal era, benefiting from active rock radio outlets as a promotional platform. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Fred Durst's Tattoo Offer (Early 1995)** : In early 1995, while Korn was on tour, a tattoo artist named Fred Durst, a huge fan, offered free tattoos to the band members in Jacksonville, Florida. Head, one of Korn's guitarists, accepted the offer and received a substantial Korn logo tattoo on his back. Durst used this opportunity to pass a Limp Bizkit demo to Fieldy, Korn's bassist. This interaction proved crucial, as Fieldy, impressed by the demo, shared it with Korn's management and producer Ross Robinson, ultimately leading to Limp Bizkit's major label deal and their rise in the "new metal" scene. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Somq2MoRi5Q
