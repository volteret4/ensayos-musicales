## albums
**Residente (2017) - Global Transcending Rap** : This self-titled album by Residente, a former member of Calle 13, goes far beyond a typical rap record. It incorporates numerous influences from world music, features multiple languages, and includes collaborations with many important artists. It is a truly complete album, considered the best and most interesting album made in Spanish, marking Residente's transcendence from rap and urban culture into a new, more comprehensive musical territory. "su disco de residente ya no es un disco de rap, el a quincorpora muchísimas influencias de música del mundo, muchos idiomas, colaboran muchísimos artistas importantes, es un disco verdaderamente completo, es yo creo que es el mejor disco el disco más interesante que se ha hecho en Castellano." ← Music Radar Clan > Mejores discos del 2017 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T34CwsFmXpI&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← residente ← residente
