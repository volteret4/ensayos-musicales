**Tantric Sex Marathon Myth** : The widespread story that Sting engages in marathon sessions of tantric sex lasting for hours is completely false. This myth originated from a 1993 "Q Magazine" interview where a drunken Sting, prompted by Sir Bob Geldof, jokingly exaggerated his yoga practice, claiming it allowed him to "retain it all and go on for longer." Sting found the story hysterical and further modified it for fun, leading to the "eight hours at a time" detail, but it was all a lie. "I can take you to higher levels. Yeah, I started to use it to have sex. Now where you don't spill your seed, you retain it all and go on for longer and you never lose control and you just keep going." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ
**Tree Frog Species** : A species of tree frog, *Dendro's Sophos Sting-I*, was named after Sting when it was discovered in 1994. This naming honors Sting's significant efforts in conservation, specifically his work to protect the rainforests of South America. "This is a species of tree frog that was named after Sting when it was discovered in 1994. Why would they name it that? Well to honor Sting's efforts in trying to save the rainforests of South America, of course." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMPLplhDk8I
