## curiosities
**Unsolved Hammer Murder in Manchester** : Dave Rowbotham, a musician from Manchester involved in several punk and post-punk bands, notably The Durutti Column (a group considered very important by Factory Records), was murdered on November 2, 1991. After his band work, he made a living playing sessions for other Factory artists. His girlfriend found him in his apartment, beaten to death with a hammer, in a pool of blood. No one has ever been arrested, and the case remains unsolved.
