**Disappearance from Embassy Hotel (February 1, 1995)** : Richey Edwards walked out of room 516 of the Embassy Hotel on Bayswater Road in London at 7:00 am on February 1, 1995, and disappeared forever, the day he was supposed to fly to the US for a promo tour with bandmate James Dean Bradfield. Leading up to his disappearance, he had withdrawn £200 daily for two weeks, accumulating nearly £3,000 in cash. He left behind a packed suitcase, toiletries, a bottle of Prozac, and a carefully wrapped box for a 19-year-old female friend with a note that said, "I love you." His car was found abandoned at a service station two weeks later, suggesting someone had been living in it. He was officially presumed dead in November 2008, and his disappearance remains one of modern Britain's greatest missing person mysteries.
