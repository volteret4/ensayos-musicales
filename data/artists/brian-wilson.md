"the fact that he was so emotionally damaged at the time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
**Musical Parent** : Brian Wilson of The Beach Boys is noted as the father of Carnie and Wendy Wilson. "daughters of the Beach Boys, Brian Wilson." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Potential Outsider Musician** : Brian Wilson of The Beach Boys is suggested as a potential inclusion in discussions of outsider music, particularly when considering his entire life story. "I could have also included Brian Wilson of the Beach Boys, which makes total sense, but only if you know his entire life story." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Bipolar Disorder Sufferer** : Brian Wilson of The Beach Boys is identified as a musician who has experienced bipolar disorder, a mental illness marked by extreme shifts in mood, including periods of mania and depression. "The list of bipolar musicians includes Scott Wyland, Pete Wenz, of Fallout Boy, Brian Wilson of the Beach Boys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trMzjWWmbZo ← brian-wilson
**Bipolar and Schizoaffective Disorder** : Brian Wilson is identified as suffering from bipolar disorder and also touching on schizoaffective disorder. "Brian Wilson of the Beach Boys is bipolar, also touching on something called Skit-so-effective disorder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NQgn0_mUh74 ← brian-wilson
**Unrealized Production of "Seasons in the Sun"** : Brian Wilson of The Beach Boys had specific creative ideas for "Seasons in the Sun," which almost became a Beach Boys single, but the song's original artist, Terry Jackson, did not have the patience to see Wilson's vision through. "that was very close to becoming a Beach Boys single, but Jackson did not have the patience for all the things that Brian Wilson wanted to do with that song." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 45 - Oh, Canada!
