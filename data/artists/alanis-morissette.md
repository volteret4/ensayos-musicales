# artist - Alanis Morissette

## albums
**Jagged Little Pill** : A massively successful album that was on its way to selling 30 million copies. Radiohead opened for Alanis Morissette on tour, providing them crucial exposure and support. "Alanis Morissette, who was on her way to selling 30 million copies of Jagged Little Pill." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JUvDp3_RBH0
**Jagged Little Pill (1995)** : Released in 1995, this post-grunge album was the outcome of a creative partnership with producer Glen Ballard, following two previous unsuccessful pop albums. Katy Perry lauded it as "the most perfect female record ever made" and a significant inspiration. The album sold an estimated 33 million copies, placing it among the 12th best-selling albums of all time, alongside works like Michael Jackson's *Bad*, Led Zeppelin's *Led Zeppelin IV*, and The Beatles' *Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band*. Only Shania Twain and Whitney Houston have achieved higher individual album sales among women. "The best estimate we have for sales is 33 million copies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IxJ0X9ytGgI

## songs
**You Oughta Know (1995)** : First heard in June 1995 while driving on the 101 Freeway in Los Angeles, this song was immediately recognized for its intense anger and was likened to "revenge porn set to music." It marked a profound transformation for Alanis Morissette from a "wannabe pop starlet" to a formidable alt-rock figure, with a real breakup experience providing its emotional core. "It was June of 1995. I was listening to the radio, driving up the 101 Freeway in Los Angeles. Wow, said my wife, who was in the car with me. That is an angry song. I love it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IxJ0X9ytGgI
