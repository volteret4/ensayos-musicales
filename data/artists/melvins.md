## curiosities
**Buzz Osborne's Recommendation to Nirvana (1990)** : Buzz Osborne, a member of The Melvins and a figure "idolized by Kurt Cobain," played a pivotal role in Dave Grohl joining Nirvana. Osborne recommended Dave for the open drummer slot after Scream broke up, directly leading to Dave's audition and subsequent entry into Nirvana. "But then Buzz Osborne of the Melvins, a guy idolized by Kurt Cobain, recommended him for the open drummer slot with Nirvana." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**Opening Act for Bam Bam** : The Melvins, another influential band in the Pacific Northwest music scene, once opened for Bam Bam, highlighting Bam Bam's significance and presence within the early grunge community. "They had the melvins opened for them and get this." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VnXBJqKwkrw
