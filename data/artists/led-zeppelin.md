**Peter Grant's 90-10 Revenue Split Demand** : Led Zeppelin's manager, Peter Grant, revolutionized concert economics by refusing the traditional 50-50 ticket revenue split with promoters. He successfully demanded, and obtained, a 90-10 split in favor of the band, a precedent that has since become the standard practice in the industry. "He demanded and got a 90-10 split for the band. And that has since become the standard almost everywhere today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8q2bRZyHnHE
**Pioneering Guitar Riffs** : Led Zeppelin is recognized for being among the first bands to create many significant guitar riffs, effectively claiming them for all time. "Bands like Black Sabbath and Led Zeppelin got to so many of the great guitar riffs first and thus have claimed them for all time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m7MbV-eCLyE
**Pioneers of Heavy Metal (Mid-1970s)** : Led Zeppelin is identified as one of the groups that contributed to the rise of a "new wave of loud, heavy guitar rock" in the mid-1970s, which led to the widespread adoption of the term "heavy metal" to describe this burgeoning genre. "But once heavy metal was introduced into the music vernacular, other people began to pick it up and use it to describe a new wave of loud, heavy guitar rock as groups like Led Zeppelin, Black Sabbath, and Deep Purple began their rise." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8GU8ZpSgY8
