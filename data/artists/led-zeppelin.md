"here's Babe I'm going to leave you from Led Zeppelin in 1967." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4cZfegexwwk

"And now here's Babe I'm going to leave you from Led Zeppelin in 1967. Which by the way was conceived around the same time that George Harrison wrote while my guitar gently weeps for the Beatles.
