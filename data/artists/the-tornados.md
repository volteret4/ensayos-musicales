## curiosities
**Connection to Muse's Matt Bellamy** : The rhythm guitarist in The Tornados was George Bellamy. Sixteen years later, George had a son named Matt, who in 1994 formed a band that became known as Muse. **First UK #1 in USA** : The Tornados, with their Joe Meek-produced hit "Telstar," achieved the distinction of being the first British act to have a song reach number one in the United States. This historic feat occurred in 1962, notably predating the widespread success and phenomenon of the British Invasion.
