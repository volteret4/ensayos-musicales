**1992 Overdose and Cardiac Arrest** : In September 1992, while on tour with Metallica, Slash was staying in San Francisco before an Oakland gig. After the Oakland show, drug dealers came to his hotel room at 5 a.m., and Slash took a powerful speedball (heroin and cocaine).
