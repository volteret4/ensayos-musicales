# artist - Lights

## curiosities
**Comic Book Creator** : Lights is a lifelong fan of comic books and realized her dream of a cross-over project between music and comics. Her 2017 album, "Skin & Earth," was released concurrently with a six-issue comic book series of the same name, which was later published in paperback in spring 2018. She aspires to one day draw for Wonder Woman. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
