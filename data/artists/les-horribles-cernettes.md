## curiosities
**First Band Picture Posted on the Internet (1992)** : Les Horribles Cernettes, a singing group formed in 1990 by CERN graphic designer Michelle DiGenerro and three female co-workers, became the subject of the first picture ever posted on the internet. In 1992, Tim Berners-Lee, the inventor of the World Wide Web, scanned their image after a party and published it on info.cern.ch, establishing them as the first band on the internet. "And that's how the Cernets not only became the first band on the internet, but their image was also the first picture ever posted on the internet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
