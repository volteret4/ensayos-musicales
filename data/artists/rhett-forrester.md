## curiosities
**Fatal Car-Jacking Attempt** : Rhett Forrester, the singer for the New York metal band Riot in the early 1980s, and later a member of groups like the Calgary-based The Black Symphony, was murdered on September 22, 1996. He was shot and killed during an attempted car-jacking while stopped at a traffic light in Tucker, Georgia, after refusing to give up his car. A confession to the crime emerged years later.
