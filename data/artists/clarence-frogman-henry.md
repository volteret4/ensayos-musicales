# artist - Clarence "Frogman" Henry

## concerts
- Maple Leaf Gardens (Toronto, 1964)

## curiosities
**Opening Act for The Beatles (1964)** : Clarence "Frogman" Henry was an opening act for The Beatles at Maple Leaf Gardens in Toronto on September 6, 1964. His performance, like those of the other support acts, was met with boos from an audience solely interested in the main event. "As the opening bands, which included...Clarence Frogman Henry...hit the stage to crowd-bood, they only wanted one band, and one band only." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2IDbX5rNT1E
