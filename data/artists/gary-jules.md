# artist - Gary Jules

## songs
**Mad World (2003)** : This cover, performed by Gary Jules with Michael Andrews, was initially issued on the official soundtrack and DVD of the movie *Donnie Darko*. It later took on a life of its own, released as a single on December 15, 2003, and immediately soared to the top of the British charts, becoming the Christmas number one that year.
