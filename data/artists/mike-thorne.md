## albums
**Quadro File (1976)** : A double album released in 1976 where the same music was recorded on all four sides, with each side featuring a different quadraphonic format. Side 1 was in QS, Side 2 in SQ, Side 3 in CD4, and Side 4 in UD4, serving as a comprehensive but impractical demonstration of the competing quad formats.
