## curiosities
**Roadie for Jimi Hendrix** : Before becoming a performer with Motorhead, Lemmy Kilmister worked as a roadie for Jimi Hendrix, where he was responsible for "humping gear." This personal experience gave him a unique understanding and sympathy for road crews.
