## curiosities
**Coined "Industrial Music for Industrial People"** : San Francisco artist Monte Cazazza coined the phrase "industrial music for industrial people," a concept that was later adopted and given sound by Throbbing Gristle, who are credited with popularizing the term "industrial music."
