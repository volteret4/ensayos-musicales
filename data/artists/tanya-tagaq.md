## curiosities
**Collaboration with Buffy Sainte-Marie** : She provided vocals on the song "You Got to Run" from Buffy Sainte-Marie's 2017 album "Medicine Songs," bringing together two Polaris Prize-winning Indigenous Canadian artists. "This double shot is called You Got to Run. Two Indigenous Canadians, two Polaris Prize winners, Buffy St. Marie and Tanya Tagak, together on a 2017 song called You Got to Run." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA ← tanya-tagaq
**Polaris Music Prize Winner** : Tanya Tagaq, an Inuit throat singer from Cambridge Bay in Nunavut, won the Polaris Music Prize in 2014, a significant recognition of her unique musical contributions. "She won the Polaris Music Prize in 2014, and she is an in-equthroat singer from Cambridge Bay in Nunavut." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA ← tanya-tagaq
