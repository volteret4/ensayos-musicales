**Underappreciated Spanish Indie Pioneers** : Los Planetas are considered "authentic gods" of independent and indie music in Spain, having introduced significant cultural influences from other countries, particularly classic Anglo-Saxon movements like guitar jangle, and transforming them into distinct interpretations for Spain. Despite their high quality and lasting influence across generations, they have never been fully valued or appreciated outside of independent circles.
