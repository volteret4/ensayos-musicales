# artist - Jimi Hendrix

## members
- Mitch Mitchell (drummer)

## instruments
- **Wah pedal** : Jimi Hendrix was one of the first two customers convinced by Henry Goldrich to purchase a newly introduced wah pedal from Manny's Music in the mid-1960s. This pedal significantly influenced his playing style and contributed to the overall direction of guitar-based rock and roll.
- High-powered concert bass amplifiers (from Sun Musical Equipment Company)
- Wah-wah pedal
