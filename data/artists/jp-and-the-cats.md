## curiosities
**Jazz Standards Repertoire** : As Jimmy Chamberlin's main gig from the age of 15, JP and the Cats played a repertoire rich in jazz standards. Their performances included works by renowned artists such as Benny Goodman, Count Basie, Tommy Dorsey, and Gene Krupa, connecting the group to the big band era of the 1930s and 1940s.
