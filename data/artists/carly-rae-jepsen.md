## songs
**Call Me Maybe (2012)** : This song was the winner of the 2012 Billboard Song of the Summer, identified as the "ideal song of the summer." It is a "bop with a struddy beat," featuring "lilleting electronic strings that suggest summer languor," and is "built for clubbing or lazing by the pool." Recorded and released in 2011 in Canada, it gained traction in America in 2012 through word of mouth and social sharing. As it peaked during the summer, numerous homemade videos for the song emerged, predating TikTok. Its sing-along chorus introduced a new catchphrase into the lexicon, and Jepsen performed it at the 2015 Capital Summertime Ball in London. "Here's my number call me maybe. How old school?
