# artist - Dr. Peter Goldmark

## curiosities
**Inventor of Polyvinyl Chloride (Vinyl) Records** : The chief engineer at Columbia Records who was tasked with finding a substitute for shellac during the 1940s supply crisis. His team successfully adapted polyvinyl chloride—previously used for sewer pipes—to create more durable records that could hold five times more music than 78 RPM discs. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
