## curiosities
**Challenges and Legacy** : Bell faced significant racial abuse as a Black female singer in the punk scene, and despite opening for future stars like Soundgarden, her band struggled to gain traction. After quitting music and moving to Europe, she returned to the U.S. and eventually died in Las Vegas in 2012; her body was undiscovered for weeks, and her creative possessions were subsequently thrown out. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
**Godmother of Grunge** : Tina Bell helped invent the Grunge sound that defined 1990s rock and was the genre's first female front person. Her band, Bam Bam, was the first Grunge group to record at Reciprocal Recording, a studio later used by Nirvana and Soundgarden. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
**Kurt Cobain Connection** : Before his own rise to fame, Kurt Cobain reportedly worked as a roadie for Bam Bam. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
