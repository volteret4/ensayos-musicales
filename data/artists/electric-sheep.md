## curiosities
**Early Band Efforts** : Formed when Tom Morello was approximately 16 or 17 years old, Electric Sheep included Adam Jones, who would later become the guitarist for Tool, playing bass. The band was noted for its enthusiastic approach and strong political convictions, despite many of its members lacking significant musical proficiency.
