## curiosities
**Founder of Mute Records** : Daniel Miller founded Mute Records using the funds he earned from his unexpected hit single, "Warm Leatherette." His vision for Mute was to create an "all synthesizer label," a focus that proved highly successful as keyboard-driven music gained traction on British charts after 1981.
