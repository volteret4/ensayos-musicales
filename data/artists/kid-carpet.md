# artist - Kid Carpet

## members
- Ed Patrick

## instruments
- Fisher price guitars
- Furby (for backing vocals)
- cheap keyboards

## songs
**Cassio Best. It's failed world record attempt (2008)** : This song was written as an ode to the Beijing Olympics and has a great YouTube video associated with it. Kid Carpet, whose real name is Ed Patrick, embraces the use of cheap keyboards and Fisher Price guitars for his music. "He wrote that one as an ode to the Beijing Olympics." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zWNzlSxrj6s

## curiosities
**Stolen Furby for Backing Vocals** : Kid Carpet, known as Ed Patrick, had planned to use a Furby for backing vocals in his music, believing in the use of unconventional and inexpensive instruments. However, the Furby was stolen at a gig, leading to the loss of his intended backup vocals. "But that Furby was stolen at a gig, so backup vocals are gone." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zWNzlSxrj6s
