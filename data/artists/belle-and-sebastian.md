## albums
**The Boy with the Arab Strap (1998)** : This album, released in 1998, features a song that is about Seymour Stein, the founder of Sire Records. Seymour Stein is noted for signing influential artists ranging from the Ramones to the Talking Heads and Madonna. Belle and Sebastian, originating from Glasgow, are presented as a prime example of the Tweetpop genre. "It's originally from a 1998 album entitled The Boy with the Arab Strap and the song is about Seymour Stein, the founder of Sire Records and the guy who signed everyone from the Ramones to the Talking Heds to Madonna." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
