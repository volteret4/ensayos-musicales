**Quirky Liner Note Stories** : Belle and Sebastian, a Scottish indie pop band, incorporated quirky short stories written by frontman Stuart Murdoch into their album liner notes. One such story humorously recounted Murdoch mailing his jacket home from a post office after overdressing for a walk on a warm day, showcasing a creative approach to engaging with listeners beyond just lyrics or credits. "Lead band Stuart Murdock liked to write quirky short stories. One album featured a story about how we overdressed for a walk, so we stopped into the post office, and mailed his jacket home back to his house, so we wouldn't have to carry it around with him on that warm day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KaIp6KVn-co ← belle-and-sebastian
