**Moanin' in the Moonlight (1959)** : This album, released in 1959, offers a "glimpse into the future" of heavy metal, particularly through its raw and powerful sound. The combination of his whaling harmonica and guttural vocals is highlighted as a precursor to the heavy metal aesthetic, especially when imagined with amplified electric guitar.
