## curiosities
**Managed by Susan Silver (1983)** : Screaming Trees were an early client of Susan Silver, who started managing bands in Seattle in 1983 while still majoring in Chinese at the University of Washington. This management role predated her work with Soundgarden and Alice in Chains. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Singles Soundtrack Contribution (1992)** : Songs from The Screaming Trees were played on the radio and in alternative clubs throughout late 1992, following the release of *Singles*. The movie and its soundtrack were instrumental in expanding the reach and popularity of grunge music. "Songs from Allison Chains, Pearl Jam, The Screaming Trees and The Smashing Pumpkins were all played on the radio and in alternative clubs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
**Subpop Roster** : Screaming Trees was featured on the Subpop label's roster by the middle of 1988, highlighting their presence within the Seattle indie scene. "The roster featured local indie groups like SoundGarden and Screaming Trees and Mud Honey." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCtQqVBtCaI
