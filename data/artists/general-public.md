## curiosities
**John Hughes Film Exposure (1980s)** : General Public's music was frequently included on the carefully curated soundtracks of John Hughes' 1980s films. These soundtracks were crucial in introducing a vast number of North American audiences to various alternative rock artists, particularly those from Britain. "So many people learned of bands like The Specials and Spandovalet and Billy Idle and General Public and Wang Chung and Yellow and New Order and the Psychedelic Furs and the Smiths and Echo and the Bunyman, Susanne Vagan in excess the Jesus and Mary chain, Flesh for Luluo and so many others from those soundtrack albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
