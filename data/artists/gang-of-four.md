**Influence on Red Hot Chili Peppers** : Hailing from Leeds, Gang of Four were recognized for their unique blend of funk and punk, significantly influencing bands like the Red Hot Chili Peppers. **Representative Post-Punk Band** : Gang of Four was identified as a significant group from the UK for whom the "post punk" categorization worked particularly well. This term helped to differentiate their songs, albums, and overall style from traditional punk rock, effectively capturing their more progressive and complex sound. "It worked quite well, especially with groups like Joy Division and Gang of Four and several others out of the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI ← gang-of-four ← gang-of-four
