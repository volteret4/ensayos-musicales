## curiosities
**Childhood Friend of Brian Doherty** : Dave Henning grew up with Brian Doherty in New York and roomed with him at Berklee School of Music, next door to Ian Thornley. He was an "obvious choice" for the bassist position in Big Wreck due to his existing connection with Brian. "Dave Hending was the obvious choice because he was, you know, grew up with Brian." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z9QChV543aQ
