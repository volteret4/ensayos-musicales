# artist - The Streets

## members
- Mike Skinner

## albums
**A Grand Don't Come for Free (2004)** : The second album by Mike Skinner, released in May 2004. It chronicles the everyday activities of a regular British guy whose life isn't going well. Each song functions as a chapter in his quest to locate a mysteriously disappeared thousand pounds. The narrative includes breaking up with his girlfriend (who is sleeping with his friend), excessive drinking and drug use, a fight with a TV repairman, and the eventual discovery of the money hidden inside his TV set.

## songs
**Fit But You Know It (2004)** : A track from the 2004 concept album A Grand Don't Come for Free, representing a chapter where the protagonist tries to pick up a girl after a night of drinking, which does not go well.
