## curiosities
**Founding The White Room** : Alex Paterson and Jimmy Cauty founded "The White Room" in 1989 at London's Club Heaven. This event was a pioneering space designed for club-goers to rest from the intensity of the dance floor, where ambient and relaxing music was played. "Sus DJs fueron Jimmy Couty y Alex Patterson, que nos acababan de fundar el seminal duro de música electrónica Diorp." ← Chill Out： La pausa que necesitaba la electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VPOAA3ZaA9s
