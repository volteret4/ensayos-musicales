# artist - ABBA

## curiosities
**Tax Deduction for Stage Outfits (1970s)** : To qualify for a tax deduction on their elaborate stage outfits, ABBA adhered to a Swedish law stipulating that such attire had to be "so crazy and unusual that they could not be worn in everyday life." This requirement is cited as the reason for the unique and often bizarre nature of their 1970s costumes. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
