# artist - ELO

## curiosities
**Album Art Designed by Storm Thorgerson** : ELO's album covers were among the many iconic designs created by the renowned Storm Thorgerson and his partner Aubrey Powell. Their creative partnership resulted in memorable visual identities for numerous influential bands.
