## albums
**Sessions (Undated)** : REO Speedwagon was featured on a TV show called "Sessions," created by Bill Arhose before he founded Austin City Limits. This experience gave Arhose an understanding of how to produce musical performances for television. "He had created a TV show called Sessions, which had acts like a Mario Speedwagon and Billy Joel, so he kind of had an idea of how to make something like this work." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 49 - Austin City Limits and the 70s Country Music Revolution | http://ftr70.com

## curiosities
**John Wayne Gacy's Musical Preference** : Serial killer John Wayne Gacy, who claimed at least 33 victims, was known to be an admirer of REO Speedwagon, among other musical acts. This information offers a glimpse into his personal tastes. "John Wayne Gacy... was into Elton John, Bob Dylan, Neil Diamond, and Aureo Speedwagon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FtGJHhqXcDI ← reo-speedwagon
