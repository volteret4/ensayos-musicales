## curiosities
**1999 E-Lectronica Explosion** : The Prodigy was identified as a leading act in the E-Lectronica scene (known today as EDM) that was experiencing an explosion in popularity around 1999, selling significant numbers of albums. "acts like the prodigy and the chemical brothers and aethics when we're selling boatloads of albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hBQE4t72i3o
**Band's Commercial Success** : The Prodigy achieved significant commercial success, selling "millions" of records during their peak. "The group sold millions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LP8NGDCe-GY
**Big Beat Influence** : The band was a pioneer of the "Big Beat" sound within the electronic music scene, influencing other artists to experiment with similar keyboard-heavy, drum machine-driven, looped, and sampled sounds. "Following the lead of what was known as Big Beat Outfits like the Prodigy and the Chemical Brothers, others started dabbling in these sounds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hBQE4t72i3o
**Continuing Late 90s Electronica Trend** : The Prodigy was still active and relevant in the 2000s, representing the continuation of late 90s trends towards electronica that contributed to the revival of dance-oriented elements in alt rock. "The prodigy and the chemical brothers were still with us, so as Fatboy Slim." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo
**Coping Mechanisms** : Boxing and jiu-jitsu were activities that helped Keith Flint manage his mental health issues, but ultimately proved insufficient to address his deeper struggles. "Boxing and jiu-jitsu helped, but it wasn't enough." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LP8NGDCe-GY
**Coroner's Inquest Ambiguity on Suicide** : The coroner's inquest was unable to definitively determine whether Keith Flint's hanging was deliberate or accidental, despite the presence of codeine, alcohol, and a small amount of cocaine in his system. The inquest concluded, "Having regard to all the circumstances, I don't find there's enough evidence for suicide. He may have been walking around, and it all went horribly wrong." "The coroner's inquest was unable to determine if Keith's hanging was deliberate or an accident." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LP8NGDCe-GY
