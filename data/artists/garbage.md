# artist - Garbage

## songs
**Number One Crush (1997)** : This song, featured on the *Romeo and Juliet* soundtrack, includes moaning in its background that is actually a sample of Madonna's voice. The sample was sourced from Madonna's song "Bedtime Story," which was co-produced by Nellee Hooper (referred to as "Mally Hooper" in transcript), who also produced for Garbage. "The moaning in the background of number one crush from the Romeo and Juliet soundtrack is actually Madonna. The sample is taken from her song Bedtime Story, which was co-produced by Garbage producer, Mally Hooper." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
