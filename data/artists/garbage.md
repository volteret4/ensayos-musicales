## curiosities
**Butch Vig's Multi-Platinum Band** : Formed by drummer and producer Butch Vig, this became a multi-platinum band. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Discovery of Shirley Manson** : Steve Marker discovered Shirley Manson singing for the Scottish band Angelfish on TV. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**First Meeting and Kurt Cobain's Death** : The band members met for the first time in London on April 8, 1994, the same day Kurt Cobain killed himself. Butch Vig, who produced Nirvana's "Nevermind," received the news during this meeting, which put the project on hold for a while. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Formation and Vision** : Formed by three male producers/engineers (Butch Vig, Duke Erikson, Steve Marker) who wanted a female frontwoman in the vein of Chrissy Hynde or Patti Smith, specifically seeking a singer with a lower register. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Influenced by My Bloody Valentine** : Counted among the bands significantly influenced by My Bloody Valentine's innovative use of guitar noise and walls of sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Record Deal Acquisition** : Secured a record deal by sending samples to labels with minimal information, primarily leveraging Butch Vig's involvement (due to his "solid run of producing hit albums"). ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Shirley Manson's Audition** : Shirley Manson initially failed her audition but was given another chance due to her shared musical taste with the guys. She "nailed it" on the second attempt. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
