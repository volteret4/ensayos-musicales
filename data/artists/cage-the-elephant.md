## curiosities
**Planned Woodstock 50 Performance** : Cage the Elephant was part of the original lineup of 80 artists slated to perform at the Woodstock 50 festival in August 2019, which aimed to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the legendary Woodstock event. The festival was ultimately canceled due to a host of unresolved issues.
