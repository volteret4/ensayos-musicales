## curiosities
**Early Lo-Fi Recording Methods** : Merrill Garbus, performing as Tune-Yards, began making music in 2006 with notably lo-fi recording techniques. Her first record was released on a used and recycled cassette, and all the music for it was recorded using nothing but a handheld voice recorder. "The first Tuneyards record was released on a used and recycled cassette. The music was recorded using nothing but a handheld voice recorder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEawe_1Fqf0
**Signing with 4AD** : The unique and experimental nature of Tune-Yards' early work, including recordings made with a handheld voice recorder, led to a deal with 4AD. This UK-based label is renowned for its experimental alt-rock roster, which includes bands like The Pixies, Bon Iver, and The National, and is known for taking chances on innovative artists. "This led to a deal with 4AD, the wonderfully experimental alt-rock label out of the UK, that also took a chance with bands like the Pixies, Bonnie Verre, the National, and so many others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEawe_1Fqf0
