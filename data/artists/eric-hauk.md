# artist - Eric Hauk

## instruments
- **Adapted Guitar Playing** : After becoming paraplegic, Eric Hauk learned to play the guitar with a posture necessitated by sitting in a wheelchair, demonstrating his determination to continue his musical career. "And as he kept up with rehab, he learned how to play guitar with a posture forced on him by sitting in a wheelchair."

## curiosities
**Life-Altering Accident** : In 2005, at the age of 25, Eric Hauk sustained a severe injury when a retaining wall in a friend's Seattle backyard collapsed, causing him to fall 12 feet onto a concrete slab. This accident resulted in three broken vertebrae and paralysis from the sternum down, leaving him permanently unable to walk. "He fell 12 feet and slammed up against a slab of concrete, breaking three vertebrain his back, and paralyzing him from the sternum down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trMzjWWmbZo
**Return to Stage After Accident** : Despite his paraplegia, Eric Hauk was determined to continue playing guitar. Within four months of his accident, he was back on stage performing with his then-band, The Lashes, at a festival. "Within four months of the accident, he was back on stage with his band The Lashes, playing a festival." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trMzjWWmbZo
