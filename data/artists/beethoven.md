# artist - Beethoven

## songs
**Ode To Joy (1824) – 9th Symphony Final Movement** : This piece, the last movement of Beethoven's 9th Symphony, was written in 1824 and inspired by Frederick Schiller's poem of the same name. Schiller's poem championed the radical idea of universal brotherhood, transcending class distinctions. Even today, this movement is frequently employed as a protest anthem, notably by Chinese students in Tiananmen Square who played it until tanks intervened.
