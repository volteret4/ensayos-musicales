**Gear of the Sneech (Unknown Year)** : This album, launched progressively over two months, generated immense expectation, as is typical for any Dead Grips release. It is seen as a return to the band's roots, satisfying listeners who may not have been fully convinced by their more recent stages, such as *Buttonlesspeak*. The album's sound is reminiscent of their early work, incorporating samples and fragments from *Ismilitar* and *Monistor*. While not a radical musical revolution, it offers significant new elements while maintaining the band's core identity, notably through the predominant use of distorted strings and guitar, marking an interesting evolution for their electronic, drums, and rap sound.
