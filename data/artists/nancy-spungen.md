**Murder at Chelsea Hotel (October 12, 1978)** : Nancy Spungen, girlfriend of Sid Vicious, was found dead from a single stab wound to her stomach in room 100 of the Chelsea Hotel in New York on October 12, 1978. Nancy had a troubled life, diagnosed with schizophrenia at 15 and supporting herself through prostitution from age 17 before becoming a groupie to bands like The Ramones, New York Dolls, and Aerosmith. She became inseparable from Sid Vicious for 19 months, and they both were heroin and Tuinal addicts. On the night of October 11, 1978, several people visited their room, and Sid allegedly took up to 52 Tuinal tablets, completely incapacitating him. At around 2:30 am on October 12th, Nancy asked Rockets Redglare, their drug connection and Sid's bodyguard, for Dilaudid. Later, around 7:30 am, female moans were heard, and at 10 am, Sid called the front desk. Nancy was found stabbed and bleeding to death, in a weird angle under the bathroom sink. Sid initially confessed but was likely too incapacitated. The theory later emerged that Rockets Redglare, who died in 2007, was the murderer, allegedly stabbing Nancy with Sid's Bowie knife during an argument over stolen cash, a confession he reportedly made while drinking at CBGB.
