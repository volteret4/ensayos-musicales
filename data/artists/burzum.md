## albums
**Dauði Baldrs (1997) - Prison Album** : Recorded while Varg Vikernes was incarcerated, this album utilized a MIDI keyboard and a cheap recorder. The MIDI wind instruments sounded unrealistic, giving the album a "cheap" quality similar to a Super Nintendo JRPG of the era. It was initially heavily criticized, especially as it followed "Filosofem," but its perception has changed over time with the rise of micro-genres like Dungeon Chiptune. **Filosofem (1996) - Predecessor to Prison Albums** : This album preceded "Dauði Baldrs" and was mentioned in the context of the criticism received by the subsequent prison albums due to their perceived lower quality sound. "y más después de ser el siguiente disco tras filósofem." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
**Two Prison Albums (Dates Unspecified) - Dungeon Synth Influence** : Varg Vikernes, under the moniker Burzum, recorded two key albums while imprisoned. Lacking access to many instruments, his tracks inadvertently sounded almost like an RPG soundtrack, an aesthetic born of necessity that profoundly influenced the second wave of Dungeon Synth, a genre currently experiencing a peak in popularity. ## curiosities
**Admiration for Dark Wave Neoclassical** : Varg Vikernes acknowledged his admiration for Anglo-Saxon dark music derived from post-punk, specifically Dark Wave Neoclassical artists like Dead Can Dance and Death in June, which notably influenced the atmospheric approach of some of his works. "Entre ellos, el propio Wurzum, que reconoció su admiración por estas propuestas." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
**Imprisonment of Varg Vikernes** : Varg Vikernes was sent to prison for several years following the 1993 murder of Euronymous, leader of Mayhem. During his incarceration, he recorded what are known as his "prison albums." "Este suceso envió a Bekerne esa prisión durante varios años." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
**Murder of Mayhem Member** : Vargh Vikernes of the band Burzum was convicted for stabbing a member of the rival band Mayhem 23 times, which resulted in the victim's death. For this crime, he was sentenced to 21 years in jail. "Vargh Vicarans of the band Burzum stabbed a guy in a rival band called Mayhem 23 times.
