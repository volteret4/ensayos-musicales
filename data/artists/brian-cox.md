## curiosities
**Distinguished Scientific Career** : Brian Cox has gone on to hold several prestigious scientific chairs, authored a number of books, and is a well-known host of science shows for the BBC, cementing his career as a full-time physicist. "Since then, he's been a full on physicist, even working at CERN's Large Hadron Collider in Switzerland." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Physicist and Academic** : While playing keyboards for the dance act D:Ream (referred to as "Dream" in the transcript), Brian Cox studied physics at the University of Manchester. After the band ended in 1997, he continued his education, earning a doctorate in philosophy and working in high-energy particle physics, including at CERN's Large Hadron Collider in Switzerland. "When he was playing keyboards with them, he studied physics at the University of Manchester." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
