## curiosities
**Death and Estate Auction (2019)** : Keith Flint, the dancer and singer for The Prodigy, took his own life on March 4, 2019. Despite owning a pub called The Leather Bottle, he was heavily in debt. Following his death, his possessions were auctioned off to settle his debts, raising a total of 350,000 pounds. Among the items sold was an unusual stuffed rabbit wearing a backpack and carrying a shotgun.
