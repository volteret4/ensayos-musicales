## albums
**The Hurting (1983) – Debut Album** : Released in 1983, this was Tears for Fears' debut album. Initially perceived as "basically another technopop project" of its era, it was later recognized as "something much more" insightful and different upon closer listening. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## songs
**Mad World (1982)** : This song was released as a single in September 1982, prior to their debut album, "The Hurting," and became an "across the board hits" across new wave, alternative rock, and pop genres. Roland Orzabal wrote the song, with bass player Curt Smith providing the vocals. Orzabal was deeply interested in psychology and psychiatry at the time, particularly fascinated by Arthur Janov's primal scream therapy, a treatment also explored by John Lennon and Yoko Ono. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
