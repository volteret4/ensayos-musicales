# artist - Roxy Music

## albums
**Avalon (1982)** : This album was recorded under analog conditions at Compass Point Studios in the Bahamas, utilizing two-inch tape likely running at 30 inches per second, before being transferred to vinyl. The vinyl version is described as exquisite and is considered an excellent record for testing audio equipment.
