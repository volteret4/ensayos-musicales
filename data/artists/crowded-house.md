## curiosities
**Active Period (1985 – 1996)** : Led by singer Neil Finn, the band had a series of best-selling albums between 1985 and 1996, gaining fans worldwide including Eddie Vedder of Pearl Jam. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Continued Togetherness** : The band is still together after spending over 10 years apart following their reunion. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Farewell Gig at Sydney Opera House (November 24, 1996)** : The band performed a giant farewell gig on the steps of the famous Sydney Opera House to raise money for the Sydney Children's Hospital, which was experiencing a cash crunch. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Motivation for Reunion (2007)** : The reunion was spurred by Paul Hester's funeral and the 10-year anniversary of the Sydney Opera House performance (coinciding with a DVD release), which led to a "reconnection" among the members. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Origin from Split Enz** : The band grew out of Split Enz, which was one of the most successful New Zealand bands of all time. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Paul Hester's Death (March 26, 2005)** : Paul Hester, suffering from personal problems and deep depression, died by hanging himself on March 26, 2005. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Paul Hester's Post-Band Career** : Paul Hester became a TV star in Australia, notably playing "Paul the cook" for The Wiggles. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Reunion Announcement (January 22, 2007)** : The band officially announced their reunion on January 22, 2007, after 10 years, one month, and 28 days apart. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
