# artist - D.A.E.C.

## albums
**The Bomb (2018)** : Released on February 9th, the band's debut album was very well-received critically, though it did not achieve significant sales. The artist's style is noted for being quite similar to James Blake, potentially placing them in Blake's shadow. However, fans of James Blake's intellectual style are likely to greatly enjoy D.A.E.C.'s music, as their first album was considered one of the notable releases from recent years.
