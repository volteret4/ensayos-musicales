# artist - Tall Dwarfs

## albums
**Slug Bucket Hairy Breath Monster (1985) - EP** : This EP from the New Zealand band Tall Dwarfs features the track "The Brain That Wouldn't Die," which serves as an example of the lo-fi sound gaining traction in the mid-1980s. The music was deliberately crafted to sound sonically inferior to the mainstream rock records of its day, showcasing an intentional embrace of low-fidelity aesthetics.
