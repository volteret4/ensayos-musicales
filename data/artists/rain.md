# artist - Rain

## members
- Chris Hutton
- Liam Gallagher
- Liam Gallagher (vocals)
- Noel Gallagher
- Paul Arthurs (Bonehead)

## curiosities
**Co-founding and Early Gigs** : Rain was co-founded on April 16, 1990, by guitarist Paul "Bonehead" Arthurs and singer Chris Hutton. The group began playing gigs at the Boardwalk in Manchester in late 1990. "The following year, April 16, 1990 to be exact, a group called Rain was co-founded by guitarist Paul Bohnhead Arthors and a singer named Chris Hutton." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IhG-WJH9om8
**Noel and Liam Gallagher's Integration** : By mid-1990, Noel Gallagher had joined Rain and taken over leadership. His younger brother, Liam Gallagher, also became the singer of the group. "By mid-year, Noel had joined up and taken over and his little brother had become the singer of Rain." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IhG-WJH9om8
**Precursor to Oasis (1991)** : Rain was the band Liam Gallagher was in prior to the formation of Oasis. After a Rain gig in 1991, Liam and his bandmates invited Noel Gallagher to become their manager, but Noel instead joined as lead guitarist, leading to the band's metamorphosis into Oasis. "Liam started a van called Rain. After a rain gig in 1991, Liam and his mates asked Noel to be their manager." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_b3KRS-Yx_s
**Predecessor to Oasis** : Rain was the original name of the band before Noel Gallagher took control and renamed them Oasis. Liam Gallagher was the singer for Rain. "his little brother Liam had become the singer in a band called Rain." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
**Renamed Oasis** : After Noel Gallagher took full creative control and insisted on being the sole songwriter, the band formerly known as Rain accepted his terms and was subsequently renamed Oasis. "Now, the rain took it and were renamed Oasis." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
