## curiosities
**Producer for Council Skies** : David Holmes produced Noel Gallagher's "Council Skies" album, marking the first instance where Noel Gallagher did not produce his own work. Holmes is characterized as a "very old school British electronic artist" who shares a good relationship with Noel Gallagher. His production on "Council Skies" was praised for its "super accurate" ability to naturally integrate various new sounds without them feeling forced or artificial.
