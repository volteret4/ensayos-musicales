It was not only an alternative hit but also reached number 15 on the Billboard Hot 100 singles chart, making it a Top 40 hit. ## curiosities
**Formation and Early Success** : Dishwalla formed in Santa Barbara, California in 1993, catching the attention of major labels during the stampede to sign alternative-sounding bands. Their major label debut, *Pet Your Friends*, was a huge success, but they struggled to follow it up. "Dishwala came out of Santa Barbara, California in 1993." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SOAL0GODN3M
**Meaning of Band Name** : The name "Dishwala" is a Hindi term referring to the person in India who provides satellite TV service to a neighborhood, specifically by installing a dish. "It's a Hindi term for the person who comes to your neighborhood in India with satellite TV service. They install a dish." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SOAL0GODN3M
**Post-Breakup and Indie Releases** : After their initial major label success and subsequent decline, Dishwalla endured a breakup. Following their reunion, they have since released a series of indie records, though none have replicated the success of their 1996 hit.
