# artist - David Whitaker

## curiosities
**Originator of "Bittersweet Symphony" Snippet** : David Whitaker was the orchestral arranger who originally wrote the distinctive five-note progression in Andrew Loog Oldham's version of "The Last Time." This specific musical phrase later served as the inspiration and sampled basis for The Verve's "Bittersweet Symphony." "He wrote that five note bit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AcADOuQUiAQ
