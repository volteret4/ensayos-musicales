**Death at Ritz Carlton Double Bay Hotel (November 22-23, 1997)** : Michael Hutchence's final hours began after dinner with his father and stepmother in Sydney on November 22, 1997, where he subsequently returned to room 524 at the Ritz Carlton Double Bay Hotel. Housekeeping discovered him dead around noon, the room in disarray with prescription pills scattered and furniture moved. He died between 9:50 and 10:30 AM, having been strangled by a belt hung on a door. His cause of death has been debated, with official findings concluding auto-erotic asphyxiation, though suicide was also considered, despite no note. "Michael was dead.
