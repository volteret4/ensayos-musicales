## songs
**The Future's So Bright, I Gotta Wear Shades (1986)** : This song gained significant popularity, reaching number 11 on the Canadian singles charts and number 19 on the Billboard Top 40 charts, and is credited with popularizing the phrase in culture. Despite offers of up to a million dollars from major corporations like Ford, Bausch and Lomb, AT&T, Ray-Ban sunglasses, and the US Army for licensing, the band consistently refused them. The song itself is not positive, but rather a grim, paranoid Cold War commentary about a nuclear scientist concerned for the planet's future. It holds the number 48 spot on the alt-rock one-hit wonders list with 14.3 million combined Spotify plays and YouTube views, and a one-hit wonder score of 1.80. **Band Origin** : Timbuk3 was a husband and wife duo from Madison, Wisconsin, who released a total of seven albums and several singles, though only one achieved widespread recognition.
