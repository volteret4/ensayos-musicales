# artist - Concrete Blonde

## curiosities
**Chris Sangerides as Producer** : The Tragically Hip expressed strong admiration for the sound of Concrete Blonde's records. This appreciation for Concrete Blonde's sound, which producer Chris Sangerides had worked on, was a key factor in The Hip's decision to collaborate with him for *Fully Completely*, even though they were initially unaware of his broader reputation in heavy metal. "We love the sound of those records. We really didn't know of his involvement as the godfather of English, heavy metal or whatever." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
