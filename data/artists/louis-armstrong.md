The widespread success of Domino's 1956 cover subsequently led to Armstrong's original version being reissued and also becoming a hit. Before this, the vibraphone was primarily a novelty instrument used for chimes. **UK Tour as Part of Exchange (1955)** : After a "one-in, one-out" agreement was established in 1955 between the American and British musicians' unions, Louis Armstrong was granted permission to tour the UK. This was contingent upon a reciprocal arrangement that allowed British trumpet player Freddie Randall to tour the US.
