# artist - The Cranberries

## members
- Dolores O'Riordan
- Dolores O'Riordan (singer, songwriter)

## albums
**Everybody Else Is Doing It, So Why Can't We?** : The band's first album, which sold six million copies.
**No Need To Argue** : The band's second album, which sold around 17 million copies.
**Third Album** : Sold another five million copies.

## songs
**Some god** : A mosh pit formed during their performance of this song, which was noted as an unusual occurrence.
**Water Circle (1990)** : The band's first recording with Dolores O'Riordan, a rough demo tape circulated to record companies in 1990.

## curiosities
**90s Cultural Impact** : Their first three albums were widely owned by "90s kids," often alongside records by Pearl Jam and Oasis.
**Dolores O'Riordan's Death** : Lived a "delicate existence" for the rest of her life, and died in a London hotel room at the age of 46.
**Dolores O'Riordan's Global Fame and Struggles** : Dolores O'Riordan became one of the most famous singers globally (male or female), but the pressures of the music business, endless touring, and constant tabloid/paparazzi harassment led to a nervous breakdown by age 24.
**Dolores O'Riordan's Voice** : Dolores O'Riordan possessed a distinctive voice that could range from "sweet as honey" to "growly and yelpy."
**Irish Origins** : Formed by four teenagers in Limorak, on the West Coast of Ireland.
**Rapid Signing in 1992** : Signed by a record company in 1992 amidst a "stampede to sign any band with a whiff of alternative about them," despite not fitting the usual guitar-heavy North American alt-rock or UK pre-Britpop sound.
