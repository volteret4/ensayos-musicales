# artist - Edward Morgan

## curiosities
**Bass Tech for Adam Clayton** : Edward Morgan served as the bass tech for U2's Adam Clayton. In an emergency on November 26, 1993, during the Zoo TV tour in Sydney, Morgan stepped in to play bass for U2 when Clayton was too drunk to perform. "He was up to a Rody Adam's base text to Edward Morgan to fill in." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
**Fill-in Performance for U2 (1993)** : Edward Morgan successfully filled in for Adam Clayton on bass during a U2 concert at the Sydney Football Stadium on November 26, 1993. This performance marked the only time U2 has performed with a substitute member. "Now that first show went off just fine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
