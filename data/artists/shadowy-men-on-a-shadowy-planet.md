## members
- Brian Connelly
- Dallas Good

## genres
- Garage rock
- Indie Surf Rock
- Punk
- Surf music

## albums

## curiosities
**Band Origins and Punk Roots** : The band was formed by two musicians from Calgary who relocated to Toronto, where they initially participated in a series of "doomed punk bands" before eventually collaborating to form Shadowy Men on a Shadowy Planet. "The We Men were built around two guys from Calgary who moved to Toronto to be involved in a series of doomed punk bands before they co-listed as this band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Band's Active Periods** : The band was active from 1984 to 1996, and then started playing occasional shows again in 2012. "It lasted from 1984 to 1996, but started playing the occasional show again in 2012." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
**Breakup and Subsequent Reunion (2012)** : The band initially broke up, leading members to join various other indie bands. However, they reunited in 2012 in a reunion that "seemed to have stuck," resulting in occasional gigs, a box set, and re-releases of their older material. "The band broke up and then everybody bounced through a long series of indie bands. [...] It much happened until 2012 when there was a reunion that seemed to have stuck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Collaboration with Fred Schneider** : Shadowy Men on a Shadowy Planet served as the backing band for Fred Schneider of The B-52s for some of his solo material, showcasing their versatility and respected musicianship. "And that time when Fred Schneider of the B-52s needed a backing band for some of his solo stuff." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**EPs and Juno Award** : The group released a number of EPs during their active period and received a Juno Award in the category of Best Instrumental Artist, recognizing their unique instrumental sound. "There were a number of EPs, a June Award for Best Instrumental Artist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Founding Member Reed Diamond's Death (2001)** : Founding member Reed Diamond passed away from cancer in 2001. "Founding member Reed Diamond died of cancer in 2001." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Producer Collaboration** : Steve Elveni, a producer/engineer known for his work with bands like Nirvana and The Pixies, was such a fan of Shadowy Men on a Shadowy Planet that he worked on one of their albums for free. "Steve Elveni, the producer slash engineer known for working with Nirvana and the pixies and so many more, liked shadowy men so much that he worked on one of their albums for free." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
