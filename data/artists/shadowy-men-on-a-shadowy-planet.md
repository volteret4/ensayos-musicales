# artist - Shadowy Men on a Shadowy Planet

## members
- Dallas Good
- Steve Elveni (producer/engineer for one album)

## genres
- Garage rock
- Surf music

## albums
**Oh, I Guess We're An Effing Surf Band After All (2016) – Compilation Album** : This compilation was released in 2016, and its title reflects the band's eventual acceptance of the "surf band" label, which they had previously resisted. "oh, I guess we're an effing surf band after all." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag

## songs
**Having an Average Weekend** : This surf music track was famously used as a TV theme song by The Kids in the Hall. Despite its surfy sound, the band initially resisted the surf music label. "The surf music track used by the kids in the hall for a TV theme." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag

## curiosities
**Band's Active Periods** : The band was active from 1984 to 1996, and then started playing occasional shows again in 2012. "It lasted from 1984 to 1996, but started playing the occasional show again in 2012." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
**Producer Collaboration** : Steve Elveni, a producer/engineer known for his work with bands like Nirvana and The Pixies, was such a fan of Shadowy Men on a Shadowy Planet that he worked on one of their albums for free. "Steve Elveni, the producer slash engineer known for working with Nirvana and the pixies and so many more, liked shadowy men so much that he worked on one of their albums for free." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
