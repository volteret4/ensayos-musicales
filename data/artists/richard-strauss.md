## songs
**An Alpine Symphony (1979 Performance)** : This symphony was performed by the Berlin Philharmonic, conducted by Herbert von Karajan, for the very first test pressing of a compact disc in 1979. It was specifically chosen to showcase the new technology's promised clarity and wide range of dynamics. "The first test pressing, a CD used to demonstrate the new technology, was a recording of the Berlin Philharmonic, conducted by Herbert von Karrihan, performing Richard Strauss' and Elpine Symphony." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BHkJFA0LMro ← richard-strauss
