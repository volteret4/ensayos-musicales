**Brain Aneurysm and Vision Loss (2001)** : In 2001, Carl Wallinger suffered a brain aneurysm that necessitated emergency surgery. Although he largely recovered, the clot's location required the removal of tissue around his optic nerve, resulting in the loss of peripheral vision on the right side of both eyes. "In 2001, Carl Walinger suffered a brain annuism and required emergency surgery.
