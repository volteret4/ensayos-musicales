## curiosities
**Live Aid Performance** : Ron Wood performed at Live Aid in Philadelphia on July 13, 1985, alongside Bob Dylan and Keith Richards of The Rolling Stones. Their collective performance was noted as arguably "one of the worst performances of his life" by Bob Dylan.
