# artist - Dylan Carlson

## curiosities
**Omission of Greenhouse During Search (1994)** : During the search of Kurt Cobain's house with Detective Tom Grant, Dylan Carlson reportedly "forgot to tell Tom" about the greenhouse in the backyard. This omission meant the greenhouse, where Cobain's body was later found, was not searched that night. "Not dealing new about the greenhouse, but he apparently forgot to tell Tom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKWgbxk46eM ← dylan-carlson
**Search for Kurt Cobain (1994)** : On the morning of April 7, 1994, Dylan Carlson, identified as Kurt Cobain's best friend, accompanied Detective Tom Grant in a search for Cobain. They checked Cobain's usual hangouts and drug dealer spots before visiting his house. "Running a car he picked up Kurt's best friend Dylan Carlson." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKWgbxk46eM ← dylan-carlson
