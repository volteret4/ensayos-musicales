## curiosities
**Example of Shoegaze-leaning Dreampop** : Slowdive is highlighted as a band that exemplifies how Dreampop can "leak into the Shoegaze world." Their music is characterized by atmosphere, textures, reverb, and echo, but with a potentially more serious attitude and increased volume compared to quieter Dreampop acts, bridging the two genres. "Yes, Dreampop can be quiet like the cocktail twins and Mazzy Star, but it can also leak into the Shougay's world and touch on groups like Slow Dive and My Bloody Valentine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g ← slowdive
**Old School Style Perception** : The band's style on their 2017 album was acknowledged as potentially being seen as "old school" or a bit behind the times, appealing mostly to "old school" fans due to their long period of silence. "es un estilo que se ha quedado como muy aparte y como muy de de gente de la vieja escuela." ← Music Radar Clan > Discos que los subsciptores echasteis en falta en la lista de lo mejor del año y tenéis razón | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=APPodZ7sl1w&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← slowdive ← slowdive
**Oxford Music Scene** : Slowdive was among the "big-ish Oxford bands" that had achieved semi-fame by playing at the Jericho Tavern, the venue where "On a Friday" had a significant gig in October 1991. "the Jericho Tavern, which by the way was semi-famous at the time for hosting other big-ish Oxford bands like Super Grass and Ride and Slow Dive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM ← slowdive ← slowdive
**Pioneer of Shoegaze** : Slowdive was among the bands that surfaced between 1989 and 1991, adopting and expanding upon My Bloody Valentine's foundation of ethereal melodies and distorted guitars within the Shoegaze genre. Their career was launched by Creation Records. "En seguida, susguieron bandas como Ride, Slowdive, Feltzaint y Lash, quienes tomaron aquella combinación de melodía setéria, guitarras distorsionadas y la llevaron aún más lejos." ← El disco imposible： La historia de Loveless y My Bloody Valentine | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q_SHNe_Ce9A
**Post-Rock Evolution** : Initially a shoegaze band, Slowdive's later material began to incorporate elements of post-rock, demonstrating an overlap between the two genres. "Slow dive, definitely Shoegaze, but their later stuff was also very post-rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY ← slowdive

**Shoegaze Band Example** : Slowdive was mentioned as an example of a Shoegaze band, a loud, fuzzy, buzzy form of guitar rock that emerged from the Thames River Valley in the very late 80s and early 90s. "There were bands like Slow Dive, Chapter House, Curve, Lush, My Bloody Valentine, Adorable, The Catherine Wheel." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU ← slowdive
**Shoegaze Continuation** : Slowdive is noted as a band that continued the Shoegaze movement as an independent offshoot from the major 90s movements, extending almost until the mid-1990s and leaving a distinctive sound.
