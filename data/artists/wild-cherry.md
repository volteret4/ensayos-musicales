## curiosities
**Appearance on Dina (1976)** : Wild Cherry appeared on Dina Shore's talk show on November 26, 1976, as part of an episode that also featured comedian George Kirby, Miss Teenage America Kathy Jordan, singer Lynn Anderson, and ABBA. This appearance helped expose the band to a more mature audience.
