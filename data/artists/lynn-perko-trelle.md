## curiosities
**Hardcore Scene Female Drummer** : Lynn Perko-Trelle was one of the few female drummers active in the American hardcore scene. She played in several bands, notably Sister Double Happiness, who toured with major grunge bands Nirvana and Soundgarden, and Imperial Teen, which featured Roddy Bottom of Faith No More.
