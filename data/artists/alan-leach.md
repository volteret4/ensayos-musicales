**From Recording Studio to Speed Quizzing** : After leaving the British band Shed Seven in the 1990s, drummer Alan Leach initially opened a recording studio. However, he discovered a passion for trivia, leading him to host pub quizzes and eventually co-found Speed Quizzing with his brother, a company that develops and sells smartphone pub quiz software. "drummer Alan Leach left the group and opened up a recording studio. But then he realized his true passion was trivia." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI ← alan-leach
