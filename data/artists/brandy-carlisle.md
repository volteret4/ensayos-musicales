# artist - Brandy Carlisle

## songs
**Right on Time (2020)** : This two-year-old single was nominated for a 2022 Grammy for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance, but the artist expressed disappointment as it was moved from the Americana category to pop. Carlisle had hoped it would be included in Americana, which she described as more than a genre, but a community, and made the song for her "island of misfits" rather than the masses.
