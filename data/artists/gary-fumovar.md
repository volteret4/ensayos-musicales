## curiosities
**Early Keyboards Member** : Gary Fumovar, a friend of Dan Auerbach's younger brother, was an early participant in The Black Keys, contributing keyboards to their foundational demo record. "And there were three keys on this record, by the way. There was Dan, there was Patrick, and Gary Fumovar, a friend of Dan's younger brother who was also there to play keyboards." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
