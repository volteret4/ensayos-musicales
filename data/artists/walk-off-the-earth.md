## curiosities

**YouTube Breakout with One Guitar Cover** : Walk Off The Earth achieved their "big break" after posting a YouTube video featuring five members playing a single guitar to cover Gotye's "Somebody That I Used To Know." This video garnered 21 million views, propelling them to online fame.
