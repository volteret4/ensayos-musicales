# artist - Big Audio Dynamite

## members
- Mick Jones

## songs
**E=MC² (1985)** : This song marked a significant commercial breakthrough for sampling. While all the music was new, Mick Jones dubbed in dialogue from the 1970 film "Performance" for added effects, a truly radical technique for its time. It became the first worldwide rock and roll hit to feature sampled sounds. The song was a massive hit single in 1985 and 1986. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
