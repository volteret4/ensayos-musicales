## curiosities
**Featured on Alt-Rock Playlists** : their alt pop funk music, along with many remixes of their songs, was found on radio playlists for late 1986 alt-rock stations.
**Taylor Hawkins' Brother's Preference** : Taylor Hawkins' brother and his friends in Laguna Beach, California, listened to Level 42, a genre that was acceptable in a scene where hard rock was "frowned upon."
