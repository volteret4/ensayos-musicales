## curiosities
**Fyre Festival Co-Founder and Promoter** : Ja Rule, in collaboration with Billy McFarland, conceived the idea for the Fyre Festival. This event was promoted as an ultra-exclusive, multi-day concert on a private Bahamian island, promising luxury experiences and top artists to attendees who paid up to $250,000 for VIP packages.
