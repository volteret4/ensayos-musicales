## curiosities
**Band Breakup and Reunion** : Following a few years after their period of significant success, Semisonic disbanded. However, the band subsequently reunited in 2017, and this reunion appears to have been sustained. "after a couple of more years, Semisonic broke up. But then there was a reunion in 2017, and it seems to be sticking." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d_uDHds2XGQ
**Dan Wilson's Songwriting Prowess** : Guitarist and singer Dan Wilson has achieved considerable recognition as a highly sought-after professional songwriter. He co-wrote Adele's massive global hit "Someone Like You," which topped charts in dozens of countries and earned him a Grammy for Song of the Year. He also received a Grammy for his production work on Adele's "21" album and was nominated for a Grammy for writing "Not Ready to Make Nice" for the Dixie Chicks. His extensive songwriting credits include collaborations with dozens of other notable acts, such as Fantagram, John Legend, Steve Perry, Josh Groban, Jason Barraiz, Leanne Rhyme, Stevie Nick's Halsey Cold War Kids, K-Flay, Weezer, Panic at the Disco, and Taylor Swift. "But the big story here is guitarist and singer Dan Wilson. He's become a renowned songwriter for hire." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d_uDHds2XGQ
