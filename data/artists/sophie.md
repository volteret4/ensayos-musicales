## albums
**Oil of Every Pearl's Un-Insides (Year not specified)** : This album propelled hyperpop to an entirely new level, revolutionizing it forever. It is a deconstructed club music album that alternates between almost industrial moments and deeply emotional landscapes. Loaded with glitches and avant-garde electronic textures, the album balances the experimental with the accessible, remaining profoundly pop. In this work, Sophie explores themes such as identity, gender dysphoria, and acceptance, making it a key work for a new generation of artists.
