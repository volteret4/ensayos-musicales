## curiosities
**Versatile Drummer and Collaborator** : Cindy Blackman, later Cindy Blackman Santana, is a highly versatile drummer known for her work in jazz, funk, Latin, and rock genres. She has performed with Lenny Kravitz, including a notable video performance, and is married to Carlos Santana, also performing with his band. "Cindy Blackman. That was her first thing, but she can also do funk, Latin, her husband is Carlos Santana and Rock. And that's where Lennie Kravitz has been. Maybe you've seen the video. And here's Cindy doing a drum solo with Santana." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY ← cindy-blackman-santana
