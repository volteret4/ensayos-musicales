**Versatile Drummer and Collaborator** : Cindy Blackman, later Cindy Blackman Santana, is a highly versatile drummer known for her work in jazz, funk, Latin, and rock genres. She has performed with Lenny Kravitz, including a notable video performance, and is married to Carlos Santana, also performing with his band. "Cindy Blackman.
