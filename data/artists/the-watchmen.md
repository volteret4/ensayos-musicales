## albums
**In the Trees (1994) - Gold-Selling Album** : Released in 1994, this album was The Watchmen's most successful, selling over 100,000 copies in Canada and achieving gold certification. Its popularity was supported by Cross Canada tours, festival appearances, and Juno nominations. **Formation and Early Success** : The Watchmen formed in Winnipeg in 1988 and quickly achieved their first gold record in 1991, establishing their presence in the Canadian music industry. Their early career included extensive Cross Canada tours, festival appearances, and Juno nominations. "They were formed in Winnipeg in 1988, had their first gold record in 1991." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
**Initial Breakup and Farewell Tour** : By 2003, the band decided that playing "just wasn't fun anymore," leading to the announcement of a farewell tour and their subsequent breakup. This marked a temporary cessation of their active musical career. "by 2003 it just wasn't fun anymore, so they announced a farewell tour and broke up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
