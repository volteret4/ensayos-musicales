Nevertheless, her influence on contemporary artists such as Billie Eilish, Lorde, and Halsey is considered immense. "Even with all that, Lana is subject to all sorts of criticism, mostly over the themes and imagery in her music. At the same time, though, her influence has been immense. **Polarizing Artistic Style** : Since her emergence in 2005, Lana Del Rey has been a polarizing artist, largely due to her distinctive musical style.
