## albums
**Born to Die (2012) - Debut Album** : This album achieved significant commercial success, reaching gold status and then platinum, and hitting number 1 in many countries. It is widely included in lists of the best albums of the decade and is noted for having six distinct singles that entered the sales charts, a feat shared only with Lady Gaga in the same century. Critics were divided, with some hailing it as a masterpiece and others in the UK calling it a "completely pre-fabricated and mediated album by a record label." The album's themes were extensively reworked with Justin Parker to be more aesthetic, with Lana Del Rey herself stating it's based on a "trilogy of three places" she lived: New York (adolescence), London (symphonic and baroque music influences), and Miami (retro 50s aesthetic). Its cover art and subsequent album covers (Ultraviolence, Honeymoon) were photographed in or around Miami. "Ella hizo un disco que fue disco de oro, después se cooperquía en disco del platinho, fue número 1, un montón de países, y hoy en día, esta incluiden, prácticamente todas las listas de el mejor disco de la década." ← Music Radar Clan > Born to Die.
