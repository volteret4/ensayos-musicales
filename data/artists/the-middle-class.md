## albums
**Out of Vogue (1979) – First West Coast Hardcore Record** : Released in January 1979, this 4-track EP is considered the first hardcore record from the American West Coast. The entire EP was just 4 minutes and 55 seconds long, with its four songs finishing in less than five minutes, embodying the stripped-down, fast, and short style of early hardcore. Because major labels and commercial radio wouldn't consider such music, The Middle Class formed their own indie label, Joke Records, to release it. "The first hardcore record from the American West Coast seems to be by a band from Santa Anna called The Middle Class that appeared in January 1979. It was a 4 track EP that was over in 4 minutes and 55 seconds. 4 songs done in less than 5 minutes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
