## songs
**How Sweet to Be an Idiot (1973)** : This song, from a 1973 sketch, was the basis for a lawsuit against Oasis. Monty Python "insisted that the 1994 song Whatever sounded way too much like" "How Sweet to Be an Idiot." Neil Innis, the writer of the Python song, "won a co-writing credit on Oasis's Whatever."
