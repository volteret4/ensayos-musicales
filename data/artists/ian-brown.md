## curiosities
**Arrest and Imprisonment (1998)** : Following the British Airways incident, Ian Brown was arrested and, despite his testimony, a judge with alleged ties to the airline industry rejected his explanations and sentenced him to a four-month term in Strangeways prison in Manchester. His appeal, arguing that his UK tour would be canceled and over 100 people would lose work, was denied, with the judge aiming to "set an example." He was ultimately released unharmed before Christmas of that year. "The message to the public, he said, must be that anyone who behaves in this way will lose their liberty." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
**British Airways Air Rage and Imprisonment (1998)** : On February 13, 1998, following an MTV event in Paris, Ian Brown boarded British Airways flight 1611 from Charles de Gaulle to Manchester. Once airborne, he reportedly argued with a flight attendant, allegedly threatening to cut her hands off, and then caused a disturbance near the cockpit by banging on the door and swearing at the crew during the plane's descent. Upon landing, he was arrested and charged with endangering the safety of an aircraft. Despite his defense, a judge with ties to the airline industry sentenced him to four months in Strangeways Prison in Manchester to "set an example," and he is now permanently banned from flying British Airways. "The message to the public, he said, must be that anyone who behaves in this way will lose their liberty." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
**British Airways Flight Incident (1998)** : On February 13, 1998, not long after releasing his first solo album and while estranged from The Stone Roses, Ian Brown boarded British Airways Flight 1611 from Charles de Gaulle Airport to Manchester after an MTV event in Paris. Once airborne, he allegedly got into a row with a flight attendant, threatening to "cut your hands off," and then caused a disturbance by banging on the cockpit door and swearing at the crew during the plane's descent. Upon landing, he was arrested and charged with endangering the safety of an aircraft in flight. "Officially, Ian got into a row with a flight attendant over something, allegedly saying, I'm going to cut your hands off." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
**Lifetime Flight Ban (1998)** : As a consequence of his actions and conviction, Ian Brown was permanently banned from flying with British Airways. "Fortunately, he was honed unharmed for Christmas of that year, but unfortunately, he'll never be permitted to fly British airways again." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
