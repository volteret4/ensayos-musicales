**Decent but Lesser Solo Work** : Jerry Cantrell has released "some decent solo records," but none have achieved the same level of impact or success as the collective accomplishments of Alice in Chains. "And a lot of some decent solo records, but nothing anywhere near the order of what Allyson accomplished together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Installation of Home Recording Outfit for Layne Staley (Post-1997)** : Jerry Cantrell, the guitarist for Alice in Chains, installed a home recording outfit in Layne Staley's condo. This was done after Staley moved into the condo in April 1997, providing him with the means to record music at home, although Staley only used it occasionally. "Allison Chains guitarist Jerry Cantrell installed a home recording outfit that Lane used once in a while, but mostly he just painted, watched TV, played video games, and did drugs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
**Missed Gig Due to Calendar Error (1991)** : In late November 1991, Jerry Cantrell went hunting, having promised to return for an important Alice in Chains gig on December 1st. He mistakenly believed that November had 31 days, leading him to miss the scheduled show. Upon his return on December 2nd (which he thought was December 1st), he was surprised to discover he had missed the performance. As a Christmas gift that year, his record label presented him with his very own pocket calendar. "Jerry honestly believed that November has 31 days and he ended up missing the gig." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dMsEj62KFEw
**The Cable Guy Soundtrack Contribution (1996)** : Jerry Cantrell of Alice in Chains contributed to *The Cable Guy* soundtrack in 1996, an album associated with the "creepy Jim Carrey vehicle" film. The soundtrack also featured other artists like Silverchair, Porno for Pyros, and Cracker. "Silver Chair Jerry Cantrell of Allyson Chains, Porno for Pyrrho's Cracker, and primitive radio gods ended up with a hit with standing outside a broken phone booth with money in my hand." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
