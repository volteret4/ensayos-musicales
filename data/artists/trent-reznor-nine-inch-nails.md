# artist - Trent Reznor (Nine Inch Nails)

## curiosities
**Collaboration with David Bowie** : Reznor's admiration for Bowie led to collaborations, including touring together and Reznor's appearance in the "creepy stalker" music video for Bowie's 1997 song "I'm Afraid of Americans." Nine Inch Nails also contributed a remix of the song.
