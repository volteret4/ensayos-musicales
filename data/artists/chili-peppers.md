## curiosities
**Tibetan Freedom Festival Performer** : The Chili Peppers were part of the extensive lineup for the first annual Tibetan Freedom Festival held on July 15, 1996, in San Francisco, an event organized by Adam Yauch. "The line up included the Beastie Boys, plus the smashing pumpkins, Bjork, Sonic Youth, the Chili Peppers, and more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9_BTe15NooI ← chili-peppers
