# artist - Bobby Lonnie

## curiosities
**Featured on Quadraphonic Demonstration Disk (1973)** : Bobby Lonnie's music was included on a 1973 quadraphonic demonstration disk, which aimed to showcase the immersive capabilities of quad sound. His segment was presented as transporting listeners from an underwater experience to Broadway. "From beneath the ocean, we transport you to Broadway.
