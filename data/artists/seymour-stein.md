# artist - Seymour Stein

## curiosities
**Founder of Sire Records** : Seymour Stein is recognized as the founder of Sire Records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Key Signings and Importations** : Seymour Stein signed pivotal artists such as The Ramones and Talking Heads, gave Madonna her first proper record deal, and was responsible for importing "unknown English bands" like The Smiths, The Pretenders, and The English Beat into North America. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Visit to Beijing Punk Club D22** : Seymour Stein visited the punk club D22 in Beijing with Alan Cross, where he listened to Chinese punk and alternative bands for hours, subsequently predicting a significant future for music in Asia, particularly China. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
