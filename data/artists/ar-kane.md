## curiosities
**"The Black Jesus and Mary Chain" Comparison** : Early in their career, AR Kane's sound was quite noisy, leading writers to compare them to The Jesus and Mary Chain, dubbing them "the Black Jesus and Mary Chain." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
**Coined the Term "Dreampop"** : Alex Ayuli originated the term "Dreampop" to describe AR Kane's music. He did this as a way to challenge journalists who were assigning labels the band disliked, providing a distinct descriptor for their atmospheric and textured sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
**EP Produced by Robin Guthrie (1987)** : In 1987, AR Kane signed with 4AD and released an EP that was produced by Robin Guthrie of the Cocteau Twins, marking a significant collaboration. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
**Tremendous Influence** : Although not widely known, AR Kane, composed of Alex Ayuli and Rudy Tambala, was tremendously influential across several genres, including Dreampop, Trip Hop, and Shoegaze, due to their unique musical approach. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
