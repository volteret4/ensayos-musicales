**Ernie Ball Music Man Signature Guitar – Custom Design** : St. Vincent is one of the few guitarists with a custom signature model, the Ernie Ball Music Man St. Vincent Signature Model. Designed based on her 20+ years of playing experience, it is lightweight, comfortable for both standing and sitting, available in various colors, and features three mini humbuckers with a "convoluted but very cool pickup configuration" for extensive tone flexibility. "She is one of the few people who has a custom signature guitar built just for her. It's called the Ernie Ball Music Man St. Vincent Signature Model." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0 ← st-vincent
