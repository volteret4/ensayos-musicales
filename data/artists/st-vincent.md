**Ernie Ball Music Man Signature Guitar – Custom Design** : St. Vincent is one of the few guitarists with a custom signature model, the Ernie Ball Music Man St. Vincent Signature Model. Designed based on her 20+ years of playing experience, it is lightweight, comfortable for both standing and sitting, available in various colors, and features three mini humbuckers with a "convoluted but very cool pickup configuration" for extensive tone flexibility. "She is one of the few people who has a custom signature guitar built just for her. It's called the Ernie Ball Music Man St. Vincent Signature Model." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0

**Personal View on "Guitar Hero" Status** : Despite her influence and recognition, St. Vincent personally does not identify with the term "guitar hero," finding it a "strange thing," but expresses excitement if she inspires young people to play guitar. "I don't think about that in those terms ever. I really don't. It would be a strange thing for me, like Psychic Lita. ... Great. I'm so excited if kids want to play guitar. It's the best thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
