## curiosities
**Comparison to Matt Bellamy** : She is considered a potential "female equivalent to Mewz's Met Bellamy," suggesting her high caliber as a guitarist and artist. "If there's a female equivalent to Mewz's Met Bellamy, it's probably her." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Continuous Musical Evolution** : St. Vincent, whose real name is Annie Clark, has demonstrated a steady ascent in her career, with her music progressively improving with each album release over the years. "Any Clark, which is St. Vincent's real name, began a slowest scent years ago and has been getting better and better and better with each album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Guitar Solo Composition Method** : St. Vincent sometimes composes her guitar solos on a MIDI keyboard before meticulously learning and transferring them to be played on her guitar. "Sometimes she works out her solos on a MIDI keyboard and then learns them for the guitar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Highly Respected by Fellow Guitarists** : St. Vincent is highly revered by other guitarists, noted as one of the few, and possibly the only woman, to have her own custom signature guitar model. "She is so revered by other guitarists. She is one of the few people and seriously, the only woman I can think of who has her own custom signature guitar built just for her." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Personal View on "Guitar Hero" Status** : Despite her influence and recognition, St. Vincent personally does not identify with the term "guitar hero," finding it a "strange thing," but expresses excitement if she inspires young people to play guitar. "I don't think about that in those terms ever. I really don't. It would be a strange thing for me, like Psychic Lita. ... Great. I'm so excited if kids want to play guitar. It's the best thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Unique Guitar Sound** : She is known for using a distinctive collection of fuzz pedals, which she combines in "weird ways" to make her guitar sound like "something other than a guitar," contributing to her signature sonic palette. "She has a collection of fuzz pedals that she combines in weird ways to make her guitar sound like something other than a guitar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
