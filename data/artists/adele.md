Its worldwide sales are noted to be somewhere north of 20 million. It is considered potentially the last album to achieve diamond status in the United States due to the ongoing decline in physical music sales and the increasing dominance of streaming. "In September of 2016 it was officially certified as having sold 10 million copies and worldwide sales are somewhere north of 20 million." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk

## songs

**Rolling in the Deep (2010) - Second Best-Selling Digital Track** : Adele's "Rolling in the Deep" was the second best-selling digital track of the 2010s, selling 8.9 million copies. Its strong digital performance highlighted Adele's popularity and ability to drive sales in a period of transition for music consumption. "They were followed by Adele's Rolling in the Deep with sales of 8.9 million." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNqqLDpUuZY
**Skyfall (2012) – Modern Classic Bond Theme** : Adele was approached by producers in 2012 to sing the theme for the James Bond film "Skyfall" due to her immense popularity with her "21" album. After reading the script, she agreed, creating what is described as "as modern a classic Bond theme as you can get." The song incorporated traditional Bond elements: a minor key, a 60s jazz feel, a dangerous vibe, big orchestration, and a powerful female singer. Co-writer Paul Epworth spent months analyzing past themes to identify commonalities, specifically discovering the consistent use of a "minor ninth." The song took 18 months from initial approach to completion. "After reading the script, she agreed. It's about as modern a classic Bond theme as you can get." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo

During this unique residency, Adele performed twice a week for a month, drawing 730,000 attendees. The event broke the record for a non-Vegas residency, grossing $240 million USD over just 10 shows and injecting half a billion euros into Munich's economy that month.
