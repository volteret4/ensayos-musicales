## albums
**25 (2015) – Diamond Certified Album** : This album was officially certified as having sold 10 million physical copies in America in September 2016, earning it a Diamond record status. Its worldwide sales are noted to be somewhere north of 20 million. It is considered potentially the last album to achieve diamond status in the United States due to the ongoing decline in physical music sales and the increasing dominance of streaming. ## curiosities
**Indie Artist Status** : Despite her massive commercial success, Adele is considered an indie artist because she records for XL Recordings, which is part of the Beggars Group, a prominent independent music collective. "She records for XL, so she's an indie artist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Pop Dominance in 2010s** : Adele was a major pop artist whose popularity contributed to the overall rise of pop music that "swamped" rock by 2011 or 2012, signifying the genre's significant cultural influence during the decade. "And you can make the argument that it certainly did swamp by not only a rise in pop music, you know, Maroon 5, Justin Bieber, Rihanna, Pink Kitty Perry, Carly Recepcion, Adele, but also by the increasing strength of hip hop and the big beats and bass lines of EDM." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Y6nHw5xx88
**Road Rage Treatment** : Scientists in Brazil found that listening to instrumental versions of Adele's songs, particularly piano versions of "Someone Like You" and "Hello," can help reduce road rage. This research is significant due to the high number of road fatalities in Brazil, with nearly 50,000 people dying on the road each year. "The study determined that piano versions of someone like you and hello work best." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
