**Skyfall (2012) – Modern Classic Bond Theme** : Adele was approached by producers in 2012 to sing the theme for the James Bond film "Skyfall" due to her immense popularity with her "21" album. After reading the script, she agreed, creating what is described as "as modern a classic Bond theme as you can get." The song incorporated traditional Bond elements: a minor key, a 60s jazz feel, a dangerous vibe, big orchestration, and a powerful female singer. Co-writer Paul Epworth spent months analyzing past themes to identify commonalities, specifically discovering the consistent use of a "minor ninth." The song took 18 months from initial approach to completion. "After reading the script, she agreed. It's about as modern a classic Bond theme as you can get." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo ← adele
