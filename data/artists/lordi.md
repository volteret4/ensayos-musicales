## curiosities
**Lordi (Finland, 1992) – Won Eurovision 2006; First Finnish and First Metal Act to Win; Mr. Lordi = Tomi Putaansuu** : Lordi is a costumed Finnish metal band formed in 1992. The frontman Mr. Lordi is Tomi Putaansuu, a metal fan and costume designer. Lordi is themed around monsters and horror, changing their masks and costumes with every new album and tour. No member of Lordi can appear in public without being masked. The most fascinating thing about them: in addition to their European success, they won the Eurovision Song Contest in 2006 with "Hard Rock Hallelujah," making them the first Finnish group and first metal band to win the prize.
