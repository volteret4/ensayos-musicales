# artist - Sadie Dupuis

## labels

## curiosities
**Pioneering African-American Female Label Owner** : Sadie Dupuis made history by founding the Northern Recording Company, the first record label owned and operated by an African-American woman. She started this venture in Detroit, having borrowed only $85 to establish the company.
