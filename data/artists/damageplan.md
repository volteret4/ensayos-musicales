## curiosities
**Concert Security Fatalities - Alrosa Villa (2004)** : During a performance on December 8th, 2004, at Alrosa Villa in Columbus, Ohio, the band's head of security, alongside a fan and a club employee, was shot and killed while attempting to intervene in the onstage assassination of guitarist Dimebag Darrell by Nathan Gale. Additionally, three members of Damageplan's road crew sustained non-fatal gunshot wounds.
