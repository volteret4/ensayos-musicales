# artist - Katrina and the Waves

## songs
**Walking on Sunshine** : This song is mentioned in the context of indie record store clerk stereotypes, noting that its "way too commercial, way too overplayed" nature would make it an unlikely favorite for an obnoxiously snobby clerk, contrasting it with the indie aesthetic. "I have a hard time believing that an obnoxiously snobby indie record store clerk would love walking on sunshine by Katrina and the waves that much." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
**Walking on Sunshine (1985)** : The horn section featured on this popular song was performed by musicians known as The Rumor Brass. This same group also contributed to The Clash's "Revolution Rock" from their "London Calling" album.
