## songs
**Boulder to Birmingham** : Written by Bill Danoff, this song appeared on Emmylou Harris's 1975 album "Pieces of Sky." Harris used it to process her profound grief and feelings of loss following Gram Parsons' death, finding power in its words to express what she otherwise could not. The lyrics speak of finding solace, enduring loss, and the desire to see a loved one's face again. "She put her feelings about losing Graham, and she worked through her grief over losing him into a song that she called Bolder to Birmingham." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 35 - Cosmic American Music - 70s Country Rock | https://seventies.libsyn.com/ep-35-cosmic-american-music-70s-country-rock
**Love Hurts (1973) - with Gram Parsons** : This duet, released on Gram Parsons' "Grievous Angel" album, is described as an emotionally powerful and definitive rendition of the song, embodying heartbreak, desperation, beauty, love, and sorrow.
