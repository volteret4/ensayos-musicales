# artist - Casador

## members
- Angus Faye (former drummer)
- Boris Baker (bass player)

## venues
- The Bathhouse

## albums
**Brude and Bloom (Year Unknown) – First Full Album** : This album was recorded at The Bathhouse with production assistance from Rob Baker and Gord Sinclair.

## curiosities
**Family Connections** : The bass player, Boris Baker, is the son of The Tragically Hip guitarist Rob Baker. For a period, the drummer, Angus Faye, was the nephew of The Tragically Hip drummer Johnny Faye.
**Kingston, Ontario Origin** : The band originates from Kingston, Ontario.
**Local Gigs** : The band started by playing gigs around Kingston.
**Queen's University Formation** : Members of Casador met at Queen's University.
