# artist - Peggy Jones

## curiosities
**The Mother of Rock and Roll** : Jones joined Bo Diddley's band as a guitarist in 1957, making her one of the first female rock guitarists in a famous band. She was also a pioneer in experimenting with guitar synthesizers, a technology that was virtually unused in R&B music at that time. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
