# artist - Jerry Hopkins

## albums
**No One Here Gets Out Alive (1980)** : Jerry Hopkins authored *No One Here Gets Out Alive* in 1980, a biography considered by many to be the first solid and well-researched account of Jim Morrison's life, possibly remaining the best to this day. Hopkins, known for his serious writing on important artists, conducted extensive interviews, including with less prominent figures like sound engineers' assistants and even Jim Morrison's brothers, aiming for a less sensationalist and more realistic portrayal of Morrison.
