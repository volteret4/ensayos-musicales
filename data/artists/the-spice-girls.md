**Vocals on World Cup Theme Song** : A song originally from the unreleased collaboration between Ian McCulloch and Johnny Marr ended up being used as a World Cup theme song, featuring vocals from The Spice Girls. This unexpected use of the track provided a different outlet for some of the material from the reportedly stolen album.
