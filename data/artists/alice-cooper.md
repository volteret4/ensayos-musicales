Lydon gyrated like a belly dancer, miming and singing the words as best he could to this song, ultimately securing his position as the band's vocalist. **Chicken Incident at Toronto Rock and Roll Revival Festival (September 13, 1969)** : At the Toronto Rock and Roll Revival Festival on September 13, 1969, Vincent Furnier, then the singer for the band Alice Cooper, threw a chicken he believed could fly into the crowd. The chicken was torn to pieces, leading to sensationalized rumors of animal sacrifice and the singer drinking its blood, which the band never denied on management's advice. "Vincent Fernier, the singer, believed that as God was his witness, chickens could fly. So he grabbed the chicken and threw it into the crowd. The chicken fell into the throng where it was torn to pieces." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SIK2YFSmf9U
