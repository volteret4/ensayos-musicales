# artist - Alice Cooper

## curiosities
**Arizona License Plate** : Residents of Arizona, where Alice Cooper resides, can obtain an Alice Cooper-themed license plate from the motor vehicle department. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qtdetc5-MQo
**Dark Vibe Influence (Pre-Goth)** : Alice Cooper's horror-themed performances and dark vibe were acknowledged as a precursor and an influence on the goth aesthetic, though his music was not yet considered fully goth itself. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
**Hollywood Sign "O" Purchase** : Alice Cooper purchased one of the "O"s from the iconic Hollywood sign for $30,000 when it was falling into disrepair, doing so in honor of his friend, Groucho Marx. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qtdetc5-MQo
**Pat Smear's Early Listening** : Pat Smear listened to Alice Cooper growing up, before punk rock was readily available. Pat Smear also believed The Germs' early recording, "Germicide," sounded like Alice Cooper. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
