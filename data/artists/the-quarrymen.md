McCartney reissued this recording in 1981, producing 25 copies each on 10-inch 78s and 7-inch 45s, with these reissues now worth nearly $20,000 each. These reissues were made in limited runs of 25 copies for both 10-inch 78s and 7-inch 45s, and are now highly valuable, fetching close to $20,000 each.
