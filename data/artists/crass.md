## curiosities
**"Thatchergate" Hoax (1983-1984)** : Following the Falklands War, this anarchist musical collective created a tape known as "Thatchergate." They cobbled together speeches from Margaret Thatcher and Ronald Reagan to create what sounded like a fake phone call between the two leaders, including a fabricated discussion about firing missiles at allies. When the tape circulated in 1983, the US State Department suspected it was KGB disinformation, and MI6 considered it a possible Argentinian intelligence operation. Its true source was not discovered until January 1984. While not a song, its samples were used in tracks by Crass and the Dead Kennedys. Crass's motivation was to expose governments as "small-minded, petty and stupid," not just corrupt or violent, and the hoax indeed embarrassed the CIA. "The band cobbled together speeches from Margaret Thatcher and Ronald Reagan into what appeared to be a fake phone call between the two of them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0ARYt4sYT90
