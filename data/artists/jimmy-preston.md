## songs
**Lock the Junk (1948)** : This song by Jimmy Preston, released in 1948, is highlighted as another example of the widespread "rock craze" that dominated rhythm and blues records between 1948 and 1951, featuring a boogie-woogie baseline and a backbeat. "There was Lock the Junk by Jimmy Preston.
