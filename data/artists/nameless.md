## curiosities
**Breakup due to Parental Divorce** : Similar to Dave Grohl's first band, Nameless also broke up when Nick Christie's parents divorced, and he moved away with his mother. "Nameless broke up when Nick moved away with his mom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA

**Self-Referential Name** : The band was notably known simply as "Nameless," a name that was ironically clever according to the transcript. "That band eventually became known as, and this is clever, not, they were known as nameless." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA
