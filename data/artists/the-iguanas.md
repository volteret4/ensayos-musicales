# artist - The Iguanas

## members
- Iggy Pop
- Iggy Pop (drums)
- James Newell Osterberg (Iggy)
- Jimmy Osterberg (drums, vocals)

## curiosities
**Dissolution and Formation of The Psychedelic Stooges (Summer 1967)** : The Iguanas disbanded in the summer of 1967, paving the way for Iggy Pop to form his new group, The Psychedelic Stooges. "by that summer, the iguanas were gone and Iggy was in a new band called The Psychedelic Stooges." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
**Early Band Experience** : The Iguanas were the first band of consequence for James Newell Osterberg (Iggy), where he played as a drummer. They were good enough to open for Motown groups and sometimes served as the backing band for out-of-town acts passing through Detroit. "The first of any consequence was the iguanas." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8
**Early Band for Iggy Pop – Michigan Gigs** : The Iguanas were a Detroit-based band for which Iggy Pop served as the drummer in the mid-sixties. They performed gigs around Michigan and recorded a few songs during this period. "He was the drummer for Detroit band called the iguanas and they played gigs around Michigan and even recorded a few songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Jimmy Osterberg's Band in 1964** : In 1964, Jimmy Osterberg (later Iggy Pop) was singing and playing drums for The Iguanas. This band was his primary musical outlet at the time. "Jimmy Osterberg singing and playing the drums for the iguanas." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
