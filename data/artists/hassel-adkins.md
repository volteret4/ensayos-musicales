## curiosities
**Background and One-Man Band Style** : Hassel Adkins hailed from a very poor rural area of West Virginia. He often performed as a one-man band, simultaneously playing guitar and drums. He is considered a pioneer of psychobilly, an amplified version of rockabilly. "He was from very poor rural West Virginia and dabbled in rock, country, blues, and rock ability, most often performing as a one-man band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Cult Stardom and Record Deal** : After years of obscurity, Hassel Adkins secured an indie record deal, which propelled him to cult stardom. Miles Copeland, head of IRS Records, showed interest in producing an album with him, though it never materialized. "After years of being an unknown, he ended up with an indie record deal that turned him into a cult star." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Dance Creations** : Each of his songs was paired with a dance of the same name. He created numerous "chicken dances" like the chicken hop, chicken flop, and chicken wobble. He also pioneered a dance called "the Slop," specifically designed for drunks, involving staggering and falling down. "He pioneered a dance called the Slop, which was designed specifically for drunks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Death and Legacy** : In 2005, just before his 68th birthday, he was run over by a teenager on a quad bike in his front yard and died eleven days later. In 2018, he was inducted into the West Virginia Hall of Fame. "Eleven days after that, he died just short of his 68th birthday. That was, like I said, 2005." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Documentary** : A documentary about him, titled *The Wild World of Hassel Adkins*, is available for those interested in learning more. "If you want to know more, there's a documentary called The Wild World of Hassel Adkins that is rather interesting." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Hero and Idiosyncrasies** : Adkins declared Colonel Sanders of KFC as his hero. He was known to drink eight liters of coffee daily and toured with gogo dancers who would imitate chickens during his performances. "He also declared that his hero was Colonel Sanders of KFC." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Incidents and Health Issues** : Adkins had a history of unusual incidents, including driving off a 70-foot cliff with friends for fun and being jailed for statutory rape. He may have suffered from manic depression and other mental illnesses. "We learned about the time he and a bunch of friends drove off a 70-foot cliff for fun." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Lyrical Themes** : His music featured diverse and unusual lyrical content. He sang about sex, but also frequently about chickens and various types of poultry. Other topics included eating peanut butter on the moon, aliens, hot dogs, and occasionally decapitation. "He liked to sing about sex, but he also had songs about chickens and various types of poultry." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
