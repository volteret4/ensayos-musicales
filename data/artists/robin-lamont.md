# artist - Robin Lamont

## songs
**Day by Day (1972)** : Robin Lamont sang the lead on "Day by Day" from the musical *Godspell*. This song spent 14 weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1972, peaking at number 13 in the last week of July of that year.
