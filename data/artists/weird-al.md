# artist - Weird Al

## curiosities
**Appeared in Weezer's Africa Video** : "Weird Al" Yankovic made an appearance in Weezer's music video for their cover of "Africa." This was noted as a distinct difference from Toto's original video for the song. "Although I don't remember weird Al being in the original back in the early 80s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM ← weird-al
**Backmasking Use (70s/80s)** : "Weird Al" Yankovic utilized backmasking effects in his music during the 1970s and 1980s, which was a peak era for the technique. "Tons of artists used back masking effects in the 70s and 80s. That seems to be when things reached their peak. Iron Maiden, Pink Floyd, Sticks, Prince B-52s, Weird Al, they were all into it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjwRApoJhA ← weird-al
**Pentium Chip Song** : Weird Al Yankovic composed a song that referenced the powerful Pentium computer chips introduced by Intel. This detail illustrates the significant cultural impact and widespread recognition that Pentium computers achieved during the 1990s. "Even Weird Al wrote a song about them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v7DXXu_1fvM ← weird-al ← weird-al
