## albums
**Milo Goes to College (1982) – Aggressive Melody** : This 1982 record packed 15 aggressive songs into just 22 minutes. It was notable for its early use of melody and harmonies, which at the time was considered hugely radical and unconventional for American punk music. "In 1982, they released a record called My Low Goes to College, 15 songs over in just 22 minutes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE

## songs
**Suburban Home (1982)** : Featured on the 1982 album *Milo Goes to College*, this song was particularly radical for American punks upon its release due to its prominent use of melody and harmonies, which was an unusual approach in the scene at that time. "When that song, which is called Suburban Home, by the way, came out in 1982, it was hugely radical for American punks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE

**Origin and Evolution to Melody** : Hailing from Manhattan Beach in the southwest part of Los Angeles, The Descendents initially played very hardcore music but eventually evolved into a unique sound. Their distinctiveness stemmed from incorporating more melody into their music, a strategy that helped them stand out from the crowd.
