## curiosities
**Birth and Origin** : Born on October 7, 1968, in the small English town of Wellingboro. "He's from a tiny English town called Wellingboro. His birthday was October 7, 1968." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**Childhood Nickname** : Due to his pale and thin appearance at school, Tom was given the nickname "Salamander." This led to him getting into "a lot of fights," which even boxing lessons from his father couldn't significantly help. "he picked up the nickname Salamander." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**College Plans and Early Jobs** : Before attending university, Tom decided to take a year off. During this time, he worked selling men's suits, a job he "hated." He then found a "better job" working in a mental hospital before eventually enrolling at Oxford. "Tom decided to take a year off before university. He spent some time selling men's suits, something that he hated. So he got a better job. It gave him a mental hospital before he went back and enrolled in Oxford." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**Criticism of Streaming Services** : Tom York is cited as one of the artists who "rage against the rise of streaming music services," indicating his strong opposition to this modern model of music distribution. "For example, guys like Tom York and David Byrne rage against the rise of streaming music services." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lGNrJgwQjgg
**DJ Gig at Campus Pub** : To supplement his income, Tom worked as a DJ at a campus pub. He was quite skilled and his regular Friday night gigs often attracted "a thousand people or more" to hear him spin. "Tom supplemented his income working as a DJ at a campus pub. And he was pretty good too." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**Early Musical Frustration** : Tom received his first guitar at the age of eight. However, he quickly became so frustrated with the instrument that he "smashed the thing to pieces in a big temper tantrum." "He got his first guitar at age eight, but he soon got so frustrated with it that he smashed the thing to pieces in a big temper tantrum." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**First Band** : After overcoming his initial frustration with the guitar, Tom formed his first band at age 10. "he eventually got his act together, and a few years later he formed his first band. This was age 10." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**First Guitar Hero** : Tom York's earliest guitar hero was Brian May of Queen, whose guitar solo in "Bohemian Rhapsody" he still admires. "And his first guitar hero, it was Brian May of Queen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**Meeting Stanley Donwood** : While performing with The Headless Chickens in Exeter, Tom York met Stanley Donwood. Donwood went on to become, and remains, Radiohead's primary artist responsible for all their artwork and promotional materials. "it was while at Exeter, and playing in this band, that Tom met Stanley Donwood. He would go on to become a radio head's key guy when it came to artwork and promotional material." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**Physical Characteristics and Health** : Described as a "delicate little guy," standing approximately 5'5". He was often "sickly and skinny," which was a source of worry for his father, a chemical engineer and championship boxer. Tom was born with a partially paralyzed left eye, with the eyelid fixed in a downward position. After several surgeries intended to correct it, the fifth operation resulted in his distinctive "droopy eye" and reduced vision of about 50% on that side. He wore an eye patch during his first couple of years in school. "Tom is a delicate little guy, standing about 5'5"." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
