## curiosities
**Adrian Sutherland's Cree Heritage and Role** : Adrian Sutherland, the band's key member, is Cree and completely fluent in the Mushkegawak language. He is deeply connected to his heritage, participating in Paoas, Harvest festivals, and hunting rituals, and acts as a traditional knowledge keeper for his people. "The key guy is Adrian Sutherland. He's Cree completely fluent in the language, which is called Mushkegawak. He's very in touch with his heritage, Paoas, Harvest festivals, hunting rituals, and he acts as a traditional knowledge keeper of his people." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
**Attawapiskat Origins** : Midnight Shine is a band from Attawapiskat, located on the shores of James Bay in Northern Ontario. This remote location, often in the news for social issues, is central to their identity and music. "They're from Anna Wapisgad on the shores of James Bay in North and Ontario." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
**Challenging Rehearsal Commutes** : Due to the lack of roads in their region, band members often travel to rehearsals on the back of a snowmobile. Adrian Sutherland recounts a journey involving a goose hunt, a two-day snowmobile ride across James Bay, a flight to Timmins, and a nine-hour drive to Toronto for band activities. "Roads? Don't need roads. Sometimes the only way to rehearsals on the back of a snowmobile. ... Adrian tells a story of going on a goose hunt and then running a scudoo across the ice of James Bay for two days, then flying to Timons, and then driving nine hours to Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
**Formation and Rise to Prominence** : The band began in 2011 when Adrian Sutherland secured a gig opening for Trooper in Timmins. The performance was so well-received that he decided Midnight Shine needed to become a permanent endeavor, leading to a series of albums, national tours, festival appearances, and widespread radio airplay across Canada. "The band started in 2011 when Adrian got a gig opening for Trooper and Timons. But that went over so well he decided that Midnight Shy needed to become a permanent thing, and that led to a series of albums and national tours and festivals and radio airplay across the country." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
**Geographically Dispersed Members** : The band members are scattered across vast distances in Northern Ontario, making rehearsals a unique challenge. Drummer George Gillies is from Fort Albany (100 km from Attawapiskat), while bass player Stan Louttit and guitarist Zachary Tomatuk are from Moose Factory (an additional 130 km south). "The band members are scattered far and wide across North and Ontario. ... So all the members are separated by about 230 kilometers of roadless wilderness." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
**Resilience in Attawapiskat** : Despite Attawapiskat being in the news for serious issues like housing crises, a major suicide epidemic, and drug problems, Adrian Sutherland and others like him refuse to give up on their home and neighbors, highlighting a spirit of resilience. "From Anna Wapisgad in Northern Ontario, a place that's been in the news for so many sad reasons like housing crisis, a major suicide epidemic, drug problems and more. But guys like Adrian Sutherland refused to give up on their home and their neighbors." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
