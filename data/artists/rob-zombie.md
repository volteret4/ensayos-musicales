**Tour Bus Movie Habits** : Rob Zombie mentions that while on tour, he tries to stock up on movies he hasn't seen. However, inevitably, one "ridiculous" movie, such as *Austin Powers* or *Dumb and Dumber*, becomes a communal favorite and is watched "a thousand times" by everyone on the bus, leading to repetitive quotes and inside jokes throughout the tour. "It'll be something like Austin Powers. There's always some movie that pops up that once it was dumb and dumber, that was the movie that everyone watched like a thousand times." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgN0vBIugLc
**`Cafe Flesh` Sample (1982)** : The distinct moaning in Rob Zombie and White Zombie's song "More Human Than Human" is sampled from the 1982 sci-fi movie `Cafe Flesh`, which depicts a post-nuclear world where most surviving humans become ill if they attempt sex, leading to live sex shows for their benefit. "The sample is taken from a 1982 sci-fi movie called Cafe Flesh. After the world is nearly annihilated in a nuclear war, they're surviving humans find that they get sick when they try to have sex. The moaning comes from someone participating in a live sex show conducted for the benefit of those humans who can't have sex anymore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SIK2YFSmf9U
