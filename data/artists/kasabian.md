## curiosities
**Tom Meighan Dismissed July 2020 After Assault Conviction; Serge Pizzorno New Frontman from October 2021** : Kasabian formed in 1997 and carved out a successful career in the UK over more than a decade. In July 2020, founding singer and member Tom Meighan announced he was leaving — a mutual decision following his admission in court that he had assaulted his ex-fiancée in front of their child, which was a clear red line for the rest of the band. He was permanently dismissed and severed from all band associations. Guitarist Serge Pizzorno took over as singer, with this new version of Kasabian making their public debut in October 2021.
