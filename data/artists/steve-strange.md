## curiosities
**"Scary Monsters" Video Extra** : Steve Strange, who later became the frontman of the New Romantic band Visage, was plucked from London's avant-garde Blitz Club to be an extra in David Bowie's groundbreaking 1980 music video for his "Scary Monsters" album. This early exposure was part of the video's significant cultural impact that helped launch the New Romantic movement. **Co-Founder of The Blitz Club** : Steve Strange, who did not fully fit with the punk scene, co-founded The Blitz Club in a small London basement in the late 1970s with Rusty Egan. The club paid homage to David Bowie's glam aesthetic and played new music, with Kraftwerk being a constant presence.
