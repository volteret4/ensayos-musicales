**Early Adopter of Wah-Wah Pedal** : Eric Clapton, described as a "hot new player from the UK," was one of the two influential guitarists Henry Goldrich convinced to purchase a wah-wah pedal at Manie's Music in the mid-1960s. His adoption of the pedal was instrumental in shaping his playing and the broader sound of guitar-based rock and roll. "One was up and coming guitarist named Jimmy Hendrix.
