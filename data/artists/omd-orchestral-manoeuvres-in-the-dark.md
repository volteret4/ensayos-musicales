# artist - OMD (Orchestral Manoeuvres in the Dark)

## members
- Andy McCluskey
- Paul Humphries

## songs
**Electricity (1979)** : This song is explicitly cited as a perfect example of late 1970s Technopop. OMD was among the first of the New Wave generation to completely abandon traditional instruments for all-electronic sounds. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY

## curiosities
**Music Aptitude Irony** : Despite his success in one of Technopop’s biggest bands, Paul Humphries’ mother once received a school report from his music teacher stating he had "absolutely no aptitude for music whatsoever". ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
**Self-Taught Punk Aesthetic** : Reflecting a punk rock ethos, members Andy McCluskey and Paul Humphries were self-taught on synthesizers, prioritizing creative attitude over technical ability. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
