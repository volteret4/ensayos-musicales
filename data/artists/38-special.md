The song's opening riff notably "borrow[ed] a little something from the cars," specifically resembling "Just What I Needed." He expressed the band's desire to offer more than the stereotypical themes of "Cowboys whiskey, Alligators and bad women" often found in other Southern rock bands like Molly Hatchet.
