## concerts

## curiosities
**Debut at 100 Club (1976)** : This new group made its debut at the 100 Club Punk Rock Festival on September 20, 1976. On the first night, their drummer savagely beat up a well-known music journalist, and on the second night, the same individual was arrested for throwing a beer glass, blinding a girl in one eye. This drummer was Sid Vicious, who would later join the Sex Pistols.
