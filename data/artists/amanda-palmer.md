# artist - Amanda Palmer

## member of
- The Dresden Dolls

## curiosities
**Early Performing Career (Late 1990s)** : Amanda Palmer began her performing career in the late 1990s, engaging in theater and street busking. "Amanda Palmer got into performing in theater and on the street as a busker in the late 1990s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Kickstarter Breakthrough (2012)** : Amanda Palmer's major breakthrough came in 2012 when she launched a Kickstarter campaign to fund her new album. Nearly 25,000 people contributed, raising almost $1.2 million, a record amount for a musical project on the platform at that time. "Her big break came in 2012 when she announced that her new album would be funded by a kickstarter campaign, nearly 25,000 people chipped in, raising almost $1.2 million." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Multi-faceted Success (Post-2012)** : After her Kickstarter success, Amanda Palmer's career "exploded," including a New York Times bestselling memoir, an acclaimed TED Talk on the "art of asking," and a global fan base through concerts, festivals, theater, a podcast, and activism. "Since then she's exploded, a memoir that was New York Times bestseller, and acclaimed Ted Talk on the art of asking concerts, festivals, more theater, a podcast, activism, her fan bass is global..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Radiohead Ukulele Album** : As part of her solo career, Amanda Palmer released an album consisting entirely of Radiohead songs performed on a ukulele. "There was even an album where she played nothing but radiohead songs in a ukulele." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Solo Career (Post-2008)** : After The Dresden Dolls disbanded in 2008, Amanda Palmer pursued a solo career, performing numerous gigs, theater productions, writing books, and releasing an album of Radiohead songs played on ukulele, developing a strong cult following. "From there she went solo, lots of gigs, lots of theater, lots of critical acclaim, a couple of books." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Success at 36** : Amanda Palmer found widespread success when she was 36 years old, after years of performing and building a cult following. "it took until she was just 36 for everything to start falling into place." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
