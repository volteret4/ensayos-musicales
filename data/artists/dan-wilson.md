## curiosities
**Breakthrough at 37 (1998)** : Dan Wilson achieved significant success at age 37, in early 1998, with Semisonic's hit song "Closing Time," after many years of trying to make it in the music industry. "When that song came out in early 1998, it quickly became a monster. And after years of trying to make it, it finally happened to Dan Wilson. He was 37." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Collaborated with Weezer** : Dan Wilson from Semi-Sonic was among the outside collaborators brought in for Weezer's album "Ratitude." "Dan Wilson from Semi-Sonic." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
**Early Career as Artist and Environmentalist** : Before his musical breakthrough, Dan Wilson worked as an artist and environmentalist, with music being a side interest. "He began working as an artist and an environmentalist, dabbling in music on the side." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Successful Songwriting Career** : After his success with Semisonic, Dan Wilson has had a highly lucrative career as a songwriter, earning "real money." He penned at least six songs for The Dixie Chicks (including a Grammy winner for Song of the Year), three songs for Adele's *21* album (including "Someone Like You"), and co-written with numerous other major artists such as Taylor Swift, Tom Morello, Celine Dion, John Legend, and Pink. "But it is as a songwriter where Dan has made the real money." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
