# artist - The Killers

## genres
- Alt Rock
- New Wave

## venues
- Battleborn Studios : Imagine Dragons recorded their second and third self-released EPs at this studio, which is owned by The Killers, another Las Vegas-based band.

## songs
**Somebody Told Me (2004)** : Released March 15th, 2004. Longtime Alt Rock fans immediately felt something older in the sound, noting DNA from New Order, Duran Duran, The Cure, The Talking Heads, and The Smiths.

## curiosities
**Band Name Origin** : Their name came from a fictional band featured in New Order's music video for the song "Crystal".
**Influences** : Members are big fans of The Smiths, Morrissey, The Cure, Blondie, and Duran Duran.
**New Wave Revivalists (Early 2000s)** : Part of a whole New Wave revival that took hold at the beginning of the 2000s.
