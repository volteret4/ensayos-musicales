# artist - Thomas Edison

## curiosities
**Talking Machine - First Demonstration (1877)** : Thomas Edison's talking machine was one of the first devices capable of capturing sound, demonstrating this capability in 1877. It had a recording limit of approximately three minutes, which subsequently influenced the standard length of popular songs.
