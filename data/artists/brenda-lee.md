# artist - Brenda Lee

## songs
**Rockin' Around the Christmas Tree** : This song was recorded onto the world's smallest "vinyl" record, which measured only 15 micrometers across. Created by the Nano Made section at the Technical University of Denmark, this tiny record played for 25 seconds in stereo. "Record runs just 25 seconds and what's on it? Brenda Lee's rocking around the Christmas tree." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B2g4QSREzpw
