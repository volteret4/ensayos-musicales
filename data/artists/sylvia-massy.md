## curiosities
**Early Career with Punk Bands** : Sylvia Massy began her career by recording punk bands on a budget, honing her skills in a raw and independent environment. This early experience laid the groundwork for her later work with major artists and diverse genres. "She began by recording punk bands on the cheap." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oRR7N-YUcRE
**Production/Engineering Credits** : Sylvia Massy worked behind the board for a wide array of prominent artists, including Aerosmith, Prince, Bobby Brown, the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Slayer, Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers, Katting Kage, Johnny Cash, a Taylor Hawkins and Civil Project, System of a Down, The Smashing Pumpkins, The Black Crowes, and The Beastie Boys. **Staff Engineer in West Hollywood** : By the end of the 1980s, Massy had moved to Los Angeles and secured a position as a staff engineer at a studio in West Hollywood. This role provided her with opportunities for more engineering and production jobs, elevating her career in the competitive LA music scene. "By the end of the 1980s, she was living in Los Angeles and managed to get a job as a staff engineer at a studio in West Hollywood." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oRR7N-YUcRE
**Studio Ownership and Academia** : Sylvia Massy owned her own studio for many years, which was a popular choice for bands like Sublime and Econoline Crush. Additionally, she has served as a visiting professor at Berklee College of Music and conducted workshops for the Abbey Road Institute, demonstrating her commitment to educating the next generation of audio professionals.
