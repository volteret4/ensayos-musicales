## curiosities
**Iconic Bassline and Payment** : Herbie Flowers was the session player responsible for creating the famous sliding bass line on Lou Reed's "Walk on the Wild Side." He layered two distinct bass lines—one from a stand-up double bass and another from a fretless electric bass—to achieve the distinctive sound. For his work, Flowers was reportedly paid $30. This iconic bass line has since been sampled countless times in hip-hop and dance songs globally. "For his trouble.
