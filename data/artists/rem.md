## curiosities
**Beneficiary of Alt-Rock Appetite** : Benefited from record labels looking for alt-rock beyond grunge after the "Grunge Gold Rush."
**Concert for Nelson Mandela (2001)** : R.E.M. was scheduled to play a concert for Nelson Mandela at Trafalgar Square in London on April 21, 2001.
**Featured on Alt-Rock Playlists** : their music was found on radio playlists for late 1986 alt-rock stations.
**Punk Rock Comparison** : Dave Grohl used R.E.M. as an example of a band with simple, three-chord songs to illustrate the accessibility of punk rock to aspiring musicians.
