**"Breathless" Promotional Scheme** : Judd Phillips, Sam Phillips' brother, devised a unique promotional campaign for "Breathless" in conjunction with Dick Clark, who hosted "The Dick Clark Show" (also known as "Dick Clark's Saturday Night Beach Nut Show," sponsored by Beach Nut Chewing Gum). Fans could send Sun Records five Beach Nut wrappers and 50 cents for postage and packing to receive a signed copy of the new record. This led to extraordinary results, with some areas of the country running out of Beach Nut Gum, and over 38,000 singles being distributed. Sun Records had to purchase an autograph stamp and hire temporary staff to manage the demand, splitting profits with Dick Clark.
