## curiosities
**Early Rock and Roll Music** : Jerry Lee Lewis's music was directly associated with the rock and roll sound of the 1950s and early 60s, which was a direct descendant of 12-bar blues. This music was characterized by clean, clear guitar sounds and mostly clean lyrics, often intended for kids and played on radio stations appealing to teenagers. "the rock and roll sound became associated and intertwined with the music of the 1950s and early 60s, Elvis, Little Richard, Jerry Lee Lewis and so on." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
**Early Rock and Roll Piano Hero** : Jerry Lee Lewis was a foundational pioneer of rock and roll, counted among the "piano heroes" whose piano playing was central to the genre's initial sound. His style was key in shaping the percussive and energetic rhythm and blues-influenced foundation of early rock. "Instead, the early pioneers were piano heroes, Little Richard, Jerry Lee Lewis, Fats Domino, Ray Charles, Huey Piano Smith." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g
**Extreme Piano Playing and Controversy** : Jerry Lee Lewis, a relative of preacher Jimmy Swaggart, was known for his extreme stage antics, which included pounding pianos to splinters and sometimes setting them on fire. His notoriety was further fueled by his controversial marriage to his 13-year-old cousin. "He pounded his pianos to splinters and sometimes set his pianos on fire. And this is before he married his 13 year old cousin." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
**Impact on Rock's Momentum** : Jerry Lee Lewis is cited as one of the key figures whose emergence, alongside Elvis and Little Richard, made rock music unstoppable. Despite widespread criticism, record burnings, and attempts by authorities to crack down on the genre, rock solidified its presence in music following his rise. "None of this actually slowed anything down, of course. We got Elvis, then Little Richard, then Jerry Lee Lewis. And then after that, the Beatles." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L0j9iDuHQVA
**Longevity in Life** : Jerry Lee Lewis lived to the age of 87, defying odds in his long life. "Jerry Lee Lewis, who divide all odds by living to the age of 87." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sdPeii-8Ysw
**Pioneering Headbanging-like Movement** : In the 1950s, Jerry Lee Lewis developed a signature stage move where he would continuously flip his curls back from his face as they came loose during his energetic performances. Some fans began imitating this movement, which is considered a potential precursor to modern rock headbanging. "The curls on his head would come loose and fall into his face. He had to continuously flip them back so he could see what he was doing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bferkEWvJg0
