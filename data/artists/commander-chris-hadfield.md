# artist - Commander Chris Hadfield

## curiosities
**Zero-G Video in Space (2013)** : In 2013, Commander Chris Hadfield produced a Zero-G music video while aboard the International Space Station. This pioneering video preceded OK Go's Zero-G music video, which was filmed within Earth's atmosphere.
