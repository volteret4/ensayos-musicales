## curiosities
**Accessible Live Shows** : Fugazi was notable for hosting many all-ages shows with highly affordable ticket prices, alongside delivering emotionally intense musical performances. "Lots of all ages shows. Lots of shows were tickets cost practically nothing and lots of emotional intensity with their music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QtMI00T5jw ← fugazi ← fugazi
**Anti-Commercial Practices** : Fugazi was deeply anti-commercial, exclusively playing all-ages shows, owning their own label (Discord Records) to issue CDs just above cost, and maintaining the lowest possible concert ticket prices. They were also heavily involved in social and community activism. "They were completely anti-commercial. They played only all ages shows. They owned their own label and issued CDs at prices just above cost. They kept the concert ticket prices as low as possible. They were into social and community activism." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk ← fugazi
**Anti-Moshing Stance** : Fugazi, a post-hardcore and straight-edge band from Washington D.C., was one of the first groups to actively take a stand against moshing at their gigs. Adhering to their straight-edge principles of no drinking, no drugs, and respect, they viewed moshing as antithetical to their values. "The first band to really take a position against moshing was Fugazi, the post-hardcore band from Washington, D.C., who would have seen moshing culture... develop at the very beginning." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y ← fugazi
**Commitment to DIY Ethos** : The band maintained a total do-it-yourself (DIY) approach, operating their own label, Discord, and expressing contempt for the conventional record industry and its operational methods. "Plus they were totaled to do it yourself. They held the entire record industry in contempt and what had nothing to do with the way it operated. So they did everything on their own, through their own label, which they called Discord." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QtMI00T5jw ← fugazi ← fugazi
**Confronting Moshers at Gigs** : When mosh pits formed at Fugazi's shows in the 1980s and early 1990s, the band would politely ask the crowd to stop. In some instances, they would even bring an audience member who was moshing onto the stage, put them on the microphone, and instruct them to apologize to the rest of the crowd for their disruptive behavior. "Sometimes they would even drag a kid out of the audience, put him at the mic, and tell him to apologize to everybody for his bad behavior." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y ← fugazi
**Ethical Standards in Rock** : Fugazi formed from Ian MacKaye's previous projects, becoming a band centered on raw emotion. Beyond creating intelligent post-hardcore music, they established incredibly strong ethical standards that influenced the broader rock scene, defining anti-commercialism for many. "Not only has this band made some great and intelligent post-Hardcore music, their sense of ethics was so strong that they basically set the standard for all of rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk ← fugazi
**Fan Letter from Teenage Dave Grohl (1983)** : In approximately 1983, when he was 14, Dave Grohl sent a fan letter to Ian MacKaye, a member of Fugazi, expressing appreciation for their "Thrasch" music and asking for contacts. "He sent a fan letter to Ian McCuy of DC Hardcore Heroes Fugazi. It read, Good Thrasch! So I was wondering, if you could give me some numbers or people to get in touch with, it would help. Thanks, Dave Grohl." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA ← fugazi

"Fugazi, a major influence on the musical universe of a teenage Dave Grohl." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA ← fugazi
**Preservers of Traditional Emo** : By the end of the 1990s, as emo diversified into various subgenres, Fugazi was credited with actively keeping traditional emo alive. "Sure, there was traditional emo, Fugazi made sure they kept that alive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk ← fugazi
**Screamo Band Dispreference** : Fugazi's music was mentioned in contrast to what Screamo bands preferred, who opted for "old Joy Division and Smith records." This suggests a distinction in musical taste and potentially a different approach to intensity and political messaging between the groups. "They preferred old Joy Division and Smith records over Weezer and Fugazi." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo ← fugazi
**Standard Bearers for Emo Core** : Fugazi not only defined the standards for emo core but also provided a centralized home for the genre through their label, Discord Records. "Not only did Fugazi set the standards for emo core, but they also gave it a home." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk ← fugazi
**The Ultimate Emo Band** : Due to their profound ethical stance, anti-commercialism, and focus on raw emotion, Fugazi earned the reputation of becoming "the ultimate emo band." "And in the process, Fugazi became the ultimate emo band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk ← fugazi
**Touring Partners** : Explosions in the Sky, a Texas-based post-rock band, toured with Fugazi. "They've toured with nine-inch nails, Fugazi, and here's that name again, Godspeed, New Black Emperor." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY ← fugazi
**Unwilling Participation in Punk Revival** : Despite their anti-establishment stance and likely disapproval, Fugazi found themselves unexpectedly drawn into the mainstream attention generated by the mid-90s punk rock revival. "They probably didn't like it much, but even they found themselves sucked into the mid-90s punk rock revival." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QtMI00T5jw ← fugazi ← fugazi
**Washington DC Origins and Straight Edge Philosophy** : Fugazi, a band from the Washington D.C. area, was known for practicing "Straight Edge" punk rock, which advocates for a lifestyle free of drugs and alcohol ("No Drugs, No Boos"). "Another punk group who found themselves in Mortemand was Fugazi, a band from the Washington DC area that practiced a form of punk rock called Straight Edge, No Drugs, No Boos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QtMI00T5jw ← fugazi ← fugazi
