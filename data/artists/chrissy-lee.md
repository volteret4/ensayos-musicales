## songs
**"Skinny Mini" (1964)** : Chrissy Lee was the drummer for The Beat Chicks, who achieved an international hit with "Skinny Mini" in 1964. The band opened for The Beatles on a tour of Spain, showcasing her talent on a significant international stage. ## curiosities
**Early Drumming and Diverse Collaborations** : Chrissy Lee began drumming at age four in a Salvation Army band. Throughout her career, she performed with a wide array of renowned artists including Frank Sinatra, Fats Domino, Tom Jones, and Dinah Washington. She also became heavily involved in education, conducting drum clinics globally.
