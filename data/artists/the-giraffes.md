## curiosities
**Sudden Cardiac Death and Implanted Defibrillator** : Aaron Lazar, lead singer of The Giraffes, experienced sudden cardiac death one night while driving his girlfriend home, causing him to slump over the steering wheel. He was later told that his heart had stopped twice. This condition is not a heart attack but a malfunction in the brain's signal to the heart, with only a 5% survival rate. Lazar now has an implanted defibrillator that shocks his heart back to life if it stops. "He was diagnosed with something called sudden cardiac death. This is not a heart attack. Something goes wrong with the signal from your brain that tells your heart to keep beating. Only 5% of people with this condition survive.
