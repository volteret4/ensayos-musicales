# artist - John Cummings

## curiosities
**Founding Member of The Ramones** : In 1973, John Cummings (who later became Johnny Ramone) visited Manie's Music with Douglas Colvin to purchase instruments after deciding to form a band. A year later, they formed The Ramones with Joey and Tommy, with their initial instrument purchases being a guitar, a bass, and a small amp from the store. "A year later, Douglas was calling himself D.D. and Johnny was Johnny. And together with Joey and Tommy, they formed the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg ← john-cummings
