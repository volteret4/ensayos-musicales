# artist - Tadges Christelot

## curiosities
**Co-host of Art Context Podcast** : Tadges Christelot co-hosts the new podcast "Art Context: The Architects of Art." This show aims to highlight the creative artists behind music videos, sharing their stories, influences, and processes. "We have Tadges Christelot and Director X." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QBCEmZ4Jheo
**Music Video Director and Producer** : Tadges Christelot is involved in the music video world and is part of the production company Fella, which gained recognition with the "Topstar" music video. He views the podcast as another medium for storytelling, focusing on the creative journey of filmmakers. "We broke our production company, Fella, with this music video for DJ Khaled Drake and Bieber called Topstar. So it's been a crazy journey." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QBCEmZ4Jheo
**Origin from Brampton, Ontario** : Tadges Christelot is noted as one of two individuals from Brampton, Ontario, who ventured into making art that achieved international recognition. "And there were two kids from Brampton, Ontario that went out to make art that broke out to the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QBCEmZ4Jheo
