## curiosities
**First Ticketmaster Event (1977)** : The Electric Light Orchestra performed at the University of New Mexico in Elbacurkey on January 23, 1977, marking the first-ever event for which tickets were sold by the newly founded Ticketmaster company. Tickets for this show were priced at $6.50, with an additional service charge of 25 cents. "the first ever ticket master event was for the Electric Light Orchestra at the University of New Mexico in Elbacurkey on January 23rd, 1977." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE ← electric-light-orchestra
