## curiosities
**First Ticketmaster Event (1977)** : The Electric Light Orchestra performed at the University of New Mexico in Elbacurkey on January 23, 1977, marking the first-ever event for which tickets were sold by the newly founded Ticketmaster company. Tickets for this show were priced at $6.50, with an additional service charge of 25 cents.
