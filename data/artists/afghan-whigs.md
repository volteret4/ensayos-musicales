# artist - Afghan Whigs

## curiosities
**"Four Minutes and 33 Seconds" Cover** : The Afghan Whigs were among the artists who contributed to a 2019 box set by Mute Records, compiling various covers of John Cage's silent composition, "Four Minutes and 33 Seconds." "mute records compiled a bunch of them in a box set featuring... afghan wigs..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
