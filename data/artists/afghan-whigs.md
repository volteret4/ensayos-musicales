## curiosities
**"Four Minutes and 33 Seconds" Cover** : The Afghan Whigs were among the artists who contributed to a 2019 box set by Mute Records, compiling various covers of John Cage's silent composition, "Four Minutes and 33 Seconds." **Musical Influence on Matthew Good** : Afghan Whigs were a significant influence on Matthew Good's songwriting, particularly in their approach to the bass guitar. He noted their use of the bass as a "straight A instrument" that moved in steps, which contrasted with the more melodic bass lines of 60s and 70s music he also admired. "But also someone that was massively influenced by bands like The Pixies and the Replacements and Afghan Wigs and that kind of things. It was a straight A instrument." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=--g0gugy3ZE ← afghan-whigs ← afghan-whigs
