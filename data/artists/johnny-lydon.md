## curiosities
**Aggressive Dance Punk with Public Image Limited** : After his time with the Sex Pistols, Johnny Lydon led Public Image Limited (PIL) in exploring a more aggressive form of dance punk. While still danceable, PIL's music had a distinct edge, described as "scary," "agitated," and "twitchy." "There was also a more aggressive version of this approach, led by Johnny Lydon's post-sex pistols band Public Image Limited." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
**Auditioning Michael Balzary (Flea) for PiL** : In 1984, Johnny Lydon auditioned a young bass player from Los Angeles named Michael Balzary (later known as Flea) for Public Image Limited. Flea got the gig but chose instead to stick with his new band, the Red Hot Chili Peppers, which he was forming with his friend Anthony. "One of the people, Johnny auditioned for the band in 1984, was a young bass player from Los Angeles named Michael Ballzary." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Dark Music Contribution** : Johnny Lydon's work with Public Image Limited was noted for its dark sound, contributing to the development of the Goth scene. "Johnny Lydon's public image limited was pretty dark, same with Killing Joke." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gd9sO7c3N1s
**Missed Pan Am Flight 103 (1988)** : Johnny Lydon and his wife, Nora, narrowly missed Pan Am Flight 103 on December 21, 1988, which was later bombed over Lockerbie, Scotland. Delays caused by Nora taking too long to pack, a subsequent argument, and getting stuck in traffic on the way to Heathrow Airport meant they missed the flight by minutes, thus saving their lives. "Nora, his wife, took too long to pack. They had a big fight, which delayed them even more, and then they got stuck in traffic on the way to Heathrow. They missed their flight by minutes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
