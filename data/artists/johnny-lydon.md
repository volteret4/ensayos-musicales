## curiosities
**Missed Pan Am Flight 103 (1988)** : Johnny Lydon and his wife Nora narrowly missed Pan Am Flight 103 on December 21, 1988, which was bombed over Lockerbie, Scotland. They were delayed because Nora took too long to pack, leading to a big fight, and then got stuck in traffic en route to Heathrow Airport, missing the flight by minutes.
**Missed Pan Am Flight 103 (December 21, 1988)** : Also known as Johnny Rotten, he and his wife Nora were scheduled to be on Pan Am Flight 103, which was bombed over Lockerbie, Scotland. They missed the flight by minutes due to Nora taking too long to pack, a subsequent argument, and getting stuck in traffic on the way to Heathrow.
