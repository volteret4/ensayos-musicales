**Rockman Inventor** : A graduate of MIT and leader of the band Boston, Tom Scholz invented the Rockman. This innovative small box device provided players with the fuzz, distortion, and sustain typically associated with a large, loud amplifier, but in a compact format that allowed for quiet enjoyment and practice.
