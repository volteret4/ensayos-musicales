## curiosities
**Performance at Bard College (November 13, 1982)** : This group played at Bard College on November 13, 1982, on the same night the Beastie Boys also performed. The band featured a singer named Adam Horowitz, who was the son of a famous playwright and would later join the Beastie Boys.
