## curiosities
**Formation in North Carolina** : Ben Folds Five was formed after Ben Folds relocated back home to North Carolina, a move that proved to be a turning point after a series of unsuccessful musical endeavors. This formation marked the beginning of the band's successful career. "It wasn't until he moved back home to North Carolina and formed a band he called Ben Foltz Five that things started to click." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g
**Unique "Punk Rock for Sissies" Style** : The band's music was uniquely described by Ben Folds as "Punk Rock for Sissies." This label highlights their distinctive approach of combining punk sensibilities with a piano-centric sound, setting them apart from traditional guitar-driven punk bands. "He called his music, Punk Rock for Sissies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g
