# artist - Mickey and Sylvia

## songs
**Love Is Strange (late 1950s)** : This song was performed by the duo Mickey and Sylvia, which included Sylvia Robinson, who would later go on to found Sugar Hill Records and produce "Rapper's Delight." The song gained renewed popular recognition from its inclusion in the movie "Dirty Dancing," where Baby and Johnny dance to it.
