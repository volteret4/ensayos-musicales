## curiosities
**Tour Routine** : John Wozniak described a typical tour bus schedule involving playing a gig, getting on the bus to sleep, waking up in the next town due to a driver, having a little free time for personal tasks or writing music, then sound check, dinner, interviews, and finally the next gig before repeating the cycle. The only saving grace mentioned were the minutes spent performing. "It's like you play a gig. You're done with the gig. You go to the van. You go wherever, hang out in the van. I mean, the bus. I'm sorry, I'm talking about a bus here. You jump on the bus, you sleep, and you wake up in the next town because there's a driver that drives you." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgN0vBIugLc
