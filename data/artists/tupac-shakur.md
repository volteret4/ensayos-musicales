# artist - Tupac Shakur

## curiosities
**Coachella Hologram Performance (2012)** : In 2012, a virtual Tupac Shakur made a striking appearance at Coachella during a set by Dr. Dre and Snoop Dogg. Though marketed as a hologram, it was technically an advanced projection using Pepper's Ghost techniques, appearing "awfully lifelike" and marking a pivotal moment in the development of simulated live performances. "Then in 2012, a very dead two-pock Shakur showed up at Coachella during a set featuring Dr. Dre and Snoop Dog." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
**Early Career with Digital Underground** : Before launching his successful solo career, Tupac Shakur gained experience in the music industry as a roadie, dancer, and backup singer for the group Digital Underground. This formative period helped him get his start in performing.
