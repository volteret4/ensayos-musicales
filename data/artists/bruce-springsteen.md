## albums
**Born in the USA (1984) - First CD Pressed in North America** : Bruce Springsteen's "Born in the USA" was reportedly the first Compact Disc to be pressed in North America, occurring in 1984. This landmark event took place as more CD manufacturing plants began operating outside of Germany and Japan, which were the exclusive production sites initially. "And apparently the first CD pressed in North America was Bruce Springsteen's Born in the USA." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
**Born in the USA (1984) – First US-Produced CD** : This album was the first compact disc produced at the new Sony manufacturing plant in Terre Haute, Indiana, which began operations in October 1984. "A Sony facility went online in Tarot Hote, Indiana, in October 1984. And the first CD it produced was Bruce Springsteen's Born in the USA." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BHkJFA0LMro
**Darkness on the Edge of Town** : This album was purchased by a fan, Merck Mercuriatis, the day it came out when he was 14 years old, highlighting the deep connection fans have with "heritage artists." "I bought darkness on the edge of town. The day that it came out as a 14 year old." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pe40SqDrpZ0
**High Hopes (2014)** : This album includes a studio re-recording of "The Ghost of Tom Joad" which features Rage Against the Machine guitarist Tom Morello. "And if you look on Springsteen's 2014 album High Hopes, there is a studio re-recording that features Tom Morello." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I
**The Ghost of Tom Joad (1995)** : This album features the title track, originally a quiet folk song that narrates the story of the main character from John Steinbeck's novel "The Grapes of Wrath." It later inspired a loud cover by Rage Against the Machine and was re-recorded for a subsequent album. "In its original form, it's a quiet folk song that told the story of the main character from the John Steinbeck novel The Grapes of Wrath." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I

These cars were once favorites of hot rod modifiers. "Springsteen is singing about revving up a four cylinder engine and not anything else, including any feminine hygiene products." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjwRApoJhA

**Signature Characteristics** : Bruce Springsteen is renowned for his portrayal as a voice for the everyman, for creating songs populated with recognizable characters, and for expressing familiar working-class attitudes, dreams, and problems. His work typically includes anthemic songs that predominantly convey messages of hope through poetic lyrics and highly energetic live performances.
