However, they have reformed multiple times over the subsequent two decades, a pattern that continues to the present day. **Formation and Radio Presence (1992)** : The Killjoys were formed in Hamilton in 1992 by Mike Treblecock. They became a pervasive presence on rock radio and Much Music throughout the mid-1990s. "Hamilton's Killjoy's were pretty much inescapable on rock radio and on much music during the middle of the decade.
