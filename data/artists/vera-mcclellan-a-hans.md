## curiosities
**Child Prodigy and Radio Performer** : Vera McClellan-a-Hans began playing drums at age five and became a featured player in an all-girl orchestra that regularly performed on the NBC Radio Network as a young woman. She was an early outlier and pioneer, challenging the prevailing notion that drums were unsuitable for girls.
