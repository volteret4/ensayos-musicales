# artist - Narada Michael Walden

**Licensed to Kill (1989) – Co-writer** : Narada Michael Walden co-wrote the theme song for the 1989 James Bond film "Licensed to Kill." Known for his work with R&B artists like The Temptations, Lionel Richie, Aretha Franklin, Whitney Houston, and Mariah Carey, Walden's involvement brought a distinctly 80s R&B sound to the track.
