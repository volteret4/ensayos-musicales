**Amen Brother (1969)** : The Winstons, an American soul group, recorded "Amen Brother" in 1969, which contains a seven-second drum bit by Gregory Coleman, known as the "Amen Break." This segment has become the "most sampled piece of music in the universe," reportedly used in nearly 6,200 songs according to whosample.com.
