## curiosities
**Discovery by Stiff Records** : In 1978, the same year Kate Bush was making her chart debut, Lene Lovich attracted the attention of Stiff Records. This independent label was a prominent force in the punk and new wave music worlds at the time. "In 1978, around the very same time Kate Bush was debuting on the charts, Lena came to the attention of stiff records, an indie label very much at the forefront of the punk and new wave worlds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEawe_1Fqf0
**Multi-Disciplinary Artistic Background** : Born in Troy, Lene Lovich relocated to England, where she attended art school. There, she honed her skills as a sculptor and cabaret performer, in addition to playing the saxophone. She also contributed to film by lending her screams for post-production on horror films. "She was born into Troy, but she ended up in art school in England working as a sculptor and a cabaret performer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEawe_1Fqf0
