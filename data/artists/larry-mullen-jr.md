## curiosities
**Boredom with Formal Training** : Became bored with the strict formality of traditional drum lessons, including rudiment exercises, sight-reading charts, and playing along to other people's music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Early Band Experience** : Before high school, he played in a band at the local post office that traveled around the country. He also performed in the significant St. Patrick's Day parade in Dublin. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Early Drumming Education** : Started playing drums at age nine. His first teacher was Joe Bonny, who was Ireland's best-known drummer at the time. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Father's Career Advice** : His father, Larry Mullen Sr., was supportive of his drumming ambitions but almost convinced him to join the Irish army for seven years to play in their band and learn all percussion instruments. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**First Drum Kit** : His mother spent 17 pounds on a secondhand drum set for him. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**First U2 Rehearsal** : Hosted the very first rehearsal of U2 in the kitchen of his house in autumn 1976. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Founding U2** : At 15, he pinned a note to the bulletin board at Mount Temple High School in Dublin, asking for other students who "had wasted money on guitars," leading to the formation of U2. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
