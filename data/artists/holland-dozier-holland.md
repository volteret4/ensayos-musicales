## curiosities
**Followed Bowie Bonds Model** : The Motown songwriting team of Holland-Dozier-Holland followed David Bowie's innovative financial model, utilizing intellectual property to secure financial instruments after the success of the Bowie Bonds. This demonstrated the broader impact of Bowie's pioneering business acumen in the music industry.
