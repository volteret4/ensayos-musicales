## curiosities
**Founders of The Batcave (1982)** : Specimen founded the Batcave club in London's Soho district in July 1982, which became the headquarters and "ground zero for Goth proper." The band was very theatrical, almost performance art-like. Their first single was released on video in 1982, a radical move for the time. Specimen's central role in the Batcave made them instrumental in spreading the "gospel of Goth" in its early days. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
