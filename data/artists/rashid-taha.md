# artist - Rashid Taha

## curiosities
**Claim of "Rock the Casbah" Influence (1982)** : After a 1982 show in Paris, Rashid Taha, an Algerian singer-songwriter and leader of the French Arab-punk band Residence Permit, gave Joe Strummer a demo cassette. A year later, Taha noted that The Clash's "Rock the Casbah" bore stylistic similarities to his demo, implying a potential influence, though Taha never expressed bitterness and later recorded his own version of the song. "he turned on the radio and heard some song called Rock the Casbah, which stylistically, anyway, sounded an awful lot like some of the stuff on the demo cassette that he gave Joe." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
