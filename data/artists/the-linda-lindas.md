## songs
**Racist Sexist Boy (2021) – Written Over Zoom During COVID Lockdown After Racist Incident at School** : The Linda Lindas are a young, all-female group from Los Angeles who started making music before any member was old enough to drive. Drummer Mela De La Garza, who is part Chinese, heard someone at her school make a racist remark — the boy's father had told him to stay away from Chinese people. She reported back to her bandmates and together they wrote a song over a Zoom call while in COVID lockdown. The song began as "Idiot Boy" but became "Racist Sexist Boy."
