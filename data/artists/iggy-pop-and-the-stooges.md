## curiosities
**Band Breakup and Legendary Status** : The proposed rematch with the Scorpions never happened, as The Stooges broke up shortly after the incident. This true story cemented their legend as "America's most dangerous rock and roll band." "That never happened, blessedly, because the Stooges then broke up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Confrontation with Biker Gang (Early 1974)** : By early 1974, the band's behavior was completely out of control. During a gig in Wayne, Michigan, a biker gang called the Scorpions heckled Iggy Pop for his skimpy, skin-tight leopard costume. Iggy confronted them, resulting in eggs and bottles being thrown at the stage. "By early 1974, things had really gotten out of control." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Extreme Behavior and Drug Use** : The Stooges were known for their wild and intense performances, with Iggy Pop being particularly unhinged. The band extensively used drugs, including heroin and Angel Dust, leading to self-destructive behavior and an out-of-control reputation. "They were on every drug known to man all the way up to heroin and Angel Dust." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Iggy Pop's Beating and Challenge** : While the rest of the band fled, Iggy Pop remained to confront the bikers, jumping into the crowd and receiving a severe beating before being dragged out, possibly saving his life. Days later, Iggy appeared on the radio, promoting an upcoming show and boldly challenging the Scorpions to a rematch. "He stayed there to confront the bikers, jumping into the crowd. And he got a pretty good beating." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Iggy Pop's Stage Antics** : Iggy Pop was notorious for scaring audiences with his antics, which included smearing peanut butter on himself and carving his body with broken glass from bottles thrown at him on stage. "He'd smear peanut butter all over himself and often carve himself up with broken glass from the beer bottles that were thrown at him on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
