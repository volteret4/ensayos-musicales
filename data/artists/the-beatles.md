The idea emerged as a compromise after Noel Gallagher's initial suggestion of a different Beatles photo was deemed uninteresting. "The inspiration was another album, specifically the back cover of a 1966 Beatles compilation entitled A Collection of Beatles Oldies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tbKuJR0cPs
**The White Album (Year Unknown) – Serial Numbered Vinyl** : The retail version of *The White Album* is highly collectible because each record has a unique serial number. The first four records (numbers 0000001 through 0000004) were given to the individual Beatles members. Ringo Starr's copy, number 0000001, was sold for charity in 2015 for $790,000. "Every single one of those records has a serial number with the first four records going to each of the Beatles." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IIJySSo276k
