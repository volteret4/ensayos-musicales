**Accusation of Cultural Appropriation** : Rosalía has been accused by certain sectors of the music industry, music critics, and classical/conservative flamenco practitioners of cultural appropriation. This criticism stems from her Catalan origin, lack of Andalusian background, and non-Roma (gitana) ethnicity, suggesting she is appropriating a culture that does not belong to her.
