# artist - Chris Birkett

## curiosities
**Producer and Engineer for Sinead O'Connor (1990)** : Chris Birkett, who had previously mixed two singles from Sinead O'Connor's debut album, was contacted by Ensign Records executive Nigel Grainge to take over production for her second album, *I Do Not Want What I Haven't Got*. This occurred after Sinead walked out on the original producer, Nellee Hooper. Birkett described the recording process as bare-bones and worked closely with Sinead, notably producing her hit single "Nothing Compares 2 U." "So the vocal company phoned me up, and signed records, Nigel Brange. And he said that we've got the studio booked, and Shanade's got all the songs ready. We don't have a producer.
