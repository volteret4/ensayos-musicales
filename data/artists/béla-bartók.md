## curiosities
**Use of Devil's Triad** : Bartók, a notable classical composer, is cited among the many classical artists who incorporated the "devil's tri tone" (flattened fifth) into their musical works. This demonstrates the broad historical application of this interval in classical music, long before it acquired negative connotations in popular culture.
