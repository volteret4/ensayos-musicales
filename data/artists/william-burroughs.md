# artist - William Burroughs

## curiosities
**Chelsea Hotel Resident** : Poet William Burroughs was a resident at the Chelsea Hotel, a famous haunt for artists, writers, and various "colorful characters." "poets William Burrow's." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Collaboration with Kurt Cobain** : In 1992, William Burroughs met and collaborated with Kurt Cobain on a spoken word piece titled "The Priest, they called him," released in 1993. Burroughs read one of his stories, accompanied by Kurt Cobain's guitar sounds. Burroughs was reportedly very pleased with the recording. "That's William Burroughs and Kurt Cobain on guitar. From 1993, with a piece called The Priest, they called him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ
**Dream Machine Invention** : William Burroughs invented a trance-inducing device called a "dream machine," which consists of a cylinder with an internal light source that rotates, causing the light to flash exactly 10.56 times per second. The theory is that staring at these flashes induces a trance-like state, and in the 1960s, several experimenters with these machines allegedly committed suicide. "The machine consists of a cylinder with a light source inside. When he switched on the cylinder rotates, causing the light to flash at a rate of exactly 10.56 times per second." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ
**Dream Machine Recommendation to Kurt Cobain** : After their 1992 collaboration on "The Priest, they called him," William Burroughs was so pleased that he told Kurt Cobain about his invention, the "dream machine." He then connected Cobain with Dave Woodward, a builder in San Francisco who created these machines for special clients, leading to Cobain ordering one immediately. "Burroughs was apparently so pleased with how this recording turned out that he told Kurt about the dream machine. He even fixed him up with a guy in San Francisco named Dave Woodward who built machines for special clients." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ
