**Wind of Change (1991)** : Released on January 20, 1991, this power ballad became a global hit, selling an estimated 14 million copies worldwide, making it the best-selling single by any German artist. The band, originally from Hanover, West Germany, recorded an English version and a Russian version under the name The Vetter Paramen. The song was a massive hit in the USSR, leading to the band presenting Soviet leader Mikhail Gorbachev with a gold record. It remains highly popular among fans in Eastern Europe, and rumors persist that it was part of a CIA operation to destabilize Soviet society with its message of freedom and revolution. "Wind of Change was a global hit for the Scorpions, a metal bandit of Hanover in what was then West Germany." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YeNWDtN_sHE
