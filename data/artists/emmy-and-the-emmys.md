## curiosities
**Madonna's First Band Name** : The band's name, Emmy and the Emmys, used "Emmy" as a shortened form of the letter "M," which itself was short for Madonna. "In this case, Emmy stood for the letter M. And M was short for Madonna." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8 ← emmy-and-the-emmys ← emmy-and-the-emmys
**Madonna's Post-Punk Attempt** : Emmy and the Emmys was Madonna's initial effort to lead a post-punk band, which ultimately "didn't work" before she achieved global fame within four years.
