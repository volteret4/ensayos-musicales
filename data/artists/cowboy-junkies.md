# artist - Cowboy Junkies

## members
- Margot Timmins

## albums
**The Trinity Session (1987) – Single-Microphone Church Recording** : Recorded on November 27, 1987, by producer Peter Moore using a single microphone in the Church of the Holy Trinity in downtown Toronto, this album cost only $150, including the price of pizza for the night. Despite its low-budget, single-night production, it became an unexpected international hit, achieving platinum status in both Canada and the United States. "The album was called the Trinity Session, and it became an unexpected international hit, going platinum in both Canada and the United States." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko

## songs

## curiosities
**Accidental Sound Development** : In 1985, Margot began singing with her brothers Michael and Peter in their garage. They accidentally developed their distinctive down-tempo, almost whispery sound after neighbors called the police on one of their rehearsals for making too much noise, forcing them to tone down their volume. "They soon developed an interesting down tempo, almost whispery sound. And then sort of came about by accident because the neighbors called the cops on one of the rehearsals for making too much noise so they just kind of toned it down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
**Hunger Project and Germanel Roots** : The roots of the Cowboy Junkies can be traced back to a punky band called Hunger Project, formed in Toronto in the early 1980s, which also existed for a time in New York. After Hunger Project collapsed, the core members reassembled into an experimental group named Germanel, which garnered good reviews, especially in the UK, but ultimately did not succeed. "The roots of the cowboy junkies are in a punky sort of band called Hunger Project, which was formed in Toronto in the early 1980s and existed for a while in New York." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
**Margot Timmins' Early Career** : Before joining Cowboy Junkies as a singer, Margot Timmins had a background in school plays and camp productions. She worked as part of the road crew for her brother Michael's previous band, Hunger Project, managing the door. While Michael was touring Germany with Hunger Project, she was in university studying to become a social worker. "When Hunger Project was a thing, she worked as part of the road crew, working the door. When Michael was off touring with Hunger Project in Germany, she was in university planning to be a social worker." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
