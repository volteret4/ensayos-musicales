## curiosities
**Claimed Invention of Stage Diving (1970s)** : Dee Snider of Twisted Sister claims he invented stage diving in the 1970s. His account involves diving into the crowd to confront and pummel a guy who had thrown a beer bottle at him. "But then de-snider of twisted sister claims he invented stage diving in the 70s when a guy threw a beer bottle at him and he dove into the crowd to pummel him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Dee Snider Wrote Celine Dion's Christmas Hit – "The Magic of Christmas Day"** : Dee Snider, frontman of Twisted Sister, is the author of the highly successful Christmas song "The Magic of Christmas Day (God Bless Us Everyone)," which was performed by Celine Dion. The song has generated substantial ongoing royalty income for Snider.
