## songs
**Night Runnin' (2019)** : This song from the "Social Cues" album was an unfinished track that Caged the Elephant had for years. They admired Beck's songwriting and sent him music files from their Nashville studio to his place in LA via services like Dropbox. Beck returned the song with new verses within 24 hours, completing it.
