## curiosities
**Spiritual Advisor on Tour** : Bono frequently toured with his own full-time priest and spiritual advisor, Reverend Jack Heeslip. Heeslip initially met U2 when he was a teacher and counselor at Mount Temple High School in Dublin, where the band members were students. Reverend Jack passed away in 2015. **Unique Award Nominations Achievement** : Bono holds the distinction of being the only individual to have been nominated for a Grammy, a Golden Globe, an Oscar, and a Nobel Peace Prize, recognizing his wide-ranging achievements in music, film, and humanitarian efforts.
