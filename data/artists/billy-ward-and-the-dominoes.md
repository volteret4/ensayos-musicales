## curiosities
**Huge Influence on Soul Music** : Billy Ward and The Dominoes were a massive influence on the development of soul music. Their lead singers, Clyde McFatter and Jackie Wilson, sang in a style heavily indebted to the church music that the Isley Brothers had also performed, making them a natural inspiration for the Isley Brothers' transition from gospel to doo-wop.
