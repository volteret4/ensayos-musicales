# artist - Peter Ebling

## curiosities
**Corpse Paint in Phantom of the Paradise (1974)** : Peter Ebling, the singer of the fictional band The Undead in the 1974 film "Phantom of the Paradise," is depicted wearing corpse paint similar to what would later be seen in modern death metal or black metal. This early use of corpse paint contributes to the theory that the film contained elements of proto-goth or proto-death metal. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
