# artist - Claire Lane

## instruments

## songs
**"Ghost Riders in the Sky" (1962)** : Claire Lane was the drummer for The Ramrods, an early rock band from Connecticut. She played drums on their 1962 hit "Ghost Riders in the Sky," making her one of the first female drummers to contribute to a rock hit.
