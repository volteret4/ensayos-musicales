# artist - The Attractions

## curiosities
**Second Album Backup Band** : The Attractions became Elvis Costello's crack backup band, but they did not form until his second album. His debut album, My Aim Is True, was recorded with a different, uncredited American band called Clover (performing as The Sham Rocks).
