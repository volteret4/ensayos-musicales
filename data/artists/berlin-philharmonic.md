## curiosities
**First CD Test Pressing (1979)** : A recording of the Berlin Philharmonic, conducted by Herbert von Karajan, performing Richard Strauss' "An Alpine Symphony," was chosen as the first test pressing CD. This recording appeared in 1979 and was selected for its exceptional clarity and wide dynamic range, qualities the compact disc technology promised to deliver.
