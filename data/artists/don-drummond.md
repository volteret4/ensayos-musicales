# artist - Don Drummond

## member of
- The Skatalites

## curiosities
**Contribution to Ska Sound** : Don Drummond was a trombone player who helped give some of the early Ska music a darker sound. His band was initially called The Satellites, but by 1964, they became known as The Skatalites. "He was a trombone player who helped give some of this music a darker sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WX2GlpMNg_8
**Murder Conviction and Death** : In 1965, Don Drummond was convicted of murdering his girlfriend and subsequently sent to an insane asylum, where he died in 1969. The exact circumstances of his death are debated, with possibilities ranging from natural causes, starvation, murder by gangsters in retaliation for his girlfriend's death, or even government agents threatened by the nationalism his band's music fostered.
