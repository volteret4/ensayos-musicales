# artist - Blake Schwarzenbach

## curiosities
**Lyrical Source from Personal Journals** : As the singer for Jawbreaker, Blake Schwarzenbach developed a unique songwriting method: he first articulated his deepest thoughts and feelings in personal journals, which then became the raw material for his songs. This confessional approach forged a strong bond with the band's fanbase. "First, he poured his heart out into personal journals. Those thoughts and feelings were then turned into songs. And in the process, he achieved a really special connection with fans. They really, really identified with him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
