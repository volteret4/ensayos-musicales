## albums
**Version (2008)** : This album featured Mark Ronson's highly popular remix of Radiohead's "Just." The version, which initially appeared on the tribute album *Exit Music: Music for Radio Heads*, was released on Ronson's own album in 2008, gaining widespread popularity and subsequently charting, accompanied by a music video that served as both an homage and a continuation of Radiohead's original "Just" video. This alternative version first appeared on the tribute album *Exit Music: Music for Radio Heads* and was later featured on Ronson's 2008 album, gaining significant popularity. Its success led to the production of a music video that paid homage to and acted as a continuation of Radiohead's original "Just" music video. "volvió a las listas y a los charts, 12 años más tarde. **Uptown Funk (Year Unspecified)** : Co-written with Bruno Mars, this song faced a barrage of plagiarism lawsuits from artists such as The Gap Band, The Sequence, Kalash, Zap, and Victoria. The legal challenges resulted in the songwriting credits and royalties being divided among Ronson, Mars, and ten other parties, none of whom participated in the song's actual recording.
