## songs
**Sword of a Thousand Men (1981)** : This song was a major alt-rock and punk-rock hit, particularly successful in the UK where it reached number 6 on the British singles chart and sold over a quarter of a million copies. Its popularity saw a resurgence when it was featured in the 2012 movie "The Pirates in an Adventure with Scientists" and in season 2 of the Amazon series "The Boys" in 2020. It has also been used in various TV commercials in the UK. The song has garnered 11 million YouTube views and 4.9 million Spotify plays, resulting in a one-hit wonder score of 2.00 out of 10. "This is their only hit which was bigger in the UK than anywhere else. It reached number 6 on the British singles chart and eventually sold over a quarter of a million copies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_qxdftwrX3E
