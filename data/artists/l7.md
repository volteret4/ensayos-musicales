# artist - L7

## members
- DeNita Sparks (songwriter)

## genres
- All-female Grunge Metal

## albums
**Bricks Are Heavy (1992)** : Released on April 14, 1992. The band's third record, it was produced by Butch Vig and helped them form their "signature sound." It is described as "all-female grunge metal" and is their best-known album.

## songs
**Pretend We're Dead (1992)** : From the album "Bricks Are Heavy." Written by DeNita Sparks.

## curiosities
**Butch Vig Remix Production** : Butch Vig produced a remix record for the group.
**DeNita Sparks' Fearlessness** : DeNita Sparks was known for her fearlessness; during a performance on the British TV show "The Word," she controversially stripped off all her clothes from the waist down "to make some kind of point."
**Early Formation and Sexism** : An all-female band from LA, they started playing in 1985 and faced sexism and indignities common for new bands, amplified by being an "all-girl group."
**Pro-Choice Activism & Band Support** : Their participation in pro-choice events brought them into contact with progressive bands like Nirvana, Pearl Jam, Rage Against The Machine, and Red Hot Chili Peppers, who became fans and offered help.
**Producer Choice** : Worked with producer Butch Vig, known for his work with Sonic Youth's "Goo" album and Nirvana's "Nevermind," as part of a "gold rush" to sign aggressive-sounding bands.
