## albums
**Nuggets (1972)** : Lenny Kaye, who would later become the guitarist for Patti Smith, took on the task of compiling songs from many bands that shared a savage sound, resulting in the seminal compilation "Nuggets," released in 1972. This compilation played a crucial role in preserving the legacy of many garage rock and protopunk bands that had been largely ignored by the music industry.
