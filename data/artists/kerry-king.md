## curiosities
**Use of Marshall Stacks** : Kerry King of Slayer utilizes Marshall stacks, specifically the JCM 800 model, which first appeared in 1981. This amplifier setup is a descendant of the original JTM45 and, by extension, the Fender Bassman, contributing to Slayer's distinctive heavy sound.
