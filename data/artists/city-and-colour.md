## curiosities
**Beneficiary of Radio Star Maker** : City and Colour's career was furthered by the funding available from the new Radio Star Maker program, introduced in 2000. This program, along with Factor, was instrumental in supporting emerging and established Canadian talent. "Soon this cash was instrumental in helping further the careers of metric, broken social scene, Alexis on fire, city and color, and many more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
**Eclipsing Alexis on Fire** : By the time Dallas Green left Alexis on Fire, his solo project, City and Colour, had grown significantly in popularity. It was noted that City and Colour had "eclipsed Alexis on Fire at that point," influencing Green's decision to leave the band to focus on his creative passion for different musical styles. "City and Colour had a clip to Alexis on fire at that point." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
