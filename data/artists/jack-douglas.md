# artist - Jack Douglas

## albums
**A Den of Thieves (2005) - Producer** : Jack Douglas, a renowned producer known for his work with Aerosmith, The Who, and John Lennon's "Double Fantasy," produced The Trews' second album, "A Den of Thieves." He agreed to produce after hearing 20 demos from the band, and the album was recorded in Toronto in Winter 2005 as he was making a comeback as a producer.
