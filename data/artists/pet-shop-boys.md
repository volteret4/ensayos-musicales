## curiosities
**Inspired by Sex Pistols Gig (1976)** : Future members of the Pet Shop Boys were present in the sparse audience at the Sex Pistols' influential gig at the Lesser Free Trade Hall in Manchester on June 4, 1976. Despite its low attendance, this concert became a crucial turning point for English rock, inspiring many who witnessed it. "In the audience that night were future members of the buscocks and the pet shop boys and joy division and new order." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1y2UuU-me_s
**Neil Tennant's Inspiration** : The earliest inspirations for the Pet Shop Boys can be traced to what singer Neil Tennant experienced and felt at a single Sex Pistols concert. "Many earliest beginnings of the pet shop boys could be traced to what singer Neil Tennant saw and felt at a single pistols concert." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gKrAXMG8sTc
**Roskilde Cancellation (2000)** : The Pet Shop Boys cancelled their set at the Roskilde Festival on June 30, 2000, sharing concerns that the organizers were not adequately prepared or had their "act together" given the challenging conditions, including heavy rain and mud. "Both the Wases and the Pet Shop boys had canceled their sets because they didn't think organizers had their act together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7v1REnqKuXE
**Two-Person Synthpop Model** : Listed as an example of a synthpop band that adopted the common two-person model, featuring a singer or front person alongside a composer, programmer, and operator. "Yes, E-Rature, the pet shop boys, Eurythmics. Many of them had this two-person model." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng
