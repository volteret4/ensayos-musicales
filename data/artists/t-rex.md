**Exceptional Sales and Chart Performance (1971)** : Despite having only one Top 10 hit in the US, T. Rex was not a one-hit wonder; in 1971, the band had four number-one singles out of five releases in England. That year, they sold approximately four million records, which surpassed The Beatles' best yearly sales, and sold more 45s (singles) than The Who and Jimi Hendrix combined. "everywhere they went, they tore the place apart." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 26 - Starmen - Glam Rock of the 70s | https://seventies.libsyn.com/026-glam-11820-6-34-pm
**Glam Rock Craze Initiation (1971)** : Mark Bolan, at 24 years old, initiated the glam rock craze when T. Rex appeared on Top of the Pops in 1971, showcasing glitter, satin pants, and a sexually heightened, androgynous look. This performance was watched by a nine-year-old David Evans (The Edge) in Dublin, who described Bolan as magical.
