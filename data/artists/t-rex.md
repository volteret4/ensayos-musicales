**Electric Warrior (1971)** : Released in September 1971, this album elevated the sound and attitude established by "Ride a White Swan." It was characterized as fun, funny, cartoony, fantastical, and provocatively naughty, making it a significant album that rocked with a distinct glam rock style and sound, and is considered the first glam rock album. "Electric Warrior, I think we can say, was the first glam rock album." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The Surprising History of Glam Rock | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG

## curiosities
**Glam Rock Catalyst (1971)** : In March 1971, T-Rex appeared on the hugely important British TV show "Top of the Pops." During this performance, Marc Bolan, the band's frontman, wore satin clothes and was covered with glitter dust, an event that David Bowie watched at home and found "brilliant," marking it as "the beginning of glam rock" and inspiring Bowie to pursue a similar direction.
