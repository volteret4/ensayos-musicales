**Proud Life and Public Harassment Before AIDS Death** : Throughout his life, Freddie Mercury proudly defended his way of living, full of parties and debauchery, never feeling uncomfortable or ashamed, emphasizing it was the life he enjoyed and that made him happy, independent of stereotypes. His diagnosis only became public two or three days before his death, after a year of intense harassment, including false blood tests confirming his illness.
