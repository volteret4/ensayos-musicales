# artist - Sam Smith

## songs
**Stay With Me (2014)** : This "huge hit single," released in spring 2014, was widely recognized as sounding similar to Tom Petty's 1989 hit "I Won't Back Down." The similarity led to lawyers getting involved and an "out of court settlement" in October 2014, resulting in Tom Petty being "awarded songwriting royalties from the Sam Smith song."
