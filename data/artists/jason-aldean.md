## curiosities
**On Stage During Route 91 Harvest Festival Shooting (2017)** : Jason Aldean was performing on stage at the Route 91 Harvest Festival in Las Vegas at approximately 10:00 PM on October 1, 2017, when Stephen Paddock began firing into the crowd from the Mandalay Bay Hotel. The shooting started at 10:05 PM and continued for ten minutes, resulting in the deadliest mass shooting by a lone gunman in American history. "At around 10, Jason L.
