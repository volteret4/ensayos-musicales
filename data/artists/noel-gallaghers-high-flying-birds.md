# artist - Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds

## members

## albums
**Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds (2011)** : The debut album from Noel Gallagher's solo project was released on October 17, 2011. It achieved significant commercial success, outselling Liam Gallagher's band Beady Eye, and reportedly sold more than 2.5 million copies globally. "Noal responded with his own project. Noal Gallagher's high-flying birds. They released a debut record on October 17, 2011. It ended up selling much better than Liam's. And last I heard, it has sold more than 2.5 million copies globally." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bB7GAUM63Y
