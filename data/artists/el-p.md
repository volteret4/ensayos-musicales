## curiosities
**Pure and Good Rap Outside Traditional Hip Hop** : El-P is recognized for creating "very pure and good" rap, despite the critical assertion that his work, along with artists like The Streets, does not necessarily align with traditional hip hop culture. This underscores the idea that rap can maintain high artistic merit even when departing from the established stylistic, rhythmic, thematic, and behavioral norms of hip hop.
