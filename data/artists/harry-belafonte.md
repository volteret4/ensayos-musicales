# artist - Harry Belafonte

## albums
**Calypso (1957) - First Gold Album Award Recipient** : Harry Belafonte was the recipient of the first official gold album award in 1957. This was in recognition of his album "Calypso" selling one million copies. This event predated the Recording Industry Association of America's (RIAA) formalization of sales awards. "The following year, Harry Belafonte was the recipient of the first gold album for selling a million copies of something called Calypso." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso ← harry-belafonte
