# artist - Mike Myers

## curiosities
**Deeter Character Inspiration** : Mike Myers was inspired to create the character Deeter from the "Sprockets" sketch on Saturday Night Live by observing a waiter at a venue called the Camerunhouse in Toronto. The sketch also featured a Kraftwerk song played at the wrong speed as its theme, and Deeter's monkey, Klaus, was named after avant-garde singer Klaus Nomi. "Lord has it that Mike Myers was inspired to create Deeter by a waiter at a venue called the Camerunhouse in Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0FYq5MBdHw4
