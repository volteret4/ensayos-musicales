**Pushing for Better Sound** : Supertramp was among the bands, including David Bowie and Yes, who actively pushed sound companies to improve their live audio. They considered "getting concert sound right was very, very important," thereby influencing the advancement of concert sound systems and techniques. "But to performers like David Bowie and Yes and Super Tramp, getting concert sound right was very, very important, so they pushed sound companies to get better." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
