**KKK Conspiracy Theory – Band Name Investigated, T-Shirts Banned, Real Origin Is Indecent Exposure Charge** : In 1993, rumors spread that 311 was code for KKK, since K is the 11th letter of the alphabet and 3×K=KKK. The band's t-shirts were banned in high schools around their hometown of Omaha, Nebraska, and police investigated. The actual origin of the name: a former guitarist named Jim Watson was caught skinny dipping by the Omaha Police Department and charged with indecent exposure, which carries the offense code 311 in the Omaha PD's system. "This is all crap. The real story behind the name relates back to the time when a former guitarist named Jim Watson was caught skinny dipping." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fMVGuHjJp9U
