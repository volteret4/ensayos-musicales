**Investigation for Black Ice (September 27, 1986)** : Following Cliff Burton's fatal bus accident, James Hetfield searched the highway extensively for the reported black ice but couldn't find any. This contributed to rumors surrounding the cause of the accident, though an investigation later ruled it a routine winter traffic accident.
