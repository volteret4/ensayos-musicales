## curiosities
**CBL & Orion Tribute to Cliff Burton** : Above his right elbow, James has the letters "CBL," which are the initials of former Metallica bassist Cliff Burton. Under his inner left arm, there are musical notes that transcribe the song "Orion," a track written by Cliff Burton, serving as a tribute to him after his death in a 1986 tour bus accident. "Just above his right elbow are the letters CBL. Then there were some musical notes under his inner left arm. That's a transcription of the song Orion, which is in tribute to former Metallica bass player Cliff Burton, who died in a tour bus accident in 1986." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
**Extensive and Intricate Tattoos** : James Hetfield of Metallica has at least 26 intricate tattoos, each with a story. "The guy from Metallica has at least 26 tats, not a lot compared to others, but they are very, very intricate." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
**Flaming Playing Cards (Birth Year & Pyro Accident)** : On his left shoulder, Hetfield has flaming playing cards (an ace, a nine, a six, and a three) representing 1963, his birth year. The flames themselves reference a 1992 incident in Montreal where he caught fire due to ill-timed pyro during a concert. "On his left shoulder are some flaming playing cards, an ace followed by a nine, a six, and a three. So 1963, the year he was born. So 1963, 1963, the year he was born. The flames are in reference to the time he literally caught fire because of some ill-time pyro during a 1992 show in Montreal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
**Injuries Requiring Substitute** : James Hetfield of Metallica has twice needed his guitar tech, John Marshall, to fill in for him due to injuries. One incident involved a broken arm from skateboarding, and another was a result of being badly "synched" by ill-timed pyrotechnics. "Once after Hetfield broke his arm while skateboarding and another time when he was badly synched by some ill-time pyro." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
**Investigation for Black Ice (September 27, 1986)** : Following Cliff Burton's fatal bus accident, James Hetfield searched the highway extensively for the reported black ice but couldn't find any. This contributed to rumors surrounding the cause of the accident, though an investigation later ruled it a routine winter traffic accident. "James Headfield went up and down the highway looking for this black ice but he couldn't find any." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
**M81 (Metallica Formation Year)** : On his right hand, Hetfield has "M81," with "M" standing for Metallica and "81" representing 1981, the year the band was formed. "On his right hand, you can see M81. That's M for Metallica and 81 for the year in which the band was formed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
