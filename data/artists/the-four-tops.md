## curiosities
**Missed Pan Am Flight 103 (1988)** : The R&B group The Four Tops had seats booked on Pan Am Flight 103 for December 21, 1988. However, they overslept after performing on BBC's "Top of the Pops" the night before, causing them to miss their flight and inadvertently escape the Lockerbie bombing.
