## songs
**Leaving Babylon** : Song where singer HR's Jamaican accent caused "mash" to sound like "mush," contributing to the popularization of the term "moshing" as he urged the audience to "mash it up" from the stage, saying "mash it, mash it down Babylon."

## curiosities
**Dave Grohl's Favorite Band** : Bad Brains are a legendary DC band and one of Dave Grohl's all-time favorites.
**Guest Musicians on "Feast or Famine"** : Featured as guest musicians on the Foo Fighters' song "Feast or Famine."
**Inner Ear Studios Recordings** : Made records at Inner Ear Studios.
**Performed During CBGB's Final Weeks (2006)** : Bad Brains performed at CBGB during its final weeks leading up to its closure in October 2006.
