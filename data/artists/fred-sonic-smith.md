# artist - Fred Sonic Smith

## curiosities
**Collaborator and Husband of Patti Smith** : Fred Sonic Smith was a member of MC5 and collaborated with Patti Smith on music in the 1980s. He is the "Frederick" addressed in Patti Smith's song of the same name. They married in 1980, after which Patti Smith largely retired from public life to raise their children with him in Detroit.
