# artist - The Tragically Hip

## curiosities
**Early Gigs for Headstones** : The Tragically Hip provided early support to the Headstones by offering them performance opportunities, recognizing their talent despite acknowledging their unpredictable nature. "the hip gave us the nod and said, we're going to give you some gigs, but you know, you guys are unpredictable and but you're on your way and you're good." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zBd13KtYlAA
**Gord Downie's Flashpoint Calls** : Gord Downie would sometimes call Hugh Dillon after seeing him on the television show "Flashpoint" while flying, indicating their ongoing connection and friendship. "I get calls from Corde Downey when they'd be flying. I saw him on the plane." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zBd13KtYlAA
**Kingston Performance with Headstones** : Hugh Dillon and the Headstones performed together with the surviving members of The Tragically Hip at a gig in Kingston. "Here is a recording of Hugh and the headstones singing with the surviving members of the hip at a gig in Kingston." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zBd13KtYlAA
