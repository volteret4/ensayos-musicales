## songs
**Bobcaygeon (1999)** : From the album "Phantom Power," this song was awarded the Juno Single of the Year in 2000. Gord Downey described it as being about a stressed-out city cop who loved visiting the Kawartha Lakes region of Ontario, with the title chosen merely because it rhymed with "constellations." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Montreal (unreleased)** : An unreleased track that the band occasionally performed live. It addresses the events of December 6, 1989, when Mark Lapine shot 27 people and killed 14 women at the École Polytechnique in Montreal. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4

## curiosities
