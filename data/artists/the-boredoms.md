## curiosities
**Extreme Band at Lollapalooza** : The Boredoms were described as another extreme band. Their frontman, Yamantaka Eye, who previously fronted Hanatarash, performed with them at Lollapalooza in 1994. "Yamantaka, I would later go on to be in the Bordom's, another extreme band that toured as part of Lala Palusa in 1994." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
