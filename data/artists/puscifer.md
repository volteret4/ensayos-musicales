## curiosities
**Maynard James Keenan's Family Winemaking Heritage** : Maynard James Keenan, known for his work in Tool, A Perfect Circle, and Puscifer, has a family history in winemaking, with relatives in northern Italy who he believes still make wines. This heritage influenced his own venture into viticulture. "His family still might make wines in northern Italy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WIFxAaReLn0
**Maynard's Other Band** : Puscifer is Maynard James Keenan's other musical project, active during periods when he was not working with Tool. "When he wasn't working with Tool, Maynard had his other band, Pussifer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI
**Merkin Vineyards and Caduceus Cellars – Maynard James Keenan's Arizona Wine Operations** : Maynard James Keenan owns Merkin Vineyards and Caduceus Cellars, high-end wine operations located in the Sedona area of Arizona, specifically in Cornville, north of Phoenix and south of Flagstaff off I-17. He invested money earned from Tool and A Perfect Circle into these ventures. Merkin refers to a wig worn in the pubic region, while Caduceus, the ancient symbol for commerce, is associated with the brand's website, www.cadocious.org. His wines, particularly the '84 cab blend, have received excellent reviews, and the tasting center invites wine lovers to sample and purchase. "Maynard owns Merckin Vineyards." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WIFxAaReLn0
