## curiosities
**All-Time Loudest Measured Band (1998)** : In 1998, Hanson unexpectedly earned the title of the loudest band ever measured, pegging a decibel meter at 140 decibels. The actual volume may have been even higher, but the meter could not register beyond that maximum level. "That's when Hansen, yes, Hansen pegged a decibel meter. They may have been even louder, but the meter didn't go any higher than 140." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ ← hanson
