## albums
**Double Fantasy (1980) – Autographed by Lennon on Assassination Day** : John and Yoko's recently released album, which John Lennon autographed for Mark David Chapman around 5 PM on December 8, 1980, before heading to a recording studio. This is believed to be Lennon's last autograph and bears both Chapman's and Lennon's fingerprints. In the chaos following Lennon's assassination five hours later, Chapman left the record in a planter at The Dakota, Lennon's New York apartment. It was found by an unknown individual who kept it under their bed for 18 years. The cover features Lennon's signature in black marker and police evidence markings. It first sold in 1998 for $150,000, then for $520,000, and again in November 2020 for $900,000, making it the most expensive piece of vinyl in the world. "John Lennon was assassinated on December 8, 1980. The shooter, of course, was Mark David Chapman. Before he killed Lennon, Chapman hung around the Dakota, Lennon's New York apartment, with a copy of John and Yoko's just released album, Double Fantasy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tbmssCZ05ro ← john-lennon
