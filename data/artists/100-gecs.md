## albums
**1000 Gecs (2019) - Debut Album** : This debut album is described as a "sonic collapse" that draws influences from trap, EDM, dubstep, J-Pop, and even monophonic mobile phone melodies. All of this is passed through an autotune filter and enveloped in an absolutely colorful and "chiclosas" (chewing gum-like) aesthetic. Far from being just a joke, the album resonates surprisingly seriously, encapsulating the essence of hyperpop as a genre where anything goes, and its impact has been enormous.
