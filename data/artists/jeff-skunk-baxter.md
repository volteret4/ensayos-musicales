## curiosities
**Military Technology Consultant** : In the 1970s, after a conversation with a retired engineer, Jeff "Skunk" Baxter became fascinated by military technology, particularly sidewinder missiles. He identified similarities between high-end recording studio gear (like digital storage and data compression) and military hardware. This led him to write a paper on how the US Navy could convert its Aegis missile system, eventually becoming a consultant on various military matters, including Ronald Reagan's Star Wars initiative, Spice satellites, and counter-terrorism efforts.
