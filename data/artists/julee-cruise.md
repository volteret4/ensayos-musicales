# artist - Julee Cruise

## albums
**Floating into the Night (1989)** : This 1989 album, a collaboration with composer Angelo Badalamenti, featured the "Twin Peaks" theme, "Falling." The album showcased very dreamy songs, becoming emblematic of Dreampop's characteristics in the late 1980s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY

## songs
**Falling (1989)** : As the theme song for David Lynch's "Twin Peaks," this track from Julee Cruise's 1989 album "Floating Into the Night" (with Angelo Badalamenti) epitomizes the dreamy, hazy, and blurry mood characteristic of late 1980s Dreampop, often featuring quiet, breathy female vocals. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
