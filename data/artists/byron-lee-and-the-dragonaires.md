# artist - Byron Lee and the Dragonaires

## curiosities
**Introduction of Electric Bass and "Ska" Term Origin** : Byron Lee, a Jamaican record producer and bandleader of the Dragonaires, is credited with introducing the electric bass guitar to Jamaica. He might also be the person who coined the term "Ska." His band, the Dragonaires, notably played Jamaican jump-up music in the Colossal Bar scene in the first James Bond movie, "Dr. No," from 1962. "He said to be the person who introduced the electric bass guitar to the country. He had a band called the Dragon Heirs. And if you go back to Dr. No, the first James Bond movie from 1962, that's them playing Jamaican jump-up in the Colossal Bar.
