# artist - Misielio

## curiosities
**Co-author of Music Response** : Misielio collaborated with the obscure artist Manicol on a song that, despite not achieving success, contained "tiny fragments" that were later sampled by The Chemical Brothers for their track "Music Response." The inclusion of these nearly imperceptible samples legally required Misielio to be credited as a co-author of "Music Response."
