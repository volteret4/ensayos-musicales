# artist - Andy Fletcher

## member of
- Depeche Mode

## curiosities
**Coping Mechanisms and Personal Life (Early 1990s)** : Andy Fletcher attempted to decompress from band stress by playing chess, becoming quite skilled. He also ventured into entrepreneurship by opening a restaurant named Guesconi near Abbey Road Studios in London, which ultimately failed. He became a father for the first time in August 1991, adding more pressure to his life. "He also opened a restaurant in London called Guesconi near Abbey Road Studios, but that failed to arrest some mental health challenges he was having." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
**Devotional Tour Breakdown and Replacement (1993)** : During the recording of "Songs of Faith and Devotion," Andy Fletcher exhibited signs of severe depression and was sent home from sessions. On the Devotional Tour, he was depressed, homesick, and hypochondriac, suffering a nervous breakdown that led to a month-long hospitalization, causing him to miss the entire second leg of the tour. He was replaced by keyboardist Darryl Bata Montey for 39 shows. "He suffered a nervous breakdown and had to be checked into the hospital for a month, missing the entire second leg of the tour." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
**Mental Health Struggles (Early 1990s)** : Andy Fletcher was diagnosed with OCD, which he believed was inherited from his father, and also struggled with anxiety and depression, requiring professional treatment. He found Dave Gahan's ego trip particularly difficult to manage, contributing to a "three against Dave" dynamic within the band. "He was diagnosed with OCD, something he believed he inherited from his father." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
**Post-Tour Financial Issues and Death (1994-2022)** : After the tumultuous 90s, Andy Fletcher managed to regain stability and continue as a functioning member of Depeche Mode, despite facing financial issues from bad investments, which he also survived. Sadly, he died at his home on May 26, 2022, at the age of 60, from an aortic dissection, a sudden and massive internal bleeding event. "But then Fletcher died of an aortic dissection in his home on May 26, 2022." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
