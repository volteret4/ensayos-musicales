## songs
**Party Time** : The title of the Beastie Boys' song "Hey Ladies" is derived from Curtis Blow's "Party Time." "And the title of the song itself is from Curtis Blows Party Time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l1jPP1FhGo8 ← curtis-blow

## curiosities
**Genuine Musical Genius** : Curtis Blow is recognized as one of the "genuine stars, genuine musical geniuses" of the early rap and hip-hop scene, whose contributions were not always taken seriously by the wider music market. "It didn't matter that there were genuine stars, genuine musical geniuses. One DMC, Curtis Blow, Africa Babata, Grandmaster Flash, LL Cool J, the scene and the sounds were just not taken seriously." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l1jPP1FhGo8 ← curtis-blow
