# artist - Santana

## concerts
- Woodstock 50 (originally slated for 2019, but canceled)

## curiosities
**2000 Sales Performance** : The band produced one of the biggest selling rock records of the year 2000, amidst a dominant pop and hip-hop music scene, indicating a challenging period for rock. "The biggest selling rock records of the year were from, well, they were from Creed and Santana..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
**Demand for Quality Live Sound** : Santana was one of several prominent musicians, including Frank Zappa and The Eagles, who became "picky" about their live sound quality. Their demand that "they sound good on stage" contributed to pushing sound companies to develop better equipment and techniques. "A lot of this could be attributed to the pickiness of the musicians, artists like Santana and Frank Zappa and the Eagles demanded that they sound good on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
**Planned Woodstock 50 Performance** : Santana was among the 80 artists originally confirmed to perform at the Woodstock 50 festival, which was planned for August 2019 to commemorate the original event. However, the festival was ultimately canceled due to numerous logistical and financial issues. "80 artists had originally signed up, including Santana, Dead & Company, The Killers, Jay-Z, Imagine Dragons, Cage the Elf and Miley Cyrus, Vampire Weekend, and more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uEe_HLUay5M
**Use of Mobile Studio** : Santana utilized the Rolling Stones Mobile Recording Studio for their recordings. "It was also used by bad company and simple mines and dire straits and Santana and Iron Maiden and others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
