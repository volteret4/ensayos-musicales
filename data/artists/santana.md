## curiosities
**2000 Sales Performance** : The band produced one of the biggest selling rock records of the year 2000, amidst a dominant pop and hip-hop music scene, indicating a challenging period for rock. "The biggest selling rock records of the year were from, well, they were from Creed and Santana..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
**Demand for Quality Live Sound** : Santana was one of several prominent musicians, including Frank Zappa and The Eagles, who became "picky" about their live sound quality. Their demand that "they sound good on stage" contributed to pushing sound companies to develop better equipment and techniques. "A lot of this could be attributed to the pickiness of the musicians, artists like Santana and Frank Zappa and the Eagles demanded that they sound good on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
