**Ozzy Osbourne as Early Rock Tattoo Adopter** : Darren Fyfer identified Ozzy Osbourne as likely the first rock star he noticed with tattoos, particularly on his hands, arms, and shoulders, which made the concept of tattoos seem "much cooler" in the rock and roll sphere.
