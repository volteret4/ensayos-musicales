## curiosities
**1982 World Tour Advertisement** : An advertisement from 1982 promoted Ozzy Osbourne's "1982 World Tour" with special guests Starfighters, live at an auditorium. The ad described an "awesome garage of sound and light" that would "rip through you in a rage of heavy metal fury," encouraging attendees to "Enter the world of a madman." The face value of a ticket for this event was $9 in 1982. "The 1982 Sunom rates Aussie Osborne in the flesh. This Tuesday at the auditorium an awesome garage of sound in light will rip through you in a rage of heavy metal fury." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OCkov6FTliE

**Death (2025) – Heart Attack at 76, Final Concert a Medical Miracle** : Ozzy Osbourne died at age 76 of a heart attack. His final concert, held in Birmingham two weeks before his death, was considered a near-miracle given his severe Parkinson's disease, chronic pain, and a secret prior hospitalization. He dictated his final autobiography, *Last Rights*, the day before he died. "His last concert in Birmingham, two weeks before his death, was considered a miracle due to his severe Parkinson's, chronic pain, and a secret prior hospitalization." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZPlsR7_WiZQ
**Early Rock Tattoo Adopter** : Ozzy Osbourne is identified as one of the first rock stars noticed with visible tattoos by many, including Darren Fyfer. He had tattoos early on, particularly on his hands, arms, and shoulders, which contributed to the perception of him being "in a rock and roll." "Ozzy Osborne, for sure. He had a few tattoos early on. And I remember thinking, well, that's cool. He must, he's in a rock and roll. He's in a rock and roll. That idea of a tattoo, that much cooler." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ

**Near-Fatal Manicure Infection** : In 2018, Ozzy Osbourne contracted a severe, near-fatal infection following a manicure. His thumb became so infected that it swelled to the size of a light bulb, requiring hospitalization and aggressive antibiotic treatment to prevent the loss of his hand. "Ozzy Osborne contracted a near-fatal infection in 2018. After a manicure, his thumb became so infected that it reached the sides of a light bulb." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO0K_N7Ys7I
**Official Record Store Day Ambassador** : Ozzy Osbourne has been an official ambassador for Record Store Day, an initiative created to revitalize independent record stores and increase the sale of vinyl records.
