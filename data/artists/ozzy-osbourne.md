## curiosities
**Death (2025) – Heart Attack at 76, Final Concert a Medical Miracle** : Ozzy Osbourne died at age 76 of a heart attack. His final concert, held in Birmingham two weeks before his death, was considered a near-miracle given his severe Parkinson's disease, chronic pain, and a secret prior hospitalization. He dictated his final autobiography, *Last Rights*, the day before he died. "His last concert in Birmingham, two weeks before his death, was considered a miracle due to his severe Parkinson's, chronic pain, and a secret prior hospitalization." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZPlsR7_WiZQ
**Early Rock Tattoo Adopter** : Ozzy Osbourne is identified as one of the first rock stars noticed with visible tattoos by many, including Darren Fyfer. He had tattoos early on, particularly on his hands, arms, and shoulders, which contributed to the perception of him being "in a rock and roll." "Ozzy Osborne, for sure. He had a few tattoos early on. And I remember thinking, well, that's cool. He must, he's in a rock and roll. He's in a rock and roll. That idea of a tattoo, that much cooler." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ

**Near-Fatal Manicure Infection** : In 2018, Ozzy Osbourne contracted a severe, near-fatal infection following a manicure. His thumb became so infected that it swelled to the size of a light bulb, requiring hospitalization and aggressive antibiotic treatment to prevent the loss of his hand.
