**Formation and Early Success (1980s)** : Missing Persons was formed in 1980 by Warren Cuccurullo, Tony Bozzio (a former Frank Zappa drummer), and Tony's wife, Dale Bozzio, who had also contributed to Zappa's work. The band found success with several new wave hits, becoming a staple on MTV in the early 1980s. Dale Bozzio, the singer, was known for her uninhibited persona, which included performing nude photo shoots for Hustler magazine in both 1979 and 1980.
