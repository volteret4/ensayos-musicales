# artist - Ry Cooder

## albums
**Bop Till You Drop (1979) - First All-Digital Recording Album** : Released in 1979, *Bop Till You Drop* by Ry Cooder is identified as seemingly the first album to be recorded entirely using digital technology. This release marked a significant milestone in the evolution of recording techniques, showcasing the early adoption of digital methods.
