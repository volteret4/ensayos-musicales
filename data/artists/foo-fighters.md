**Wasting Light (2011) - Analog Garage Recording & Nirvana Reunion** : Released after a four-year hiatus, the 2011 album "Wasting Light" was uniquely recorded in Dave Grohl's garage in 2012, eschewing expensive modern studios for old analog equipment and the production expertise of Butch Vig, known for Nirvana's "Nevermind." This decision reflected Dave Grohl's desire for a primal, raw, and old-school sound, with edits made using razor blades and a backyard tent serving as the control room, and no digital processes until mastering. The recording process famously reunited three key figures from Nirvana's "Nevermind" – Dave Grohl, Butch Vig, and bassist Krist Novoselic, who played on the song "I Should Have Known." "There was absolutely nothing digital about this album until the mastering process. No computers.
