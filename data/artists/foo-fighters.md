**Dave Grohl Leg Break Hoax (June 2015)** : During a concert in Guttenberg, Sweden, in June 2015, Dave Grohl broke his leg after falling off the stage during the band's second song. Grohl famously returned to complete the show with a bandaged leg, even quoting himself saying, "I'm going to go to the hospital. I'm going to fix my leg. But then I want to come back. And we're going to play for you again." However, the severe break led to numerous canceled shows, including a major Wembley Stadium performance. A conspiracy theory arose claiming the injury was faked, with public X-rays also being dismissed as false. Proponents suggested it was a ploy to generate buzz and allow the band to return triumphantly with Grohl's custom throne and sell commemorative T-shirts, despite the entire tour, including Wembley, already being sold out. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg
