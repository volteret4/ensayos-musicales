## songs
**Holy Man (Unfinished Song)** : An unfinished recording originally left behind by Beach Boy Dennis Wilson. Taylor Hawkins was commissioned to complete it, adding music, writing new lyrics, and singing the song himself. He was backed by Roger Taylor and Brian May of Queen, and it was released as a Record Store Day release in 2019.
