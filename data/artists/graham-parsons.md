## curiosities
**Death and Desert Cremation (September 19, 1973)** : Graham Parsons, former member of The Byrds, died of an overdose at age 26 on September 19, 1973, in room 8 at the Joshua Tree Inn in California. His body was then spirited away by his road manager from LAX to be cremated in the desert in a private ritual. A pile of stones known as Cap Rock is believed to be where the cremation took place.
