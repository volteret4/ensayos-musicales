# artist - Wynonie Harris

## songs
**Good Rockin' Tonight (Released after 1947)** : This song, originally a hit for Roy Brown in 1947, was also released by Wynonie Harris, another Black performer. Both versions contributed significantly to embedding the idea of "rockin'" as a musical element into public awareness.
