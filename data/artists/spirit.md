# artist - Spirit

## songs
**Taurus (1967)** : This 1967 instrumental was the basis for a lawsuit against Led Zeppelin, alleging that "Stairway to Heaven" was lifted from it. Despite sonic similarities when played side-by-side, the legal case was won by Led Zeppelin due to "very, very stark and pronounced" differences when the songs were compared "note by note" on sheet music. "This is how Led Zeppelin beat the charges that stairway to heaven was lifted from a 1967 instrumental by a band called Spirit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
