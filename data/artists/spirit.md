**Taurus (1967)** : This 1967 instrumental was the basis for a lawsuit against Led Zeppelin, alleging that "Stairway to Heaven" was lifted from it. Despite sonic similarities when played side-by-side, the legal case was won by Led Zeppelin due to "very, very stark and pronounced" differences when the songs were compared "note by note" on sheet music.
