# artist - Tom Jones

## songs
**Thunderball (1965) – Title Theme** : Tom Jones, a singer from Wales, was recruited by John Barry to perform the title theme for the 1965 James Bond film "Thunderball." His powerful vocal performance contributed to the film's musical identity and continued the trend of grand, orchestrated themes for the franchise.
