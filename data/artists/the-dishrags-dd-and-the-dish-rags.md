# artist - The Dishrags (DD and the Dish Rags)

## curiosities
**Canada's First All-Female Punk Band – Victoria BC, Jamming 1976, Part of Vancouver's First Punk Show** : DD and the Dish Rags originated in Victoria, British Columbia, with three members who were around 15 years old and already Ramones fans when they started rehearsing around 1976. They were part of Vancouver's first punk rock show on July 30, 1977, before approximately 400 people — a large audience for that era. Their run ended by 1980, but they left behind two EPs and the distinction of being, by the available dates, Canada's (and possibly the world's) first all-female punk band.
