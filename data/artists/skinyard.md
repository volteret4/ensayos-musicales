## curiosities
**Incubator of Key Grunge Figures – Studio and Label Founders** : Skinyard was a band that included Matt Cameron (later of Soundgarden and Pearl Jam), Jack Endino, and Daniel House. Endino was known for running Reciprocal Recording, a local studio favored by indie bands like Nirvana, while House founded CZ Records, which issued the first proto-grunge compilation, *Deep Six*. "That group featured two notable people. The first was Jack Indino. As well as playing in Skinyard, he had a local recording studio called Reciprocal Recording... The other guy of note in Skinyard was Daniel House. He'd been in an instrumental prog band with Matt Cameron called Feedback." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
