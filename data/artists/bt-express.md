## songs
**Do It To Your Satisfied (1973)** : This track from 1973 is rumored to have taken 80 hours to put together and may be the first-ever extended remix specifically created for the dance floor. It was carefully spliced together by Tom Molten on a Reebok's Reel to Real tape machine, adding eight or sixteen bars at various points to create a long, unbroken version of about five and a half minutes of perfect dance beats. "The track we're now hearing, BT Express, and Do It To Your Satisfied, is from 1973.
