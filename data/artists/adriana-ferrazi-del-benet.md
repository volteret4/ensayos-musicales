# artist - Adriana Ferrazi del Benet

## curiosities
**Mozart's Dislike for Her Singing Style** : The singer Adriana Ferrazi del Benet was a target of Mozart's disdain due to her physical mannerisms while singing, particularly her head movements on low and high notes. Mozart even composed a piece with specific note jumps to intentionally make her head bob "like a chicken" during her performance. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
