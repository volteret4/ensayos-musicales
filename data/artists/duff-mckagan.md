**Cool and Friendly Demeanor (2004)** : At a press conference for Velvet Revolver's "Contraband" album, Duff McKagan, along with Slash and Matt Sorum, were observed as "cool, all very friendly, all hanging out together," in stark contrast to Scott Weiland's aloofness. **Encounter with Kurt Cobain (April 1, 1994)** : Duff McKagan, the bassist for Guns N' Roses, coincidentally sat next to Kurt Cobain in business class on Delta Flight 788 as Cobain flew back to Seattle from Los Angeles. McKagan reported that Kurt seemed fine and even offered him a ride home from the airport, but Kurt declined, signed an autograph for two people, and then disappeared. "By coincidence, sitting next to him in business class was Guns and Roses' bass player, Duff McCagan. Duff says the Kurt seemed fine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU ← duff-mckagan
**Intervention for Scott Weiland (2003)** : When Scott Weiland struggled with addiction during the recording of Velvet Revolver's debut album, Duff McKagan personally took him to a rural retreat in Washington state. There, Weiland underwent intense physical training with a martial arts expert to help him overcome his issues. "Duff took him to a quiet rural place in Washington state to commune with his trainer, a martial arts expert who promised that Scott would work out his issues with him intense physical training." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY ← duff-mckagan ← duff-mckagan
