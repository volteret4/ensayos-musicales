## curiosities
**Encounter with Kurt Cobain (April 1, 1994)** : Duff McKagan, the bassist for Guns N' Roses, coincidentally sat next to Kurt Cobain in business class on Delta Flight 788 as Cobain flew back to Seattle from Los Angeles. McKagan reported that Kurt seemed fine and even offered him a ride home from the airport, but Kurt declined, signed an autograph for two people, and then disappeared. "By coincidence, sitting next to him in business class was Guns and Roses' bass player, Duff McCagan. Duff says the Kurt seemed fine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
