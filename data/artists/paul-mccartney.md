**Longevity of Superstars** : cited as an example of artists capable of drawing arena or stadium-sized crowds who first appeared in the 1960s.
**Marriage to Heather Mills** : He had one child (Beatrice) after marrying Heather Mills.
**Marriage to Linda McCartney** : He had three children (Stella, Mary, James) with Linda McCartney.
