## curiosities
**One-Off Drummers Group for Live Earth (2007)** : SOS All Stars was a unique, one-off group comprised of drummers Taylor Hawkins, Roger Taylor (Queen), and Chad Smith (Red Hot Chili Peppers), assembled specifically for the Live Earth show in London in 2007. This project highlighted a special gathering of influential rock drummers.
