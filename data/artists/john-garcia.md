# artist - John Garcia

## curiosities
**Kyuss Singer** : John Garcia was the singer for Sons of Kyuss, participating in their 1987 demo and remaining the vocalist at the time of Kyuss's breakup in October 1995. "Josh is buddy Nick Olivary, drummer Brett Bjork and singer John Garcia, recorded a demo." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG1TQuhcf1o
**Post-Kyuss Career Change** : After the breakup of Kyuss, John Garcia made a significant career shift, becoming a veterinary surgeon. "But John Garcia, the singer, pulled a big left turn. He became a veterinary surgeon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG1TQuhcf1o
