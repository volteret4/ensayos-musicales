## songs
**Revolution - Considered for Nike Ad** : The Georgia Satellites' song "Revolution" was initially considered by Nike for their 1987 "Air Revolution" commercial. However, an editor at the ad agency Wieden+Kennedy ultimately opted to use The Beatles' song instead.
