# artist - Helmet

## songs
**Just Another Victim (1993)** : Helmet collaborated with House of Pain on the song "Just Another Victim" for the 1993 *Judgment Night* soundtrack. This "ground breaking" collaboration was part of a radical experiment to pair rock and hip-hop acts, serving as a major catalyst for the rap rock and nu metal genres that became very big later in the decade. **You're Just Another Victim** : Featured on the "Judgment Night" soundtrack, this song was used by Ash Boo Shultz of USS in an experimental stage performance. He would play it on a cassette tape simultaneously with a "how to heal your inner child" tape to create discomfort and explore neuroplasticity.
