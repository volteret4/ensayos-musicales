## curiosities
**Defining Musical Period with Tonto** : Stevie Wonder's collaboration with Malcolm Cecil and Robert Marguliffe, utilizing the Tonto synthesizer, defined a "greatest period" in his career during the 1970s. Over just three and a half years, they produced three highly influential albums—"Talking Book," "Innervisions," and "Fulfillingness' First Finale"—which featured numerous hit songs and showcased the transformative power of electronic music in Black Pop. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
**Post-Motown Creative Freedom** : After fulfilling his contract with Motown Records, Stevie Wonder sought a new direction for his music. He was keen to record the sounds he heard in his head and, after hearing the Tonto synthesizer, contacted its creators, Malcolm Cecil and Robert Marguliffe. This collaboration provided him with the tutorial and technology needed to explore new sonic possibilities. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
