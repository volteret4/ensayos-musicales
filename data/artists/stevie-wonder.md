## curiosities
**Break from Motown and Synth Exploration** : After leaving his old contract with Motown Records, Stevie Wonder sought out Malcolm Cecil and Robert Margouleff and their TONTO synthesizer for a tutorial. He desired to explore synthesizers to finally record the music he envisioned, marking a significant shift in his artistic direction. "Stevie was out of his old contract with Motown Records and wanted a tutorial and synthesizers so he could finally start recording the music that he heard in his head." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg ← stevie-wonder
**Career Transformation with TONTO** : Stevie Wonder's collaboration with Malcolm Cecil and Robert Margouleff, utilizing the TONTO synthesizer, profoundly changed the direction of his career. This partnership led to his "greatest period" in the 1970s, encompassing iconic albums like "Talking Book," "Innervisions," and "Fulfillingness' First Finale," and fundamentally altered the landscape of Black Pop music. "Tonto changed the direction of Stevie Wonder's career. And he changed what Black Pop music could be." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg ← stevie-wonder
