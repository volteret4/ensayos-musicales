**You Haven't Done Nothin' (1974)** : Recorded in the midst of the Watergate scandal in 1974, this upbeat funk dance song subtly critiques Richard Nixon and the government. The song reached number five on the charts the week ending October 5th, 1974. Stevie Wonder intentionally crafted a catchy, lighter melody to embed a heavy message, believing people are more likely to listen and think if entertained.
