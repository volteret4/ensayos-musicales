## curiosities
**Arthur Brown (1968) – First Rock Star to Use Extreme Stage Makeup; Flaming Helmet; Set Stage for What Followed** : Arthur Brown of the Crazy World of Arthur Brown appears to have been the first rock star to use extreme makeup on stage, making it difficult to know what he really looked like. He also wore a helmet on stage which was often set on fire. Brown was born in Yorkshire, went to university in London and Reading, worked in theater, and was in several bands before this project. He was not exactly anonymous, but set the stage for the theatrical identity concealment that would follow.
