- Blur

## curiosities
**Blur's Influence on Gorillaz's Sound** : Touring the world with Blur exposed Damon Albarn to diverse musical influences, which he wanted to experiment with. Gorillaz provided the perfect vehicle for this experimentation, as Blur was not the appropriate outlet for these new sounds. "Damon says that touring the world with blur brought him to contact with all kinds of music. He wanted to fiddle with these new influences, but blur wasn't really the place to do that." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Britpop "First Couple"** : Damon Albarn of Blur formed a relationship with Justine Frischmann of Elastica, and they were recognized as the "first couple of the whole Britpop scene." "Justine hooked up with Damon Albart of Blur and together acted as the first couple of the whole Britpop scene." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Co-Creator of Gorillaz** : Along with Jamie Hewlett, Damon Albarn is one of the two human creators at the center of Gorillaz, responsible for the music of the virtual band. "The two humans at the center of all this are Damon Albarn of Blur, a very good musician with a pedigree and track record, and Jamie Hewlett." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Demon Days Inspiration** : The thematic depth of the "Demon Days" album, which explored themes of ego and a dark world, was partly informed by a train trip Damon Albarn took from Beijing to Mongolia. "concepts that were released partly informed by a train trip Damon Albarn took from Beijing to Mongolia." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Dispute with Jamie Hewlett (Post-2010)** : After 2010, Damon Albarn and Jamie Hewlett had a dispute and stopped working together for approximately four years, reportedly because Hewlett felt his comic creations were sidelined as the music gained prominence. "Surely after that, Damon and Jamie had some kind of dispute and weren't talking to each other for a while." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Feud with Noel Gallagher** : Damon Albarn and Noel Gallagher were once considered "mortal enemies," but this rivalry had ended by the time of the "Humans" album, with Gallagher contributing to the record, though his vocals were ultimately unused without malice. "He and Damon used to be mortal enemies, but that's all in the past." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Gorillaz Co-creator (Pre-2005)** : He "had left blur behind and had created gorillas with comic book artist Jimmy Hewlett," leading to significant record sales during the mid-2000s rock resurgence. "Damon Alburn had left blur behind and had created gorillas with comic book artist Jimmy Hewlett." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
**Inspiration for Gorillaz** : While sharing a London flat with Jamie Hewlett in 1997, Damon Albarn watched MTV and was struck by the perceived vacuity and lack of substance in many contemporary artists, leading to the idea of forming a "fake band." "The TV was on MTV a lot and both of them were fascinated by how vacuous so many of the artists were, how empty the music was, how little substance there seemed to be." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Ping Pong Skill** : Damon Albarn of Blur is noted for his exceptional skill at ping pong. "Damon Albarn of Blur is a killer at Ping Pong." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
**Recording "The Fall" on an iPad** : Damon Albarn entirely recorded the 2010 fan club album "The Fall" on his iPad while on the Gorillaz World Tour. "This era included a fan club album entitled The Fall, which was recorded entirely by Damon on his iPad during that 2010 World Tour." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
