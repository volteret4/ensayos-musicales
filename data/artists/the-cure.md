## curiosities
**Categorization in New Wave (Early 1980s)** : Categorized under New Wave acts who preferred "darker guitars" alongside Public Image Limited and Joy Division.
**Contribution to The Crow Soundtrack (1994)** : One of the big names featured on the critically acclaimed soundtrack for *The Crow* movie.
**Featured on Alt-Rock Playlists** : their music was found on radio playlists for late 1986 alt-rock stations.
**Influence on 2000s Revival** : Was a band rediscovered and mined for inspiration by early 2000s New Wave revivalists, including The Bravery.
**Influence on The Killers** : Their sound contributed to the "DNA" of The Killers.
