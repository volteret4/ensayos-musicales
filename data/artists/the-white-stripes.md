## songs
**Hay Operator (2000)** : The White Stripes' studio version of "Jolene" was released as a B-side to this single, which came from their "De Stijl" album. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Jolene (2000) – Dolly Parton Cover** : Jack White of The White Stripes was a great fan of Dolly Parton's "Jolene." A studio version of their cover was released in 2000 as a B-side to the single "Hay Operator" from the "De Stijl" album. A live track of the cover later appeared on the film and album "Under Great Northern Lights," which documented the band's Canadian tour, and it eventually became a concert favorite. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## curiosities
**"Brother and Sister" Misconception** : Jack and Meg White perpetuated a joke that they were brother and sister, though they were actually ex-husband and wife who continued as bandmates after their divorce in 2000. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUQ2vZzmRiI
