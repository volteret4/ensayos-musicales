# artist - Jack Sinclair

## curiosities
**Weezer Fan and Producer** : Jack Sinclair produced Weezer's White Album. He was a longtime Weezer fan and also fronted a tribute band called Wannabeezer, giving him unique insights into the band's sound. Brian Bell noted that Sinclair understood Weezer's sound better than the band themselves.
