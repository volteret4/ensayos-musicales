## curiosities
**Beverly Hills Supper Club Fire (May 28, 1977)** : Singer John Davidson was the featured attraction at the Beverly Hills Supper Club, south of Cincinnati, on May 28, 1977, when a fire claimed 165 lives. The club was severely overcrowded, with 3,000 people present despite a fire code capacity of 1,500, and the disaster was attributed to faulty wiring and insufficient fire exits. "The attraction that night was singer John Davidson. The fire code capacity was 1500, but there were 3,000 people there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
