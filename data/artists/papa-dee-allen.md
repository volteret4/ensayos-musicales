# artist - Papa Dee Allen

## curiosities
**"Universal Anthem" Goal for "Why Can't We Be Friends" (1975)** : In an interview during the summer of 1975, percussionist Papa Dee Allen discussed how War's recent "listening" on the streets informed their music. He expressed that "Why Can't We Be Friends" was an attempt to create a "universal anthem" to reflect a world where people might not expect "love to conquer," but could at least aspire to friendship. "We heard the message and we put it on tape and we're trying to make this a universal anthem.
