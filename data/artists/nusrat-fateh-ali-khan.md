# artist - Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan

## genres
- Qawwali

## albums
**Passion (Soundtrack)** : Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan was featured on the *Passion* soundtrack, a project from Real World Studios, where David Botrell was engineering. This connection led to further collaborations with producer Michael Brook. "we used him on the passion soundtrack." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw

## songs
**Must Must** : This song is highlighted as brilliant by David Botrell, despite the potential barrier of Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan singing in a non-English language. It has been included on numerous playlists and was famously remixed by Massive Attack. "There's one song of his called Must Must. The Must. That I think is just brilliant." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw

## curiosities
**Collaborations with Michael Brook** : Beyond his work on the *Passion* soundtrack, Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan made "a number of records" with Michael Brook, an originally Torontoanian producer. David Botrell engineered these records. "he did a number of records with Michael Brook." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
**Undeniable Passion and Language Barrier** : Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan, a Pakistani Qawwali singer, is noted for the "undeniable passion" in his singing. However, his lack of English lyrics presents a challenge for casual North American listeners to fully "get it," even though many find his music musically transcendent. "I think that the passion that he sings with is undeniable." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
