**Undeniable Passion and Language Barrier** : Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan, a Pakistani Qawwali singer, is noted for the "undeniable passion" in his singing. However, his lack of English lyrics presents a challenge for casual North American listeners to fully "get it," even though many find his music musically transcendent.
