**Dave Rave Ogilvie's Continued Production Work** : Following Jackalope, Dave Rave Ogilvie has continued his career in music production and engineering. "Dave Rave Oglevy continues his production and engineering work." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EE8U4yyKtd0
**Katie B as Voice and Face** : Katie B, originally a receptionist at Brian Adams' Warehouse Studio in Vancouver, became the primary vocalist and public persona for Jackalope after meeting Dave Rave Ogilvie. "Things began to come together when he met Katie B, the receptionist at the warehouse studio, Brian Adams recording studio in Vancouver. They got along great, and she became the voice and the face of Jackalope." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EE8U4yyKtd0
**Katie B's Post-Jackalope Projects** : Katie B collaborated with Sébastien Lefebvre of Simple Plan on a Christmas EP and also formed a band called Nice Horse, which released a couple of country-tinged albums. "Meanwhile, Katie B went on to work with Sebastian LaFave of Simple Plan on a Christmas EP, plus she had a band called Nice Horse, which released a couple of albums. Those ones had more of a country vibe." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EE8U4yyKtd0
**Notable Contributors to Music** : Over the years, Jackalope's music featured contributions from a range of distinguished artists, including members of Sloan and Monster Magnet, Trent Reznor of Nine Inch Nails (who also co-produced), Alex Lifeson of Rush, and the three members named Don from Sons of Freedom. "Over the next few years, a number of really interesting artists contributed to Jackalope's music. The members of Sloan, the members of Monster Magnet, Trent Resner of Nine-H nails acted as a co-producer for a while. Alex Lifeson of Rush, and like I said, the three Don's from Sons of Freedom." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EE8U4yyKtd0
**Theme Song for Degrassi: The Next Generation** : Jackalope composed and performed the theme song for the television series "Degrassi: The Next Generation" from season 4 onwards. "If you were a fan of Degrassi, the next generation, Jackalope did the theme song from season 4 onward." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EE8U4yyKtd0
