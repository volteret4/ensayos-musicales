**Polio's Influence on Guitar Technique and Tunings** : A childhood bout with polio resulted in a weakened left hand, compelling Joni Mitchell to invent creative chord voicings. This led to her extensive use of unusual guitar tunings, reportedly employing at least 51 different tunings throughout her career. "And because about of polio left her with a weakened left hand, she had to be creative with how she formed chords and that led to all kinds of exotic tunings." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0 ← joni-mitchell

She has also battled Morgellons disease, a rare condition characterized by fibers appearing under the skin, which nearly halted her recording and performing career multiple times.
