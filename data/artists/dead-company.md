## curiosities
**Planned Woodstock 50 Performance** : Dead & Company were listed as one of the 80 artists initially scheduled to perform at the ill-fated Woodstock 50 festival in August 2019. Their participation was part of the attempt to create a diverse and commemorative lineup for the 50th anniversary of the legendary event.
