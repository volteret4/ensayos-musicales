## curiosities
**Inspiration for Lollapalooza (1990)** : While attending the 1990 Reading Festival with his band Jane's Addiction, Perry Farrell was deeply impressed by the atmosphere and had been contemplating a similar annual festival for new bands in North America. The sight of 30,000 Pixies fans singing along to "Debaser" solidified his conviction that his concept for what would become the Lollapalooza Festival would work. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z9T9wi0vEvc
**Lollapalooza Creator** : Perry Farrell was the creator of the Lollapalooza Festival, a "traveling roadshow in circus" which held its second iteration in 1992, featuring a lineup described as arguably the best in its history. "This was the second year of Perry Farrell's traveling roadshow in circus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
