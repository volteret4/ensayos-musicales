# artist - Nancy Spungin

## curiosities
**"Nauseating Nancy" by the Press** : Due to her loud American attitude and constant presence around the infamous Sex Pistols, Nancy was dubbed "nauseating Nancy" by the press. "Nancy who inserted herself everywhere was dubbed nauseating Nancy by the press because of her loud American attitude." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Birth Complications & Early Trauma** : Born Nancy Lauren Spungin on February 27, 1958, in Philadelphia, she nearly died during birth because her umbilical cord was wrapped around her neck, causing cyanosis. She required immediate blood transfusions, suggesting possible oxygen deprivation. "She almost didn't survive her own birth because the umbilical cord was wrapped around her neck, almost choking her, on her way out into the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Childhood Emotional Disturbances** : Nancy displayed emotional problems from an early age, throwing tantrums at three months. By age three, a doctor prescribed barbiturates to control her constant screaming, and she began psychiatric treatment at four. She exhibited violent behavior, including threatening a babysitter with scissors and physically attacking her psychiatrist. "A doctor was worried enough to prescribe baby Nancy barbituates when she was just three in hopes that would stop her from always screaming." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Early Adult Life in New York** : At seventeen, Nancy moved to New York City where she worked as a stripper and occasionally as a prostitute to earn extra money. She became involved in the punk scene, frequenting clubs like CBGB and Max's Kansas City, sometimes carrying drugs for bands. "She moved to New York, where she worked for a while as a stripper, and when she needed extra money, she turned tricks. She was just 17." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Expulsion and Suicide Attempt** : She was expelled from school at age eleven and later ran away from her next school, attempting suicide by cutting her wrists with scissors. She escaped from a home for troubled kids where she had been enrolled. "She was expelled from school at eleven. She ran away from her next school and attempted suicide by cutting her wrists with scissors." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Move to London and Groupie Lifestyle** : In February 1977, Nancy moved to London, believing it was "where the action was." She fully embraced the groupie lifestyle, with the Sex Pistols as her primary target. "She deterred that that's where the action was. She needed to be there. And Nancy embraced everything she could about the groupie lifestyle." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Murder in the Chelsea Hotel** : Nancy Spungin died in Room 100 of the Chelsea Hotel between 8 and 10 AM on October 12, 1978. She was found in the bathroom under the sink, having been stabbed in the lower abdomen with a Jaguar Wilderness K-11 knife, causing fatal external and internal hemorrhaging. The medical examiner concluded it was murder, not suicide. "It was 10.45 on the morning of Thursday, October 12th, 1978. Sid was in shock, initially in a panic. He confessed, I stabbed her, but I never meant to kill her." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Teenage Diagnoses and Ban from Colorado** : Diagnosed with schizophrenia at age fifteen, Nancy was also brilliant and enrolled in university in Colorado at sixteen. However, she was thrown out due to "bad and weird behavior," including an arrest for trying to buy pot and accusations of theft. As a result of her issues, she was banished from the state to avoid legal consequences. "Nancy was diagnosed with schizophrenia when she was 15." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Wrist-Cutting Incident in Paris** : While Sid was filming parts for "The Great Rock and Roll Swindle" in Paris, Nancy, upset at being left alone in their hotel room, cut her wrists. This was not a serious attempt but rather an act to "make a point." "Nancy was upset at being left alone so she cut her wrists. Nothing serious though. She just wanted to make a point." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
