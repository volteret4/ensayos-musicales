# artist - U-Men

## curiosities
**Seattle Group Predating Grunge (1981) – First Client of Susan Silver** : The U-Men were a Seattle group that formed in 1981, predating the mainstream emergence of grunge by a few years. They were the first band managed by Susan Silver, who later became a prominent manager in the Seattle music scene for bands like Soundgarden and Alice in Chains.
