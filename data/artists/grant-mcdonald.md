## curiosities
**First Bank Studios Operations** : Grant McDonald operates his unique musical vision and personal mission out of the "mysterious First Bank studios," which are reportedly located somewhere on the lake in downtown Toronto. This adds to the enigmatic nature of his independent artistic enterprise. "The man is on a personal mission operating out of the mysterious First Bank studios, which is somewhere on the lake in downtown Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c8CD_FcWcg8
**Getty Oil Controversy and Lawsuit** : Grant McDonald wrote and directed a 40-minute movie alleging the Getty Oil Company sold oil to the Nazis before Pearl Harbor, with *4 Billion Bucks* serving as its soundtrack. In June 2005, a press release on his website announced a $40 billion lawsuit against Getty Oil regarding these alleged espionage activities during World War II. "A press release issued on Grant's website in June 2005 announced a $40 billion lawsuit against Getty Oil in regards to this alleged espionage in trees and back during World War 2." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c8CD_FcWcg8
