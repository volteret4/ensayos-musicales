# artist - Elvis Costello

## genres
- New Wave

## albums
**This Year's Model (1978)** : His second album, released in 1978, serving as an example of UK New Wave. His music was not punk, straight rock, or traditional pop, but something different: spiky, angular, and somewhat agitated.

## curiosities
**Influence on Hot Hot Heat** : His early new wave style inspired and informed the band Hot Hot Heat.
**UK New Wave Example (1977-78)** : Considered an example of UK New Wave from the first wave (around 1977-78).
