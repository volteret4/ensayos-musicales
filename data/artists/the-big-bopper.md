# artist - The Big Bopper

## curiosities
**Plane Crash Death (1957)** : On February 3, 1957, a day famously known as "the day the music died," The Big Bopper was killed when the small plane he was a passenger in went down in a cornfield outside of Mason City, Iowa. He died alongside fellow musicians Buddy Holly and Richie Valens. "February 3rd, 1957 has gone down in history as the day the music died." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
