# artist - The Vicious White Kids

## members
- Glenn Matlock
- Rat Scabies
- Sid Vicious

## concerts
- Electric Ballroom, London (late August 1978)

## songs
**Something Else (1978) – Eddie Cochran Cover** : This was one of the nine songs performed by The Vicious White Kids at their single concert at the Electric Ballroom. It was an old Eddie Cochran song dedicated to Nancy Spungen, which they repeated three times over three sets. "Sid Vicious and the Vicious White Kids doing the old studio song, a one-up of your dog dedicated to Nancy, no less." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8

## curiosities
**Funding for New York Move – Gig Money and McLaren Loan** : The money earned from the Electric Ballroom gig, combined with a £10,000 loan (in £10 notes) from Malcolm McLaren, funded Sid and Nancy's flight to New York City. "With the money from that gig at the Electric Ballroom in a 10,000-pound loan from Malcolm McLaren... Sid and Nancy flew to New York City." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8
**Limited Repertoire – Nine Songs Repeated** : The Vicious White Kids had a limited repertoire, consisting of just nine songs. For their three sets at the Electric Ballroom, they simply repeated these nine songs three times. "They had just nine songs. So for the three sets, they just repeated them three times." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8
**One-Off Band for a Single Show – Late August 1978** : Sid Vicious put this band together and played only one show at the Electric Ballroom in London in late August 1978. The lineup included Rat Scabies of The Damned on drums and Glenn Matlock, the Sex Pistols' former bassist, on bass, with Sid on vocals. "Sid put together a band and played one show at the Electric Ballroom in London in late August of 1978." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8
**Post-Sex Pistols Project** : Before his death, Sid Vicious was attempting to launch a career independent of the Sex Pistols with a new band called The Vicious White Kids. Some recordings from this project exist. "Before he died, Sid was trying to launch a post-Sexpistols career with a band called The Vicious White Kids." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
