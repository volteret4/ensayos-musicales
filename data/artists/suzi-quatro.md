## curiosities
**Acting Role on Happy Days** : In North America, Suzi Quatro is perhaps most recognized for her acting role as Leather Tuscadero, Fonzie's girlfriend, on the popular old show *Happy Days*. "Remember, Fanzi's girlfriend, Leather Tuske-Darrow? Yeah, that was Susie Quattro." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XjQiZqpmQlE
**Family Connection to Fanny** : Suzi Quatro is the sister of Patty Quatro, who joined the band Fanny in 1974. "She had a sister named Susie, and Susie became a big deal in the UK, Europe, and Australia." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XjQiZqpmQlE
**Global Success vs. North American Obscurity** : Suzi Quatro achieved massive success in the UK, Europe, and Australia, selling over 50 million albums worldwide. However, she remains relatively unknown in North America, never reaching the same level of stardom there as she did elsewhere. "But never in North America, where she still isn't that well known, which is crazy since she sold over 50 million albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XjQiZqpmQlE
**Times Square Soundtrack Contribution (1980)** : Suzi Quatro contributed to the soundtrack for the 1980 movie *Time Square*. This soundtrack was significant for being among the earliest compilations to feature the nascent alternative music of the day, which included punk rock and new wave. "There was Lou Reed and the Cure and Patty Smith and Susie Quattro." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
