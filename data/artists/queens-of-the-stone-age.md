# artist - Queens of the Stone Age

## labels
- Beggars Banquet Records
- Matador
- Rough Trade
- XL Recordings
- Young Turks

## albums
**Songs for the Deaf (2002)** : Released in the summer of 2002, this album was conceived as a concept record. It is intended to be the soundtrack of a drive from Los Angeles through to Joshua Tree National Park in the desert, with the listener flipping through small-town radio stations along the way. The artwork of some versions of the album depicts the dashboard of a Fiat 124 Spider to emphasize this concept.

## curiosities
**Certified Indie Artist** : Recognized as a certifiably indie artist due to recording for labels within the Beggars Group, which includes Matador, Rough Trade, XL Recordings, and Young Turks.
**Joshua Tree Studio Clients** : Queens of the Stone Age were clients at the Joshua Tree studio where the Foo Fighters recorded "Outside."
