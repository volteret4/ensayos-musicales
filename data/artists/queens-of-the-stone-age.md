## albums
**Songs for the Deaf (2002)** : Released in the summer of 2002, this album was conceived as a concept record. It is intended to be the soundtrack of a drive from Los Angeles through to Joshua Tree National Park in the desert, with the listener flipping through small-town radio stations along the way. The artwork of some versions of the album depicts the dashboard of a Fiat 124 Spider to emphasize this concept. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc
