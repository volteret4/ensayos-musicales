## albums
**Something Bitchin This Way Comes (1989)** : This was Lock Up's only album, released in 1989. Despite securing a deal with Geffen Records, the album was commercially unsuccessful, leading to the band being dropped by their record label by early 1991. ## curiosities
**Pre-Rage Existence** : Lock Up was Tom Morello's first band after moving to the West Coast. The lineup included drummer D.H. Pellegrot, who had previously played with punk legends Dead Kennedys and the Red Hot Chili Peppers. The band ultimately dissolved by early 1991 after their debut album failed commercially and they were released from their contract with Geffen Records.
