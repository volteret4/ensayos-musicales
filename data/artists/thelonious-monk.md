## curiosities
**Improvisational Genius Comparison** : Matthew Good cited Thelonious Monk as an example of musical genius and a reference for live-off-the-floor recording. He described a famous image of Monk changing music on the fly at his piano, with 17 session musicians observing over his shoulders to capture the evolving changes without any written notation.
