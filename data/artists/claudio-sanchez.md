## curiosities
**Comic Book and Graphic Novel Creator** : Claudio Sanchez, the lead singer and guitarist for Coheed and Cambria, ventured into comic book creation in 2008 with two issues of "The Bag Online Adventures." He later transitioned to graphic novels, most notably the complex sci-fi series "The Armory Wars," which tells the intricate story of the 78 planets of Heaven's Fence and the energy beams, known as the Key Work, that sustain them. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
