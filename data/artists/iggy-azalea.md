# artist - Iggy Azalea

## songs
**Fancy (2014)** : This song, featuring Charli XCX, was the winner of the 2014 Billboard Song of the Summer. While the career of the "Tri-Hard Supermodel rapper" Iggy Azalea "didn't endure," she was "supported by the still great Charlie XCX" on the track.
