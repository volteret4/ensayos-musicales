## curiosities
**Fashion and Sound (Late 1960s)** : Nico adopted a distinctive look of a completely black wardrobe with very bright red hair. She played an old pump organ, enhancing the stark and dramatic, gothic sound of her 1968 album *The Marble Index*. Her music was definitively described as gothic. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
