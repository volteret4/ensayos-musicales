## curiosities
**Claim to Devil Horns (November 1974)** : Gene Simmons claimed to be the first to flash the "devil horns" gesture in November 1974. He attempted to trademark the move with the US Patent and Trademark Office on June 16, 2017, but withdrew the claim due to significant public backlash. "He even tried to trademark the move with the US patented trademark office on June 16, 2017." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pyoCg3jDzmA
**Wig Hairspray Catching Fire** : Gene Simmons, a member of KISS, experienced incidents where the hairspray on his wig would catch fire during performances. His roadie, Dave Matthews, was tasked with dousing him with a wet towel to put out the flames. "Sometimes he'd be called upon to douse Gene Simmons with a wet towel when the hairspray he used on his wig caught fire." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
