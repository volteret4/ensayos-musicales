- **Drumming with MS Challenges** : Aaron Solowoniuk faces significant challenges playing the drums due to his multiple sclerosis. He noted that symptoms like fatigue make tasks such as navigating stairs daunting, and the physical demands of drumming become very tiring. "Anyone with MS can tell you a set of stairs can be daunting at times and imagine having to sit down and play drums. We can get very tiring."

**Early MS Diagnosis and Symptoms** : Aaron Solowoniuk was diagnosed with multiple sclerosis in 1998 at the age of 24. His initial symptoms included a buzzing sensation in his legs from the hips down, which made walking difficult, and optic neuritis in his left eye, causing significant vision loss, alongside general confusion. "Buzzing legs from my hips down. I could barely walk to my job at Chrysler. **Goal for Return to Drumming** : Despite the challenges, Aaron Solowoniuk's ultimate goal is to return to playing drums, leveraging the knowledge gained from listening to his body to come back even stronger. **Long-Term MS Management and Relapse** : For over a decade, Aaron managed his MS effectively through a regimen of drugs, prescription adjustments, and a positive outlook, including needle therapy. However, the treatment eventually ceased being effective, leading to a relapse in the six to seven months preceding January 2016, with symptoms causing him to slow down. "I was on this needle therapy for about over a decade. It all seemed to work and then it just stopped. So, feel myself getting slower as I get older, as we all do." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trMzjWWmbZo ← aaron-solowoniuk ← aaron-solowoniuk
**MS Awareness Advocacy (2006 onwards)** : In 2006, after learning about children as young as three being diagnosed with MS, Aaron decided to publicly share his diagnosis to raise awareness. He actively works with the MS Society of Canada and is involved with FUMS, an organization that hosts benefit concerts and golf tournaments to support MS charities. "he thought it was maybe time that he did what he could to raise awareness of MS." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NQgn0_mUh74 ← aaron-solowoniuk ← aaron-solowoniuk

**Stepping Back from Recording Drums (2016)** : On January 15, 2016, Aaron Solowoniuk announced via YouTube that he would temporarily step away from recording drums for Billy Talent's upcoming album due to an MS relapse. He explained that his symptoms were slowing him down, and he needed to focus on his health, expressing uncertainty about the duration of his recovery.
