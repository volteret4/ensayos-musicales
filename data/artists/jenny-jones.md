## instruments

## curiosities
**Drummer to Talk Show Host** : Jenny Jones, originally a runaway from London, Ontario, became a drummer for an all-girl band called The Cover Girls in Los Angeles. She was later discovered by Wayne Newton, which led her to gigs in Las Vegas, eventually transitioning to become the host of the daytime talk show, The Jenny Jones Show, in the 1990s.
