**Heartbeat, It's a Lovebeat (1973)** : Released in 1973, this song was Canada's answer to the Osmonds and became an international hit, reaching number three on the American singles charts. Its success provided the "all-important validation at home," which helped remove some of the stigma associated with being a Canadian artist at the time.
