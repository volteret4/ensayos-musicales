**UK Single Chart Disqualification for Music Response** : The commercial single release of "Music Response" included five songs: the main track, two remixes, and two previously unreleased compositions. However, the United Kingdom's music industry, a crucial market, refused to classify it as a single. This was because the UK's technical definition for a single on the charts limits releases to a maximum of three songs, leading to its classification as an EP. Consequently, it was barred from the single charts and single format sections, contributing to its low sales as many potential buyers were unaware of its release due to its absence from chart listings. "El problema fue que el reino nido que es el de nace, el nido de crece, toda la música prácticamente se negó a considerar un single porque el reino nido al parecer técnicamente solo consideró el single cuando tiene solo tres canciones y este disco yo tenía cinco." ← Music Radar Clan > Music Response y el single gafado | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BuVBeJ6maAk&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← the-chemical-brothers
