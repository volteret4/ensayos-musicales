**1999 E-Lectronica Explosion** : The Chemical Brothers were a prominent act in the E-Lectronica scene (now known as EDM) around 1999, contributing to its explosion in popularity and selling a large volume of albums. **Big Beat Influence** : This group was a notable "Big Beat" outfit, leading the way for other artists to adopt and integrate electronic elements like keyboards, drum machines, loops, and samples into their music. "Following the lead of what was known as Big Beat Outfits like the Prodigy and the Chemical Brothers, others started dabbling in these sounds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hBQE4t72i3o
**Continuing Late 90s Electronica Trend** : The Chemical Brothers continued to be a presence in the 2000s, signifying the ongoing influence of late 90s electronica trends that fostered the re-emergence of dance and electronic elements within alt rock.
