## songs
**Borderline (2009) – Madonna Cover** : In the spring of 2009, Star Death and White Dwarfs collaborated with The Flaming Lips to cover Madonna's "Borderline." This rendition was released as part of a split 7-inch single with The Black Keys for Record Store Day, pressed on green vinyl. The cover is notable for its slower tempo compared to the original, which both Wayne Coyne and a commentator agreed enhanced the song. The juxtaposition of avant-garde bands covering a mainstream pop song was highlighted as particularly satisfying. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## curiosities
**Collaborations with The Flaming Lips** : Star Death and White Dwarfs, an experimental band from Oklahoma, have a history of collaborations with The Flaming Lips, including covering Pink Floyd's "Dark Side of the Moon" album in its entirety in 2009.
