## curiosities
**Matt Walst Replaced Adam Gontier in 2013; Band Subsequently Broke Van Halen's Billboard Record** : Adam Gontier, who had checked himself into rehab for OxyContin addiction in 2005, suddenly left Three Days Grace on January 13, 2013. His replacement was Matt Walst, brother of bassist Brad Walst and formerly of My Darkest Days. With Walst, Three Days Grace has achieved more number one hits on the Billboard Mainstream Rock Songs chart than any other act in history, including Van Halen.
