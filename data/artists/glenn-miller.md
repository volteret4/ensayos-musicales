# artist - Glenn Miller

## curiosities
**Disappearance During World War II (December 1942)** : Big Band leader Glenn Miller went missing in December 1942 when his military plane reportedly went down over the English Channel during a mission in World War II. Despite extensive efforts, no wreckage or remains were ever found, though wartime conditions and severe weather were cited as contributing factors.
