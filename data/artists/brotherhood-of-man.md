## curiosities
**"Mamma Mia" Song Offer (1975)** : ABBA offered their song "Mamma Mia" to Brotherhood of Man, the winners of the 1976 Eurovision, as ABBA themselves were unsure about releasing it due to its perceived "too European" title. Brotherhood of Man did not record the song immediately, allowing ABBA to release it themselves, which proved to be a career-defining decision for ABBA. "so they offered the song to a British pop group and the winners of the 1976 Eurovision Brotherhood of Man.
