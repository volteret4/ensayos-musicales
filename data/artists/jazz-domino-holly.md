# artist - Jazz Domino Holly

## curiosities
**Founder of Shortage Sisters** : Jazz Domino Holly founded Shortage Sisters, a women's group. This group meets to discuss current issues, and then engages in domestic activities such as knitting, sewing, and embroidery. "She's the founder of Shortage Sisters. This is a woman's group." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
**Rebellion Through Domesticity** : Jazz Domino Holly described her interest in traditional female pastimes like knitting as a form of rebellion against her "rock and roll childhood." She learned to knit from her great-grandmother because her grandmother was a hippie and her mother was a punk, seeking to reclaim traditionally female pastimes. "Because I had such a rock and roll childhood, the only way for me to rebel was to go the other way." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
**Strumberville Trustee** : Jazz Domino Holly is one of the trustees of Strumberville, a charitable foundation established in Joe Strummer's name. The foundation aims to encourage the creation of new music and helps kids channel their energies into music and being in bands. "Jazz is a trustee too." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
