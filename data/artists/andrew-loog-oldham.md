# artist - Andrew Loog Oldham

## curiosities
**Rolling Stones Manager** : Andrew Loog Oldham was the manager of The Rolling Stones during their early career, releasing "The Rolling Stones Songbook" in 1966 which contained the orchestral arrangement of "The Last Time" later sampled by The Verve. He was later succeeded by Allan Klein. "Andrew Lou Geoldem, the manager of the Rolling Stones back in the day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
