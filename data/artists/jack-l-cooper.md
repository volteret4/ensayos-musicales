# artist - Jack L. Cooper

## venues

## curiosities
**The First Professional Radio DJ** : A former boxer and semi-pro baseball player, Cooper became the host of _The All-Negro Hour_ on November 3rd, 1929. He is cited as potentially the first professional radio DJ because he brought his own phonograph and collection of jazz and gospel records to play on the air. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
