## curiosities
**Contribution to New Wave** : The Talking Heads, with former Modern Lovers keyboardist Jerry Harrison, became a major component of the American New Wave era. This demonstrates the later success of musicians who emerged from the less-known scene. "And both the talking heads and the cars would eventually become major components of the American New Wave era." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4
**Early Sire Records Signing** : The Talking Heads were among the new artists signed by Seymour Stein to Sire Records, who loved the emerging post-punk music. Sire Records used the term "New Wave" to market these bands in America, intentionally avoiding the "punk" label which carried too much baggage in North America. "He signed the Talking Hands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
**Example of American New Wave (1979)** : Their music from 1979 is presented as a prime example of American New Wave, distinguishing it slightly from its British counterpart. "Talking Hits from 1979 and an example of American New Wave." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
