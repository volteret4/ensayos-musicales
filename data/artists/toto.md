## curiosities
**Collaboration on "Beat It"** : Three members of Toto contributed to Michael Jackson's song "Beat It," with Steve Lukather specifically playing the main guitar riff. "In fact, three members of Toto play on Michael Jackson's beat it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KtdIMNoHqA8
**High-Caliber Session Musicians** : The band was comprised of some of the best session players in the world; for instance, guitarist Steve Lukather played the guitar on Michael Jackson's "Beat It" (excluding Eddie Van Halen's solo), and Jeff Porcaro was the drummer. "Totos was made up of some of the best session players in the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Original Artists of Covered Songs** : Toto are the original artists of the songs "Africa" (1982) and "Rosanna," both of which were covered by Weezer, largely due to fan requests. "Weezer's version of Africa, the total hit from 1982." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
**Reaction to Weezer's "Africa" Cover (2018)** : The original band Toto was "absolutely thrilled" about Mary Klym's campaign and Weezer's subsequent cover of "Africa." Steve Lukather even sent Mary Klym a video message, with Ringo Starr humorously "cooking around" in the background. "And total, the original band behind the song, is absolutely thrilled about what Mary Clem of Cleveland did for the song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Reputation as a "Corporate Rock Band"** : Toto was known as a "corporate rock band," a term for projects intentionally designed to be mainstream and sell as many records as possible. This made them "highly uncool" to a segment of the rock audience, particularly the alt-rock crowd, who felt they were "not supposed to like Toto." "Toto was what we used to call a corporate rock band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Shared Tour with The Ramones (January 26, 1979)** : In a bizarre pairing reflecting major labels' misunderstanding of punk, The Ramones were chosen to open shows for Toto on a tour. A notable instance occurred on January 26, 1979, in Lake Charles, Louisiana, where the bill also included Foreigner and Blue Oyster Cult. "the Ramones were picked to open shows for Toto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XjTWcy8RHEs
