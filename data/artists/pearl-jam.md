## albums
**Debut Album Release (1991)** : Pearl Jam released their debut record on August 28, 1991, the same day the first Lollapalooza tour ended and the day before Nirvana's "Smells Like Teen Spirit" was released. This marked a pivotal moment that would completely change the music landscape a year later. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Lightning Bolt (2013)** : A 2013 studio album that features the track "Let the Records Play." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
**Official Bootlegs Series (2000) – Massive Live Release Project** : Beginning in the fall of 2000, Pearl Jam released a series of 72 live albums, intentionally branded as “Official Bootlegs.” These recordings were sold in simple, “illegal-looking” brown cardboard sleeves and collectively sold nearly 14 million copies. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OrHtPVBjXw4

## songs
**"Let the Records Play" (2013)** : A song from the album _Lightning Bolt_ featuring frontman Eddie Vedder, a "hardcore vinyl fan." The track is associated with the history and enduring legacy of vinyl records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
