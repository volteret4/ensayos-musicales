## curiosities
**Continuing Popularity in 21st Century** : remains popular decades after its emergence, making it harder for new artists to break through.
**Distance from Grunge Roots** : By 1994, the band had "distanced itself from their Grunge roots."
**Frequent Drummer Changes** : Pearl Jam has changed drummers so many times over the years that few people can remember all their names.
**Guest Appearance with Ramones (1996)** : Eddie Vedder joined the Ramones on stage to sing their last ever live song, "Anywhere You Wanted," at The Palace in Hollywood on August 6, 1996.
**Key Grunge Band** : contributed to Alt Rock coalescing around hard guitar rock with "riff-ready songs."
**Lollapalooza Festival Performance (1992)** : Pearl Jam was a main stage act at the 1992 Lollapalooza Festival, which featured an "awesome" energy and an arguably best-ever lineup.
**Ramones Influence (1990s)** : Along with Green Day and Nirvana, Pearl Jam members frequently spoke about the importance of the Ramones' influence on their music.
**Strong Bottom End** : A significant part of Pearl Jam's success is attributed to their "bottom end" (rhythm section).
**Support for L7** : Along with Nirvana, Rage Against The Machine, and Red Hot Chili Peppers, they became fans of L7 after playing pro-choice events and offered support to the all-female band.
