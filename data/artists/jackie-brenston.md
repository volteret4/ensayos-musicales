## songs
**Rocket 88 (Early 1950s)** : This song, featuring Ike Turner on vocals, was among the early rock and roll tracks emerging in the early 1950s. Its appearance was described as "a little frightening" and was dubbed "the devil's music" by some. It represented a blend of rhythm and blues (previously known as "race records") with hillbilly and country music, creating a "strange offspring" that scared "decent folks."
