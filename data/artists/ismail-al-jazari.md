**Programmable Drummer (1206)** : In 1206, al-Jazari unveiled a boat featuring four humanoid musicians that floated on a lake, utilizing hydraulics for entertainment at royal drinking parties. This innovative creation included a programmable drummer that used wooden pegs to set different patterns, rhythms, and movements, marking a significant early precursor to drum machines.
