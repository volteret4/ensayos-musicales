# artist - Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers

## songs
**Mary Jane's Last Dance (1993)** : This song, recorded in 1993, later garnered attention for its sonic similarities to the Red Hot Chili Peppers' "Dani California" (2006). Despite the perceived likeness, Tom Petty ultimately did not pursue legal action, though the idea was reportedly considered. ## curiosities
**Influence from Gram Parsons** : Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers are included among the "descendants" influenced by The Flying Burrito Brothers' seminal album "Gilded Palace of Sin," illustrating Gram Parsons' enduring legacy on subsequent rock acts. "And you can even throw Tom Petty the heartbreakers in there." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 35 - Cosmic American Music - 70s Country Rock | https://seventies.libsyn.com/ep-35-cosmic-american-music-70s-country-rock ← tom-petty-the-heartbreakers
**Sonic Similarities with Red Hot Chili Peppers** : Tom Petty's "Mary Jane's Last Dance" (1993) was noted for sounding very similar to the Red Hot Chili Peppers' "Dani California" (2006). Although people suggested Petty considered it, he never sued the Red Hot Chili Peppers over the resemblance.
