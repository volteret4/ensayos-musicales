## curiosities
**Conditional Permanent Return to Jefferson Starship** : After his initial departure, he was "slowly coaxed back" to contribute "Caroline" to *Dragonfly*. He agreed to rejoin the band on a "more permanent basis" only if they would record his song "Miracles."
