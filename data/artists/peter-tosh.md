## curiosities
**Inspiration for The Specials' Logo** : Peter Tosh, a Jamaican musician and former member of Bob Marley's Wailers, was the real-life individual whose photograph inspired the stylized Jamaican rude boy character, Walt Japsco, featured in The Specials' band logo. "Walt was actually based on a photograph of Jamaican musician Peter Tosh, who was a former member of Bob Arley's Whalers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
