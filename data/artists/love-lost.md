# artist - Love Lost

## curiosities
**Early Screamo Influence** : Love Lost was cited by Dallas Green as an influence on the early "screamo" sound that shaped Alexis on Fire's music. This was part of a specific subgenre of aggressive music that required active searching to discover, often through vinyl distros at local shows. "I was kind of more the deep head early first-screamo stuff like where I was listening to bands like Sasha, Love Lost, Penoffa, Godden, these are all different." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so ← love-lost
