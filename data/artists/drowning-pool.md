## songs
**Bodies (2001)** : This song was originally written about the brotherhood of the mosh pit. It was twisted into a weapon of war starting in 2003 when the US military at Guantanamo Bay, Cuba, decided to use it for "extra firepower." Muhammad Ud Salih, a prisoner from Mauritania held without charge for 13 years, was a famous victim, subjected to the song at high volume for 10 straight days in 2006 as part of "enhanced interrogation" and "musical tricks," which is recognized by international bodies as torture. "That song was written about the brotherhood of the Mosh pit, but it has been twisted into a weapon of war ever since 2003, when the US military at Guantanamo Bay in Cuba decided that they needed some extra firepower." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1wdsGt07Gg
**Bodies (Year not given)** : Joshua Cook listened to this song "over and over and over again" on February 17, 2003, including through headphones as he murdered his parents in Fairfax, Virginia. The same song was also the favorite of Jared Loughner, who created an unofficial YouTube video for it featuring a burning American flag before his 2011 shooting spree targeting Congresswoman Gabby Giffords. "On that day he was listening to bodies by drowning pool over and over and over again. What was he listening to on headphones as he murdered his parents? That song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FtGJHhqXcDI

## curiosities
**Dave Williams' Death on Tour (2002)** : On August 14, 2002, during Ozzfest, Drowning Pool's lead singer Dave Williams was found dead in his bunk on the tour bus, having died in his sleep. His death was attributed to cardiomyopathy, a disorder affecting the heart muscle often caused by viral infections or high blood pressure, thus determined to be from natural causes. The band later released a memorial DVD, with proceeds directed towards achieving Williams' dream of buying his parents a house. "He died of something called cardiomyopathy, a disorder that affects the heart muscle caused by things like viral infections or high blood pressure." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgN0vBIugLc
