## albums
**Beach House (2004) – Self-Titled Debut** : This self-titled debut album was recorded in just two days in guitarist Alex Scally's basement, utilizing a simple four-track machine. It features the song "Master of None," which was later sampled by The Weeknd. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY

## songs
**Master of None** : This song is from Beach House's 2004 self-titled debut album. It gained wider recognition when The Weeknd sampled a portion of it for his song "The Party and the After Party." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY

## curiosities
**Formation (2004)** : Beach House, a trio, was formed in Baltimore in 2004, contributing to the resurgence of Dreampop and the emergence of Chill Wave in the 21st century. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
