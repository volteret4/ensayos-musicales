## curiosities
**Enema of the State Album Cover Model** : Janine Lindemueller, who was a major porn star at the time, was featured on the cover of Blink-182's "Enema of the State" album. The band claimed to be unaware of her identity, merely selecting her photo from a stack to represent a "sexy nurse" motif for the album's title.
