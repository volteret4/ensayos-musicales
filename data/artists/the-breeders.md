# artist - The Breeders

## members
- Kelly Deal (Kim's twin sister)
- Kim Deal (bass player, songwriter)
- Tanya Donnelly (Throwing Muses, early member)

## albums
**Last Splash (1993)** : Released at the end of August 1993. This second album "blew up," selling more copies than all The Pixies' records combined up to that point. It was a major alt-rock hit in North America and hugely popular in the UK and especially France.
**Pod** : The band's first album. It was the result of a demo funded by The Pixies' UK record label, which provided $11,000. The band was put on hold after its release as members returned to their primary groups.

## songs
**Cannonball (1993)** : A "big single" from the album "Last Splash." It features a recording mistake where Kim Deal's bass was tuned flat at the beginning, high on the E string, before she corrected herself when the guitars entered, creating a "cool" effect that set up "false expectations."

## curiosities
**Kim Deal's Outlet** : Formed by Kim Deal because she was frustrated that the Pixies would not record a bunch of songs she had written.
**Music Video Co-production** : The music video for "Cannonball" (1993) was co-produced by Kim Gordon of Sonic Youth.
**Reactivation** : The band was reactivated after The Pixies broke up, allowing Kim Deal to write new songs and return to the studio for their second album.
