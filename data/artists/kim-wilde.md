## concerts
- **Highest Airplane Concert (2017)** : Kim Wilde, along with Tony Hadley and others, performed a charity gig aboard a chartered British Airway 767. This event established a new record for the highest airplane concert, with the plane reaching an altitude of 43,000 feet. "And the highest airplane concert record belongs to Tony Hadley, he of Spandobele, and 80 star Kim Wilde, among others, who performed a charity gig aboard a chartered British Airway 767, which reached 43,000 feet."

## curiosities
**Charity Gig at 43,000 Feet (2013)** : On March 10, 2013, Kim Wilde, along with Tony Hadley of Spandau Ballet, performed a charity gig aboard a British Airways 767 at an altitude of 43,000 feet. This event pushed the boundary of high-altitude concerts, reaching near the operational ceiling of current passenger jets. **Highest Charity Gig (2013)** : On March 10, 2013, Kim Wilde, alongside Tony Hadley, performed a charity gig aboard a British Airways Boeing 767.
