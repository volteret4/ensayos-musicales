## curiosities
**1990s Multi-Platinum Album Success** : Blue Rodeo, which started as an "old country band," saw all of its albums released in the 1990s achieve multi-platinum sales. "All their 90s albums went multi-platinum." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2kYleeMnl8 ← blue-rodeo ← blue-rodeo
**Contribution to Al Purdy Tribute** : Bruce Cobra and Greg Keelor from Blue Rodeo were among the artists who contributed to the soundtrack of the 2015 documentary on Al Purdy, which featured songs and poems inspired by the poet. "Bruce Cobra and Greg Kieler of Blue Rodeo, Sarah Harmer, Jason Collette of Broken Social Scene, even Margaret Atwood appeared on that album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnz_Ycd_q7s ← blue-rodeo
**Greg Keelor's Wise Advice on Music Career Options** : While playing a show in Regina, 5440's Neil Osborne had a conversation with Greg Keelor of Blue Rodeo in the dressing room. When Neil confessed he couldn't imagine doing anything else if he stopped being a musician, Keelor offered a poignant piece of advice, "nice to know your options are behind you," highlighting the commitment and lack of alternative paths for long-term musicians. "Keylor came over to check out our set... I said to him... I don't think I could do anything else like if I had to stop this Being a musician thing. I don't think I could do anything else and he goes nice to know your options are behind you." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W9gdKha0PLg ← blue-rodeo
**Late 1980s Success** : Blue Rodeo was among the new acts signed throughout the late 1980s who achieved hits and solid-selling albums. This success was incentivized by the serious prospect of radio airplay, coverage by MuchMusic, and funding assistance, reflecting a growing investment in Canadian talent by the recorded music industry. "Throughout the late 1980s, new acts were signed and had hits in solid-selling albums, Blue Rodeo, The Box, Pursuit of Happiness, Northern Pikes, National Velvet, Alana Miles, 5440, Cowboy Junkeys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo ← blue-rodeo
**Recording at The Bath House** : Blue Rodeo recorded at The Bath House, The Tragically Hip's residential studio located west of Kingston, Ontario. "Blue rodeo, Ronnie Hawkins, Sam Roberts, Big Rec, and dozens and dozens of others have worked there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk ← blue-rodeo ← blue-rodeo
**Vineyard Arrangements** : The members of Blue Rodeo have established arrangements with vineyards. "All have arrangements with vineyards." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk ← blue-rodeo
