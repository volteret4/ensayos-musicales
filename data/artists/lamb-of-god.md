**Manslaughter Charge After Fan Death (2010)** : In 2010, during a show in Prague, a 19-year-old fan named Daniel Nosek climbed onto the stage. Singer Randy Blythe pushed him back into the audience, a common action for stage trespassers. However, Nosek fell hard, was not caught by the crowd, and hit his head, later dying from his injuries. "At one point, someone from the audience climbed on stage. Singer Randy Blithe did what he'd done a hundred times in the past to stage trespassers. **Manslaughter Charges for Singer Randy Blythe (2010)** : In 2010, Lamb of God's singer, Randy Blythe, was charged with manslaughter in Prague after a fan who crowd-surfed onto the stage was pushed back by Blythe. The crowd reportedly parted, causing the fan to fall and hit his head on the concrete floor, leading to his death. This resulted in a lengthy court case against Blythe.
