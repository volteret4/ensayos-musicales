## songs
**Initial Composition of "Rock the Casbah" (Early 1980s)** : Drummer Topper Headon was alone in the studio when he developed the initial idea for "Rock the Casbah." He recorded most of the instrumental parts, including piano, drums, and bass, and even wrote some preliminary lyrics, though these were later replaced by Joe Strummer. "He decided to work on an idea that he had in his head. He recorded most of the parts, piano, drums, bass, himself, and he also wrote some lyrics." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU
