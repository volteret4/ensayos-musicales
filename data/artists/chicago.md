**Dialogue (1972)** : Featured on *Chicago 5*, this song presents a lyrical dialogue between Peter Cetera, portraying an apathetic college student, and Terry Kath, embodying an activist. The exchange touches on themes of optimism, societal worries, and the practical value of a Bachelor of Arts degree. **Saturday in the Park (1972)** : This song reached number three on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1972. **Distinction from Blood, Sweat & Tears** : Chicago has consistently maintained a clear distinction between their sound and that of Blood, Sweat & Tears, asserting themselves as a "rock band with horns" rather than a jazz-rock band.
