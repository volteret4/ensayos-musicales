# artist - George Coy

## instruments
- Quarter-inch Phone Plug

## curiosities
**Central Telephone Exchange Invention** : Coy solved the problem of early telephone connectivity by proposing a central exchange where operators manually connected calls using physical plugs and wires. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Quarter-inch Phone Plug (1878)** : Coy developed a durable, quick-connect plug (patented in 1902) that became the universal standard for connecting guitars, microphones, and headphones. He reportedly experimented with women's bra wires during the design process. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
