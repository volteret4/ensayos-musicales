## curiosities
**New Wave Powerpop with Punk Influence** : Joe Jackson is included among the artists who integrated punk elements into the new wave interpretation of Powerpop. His work is representative of the era when Powerpop began its integration into alternative rock, marked by a post-punk sensibility. "Elvis Costello and Joe Jackson. They brought a little punk into the mix." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
**Times Square Soundtrack Contribution (1980)** : Joe Jackson was featured on the soundtrack for the 1980 movie *Time Square*. This soundtrack was notable for being one of the first to highlight the developing alternative music of the era, encompassing punk rock and new wave. "The soundtrack featured the pretenders, Roxy music, Gary Newman, XTC, Joe Jackson." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
