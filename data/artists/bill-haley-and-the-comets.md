# artist - Bill Haley and the Comets

## songs
**Rock Around the Clock (1955)** : This song was used in the opening titles of the 1955 MGM film *Blackboard Jungle*, marking the first time a rock and roll song was ever featured in a Hollywood feature film. The movie was intended to warn against juvenile delinquency, but the song's inclusion famously led to kids dancing in theaters, sometimes resulting in violence and vandalism. The film was subsequently banned in many cities and throughout the UK.
