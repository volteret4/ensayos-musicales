**Rock Around the Clock (1955)** : This song was used in the opening titles of the 1955 MGM film *Blackboard Jungle*, marking the first time a rock and roll song was ever featured in a Hollywood feature film. The movie was intended to warn against juvenile delinquency, but the song's inclusion famously led to kids dancing in theaters, sometimes resulting in violence and vandalism. The film was subsequently banned in many cities and throughout the UK. ## curiosities
**First American Rock & Roll Group to Tour UK (1957)** : Bill Haley and the Comets made history in 1957 as the first American rock and roll group to tour the UK. This tour was made possible by the "one-in, one-out" union exchange agreement, which allowed Lonnie Donegan to tour the US simultaneously, this time with permission to play his guitar.
