## curiosities
**Discovery of The Smiths (1983)** : John Peel, an influential DJ for Radio One and considered a "great patron" of pop music for 40 years, was completely amazed upon first seeing The Smiths perform live at one of their early concerts at London University in 1983. He noted their unique sound, remarking that he couldn't discern their musical influences. **Launch of The Smiths' Career** : Just a few weeks after witnessing their concert, John Peel invited The Smiths to record a Peel Session for Radio One. This opportunity was pivotal, as it completely launched and propelled The Smiths' career forward.
