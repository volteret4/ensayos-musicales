**Depeche Mode "Give It Away" Remix with Stoned Chicken** : Al Jourgensen was commissioned to create a new mix of the Red Hot Chili Peppers' hit song "Give It Away" from their album "Blood Sugar Sex Magik," working with John Frusciante in the studio. **Kubrick's "AI" Film Project and Jourgensen's Antics** : Al Jourgensen was reportedly approached by director Stanley Kubrick to compose music for a new film titled "AI." **Life-Threatening Health Crises** : Al Jourgensen experienced a near-fatal overdose on New Year's Eve 1992, during which he was clinically dead for a period. He contracted Hepatitis C from a contaminated needle and suffered a toe infection from excessive injecting, which led to gangrene and required amputation. He also almost lost an arm after being bitten by a spider. In addition to heroin and cocaine, he extensively used crack, ketamine, and meth. His ultimate decision to stop drug use came after his gastrointestinal tract exploded, resulting in the loss of 65% of his blood and another clinical death experience. "Al ended up with Hepp C from a contaminated needle. After injecting a toe so much, it turned gangrous and he had to have it amputated." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lzwQggT0qR0
