# artist - Ministry

## members
- Al Jourgensen

## venues
- Molson Park

## curiosities
**Al Jourgensen's Backstage Antics (1992)** : Backstage at Lollapalooza 1992, Ministry leader Al Jourgensen was observed throwing furniture (table, lamp, couch, chair, carpet) out of his trailer and later drinking from a broken bottle of Bushmills whiskey, then enlisting assistance to "set up his living room" outside.
**Continued Presence in Alt Rock Post-Grunge** : were among the acts still present in Alt Rock playlists after grunge became dominant.
**Lollapalooza Festival Performance (1992)** : Ministry was a main stage act at the 1992 Lollapalooza Festival, including the Canadian stop at Molson Park in Barry, north of Toronto.
**Mosh Pit Observation (1992)** : The mosh pit during Ministry's set at Lollapalooza 1992 was described as "what hell must look like from above."
