# artist - Casey Harris

## members
- Sam (band singer)

## instruments
- **Custom Keyboard Setup** : Due to being legally blind, Casey Harris utilizes a specialized keyboard setup that features larger knobs and controllers than standard equipment, as he is unable to see the smaller, typical components. "He has a set up for playing in the band, which involves larger knobs and controllers than normal. Otherwise, you just can't see them."

## curiosities
**Exceptional Pitch Sense** : To compensate for his visual impairment, Casey developed his other senses, notably an extremely good sense of pitch. This skill was so pronounced that he worked as a piano tuner before X Ambassadors gained prominence. "Before ex-embacitors took off, he worked as a piano tuner because he has an extremely good sense of pitch." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trMzjWWmbZo
**Kidney Transplant** : In 2010, Casey required a kidney transplant, for which his mother served as the donor. "He also needed a kidney transplant in 2010. His mum was the donor." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trMzjWWmbZo
**Senior Loken Syndrome** : Casey Harris suffers from Senior Loken Syndrome, a rare genetic condition that impacts both the retina and kidneys. He has been visually impaired since birth and is legally blind, though he can make out print if it is exceptionally large. "Casey suffers from a rare genetic condition called Senior Loken Syndrome, which affects both the retina and kidneys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trMzjWWmbZo
