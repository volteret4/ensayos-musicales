# artist - Frank Wilson

## labels
- Motown

## songs
**Do I Love You Indeed I Do? (1965)** : The only track Frank Wilson recorded for Motown, for which about 250 demo copies were pressed. The majority were destroyed because Motown head Berry Gordy didn't think the demo was good, leaving only an estimated two to five copies in existence. Ten years later, around 1975, the song became a hit with Northern Soul fans in England, who prized rare American R&B. In 2009, one of these super rare 7-inch singles sold for 25,742 pounds, equivalent to $31,637 American or $43,197 Canadian. "In 1965, Frank Wilson recorded a song called, Do I Love You Indeed I Do?
