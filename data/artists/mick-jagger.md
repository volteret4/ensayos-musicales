**Relationship with David Bowie** : In the early 1970s, Mick Jagger and David Bowie, both known for sexual experimentation, became close friends after a 1973 Bowie concert. They were frequently seen together in public, including clubs, concerts, and even at a Muhammad Ali fight, with one biographer crediting Bowie for introducing Jagger to gay culture. Bowie's backup singer, Aiba Cherry, claimed the two were "sexually obsessed with each other" and she witnessed them having sex, with associates confirming their close relationship during which they "practically lived together for several months." David's ex-wife, Angela Bowie, claimed on the Joan Rivers show in 1990 that she caught them naked and asleep in bed together. "Mick and Dave were really sexually obsessed with each other. Even though I was in bed with them many times, I ended up just watching them have sex. They became very close and practically lived together for several months." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ
**Rumored Affair with David Bowie (1990)** : A rumor concerning an affair between Mick Jagger and David Bowie was ignited on the Joan Rivers show in 1990 by David's ex-wife, Angela Bowie. Jagger strongly denied the accusation, and David Bowie's lawyer issued a statement calling the implication "an absolute fabrication." The situation was further complicated by rumors that Angela Bowie and Mick Jagger might have had an affair while she was married to Bowie, leading some to believe the Rolling Stones song "Angie" was about her, though others suggested it was coded for David. "Jagger came up swinging, denying everything." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DA2iJsu3GRY
