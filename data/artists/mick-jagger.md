**Relationship with David Bowie** : In the early 1970s, Mick Jagger and David Bowie, both known for sexual experimentation, became close friends after a 1973 Bowie concert. David's ex-wife, Angela Bowie, claimed on the Joan Rivers show in 1990 that she caught them naked and asleep in bed together. "Mick and Dave were really sexually obsessed with each other. **Rumored Affair with David Bowie (1990)** : A rumor concerning an affair between Mick Jagger and David Bowie was ignited on the Joan Rivers show in 1990 by David's ex-wife, Angela Bowie. Jagger strongly denied the accusation, and David Bowie's lawyer issued a statement calling the implication "an absolute fabrication." The situation was further complicated by rumors that Angela Bowie and Mick Jagger might have had an affair while she was married to Bowie, leading some to believe the Rolling Stones song "Angie" was about her, though others suggested it was coded for David.
