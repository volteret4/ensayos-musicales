## curiosities
**Detroit Techno Pioneer** : Kevin Saunderson was one of three friends who were central to the small Detroit scene that fused various electronic and dance music elements into a new and highly influential style: techno. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Musical Mission** : Along with Juan Atkins and Derrick May, his mission was to create funky dance music using cheap synthesizers. They were fascinated by early synthesizer pioneers, avant-garde electronic composers, Kraftwerk, David Bowie, the English techno pop scene, Afrika Bambaataa, Motown, Disco, and general dance music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
