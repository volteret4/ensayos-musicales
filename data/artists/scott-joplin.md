**Maple Leaf Rag (1899)** : This ragtime composition, written by African American pianist Scott Joplin in 1899, played a pivotal role in Western music's "Great Metric Shift" from 3/4 to 4/4 time. Its highly rhythmic, syncopated, and choppy style, often in 2/4 time, was considered radical for its era, contrasting with the prevailing waltz-time music of the Victorian age.
