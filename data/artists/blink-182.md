**Travis Barker's Clothing Company – Famous Stars and Straps** : Travis Barker, drummer for blink-182, founded the clothing company Famous Stars and Straps in 1999, based in Corona, California. The brand targets the skate crowd and offers t-shirts, jackets, and hats for men, along with shirts, pants, and bikinis for women, in addition to accessories like belt buckles and bandanas. The company's success is bolstered by famous friends, including Tim Armstrong of Rancid, Steve-O of Sum 41, members of Avenged Sevenfold, and B-Real of Cypress Hill, who wear the brand publicly. Famous Stars and Straps generates millions in annual revenue, and its presence is notable at events like the Warped Tour.
