## albums
**Dude Ranch** : The band's mainstream breakthrough album; displayed on the road during the Warped Tour.
**Enema of the State (1999)** : Sold at least 15 million copies worldwide.
**Neighborhoods (2011)** : Released almost three years to the day after drummer Travis Barker's severe plane crash, signifying a reunion of the band.
**Take Off Your Pants and Jacket (2001)** : An album released in 2001; sold another 5 million copies.
