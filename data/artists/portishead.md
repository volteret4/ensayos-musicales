## curiosities
**Beth Gibbons' Enigmatic Persona** : Beth Gibbons was one of the most enigmatic frontwomen of the decade, giving "zero interviews" and possessing a "fragile" singing voice. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Bristol Origins** : From Bristol, about 12 kilometers away from a town also named Portishead. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Trip Hop Innovators** : Helped define the sound known as Trip Hop in 1994. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**UK Dominance** : Helped turn Trip Hop into one of the "most dominant sounds in the UK in the middle 90s," even competing with Britpop bands like Blur and Oasis. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
