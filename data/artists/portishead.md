**Origin and Defining Trip Hop** : Hailing from Bristol, UK, Portishead helped to define the "Trip Hop" sound in 1994. This genre is characterized as a form of down-tempo music subtly integrating elements of electronic and hip hop, along with dashes of funk, soul, jazz, samples, and various sonic experimentation. "In 1994 they helped define a sound that became known as Trip Hop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI ← portishead
**Pillar of Trip Hop** : Portishead is considered one of the three great pillars of Trip Hop, alongside Massive Attack and Tricky. These three entities converged in the same studio during the *Blue Lines* recording, marking the simultaneous beginning of the Trip Hop movement. "como los tres grandes pilares del trip hop, que fueron la desmasiva atac por el otro lado Strixi y por el otro lado Sportishead, coincidieron y empezaron a gestar lo que sería el movimiento trip hop dentro del mismo estudio al mismo tiempo." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack y El Origen del Trip Hop en Bristol. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bpOr4KKm5rM&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← portishead
