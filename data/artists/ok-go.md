# artist - OK Go

## songs
**Upside Down and Inside Out (2014)** : This song is central to OK Go's groundbreaking music video, a collaboration with Russian airline S7. The video, filmed aboard a plane performing extreme parabolic trajectories, is notable for being the first-ever Zero-G music video created entirely within Earth's atmosphere, producing momentary periods of weightlessness. "Ok go teamed with a Russian airline called S7 to create the first ever Zero-G Music video for a song called Upside Down and Inside Out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo

## curiosities
**First Zero-G Music Video (2014)** : OK Go partnered with Russian airline S7 to produce the first-ever Zero-G music video for their song "Upside Down and Inside Out." The video was filmed by flying a plane through a series of extreme parabolic trajectories, creating momentary periods of zero gravity. While Commander Chris Hadfield filmed a Zero-G video in space in 2013, OK Go's video is recognized for being filmed within the Earth's atmosphere. "Ok go teamed with a Russian airline called S7 to create the first ever Zero-G Music video for a song called Upside Down and Inside Out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
