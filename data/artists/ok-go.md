# artist - OK Go

## songs
**Upside Down and Inside Out** : The song for which OK Go created the first Zero G music video within the Earth's atmosphere, in collaboration with S7 airline.
**Upside Down and Inside Out (Year not specified)** : The song for which the band created the first-ever Zero G music video.

## curiosities
**Atmospheric Distinction** : The band's Zero G video is specifically noted for being filmed within Earth's atmosphere, distinguishing it from Commander Chris Hadfield's 2013 Zero G video on the International Space Station.
**First Zero G Music Video (Atmospheric)** : OK Go teamed with Russian airline S7 to create the first-ever Zero G music video for their song "Upside Down and Inside Out." The video was filmed by flying a plane in a series of extreme parabolic trajectories that produced momentary periods of zero gravity within the Earth's atmosphere. The band received points for this distinction, differentiating it from Commander Chris Hadfield's 2013 Zero G video on the International Space Station.
**First Zero G Music Video (Year not specified)** : Collaborated with Russian airline S7 to create the first music video filmed entirely in Zero G, achieved by flying a plane through a series of extreme parabolic trajectories that produced momentary periods of weightlessness.
