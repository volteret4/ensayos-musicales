# artist - Kenny Loggins

## songs
**Danger Zone** : From the "Top Gun Soundtrack," this song was played by the US Army during Operation Just Cause in December 1989. It was part of a tactic to bombard the Vatican Embassy in Panama City with loud music to make Manuel Noriega surrender. "Too much never going to give you up from Rick Astley, Kenny Loggins and Danger Zone from the Top Gun Soundtrack." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1wdsGt07Gg
**Take My Breath Away** : This song is mentioned as the primary reason "Frank" watched the movie "Top Gun" over 2,000 times. While "Take My Breath Away" was actually performed by Berlin, the transcript associates it with Kenny Loggins due to fan's confusion or broad association with the movie's soundtrack. "I've also watched Top Gun more than 2,000 times just to hear that take my breath away song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vev8lzfW6Xo
