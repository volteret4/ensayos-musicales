## albums
**4:44 (2017) - Experimental Evolution** : Released recently, this album is described as an "incredible experimental hip hop" record that completely deviates from Jay-Z's initial sound. It exemplifies his evolution to align with the sounds and inspirations pioneered by Kanye West, marking a new golden age for the artist. "J.S.T hace poco ha sacado el 444 que es un increíble disco de hip hop casi experimental que se aleja totalmente de lo que habíamos conocido en sus inicios del propio J.S.E.T." ← Music Radar Clan > Poniendo a Kanye West en su lugar | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ih5F70jcm6M&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc

## curiosities
**"Old School" Classification with Evolution** : Jay-Z is still considered part of the "old school" of hip hop. However, his later works like "4:44" demonstrate a significant evolution, moving away from his initial style and embracing more experimental sounds influenced by Kanye West. "Incluso hoy en día artistas que tenemos en activo como J.S.E.T. como 50 Cent, los seguimos llamando lo old school, la vieja escuela." ← Music Radar Clan > Poniendo a Kanye West en su lugar | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ih5F70jcm6M&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**AI Voice Cloning for Hamlet Soliloquy (2022)** : In the summer of 2022, the text-to-speech program TacoTron generated a rap track that utilized a sampled and cloned version of Jay-Z's voice to rap the "to be or not to be" soliloquy from Hamlet, which was then posted on YouTube. This incident was one of the first public examples of a "deep fake" of a well-known musician, and Jay-Z's representatives expressed their displeasure. "Jay-Z's people were not happy at that little bit of hamlet but there was no going back." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The History and Future of AI in Music | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QFtS8fl553Y&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Early Collaboration with Kanye West** : Kanye West worked on Jay-Z's early albums as a producer, and later played a pivotal role in his career resurgence with "The Blueprint." "Había trabajado en los primeros discos de GAYSET." ← Music Radar Clan > Poniendo a Kanye West en su lugar | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ih5F70jcm6M&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Evolution Towards Kanye West's Sound** : Jay-Z, originally a figure of the "classic gangster rap culture," eventually evolved his musical style to embrace the sound and inspiration of Kanye West. This adaptation led to a "new golden age" for Jay-Z, distancing him from the movement that initially brought him fame. "Jay-Selt acabaría evolucionando para acercarse al sonido y en la inspiración de Kenny West y hoy en día J.S.E.T. está viviendo una nueva época dorada." ← Music Radar Clan > Poniendo a Kanye West en su lugar | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ih5F70jcm6M&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc

"Negativas a los propios artistas para los que trabajaba como en un principio el mismo J.S.E." ← Music Radar Clan > Poniendo a Kanye West en su lugar | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ih5F70jcm6M&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Maybach Exelero Custom Car Purchase** : Jay-Z made an extravagant purchase, spending eight million dollars on a single, custom-made car: a Maybach Exelero. This particular model is noted for its rarity, being unfamiliar even to car enthusiasts. "Jay Z spent eight million dollars on a single car. It's a custom made Maybach exolero." ← Ongoing History of New Music > 60 Mind-Blowing Facts in 60 Minutes： 4th Edition | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO0K_N7Ys7I&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Ongoing Battle with Spotify** : Jay-Z maintains an active dispute with Spotify, expressing anger towards the platform. Consequently, his full discography is not available, with only shared rights content like remixes or compilation appearances present. "Jset sigue ahí completamente enfadado con Spotify y no tenemos nada de Jset dentro de Spotify que no sean derechos compartidos." ← Music Radar Clan > Artistas que no están en Spotify y por qué | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RQYV60lqU8Y&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Planned Woodstock 50 Performance** : Jay-Z was included in the extensive lineup of 80 artists initially committed to perform at the Woodstock 50 festival in August 2019. His planned participation highlighted the festival's ambition to draw a broad audience with a diverse array of musical acts.
