## curiosities
**AI Voice Cloning for Hamlet Soliloquy (2022)** : In the summer of 2022, the text-to-speech program TacoTron generated a rap track that utilized a sampled and cloned version of Jay-Z's voice to rap the "to be or not to be" soliloquy from Hamlet, which was then posted on YouTube. This incident was one of the first public examples of a "deep fake" of a well-known musician, and Jay-Z's representatives expressed their displeasure.
