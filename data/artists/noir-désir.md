## curiosities
**Aftermath of Release** : Following Cantat's release from prison, his house was burned down by Marie's fans. Later, his wife died by suicide, adding to the tragic story surrounding him. "Meanwhile, Marie's fans burned down his house." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Conviction and Parole** : Cantat was arrested, charged, and sentenced to eight years in jail for the death of Marie Trintignant. He was paroled in 2007 after serving four years of his sentence. "Contat was arrested, charged, and sentenced to eight years in jail, but was paroled in 2007 after four years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Homicide of Marie Trintignant (2003)** : In 2003, during an affair in Vilnius, Lithuania, frontman Bertrand Cantat got into a fight with French actress Marie Trintignant over a text message from her husband. Cantat admitted to slapping her four times before putting her to bed, but she was dying. She died a few days later after emergency surgery in France; a post-mortem revealed 19 blows to the head, likely from large rings Cantat was wearing, akin to brass knuckles. "Seven hours later, Marie was in the emergency ward, Contat admitted to slapping Marie four times before he put her to bed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
