## curiosities
**Pioneering Instrument Smashing** : In 1956, Rockin' Rocky Rockwell performed a "not-so-respectful" version of Elvis Presley's "Hound Dog" on "The Lawrence Welk Show." Upon finishing, he smashed his acoustic guitar to pieces, an act interpreted as commentary on the value of rock and roll music at the time and marking an early instance of instrument destruction. "That's when a guy named Rockin' Rocky Rockwell performed a not-so-respectful version of Elvis Presley's Hound Dog.
