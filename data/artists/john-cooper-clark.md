# artist - John Cooper Clark

## genres
- Punk Poet

## curiosities
**Distinctive Lyrical Style** : John Cooper Clark's unique and somewhat unconventional lyrical style is referenced as part of a Manchester tradition of distinctive writing, similar to that of Mark E. Smith of The Fall and Sean Ryder of Happy Mondays. "You listen to Marquis Smith in the fall, or you listen to John Cooper Clark, and you know, they're very kind of, you know... Serim Boral." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZStbEe4Gkk
**Major Influence on Alex Turner** : John Cooper Clark is a Manchester punk poet whose lyrics were read aloud in Alex Turner's English class. This exposure became a "major, major influence" on a young Alex Turner, leading him to realize that songs could be written in such a unique style. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YvcnHKI4oIw
