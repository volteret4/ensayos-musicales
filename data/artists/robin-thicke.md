# artist - Robin Thicke

## songs
**Blurred Lines (2013)** : This song became the center of an infamous copyright trial because it was felt to mimic the "vibe" of Marvin Gaye’s 1977 song "Got to Give It Up". A jury eventually awarded $7.2 million to the Gaye estate based on a "note by note" comparison of the sheet music rather than the recording itself, relying heavily on music theory arguments. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
