**Blurred Lines (2013)** : This song was the subject of a high-profile "infamous blurred lines trial" due to its perceived similarity to Marvin Gaye's 1977 song "Got to Give It Up." After a court case comparing both songs "note by note," a jury found "Blurred Lines" infringed on "Got to Give It Up" and awarded $7.2 million to the plaintiffs.
