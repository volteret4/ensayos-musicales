## genres

## songs
**Telemusik (1966)** : This 1966 creation by Stockhausen was considered "wild stuff" at the time, featuring sounds that were far beyond contemporary musical thought, showcasing the experimental edge that would later inspire Krautrock. "Now, that's wild stuff today, but in 1966, these were sounds beyond what we were thinking." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw

## curiosities
**Father of Electronic Music** : Stockhausen is recognized as a highly influential German composer and one of the most important experimental musicians of the 20th century, especially for his pioneering work in electronic music. His radical approach in the 1960s was a significant inspiration for young German musicians. "In fact, he has been called the father of electronic music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
