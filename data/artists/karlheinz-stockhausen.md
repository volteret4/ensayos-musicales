## curiosities
**Father of Electronic Music** : Stockhausen is recognized as a highly influential German composer and one of the most important experimental musicians of the 20th century, especially for his pioneering work in electronic music. His radical approach in the 1960s was a significant inspiration for young German musicians. "In fact, he has been called the father of electronic music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw ← karlheinz-stockhausen ← karlheinz-stockhausen
**Influence on Can's Atonal Spirit** : Can's music, particularly evidenced in tracks like "Aumgn" from "Tago Mago," demonstrated a return to the atonal spirit that was characteristic of Stockhausen's avant-garde compositions.
