## curiosities
**Hospitality Towards Interviewers (Mid-2000s)** : Around 15 years prior (circa 2008), John Paul Jones of Led Zeppelin offered to make tea for an interviewer while they were preparing for an interview. This act of hospitality highlights a pattern among "bigger" artists. "About 15 years ago, John Paul Jones of Led Zeppelin offered to make me tea while I was getting ready for an interview." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bI6TTA-fn7c
**Producer Role for Butthole Surfers** : John Paul Jones, widely recognized as the bass player for Led Zeppelin, also took on a producing role for the Butthole Surfers. He produced their 1993 track, "The Annoying Song," from the album "Independent Worms Saloon." "By the way, that song was produced by John Paul Jones, the bass player for Let's Set Plane." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Zniri9uSlc
