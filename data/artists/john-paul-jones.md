# artist - John Paul Jones

## curiosities
**Producer Role for Butthole Surfers** : John Paul Jones, widely recognized as the bass player for Led Zeppelin, also took on a producing role for the Butthole Surfers. He produced their 1993 track, "The Annoying Song," from the album "Independent Worms Saloon."
