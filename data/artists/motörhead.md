## curiosities
**Adoption of the "Rock Dot" Umlaut** : Motörhead famously adopted the umlaut (or "rock dot") in their name, contributing to the association of these diacritics with metal bands, primarily for their cool, foreign, or menacing aesthetic. "There's Motley Crew, Queen's Reich, Spinal Tap, Green Jelly, Motorhead, Maximal Park, Moxie Frouze, and tons of others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE ← motörhead
**Axis Memorabilia Collection** : During his lifetime, Lemmy Kilmister was known for his collection of memorabilia from Axis countries of World War II, which included items from Germany, Hungary, and Italy. "he collected material from all the Axis countries of World War II. So Germany, yes, but also Hungary, Italy, and so on." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk ← motörhead

**Extreme Volume Causes Structural Damage (1986)** : In 1986, Motörhead reportedly achieved a volume of 130 decibels during a gig, a level so intense that it "caused structural damage to the variety, theater and cleveland." This event further pushed the boundaries of live concert volume. "Later it was claimed that Motorhead hit 130 decibels in 1986." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ ← motörhead
**Fan Injury from Headbanging** : A 50-year-old German man developed a subdural hematoma after enthusiastically headbanging at a Motörhead show a month prior to his symptoms. Doctors performed surgery to remove the blood clot, and he recovered, with headbanging identified as the likely cause. "He'd been to a motor head show a month earlier and had been enthusiastically banging his head." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bferkEWvJg0 ← motörhead
**Major Influence on UK Hardcore** : Motörhead was identified as the biggest influence from the New Wave of British Heavy Metal scene on UK Hardcore, which itself was an amalgamation of British punk and NWOBHM. "The biggest influence from that scene was Motorhead." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U ← motörhead
**Managed by Sharon Osborne** : Motörhead were among the clients managed by Sharon Osborne, daughter of the old-school manager Don Arden. Osborne's management career encompassed a range of heavy rock and metal acts. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc ← motörhead

**Origin of "Headbanging" Term** : According to Lemmy, the term "headbanging" was derived from the name of his band, suggesting a direct link such as "Motörhead banging." The band's fast songs, some reaching 200 beats per minute, put extreme strain on the neck.
