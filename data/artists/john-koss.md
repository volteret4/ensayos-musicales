## curiosities
**JC Koss Television Rental Company** : Koss’s initial success came from renting televisions to patients in hospitals. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Koss SP3s (1958)** : Recognizing a market for private listening, Koss introduced these as the first standalone consumer-grade headphones. They became an immediate sensation and sparked the widespread public adoption of headphones for music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Portable Record Player Privacy Switch** : In the 1950s, Koss developed one of the first truly portable record players. It featured a "privacy switch" that allowed users to route audio to headphones instead of the internal speaker. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
