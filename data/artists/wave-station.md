## members
- Two brothers (based out of Toronto, Canada)

## albums

## songs
**Wave Station (1991)** : This song was first released as a demo in 1991 and later re-released on the album "Mona Lisa" in 1994. It was the subject of a massive online Lost Wave search for years. "They first released the song in 1991, which is the version I played on the radio that day, and then re-released the song in 1994 on an album entitled Mona Lisa." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← wave-station

## curiosities
**Band Member's Surprise at Search** : One of the brothers from Wave Station was reportedly very surprised to learn that their old song had become the subject of a massive online search as a Lost Wave track. "No one is more surprised than him that this old song has been the subject of a massive online Lost Wave search." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← wave-station
**Brief Prominence and Disappearance** : Wave Station experienced a period of being "hot for a minute" after their 1991 song release, but then disappeared from the public eye for years, leading to their track becoming a "Lost Wave" mystery. "Wave Station was hot for a minute and then disappeared for years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← wave-station
