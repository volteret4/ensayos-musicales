## genres

## songs
**See The U.S.A. in Your Chevrolet (1950s-1960s)** : This famous car commercial jingle was sung by Dina Shore and was intrinsically linked to her "Dina Shores Chevy Show," as Chevrolet was the sponsor. The jingle became one of the most recognized car commercial jingles of all time. **First Prime Time Variety Show (1956-1963)** : Shore hosted her first prime-time variety show, "The Dina Shores Chevy Show," from 1956 to 1963, which famously featured her singing one of the most well-known car commercial jingles of all time. "And she had her first variety show during prime time all the way back in 1956. And it was on until 1963. **Guest Comfort and Wide Range of Acts** : Dina Shore was known for her ability to put guests at ease, making her show a comfortable environment for celebrities who knew she wouldn't put them "on the hot seat," unlike hosts such as Dick Cavett. This reputation allowed her to attract a wide range of guests, from Rosemary Clooney to David Bowie and Iggy Pop, and also contemporary acts like Wild Cherry and ABBA, helping to introduce these newer artists to a more mature audience. "She literally had rosemary cluny on the same episode that she had David Bowie and Iggy pop." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 56 - Talk Shows of the 1970s | https://www.ftr70.com/
