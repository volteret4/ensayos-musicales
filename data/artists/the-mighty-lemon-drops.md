## albums
**C86 Compilation (1986)** : The band's appearance on the NME's *C86* cassette in 1986 with the track "Happy Head" marked a pivotal beginning for The Mighty Lemon Drops. Several years later, the West Midlands group released an album on Sire Records, a New York-based label led by Seymour Stein, and went on to secure a couple of alt rock hits in North America.
