**Innovative Sound Processing Techniques** : Burial employs a sophisticated and unconventional approach to sound manipulation. **Revolutionary Rhythmic Disruption** : Burial is celebrated for pioneering the "ruptura del ritmo" (rupture of rhythm), defining himself as a "great rhythmic artist." Unlike conventional electronic music that relies on synchronized loops and samples, Burial intentionally desynchronizes his sounds and uses inexact loops, creating a complex, characteristic rhythm that features subtle spatial gaps and timing dislocations. "burial introduce la gran revolución de la ruptura del ritmo burial muchas veces se define como la rítmica como el gran artista rítmico." ← Music Radar Clan > BURIAL.
