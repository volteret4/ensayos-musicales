## curiosities
**Confirmed Green Day Side Project** : Foxboro Hot Tubs is a "Garage Band side project" that Green Day undertook with friends to "blow up some creative steam" following their work on the American Idiot album. Unlike other pseudonymous projects, Green Day has been completely transparent about this identity, with Billie Joe Armstrong explicitly stating, "The only similarity between Foxboro Hot Tops and Green Day is that we are the same band." They even performed live unmasked, sometimes covering songs by The Network, their other suspected alter ego. "The only similarity between Foxboro Hot Tops and Green Day is that we are the same band, said Billy Joe Armstrong." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E
**Name Origin** : The name "Foxboro Hot Tubs" originates from a "Hot Tops star" (likely a typo in transcript, meaning 'store' or 'spot') where the band members used to discreetly drink when they were younger. "The name? Well it's from a Hot Tops star where the band used to sneak off and drink when they were young." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E
