# artist - Chris Carter

## curiosities
**"The X Files" Movie Soundtrack Spoken Word** : Chris Carter, the creator of "The X Files," contributed a strange spoken word piece to the soundtrack of "The X Files" movie. This hidden track, found at the very end of the album, discusses a syndicate's secret vaccine against "black oil," the breakdown of their code of honor, and threats from within and without, mentioning agents Mulder and Scully.
