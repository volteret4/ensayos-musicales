# artist - Felicia Collins

## instruments
- Lead guitar

## curiosities
**Extensive Touring and TV Work** : Felicia Collins toured with several "big names" in music and was a long-standing member of the house band on the "David Letterman" show for many years. "Felicia Collins, toured with some big names who was part of the house band on David Letterman for years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Lead Guitarist for The CBS Orchestra** : Felicia Collins served as the lead guitarist for Paul Shaffer in The CBS Orchestra, the house band for "Late Night with David Letterman." "She was the lead guitarist with Paul Schaefer in the CBS Orchestra as part of Late Night with David Letterman." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0
**Session and Touring Musician** : Collins was a highly sought-after guitarist, frequently called upon by producer Nile Rodgers for recordings with his clients, including Madonna. She also toured with George Clinton and P-Funk and served as the studio and touring guitarist for the Thompson Twins during their peak in the mid-1980s. "For that, she was a go-to person for guitarist and producer Nile Rogers, who had her play on many records for his clients, including Madonna." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0
