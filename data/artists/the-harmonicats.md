# artist - The Harmonicats

## songs
**Peg O' My Heart (1947)** : This 1947 recording is cited as potentially the first use of reverb on a musical recording. The reverb effect is noticeable behind the harmonica, adding life to an otherwise dry sound. "The first use of reverb on a recording seems to be a song called Pegamai Heart, a 1947 recording by the harmonic cats." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag ← the-harmonicats
