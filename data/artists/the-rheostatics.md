**The Rheostatics Formation and Influence (1978)** : Formed in Toronto in 1978, The Rheostatics befriended The Tragically Hip in the 80s and 90s. Despite not being commercially focused, their unconventional and eclectic approach to rock garnered critical praise and significant influence. "Their history goes back to 1978 in Toronto.
