# artist - Eddie Dujan

## songs
**Old Man Moses (1938)** : Released in 1938, this jazz record featuring singer Patricia Norman has gone down in history as possibly the first recording with a swear word. However, the alleged swear word is actually Patricia Norman stuttering on the word "bucket," though listeners on low-fidelity equipment might have perceived it differently.
