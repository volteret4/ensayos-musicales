# artist - Eddie Dujan

## songs
**Old Man Moses (1938)** : Released in 1938, this jazz record featuring singer Patricia Norman has gone down in history as possibly the first recording with a swear word. However, the alleged swear word is actually Patricia Norman stuttering on the word "bucket," though listeners on low-fidelity equipment might have perceived it differently. "As far as anybody could tell, the first record to maybe appear with a swear word is Old Man Moses by jazz pianist Eddie Dujan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
