## albums
**Bleed American (2001)** : This Emo-style album achieved significant commercial success, selling a million copies between 2002 and 2003. Its popularity played a major role in bringing Emo music into the mainstream and spurred a trend for record labels to sign other Emo and Emo-ish bands. **Clarity (1999)** : Jimmy Eat World's early albums, especially "Clarity," were highly influential for the emo scene. Released near the end of the 1990s, it contributed to emo's breakthrough as the genre evolved to be more poppy and punky, incorporating hooks and melodies.
