## curiosities
**Hide Your Sheep Tour Rider (1982-1983) – Brown M&M's Clause** : The band's rider for their 97-show, 10-month tour across six countries in North and South America, included a legendary clause on page 36 (specifically page 40, under "munchies") that stated "absolutely no brown ones" among the M&Ms. This was not a serious demand but a test to ensure that promoters had thoroughly read the entire 50-plus page contract, as failure to do so often led to "fractious relations" due to overlooked logistical and technical requirements.
