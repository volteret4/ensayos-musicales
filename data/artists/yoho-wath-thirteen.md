## curiosities
**Continuation of the Group** : Despite Father Yod's death, the group continued to exist in various incarnations. "But the group carried on in various incarnations." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Cult Following** : Yoho Wath Thirteen and Father Yod developed a cult following among certain music enthusiasts. "And this cult has a cult following." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Distribution Method** : Their albums were pressed in small batches, typically no more than a thousand copies, and sold through mail order and a store called "the Hermit Hut," which was located next door to Father Yod's vegetarian restaurant, The Source. "These were pressed in batches of no more than a thousand and sold through the mail and a distor known as the Hermit Hut, which was next door to Father Yod's vegetarian restaurant called The Source, Unsensitble of Art." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Father Yod's Death** : Father Yod, the group's leader and founder, died in 1975 at the age of 53 in a bizarre hang gliding accident in Hawaii. Lacking experience or training, he jumped off a 1300-foot cliff on the eastern shore of Oahu, crashed on the beach, and died nine hours later. "Father Yod stopped participating in 1975 after he died in a bizarre hang gliding accident in Hawaii." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
**Formation and Philosophy** : Yoho Wath Thirteen was formed in 1970 and conceived as a "trippy psych group." Their recordings were entirely wild improvisations, made without rehearsals or prior planning, reflecting their spontaneous and uninhibited approach to music. "Yoho Wath Thirteen was conceived in 1970 as a trippy psych group. Their recordings were wild improvisations." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HcK4M7-02ik
