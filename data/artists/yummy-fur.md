# artist - Yummy Fur

## members
- Alex Kapranos
- Keyboard player (committed suicide in 1999)
- Paul Thompson

## genres
- Retro-electronica

## curiosities
**Keyboard Player's Demise** : The keyboard player for Yummy Fur, who notably wore a Canadian flag t-shirt in a video, committed suicide in 1999. "Oddly retro-electronica from the pre-Franc band called Yummy Fur in the video of the keyboard players wearing a Canadian flag t-shirt. He committed suicide in 1999." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RWbKqqW78yg
**Meeting Point for Alex Kapranos and Paul Thompson** : Yummy Fur was the band where Alex Kapranos and Paul Thompson first met and played together, forming a connection that would later lead to Franz Ferdinand. "Yummy Fur came next. This is where Alex met Paul, and they sounded like this." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RWbKqqW78yg
