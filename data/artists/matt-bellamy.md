**Early Band Formations** : In the early 1990s, Matt Bellamy was the guitarist for drummer Dominic Howard's high school band, Carnage Mayhem, which subsequently became Gothic Plague. He later joined Dominic and another member in a band called Rocket Baby Dolls, which, at a teacher's suggestion, changed its name to Muse.
