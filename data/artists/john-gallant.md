# artist - John Gallant

## members
- John Gallant (bass player)

## instruments
- Tascam 4-track (used for Demoluca)

## curiosities
**Hectic School, Band Struggles** : During the 1999-2003 period, John found things "hectic and crazy" while trying to finish school, noting that the band was barely existing after seven or eight years, questioning its future. "For me, it was things were just so hectic and crazy trying to finish school and the band barely existing and same feeling like seven or eight years into it, like what's happening." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Humber College (Music then Business)** : In 1996, John attended Humber College for a year, studying music, before switching to a business program and working various part-time jobs to make ends meet. "John, at that point, I think I was at in Humber in 96 for music. I only did that for a year and then I ended up switching over and taking a business program through school and then working, you know, trying to make ends meet that way of where I worked just like part-time jobs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Pre-Band Jobs** : Before the band found success, John worked at a car auction, and then later at Chrysler in 1996. "Well, I would have been working at a car auction at this time and then Chrysler would have happened shortly after that. Like, you had 96." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Temporary Departure** : During a dark period for the band, after Aaron Solowaniak's MS diagnosis, John Gallant left the band for a few months, contributing to the feeling that the band was "not happening anymore." "It was a really dark time. And then John had left the band for a few months." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
