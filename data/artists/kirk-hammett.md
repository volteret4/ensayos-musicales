## curiosities
**Extensive Horror Movie Poster and Memorabilia Collection** : Kirk Hammett has a profound obsession with collecting old horror movie posters and memorabilia, a passion he has held since childhood (around age five or six). His collection is considered one of the most comprehensive in the world, is extremely valuable, and frequently tours museums globally.
