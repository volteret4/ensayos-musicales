## curiosities
**Accusations of Selling Out** : As Rancid gained popularity and commercial recognition, they, alongside other successful pop punk bands like Green Day and The Offspring, were accused of "selling out" by punk purists. "purists became annoyed, accusing groups like Green Dan, the offspring and Rancidov selling out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Clash-like Sound** : This band was noted for sometimes sounding more like The Clash than The Clash themselves. "Ransett, who sometimes sounded more clash than the clash." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=44dXy0StjIQ
**Evolution from Operation Ivy** : Rancid formed and evolved from Operation Ivy, a Bay Area band that preceded them in the punk scene. "They evolved out of a Bay Area band called Operation Ivy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Melodic Enough for Prime Time** : Rancid was identified as a "pure punk band" whose melodic qualities made them suitable for broader mainstream exposure. This characteristic helped them appeal to a wider audience beyond traditional punk circles. "Another pure punk band that was deemed melodic enough for prime time was Rancid." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Multiple Warped Tour Appearances** : Rancid has agreed to participate in the Warped Tour at least three times. "Ransid has agreed to participate at least three times." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
