## songs
**The Wolfman (1964)** : Released in 1964, "The Wolfman" is a treated musical composition by experimental musician and composer Robert Ashley, characterized by droning sounds and extensive feedback. It was designed to be played through speakers simultaneously with his live stage performance. This work is believed to be the first instance where feedback was consciously harnessed to create a musical landscape, achieved simply by placing a vocal microphone in an intentionally "wrong" but acoustically effective position.
