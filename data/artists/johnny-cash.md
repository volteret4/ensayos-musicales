# artist - Johnny Cash

## albums
**American IV: The Man Comes Around (2002) – 87th Studio Album** : Following a period where his career had "run aground" in the early 2000s, Johnny Cash collaborated with producer Rick Rubin to create this, his 87th studio album. The concept was for Cash to cover contemporary songs in his distinctive style. The album features material originally written by Paul Simon, Depeche Mode, Sting, The Beatles, The Eagles, a duet with Nick Cave, and most notably, his rendition of "Hurt" by Nine Inch Nails. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## songs
**Hurt (2002) – Nine Inch Nails Cover** : Featured on his 87th studio album, "American IV: The Man Comes Around," Johnny Cash's cover of Nine Inch Nails' "Hurt" is described as "one of the most painfully honest recordings of all time." The accompanying video intersperses shots of a young Cash in his prime with footage of him as an older, frail man (he was 71 and in "very poor health" when the video was shot), a portrayal that brought Trent Reznor of Nine Inch Nails to tears. This cover transformed Cash into an "alternative hero," solidifying his image as "the original man in black." It became the biggest hit of his career, selling nearly 3 million downloads, garnering tens of millions of YouTube views, and winning numerous music awards from both the rock and country establishments. Cash died seven months after the video was filmed. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
