**Ring of Fire (1963)** : In 2004, the makers of Preparation H hemorrhoid ointment offered a significant sum of money to Johnny Cash's estate for the use of "Ring of Fire" in a commercial. The estate, however, declined the offer. "In 2004, the people who make preparation H approached the estate of Johnny Cash with bags of money asking, pretty please, could they use a song Ring of Fire in a commercial for their hemorrhoid ointment? ## curiosities
**Army Service Intercepting Soviet Messages** : As a 19-year-old Air Force recruit in the army, Johnny Cash spent three years stationed in Germany. During this time, his duties involved intercepting Soviet messages that were transmitted in Morse code to their combat aircraft. "he spent three years in Germany intercepting Soviet messages sent in Morse code to their combat aircraft." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**Backstage Rider – American Flag Requirement** : Johnny Cash, known as "the man in black" and a simple individual, had one non-negotiable item in his rider: an American flag that had to be displayed in full view of the audience during his performances. "The one thing he would not skip on was an American flag that had to be displayed in full view of the audience." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z2hvUDtBxho
