## curiosities
**"Gig in the Sky" Event (February 27, 2007)** : He and his band boarded a private Boeing 757 chartered by Sony Ericsson (a mobile phone company) from Munich to Athens. The cabin was specially outfitted with lighting and a stage, and the event included 200 contest winners.
**"Gig in the Sky" World Record (2007)** : On February 27, 2007, Jamiroquai and his band performed a "Gig in the Sky" at 35,000 feet aboard a private Boeing 757 chartered by Sony Ericsson, flying from Munich to Athens. The stunt, which featured 200 contest winners and a specially outfitted cabin, was to promote the "High Times" album. A Guinness World Record representative certified six records, including highest concert, fastest concert (nearly 600 mph ground speed), and highest and fastest recording in history.
**Guinness World Records (February 27, 2007)** : Performed five songs at 35,000 feet, leading a representative from the Guinness Book of World Records to certify six new records, including highest concert at 35,000 feet, fastest concert (at a ground speed of nearly 600 miles per hour), and the highest and fastest recording in history.
**Record Later Broken** : His record for the highest concert was subsequently broken by other artists.
