## concerts
- **The Great Gig in the Sky (2007) – Highest Concert Ever** : On February 27, 2007, Jamiroquai, featuring singer J.K., performed "The Great Gig in the Sky" for Sony Ericsson. This concert took place on a converted Boeing 757 plane that departed from Munich, Germany, and landed in Athens, Greece, where an after-party and another performance ensued. Two hundred fans attended the in-flight performance of six recorded songs. This event was certified to have broken six world records, including highest ever concert, highest ever recording session, fastest concert ever, fastest recording session ever, highest ever gig in a plane, and fastest gig in a plane, with a Guinness Book of World Records official present to verify. "It took place on February 27th, 2007, on a converted 757 that took off from Munich, Germany. 200 fans came along to see a performance of six songs, all of which were recorded."
