## concerts
- **The Great Gig in the Sky (2007) – Highest Concert Ever** : On February 27, 2007, Jamiroquai, featuring singer J.K., performed "The Great Gig in the Sky" for Sony Ericsson. This concert took place on a converted Boeing 757 plane that departed from Munich, Germany, and landed in Athens, Greece, where an after-party and another performance ensued. Two hundred fans attended the in-flight performance of six recorded songs. This event was certified to have broken six world records, including highest ever concert, highest ever recording session, fastest concert ever, fastest recording session ever, highest ever gig in a plane, and fastest gig in a plane, with a Guinness Book of World Records official present to verify. "It took place on February 27th, 2007, on a converted 757 that took off from Munich, Germany. 200 fans came along to see a performance of six songs, all of which were recorded."

## curiosities
**Extensive and Eventful Car Collection** : JK, the frontman for Jamiroquai, is an avid car collector with approximately 37 vehicles, including motorcycles. His collection boasts rare and high-performance cars such as a Ferrari F40, two vintage Ferrari 275s, a 1964 Aston Martin DB5 (the James Bond car), a customized Porsche 911 R, a rare Ferrari 250, a Ferrari Enzo, a Porsche Carrera GT, multiple 911s, several Maseratis, and various Lamborghinis. One notable incident involved his 1994 Lamborghini Diablo, which was totaled by a film company employee who took it for a joyride during a video shoot. "JK, the frontman for the British band Jameer Quy, is crazy about his collection." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_KJ60im4KAc
**Ferrari Enzo Incident and Song Inspiration** : JK owned one of the approximately 400 Ferrari Enzos produced in the early 2000s, known for its six-liter V12 engine, 651 horsepower, and a top speed of 218 mph. JK actively drove it, including a 13-hour trip from Rome to Calais. In 2009, after dinner, he got into a drunken argument with a pastry chef who claimed his Toyota Supra was superior to JK's Enzo. The altercation ended with the chef smashing several of the Enzo's windows, an event that inspired JK to write the song "Black Devil Car" for Jamiroquai's 2005 album, *Dynamite*. "J wrote a song about that car. It's from a 2005 album called Dynamite and it's called Black Devil Car." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_KJ60im4KAc
**Gig in the Sky - Highest Concert Record (2007)** : On February 27, 2007, Jamiroquai and his band performed a "Gig in the Sky" aboard a private Sony Ericsson-chartered Boeing 757, flying at 35,000 feet from Munich to Athens. This event, which included 200 contest winners, was a promotional stunt for his greatest hits album, "High Times," and earned six Guinness World Records, including highest concert and fastest recording at nearly 600 miles per hour.
