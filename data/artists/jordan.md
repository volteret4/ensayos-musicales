**Final Definition of Punk Aesthetic** : Jordan is credited with providing the final impetus and definition to the punk aesthetic movement. She took the ideas from Vivienne Westwood and Malcolm McLaren (which originated from Richard Hell) and, through her DIY approach, solidified the iconic look.
