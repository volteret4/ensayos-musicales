## members

## genres

## curiosities
**Early Use of Corpse Paint** : the singer, Peter Ebling, and the rest of the band were made up in corpse paint, similar to modern death metal or black metal, in a 1974 film.
**Fictional Band in Phantom of the Paradise (1974)** : a made-up band featured in the movie *Phantom of the Paradise*, performing a sequence related to the construction of a Frankenstein-like monster named Beef.
**Potential Inventor of Goth/Death Metal Aesthetic** : Alan Cross theorizes that this band's appearance and music in *Phantom of the Paradise* in 1974 could be considered a proto-goth or proto-death metal group, predating the emergence of these genres.
