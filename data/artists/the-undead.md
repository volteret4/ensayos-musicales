## curiosities
**Phantom of the Paradise Fictional Band (1974)** : The Undead is a made-up band featured in the 1974 film "Phantom of the Paradise" during the sequence depicting the construction of the Frankenstein-like monster Beef. The band members, especially the singer Peter Ebling, are depicted wearing corpse paint similar to that seen in modern death metal or black metal, leading to the theory that they represent a proto-goth or proto-death metal group from 1974. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
