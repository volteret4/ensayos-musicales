# artist - Quincy Jones

## curiosities
**Tonto's Influence on Production** : Quincy Jones was greatly impressed by the Tonto synthesizer and the new possibilities of electronically generated music demonstrated by Malcolm Cecil and Robert Marguliffe's work with Stevie Wonder. The knowledge he gained from Tonto shaped his approach to producing Michael Jackson's albums, contributing to a new sound for Black Pop music and drawing attention to electronic music's potential. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
