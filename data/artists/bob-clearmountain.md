# artist - Bob Clearmountain

## curiosities
**Unofficial Remix of Fake Plastic Trees** : Bob Clearmountain created an unauthorized, "guitar-heavy" alternative remix of Radiohead's "Fake Plastic Trees" for the US market. Commissioned by the US distributor, who deemed the original version insufficiently commercial or "grunge-like," this remix was distributed to US radio stations and media for airplay without the band's consent or knowledge. Radiohead, particularly Thom Yorke, was horrified by this version and immediately ceased its distribution.
