## songs
**Joyful Noise (2008)** : This 2008 Grammy-winning Christian rap track was the basis of a plagiarism lawsuit against Katy Perry's 2012 song "Dark Horse." Flame claimed that a keyboard ostinato in "Dark Horse" infringed on his song's ostinato and that his song's religious message was "irreparably tarnished" by Perry's video imagery. Despite tempo and pitch differences, Flame initially won the first round but lost on appeal.
