# artist - Tony Blair

## curiosities
**Prime Minister's Britpop Fandom (1997)** : Upon his election as Prime Minister, Tony Blair, who had played in a rock band during university and loved Britpop, invited figures like Noel Gallagher to 10 Downing Street. This interaction, however, created "bad optics" for Noel, suggesting that the "music of the people" had been co-opted by the establishment. "Tony Blair was elected Prime Minister. Back in university, he played an rock band, and he loved Brit Pop. He even invited people like Noel Gallagher round to 10 Downing Street for a chat and a glass of wine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg ← tony-blair
