**Short-Lived Group (1972)** : Stars was a group formed by Sid Baret in 1972 as a final attempt to continue his musical career, but it only lasted for a few months. Live recordings from Stars concerts are described as even "more impactful and heavy" than Pin Floyd's 1967 recordings, illustrating the severity of Sid Baret's mental decline by that point.
