**Power (2010)** : Released in 2010, this critically acclaimed song controversially featured a sample from the first track of King Crimson's debut album. Robert Fripp discovered the unauthorized use in 2012 via news reports and YouTube, as no prior permission had been secured. Although Universal Music, which owned both Island Records (co-owner of the King Crimson material) and West's label, eventually sought permission a week to ten days after the song's publication, Fripp's anger stemmed from the lack of notification and prior consent. "sacaba un tema que se llama Power que además tenía unas enormes críticas y usaba un sampling del primer tema de la primer disco de King Crimson." ← Music Radar Clan > King Crimson, derechos y Spotify | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xg0XR9r95cc&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← kanye-west ← kanye-west
