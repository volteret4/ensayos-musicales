**David Bowie's Alleged Prediction of Kanye's Rise (1972)** : A conspiracy theory suggests that David Bowie predicted Kanye West's rise to fame in 1972, five years before Kanye's birth on June 8, 1977. This theory is built upon elements from Bowie's work, including the "K West" sign on his *Ziggy Stardust* album cover, the prophetic song "Five Years," and his final album *Blackstar*, which theorists interpret as an anointment of Kanye, a "Black Star."
