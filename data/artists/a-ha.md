# artist - A-ha

## curiosities
**Pioneering Electric Vehicle Adoption in Norway (1989)** : In 1989, A-ha was so impressed with an electric Fiat at a Swiss auto show that they imported one to Norway, making it the country's first modern-day EV. They used civil disobedience (refusing tolls, illegal parking, non-payment of vehicle taxes) over seven years to advocate for EVs, ultimately influencing the Norwegian government to promote electric vehicles, leading to over half a million EVs in the country. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qtdetc5-MQo
