## albums
**Blood Sugar Sex Magic (1991) – Breakthrough Album** : Released on September 24, 1991, the same day as Nirvana's *Nevermind*. This was the first album to feature John Frusciante as guitarist and led the band from a mid-level group to one of the biggest acts of the alternative era, selling over 15 million copies. The album was produced by Rick Rubin and recorded in a historic mansion at 351 Laurel Canyon Boulevard in April 1991. Six singles were released from this record. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Blood Sugar Sex Magik** : Features Flea's slap bass technique, which he later became annoyed with due to its widespread copying after the band's breakthrough. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CVPPymc_qrk
**Californication (1999)** : The first album released by the Red Hot Chili Peppers after John Frusciante rejoined the band clean and sober, following his severe heroin addiction. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKvFJMkZdBE
**Mother's Milk (1989)** : This album, released in 1989, represents the peak of Flea's slap-based playing style. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=95clNVd0hYg
**Mother's Milk (1989) – Debut of Classic Lineup** : This album featured John Frusciante on guitar for 9 songs and Chad Smith on drums, marking the beginning of what would become the classic Red Hot Chili Peppers lineup. Produced by Michael Beinhorn, who convinced Frusciante to play in a heavier style. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Stadium Arcadium (2006) – Double Album** : The album featuring the single "Dani California". ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
**The Uplift Mofo Party Plan (1987) – Hillel Slovak's Last Full Album** : This was the last full album to feature Hillel Slovak on guitar before his death. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
