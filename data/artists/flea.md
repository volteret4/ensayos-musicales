# artist - Flea

## curiosities
**Creative State Before Reunion** : Anthony Kiedis observed that in the band's previous incarnation (before John's return), Flea was not "feeling his best self" and was not "on fire" musically. This perceived lack of enthusiasm from Flea was a factor that led Anthony to start contemplating John's potential involvement. "I knew that Flea was in a mood in a disposition where he wasn't feeling his, his, his best self in the previous incarnation. For whatever reason, it just like he wasn't, he wasn't on fire." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Immediate Offer to John Frusciante** : Within seconds of John Frusciante suggesting a jam, Flea quickly moved beyond that idea and directly asked John if he wanted to rejoin the Red Hot Chili Peppers. This direct approach fast-tracked the reunion discussion from a casual musical get-together to a full commitment. "It was within seconds after I suggested that Flea was like, you ever think about being in the Red Hat Chili Peppers again?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Inspired Creative Contribution** : In the band's collaborative songwriting process, Flea was often inspired by John Frusciante's musical contributions. When John would bring a "gem" (ideas, chord progressions, arrangements) to practice, Flea would respond by bringing his own "gem" to the table, fostering a natural back-and-forth creative flow. "It's like every time John would bring a gem into practice, you know, flee would feel like, okay, I'll bring a gem." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Pre-Anthony Jam with John** : Unbeknownst to Anthony Kiedis, Flea had already jammed with John Frusciante before both Flea and Anthony simultaneously brought up the idea of John's return. This pre-existing musical interaction gave Flea a basis for his conviction in John rejoining. "And he's like, yeah, I've actually jammed with him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Prioritized Commitment Over Initial Jam** : Flea focused on John Frusciante making a firm decision to *join* the band rather than first testing their chemistry with a casual jam session. The first time the reunited lineup played together was in rehearsal about a month after John agreed to rejoin. "Flea went right past that. He was just like, he just, he wanted to concentrate on the topic of whether or not I was going to actually join." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Recipient of Jam Suggestion** : John Frusciante mentioned to Flea that Chad Smith had expressed missing playing with them and suggested a jam session. This conversation took place while John and Flea were in John's kitchen. "I didn't really talk about it until, Flea and I were standing in my kitchen and Chad had said something to me when I saw him at Flea's wedding." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
**Simultaneous Reunion Discussion with Anthony** : Flea and Anthony Kiedis remarkably approached each other at the same time to discuss John Frusciante's return, both having independently been thinking about it. Flea had a "more important" thing to tell Anthony, which turned out to be the same subject. "Basically, we were both saying the exact same thing at the exact same time, which was what do you think about John?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nr-3Y3UWQcI
