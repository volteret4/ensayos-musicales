## curiosities
**Death from Heroin Overdose (June 25, 1988)** : Hillel Slovak, the guitarist for the Red Hot Chili Peppers, died of a heroin overdose on June 25, 1988, while the band was working on a new album. His death led to drummer Jack Irons quitting the band due to his inability to cope. **Musical Style - Riffs and Solos** : Hillel Slovak's guitar playing style was characterized by "riffs and solos," representing the "old Chili Peppers sound." This style served as a contrast to John Frusciante's more melodic approach that would later evolve within the band. "He plays riffs and solos. Not that there's anything wrong with that, but listen.
