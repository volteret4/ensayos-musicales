# artist - Vicious White Kids

## concerts
- Electric Ballroom (August 15, 1978)

## curiosities
**Fundraising Gig for New York Move** : Formed as a temporary group featuring Sid Vicious on vocals, Glenn Matlock (whom Sid had replaced in the Sex Pistols) on bass, Rats Scabies of The Damned on drums, and Steve New on guitar, this group played a quick solo gig at the Electric Ballroom in Camden on August 15, 1978. The purpose was to raise money for Sid and Nancy to fly to New York. "To raise money, there was a quick solo gig at the electric ballroom in Camden under the name Vicious White Kids." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Nancy Spungin's Attempted Backing Vocals** : Nancy Spungin insisted on singing backup vocals at the Vicious White Kids' gig, but the sound engineer deliberately turned off her microphone. "He insisted on singing backup vocals, but the sound guy made sure her mic was turned off." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
