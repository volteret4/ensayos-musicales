# artist - Elton Motello

## songs
**Jet Boy, Jet Girl (1978)** : This song, released in 1978, was a radio hit that generated controversy due to its lyrics depicting a 15-year-old boy's relationship with an older man, only for the older man to later reject him for a woman. It was considered "a little risky" for mainstream radio but was covered by various punk and new wave bands, some of whom were gay and others who simply appreciated its shock value.
