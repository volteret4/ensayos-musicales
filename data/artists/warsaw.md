## curiosities
**Confusion with Warsaw Pact** : The band changed their name from "Warsaw" to Joy Division because they were frequently confused with a London metal band named Warsaw Pact, opting to avoid conflict by adopting a new identity. "Warsaw was always getting confused with a London metal band called Warsaw Pact. And rather than get into some kind of fight, they just thought they would change their name." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w ← warsaw ← warsaw
**Demo Tape Recording** : On July 18, 1977, Warsaw recorded a demo tape which featured a studio version of the song "At a Later Date." This tape, initially misplaced, provided an early glimpse into their sound. "And that's taken from a long, misplaced demo tape recorded on July 18th, 1977." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w ← warsaw ← warsaw
**Final Gig as Warsaw** : The band performed their last gig under the name Warsaw on New Year's Eve, 1977, before officially adopting "Joy Division." "So after one last gig as Warsaw on New Year's Eve 1977, Ian Curtis, Barney Albrecht, soon to become Bernie Sumner, Peter Hook and Steven Morris became Joy Division officially." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w ← warsaw ← warsaw
**First Live Performance** : Warsaw performed their first gig on May 29, 1977, at the Electric Circus club in Manchester. The reviews were noted as mixed, yet acknowledged an "elusive spark of dissimilarity" in their sound, hinting at their unique potential. "Warsaw played their first gig on May 29th, 1977, and a Manchester club called the Electric Circus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w ← warsaw ← warsaw
**Initial Band Name** : The band that would become Joy Division initially went by the name "Warsaw" after Ian Curtis rejected "Stiff Kittens." This name was inspired by David Bowie's song "Warzawe" from his "Low" album. "Everyone soon settled on Warsaw. That sounded different." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w ← warsaw ← warsaw
**Precursor to Joy Division** : Formed by Bernie and Peter in the spring of 1976, inspired by a Sex Pistols concert in Manchester. This initial group later became Joy Division. "Their first group was called Warsaw. Warsaw became Joy Division." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo ← warsaw
**Search for a Drummer** : By 1977, Warsaw was ready to make a demo tape but struggled to find a permanent drummer. Stephen Morris was eventually found in August, as the sole respondent to their advertisement for a drummer in a local music magazine. "By 1977, despite the fact that they couldn't find a permanent drummer, Warsaw was ready to make a demo tape." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w ← warsaw ← warsaw
