## curiosities
**Non-Traditional Harpist** : Tom Munger began his musical journey with classical harp lessons in college, but his instructors became "tired of him getting all jazzy and rocky" with the instrument, prompting him to leave. He then experimented by adding various effects pedals to his harp, establishing himself as a unique and unconventional harpist, which appealed greatly to Florence Welch and Isabella Summers.
