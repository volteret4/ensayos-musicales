## curiosities
**Collaboration with Johnny Marr** : Zach Starky worked with Johnny Marr after Marr left The Smiths. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Drummer for Oasis (Employee Status)** : In 2004, he joined Oasis as an employee rather than an official member, a status he preferred as it allowed him to pursue other projects when not needed by the band. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Early Bands** : His first band was a garage band called The Next. He later joined a reformed version of The Spencer Davis Group. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**First Drum Kit from Keith Moon** : He received his first drum kit from his godfather, Keith Moon of The Who. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Gig with The Who** : He secured a gig with The Who, which was fitting given his godfather's connection to the band. He has been on call with them for live performances ever since. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Son of Ringo Starr and Maureen Starkey Tigrett** : Zach Starky is the son of Ringo Starr and Maureen Starkey Tigrett. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Working with Father's Band** : His father eventually gave up trying to discourage him and had Zach work with his own band. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
