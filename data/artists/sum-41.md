## curiosities
**"Still Waiting" and Italian Recording Similarity** : The Sum 41 hit "Still Waiting" was found by internet users to bear a strong resemblance to an Italian recording from the mid to late 1990s. Despite the striking likeness, this was categorized as an "unfortunate sonic coincidence" because pop songs often have simple structures, and the melody (based on two alternating notes punctuated by three or four more) could easily be independently discovered by different composers. "Sounds very, very similar, doesn't it? ... I'd simply chalk this up to unfortunate sonic coincidences and we're only aware of it because of the power and the reach of the internet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L09R2evjyMc

**Post-COVID Tour Bus Shortage** : Following the lifting of COVID-19 restrictions, Sum 41, an internationally successful and multi-platinum band, encountered severe logistical challenges when planning a European tour. Many tour buses had been idle for two years and were not operational, leading to a critical shortage. The few available buses were prohibitively expensive, affordable only to acts like Metallica, forcing Sum 41 to tour Europe in a rented van, reminiscent of their early days. "After the restrictions were lifted, the band wanted a tour Europe. But when they started planning things, they found that there were no tour buses available." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
