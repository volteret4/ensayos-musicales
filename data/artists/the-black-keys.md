## albums
**Attack & Release (2008) – Fifth Studio Album** : Their first collaboration with producer Danger Mouse, featuring additional musicians and a more polished sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Brothers (2010) – Commercial Breakthrough** : Recorded at Muscle Shoals during a period of intense personal friction, this album won three Grammys and achieved massive commercial success. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUQ2vZzmRiI
**Brothers (2010) – Sixth Studio Album & Breakthrough** : Recorded at Muscle Shoals during a period of intense personal friction, this album won three Grammys and achieved massive commercial success. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**El Camino (2011) – Global Success** : Produced by Danger Mouse in Nashville, this album went platinum worldwide and was inspired by 60s and 70s rock hooks. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUQ2vZzmRiI
**El Camino (2011) – Seventh Studio Album & Global Success** : Produced by Danger Mouse in Nashville, this album went platinum worldwide and was inspired by 60s and 70s rock hooks. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Magic Potion (2006) – Fourth Studio Album** : The band's first major-label release on Nonesuch, it reached the Top 100 in the US and the Top 12 in Australia despite the band's dissatisfaction with its mastering. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Rubber Factory (2004) – Third Studio Album** : Recorded in an abandoned tire factory, this album was the band's first to chart on the Top 200 and secured them a slot at Lollapalooza. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**The Big Come Up (2002) – Debut Studio Album** : Recorded in Patrick Carney's parents' basement, this indie release was well-received by critics and featured limited edition colored vinyl. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Thickfreakness (2003) – Second Studio Album** : Recorded in a single 14-hour session and financed by the members mowing lawns; it was retitled _Inezuma Rock and Blues_ in Japan. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Turn Blue (2014) – Eighth Studio Album** : A psych-rock leaning record influenced by Dan Auerbach's divorce, announced uniquely via a tweet from Mike Tyson. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I

## songs
**Captain Beefheart Cover (2009)** : In the spring of 2009, The Black Keys covered a song by Captain Beefheart. This cover was released as part of a split 7-inch single with The Flaming Lips and Star Death and White Dwarfs for Record Store Day, pressed on green vinyl. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**I Got Mine (2008)** : Considered the biggest hit of their pre-superstar era, frequently used in TV themes. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Lonely Boy (2011)** : A global hit featuring a viral music video of security guard Derek T. Tuggle dancing in a single shot. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Set You Free (2003)** : A single from _Thickfreakness_ that gained fame after its inclusion in the film _School of Rock_. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Tighten Up (2010)** : The only Danger Mouse-produced track on _Brothers_, it earned the band a Grammy for Best Rock Song. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Weight of Love (2014)** : A Pink Floyd-inspired opening track that reflects the emotional weight of Auerbach's divorce. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I

## curiosities
**Band Formation (2001)** : The duo formed in Akron, Ohio, after no other musicians showed up to a scheduled demo recording session. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Continued Performance in 2010s** : one of the older groups that kept doing what they did best throughout the 2010s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Initial Anti-Commercial Stance** : The band famously turned down £200,000 for a mayonnaise commercial early on to protect their "indie cred". ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Internal Strife** : By 2010, the duo was on the verge of breaking up due to depression, drinking, and a lack of communication regarding solo projects. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Management by QPrime** : The Black Keys are among the current clients managed by QPrime, the management company founded by Cliff Burnstein and Peter Mensch. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**The Grey Ghost** : The band's original 1994 Chrysler touring van, which was later honored on the cover of _El Camino_. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
