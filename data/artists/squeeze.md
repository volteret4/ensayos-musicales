# artist - Squeeze

## members
- Chris Difford
- Glen Tilbrook
- Paul Carrack

## genres
- New Wave
- New Wave Revival
- Powerpop

## songs
**Tempted (1981)** : This song, released in 1981, became Squeeze's biggest hit. It features keyboardist Paul Carrack on lead vocals, who was only in the band for about a year. The track has been widely covered by artists like Sting and Joe Cocker, and has been used extensively in TV commercials for brands such as Heineken and Burger King, as well as in video games like Grand Theft Auto. "Squeeze and tempted from 1981 featuring Paul Carrack on vocals and Like we saw with Guns of Brixton from the clash it was the band's third voice on lead vocals" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M1JTdUxOEMU

## curiosities
**New Wave Powerpop** : Squeeze is included in the list of bands associated with the new wave form of Powerpop. Their work exemplifies the genre's ability to evolve and integrate into the broader alternative rock landscape of the late 1970s and early 1980s. "We can probably include the jam, the smiths, XTC, squeeze, and the vapors." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
**Paul Carrack's Short Tenure and Future Role** : Paul Carrack, who sang lead on Squeeze's biggest hit "Tempted," was only a member of the band for approximately one year and did not stay much longer. He later became the permanent lead singer for Mike + The Mechanics.
