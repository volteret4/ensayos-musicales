## curiosities
**First "Punk Rock" in Print (May 1971)** : Dave Marsh, a writer, published an article in May 1971 detailing the reformation of this 60s garage band, describing their reunion as "a landmark explosion of punk rock." This publication is recognized as the first documented use of the phrase "punk rock" in print, although at the time it signified more of an aesthetic than a specific musical genre.
