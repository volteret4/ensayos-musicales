## songs
**Tomorrow Never Dies (1997) – Title Theme** : Sheryl Crow's submitted song for the 1997 James Bond film "Tomorrow Never Dies" was ultimately chosen for the title sequence, despite k.d. lang initially winning the tender. The producers decided to use Crow's song for the opening credits while k.d. lang's song was renamed "Surrender" and used for the closing credits. "In the end Cheryl Song got the title sequence, the opening sequence." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo ← sheryl-crow

## curiosities
**Another Roadside Attraction International Tourmate** : Sheryl Crow was an international act that toured with The Tragically Hip's Another Roadside Attraction festival in 1993, 1995, and 1997, alongside Canadian performers. "But they also brought along Midnight Oil, Blues Traveler, Matthew Sweet and Cheryl Crow." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2kYleeMnl8 ← sheryl-crow ← sheryl-crow
**Guest Musician on 12 Bar Blues** : Sheryl Crow was among the guest musicians brought in for Scott Weiland's solo album, 12 Bar Blues, where she played the accordion. This unusual choice of instrument for a rock record was noted as fascinating. "All kinds of guest musicians were brought in, including people like Cheryl Crow. She was brought in to play of all things the accordion." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo ← sheryl-crow
