## curiosities
**Pre-Sloan Halifax Band** : Carney Lake Road was an obscure pre-Sloan band from the Halifax area, featuring Jay Ferguson and Chris Murphy, who had been playing together since age seven. They gained early excitement from an appearance on "Indie Street" on Much Music, which aired at 3 AM on Sunday nights, after performing at venues like the Rivoli. "Starting with a group called Carney Lake Road. Very obscure. I don't expect anyone to know it. Jay and I were both in it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mbic-70ZhpE
