## curiosities
**Big Band Leader** : Tommy Dorsey was a significant band leader during the Big Band era, leading large orchestras in which he was the central figure. His musicians and singers were employees, following his precise instructions. "These were orchestras of ten or more people led by a big name, Paul Whiteman, Tommy Dorsey, Benny Goodman, and so on." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk ← tommy-dorsey

**Host of "Stage Show"** : Tommy Dorsey, a swing band-leader, co-presented the "Stage Show" TV program with Jimmy Dorsey, which was one of Elvis Presley's early television appearances. "a program that was presented by the swing band-leaders Tommy and Jimmy Dorsey." ← Episode 38： ＂Heartbreak Hotel＂ by Elvis Presley | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ES35PYeAwfs
**Impact of Musicians' Union Strike** : Tommy Dorsey's band, as an instrumental ensemble on a major label, was unable to record any new music for two years during the Musicians' Union Strike of 1942-44 due to the dispute over royalties. However, his former vocalist, Frank Sinatra, could still record, backed by vocal groups.
