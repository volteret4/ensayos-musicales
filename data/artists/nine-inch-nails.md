# artist - Nine Inch Nails

## members
- Trent Reznor

## genres
- Alt Rock

## albums
**Year Zero (2007)** : Conceived by Trent Reznor as a potential soundtrack for a movie or TV series. This album presents a fictionalized, alternate future history depicting the United States in the years after 2007 under an increasingly repressive government. The promotion of the album was intricate and multidimensional, involving extensive online components and a global scavenger hunt for clues that actively engaged fans. This album is cited as the speaker's favorite alt-rock concept album of all time.

## songs
**Capital G (2007)** : A single from the Year Zero album, where the "G" in the title simultaneously stands for Greed and for George W. Bush.

## curiosities
**Contribution to The Crow Soundtrack (1994)** : One of the big names featured on the critically acclaimed soundtrack for *The Crow* movie.
**Early Lollapalooza Performer** : performed at the first Lollapalooza festival in 1991, described as "some weirdo group that was never going to go anywhere."
**HBO and BBC Miniseries Adaptation** : HBO and the BBC are collaborating to create a sci-fi miniseries based on the Year Zero record, involving significant investment and prominent industry figures.
**Influenced by My Bloody Valentine** : Counted among the bands significantly influenced by My Bloody Valentine's innovative use of guitar noise and walls of sound.
**Vigorous Mosh Pits (1990s)** : Saw vigorous mosh pits at their shows during the 1990s Alt Rock era.
