## curiosities
**Attended Ramones' Last Show (1996)** : Lemmy of Motorhead was among the attendees who came to pay respects at the Ramones' final show on August 6, 1996, at The Palace in Hollywood.
**Encounter in London** : Hugh Dillon drank with Lemmy in London when Dillon was 19, during his period of self-exile from Kingston.
**Influence on Dave Grohl** : Dave Grohl learned to play drums and guitar by playing along to their records.
