# artist - Smashing Pumpkins

## members

## albums
**Machina/The Machines of God (2000)** : A double album released in 2000. It was conceived during a period when Billy Corgan was bitter and disillusioned by the band's media portrayal, leading to the group's breakup by the end of the year. The album's narrative involves a rock star character named Zero, whom Billy based on himself. Upon hearing the voice of God, Zero renames himself Glass, and his band becomes The Machines of God. They attract a fan base known as The Ghost Children. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc
