# artist - Smashing Pumpkins

## members
- Billy Corgan

## albums
**Machina/The Machines of God (2000)** : A double album released in 2000. It was conceived during a period when Billy Corgan was bitter and disillusioned by the band's media portrayal, leading to the group's breakup by the end of the year. The album's narrative involves a rock star character named Zero, whom Billy based on himself. Upon hearing the voice of God, Zero renames himself Glass, and his band becomes The Machines of God. They attract a fan base known as The Ghost Children.

## songs
**Stand Inside Your Love (2000)** : A single released from the 2000 concept album Machina/The Machines of God.

## curiosities
**Band Breakup (2000)** : The group broke up by the end of 2000, a period reflected in the context and themes of the Machina/The Machines of God album.
**Butch Vig Production** : Butch Vig produced for the band.
**Machina Mystery Online Component (2000)** : A clever marketing initiative for the album Machina/The Machines of God, which included odd video clips, strange websites, and an alternate reality game.
**Planned Animated Shorts (2000)** : Sony was at one point planning to create a series of animated shorts for the web based on the Machina concept, but this project never materialized due to the dot-com bubble burst.
