# artist - James Brown's band

## members
- Clyde Stubblefield (drummer)

## songs
**Funky Drummer (1969)** : Recorded on November 20, 1969, this song features an eight-bar drum break played by Clyde Stubblefield. At James Brown's insistence, the rest of the band stops playing, allowing Stubblefield's groove to continue, creating a distinct and highly influential rhythmic section. This break has since been sampled and looped in at least 1,700 different songs.
