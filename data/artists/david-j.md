## curiosities
**Declaration at Final Bauhaus Gig** : At the conclusion of Bauhaus's final gig at the Hammersmith Palais on July 5, 1983, David J famously grabbed the microphone and declared, "rest in peace," signifying the band's official end. "As the gig at the Hammersmith Pele came to an end, David J grabbed the mic and said, rest in peace." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
**Name Preference - David J** : Although his full name is David John Haskins, he prefers to be addressed by the initial of his middle name, "David J." "although we know David by the initial of his middle name, John, he prefers to be called David J." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Post-Bauhaus Solo and The Sinister Ducks** : After the splintering of Bauhaus in 1983, David J initially pursued a solo career before forming a group called The Sinister Ducks, which, however, did not last for a significant period. "at first, he went solo, too. Then he formed a group called The Sinister Ducks, but they didn't last very long." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
**Producing and Joining The Jazz Butcher** : David J was invited to produce an album for the group The Jazz Butcher. He found the experience so enjoyable that he subsequently joined the band as a member for a period. "Then he was invited to produce an album for a group called The Jazz Butcher, and he enjoyed the experience so much that he actually ended up joining the band for a while." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
