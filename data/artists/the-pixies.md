## curiosities
**Band Breakup** : The Pixies broke up, which led to the reactivation of The Breeders as Kim Deal was "without anything to do."
**Kim Deal's Frustration** : Kim Deal formed The Breeders because her original songs were not being recorded by The Pixies.
**Sales Comparison** : The Breeders' second album, "Last Splash" (1993), sold more copies than all The Pixies' records combined up to that point.
**Support for The Breeders Demo** : Their UK record label provided Kim Deal with $11,000 to record a demo for The Breeders' first album, "Pod."
