## curiosities
**Britpop's "First Couple"** : While in Elastica, Justine Frischmann had a relationship with Damon Albarn of Blur, and together they were considered the "first couple of the whole Britpop scene." "Justine hooked up with Damon Albart of Blur and together acted as the first couple of the whole Britpop scene." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI ← justine-frischmann
**Family and Personal Life** : Justine Frischmann's father was a prominent architect, and she herself studied architecture before pursuing music. She is married to meteorologist Professor Ian Faluña and lives in San Francisco. "Her dad was a big architect and she studied architecture for a while before bailing for a career in music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI ← justine-frischmann
**Post-Music Artistic and Academic Pursuits** : After Elastica's breakup, Frischmann maintained a connection to music by helping her former roommate MIA develop her career. She also hosted two architecture series for the BBC, a subject she had studied previously. In 2005, she moved to Colorado to enroll in a master's program in visual arts at a Buddhist-inspired university, eventually becoming an acclaimed painter in San Francisco. "In 2005 she moved to Colorado, where she enrolled in a master's program in a small Buddhist inspired university to learn everything she could about visual arts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI ← justine-frischmann
**Privacy in America** : Frischmann enjoyed living in the United States because no one recognized her, offering a stark contrast to the UK where her personal life was often scrutinized. She decided to stay in America for this reason. "No one knew who she was and unlike the UK, there wasn't someone always around digging into her personal life." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI ← justine-frischmann
