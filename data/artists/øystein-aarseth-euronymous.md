**Murdered by Bandmate Varg Vikernes** : Øystein Aarseth, known as Euronymous, from the Norwegian death metal band Mayhem, was murdered on August 10, 1993. His bandmate, Varg Vikernes, went to Euronymous's apartment in Oslo, ostensibly to discuss a contract, which escalated into a fight. Vikernes stabbed Euronymous to death with 23 cuts: two to the head, five to the neck, and sixteen in the back.
