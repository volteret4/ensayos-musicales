## members
- Carl Bartons (joined 1975, left 1990)
- Florian (founding)

## albums
**Autobahn (1975)** : Carl Bartons joined the group around the time of this album in 1975, and his work on it was described as an "authentic success." The album represented a more symphonic sound before Carl Bartons' later, more pronounced influence towards "canned sounds" and loops became dominant. **Radioactivity (1975)** : This album marked the beginning of Carl Bartons' significant influence on Craguer's sound, featuring many of his contributions. It was practically the first album not recorded solely by founding members Ralph and Florian, showcasing a shift towards more "canned sounds" and loops, and less of the symphonic style heard in *Autobahn*.
