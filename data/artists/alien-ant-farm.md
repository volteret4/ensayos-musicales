## curiosities
**Spanish Bus Crash & Dryden Mitchell's Injury (2002)** : In May 2002, Alien Ant Farm's bus crashed into a parked car about 100 miles outside Madrid, Spain. The driver was killed instantly, and the band suffered multiple broken bones. Lead singer Dryden Mitchell sustained the most severe injury, breaking his C2 vertebrae, an injury compared to Christopher Reeve's. He underwent fusion surgery, had to wear a halo brace on his head for three months (secured by screws drilled into his skull), and endured months of therapy. He still experiences nerve damage, including reduced feeling in his fingertips, making guitar playing very difficult, and temporary arm numbness if he moves his head down.
