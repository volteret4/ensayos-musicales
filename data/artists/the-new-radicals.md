## curiosities
**Distinguished as a Piano-Based "One Hit Wonder"** : The New Radicals are widely recognized as a "one hit wonder" of the late 1990s, specifically noted as a piano-based act within the alt rock world. Their prominence stemmed from a single, successful piano-driven track from their album "Maybe You've Been Brainwashed Too." "this piano-based single became one of the great one hit wonders of the late 90s. Not just a one hit wonder from the alt rock world, but a piano-based, one hit wonder period." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g
**Functioning as a Solo Project** : The New Radicals operated more as a vehicle for Greg Alexander than a conventional band, with him being the only permanent member and Daniel Brisebois as a collaborator. This structure effectively made it Alexander's primary creative outlet.
