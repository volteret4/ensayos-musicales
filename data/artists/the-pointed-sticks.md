## curiosities
**First Canadian Band to Sign to Stiff Records – Power Pop Side of Vancouver Punk** : The Pointed Sticks formed in Vancouver in 1978, five former members overlapping with D.O.A. and the Subhumans. Their sound was more melodic and power pop-leaning than their contemporaries — five members singing harmonies was unusual in punk. Their name came from a Monty Python sketch about self-defense against pointed sticks. They became the first Canadian band to sign to Stiff Records in the UK, home to the Damned, Nick Lowe, Elvis Costello, and Ian Dury. Several strong singles and one album followed. Their relative accessibility broadened the audience for Vancouver punk. "Stiff Records, one of the groundbreaking indie labels in the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0X7TQMGgqbY
