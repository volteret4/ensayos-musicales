# artist - Ike Turner and his Kings of Rhythm

## songs
**Rocket 88 (1951)** : Recorded on March 3, 1951, this song about an Oldsmobile, performed for Jackie Brenston and his Delta Cats, is considered by many musicologists to be the first true Rock and Roll record, marking "Ground Zero for Rock and Roll." "That's the day Ike Turner and his Kings of Rhythm recorded a song about an old's mobile for Jackie Brenston and his Delta Cats. The song was called Rocket 88, and for many musicologists, that was the first true Rock and Roll record." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
