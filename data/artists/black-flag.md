# artist - Black Flag

## members
- Greg Ginn
- Henry Rollins
- Henry Rollins (vocals by 1982)

## genres
- Hardcore
- Hardcore Punk
- Pop (with pop sensibilities)

## labels
- SST

## albums
**Damaged (1981)** : This album was pivotal for Nate Mendel's understanding of what hardcore was about. He initially didn't understand it but then it "clicked" with its energy, aggression, and weirdness. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
**Nervous Breakdown (1979) – EP** : The first release on the SST label in 1979. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8

## songs
**TV Party (1982)** : A "fun dumb song" from 1982, with Henry Rollins on vocals. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Wasted (1979)** : A 51-second track from the EP "Nervous Breakdown", released in 1979, described as reflecting the typical sentiments of a hardcore kid at the time. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8

## curiosities
**Collaboration on Mike Watt's Album (1995)** : Members of Black Flag contributed to Mike Watt's solo album "Ball-Hog or Tugboat," which also featured Dave Grohl. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**Documented Moshing (1980)** : Featured in Penelope Spheeris's 1980 movie "The Decline of Western Civilization" with audience members moshing to their music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Important American Indie Act** : Became one of the most important American indie acts of all time, particularly after Henry Rollins joined as their vocalist. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Influence on Dave Grohl** : Dave Grohl learned to play drums and guitar by playing along to their records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Label Genesis** : Guitarist Greg Ginn transformed his SST company into a record label in 1978 specifically to distribute Black Flag's material because no other label would sign them. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Nate Mendel's Formative Years** : Nate Mendel's formative musical knowledge for about 10 years was primarily focused on hardcore, sparked by "Damaged." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
