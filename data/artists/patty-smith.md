## curiosities
**Early UK Punk Performance** : Patty Smith, referred to as a "punk priestess," played a show in England a couple of months before The Ramones' July 1976 appearances. Her performance was one of the few instances of US-style punk rock exposure in the UK before the Ramones' more significant impact.
