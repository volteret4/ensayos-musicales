# artist - Patty Smith

## genres
- Punk rock

## curiosities
**Early UK Punk Performance** : Patty Smith, referred to as a "punk priestess," played a show in England a couple of months before The Ramones' July 1976 appearances. Her performance was one of the few instances of US-style punk rock exposure in the UK before the Ramones' more significant impact. "Outside of a show, punk priestess Patty Smith had played a couple of months earlier, the Ramones appearance would be England's first proper exposure to US style punk rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
