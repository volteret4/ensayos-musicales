**Struggling Against Beatle Sound** : Despite their strong consolidation in Ritman Blues, The Jarpers struggled to gain traction in the wake of the immense popularity of The Beatles, The Hoos, and The Kings. Their Ritman Blues sound, which they favored, didn't resonate as much with a public more attuned to the "Beatle sound." **Unstable Lineup and Internal Strife** : The Jarpers were characterized by numerous internal fights and a perpetually unstable lineup, despite attracting some of the greatest guitarists in music history.
