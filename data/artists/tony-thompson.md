## curiosities
**Gated Reverb Snare Demonstration** : Drummer Tony Thompson's powerful snare hits effectively demonstrate the sound of a reverb gate maxing out, producing a distinct crackle that follows the initial crack of the drumstick. This technique was a signature element contributing to the big, powerful snare drum sound highly sought after in the 1980s. "You can actually hear the reverb gate max out when Tony Thompson hits his snare really, really hard. That's the crackle that comes after the crack of the stick." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TsiYLGGSJ2U
