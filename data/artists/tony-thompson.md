## curiosities
**Drumming Influence on Dave Grohl** : Dave Grohl (Nirvana, Foo Fighters) expressed gratitude to Tony Thompson, acknowledging that he had "ripped off" Thompson's drumming style throughout his career, including on Nirvana's "Nevermind" album. Thompson's response was simply, "I know." "And Girl told Thompson, man, I just want to thank you because I owe you so much for ripping you off my whole life. And you know what Tony Thompson said? I know." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 1 - Disco Doesn't Suck (Encore) | https://www.ftr70.com/blog/tag/70s+podcast
**Gated Reverb Snare Demonstration** : Drummer Tony Thompson's powerful snare hits effectively demonstrate the sound of a reverb gate maxing out, producing a distinct crackle that follows the initial crack of the drumstick. This technique was a signature element contributing to the big, powerful snare drum sound highly sought after in the 1980s. "You can actually hear the reverb gate max out when Tony Thompson hits his snare really, really hard. That's the crackle that comes after the crack of the stick." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TsiYLGGSJ2U ← tony-thompson
