## curiosities
**Formation and Early Success** : The Skids, an important early Scottish Celtic punk band from Dunfermline, formed in 1977 when Stuart Adamson assembled a group of friends. They released several strong singles between 1978 and 1979, with one reaching the top 10 of the UK singles chart. "They came together in 1977 when Stuart Adamson roped together a bunch of friends. The Skids ended up having several strong singles out of the gate through 1978 and 1979. One of them reached into the top 10 of the UK singles chart." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Post-Breakup Projects** : The group disbanded in 1982, leading its members in different directions. Guitarist Stuart Adamson went on to form Big Country, one of the most successful Scottish bands of the 1980s. "The group lasted until 1982 when they cleaved in several different directions. Guitarist Stuart Adamson's next project was Big Country, one of the most successful Scottish bands of the 80s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Reunions** : The Skids reformed for a three-year period following a reunion in 2007, and another reunion initiated in 2016 has remained active. Stuart Adamson was not part of these reunions. "Meanwhile the Skids reformed for three years following a reunion in 2007. And another reunion in 2016 has stuck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
