# artist - SWMRS

## members
- Jacob Danger Armstrong (temporary appearance)
- Joey Armstrong (drums)

## albums
**Berkeley's On Fire (2018)** : The second album released under the name SWMRS.
**Don't Be a Dick (2011)** : The debut record, produced by Billy Joe Armstrong, when the band was known as Emily's Army.
**Drive North (2016)** : The first album released under the name SWMRS.
**Lost at 17 (2013)** : The second album, produced by Billy Joe Armstrong, when the band was known as Emily's Army.

## songs
**Berkeley's On Fire (2018)** : The title track and first single from the 2018 album.

## curiosities
**Billy Joe Armstrong's Production** : Joey Armstrong's father, Billy Joe Armstrong, produced their first two albums.
**Previous Name Emily's Army** : The band was initially known as Emily's Army and underwent a series of name changes before settling on SWMRS.
