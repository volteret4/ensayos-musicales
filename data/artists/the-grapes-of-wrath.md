## curiosities

**Kevin Kane's Solo Career** : After the band's split, Kevin Kane pursued a solo career. "Kevin went solo." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE

**Partial Reunion and Acoustic Gigs (1999)** : A partial reunion of the band occurred in 1999, resulting in the *Field Trip* album, followed by a series of acoustic performances. "a partial grapes of Wrath reunion in 1999 which resulted in an album entitled Field Trip, Acoustic Gigs followed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Radio Presence (Late 1980s-Early 1990s)** : The band released many singles during this period and became a consistent presence on radio stations. "There were plenty of singles during that period. But Grapes of Wrath became a staple on the radio for a while." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**The Grapes of Wrath Formation (1983)** : The band was formed in Kelowna in 1983 by Kevin Kane, Chris Hooper, and Tom Hooper. "They came from Kelona in 1983. Three guys here, Kevin Kane and Chris and Tom Hooper." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**The Grapes of Wrath Split (Reasons)** : The band eventually split, possibly due to creative differences or an issue regarding their name. It was suggested that the people entrusted with the legacy of John Steinbeck's novel *The Grapes of Wrath* were not pleased to share the name with the band. "But then the band split. It might have been creative differences or it might have been an issue over their name." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
