## albums
**Black Album (1991)** : Achieved a Double Diamond award, claiming 30 million sales and confirmed at least 22 million. Cited as part of the "old breed" of artists to achieve such high sales figures.

## curiosities
**Copyright Violations on Napster** : Documented 1.4 million copyright violations of 95 Metallica songs, linked to approximately 350,000 Napster users.
**Napster Lawsuit** : In April 2000, the band sued Napster after discovering unreleased Metallica files on the platform.
**Vigorous Anti-Downloading Stance** : Lars Ulrich, the drummer, was the public face of the band's strong opposition to free music downloading and their legal actions against Napster.
