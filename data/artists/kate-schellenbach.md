**Early Beastie Boys Member and Multi-Award-Winning Producer** : Kate Schellenbach is a drummer who was an early member of the Beastie Boys before their ascent to widespread fame. Beyond her musical contributions to bands like Luscious Jackson, she has forged a successful career in television production, including winning an Emmy Award as a segment producer on *The Ellen DeGeneres Show* and serving as a producer for *The Late Late Show with James Corden*.
