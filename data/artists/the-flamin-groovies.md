# artist - The Flamin Groovies

## concerts
- Roundhouse (July 4, 1976)

## curiosities
**Headliners for The Ramones (July 4, 1976)** : The Flamin Groovies were the official headliners on the bill for the July 4, 1976 concert at the Roundhouse in Camden, London, where The Ramones also played. "Two other bands were on the bill on July 4th. The stranglers started the night and the official headliners were a group called the Flamen Groovies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
