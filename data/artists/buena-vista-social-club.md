# artist - Buena Vista Social Club

## curiosities
**Extensive Takes for Trumpet Solo** : Chris Berkett worked with Buena Vista Social Club on their second album, recording in his studio in the South of France. He recalls taking 250 takes of a trumpet solo and then having to edit it all together, highlighting the patience required in production. "I worked with this band, Buena Vista Social Club. I did a record with them. I'd want the second album down in my studio in South of France.
