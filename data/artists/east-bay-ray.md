## member of

## curiosities
**Musical Influences** : Growing up in a musical family, East Bay Ray was exposed to a wide range of influences, including instrumental surf rock. His heroes included Dick Dale, George Tomscow of The Fireballs, and Scotty Moore (who played in Elvis Presley's band) due to Moore's use of echo and reverb. Ray developed a distinctive guitar sound that was appealing to hardcore fans, a style heavily influenced by surf music.
