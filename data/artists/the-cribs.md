## curiosities
**Johnny Marr's Brief Membership** : The Smiths guitarist, Johnny Marr, was a member of The Cribs for three years, joining because he was a fan of the group. "With the exception of the time Johnny Mar, yes the Smiths guitarist, was part of the band. He stuck around for three years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
**UK and European Success** : The Cribs achieved substantial stardom within the UK and Europe, selling numerous records and earning many awards, even though they did not manage to become big outside of these regions. "The cribs never managed to make it big outside the UK and Europe, but they were substantial stars at home, selling plenty of records and winning a whack of awards." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gxbuajGKbko
