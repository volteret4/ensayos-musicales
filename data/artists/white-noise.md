## albums
**Electric Storm (1968)** : This album, almost universally credited as the first official electronic music album in history, was released by White Noise. It stands as a monumental achievement, preceding the major works of later electronic music pioneers like Tangerine Dream and Kraftwerk. The album is considered a "holy grail" of electronic music, emphasizing its groundbreaking significance. Their groundbreaking work led to the release of "Electric Storm" in 1968, marking a pivotal moment in the development of the genre.
