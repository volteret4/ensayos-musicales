# artist - Holly Woodlawn

## curiosities
**Inspiration for "Walk on the Wild Side"** : Holly Woodlawn, a transsexual from Miami, was one of the true-life figures from Andy Warhol's circle whose story was recounted in the lyrics of Lou Reed's song "Walk on the Wild Side."
