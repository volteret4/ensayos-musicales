## curiosities
**Chart Success for Indie Single (May 1979)** : Their very first record released on Two-tone in May 1979, with 5,000 copies distributed, included a song that reached number six on the British charts, a rare and significant achievement for an indie record at the time.
**Foundational Influence** : Their efforts created the foundations for the ska-punk genre that continues to thrive today.
**Ska Revival Flag Carriers** : Became one of the most important flag carriers in the revival of Ska, specifically the "Third Wave Ska" movement, driven by Jerry Dammers' commitment to bringing Ska to a new generation.
