## curiosities
**Classic American Singer-Songwriter** : Tim Buckley is described as one of the "grandes nombres clásicos americanos" (great classic American names). Despite his talent, he did not achieve widespread success during his relatively short career. A memorial concert was held in his honor, at which his son, Jeff Buckley, performed his songs, marking a pivotal moment in Jeff's own emerging career. **Critical Acclaim vs. Commercial Recognition** : Although critics highly praised his albums, Tim Buckley's talent was not widely recognized until after his death in 1975. At the time of his passing, he was deeply in debt, owning only the clothes he wore, a guitar, and an amplifier. "Critics loved his albums, but he wasn't recognized for the talent that he was until well after he died in 1975 of what appears to be a heroine overdose at age 28. He was very deep in debt, and at the time of his death, owned nothing more than the clothes on his back, a guitar, and an amplifier." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk ← tim-buckley
