## curiosities
**Critical Acclaim vs. Commercial Recognition** : Critics praised his albums, but he was not widely recognized for his talent until after his death.
**Death by Heroin Overdose** : He died in 1975 at age 28 from what appeared to be a heroin overdose.
**Exploration of Diverse Genres** : His music career saw him cycle through folk, psychedelic elements, jazz, funk, and soul.
**Father of Jeff Buckley** : Tim Buckley is the father of musician Jeff Buckley, whom he only met once when Jeff was eight years old.
**Financial Struggles** : At the time of his death, he was deep in debt and owned only his clothes, a guitar, and an amplifier.
**Marriage and Divorce from Mary Guibert** : He met and married Mary Guibert in 1965 after meeting in French class in high school in 1964. They divorced about a month before their son Jeff was born.
**Self-Destructive Behavior** : Tim Buckley was known to pursue self-destructive behavior.
**Started as Folk Singer** : Tim Buckley began his career as a folk singer in the 1960s.
