## songs
**Cloud Nine (1968)** : This song is highlighted as a track that listeners can use for comparison with Sly and the Family Stone's "Dance to the Music" to observe a common musical thread and connection, implying a shared stylistic or structural influence from the early 70s sound. "or definitely cloud nine by the temptations and listen for yourself and see if you can find that thread in that connection." ← For the Record - The 70s > FTR70 Sound Bite - Remembering Sly Stone | https://www.ftr70.com/
**I'm Going to Make You Love Me (1968)** : The Temptations, in collaboration with The Supremes, elevated this Gamble and Huff composition to a Top 10 hit in 1968, demonstrating the early and significant influence of the songwriting pair on popular music. "I'm going to make you love me, which was turned into a Top 10 hit in 1968 by the Supremes and the Temptations." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 37 - Philly Soul and the Sound of the 70s | https://seventies.libsyn.com/ep-37-philly-soul-and-the-sound-of-the-70s
**Masterpiece (1973)** : On the Billboard Top 40 chart for the week ending May 19, 1973, "Masterpiece" by The Temptations was at number 40. The transcript notes that this song "is not really going to go anywhere, at least not in the pop charts." "So at number 40 is masterpiece by the temptations. That's not really going to go anywhere, at least not in the pop charts." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 39 - American Top 40 - Keeping Our Feet on the Ground and Reaching for the Stars (Part 1) | https://seventies.libsyn.com/ep-39-american-top-40-keeping-our-feet-on-the-ground-and-reaching-for-the-stars-part-1
**Papa Was a Rollin' Stone (1972)** : Critic Greal Marcus mentioned this song as one of the powerful and clear records that defined the early 1970s soundscape, representing the collective musical response of black artists of the era. "The temptations Papa was a rolling stone." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 22 - Power to the People - The Music of the Black Power Movement | https://seventies.libsyn.com/022-power-to-the-people-62520-11-01-am ← the-temptations
