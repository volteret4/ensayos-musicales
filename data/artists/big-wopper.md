# artist - Big Wopper

## curiosities
**Plane Crash Death (1959)** : Big Wopper was killed in a small plane crash in a cornfield outside Mason City, Iowa, on February 3, 1959. This tragedy, which also claimed the lives of Buddy Holly and Ritchie Valens, is historically referred to as "the day the music died."
