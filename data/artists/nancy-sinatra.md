# artist - Nancy Sinatra

## songs
**These Boots Are Made for Walkin' (1966)** : This song was part of the loud music bombardment directed at the Branch Davidian cult compound in Waco, Texas, in 1993. It was one of the methods used by authorities to try and break the will of David Koresh and his followers during a 51-day standoff. "That playlist included AQ Breaking Heart by Billy Ray Cyrus, Nancy Sinatra, these boots are made for Wacan, some chanting by Tibetan monks, songs by Andy Williams, and some Christmas sing-alongs by Mitch Miller from the 1950s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1wdsGt07Gg
**You Only Live Twice (1967) – Title Theme** : Nancy Sinatra, daughter of legendary singer Frank Sinatra, sang the title theme for the 1967 James Bond film "You Only Live Twice." While a different version of the song became a hit single, her rendition was the one featured in the movie and heard by cinema audiences. "Nancy Sinatra, daughter of Frank got the gig." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo
