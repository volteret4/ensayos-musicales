# artist - Nancy Sinatra

## songs
**You Only Live Twice (1967) – Title Theme** : Nancy Sinatra, daughter of legendary singer Frank Sinatra, sang the title theme for the 1967 James Bond film "You Only Live Twice." While a different version of the song became a hit single, her rendition was the one featured in the movie and heard by cinema audiences.
