# artist - Lee DeForest

## instruments
- Audio Amplifier
- Triode Vacuum Tube

## curiosities
**Accidental Gas Flame Observation** : While conducting an experiment, DeForest mistakenly believed he saw a gas flame shiver in response to electromagnetic signals. Although his conclusion was scientifically incorrect, this observation inspired him to create the triode vacuum tube. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**First Audio Amplifier Prototype (1914)** : DeForest’s work led to the first prototype amplifier, which was quickly adopted for use in telephones, radio, movies, and public address systems. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Invention of the Triode Vacuum Tube (1912)** : Working by 1912, this tube could amplify any electrical signal, including sound from a microphone. It is the foundational technology for all modern audio amplification. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
