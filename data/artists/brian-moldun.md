# artist - Brian Moldun

## member of
- The Upholsterers

## instruments
- drums

## curiosities
**Hidden Singles Initiative** : Brian Moldun collaborated with Jack White in hiding about 100 vinyl copies of their 3-track 7-inch single, "Your Furniture was always dead, I was just afraid to tell you," within furniture they had reupholstered. "Jack and Brian hid some singles inside couches and chairs that they were fixing up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8
**Upholstery Shop Mentor** : Brian Moldun ran an upholstery shop where Jack White (then Jack Gillis) apprenticed at age 15, introducing him to punk rock. Moldun convinced Jack to make a record together, playing drums himself while Jack played guitar. "When he was 15, Jack became an apprentice to a guy named Brian Moldun, who ran a place that re-apholstered furniture." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8
