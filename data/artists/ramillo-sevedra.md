## curiosities
**Kurt Cobain Alive Theory** : Ramillo Sevedra, a singer from Peru who strongly resembles Kurt Cobain, sparked a conspiracy theory when footage of him performing "Come As You Are" on local TV went viral, suggesting Cobain was hiding in South America. The theory was debunked because Sevedra plays guitar right-handed, whereas Kurt Cobain was left-handed.
