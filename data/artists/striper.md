## curiosities
**Acronym Meaning** : The band's name, Striper, is an acronym for "Salvation Through Redemption, Yielding Peace, Encouragement, and Righteousness," described as a "strain" of an acronym. "By the way, Striper stands for salvation through redemption, yielding peace, encouragement, and righteousness." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Image and Stage Antics** : The band was known for their distinctive visual image, dressing in "bumblebee spandex," and for their unusual stage performance of firing Bibles into the crowd using specially designed cannons. "They all dressed in bumblebee spandex and fired bibles into the crowd, using specially designed cannons." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Mainstream Breakthrough in the 1980s** : Striper was an Orange County band that successfully broke through into mainstream consciousness, becoming known for their unique blend of wholesome messages and metal sound. "It took an orange county band called Striper to break through into mainstream consciousness." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Sound and Appeal** : Striper's musical style was compared to a cross between Van Halen and Def Leppard, allowing them to attract metalheads despite their explicitly wholesome message. "Striper sounded a lot like a cross between Van Halen and Def Leopard." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
