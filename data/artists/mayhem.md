## curiosities
**Band Member Murdering Another** : Mayhem is cited as the only known instance where one band member murdered another. This occurred when Varg Vikernes killed his bandmate Øystein Aarseth (Euronymous) on August 10, 1993, following disputes over the band's direction and philosophical points concerning the Norwegian black metal scene. "Marways Mayhem, the only time I know of, where one band member murdered another." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q6dAX6HCjgw
**Extreme Inter-Band Violence and Murder** : Mayhem was involved in what is described as the "most violent rock feud of them all." After bandmate "Dead" committed suicide, members Varg Vikernes and Euronymous argued over what to do with fragments of Dead's skull. Euronymous proposed making a necklace from them and blowing up a cathedral in Dead's honor. Vikernes, who disagreed with these ideas, became convinced Euronymous was plotting to kill him. This led Vikernes to stab Euronymous 23 times, resulting in Vikernes's arrest and imprisonment. "Two members, Varg Vicarness and Uranamis, argued over what to do with the pieces of skull blown off. When bandmate Dead, seriously, that was his name, Dead, committed suicide." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUr9sjWgUcw
