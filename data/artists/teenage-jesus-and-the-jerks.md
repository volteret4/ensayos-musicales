## songs
**Burning Rubber (1978)** : This track, featuring Lydia Lunch, is described as one of the more accessible songs from the avant-garde New York No Wave scene. Hearing it in 1978 would have been a "tough listen for most," marking it as distinct from bands like Van Halen or The Clash. "This is Teenage Jesus in the Jerks with their front person Lydia Lunch.
