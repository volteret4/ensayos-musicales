# artist - Dave Matthews Band

## curiosities
**Chicago River Raw Sewage Incident – August 8, 2004, Kinzie Street Bridge, 800 lbs of Waste onto Tourist Boat** : At 1:18 pm on August 8, 2004, a Dave Matthews Band tour bus driven by Stephen Wooll crossed the Kinzie Street Bridge in Chicago — a metal grate structure over the Chicago River. Wooll took the opportunity to empty the bus's septic system through the grate. Directly beneath the bridge at that moment was the sightseeing boat *Little Lady*, loaded with approximately 100 tourists looking up at the Chicago skyline. Security cameras on the bridge captured the entire incident. Wooll was sentenced to 18 months probation, 150 hours of community service, and fined $10,000; a $70,000 lawsuit followed for violation of environmental laws. Dave Matthews and the band cooperated fully with the investigation, offering DNA samples to positively identify the waste, and donated $50,000 to Friends of the Chicago River.
