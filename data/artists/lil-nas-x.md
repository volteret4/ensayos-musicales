## songs
**Old Town Road (2018)** : This song was created by rapper Montero Hill (Lil Nas X), who, being "desperate" and seeing it as his "last shot," purchased a remix of Nine Inch Nails' "34 Ghosts 4" from Dutch beatmaker YoungKio. He wrote lyrics over it in about a day and released it independently on December 3, 2018. The song exploded thanks to YouTube and TikTok, especially after Billy Ray Cyrus got involved. It hit number one worldwide and spent a record 19 weeks at the top of the Billboard Hot 100, making Lil Nas X an international superstar. Due to commercial use of the Creative Commons-licensed "34 Ghosts 4," Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross received official songwriting credits and shared the song's chart records. "The song was Old Town Road. It hit number one of the charts around the world, spending a record 19 weeks at the top of the Billboard Hot 100." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DKdF8NgF5Bw

**Old Town Road (2019)** : This song propelled Montero Lamar Hill, known as Lil Nas X, to global fame after he independently released it on SoundCloud and iTunes using a beat bought online from producer Young Kio for $30. It gained traction through the "Yeehaw Challenge" meme, quickly rose on the Billboard charts, and secured him a deal with Columbia Records, ultimately selling 18 million copies worldwide, garnering 1.5 billion YouTube views, and over 3 billion streams. Its beat originated from a sample of Nine Inch Nails' instrumental "34 Ghosts 4," leading to Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross receiving songwriting credits. "Montero wrapped over the beats and released it independently on SoundCloud and iTunes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhdsufCUC2w
