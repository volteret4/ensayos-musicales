## songs
**Old Town Road (2018)** : This song was created by rapper Montero Hill (Lil Nas X), who, being "desperate" and seeing it as his "last shot," purchased a remix of Nine Inch Nails' "34 Ghosts 4" from Dutch beatmaker YoungKio. He wrote lyrics over it in about a day and released it independently on December 3, 2018. The song exploded thanks to YouTube and TikTok, especially after Billy Ray Cyrus got involved. It hit number one worldwide and spent a record 19 weeks at the top of the Billboard Hot 100, making Lil Nas X an international superstar. Due to commercial use of the Creative Commons-licensed "34 Ghosts 4," Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross received official songwriting credits and shared the song's chart records.
