**Judgment Night Collaboration with Dinosaur Jr. (1993)** : Del the Funky Homosapien collaborated with rock band Dinosaur Jr. for the 1993 *Judgment Night* soundtrack. This pairing was considered radical at the time due to the limited crossover between rock and hip-hop. The project was a major catalyst for the creation of rap rock and nu metal, and helped bridge the divide between rock and hip-hop fans.
