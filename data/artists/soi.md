## songs
**Change (1989)** : Soi, a highly popular singer, recorded the song "Change" (translates from Russian) with his band Kino during the final years of the Soviet period. The song was released around 1989 and became a significant political rallying cry during the rapidly changing era of 1989-1991. There are claims that Soi, along with other Russian bands, may have been supplied with songs written by Hollywood songwriters as part of a CIA operation to introduce subversive rock into the USSR.
