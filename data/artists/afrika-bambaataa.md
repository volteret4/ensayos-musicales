## songs
**Planet Rock (1982)** : This track, released by New York DJ and MC Afrika Bambaataa, is considered "Ground Zero" for the development of genres such as house, electro, garage, and Miami bass. It also had a massive impact on the future of hip-hop. The song famously features a sample from Kraftwerk's "Trans-Europe Express." It is regarded as one of the most influential records of the 1980s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
