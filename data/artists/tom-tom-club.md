## curiosities
**Formation as Side Project (1980)** : Chris France and Tina Weymouth, while still members of Talking Heads, formed the Tom Tom Club in 1980. This new venture was characterized as a funky, dance-oriented band. "There were all those talking heads albums and then in 1980, they formed the Tom Tom Club, a funky dance-oriented band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Dhh5_PDhd0
**Production Work for Other Acts** : Chris France and Tina Weymouth also worked as producers for other musical acts, often in a collaborative "tag team" approach. Their production credits include the Happy Monday's "Yes Please" album in 1992, as well as contributions to records by Ziggy Marley, the Melvoin makers, and Gorillas. "They've also worked as producers for other acts and sort of a tag team way. And that includes the Happy Monday's and the Yes Please album in 1992." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Dhh5_PDhd0
**Talking Heads Spin-off** : Formed by Chris Frantz and Tina Weymouth, Tom Tom Club emerged as a creative outlet following their work with Talking Heads. "Chris and Tina are the Tom Tom Fub." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
