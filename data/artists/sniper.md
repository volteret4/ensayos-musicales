**Joey Ramone's First Band** : This was Joey Ramone's first "real band," where he performed as the singer under the name Jeff Starship. They were active from 1972 to 1974 and specialized in writing songs about "weirdness and perversion." No known recordings exist of Joey Ramone's version of the band. "His first real band was called Sniper. Joey was their singer, going by the name Jeff Starship. And he specialized in running songs about weirdness and perversion." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
**Pre-Ramones Band – Jeff Hyman's Role** : Jeff Hyman, who would later become Joey Ramone, formed a band called Sniper. In this group, he not only played the drums but also served as the singer, showcasing his dual talents before the formation of The Ramones. "Finally, he formed a band called Sniper and as well as playing the drums he ended up being the singer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
