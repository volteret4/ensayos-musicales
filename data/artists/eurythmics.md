**Telegenic New Wave Band** : The Eurythmics were identified as one of the "Telegenic New Wave bands" that rose to prominence with the debut of music videos on MTV (1981) and MuchMusic (1983). Their visual style and sound made them stars in the burgeoning music video landscape.
