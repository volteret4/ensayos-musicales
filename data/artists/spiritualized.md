## concerts
- **CN Tower Sky Pod (1997) – Billed as "Highest Show Ever"** : On November 26, 1997, the British band Spiritualized played an invitation-only concert in the Sky Pod, the upper observation deck of the CN Tower in Toronto, Canada. This performance, 447 meters above the ground, was promoted as the "highest show ever" at the time. "On November 26th, 1997, the British band spiritualized a group known for their, uh, high-ness, played an invitation only show in the sky pod, which is the upper observation deck of the CN Tower in Toronto."
