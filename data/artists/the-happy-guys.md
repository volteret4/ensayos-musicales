## curiosities
**State-Commissioned Soviet Rock Band** : In 1968, the Soviet state commissioned the creation of an "official rock band" known as The Happy Guys. This clean-cut group was tasked with singing songs that affirmed the positivity of the world and specifically lauded the greatness of the USSR, an attempt by the authorities to control the narrative of rock music. "They were a clean cut bunch called the Happy Guys, and they sang songs that said the world is fine, and the USSR is especially great." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NyKvAhO7MHE
