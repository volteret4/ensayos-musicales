## curiosities
**Considered "Too Light" for Punk** : In the mid-2000s, Hedley was categorized as a band that was "too light" or "too poppy for punk." This led to alt-rock radio stations refusing to play their music, citing that their fans were "too young" and lacked the "true spirit of punk and alternative music." Audiences would immediately complain upon hearing their songs. "groups like Headley and Simple Plan and Good Charlotte were maybe a little too light." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o ← hedley
**Influenced by Blink-182** : Hedley is recognized as one of the artists whose work reflects the influence of Blink-182, pointing to Blink-182's impact on their sound or stylistic choices. "Some 41 good Charlotte simple-plan panic at the disco, followed boy, headly, billy talent, you would meet six, paramour, you know, you get the idea." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q-QP7ePxkQE ← hedley
