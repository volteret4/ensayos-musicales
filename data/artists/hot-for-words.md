# artist - Hot for Words

## curiosities
**YouTube Invasion Participant** : "Hot for Words" was one of 15 amateur online creators who participated in Weezer's "YouTube Invasion" promotion for the "Hurley" album. They attempted to explain the origins of the words "Weezer" and "Hurley" in a 30-minute video. "This is from somebody named Hot for Words." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
