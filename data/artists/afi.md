## curiosities
**924 Gilman Street Alumni** : AFI are listed among the alumni of 924 Gilman Street, the important all-ages punk club in West Berkeley, California. Due to the club's policy against major label bands, AFI, having achieved mainstream success, are no longer welcome there.
