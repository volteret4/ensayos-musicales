## curiosities
**New Orleans Piano Player** : Huey "Piano" Smith was one of two great New Orleans piano players brought in for Little Richard's first Specialty Records session. He played in the same style as Fats Domino and was a veteran of sessions for Domino or artists who worked in Domino's style.
