## curiosities
**Denial of Goth Affiliation** : Robert Smith later disavowed The Cure's Goth label, claiming it was a "big misunderstanding" and that they were never truly a Goth band. "And Robert Smith would later claim that the cure were never really Goth in the first place." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gd9sO7c3N1s
**Email Communication Habits** : Robert Smith of The Cure has a distinct habit of responding to emails entirely in capital letters, which is simply his preferred style and not an indication of yelling. He also limits his email interactions to only reading and responding on Thursdays. "He's not yelling at you. That's just how he does things." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
**Essential to Goth Scene** : Robert Smith is considered essential to the Goth scene, as the scene probably wouldn't be what it is today without his influence, regardless of whether he acknowledges the Goth classification. "But let's face it, the Goths seem probably would not be what it is today without Robert Smith, whether he wants to admit it or not." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=evlGhfUe6QQ
