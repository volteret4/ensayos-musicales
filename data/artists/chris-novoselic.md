**Sweet 75 (1997)** : Sweet 75's self-titled debut album was released in 1997 on DGC, Nirvana's former label. It featured Peter Buck of R.E.M., members of Ministry, and the brass section from The Tonight Show with Jay Leno's band. The album was more experimental alt-pop than grunge, leading to fan disappointment and its commercial failure.
