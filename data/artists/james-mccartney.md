# artist - James McCartney

## curiosities
**Appearances on Parents' Albums** : He has appeared on albums by both Paul and Linda McCartney.
**Idea of Forming a "Beatles Offspring" Band** : He befriended Danny Harrison (George's son), Zach Starky (Ringo's son), and Sean Lennon (John's son), and they considered forming a band together, though it never materialized.
**Own Records** : James McCartney has released several records of his own.
**Son of Paul and Linda McCartney** : James McCartney is the son of Paul and Linda McCartney.
