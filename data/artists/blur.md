## curiosities
**Alex James's Cheesemaking Career** : Alex James, the bass player for Blur, is also a master cheesemaker. His farm is located in the Cotswolds in the UK, and he named a private road on his property "Steven Street" to honor producer Steven Street, who produced albums for Blur, The Cranberries, and The Smiths. "His farm is in the Cotsweld in the UK on a road he calls Steven Street. In honor of producer Steven Street, who produced albums for Blur, The Cranberries, The Smiths and many others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO0K_N7Ys7I

**Graham Coxon's Departure (Pre-2003)** : Guitarist Graham Coxon was asked to leave the band before *Think Tank*'s release due to a "bad attitude" and for "stopped showing up to recording sessions." His absence meant the end of the original Blur lineup. "By this time, guitarist Graham Coxon had been asked to leave the band. Apparently he had a bad attitude and he had stopped showing up to recording sessions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yR3xc3ra5tE
**Influence on Cranberries' Album Art** : Blur's recent photoshoot, which featured the band members posing in suits, directly influenced The Cranberries' decision to adopt a similar style for the cover of their 1994 album, "No Need to Argue." This choice aimed to achieve a stark and unified look. "Everyone is in suits because blur at just recently posed for a photo the same way." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tbKuJR0cPs
