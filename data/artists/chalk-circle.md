**April Fool (1986)** : This debut single from 1986 serves as an example of a new generation of Canadian musicians who emerged in the 1980s. Chalk Circle recorded for the indie label Duke Street, which had access to Factor funding, illustrating how the support system began to foster talent. The song gained play on both FM and AM radio, received significant support from MuchMusic, and allowed the band to build a career primarily on domestic backing.
