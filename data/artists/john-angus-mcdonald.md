## albums

## songs
**Highway of Heroes (2009) - Co-writer** : John Angus McDonald co-wrote the song "Highway of Heroes" with his brother Colin McDonald and Gordy Johnson over the phone in about 15 minutes, just before the band's first acoustic tour in October 2009. The song was inspired by the story of Nikola Goddard, a high school friend. "I me and Janangus and Gordy Johnson wrote highway of heroes over the phone." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
