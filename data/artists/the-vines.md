## albums
**Highly Evolved (2002)** : The Vines released their debut album on July 14, 2002, after having accumulated around three dozen songs by 2001 and recording in Los Angeles. Its release coincided with albums from The Strokes, The White Stripes, The Von Bondis, The Ye-Eyes, and The Hives, contributing to the "critical mass" of the Garage Rock revival.
