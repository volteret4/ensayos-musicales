## curiosities
**Onstage Dance Influencing Slam Dancing** : Jim Decker, a player for The Crowd from Huntington Beach, had a distinctive way of dancing on stage. He would wildly swing his arms when he started, which contributed to the evolution of slam dancing as dozens of people in the crowd would imitate his movements and crash into each other. "It was the way he wildly swung his arms when he started up so it advanced and dozens of them at the same time crashing into each other." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
