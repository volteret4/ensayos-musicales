# artist - Robert Johnson

## curiosities
**Crossroads Deal with the Devil** : In the 1930s, Robert Johnson struggled to impress established guitarists and was repeatedly dismissed for his lack of skill. After a period away, he reappeared months later as a master, demonstrating unprecedented guitar techniques. This transformation was deemed impossible, leading to the legend that Johnson sold his soul to the devil at a crossroads in rural Mississippi in exchange for his virtuosity. This purported intersection, located at highways 49 and 61 near Clarksdale, Mississippi, is still visited by people today. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg
