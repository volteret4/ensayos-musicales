# artist - Robert Johnson

## curiosities
**Crossroads Legend** : In the 1930s, Robert Johnson was repeatedly told to go away by other guitarists because he wasn't good enough. When he returned just months later, he was a master, doing things no one else had thought of. This led to the story that he sold his soul to the devil at a crossroads near Clarksdale, Mississippi (highways 49 and 61) in exchange for guitar virtuosity, a site still visited by people today.
