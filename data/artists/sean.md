## curiosities
**Accosted by Robert Plant** : Sean, the drummer for The Trews, was "accosted" by Robert Plant the day after the band performed a "punky version" of Queen's "Bohemian Rhapsody" at Rexall Place. Plant expressed his displeasure over the cover, telling Sean he knew Freddie Mercury and did not appreciate the past being relived, a moment Sean recounted with disbelief given his background. "He actually accosted our drummer Sean as he was setting up his kit the next day at Calgary." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
