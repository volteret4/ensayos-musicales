## curiosities
**Disappearance After Moving to California (1990)** : Christine McKecney, a member of The Incredible String Band, moved from Scotland to California where she subsequently "fell off the grid." She was last seen hitchhiking in Arizona in 1990, and her whereabouts remain unknown. "She moved from Scotland to California where she fell off the grid.
