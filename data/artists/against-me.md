## curiosities
**Diverse Approach to Punk Revival** : Against Me!, featuring Laura Jane Grace, was cited as an example of the wide variety of approaches within the mid-decade punk revival of the 2010s. This revival embraced Garage Punk and Post Punk styles, influenced by previous punk eras while not being pure 70s, 80s hardcore, or 90s punk.
