## songs
**I Just Want to Be Your Everything (1977)** : Barry Gibb wrote this song for his brother Andy Gibb during the same writing session as "Love Is Thicker Than Water." He also provided backup vocals for the track. **Love Is Thicker Than Water (1977)** : Barry Gibb wrote the primary portion of this song for Andy Gibb in the same session as "I Just Want to Be Your Everything." Andy Gibb contributed the "thicker than water" part of the title. Barry wrote the song in "less time than it took to watch an episode of Happy Days or LeVernin Shirley" and provided backup vocals.
