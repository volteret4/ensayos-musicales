## curiosities
**Contemporary Goth-ish Sound** : Interpol, hailing from New York, is considered one of the best-known contemporary bands that, while "Goth or at least Goth-ish," seeks to preserve the spirit of 1979 Goth. "Interpol, one of the best known of the contemporary Goth or at least Goth-ish bands that seek to keep alive the spirit of 1979." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=evlGhfUe6QQ ← interpol
**Critical Acclaim and Comfort Zone** : Interpol, despite consistently receiving positive critical reception and reviews throughout their career, is noted for not significantly venturing outside their musical comfort zone. They managed to reinterpret post-punk, but maintained a distinct personal vision within that framework. "Incluso Interpol, que yo creo que es una de las bandas que mejor es crítica, si mejor de reseñas ha tenido en lo largo de su carrera, tampoco es una banda que se haya salido demasiado de su zona de confort." ← Music Radar Clan > ARCTIC MONKEYS - Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino. **Influence on Vestal Watch Design** : Interpol's music played a significant and direct role in the design process for Vestal watches. The band's tracks were reportedly listened to on a continuous loop for a couple of months by Vestal designers while they were developing new watch models, indicating a deep artistic connection. "And by the way, Interpol's music is a big part of the design process at Vestel. They listened to the band's music on a loop for a couple of months when they were putting together these watches." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mhQ36Y-c9fM ← interpol

**Pioneers of the Indie Rock Revival (Early 2000s)** : Interpol were among the "new, sharp clever bands" that emerged during the Indie Rock Revival or Post-Punk Revival period around 2000-2001. This movement was a refurbished version of old-school early alt-rock, garage rock, and punk styles. "We gave us new, sharp clever bands like the strokes and the hives, the yay-yaz, friends ferdinand, interpool, and the arctic monkeys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vJszsJQpINQ ← interpol
