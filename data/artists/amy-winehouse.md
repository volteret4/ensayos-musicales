**Death by Alcohol Toxicity (July 23, 2011)** : Amy Winehouse died tragically at age 27 on July 23, 2011, from alcohol toxicity, officially ruled a "death by misadventure." Her blood alcohol level was 0.416, more than five times the legal limit for driving, leading her to pass out, go into respiratory arrest, and fall into a coma. She was a deeply troubled woman, struggling with alcoholism, bulimia, and drug addiction. She had been diagnosed with emphysema in her mid-20s from crack cocaine use, leaving her lungs operating at only 70% capacity. She also had an irregular heartbeat, severe nicotine dependency, and recurring chest infections. In her final hours on July 22, 2011, she was in her Camden Square, London bedroom watching YouTube videos of herself and drinking heavily, after a two-week period of abstinence. Her live-in bodyguard, Andrew Morris, found her unresponsive the next afternoon. "Her blood alcohol level was 0.416. More than five times the legal limit for driving. Basically, she died of alcohol toxicity." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU

**Final Days with Bodyguard (July 20-23, 2011)** : Amy Winehouse spent her final days at her home in Camden Square, London, under the observation of her live-in bodyguard, Andrew Morris, who was tasked with keeping an eye on her. From July 20 to 22, Morris observed her engaging in "moderate drinking," mostly vodka. On the evening of July 22, her last hours were spent in her bedroom, watching YouTube videos of herself on her laptop and drinking heavily, despite having abstained for a period before hitting the bottle hard for two weeks. She appeared tipsy but not completely drunk when she briefly showed Morris a YouTube video. Morris checked on her at 2:30 AM on July 23 (she seemed asleep) and again at 10:00 AM (still seemed asleep). By 3:00 PM, he noticed she hadn't moved, and with two empty vodka bottles on the floor, he realized something was wrong, calling an ambulance at 3:54 PM. "Amy came at least once to show Andrew a YouTube video of someone she once dated. She was tipsy, but not completely drunk. That was the last time anybody saw her alive." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
