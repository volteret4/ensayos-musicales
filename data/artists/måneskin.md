## curiosities
**Eurovision Win and Global Breakthrough (2021)** : In 2021, Italy's Måneskin unexpectedly won Eurovision, an event typically ignored by non-participating countries and often associated with pop or novelty acts. Their victory marked the first time in years a rock band brought the title home, propelling them to global stardom with over half a billion collective streams within months and commencing world tours. "Manaskin winning Eurovision in 2021... because after years of the title going to pop bands, Manaskin brought the title home for rock.
