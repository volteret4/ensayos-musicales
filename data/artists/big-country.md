## curiosities
**Commercial Success** : The band experienced its most successful years in the 1980s, maintaining a dedicated cult following until their breakup in 2000. They briefly reunited in 2007 and again in 2010. "Big countries best years were in the 80s, although they maintained a cult following through to their breakup in 2000. There was a brief reunion in 2007 and then another one in 2010." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Distinctive Sound** : Big Country's sound was immediately recognizable for its guitars, which were made to sound like bagpipes, and its martial rhythm tracks. "Big Country was immediately recognizable by the guitar that sounded like bagpipes and their martial rhythm tracks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Dread Zeppelin Fan** : Stewart Adamson, a member of the band Big Country, was noted as a big fan of Dread Zeppelin. "Steward Adamson, a big country, remember them? He was a big fan of these guys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU
**Stuart Adamson's Tragic Death** : Co-founder Stuart Adamson was not involved in any of the band's reunions; he tragically took his own life in 2001 after years of struggling with alcohol abuse and depression. "Stuart Adamson sadly is not part of any of these reunions because he took his own life in 2001 after years of alcohol abuse and depression." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
