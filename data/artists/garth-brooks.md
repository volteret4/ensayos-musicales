## curiosities
**SoundScan Beneficiary - Country Sales** : Before the introduction of SoundScan technology, country records like those by Garth Brooks were widely perceived as not selling well. However, SoundScan revealed that country music was actually selling far more copies than anyone in the industry had previously realized, making artists like Brooks significant beneficiaries of the new accurate sales tracking system.
