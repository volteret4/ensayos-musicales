## curiosities
**Anti-Auto-Tune Stance** : Death Cab for Cutie maintains a strong opposition to the use of auto-tune, declaring themselves "100% dead against" it and proclaiming "death to auto-tune at every available opportunity." The band, including Ben Gibbard, ensures their recordings are "uncorrected," representing the authentic sound of the band working together in a studio without pitch modification. "They are 100% dead against the use of auto-tune." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Zniri9uSlc
**Appeal to Tweetpop Fans** : Death Cab for Cutie is mentioned as a band that appeals to Tweetpop fans, implying their music aligns with the genre's introspective, melodic, and emotionally sensitive qualities. This makes them a fitting choice for listeners who identify as shy, introspective, and find much contemporary music too loud or aggressive. "And the decemberists tend to appeal to most Tweet fans, same thing with Death Cab for Cutie." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
**Guest Musician on Sonic Highways** : Ben Gibbert, from the band Death Cab for Cutie, was a local guest musician featured on the Foo Fighters' song "Subterranean" from the "Sonic Highways" album. The track was recorded at Robert Lang Studios north of Seattle. "The local guest for this track was Ben Gibbert of Death Cab for Cutie." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
**Long-Distance Running Hobby** : Ben Gibbard has a particular enthusiasm for long-distance running. "Ben Gibber of Death Cab for Cutie had a thing for long distance running." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
**Twilight Soundtracks Contribution** : Death Cab for Cutie contributed to the various *Twilight* soundtracks that accompanied the film series. These soundtracks featured a nice selection of tunes, mostly album cuts and outtakes, from a range of artists spanning different genres. "The Paramore, Lincoln Park, Collective Soul, Death Cab for Cutie, Tom York, the Killers, Editors, Metric, Block Keys, Dead Weather, Beck, Vampire Weekend, Florence in the Machine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
