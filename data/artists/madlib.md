**Madvillainy Production Approach** : For the album Madvillainy, Madlib contributed impressive work, incorporating world music influences after traveling to Brazil during a break. He used classic breakbeat techniques and fragments from existing records. Notably, despite working on an album released in 2004, he employed completely classic manual sampling methods with a traditional sampler, resulting in slight, intentional desynchronization of samples, reminiscent of 1980s old-school hip hop. **Manual Sampling Revival** : Madlib, particularly in his work with MF Doom as Madvillain, is praised for using manual sampling processes that recall older techniques. This approach harkens back to the artisanal methods of pioneers like Cut Chemist, DJ Shadow, and J Dilla, embodying pure craftsmanship in sampling.
