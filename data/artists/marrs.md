# artist - MARRS

## genres
- Alt Rock
- Dance Music
- Hip Hop

## labels
- 4AD

## songs
**Pump Up the Volume (1987)** : Created through a groundbreaking process of deconstruction and reconstruction, using samples from over two dozen other recordings, including artists like James Brown, Run DMC, Afrika Bambaataa, Kool & The Gang, Public Enemy, Islamic music, chanting, a movie trailer, and various sound effects.

## curiosities
**4AD Studio Project** : A studio collaboration involving 4AD musicians, producers, and DJs.
**Incalculable Influence** : Their single "Pump Up the Volume" had an immense and incalculable effect on dance music, hip hop, and any form of alt-rock that integrated sampling. It is considered one of the most important indie dance records of the 1980s.
**Pioneering Sampling Technique** : Represented an unprecedented approach to music production by deconstructing and reconstructing songs entirely through sampling.
