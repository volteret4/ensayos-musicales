**Aromanticism (2017) - A Young Diamond** : This is a fantastic album, variously categorized as soul, electronic, pop, or downtempo. It is a debut album from a very young artist, merely twenty-six years old, yet it displays an incredible level of stylistic influences. The album features truly intimate, deep, and personal lyrics for such a young individual, subtly incorporating a multitude of influences. Despite its many influences, the record sounds very clean, pure, and calm, making it a small diamond.
