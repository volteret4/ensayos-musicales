## curiosities
**Puppet Show Video Project** : This was a collaborative project between Florence Welch and Isabella Summers, involving elaborate puppet shows set to music and videoed. One such track was "Little Donkey." The videos were described as "very weird and violent and kind of bloody" and were an early precursor to the "Florence and the machine" concept, where they experimented with taking on characters during performance.
