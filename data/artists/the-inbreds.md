# artist - The Inbreds

## curiosities
**Early Two-Piece Band (1992)** : The Inbreds, from Kingston, Ontario, emerged around 1992 as one of the early two-piece rock bands. Their appearance preceded the widespread trend of rock duos in the 2000s.
