## curiosities
**Departure from Zero** : Mark Wakefield was the original singer for the band Zero, featured on their 1997 demo EP. He left the band after facing numerous rejections, either due to discouragement or boredom, and subsequently became the manager for the band Taproot. "Part of the issue for zero's lack of success was, let's be honest, singer Mark Wakefield." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YcdidjWaIqE ← mark-wakefield
**Singer for Zero** : Mark Wakefield was the singer for the band Zero, the precursor to Linkin Park. He recorded the band's 1997 4-track cassette in his bedroom using a basic computer setup. "Everything was recorded in the bedroom of Singer Mark Wakefield using a root-of-entry computer setup." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM ← mark-wakefield
