# artist - Nellee Hooper

## curiosities
**Initial Producer for *I Do Not Want What I Haven't Got* (1990)** : Nellee Hooper was initially hired to produce Sinead O'Connor's second album, *I Do Not Want What I Haven't Got*. However, Sinead walked out after just two days, stating that they "couldn't get on together," leading to Chris Birkett taking over production. "Basically, the story is that they started off this album with another producer, Nelly Hooper.
