## curiosities
**Collaboration with Son** : Michael worked with his son Robert and Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, serving as a soundman for the band.
**Composer for Films** : Beyond his work with The Call, he composed music for films.
**Fatal Heart Attack** : On August 19th, 2010, Michael Been suffered a fatal heart attack at age 60 while working as a soundman for Black Rebel Motorcycle Club at a festival in Brussels.
**Father of Robert Been** : Michael Been is the father of Robert Been, who performs as Robert Turner and later reverted to Robert Been, in the band Black Rebel Motorcycle Club.
**Head Guy of The Call** : Michael Been was the lead figure in the band The Call.
**Hired Gun for Other Performers** : He also worked as a hired musician for other artists.
**Posthumous Pride in Son** : Michael Been was very proud of his son's achievements with Black Rebel Motorcycle Club.
