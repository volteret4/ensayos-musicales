## curiosities
**Florence Welch's First Band** : This punk group was Florence Welch's very first band. Florence played guitar and sang, while her friend Mira played drums. Their practice space was spray-painted with the name of Daniel Johns from Silverchair, a crush of Florence's at the time. "Florence's first band was a punk group called The Toxic Cockroaches." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=24NeCYqlVco
