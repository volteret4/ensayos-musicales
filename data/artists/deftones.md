**Extensive Use of Marshall Amplifiers** : The Deftones are listed among hundreds of musicians, both famous and lesser-known, who utilize Jim Marshall's amplifiers. "Over the decades, Jim's amplifiers became synonymous with loud, powerful rock and today they used by slash, oasis, the death tones, rob zombie, Paul Weller, some 41, pop erosion, hundreds of others, famous and not so famous." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNiL63fY1Y8
