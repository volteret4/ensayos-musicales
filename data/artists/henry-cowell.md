# artist - Henry Cowell

## curiosities
**Co-creation of the Rhythmicon (1932)** : Cowell collaborated with Russian inventor Leon Theremin to create the Rhythmicon, an early electronic rhythm device. A prototype was unveiled in 1932, with a second unit now at the Smithsonian Institute. "He eventually got hooked up with Russian inventor Leon Thereman... And together they created something they called the Rhythmicon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0 ← henry-cowell ← henry-cowell
**Quest for Precise Rhythms (1930s)** : In the 1930s, American composer Henry Cowell, bored with Western music conventions, sought new sounds, specifically rhythms that were 100% precise and more regular than a human could produce. "He specifically wanted rhythms that were 100% precise, far more regular than a human could produce." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dablAKDOOV0 ← henry-cowell ← henry-cowell
