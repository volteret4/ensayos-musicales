# artist - The Hollies

## songs
**The Air That I Breathe (1972)** : This 1972 song was the subject of an infringement claim against Radiohead's "Creep." The co-writers of "The Air That I Breathe," Mike Hazelwood and Albert Hammond, were subsequently added to the songwriting credits for "Creep." **The Air that I Breathe (1974)** : The composers of "The Air that I Breathe" successfully sued Radiohead, claiming melodic similarities with Radiohead's 1993 song "Creep," leading to them receiving songwriting credits and a share of the royalties from "Creep." One of the composers is the father of Albert Hammond Jr. of The Strokes.
