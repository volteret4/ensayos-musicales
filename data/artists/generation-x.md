# artist - Generation X

## members
- Billy Idol
- Billy Idol (William Broad)

## genres
- Pop Punk

## concerts
- **Dingwalls (July 6th, 1976, London)** : Generation X, with a young Billy Idol among its members, attended this Ramones concert, drawing inspiration that would significantly contribute to the British punk rock movement. "Generation X featuring a very young Billy Idol."
- **The Roundhouse (July 4th, 1976, London)** : Generation X, featuring a "very young Billy Idol," was present at this Ramones gig, one of two shows that profoundly transformed major players in the British punk rock explosion. "Generation X featuring a very young Billy Idol."

## songs
**Dancing with Myself (1980)** : This original "dancing, poppy punk song" was released in the fall of 1980 by Generation X, which was known as Gen X at the time. Its primary songwriter and performer was William Broad, who later became Billy Idol. ## curiosities
**Billy Idol Witnesses The Ramones (July 4, 1976)** : A "very young Billy Isle" (Idol), then part of Generation X, was present at The Ramones' influential gig at the Roundhouse in London on July 4, 1976. He was among the many future British punk figures who were "transformed by what they saw and heard." "Generation X featuring a very young Billy Isle.
