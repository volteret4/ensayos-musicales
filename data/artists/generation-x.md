## concerts
- **Dingwalls (July 6th, 1976, London)** : Generation X, with a young Billy Idol among its members, attended this Ramones concert, drawing inspiration that would significantly contribute to the British punk rock movement. "Generation X featuring a very young Billy Idol."
- **The Roundhouse (July 4th, 1976, London)** : Generation X, featuring a "very young Billy Idol," was present at this Ramones gig, one of two shows that profoundly transformed major players in the British punk rock explosion. "Generation X featuring a very young Billy Idol."

## curiosities
**Billy Idol Witnesses The Ramones (July 4, 1976)** : A "very young Billy Isle" (Idol), then part of Generation X, was present at The Ramones' influential gig at the Roundhouse in London on July 4, 1976. He was among the many future British punk figures who were "transformed by what they saw and heard." "Generation X featuring a very young Billy Isle. They were all at these gigs and they were all transformed by what they saw and heard." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q ← generation-x
**Influenced by The Ramones** : Generation X, founded by Billy Idol, was profoundly influenced by The Ramones after seeing them perform in England in 1976. Billy Idol found The Ramones "brilliant," and their style impacted how his own band chose to play. "a punk band called Generation X Which was started by someone called Billy Idol saw them play Billy Idol said he thought they were brilliant and it influenced how his band chose to play." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 57 - Punk and New Wave at New York's CBGB | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Recording at Wessex Studios** : Generation X recorded at Wessex Studios, an old church hall in North London that attracted many punk and post-punk bands for its distinctive live sound.
