# artist - Hank Harrison

## curiosities
**Belief in Daughter's Involvement in Kurt Cobain's Death (Post-1994)** : Hank Harrison, Courtney Love's biological father, publicly expressed his belief that his daughter "had anything to do with" Kurt Cobain's death. He stated she had "confessed to various aspects of it" in letters and interviews, and noted her financial benefit from the death. "Do you believe your daughter had anything to do with Kurt's death? Oh, sure." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKWgbxk46eM
**Financial Motive Regarding Kurt Cobain (Post-1994)** : Hank Harrison articulated a financial motive surrounding Kurt Cobain's death, stating that if Cobain had not died that weekend, he would have emptied bank funds and signed divorce papers and other contracts. This action, Harrison explained, would have financially cut out Courtney Love and "a lot of other people." "he would have gone to the bank and taken all the money out of the funds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKWgbxk46eM
**Public Opinion on Reopening Cobain Case (1997)** : Hank Harrison recalled being interviewed on the "Jane Houghton show" in Toronto in 1997. During this show, a survey indicated that approximately 95% of the listeners were interested in reopening the Kurt Cobain case and voted in favor of it, suggesting widespread public doubt about the official verdict. "about 95% of the people listening to the Jane Houghton show at that time where we're interested in reopening the case and voted that way, called in and voted that way." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKWgbxk46eM
