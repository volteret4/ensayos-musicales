# artist - Sloan

## genres
- Grunge (sound-alike)

## labels
- Sub Pop

## albums
**Smeared (1992)** : Released in 1992, this album was their first and led to the band being signed to Sub Pop. Sloan's emergence from Halifax contributed to the city being considered the "new Seattle" for a period, and the band went on to become a major part of the Can Rock Revolution. "Sloan, from the smeared album of 1992, they would go on to and continue to be a major part of the Can Rock Revolution." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo

## curiosities
**Canada's Answer to Nirvana** : Sloan, from Halifax, was initially perceived as "Canada's answer to Nirvana," leading to Halifax being briefly considered "Seattle East" due to the widespread frenzy and saturation of grunge. "Sloan originally pegged as Canada's answer to Nirvana. And for a short while, Halifax was considered to be Seattle East." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tFBSrKMEZxI
**Collaborated with Weezer** : The guys from Sloan collaborated with Weezer on the album "Ratitude," though their contributions were uncredited and recorded in 2003. "And the guys from Sloan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYRPxtP61JM
**Featured in "In Their Own Words" Series** : Sloan was the first band to be featured in the "In Their Own Words" series, which aims to bring multiple band members into the studio to share their personal history. "Sloan was first..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bI6TTA-fn7c
**Major Part of Can Rock Revolution** : Sloan became and continued to be a major part of the Can Rock Revolution, solidifying their place in Canadian music history through their sustained influence and success. "they would go on to and continue to be a major part of the Can Rock Revolution." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
**Signed to Sub Pop** : Sloan was signed to Sub Pop for their first album, "Smeared." This significant label signing, coupled with their origin in Halifax, contributed to the city's brief reputation as the "new Seattle" in the early 1990s. "Sloan, from the smeared album of 1992, they would go on to and continue to be a major part of the Can Rock Revolution." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
