## albums
**Elastica (1995)** : The band's first album contained songs like "Connection" and "Waking Up," both of which led to legal disputes due to their similarities with tracks by other artists. As a result of these cases, Elastica was ordered to surrender a portion of the royalties from this album to both Wire and The Stranglers. "Elastica had to surrender a certain amount of the royalties from that first album to Wire in exchange for... paying too much homage to them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L09R2evjyMc
**Elastica (1995) – Debut Album** : Released in March 1995, this album became the fastest-selling debut album in UK history, achieving worldwide sales of over a million copies and earning numerous awards, including a Mercury Prize nomination. It spawned five hit singles. "When Elastica's debut album was released in March 1995, it became the fastest-selling debut album in the history of the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Elastica (1995) – Debut Record** : Released in 1995, this album quickly became the fastest-selling debut record since the first Oasis album released the previous year. "When that first record came out, it became the fastest-selling debut record since the first oasis album the year before." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Elastica (1995) – Fresh and Fun Debut** : This self-titled debut album was described as "fresh, fun, and very good," contributing to Elastica becoming a favorite target of coverage for the British music media. **Elastica (1995) – Self-Titled Debut** : Elastica's debut album, released in March 1995, was characterized as fresh, fun, good, and familiar-sounding. **The Menace (2000) – Second Album** : This album was released in the spring of 2000 after a long wait from fans, but by then Britpop had faded, the songs were perceived as lacking, and sales were dismal.
