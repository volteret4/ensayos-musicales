# artist - Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky

## albums
**The Nutcracker Suite (1909) - First Record Album** : A four-disc set of Tchaikovsky's "Nutcracker Suite" was released by Odion Records in April 1909, making it the first compilation packaged as a "record album." Each disc was held in a sleeve within a book, a format that resembled a photo album, leading to the adoption of the term. The album cost 16 shillings, which would be equivalent to just over $100 today.
