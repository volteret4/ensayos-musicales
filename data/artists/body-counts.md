## concerts

## curiosities
**Lollapalooza Performance (1991)** : Body Counts was among the bands performing at the first Lollapalooza festival, an initiative to bring the alternative music scene to a wider audience in America. "It featured Jane's Addiction as the headliners along with up and coming bands like Nine Inch Nails and the Rollins Band along with Ice Tea and Body Counts and the Butthole Surfers and Suzy and the Banshees and more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xdn31jMOOA4
