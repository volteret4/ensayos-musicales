# artist - Imperial Orchestra

## curiosities
**Association with Richard Rodgers** : The Imperial Orchestra was highlighted on a marquee sign at the Imperial Theatre in New York City, alongside Richard Rodgers and Lorenz Hart, as part of a promotion for a new record release in 1938. This specific marquee setup was photographed to create what became the music industry's first-ever album cover artwork.
