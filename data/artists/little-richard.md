## curiosities
**Breaking the Color Barrier** : Richard was the first Black artist to truly cross over to white audiences, attracting integrated crowds to his high-energy shows and helping to break down racial barriers in the culture. His influence was massive, affecting artists from The Beatles and David Bowie to Lemmy of Motorhead. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Rock's First Openly Gay Star** : Richard identified as gay or bisexual and used flamboyant, effeminate mannerisms and makeup to define his stage persona. He famously challenged Elvis Presley's "King" title by proclaiming himself "the queen" of rock and roll. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
