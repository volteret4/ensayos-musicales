## songs
**Carry On Wayward Son (1976)** : As the songwriter for Kansas, Kerry Livgren later revealed in a 2004 interview that this song was autobiographical, reflecting his personal "spiritual sojourn, looking for truth and meaning." He became a born-again Christian in late 1979 and subsequently became a Christian rock artist. "Wayward's Son was about his own spiritual journey. ## curiosities
**Spiritual Journey and Christian Conversion** : Kerry Livgren, songwriter for Kansas, described his songs like "Carry On Wayward Son" and "Dust in the Wind" as reflections of his "spiritual sojourn." He became a born-again Christian in late 1979 and transitioned into a Christian rock artist.
