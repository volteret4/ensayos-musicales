# artist - Albert Hammond

## songs
**The Air That I Breathe (1972)** : Albert Hammond co-wrote this song for The Hollies. He later contended that Radiohead's song "Creep" sounded too much like "The Air That I Breathe," leading to him receiving songwriting credits on "Creep." "Radiohead has to share songwriting credits on the song Creep with a guy named Albert Hammond, who contended that Creep sounded too much like a song he wrote in 1972 for the hallways called The Air That I Breathe." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
