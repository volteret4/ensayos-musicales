## songs
**I Will Survive (1996) – Gloria Gaynor Cover** : Cake recorded their cover of Gloria Gaynor's "I Will Survive" for their 1996 album "Fashion Nugget." This version included a swear word in the first verse that was not in the original, a change that Gloria Gaynor explicitly "hates." Radio broadcasts of Cake's cover have typically been edited to remove this "bad word." The Cake version is characterized by flipping the gender role, being played at a slower tempo, and being an "enduring alt rock" and "quirky alt rock version of a disco anthem." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
