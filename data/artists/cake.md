## songs
**I Will Survive (1996)** : This alt-rock cover of Gloria Gaynor's disco anthem, recorded for the album *Fashion Nugget*, flips the gender role of the narrator and is played at a slower tempo than the original. It gained enduring popularity despite Gloria Gaynor's dislike for it due to the inclusion of a swear word in the first verse, which is typically edited out for radio play. "It flips the gender role and is played slower." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Short Skirt/Long Jacket** : This song was prominently featured as the opening theme for every episode of the TV show "Chuck," showcasing another successful example of music licensing for television. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfie4YYncxk
**The Distance** : This song features an example of a trumpet mute being used. The mute first appears at about the 33-second mark and then weaves in and out of the song's arrangement for the duration. "It first comes in at about 33 seconds and then weaves in and out for the rest of the song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8GU8ZpSgY8
