## curiosities
**Obscurity and Breakup** : The Modern Lovers, fronted by Jonathan Richman, toiled in obscurity for several years. Despite their distinctive sound, which was ahead of its time, they did not achieve widespread recognition. The band ultimately broke up in 1974. "Jonathan and his band, the modern lovers, toiled in obscurity for a couple of years before breaking up in 1974." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4 ← the-modern-lovers
**Post-Breakup Success of Members** : Following the band's dissolution in 1974, two of its members went on to achieve greater success. Drummer David Robertson found work with another Boston group, The Cars. Keyboardist Jerry Harrison joined the new band The Talking Heads. Both The Cars and The Talking Heads would later become significant components of the American New Wave era. "However, two members of the band went on to bigger and better things. Drummer David Robertson found work with another Boston group called The Cars and Keyboard is Jerry Harrison soon hooked up with a new band called The Talking Heads." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4 ← the-modern-lovers
