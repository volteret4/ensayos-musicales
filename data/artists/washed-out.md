**Song for Relaxation (Year not specified)** : A song by Washed Out, approximately four minutes long, is mentioned in the context of a study by the British Academy of Sound Therapy. This study suggested that optimal relaxation occurs after listening to songs between nine and thirteen minutes long, implying that two or three songs of similar length and style, like this one, could create a "blissed out" state.
