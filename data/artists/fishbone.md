## curiosities
**Angelo Moore Stage Dive Lawsuit (2014)** : The band had to pay $1.4 million in 2014 to a woman whose collarbone was broken when singer Angelo Moore stage-dived on top of her. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Enduring Legacy** : Despite challenges, Fishbone perseveres and maintains a fanatical following. They are cited as a significant influence by bands such as No Doubt, Sublime, 311, Goldfinger, Rage Against the Machine, and Faith No More. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Festival Presence** : Were main stage performers on the 1993 Lollapalooza tour. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Formation and Early Sound** : Formed in 1979 by four black musicians from South Central Los Angeles, later growing to six members. Their music was socially conscious, anti-racist, clever, witty, and generally fun. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Genre Blending Pioneers** : Recognized as one of the most important groups for their innovative blending of punk, funk, ska, hard rock, soul, and elements of hip hop and rap. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Influence on Other Bands** : Played alongside punk bands and were particularly admired by a group that eventually formed the Red Hot Chili Peppers. The Beastie Boys later recruited Fishbone to open one of their tours. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Label Disputes** : Faced various record company problems. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Legal Troubles** : Sued by a woman in Philadelphia for $1.4 million after one of the members stage-dived on top of her. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Lollapalooza Performance (1991)** : Fishbone performed at the very first Lollapalooza show, which began outside Phoenix on July 18, 1991. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Peak Years** : Their most impactful period spanned from 1988 to 1994. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Personnel Problems** : The band experienced internal issues, including one member quitting to join a religious cult. When another member attempted to retrieve him, that member was charged with attempted kidnapping. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Pioneering Status** : Described as all-black punk-funk pioneers. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Warped Tour Performer (1996)** : Booked for the 1996 Warped Tour as part of the content pivot to punk and skate culture music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Warped Tour Veteran** : Participated as a "seriously grizzled veteran" of the tour. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
