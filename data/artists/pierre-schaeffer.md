# artist - Pierre Schaeffer

## curiosities
**Music Concrete Pioneer (1940s)** : French composer who, in the 1940s, originated the "music concrete" method of songwriting, which involved recording various sounds (trains, vehicles, kitchen utensils, human body vibrations), cutting them into bits, and rearranging them into audio collages, with an example piece from 1948. "He made various recordings, trains and other vehicles, kitchen utensils, even vibrations of the human body, and then he cut them into bits and then rearrange them into audio collages." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnQ1pNbIUjw
