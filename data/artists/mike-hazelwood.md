## curiosities
**Involvement in Copyright Case** : As a partner to Albert Hammond Sr., Mike Hazelwood was involved in the copyright infringement lawsuit against Radiohead regarding "Creep." He now receives 25% of the writing credit for the song due to its similarities to "The Air That I Breathe."
