# artist - Taj Khrishlo

## curiosities
**Co-founder of Fella Production Company** : Taj Khrishlo co-founded the production company Fella, which broke out with the music video for "Popstar" by DJ Khaled, Drake, and Justin Bieber. He and Director X are described as "two kids from Brampton, Ontario that went out to make art that broke out to the world." "We broke our production company, Fella, with this music video for DJ Khaled, Drake and Bieber called Popstar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
**Music Video Director / Podcast Host** : Taj Khrishlo is a co-host of the new podcast ArtCotex, which focuses on interviewing music video directors. He is also involved in the music video industry and co-founded the production company Fella with Director X. "I have two of the hosts of ArtCotex with me here. We have Taj Khrishlo and DirectorX." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS8V3kYXT6w
