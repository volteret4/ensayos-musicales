## curiosities
**Band Name Changes and Lineup** : Joey Armstrong's band initially known as Emily's Army, went through multiple name changes before settling on SWMRS (spelled SWMRS). During its lineup changes, his younger brother, Jacob Armstrong, temporarily played bass. "That group was known as Emily's Army, and they went through a bunch of name changes until 2011...There were some lineup changes, including the temporary appearance of little brother Jacob Armstrong on bass. And then the group changed their name to Swimmers, which is spelled SWMRS." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
**Inspiration and Band Formation** : Joseph Marciano Armstrong, nicknamed Joey after Joey Ramone, was born on February 28th, 1995. He decided to form his own band, Emily's Army, with a friend named Cole after they watched the movie "School of Rock" starring Jack Black. "And on February 28th, 1995, Joseph Marciano Armstrong came into the world. He's called Joey after Joey Ramon. Joey finally decided to form his own band after he and his buddy Cole saw School of Rock with Jack Black for the first time. That group was known as Emily's Army." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
