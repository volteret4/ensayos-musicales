# artist - Philadelphia Orchestra

## curiosities
**Binaural Broadcast (1933)** : The Philadelphia Orchestra participated in an EMI experiment on April 27, 1933, where their performance was broadcast binaurally down telephone wires to Washington, D.C. This experimental transmission used a rubber head named Oscar, fitted with microphones in place of ears, to capture sound more like human hearing. "A performance by the Philadelphia Orchestra was broadcast this way down some telephone wires to Washington, D.C." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbSmBrhNACg
