## curiosities
**Johnny Otis's Proud Discovery** : Etta James was a musical artist whom Johnny Otis was most proud of discovering. Coincidentally, Otis died three days before James, leading her to overshadow him in obituaries, an outcome he would have desired. "He died three days before the discovery of whom he was most proud, ethy James, and she overshadowed him in the obituaries, as he would have wanted." ← REUPLOAD Episode 71： ＂Willie and the Hand Jive＂ by Johnny Otis | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ez6BCUlI6Rg
**Recording at Fame** : Etta James recorded at Fame Recording Studios, a facility recognized for offering black artists a place to work without disturbance during a time of widespread racism and discrimination. "Wilson Pickett, Aretha Franklin, Edda James. They all recorded there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk ← etta-james ← etta-james
**Reported Refusal to Perform at Zaire 74 (1974)** : Etta James reportedly refused to perform at Zaire 74, contributing to the festival's challenges and its eventual financial failure.
