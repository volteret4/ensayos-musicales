**Major 90s Goth Band** : Sisters of Mercy were identified as one of the major Goth bands of the 1990s, a genre that often incorporated elements of the occult and the bizarre, extending its culture to clubs and magazines beyond just the music. "The major goth bands of the 90s were Bauhaus and Suzy and the Banshee's, Sisters of Mercy, and if we go back to the very beginning we'll find that Joy Division was a major influence." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
