# artist - Nathaniel Baldwin

## instruments
- Headphones

## curiosities
**Baldwin Radio Company** : Baldwin’s company employed other polygamist Mormons, and the profits were used to support a fundamentalist Church offshoot. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Financial Ruin** : Despite the success of his technology, Baldwin went bankrupt in 1924 and was later hit with a fraud conviction. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Invention of Early Headphones** : A Mormon fundamentalist, Baldwin invented an improved telephone receiver that functioned as the first set of headphones. He personally assembled early orders at his kitchen table. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Lack of Patent** : Baldwin notably never patented his headphone invention, which allowed the technology to be developed freely by other inventors and companies later on. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**US Navy Adoption** : The US Navy adopted Baldwin’s invention for clearer radio signals, which provided the funding to start his business. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
