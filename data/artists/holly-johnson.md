## curiosities
**David Bowie Fan** : Johnson was a huge fan of David Bowie, an influence that likely played a role in his own theatrical stage presence and embrace of provocative imagery. "He was also a huge David Bowie fan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hswtuenFwvM
**Impact of Bowie's "Coming Out"** : Holly Johnson of Frankie Goes to Hollywood credited David Bowie's "coming out" statement in January 1972 as having changed his life. Bowie's public bravery gave Johnson the courage to be who he was. "Boy George of Culture Club and Holly Johnson of Frankie Ghost of Hollywood say that their lives were changed by Bowie's comments." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sPCrATqBdeA
**Out Gay Frontman** : Holly Johnson, the frontman for Frankie Goes to Hollywood, was an openly gay man. His real name was William, but he adopted the name Holly in high school, inspired by the transvestite Holly Woodlawn, who was famously mentioned in Lou Reed's song "Walk on the Wild Side."
