## curiosities
**Early Life and Education** : Grew up in Guelph, Ontario. Played the flute in his school band, which he described as a "real rough time," motivating him to pick up the electric guitar. Attended McMaster University, also studying Political Science, similar to Max Kermann, seeking a new social experience and to meet people. "Yeah, I grew up in Gualfantario. And similarly, I played in high school with friends. I played in the school band. And that was, I played the flute. It was a real rough time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**First Impression of Max Kermann** : Mike DeAngeles met Max Kermann on the first day of university, describing Max as having a lot of "outward energy" and wearing a "funny like conductors hat." Mike recognized Max's potential as a frontman, a role Mike himself was uncomfortable with in his high school band. "And when I met Max, he's not lying when he sort of like half pushed me over. And he was wearing a really funny like conductors hat and just had a lot of like this sort of like outward energy that I noticed right away as, hey, maybe a good guy to be a front man." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**University Jobs and Firing** : During his university summers, Mike held "terrible" temp jobs, including picking up needles on top of Jackson Square and working in a distribution warehouse. He was also fired from East Side Marios, a moment Max Kermann called him about immediately after they met. "I got fired as a waiter. The job was fine. I was probably deserving of the firing. Max called me right after we met for a drink." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
