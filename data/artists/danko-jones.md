**Formal Black Attire and Shoe Polish Ritual** : The Toronto-based band Danko Jones is characterized by its members' exclusive use of black attire, a style inspired by blues legend Robert Johnson and the punk band Nation of Ulysses. Prior to every gig, they meticulously press their clothes to ensure a formal appearance, and no tour can commence without a full supply of shoe polish, underscoring their commitment to this sharp, polished image. "Dango Jones, the Toronto band is all about dressing in black. They were inspired by blues legend Robert Johnson, and a punk band called Nation of Ulysses.
