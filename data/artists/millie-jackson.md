# artist - Millie Jackson

## albums
**Cocktop (1974)** : An album from 1974 described as "incredible." Millie Jackson's curious singing style on this record was a major influence on later artists like Grace Jones. On this album, she started reciting song lyrics with rhymes, moving beyond simple narration and representing a significant step in proto-rap development. ## curiosities
**Influence on Grace Jones** : Millie Jackson's unique "half-singing, half-talking" style and her curious way of singing, particularly exemplified on her 1974 album "Cocktop," had a very important influence on the singing style of the later diva, Grace Jones.
