# artist - Mr. Epp and the Calculations

## members
- Mark Arm
- Mr. Epp

## genres
- Grunge (first use in print)
- Pure Noise

## curiosities
**Breakup and Formation of Green River** : Mr. Epp and the Calculations disbanded after a final show in February 1984, with Mark Arm and his bandmate Steve Turner moving on to form the influential grunge band Green River within weeks. "Mr. Ep in the Calculations broke up after a final show in February 1984. Within weeks, Mark and his buddy Steve Turner formed Green River." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cy5SCQV0HAY
**First Documented Use of "Grunge" in Music Context** : In 1981, Mark Arm wrote a letter to a local alternative weekly, "Desperate Times," nominating Mr. Epp and the Calculations as one of the worst and most overrated bands in Seattle, describing them with the phrase "For Grunge, pure noise." This is the first known appearance of the word "Grunge" in print to describe a music style from the American Pacific Northwest, although Arm did not invent the word itself. "In 1981, Mark wrote a letter to Desperate Times, a local alternative weekly that was more of a fan-zene than anything else. To stir things up, the zine invited readers to nominate the worst and most overrated bands in Seattle. Mark took up the mission and wrote this. I hate Mr. Ep in the Calculations. For Grunge, pure noise, everyone I know loves them. I don't know why. This is the first known appearance of the word Grunge in print as a description for a certain style of music from the American Pacific Northwest." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cy5SCQV0HAY
**Origin as a Joke Band** : The band was formed while guitarist Mark Arm was attending Bellevue Christian High School, with their math teacher, Mr. Epp, serving as the namesake. The entire project was treated as a joke, and it took three years before the members learned to play any musical instruments. "One of the co-founders, guitarist Mark Arm, was previously in a band called Mr. Ep in the Calculations. They were formed while Mark was attending Bellevue Christian High School. Mr. Ep was actually their math teacher, and the whole thing was treated as a joke. They took three years before the members actually learned how to play any kind of musical instruments." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cy5SCQV0HAY
