## curiosities
**Blues Focus and Iggy's Name** : The Prime Movers was a blues band where James Newell Osterberg played as a drummer. It was during his time with this band that he became known as "Iggy," a nickname derived from his previous band, The Iguanas. "Next came the prime movers and they were more of a blues band and here's where Jim became known as Iggy because, well, he used to play in the iguanas." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8
