## curiosities
**Band Name Change Origin** : The band originally formed in 1971 under the name Rockfire Funk Express. After their father died in a car accident, the brothers changed their band's name to Death, reflecting a profound personal impact. "But then their father died in a car accident and that's when rockfire Funk Express changed their name to death." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
**Limited Early Release and Rediscovery** : Death's early records, produced in runs of only about 500 copies, largely went unnoticed, and the band broke up in 1977. However, they experienced a rediscovery in 2009 when their early 1970s recordings began to circulate, leading to reconsideration of their place in punk history. "They started making records that sadly went largely unnoticed, partly because they only came out and runs of maybe 500 copies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
**Possible First Punk Band** : Death, a band formed by three Black brothers from Detroit (David, Bobby, and Dennis Hackney) in 1971 after their father's death, is argued to be the first punk band. Inspired by The Beatles' Ed Sullivan performance in 1964, their early 1970s recordings, rediscovered in 2009, sounded like convincing punk rock possibly predating The Ramones' 1974 debut. "It is possible that death was making music like this before the Ramones played their first gig in 1974." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
