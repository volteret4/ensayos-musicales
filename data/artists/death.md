## curiosities
**Ahead of Their Time** : Despite their talent, Death was considered too far ahead of their time, leading to their breakup in 1977 and subsequent obscurity. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**First Black Punk Band** : Composed of three brothers from Detroit, Death is cited by some as the first true punk band, potentially recording punk-like music before The Ramones' 1974 debut. They were inspired to form after seeing The Beatles perform on Ed Sullivan in 1964. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Formation and Early Sound** : Formed in Detroit in 1971 by two brothers and a friend, who were black men. The band initially started as a funk band. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Musical Epiphany** : After seeing The Who and Alice Cooper, the band had an epiphany that shifted their musical direction. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Name Change and Breakup** : Originally named Rockfire Funk Express, they changed their name to Death after their father died in a car accident. The band broke up in 1977 after releasing several overlooked records in small runs of roughly 500 copies. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Pre-Punk Pioneers** : Retroactively, Death is considered one of the most important pre-punk bands in America. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Punk Pioneers** : Composed of three brothers from Detroit, this band is argued by some to be the first punk band, potentially making punk music before The Ramones' 1974 debut. They were originally called Rockfire Funk Express but changed their name to Death following the loss of their father in a car accident. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
**Rediscovery (2009)** : Their early 1970s recordings were rediscovered in 2009, gaining a new generation of fans for their pioneering "punky" sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Rediscovery and Reissue** : The band was rediscovered when the three sons of one of the members, forming a band called Rough Frances, found their father's previous musical life online. This led to a reissue of the original tapes in 2009. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
