## curiosities
**"Heavy Metal Robots" Description (1970)** : In the February 7, 1970, edition of Cream Magazine, music critic Lester Bangs described The Guess Who as "heavy metal robots" following their hit single "Undun." While this marked an early reference to music using the term "heavy metal," it was intended as an insult rather than a genre classification.
