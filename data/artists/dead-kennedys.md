## curiosities
**Alternative Tentacles Founding** : Guitarist East Bay Ray established Alternative Tentacles in 1979 as their own indie label to release their music, which later became a home for many other hardcore punk bands. "DK's guitarist East Bay Ray formed alternative tentacles in 1979, and it eventually became the home of a ton of other hardcore punk bands over the years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEHkKfmmDiI
**Band Name Controversy** : The band's name was considered "super tasteless" and caused problems, often forcing them to perform under pseudonyms to avoid issues. "Their name gave them problems, sort of super tasteless, so they often had to perform under pseudonyms." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Bay Area Hardcore** : This band is associated with the Bay Area Hardcore scene, contributing to the broader punk landscape that influenced skate punk. "and the Dead Kennedys, Bay Area Hardcore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=44dXy0StjIQ
**Documented Moshing in Film** : The Dead Kennedys were among the bands whose performances featuring moshing were documented in the film "The Decline of Western Civilization," contributing to the spread of this form of audience behavior. "The bands ranging from the Ramones to the dead canadies to DOA to minor threat." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Formation and Political Stance** : The Dead Kennedys' roots trace back to around 1979, and they quickly established themselves as the most political of all the early hardcore bands. "Their roots go back to about 1979, and they ended up being the most political of all the early hardcore bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0qigzi1j81U
**Founding Alternative Tentacles** : The Dead Kennedys formed Alternative Tentacles, a California label, initially just to release one of their singles. They enjoyed the experience so much they decided to turn it into a full-time business, following the model of Black Flag with SST and Bad Religion with Epitaph. The label also played a key role in exporting American hardcore by developing relationships with other indie labels worldwide. "At first, they just wanted to get one of their singles out there, but they enjoyed the experience so much that they decided to turn Alternative Tentacles into a full-time business..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Influence on Pacific Northwest Rock** : This band was identified as a source of hardcore punk from California that influenced the developing rock scene in the Pacific Northwest, which later birthed grunge. "bands like the dead Kennedys and black flag and bad religion." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tFBSrKMEZxI
**Influential West Coast Hardcore** : The Dead Kennedys from San Francisco are identified as one of the most important West Coast hardcore bands. "East Bay Ray was the guitarist for San Francisco's Dead Kennedys, one of the most important of all the West Coast hardcore bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
**Jello Biafra's Political Career and Activism** : Singer Jello Biafra later ran for mayor of San Francisco, finishing fourth, and became a powerful public speaker actively rallying against censorship. "The dead Kennedys featuring singer Jello Biopro, who would later run for mayor of San Francisco he finished fourth." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEHkKfmmDiI
**Pseudonyms for Performances** : Due to their controversial name, which was considered "super tasteless," the band often had to perform under pseudonyms. "Their name gave them problems, sort of super tasteless, so they often had to perform under pseudonyms." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEHkKfmmDiI
**Used "Thatchergate" Samples** : The Dead Kennedys incorporated samples from Crass's infamous "Thatchergate" tape into their own musical tracks. This act connected them to a significant political hoax that fooled major intelligence agencies. "samples from this tape were used in tracks by both Crasse and the dead Kennedys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0ARYt4sYT90
