## curiosities
**Multi-faceted Role in Can** : Beyond his role as bassist, Czukay was also responsible for the band's production and the manipulation of hundreds of effects. He later prominently utilized magnetic tapes to project luminous synthesizer notes, particularly contributing to the atmospheric sound of albums like "Future Days." His pulsating, repetitive bass lines were a constant and defining feature, notably on "Ege Bamyasi." "que tendría el rol de bajista, se encargaría además de la producción y manipulación de cientos de efectos. ...
