# artist - Freak Baby

## members
- Dave Grohl (second guitarist)

## albums
**Eight Song Cassette (1984)** : The band recorded eight songs with Barrett Jones at his Laundry Room Records studio on November 11, 1984. These tracks were dubbed onto cassette, which Dave Grohl unsuccessfully attempted to sell through a local punk-friendly record store. "He recorded eight songs with Freak Baby at that house, all on November 11, 1984. They were dubbed onto cassette, which Dave then tried to sell through a local punk-friendly record store and absolutely nothing happened." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA
