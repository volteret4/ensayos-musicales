**Transition to Horology** : After playing lead guitar for Anthrax for 15 years, Dan Spitz decided to dedicate his life to horology, the study, making, and repairing of timepieces. He earned a full scholarship to a Swiss watchmaking school, becoming certified as a mechanical complication specialist with degrees in micro-mechanical engineering, and later worked as an instructor for Chopard.
