# artist - The Demics

## curiosities
**New York City (1979) – Genuine Canadian Punk Classic About Toronto Bands Making Road Trips to CBGB** : The Demics formed in London, Ontario in 1977 and regularly made the drive to Toronto to participate in the scene. Drummer Marcy Saddy later left to join the B-Girls. Their EP *Talk's Cheap* appeared in 1978 on Toronto's Ready Records, followed by an album in 1980. Their best-known song, "New York City," is a tribute to the Toronto punk bands — the Viletones, the B-Girls, the Curse, the Diodes, and others — who would regularly drive nine hours to New York City to play CBGB and couch-surf.
