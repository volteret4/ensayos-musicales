# artist - Kanye

## curiosities
**Pioneer of Exaggerated Auto-Tune Use** : Kanye is recognized as one of the artists who "really went to town with auto-tune," utilizing the technology not just for subtle pitch correction but for highly exaggerated and distinct vocal effects. His influential work contributed significantly to the trend of Auto-Tune being used as a deliberate stylistic choice in popular music.
