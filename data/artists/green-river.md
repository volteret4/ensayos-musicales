## curiosities
**Deep Six Compilation Appearance** : Green River was featured on the "Deep Six" compilation, an important record for the Seattle scene. The band included guitarist Jeff Ament, who would later form Pearl Jam, and Mark Arm, who had a day job at MuZac Corporation.
