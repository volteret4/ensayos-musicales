## curiosities
**Commercial Success** : Since the release of his debut album `Let Love Rule`, he has achieved a string of platinum records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Current Endeavors** : Continues to perform and record music, and is also highly successful in the real estate business, renovating properties in New York, the Bahamas, Miami, Paris, and Toronto. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Diverse Influences** : His musical exposure encompassed jazz and classical music, alongside rock acts like The Who, Led Zeppelin, KISS, Pink Floyd, Jimi Hendrix, and The Rolling Stones. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Early Career Attempts** : By 1988, he had explored several musical phases, including a period performing under the name "Romeo Blue," which was not well-received. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Early Musical Exposure** : Grew up immersed in music, aspiring to be a musician from the age of five. He performed with the California Boys Choir and sang with the Metropolitan Opera in New York as a child. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Family Background** : Son of Roxy Roker, an actress who played Helen Willis on the TV show "The Jeffersons." He is also a cousin of TV weatherman Al Roker. His father, Sy Kravitz, was a producer with NBC News and a jazz promoter, who counted Ella Fitzgerald, Miles Davis, and Duke Ellington among his personal friends. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Grammy Recognition** : Won the Grammy for Best Male Rock Vocalist four consecutive years. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Record Label Bidding War** : His unique music generated a five-way bidding war among record labels. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Signing and Rebranding** : He signed with Virgin Records and, as part of the agreement, reverted to using his real name, which proved to be a beneficial decision. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
