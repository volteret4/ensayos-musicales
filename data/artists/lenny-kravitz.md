# artist - Lenny Kravitz

## curiosities
**Preference for Analog Recording** : Lenny Kravitz often opts for traditional analog recording methods, utilizing "real to real tape, analog recording consoles, and take after take after take until they nail their parts." This approach stands in contrast to modern digital trickery, allowing for natural imperfections to contribute to the music's beauty. "You don't have to be as good as some of the people from the old analog days when you couldn't fix glaring mistakes with digital trickery." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Zniri9uSlc
**Toronto Condo Design** : Lenny Kravitz is involved in designing condos for the Toronto real estate market, similar to Pharrell Williams. "Lenny Kravitz isn't the only artist to get into designing condos for the Toronto market." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
