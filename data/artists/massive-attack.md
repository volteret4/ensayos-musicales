"My personal favorite is Mezzanine from 1998, which features this song called Angel. **Pioneers of Trip-Hop** : Massive Attack is recognized as one of the all-time best trip-hop bands and has been actively making music since 1988, producing a substantial catalog of excellent records. **Zero DB Initiative Signatory** : Massive Attack is among the groups that signed the Zero DB petition, an initiative launched in 2008 by the charity Reprieve. This initiative aims to condemn the use of music as torture and to advocate for governments to enforce the Convention Against Torture and other related treaties. "Here's another group that signed the Zero DB petition. Massive attack." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1wdsGt07Gg
