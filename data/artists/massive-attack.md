**Mezzanine (1998) - 20th Anniversary Album** : Celebrating its 20th anniversary, this album is highlighted as one of the most important records of the 90s. It is seen as the work that best crystallizes and culminates the fusion movement that Trip Hop became, bringing together all the influences the genre incorporated over the years. It marks a point of inflection for Trip Hop, after which the genre became very diffuse and complex. This album is noted for its emotional intensity, with moments described as "penetrant," and incorporates influences from classic American soul, deep downtempo rhythmic bases of Trip Hop, Björk's more melodic and intimate Trip Hop, synthesizers, new wave, and classical electronic music. Critically, it is considered by many to be Massive Attack's best work. "posiblemente esté disco del 98 al mesanín de Masi Batak. **Pitch-shifting - Extensive Use on Mezzanine** : Massive Attack extensively employed pitch-shifting on `Mezzanine`, a technique where melodies are recorded at a different speed than they are reproduced, generating unique audio effects. While common among hip hop beatmakers, the band took this process to an extreme, with nearly all melodies on the album being recorded at a speed different from their playback. "casi todas las melodías que vemos en este disco masibata que en este disco esta grabada sonad de velocidad distinta de la que se está reproduciendo, lo cual genera efectos de audio muy interesantes." ← Music Radar Clan > MASSIVE ATTACK. 20 años de MEZZANINE | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wG6NEzM5CEQ&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← massive-attack
