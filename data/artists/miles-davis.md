**Pioneer of Bebop Jazz** : Miles Davis was a significant figure in the bebop movement, a challenging and highly virtuosic form of jazz that emphasized improvisation among musicians. Bebop's complex nature moved jazz in a new direction, away from the more structured, dance-oriented big band sound. "Charlie Parker, Sonny Rollins, Dexter Gordon, Miles Davis, Dizzy Gillespie, Thelonius Monk. Their work helped put the nail in the coffin of the big bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk
