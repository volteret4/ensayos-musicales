**Pioneer of Bebop Jazz** : Miles Davis was a significant figure in the bebop movement, a challenging and highly virtuosic form of jazz that emphasized improvisation among musicians. Bebop's complex nature moved jazz in a new direction, away from the more structured, dance-oriented big band sound. "Charlie Parker, Sonny Rollins, Dexter Gordon, Miles Davis, Dizzy Gillespie, Thelonius Monk.
