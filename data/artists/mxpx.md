## curiosities
**Criticism from within Christian community** : MXPX has been subject to condemnation by some individuals who believe that Christian bands, regardless of their message, are perverting Christianity by playing rock music. "And they spend lots of time quoting scripture to support their opinions on the evils and perversions of Creed and jars of clay and MXPX and everybody else." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Crossover Success** : MXPX was one of Tooth and Nail's early significant successes, eventually signing with a major label. They toured with the Warped Tour and appeared in a Pepsi commercial, demonstrating their crossover into mainstream culture. "Although the band has since been signed to a major label and has moved on to touring with a warp tour and can be seen in a Pepsi commercial, MXPX was one of Tooth and Nail's first big victories." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Formation and Early Releases** : MXPX, a Christian punk band from Bremerton, Washington, formed in 1993. While still in high school, they released a series of seven-inch singles through Tooth and Nail, a Christian-owned Seattle-based record label. "They were formed in 1993 and by the time the guys got out of high school, they had already released a series of seven-inch singles for a label called Tooth and Nail." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Success Following The Offspring** : This band was discovered by fans due to The Offspring's significant success in late 1994, marking the beginning of Skate Punk's "glory years." "And this led to fans discovering bands like Goldfinger, Lagwagon, Face to Face, MXPX, and Unwritten Law." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=44dXy0StjIQ
