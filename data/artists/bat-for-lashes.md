# artist - Bat for Lashes

## songs
**You Somebody (Spring 2009) - Kings of Leon Cover** : Bat for Lashes recorded a cover version of Kings of Leon's song "You Somebody" in the spring of 2009. Her interpretation incorporated a church organ and tambourine, featuring her distinct vocal style, which Nathan Followell found "pretty cool" and "great." "Bat for Lashes... recorded a version of you, somebody. ...a church organ, tambourine, and that voice of hers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KyvYpggZXiU
