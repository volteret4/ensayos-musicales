# artist - The Stages

## curiosities
**Soviet Blacklist - Violence** : This band was listed on the USSR's 1985 "not recommended music" list due to associations with "Violence," with the possibility that the Soviet authorities mistakenly referred to Iggy Pop and The Stooges. Their inclusion reflects the Soviet Union's concerns about the perceived aggressive or disruptive nature of certain Western rock acts.
