## curiosities
**John Hughes Film Exposure (1980s)** : Wang Chung's music was heavily featured on the meticulously curated soundtracks of John Hughes' 1980s films. These soundtracks proved to be a vital avenue for introducing a broad North American audience to numerous alternative rock bands, particularly those originating from the UK. "So many people learned of bands like The Specials and Spandovalet and Billy Idle and General Public and Wang Chung and Yellow and New Order and the Psychedelic Furs and the Smiths and Echo and the Bunyman, Susanne Vagan in excess the Jesus and Mary chain, Flesh for Luluo and so many others from those soundtrack albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM ← wang-chung
