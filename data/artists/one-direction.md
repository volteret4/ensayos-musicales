# artist - One Direction

## concerts
- **Concert Linked to Boy Band Induced Pneumothorax** : A young girl was admitted to a hospital after attending a One Direction concert, and this incident was documented in medical journals as the first known case of "Boy Band Induced Pneumothorax." The condition is caused by screaming and singing extremely loudly at a concert, leading to a spontaneous lung collapse. "This condition went down in the medical journals after a young girl was presented at hospital after a one-direction concert."

## curiosities
**Boy Band Comeback (2010s)** : One Direction represented the resurgence of boy bands in the 2010s, becoming a prominent example of this cultural phenomenon. Their success indicated a return of the multi-member vocal group format to mainstream popularity. "Boy bands. Girl bands came back.
