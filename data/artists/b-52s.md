# artist - B-52s

## curiosities
**Backmasking Use (70s/80s)** : The B-52s were known to use backmasking effects in their music during the 1970s and 1980s, a period marking the peak of this recording technique. "Tons of artists used back masking effects in the 70s and 80s. That seems to be when things reached their peak. Iron Maiden, Pink Floyd, Sticks, Prince B-52s, Weird Al, they were all into it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjwRApoJhA
**Music Video Pioneers (Early 1980s)** : The B-52s were grouped with Devo and other "left of center types" as bands that made for great music videos when the medium became popular in the early 1980s, due to their unique visual styles. "Divo was joined by the B-52s and a few other left of center types, all of whom made for great music videos when that became a thing in the early 1980s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9_3EbwKPnU ← b-52s
