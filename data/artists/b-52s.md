# artist - B-52s

## curiosities
**Backmasking Use (70s/80s)** : The B-52s were known to use backmasking effects in their music during the 1970s and 1980s, a period marking the peak of this recording technique. "Tons of artists used back masking effects in the 70s and 80s. That seems to be when things reached their peak.
