## songs
**Born to Be Wild (1967) – Heavy Metal Naming Misconception** : Written by Mars Bonfire in 1967, this song contains the phrase "heavy metal" but is not considered the origin of the heavy metal music genre's name. The lyric refers to the "noise made by big, loud, heavy motorcycles" (specifically Harley Davidson's), not a type of music, and the sound of what is now known as heavy metal music did not exist at the time the song was written.
