**Ghost (Sweden, 1996) – Papa Emeritus = Tobias Forge; Nameless Ghouls; Arena-Level Success Despite Satanic Imagery** : Ghost came together in Sweden in 1996 and became a worldwide phenomenon filling arenas. The only named member is the frontman Papa Emeritus (stage name of Tobias Forge), who dresses like a Pope from hell and cycles through different character names (Cardinal Copia, Papa Emeritus IV). All other band members are called Nameless Ghouls, each representing an ancient element (fire, water, wind, earth, plus ether). Rumored past Ghouls have included Dave Grohl. The satanic imagery has caused problems in the US Bible Belt. Their sound is a blend of hard metal, arena rock, doom metal, psych, symphonic metal, and gothic rock. "Ghost is hard metal, metal, arena rock, do metal, psych, symphonic metal, gothic rock, and more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DiyIPBKiVW4
