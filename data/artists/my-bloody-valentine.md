# artist - My Bloody Valentine

## genres
- Fuzz
- Shoegaze

## labels
- Creation Records

## instruments
- Guitar

## albums
**Loveless (Year)** : This album took two years to produce and literally bankrupted Creation Records. It remains widely cited as a sonic blueprint that influenced hundreds, if not thousands, of bands from the 1990s through to today.

## songs
**Only Shallow (1981)** : A track from the album "Loveless", released on the Creation label.

## curiosities
**Broad Influence** : Influenced a vast number of new bands, including The Verve, The Smashing Pumpkins, Nine Inch Nails, Silver Sun Pickups, Garbage, Nirvana, and an uncountable number of shoegaze and fuzz bands.
**Guitar Noise and Wall of Sound Pioneers** : An Irish band signed by Creation Records that pushed the boundaries of guitar noise and "walls of sound" to new and unprecedented levels.
