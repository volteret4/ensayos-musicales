## albums
**Loveless (1991) – Groundbreaking Noise Pop Album** : A groundbreaking noise pop album released on November 4, 1991, which was the epitome of the shoegazer sound and influenced subsequent music. Its high production cost practically bankrupted their original label. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Loveless (Year)** : This album took two years to produce and literally bankrupted Creation Records. It remains widely cited as a sonic blueprint that influenced hundreds, if not thousands, of bands from the 1990s through to today. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
