## curiosities
**Album Sales (Career)** : Over their time together, the band sold approximately 100,000 albums. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Bob Death's Career (Post-1990s)** : Original keyboardist Bob Death is a lawyer by training, specializing in entertainment law. He served as the executive director of Music BC and has authored a couple of books on navigating the Canadian music industry. He has also written a science fiction novel entitled "The Displaced". ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**British Vocalist (1990s)** : Their singer was born in Britain, which is noted as unsurprising given the band's influence by the Manchester Sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Formation (1991)** : The band was formed in Vancouver in 1991. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Juno Nomination (1996)** : The band received a Juno nomination in 1996. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Kevin Spencer's Career (Post-1996)** : Kevin Spencer, originally from Hamilton, joined the band in 1996. He later became a performer in the touring version of the musical "Rent", appearing in over 500 performances. He has also worked and toured with Daniel Lanois and currently lives and works in music in Atlanta. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Never Officially Broke Up (Current)** : The band never officially broke up and continues to exist in some form or another, despite lineup changes. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
