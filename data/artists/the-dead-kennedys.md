# artist - The Dead Kennedys

## genres
- Hardcore Punk

## labels
- Alternative Tentacles

## songs
**California Über Alles (1979)** : Their self-produced single from 1979, which was the very first release on their Alternative Tentacles label.

## curiosities
**Exporting American Hardcore** : By developing relationships with other indie labels around the world, Alternative Tentacles successfully exported American hardcore to new territories.
**Full-time Business** : Enjoyed the experience of self-release so much that they decided to transform Alternative Tentacles into a full-time business.
**Label Formation** : Formed Alternative Tentacles after struggling to find any other label willing to believe in them, initially just to release a single.
