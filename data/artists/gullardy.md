# artist - Gullardy

## curiosities
**"Turn Blue" Album Homage** : Gullardy, a Cleveland host of a late-night horror movie show in the 1960s, is honored in The Black Keys' *Turn Blue* album. The album's title was inspired by his catchphrase, and its cover art visually references his fascination with mind control, brainwashing, and hypnotism as themes in movies. "The title was co-opted from a catchphrase by a dude named Gullardy, who hosted a late night horror movie show in Cleveland back in the 1960s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
