# artist - Roger Taylor

## member of
- Queen
- SOS All Stars

## albums
**Fun in Space (1981) - Solo Album** : This 1981 solo release by Queen drummer Roger Taylor was found as a poster on the wall at the Foo Fighters' 606 Studios. It was particularly notable as Taylor Hawkins expressed immense admiration for Roger Taylor, calling him his "favorite drummer ever." "It was a 1981 release entitled Fun in Space." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA

## curiosities
**Collaborator on Dennis Wilson's "Holy Man"** : Roger Taylor contributed to the completion of Dennis Wilson's unfinished song "Holy Man," working alongside Taylor Hawkins and Brian May. This collaboration highlights his respect for and connection with Hawkins. "Taylor added to music, wrote some new lyrics, sang the song, and he was backed up by Roger Taylor and Brian May." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA
**Member of SOS All Stars** : Roger Taylor joined Taylor Hawkins and Chad Smith in the one-off drummers' group SOS All Stars for the Live Earth show in London in 2007. This performance brought together influential drummers for a special event. "He was joined by Roger Taylor, of course, and Chad Smith of the Red Hot Chili Peppers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA
**Taylor Hawkins' Favorite Drummer Ever** : Roger Taylor was Taylor Hawkins' favorite drummer of all time. This admiration was evident from Hawkins' enthusiastic reaction to a poster of Taylor's solo album "Fun in Space" at the Foo Fighters' studio. "That's Roger Taylor, man. He's my favorite drummer ever." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA
