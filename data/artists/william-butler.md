## curiosities
**Early Life and Education** : William Butler, Win Butler's younger brother by two years, shared the same upbringing, moving with his family to The Woodlands near Houston in 1984. He attended prep school in Boston alongside his brother, where he was exposed to influential post-punk and alternative bands. "But then, baby brother William Butler was drafted along with Tim Kingsbury of Guelph Ontario" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
