**Piss Factory (1974) - First Punk Rock Record** : This one-off seven-inch single, with "Piss Factory" as its B-side, was released in the summer of 1974 and is regarded by some music historians as the first-ever punk rock record. The song was recorded almost by accident after Patti Smith and Lenny Kay finished an earlier session for a Jimi Hendrix cover. It told the story of Smith's experiences working in a doll factory. A thousand copies were pressed and distributed to independent bookstores and record sellers, selling out almost instantly and launching Smith's recording career.
