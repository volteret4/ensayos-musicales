# artist - Patti Smith

## genres
- Punk Rock

## venues
- CBGB

## curiosities
**Early UK Punk Exposure (Spring 1976)** : Punk priestess Patti Smith played a show in the UK a couple of months before The Ramones' July 1976 gigs, providing England's first exposure to US-style punk rock.
**Influence for Garbage** : Her style of singing in a lower register was an influence for the type of female frontwoman Garbage sought.
**Inspiration for Kim Gordon** : Mentioned as a punk artist who inspired Kim Gordon of Sonic Youth.
**Last Performer at CBGB (2006)** : Patti Smith performed the very last gig at the storied CBGB in New York on October 15, 2006, the night the club closed.
