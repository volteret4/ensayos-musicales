## curiosities
**Early UK Punk Exposure (Spring 1976)** : Punk priestess Patti Smith played a show in the UK a couple of months before The Ramones' July 1976 gigs, providing England's first exposure to US-style punk rock. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
**Influence for Garbage** : Her style of singing in a lower register was an influence for the type of female frontwoman Garbage sought. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Inspiration for Kim Gordon** : Mentioned as a punk artist who inspired Kim Gordon of Sonic Youth. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Last Performer at CBGB (2006)** : Patti Smith performed the very last gig at the storied CBGB in New York on October 15, 2006, the night the club closed. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
**Lollapalooza Participation (1992-1995)** : Patti Smith was among the major participants during Lollapalooza's "glory years" between 1992 and 1995. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Morrissey Autographing** : Morrissey reportedly signed Patti Smith's records and sold them at his own gigs. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qtdetc5-MQo
