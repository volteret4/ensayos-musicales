## albums
**A Dedication to David Bowie (2003)** : Released in 2003, this album by David Hart and Amman Ra is not a tribute album featuring covers of David Bowie's songs. Instead, it is an entire album composed of songs praising Bowie, noted for its unusual concept and sonic quality. "This is not a tribute album in the sense that they recorded covers of Bowie songs.
