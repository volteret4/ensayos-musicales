# artist - Pharrell Williams

## songs
**Blurred Lines (2013)** : Co-written with Robin Thicke, this song led to a lawsuit from Marvin Gaye's estate, accusing them of copying the "feel and sound" of Gaye's "Got to Give It Up." Williams and Thicke admitted to deliberately trying to copy the song's groove and feel as an homage. Despite expert testimony on musical differences, a jury awarded the Gaye estate over $8 million, a ruling that caused outrage in the music community and set a precedent for "feel"-based lawsuits.
