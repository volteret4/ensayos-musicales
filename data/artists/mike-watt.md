# artist - Mike Watt

## genres
- Punk Rock

## albums
**Ball-Hog or Tugboat (1995) – Solo Album** : This solo album featured Dave Grohl on drums and guitar, alongside a diverse lineup of collaborators from bands like Sonic Youth, Black Flag, Jane's Addiction, Dinosaur Jr., and musicians like Eddie Vedder, Flea, Evan Dando, Mike D, and Ad-Rock.

## songs
**Big Train (1995)** : A track from the "Ball-Hog or Tugboat" album, on which Dave Grohl played drums. This collaboration was instrumental in re-igniting Dave Grohl's enthusiasm for making music.
