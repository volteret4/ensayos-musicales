## albums
**Ball-Hog or Tugboat (1995)** : This solo album by punk bass player Mike Watt featured a diverse lineup of guest musicians, including Dave Grohl on drums and guitar. The project was instrumental in re-igniting Dave Grohl's excitement about making music after his deep depression following Kurt Cobain's death. "Dave had a lot of fun with this record on which he played drums and guitar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
