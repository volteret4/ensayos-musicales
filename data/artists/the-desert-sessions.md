## curiosities
**Jam-Oriented Project** : The Desert Sessions were designed as an outlet for jamming and collaboration among a changing collective of musical friends, serving as an inspiration and a source of enjoyment rather than a conventional, long-term band project. "But the desert sessions was meant as a jam, not a permanent thing. It was an inspiration, a lot of fun, but not really a long-term prospect." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG1TQuhcf1o
**Ongoing Recording Series** : The Desert Sessions is a continuous series of recordings that started in the summer of 1997 and remains ongoing. The project originated from Josh Homme assembling friends at Rancho de la Luna to jam, returning to the desert to rekindle his musical inspiration. "The ultimate result has been an ongoing series of recordings called The Desert Sessions that continues even today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG1TQuhcf1o
