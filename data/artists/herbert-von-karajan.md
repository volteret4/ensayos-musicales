## curiosities
**Champion of the Compact Disc (1981-1985)** : Herbert von Karajan, a distinguished orchestra conductor, was greatly impressed by the digital system of the Compact Disc. He became a significant promoter of the format, leading to numerous classical music releases between 1981 and 1985. His advocacy contributed to a massive shift of classical music enthusiasts towards the CD.
