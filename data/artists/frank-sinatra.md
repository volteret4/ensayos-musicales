# artist - Frank Sinatra

## songs
**My Way** : This song is associated with a disturbing phenomenon in the Philippines, where at least a dozen people were shot dead in karaoke bars between 2002 and 2012 while singing it. It is advised not to sing this song in karaoke bars in the Philippines. "In 2002 and 2012, at least a dozen people were shot dead while singing that song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
**My Way (1969) – Karaoke Murder Catalyst** : This song became extremely risky to sing in karaoke establishments in the Philippines after January 16, 1998, when someone was killed over its performance. Since then, about a dozen people have been murdered in disputes related to karaoke performances of "My Way," leading to many Filipino karaoke bars no longer offering it and many Filipinos avoiding it due to the associated stories. "In January 16, 1998, someone was killed over their performance of My Way by Frank Sinatra." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVJuITJyNTU

## curiosities
**Early Microphone Singer** : Frank Sinatra is identified as one of the performers who emerged with the advent of electrical recording and the microphone. This technology allowed singers to adopt a more nuanced style, singing softly and romantically, which led to the term "microphone singers" and later "crooners." "This is where we get performers like Frank Sinatra and Bing Crosby." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Model Train Enthusiast** : Frank Sinatra is noted for his love of model trains, a hobby he shares with Neil Young and Rod Stewart. "All love their model trains." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
**Symbol of "Real Music" (1960s)** : During the decline of rock and roll in the early 1960s, many record executives declared rock a mere fad and suggested a return to "real music" with "real artists" like Frank Sinatra. "Conventional wisdom was that everybody could go back to making real music with real artists like Frank Sinatra." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
