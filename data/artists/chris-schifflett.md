## curiosities
**Early Influences and Shift to Punk** : Chris Schifflett's early influences came from his brothers' music, including The Beatles, The Stones, Bowie, Led Zeppelin, and Aerosmith. In high school, he discovered punk rock, which was the prevalent live music scene in Santa Barbara and offered a more accessible social outlet compared to larger rock shows. "It wasn't until high school that I really got turned on to punk rock. And it was just like, because that was, you know, if you wanted to go to a show, there was no big rock show in Santa Barbara." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
**Santa Barbara Music Scene** : Chris Schifflett grew up in Santa Barbara, California, a town he initially considered boring but later recognized for its vibrant music scene. The area hosted weekly keg parties with bands, a barn venue in Isle Vista, and was close enough to LA for him to attend shows. "But we got a great music scene. Everybody played music every week and there were keg parties with bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
