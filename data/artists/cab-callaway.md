# artist - Cab Callaway

## songs
**Minnie the Moocher (1942)** : This song was featured in a Panorama Soundi film from 1942, showcasing Cab Callaway performing with cuts to members of his orchestra. These soundies were short, black-and-white films projected in machines found in public places, popular for a few years before being eclipsed by World War II production problems and the rise of television.
