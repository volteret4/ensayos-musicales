# artist - Merv Griffin

## songs
**Jeopardy! Thinking Music (Year Not Specified)** : The iconic "thinking music" heard on the game show Jeopardy! was composed by Merv Griffin, the creator of the show. He originally wrote it as a lullaby for his son, dashing it off in approximately one minute. This short piece of music has since generated more than seventy million dollars in royalties for the Griffin estate.
