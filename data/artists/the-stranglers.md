## curiosities
**Band Formation and Royalty Settlement** : Formed in 1974, this British band later noticed a strong similarity between their 1977 song "No More Heroes" and Elastica's song "Waking Up." This similarity resulted in The Stranglers being awarded a piece of the royalties from Elastica's first album, which was described as an actual case of "theft." "British band that was formed in 1974, noticed that the Elastica song Waking Up sounded awfully like one of their songs from 1977 called No More Heroes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L09R2evjyMc

His approach redefined the instrument's role within his band.
