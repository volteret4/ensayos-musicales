# artist - Prince Buster

## albums
**Al Capone (1967) – First Wave Ska; Prince Buster Shifted the Beat to Create Ska** : Cecil Bustamante Campbell — known by his stage name Prince Buster — was a pivotal figure in the creation of ska. Together with guitarist Jerry Johnson, he moved the beat emphasis from the 1 and 3 to the 2 and 4, stressing the offbeat. This change in syncopation, made in 1962, was the spark that created ska as a distinct genre. Prince Buster was also the source of the name for Madness — their name came from an old Prince Buster song. "Cecil moved the beat emphasis from the 1 and the 3 to the 2 and the 4. In other words, the off beat was stressed. This change in syncopation was the spark and SCA was born." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VxWp7HrT8oE
