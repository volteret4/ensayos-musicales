## curiosities
**New Wave Group (1988)** : Trent Reznor was with this new wave group for about six months in 1988 before leaving to focus on his janitorial duties and all-night demos at Right Track studio, which led to the formation of Nine Inch Nails. This was the only one of his pre-Nine Inch Nails bands that sounded anything like his future work. "Another gig followed with a band called Slam Bamboo and then a new wave group called Lucky Pierre.
