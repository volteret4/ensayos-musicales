## curiosities
**Dislike of Mellotrons (Unspecified)** : Rick Wakeman, a renowned keyboardist, is noted for his strong aversion to Mellotrons, likely due to their notorious unreliability and performance issues. "I talked to Rick Wakeman once about playing a melaton and he ate them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0fQQubt9UTI ← rick-wakeman
**Keyboardist for Yes** : Rick Wakeman is named as a keyboardist for the progressive rock band Yes. His contributions to the band's intricate compositions highlight his role in piano-based music. "Rick Wakeman of Yes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dJiSZ4X0F2g ← rick-wakeman
**Recording "Life on Mars" Piano Part** : While recording David Bowie's "Life on Mars," Rick Wakeman played the 100-year-old Bechstein piano, and it's noted that microphones might have been set up in the men's bathroom to capture its special natural reverb. "It's also quite possible that the microphones were arranged in the nearby men's bathroom, because there was something special about the natural reverb in there that just made a recording sound so special." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk ← rick-wakeman ← rick-wakeman
**Trident Studios House Pianist** : Rick Wakeman was the resident house pianist at Trident Studios, regularly called upon to contribute piano parts to various artists' recordings. "Rick Wakeman of Yes was called into play that Beckstein.
