## songs
**King of Love (1990) – 78 RPM Promotional Release** : In 1990, Dave Edmunds released this song as a 10-inch single. While the A and B sides played at the standard 45 RPM, a unique version of "King of Love" on the A side required the turntable speed to be flipped to 78 RPM, making it one of the last known performances to release a song on a 78 RPM record. "This vinyl record featured that song on the B side and on side A. Both played at 45 RPM, which is exactly the speed they were supposed to play. Looked the record over, and there was another version of King of Love, but this time you had to flip the speed to 78, which you gotta admit, it's kinda cool." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk ← dave-edmunds
