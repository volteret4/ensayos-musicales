# artist - Meet Whiplash

## concerts
- **North London Polytechnic College (March 15th, 1985)** : Meet Whiplash served as the opening band for The Jesus and Mary Chain's riot-inducing concert. A member of the "long forgotten group" exacerbated tensions by throwing a wine bottle back into the agitated crowd, an action that nearly sparked a full-scale stage invasion before the main act had even begun.
