## albums
**The Black Parade (2006)** : Released on October 23, 2006, this rock opera centers on a character known as "the patient." As the patient approaches death and transitions to the other side, his memories and impressions are captured within the songs. Upon his eventual death, he is transported by a parade, which is the "Black Parade" of the title. This concept is apparently linked to a fun childhood memory of leader Gerard Way and his personal thoughts on how death might arrive.

## songs
**Welcome to the Black Parade (2006)** : The first single from The Black Parade. Its music video effectively captured the theatricality of the band's concert performances, featuring role-playing and costumes.

## curiosities
**New Twist to Emo/Pop Punk** : Added a new twist to the scene when Emo went mainstream, contributing to pop punk picking up steam in the mid-2000s.
**Theatrical Live Performances** : My Chemical Romance's live concerts for "The Black Parade" were highly theatrical, incorporating extensive role-playing and costumes to embody the album's narrative.
