## albums
**The Black Parade (2006)** : Released on October 23, 2006, this rock opera centers on a character known as "the patient." As the patient approaches death and transitions to the other side, his memories and impressions are captured within the songs. Upon his eventual death, he is transported by a parade, which is the "Black Parade" of the title. This concept is apparently linked to a fun childhood memory of leader Gerard Way and his personal thoughts on how death might arrive. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc
