## albums
**The Black Parade (2006)** : Released on October 23, 2006, this rock opera, like Green Day's *American Idiot*, was released on the Reprise label. The album's central character is "The Patient," whose memories and impressions are captured in the songs as he faces death. Upon his death, The Patient is transported away by a parade, known as the Black Parade, which is the album's namesake. The concept is linked to a fun childhood memory and Gerard Way's view on how death approaches. "The central character was the patient. **Comic Book Artist and Creator** : Gerard Way began his journey in comic books at the age of 16, with his first comic, "On Raven's Wings," having two issues published. By 2001, he was interning as a sketch artist at Cartoon Network in New York, where he pitched an animated TV series called "The Breakfast Monkey," though it was not picked up. The experience of living through 9/11 profoundly impacted him, prompting him to leave animation to pursue music, which led to the formation of My Chemical Romance. In 2007, he began work on the mini-series "The Umbrella Academy," which became a significant success and was later adapted into a popular Netflix series. He also worked with the Marvel Universe on Spider-Man, creating the character of Penny Parker.
