## curiosities
**Martian Crater** : Paul Stanley of Kiss has a Martian crater named after him, an unusual distinction given that typically craters on Mars are named after deceased scientists and writers who contributed to Mars study and lore, and Stanley is neither deceased nor a scientist. "Paul Stanley of Kiss has a Martian crater named after him, but notice that he's neither dead nor a scientist nor anyone who has really anything to do with Mars." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMPLplhDk8I
**Theatrical Guitar Smashing** : During KISS's performances in the 1970s, Paul Stanley would theatrically destroy numerous guitars as a regular component of the band's live show, enhancing their visual spectacle. "Paul Stanley, theatrically, went through many, many guitars as part of his show." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
