## albums
**Pocketwatch (1992)** : A solo album recorded under the alias "Late." Dave Grohl recorded four songs in December 1990, just a few months after joining Nirvana, and the remaining six songs were completed over two days in July 1991, about two months before Nirvana's *Nevermind* was released. It appeared on the "cheapo label" Simple Machines, run out of a house in Arlington, Virginia. The album, which credits "Dave G" on all instruments, didn't get much attention due to Dave's insistence; he didn't want to be seen as "crashing the party" with his own material as Nirvana was taking off. The label, Simple Machines, begged him to release it on CD, but it remained a cassette-only release. "If you look at the liner notes of Pocketwatch, you'll see that all performances on all instruments are credited to one Dave G." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**Sound City (2013) – Documentary** : Dave Grohl directed this documentary, which he described as an easy project where he assembled a team of "like-minded people that love music." He initially didn't intend to become a filmmaker, stating that the project "just sort of fell into my lap." "that whole thing just sort of fell into my lap with the Sound City project." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mh-qg1izM98
**There Is Nothing Left to Lose (1999) – Album Cover Art** : The cover of this Foo Fighters album features a black and white photograph of the back of Dave Grohl's head, prominently displaying the Foo Fighters logo tattooed on his neck. ## curiosities
**"Foo Fighters" Name Origin and Dislike** : Dave Grohl chose the name "Foo Fighters" for his project after a term used by World War II pilots to describe mysterious "balls of fire over Allied lines" (UFOs). The French word "feu" (fire) influenced the term. He preferred a cloaked, anonymous name, wanting people to think it was a band even though there wasn't one at the time. Despite the name's origins, Dave Grohl "thinks the name Foo Fighters is dumb" and feels "stuck with the name" because the project unexpectedly succeeded. "Dave, by the way, Dave thinks the name Foo Fighters is dumb." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
