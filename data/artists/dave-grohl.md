## curiosities
**Preferred Guitar for Foo Fighters Recordings** : Dave Grohl's absolute favorite guitar is a 1967 ES-335. This particular instrument has been exclusively used to record every single guitar part on every Foo Fighters song ever produced. "His absolute favorite guitar is a 1967 ES-335. It's been used to track every single guitar part on every single Fufiter song ever recorded." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
**Signature Gibson DG-355** : Dave Grohl's affection for his ES-355 guitar led him to collaborate with Gibson in 2005 to design his own signature model, the DG-355. This guitar is notably based on a model that Trini Lopez utilized during the 1960s. "Dave Grohl loves his ES-355 so much that he got together with Gibson in 2005 to design a signature guitar, and it's called, of course, the DG-355." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
