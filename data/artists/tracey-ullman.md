# artist - Tracey Ullman

## songs
**The B-side (1983)** : Featured as the B-side to Tracey Ullman's hit single "They Don't Know," this track stood out for its self-referential nature. It consisted of a spoken-word piece where the artist, identifying herself as "Belle from the band," acknowledged its B-side status and mused about playing some music. "The B side featured a track called The B side in which she, well you'll see. Hi. I'm Belle from the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QBCEmZ4Jheo ← tracey-ullman
