# artist - Wendy Carlos

## curiosities
**Collaboration with Bob Moog and Film Score Work** : Wendy Carlos worked directly with synthesizer pioneer Bob Moog, using his innovative instruments. This collaboration led her to create the first all-synthesizer soundtrack for Stanley Kubrick's seminal futuristic film, *A Clockwork Orange*, demonstrating the significant artistic potential of the new technology.
