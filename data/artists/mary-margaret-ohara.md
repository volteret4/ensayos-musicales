## curiosities
**Aborted Debut Production with Andy Partridge** : In the early 1980s, her debut record was scheduled to be produced by Andy Partridge of XTC, known for his quirky style. However, the collaboration ended abruptly on the first day in the studio, with Partridge being fired because the combination of their "quirk plus quirk" did not work. "Her debut record was scheduled to be produced by Andy Partridge of XTC, another super quirky guy. That quirk plus quirk did not work and he was fired on the first day in the studio." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Admiration from Renowned Artists** : O'Hara garnered significant praise and admiration from many influential musicians; Morrissey requested her for vocalizations on his single "November Spawned a Demon," and Michael Stipe of R.E.M., Radiohead, Bruce Cockburn, Dave Matthews, and Nick Cave were all noted fans of her distinct music. "Moresy was such a fan that he asked her to do some vocalizations on his single November Spawn Demonstrer. Michael Steip couldn't get enough of her. Radiohead, Bruce Coburn, Dave Matthews, Nick Cave, they were all fans." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Family Connection to Catherine O'Hara** : Mary Margaret O'Hara is the younger sister of the renowned actress Catherine O'Hara. "She is the younger sister of actress Catherine O'Hara." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Film Soundtrack and Acting (2001)** : Beyond her primary music releases, O'Hara contributed to a Canadian film in 2001, providing the soundtrack for "Apartment Hunting" and also acting in the movie. "She also provided the soundtrack for a Canadian film called Apartment Hunting in 2001. She also acted in it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rrkmfJQBpHQ
**Shift to Acting and Limited New Music** : Over the past two decades, Mary Margaret O'Hara has increasingly focused on acting roles in theater, film, and musicals. She has also continued to contribute to songs by other artists, such as Bob Wiseman of Blue Rodeo, with only one new song released under her own name in 2014.
