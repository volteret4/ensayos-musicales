## curiosities
**Covered "1969"** : Red Cross is among the artists who have covered The Stooges' song "1969." "Tracks on this album have been covered by the Sex Pistols, Joey Ramone, Sonic Youth, the Sisters of Mercy, Joan Jett, Red Cross, and the Black Keys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8 ← red-cross
**Influence on Other Musicians** : Red Cross is noted as being very important to the musical education of musicians like Eddie Vedder and Dave Grohl. "Talk to Eddie Vetter or Dave Grohl, and they'll tell you how some of these bands were really, really important to their musical education." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEHkKfmmDiI ← red-cross
**Produced by Tommy Ramone** : Following his departure from the Ramones, Tommy Ramone continued his work as a producer, creating albums for the band Red Cross. "He also produced albums for the replacements and Red Cross." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU ← red-cross
