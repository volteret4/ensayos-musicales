## curiosities
**Mid-70s Boy Band Equivalent** : The Bay City Rollers are described as the "mid-70s equivalent of a modern boy band like One Direction," highlighting their pop appeal and mass fan base. "These are the Basidy Rollers, the mid-70s equivalent of a modern boy band like One Direction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
**Simplistic Pop Appeal (Early 1970s)** : Represented the "simplistic pop" that entranced the generation following the hippies, much to the dismay of older music fans. "The generation following them seemed to be totally entranced by simplistic pop made by David Cassidy and Bobby Sherman and the Bay City Rollers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
