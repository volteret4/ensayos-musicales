# artist - Barry Manilow

## curiosities
**Catalyst for Synth Backlash** : In 1981, Manilow decided to tour with only a few synthesizer players instead of his usual full orchestra. This move was seen as the "last straw" for the UK Musicians Union, leading them to formally protest the loss of jobs to electronic instruments. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
