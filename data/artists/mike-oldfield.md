## albums
**Tubular Bells** : Mike Oldfield's "Tubular Bells" was the first signing and release for Virgin Records, proving to be a huge hit. Its commercial success provided Virgin with the necessary funds to finance its expansion. "So Branson and his partners did, and their first signing was 2-biter bells by Mike Oldfield. ## songs
**Taurus 3 (Year not specified)** : On this track, Mike Oldfield, a guitar virtuoso and multi-instrumentalist, performs distinctive flamenco-like arpeggios. These passages are characterized as very clean and cold, deliberately contrasting with the inherent passion and raw emotion typically associated with traditional flamenco. "quien en Taurus 3 se marca unos punteos afremencados muy curiosos, los cuales son muy limpios y fríos en contra posiciona la pasión y el desgarro interinsico del flamenco." ← Cómo el Flamenco Influyó a Músicos Extranjeros (Y Quizá No Lo Notaste) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ReRvRfbHec
