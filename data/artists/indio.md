# artist - Indio

## members
- Gordon Peterson

## labels
- N.M. Records

## albums
**Big Harvest (1989)** : This record was issued in 1989 on N.M. Records in Canada and featured "Hard Sun" as its main single. After its release, Gordon Peterson (Indio) "walked away from the music industry," causing the album to go out of print and largely be forgotten. Decades later, following Eddie Vedder's hit cover, "Big Harvest" was reissued and made available again. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## songs
**Hard Sun (1989)** : Released in 1989, this song became a top 10 single in Canada for Indio (Gordon Peterson). Its original lyrics included lines such as "when she comes to greet me, she is mercy at my feet. When I stayed to pillage her, she just throws it back at me." After its success, Peterson quit the music industry due to his dislike of its workings. The song later gained renewed fame when Eddie Vedder covered it for the "Into the Wild" soundtrack in 2008, leading to a lawsuit from Peterson regarding lyrical changes, which Vedder ultimately won in April 2010. Despite the legal dispute, Peterson received significant royalties from the cover. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## curiosities
**Lawsuit Against Eddie Vedder (2010)** : Gordon Peterson sued Eddie Vedder for covering "Hard Sun" without explicit permission (which is not legally required under blanket license rules) and for "eroding the integrity of the song" by changing some of the lyrics. The case went through the courts until April 2010, when a judge ruled that Eddie Vedder did not materially infringe on Peterson's song, noting that Vedder kept the general structure and melody largely the same. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Retirement from Music Industry** : Gordon Peterson, known as Indio, walked away from the music industry after the release of his 1989 album "Big Harvest" because he disliked how it operated, how it treated people, and how it made him feel. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
