## curiosities
**Nightclub Employee** : Before his fame, Boy George worked at the coat check of one of the London nightclubs that were central to the rise of the New Romanticism movement. "Boy George used to work the code check at one of these clubs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng
**Openly Gay Performer in New Wave** : Boy George was one of the performers in the new wave scene who was, in fact, openly gay. His presence contributed to the genre's embrace of diverse sexual expressions and androgynous looks. "Some of these performers, such as Boy George, were in fact gay." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hswtuenFwvM
**Visuals in New Pop** : Boy George, as part of Culture Club, embodied the visual aspect of New Pop, where artists' unique looks and music videos turned them into brief cultural phenomena in the early 80s. "Think Boy George and Culture Club, ABC in their Sharp Tuxedos, Orange Juice, a band of Scotland featuring Edwin Collins, Asda Camer was another, Heaven 17 was still another." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
