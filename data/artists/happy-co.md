## curiosities
**Patrick Pentland's Pre-Sloan Band** : Happy Co. was a band in which Patrick Pentland played prior to joining Sloan. Chris Murphy was aware of Pentland's skills from this band, leading to his recruitment as a guitarist and singer for Sloan.
