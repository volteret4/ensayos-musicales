# artist - Dropkick Murphys

## songs
**Fortunate Son (Cover)** : The Dropkick Murphys performed a cover of Creedence Clearwater Revival's "Fortunate Son." The original song, which critiques social inequality regarding Vietnam War draft deferments, was controversially used during Donald Trump's presidential campaign. "Okay, let's do this cover of fortune at sun. These are the dropkick Murphy's." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**Rose Tattoo (Year Unspecified)** : This track was used to introduce a program on rock and roll tattoos. "That's the dropkick Murphy's and a track called Rose Tattoos. And a nice way to start this program on rock and roll tats." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
