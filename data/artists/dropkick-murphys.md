"Here are the drop kick Murphy's covering ACDC. I'm playing this version because personally I feel it's better than the original." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1HMW9rGtJHI

## curiosities
**Band Origins and Early Gigs** : The band was formed in Massachusetts in 1996. Their initial performances took place in the basement of a barber shop. They significantly developed their sound and presence after touring with The Mighty Mighty Bosstones, another band from the Boston area. "Their first gigs were in the basement of a barber shop, but after the muddy, muddy boss tones, another Boston area band, took them on tour, they really came into their own." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Covering AC/DC** : The Dropkick Murphys, described as a hardcore new rock/alt rock/alternative band, are noted for recording AC/DC songs. This action exemplifies the disappearance of traditional genre snobbishness among contemporary young music fans. "And today it's to the point where even hardcore new rock bands, alt rock bands, alternative bands like the Dropkick Murphy's are having fun recording AC DC songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
