# artist - Cream

## songs
**I Feel Fine (Cover)** : The Foo Fighters covered this Cream song, with Taylor Hawkins taking on the lead vocal duties. This performance highlighted Hawkins' ability to step into the frontman role for certain tracks. "When the band covered I Feel Fine from Cream, it was Taylor up front." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA

## curiosities
**Inspiration for Tin Machine** : Cream was cited as an inspiration for David Bowie's band Tin Machine. Bowie aimed to create a four-piece rock band with himself solely as the lead singer, harkening back to the dynamic and influential styles of bands like Cream. "Inspiration was taken from Jimmy Hendrix, Cream and the Pixies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2FyAZGUI_mk
