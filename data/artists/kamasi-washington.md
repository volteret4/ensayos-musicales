## albums
**Heaven and Earth (June 22nd) - Seminal Experimental Jazz Album** : This new album from Kamasi Washington, referred to in the transcript as "Kevin Ann Earth," is from one of the most significant living saxophonists, especially within the jazz and experimental jazz scenes. He is noted as possibly the only experimental jazz artist under 40, is highly respected in the jazz world, and has collaborated with major figures like David Bowie. This album is presented as an excellent entry point for those less familiar with jazz.
