# artist - AC/DC

## albums
**Back in Black (1980)** : Sold 50 million copies, cited as an example of a past mega-seller unlikely to be replicated in the current music sales landscape.

## songs
**You Shook Me All Night Long** : A line from this song (referred to as "Should Me All Night Long" in the transcript) provided the title for Veruca Salt's 1994 debut album, "American Thighs."
