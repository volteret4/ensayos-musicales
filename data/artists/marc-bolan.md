# artist - Marc Bolan

## curiosities
**Glam Rock Pioneer (1971)** : Marc Bolan, a former rival who became friends with David Bowie, was a key figure in the emergence of glam rock. In March 1971, he appeared on "Top of the Pops" wearing satin clothes and covered with glitter dust, an appearance that profoundly inspired David Bowie.
