## songs
**Ashes (1980s)** : "Ashes" (translates from Russian) is an example of underground Soviet rock from the 1980s by the band Aquarium. The band was featured on Joanna Stingray's 1986 "Red Wave" double LP, which smuggled Soviet underground music to the West. "This is a band called Aquarium and the song is Ashes.
