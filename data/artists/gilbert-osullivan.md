## curiosities
**Lawsuit against Biz Markie (1991)** : Gilbert O'Sullivan sued Biz Markie in 1991 for an unauthorized sample of his 1972 song, "Alone Again Naturally." This lawsuit set a major precedent, forcing the withdrawal of Markie's album *I Need A Haircut* from the marketplace, emphasizing the legal consequences of uncleared samples.
