## curiosities
**Formation of Merge Records (1989)** : Members of the independent band SuperChunk formed Merge Records in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, in 1989. The band established the label due to dissatisfaction with the major label system, and it eventually found significant success with acts like Arcade Fire after struggling for years.
