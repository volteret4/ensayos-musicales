## songs
**FOH (2013)** : This song, released on Superchunk's 2013 album "I Hate Music," stands for "Front of House." It pays tribute to the sound person who sits at the front of house console to ensure the band sounds as good as possible, highlighting the often-unsung heroes of live music. "In 2013, they released an album and entitled, I Hate Music. And it featured a song called F-O-H, which stands for Front of House. That's where the sound person sits, and make sure that the man sounds as good as possible." ← Ongoing History of New Music > We Are The Road Crew - A History of Roadies | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG

## curiosities
**Founders of Merge Records (1989)** : Two members from the band Superchunk established Merge Records in 1989 after experiencing difficulty finding a major label or subsidiary willing to release their music, leading them to create their own company. "The company was established in 1989 by two people from a band called SuperChunk, and theirs was the same story that we've heard again and again. They couldn't find any major or major label subsidiary to release their music, so they just created their own company and did it themselves." ← Ongoing History of New Music > History of Indie Rock： Part 4 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q4dEV1yn3OY&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
