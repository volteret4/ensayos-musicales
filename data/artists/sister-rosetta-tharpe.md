**The Godmother of Rock and Roll** : A queer Black woman from Arkansas, Tharpe was a pioneering electric guitarist who bridged the gap between gospel and swing. She is considered the first female guitar hero and was revered by legends like Johnny Cash, Elvis Presley, and Little Richard. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
