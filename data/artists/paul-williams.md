## curiosities
**Proto-Goth/Death Metal Inventor Theory** : Alan Cross theorizes that Williams, through his work on *Phantom of the Paradise* in 1974, accidentally invented or created a proto-goth/death metal band ("The Undead") concept due to the Frankenstein-like monster, corpse paint, and dark musical themes, predating actual goth and death metal movements. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Star and Composer for Phantom of the Paradise (1974)** : hired in 1974 by director Brian De Palma to star in and write the music for *Phantom of the Paradise*, a rock musical horror comedy. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
