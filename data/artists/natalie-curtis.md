# artist - Natalie Curtis

## curiosities
**Cameo in "Control" Movie** : Natalie Curtis made a cameo appearance as an extra in the movie "Control," a biography about her father. In one scene, she tells someone to "f off" when an ambulance driver has to stand in for Ian, who was too ill to perform during a show, an event described as "kind of nice irony." "If you know where to look in the movie control, Natalie plays an extra. She tells someone to f off in one scene where an ambulance driver has to sub in for Ian during a show where Ian was too ill to go on." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
**Early Awareness of Father's Music** : Natalie Curtis was 13 months old when her father, Ian Curtis, died. She first learned he was a singer when she was about three years old, told by her mother. As she grew up, she was mostly an "outside observer" to the Joy Division phenomenon but gradually became aware that her dad was the subject of much discussion. "She would have been 13 months old when dad died. What became of her? Well she first learned that dad was a singer when she was about three, mom told her." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
**Fascination with Photography** : Natalie became fascinated by the stylish photos of her father and his band displayed throughout their home. She started taking her own pictures at age four using her grandmother's camera. "But she became fascinated with all the stylish photos of her father and his band that were all over the house. She started taking her own pictures of age four with a grandmother's camera." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
**Influenced by Kevin Cummings** : She became fascinated with the work of Kevin Cummings, a photographer renowned for shooting many photos for the British music press, including numerous Joy Division photos, which her mother displayed at home. "Over the years she became fascinated with the work of Kevin Cummings. Now Kevin was the guy who shot a lot of photos for the British music press, including tons and tons of Joy Division photos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
**Normal Childhood** : Despite her father's fame and tragic death, Natalie Curtis had a pretty normal childhood and attended Henry High School in Macclesfield, where Ian and Deborah lived. "Her childhood was pretty normal. She went to Henry High School in Macosfield, which is where Ian and Deborah lived." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
**Photography Career** : After high school, Natalie enrolled in Macclesfield College to study photography. In a "stunning symmetry," she later met Kevin Cummings and, as last reported, was working as his assistant. "When she got out of high school Natalie enrolled in Macosfield College where she studied photography." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U40hOYy_94E
