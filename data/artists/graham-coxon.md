## curiosities
**Introduction to Jamie Hewlett** : Graham Coxon of Blur was a big fan of Jamie Hewlett's work. He introduced Damon Albarn to Jamie, leading to their eventual collaboration on Gorillaz. "Graham became a big fan of Jamie's work. And after Graham introduced Damon to Jamie, they became acquaintances." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
**Unused Vocals for Humans Album** : Graham Coxon recorded vocals for the Gorillaz album "Humans," but his parts were ultimately not used on the final record. "Same thing happened with some vocals from Blur Band-made Grain Coxon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVdTCqpd798
