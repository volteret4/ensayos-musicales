# artist - The Smiths

## genres
- Indie Rock

## songs
**How Soon Is Now** : This song was featured on a Spotify playlist developed by Queen Mary University in London, comprising 700 songs chosen for their high likelihood of inducing chills or goosebumps. Research suggested that sadder, slower, less intense, and more instrumental songs were typically most effective in producing this physiological response. "How soon is now from the Smiths." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mTbagQE7akQ

## curiosities
**Influence on The Killers** : The band was explicitly mentioned as an influence on The Killers, who showed an "obvious love for music from Durand Durand and the Smiths and other groups from the 80s." "An indie band from Las Vegas, ball places, who had an obvious love for music from Durand Durand and the Smiths and other groups from the 80s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
**Refusal to Reunite for Recording** : The Smiths are mentioned as an example of a band, similar to The Clash and The Stone Roses, that despite significant offers, did not get back together to record new music after their initial run. This comparison is made in the context of Rage Against the Machine's decision not to record new material after their reunion. "Same with the Smiths... If they all did try to get back together and make the same kind of music they did when they were young angry men, what would the results be?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t2uXHP9cBdM
