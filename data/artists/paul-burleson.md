# artist - Paul Burleson

## songs
**The Train Kept A-Rollin' (1956)** : While playing with the Johnny Burnett Trio in 1956, Paul Burleson discovered a distorted sound by deliberately dislodging one of the tubes in his amp. He did this to replicate the sound of a damaged amplifier, leading to the distinct tone heard on this song. "He discovered that if he dislodged one of the tubes in his amp, something that he did to try to replicate the sound from a damaged amplifier, he ended up with this sound for a song called, The Train kept a rolling." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iGav9uJwA_w ← paul-burleson
