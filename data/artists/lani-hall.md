# artist - Lani Hall

## labels
- A&M Records (associated through marriage)

## songs
**Never Say Never Again (1983) – Title Theme** : Lani Hall, married to Herb Alpert of A&M Records, sang the main theme for the 1983 non-canonical James Bond film "Never Say Never Again." Despite having a "fine career on her own," her performance for this film, which was outside the traditional Bond universe and based on "Thunderball," is noted as making her "probably the least remembered singer of a Bond song."
