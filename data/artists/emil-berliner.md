# artist - Emil Berliner

## curiosities
**Gramophone Debut (1887)** : Emil Berliner's gramophone, which debuted ten years after Edison's talking machine, was another pioneering device for capturing sound. Like Edison's invention, it was limited to about three minutes of recording, a constraint that helped establish the standard length for popular songs.
