## curiosities
**Early 1990s Prime Time Success** : Limblifter was listed as one of the Canadian bands that gained "prime time" exposure in the early 1990s. "The early 90s were pretty amazing with a long list of Canadian bands getting a shot at prime time. We had moist, the watchman, lowest of the low, sons of freedom, grapes of wrath, rhymes with orange, pure Gandarva's limb lifter." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2kYleeMnl8
**Formation and Early Success** : Limblifter was formed as an offshoot band by brothers Ryan and Kurt Dahle, who were previously in Age of Electric. They achieved several hits that extended their presence in the music scene through the early 2000s. "They formed an offshoot band called Limlifter. And they had some hits that extended through the early 2000s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
**Lineup Change and Continued Activity** : Kurt Dahle left Limblifter to join The New Pornographers, while Ryan Dahle continued with Limblifter. This change illustrates the dynamic nature of band memberships in the Canadian music landscape, with Ryan Dahle remaining active in the project. "Kurt left to join the new pornographers, where Ryan stayed behind in Limlifter." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
