# artist - Sky Cries Mary

## curiosities
**Possible First Musical Netcast (Two Weeks Before Stones, 1994)** : Two weeks prior to The Rolling Stones' November 1994 netcast, Seattle band Sky Cries Mary partnered with the company Starwave to deliver a live performance that many consider to be the first netcast. This event also coincided with the band's creation of their own CD-ROM, titled "Coconut Soup and other advertisers."
