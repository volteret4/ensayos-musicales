# artist - Leav Prinz

## curiosities
**Artist for Fully Completely Album Artwork** : Leav Prinz is the artist responsible for the distinctive album cover of The Tragically Hip's *Fully Completely*. Rob Baker discovered her work in *Zoom* magazine, a European photo magazine, where she was featured for her unique technique of creating images with depth by pressing objects like bodies, coins, fish, and fruit onto a laser copier. She developed the album art based on Baker's conceptual ideas of Dutch life cycles, decay, and an "alien feast," creating the "beautiful life-size piece of art" in Battery Studios over a weekend. "And she creates pieces by pressing bodies and coins and fish and fruit and whatever she can find onto a laser copier." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
**Producer for The Tragically Hip's Fully Completely** : Chris Sangerides, a Greek Cypriot, produced The Tragically Hip's *Fully Completely*. He was known for working with heavy metal bands such as Judas Priest, Black Sabbath, Ozzy, and Thin Lizzy, but The Hip were specifically impressed by his work with Concrete Blonde. Sangerides was enthusiastic about producing *Fully Completely*, traveling to Toronto to see the band perform live before committing. He introduced a "traditional 1970s recording" approach, which was initially "completely alien" to The Hip, by stripping everything down and building tracks from the bass and drums up over three weeks, which resulted in a bigger, more isolated sound. "Chris really wanted to strip everything down in a way that I think is much more traditional 1970s recording, but for us it was completely alien." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
