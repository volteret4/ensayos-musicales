# artist - Douglas Colvin

## curiosities
**Founding Member of The Ramones** : In 1973, after visiting Manie's Music with John Cummings to buy instruments, Douglas Colvin (who later became D.D. Ramone) formed The Ramones a year later with John (Johnny Ramone), Joey, and Tommy. The purchase of a guitar, a bass, and a small amp from the store was a direct precursor to the band's formation. "A year later, Douglas was calling himself D.D. and Johnny was Johnny. And together with Joey and Tommy, they formed the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg ← douglas-colvin
