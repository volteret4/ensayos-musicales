# artist - Lars Ulrich

## genres
- Rock and Roll

## instruments
- Drums

## curiosities
**Anti-Downloading Activism** : Known for his vigorous opposition to music downloading, serving as the public face who led the charge against anyone downloading music for free.
**Controversial Public Statements** : Made strong public statements condemning Napster users, including suggestions to "go down to Tau Records and grab them off the shelves instead" of stealing, and threatening, "Our team of lawyers and researchers have your names And we're going to hunt you down like a table scrap pilfering grab as you are."
**Lawsuit Against Napster (2000)** : In spring 2000, after discovering unreleased Metallica files on Napster, he initiated a lawsuit against the company in April 2000, seeking to shut down the original Napster.
**Public Stance Against Piracy** : Participated in a "grand photo op" where a list of approximately 350,000 Napster users trading Metallica files (totaling 60,000 pages, representing 1.4 million copyright violations of 95 Metallica songs) was dropped in front of TV cameras.
