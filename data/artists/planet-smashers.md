## curiosities
**Planet Smashers (Montreal, 1994) – Founded Stomp Records; Survived the "Ska Death" of 1999–2002** : The Planet Smashers formed in 1994 and created their own label, Stomp Records, which has promoted dozens of ska, ska-punk, and ska-friendly bands including Buckle 9, Bedouin Soundclash, The Flatliners, The New York Ska-Jazz Ensemble, St. Alvia, and even The Toasters. The ska scene largely crashed in the US between 1999 and 2002, described by Matt Collier as "brutal" — the band stopped touring in the US for three years. The recovery beginning in 2003 brought ska back to its natural home as a subculture. "You do not want to be Scott from 1999 to 2002.
