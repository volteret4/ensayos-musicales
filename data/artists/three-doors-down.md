# artist - Three Doors Down

## members

## curiosities
**Fatal Drunk Driving Incident (2013)** : In 2013, Todd Harrell, the bassist for Three Doors Down, was driving drunk and high on OxyContin and other prescription drugs when his speeding car caused the death of a truck driver. This was his second drunk driving conviction. "In 2013, he was driving drunk and high on oxy and other prescription drugs when his speeding car resulted in the death of a truck driver." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Incarceration and Expulsion from Band** : Harrell served a year in jail for the fatal accident. Due to this being his second drunk driving conviction, he was also removed from Three Doors Down. "He spent a year in jail, and since that was the second drunk driving conviction, he was also kicked out of the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Ongoing Legal Troubles (2014, 2018)** : Harrell continued to face legal issues, including another DUI charge in 2014 and being caught sending drugs to friends in prison. In 2018, police responding to a domestic disturbance call at his home found drugs and guns, violating his release conditions and leading to a 10-year sentence with the Mississippi Department of Corrections. "There was another DUI charge in 2014, and then Herrell was caught sending drugs to buddies in prison." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
