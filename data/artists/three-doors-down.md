## curiosities

"A gig at CBGB got the attention of Republic Records, a subsidiary of Universal Music, and they signed them to a deal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Continuing Career** : Despite some tailing off in sales after their initial success, Three Doors Down has remained together, continuing to release albums and tour. "Things tailed off a little bit after that, but the band is still together and they're still releasing albums and still touring." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Escatawpa, Mississippi Origins - Population 3,566** : Three Doors Down formed in Escatawpa, Mississippi, a town of 3,566 people located on the Gulf Coast along Interstate 10, in an area referred to as the "Redneck Riviera." "Way down in Southern Mississippi, right on the Gulf Coast along Interstate 10, an area called, and I'm not making this up, the Redneck Riviera, is the town of Escatapa. It's east of Bloc Sea, west of Mobile, Alabama, south of Haddie's Burg, and part of the area around Pascagula. This is where you'll find three doors down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Fatal Drunk Driving Incident (2013)** : In 2013, Todd Harrell, the bassist for Three Doors Down, was driving drunk and high on OxyContin and other prescription drugs when his speeding car caused the death of a truck driver. This was his second drunk driving conviction. "In 2013, he was driving drunk and high on oxy and other prescription drugs when his speeding car resulted in the death of a truck driver." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Formation Out of Boredom** : The band formed in 1996 seemingly out of a lack of other activities, with things surprisingly going well and leading them to record original material. "They were formed in 1996 because they really had nothing better to do, but things went surprisingly well and they decided to record some original material." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Guitarist Matt Roberts' Death** : Original guitarist Matt Roberts, who had left the band in 2012, tragically died of an overdose in 2016. "Originally, the guitarist Matt Roberts died of an overdose in 2016. He'd left the band in 2012." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CyTI00SIrLE
**Incarceration and Expulsion from Band** : Harrell served a year in jail for the fatal accident. Due to this being his second drunk driving conviction, he was also removed from Three Doors Down. "He spent a year in jail, and since that was the second drunk driving conviction, he was also kicked out of the band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Ongoing Legal Troubles (2014, 2018)** : Harrell continued to face legal issues, including another DUI charge in 2014 and being caught sending drugs to friends in prison. In 2018, police responding to a domestic disturbance call at his home found drugs and guns, violating his release conditions and leading to a 10-year sentence with the Mississippi Department of Corrections. "There was another DUI charge in 2014, and then Herrell was caught sending drugs to buddies in prison." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vKSjpvVQa8
**Reputation Spread to New York** : After their local radio hit, the band's reputation quickly spread throughout the region and then up the East Coast, reaching New York City.
