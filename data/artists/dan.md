## curiosities
**Guitar Influence on Ian Thornley** : Dan was a highly skilled guitar player and a friend of Ian Thornley, whom Ian met during a summer job at Sounds Interchange. Dan's ability to play Frank Zappa riffs and Led Zeppelin's "Stairway to Heaven" with accuracy greatly inspired Ian to seriously pursue the guitar.
