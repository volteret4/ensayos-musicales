# artist - Hole

## members
- Courtney Love (singer)

## labels
- Geffen

## albums
**Pretty on the Inside (1991)** : The band's debut record, co-produced by Kim Gordon (Sonic Youth) and Don Fleming (known for work with Dinosaur Jr.). Released in September 1991, it eventually became a cult favorite.

## songs
**Teenage Horde (1991)** : A single from the debut album "Pretty on the Inside" (1991). It reached number one on the UK indie chart.

## curiosities
**Connection to Riot Grrrl** : Hole and Courtney Love eventually made connections in the Pacific Northwest, discovering the riot grrrl scene, which also cited Kim Gordon as an influence.
**Courtney Love's Early Career** : Before Hole, Courtney Love had been in bands like Sugar Baby Doll and Babes in Toyland, and was briefly the singer for Faith No More (approximately 15 minutes). She also pursued art, acting, and stripping, using money from stripping to buy gear for Hole.
**Ramones Video Appearance** : Courtney Love appeared as one of the background partiers in The Ramones' 1988 video for "I Wanna Be Sedated."
**Seeking Kim Gordon's Production** : Courtney Love, as a 20-something, wrote a letter to Kim Gordon, asking her to produce Hole's debut record; Kim Gordon agreed.
**Visibility in the 90s** : Courtney Love was one of the "most visible and most talked about women" of the 1990s, influencing untold numbers of women and men.
