## concerts
- **George McGovern Benefit Concert (1972)** : On April 5th, 1972, Barbra Streisand performed at the Los Angeles Forum as part of Warren Beatty's "rock and rhetoric rallies" to benefit George McGovern's presidential campaign. Despite not doing live performances at the time due to her movie career, she was persuaded by Beatty to participate and was backed by Quincy Jones and his orchestra. The concert also featured Carol King and James Taylor, with tickets ranging from $4 to $400. "And then around 11 o'clock, outcomes Barbara Streisand."

## albums
**Stoney End (1971)** : The title track from this 1971 album is a cover of a song by Laura Nyro. **Support for George McGovern (1972)** : Barbra Streisand, despite her busy movie career, was convinced by Warren Beatty to perform at a benefit concert for George McGovern in Los Angeles on April 5th, 1972. She expressed no regrets for supporting McGovern, even after his landslide defeat, stating in 2010 at an American Film Institute award ceremony that she admired him and was glad to perform for his cause.
