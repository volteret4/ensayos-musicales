# artist - Ray Parker Jr.

## songs
**Ghostbusters Theme Song (1984)** : Ray Parker Jr. was enlisted to create the iconic theme song for the "Ghostbusters" film after an earlier, less effective theme was used in promotional pleas to Canadian movie chains. His version became synonymous with the 1980s movie starring Bill Murray, Harold Ramis, Ernie Hudson, and Dan Aykroyd.
