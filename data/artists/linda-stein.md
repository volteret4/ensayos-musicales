# artist - Linda Stein

## curiosities
**Early Career - Teacher and Music Manager** : Before her career in real estate, Linda Stein was initially a teacher. She later transitioned into the underground music scene in New York, where she managed The Ramones for a period. "Back in the day, Linda used to be a teacher. Then she drifted into the underground music scene in New York and ended up for a time managing the remones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcYKZ5ovoy4 ← linda-stein
**Marriage to Seymour Stein - Sire Records Connection** : Linda was married to Seymour Stein, the founder of Sire Records. This New York record label signed influential artists such as The Ramones, Talking Heads, The Tragically Hip, The Pretenders, and Madonna. "She was also married to see more Stein, the founder of Sire Records, a New York record label that signed everyone from the remones to the talking heads, to the tragically hip, to the pretenders to Madonna." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcYKZ5ovoy4 ← linda-stein
**Murder - October 30, 2007** : On October 30, 2007, Linda Stein was found dead in her Fifth Avenue home, having been bludgeoned to death around her head and neck. Her assistant, Nativia Lowry, was arrested less than two weeks later and charged with second-degree murder, though Lowry's family alleged the confession was coerced and pointed blame at Linda's daughter, Mandy Stein. "But then on October 30th of 2007, she was found dead in her fifth avenue home. She had been bludgeoned to death around her head and neck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcYKZ5ovoy4 ← linda-stein
**New York Scene Figure - CBGB's and Gossip Columns** : She was an active participant in the New York music scene, frequently attending prominent clubs like CBGB's and often appearing in gossip columns. "Linda was very much part of the scene, hanging out at all the right clubs like CBGB's and getting in all the right gossip columns." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcYKZ5ovoy4 ← linda-stein
**Real Estate Career - Selling to Celebrities** : In the 1980s, Linda shifted careers to become a real estate agent and was highly successful, selling properties to notable figures like Sting, Angelina Jolie, and Steven Spielberg. She was also reportedly the inspiration for the real estate agent character in Oliver Stone's movie "Wall Street." "But in the 1980s, she decided that she had had enough and became a real estate agent. She did very, very well selling houses and apartments and condos to people like Sting, an Angelina Jolie and Steven Spielberg." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcYKZ5ovoy4 ← linda-stein
