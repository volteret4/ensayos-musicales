# artist - Glenn Miller Orchestra

## curiosities
**First Gold Record Award Recipient (1941)** : The Glenn Miller Orchestra received the first gold record award in 1941 for one of their million-plus selling records. This recognition initiated a lasting trend within the music industry of presenting awards for sales milestones.
