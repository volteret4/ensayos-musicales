**Sin City (1969)** : Co-written with Chris Hillman, this song appeared on The Flying Burrito Brothers' "Gilded Palace of Sin." Its origin began with Hillman waking up with the line, "This old town is filled with sin, it'll swallow you in," to which Parsons added the melody. The song captures the darker side of Hollywood and the shift from the 1960s to the 1970s, with verses alluding to Bobby Kennedy's assassination in Los Angeles on June 6, 1968. ## curiosities
**Death and Body Snatching** : Parsons died on September 19, 1973, from a drug overdose, embodying a "true 70s rock star fashion." He had expressed a wish to be cremated and have his remains scattered in the Joshua Tree desert, contrary to his family's desire for a burial in Louisiana. His manager, Phil Kaufman, stole Parsons' body from Los Angeles International Airport and performed a DIY cremation in the desert, an event that significantly contributed to his legend and popularity.
