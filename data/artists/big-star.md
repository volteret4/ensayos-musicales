## albums
**Keep an Eye on the Sky (2013) - Box Set with Grammy-Winning Liner Notes** : In 2013, this box set by the cult-favorite Power Pop band Big Star was awarded a Grammy for Best Liner Notes. The notes, written by American rock-a-billy singer Robert Gordon, provided extensive insight into 52 previously unreleased tracks from the band's first three albums of the early and mid-1970s.
