# artist - Taj Khrushlo

## curiosities
**Fella Production Company Founding** : Taj Khrushlo, along with Director X, founded the production company Fella, which achieved a significant breakthrough with the music video for DJ Khaled, Drake, and Justin Bieber's song "Popstar." Originally from Brampton, Ontario, they transitioned from visual storytelling to audio through their podcast, "Art Context." "We broke our production company, Fella, with this music video for DJ Khaled, Drake and Bieber called Popstar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KaIp6KVn-co
