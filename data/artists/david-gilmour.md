This acquisition highlights the store's importance as a source for significant musical instruments. "David Gilmore's famous Black Strat, the legendary guitar, used on so many iconic Pink Floyd recordings, was purchased at Manie's in 1969." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
**Roadie for Pink Floyd, then Member** : In the mid-1960s, David Gilmour initially worked as a roadie for Pink Floyd. He was later invited to join the band as a guitarist by drummer Nick Mason, marking his transition from support staff to a full-fledged member. "Way back in the mid 1960s David Gilmore roadied for a young band called Pink Floyd. **The "Black Strat"** : David Gilmour's iconic "Black Strat" was purchased at Mani's Music in New York in 1970. This specific guitar became a cornerstone of his sound, being utilized for every Pink Floyd album and tour following its acquisition.
