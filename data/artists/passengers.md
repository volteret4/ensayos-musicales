# artist - Passengers

## members
- U2 (all members)

## albums
**Original Soundtracks 1 (1995) - Pseudonymous Album** : Released on November 7, 1995, this album was created by U2 under the pseudonym Passengers, with Brian Eno as producer. Approximately half of the album consisted of instrumental tracks, which U2 described as "great time music." Despite its unique nature, it achieved moderate chart success, reaching number 15 on the Canadian album charts, number 12 in the UK, but only number 76 in the US. "their album was released on November 7, 1995. About half of it was instrumental." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E

## songs
**Miss Sarajevo** : This was the sole official single released from Passengers' album, Original Soundtracks 1. Notably, it featured Italian opera star Luciano Pavarotti on vocals. The song took its name from "The Pillow Book," the fake film for which the Passengers project was originally conceived as a soundtrack. "There was just one official single, and it featured of all people, Italian opera star Luciano Pavarotti. This song took its name from the fake film for which it was written." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E

## curiosities
**Brian Eno's Pseudonymous Liner Notes** : For the Passengers album, Original Soundtracks 1, producer Brian Eno contributed liner notes under two different fake names: Ben O'Rion and CS Both-Fop. The liner notes were filled with various in-jokes and hidden references, adding to the mystique of U2's pseudonymous project. "Who was this Ben O'Rion and CS both-fop who wrote the liner notes? Well both were fake names given to Brian Eno, and the deeper you go into the liner notes, the more in jokes you'll find." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E
