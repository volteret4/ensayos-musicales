## songs
**The Night the Lights Went Out in Georgia** : Vicki Lawrence recorded this song after Sonny Bono prevented Cher from doing so. Lawrence famously responded to Cher's situation with "thank you very much. I will take it from here." **The Night the Lights Went Out in Georgia (1972)** : Written by country songwriter Bobby Russell, this song was initially offered to Cher but turned down by Sonny Bono. Russell then gave it to his wife, Vicki Lawrence, who recorded it backed by the famed Wrecking Crew Studio Musicians. Released in November 1972, it became a number one hit on the Billboard pop chart (not country) on April 7, 1973. "The night the lights went out in Georgia was released in November 1972, and on April 7, 1973, it hit number one on the Billboard chart.
