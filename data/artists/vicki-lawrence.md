## songs
**The Night the Lights Went Out in Georgia (1972)** : Written by country songwriter Bobby Russell, this song was initially offered to Cher but turned down by Sonny Bono. Russell then gave it to his wife, Vicki Lawrence, who recorded it backed by the famed Wrecking Crew Studio Musicians. Released in November 1972, it became a number one hit on the Billboard pop chart (not country) on April 7, 1973.
