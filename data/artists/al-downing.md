# artist - Al Downing

## songs
**All Because of Holding On** : This R&B track is widely considered to be the very first 12-inch single ever created. The 12-inch format, which was accidentally developed by engineer Tom Moulton, gained significant popularity among disco DJs. "We think that the very first twelve inch single ever created was, all be holding on by an R&B singer named Al Downing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**All Beholding (XXXX)** : This was the very first track to be pressed as a 12-inch single. In 1974, Tom Molten, needing a blank record larger than the standard 7-inch, asked an engineer to press his new mix onto a 12-inch blank, with the instruction to play it at 45 RPM instead of the usual 33 RPM, thus birthing the 12-inch single format. "The very first track to be pressed this way was all beholding by a guy named Al Downing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
