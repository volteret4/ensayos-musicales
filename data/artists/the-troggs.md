## curiosities
**Mid-60s Garage Band** : The Troggs were listed as one of the rough, ragged, and raw-sounding bands from the 1960s, emerging from North American garages and basements. Their rebellious aesthetic, often featuring leather jackets, drew comparisons to figures like Marlon Brando and James Dean, leading some to describe them visually as "punks."
