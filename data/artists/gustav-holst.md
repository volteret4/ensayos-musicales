# artist - Gustav Holst

## albums
**The Planets (1918) - Early Example of Gradual Fade-Out in Performance** : Gustav Holst's orchestral suite "The Planets" premiered on September 29, 1918, with specific instructions for its final movement, "Neptune." A women's chorus was to be placed in an unseen room off-stage, singing the final bar repeatedly as the door slowly closed, creating a gradual fade-out effect for the audience.
