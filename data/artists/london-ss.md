# artist - London SS

## members
- Chrissie Hynde
- Future members of Generation X
- Future members of Public Image Limited
- Mick Jones

## curiosities
**Pre-Clash Band** : The London SS was Mick Jones's band before The Clash, known for a "rotating line" of members. Despite managing to rehearse sometimes, they "never really got around to actually playing a gig." The group included future members of bands like Generation X and Public Image Limited, and notably featured Chrissie Hynde, who would later form The Pretenders.
