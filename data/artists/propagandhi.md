## curiosities
**Portage la Prairie, Manitoba to Global Punk Influence – Vegan Anarchist Politics and Fat Wreck Chords** : Propagandhi formed in 1986 in Portage la Prairie, Manitoba (population approximately 13,000), 50 miles west of Winnipeg. They built a reputation for strongly left-wing, sometimes anarchist, vegan politics targeting human rights violations, sexism, racism, capitalism, and organized religion. They aligned with American bands NOFX and Fugazi and were signed to Fat Mike's Fat Wreck Chords punk label out of California. Their debut record, *How to Clean Everything*, appeared in 1993. Still based in Winnipeg, they remain one of Canada's premier punk bands. "Very left-wing, sometimes anarchist, often vegan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0X7TQMGgqbY
