# artist - Pinchico

## members
- Andy
- John (original drummer, currently missing)
- Owen (Owain Davis)
- Sean

## albums
**Death Metal (2000) – EP** : This demo EP was ripped to a CDR and reportedly released in 2000. It was found in a British charity shop, but despite having the title, artist, and track listing, it became a Lost Wave song due to the music community's obsession with finding more about the band. "It had the title of Death Metal, and was apparently released in 2000 by a group called Penchico." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← pinchico

## curiosities
**Band Reunion and New Releases** : Following Owen Davis's discovery, he contacted two other band members, leading to a reunion (minus the original drummer, John, who remains missing). Although they didn't have the original master tapes, a friend possessed a copy of the EP in good condition. Four tracks were remastered and officially released, and the band has since played many gigs, undertaken international tours, and released a proper debut album. "No one had the original master tapes, but a friend had a copy of the EP in good condition. Four tracks from that CD were remastered and released." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← pinchico
**Barcode Leads to Identification** : The search for Pinchico intensified after the "Death Metal" EP was uploaded to YouTube in 2017, garnering approximately 200,000 views and extensive discussions. Someone eventually noticed the barcode on the CD's cover, which helped trace the origin back to the charity shop in Sherwood, Noneham. "Someone noticed the barcode on the CD's cover. Finally, this turned the search back to the charity shop which was in Sherwood in Noneham." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← pinchico
**Discovery in British Charity Shop** : The "Death Metal" EP was discovered in a British charity shop, ripped to a CDR. The disc itself was in poor condition, suffering from "Discrot," a phenomenon where CDR layers separate, resulting in distortion and skips. "He'd found in a British charity shop. It had the title of Death Metal, and was apparently released in 2000 by a group called Penchico." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← pinchico
**Limited Initial Information** : The original CD offered very little information about the band members, identifying them only by their first names: Owen, Andy, Sean, and John. This scarcity of detail fueled the online community's determination to learn more. "Again, we look at this CD and there were four guys identified only by their first names, Owen, Andy, Sean and John." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← pinchico
**Tracking Down Owen Davis** : Owen Davis, one of the band members who used the unusual spelling "O-W-A-I-N," was tracked down via Facebook in January 2020. He was reportedly shocked to learn that Pinchico had been the subject of a massive online search. "Boom. Owen Davis was tracked down via Facebook in January 2020. He was shocked that Pinchico had been the target of this search." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsPoPUByjYw ← pinchico
