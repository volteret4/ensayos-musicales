## curiosities
**Dog Named Sid Barrett** : Kim Gordon was a devoted admirer of Pink Floyd's early musical output. Her admiration was such that she named her dog after Syd Barrett, one of the founding members of Pink Floyd. "Kim Gordon, was such a fan of Pink Floyd's early work that she named her dog Sid Barrett." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
**Early Life and Education** : Born in Rochester, New York, and raised in Los Angeles, Kim Gordon formed her first band during her time as a student at York University in Toronto. "She was born in Rochester, New York, raised in Los Angeles, but she formed her first band while she was a student at York University in Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Female Pioneer Inspiration** : Kim Gordon of Sonic Youth is cited as an inspiration for the Riot Grrrl movement, representing a legacy of female pioneers in punk and post-punk. "Taking inspiration from the female pioneers of punk and post-punk, it would be Patty Smith and Susie Sue and Kim Gordon of Sonic Youth, Chrissy Hine, Oni DeFranco, and so on." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zZM9ajwYr9E
**Influence and Mentorship** : Kim Gordon became a significant inspiration and influence for many young women in music, particularly within the indie scene. Her impact was such that a young Courtney Love from San Francisco wrote to her asking her to produce Hole's debut record. "So many young women were inspired and influenced by her, including a 20-something young woman from San Francisco who wrote Kim a letter saying, look, I love what you do. I have a new band. Will you produce our debut record for us?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
