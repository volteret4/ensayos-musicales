**Pioneer of Bebop Jazz** : Dexter Gordon contributed to the rise of bebop jazz, an experimental and virtuosic style that emerged as a reaction against the constraints of big band music. Bebop featured complex improvisations and often lacked discernible beats or melodies for dancing. "Charlie Parker, Sonny Rollins, Dexter Gordon, Miles Davis, Dizzy Gillespie, Thelonius Monk.
