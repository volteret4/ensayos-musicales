# artist - Vivienne Westwood

## curiosities
**Collaboration with Malcolm McLaren** : Vivienne Westwood co-owned a clothing store in Chelsea, London, with her partner Malcolm McLaren. The store, initially called "Let It Rock," focused on fashion for Teddyboys, bondage gear, and sex toys. After McLaren's pivotal trip to New York, the store was rebranded as "Sex" and became instrumental in popularizing punk fashion by selling garments held together with safety pins, a style inspired by Richard Hell.
