# artist - Isley Brothers

## curiosities
**Attempted Bowie Bonds** : The Isley Brothers were among the artists who tried to leverage their future music royalties through a financial mechanism inspired by Bowie Bonds. "James Brown, the songwriting team of Holland Dozier Holland, the Isle of Brothers, Ashford and Simpson, even the guy who wrote songs like I Love Rock and Roll for Joan Jett, and another songwriter who wrote songs for Rod Stewart and Tupac." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yq2A_o9-ulM ← isley-brothers
**Attempted Royalty Securitization** : The Isley Brothers attempted to package their royalties against the market, similar to David Bowie's "Bowie Bonds," but did not achieve the same level of success. "James Brown, the Isle brothers, and a few others tried the same thing, but not with the same success." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pe40SqDrpZ0 ← isley-brothers
