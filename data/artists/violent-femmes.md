**The Crow Soundtrack Contribution (1994)** : The Violent Femmes contributed to *The Crow* soundtrack in 1994, which was released at the peak of the alternative and Lollapalooza period. The film's music supervisors successfully curated a dark and fitting collection that reflected the movie's themes and its tragic production history.
