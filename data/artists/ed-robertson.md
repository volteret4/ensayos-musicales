## curiosities
**"Ed's Up" TV Show** : Starred in a TV show called "Ed's Up," which documented his efforts to obtain a float plane license. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
**Cessna Plane Crash (August 24, 2008)** : While piloting a Cessna 206 amphibious plane from Baptiste Lake near Bancroft, Ontario, the plane lost speed at around 12:30 pm and crashed into a grove of trees about 300 meters from shore. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
**Continued Flying** : Has continued to fly whenever he can following the crash. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
**Crash Investigation (2008)** : The Transportation Safety Board investigated the August 2008 crash but could not find a concrete cause, ruling out pilot error, mechanical malfunction, and weather. They suggested a possible air current anomaly leading to a loss of lift, airspeed, and altitude. Ed was commended for his cooperation. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
**Pilot's License (2005)** : Obtained his pilot's license in 2005. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
**Plane Crash and Survival (2008)** : On August 24, 2008, in the afternoon, Ed Robertson took off in a Cessna 206 amphibious plane from Baptiste Lake near Bancroft, Ontario. Around 12:30 PM, the plane lost speed and crashed into a grove of trees about 300 meters from shore. Ed and his three passengers were all able to walk away. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
**Survival and Investigation (August 2008)** : He and his three passengers were able to walk away unharmed. A Transportation Safety Board investigation could not determine a concrete cause, ruling out pilot error, mechanical malfunction, or weather, suggesting a possible air current anomaly that led to a loss of lift and airspeed. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3eGBnwPWRGo
