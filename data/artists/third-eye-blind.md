## curiosities
**First-Person Lyrical Approach** : Third Eye Blind was cited as an example of a Post-Grunge band whose lyrical style leaned heavily on the "first person approach," illustrating a key distinction from the more indirect lyrical themes of Grunge. "Now think about the lyrical writing style of collective soul or bush or third eye blind and matchbox 20. You see what I mean?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU
**Tour Bus Bunk Description** : Arion Salazar describes the bunks on a tour bus as small, coffin-like compartments. These bunks have curtains that can be pulled and buttoned shut, creating a "little cocoon" that serves as a private space for band members, likened to the "hellbop" concept. "We're in these little things that are like coffins, the curtains you pull them shut and button them shut and you're like securing your little cocoon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgN0vBIugLc
