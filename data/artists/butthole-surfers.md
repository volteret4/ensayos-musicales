# artist - Butthole Surfers

## members
- Gibby Haynes

## curiosities
**Introduction to Heroin** : Gibby Haynes was identified as the person who apparently introduced Scott Weiland to heroin.
**Lollapalooza 1991 Performer** : performed at the first Lollapalooza festival in 1991.
**Rehab Stay at Exodus Recovery Center (Spring 1994)** : Gibby Haynes was also present at the Exodus recovery center in Marina del Rey when Scott Weiland considered checking in for detox, contributing to Weiland's decision not to check in.
