## curiosities
**Computer Animation and Pilot's License** : Rowntree is a computer animator with an ownership stake in Nanomotion, an animation company, and has contributed to three research papers on computer graphics. He also holds both a private pilot's license and an "amateur radio license." "He's a computer animator with an ownership stake in an animation company called Nanomotion." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Legal and Political Career** : During Blur's hiatus in the early 2000s, Rowntree pursued a career as a solicitor (lawyer) in the UK, specializing in criminal law and working as an activist for American prisoners on death row. He also campaigned against the persecution of people who illegally shared music online and, after three unsuccessful attempts, was elected as a county counsellor for the Labour Party in 2017. "During Blur's hiatus in the early odds, Dave decided he wanted to be a solicitor, which is a type of lawyer in the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Social Activism and Charity Work** : A former cocaine addict, Rowntree now works with a charity that advocates for humane drug policies and has raised money for Syrian refugees. "Dave is a former cocaine addict who now works with a charity that helps formulate humane drug policies." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Space and Aeronautics Enthusiast** : Dave Rowntree developed a lifelong interest in space, astronomy, rockets, and exploration. In 1998, he and bandmate Alex James toured NASA facilities in Houston, an experience that led them to get involved with the European Space Agency's Mars Express mission, for which Blur recorded a musical call sign. "Dave has always been into space, astronomy, rockets, exploration, that kind of thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
