**Criticism of The Babys (1980)** : In December 1980, Mellencamp, then known as John Cougar Mellencamp (after going by Johnny Cougar), told a reporter for the San Francisco Examiner that The Babys were "a bullshit band," specifically targeting their song "Every Time I Think of You." **Self-Assessment of Own Hits (1980)** : Mellencamp considered his own 1980 hits, "This Time" and "Ain't Even Done With The Night," to be "stupid little pop records." He desired to make rock music for a specific group, feeling he and The Babys were falling short.
