# artist - Harry Chamberlain

## curiosities
**Inventor of Mellotron Precursor** : Harry Chamberlain invented a mechanical, tape-based device that started as a drum machine. He recorded drum rhythms on tape loops that could be sped up or slowed down. Only about ten units of this "Chamberlain Rhythm Mate" were built before Chamberlain shifted focus to other instrument sounds for subsequent models.
