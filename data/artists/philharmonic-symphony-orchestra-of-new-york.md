# artist - Philharmonic Symphony Orchestra of New York

## albums
**Mendelssohn Violin Concerto in E Minor (1948) - First LP Release** : This performance by the Philharmonic Symphony Orchestra of New York was featured on the first LP (12-inch, 33 and a third album) ever released. It was introduced by Columbia Records in 1948, marking a new era for recording formats after the 78 RPM record.
