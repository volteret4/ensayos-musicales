## instruments
- **Electro-Harmonix Big Muff** : Kurt Cobain utilized an Electro-Harmonix Big Muff distortion pedal as an "important detour" in his signal chain when recording a song, demonstrating the continued use of distortion pedal technology decades after its accidental invention by Grady Martin.
- **Fender Telecaster (Japanese-made)** : Kurt Cobain used a Japanese-made Fender Telecaster in his recording setup that prominently featured a distortion pedal.
- **Marshall Amplifier** : Kurt Cobain's guitar signal was routed through a Marshall amplifier as part of his recording setup.

## curiosities
**"Mera Gold" – Only Non-Cobain Song** : "Mera Gold," recorded by Dave Grohl during the "In Utero" sessions, is the only song in the entire Nirvana canon that was neither written nor sung by Kurt Cobain. It did not make the album but was released as the last track on the "Heart Shaped Box" CD single and later on the "With The Lights Out" box set. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**1992 Reading Performance** : Nirvana played the Reading Festival twice. Their 1992 performance became famous due to rumors that Kurt Cobain was "deathly ill."
