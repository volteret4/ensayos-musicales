## curiosities
**Father's Diverse Ventures and Tragic Death** : Hugh Everett III successfully applied mathematics, specifically Lagrange multipliers, to become a millionaire. He then used some of his wealth to establish a company focused on studying the parallels between human speech and machine speech. Sadly, he died of a heart attack in 1982, and his son Mark was the one who found him. "Later, Mr. Everett applied mathematics to making money." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU ← the-eels
**Quest to Understand Father's Theories** : Following his father's death, Mark Oliver Everett embarked on a deeply personal quest to understand his father's complex scientific theories, a journey that has been documented in a PBS special. "And Mark has since been on a quest to understand his father's theories." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU ← the-eels
**Son of a Renowned Physicist** : Mark Oliver Everett, known as E from The Eels, is the son of Hugh Everett III, a physicist famous for developing the Many Worlds Theory. His father's theories, initially met with skepticism, are now highly regarded for their ability to resolve paradoxes in quantum theory and have significantly influenced science fiction literature. "His father was Hugh Everett III." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU ← the-eels
