## songs
**The United States of Whatever (2003)** : A largely improvised song from Liam Lynch's 2003 album, "Fake Songs," released on the Global Warming label. It became a hit at Alt Rock Radio in Canada and the US, and was a genuine top 40 hit in Belgium, New Zealand, and the UK (reaching number 10). Australia went "nuts" over the song, pushing it to number one. It has been performed live with Foo Fighters and No Doubt, used in video games and movies, and remixed at least once. It is categorized as a comedy rock song and is the shortest on the list, running just 106 seconds. As of February 2026, it had 28 million Spotify plays and 10.4 million YouTube views, with a One Hit Wonder Power Ranking of 3.56 out of 10. "It was largely improvised.
