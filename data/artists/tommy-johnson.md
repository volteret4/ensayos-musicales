## songs
**Alcohol and Jake Blues (1930)** : A super rare 78 RPM record released on the Paramount label in 1930. Records from this era were made using shellac, making them very brittle and prone to smashing easily. A small but dedicated community of collectors trades in these old blues records. In 2009, this specific record sold for $37,000 US, which was just over $50,000 Canadian.
