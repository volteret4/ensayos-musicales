## curiosities
**Funeral Arrangement Posed Behind Drums (2021)** : Bonnie Brantz, a Toronto drummer who passed away at 73, had an unconventional funeral arrangement where his embalmed body was posed behind his drum kit for viewing. His family collaborated with a funeral home to present him in this manner, foregoing a traditional casket.
