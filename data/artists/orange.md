## curiosities
**Formation and Disbandment** : Orange was an early group formed by five artists, including Tien de Crecy and the future members of Air, before Air officially came into existence. The band ultimately failed to coalesce, primarily because its members possessed widely disparate musical interests. The future members of Air were drawn to ambient and symphonic electronic music, influenced by acts like Banger and Brian Eno, while Tien de Crecy leaned towards a more "discotecquero" sound. The group existed for a very short period, failing to produce any notable output.
