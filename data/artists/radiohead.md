## albums
**OK Computer (1997) – Masterpiece Album** : Released just prior to their Glastonbury 1997 performance, this album received glowing reviews and quickly became recognized as the band's masterpiece.

## songs
**Karma Police (1997 Live Version)** : Radiohead performed "Karma Police" from their album "OK Computer" during their legendary Glastonbury Festival set on June 28th, 1997.

## curiosities
**"Best Show of All Time" Acclaim (June 28, 1997)** : Many attendees and critics maintained that Radiohead's Glastonbury gig on June 28th, 1997, was simply the best show of all time, solidifying their legend.
**Certified Indie Artist** : Recognized as a certifiably indie artist due to recording for labels within the Beggars Group, which includes Matador, Rough Trade, XL Recordings, and Young Turks.
**Glastonbury Festival Performance (June 28, 1997)** : Radiohead's set at the Glastonbury Festival on June 28th, 1997, occurred shortly after the release of their acclaimed album "OK Computer," with high expectations.
**Technical Difficulties and Breakthrough (June 28, 1997)** : Midway through their Glastonbury 1997 set, the band experienced severe technical problems including stage monitor issues and missed cues, with a roadie shining a spotlight directly into Thom Yorke's eyes; Yorke's demand to turn the light on the crowd coincided with the problems clearing and "magic set in."
