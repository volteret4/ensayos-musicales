**Artwork Dispute with Friend** : In 2007, a friend of Adam Jones named Cam De Leon, a well-known digital illustrator who worked on films like *Ghostbusters*, claimed credit for some artwork he had created for Tool. This dispute led to a complex, multi-level lawsuit involving the band and their insurance company, which lasted for eight years. "A friend of guitarist Adam Jones named Cam De Leon, claimed that he created some artwork for the band and wanted some credit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGJXOoT2PI ← adam-jones
