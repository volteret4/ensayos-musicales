## songs
**Stranger Thoughts** : Another single by Camouflage, mentioned as one that "almost sort of kind of broke through" but is only remembered by "a real fan," reinforcing "The Great Commandment" as their overshadowed hit. "And if you're a fan of 80s retro and you're still rocking your brains, trying to remember the other single, it was stranger thoughts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcF5JMhBj2M
**The Great Commandment (1987)** : Camouflage's first and biggest single from 1987. It was a major dance hit in North America, reaching number one on those charts, and peaked at number three on the alternative charts and alternative radio. It was initially mistaken for a new Depeche Mode song due to its similar sound. As of February 2026, it had 49.3 million Spotify plays and 15.3 million YouTube views, totaling 65.6 million, with a One Hit Wonder Power Rating of 4.49 out of 10. "It didn't do much top 40 wise, but it was a major dance hit in North America. Number one on those charts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcF5JMhBj2M
