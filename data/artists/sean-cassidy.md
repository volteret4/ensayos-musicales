# artist - Sean Cassidy

## curiosities
**Career Trajectory - Teenage Idol to Executive Producer** : Sean Cassidy's star began to fade after his third album, leading him to transition into acting. Following his acting career, he became a TV writer and producer. Today, he holds the position of executive producer for the TV show "New Amsterdam." This career pivot suggests he might have learned from his brother David's experiences regarding the transient nature of teenage idol fame.
