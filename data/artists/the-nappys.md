## songs
**I'm Your Baby's Daddy** : This song was recorded by The Nappys, which is Coldplay operating under a pseudonym. It was written by Chris Martin as a goofy tribute to his then-wife Gwyneth Paltrow, who was expecting their first child. The accompanying video for the song was notably introduced by Sir George Martin, wearing a curly red wig. "The song is called I'm Your Baby's Daddy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E

## curiosities
**Sir George Martin's Video Introduction** : The video for The Nappys' song "I'm Your Baby's Daddy" features an introduction by Sir George Martin. He appears in the video wearing a curly red wig, adding to the goofy and lighthearted nature of the project, which was actually Coldplay's pseudonymous effort. "But the guy in the wig introducing the video is definitely Sir George Martin." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itK5fEqnA5E
