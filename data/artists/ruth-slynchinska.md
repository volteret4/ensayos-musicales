# artist - Ruth Slynchinska

## labels
- Deca

## albums
**My Life in Music (2022) - Solo Album at 97** : Ruth Slynchinska signed a new record deal with Deca in early 2022 for this solo album, released when she was 97 years old. She had previously recorded classical records for Deca in the 1950s and early 1960s, but her contract had lapsed and was not renewed until this new deal. "Ruth Slynchinska got this record deal at the age of 97." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI

## curiosities
**Last Surviving Pupil of Rachmaninoff** : Ruth Slynchinska was the last surviving pupil of classical legend Sir Guy Rachmaninoff, whom she first met in the 1920s when she was declared a child prodigy.
