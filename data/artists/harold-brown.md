# artist - Harold Brown

## curiosities
**Inspiration for "Why Can't We Be Friends"** : Harold Brown, drummer for War, conceived the idea for the song "Why Can't We Be Friends" while traveling in Japan in the early 1970s. He observed universal human connections, noting that despite superficial differences, people are "more alike inside than we are on the outside." "We're all connected by language, he said, and by our food and by our culture. Most races don't know why they are racist, but you pick them up and take them over and drop them in a country like India or Pakistan and guess what? Why Can't We Be Friends?
