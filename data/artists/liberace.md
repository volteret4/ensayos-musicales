**Libel Suit Against London Daily Mirror (1959)** : Liberace, a famous pianist, sued the London Daily Mirror for libel in 1959 after the newspaper implied he was gay. During the trial, under oath, he denied ever indulging in homosexual practices. The judge believed him and awarded him $24,000 in damages. "He had never indulged in any homosexual practices." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hswtuenFwvM
