## curiosities
**Pioneering the "Rock Dot" Umlaut** : According to lore, Blue Oyster Cult was the first band to incorporate an umlaut (or "rock dot") into their name in 1970. Guitarist Alan Lanyay is credited with the idea, which was adopted simply because "it just looked cool," inspiring many other bands to follow suit for aesthetic reasons. "As far as anybody can tell, the first band to put dots above their letters was blue oyster cult." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
