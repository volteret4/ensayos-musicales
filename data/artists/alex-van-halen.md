**Hearing Concerns Inspire In-Ear Monitor Invention** : As the drummer for Van Halen, Alex Van Halen expressed concerns that the "constant on-stage volume was killing his hearing." His complaints were the direct impetus for the band's sound engineer, Jerry Harvey, to innovate and create the first in-ear monitors to protect musicians' hearing and improve their on-stage experience.
