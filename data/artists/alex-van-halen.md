## curiosities
**Hearing Concerns Inspire In-Ear Monitor Invention** : As the drummer for Van Halen, Alex Van Halen expressed concerns that the "constant on-stage volume was killing his hearing." His complaints were the direct impetus for the band's sound engineer, Jerry Harvey, to innovate and create the first in-ear monitors to protect musicians' hearing and improve their on-stage experience. "When drummer Alex Van Halen started to complain that the constant on-stage volume was killing his hearing, Jerry decided to do something about it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ ← alex-van-halen
