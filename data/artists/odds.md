**Official Breakup (1999)** : The band officially disbanded in 1999 after vocalist and guitarist Craig Northe decided to leave, leading to the dissolution of the group. **Reunion and Name Change (2007)** : The band reunited in 2007, initially performing as "The New Odds" because they had lost the rights to their original name, which they later regained.
