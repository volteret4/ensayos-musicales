## curiosities
**Guitar Painting Incident (October 30, 1967)** : The day before The Stooges' first show, Ron Ashton, then an artist in the band, became "excited" and painted Iggy Pop's Hawaiian guitar, including its pickups. This made the guitar completely useless for the upcoming performance and inadvertently led to Iggy Pop's iconic stage persona. "The artist, Ron Ashton, got a little excited. He painted Iggy's Hawaiian guitar. He painted it, including the pickups." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2RKchFW42Jc ← ron-ashton
