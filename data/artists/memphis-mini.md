Known for her toughness, she was unafraid to fight, even resorting to a knife or pistol if a situation became confrontational. "She was tough. The inscription highlights how her hundreds of recorded sides serve as essential material for understanding the genre, speaking for millions through a singular, individual voice that reflects personal fantasies, dreams, and desires.
