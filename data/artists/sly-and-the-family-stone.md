# artist - Sly and the Family Stone

## songs
**Family Affair (1971)** : This 1971 song by Sly and the Family Stone featured pre-programmed rhythm sounds from a drum machine, indicative of the 1970s trend where artists experimented with such devices despite their limitations to preset patterns.
