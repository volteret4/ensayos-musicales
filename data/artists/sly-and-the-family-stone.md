## curiosities
**Influence on Living Color** : Sly and the Family Stone was one of the key inspirations for Living Color, influencing their unique funk metal sound. "They were inspired equally by James Brown, Funkadelic, Run DMC, Slion the Family Stone, and Led Zeppelin Van Halen, and the Clash." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc ← sly-and-the-family-stone
**Intersection of Music, Politics, and Culture** : Sly and the Family Stone's body of work is considered to stand directly at the intersection of music, politics, and culture, reflecting and influencing societal dynamics of the 1970s and beyond. "I think that Sly stones work stands right in that intersection for that reason I say go give your favorite song by Sly in the family stone a listen today" ← For the Record - The 70s > FTR70 Sound Bite - Remembering Sly Stone | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Mixed Race and Gender Lineup** : The band was notably composed of members of mixed race and gender, which was significant for its era.
