## songs
**Are 'Friends' Electric? (1979)** : Constructed in the spring of 1979, the song was a massive hit that sold over a million copies in the UK alone. The lyrics were based on Numan's vision of a dystopian future where machines provide services, with the "friend" in the song acting as a prostitute. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY

**Stunt and Test Pilot** : Gary Numan initially aspired to be a professional pilot, but his music career took precedence. He is now both a stunt and test pilot, performing aerobatics, flight formations, and flying vintage World War II planes. He temporarily gave up flying after losing friends in accidents. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
**Transition from Punk to Synth** : Numan was originally a fan of guitars and was in the process of recording a punk album when he stumbled upon a Mini-Moog synthesizer in the studio. This discovery led him to immediately abandon his guitar-based tracks in favor of electronic punk songs. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
