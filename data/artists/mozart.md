# artist - Mozart

## curiosities
**"Chicken Head Bob" Composition for Adriana Ferrazi del Benet** : Mozart intensely disliked the singing style of Adriana Ferrazi del Benet, specifically her tendency to drop her chin on low notes and throw her head back on high notes. To spite her, he composed a piece featuring constant jumps between low and high notes, intending to make her head bob like a chicken during the performance. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
