## curiosities
**Grammy Award Revocation (1990)** : Fab Morvan, as part of Milli Vanilli, had to return his Grammy Award in 1990 when it was revealed that he and Rob Pilatus had lip-synced on all their records and live performances. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
**Grammy Nomination for Audiobook (2025)** : Thirty-five years after the Milli Vanilli lip-syncing scandal, Fab Morvan was nominated for a Grammy in 2025 in the Best Audio Book category for his narration of "You Know What's True, the real story of Milli Vanilli." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
