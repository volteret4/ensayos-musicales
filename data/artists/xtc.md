# artist - XTC

## genres
- New Wave

## albums
**Drums and Wires (1978)** : A 1978 album from which the song "Making Plans for Nigel" was released, showcasing British New Wave.

## songs
**Making Plans for Nigel (1978)** : Released from their 1978 album "Drums and Wires", this song is an example of British New Wave from Swindon, West of London, characterized by a spiky, angular, and somewhat agitated sound.

## curiosities
**Geographic Origin** : From Swindon, West of London.
**Influence on "New Wave of New Wave"** : Groups in the early 1990s "new wave of new wave" movement mined their material for inspiration.
**Influence on Hot Hot Heat** : Their early new wave style inspired and informed the band Hot Hot Heat.
