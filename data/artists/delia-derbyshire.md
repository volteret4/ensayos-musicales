## albums
**Electric Storm (1968)** : Delia Derbyshire, as a member of White Noise (a group she formed with two other electronic experimenters), released this album. It is widely regarded and almost always credited as the first official, 100% electronic music album in history, preceding works by artists like Tangerine Dream, Kraftwerk, and Jean-Michel Jarre. This album is considered one of the "holy grails" of electronic music and a pivotal work in music history, yet the woman behind it often goes unrecognized. ## songs
**Doctor Who Theme (1963)** : Delia Derbyshire created what is possibly the first 100% electronic piece of music in history for the "Doctor Who" television series. She took some basic melodies and pieces developed by Ron Grainer, another Radiophonic Workshop member, and transformed them into a fantastic, entirely electronic composition that Grainer himself reportedly did not recognize. This piece served as a musical revolution, inspiring many to explore fully electronic music, years before other pioneers emerged.
