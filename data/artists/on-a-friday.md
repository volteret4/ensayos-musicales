## curiosities
**Early Lineup with Saxophone** : The 1986 4-track cassette lineup of On a Friday included a saxophone player, and Jonny Greenwood had not yet joined the band. "At the time, Anna Friday had a saxophone player and did not yet include Colin Greenwood's younger brother Johnny." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8 ← on-a-friday ← on-a-friday
**Name Origin** : The band adopted the name "On a Friday" because their rehearsal time was limited to Friday afternoons in the music room of Oxford Shears Abingdon School due to their studies. "Studies got in the way of rehearsal time except on Friday afternoons in the school's music room, which is how the band came to be known as Anna Friday." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8 ← on-a-friday ← on-a-friday
