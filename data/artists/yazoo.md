## curiosities
**Difficult Recording of Second Album** : The second Yazoo album was recorded under strained circumstances, with Vince Clarke and Alison Moyet literally not speaking to each other. Clarke would work on the music, leave the studio, and then Moyet would come in to record vocals, with messages about retakes left through the recording team. "el segundo disco, a pesar de ser increíble, era grabado con los dos miembros de la banda sin literalmente dirigirse la palabra." ← Music Radar Clan > Erasure y Vince Clarke | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aqEBoxXXAv8&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← yazoo
**Pioneers of 1980s Techno Pop** : Yazoo is mentioned as one of the groups central to the techno pop sound of the 1980s, alongside bands like Depeche Mode, Human League, and Visage. This era saw widespread adoption of synthesizers in popular music. "And this brings us to the techno pop sounds of the 1980s, to Pesh Mode, Human League, Yazoo, Visage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0 ← yazoo
