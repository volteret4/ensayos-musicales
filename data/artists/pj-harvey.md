**Evolving Image** : Initially a tomboy, PJ Harvey adopted an approach similar to David Bowie in constantly transforming her image for almost every album project. This involved experimenting with both her clothing and physical appearance, often collaborating with her photographer friend, Maria Monachic. "PJ was originally very much a tomboy, but once she got started in the music business, she became almost bowie-esque with the way she changes her image for almost every album project." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IxJ0X9ytGgI
**Fearless and Theatrical Artist** : PJ Harvey, whose career began in 1988, is renowned for her fearless approach to music, consistently doing things her own way. She explores challenging themes such as religion, sex, love, and death in her work, and her performances can range from quiet piano pieces to bruising, punk-like displays. She is also known for her theatricality and her practice of shifting her image with each new project. "she forged a reputation as being unnervingly fearless when it came to doing things her way, and exploring themes of religion, and sex, and love, and death, and other difficult subjects." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEawe_1Fqf0
**Influential Songwriter and Versatility** : As a singer-songwriter, PJ Harvey was among the most influential of the 90s alt-rock women, known for her honest, often dark, theatrical, and sometimes punk-like material. Her brilliant versatility allows her to fearlessly explore diverse musical forms. "She is brilliantly versatile." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IxJ0X9ytGgI
**Multi-Instrumentalist and Poet** : PJ Harvey is proficient in playing "about any musical instrument," is a poet, and occasionally takes on acting roles. "She complies about any musical instrument she writes poetry, has the occasional acting gig, wins all kinds of awards, has a most excellent order of the British Empire award, and never makes the same kind of album twice." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IxJ0X9ytGgI
**Only Two-Time Mercury Music Prize Winner** : PJ Harvey holds the unique distinction of being the only artist to win the Mercury Music Prize twice. Her first win was for "Stories from the City, Stories from the Sea" in 2001, and her second was for "Let England Shake" in 2011. "PJ remains the only person to win the Mercury Prize twice." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEawe_1Fqf0
