## curiosities
**Buzz Osborne's Recommendation (1990)** : Buzz Osborne, who was idolized by Kurt Cobain, recommended Dave Grohl for the open drummer position in Nirvana after Scream broke up.
**Nirvana Connection** : Buzz Osborne, a member of this Seattle band, provided Dave Grohl with Krist Novoselic's phone number, facilitating Grohl's eventual recruitment into Nirvana.
