**Ferrari Expert and Multi-Million Dollar Car Collection** : Nick Mason is an expert on Ferraris, having even authored a book on the subject. His collection, valued at well over $100 million, includes some of the rarest and most valuable cars in the world, such as a 1962 Ferrari 250 GTO (valued at over $50 million) and a Ferrari Enzo (valued at over $3 million). His collection of at least 40 cars also features a McLaren F1, a 1955 Jaguar D-Type, a 1927 Bugatti, and a 1935 Aston Martin.
