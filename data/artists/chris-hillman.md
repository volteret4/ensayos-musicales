## curiosities
**Co-founding The Flying Burrito Brothers** : After leaving The Byrds, Chris Hillman, along with Gram Parsons, formed The Flying Burrito Brothers. He was Parsons' roommate when they wrote songs for their debut album, "Gilded Palace of Sin." **Composing Sin City** : Hillman woke up one morning with the opening line for "Sin City," "This old town is filled with sin, it'll swallow you in," and then woke up his roommate, Gram Parsons, who composed the melody for the song. This collaboration produced a key track on "Gilded Palace of Sin." "Some of them said he woke up one morning with this line in his head. This old town is filled with sin. It'll swallow you in. So it goes up and he wakes up his roommate, Grand Parsons, who came up with the melody for the song." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 35 - Cosmic American Music - 70s Country Rock | https://seventies.libsyn.com/ep-35-cosmic-american-music-70s-country-rock ← chris-hillman
**Discovering Emmylou Harris** : Chris Hillman heard Emmylou Harris sing at a club outside Washington, D.C., in the early 1970s. Although he considered asking her to join The Flying Burrito Brothers (who were then without Gram Parsons), he instead recommended her to Parsons for his solo album, leading to their famous musical partnership.
