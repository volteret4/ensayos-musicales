## curiosities
**All-Female Tribute Band (The Ramoms)** : Songs by The Ramones are exclusively played by The Ramoms, a tribute band composed of middle-aged women from Philadelphia. "The Ramams are some middle-aged women from Philadelphia that play nothing but songs by the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72Lei7uug8c
**Backstage Window Encounter (July 4, 1976)** : Joe Strummer of The Clash recounted how he, Johnny Rotten, and members of the Sex Pistols met The Ramones backstage at the Roundhouse by throwing a rock at a window. Johnny Ramone stuck his head out, and the British punks introduced themselves, being subsequently pulled up through the window in a "human chain" to get in. "And so they kind of formed a sort of human chain and pulled us up through this window. And that was the first time we met him and that was just a really great punk rock moment." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
**Band Name Significance** : The name "Ramones" was chosen to signify unity, family, brother, gang, and brotherhood. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z9T9wi0vEvc
**Conquest of England (July 4 & 6, 1976)** : The Ramones played two pivotal shows in London, first at the Roundhouse (capacity 2,000) on America's bicentennial, and then at Dingwalls two nights later. Despite their European record company trying to cancel gigs, the band delivered explosive performances. These concerts were England's "first proper exposure" to US-style punk rock and are credited with transforming nearly every major player in the burgeoning British punk rock scene, including members of the Sex Pistols, The Clash, The Pretenders, The Damned, and Generation X. "And over the course of those two nights, July 4th and July 6th, 1976, just about every single person who would become a major player in the British punk rock explosion were taught a lesson by the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
**Cuckoo's Nest Performances** : The Ramones were among the punk bands that frequently played at The Cuckoo's Nest in Costa Mesa, a club often called "The Birthplace of Slam Dancing" in Southern California music lore. "The place hosted a lot of punk shows, from the Ramones to the Dam to Social Distortion." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Documented Moshing in Film** : The Ramones were one of the bands whose performances featuring moshing were documented in the film "The Decline of Western Civilization," helping to spread the knowledge and practice of moshing to wider audiences. "The bands ranging from the Ramones to the dead canadies to DOA to minor threat." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Formation and Lineup Change – Drummer to Frontman** : The Ramones were formed in early 1974, with Jeff Hyman (later Joey Ramone) on drums, John Cummings (later Johnny Ramone) on guitar, and Douglas Colvin (later D.D. Ramone) on bass. Their first gig was on March 30, 1974. Due to Jeff's powerful, fast drumming style and his superior singing ability, he transitioned to lead vocalist, with a friend named Tommy taking over on drums using a basic $100 kit. "This arrangements worked out pretty good Especially after John became Johnny and Douglas became D.D. and Jeff adopted the name Joey and they all adopted the same last name Ramon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Image and Sound** : The Ramones were known for their quintessential punk image, characterized by leather jackets and torn jeans, and for playing music that was really fast and really loud. However, beneath this surface, their musical core was deeply influenced by the pop radio of the 1960s, essentially playing their unique version of pop songs. "The pop radio of the 1960s was a huge influence of the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Influence on Other Bands** : The Ramones' existence was crucial for the formation and style of numerous bands that came after them, specifically cited are Green Day, Nirvana, The Offspring, and Pearl Jam. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z9T9wi0vEvc
**Influence on The Black Keys** : The Ramones were among the favorite bands that inspired the short hooks for the songs composed on The Black Keys' *El Camino* album, revealing their punk and garage rock sensibilities. "and the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PmwfC4HR3_I
**Mainstream Perception vs. Importance** : Although the mainstream treated The Ramones as a joke for decades, they were, in fact, one of the most important bands rock and roll had ever seen. Their foundational influence is noted as essential for bands like Green Day, Nirvana, The Offspring, and Pearl Jam. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z9T9wi0vEvc
**Openness to Pop Influences** : Unlike some early punk bands that professed disdain for previous generations, The Ramones were open about their pop sensibilities and expressed no issues with iconic popular acts like The Beatles, The Beach Boys, Motown, or 60s girl groups. They frequently covered 60s pop songs, overtly demonstrating their appreciation for the genre. "The Ramones definitely a punk band, but under the surface, they were also very much a pop band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Radio Ban in North America** : In North America, punk music, including The Ramones, faced significant resistance from radio stations due to its perceived political, class-oriented, crass, vulgar, and violent nature. "Radio stations wouldn't touch the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
**Signed by Seymour Stein** : The Ramones were one of the iconic bands signed by Seymour Stein, the founder of Sire Records. Stein's signing of the band was a significant moment in their career and in music history. "He's the guy who signed the Ramones and the Talking Heds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Sire Records Signing** : Seymour Stein, recognizing the potential of this new music despite punk's "baggage" in North America, signed The Ramones to Sire Records. This was part of a broader strategy to market these bands under the "New Wave" label. "He signed the Ramones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
