## songs
**High and Dry (Early Version)** : This band performed an early rendition of "High and Dry," a song that would later be refined and properly recorded by Radiohead. Tom York was a member of The Headless Chickens while he was based in Exeter. ## curiosities
**Formation and Gigs** : The Headless Chickens were formed by Tom York, while he was at Exeter, and a bouncer named Shaq. The group was active in the city of Exeter, where they played "dozens of gigs." "he connected with a bouncer named Shaq, and they formed a group called The Headless Chickens, and they ended up playing dozens of gigs around the city of Exeter." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM ← the-headless-chickens ← the-headless-chickens
