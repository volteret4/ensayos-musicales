## curiosities
**Boost from Apple Commercial** : The Ting Tings experienced a significant career boost from their music being featured in an Apple commercial, similar to the success seen by other artists like The Fratellis. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfie4YYncxk ← the-ting-tings
**Early 21st-Century "Broken Toy"** : The Ting Tings are considered one of the first "broken toys" of 21st-century music, a term used for acts that started very strong but eventually declined. "era uno de los juguetes rotos uno de los primeros juguetes rotos que teníamos en la música en este siglo XXI... y al final pues son de esa caba no viniendo abajo fue así como un jugueterroto que se ruita." ← Music Radar Clan > Lanzamientos Enero 2018 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m0uzqiwh7Tw&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Embracing 80s Alt Rock** : The Ting Tings were mentioned as a band that infused their sound with "more than a tinge of 80s alt rock," aligning them with the New Wave Revival movement. "Phoenix, Passion Pits, the Tingtings all had more than a tinge of 80s alt rock to what they did." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo ← the-ting-tings
