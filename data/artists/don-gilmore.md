# artist - Don Gilmore

## curiosities
**Inspiration for "One Step Closer" Lyrics** : Chester Bennington directly addressed Don Gilmore through the lyrics of "One Step Closer," expressing frustration over Gilmore's "blind dismissal" of his lyrical submissions and the pressure he exerted. The "shut up" part in the song was 100% directed at Gilmore. "Chester is actually talking to producer Don Gilmore with these words about how he so blindly dismissed Chester whenever he submitted lyrics for his inspection." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM ← don-gilmore
**Producer of Hybrid Theory** : Don Gilmore produced Linkin Park's debut album, Hybrid Theory. His demanding approach in the studio caused significant tension with the band, particularly with Chester Bennington. "He was driving them nuts with the way he was pushing them in the studio." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM ← don-gilmore
