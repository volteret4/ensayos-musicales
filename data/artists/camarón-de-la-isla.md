## albums
**La Leyenda del Tiempo (Year Not Specified)** : This album is considered one of the most important in the history of flamenco. It incorporated significant influences from funk music, disco music, and electronic keyboards, pushing the boundaries of the traditional flamenco sound. Initially, some flamenco purists described it as an "aberration."
