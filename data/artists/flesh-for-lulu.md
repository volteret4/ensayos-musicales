## curiosities
**John Hughes Film Exposure (1980s)** : Flesh for Lulu's music was heavily mined for the soundtracks of John Hughes' 1980s films. These carefully curated soundtracks played a significant role in exposing numerous North American audiences to various alternative rock acts, particularly British performers. "So many people learned of bands like The Specials and Spandovalet and Billy Idle and General Public and Wang Chung and Yellow and New Order and the Psychedelic Furs and the Smiths and Echo and the Bunyman, Susanne Vagan in excess the Jesus and Mary chain, Flesh for Luluo and so many others from those soundtrack albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
