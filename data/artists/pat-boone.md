# artist - Pat Boone

## songs
**Tutti Frutti (1956)** : A toned-down, sanitized cover version of Little Richard's song, released a year after the original. This version allowed the song to be played in mainstream (white) places, including white radio stations, and helped cover the rest of the marketplace. "Having Pat Boone record 2D Fruity that way, allowed the song to be played in more places, including all those hard to get white radio stations." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnQ1pNbIUjw
