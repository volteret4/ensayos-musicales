## curiosities
**Non-Guitar Player Designer** : Despite being the owner of the Fender company and a co-designer of the iconic Stratocaster, Leo Fender himself could not play the guitar. This highlights his innovative approach to instrument design focused on engineering rather than performance. "Leo Fender, the owner of the company, who couldn't play guitar, by the way, was one of the co-designers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw ← leo-fender
**Solution for Bass Amplification** : Leo Fender was instrumental in addressing the problem of bass amplification. After introducing the Fender Precision Bass in 1951, he recognized the inadequacy of existing guitar amps for bass, leading him to invent the dedicated Bassman amplifier in 1952. "Because there were no bass guitars, there were no bass amps, so Fender had to invent that too." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw ← leo-fender
