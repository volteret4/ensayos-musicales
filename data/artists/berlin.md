## songs
**Sex (I'm A...) (1982)** : This song was played on top 40 radio for a while in 1982. It reached number 62 on the charts in the US, but its potential for higher chart performance was hampered by radio stations banning it due to its perceived obscenity, and MTV playing it only sporadically for the same reason. In Canada, the song was received more openly, climbing to number four on the charts, which was its best chart performance globally.
