## curiosities
**American New Wave Marketing** : Sire Records used them in their marketing campaign for American New Wave.
**Attendance at Ramones London Gigs (July 1976)** : Chrissie Hynde, of the then-unformed Pretenders, was among the key figures transformed by seeing The Ramones perform in London on July 4th and 6th, 1976.
**MTV Exposure (Early 1980s)** : Mentioned as a New Wave band that was successful due to MTV exposure.
**Seymour Stein Signing** : Signed by Seymour Stein for Sire Records.
