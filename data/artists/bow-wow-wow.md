## songs
**C30, C60, C90 Go (1980)** : This single was released by the British group Bow Wow Wow at the start of the 1980s, intentionally encouraging home taping. Its title referenced common lengths of blank cassette tapes (30, 60, and 90 minutes), and one unique release format included the song on side one with side two left blank specifically for users to record their own content.
