# artist - Missy Elliott

## songs
**Throw It Back (2019)** : The music video for Missy Elliott's 2019 song "Throw It Back" was produced for an exceptionally low cost of $25. This contrasts sharply with the multi-million dollar budgets of music videos from previous decades, illustrating the impact of accessible high-quality video production tools. ## curiosities
**Videos for Branding** : Missy Elliott, like other hip-hop artists, strategically used her music videos for branding purposes rather than solely for promoting singles or albums. This approach allowed artists to project themes of status, luxury, and identity, which were highly valued in the hip-hop genre. "Tupac, Missy Elliott, and Dr. Dre used their videos as branding, not just to sell a single or a album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7IDbqdIw3WU
