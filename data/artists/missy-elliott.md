# artist - Missy Elliott

## curiosities
**Videos for Branding** : Missy Elliott, like other hip-hop artists, strategically used her music videos for branding purposes rather than solely for promoting singles or albums. This approach allowed artists to project themes of status, luxury, and identity, which were highly valued in the hip-hop genre.
