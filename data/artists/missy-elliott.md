## curiosities
**Hip Hop Era Producer Turned Artist** : Missy Elliott is one of a trio of "Virginia Beach Polymaths" from the hip hop era who successfully moved from working behind the mixing board to performing at the mic. "The Virginia Beach Polymaths, Forell Williams, Missy Elliott, and Timberland." ← Hit Parade Music History and Music Trivia > Pour Some Sugar on Me Edition Part 1 | https://shows.acast.com/hit-parade/episodes/695d4ef5d1ba84fb8f04493d
**Sampler of Jamiroquai's Music** : Missy Elliott is mentioned as one of the artists who directly sampled Jamiroquai's music. Her use of Jamiroquai's tracks underscores the artist's role as a rich source for samples in genres such as rap, electronic, and pop. "ampliados directos de Jaimiro Kuey en gente, pues como [...] Missy Elliott." ← Music Radar Clan > Jamiroquai y el new funk de los 90 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EhNXj1YtvxA&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← missy-elliott ← missy-elliott
**Videos for Branding** : Missy Elliott, like other hip-hop artists, strategically used her music videos for branding purposes rather than solely for promoting singles or albums. This approach allowed artists to project themes of status, luxury, and identity, which were highly valued in the hip-hop genre. "Tupac, Missy Elliott, and Dr. Dre used their videos as branding, not just to sell a single or a album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7IDbqdIw3WU ← missy-elliott
