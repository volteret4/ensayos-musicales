## curiosities

This approach allowed artists to project themes of status, luxury, and identity, which were highly valued in the hip-hop genre. ## songs
**Throw It Back (2019)** : The music video for Missy Elliott's 2019 song "Throw It Back" was produced for an exceptionally low cost of $25. This contrasts sharply with the multi-million dollar budgets of music videos from previous decades, illustrating the impact of accessible high-quality video production tools.
