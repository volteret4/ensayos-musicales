## curiosities
**Early Seattle Band – Brief Stone Gossard Connection** : March of Crimes was a band in Seattle that featured Ben Shepherd, who would later become Soundgarden's bassist. Notably, a teenage Stone Gossard, future member of Mother Love Bone and Pearl Jam, briefly played guitar in this group. "Ben had been around Seattle for years, once playing in a band called March of Crimes, which briefly featured a teenage guitarist named Stone Gossard." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
