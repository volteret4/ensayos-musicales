## curiosities
**Serious Toronto Surf Band** : This Toronto-based band takes their surf music seriously, evidenced by their use of Rickenbacker guitars, Fender Amps, old-school Ludwig drum kits, matching uniforms, white Go-Go boots, and semi-beehive hairdos. They are considered a contemporary surf band that "does it upright." "They've got the Rikitas, the Fender Amps, the old school Ludwig Drumkit, plus matching uniforms, including the white Go Go boots. Oh, and the semi-behyde hairdes. They really do it upright for a contemporary surf band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
