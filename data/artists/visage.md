## curiosities
**Pioneers of New Romanticism** : Visage, like Spandau Ballet, was a New Romantic group whose origins lay in British clubs such as Billies and The Blitz Club, which fostered a scene for synth-based new music and a specific aesthetic. "One such offshoot was known as New Romantic, which had its roots in a couple of British clubs dedicated to new music. One was called Billies, and the other was the Blitz Club. This was the breeding site of groups like Spandovalet, Visage, and Culture Club." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
