# artist - Sylvia Moe

## curiosities
**Credit for Oasis's "Step Out"** : As a co-writer of Stevie Wonder's "Uptight Everything's All Right," Sylvia Moe was later granted co-writing credit for Oasis's song "Step Out" and entitled to at least a quarter of the proceeds, following a court case recognizing the similarity between the two songs. "There's a songwriter named Sylvia Moe... They each get at least a quarter of the proceeds from any version of Step Out by Oasis." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L09R2evjyMc
