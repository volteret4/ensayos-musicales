# artist - Backstreet Boys

## albums
**Millennium (1999) – Record-Breaking Debut** : Released on May 18, 1999, this CD sold over 500,000 copies on its first day and achieved 1.134 million sales by the end of its first week in the US, setting a new all-time sales record at the time. Worldwide totals were much higher than just the US numbers. "On that first day it sold more than 500,000 copies.
