## albums

## songs
**Confessions Part II (2004)** : This title track from Usher's diamond-certified "Confessions" album was the winner of the 2004 Billboard Song of the Summer. It is noted for its "fiery sound and soap opera worthy lyrics." "He took song of summer again with the title track Confessions Part 2." ← Hit Parade Music History and Music Trivia > Song(s) of the Summer Edition Part 2 | https://shows.acast.com/hit-parade/episodes/695d4ef50c30a1408dc292e4
**U Remind Me (2001)** : This song was the winner of the 2001 Billboard Song of the Summer. Usher "commanded the charts for the entire month of July" with this "buttery, not quite ballad." "In 2001, Usher commanded the charts for the entire month of July with the buttery, not quite ballad you remind me." ← Hit Parade Music History and Music Trivia > Song(s) of the Summer Edition Part 2 | https://shows.acast.com/hit-parade/episodes/695d4ef50c30a1408dc292e4

## curiosities
**Album Sales (2004)** : Usher's album sales in 2004 reached approximately 7 million copies, matching the previous year's top sellers. His commercial success was notable as the music industry grappled with evolving distribution models and declining physical sales.
