## curiosities
**Influence on Radiohead** : Tom Yorke was "deep into techno bands like... O-tecker" (Autechre) during the recording of "Kid A," which contributed to Radiohead's shift towards an electronic sound with loops, samples, and artificial beats. "Tom was deep into techno bands like the Apex twin and O-tecker." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JUvDp3_RBH0 ← autechre
**Prolific and Unfiltered Output** : Autechre is known for releasing everything they produce, a characteristic shared by many IDM artists. Recently, the artist released a large box set of numerous discs and vinyl records containing many previously made and stored sessions, which they then decided to publish. "Autor que hace poco se ha con una caja de no sé cuántos, discos y vinilos, con un montón de sesiones que tenían hechas y guardadas, pues mira, cojo las público." ← Music Radar Clan > BOARDS OF CANADA.
