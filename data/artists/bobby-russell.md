**Writer of "The Night the Lights Went Out in Georgia" (1972)** : Russell wrote this murder ballad and initially offered it to Sonny Bono for Cher, but Sonny declined due to concerns about potentially offending Southern fans with specific lyrics. Russell then gave the song to his wife, Vicki Lawrence, who recorded it to number one success.
