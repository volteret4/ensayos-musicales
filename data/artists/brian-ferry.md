## curiosities
**Rejected "Don't You (Forget About Me)"** : Brian Ferry of Roxy Music was the first artist approached to record the song "Don't You (Forget About Me)" for *The Breakfast Club* soundtrack, but he disliked it and turned it down. The song, written by Keith Forsey with Ferry initially in mind, went on to become a massive hit for Simple Minds after several other artists also rejected it.
