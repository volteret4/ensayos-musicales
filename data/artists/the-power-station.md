## curiosities
**Exemplar of 1980s Drum Sound** : The band, featuring Robert Palmer, members of Duran Duran, and drummer Tony Thompson, is highlighted as a prime example of the pervasive drum sound of the 1980s. This sound was characterized by its big, boomy, and powerful quality, often achieved by recording in large, reverberant rooms and augmenting with electronic effects like gated reverbs. "The Power Station featuring Robert Palmer, a couple of guys from Durandoran and drummer Tony Thompson.
