# artist - Long Gone John

## curiosities
**Head of Sympathy for the Record Industry** : Long Gone John ran Sympathy for the Record Industry, an Olympia, Washington-based label that had been dedicated to Garage Rock and Punk since 1988. This label was instrumental in the breakthrough success of The White Stripes.
