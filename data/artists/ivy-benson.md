# artist - Ivy Benson

## curiosities
**English Bandleader Employing Female Drummers** : Ivy Benson, an English band leader, notably employed a series of female drummers in her orchestra. This practice, though still occurring in a sexist and patronizing environment where female musicians were not always taken seriously, provided opportunities for women to perform professionally.
