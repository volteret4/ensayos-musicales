# artist - Laurie Anderson

## curiosities
**First Alternative Album Grammy Nominee (1991)** : Laurie Anderson was among the first artists to be nominated in the "Alternative Album" Grammy category when it debuted in 1991. Her nomination highlighted the diverse range of artists being recognized in this new genre classification. "The first nominees were Kate Bush, the replacements, World Party, and Laurie Anderson." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso ← laurie-anderson
