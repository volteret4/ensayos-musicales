**Dallas Good's Coronary Illness and Death** : Dallas Good, the singer and guitarist for The Sadies, received a diagnosis of a coronary illness in February 2022 and tragically died a week later on February 17th, at the age of 48, while still under a doctor's care. "Back in February, he got news from a doctor that he was suffering from a coronary illness, a heart condition that he didn't know he had.
