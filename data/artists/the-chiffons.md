# artist - The Chiffons

## songs
**He's So Fine (1963)** : This song was at the center of a long-running legal battle with George Harrison, whose 1971 song "My Sweet Lord" was found to have a melody too similar to "He's So Fine." The court ruled in favor of The Chiffons, concluding that Harrison had subconsciously plagiarized their song.
