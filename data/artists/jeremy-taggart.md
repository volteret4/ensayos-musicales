## curiosities
**First Demo Delivery** : In the early 1990s, Jeremy Taggart, then a shy kid with big glasses, personally delivered Our Lady Peace's first-ever demo cassette to a radio station host. The host, unfortunately, didn't recognize its significance at the time. "It wasn't until much, much later that I realized that the kid with the big glasses was Jeremy Taggart, the drummer for RLAD Peace, and that he had presented me with the group's first ever demo." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8
