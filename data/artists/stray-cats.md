## curiosities
**Prominence in Early 80s Rockabilly** : The Stray Cats were a prominent band in the "alt-rock style rockabilly" genre, which was very big in the early 1980s for about 18 months. However, this style subsequently faded out and has never truly had a full-blown revival in the alt-rock sense.
