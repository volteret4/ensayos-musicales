# artist - Van Morrison

## genres
- Blues Rock

## songs
**Man Called Them (1964)** : This Van Morrison song, recorded in 1964, was cited as an example of the covers that deeply influenced The Tragically Hip's original material. The band's transition from covering such songs to writing their own "Blue Z, blue color, guitar-driven rock" was a key step in their development. "It covers like that, which is a van Morrison song recorded in 1964, the man called them, informed a lot of the hip's original material." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oMBmdfrA47Q ← van-morrison

## curiosities
**Influence on Early Tragically Hip** : The Tragically Hip's early setlists largely consisted of blues rock standards, including material from Van Morrison. "Early stuff from the stones, material from the doors, the yard birds, Marvin Gaye, Otis Redding, Ben Morrison, that kind of thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnz_Ycd_q7s ← van-morrison
