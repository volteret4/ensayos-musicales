# artist - Henry Mancini

## songs
**Peter Gunn Theme (1959)** : This theme from a 1950s American TV series was found to have melodic similarities to The Strokes' 2006 single "Juice Box." Consequently, the famous American composer Henry Mancini received a songwriting credit for "Juice Box." However, Mancini himself did not benefit from this credit as he had passed away approximately ten years before the song's release.
