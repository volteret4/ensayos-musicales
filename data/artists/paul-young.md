**Toast (1978) – Novelty B-Side That Became a Hit During the UK Baker's Strike, Launched by Street Band** : Released as the B-side to "Hold On" by Street Band, a straight-ahead UK rock group, "Toast" was a song about the joys of making toast, recorded when the band needed filler. During the Baker's Strike of the late 1970s, someone at the BBC discovered the track and put it on air; it became an unexpected quirky hit, but branded the band as novelty artists, leading to their breakup. One member of Street Band was Paul Young, who went on to have major multi-platinum success as a blue-eyed soul singer in the 1980s. "Street Band, with a song called Toast from 1978.
