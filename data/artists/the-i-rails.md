# artist - The I-Rails

## members
- Chris O'Connor

## albums
**The I-Rails Albums (1985-1991)** : The I-Rails released four albums between 1985 and 1991, but these records "amounted to exactly nothing" in terms of commercial or critical success. The band eventually broke up, leading singer Chris O'Connor to pursue a solo project. "They evolved out of a Southern California band called the I-Rails, a group that amounted to exactly nothing over four albums between 1985 and 1991." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SOAL0GODN3M

## curiosities
**Predecessor to Primitive Radio Gods** : The I-Rails was a Southern California band from which Primitive Radio Gods frontman Chris O'Connor evolved. Despite releasing four albums between 1985 and 1991, the band achieved no significant success and eventually broke up. "They evolved out of a Southern California band called the I-Rails, a group that amounted to exactly nothing over four albums between 1985 and 1991." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SOAL0GODN3M
**Reunion for Primitive Radio Gods Tour** : When Primitive Radio Gods' single "Standing Outside a Broken Phone Booth with Money in My Hand" took off, singer Chris O'Connor reunited his former bandmates from The I-Rails to go on tour as Primitive Radio Gods. "When the song took off, singer Chris O'Connor got his old mates and the I-Rails back together and went on tour." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SOAL0GODN3M
