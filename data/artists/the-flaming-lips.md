## curiosities
**Admiration for Mark Linkous** : The Flaming Lips were noted as admirers of Mark Linkous, the songwriter and frontman for Sparklehorse, highlighting his respected status among his peers in the music industry. "He had fans of the flaming lips, and metric of the Silver Sun pickups, and Patty Smith and many others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SOAL0GODN3M ← the-flaming-lips ← the-flaming-lips
**Blood Art Creation** : The Flaming Lips are known for incorporating their blood into their art, a practice similar to KISS. In 2010, Wayne Coyne extracted a substantial amount of his own blood to create a poster for the band, documented in a YouTube video. "In 2010, Wayne Coyne extracted some blood from his body, a fairly substantial amount, too, and created a lips poster with it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ ← the-flaming-lips ← the-flaming-lips
**Blood-Infused Vinyl Edition** : For their 2012 Record Store Day release, "Hetty Fwins," The Flaming Lips created a special clear vinyl edition that visibly contained traces of the band's blood. They also extended this practice to their collaborators, demanding that artists like Chris Martin of Coldplay, Kesha, and Yoko Ono contribute their own blood for ten ultra-rare special editions of the record. "Not only did the clear vinyl vividly show traces of flaming lips blood, but the band also demanded that any of the collaborators on this record also contribute their precious bodily fluid for 10 special editions of the record." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQrbNUDZuOQ ← the-flaming-lips ← the-flaming-lips
