# artist - Jack Mullen

## instruments
- Magnetophone

## curiosities
**Discovery of the Magnetophone** : A member of the US Army Signal Corps, Mullen discovered the Nazi "magnetophone" near Frankfurt, Germany. The machine used reels of magnetic tape to provide high-quality audio storage and playback, explaining how Hitler had broadcast synchronized speeches across different cities. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
**Post-War Technology Transfer** : Due to the cancellation of German wartime patents, Mullen was able to bring two magnetophone machines and several reels of magnetic tape back to the United States. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
