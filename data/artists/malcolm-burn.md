## curiosities
**Diverse Production and Engineering Work** : With the assistance of Daniel Lanois, Malcolm Burn has produced and engineered for a variety of artists including Iggy Pop, Patti Smith, Bob Dylan, Blue Road View, Midnight Oil, Junkhouse, and John Mellencamp. "With Dan's help Malcolm has had gigs producing an engineering for Iggy Pop and Patty Smith and Bob Dylan and Blue Road View and Midnight Oil and junk house and John Mellon Camp in many many more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkSKxm_U9Yc
**From Assistant to Producer** : Malcolm Burn was an assistant at Grand Avenue Studios and was later hired by Daniel Lanois to run Kingsway Studios in New Orleans. Like Bruce Fairbairn and Bob Rock, he transitioned from a band member (Boys Brigade) into full-time production. "And one of the guys he hired to run the place was an assistant at Grand Avenue named Malcolm Bern. Malcolm used to be in a band-2 they were called Boys Brigade but like Bruce Fairburn and Bob Rock Malcolm eventually got into producing full-time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkSKxm_U9Yc
