## curiosities
**College and Jobs** : Colin secured a coveted spot at Cambridge University, where he pursued studies in English literature. During his pre-Radiohead years, he worked at two local record stores: Manic Hedgehog and Our Price. At these stores, he proactively distributed "On a Friday" demos to visiting record company representatives. He also held a position as the school's entertainment director. "Colin landed a hard-to-get-spotted Cambridge where he studied English literature." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
**Early Life and Music** : Colin, along with his younger brother Johnny, became involved in music during their early teenage years. Their interest was significantly influenced by an older sister who was keen on bands such as George Evasion in the Fall. "Thanks to an older sister who was in debates like George Evasion in the Fall, the two brothers Colin and Johnny Greenwood got into music together in their early teens." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
