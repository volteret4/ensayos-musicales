This initiative took place during a time when the music industry saw a lot of money and the success of traveling festivals like Lollapalooza inspired new concepts. "Sarah McLaughlin founded Lilith Fair, the female centric festival that spun through North America for three consecutive summers starting in 1997." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
