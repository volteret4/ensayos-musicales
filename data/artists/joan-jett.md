**Kim Fowley's Training Method** : Kim Fowley, the manager of The Runaways, prepared the band for live performances by having road crew members throw various items like bottles and paper at them during rehearsals. This method was intended to familiarize the band with the challenging reactions they might face on stage. "Kim Fowley, who was the manager of the runaways, actually prepared the band for playing live by having the road crew throw junk at them, bottles, paper, whatever, while they were rehearsing, just so they knew what they were getting into." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Signature Gibson Melody Maker** : Joan Jett's primary guitar for many years was a white Gibson Melody Maker, which she acquired from Eric Carmen after The Raspberries disbanded. This guitar was famously used on The Raspberries' hit "Go All The Way" and is heard on many of Jett's own successful songs. She later collaborated with Gibson on her own signature Melody Maker models, released in 2008 and 2010. "For the longest time, Joan Jett's main guitar has been a white Gibson melody maker." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0

**The Runaways – First All-Female Hard Rock Band** : As a member of The Runaways, Joan Jett was part of arguably the first all-female hard rock band to achieve international attention, cementing her status as a groundbreaking artist. **Trailblazer and Rhythm Player** : Joan Jett is recognized as a trailblazer in rock music, primarily for her hard-riffing rhythm guitar style rather than virtuoso soloing.
