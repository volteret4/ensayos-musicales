**Kim Fowley's Training Method** : Kim Fowley, the manager of The Runaways, prepared the band for live performances by having road crew members throw various items like bottles and paper at them during rehearsals. This method was intended to familiarize the band with the challenging reactions they might face on stage. "Kim Fowley, who was the manager of the runaways, actually prepared the band for playing live by having the road crew throw junk at them, bottles, paper, whatever, while they were rehearsing, just so they knew what they were getting into." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
