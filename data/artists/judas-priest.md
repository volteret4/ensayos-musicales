**Influence on Grunge Bands** : Judas Priest was recognized by leading grunge figures such as Kurt Cobain and members of Soundgarden and Alice in Chains as a "pretty good band" and a "huge influence," indicating a breaking down of genre barriers and a newfound appreciation for traditional metal in the 90s alt-rock landscape. "Boys like Kurt Cobain and the dudes from Soundguard, Dinales and Chains weren't shy about saying, hey, don't diss black Sabbath and Judas Priest and kiss, they were pretty good bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PqOJ3YiFgYE
**Influence on Ice-T** : Ice-T listed Judas Priest among the rock bands he admired, alongside Led Zeppelin and Black Sabbath, influencing his move into metal with Body Count. "Zeppelin, Black Sabbath, Judas Priest, that kind of thing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
