# artist - Jenny Lind

## curiosities
**Victim of Early Ticket Scalping Scandal (1851)** : During her 1851 tour of the United States, known as "the Swedish Nightingale," Jenny Lind's tickets quickly disappeared from box offices upon release. These tickets subsequently reappeared in the hands of "sidewalk men," who resold $3 tickets for $6, causing widespread outrage. Rumors of her agent and manager being complicit in the scheme significantly damaged her reputation.
