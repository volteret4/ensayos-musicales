## curiosities
**Subsidized by Nickelback Profits** : Killswitch Engage, a metal band, was one of the acts whose releases were subsidized by the millions in sales Nickelback generated for Roadrunner Records. This demonstrates the financial interdependence within the music industry, where successful bands help fund the development and releases of other artists.
