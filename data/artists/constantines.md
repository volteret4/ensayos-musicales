## curiosities
**Hamilton Local Act and Influence** : The Constantine's were a band that frequently played in Hamilton venues like the Casbah and Underground around 2006, during what was considered the "Golden Age" of indie rock. Their guitar-driven indie rock sound influenced Arkells' early musical direction, particularly on songs like "The Boss Is Coming." "So every week at the club it seemed like at the Casbore, the Underground there was bands like Winner Sleep or Joel Plasket, Constantine, Stars, Sam Roberts, Arcade for all of these bands just coming through Hamilton that we got to go." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA ← constantines
