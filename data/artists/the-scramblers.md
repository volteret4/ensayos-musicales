# artist - The Scramblers

## curiosities
**Moscow Exchange Performance (Gorbachev Era)** : The Scramblers were a band who performed with 5440 during a week-long cultural exchange trip to Moscow in the Gorbachev era. They later got into trouble and were jailed by authorities but were released after mentioning the name "Gretzky," implying a shared cultural reference. "We did a show there with a band called the scramblers, you know who ended up getting in jail and stuff like that Once they said the word gretzky they got let out a jail." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W9gdKha0PLg ← the-scramblers
