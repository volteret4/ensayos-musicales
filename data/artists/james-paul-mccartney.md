## curiosities
**Birth (1942)** : James Paul McCartney was born on June 18, 1942, to Jim and Mary McCartney. His parents met and married on April 15, 1941, under circumstances heavily influenced by World War II, including his father's exemption from military service due to a childhood ear injury and an air raid in Liverpool.
