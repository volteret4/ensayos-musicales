## curiosities
**2022 Reunion Tour Cut Short** : The band embarked on a reunion tour in 2022, which lasted only 36 days, from July 9, 2022, in Troy, Wisconsin, to August 14, 2022, at Madison Square Garden. The tour was cut short after vocalist Zach D. LaRocca tore his Achilles tendon on stage. "Although their reunion lasted all of 36 days before it fell apart, the first show July 9, 2022 in Troy, Wisconsin, last show, August 14, 2022 at Madison Square Garden. It might have gone on longer and might have been much bigger. Ted Singer, Zach D. LaRocca, not torn his Achilles on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZzIqIydZTlA
**21st Century Reunion** : Rage Against The Machine was mentioned as one of the prominent alt-rock bands that reunited in the 21st century, helping to inject temporary vitality back into the alt-rock scene and bring lapsed fans back. "In addition, the Pixies, smashing pumpkins, the lemon heads, rage against the machine...These reunions injected some life into the scene, albeit temporary life." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo
**Breakup Date (October 18, 2000)** : The band officially "fell apart" on this date. "They fell apart on October the 18th, 2000." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yR3xc3ra5tE
**Creative/Political Dispute Leading to Singer's Departure** : Singer Zack de la Rocha left the band due to a "creative dispute, maybe a political dispute," as he sought to take the band in a direction that the other members did not agree with. "Zach, Rage's singer, had decided to leave." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Decision to Continue as a Unit** : Following Zack de la Rocha's departure, the remaining three members—Tom Morello, Timmy, and Brad—decided they wanted to stay together and continue making music. "However, the other three guys, Tom, Timmy and Brad, decided that they really wanted to stay together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
**Reunion Decision Date (January 22, 2007)** : The band decided to play together again on this date. "and didn't decide to play together until January 22nd, 2007." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yR3xc3ra5tE
**Time Apart (6 years, 3 months, 5 days)** : The band was apart for this duration, calculated from their breakup to their decision to reunite. "So that works out to about six years, three months and five days." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yR3xc3ra5tE
**Transition to Audioslave** : After Chris Cornell joined the remaining members of Rage Against The Machine, they collectively decided to "jettison the Rage name" and reform as Audioslave. "And I'm not really sure why there was a collective decision to jettison the Rage name in favor of Audio Slive, which is where we are today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-f9jhqAHkQo
