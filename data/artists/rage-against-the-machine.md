# artist - Rage Against The Machine

## curiosities
**Contribution to The Crow Soundtrack (1994)** : One of the big names featured on the critically acclaimed soundtrack for *The Crow* movie.
**Support for L7** : Along with Nirvana, Pearl Jam, and Red Hot Chili Peppers, they became fans of L7 after playing pro-choice events and offered support to the all-female band.
