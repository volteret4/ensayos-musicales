## songs
**Telstar (1962) – Second British #1 in America, 5 Million Copies Sold** : A surf-like instrumental capitalizing on excitement around the launch of the Telstar communications satellite. It became the second British song to reach number one on the US charts and sold over 5 million copies worldwide. George Bellamy, Matt Bellamy's father, played rhythm guitar on the recording for producer Joe Meek.
