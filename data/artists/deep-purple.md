## songs
**Smoke on the Water** : One of the most iconic guitar riffs of all time, guitarist Ritchie Blackmore stated he based it on a backwards rendition of the opening notes of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony. It also bears a strong resemblance to a 1963 bossa nova song titled "Maria Moita." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
