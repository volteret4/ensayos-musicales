## curiosities
**DIY Amp Modification** : Dave Davies, frustrated with not getting the right sound from his Vox Elpico amp during a recording session for The Kinks, intentionally hacked up its speaker cone with a razor blade and poked holes in it with a pin. This extreme modification was a deliberate effort to achieve a specific distorted sound. "But not before Brother Dave Davies hacked up the speaker code of his Vox Elpico amp with a razor blade and poking some holes in it with a pin for good measure." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iGav9uJwA_w ← dave-davies
**Intentional Speaker Damage for Distortion (1964)** : Dave Davies was an early pioneer in physically modifying amplifiers to achieve distortion. In 1964, while recording a song with The Kinks, he intentionally damaged the speaker cone of his small amplifier, using either a razor blade or a screwdriver, to produce a distorted sound. While effective in creating the desired effect, this method offered little control over the specific type or intensity of the distortion. "When the band was recording this song in 1964, he slashed the speaker cone of his small amp." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNiL63fY1Y8 ← dave-davies
