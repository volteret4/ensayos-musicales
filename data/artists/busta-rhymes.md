# artist - Busta Rhymes

## curiosities
**High Video Budgets** : Busta Rhymes was one of several hip-hop artists known for spending a couple of million dollars on his music videos. This trend in hip-hop contrasted with the alt-rock scene's anti-glamour approach, instead using high production values to showcase themes of money, status, and luxury. "MC Hammer, Buster Rhymes, and Puff Daddy had no trouble spending a couple of million on videos." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7IDbqdIw3WU
