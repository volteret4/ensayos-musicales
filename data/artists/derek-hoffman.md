# artist - Derek Hoffman

## curiosities
**Producer of "Civilianaires"** : Derek Hoffman, a young producer based in Toronto, produced The Trews' 2018 album "Civilianaires." The album was recorded in his basement studio on the East End of Toronto, where tracks were built and vocals were layered, marking a sonic departure for the band.
