## songs
**American Girl** : This song was "took" by The Strokes for their song "Last Night," an act which they openly admitted to and Tom Petty found amusing rather than litigious. "The strokes took American girl for their song last night." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4cZfegexwwk
**I Won't Back Down (1989)** : A hit song from 1989. Its recognized similarity to Sam Smith's 2014 hit "Stay With Me" led to legal involvement and an out-of-court settlement in October 2014, resulting in Tom Petty being awarded songwriting royalties from the Sam Smith track. "Tom Petty's 1989 hit, I won't back down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4cZfegexwwk
**Mary Jane's Last Dance (1993)** : Released in 1993, this song was noted for its similarity to the Red Hot Chili Peppers' "Dani California." Tom Petty, however, downplayed any "negative intent," stating that "a lot of rock and roll songs sound alike" and expressing disinterest in frivolous lawsuits over pop songs. "This is a clip of a Tom Petty song called Mary Jane's Last Dance, which was released in 1993." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4cZfegexwwk
