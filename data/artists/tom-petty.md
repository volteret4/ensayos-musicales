# artist - Tom Petty

## songs
**American Girl (1976)** : A song from which The Strokes admitted to borrowing elements for their hit "Last Nite". ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
**I Won't Back Down (1989)** : A 1989 hit that shares significant melodic similarities with Sam Smith’s 2014 song "Stay With Me". This led to an out-of-court settlement where Petty was awarded songwriting royalties. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
**Mary Jane's Last Dance (1993)** : A 1993 release noted for its similarity to the Red Hot Chili Peppers' later hit "Dani California". Petty declined to sue, stating that many rock songs sound alike and he doubted there was negative intent. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
