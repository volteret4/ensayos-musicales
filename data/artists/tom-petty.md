## songs
**American Girl** : This song was "took" by The Strokes for their song "Last Night," an act which they openly admitted to and Tom Petty found amusing rather than litigious. "The strokes took American girl for their song last night." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4cZfegexwwk
**I Won't Back Down (1989)** : This 1989 hit was recognized by many people as being similar to Sam Smith's 2014 hit single "Stay With Me." Lawyers became involved, leading to an out-of-court settlement in October 2014 where Petty was awarded songwriting royalties from the Sam Smith song. "But I'll stand my ground and I won't back down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
**Mary Jane's Last Dance (1993)** : This song was released in 1993 and was later compared to the Red Hot Chili Peppers' song "Dani California" from their 2006 *Stadium Arcadium* album due to similar sounds. Petty expressed doubt about "any negative intent here," noting that "a lot of rock and roll songs sound alike," and stated he doesn't "believe in lawsuits much." "I seriously doubt there is any negative intent here. And a lot of rock and roll songs sound alike." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE

**Initial New Wave Categorization** : When Tom Petty first emerged, some in the music industry categorized him as a New Wave artist due to his short, sharp songs and anti-corporate stance, illustrating the broad initial application of the term. "For example, when Tom Petty first appeared with his short, sharp songs, and anti-corporate stance, some people in the industry categorized him as a new wave artist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYsV3ozbk4M
**Opposition to Trump Campaign Music Use** : Tom Petty was among a long list of artists who expressed strong opposition to Donald Trump's campaign using their music at political rallies. Despite venues often holding blanket performing rights licenses, artists did not want their work associated with the campaign and many pursued cease and desist orders or lawsuits. "The list of artists against Trump using their music is long." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
