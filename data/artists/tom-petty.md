## songs
**I Won't Back Down (1989)** : This 1989 hit was recognized by many people as being similar to Sam Smith's 2014 hit single "Stay With Me." Lawyers became involved, leading to an out-of-court settlement in October 2014 where Petty was awarded songwriting royalties from the Sam Smith song. "But I'll stand my ground and I won't back down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE
**Mary Jane's Last Dance (1993)** : This song was released in 1993 and was later compared to the Red Hot Chili Peppers' song "Dani California" from their 2006 *Stadium Arcadium* album due to similar sounds. Petty expressed doubt about "any negative intent here," noting that "a lot of rock and roll songs sound alike," and stated he doesn't "believe in lawsuits much." "I seriously doubt there is any negative intent here. And a lot of rock and roll songs sound alike." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rfObVld9rIE

## curiosities
**Initial New Wave Categorization** : When Tom Petty first emerged, some in the music industry categorized him as a New Wave artist due to his short, sharp songs and anti-corporate stance, illustrating the broad initial application of the term.
