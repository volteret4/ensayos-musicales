# artist - Seven Year Bitch

## curiosities
**Postcard to Dave Grohl** : Seven Year Bitch sent a postcard to Dave Grohl following Kurt Cobain's suicide, understanding the grief he was experiencing. The message on the postcard encouraged him to continue with music: "We know what you're going through. The desire to play music is gone for now, but it will return. Don't worry." This message was a "deciding factor" in convincing Dave to keep making music. "It read, We know what you're going through. The desire to play music is gone for now, but it will return. Don't worry." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**Tragedy and Perseverance** : This Seattle band experienced significant hardships. In 1992, one of their members overdosed and died. The following year, in 1993, another member was kidnapped, raped, and murdered. Despite these tragedies, the band chose to "stick together and continued to make music," serving as an inspiration to Dave Grohl. "In 1992, one of their members O.D. and died. And then in 1993, another member was kidnapped, raped, and murdered." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
