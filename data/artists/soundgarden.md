## songs
**Black Hole Sun** : During the recording of this song, Chris Cornell utilized a Gretsch Duo Jet guitar. The distinctive warbling sound at the beginning of the track was achieved by running the guitar through a Marshall JMP-50 half-stack for higher frequencies and a Mesa Boogie Dual Rectifier Solo half-stack for low-end, both used in tandem with a Leslie cabinet. "It's that last guitar we hear on this song, running through a Marshall JMP-50 half-stack, which provide the higher frequencies, as well as a Mesa Boogie dual rectifier solo half-stack for low-end grunt. The two amps were used in tandem, along with a Leslie cabinet, to get that distinctive warbling sound at the beginning." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
**My Way (1994)** : This song is from Soundgarden's 1994 album, "Superunknown." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
