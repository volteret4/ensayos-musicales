**Steve Harwell Death (September 4, 2023) – Alcoholism, Son's Leukemia Death, Cardiomyopathy, Age 56** : Steve Harwell struggled with alcoholism for most of his adult life, a condition that worsened after his infant son Presley died at six months from leukemia. Alcohol destroyed his liver. In 2013 he was diagnosed with cardiomyopathy (heart muscle disease); by 2015 he had Wernicke encephalopathy from alcohol damage, causing balance problems, confusion, and vision impairment. By 2021, his health made continuing with Smash Mouth impossible. Following an erratic onstage performance at a New York festival, the band issued a statement confirming he was retiring to focus on his health. In August 2023, he began hospice care at his home in Boise, Idaho. He died September 4, 2023, age 56. "The truth was, nothing could be done for Steve." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-JPLLJyOid8
