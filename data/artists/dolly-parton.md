# artist - Dolly Parton

## genres
- Country Music

## songs
**Jolene (1973)** : Released in Fall 1973, this song features a narrator pleading with another woman not to take her man. It was inspired by Dolly Parton's real-life husband flirting with a local bank clerk, while the visual image of Jolene came from a red-headed young fan with green eyes who asked for an autograph. It became a big hit for Parton, won a Grammy, and has been covered many times. "It was inspired when her real-life husband started flirting with a local bank clerk." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

**Highly Honored Female Songwriter** : Dolly Parton is recognized as one of the greatest songwriters of all time and is the most honored woman in country music. She has received at least one Grammy nomination and some wins from each of the Academy Awards, the Grammy Awards, the Tony Awards, and the Golden Globes. "No woman has been honored more in the realm of country music than her." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Imagination Library Foundation** : Dolly Parton founded an organization called the Imagination Library, dedicated to promoting childhood literacy. Since its inception, the organization has successfully donated one hundred million books to children. "Dolly Parton founded an organization called the Imagination Library since it began she's donated one hundred million books to children." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO0K_N7Ys7I
