# artist - Dolly Parton

## genres
- Country

## songs
**Jolene (1973)** : Released in the fall of 1973, "Jolene" features a narrator pleading with another woman not to take her man. The song was inspired by Dolly Parton's real-life husband flirting with a local bank clerk, while the visual image of Jolene was based on a red-headed young fan with green eyes who asked for an autograph around the same time. It became a big hit for Dolly Parton and won a Grammy. The song has been covered many times. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw

## curiosities
**Awards and Recognition** : Dolly Parton is considered "one of the greatest songwriters of all time," and no woman has been honored more in country music than her. She has received at least one Grammy nomination and some wins from each of the Academy Awards, the Grammy Awards, the Tony Awards, and the Golden Globes. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
