# artist - Rockets Red Glare

## curiosities
**Actor, Drug Dealer, and Key Witness** : Rockets Red Glare was a drug dealer, bit actor, club bouncer, stand-up comedian, occasional roadie, and sometime police informant. He appeared in over 30 films, including "Desperately Seeking Susan" and "After Hours." He was called by Nancy Spungin for drugs the night she died and is a central figure in alternative theories of her murder, with some alleging he killed her during a robbery. "Let's talk about him for a second. Rockets was an actor who ended up in more than 30 films." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Alternative Murder Suspect** : The most popular alternative theory states that Rockets Red Glare murdered Nancy Spungin while Sid Vicious was incapacitated by drugs, in the process of robbing the couple of $1400 (and an alleged $20,000 in royalties). Author Phil Strongman's book "Pretty Vacant" advances this theory, citing Red Glare's sudden acquisition of money and new clothes shortly after Nancy's death, despite previously being broke. "The most popular alternative theory is that Nancy was killed by Rockets Red Glare." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
**Death and Unanswered Questions** : Rockets Red Glare denied all accusations of murder, though he reportedly blabbed about what he did to friends and even tried to sell a snuff film of Nancy's murder. He died on May 28, 2001, from kidney failure, liver failure, cirrhosis, and Hepatitis C, exacerbated by morbid obesity, taking with him any potential answers regarding Nancy's death.
