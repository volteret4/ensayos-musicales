**Co-founder of Detroit Techno** : Juan Atkins is one of the celebrated "Holy Trinity" who pioneered techno in Detroit in the mid-1980s. "Aunque el tecno en la acción de Detroit ha mediado los 80 de la mano de Juan Atkins, Derrick Meill y Kevin Sanderson, la celebre santísima trinidad..." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs

**Cybotron Formation and Interests** : In 1980, Atkins formed Cybotron with Richard Davis, an older Vietnam veteran he met while studying music. They bonded over shared interests in science fiction, futurism, and electronic music, though Atkins's roots were in funk while Davis leaned towards rock. "Ambos eran la ficionadas a la fíjana ficción, a los futuros logos y a la música electrónica." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw
**Deep Space Soundworks DJing** : Juan Atkins and Terry McMillan began DJing under the name Deep Space Soundworks. They mixed their own early productions with new House music emerging from Chicago, traveling there frequently to establish connections with DJs like Ron Hardy and Frankie Knuckles. "En esas sesiones mezclaban la música de Baylor, Peacus, sus primeras producciones, y una música nueva que estaba soquiando de Chicago, El House." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw
**Early Influences and Environment** : As Jonathan Atkins, he, Derrick May, and Kevin Saunderson, all from a middle-class background in Belleville, less than 50 km from Detroit, spent their late 1970s youth listening to funk and European electronic music. Their ability to grow up in a prosperous environment, rather than the ghettos, was attributed to the job opportunities at Ford and General Motors for both Black and white residents. "Atkins la tribuye que por primera vez, en Detroit, tanto blancos como nigos, podían trabajar en las mismas condiciones." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw

**First Synthesizer Acquisition** : In a context of significant economic and social changes in Detroit, Atkins acquired his first synthesizer, a Korg MS-10, in 1980, marking a pivotal moment in his musical development. **Founding Metroplex Records** : Following the split of Cybotron, Atkins, now working solo, founded his own record label, Metroplex. This label became a crucial platform not only for releasing his own music but also for showcasing other like-minded producers, helping to solidify the techno sound. "Para el elamente, establece su propio sello discográfico, Metroplex, donde no solo lanzaría su propia música, sino también la de otros productores afines." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw
**Introverted Nature and Career Impact** : Juan Atkins was a very withdrawn, introverted, and closed-off individual who never wanted to leave Detroit. **Techno Pioneer's Influences** : Juan Atkins, a pioneer of techno, cited Kraftwerk, Parliament, and Funkadelic as his primary influences. His early tracks with projects like Cybotron and Model 500 are described as a "bastard child" of American funk and Kraftwerk's technological minimalism.
