## curiosities
**Small Faces Became Faces When Rod Stewart and Ronnie Wood Joined – Both Later Became Rock Royalty** : When Steve Marriott left the Small Faces in 1969 to form Humble Pie, the rest of the group hired a new guitarist and a new singer. The guitarist was Ronnie Wood, who six years later would replace Mick Taylor in the Rolling Stones. The singer was a gravel-voiced former grave digger named Rod Stewart. The name changed from Small Faces to simply the Faces — because the two tall new members made the "small" descriptor irrelevant. "The new guitarist was Ronnie Wood.
