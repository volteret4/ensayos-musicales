# artist - David Botterall

## curiosities
**Grammy Awards Location** : David Botterall, a Grammy award-winning record producer, keeps his Grammy Awards at his family cottage. He personally doesn't like to see them on display, but his family does. "You know, they're up at my cottage. I have a cottage. I don't put them up in places. I don't like to see them but my family does." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
**Humanitarian Efforts Through Art** : David Botterall is involved in initiatives that treat individuals, particularly women in the DRC and children in places like Fox Lake, Guinea, and South Africa, as proper artists. This approach, while not generating significant monetary sales, is seen as a way to facilitate the healing of trauma and enable people to move forward with their lives. "But the fact that we're treating them as proper artists also helps to the retention of the healing of the trauma." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
