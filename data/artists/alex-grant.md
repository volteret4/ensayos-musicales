# artist - Alex Grant

## curiosities
**Bowie Net Song Contest Winner (1999)** : Alex Grant, a 20-year-old American, won the Bowie Net song contest in 1999, which had 80,000 lyric submissions. His winning lyrics were featured on David Bowie's *Hours* album. "The winner was Alex Grant, a 20-year-old American." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yq2A_o9-ulM
