## curiosities
**Catalyst for Band Connection** : Max Kermann was wearing a Sam Roberts Band T-shirt on the second day of university, which prompted Nick Dica to introduce himself, saying, "I like Sam Roberts." This interaction led to Nick joining Arkells as the bassist. "And then the next day Nick introduced himself to me because I was wearing a Sam Roberts band T-shirt. And Nick said, I like Sam Roberts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WSe0wdljSGA
**Performance at SARS-Stock (2003)** : The Sam Roberts Band performed at SARS-Stock on July 30, 2003, an event aimed at boosting Toronto's morale and economy after the SARS virus crisis.
