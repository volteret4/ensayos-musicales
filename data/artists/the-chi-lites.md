# artist - The Chi-Lites

## genres
- Soul

## albums
**For God's Sake, Give More Power to the People (1971)** : Released shortly after the assassination of Fred Hampton, this album's title track, "For God's Sake, Give More Power to the People," adopted the phrase "power to the people" and injected a newfound urgency into the band's sound. Ricky Vincent, author and funk historian, identified this song as a direct response to Hampton's killing, serving as a popular acknowledgment of the power and influence of the Black Panther Party in Chicago and nationwide. "Took that phrase, power to the people and dropped it right into their 1971 album.
