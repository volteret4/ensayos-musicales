# artist - Henry Purcell

## curiosities
**"Fantasia Upon One Note" (1680) - Inspiration for Pinball Wizard** : Henry Purcell composed "Fantasia Upon One Note" in 1680, a piece characterized by one instrument maintaining a middle C throughout while other instruments weave around it. Speculation suggests Purcell might have created this piece to allow a friend with limited musical ability to participate by simply holding the middle C. Pete Townshend later found this composition "a stunning thing to call upon" while writing "Pinball Wizard," analyzing its chords to adapt them for guitar. "The speculation is that Purcell maybe had a friend who could not really play.
