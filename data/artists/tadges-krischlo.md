# artist - Tadges-Krischlo

## curiosities
**Behind-the-Scenes Storytelling on Podcast** : On the "Art Context" podcast, Tadges-Krischlo aims to reveal the often-unseen aspects of music video creation, from the directors' humble beginnings and influences to the intricate details of storyboarding, art direction, and collaborative processes. This offers a "close set behind experience" for listeners to understand how a finished piece of art comes to fruition. "it's pretty much we're giving them that kind of close set behind experience where you get to see the process of how we get to the finish line." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
**Co-Host of Art Context** : Tadges-Krischlo is a co-host of the new podcast "Art Context: The Architects of Art," which focuses on the stories and creative processes of music video storytellers. He and his co-host, Director X, aim to celebrate these often-unsung creative artists in the music industry. "We have Tadges-Krischlo and Director X. And we want to give you a bit of an introduction to what this new podcast is all about." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0FYq5MBdHw4
**Co-founder of Fella Production Company** : Tadges-Krischlo is a co-founder of the production company Fella, which achieved its breakthrough with the music video for "Popstar," featuring DJ Khaled, Drake, and Justin Bieber. This venture started with two individuals from Brampton, Ontario, creating art for a global audience. "We broke our production company, Fella, with this music video for DJ Khaled, Drake and Bieber called Popstar. So it's been a crazy journey. And there were two kids from Brampton, Ontario that went out to make art that broke out to the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY

**Inspiration from 80s Music Videos** : Tadges-Krischlo's passion for music videos stemmed from watching influential artists like Madonna, Peter Gabriel, Phil Collins, Michael Jackson, and Aerosmith in the 1980s. He was fascinated by the storytelling, dancing, and style, which fueled his desire to enter the art form. "I came up, I came up in the 80s era where that's what made me fall in love with music videos, right? I was watching music videos by like Madonna and Peter Gabriel and like Phil Collins and Michael Jackson and Eros Smith. And I was always fascinated by music videos and the storytelling and the dancing and the style and all that stuff. And that's what got that's what made us fall in love with the art." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
