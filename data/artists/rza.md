**Co-Founder and Business Leader of Wu Tang Clan** : RZA, alongside GZA (Jason), was the architect behind the formation of the Wu Tang Clan, envisioning a unique project different from the market. He served as the primary creative and, more significantly, entrepreneurial leader of the group, meticulously planning their business strategy, particularly the innovative record deal and the "five-year plan" for solo releases.
