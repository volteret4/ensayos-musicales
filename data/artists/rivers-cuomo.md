# artist - Rivers Cuomo

## curiosities
**Post-Pinkerton Isolation and Songwriting Plan** : Following the underperformance of Weezer's second album, *Pinkerton*, Rivers Cuomo withdrew from Harvard, where he was studying music. He secluded himself in a room painted entirely black and insulated the windows, intending to purge all weaknesses and dedicate himself to crafting the "perfect pop song." "When Weezer's second album, Pinkerton, didn't do as well as Rivers Quomo had hoped, he dropped out of Harvard where he had been studying music and hid in his room, which are the walls and ceilings painted black and had the windows blacked out with layers of fiberglass insulation. The plan was to purge himself of all weaknesses so we could concentrate on writing the perfect pop song." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
