# artist - Romeo Savidra

## curiosities
**Kurt Cobain Lookalike and Conspiracy** : Romeo Savidra is a Peruvian singer who gained viral attention for his resemblance to Kurt Cobain while performing Nirvana's "Come As You Are" on local TV. This led to a conspiracy theory suggesting that Kurt Cobain was alive and hiding out in South America. However, this theory is debunked by the fact that Savidra plays guitar right-handed, in contrast to the left-handed Cobain. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pmKjZzypzvg
