## curiosities
**Radical Female Drummer Style** : Mo Tucker was noted as a radical female drummer. Instead of sitting behind a standard drum kit, she played standing up, with bass drums turned on their sides, and used mallets instead of traditional drumsticks to strike everything. "Yeah, a woman drummer. That was radical and she played like a radical." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
