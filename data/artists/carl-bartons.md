**O of the Record (~2021) - Compilation of Unreleased Material** : This important and interesting album was released approximately three years prior to the discussion. It is not a new recording but a compilation of old tapes Carl Bartons discovered during a house move. These tapes contained numerous recordings he had made for the *Radioactivity* album that were either never finished or were surplus material. Carl Bartons compiled the best of these recordings, which were not heavily promoted, but are of great interest to Craguer fans, often interpreted as the unreleased B-sides of *Radioactivity*. The album transports listeners back to 1975 and illuminates the unique contributions Carl Bartons brought to Craguer's sound that were not present before his involvement. **Distinct Musical Contributions to Craguer** : Carl Bartons brought significantly different ideas to Craguer compared to Ralph and Florian, making a substantial contribution. He was a less melodic and less symphonic individual, much more interested in "canned sounds," synthesizers, and especially "looped canned sounds," which later became characteristic of Craguer's sound, particularly in albums like *Computer World*. "Él leó una persona mucho menos melodica, mucho menos sinfónica que le interesaba mucho más los sonidos enlatados, los sintetizadores, y sobre todo los sonidos loops enlatados que fueron después de sacar aterísticos de Craguerda y el mando así o el en Computer World." ← Music Radar Clan > Karl Bartos.
