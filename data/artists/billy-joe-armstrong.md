## members

## curiosities
**Adrian's Influence** : Adrian Nesser brought stability to Billy Joe Armstrong during a difficult period with previous girlfriends.
**Father of Joseph Marciano Armstrong and Jacob Danger Armstrong** : Billy Joe Armstrong of Green Day is the father of musicians Joseph Marciano Armstrong (Joey) and Jacob Danger Armstrong.
**Marriage to Adrian Nesser** : Married Adrian Nesser on July 2nd, 1994, after meeting her through her brother, professional skateboarder Steve Nesser, in 1990.
**Southwest Airlines Air Rage Incident (2011)** : On September 1, 2011, Billy Joe Armstrong was chastised by a Southwest Airlines crew member about his baggy pants while trying to fly from San Francisco to Burbank. After refusing to pull up his pants, he was removed from the plane. The airline later apologized and arranged for him to be on the next flight.
