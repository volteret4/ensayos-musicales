## curiosities
**Post-WWI Drummer for Hollywood Redheads** : Still-May Ditty played drums for the Hollywood Redheads, an all-girl band, starting just after World War I. Her involvement reflects the gradual, albeit slow, increase in opportunities for female drummers in the early 20th century.
