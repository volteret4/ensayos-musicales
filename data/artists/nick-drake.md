**Controversial Death Details** : The circumstances surrounding Nick Drake's death are subject to differing accounts, even within his family. "His family contends, or some of his family contend that he had taken a pill and before and fallen asleep for a brief period or not at all. **Record Label Marketing after VW Ad** : After witnessing the significant increase in sales following the Volkswagen commercial, Nick Drake's record label responded by adding stickers to his CDs.
