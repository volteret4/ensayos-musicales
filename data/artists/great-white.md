## curiosities
**Station Nightclub Fire (February 20, 2003)** : On February 20, 2003, during a Great White performance at the Station Nightclub in Warwick, Rhode Island, pyrotechnics ignited flammable acoustic foam on the walls and ceilings. Within approximately one minute, the club was engulfed in flames, and within two minutes, visibility was lost due to thick black smoke. The subsequent rush to escape led to 100 deaths and 230 injuries.
