## songs
**Borderline (2009)** : This cover, a collaboration with The Flaming Lips, was released in Spring 2009 as part of a split 7-inch single with The Black Keys for Record Store Day, on green vinyl. The experimental band deliberately slowed down Madonna's mainstream pop song, creating an interesting juxtaposition. "The Flaming Lips along with Star Death and White Dwarfs covering Madonna's Borderline that was released in 2009." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw ← stardeath-and-white-dwarfs

## curiosities
**Approach to Cover Versions** : Stardeath and White Dwarfs are described as experimental, meaning they are willing to try almost anything once. This often translates to creating numerous cover versions of other artists' songs. "Star Death is best described as experimental, meaning that they'll try just about anything once and for them that means a lot of cover versions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw ← stardeath-and-white-dwarfs
