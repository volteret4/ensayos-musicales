**The Voyager Golden Disk (1977)** : Berry's music was the only rock and roll song included on the Voyager Golden Disk, a gold phonograph record launched on Voyager I and II. This ensures that if an alien civilization ever finds the record, Berry's work will be their first exposure to the genre. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
