**Rockabilly Fizzle** : Bill Haley was one of the white rockabilly artists who gained prominence in 1955 and 1956, but whose momentum on the US charts declined significantly by late 1956, preventing him from securing another major domestic hit. He, like other rockabilly acts, found continued success and produced important music internationally.
