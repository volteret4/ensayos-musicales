# artist - Late

## albums
**Pocket Watch (1992) – Solo Album by Dave Grohl** : A cassette-only release on Simple Machines, featuring all instruments played by Dave Grohl (credited as Dave G). It contained songs like "Color Pictures of a Mera Gold" and "Friend of a Friend." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU

## songs
**Color Pictures of a Mera Gold (1992)** : The original version of the song that later became "Mera Gold" on Nirvana's "Heart Shaped Box" CD single. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**Friend of a Friend (1992)** : A song written about the friendship between Kurt Cobain and Chris Novoselic, later re-recorded by Foo Fighters for "In Your Honor." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU

## curiosities
**Cassette Collectibility (1992)** : The "Pocket Watch" cassette is a highly sought-after collector's item, with copies in good condition fetching $700 or more. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
**Dave Grohl's Solo Project** : This was an alias used by Dave Grohl for his solo material, particularly the "Pocket Watch" album, which he recorded mostly in late 1990 and mid-1991. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_AY5lS-FmlU
