# artist - Late!

## curiosities
**Dave Grohl's Solo Indie Project – "Marigold" Release** : Late! was the name Dave Grohl used for an indie cassette release in 1990, on which he featured his original song "Marigold." This project predated the recording of an alternate version of "Marigold" for Nirvana's *In Utero* album. "He actually recorded it then and released the song on an indie cassette under the name late." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
