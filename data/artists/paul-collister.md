# artist - Paul Collister

## curiosities
**Owner of Winston the 4-track (Unspecified)** : A friend of Paul Humphreys, Paul Collister owned the 4-track reel-to-reel tape machine named Winston, which OMD borrowed to use as their backing band for live performances in their early days.
