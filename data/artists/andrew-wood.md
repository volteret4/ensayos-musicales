## curiosities
**Tribute Concept for Shangri-La Dee Da (1980)** : Andrew Wood, from the Seattle band Mother Love Bone, died of an overdose in 1980. Stone Temple Pilots' "Shangri-La Dee Da" album was originally intended as a double album, partly as a tribute to Wood, complete with a documentary film and a coffee table book, though the concept was scaled back by the record company. "This record was supposed to be a double album, something about it being a tribute to Andrew Wood of the Seattle band Mother Love Bone, who died of an overdose back in 1980." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY ← andrew-wood
