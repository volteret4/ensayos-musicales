**Evolution to Pop Sound** : By their second album, following a change in producers, Cheap Trick's rock sound began to incorporate more pop elements. They maintained their guitar presence but opted for simpler melodies and bigger hooks, echoing the earlier Beatles' approach. Their third album in 1978 showcased a polished sound with a hit single, blending rock with significant pop sensibilities. They began by playing in various small venues like pubs and bowling alleys.
