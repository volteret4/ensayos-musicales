## curiosities
**Hebrew-con Airplay** : T-Packs is an Israeli band that receives airplay in Israel under the country's "Hebrew con" music regulations. These rules are designed to ensure a certain proportion of Hebrew-language music is played on Israeli radio stations. The band's name is reportedly derived from a brand of liquid paper type product.
