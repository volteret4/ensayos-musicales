# artist - George Barnes

## instruments
- Electric guitar

## songs
**Low Down Dirty Shame (1938)** : Recorded on March 1st, 1938, this song with blues guitarist Big Bill Broonzy is one of the two tracks that mark George Barnes's achievement as the first person to commit an electric guitar solo to record. Barnes was only 16 years old at the time of the recording. "Here's one of them, a low-down dirty shame." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ

## curiosities
**First Electric Guitar Solo Recording** : George Barnes holds the distinction of being the first person to record an electric guitar solo. This historic recording took place on March 1st, 1938, when he was just 16 years old, playing with blues guitarist Big Bill Broonzy on two songs, including "Low Down Dirty Shame."
