## curiosities
**Guitar Tech for The Cure** : Perry Bamonte, brother of Daryl Bamonte (who filled in for Depeche Mode), started his career as a guitar tech for The Cure. "Perry Bimonte, the brother of the affirmation Darrell Bimonte who bailed out Depeche mode in 1994 started as a guitar tech for the cure." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
**Joined The Cure as Keyboardist, then Guitarist** : In 1991, Perry Bamonte joined The Cure as their keyboardist, stepping in when Roger O'Donnell left just before the final leg of the Disintegration tour. Following this, he also played guitar on the band's next four albums. "But when Roger O'Donnell left the cure just before the final leg of the disintegration tour in 1991, Perry Bimonte became the band's keyboardist and after that he played guitar on the next four albums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
