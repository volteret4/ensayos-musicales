## curiosities
**Disbandment Due to Monkhood** : The band broke up when their guitarist made the decision to become a Hare Krishna monk, an event that effectively destroyed their momentum and plans. At the time, they had already written material for what was intended to be their second album, which they planned to title "Rage Against The Machine." "See, their guitars decided to become a Harry Christian monk, and that kind of destroyed everything." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xo5DRSW95QQ
**National Underground Following** : Inside Out successfully cultivated a considerable following within the National Underground Hardcore scene, achieving particular success and recognition in the Orange County area and its surroundings. "They were actually able to get a decent following on the National Underground Hardcore scene, and did especially well in and around Orange County." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xo5DRSW95QQ
