**Use of Devil's Triad** : Wagner, a highly influential classical composer, is noted for his use of the "devil's tri tone" (flattened fifth) in his works. His inclusion in this list highlights that this particular dissonant interval was a recognized compositional element within the classical tradition, long before its later controversial associations.
