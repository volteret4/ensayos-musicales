# artist - Ronnie Ross

## instruments
- Saxophone

## curiosities
**Musical Mentorship and Contribution** : Ronnie Ross played the saxophone part on Lou Reed's "Walk on the Wild Side." He also had a significant connection to David Bowie, having taught Bowie how to play the saxophone back in the 1950s. "The saxophone bit at the end was played by Ronnie Ross, the guy who taught Bowie how to play sax when he was a kid back in the 1950s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4
