## curiosities
**Female-Centric Gigs** : Encouraged women to come to the front of the stage and men to the back at their gigs, promoting a female-centric environment. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Influence on "Smells Like Teen Spirit"** : In 1991, Kathleen Hanna went drinking with Kurt Cobain; she remarked that he "smelled sweet, almost like a particular type of deodorant marketed to teens." Cobain, who never wore deodorant, was inspired by this comment to title his new song "Smells Like Teen Spirit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Kathleen Hanna's Confrontational Style** : Singer Kathleen Hanna was known for her fearlessness, often diving off stage at male hecklers, becoming a hero to both punk women and men. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Kathleen Hanna's Marriage** : Kathleen Hanna married Adam Horovitz of the Beastie Boys in 2006. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
**Riot Grrrl Pioneers** : Formed by four women in 1990, becoming one of the most important and covered groups in the riot grrrl scene during its peak in the early 1990s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI
