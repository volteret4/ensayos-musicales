## curiosities
**Breakthrough Production for Killdozer** : His production work on Killdozer's 1989 record, *For Ladies Only*, began to attract national attention.
**Early Bands** : Formed the group Spooner, releasing three albums. Also released one album with the group Firetown.
**Early Life and Education** : Originally from Viroqua, Wisconsin. Took piano lessons as a child and drums as a teenager. Studied pre-med at the University of Wisconsin for two years and completed four semesters of electronic music theory before dropping out.
**Early Production Work** : Became interested in producing music and set up a small studio space in Madison, Wisconsin, working with local indie groups like Killdozer.
**Extensive Production Credits** : Produced a remix record for Sonic Youth, Depeche Mode, Solosyle, U2, and L7, and also produced for Smashing Pumpkins and Nirvana.
**Formation of Multi-Platinum Band** : Formed his own multi-platinum band, Garbage.
**Producing Nirvana's Nevermind** : Kurt Cobain decided to relocate Nirvana to Butch Vig's Wisconsin studios for demos that would lead to *Nevermind*, and then asked Vig to produce the entire album.
**Role in Grunge Revolution** : Helped shape the entire grunge revolution of the early 1990s, primarily as a producer rather than a performer.
