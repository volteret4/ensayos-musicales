# artist - Homey

## songs
**American Gigolo (1999)** : This was the only song ever released by Homey, a campus band Rivers Cuomo played in while at Harvard in 1997 and 1998. It featured Greg Brown from Cake, Matt Sharp (before he left to form The Rentals), Adam Orruth of Shufflepuck, and Yu-Vell Gabel of Soul Coughing. The song was released as part of the soundtrack for the comedy "Meet the Deedles," which was a box office bomb despite starring Paul Walker, Dennis Hopper, and Robert Englund.
