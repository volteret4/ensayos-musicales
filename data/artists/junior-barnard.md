## songs
**Rolly Polly (1946)** : Recorded in 1946, this song features one of the very first experiments with distortion by Junior Barnard, a country-swing guitarist. In 1945, he plugged an Epiphone Emperor archtop electric guitar into a tiny vacuum-tube-powered amp and turned everything up, pushing the guitar, pickup, and amp beyond design limits. This created a gritty, growly sound, with the distortion effect being subtle but present. "The distortion effect is subtle, but it's definitely there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iGav9uJwA_w
