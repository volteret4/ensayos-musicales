## curiosities
**Collaborations with Notable Artists** : Sean Lennon has collaborated on records with artists such as Albert Hammond Jr. of The Strokes, The Flaming Lips, Lady Gaga, and Lana Del Rey.
**Film Composer** : Sean has scored several films.
**Idea of Forming a "Beatles Offspring" Band** : He befriended James McCartney (Paul's son), Danny Harrison (George's son), and Zach Starky (Ringo's son), and they considered forming a band together, though it never materialized.
**Involvement in Multiple Bands** : He has been a member of various bands.
**Producer for Other Artists** : He has produced material for other musicians.
**Solo Albums** : He has released a couple of albums under his own name.
**Son of John Lennon and Yoko Ono** : Sean Lennon is the son of John Lennon and Yoko Ono.
**The Claypool Lennon Delirium** : He records with Les Claypool of Primus under the name The Claypool Lennon Delirium.
