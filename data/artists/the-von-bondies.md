## curiosities
**Assault by Jack White** : In December 2003, Jason Stollsteimer was allegedly assaulted by Jack White at The Magic Stick club in Detroit during a record release party. White, who had previously produced an album for The Von Bondies and invited them to open for The White Stripes, reportedly became enraged after Stollsteimer brushed him off, leading to White allegedly punching him multiple times and spitting on him. "This happened at a club called the Magic Stick where Jason was at a record release party for another band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUr9sjWgUcw
**Jack White's Conviction for Assault** : Following the incident, Jack White pleaded guilty to aggravated assault, was fined $750, and ordered to attend an anger management course. "In the end, Jack pleaded guilty to aggravated assault. He was fined $750 and told to take an anger management course." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUr9sjWgUcw
