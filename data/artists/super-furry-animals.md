# artist - Super Furry Animals

## albums
**Rings Around the World (2001) – Featuring Paul McCartney's Celery** : In 2001, the Welsh band Super Furry Animals invited Paul McCartney to contribute to their album. His contribution arrived as a tape recording of him audibly munching on celery, which the band decided to incorporate into their "Rings Around the World" album for the song "Receptacle for the Respectable." For the record's 20th anniversary, this isolated celery track was made publicly available.
