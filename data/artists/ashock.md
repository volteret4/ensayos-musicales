# artist - Ashock

## members
- Florence Welch

## labels
- Tiny little label

## albums
**Plans** : This record was released on a "tiny little label" and contained a song called "Happy Slap," which Florence co-wrote with Matt Alchon. This song was later re-worked and released as "Kiss With A Fist" on Florence + The Machine's *Lungs* album. "Florence drifted into a group called Ashock, who recorded a record called Plans for a tiny little label." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=24NeCYqlVco

## songs
**Happy Slap** : This song was featured on Ashock's album "Plans" and was co-written by Florence Welch and Matt Alchon. It later underwent "serious reworking" and was released as "Kiss With A Fist," the first official Florence + The Machine single on the *Lungs* album. "That album contained a song called Happy Slap. Matt co-wrote that one too, and it would later end up in the lungs album under the title Kiss With A Fist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=24NeCYqlVco

## curiosities
**Florence Welch's Departure** : Florence Welch was approximately 19 years old when she was part of Ashock. There were "issues" with the band, leading to Florence's departure, which then brought her back to collaborating with Isabella Summers. "There were issues with Ashock and Florence's quit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=24NeCYqlVco
