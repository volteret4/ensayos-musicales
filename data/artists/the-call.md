## songs
**Let the Day Begin (1989)** : This song, from the album of the same name, was used by Al Gore in his 2000 presidential campaign. It garnered admiration from U2 and Peter Gabriel. "You too and Peter Gabriel were big fans. And Al Gore used the song we just heard, let the day begin, as part of his 2000 presidential campaign." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
