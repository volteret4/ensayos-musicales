## curiosities
**Influential to Other Artists** : U2 and Peter Gabriel were notable fans of The Call.
**Poetic, Artsy, Compassionate Style** : The band was described as very poetic, artsy, and compassionate.
**Reunions with Robert Been** : Following Michael Been's death, there have been some Call reunions where his son, Robert Been, filled in for him.
**Santa Cruz Origin** : The band originated from Santa Cruz, California.
**Success in 1980s and 1990s** : The Call achieved success in the Alt Rock world throughout the 1980s and 90s, releasing nine studio albums.
