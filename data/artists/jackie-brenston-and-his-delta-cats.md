## curiosities
**Busted Bass Amp Innovation** : During a 75-mile drive to the recording session on March 2nd, 1951, bass player Willie Kizart's amp was dropped, causing the speaker cone to loosen. Producer Sam Phillips liked the resulting distorted "buzz" sound and recorded it as is, inadvertently creating a foundational rock and roll sonic element. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**The Busted Bass Amp Innovation** : During a drive from Clarkstale, Mississippi, to Memphis, bass player Willie Kizart's amp was dropped, causing the speaker cone to loosen. Producer Sam Phillips liked the resulting distorted, vibrating sound and decided to record it as is, inadvertently creating a foundational rock sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
