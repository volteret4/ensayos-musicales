# artist - Jackie Brenston and His Delta Cats

## songs
**Rocket 88 (1951)** : This song, inspired by the Oldsmobile Rocket 88 car, is frequently cited by many as the first ever rock and roll song. It was recorded in early March 1951 in Memphis under unusual circumstances. ## curiosities
**Buzz-Inducing Guitar Amp Recording** : In early March 1951, while heading to Memphis to record, the band had a flat tire. During the unloading of their car to access the spare, a guitar amplifier fell and dislodged its speaker cone. Lacking funds for a new amp or repairs, they improvised by stuffing newspaper around the cone, which created a distinctive "buzz" that became a defining characteristic of their song "Rocket 88."
