# artist - Danny Harrison

## curiosities
**Diverse Musical Career** : He works as a performer, a composer for film and television, and a record producer.
**Idea of Forming a "Beatles Offspring" Band** : He became friends with James McCartney (Paul's son), Zach Starky (Ringo's son), and Sean Lennon (John's son), and they considered forming a band together, though it never materialized.
**Son of George and Olivia Harrison** : Danny Harrison is the only child of George and Olivia Harrison.
