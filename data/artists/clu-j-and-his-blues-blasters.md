## curiosities
**Early Ska Innovators** : In the late 1950s and early 1960s, this band was one of the first to adopt the new offbeat way of playing, contributing to the birth of Ska. Their leader, Cluett Johnson, used the made-up word "Scavuvi" to describe their sound, which some believe is the origin of the term "Ska."
