# artist - Harry Nyquist

## curiosities
**Nyquist-Shannon Sampling Theorem Contribution (1928)** : Harry Nyquist, an electronic engineer, introduced the foundational concept of the sampling theorem in 1928. This idea was presented in a paper published by what is referred to as "el suelo de americanos americanos" (likely a journal like the Bell System Technical Journal). His work posited that to accurately reconstruct a frequency, it must be sampled at a rate of at least twice its frequency.
