# artist - Sheila Pearson

## curiosities
**Post-WWII Female Drummer** : Sheila Pearson was one of the few women able to maintain a career as a musician after World War II, when opportunities for female musicians became scarce and sexist attitudes returned. She defied the prevailing notion that "girls can't play drums." "A few women were able to fight that. Tiny Davis, Helen Cole, Sheila Pearson and Kate Carlson were a few names of the era." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY ← sheila-pearson
