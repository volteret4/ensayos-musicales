## songs
**Help Me Make It Through the Night (N/A)** : This song was written by Chris Christopherson and became a crossover hit for Sammy Smith in November 1970, reaching number one country, number eight on the Billboard Hot 100, and number three on the easy listening chart. Olivia Newton-John also released her own cover version in 1971. ## curiosities
**Performance at The Troubadour** : Kris Kristofferson was observed performing at The Troubadour, which routinely featured artists playing multiple shows throughout the week, including two shows Tuesday through Thursday and three shows on Friday and Saturday.
