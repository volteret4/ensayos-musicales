# artist - Chet Baker

## albums
**Chet Baker Sings (1954)** : This album is cited as an example of the revolutionary sound made possible by microphone technology in 1954. The recording captures subtle nuances of performance, including intimate vocals that sound as if the singer is "right into your ear," and a "divinely played double bass," a sound that would have been impossible to achieve with pre-microphone acoustic recording methods.
