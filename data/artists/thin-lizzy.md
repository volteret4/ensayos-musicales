## curiosities
**Chris Sangerides as Producer** : Chris Sangerides, who produced The Tragically Hip's *Fully Completely*, was recognized for his previous work with Thin Lizzy, among other prominent heavy metal bands. "The producer was Chris Sangerides, a Greek Cypriot breads who was better known for working with Judas Priest and Black Sabbath and Ozzy and Thin Lizzy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
**Early Influence of Traditional Irish Music** : Formed in Dublin in 1970 as a rock band, Thin Lizzy incorporated traditional Irish music into their sound, especially evident in their first couple of records. "They were formed in 1970 as a rock band that drew on traditional Irish music for the first couple of records." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Impact on Irish Music Revival** : Thin Lizzy, along with Horslips, faced considerable criticism from musical traditionalists in Ireland, yet they made significant contributions to a revival of interest in traditional Irish music. "Both Thin Lizzy and Horse-lips took a lot of abuse from the musical traditionalists of Ireland, but both contributed greatly to a revival of interest in traditional Irish music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Influence on Metal and Rock** : Thin Lizzy, through its member Phil Lynott, is referenced to underscore the "big debt" that metal in the 1980s owed to black artists, acknowledging their significant contributions to rock and its subgenres. "Phil Linatov, then Lizzie." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PqOJ3YiFgYE
