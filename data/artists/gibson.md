## curiosities
**Commercialization of the Fuzz Pedal** : After hearing the distinctive fuzzy guitar sound on Marty Robbins' "Don't Worry," Gibson Guitars approached Grady Martin to develop a commercial product. Martin, along with Glenn Snottie and Revis Hobbs, designed the circuit, and Gibson manufactured the first-ever fuzz pedal, the Maestro FZ1 Fuzz tone. This product made distortion widely available to guitarists. "When the prototype was ready, Grady and Glenn Snottie took the contraption, which had taken on the form of a foot pedal, to Gibson's offices in Chicago.
