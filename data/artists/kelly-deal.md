## curiosities
**Career Transition to Professional Knitting** : After a career that included success with The Breeders and other bands, Kelly Deal transitioned to become a professional knitter, specializing in handbags, and has even released a book of patterns. "These days, she's a professional knitter, especially as handbags. She's even released a book of patterns." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0
**Defense Contractor Career** : Before joining The Breeders, Kelly held a job with a defense contractor, which included having a top-secret clearance. "By the time the breeders came along, Kelly had a job with a defense contractor complete with a top secret clearance." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Early Drumming and Pixies Opportunity** : Kelly Deal began playing drums at age eight and was nearly recruited to join the Pixies as their drummer alongside her sister. However, after spending time with the band, she concluded she "just didn't have the chops." "She was originally a drummer from the age of eight, and at one point she was almost drafted in to join the Pixies with her sister as the band's drummer, but after hanging out with them, she realized that she just didn't have the chops." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
**Identical Twin and Untrained Guitarist** : Kelly Deal is the identical twin sister of Kim Deal, the original bassist for the Pixies. In 1992, Kelly joined Kim in The Breeders as the guitarist, despite having no prior experience playing the instrument, quickly learning and mastering it. "She was the guitarist. And to be honest, she knew nothing about the instrument. She did not know how to play, but she was a fast learner and nailed it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0
**Late Start on Guitar** : She started playing the guitar for the first time at the age of 30, convinced by her sister Kim to join The Breeders. "So she started playing the guitar for the first time at age 30." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLC49nCf0ZM
