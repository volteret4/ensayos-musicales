# artist - Taj Krishlow

## curiosities
**Brampton, Ontario Origin** : Taj Krishlow, along with Director X, originated from Brampton, Ontario, where they began their artistic endeavors. "And there were two kids from Brampton, Ontario that went out to make art that broke out to the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw ← taj-krishlow
**Co-host of Art Context** : Taj Krishlow is a host of the new podcast "Art Context, the Architects of Art," which delves into the stories and creative processes of music video directors. "We have Taj Khrishlow and director X." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw ← taj-krishlow
**Experience in Music Video Industry** : With over 20 years in the music video business, Taj Krishlow and Director X created "Art Context" to highlight the creative artists behind impactful music videos. "And coming from the world of music video, being in the business for over 20 years, we felt it was necessary to create a show like architects do sit down and hear their stories, their come-ups, their journey, their process of creating some of the most iconic music videos, films and content on the planet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw ← taj-krishlow
**Fascination with Music Videos** : Taj Krishlow, like Director X, developed a love for music videos during the 1980s MTV and Much Music era, appreciating the storytelling, dancing, and style. "And that's what got that's what made us fall in love with the art." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw ← taj-krishlow
