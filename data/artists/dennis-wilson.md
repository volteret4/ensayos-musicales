## songs
**Holy Man (Unfinished Recording)** : This song was an unfinished recording that Dennis Wilson left behind before his death in 1983. Taylor Hawkins was later commissioned to complete it, adding music, writing new lyrics, and performing the vocals, with support from Roger Taylor and Brian May. ## curiosities
**Drowning Accident (1983)** : Dennis Wilson, a member of The Beach Boys, tragically drowned in a diving accident in 1983. He left behind an unfinished recording titled "Holy Man," which Taylor Hawkins later completed in 2019 with the help of Roger Taylor and Brian May.
