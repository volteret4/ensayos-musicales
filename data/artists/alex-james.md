## curiosities
**Award-Winning Cheese Maker** : After Blur achieved significant financial success, Alex James relocated to the countryside and purchased a farmhouse on 200 acres with hundreds of sheep and cows, transforming it into a cheese-making operation. His cheeses are award-winning and sold in British supermarkets. Popular varieties include "Blue Monday" (named after the New Order song), a creamy Shropshire blue cheese, as well as "Queen Maud," "Rotten Bastard," and "Mad Bitch." Alex has served as a judge in cheese competitions and authored books on cheese, including "All Cheeses Great and Small."
