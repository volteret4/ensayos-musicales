**Geographic Base** : Headquartered in Birmingham, another city favored by many West Indian immigrants. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Imported to North America by Seymour Stein** : Seymour Stein was instrumental in importing "unknown English bands" like The English Beat into North America. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Inclusion and Anti-Racism** : Like The Specials, The English Beat championed inclusion and actively fought racism. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Jamaican Influence** : Vocalist Ranking Roger and saxophonist Saxa, both black, heavily contributed Jamaican influence to the band's music. Saxa had previously played with ska giants like Prince Buster and Desmond Dekker. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Post-Punk Shaping** : Along with bands like The Specials, The English Beat played a crucial role in shaping the post-punk world through their diverse, interracial sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
