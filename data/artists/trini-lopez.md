# artist - Trini Lopez

## instruments
- Gibson ES-335 (1960s model)

## curiosities
**Influence on Dave Grohl's Signature Guitar** : A model of the ES-335 used by Trini Lopez in the 1960s served as the specific inspiration for Dave Grohl's Gibson DG-355 signature guitar. This highlights how historical instrument usage can inspire contemporary designs. "Dave's guitar is based on a model used by Trini Lopez used back in the 1960s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0HW8LQKvJw
