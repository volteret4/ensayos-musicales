## curiosities
**Influence on Dave Grohl's Signature Guitar** : A model of the ES-335 used by Trini Lopez in the 1960s served as the specific inspiration for Dave Grohl's Gibson DG-355 signature guitar. This highlights how historical instrument usage can inspire contemporary designs.
