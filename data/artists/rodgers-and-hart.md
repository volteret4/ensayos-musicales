# artist - Rodgers and Hart

## albums
**Smash Song Hits (1938) - First Record with Original Cover Artwork** : "Smash Song Hits," a recording by Broadway composers Rodgers and Hart, was the first record to feature original graphic artwork designed specifically for its sleeve in 1938. The artwork, created by Alex Steinweiss, revolutionized dull, text-only record packaging and significantly boosted sales.
