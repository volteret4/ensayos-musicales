## songs
**One Headlight (1996)** : This song achieved significant critical and commercial success, earning two Grammy Awards. It holds the distinction of being the first song to reach number one on three different charts simultaneously: Alternative Songs, Mainstream Rock Songs, and Adult Alternative Songs. "This song won two Grammy Awards. Plus, it was the first song to reach number one on three charts. Alternative songs, mainstream rock songs, and adult alternative songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk ← the-wallflowers

## curiosities
**Band Formation and Consistency** : The Wallflowers formed from an earlier band called The Apples, morphing into their new identity by 1989 in Los Angeles. Jacob Dylan has been the sole constant member throughout the band's history, which continues to be an active concern. "And then by 1989, he was in Los Angeles with a band called The Apples, who would soon morph into The Wallflowers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk ← the-wallflowers
