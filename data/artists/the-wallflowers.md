## songs
**One Headlight (1996)** : This song achieved significant critical and commercial success, earning two Grammy Awards. It holds the distinction of being the first song to reach number one on three different charts simultaneously: Alternative Songs, Mainstream Rock Songs, and Adult Alternative Songs. "This song won two Grammy Awards. Plus, it was the first song to reach number one on three charts. Alternative songs, mainstream rock songs, and adult alternative songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
