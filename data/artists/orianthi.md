## curiosities
**High-Profile Tours and Collaborations** : Orianthi served as the guitarist in Carrie Underwood's band and was slated to be Michael Jackson's lead guitarist for his final planned tour. Her extensive collaborative work includes performances with Richie Sambora (Bon Jovi), Hollywood Vampires, Don Felder (The Eagles), and Michael Bolton, as well as recording with Dave Stewart. "She ended up as the guitarist and carry Underwood's band and had Michael Jackson lived to tour one more time Orienthi was going to be in his band as lead guitarist." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0

**Prodigious Talent and Early Collaborations** : Hailing from Adelaide, Australia, Orianthi is renowned for her exceptional speed on the guitar. She performed with Steve Vai at age 15 and was jamming with Carlos Santana by age 18, demonstrating her early and remarkable talent. "She played for Steve Vy, no slouchy, when she was just 15 and by 18 she was jamming with Carlos Santana." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hbNJQ6OYmd0
