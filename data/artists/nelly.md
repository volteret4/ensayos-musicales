## songs
**Hot in Here (2002)** : This song was the winner of the 2002 Billboard Song of the Summer. Produced by The Neptunes (Pharrell Williams and Chad Hugo), it was created by taking an old Chuck Brown go-go record and turning up the heat "high enough to make you 'take off all your clothes'." The St. Louis rapper, Nelly, spelled "here" with two R's in a "Dirty South Style," and the song was number one for 7 weeks from June to September.
