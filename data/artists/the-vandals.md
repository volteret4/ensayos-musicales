## curiosities
**Fanbase** : The Vandals enjoyed a notable fanbase among prominent figures in alternative rock, including Kurt Cobain and Eddie Vedder, indicating their influence and appeal within the wider rock community. "Kurt Cobain was a fan of the vandals, and so is Eddie Vetter." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE ← the-vandals
**Humorous West Coast Punk** : Formed in Huntington Beach in 1980, The Vandals were known for injecting humor and a "goofy" attitude into the West Coast punk scene. They loved punk but consciously chose not to take themselves too seriously, setting them apart. "Some of the other proto pop punks thought it was time to lighten up and inject some humor into the whole West Coast punk scene." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE ← the-vandals
**Tony Hawk Video Game Feature** : Music from The Vandals was featured in Tony Hawk's popular skateboarding video games. "Tony Hawk's video games sold by the millions, and each one featured appropriate music from groups like The Vandals and Laguewagon, Suicidal tendencies, Millen-Call and Bad Religion, and so many more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=44dXy0StjIQ ← the-vandals
