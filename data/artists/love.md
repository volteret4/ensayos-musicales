## albums
**Forever Changes (1967)** : This album by the Los Angeles-based band begins with a Spanish guitar piece imbued with flamenco inspiration. The composition, created by Bryan MacLean, drew influence from flamenco records played by his mother, who was a flamenco dancer. "El disco Forever Changes de la banda de Los Ángeles Love empieza con una una guitarra española inspirada en el flamenco." ← Cómo el Flamenco Influyó a Músicos Extranjeros (Y Quizá No Lo Notaste) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ReRvRfbHec

## curiosities
**Paisley Underground Influence** : Love, an important California band from the 1960s, contributed to the foundation of the American Neo-Psych scene known as the Paisley Underground. Their California-style psychedelic sounds were advanced by groups emerging in the 1980s. "These groups dug into California-style psych from the 60s, advancing the sounds first put forward by the birds and love, another important California band from back in the day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4 ← love
