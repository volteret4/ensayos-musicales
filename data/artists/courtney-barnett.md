## curiosities
**Deadpan Singing Style** : Courtney Barnett, a singer-songwriter from Melbourne, is known for her deadpan singing style. This delivery can often mask the deeper, weirder, and more complex lyrical content she presents, requiring careful listening to fully appreciate her artistry. "Her singing style is pretty deadpan, and you may not pay attention to what she's actually saying." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rEawe_1Fqf0 ← courtney-barnett ← courtney-barnett
