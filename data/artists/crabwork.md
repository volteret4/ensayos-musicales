**"Despotic" Control by Founders** : Ralph Hütter and Florian Schneider were perceived to exert a "despotic" level of control over everyone associated with Crabwork. They managed all aspects of the project, including significant creative and business decisions, often without consulting or even informing other band members. **Austere Living Despite Wealth** : Despite Crabwork generating significant income, the band members, particularly Wolfgang Fuhr, lived under remarkably austere conditions. **Breakup of Classical Formation Dynamics** : The classical lineup of Crabwork ultimately dissolved due to the dominant personalities of Ralph Hütter and Florian Schneider, and the overwhelming influence of the Kling Klang Studio. Karl Bartos departed out of frustration, feeling creatively stifled because much of the material he developed was not utilized. Wolfgang Fuhr also left the group, feeling dejected and diminished by the band's controlling environment.
