# artist - Graham Goble

## songs
**Reminiscing (1978)** : He wrote this song, which was inspired by a C9 chord shown to him by David Briggs. He expressed his belief that it should be covered as frequently as The Beatles' "Yesterday," given its similar theme of looking back with longing. He further explained that the song's greater popularity in the United States compared to Australia stemmed from its depiction of "the world that was my internal world," a fantasy drawn from 1940s and 1950s American pop culture, which resonated deeply with Americans of that era.
