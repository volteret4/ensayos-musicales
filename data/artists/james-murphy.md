## curiosities
**Breakthrough at 35** : James Murphy, already 35 and showing signs of aging, achieved his breakthrough with LCD Sound System's self-titled debut album in 2005, following years of working in the indie scene and with his label. "But at the time all this happened, James Murphy was 35 and going gray." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Co-founded DFA Records** : About a year after declining the *Seinfeld* offer, James Murphy co-founded the indie label DFA, an acronym for Death From Above, working with New York City indie artists for eight years. "About a year later, he started up an indie label called DFA, which stood for Death From Above." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
**Declined Seinfeld Scriptwriting Job** : James Murphy was once offered a job as a scriptwriter for the popular TV show *Seinfeld*. He turned down this opportunity to instead continue pursuing his music career, which ultimately led to the formation of LCD Soundsystem. "James Murphy was once offered a job as a script writer for Seinfeld, but he turned that down in favor of sticking with music, which resulted in LCD sound system." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
**Declined Seinfeld Writing Offer (Age 22)** : As an English major dropout, James Murphy was offered a writing position for a new TV show called *Seinfeld* at age 22, but he declined, believing the show "about nothing" would not work, choosing instead to focus on music. "But then he thought, eh, this show is never going to work. It's about nothing. Pass. I'll stick with my music instead." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P45lJTdneoI
