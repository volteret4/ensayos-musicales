## curiosities
**21st Century Tweetpop Descendant** : Girl in Red is identified as a 21st-century artist whose Bedroom Pop sound is a direct descendant of the various forms of Tweetpop. This modern genre is enabled by affordable and powerful laptops, along with accessible recording software and microphones, allowing for the creation of dreamy, introspective, sensitive, and vulnerable music. "We wouldn't have artists like Girl in Red or Beba Dooby or Arlo Parks, Rex Orange County, and Sockermummy if it wasn't for the various flavors of Tweet Pop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g ← girl-in-red
**Bedroom Origins in Norway** : Marie Ulvin Ringheim, known as Girl in Red, began her musical journey in her bedroom in Horton, Norway, after her father gifted her a USB microphone for her laptop, marking a quintessential start for a bedroom pop artist. "She started making music in her bedroom in Horton, Norway after her father gave her a USB mic for her laptop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c ← girl-in-red
**SoundCloud Debut as Lydia X** : Before finding wider recognition as Girl in Red, Marie Ulvin Ringheim initially posted her songs on SoundCloud using the pseudonym Lydia X, highlighting the platform's role in the early development of bedroom pop artists.
