**Arrest Resistance and Assault (2014)** : Upon landing in Shannon on November 10, 2014, Dolores O'Riordan resisted arrest, shouting, "You can't arrest me. I'm an icon. I'm the queen of Limerick. You don't know who you're dealing with. I pay my taxes so I pay your wages and I am going to sue." Described as "irrational, aggressive, quite difficult," she kicked and banged in the patrol car, then escaped, requiring a cop to chase her. When caught, she headbutted the officer. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hmnMG6f80Sg
