# artist - Cameron Crowe

## curiosities
**1977 Article on Little River Band** : In 1977, he authored an article about Little River Band, describing them as having "melodic lead guitarists and for sophisticated singer songwriters" who "mastered an effortless commerciality." During his interview with the band, lead guitarist David Briggs asked him to avoid comparing them to CSNY, while Crowe personally felt their musical spirit was closer to The Beatles.
