# artist - Dave Matthews (roadie for KISS)

## curiosities
**Long-term KISS Roadie and Wig Specialist** : Dave Matthews worked for KISS for 30 years, primarily responsible for looking after the band's wigs. His duties included responding to emergencies, such as dousing Gene Simmons with a wet towel when the hairspray on his wig caught fire.
