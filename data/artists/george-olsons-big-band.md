# artist - George Olson's Big Band

## songs
**Beyond the Blue Horizon (1930)** : This 1930 recording by George Olson's Big Band features an ending with the sound of a train receding into the distance, simulating a fade-out effect. This early example predates electronic fade-outs and relied on the band physically moving away from the recording horn.
