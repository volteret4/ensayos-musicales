# artist - Allman Brothers Band

## labels

## curiosities
**Marshall Tucker Band Connection** : The manager of The Allman Brothers Band discovered The Marshall Tucker Band, who then signed with the same label, Capricorn, and opened for them on tour. Both bands also featured brothers among their members.
