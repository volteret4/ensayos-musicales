## curiosities
**Channeling Paranoia into Music** : Tricky emerged from his period of deep paranoia and existential dread as a "dark sorcerer," consciously deciding to channel all his hallucinations, anguish, and terror into his music, which directly informed his solo debut. "Tricky energió de esta etapa convertida en una especie de oscuro magosomuro que decidió canalizar todas sus alucinaciones, toda esta angustia y terror en su propia música." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Dislike for "Trip Hop" Label** : Along with Geoff Barrow and 3D, Tricky openly disliked being categorized under the "Trip Hop" label. "Jo Farrow, Tricky o 3D, detestaban la tiqueta TripHop." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Early Influence on Rap and Massive Attack** : Tricky was a major influence on rap music, particularly in terms of rhythms and scratching, and this influence was a prominent feature of Massive Attack's sound in its early stages. "uno de los grandes sonidos de masiva tacto sobre todo al principio era tricky que es una de las grandes influenciados sobre la música rap y los ritmos y el scratching." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack - Blue Lines. Un antes y un después en la música. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ik7PQ2X1o3M&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← tricky ← tricky
**Internal Conflict within Massive Attack (1998)** : During Massive Attack's *Mezzanine* recording, Tricky was obsessed with steering the band toward an aggressive, industrial, and radically dark sound, breaking away from their previous style, which led to significant internal disputes. "Tricky estaba obsesionado con llevar a la banda hacia un sonido agresivo, mindo estrial y radicalmente oscuro para romper con todo." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Member of The Wild Bunch and Massive Attack** : Tricky was a member of The Wild Bunch, the Bristol hip hop association, and was also specifically described as a "strange member" of Massive Attack. He was present during the pivotal recording sessions for Massive Attack's *Blue Lines*. "pero también tenemos a Tricky, que he dicho era un miembro extraño del Masyl Atac también." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack y El Origen del Trip Hop en Bristol. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bpOr4KKm5rM&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← tricky ← tricky
**Persistence in Trip Hop** : Despite the trip hop movement having "died out a bit" in the early 21st century, Tricky continues to operate effectively and solo within this genre, releasing new material. "A pesar de que el movimiento trip hop ha quedado ahí un poco muerto a primeros del siglo XXI, Tricky continúa un poco del solo en esta vertiente, que vaya una manera bastante buena y vuelve a ser un nuevo material de Tricky." ← Music Radar Clan > Lanzamientos de Septiembre 2017 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EXtTomCLjsg&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← tricky ← tricky
**Post-Blue Lines Lifestyle and Crisis (Early 1990s)** : After being hired by Massive Attack in 1991, Tricky spent the next two years predominantly outside the recording studio. He instead used his salary to frequent clubs, drink in pubs, and consume "industrial quantities of weed," which led to "infernal hangovers," a profound existential crisis, and paranoia, where he claimed to see ghosts in his living room. "Durante los siguientes dos años, Tricky prácticamente no piso en estudio de grabación. Gastaba su salario en clubs, bebiendo en pubs y consumiendo cantidades industriales de hierba." ← El género más OSCURO de los 90 (...que acabó en anuncios de coches) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gAEaRBEw9c
**Separation from Massive Attack** : Tricky, an original member of Massive Attack, had separated from the band prior to the recording of their third album, `Mezzanine`.
