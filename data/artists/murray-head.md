## songs
**Superstar (1969)** : Released in the fall of 1969 with the Trinidad Singers, this single served as a precursor to the *Jesus Christ Superstar* album and stage production. It entered the Billboard Hot 100 in January 1970, reaching approximately number 74, and appeared on and off the charts for several months.
