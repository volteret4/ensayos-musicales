# artist - Derek Deal

## curiosities
**Opus Number One Co-Creator (1989)** : Derek Deal, alongside Tim Carlton, recorded "Opus Number One" in 1989 for a system built by Cisco called Call Manager. This approximately six-minute track is widely recognized as the most famous on-hold music, even appearing in a Bud Light Super Bowl commercial in 2023. "It's probably the most famous on hold music ever created." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8GU8ZpSgY8
