**Attendance at Ramones' Final Show** : Lemmy of Motorhead was among the notable figures who came to pay their respects at the Ramones' final concert on August 6, 1996, in Hollywood. "All kinds of people came to pay their respects, including Lemmy of Motorhead." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
**Failed Petition for Heavy Metal Element** : A petition to name a heavy metal element after Lemmy of Motorhead failed. The primary reasons were that scientific rules for naming new elements do not allow for musicians as sources, only mythological concepts, minerals, places, countries, properties, or scientists. Additionally, the new "heavy" elements (113, 115, 117, 118) are actually classified as non-metals on the periodic table. "This petition was inspired by the idea that an actual heavy metal element be named after Lemmy and with atomic weights between 113 and 118, these new elements are the heaviest yet discovered. Perfect. Awesome. Sounds great. However, if we look at the periodic table, you'll see that these new elements occupy space on the right hand side. This is the area of the table reserved for non-metals." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMPLplhDk8I
**Marine Worm Species** : Lemmy Kilmister of Motorhead has a marine worm named after him, making him one of several musicians honored in this way. "Sepul Turah, the Brazilian heavy metal band, has won as does King Diamond, so does Lemmy from Motorhead." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMPLplhDk8I
**Rainbow Bar & Grill Tribute** : Lemmy Kilmister was a regular patron at The Rainbow Bar & Grill on Sunset Boulevard in Los Angeles. Following his death, the venue preserved his favorite chair at the end of the bar as a permanent tribute. Patrons can also order "a Lemmy," which is his preferred drink of Jack Daniel's and Coke. "Lemmy of Motorhead was a fixture at a venue called the Rainbow in Sunset Boulevard in Los Angeles. After he died, they preserved his favorite chair at the end of the bar as a tribute. You can also order a drink in the bar called a Lemmy, which is favorite drink, Jack Daniels and Coke." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
