# artist - The In-Breds

## curiosities
**Founder of The Enclave and Todd Sullivan's Mentor** : Tom Zutat founded The Enclave, a short-lived boutique record label with significant funding, and was the mentor to Todd Sullivan, who signed Sloan to Geffen. Zutat's ambition was to make Sloan a major hit to prove Geffen wrong, but The Enclave ceased operations within six months. "It was Tom Zutat, the guy who was the mentor of Todd Sullivan, who was signed to Geffen originally." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mbic-70ZhpE
**Jay Ferguson's Hiatus Project** : During Sloan's temporary hiatus after "Twice Removed," Jay Ferguson co-managed The In-Breds while also working at Murder Records, demonstrating his continued involvement in the music scene. "Jay spent some time working at Murder Records and co-managed the in-breds." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mbic-70ZhpE
