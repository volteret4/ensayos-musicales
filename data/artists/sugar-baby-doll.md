**No Surviving Official Recordings** : Despite word spreading about the kind of music Sugar Baby Doll was attempting to create, there appear to be no surviving official recordings of the band. **Portland Supergroup Attempt (circa 1985)** : Sugar Baby Doll was formed in Portland around 1985, featuring a lineup of strong personalities: former stripper Courtney Love, screaming guitarist Cat Bjelland from Minneapolis, and LA bassist Jennifer Finch. The band's inability to "hold it together" due to these personalities led to its quick breakup in late 1985, with members moving on to form other notable bands. "Sugar Baby Doll was formed down the coast in Portland in about 1985." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qss5kC2FUx0 ← sugar-baby-doll
