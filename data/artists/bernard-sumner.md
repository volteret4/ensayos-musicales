## curiosities
**Sequencing Bass Line Idea** : Bernard Sumner, the singer for New Order, originated the idea of sequencing a bass line from Peter Hook. This concept was pivotal in establishing the foundations of a new song played entirely by machines, influencing the band's future electronic direction.
