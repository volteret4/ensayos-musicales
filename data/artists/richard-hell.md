## curiosities
**Poverty and the Safety Pin** : Living as a struggling poet and musician in New York, Hell used safety pins to hold his falling-apart clothes together because he was extremely poor. This look was observed by Malcolm McLaren, who brought the "fashion statement" back to London and shared it with Vivienne Westwood. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**The Neon Boys and CBGB** : In 1972, Hell played in The Neon Boys, which evolved into the band Television. The band secured a residency at CBGB in 1973, which would become known as "ground zero" for the birth of punk rock. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
