# artist - Arca (as "Arque Fayer")

## albums
**First Two Works (Vinyl Re-edition)** : This is a new vinyl re-edition of Arca's initial two albums, referred to as "Arque Fayer" in the transcript. The previous vinyl edition was considered somewhat "weak" and had been subject to certain controversies. The new re-edition aims to address these issues.
