**At Least 11 Different Lead Singers Over the Band's History** : Black Sabbath's vocal history is extraordinarily convoluted: Ozzy Osbourne (1968–1977), Dave Walker (briefly 1977–78), Ronnie James Dio (1979–1982), Ian Gillan (1982–1984), Ron Keel and Dave DeNato (both 1984), Jeff Fenholt (1985), Ozzy briefly (1985), Glenn Hughes (1985–1986), Ray Gillan (1986–1987), Tony Martin (1987–1991), Ozzy returned (1992), Tony Martin again (1993–1997), Ozzy from 1997 through various reunion configurations until Eddie Van Halen— no, that's a different band. By conservative count, at least 11 different people have fronted Black Sabbath. "By my count, at least 11 different lead singers for one band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0F22NTnQzQ
