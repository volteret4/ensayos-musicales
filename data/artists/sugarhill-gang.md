# artist - Sugarhill Gang

## albums
**Rapper's Delight (1979)** : This album is often considered the first and most important modern rap song, though the speaker notes this is sometimes an overvaluation, as it was just one of several significant Sugar Hill Records releases from the same year. It is frequently cited as the first modern rap release largely because it was Sugar Hill Records' inaugural offering, but other contemporary releases could equally mark this historical milestone.
