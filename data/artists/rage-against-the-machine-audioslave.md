## curiosities
**Audioslave Formed After Rick Rubin Suggested Chris Cornell; Be Real of Cypress Hill Also Auditioned** : After Zack de la Rocha quit Rage Against the Machine in 2000, the remaining three members tried to continue under the RATM name with a new singer. Be Real of Cypress Hill auditioned. But after some jamming, they decided they didn't want a Zack sound-alike — they wanted something new. Producer Rick Rubin suggested they talk to ex-Soundgarden singer Chris Cornell. The chemistry was immediate. They began writing as "Civilian" or "the Civilian project" but had to rename when they discovered another group was using that name. They chose Audioslave. The group was essentially Rage with a different singer, and they were productive from 2001 through 2007. "The chemistry was immediate." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0F22NTnQzQ
