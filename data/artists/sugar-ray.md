## songs
**Fly (1997)** : This single became a massive hit in the summer of 1997, fueling Sugar Ray's rapid rise to fame. Its success occurred while the band was playing on the small Volcom stage during the Warped Tour, contributing to their album _Floored_ selling 70,000 copies a week.
