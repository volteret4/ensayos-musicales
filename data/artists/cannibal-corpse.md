**Mean Worm Fossil** : In 2017, an Ontario geologist named Derek Armstrong discovered the fossil of an ancient worm with giant snapping jaws, which he described as "one mean worm." He named the worm *Webster O'Brien Armstrong Eye*, with the first part derived from Alex Webster, the bass player for the death metal band Cannibal Corpse.
