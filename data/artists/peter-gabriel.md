**Security (1982)** : This fourth solo album by Peter Gabriel, released in 1982, was recorded digitally. Initially, when only vinyl was available, it was an AAD (Analog-Analog-Digital) recording due to analog mastering for vinyl. However, with the advent of CDs in 1984, it was re-released as a full DDD (Digital-Digital-Digital) recording, making it possibly the first album to be entirely digital from recording to mastering on CD. "I do know that Peter Gabriel recorded Security, his fourth solo album, digitally. When it came out in 1982, there was only vinyl, so at best it was an AAD recording." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
**So (1986) – Album Featuring Sampled Drums** : Peter Gabriel extensively utilized sampling on his album "So."
