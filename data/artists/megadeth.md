**Formation as Revenge** : Following his firing from Metallica on April 11, 1983, Dave Mustaine, during a four-day Greyhound bus ride back to California, decided to form his own band as an act of revenge. He began writing lyrics on a political handbill from Senator Alan Cranston, which warned about stockpiling nuclear weapons during the Cold War. "He decided that the best revenge would be to form his own band. So he started writing out some lyrics on a piece of scrap paper, which was actually a political hand bill from Senator Alan Cranston." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUr9sjWgUcw

**Self-Reflection on Firing** : Dave Mustaine eventually realized that his firing from Metallica was largely justified, acknowledging his past behavior as "physically reckless," unpredictable, and embarrassing to others due to excessive drinking. "I had been physically reckless, and there had been a lot of pushing and shoving on my part with everybody in the band. I was very unpredictable.
