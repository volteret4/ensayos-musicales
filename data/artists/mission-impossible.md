## curiosities
**Evolution from Freak Baby (1985)** : The band formed around Dave Grohl's 16th birthday in January 1985, evolving from Freak Baby. This marked Dave Grohl's transition to playing the drums. "Then Freak Baby morphed into Mission Impossible, which happened around Dave's 16th birthday in January of 1985. By this time, David moved to Drums" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA
**Short-lived Tenure** : The band lasted only a few months, disbanding when two of its members graduated from high school and left for college. "So within a few months, when two members graduated from high school and moved off to college, Mission Impossible came to an end" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA
