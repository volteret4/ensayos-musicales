# artist - Ron Grainer

## songs
**Doctor Who Theme (1963)** : Ron Grainer provided some basic, simple melodies for the "Doctor Who" theme, which Delia Derbyshire then extensively transformed into the final 100% electronic composition. Initially, Grainer was the sole person credited for the piece, despite not recognizing Delia's final version and only having contributed rudimentary elements. Today, the credit is shared equally between them. ## curiosities
**Original Contributor, Later Credit Dispute** : Ron Grainer was a colleague of Delia Derbyshire at the BBC Radiophonic Workshop. He supplied rudimentary melodies for the "Doctor Who" theme. However, his contribution was so basic that he reportedly did not even recognize the final, fully electronic version after Delia Derbyshire's extensive compositional and production work. Despite this, he was initially the only one credited for the iconic theme.
