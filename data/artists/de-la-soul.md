**Lawsuit by The Turtles (1989)** : In 1989, De La Soul faced a $1.7 million lawsuit from The Turtles for "stealing" a portion of their 1969 song "You Showed Me" for the track "Transmitting Why From Mars" on the album *3 Feet High and Rising*. The lawsuit was settled out of court, establishing a precedent against unauthorized sampling. "That resulted in a $1.7 million lawsuit against Dela Sol and their record company. Settled off court, but the message was clear. Do not sample without permission." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ak6OuOUkRrQ ← de-la-soul ← de-la-soul
**Streaming Legalities Sorted (Until 2023)** : De La Soul's early music was so "sample-heavy" that it took until 2023 for all the legalities surrounding the samples to be completely sorted out. This extensive legal process prevented their songs from being made available on streaming music services for an extended period. "Dela Sol's early music was so sample-heavy that it took until 2023 for all the legalities to be sorted out and their songs could finally be made available on streaming music services." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ak6OuOUkRrQ ← de-la-soul ← de-la-soul
