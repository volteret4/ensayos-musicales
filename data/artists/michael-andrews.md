# artist - Michael Andrews

## curiosities
**Donnie Darko Soundtrack Production (2001)** : In 2001, San Diego musician Michael Andrews was contacted by director Richard Kelly to work on the movie *Donnie Darko*. Andrews, who loved "Mad World" as a kid, decided it would be perfect for the soundtrack but needed to adapt it. He stripped the song down to basics—piano, a mellotron, and a few other flourishes—partly for artistic reasons and partly due to budgetary constraints, as he couldn't afford many additional musicians. He also slowed the tempo by approximately 50%. "Andrew thought that Mad World would be perfect for the soundtrack." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
