## curiosities
**Vintage Car Collector Without a Driver's License** : Charlie Watts was strictly a vintage car collector. His collection included a 1937 Lagonda Rapide with a V12 engine (one of only 25 made by Aston Martin) and a Citroën de Chirvaux. Despite owning these vehicles, Charlie Watts never obtained a driver's license and therefore did not drive any of them. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc85sLj0Cnk
