**Transition from Drummer to Singer – Founding The Ramones** : In early 1974, Jeff Hyman was playing drums and singing in a band called Sniper. He then connected with John Cummings (guitar) and Douglas Colvin (bass), forming a new group that played its first gig on March 30, 1974. However, Hyman's drumming was too loud and fast for the band, and he proved to be a better singer than his bandmates. He moved to frontman, adopting the name Joey Ramone, while a friend named Tommy took over on a $100 drum set. "Jeff moved down front and became the singer and a friend named Tommy was forced to keep time using a hundred dollar drum set." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
