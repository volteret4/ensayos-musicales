# artist - Derek T. Tuggle

## curiosities
**"Lonely Boy" Music Video Star** : Derek T. Tuggle, originally a security guard, achieved unexpected fame as the star of The Black Keys' "Lonely Boy" music video. After an initial video shoot with many extras proved ineffective, Dan Auerbach and Patrick Carney noticed Tuggle's distinctive dancing in preview edits and decided to feature him exclusively in a single-shot video, which quickly amassed nearly half a million YouTube views.
