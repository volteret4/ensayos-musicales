## curiosities
**Brian May as Primary Influence** : Adam Slack considers Brian May of Queen his greatest influence, even commissioning a custom KZ Guitar Works instrument that was directly inspired by one of Brian May's guitars. "If you had to ask Adam about his greatest influence, he would probably tell you it was Brian May of Queen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVIswnZYFkU
**Early Musical Influences** : Adam Slack's musical upbringing included The Rolling Stones (especially Keith Richards), 1970s Glam Rock artists like David Bowie and T. Rex, and the band Queen. "He grew up listening to the Rolling Stones, he loves Keith Richards, 70s Glam Rock from Bowie and T. Rex, and Queen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVIswnZYFkU
**New Young Band Inspired by Classic Rock** : The Struts, a British band, were highlighted as a new young group "brought up on Queen, Aerosmith Oasis, the killers and the Rolling Stones." This demonstrates a trend of contemporary bands drawing direct inspiration from old school classic rock to create their sound, fitting into the Alternative Hard Rock space. "And we should also pay attention to the struts. A British band brought up on Queen, Aerosmith Oasis, the killers and the Rolling Stones." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HSvgRyGiOpE
**Reliable Les Paul from Mike McCready** : He owns a full-size Les Paul that he purchased from Mike McCready of Pearl Jam, which he regards as his best instrument due to its consistent ability to stay in tune. "He actually calls this the best instrument he's ever owned because it never seems to go out of tune." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVIswnZYFkU
