# artist - Wolfman Jack

## curiosities
**Encouragement of Disorder** : At the Ozark Music Festival, Wolfman Jack encouraged much of the misbehavior, including the display of naked breasts, stating he wanted to see "a lot of love." "He encouraged a lot of it including the display of naked boobs because he said he wanted to see a lot of love." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 46 - The Chaos and Music of the Ozark Music Festival of 1974 | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Misintroduction of Bloodstone** : Wolfman Jack did Bloodstone "no favors" by announcing them as a "band from London" at the Ozark Music Festival, leading the audience to expect a white rock band (like The Rolling Stones) instead of the African American R&B band from Kansas City that they actually were. "This man Jack did not do Bloodstone any favors when he announced them as a band from London, which got everybody thinking that they were going to be like the Rolling Stones or something a white rock band." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 46 - The Chaos and Music of the Ozark Music Festival of 1974 | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Ozark Festival MC** : Wolfman Jack served as the master of ceremonies for the Ozark Music Festival in 1974. "If they had any hope at all that the MC the legendary wolf man Jack would help keep things under control that was a mistake." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 46 - The Chaos and Music of the Ozark Music Festival of 1974 | https://www.ftr70.com/
