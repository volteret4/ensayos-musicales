**Radio Icon and Style** : Wolfman Jack became a radio icon in the 1960s, renowned for his upbeat and energetic style, often talking over the music on his legendary radio shows. His unique vocal delivery was a signature part of his persona. "Wolfman was a DJ and he is a legend.
