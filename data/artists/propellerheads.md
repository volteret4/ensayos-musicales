## curiosities
**First iPod Commercial Song** : Propellerheads, a British outfit, hold the distinction of being the very first band to have one of their songs featured in an iPod commercial. Their track "Take California" was used in the inaugural iPod commercial in the fall of 2001, preceding the famous silhouette-style campaigns. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfie4YYncxk
**Major Alternative Dance Act** : Propellerheads were identified as one of the "bigger acts" in Alternative Dance, having secured big record deals and receiving worldwide promotion for their music.
