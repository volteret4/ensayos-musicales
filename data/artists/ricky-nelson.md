**Early TV/Pop Star Hybrid** : Ricky Nelson in the 1950s is cited as an early example of an artist who successfully combined a TV star image with a pop music star image, a model later followed by artists like David Cassidy and Shaun Cassidy. "In the tradition of Ricky Nelson in the 1950s and Sean Cassidy's brother David Cassidy Sean Cassidy combined this TV star with pop music star image." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 30 - The Sweet Sound of 70s Bubblegum Music | https://seventies.libsyn.com/030-bubblegum
**Image Reinvention Attempt** : Ricky Nelson, even before the 1970s, attempted to change his image from a teenage idol to appeal to a broader audience. As part of this effort, he changed his name from Ricky to Rick Nelson, a difficult transition for many teenage idols.
