# artist - Queensrÿche

## curiosities
**Adoption of the "Rock Dot" Umlaut** : Queensrÿche is listed as a band that adopted the umlaut (or "rock dot") in their name for aesthetic reasons, contributing to the visual style often associated with metal bands. "There's Motley Crew, Queen's Reich, Spinal Tap, Green Jelly, Motorhead, Maximal Park, Moxie Frouze, and tons of others." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yPok3MemDmE
**Uncool 1980s Concept Albums** : Queensrÿche was mentioned as one of the bands doing concept albums in the 1980s, a period when the format was considered "pretty uncool" in alternative music circles. "I mean, when you have bands like Sticks and Queenswright doing them, that's pretty uncool." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dm5N6yDRxXQ
