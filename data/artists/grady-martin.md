## songs
**The Fuzz** : After the success of "Don't Worry" and the distinctive fuzzy guitar sound it featured, Grady Martin capitalized on the malfunction that created it by recording his own song titled "The Fuzz." This brought him to the attention of Gibson guitars, who sought to replicate his sound on demand.
