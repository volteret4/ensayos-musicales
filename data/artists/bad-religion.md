## curiosities
**"Selling Out" Accusations** : Sparked "blasphemy" and "sacrilege" accusations from purists for leaving their indie imprint, Epitaph, for a major label. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Early Booking (Early 1980s)** : Kevin Lyman booked Bad Religion at the California State Polytechnic University in Pomona when they were just starting out. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Epitaph's Initial Focus** : Epitaph was initially solely a vehicle for Bad Religion's records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Featured on Early Warped Tour** : Was one of the dozens of acts featured in the first few years of the Vans Warped Tour. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Greg Graffin's Academic Writings Availability** : Links to Greg Graffin's academic writings are available on the Bad Religion website. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
**Greg Graffin's Doctoral Thesis on Evolutionary Biology** : His doctoral thesis was titled "Monism, Atheism, and the Naturalist Worldview, Perspectives from Evolutionary Biology," which linked evolutionary biology with history and the philosophy of science. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
**Greg Graffin's Harvard Secular Society Award** : In April 2008, Greg Graffin received an Outstanding Lifetime Achievement Award in Cultural Humanism from the Harvard Secular Society. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
**Greg Graffin's Teaching Career at UCLA** : When not working with Bad Religion, Greg Graffin teaches life sciences at UCLA. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
**Greg Graffin's UCLA & Cornell Education** : Greg Graffin double-majored in anthropology and geology at UCLA, earned a master's degree in geology from UCLA, and later a PhD in Zoology from Cornell University. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
**Independent Career on Epitaph Records** : Had been working on their own independent Epitaph Records before being convinced to sign a major record deal. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Label Formation** : Established their own label, Epitaph, because no existing record labels were willing to release their music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Melodic Hardcore Approach** : Maintained impeccable hardcore credentials, lots of politics, social awareness, and rage, but simultaneously incorporated melodic vocals and harmonies for a smooth edge. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
