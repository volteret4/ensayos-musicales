**Birthplace and Early Life (1980)** : Win Butler, whose real name is Edwin, is the singer for Arcade Fire. He was born in Truckee, California, a remote location in the northeast corner of the state on the border with Nevada, before his family relocated to The Woodlands, a planned community north of Houston, Texas. **Canadian Outsider Perspective - Neon Bible Themes** : After living in Canada for several years, Win Butler began to view the United States from an outsider's perspective, a feeling he says influenced the themes of "dissonance and the occasional feeling of dread" that permeated Arcade Fire's *Neon Bible* album. "When Butler felt a little weirded out after having lived in Canada for a few years. He began to look at the US from an outsider's perspective, something that he says resulted in the record having themes of dissonance and the occasional feeling of dread." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Family of Musicians - Grandfather Elvino Ray** : Win Butler comes from a family with a deep musical heritage. His grandfather, Elvino Ray, was a jazz steel guitarist and band leader who worked as a musician for over 70 years, significantly contributing to the invention of the modern electric guitar pickup while employed by Gibson Guitar Corporation. "A guy named Elvino Ray was one of his grandfather's. He was a jazz steel guitarist and band leader who worked as a musician for more than 70 years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q_c-m_NDznw
**Family of Musicians - Grandmother King Sisters** : Win Butler's grandmother, Elvino Ray's wife, was a member of The King Sisters, a prominent big band singing group.
