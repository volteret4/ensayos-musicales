# artist - Verbal Assault

## curiosities
**Acceptance of "Emo Core" Term** : Verbal Assault was one of the few groups that willingly embraced and identified with the term "emo core," which was otherwise almost universally disliked by practitioners of this music in the mid-1980s. "Except a group called Verbal Assault, who were only too happy to call themselves emo core." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
