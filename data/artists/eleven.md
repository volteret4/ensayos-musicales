## curiosities
**Jack Irons' Drumming Role – Early Member** : Jack Irons, who later joined the Red Hot Chili Peppers and worked with Pearl Jam, was a drummer for Eleven at one point. His connections to the Seattle music scene further highlight the interconnectedness of many of the era's bands. "There Elaine and a friend named Natasha Schneider formed a group called Eleven, which at one point had a drummer named Jack Irons." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
**Producers for Chris Cornell's Solo Album – Launchpad for Other Gigs** : The group Eleven, comprising Elaine Johannes and Natasha Schneider, played a crucial role in producing Chris Cornell's debut solo record, *Euphoria Morning*. Their work on this album garnered them further producing gigs for notable artists like No Doubt and Josh Homme's Desert Session projects, connecting them to members of A Perfect Circle, Nine Inch Nails, Danzig, Marilyn Manson's band, and PJ Harvey. "Chris reliant on both of them to help produce his debut solo record Euphoria Morning... who, based on this work, got producing gigs for no doubt in Josh Hommie in the early days of his desert session projects." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
