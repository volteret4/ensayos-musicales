## curiosities
**Artist in 2010s Psych Resurgence** : Ariel Pink was mentioned as one of the artists contributing to the resurgence of Psychedelic or Psych Rock in the 2010s. This genre, which was born in the 1960s and fell out of favor in the 70s, saw a significant comeback during the decade.
