## curiosities
**Blackie O'Nassis Death (June 13, 2023) – Drummer on Pulp Fiction's "Girl, You'll Be a Woman Soon"; Age 57, Cause Unknown** : John Rowan, who played under the name Blackie O'Nassis, was the drummer for Urge Overkill — the Chicago band whose cover of Neil Diamond's "Girl, You'll Be a Woman Soon" (1967) appears in the famous dance scene in *Pulp Fiction*. The band was also a cult favorite in the alt-rock community and opened for Nirvana on the *Nevermind* tour, during which they befriended Kurt Cobain, Chrissie Hynde of the Pretenders, and Liz Phair. Blackie left the band in 1996; there were reports of heroin use and at least one arrest. The cause of his death on June 13, 2023, at age 57 was never disclosed.
