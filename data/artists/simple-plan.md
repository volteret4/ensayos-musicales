# artist - Simple Plan

## genres
- Pop Punk (criticized)

## curiosities
**"Too Light" Criticism** : Considered "a little too light" and "too poppy for punk" in the mid-2000s, leading some alt-rock radio stations to refuse to play them.
**Audience Rejection** : Alt-rock audiences were not receptive, generating immediate complaints like "why are you playing that kiddie punk?" when their songs were aired.
