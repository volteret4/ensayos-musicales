**Montreal Job and Commuting** : During a difficult period for the band, Ian took a job in Montreal. He would commute back to Mississauga every other week to attempt to hold rehearsals, which often dwindled to just himself and Ben, with Aaron occasionally joining. "And I took a job in Montreal. ...
