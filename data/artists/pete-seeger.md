# artist - Pete Seeger

## genres

## curiosities
**Labeled as Communist (1930s-1940s)** : Pete Seeger, along with Woody Guthrie, used folk music in the 1930s and 1940s to spread messages that resulted in both of them being labeled as communists. His membership in the American Communist Party later caused him significant difficulties.
