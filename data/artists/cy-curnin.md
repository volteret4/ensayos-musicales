## curiosities
**Considered for "Don't You (Forget About Me)"** : Cy Curnin of The Fixx was considered as a potential artist to record "Don't You (Forget About Me)" for *The Breakfast Club* soundtrack. However, the idea "didn't go anywhere," and the song was eventually recorded by Simple Minds, becoming their biggest hit.
