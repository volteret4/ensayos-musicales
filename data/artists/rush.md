# artist - Rush

## members
- Alex Lifeson (guitar)
- Geddy Lee (vocals)
- Neil Peart (drums)

## albums
**2112 (1976)** : A concept album inspired by Ayn Rand, the objectiveist philosopher who wrote books like Atlas Shrugged. The album tells a complex narrative involving the Red Star, the Solar Federation, priests who run the temples of Syrikes, and their command over existence, featuring a rebel guitar player who rises up and commits suicide.

## curiosities
**Dave Grohl's Guitar Influence** : Dave Grohl admits to "ripping off" Alex Lifeson's guitar playing, specifically sustained notes and riff patterns, in many Foo Fighters songs like "Winnebago" and "Times Like These."
**Rock and Roll Hall of Fame Induction** : The Foo Fighters inducted Rush into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
**Taylor Hawkins' Drumming Idol** : Neil Peart was Taylor Hawkins' drumming idol ("my guy on the wall"), and Taylor still "rips off" Peart's drum fills for Foo Fighters songs.
