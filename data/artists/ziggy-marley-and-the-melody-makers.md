# artist - Ziggy Marley and the Melody Makers

## members
- David "Ziggy" Marley
- Rita Marley (mother of some members)
- Sadella Marley
- Sharon Marley
- Steven Marley

## genres
- Reggae

## songs
**Tomorrow People (1988)** : A hit song that performed well on alternative radio.

## curiosities
**Formed in 1979** : The group was formed by siblings David (Ziggy), Steven, Sadella, and Sharon Marley.
**Sadella Marley's Career** : Sadella works in fashion, having designed uniforms for the Jamaican Olympic team in 2012 and worked for Puma.
**Sharon Marley's Role** : Sharon manages the Bob Marley Museum in Kingston.
**Steven Marley's Achievements** : Steven is Bob and Rita Marley's second son, a singer and producer with eight Grammy Awards.
**Ziggy Marley's Achievements** : Ziggy is Bob's eldest son, has won five Grammy Awards, wrote the theme song for the children's TV show "Arthur," and published a comic book called "Marijuana Man."
