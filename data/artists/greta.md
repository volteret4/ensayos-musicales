## curiosities
**Record Label Collapse** : Greta's promising trajectory was halted by significant turmoil at their record label. Both the presidents of the label and the band's A&R representative, who had been their main champion, were fired. This series of events led to the complete collapse of the band's enterprise. "then the presidents of their record label got fired.
