## curiosities
**Record Label Collapse** : Greta's promising trajectory was halted by significant turmoil at their record label. Both the presidents of the label and the band's A&R representative, who had been their main champion, were fired. This series of events led to the complete collapse of the band's enterprise. "then the presidents of their record label got fired. Then their A&R guy, the guy who would champion them to the label, also got fired and the whole enterprise collapsed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xo5DRSW95QQ
