**Impact of Drug Addiction on Creativity** : Following the success of "Different Class," Jarvis Cocker developed a severe cocaine addiction in 1997. This addiction, along with other internal band issues, led to a creative paralysis where he struggled to write new material, managing to produce only the song "Help the Aged" over several months. This period significantly impacted Pulp's ability to record their next album.
