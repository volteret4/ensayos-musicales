## songs
**Mr. Bojangles (1968)** : Walker wrote this song in 1968, which was later covered by the Nitty Gritty Dirt Band. He met the inspiration for "Bojangles" in a New Orleans jail after being arrested for public disturbance while drunk; the actual man's name was not Bojangles, but the moniker derived from the African American dancer Bill Bojangles Robinson. ## curiosities
**Buffett's Key West Introduction** : Jerry Jeff Walker is credited with playing a pivotal role in Jimmy Buffett's career by introducing him to Key West and, by extension, the "Caribbean way of thought." This introduction significantly shaped Buffett's iconic musical style and laid-back image. "Much like Jerry Jeff Walker, who first introduced Buffett to Key West And the Caribbean way of thought." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 44 - Streaking, Werewolves, Sharks, & Drinking - 70s Novelty Songs | https://traffic.libsyn.com/seventies/334334714-44100-2-e5ed6adac25ea.m4a
**Country Rock Originator** : Jerry Jeff Walker is recognized as one of the originators of the country rock sound, which was heavily influenced by folk music and evolved into the Outlaw Country or Progressive Country movement of the 1970s.
