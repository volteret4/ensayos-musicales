**Intervention in 5440 Stage Branding Dispute (c. 1997)** : Raine Maida, the singer for Our Lady Peace, intervened to help 5440 resolve a dispute with promoters at a festival in Manitoba around 1997. 5440 refused to play due to a prominent Labatt's balloon on stage, violating their contract terms. Maida explained 5440's contractual clauses to the promoters, leading to the balloon's deflation and allowing 5440 to perform. "he hustles off with the promoters and other members of the band and explained to all these guys have this thing in their contract and you know, and he should take it down.
