## curiosities
**Career Transition to Chiropractic** : After numerous gigs with various bands, Terry Chimes retired from music by 1994 to pursue his passion for biology, medicine, and healing. He opened a chiropractic clinic in Essex, which later expanded into a chain of clinics, and he began giving lectures on alternative medicine. "by 1994, he'd retired from music and opened a chiropractic clinic in Essex." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Multiple Stints with The Clash** : Terry Chimes was initially the drummer for The Clash in 1976 and for a period in 1977, appearing on their debut album. He was replaced by Topper Headon in mid-1977 but returned to replace Topper in 1982 and 1983 before leaving for a third time, again replaced by Topper. "He was in the clash a couple of times in 1976 and for a period in 1977, which was long enough for him to appear on that first record." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
**Spiritual Awakening and Catholicism** : Around the turn of the millennium, Dr. Chimes experienced a spiritual awakening that led to a strong devotion to Catholicism. He subsequently began working with the Catholic Church, a significant shift for someone who came up through gritty punk bands and played with Black Sabbath. "Dr. Chimes experienced a spiritual awakening around the turn of the millennium, prompting a strong devotion to Catholicism and working with the Catholic Church." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jMfH9xAOpI
