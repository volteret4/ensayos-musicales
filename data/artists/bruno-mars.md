**Uptown Funk (Year Unspecified)** : This song, co-written with Mark Ronson, became the target of multiple plagiarism lawsuits following the "Blurred Lines" precedent. Various groups, including The Gap Band, The Sequence, Kalash, Zap, and Serbian singer Victoria, accused them of infringement. As a result, the songwriting credits and royalties for "Uptown Funk" are now split among Mark Ronson, Bruno Mars, and ten other individuals who had no direct involvement in the specific recording.
