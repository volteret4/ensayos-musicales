## curiosities
**Instrumental Hits and Opening for The Beatles (1960s)** : Formed by Bill Black after his permanent departure from Elvis's band, Bill Black's Combo achieved a series of instrumental hits over several years, including eight Top 40 singles. Their popularity earned them the prestigious role of opening act for The Beatles' first US tour. **Opening Act for The Beatles (1964)** : Bill Black's Combo served as an opening act for The Beatles' concert at Maple Leaf Gardens in Toronto on September 6, 1964. However, like the other supporting acts, they were met with boos from the impatient crowd, who were exclusively eager to see The Beatles perform.
