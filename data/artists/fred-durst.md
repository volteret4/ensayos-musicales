## curiosities
**Association with Nu Metal** : Fred Durst was presented as a figure associated with Nu Metal, a genre that was dominant in North American new rock for a period after 1995. **Leaked Sex Tape and Lawsuit (2005)** : In 2005, a three-minute video of Fred Durst and his girlfriend was leaked online. Durst responded by suing 10 websites, seeking $70 million in damages. "2005, Fred Durst of Lempisket. Now, three minutes of him and his girlfriend was somehow leaked online." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DA2iJsu3GRY
**Warner Brothers' Rap Suggestion** : Warner Brothers Records suggested that Mike Shinoda of Linkin Park should learn to rap in a style similar to Fred Durst. This was part of the label's efforts to influence Linkin Park's sound. "Mike should learn to wrap like Fred Durst." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM
