## curiosities
**Political Activism and Censorship (1967-1969)** : Initially appearing as innocuous "young white young men" in matching blazers, the Smothers Brothers quickly became television renegades on their CBS show. They grew increasingly political and critical of the Johnson administration and featured rock and roll bands to connect with a younger audience. Their material, especially its anti-Vietnam War stance, led to frequent censorship of segments and even entire episodes, ultimately resulting in the show's abrupt cancellation in April 1969 after two years. **Smothers Brothers Summer Show (1968)** : In 1968, Tommy and Dick Smothers produced a summer show during the traditional network TV break. This program was hosted by Glenn Campbell and performed well enough to lead to Campbell's own variety series as a replacement for the canceled Smothers Brothers Show.
