# artist - John Bannister

## curiosities
**First Public Gig for Admission (1672)** : John Bannister, an English violinist, performed what is recorded as the first public gig to charge admission for a musician. This event took place in London in 1672, marking a significant moment in the history of concerts as we know them today.
