# artist - Vasuka Jo

## members

## curiosities
**Headliner at Sex Pistols' First Gig (November 6, 1975)** : Vasuka Jo, featuring singer Adam Ant, was the headlining act at St. Martin's art school in London on November 6, 1975, when the Sex Pistols played their notoriously short first live gig as an opening act. "They were all there to see the headliners, group called Vasuka Jo that featured a singer by the name of Adam Ant." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ua93Ui1u6UI
