# artist - The Verve

## curiosities
**Brian Cannon Design Work** : Brian Cannon, the art director who later designed Oasis's debut album "Definitely Maybe," had previously created artwork for The Verve. His designs for The Verve impressed Noel Gallagher, leading to their subsequent collaboration. "Noal liked what Brian had done with the verb around that time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tbKuJR0cPs
**Influence on Sigur Rós** : The Verve is cited as an important influence for Sigur Rós, alongside dream pop acts like the Cocteau Twins and Radiohead's later work. "Dreampop, the Cocktoe twins are very important here, along with the Verve and anything Radiohead did with OK Computer and Beyond." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
