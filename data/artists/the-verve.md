## curiosities
**Hiatus (September 1995 – 1996)** : A period where the band "ceased to exist" before their major breakup, later described as a hiatus rather than a formal breakup. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Influenced by My Bloody Valentine** : Counted among the bands significantly influenced by My Bloody Valentine's innovative use of guitar noise and walls of sound. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Internal Conflict in Dusseldorf (June 7, 1998)** : Singer Richard Ashcroft and guitarist Nick McCabe had a physical altercation after a show in Dusseldorf, Germany, resulting in Ashcroft's face being "pounded" and McCabe's broken hand. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Last Show Before Breakup (August 29, 1998)** : The band played their final show together in Ireland on August 29, 1998, though the official breakup announcement came later. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Lollapalooza Participation (1992-1995)** : The Verve was among the major participants during Lollapalooza's "glory years" between 1992 and 1995. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Nick McCabe's Initial Departure (June 7, 1998)** : Following the fight in Dusseldorf, Nick McCabe decided he was done with the band and was replaced by session guitarist BJ Cole to complete remaining commitments. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Official Breakup Announcement (April 28, 1999)** : The band made the official announcement of their breakup on April 28, 1999. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Reunion Announcement (June 26, 2007)** : After eight years, one month, and 30 days apart, the band announced their reunion. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Second Breakup (August 10, 2008)** : The band broke up again, allegedly because Nick McCabe and Simon Jones believed Richard Ashcroft was using the reunion to further his solo career, leading to a "big fight." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Serial Breaker-Upper Status** : Following their second breakup, the band was declared a "serial breaker, upper" and banned from future reunion programs by the podcast host. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Simon Tongue Excluded from Reunion** : Guitarist Simon Tongue was available but was deliberately kept out of the reunion. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
