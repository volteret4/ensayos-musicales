## curiosities
**Peele Session with David Bowie (May 23rd, 1972)** : The Spiders from Mars, as David Bowie's band, participated in a "Peele Session" on May 23rd, 1972. These sessions, overseen by DJ John Peel at the BBC, were a significant platform for artists to perform and record live, especially during periods of "Needle Time" restrictions. "Here's something from May 23rd, 1972, when an artist on the asset named David Bowie dropped in for a session with his band, The Spiders from Mars." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ak6OuOUkRrQ
**Top of the Pops Performance (July 6, 1972)** : The Spiders from Mars performed with David Bowie on Top of the Pops on July 6, 1972. Guitarist Mick Ronson was dressed in a gold one-piece, and shared a moment of public affection with Bowie (Bowie draped his arm around Ronson's shoulders) that was highly shocking for the era. "Onstage with him was his guitarist, Mick Ronson, dressed in a gold one piece." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
