# artist - Anthony Newley

## songs
**Goldfinger (1964) – Co-writer** : Anthony Newley, a British song and danceman greatly admired by a young David Bowie, co-wrote the theme song for the 1964 James Bond film "Goldfinger." He collaborated with John Barry on the track, which became a top 40 hit despite fierce opposition from one of the film's producers, Harry Saltzman. "John Barry had a hand in this as did Anthony Newley, a British song and danceman who was admired greatly by a young David Bowie." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K0pgJ7XAlpo
