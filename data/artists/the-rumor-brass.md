# artist - The Rumor Brass

## curiosities
**Horn Section for The Clash and Katrina and The Waves** : The Rumor Brass is a horn section that provided musical contributions to The Clash's song "Revolution Rock" on their "London Calling" album. They are also credited as the musicians playing the horns on Katrina and The Waves' popular song "Walking on Sunshine." "Those musicians playing the horn were known as the Rumor Brass. And they're also the people you hear on Katrina and the waves walking on sunshine." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoBx6s3JmVQ
