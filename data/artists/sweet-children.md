## curiosities
**Drummer John Cliffmeyer's Nickname** : The drummer, John Cliffmeyer, preferred to be known as "El Sobrante," a name derived from his hometown. His later decision to drop this nickname sometimes led to the misconception that Green Day had changed drummers when they changed their band name. "But he wanted everyone to call him El Sorbrante. Because that's where he came from, a town called El Sorbrante." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nyfdGYWelic ← sweet-children
**First "Real Band" for Billy Joe and Mike** : Sweet Children was the initial "real band" formed by Billy Joe Armstrong and Mike Dirnt before they reformed as Green Day. "Their first real band was called Sweet Children. It was Billy Joe on guitar, Mike on bass, and a guy named John Cliffmeyer on drums." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nyfdGYWelic ← sweet-children
