**Origins in Vancouver** : This band was formed in Vancouver in 1983, bringing their unique perspective to Celtic music. "Enter to the West was formed in Vancouver in 1983 and put their own spin on things." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Rise in 1991** : Spirit of the West began their rise in 1991, becoming part of a new crop of Canadian artists gaining prominence. Their emergence signaled the expansion of the "Can Rock explosion" beyond its initial wave. "1991, so the beginning of the rise of the Barodicad Ladies, Spirit of the West, Sarah McLaughlin, Crash Vegas, Crash Test Dummies, and Grapes of Wrath." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
**The World's Greatest Drinking Song** : One of Spirit of the West's most popular songs within the Celtic diaspora was written during a 1989 tour of England. It originated as a humorous piece about the band's misbehavior in pubs on that tour and was never intended to be recorded. However, an album producer heard it and insisted the group complete it, despite it never being officially released as a single. It has since become one of Canada's, and the world's, greatest drinking songs. "It was never supposed to be recorded, but their producer for one of their albums heard it and demanded that the group finish it. Nevertheless, it was never, ever officially released as a single. Yet somehow, this has become one of Canada's. One of the worlds greatest drinking songs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
