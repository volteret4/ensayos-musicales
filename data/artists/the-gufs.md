## curiosities
**Breakup Due to Alt Rock Decline** : The band broke up in 2000, identified as "victims of a low in interest in 90s style alt rock." This suggests their sound fell out of fashion, contributing to their dissolution. "they were victims of a low in interest in 90s style alt rock, and they broke up in 2000." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22SSwvfRMVg
