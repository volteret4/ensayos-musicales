# concert - Olympics in Barcelona

## curiosities
**Background Context During Recording of Fully Completely** : During the summer of 1992, while The Tragically Hip were recording *Fully Completely* at Battery Studios in London, band members, including Rob Baker, would relax by watching the Barcelona Olympics in the evenings. The news and discussions surrounding Canadian athlete Ben Johnson's scandal from four years prior were still prevalent during these broadcasts. "And they were still talking about Canada and what happened with Ben Johnson four years earlier." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
