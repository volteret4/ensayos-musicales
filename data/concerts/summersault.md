# concert - Summersault

## curiosities
**Artist-Founded Festival** : Summersault was started by Our Lady Peace in 1998, as a traveling festival. It was part of a trend in the late 1990s where artists capitalized on the financial prosperity of the music industry and the proven concept of traveling alt-rock tours. "Our Lady P started Summersault in 1998." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Defunct Status** : Summersault is currently defunct. "Edgefest, little fair, summer salt, and another roadside attraction are all defunct." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Lollapalooza Imitator** : Summersault was a touring music festival that followed in the footsteps of Lollapalooza, emerging in the 1990s. "This spawned imitators, edgefest, little fair, summer salt, another roadside attraction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
