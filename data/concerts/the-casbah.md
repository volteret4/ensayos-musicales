# concert - The Casbah

## curiosities
**Ecstasy-Spiked Smoothie Incident** : After a USS show at The Casbah in Hamilton, Ash, who was heavily engaged in meditation at the time, was prompted by front-row audience members to finish his on-stage smoothie. Upon consuming it, he experienced what he initially interpreted as a profound spiritual enlightenment, or "satori," marked by waves of joy and peace. It was only later, in the band van, that he realized his smoothie had been secretly spiked with ecstasy, leading to a memorable experience of unknowingly being under the influence of drugs. "When all of a sudden there's a term in Eastern meditation language called satore which is like a flash of enlightenment...And all of a sudden I realized in the van that someone had spiked my smoothie with ecstasy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
