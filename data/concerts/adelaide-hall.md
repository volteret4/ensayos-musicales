# concert - Adelaide Hall

## curiosities
**Scott Weiland's Last Gig (December 1, 2015)** : Adelaide Hall, a club in Toronto, was the venue for Scott Weiland's final performance with The Wildabouts on December 1, 2015, just two days before his death. "He did a couple of interviews before playing a club called Adelaide Hall. It would be his last gig." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
