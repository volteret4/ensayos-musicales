# concert - Phantom of the Paradise festivals

## curiosities
**Winnipeg Cultural Phenomenon** : Annual "Phantom of the Paradise" festivals are held in Winnipeg, Canada, where the film and its soundtrack became an essential part of the city's musical DNA despite being a box office flop elsewhere.
