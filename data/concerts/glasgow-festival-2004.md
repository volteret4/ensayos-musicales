## curiosities
**Jandec's First Live Performance** : In 2004, the highly reclusive artist Jandec made his first-ever public live appearance at a festival in Glasgow. This performance marked a significant moment in the unraveling of the mystery surrounding the enigmatic musician, though questions about his identity persisted.
