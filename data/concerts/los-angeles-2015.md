# concert - Los Angeles 2015

## curiosities
**Rush's Final Concert** : Rush's very last live performance took place in Los Angeles on August 1st, 2015. While the band knew it was the end of their touring career, the event itself was not treated as a significant farewell show, but rather "just another gig." Neil Peart had to be persuaded to undertake this final tour due to his declining health, suffering from chronic tendonitis, shoulder problems, and lower back issues. "The Last Rush Show was in Los Angeles on August 1st, 2015." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B0CZ1deEgic
