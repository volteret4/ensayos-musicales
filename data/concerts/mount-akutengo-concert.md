# concert - Mount Akutengo concert

## curiosities
**Highest Ground-Based Concert (Bolivia)** : A concert held for mountain climbers on Mount Akutengo in Bolivia set the record for the highest gig performed without leaving the ground. This performance took place at an altitude of 19,911 feet, featuring musicians from Germany and Bolivia. "And that record belongs to a group of musicians from Germany and Bolivia, who held a small concert for mountain climbers on Mount Akutengo in Bolivia. This gig happened at 19,911 feet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
