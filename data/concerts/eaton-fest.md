# concert - Eaton Fest

## curiosities
**Ill-Fated Music Festival (Mossport)** : Eaton Fest was a one-time, "ill-fated music festival" held at Mossport. Its uniqueness and problematic nature suggest it did not become a recurring event. "Remember Eaton Fest, it was a one time only ill-fated music festival at Mossport." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bI6TTA-fn7c
**Shortwave Broadcast of Radio Show** : During the one-time, ill-fated Eaton Fest music festival at Mossport, the speaker learned that his radio show was being broadcast over shortwave radio somewhere in the world, discovered by someone with a shortwave radio. "Remember Eaton Fest, there was a one time only ill-fated music festival at Mossport, somebody came in with a shortwave radio and said, listen to this. And somebody somewhere in the world was broadcasting the show over shortwave." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4xxgPX-aR90
