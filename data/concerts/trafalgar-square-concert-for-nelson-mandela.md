# concert - Trafalgar Square Concert for Nelson Mandela

## curiosities
**R.E.M.'s Scheduled Performance (2001)** : R.E.M. was slated to perform at this concert in London on April 21, 2001. However, their guitarist, Peter Buck, experienced a significant air rage incident on his British Airways flight en route to the event, leading to his arrest and subsequent legal proceedings.
