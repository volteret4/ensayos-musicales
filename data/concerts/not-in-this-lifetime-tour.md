**Fourth Most Profitable Tour of All Time (2016-2019)** : The "Not in This Lifetime... Tour" by Guns N' Roses, featuring 60% of their original lineup, ran from 2016 to 2019. It grossed nearly $600 million US dollars over 158 shows, making it the fourth-most profitable tour of all time up to that point, and easily the biggest rock comeback of the 2010s.
