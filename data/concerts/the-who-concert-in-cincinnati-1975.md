# concert - The Who Concert in Cincinnati (1975)

## curiosities
**Festival Seating Blame** : The situation at the Who Concert, like other 1970s events with disruptive behavior, was largely attributed to "festival seating or general admission seating," where attendees would rush the stage for the best spot, a dangerous combination with alcohol and drugs. "I stand firm that festival seating or general admission seating was at the root of much of it." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 46 - The Chaos and Music of the Ozark Music Festival of 1974 | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Tragedy of 1975** : This concert, held in Cincinnati in 1975, resulted in the deaths of 11 people, contributing to the negative reputation of rock fans at the time.
