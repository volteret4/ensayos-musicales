# concert - Ed Sullivan Debut

## curiosities
**Ringo Starr's Influence on Rock Drumming** : Ringo Starr's appearance on The Ed Sullivan Show in 1964 was a pivotal moment. His drumming and choice of the matched grip heavily influenced subsequent rock drummers, nearly all of whom adopted this grip after seeing him. His Ludwig drum set from this performance also became a template for common 4- or 5-piece kits used by many players today. "After seeing Ringo and Ed Sullivan and in movies and elsewhere in the middle of the 60s, almost every rock drummer that came later went with the matched grip." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WL3zEWJJNuw
