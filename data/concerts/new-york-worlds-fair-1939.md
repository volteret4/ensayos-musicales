# concert - New York World's Fair (1939)

## curiosities
**Bell's Two-Channel Groove Demonstration** : At the 1939 New York World's Fair, the Bell company demonstrated an innovative technique for cutting two distinct audio channels into a single record groove. Despite this technological showcase, the system did not lead to widespread commercial adoption.
