# concert - Quadrophonic Joystick Action Tour

## curiosities
**Charitable Donations** : A significant aspect of this tour was the band's commitment to philanthropy, with one dollar from each ticket sold being donated to the Milarepa Fund. "And even more important was that one dollar from each ticket was donated to the Miller Rapa Fund." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9_BTe15NooI
**Successful Headlining Tour** : Following Lollapalooza in 1994, the Beastie Boys embarked on their own highly successful headlining tour, which notably included a Madison Square Garden show that sold out within 30 minutes.
