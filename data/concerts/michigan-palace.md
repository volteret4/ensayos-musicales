# concert - Michigan Palace

## curiosities
**Violent Iggy Pop Performance (1974)** : In 1974, after a brutal confrontation with a Detroit gang, Iggy Pop played a gig at Michigan Palace where he demanded the gang come on stage and beat him. The audience responded by throwing beer bottles at the stage, a chaotic event captured on the live album "Metallic KO."
