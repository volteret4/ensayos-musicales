# concert - Lollapalooza 1995

## curiosities
**Sinead O'Connor's Departure (1995)** : Sinead O'Connor was scheduled to perform on the 1995 Lollapalooza tour but had to leave after only a few shows because she became pregnant. "She was on the 1995 Lala Paloza tour for a while, but had to leave after a few shows because she was pregnant." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=afuqjWl1JyI
**Sonic Youth Headlining Appearance** : Sonic Youth performed as headliners at the Lollapalooza festival in 1995, highlighting their prominent position in the music scene. "They even hit line Lollapalooza in 1995." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Dhh5_PDhd0
