# concert - Ilda Cove's on Bloor Street

## curiosities
**First Sean Penn Atenshry Gig (c. 1986)** : This Toronto venue on Bloor Street hosted the first documented live performance of Sean Penn Atenshry, the precursor band to The Headstones, around 1986.
