# concert - North London Polytechnic College

## curiosities
**The Jesus and Mary Chain Riot (March 15, 1985)** : This college venue was the site of the notorious "great Jesus and Marychain riot" on March 15, 1985. Promoters oversold tickets, creating an impatient and cramped crowd. The situation escalated when an opening band member threw a bottle, and the Jesus and Mary Chain's delayed, short, and feedback-laden set provoked the audience to throw beer cans, invade the stage, and destroy equipment. "When it was announced that the chain would be playing North London Polytechnic College, they were lined up around the block." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
