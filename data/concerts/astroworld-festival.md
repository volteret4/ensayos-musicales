**Ten Deaths and Mass Injuries (November 5, 2021)** : The Astroworld Festival in Houston on November 5, 2021, witnessed a catastrophic crowd crush during Travis Scott's performance, resulting in the deaths of ten people (eight on-site and two later in hospital) from compressive asphyxiation. Victims ranged from 14 to 27 years old, and at least 300 additional people were injured due to severe failures in crowd control, safety, and security.
