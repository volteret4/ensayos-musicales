# concert - Donington

## curiosities
**Two Deaths in Crowd Collapse (August 28, 1988)** : At a large outdoor rock show at a racetrack in Donington, England, on August 28, 1988, which included bands like Guns N' Roses, Kiss, and Iron Maiden, a crowd collapse occurred during Guns N' Roses' set. Two men were fatally trapped and suffocated at the bottom of the pile, pushed so deeply into the mud that their bodies had to be dug out. "Two men were stuck at the bottom of the pile and they were pushed so deep into the mud that their dead bodies literally needed to be dug out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
