# concert - The Sphere

## curiosities
**Advanced Audio and Sensory Features** : The venue incorporates 163,000 speakers to ensure optimal sound quality throughout the building, along with haptic seating that mimics vibrations synchronized with on-screen visuals or stage events. Its sophisticated air handling systems not only maintain fresh air but can also simulate environmental effects like wind. "For audio, it uses 163,000 speakers so that no matter where you are in the building, it sounds great." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhdsufCUC2w
**Architectural and Technical Specifications** : The Sphere stands 366 feet tall and 516 feet wide, making it the largest spherical structure globally. Its exterior is covered with around 1.2 million LED pucks, each containing 48 diodes capable of millions of colors and high-speed visuals, so bright it can be seen from space at night. The interior features a massive 560,000 square foot, 16K by 16K video display covering 270 degrees in front of an 18,000-person grandstand, lauded as the highest resolution screen on the planet. "The interior is dominated by a 560,000 square foot 16k by 16k video display that covers about 270 degrees in front of a grandstand that holds about 18,000 people." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhdsufCUC2w
**Groundbreaking Las Vegas Venue (2023)** : The Sphere, a $2.3 billion venue in Las Vegas, opened as a state-of-the-art performance space, with U2 as its first booked act. Its advanced technology and immersive experience led to a highly successful residency, attracting 663,000 people and generating a box office revenue of $245 million USD. "You too was the first act booked at the $2.3 billion sphere." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
**Impact on Artist Residencies** : The Sphere is expected to redefine the concept of an artist residency, offering an unparalleled immersive musical experience. Following U2's inaugural run, other acts like Fish, Dead & Company, and the Eagles have performed, with many more artists evaluating its unique performance requirements. "The sphere is inevitably going to redefine the idea of an artist residency." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhdsufCUC2w
**International Expansion Plans** : As of January 1, 2025, there are plans to build a second Sphere in Abu Dhabi. A proposal for a London location was rejected by the city, which deemed it too much of a distraction. "As of January 1, 2025, there were plans to build a second sphere this time in Abu Dhabi." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhdsufCUC2w
**Las Vegas Grand Opening (2023)** : The Sphere, an extraordinary live performance venue, officially opened in Las Vegas on September 29, 2023, with the first of 40 shows by U2 titled "U2UV." It was constructed by MSG, the company behind Madison Square Garden, at a cost of approximately $2.3 billion US dollars, and designed by the architectural firm Populous, with construction lasting five years. "It opened for business on September 29, 2023, with the first of 40 shows by U2." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhdsufCUC2w
