# concert - Sony Convention (1993)

## curiosities
**Oasis Performance and Soundcheck** : Oasis performed, or at least conducted a soundcheck, at a Sony convention held in the Glen Eagles Hotel in 1993. This event took place shortly before the band signed with Creation Records. A recording from this soundcheck was later deceptively presented as a live gig in Glasgow, Scotland, by the band. "That was actually recorded in the sound check for the Sony convention in Glen Eagles Hotel in 1993." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dMsEj62KFEw
