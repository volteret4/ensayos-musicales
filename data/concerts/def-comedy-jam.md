## curiosities
**Influential Stand-up Comedy Show** : This popular and influential HBO stand-up comedy show was produced by Russell Simmons. It served as a platform where Fox executives noticed Martin Lawrence hosting, leading them to offer him a co-creator and starring role in his own TV show, *Martin*.
