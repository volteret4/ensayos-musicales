**Twin Festival with Leeds** : Reading and Leeds are unique twin festivals held on the same days every August. Reading is in the South West of London, and Leeds is in the North. They share the same lineup, with schedules juggled to allow acts to make the 300-kilometer journey between the cities. "What makes these events unique is that there are really two festivals, Leeds in the North, Reading in the South, West of London. The gigs are held on the same days every August and share the lineup with schedules juggled accordingly." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
