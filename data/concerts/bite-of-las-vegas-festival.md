# concert - Bite of Las Vegas festival

## curiosities
**Imagine Dragons' Unexpected Breakthrough (2009)** : On October 17, 2009, Imagine Dragons received an unforeseen opportunity to perform at the "Bite of Las Vegas" festival when headliner Train's singer, Pat Monahan, fell ill. They were drafted last-minute to perform for 26,000 people at a skate park, a chance they maximized, which consequently launched them onto a new trajectory, leading to increased local recognition and headlining offers. "On October 17th, 2009, the bite of Las Vegas festival happened at a skate park in the city." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GmYlTwI_TQQ
