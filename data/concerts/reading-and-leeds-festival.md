# concert - Reading and Leeds Festival

## curiosities
**Recording Album During Tour Schedule** : While recording their album "Does This Look Infected," Sum 41 performed at the Reading and Leeds Festival. The tight schedule meant they had to immediately drive back to London the following day to continue recording at Lipic Studios, highlighting the rushed production process. "we played Reading and Leeds Festival, and we'd have to drive straight back and go to London the next day, and we were in the Lipic Studios finishing up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6L4xKBFEYkE
