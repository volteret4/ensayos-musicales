## curiosities
**David Bowie's Attributed Performance at Opening Ceremony** : David Bowie was attributed at the opening ceremony of the Olympics, highlighting his enduring cultural significance. This recognition occurred during a period when Bowie was largely invisible following his heart attack, and notably, without his direct input.
