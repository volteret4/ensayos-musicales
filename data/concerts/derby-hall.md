# concert - Derby Hall

## curiosities
**Riot After Ian Curtis's Performance** : On April 8, 1980, at Derby Hall, Ian Curtis was so shaky after his suicide attempt that he could only sing two songs, leading the crowd to "freak out." A mini-riot ensued, resulting in five people being hospitalized. "This was April 8, 1980. Ian was so shaky that he was only able to sing two songs, and the crowd freaked out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w
