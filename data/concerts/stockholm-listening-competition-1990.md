# concert - Stockholm Listening Competition (1990)

## curiosities
**International Audio Encoding Standard Search** : In January 1990, the Moving Pictures Expert Group (MPEG), acting on behalf of the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), conducted a blind listening competition in Stockholm to select a global audio encoding standard. Various research groups, including Fraunhofer and Musicam, presented their technologies, with participants evaluating ten diverse audio pieces, from a trumpet solo and glockenspiel to Suzanne Vega's "Tom's Diner," on a scale of 1 to 5. "The search was conducted by the Moving Pictures Expert Group or MPEG for short... there was a listening competition in Stockholm in January 1990." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v7DXXu_1fvM
