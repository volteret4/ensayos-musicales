## curiosities
**Suicide Bombings (2003)** : The Krila Festival, an annual event held on July 4, 2003, at Toshino airfield north of Moscow with an attendance of around 20,000 people, was targeted by suicide bombers. Two women detonated explosives belts at separate entrance gates after being denied entry, approximately 15 minutes apart. The attacks resulted in 14 deaths and dozens of injuries. Russian authorities attributed the bombings to Chechen rebels, noting the casualties could have been far worse had the bombers infiltrated the main crowd.
