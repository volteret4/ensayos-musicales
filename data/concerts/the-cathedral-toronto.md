# concert - The Cathedral (Toronto)

## curiosities
**Early Performance Venue** : The Cathedral in downtown Toronto was an early, significant venue for bands like Alexis on Fire in the aggressive music scene. Playing to 150-200 people at this venue represented the peak of their early aspirations. "The biggest bands that were playing, the style of music that we were playing, were, you know, maybe they'd play at the cathedral downtown Toronto to 150 kids, when we sold out or whatever." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
