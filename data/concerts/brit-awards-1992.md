**KLF's Controversial Performance** : At the 1992 Brit Awards, The KLF, known for their unusual antics, planned to dump buckets of blood and disembowel a dead sheep on stage, which was prevented by organizers. Instead, Bill Drummond fired blanks over the audience with a machine gun, and the sheep purchased for the event was later dumped at an after-party.
