## curiosities
**Chester Bennington Tribute (October 27, 2017)** : On October 27, 2017, the Hollywood Bowl hosted a three-hour tribute concert for Chester Bennington. The event featured appearances from friends and bands like Blink 182, System of a Down, Korn, and Avenged Sevenfold, and was streamed live, reaching millions of viewers.
