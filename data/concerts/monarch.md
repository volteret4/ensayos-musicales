# concert - Monarch

## curiosities
**Muse's Early Ambition** : Muse performed a "little tiny gig" at The Monarch in London (near Kentish Town) early in their career. David Botrell observed them playing "like it was Wembley Stadium," and Matt Bellamy explained this was their philosophy to prepare for future success. "We saw them at the Monarch in London, which was a little tiny gig where it used to be, just below Kentish Town, small gig." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
