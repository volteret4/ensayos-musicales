# concert - Pacific National Exhibition

## curiosities
**The Beatles' First Official Canadian Performance (1964)** : The Beatles performed their first official concert in Canada on August 22, 1964, at the Pacific National Exhibition (PNE) in Vancouver, British Columbia. The PNE is an annual 15-day summer fair and amusement park that typically runs from mid-to-late August into September.
