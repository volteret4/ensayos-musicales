# concert - The Virgin Tour

## curiosities
**Pirated Recordings & Official VHS Release (1985)** : The concerts during "The Virgin Tour" were incredibly popular, leading to a widespread circulation of pirated recordings among fans. Madonna and her record label capitalized on this demand by releasing an official VHS titled "Madonna Live: The Virgin Tour," which for an extended period became the best-selling music VHS in history. **Ticket Demand & Venue Changes (1985)** : Following Madonna's explosive 1984 MTV Music Awards performance, tickets for "The Virgin Tour" sold out within days, and within two months, all tour dates were exhausted. The demand was so unprecedented that many planned concert venues were deemed too small, forcing organizers to upgrade Madonna's performances to larger stadiums to accommodate the massive public interest.
