# concert - Expo 67 (Montreal)

## curiosities
**Catalyst for Canadian Cultural Pride** : Expo 67 in Montreal was a highly successful "coming-out party" for Canada's 100th birthday, which significantly elevated national pride in Canadian culture and motivated individuals to develop a stronger domestic music industry. "But then along came Expo 67 in Montreal. It was an amazing success, a coming-out party for our 100th birthday. It had the interesting effect of raising our pride when it came to our culture, and a number of people decided to do something." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2kYleeMnl8
