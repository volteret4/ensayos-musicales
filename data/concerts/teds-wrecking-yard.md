# concert - Ted's Wrecking Yard

## curiosities
**"Cancel Band" Reputation** : Pezz frequently played at Ted's Wrecking Yard in Toronto, earning them the nickname "the cancel band" because Vaughan Matsell, an early supporter, would book them to fill last-minute cancellation slots. "And I remember a guy named by the name of George Straumelopoulos would come and see us play because we were playing like Ted's wrecking our knowledge. We kind of became like the cancel band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Vaughan Matsell's Support** : Vaughan Matsell was a significant figure in Pezz's early success, responsible for booking them regularly at Ted's Wrecking Yard. "So when there was like a, you know, a Vaughan Matsell who was a big part of our success in the early days. She would book us at Ted's and if there was a cancellation, we would always play." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
