# concert - Woodstock 1994

## curiosities
**25th Anniversary Event Issues** : The 25th anniversary Woodstock show in 1994 was held in Saugerties, New York, as the original 1969 site was unavailable. The event was characterized by "more rain and more mud and a lot of misery," and generated significant fan complaints regarding price gouging for basic necessities like food and water. "I was at that one, and there was more rain and more mud and a lot of misery." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uEe_HLUay5M
**25th Anniversary Festival (August 12, 1994)** : Held in Saugerties, New York, Woodstock '94 commemorated the 25th anniversary of the original festival. The event, which drew 350,000 attendees, was plagued by heavy rain that began shortly after people arrived and continued throughout, creating muddy conditions. "And the second was a festival in Socrates, New York commemorating the 25th anniversary of the original Woodstock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2RKchFW42Jc
**Dave Navarro's RHCP Debut** : The 25th anniversary Woodstock show in August 1994 marked guitarist Dave Navarro's first live performance as a member of the Red Hot Chili Peppers. "His first live appearance with the group was in August 1994 during the 25th anniversary Woodstock show." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pAOFzQZPjSM
**Rain and Mud Conditions (August 12, 1994)** : Shortly after 350,000 attendees arrived at Woodstock '94 in Saugerties, New York, persistent rain began, continuing for an extended period and creating extremely muddy conditions that defined the festival experience. "Surely after the 350,000 people arrived, it began to rain. And then it rained and rained again and rained some more." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2RKchFW42Jc
