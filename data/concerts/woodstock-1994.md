# concert - Woodstock 1994

## curiosities
**25th Anniversary Festival (August 12, 1994)** : Held in Saugerties, New York, Woodstock '94 commemorated the 25th anniversary of the original festival. The event, which drew 350,000 attendees, was plagued by heavy rain that began shortly after people arrived and continued throughout, creating muddy conditions. **Rain and Mud Conditions (August 12, 1994)** : Shortly after 350,000 attendees arrived at Woodstock '94 in Saugerties, New York, persistent rain began, continuing for an extended period and creating extremely muddy conditions that defined the festival experience.
