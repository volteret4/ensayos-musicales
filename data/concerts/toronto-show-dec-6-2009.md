# concert - Toronto show (Dec 6, 2009)

## curiosities
**Preceded Bus Accident** : Weezer had just played a show in Toronto on December 6, 2009, as part of their "Ratitude" tour, before their tour bus hit a patch of ice in New York and crashed.
