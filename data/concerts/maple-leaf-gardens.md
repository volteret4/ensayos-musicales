# concert - Maple Leaf Gardens

## curiosities
**Sold-Out Show** : Billy Talent successfully sold out Maple Leaf Gardens during one of their tours, highlighting their popularity and ability to fill major venues in Canada. "wasn't this the tour where you guys sold out Maple Leaf Gardens or the ACC." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GT_u0eFDlk8
**The Beatles' Toronto Concerts (1964, 1965, 1966)** : This iconic Toronto venue hosted multiple Beatles concerts across three years, including two chaotic shows on September 6, 1964, where 109 fans fainted and required a police presence of over 4,000 officers. It also served as the site for their 1965 return and their final two Canadian concerts in August 1966, where the deafening screams often rendered the music inaudible and fans continued to faint due to the intense fervor. "By 10am, the scene outside the gardens, mirrored that of the hotel, and now ticket holders entered the fray." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2IDbX5rNT1E
