# concert - Rosemont Horizon

## curiosities
**Cancelled Super Major Headlining Gig (August 1996)** : This 16,000-seat venue in Chicago was set to host Oasis's first "super major headlining gig" in North America. The concert was cancelled because Liam Gallagher left the plane at Heathrow Airport, refusing to travel for the tour, thus preventing the band's performance.
