# concert - International Space Station performance

## curiosities
**All-Time Altitude Record for a Performance** : Commander Chris Hadfield holds the all-time altitude record for a concert performance, having performed a show for people on the ground from aboard the International Space Station. He was in orbit at a height of 400 kilometers when he gave the performance. "But if we really want to get pedantic about it, the all-time altitude record goes to Commander Chris Hadfield aboard the International Space Station. He performed a show for people on the ground when he was in orbit at a height of 400 kilometers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
