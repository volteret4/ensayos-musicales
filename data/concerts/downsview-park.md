# concert - Downsview Park

## curiosities
**Fatal Stage Collapse (June 16, 2012)** : On June 16, 2012, a stage being built at Downsview Park in Toronto for a Radiohead headlining performance collapsed. The tragic incident, occurring before the band's soundcheck, killed drum tech Scott Johnson, who was crushed by a 5,000-pound video monitor, and seriously injured others. "On Saturday, June 16, 2012, a stage was almost finished being built at Downsview Park in Toronto ahead of that night's gig, headlined by Radiohead." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
