# concert - Don Kirschner's Rock Concert

## curiosities
**ABBA's TV Taping (1976)** : ABBA taped an appearance for "Don Kirschner's Rock Concert" in Los Angeles in October 1976. This was part of their efforts to promote their music in the United States through television rather than live performances.
