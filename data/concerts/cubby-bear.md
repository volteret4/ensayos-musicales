# concert - Cubby Bear

## curiosities
**Communal Atmosphere** : The concert environment at the Cubby Bear was characterized by its non-violent, friendly, and communal nature, where audience members and bands seemed to know each other, fostering a sense of camaraderie. "it wasn't violent at all. It was all these people hanging out, everyone's friends, everybody knew each other, you know, everybody knew the band, the band knew everybody, and just playing this show." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA
**Dave Grohl's First Live Show** : Located in Chicago, this venue hosted Dave Grohl's very first live concert experience around 1982 or 1983, where he saw Naked Ray Gun and Rights of the Accused. "a place called the Cubby Bear in Chicago." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ynrwaUi-sA
