# concert - Lollapalooza 1992

## curiosities
**Eddie Vedder and Chris Cornell Drinking Contest** : During the 1992 Lollapalooza tour, Eddie Vedder of Pearl Jam and Chris Cornell of Soundgarden famously engaged in a drinking contest, with the "drink" being the regurgitated cocktail of goo, bile, and Matt "The Tube" Crowley's half-digested last meal. Eddie Vedder won this contest. "On one stop on the tour, which featured both Pearl Jam and Soundgarden, Eddie Vetter and Chris Cornell decided they would have a drinking contest. And the drink was the stuff pumped out of Matt the tube crowley's stomach." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7kH_AfT3i4k
**Impact on Alternative Music** : By the time Lollapalooza returned exactly one year after its debut in 1991, alternative music was no longer an "alternative to anything" but was well on its way to supplanting the mainstream. This rapid shift was largely attributed to the rise of grunge. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Jim Rose Circus Sideshow Feature** : The 1992 edition of the Lollapalooza tour included the Jim Rose Circus Sideshow, featuring performers like Mr. Lyfto and Matt "The Tube" Crowley, whose acts involved various physical oddities and gross-out performances. "The 1992 edition of the tour featured something called the Jim Rose Circus side show." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7kH_AfT3i4k
**Matt "The Tube" Crowley's Performance** : Matt "The Tube" Crowley was a featured act known for his ability to eat and regurgitate anything. His performance involved swallowing a seven-foot hose, through which he would pump various substances like beer, ketchup, mustard, chocolate, Pepto-Bismol, and syrup directly into his stomach, only to then pump them all back out into a glass and offer the "cocktail of goo and bile" to audience members as a dare. "Part of his act involved swallowing a seven foot hose. And at the other end was a hand pump. Matt would then pump all sorts of things directly into his stomach, beer, ketchup, mustard, chocolate, pink pepto bismill, syrup, anything that was available. And then he would pump it all back out again." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7kH_AfT3i4k
**Red Hot Chili Peppers' Performance with Arik Marshall** : The Red Hot Chili Peppers performed at Lollapalooza in 1992 with Arik Marshall as their guitarist, following John Frusciante's first departure. The festival was one of the "big festival dates" mentioned as part of the tour where Marshall was brought in as a temporary replacement. "While the Chili Pepper's regrouped, eventually bringing in a guy named Eric Marshall for a bit... Here's the band performing at Lola Palusio later back here." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
