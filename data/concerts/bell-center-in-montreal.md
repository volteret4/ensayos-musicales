# concert - Bell Center in Montreal

## curiosities
**"Montreal" Live Performance (2000)** : In 2000, The Tragically Hip performed "Montreal," a song about the Polytechnique Massacre, at the Bell Center in Montreal. This performance occurred on an anniversary of the massacre and is the version included on the *Saskadelvia* album. During the encore, Gordon Downie, having forgotten the lyrics, had them printed from a fan's phone to refresh his memory. "We were in the Bell Center, the Wink and Montreal, realized it was the anniversary of the massacre at the Ecopolic Technique." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G9H9q7BYqEA
