# concert - Silver Dollar

## curiosities
**Early Headstones Performance Venue** : An early venue in Toronto where The Headstones, or their precursor band, performed. Hugh Dillon specifically mentioned this as the place where he first played the song "Hard of Darkness" live. "You used to come and see us at the Silver Dollar. Yeah, and that's where I played the first time I played Hard of Darkness." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7kmkWJ54ww
