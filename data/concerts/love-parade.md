## curiosities
**Fatal Tunnel Crush (Duisburg, Germany, July 24, 2010)** : The annual free Love Parade electronic dance music festival in Duisburg, Germany, on July 24, 2010, resulted in 21 deaths and over 700 injuries. The single, narrow tunnel entrance to the festival grounds became dangerously crowded; despite police efforts to seal it off and direct people to turn back, persistent pushing from behind created an immense crush, so powerful that autopsies revealed all victims suffered broken ribs.
