## curiosities
**All-Girl Orchestra's Regular Performances** : An all-girl orchestra featuring the talented young drummer Vera McClellan-a-Hans regularly performed on the NBC Radio Network. This provided early, albeit somewhat constrained, national exposure for female musicians in the era before World War II.
