# concert - Madison Square Garden

## curiosities
**Bob Marley's Last New York Shows (September 1980)** : Bob Marley performed two shows at Madison Square Garden in New York City on September 19th and 20th, 1980, as part of his ongoing tour. "then included two shows at Madison Square Garden in New York City on September 19th and 20th, 1980." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZhzjZixCcK8
**David Bowie's 50th Birthday Celebration (1997)** : Madison Square Garden was the venue for David Bowie's large pay-per-view 50th birthday celebration in January 1997, preceding the release of his *Earthling* album. "a 50th birthday celebration at Madison Square Garden, which was a big pay-per-view event." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yq2A_o9-ulM
**Dream Performance Destination (1977)** : Leonard Skinnerd harbored a long-standing dream of performing at Madison Square Garden. This aspiration was on the verge of being realized, as the band was scheduled to play at the venue several weeks into their tour leg that began on October 13, 1977. "The next leg of the tour began on October 13th in statesboro, Georgia, and would eventually see the band realize their dreams by playing Madison Square Garden several weeks down the road." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dgzF_KBSQWM
**Ricky Gervais Show Appearance (2007)** : On May 19, 2007, David Bowie made his final stage singing appearance at Madison Square Garden, unannounced, during a show by his friend Ricky Gervais, where he improvised and performed the song "Little Fat Man." "But the very last time was on May 19th, 2007, when his friend Ricky Gervais played a show at Madison Square Garden." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yq2A_o9-ulM
