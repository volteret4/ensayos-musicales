# concert - Caesar's Palace, Las Vegas (1969)

## curiosities
**Blood, Sweat & Tears' Record-Breaking Residency** : In 1969, Blood, Sweat & Tears achieved remarkable success at Caesar's Palace in Las Vegas, becoming the first rock band to play on the Strip. They performed six sold-out shows in three nights, breaking previous attendance records held by Frank Sinatra.
