# concert - Rock in Rio Festival 2001

## curiosities
**Nick Oliveri's Naked Performance and Arrest** : At the 2001 Rock in Rio Festival in Brazil, Nick Oliveri of Queens of the Stone Age performed his entire set naked. This act was considered a crime in Brazil, leading to his subsequent arrest. "At the Rock and Real Festival in 2001, he played the entire set Naked, which, believe it or not, was a crime in Brazil.
