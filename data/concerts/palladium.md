# concert - Palladium

## curiosities
**The Clash's London Calling Artwork Photo Location (September 21, 1979)** : The iconic photograph of Paul Simonon smashing his bass, featured on the cover of The Clash's "London Calling" album, was taken at the Palladium in New York City at precisely 10:50 PM on September 21, 1979. The exact time is known because Simonon's watch also broke and stopped at that moment. "And that shot of Paul Simmonon trashing his base, it was taken at the palladium in New York City at exactly 10.50 pm on September 21, 1979." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AymMybE33zU
