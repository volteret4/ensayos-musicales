# concert - Fox Theatre gig (Soundgarden)

## curiosities
**Chris Cornell's Last Performance (March 3, 2017)** : Chris Cornell finished a gig at the Fox Theatre in Detroit on March 3, 2017, walking off stage at 11:15 pm after the encores. This was just hours before his death at the MGM Grand hotel. "Chris walked off the stage at 11.15 pm. The show was over. Encore's were done." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zM3gu_nbw-k
