# concert - O2 in London

## curiosities
**Led Zeppelin Reunion Concert (2007)** : On December 10, 2007, the O2 Arena in London hosted a one-night-only reunion concert for Led Zeppelin. This event, precipitated by the death of Atlantic Records founder Ahmet Ertegun, featured Jimmy Page, Robert Plant, John Paul Jones, and Jason Bonham on drums. It marked the band's first significant appearance in 27 years and generated immense public demand, with 20 million ticket applications.
