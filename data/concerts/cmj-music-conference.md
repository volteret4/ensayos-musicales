# concert - CMJ Music Conference

## curiosities
**Discovery of The Tragically Hip (1989)** : In 1989, The Tragically Hip played at the CMJ Music Conference in New York. Organizers distributed CDs featuring highlighted artists to delegates, and the Hip's inclusion on this disc led to their discovery by Canadian record company representatives, ultimately resulting in their first record deal back in Toronto. "The hip played the CMJ music conference in New York. And as was common back in those days, the organizers distributed a CD featuring some highlighted artists to all the delegates. The hip were on that disc. And that's what the Canadian record people company heard." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PnLdgohA5gY
