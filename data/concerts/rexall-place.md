# concert - Rexall Place

## curiosities
**Robert Plant Tour Venue and "Bohemian Rhapsody" Incident** : Rexall Place, then home of the Edmonton Oilers, was a sold-out arena where The Trews performed as an opener for Robert Plant. It was at this venue that they famously played a "punky version" of Queen's "Bohemian Rhapsody" on a fan dare, leading to a standing ovation and Robert Plant's subsequent disapproval. "we were going down to play like a sold out wherever the oilers play Rex Hall or whatever it's called." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
