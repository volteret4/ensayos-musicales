# concert - Trash Rateria

## curiosities
**Jay Parsons' First DJ Gig Venue** : The Trash Rateria in Peterborough, Ontario, was the venue where Jay Parsons had his first DJ gig. A local promoter asked him to open a rap show, at which point Jay adopted his "human kabab" handle. "A local promoter asked me if I would open a rap show at the Trash Rateria and Peterborough, Ontario." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N-3eddy4J7g
