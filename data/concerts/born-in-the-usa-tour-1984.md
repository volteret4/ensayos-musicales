# concert - Born in the USA Tour (1984)

## curiosities
**U2's Study of Springsteen's Performance** : U2, specifically Bono and the band, meticulously studied Bruce Springsteen during this tour in 1984 to learn how he effectively scaled his "big songs" to stadium settings without losing their emotional impact. "When they were on their way up, Botto and the rest of the band studied Springsteen on his born in the USA tour in 1984." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I
