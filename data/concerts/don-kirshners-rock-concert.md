# concert - Don Kirshner's Rock Concert

## curiosities
**1970s Live Music TV Show** : *Don Kirshner's Rock Concert* was a live music television show that existed in the same vein as *Burt Sugarman's Midnight Special*. Don Kirshner was also a record label executive who almost dropped Kansas in 1975.
