# concert - Magnetic Hill

## curiosities
**Bruce Springsteen Concert Location** : Magnetic Hill in Moncton, New Brunswick, was the outdoor concert site where The Trews opened for Bruce Springsteen. The event also featured Tom Cochrane and took place in late August on a "beautiful day."
