# concert - International Essen Song Festival

## curiosities
**Birth of Independent German Rock (September 1968)** : Held in September 1968, the International Essen Song Festival is historically recognized as the birthplace of independent German rock music. It provided the first significant platform for German musicians to showcase their unique, experimental "psych-slash-prog-slash-electronics-slash-rock" headspace to a large audience. "Then in September 1968, there was the International Essence Song Festival, a music festival, that has gone down in history as the time when independent German rock music was born." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
