## curiosities
**World's First Amphitheater (80 BCE)** : The Romans opened their first outdoor theater in Pompeii around 80 BCE. Its circular design led to the coining of the term "amphitheater," derived from the Latin "empha" meaning "around." Like its Greek predecessor, it likely saw early instances of scalping. "When the Romans opened their first outdoor theater in Pompeii in 80 BCE, it was circular. The word around in Latin is empha.
