# concert - Boston Tea Party

## curiosities
**Early Headbanging by Fans** : Between January 22 and January 25, 1969, Led Zeppelin performed at the Boston Tea Party. During these shows, fans in the front rows were observed banging their heads on the stage in rhythm with the music, marking one of the earliest documented instances of rock-based headbanging by an audience. "The band played a venue called the Boston Tea Party and fans down front were seen banging their heads on the stage in time with the music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bferkEWvJg0
