# concert - Birmingham University

## curiosities
**Joy Division's Final Performance (May 2, 1980)** : Birmingham University was the setting for Joy Division's last gig on May 2, 1980. The show was recorded and tragically marked singer Ian Curtis's final stage appearance, as he suffered an epileptic seizure on stage during the song "Decades," dying by suicide less than two weeks later.
