# concert - Birmingham University

## curiosities
**Joy Division's Final Gig** : The last live performance by Joy Division took place on May 2, 1980, at Birmingham University. During this show, Ian Curtis fainted on stage due to another seizure, although the crowd mistakenly interpreted it as part of the act. "The final Joy Division gig was held on May 2, 1980 at Birmingham University." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w
**Joy Division's Final Performance (May 2, 1980)** : Birmingham University was the setting for Joy Division's last gig on May 2, 1980. The show was recorded and tragically marked singer Ian Curtis's final stage appearance, as he suffered an epileptic seizure on stage during the song "Decades," dying by suicide less than two weeks later. "The final joy division gig was held on May 2nd, 1980 at Birmingham University. The show was recorded." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
