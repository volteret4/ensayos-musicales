## curiosities
**Michael Been's Passing** : At a festival in Brussels on August 19th, 2010, Michael Been suffered a fatal heart attack at age 60. He was working as a soundman for his son Robert Been's band, Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, at the time. "On August 19th of 2010, Dad was working as a soundman for his son's band, a festival in Brussels. When he had a fatal heart attack, he died right there.
