# concert - Concert of Valor (2014)

## curiosities
**Purpose as Fundraiser** : This special event in 2014 served as a fundraiser dedicated to supporting American military servicemen and women, as well as veterans. "they played a special event together called The Concert of Valor, which was a fundraiser for American military servicemen and women." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I
**Unusual Song Choice** : Dave Grohl, Bruce Springsteen, and Zac Brown performed an unnamed anti-war song by Creedence Clearwater Revival at this benefit concert, a choice that was noted as "a bit odd" given the context of honoring military personnel. "I'm not entirely sure about their choice of material. You know, an anti-war song by Credence Clearwater Revival, which seemed a bit odd." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I
