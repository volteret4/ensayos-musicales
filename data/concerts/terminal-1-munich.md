# concert - Terminal 1 (Munich)

## curiosities
**Nirvana's Last Concert Venue (March 1, 1994)** : Terminal 1 in Munich, originally a former airport terminal, became a music venue for concerts and raves after the old airport closed in 1992. It hosted Nirvana's final concert on March 1, 1994. Tickets for this show were 35 Deutsch marks.
