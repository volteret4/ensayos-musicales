# concert - Energy (Burlington)

## curiosities
**Early "Smells Like Teen Spirit" Play (1991)** : Nirvana's "Smells Like Teen Spirit" was played at Energy (possibly also known as The Kingdom) in Burlington during an alternative dance night on a Thursday after its CD single release in August 1991. The audience's immediate familiarity with the lyrics highlighted the song's rapid impact. "We played the CD single at the kingdom in Burlington. We might have been called energy at the time I can't remember, but we played it on the Thursday night for an alternative dance night. Everybody knew all the words already." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bI6TTA-fn7c
