# concert - The Comet Tavern

## curiosities
**Mia Zapata's Last Known Location** : The Comet Tavern in Seattle's Capitol Hill District was the last venue Mia Zapata was known to have left before her murder on July 7, 1993. She departed the venue around 2 AM, briefly visiting a friend nearby before heading home. "On two in the morning of Wednesday, July 7, 1993, Mia left a Seattle venue called The Comet Tavern in the Capitol Hill District." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q6dAX6HCjgw
