# concert - Sound and Vision Tour

## curiosities
**Bowie's Hits Retirement Strategy - 90s** : The "Sound and Vision Tour" was the first stage of David Bowie's 90s reinvention, during which he promised to play his most famous old hits, including "Space Oddity," "Ziggy Stardust," "Rebel Rebel," "Young Americans," "Heroes," and "Let's Dance," one last time before retiring them permanently. His goal was to shed past expectations and embark on a fresh artistic start.
