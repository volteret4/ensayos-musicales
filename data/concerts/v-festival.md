# concert - V-Festival

## curiosities
**Cancelled Oasis Appearance (August 2009)** : Oasis canceled their performance at the V-Festival in August 2009, with Liam Gallagher claiming laryngitis, a reason publicly disputed by Noel Gallagher who suggested Liam was hungover. This incident highlighted the escalating tensions between the brothers. "Just days after they didn't appear at the V-Festival." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=19Z_AAEy8Bc
**Gallagher Brothers' Separate Arrivals (Pre-Split)** : Before playing the V-Festival in Staffordshire, Liam and Noel Gallagher maintained their personal separation, attending a Manchester City game at opposite ends of the stadium and arriving at the gig in separate cars, only reuniting on stage. "as I understand it last week, when they were playing at the V-Fest, the first of all, in Staffordshire, on the Saturday, they both went to the Manchester City game. We're at different ends of the ground and then got separate cars to the gig and eventually met up when they walked on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=19Z_AAEy8Bc
