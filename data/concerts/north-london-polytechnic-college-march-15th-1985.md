# concert - North London Polytechnic College (March 15th, 1985)

## curiosities
**The Great Jesus and Mary Chain Riot** : This event is remembered as "the great Jesus and Mary chain riot." Promoters oversold tickets, leading to an impatient and cramped crowd. Tensions escalated when the opening band, Meet Whiplash, threw a wine bottle into the audience. The Jesus and Mary Chain then aggravated the situation by making the crowd wait an hour before playing a provocative, 20-minute set of squalling feedback with their backs to the audience, leading to a full-blown riot with beer cans, stage invasion, and gear destruction, which received extensive British music press coverage.
