**Creation Inspired by The Pixies' Performance** : The Lollapalooza Festival, conceived by Perry Farrell, was indirectly inspired by The Pixies' headlining performance at the 1990 Reading Festival. The enthusiastic reaction of 30,000 fans singing along to "Debaser" was the catalyst that convinced Farrell his idea for an annual North American festival of new bands would be successful. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z9T9wi0vEvc

"That first Lala Pulusa tour was designed primarily as a farewell tour for Jane's addiction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0hnBC0y59QA
**Inception by Perry Farrell (1990)** : The concept for the Lollapalooza Festival was developed by Jane's Addiction singer Perry Farrell in the summer of 1990, inspired by a Pixies performance he witnessed at the Reading Festival in England. "Perry Farrell came up with the concept of the Lala Pulusa Festival in the summer of 1990." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0hnBC0y59QA
