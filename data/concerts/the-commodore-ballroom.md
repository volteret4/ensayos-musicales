# concert - The Commodore Ballroom

## curiosities
**Shift in Venue Function in Vancouver** : Once a staple in Vancouver's grassroots music scene, The Commodore Ballroom has been "redone" and now primarily caters to established touring acts or those guaranteed to fill its capacity. This shift signifies a loss of opportunities for developing local artists to play smaller, formative gigs.
