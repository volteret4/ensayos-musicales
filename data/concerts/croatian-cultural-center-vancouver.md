# concert - Croatian Cultural Center (Vancouver)

## curiosities
**Successful Canadian Show** : Alexis on Fire played an incredibly successful show at the Croatian Cultural Center in Vancouver with Billy Talent, drawing a thousand people. This event was considered one of the best nights of their lives and showcased the strong demand for aggressive music in Canada. "Literally play the Croatian Cultural Center in Vancouver with Billy Talent to a thousand people and it'd be one of the best nights of our life." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
