# concert - Woodstock 25th anniversary

## curiosities
**Mud Fight and Mike Dirnt's Injury (August 1994)** : Green Day's performance at the 25th anniversary of Woodstock in August 1994, held in Socrates, New York, is remembered for its chaotic nature, including a massive mud fight that erupted with the audience. During the event, bassist Mike Dirnt sustained an injury, losing two teeth after colliding with a security guard on stage and hitting his face against a speaker cabinet. "That set was famous for descending into a mud fight with a crowd. And for bass player Mike Durant, losing a couple of teeth when he ran into a security guard on stage and bounced his face off a speaker cabinet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nyfdGYWelic
