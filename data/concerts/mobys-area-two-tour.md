# concert - Moby's Area Two Tour

## curiosities
**Ash Bus Accident (2002)** : Ash was part of Moby's Area Two Tour in August 2002 when their bus tipped over on Interstate 5 in Oregon while traveling to Detroit, resulting in drummer Rick McMurray sustaining broken ribs after the driver swerved to avoid tire debris. "Ash was a part of Moby's area two tour and they were on their way to Detroit on the night of August 16th, 2002 when their bus tipped over on interstate five in Oregon." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgN0vBIugLc
