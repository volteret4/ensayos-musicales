**Advances in Festival Amplification (1967)** : Monterey Pop in 1967 is recognized as one of the festivals where significant advances in sound systems for large-scale musical events began to be made. These developments were crucial in addressing the growing demands for amplified music at outdoor festivals and improving the listening experience for large audiences.
