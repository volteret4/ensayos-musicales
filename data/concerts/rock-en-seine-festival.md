# concert - Rock en Seine festival

## curiosities
**Oasis Breakup Catalyst (August 28, 2009)** : The Rock en Seine festival near Paris was the site of the final, violent backstage confrontation between Liam and Noel Gallagher on August 28, 2009. This fight directly led to Noel's departure from Oasis minutes before their scheduled performance, effectively ending the band.
