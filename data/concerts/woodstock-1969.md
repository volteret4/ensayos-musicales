# concert - Woodstock (1969)

## curiosities
**Peaceful Festival** : The producers, Michael Lang and Artie Kornfeld, were considered fortunate that Woodstock was largely a peaceful festival, especially when compared to the chaotic and problematic Ozark Music Festival of 1974.
