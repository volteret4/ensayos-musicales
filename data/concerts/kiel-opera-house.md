# concert - Kiel Opera House

## curiosities
**AC/DC High Voltage Tour (September 18, 1980)** : The Kiel Opera House was the site of AC/DC's "High Voltage Tour" on September 18, 1980. Tickets for this "electrifying, scorching" performance were sold at various local outlets including Backstage Records, Lane Co-op, Spectrum, all three Peaches locations, the Kiel Opera House box office, and via Dial Tix.
