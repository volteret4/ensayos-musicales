# concert - Brixton Academy

## curiosities
**Happy Mondays Reunion Gig (2010s)** : Brixton Academy in London was the venue for one of the Happy Mondays' reunion shows, where they performed with Inspiral Carpets as the opening act. The event was described as awesome for fans of the Madchester era. "Come to the gig at Brixton Academy, he said, the Inspiral Carpets are opening, it'll be great." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM ← brixton-academy
**Plaster Damage from Extreme Volume (1998)** : A performance by the electronic band Leftfield at the Brixton Academy in 1998 reached a volume of 137 decibels. This immense sound pressure caused "plaster from the roof of the Brixton Academy to fall," illustrating the destructive potential of exceptionally loud concert sound. "the electronic band left field caused plaster from the roof of the Brixton Academy to fall when they were clocked at 137 decibels." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ ← brixton-academy
