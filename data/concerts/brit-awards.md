# concert - Brit Awards

## curiosities
**1995 Brit Awards Results** : At the 1995 Brit Awards, Oasis won three trophies, while their rivals Blur received none, despite Blur having won the "Battle of Britpop" singles chart war earlier that year. "At the 1995 Brit Awards, Oasis won three trophies and Blur got none." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUr9sjWgUcw
**Jarvis Cocker's Protest (1996)** : On February 19, 1996, during Michael Jackson's "Earth Song" performance, Jarvis Cocker of Pulp walked on stage and "shook his bum" at the audience as a protest against Jackson's "Christ-like figure" image. "The Brit Awards are Britain's answer to the Junos or the Grammys." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MN97R6cxQVw
