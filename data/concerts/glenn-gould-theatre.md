# concert - Glenn Gould Theatre

## curiosities
**Recording Location for "Friends & Total Strangers"** : The Glenn Gould Theatre, located within the CBC headquarters in downtown Toronto, served as the recording venue for The Trews' live acoustic album, "Friends & Total Strangers." The choice of venue reflected the acoustic nature of the recording, inspired by a successful acoustic set in Japan.
