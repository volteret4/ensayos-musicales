# concert - The Mud Club

## curiosities
**New York Punk Scene Hub – Early Gathering Place** : The Mud Club was a significant venue for the original punk scene in New York. It attracted a small community of "weirdos" who congregated in the "uglier end of town" to foster the burgeoning punk movement. "The original punk scene in New York consisted of a few dozen weirdos who hung out at places like CBGB, the Mud Club, and Maxx's Kansas City, in the uglier end of town." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
