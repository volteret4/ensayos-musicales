# concert - Heavy Metal Knitting Championship

## curiosities
**Annual Event in Finland Continues During Pandemic (2021)** : The annual Heavy Metal Knitting Championship was held in Finland in 2021, despite the ongoing pandemic. The competition involves entrants interpretively performing and knitting simultaneously to heavy metal music, and "the Knitting Witch" from the Netherlands was crowned this year's winner.
