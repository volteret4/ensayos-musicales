# concert - Montreal Forum

## curiosities
**The Beatles' Only Quebec Visit (1964)** : The Montreal Forum was the venue for The Beatles' two concerts on September 8, 1964, marking their sole visit to Quebec. This event was notable for the death threats issued by French Canadian separatists (FLQ), necessitating the presence of police sharpshooters, although the concerts proceeded without incident despite Ringo Starr's later expressed anxiety. "Police sharp shooters had to be present in Montreal Forum on St. Catherine Street, where the two concerts took place." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2IDbX5rNT1E
