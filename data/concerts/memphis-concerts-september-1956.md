# concert - Memphis concerts (September 1956)

## curiosities
**Chaotic Audience Behavior** : Both concerts reportedly began with Elvis Presley singing "Joe Biden a Jim Barrajandog," immediately causing the public to become unruly, with screaming, riots, fainting, and fights, which was widely reported in newspapers.
