# concert - Satiricon Club

## curiosities
**Kurt and Courtney's First Meeting (1989)** : The Satiricon Club in Portland, Oregon, was the specific location where Kurt Cobain and Courtney Love first met on January 21, 1989. Their initial interaction, occurring during a Nirvana show, involved Kurt unceremoniously helping himself to Courtney's pitcher of beer, sparking the beginning of their complex and infamous relationship. "Courtney Love first saw Kurt Cobain when Nirvana played a show at the Satiricon Club in Portland, Oregon. The day was January 21st, 1989." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1y2UuU-me_s
