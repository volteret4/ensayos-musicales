# concert - Massey Hall

## curiosities
**The Tragically Hip Performance for MCA Records** : The Tragically Hip performed a two-song set at Massey Hall in Toronto, which impressed Bruce Dickinson, the new president of MCA Records. This performance was crucial in securing the band's record deal after Dickinson flew from New York to see them. "he flew into Toronto from New York and was impressed with the two songs set the band showcased at Massey Hall." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnz_Ycd_q7s
**Venue for Live Recording** : Massey Hall was the location where Matthew Good's 2008 live album, `Live at Massey Hall`, was recorded. "That recording was from a 2008 live record entitled Live at Massie Hall." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=--g0gugy3ZE
