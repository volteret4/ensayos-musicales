# concert - Rainbow

## curiosities
**Ian Curtis's On-Stage Collapse** : On April 4, 1980, during a show with The Stranglers at the London venue called the Rainbow, Ian Curtis suffered three epileptic attacks within the first five songs of Joy Division's set before completely collapsing, forcing the band to stop.
