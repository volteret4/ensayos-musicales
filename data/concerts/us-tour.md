# concert - US Tour

## curiosities
**Extended Duration and Living Conditions** : Crabwork embarked on a US tour that lasted for nearly a year. During this extensive period, the band members received a salary and were required to share hotel rooms, indicating relatively austere living conditions despite the band's commercial success.
