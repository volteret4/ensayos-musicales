# concert - Reading Festival

## curiosities
**Britpop Promotion** : The annual Reading Festival played a crucial role in the rise of Britpop by rebranding itself as an "indie fest" and offering "huge exposure" to many of the new and exciting Britpop bands. "The organizers at the annual Reading Festival were a big help too. The festival was reborn as an indie fest offering huge exposure to some of these new exciting bands." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSvoppf1dGY
**Inspiration for Lollapalooza (1990)** : In the summer of 1990, Perry Farrell of Jane's Addiction attended the Reading Festival in the UK. He was greatly inspired by the large crowd singing along to The Pixies' song "Debaser," which led him to plan a similar festival for America, eventually becoming Lollapalooza. "Perry Farrell of Jane's Addiction was with some associates at the Reading Festival in the UK, and he was blown away by the crowd singing along to the Pixies as they played their song Debacer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rf1tFWQg51Y
**Pixies Performance Inspiration (1990)** : In 1990, Perry Farrell observed a Pixies set at the Reading Festival in England where a crowd of kids were "losing their minds," which directly inspired him to create the Lollapalooza Festival for the US. "What he saw was a field full of kids losing their minds to a pixie set at the 1990 Reading Festival in England." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0hnBC0y59QA
