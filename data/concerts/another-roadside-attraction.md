# concert - Another Roadside Attraction

## curiosities
**Artist-Curated Touring Festival** : "Another Roadside Attraction" was a traveling caravan festival curated by The Tragically Hip at the peak of their powers, taking a bunch of their friends on the road. It was staged three times: in 1993, 1995, and 1997. "Their traveling caravan was called another roadside attraction. It was staged three times, 1993, 1995, and 1997." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Defunct Status** : Another Roadside Attraction is now defunct. "Edgefest, little fair, summer salt, and another roadside attraction are all defunct." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Hip-Organized Festival** : The Tragically Hip organized and headlined their own festival called Another Roadside Attraction. "their own another roadside attraction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Lollapalooza Imitator** : Another Roadside Attraction was among the imitator festivals spawned by Lollapalooza's success in the 1990s. "This spawned imitators, edgefest, little fair, summer salt, another roadside attraction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**The Tragically Hip Initiative** : Another Roadside Attraction was a Canadian-based summer music festival organized by The Tragically Hip, which took place in 1993, 1995, and 1997. It was one of several festivals contributing to the vibrant Canadian music scene of the 1990s. "There was another roadside attraction, which was organized by the Trajically Hip in 1993, 1995 and 1997." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PGgNCaW4xo
**The Tragically Hip's Cross-Canada Festival (1993)** : In 1993, The Tragically Hip initiated and staged their own cross-Canada caravan festival named "Another Roadside Attraction." This festival, launched during a period of financial abundance in the music industry, featured a diverse lineup of other artists, including Matthew Sweet, The Rheostatics, Hot House Flowers, Midnight Oil, Daniel Lanois, World Party, Ziggy Marley, and Wilco. "Back when the music industry had all kinds of money to burn, the hip stage their own cross-Canada caravan festival called another roadside attraction. The first one hit the road in 1993 and Matthew Sweet, the reostatics, hot house flowers, midnight oil, Daniel Landwine, world party, Ziggy Marley, Wilcoe and others went along for the ride." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PnLdgohA5gY
**The Tragically Hip's Touring Festival** : Another Roadside Attraction was a touring festival hosted by The Tragically Hip in 1993, 1995, and 1997. It featured a mix of Canadian performers such as Daniel Lanois, Spirit of the West, and Rheostatics, alongside international acts including Midnight Oil, Blues Traveler, Matthew Sweet, and Sheryl Crow. "The tragically hip had lined another roadside attraction in 1993, 95 and 97." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2kYleeMnl8
