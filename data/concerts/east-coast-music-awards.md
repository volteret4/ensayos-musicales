# concert - East Coast Music Awards

## curiosities
**Sandbox's Recognition** : Sandbox won three East Coast Music Awards. "Sandbox won three East Coast Music Awards and were nominated for a Juno before they broke up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUQ2vZzmRiI
**Sloan's Unofficial Showcase** : During the East Coast Music Awards, Sloan organized an unofficial showcase at an art gallery. This event was a strategic move to invite record label representatives, including Cam Carpenter, leading to their eventual signing with Geffen Records. "And I think we were going to set up our own show at an art gallery. It was kind of like not official showcase." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mbic-70ZhpE
