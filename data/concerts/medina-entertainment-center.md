# concert - Medina Entertainment Center

## curiosities
**Scott Weiland's Canceled Performance (December 3, 2015)** : Scott Weiland was scheduled to play at the Medina Entertainment Center on the night of December 3, 2015, in Minnesota. However, he was found dead on his tour bus earlier that day, leading to the cancellation of the performance. "He was set to play the Medina entertainment center that night and Scott was still in his room at the back of the bus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP75_gbZzIU
