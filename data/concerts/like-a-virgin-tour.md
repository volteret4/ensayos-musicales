# concert - Like A Virgin Tour

## curiosities
**Beastie Boys as Opening Act** : The Beastie Boys opened for Madonna's 1984 "Like A Virgin" tour. Madonna had initially wanted Run DMC to open, but they were too expensive, leading her to select the Beastie Boys as a more affordable alternative, with assistance from Russell Simmons. "Madonna, who threw a mix up with Simmons, ended up with the Beastie Boys as her opener for her 1984 like a virgin tour." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cYwk-B3qwwk
