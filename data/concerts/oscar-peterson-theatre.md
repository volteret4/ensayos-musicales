# concert - Oscar Peterson Theatre

## curiosities
**Venue for Japan Consulate Show** : The Oscar Peterson Theatre in Tokyo was the location for a "really versatile bill" of Canadian artists, including The Trews, Bedouin Soundclash, The Rural Alberta Advantage (as Wooden Hands), The Russian Futurists, and Alex Cuba, performing at the Canadian consulate in Japan. Constraints at this venue led The Trews to perform an acoustic set. "We went and did this like really versatile bill at the Oscar Peterson theater over in in Tokyo." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
