# concert - Glastonbury (2009)

## curiosities
**Springsteen's Endorsement of The Gaslight Anthem** : During the 2009 Glastonbury Festival, Bruce Springsteen joined The Gaslight Anthem on stage during their performance, which was considered a significant endorsement of the band's work. "In 2009, when the band was performing at Glastonbury, Springsteen joined them on stage. You cannot get a bigger endorsement than that." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I
