# concert - Cromañón

## curiosities
**Extensive Safety Failures** : The Cromañón fire disaster was severely exacerbated by critical safety failures: four of the six exit doors were locked, two emergency exits were blocked, there were no fire safety systems or alarms, and 10 of the 15 fire extinguishers on site were non-functional. These deficiencies contributed significantly to the high death toll and injury count. "Four of the six doors leading out were locked. Two emergency exits were blocked. There were no fire safety systems, including no alarm." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
**Fatal Pyrotechnics Fire (Buenos Aires, December 30, 2004)** : On December 30, 2004, a fire at the Cromañón venue in Buenos Aires during a Kelaero's rock concert resulted in 194 deaths and 1,432 injuries. The venue, with a legal capacity of 1,500, was hosting 4,000 people. Pyrotechnics ignited insulating foam on the roof and a blasting net filled with decorations, causing "fire to literally start raining" on the crowd within seconds. "This was December 30th, 2004. The problem was that the legal capacity of this venue was just 1500." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
