# concert - Great Moon Gathering

## curiosities
**Influence on The Tragically Hip's Activism** : This annual educational conference, held by various Indigenous communities along James Bay, was attended by The Tragically Hip as part of their environmental activism. Their participation here eventually led to the song "Good Night at a Wapasca" on the "Plan A" album and influenced Gord Downie's broader advocacy for Indigenous people. "The band's environmental activism led them to the Great Moon Gathering." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnz_Ycd_q7s
