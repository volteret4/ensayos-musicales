# concert - Horseshoe Tavern

## curiosities
**Fully Completely Record Release Show (September 13, 1992)** : The Tragically Hip hosted an invitation-only record release show for *Fully Completely* at the Horseshoe Tavern in Toronto on September 13, 1992, roughly three weeks prior to the album's official release. The complete audio recording of this performance was never publicly available until its inclusion in the 2014 Deluxe Reissue of the album, having been preserved in the band's archives. Additionally, Chris Sangerides, the album's producer, attended a show at the Horseshoe Tavern to see The Hip live before agreeing to produce the record. "That recording, made at an invitation only record release show, has never been available until the special 2014 Deluxe Reissue of the album." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
**Interview Location** : The interview for this transcript, involving Neil Osborne, Brad Merritt, Matt Johnson, and David Genn of 5440, took place in the basement of the Horseshoe Tavern in Toronto. "Neil Brad, Matt and David all took part in this chat in the basement of the horseshoe tavern in Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W9gdKha0PLg
**The Tragically Hip Record Deal Showcase** : The Tragically Hip showcased their material at the Horseshoe Tavern in Toronto, a performance that led to their signing with MCA Records. "The tragically hip was signed to MCA Records after they showcased their stuff at the horseshoe tavern in Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oMBmdfrA47Q
