# concert - Dire Straits Tour

## curiosities
**Philips Sponsorship for "Brothers in Arms"** : The Dire Straits tour supporting their digitally recorded album "Brothers in Arms" was sponsored by Philips, one of the co-developers of the compact disc. This sponsorship was a marketing effort to promote the new CD format, and the band's leader, Mark Knopfler, even appeared in a TV commercial for CDs.
