# concert - The Smiling Buddha

## curiosities
**Lost Grassroots Venue in Vancouver** : The Smiling Buddha was a staple grassroots live venue in Vancouver, mentioned by Matthew Good as one of the spaces whose disappearance reflects the broader diminishment of the city's arts community.
