# concert - Hard Rock Cafe, Toronto

## curiosities
**U2 "Joshua Tree" Interview Venue** : On March 12, 2017, an interview with U2 co-producer Daniel N. Waw about the making of The Joshua Tree took place here. The event was held in front of a live audience of hardcore U2 fans and involved a track-by-track examination of the album. "It was in front of a live audience of hardcore YouTube fans at the Hard Rock Cafe in Toronto and we're examining the record track by track." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VR5Fncdc2kQ
