# concert - Virgin Megastore (Times Square)

## curiosities
**Last Gathering of Original Ramones** : On July 20, 1999, all four original members of the Ramones — Joey, Dee Dee, Johnny, and Tommy — appeared together for the final time at an autograph session held here. "The last time they were all together was on July 20, 1999, when they appeared for an autograph session at the Virgin Megastore in Times Square." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
