# concert - U2 360 World Tour

## curiosities
**Blackberry Sponsorship** : The U2 360 World Tour, noted as one of the most expensive tours ever staged, was underwritten by Research In Motion and its Blackberry brand. This exemplified how massive tours became reliant on corporate sponsorship to cover immense upfront capital requirements.
