# concert - Piper's Pub

## curiosities
**Popular Antigonish Venue** : Piper's Pub was recognized as the most popular bar in Antigonish, Nova Scotia, frequented by students. The Trews (as One-eyed Trouser) aspired to play there often during their early years performing cover gigs. "Piper's Pub was like the most popular bar where all the students went." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
