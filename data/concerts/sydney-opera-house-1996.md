# concert - Sydney Opera House (1996)

## curiosities
**Anniversary and DVD Release Catalyst (2005)** : The 10-year anniversary of this farewell performance, along with the planned release of its DVD, played a role in reconnecting the members of Crowded House, especially after the funeral of Paul Hester. "The funeral coupled with the 10 year anniversary of the Sydney Opera House performance, the DVD was coming out to see. A reconnection began." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
**Crowded House Farewell Gig for Charity (1996)** : On November 24, 1996, Crowded House performed a large farewell concert on the steps of the famous Sydney Opera House. The event was held to raise funds for the Sydney Children's Hospital, which was experiencing a financial crisis. "So on November 24th of 1996, Crowded house did their part by performing a giant farewell gig on the steps of the famous Sydney Opera House." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DJGFUlQTUhQ
