## curiosities
**Site of First Official CD Recordings (1981)** : The first official recordings on Compact Disc took place at the Salzburg Classical Music Festival in 1981. This early adoption by the classical music world, particularly championed by conductor Herbert von Karajan, helped validate the new digital format.
