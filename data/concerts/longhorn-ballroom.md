# concert - Longhorn Ballroom

## curiosities
**Sex Pistols Performance and Historical Connection** : The Sex Pistols played at the Longhorn Ballroom in Dallas during their controversial 1977 US tour. In its previous incarnation as the Carousel Club, the venue was run by Jack Ruby, the mob-connected individual who famously killed Lee Harvey Oswald live on television in 1963. "In Dallas, the sex pistols played the long-horn ballroom.
