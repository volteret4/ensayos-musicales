# concert - St. Patrick's Day Parade in Dublin

## curiosities
**Early Performance for Larry Mullen – Major Honor** : Before he even reached high school, Larry Mullen had the significant honor of performing in the St. Patrick's Day parade in Dublin. This performance was part of his early musical experiences, alongside playing in a band for the local post office that toured the country.
