## curiosities
**Birthplace of Chicago House Music** : The Warehouse was a club where DJ Frankie Knuckles spun "awesome sets" in Chicago, leading to the development of a distinct Chicago style of electronic dance music. This style soon became known as House Music, making The Warehouse a pivotal location in its origins.
