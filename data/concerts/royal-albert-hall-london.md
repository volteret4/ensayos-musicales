# concert - Royal Albert Hall, London

## curiosities
**Oasis MTV Unplugged Taping (August 23, 1996)** : The Royal Albert Hall in London was the venue for Oasis's planned MTV Unplugged performance on August 23, 1996. The event was a disaster, marked by turbulent rehearsals, a refusal by Liam Gallagher to sing, and his subsequent heckling of Noel Gallagher from a balcony, ultimately leading to the live album's cancellation. "Nole and Liam came to blows several times, and when the night of the taping arrived, which was August 23, 1996, Liam said, not can't sing tonight, I have a sore throat. But rather than cancel, the show went on, with Nole handling all the vocals. Liam, meanwhile, was seen up in a balcony, drinking beer and heckling Nole. MTV was furious all this time and expense ruined by Liam's little drama play." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gdWLtMy4TBU
