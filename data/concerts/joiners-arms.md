# concert - Joiners Arms

## curiosities
**Location of First Public Oasis Sibling Brawl (March 29, 1994)** : The Joiners Arms club in Southampton was the site of the first documented public altercation between Noel and Liam Gallagher. During a show, Noel punched Liam in the face and chased him off stage, marking an early instance of their notorious sibling rivalry. "The first documented evidence of a public war between Noel and Liam was at a show at a club called Joiners Arms in Southampton on March 29, 1994." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_b3KRS-Yx_s
