# concert - The Whiskey (Sunset Boulevard, Los Angeles)

## curiosities
**Mighty Joe Young's First Gig Venue** : The Whiskey on Sunset Boulevard in Los Angeles was the location of Mighty Joe Young's (the precursor to Stone Temple Pilots) very first public performance in August 1990. "The first gig was in August of 1990 at the whiskey on Sunset Boulevard in Los Angeles." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r6vVw66-gmI
