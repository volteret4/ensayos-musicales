## curiosities
**Nikolai Fraiture Backstage Incident** : During an early show at Don Hills, where The Strokes opened for Girl Harbor, bassist Nikolai Fraiture was photographed having sex backstage in the basement. Girl Harbor subsequently used this photo for a flyer to promote The Strokes' next gig, with the caption, "this is what happened at our last show." "This was at a venue called Don Hills. Nik was caught having sex backstage down on the basement and was photographed." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jEfofyobGiI
