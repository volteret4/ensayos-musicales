# concert - Toronto Rock and Roll Revival

## curiosities
**Origin of Concert Lighters (September 13, 1969)** : The tradition of audiences holding up lighters (now cell phones) at concerts originated at this event at Varsity Stadium. MC Kim Fowley asked the 20,000-strong audience to create a "candlelit vibe" for John Lennon's entrance, who was suffering from stage fright, by holding up lighters, matches, or any fire source. "His idea was for everyone to hold up some fire, lighters, matches, whatever, to create a candlelit vibe for John's entrance." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pyoCg3jDzmA
