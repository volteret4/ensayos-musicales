# concert - Free Comic Book Day

## curiosities
**Inspiration for Record Store Day** : Free Comic Book Day, an event held on the first Saturday in May offering special issues to boost comic book sales, served as the inspiration for the creation of Record Store Day. "In an effort to boost comic book sales, a bunch of distributors and stores established something called Free Comic Book Day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XM1RvL7Kdts
