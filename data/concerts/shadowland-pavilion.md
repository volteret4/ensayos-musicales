# concert - Shadowland Pavilion

## curiosities
**First Public Performance of an Electric Guitar** : On Halloween night, 1932, at the Shadowland Pavilion in Wichita, Kansas, Gage Brewer publicly played a Rickenbacker A25 for the very first time. The gig was promoted as a scientific novelty due to the futuristic nature of anything powered by electricity. "And on Halloween night, 1932, at the Shadowland Pavilion in Wichita, Kansas, he played it in public for the very first time. Because anything powered by electricity was so new and futuristic, the gig was also promoted as a scientific novelty, a new achievement in technology." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6cXyaHALBQ
