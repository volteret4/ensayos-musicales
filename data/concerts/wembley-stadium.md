# concert - Wembley Stadium

## curiosities
**Foo Fighters' Landmark Performance (2008)** : In the summer of 2008, Wembley Stadium in London hosted a Foo Fighters concert where the band performed for 86,000 fans. This event underscored the band's immense popularity and the continued importance of large-scale live performances for major rock acts.
