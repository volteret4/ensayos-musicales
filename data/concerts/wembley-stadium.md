# concert - Wembley Stadium

## curiosities
**Foo Fighters' Landmark Performance (2008)** : In the summer of 2008, Wembley Stadium in London hosted a Foo Fighters concert where the band performed for 86,000 fans. This event underscored the band's immense popularity and the continued importance of large-scale live performances for major rock acts. "By the summer of 2008, the Foo's were able to play in front of 86,000 fans at Wembley Stadium in London." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-CT7yYXonIk
**Muse's Aspirational Performance** : Muse adopted a philosophy of playing every small gig as if it were a performance at Wembley Stadium. This mindset reflected their ambition to achieve major stadium success. "if we want to make it to Wembley, we got to play every gig like it's Wembley." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw ← wembley-stadium
**Pink Floyd's "Animals" Tour (March 1977)** : In March 1977, Pink Floyd performed a series of five shows at Wembley Stadium in London as part of their tour for the *Animals* album. These concerts were characterized by the band's signature large inflatable pig. A review by Ed Jones in *The Spectator* criticized the events as "porcine grossness" and described the band as cultivating anonymity on stage, acting as "little more than backing musicians" for their elaborate presentation gimmicks. "He's about a series of five shows that Pink Floyd did at Wembley Stadium in London on their tour for their animals record.
