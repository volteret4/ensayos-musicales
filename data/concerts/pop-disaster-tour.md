# concert - Pop Disaster Tour

## curiosities
**Blink 182 and Green Day Pairing** : This tour paired Blink 182, who were at a career high, with Green Day, whose career seemed to be on the downside. It was a strategic move to revitalize Green Day's career, mirroring the "monsters of rock" concept for metal bands. "Blink was paired with Green Day on something called the Pop Disaster Tour." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0
**Impact on Blink 182** : Green Day's performances during the Pop Disaster Tour compelled Blink 182 to enhance their own live shows, thereby making Blink 182 a better band. "Green Day's performance is forced Blink to up their game in concert. They made Blink a better band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_tkLRJiOBu0
**Unexpected Success and Revitalization** : The pairing of Blink 182 and Green Day on this tour worked "better than anyone could have hoped." It introduced Green Day to a new, younger audience, helping them to be accepted as "punk elder statesmen" and leading to their creative resurgence with "American Idiot."
