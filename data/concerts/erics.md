# concert - Erics

## curiosities
**Liverpool Hot Club** : Erics was a popular club in Liverpool where Orchestral Manoeuvres in the Dark (OMD) began playing their electronic music, contributing to the local scene. "They started playing in and around Liverpool, especially at a hot club called Erics." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
**Liverpool's Pivotal Club (Unspecified)** : Erics was a famous basement club in Liverpool that hosted a wide array of influential bands including The Clash, Sex Pistols, Devo, Pere Ubu, The Cure, and XTC. It had an "open door policy" on Thursday nights, allowing aspiring local bands, like OMD for their first gig, to perform for free. "We used to go to a club in Liverpool called Erics, which was great. A very famous club. All of the, all of the, all of the most interesting bands played there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0fQQubt9UTI
