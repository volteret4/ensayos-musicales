# concert - Warp Tour

## curiosities
**Live Recording Efforts** : During their Warp Tour dates, Weezer used an eight-track mobile unit to capture some of their performances, collecting soundtrack recordings that included songs which would later appear on official releases, like "Hashpipe." "They had an eight track mobile unit capturing some of their performances, but most of what we got were soundtrack recordings." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DoJa8jcrlUA
**Sum 41's 2016 Performance** : Sum 41 performed on the Warp Tour in 2016, following the release of their album "13 Voices." "a record that saw them head out on the warp tour in 2016." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=puFgo2ft7hQ
**Weezer's Nine-Date Participation (2000)** : After gaining excitement from the Fuji Rock Festival, Weezer joined the Warp Tour for nine dates in the summer of 2000. During these performances, they played songs from "The Blue Album" and "Pinkerton," a handful of B-sides, and up to 14 brand new songs that fans anticipated would be on a third album. "They were so jazzed that they even joined up with the warp tour for nine dates that summer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DoJa8jcrlUA
