# concert - Elvis Mondays

## curiosities
**Continued Bookings** : Following their successful debut, William New continued to book Pezz for multiple Elvis Mondays nights. "And William kept booking us and we ended up doing a bunch of those Elvis Mondays nights." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Old El Mocambo Debut** : Pezz received their first major break in downtown Toronto by performing at Elvis Mondays, a recurring event hosted by William New at the Old El Mocambo. They secured a last-minute slot when another band cancelled. "So our first big break downtown was we gave this tape to a guy named William New who had the Elvis Mondays at the Old Elmucongo Monday nights downstairs. And like months went by, he never called. And finally, we got a phone call on a Monday at like six o'clock. Last minute, he said, I had a band cancel at nine o'clock, why don't you guys come up and play." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Pez's Toronto Breakthrough** : "Elvis Mondays" at the Old Elmucongo was where Pez (later Billy Talent) got their first significant break in downtown Toronto. William New, who ran the event, booked them last-minute after a cancellation, leading to multiple bookings and networking opportunities for the band. "Our first big break downtown was we gave this tape to a guy named William New, who had the oldest Mondays at the Old Elmucongo. Monday nights." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4QJMx1LES8
**Start of Toronto Presence** : The band's performance at Elvis Mondays at the Old El Mocambo was considered the beginning of their presence in the Toronto music scene for the four members from Mississauga. "And that, I think that's kind of what started our whole Toronto thing for four guys from Susaga." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
