# concert - Hammersmith Odeon (July 3rd, 1973)

## curiosities
**David Bowie's Ziggy Stardust Retirement Show** : This London venue hosted David Bowie's highly theatrical "quits show," where he announced the retirement of his hugely popular Ziggy Stardust persona. The event, captured by documentary filmmaker DA Pennebaker, marked the end of an era.
