# concert - Front 242 show in Chicago

## curiosities
**Al Jourgensen's Epiphany** : This particular Front 242 concert in Chicago is legendary for being the location where Al Jourgensen of Ministry experienced a transformative epiphany, which reportedly led him to abandon his "meek, techno pop ways" and transition Ministry into a more aggressive industrial sound. "But legend has it that it was at a Front II-IV II show in Chicago that Alan Durgensen had as epiphany, of sorts, the one that showed him the error of his meek, techno pop ways." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zSFhhEaiYwE
