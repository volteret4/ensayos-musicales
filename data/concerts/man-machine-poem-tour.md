# concert - Man-Machine Poem Tour

## curiosities
**Final Kingston Show (2016)** : The tour concluded with a final show in Kingston on Saturday, August 20, 2016. This concert was broadcast commercial-free on CBC and attracted an official audience of approximately 12 million viewers, with an estimated total viewership closer to 20 million nationwide. "That final show in Kingston, shown commercial free on the CBC on Saturday, August 20th, officially attracted about 12 million viewers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Gord's Performance** : Despite severe health challenges, Gord surprised everyone by completing all scheduled shows and even adding new ones. However, his stage movements were subdued, and teleprompters were used for lyrics, though his powerful personality remained intact. "But if you went to any of those shows, you'll have noticed that his movements on stage were much more subdued. He was not working at full power physically." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Medical Precautions** : Due to Gord Downie's condition, a dedicated EMS team was hired for the tour, and his surgeon, Dr. Douglas DJ Cook, was present backstage to monitor for potential seizures. "A separate EMS team was hired just for Gord to go on tour. Dr. Cook, Gord's surgeon, was in the wings." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**National Galvanization** : The tour became a profoundly unifying event for Canada, inspiring countless cancer treatment fundraisers and fostering a widespread sense of community. "That tour galvanized a nation, bringing us closer together than just about any other event I can think of in my lifetime." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Response to Cancer Diagnosis** : This tour was announced on May 25, 2016, the day after Gord Downie's incurable brain tumor was publicly announced, leading to a period of denial for fans. Tickets sold out across Canada in minutes on June 3rd. "one day later, the hip announced the man-machine poem tour. Now, from our perspective, the news was dire, but hey, it couldn't be that bad yet, right?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Start Date** : The tour was set to begin in Victoria on July 22, 2016. "Everything was set to start in Victoria on July 22nd." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
