# concert - Norwich Art Center

## curiosities
**Site of "For Real" Incident (May 15, 1991)** : The Norwich Art Center hosted a Manic Street Preachers show on May 15, 1991, which became infamous as the location where Richie Edwards carved the words "for real" into his arm during an interview with NME reviewer Steve LeMack. This act garnered significant media attention for the band. "On May 15, 1991, the man explain to show at the Norwich Art Center." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCwgvHg86Oc
