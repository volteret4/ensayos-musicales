# concert - Fox Theatre

## curiosities
**Soundgarden's Final Performance (May 17, 2017)** : The Fox Theatre in Detroit was the venue for Soundgarden's last concert with Chris Cornell on May 17, 2017. The show's quality was debated, with some noting Cornell's struggles while others found the band "on fire."
