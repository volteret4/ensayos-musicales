# concert - Rock and Roll Hall of Fame

## curiosities
**Nirvana's Induction Performance** : Nirvana's induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame featured a performance with female vocalists Lorde, St. Vincent, Kim Gordon, and Joan Jett. This event was referenced by The Tragically Hip as a precedent for their own decision to perform with Feist at the Juno Awards. "Nirvana did it with Lord St. Vincent, Kim Gordon and Joan Jett when they were inducted to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. I thought that was a brilliant move." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G9H9q7BYqEA
