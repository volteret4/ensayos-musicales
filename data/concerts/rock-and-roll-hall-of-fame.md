## curiosities
**Clash Induction and Failed Reunion Prospect (March 10, 2003)** : The induction ceremony for The Clash into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in New York on March 10, 2003, was seen as "the best ever prospect for a clash reunion." There were strong indications that the band would perform up to three songs, but Joe Strummer's death in December 2002 meant he never attended the ceremony. **Martha and the Van Dellers Induction** : Martha Reeves, Rosalind Ashford, Betty Kelly, Annette Beard, and Lois Reeves of Martha and the Van Dellers were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1995. This made them only the second all-female group to receive this honor. **Nirvana's Induction Performance** : Nirvana's induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame featured a performance with female vocalists Lorde, St. Vincent, Kim Gordon, and Joan Jett. This event was referenced by The Tragically Hip as a precedent for their own decision to perform with Feist at the Juno Awards.
