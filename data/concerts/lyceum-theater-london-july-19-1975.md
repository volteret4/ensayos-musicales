# concert - Lyceum Theater, London (July 19, 1975)

## curiosities
**Bob Marley Live Recording** : Bob Marley and The Wailers recorded their "Live" album during a performance at the Lyceum Theater in London on July 19, 1975, with the entire event captured using the Rolling Stones Mobile Recording Studio. "Bob Marley and the Whalers recorded live at the Lyceum Theater in London on July 19, 1975 on a record called Live, and it was all captured using the Rolling Stones mobile recording truck." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk
