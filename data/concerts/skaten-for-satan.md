# concert - Skaten for Satan

## curiosities
**Warped Tour and Ozzfest Merger (1998)** : "Skaten for Satan" was the name given to a joint event in 1998 where the Warped Tour merged with Ozzfest, creating an "interesting crowd" for the combined festival. "In 1998, the Warped Tour emerged with Oz Fest for something that was called Skaten for Satan. That was an interesting crowd." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
