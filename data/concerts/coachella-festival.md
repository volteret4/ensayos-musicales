## curiosities
**The Stooges Reunion Performance (2003)** : The surviving members of Iggy Pop and The Stooges performed at the Coachella Festival on April 27, 2003, marking their official reunion after nearly three decades apart. **The Stooges' Reunion Performance (2003)** : The Stooges officially regrouped and performed at the Coachella Festival in California on April 27, 2003. This marked their first performance together after a hiatus of 29 years, two months, and 19 days since their last gig in February 1974. "And so it came to pass that Iggy Pop and the stuages regrouped and performed at the Coachella Festival in California on April 27, 2003." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w-rihv544I8
