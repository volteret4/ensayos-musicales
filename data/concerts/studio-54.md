# concert - Studio 54

## curiosities
**Symbol of 1970s Mainstream Music Scene** : Studio 54 in New York City was a prominent symbol of the mainstream music scene in the mid-1970s, particularly the disco era. Record executives were "too busy going to big stadium shows and getting down at Studio 54" to pay attention to the emerging punk scene.
