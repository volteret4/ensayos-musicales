# concert - Hollywood Athletic Club

## curiosities
**Site of Bauhaus's Reunion Gig (1998)** : The Hollywood Athletic Club was the venue where Bauhaus reunited on stage 15 years and four days after their final show in London. A couple of thousand people paid $55 US each to attend this event. "Baha's appeared on stage at the Hollywood Athletic Club. A couple thousand people gladly played $55 US to be there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Venue for Bauhaus Reunion Gig** : The Hollywood Athletic Club hosted Bauhaus's reunion performance, occurring 15 years and four days after their final London show. Thousands of attendees paid $55 US each for the event, which marked the beginning of their successful reunion tour. "Baha's appeared on stage at the Hollywood Athletic Club.
