# concert - Montreal 1992

## curiosities
**James Hetfield Pyro Accident** : During a 1992 concert in Montreal, James Hetfield of Metallica suffered third-degree burns when he literally caught fire due to an "ill-timed pyro" incident. This event is memorialized in his flaming playing card tattoo. "The flames are in reference to the time he literally caught fire because of some ill-time pyro during a 1992 show in Montreal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
