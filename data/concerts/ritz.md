## curiosities
**P.I.L.'s Disastrous New York Gig (May 15, 1980)** : On May 15, 1980, the New York club the Ritz hosted a Public Image Limited (P.I.L.) concert that turned into one of the most legendary and disastrous concert appearances of the 1980s. The band, ill-prepared and dysfunctional, pantomimed to records behind a screen, leading to audience frustration, insults from Johnny Lydon, and ultimately a riot that ended the 25-minute gig with stage storming and equipment destruction. "So when there was a last minute cancellation at a New York club called the Ritz, P.I.L. jumped at the chance because the fee was $12,000 for two nights." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AymMybE33zU
