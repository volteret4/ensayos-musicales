# concert - The Boardwalk

## curiosities
**"Columbia" Debut Performance** : "Columbia" was first performed in early 1992, either on January 14th or Valentine's Day, during Oasis's fourth gig at The Boardwalk. "Columbia was first performed in early 1992, either on January 14th or on Valentine's Day during the group's fourth gig at the boardwalk." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IhG-WJH9om8
**Early Oasis Gigs** : In late 1990, the group Rain (which would become Oasis) started playing gigs at The Boardwalk in Manchester. "Bring It Down" was a massive hit with audiences when Oasis played this club. "They started playing gigs that have been called the Boardwalk in Manchester in late 1990." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IhG-WJH9om8
