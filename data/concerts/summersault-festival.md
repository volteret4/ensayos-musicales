# concert - Summersault Festival

## curiosities
**Our Lady Peace's Touring Festival** : Summersault Festival was a tour organized by Our Lady Peace in 1998. Its lineup combined Canadian talent like I Mother Earth and Gandharvas (and Matt Good) with international acts such as Garbage, The Crystal Method, and Eve 6.
