# concert - Hammersmith Odeon Theatre

## curiosities
**Final Ziggy Stardust Show (July 2, 1973)** : This London theatre was the venue for David Bowie's final show on the Ziggy Stardust tour, held on July 2, 1973. It was at this location that Steve Jones, who would later form The Sex Pistols, stole equipment on the following day after a night watchman fell asleep. "July 2nd, 1973, all the gear for Bowie's final show on the Ziggy Stardust tour was set up and ready to go at the Hammersmith Odian Theatre in London." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P3xjW0A57Wk
**Ziggy Stardust Farewell Show (1973)** : This London venue was the site of David Bowie's final show on the Ziggy Stardust tour on July 3rd, 1973. It was here that Bowie famously announced the "retirement" of the Ziggy Stardust persona. On the night before the show, July 2nd, 1973, a young Steve Jones (later of the Sex Pistols) broke in and stole equipment. "July 2nd 1973, all the gear for Bowie's final show on the Ziggy Stardust tour was set up and ready to go at the Hammersmith Odian Theatre in London the following day." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sPCrATqBdeA
