# concert - Australian Tour

## curiosities
**Historic Recording Venue for Fully Completely** : Battery Studios, located in the "grimy northwest London neighborhood of Willesden Green," was the recording facility for The Tragically Hip's *Fully Completely* in July 1992. The studio was protected by a large metal gate and possessed a significant historical legacy, having been the site where numerous classic albums were made by artists such as Pink Floyd, Paul McCartney, Black Sabbath, Rod Stewart, The Cure, and The Who. "The tragically hip were in the UK, recording what would be their third full studio album at Battery Studios, a facility protected from the rest of the surrounding grimy northwest London neighborhood of Williston, Green, by a big metal gate." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
**Recording Environment for Fully Completely** : While the building itself was rich with history, the exterior of Battery Studios and its surrounding neighborhood were described as "dreary" and "wet" during the July 1992 recording sessions for *Fully Completely*. The band had initially envisioned recording in the English countryside, expecting "rolling hills and flocks of gentle sheep," a stark contrast to the urban reality. "It may have been dreary on the outside, but the building itself was full of history, pink Floyd, Palmer Cartney, Black Sabbath, Rod Stewart, the Cure, the Who, and dozens more had all made classic albums there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
**Road Testing Songs for Fully Completely** : Prior to entering the studio to record *Fully Completely*, The Tragically Hip undertook a brief tour of Australia. This tour served as a crucial opportunity for the band to "road test" the new songs in front of challenging audiences, helping them refine and tighten their material before the formal recording sessions began. "Second, they were able to squeeze in a quick tour of Australia where they were able to road test the songs in front of some very tough audiences." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vs1YyXgLpTE
