# concert - Hurricane Festival

## curiosities
**Bowie's Heart Attack (2004)** : In June 2004, David Bowie suffered a heart attack on stage at the Hurricane Festival in Germany, which led to an emergency angioplasty and the cancellation of his major tour—the first time he had done so due to health reasons. "In June 2004, he was on stage at the Hurricane Festival in Germany when he felt a pain into shoulder." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yq2A_o9-ulM
**David Bowie's Last Headlining Set (2004)** : David Bowie's final headlining performance took place at Germany's Hurricane Festival on June 25, 2004. "His last headlining set was at Germany's Hurricane Festival on June 25th, 2004." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
