# concert - Elmacombo

## curiosities
**Aquarius Records Discovery of Sum 41** : Sum 41 was playing a show at the Elmacombo when a representative from Aquarius Records attended, leading to Aquarius becoming the first record label to express serious interest in the band. "We were playing a few shows around town. I know we were playing at the Elmacombo one night and somebody from Aquarius Records was there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6L4xKBFEYkE
