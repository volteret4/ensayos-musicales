# concert - Colombian Exhibition of 1893

## curiosities
**Introduction of Ferris Wheel and Algerian Village** : The Colombian Exhibition of 1893, held in Chicago, was a major world exposition that showcased wonders from across the globe, including the introduction of the Ferris wheel. Saul Bloom, an organizer, created an exotic Algerian village display with a dozen dancers, which he promoted on the road. This exhibition was a setting for spontaneous musical innovation when Bloom created a tune for his dancers at a Chicago press club in 1892.
