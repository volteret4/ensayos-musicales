## curiosities
**Birthplace of New Romanticism** : Billies was one of two key British clubs, along with The Blitz Club, that served as the "breeding site" for the New Romantic movement. These clubs were dedicated to new music and fostered the emergence of synth-based groups like Spandau Ballet, Visage, and Culture Club. "One such offshoot was known as New Romantic, which had its roots in a couple of British clubs dedicated to new music. One was called Billies, and the other was the Blitz Club." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
