## curiosities
**Early Pogoing Site (1966)** : The UFO Club in London was a venue for "freak out gigs" by bands like The Pink Floyd in 1966. It is noted as a place where people sometimes engaged in "Pogoing in place," predating the general popularization of the Pogo during the later British punk rock explosion.
