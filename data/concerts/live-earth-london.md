# concert - Live Earth London

## curiosities
**SOS All Stars Performance (2007)** : The Live Earth show in London in 2007 featured a special one-off drummers' group called SOS All Stars. Taylor Hawkins participated alongside Roger Taylor and Chad Smith, coming together for this global environmental awareness event. "This was a one-off drummers group, but together for the live earth show in London in 2007." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA
