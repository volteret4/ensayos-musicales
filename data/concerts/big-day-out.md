# concert - Big Day Out

## curiosities
**Sydney Tragedy and New Rules (2001)** : On January 21, 2001, the Big Day Out festival in Sydney, Australia, which attracted between 55,000 and 65,000 attendees, saw a massive front-of-stage crush during Limp Bizkit's headline set amidst near 50-degree temperatures. This crush caused approximately 30 injuries and led to the death of 15-year-old Jessica Milicic, who succumbed to a heart attack caused by asphyxiation five days later. Although an investigation closed without charges, the incident prompted the implementation of new rules for security and barriers at the festival.
