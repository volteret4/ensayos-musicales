# concert - Bataclan

## curiosities
**Terrorist Attack Target (Paris, November 13, 2015)** : The Bataclan, a sold-out Paris venue with 1500 people attending an Eagles of Death Metal concert, became the target of a coordinated terrorist attack on November 13, 2015. Three gunmen, wearing suicide vests, stormed the building at 9:49 PM, opening fire with AK-47s into the crowd and ultimately killing 89 people, including the band's tour manager.
