# concert - Late Show with David Letterman (2002)

## curiosities
**Audioslave's Debut Performance** : Audioslave made their first public appearance on November 25, 2002, by performing on top of the marquee of the Ed Sullivan Theater as part of an appearance on the *Late Show with David Letterman*.
