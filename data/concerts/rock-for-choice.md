## curiosities
**Pro-Choice Concert Series** : Formed by the band L7 in 1991, Rock for Choice is a pro-choice women's rights group and concert series. Its purpose was to raise money for individuals affected by anti-abortion violence, particularly in response to clinic bombings in the 1980s and early 1990s. The organization continues its work today.
