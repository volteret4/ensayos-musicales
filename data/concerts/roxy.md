## curiosities
**Early Slamming Location (1977)** : The Roxy in Los Angeles was a venue where David Wood, roadie for The Weirdos, would initiate slamming into people when bands started playing in 1977. This club was an early site for the development of aggressive dance styles. "When he'd go to shows at the Roxy in Los Angeles, he'd start slamming into people once the band started.
