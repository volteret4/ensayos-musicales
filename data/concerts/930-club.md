# concert - 930 Club

## curiosities
**Early 5440 Performances and Hootie & the Blowfish's Fandom** : 5440 frequently played at the 930 Club in Washington, DC, during the period of their "Green Record" and "Show Me" albums. Hootie & the Blowfish, prior to their own fame, were regular attendees and fans at these shows, even covering 5440's songs in their own band. "We would play this club in Washington, DC called the 930 Club back on the Green Record and show me. And they came up from University of South Carolina and they were fans." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W9gdKha0PLg
