# concert - Adele World

## curiosities
**Record-Breaking Temporary Residency (Munich, 2024)** : In 2024, Live Nation constructed "Adele World," a temporary pop-up stadium in Munich with an 80,000-person capacity, specifically for Adele's residency. Over a month, she performed twice weekly to 730,000 attendees across 10 shows, setting a new record for a non-Vegas residency by grossing $240 million USD and bringing half a billion euros into the city. "They created Adele World, a place where she could perform twice a week for a month." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GYUuTbOvyak
