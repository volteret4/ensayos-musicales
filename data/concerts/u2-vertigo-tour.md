# concert - U2 Vertigo tour

## curiosities
**Scalping by Wiseguy (2005)** : The 2005 U2 Vertigo tour was heavily impacted by scalping activities carried out by the company Wiseguy. Wiseguy invested $200,000 to acquire 5,000 fan club memberships, which provided early access to discounted tickets. These tickets were then resold at significantly inflated prices, resulting in $2.5 million in profits for Wiseguy. The extensive scalping contributed to fan frustration and difficulty in obtaining tickets for the inner stage ring.
