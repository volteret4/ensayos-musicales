**Catalyst for Jeff Buckley's Career** : The memorial concert held in honor of Tim Buckley, Jeff Buckley's father, was a pivotal event for Jeff. It was at this concert, where he performed and reinterpreted his father's songs, that he first began to generate significant interest in his own musical talent, eventually leading to a record deal.
