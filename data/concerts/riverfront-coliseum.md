# concert - Riverfront Coliseum

## curiosities
**The Who Concert Stampede (Cincinnati, December 3, 1979)** : At a sold-out Who concert at Riverfront Coliseum in Cincinnati on December 3, 1979, 11 people died of asphyxiation and 26 were injured in a stampede outside the venue. The tragedy occurred when approximately 18,348 general admission ticket holders, having waited for hours, rushed the two initially opened doors after hearing music, believing the band had started performing earlier than scheduled.
