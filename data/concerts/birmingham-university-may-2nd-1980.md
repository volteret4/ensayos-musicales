# concert - Birmingham University (May 2nd, 1980)

## curiosities
**Joy Division's Tragic Final Performance** : This recorded concert marked Joy Division's last gig. During the show, singer Ian Curtis fainted on stage due to an epileptic seizure, initially perceived by the audience as part of his unique dancing. Despite being helped off stage, Curtis recovered and returned for the encore, but tragically died by suicide less than two weeks later, making this gig his final stage appearance.
