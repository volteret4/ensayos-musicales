# concert - Serious Moonlight Tour

## curiosities
**Massive Global Success - 1983** : The "Serious Moonlight Tour," accompanying David Bowie's "Let's Dance" album, was a massive global success in 1983. It drew 2.6 million people as it traveled through 16 countries. "Let's Dance was a massive success for Bowie, and drew 2.6 million people to the serious moonlight tour as it wound its way through 16 countries." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rw0uXf5UJvo
