# concert - Knebworth

## curiosities
**Oasis's Triumph (August 10-11, 1996)** : Oasis performed two huge shows at Knebworth on August 10th and 11th, 1996, drawing 125,000 people per night. The demand was so overwhelming that one in every 20 British citizens tried to get tickets, and the event required 3,000 crew members. VIPs were provided Oasis-branded binoculars, and the guest list had 7,000 names. "The biggest single event at that party had to be the two Oasis shows at a place called Nebworth on August 10th and 11th, 1996." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5q5EnZj1FwE
**Symbol of Britpop's Peak** : Oasis's Knebworth concerts were seen as the zenith of Britpop's popularity, demonstrating how the band had gone from unknowns to "Beatles level crazy" fame in just two years. "Like I said, the Britpop party could not get any bigger." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5q5EnZj1FwE
