# concert - Super Bowl 2004 Halftime Show

## curiosities
**Nipplegate Controversy and YouTube's Inspiration** : The Super Bowl halftime show on February 1, 2004, featured Janet Jackson and Justin Timberlake in a performance produced by MTV. During the song "Rock Your Body," Timberlake pulled off part of Jackson's costume, briefly revealing her right nipple, leading to the "Nipplegate" controversy. This incident, and the inability of three PayPal tech guys (Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim) to rewatch the event online, directly inspired them to create YouTube as a video-sharing platform. "The halftime show featured Janet Jackson and Justin Timberlake, which was produced by MTV. At the end of the song, Rock Your Body.
