# concert - The Horseshoe

## curiosities
**Breakthrough Toronto Venue** : The Horseshoe, a prominent venue in Toronto, provided The Trews with one of their first "legit gigs" on a new music night. Their performance led booker Craig Lasky to immediately rebook them, sparking their ambition to regularly return to Toronto. "one of the only like legit gigs we managed to get was Craig Lasky from the horseshoe, booked us on a new music night." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
