# concert - Cotton Bowl

## curiosities
**Site of The Rolling Stones' Netcast Claim (1994)** : On November 18, 1994, The Rolling Stones streamed approximately 20 minutes of their concert performance from the Cotton Bowl in Dallas onto the internet. They claimed this as the first instance of a band in cyberspace, though this claim is challenged by earlier netcasts. "On November 18th, 1994, they dumped about 20 minutes of a performance of their concert at the Cotton Bowl and Dallas onto the internet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gP3oQeYEvso
