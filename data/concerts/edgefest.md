# concert - Edgefest

## curiosities
**Defunct Status** : As of the present, Edgefest is no longer active. "Edgefest, little fair, summer salt, and another roadside attraction are all defunct." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Incarnations** : Edgefest has gone through various incarnations over the years. "the incarnations of Edgefest." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ua93Ui1u6UI
**Linkin Park's Toronto Performance** : Linkin Park performed at Edgefest, held at Dan's View Park in Toronto, on July 12, 2008. A live rendition of their song "Shadow of the Day" from this performance was mentioned. "It's Lincoln Park live at Edgefest at Dan's View Park in Toronto on July 12, 2008." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIlCaKCvylI
**Lollapalooza Imitator** : Edgefest was one of several touring music festivals that emerged in the 1990s, inspired by the success of Lollapalooza. "This spawned imitators, edgefest, little fair, summer salt, another roadside attraction." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Panda Stage Dive Incident (1999)** : At Edgefest in Wilson Park, Barrie in 1999, during a Matthew Good Band performance, Universal Music marketing representative Iber Hamilton, dressed in a giant panda costume, attempted a stage dive from the 10-12 foot high stage. The crowd parted, causing him to land face down, sustaining a bleeding nose and being kicked by some audience members. The event was during the song "Everything's Automatic" and inspired the artwork for the "Loser Anthem" EP. "In 1999, the Matthew Good Band played Edgefest where songs from Beautiful Midnight, the third album, were road tested." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-s0gwXY_CZQ
**Pioneering Canadian Rock Festival** : The first Edgefest show in 1987 pioneered the concept of a mostly Canadian summer rock music festival. By the early 1990s, it became an annual event, expanding to four festivals in 1995, and then national (1997-1999) playing eight cities with predominantly Canadian lineups (Our Lady Peace, I Mother Earth, Finger Eleven, Gob, Sloan, Matt Good, The Tea Party), while also featuring international acts like Green Day and Foo Fighters. "The idea of an all-Canadian, or at least mostly Canadian, summer-all-rock music festival, and we traced back to the first Edgefest show in 1987." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2kYleeMnl8
**STP Reunion Performance (2008)** : Stone Temple Pilots performed at Edgefest in Toronto in 2008 as part of their reunion tour. Scott Weiland arrived separately from his bandmates to manage internal tensions, and his performance was noted for its confidence and prowess. "I held book a reunited Stone Temple Pilots for Edgefest to dance with Park and Toronto back in 2008." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfTq9d2YadY
**Toronto Argonauts Cheerleaders** : Before The Matthew Good Band's main stage performance at Edgefest 1999 in Wilson Park, Barrie, various costumed characters were invited on stage, including the Toronto Argonauts cheerleaders. "I think there were some Argo cheerleaders. Yeah, the Toronto organo cheerleaders started it off with time." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-s0gwXY_CZQ
