# concert - Today (ITV)

## curiosities
**Bill Grundy as Host** : This independent ITV talk show was hosted by Bill Grundy, who was a prominent TV personality, likened to "the Oprah of his day," and known for being the first TV host to present The Beatles in 1962. "And one of those big stars was a guiding Bill Grundy, who hosted a separate time talk show on ITV called Today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YaYaFW2q6qI
**Sex Pistols Scandal (December 1, 1976)** : On December 1, 1976, at 6:15 pm, the Sex Pistols appeared as last-minute guests, replacing Queen. The show, which was live and without tape delay, saw the intoxicated band members swear repeatedly on air. This incident abruptly ended Bill Grundy's TV career by 6:18 pm, caused widespread national shock and outrage, and catapulted punk rock into the public consciousness. "It was 6.15pm Wednesday, December 1, 1976. By 6.18pm, Bill Grundy's TV career was over, and everyone was talking about those filthy young men on the telly." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YaYaFW2q6qI
