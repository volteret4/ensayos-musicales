# concert - Astoria, London

## curiosities
**Richie Edwards' Final Performance (December 21, 1994)** : The Astoria in London was the venue for Richie Edwards' last known performance with the Manic Street Preachers on December 21, 1994. This gig was the culmination of a three-night stand, during which he notably smashed his guitar and then proceeded to beat himself over the head with the broken instrument. "Richie's last appearance with the manics was three weeks later at a gig at the Astoria in London, December 21, 1994." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCwgvHg86Oc
