# concert - London University

## curiosities
**Early Smiths Performance (1983)** : In 1983, London University was the venue for one of The Smiths' first public concerts. This event was notable for being attended by key figures such as John Peel and Ballou Waters, both of whom would later play significant roles in the band's career and Morrissey's lyrical inspiration.
