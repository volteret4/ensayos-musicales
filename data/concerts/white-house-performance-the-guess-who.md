# concert - White House Performance (The Guess Who)

## curiosities
**Controversial Performance and Publicity Stunt** : The Guess Who faced significant criticism for performing at the White House when invited by Richard Nixon. A myth was later propagated by their PR manager, who suggested it would be "good publicity" to claim that Nixon had requested them not to play their hit song "American Woman," a claim that was, in fact, untrue.
