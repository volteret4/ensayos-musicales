# concert - Wrockstock

## curiosities
**Wizard Rock Festival** : Wrockstock is a festival specifically dedicated to the Wizard Rock genre, which consists of bands inspired by the Harry Potter series. The name is spelled with a silent 'W' at the beginning. "That scene is big enough to have its own festival, which they call Rockstock, which is spelled with a silent W at the beginning." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8lQv8eMJ868
