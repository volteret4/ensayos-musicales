# concert - National Association of Music Merchants convention (July 1958, Chicago)

## curiosities
**Stereo's Dominance at NAMM** : The National Association of Music Merchants (NAMM) convention in Chicago in July 1958 served as a definitive moment for stereo. It became abundantly clear that stereo was the unequivocal way forward for the music industry, with a quarter of all exhibitors displaying stereo equipment.
