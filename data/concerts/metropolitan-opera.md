## curiosities
**First Radio Broadcast (1910)** : The Metropolitan Opera in New York was the site of the first ever radio broadcast in 1910. This experimental event utilized Thomas Edison's improved microphone from 1886, marking an early milestone in the public transmission of sound. "And this was the first mic used at the first ever radio broadcast, an experimental thing, at the Metropolitan Opera in New York in 1910." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAroxFbYkRw
