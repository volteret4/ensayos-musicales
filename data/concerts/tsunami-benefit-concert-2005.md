# concert - Tsunami benefit concert (2005)

## curiosities
**Alice in Chains Reunion** : Drummer Sean Kinney proposed reuniting Alice in Chains in 2005 for a benefit concert to raise funds for the victims of the tsunami that devastated Thailand, Indonesia, and other parts of Southeast Asia. This concert was well-received and preceded their full reunion.
