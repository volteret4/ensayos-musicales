# concert - Nashville

## curiosities
**Historic Gig Location (Pre-Clash)** : The Nashville club hosted a pivotal gig where The 101ers performed and the Sex Pistols opened. This event directly led to Joe, The 101ers' guitarist, leaving to form The Clash due to the Sex Pistols' influential attitude and energy. "One night they had a gig at a club called Nashville." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
