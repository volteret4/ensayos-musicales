# concert - Mothers

## curiosities
**Ramones Record Deal Catalyst** : This "gay bar on 23rd Street" hosted the Ramones for a three-night stand in October 1975. Seymour Stein, the head of Sire Records, attended one of these performances, which directly led to the band signing a record deal with his label for $6,000. "Stein went to see the band at a three-night standard Mothers, which was this gay bar on 23rd Street that October." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
