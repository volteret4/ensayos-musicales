# concert - Capitol Theater (Paseg, New Jersey)

## curiosities
**Site of Teapot Incident** : Backstage at this venue, Joey Ramone sustained second and third-degree burns to his face when a makeshift humidifier (a teapot on a hot plate) exploded. Despite the severe injury, he was rushed from the hospital back to the venue to play the show. "It was backstage at the Capitol Theater in Paseg, New Jersey. Joey had a cold and rigged up a makeshift humidifier by heating up the teapot on a hot plate in the dressing room." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
