# concert - The Mark Tom and Travis Tour

## curiosities
**Success and Setup** : This supporting tour for Blink 182's "Enema of the State" was their biggest to date, designed like a drive-in movie theater. It was massively successful, bringing in approximately $7 million in gate receipts.
