## curiosities
**Advances in Festival Amplification (1969)** : Woodstock in 1969 is cited as a festival that benefited from, and contributed to, advances in sound system technology for large outdoor musical events. Building on earlier efforts at Monterey Pop, Woodstock pushed the boundaries of live sound, though the Grateful Dead's "Wall of Sound" a few years later would mark an even bigger breakthrough.
