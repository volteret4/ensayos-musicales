# concert - Hammersmith Odeon

## curiosities
**Bowie Gear Theft (July 1973)** : In July 1973, during a multi-night engagement by David Bowie and The Spiders from Mars, their microphones and amplifiers were stolen by Steve Jones, who later became the guitarist for the Sex Pistols. This incident potentially linked the emerging punk scene to the established glam rock era through stolen equipment. "When Bowie and the spiders from Mars loaded into the Hammersmith Odien in London in July 1973 for a multi-night stand, a young thief named Steve Jones broke into the theatre and made off with a bunch of microphones and amplifiers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
**David Bowie's "Retirement" Show (July 3, 1973)** : This famous London rock venue was the site of David Bowie's theatrical announcement of Ziggy Stardust's retirement, where he declared it would be the "last show that we'll ever do." The event was captured by documentary filmmaker D.A. Pennebaker. "The infamous Bowie quits show at the Hammersmith Odeon in London July 3rd, 1973." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
