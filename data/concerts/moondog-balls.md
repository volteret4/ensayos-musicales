# concert - Moondog Balls

## curiosities
**First Rock Concerts (1950s)** : "Moondog Balls" were live shows promoted by radio announcer Alan Freed, featuring the R&B acts he played on his late-night radio program. These events, which emerged after Freed gained popularity as "the Moondog," are historically considered the first-ever rock concerts. "He called the moon dog balls. And looking back, these were the first ever rock concerts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
