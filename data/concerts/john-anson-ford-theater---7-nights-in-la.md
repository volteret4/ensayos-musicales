# concert - John Anson Ford Theater - 7 Nights in LA

## curiosities
**Perry Farrell's Shocking Performance** : Taylor Hawkins attended Jane's Addiction's "seven nights up here in LA" at the John Anson Ford Theater, where he witnessed Perry Farrell's eccentric stage presence. Farrell appeared with green dreadlocks and a woman's hat, directly challenging the audience from the moment he stepped on stage, deeply impressing Hawkins with his "crazy" antics. "I went to John Hanson for theater. They did seven nights up here in LA. And I was just like, a... Perry Farrell came out on stage with green dreadlocks. Woman's hat on and told the first guy in the front row to... The second he came out on stage, and I was like, these guys are crazy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA
