# concert - Blitz Club (Covent Garden)

## curiosities
**English Technopop Scene Hub – Focused Gathering Place** : The Blitz Club in Covent Garden was a key location around which the early English Technopop scene was focused. It served as a gathering point for the few people who were instrumental in the development of this sound.
