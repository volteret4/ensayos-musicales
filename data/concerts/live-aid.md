# concert - Live Aid

## curiosities
**Fundraising Success (July 13, 1985)** : Over the course of 16 hours on July 13, 1985, Live Aid, with performances from London's Wembley Stadium and Philadelphia, engaged more than two billion people and successfully raised $70 million US to alleviate famine in Ethiopia. "By the time things wrapped up in Philadelphia 16 hours later, more than two billion people had helped raise $70 million US for famine relief in Ethiopia." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2RKchFW42Jc
**Global Charity Event (July 13, 1985)** : Live Aid was a massive charity event held on July 13, 1985, simultaneously at Wembley Stadium in London and in Philadelphia. Kicking off at 12:01 PM GMT in London, the 16-hour concert series raised $70 million US for famine relief in Ethiopia, reaching over two billion people worldwide. "It was the start of Live Aid, one of the most spectacular charity events ever conceived." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2RKchFW42Jc
**Greg Carroll's Stage Presence** : Greg Carroll, Bono's personal stage assistant, was observed standing next to Bono during U2's performance at Live Aid in 1985. "If you've ever seen YouTube's performance at Live Aid, you'll have seen Greg standing next to Bono on stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VR5Fncdc2kQ
