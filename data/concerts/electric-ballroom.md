# concert - Electric Ballroom

## curiosities
**Vicious White Kids Fundraising Gig** : The Electric Ballroom in Camden hosted a quick solo gig by the "Vicious White Kids" (featuring Sid Vicious, Glenn Matlock, Rats Scabies, and Steve New) on August 15, 1978. This concert was organized to raise a couple of thousand dollars for Sid and Nancy Spungin to fly to New York. "To raise money, there was a quick solo gig at the electric ballroom in Camden under the name Vicious White Kids." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE4YenwmeXM
