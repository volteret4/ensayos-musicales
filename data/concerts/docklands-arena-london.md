# concert - Docklands Arena, London

## curiosities
**BBC Live Recording of Papercut** : A live version of Linkin Park's song "Papercut" was recorded at the Docklands Arena in London for the BBC. This recording was later included on the bonus disc of the special two-disc Japanese version of Linkin Park's debut album, Hybrid Theory. "It's a version of PaperCut recorded of the Docklands Arena in London for the BBC." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUqdY_T7luM
