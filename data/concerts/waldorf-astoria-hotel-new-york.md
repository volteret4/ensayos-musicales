# concert - Waldorf Astoria Hotel, New York

## curiosities
**Unveiling of the Gibson Les Paul** : On June 24, 1952, a special event for musicians was held at the Waldorf Astoria Hotel in New York, where the first Gibson Les Paul guitar was officially unveiled. This event marked the public debut of one of rock's most iconic instruments.
