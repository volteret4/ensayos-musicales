# concert - Haçienda

## curiosities
**"The Mad Corner" Hangout Spot** : A specific location under the stairway at the Haçienda was known as "the corner" or "the mad corner," serving as a popular hangout within the club. It was at this spot that Gaz Wheelan met his wife, Vanessa, highlighting the personal connections forged amidst the club's vibrant and often wild atmosphere. "Used to call it the corner, yeah. The mad corner, yeah. Yeah. Where I met my wife. Yeah, where I met Vanessa there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZStbEe4Gkk
**Epicenter of Manchester's Ecstasy and House Scene** : The Haçienda was the central venue for Manchester's burgeoning house music and ecstasy culture. Ecstasy first became widely available and used at its gay-oriented "Nude Night" or "Beef Night" where it was also considered safer. These gay nights expanded to attract a diverse crowd (80% gay, 20% others), leading to the drug's wider spread and the club becoming packed every night, fostering a chaotic atmosphere described as "Sodom and Gomorrah." "The gayness was where ecstasy was around. It wasn't any more. So that's when you could get it all. But we have the gay village in Manchester. People used to go there because it was safer." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZStbEe4Gkk
