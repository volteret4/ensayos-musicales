## curiosities
**Mozart's Marriage of Figaro Encore Demands** : The premiere of Mozart's "Marriage of Figaro" at the Berg Theater in Vienna on May 1, 1786, saw the audience demanding so many encores that the orchestra was compelled to replay certain portions and movements repeatedly. As a result, the production ran for twice its intended length. "This happened on May 1st, 1786, at the Berg Theater in Vienna. The audience loved it so much that the orchestra was obliged to play certain portions and movements of that opera again and again." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bferkEWvJg0
