# concert - Sex Pistols Manchester concert

## curiosities
**Inspiration for Future Musicians** : A concert by the Sex Pistols in Manchester served as a direct inspiration for Peter Hook and Barney (Bernard Sumner), leading them to decide they could also "make that kind of noise" and subsequently form Joy Division. "After seeing the sex pistols play in Manchester, he and his friend Barney decided that they could make that kind of noise too." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kbFfT0pKchI
