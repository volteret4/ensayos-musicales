# concert - Juno Awards

## curiosities
**50th Anniversary Performance (June 6th)** : The surviving members of The Tragically Hip performed at the 50th anniversary of the Juno Awards on June 6th, featuring Feist on vocals. The decision to reunite on stage was made after initial "resistance" from the band, but they felt it was appropriate given the significant anniversary, the honor being bestowed upon them, and to support musicians and live venues during a challenging time. The choice of Feist as a singer was met with universal approval by the band, who believed Gordon Downie would have approved. "it's the 50th anniversary of the Junos and that we, you know, receiving an incredible honor that maybe it would be a good thing to play." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G9H9q7BYqEA
**Sandbox's Nomination** : Sandbox was nominated for a Juno Award. "Sandbox won three East Coast Music Awards and were nominated for a Juno before they broke up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUQ2vZzmRiI
