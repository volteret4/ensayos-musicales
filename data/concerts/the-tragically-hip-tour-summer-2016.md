# concert - The Tragically Hip tour (Summer 2016)

## curiosities
**Overwhelming Demand** : The Tragically Hip's tour in the summer of 2016 experienced extraordinary demand, with 4 million individuals attempting to buy tickets for only 200,000 available spots. This created a scenario where a vast majority of fans were unable to purchase tickets, leading to significant anger and disappointment.
