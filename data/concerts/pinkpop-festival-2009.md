# concert - Pinkpop Festival (2009)

## curiosities
**The Killers and Springsteen Performance** : At the 2009 Pinkpop Festival in the Netherlands, The Killers performed just before Bruce Springsteen. Later, Brandon Flowers was invited back on stage during Springsteen's E Street Band set to sing "Thunder Road," highlighting the mutual artistic respect. "The killers played right before Springsteen, and Brandon was brought back on during the East Street band set to sing Thunder Road." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I
