# concert - Maxx's Kansas City

## curiosities
**New York Punk Scene Hub – Early Gathering Place** : Maxx's Kansas City was a key venue in the early New York punk scene, drawing a limited number of enthusiasts. It was one of several spots in the less glamorous parts of town where the nascent punk movement found its community. "The original punk scene in New York consisted of a few dozen weirdos who hung out at places like CBGB, the Mud Club, and Maxx's Kansas City, in the uglier end of town." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
