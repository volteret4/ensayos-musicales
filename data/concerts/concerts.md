**Future Sound Applications** : In the context of concerts, psychoacoustics is being researched for future applications to enhance the audience experience. This involves working with frequencies that can generate physical responses in the human body, not just through the ears but also via the skin or stomach, allowing for a richer transmission of information and sensations during live performances.
