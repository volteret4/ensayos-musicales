# concert - The Cuckoo's Nest

## curiosities
**"The Birthplace of Slam Dancing" (Costa Mesa)** : Southern California music lore attributes The Cuckoo's Nest in Costa Mesa as "The Birthplace of Slam Dancing." This club hosted numerous punk shows by bands like The Ramones, The Dam, Social Distortion, and Fear, becoming a focal point for the early development of moshing. "Southern California music lore has it that a club in Costa Mesa called The Cuckoo's Nest is where moshing all started. This place somehow has the nickname The Birthplace of Slam Dancing." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
**Police Intervention and Increased Intensity** : The Cuckoo's Nest faced police intervention, with authorities busting shows and attempting to shut down the club, labeling it a "den of violence." These crackdowns, however, only served to intensify the atmosphere and behavior at the venue. "The local police started moving in and busting shows at the Kuku's Nest, charging that the place was a den of violence, which it kind of was." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMx4bmq8o2Y
