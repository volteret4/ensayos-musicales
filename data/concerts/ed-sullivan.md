## curiosities
**Impact of Beatles' Appearance (February 1964)** : The Beatles' appearance on Ed Sullivan in February 1964 is identified as a pivotal event that spurred the flourishing of the original Garage Rock era, which then lasted until approximately 1968. "And its original era flourished between 1964, especially after the Beatles appeared on Ed Sullivan in February of that year, and sometime in 1968, when Rock began to get more polished and complex." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
**Inspiration for Death** : In February 1964, the three Hackney brothers—David, Bobby, and Dennis—who would later form the punk band Death, were inspired to pursue music after watching The Beatles perform on the Ed Sullivan Show. "In February 1964, David, Bobby and Dennis Hackney saw the Beatles perform on Ed Sullivan." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hQJ3r-DCoMQ
