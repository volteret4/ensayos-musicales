# concert - Monterey Pop Festival

## curiosities
**Jimi Hendrix's Iconic Guitar Burning** : In 1967, Jimi Hendrix famously set his guitar on fire and then broke it into pieces at the end of his performance at the Monterey Pop Festival. This dramatic act, involving lighter fluid and a match, became one of the most memorable and iconic moments in rock and festival history.
