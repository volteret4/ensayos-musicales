# concert - The Town Pump

## curiosities
**Lost Grassroots Venue in Vancouver** : The Town Pump was a significant grassroots live venue in Vancouver where emerging bands could play to small audiences and gradually build a following. Its disappearance, alongside other staple rooms, is cited by Matthew Good as a key indicator of the diminishing arts community in the city.
