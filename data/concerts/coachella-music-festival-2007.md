# concert - Coachella Music Festival 2007

## curiosities
**Rage Against the Machine Reunion Performance** : Rage Against the Machine reunited for a performance at the Coachella Music Festival in California in April 2007. This reunion came after significant rumors following Christmas 2006, overcoming past animosity, and was reportedly motivated by the band's desire to oppose the political climate under President George Bush. "rumors began to take hold that Rage would reunite for the Coachella Music Festival in California in April of 2007. And against what everybody thought would happen, it did happen." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t2uXHP9cBdM
