# concert - Disco clubs

## curiosities
**Audience for New Remix Strategies** : Post-disco, record labels and artists recognized the dance club crowd as a new, significant audience for music. Dance remixes provided a way to tap into this market, with different mixes tailored to appeal to diverse club segments, such as rock remixes for rock crowds. "The dance club crowd was there for the picking." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
**Driving Force for Extended Remixes** : In the disco era, clubs filled with dancers compelled DJs to maintain continuous grooves. The inherent shortness of standard pop songs presented a challenge, leading to innovations like extended remixes and the 12-inch single to keep the party momentum going. "People were heading to clubs in record numbers to dance, and the goal of the DJ was to keep the groove and to keep the party going." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08i9lVd6T8c
