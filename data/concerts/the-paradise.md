# concert - The Paradise

## curiosities
**New England Patriots Stage Dive Incident (November 13, 1997)** : The Paradise in Boston was the venue for an Everclear concert on November 13, 1997, where three New England Patriots players – Drew Bledsoe, Scott Zolak, and Max Lane – stage-dived into the crowd. Max Lane, weighing 305 pounds, landed on 23-year-old Tamika Messier, causing severe injuries and leading to a significant $1.2 million settlement. **Stage Diving Incident and Lawsuit** : On November 13, 1997, during an Everclear concert at The Paradise in Boston, a stage diving incident occurred involving Max Lane, a 305-pound lineman for the New England Patriots, who landed on audience member Tamika Messier. Messier sustained severe injuries, leading to a lawsuit on December 10 against the players, their club, and the band, which resulted in a $1.2 million settlement.
