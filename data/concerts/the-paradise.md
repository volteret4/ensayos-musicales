# concert - The Paradise

## curiosities
**New England Patriots Stage Dive Incident (November 13, 1997)** : The Paradise in Boston was the venue for an Everclear concert on November 13, 1997, where three New England Patriots players – Drew Bledsoe, Scott Zolak, and Max Lane – stage-dived into the crowd. Max Lane, weighing 305 pounds, landed on 23-year-old Tamika Messier, causing severe injuries and leading to a significant $1.2 million settlement.
