# concert - Hollywood (Oxford)

## curiosities
**Radiohead's First Post-Graduation Gig (July 22, 1991)** : On July 22, 1991, "On a Friday" performed their first post-graduation concert at the Hollywood in Oxford. Despite having played together "at least 100 times before," this gig was characterized by its modest setup: "five friends, two strobe lights and a smoke machine." "Just when they played a show at the Hollywood in Oxford, their first post-graduation concert." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-G22K0vLnM
