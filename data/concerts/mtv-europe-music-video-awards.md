# concert - MTV Europe Music Video Awards

## curiosities
**Gorillaz 3D Illusion Performance (2005)** : In 2005, the animated band Gorillaz appeared at the MTV Europe Music Video Awards using 3D illusions of their characters. This performance showcased advanced visual techniques for a virtual band in a live televised setting, though it was noted as not being an actual hologram.
