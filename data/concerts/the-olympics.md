# concert - The Olympics

## curiosities
**First Live Performance of "Whistleblower"** : Arkells performed their song "Whistleblower" live for the first time at the Olympics. The version played at this event was noted to be "quite different" from its final recorded state on the "Michigan Left" album. "I remember we played it live at the Olympics was the first time we ever played it live." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kKNn01M8GG4
