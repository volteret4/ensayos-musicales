# concert - Madison Square Garden Rock and Roll Spectacular

## curiosities
**Rick Nelson's Booed Performance** : In October 1971, the "Rock and Roll Spectacular" (originally "Rock and Roll Revival," renamed at Rick Nelson's request) featured 1950s rock idols like Gary U.S. Bond and Chuck Berry. Rick Nelson was booed by the audience for performing his country rock material and not embodying his earlier 1950s image.
