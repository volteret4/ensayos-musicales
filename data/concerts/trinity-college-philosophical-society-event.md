# concert - Trinity College philosophical society event

## curiosities
**Catalyst for The Cranberries Reunion (2009)** : Trinity College invited Dolores O'Riordan to perform at their philosophical society event after making her a member. This prompted her to invite her Cranberries bandmates (Mike and Noel Hogan, and Fergal Lawler) to play with her, marking their first reunion after a long separation and initiating their full comeback. "Trinity College made me a member of their philosophical society and they wanted me to do a couple of songs, you know?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yR3xc3ra5tE
