# concert - Donington Park

## curiosities
**"Donington Disaster" (1988)** : On August 28, 1988, a major outdoor rock show at Donington Park racetrack in England became known as the "Donington disaster." During a performance by Guns N' Roses, a crowd collapse tragically led to the deaths of two men who were found face down in four inches of mud, requiring them to be "dug out" before being declared dead on arrival at the hospital.
