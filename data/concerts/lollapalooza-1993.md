# concert - Lollapalooza 1993

## curiosities
**Rage Against the Machine's Early Main Stage Performance** : Rage Against the Machine performed at the 1993 Lollapalooza Festival as one of the first bands on the main stage. Their performance, particularly of "Killing in the Name Of," caused the crowd to "lose its collective mind," establishing them as a powerful new force. "I remember seeing them play the 1993 Lala Palluza Festival. They were one of the first bands on the main stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t2uXHP9cBdM
