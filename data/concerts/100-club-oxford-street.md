# concert - 100 Club (Oxford Street)

## curiosities
**UK Punk Festival (Fall 1976) – Central Gathering for Early Scene** : In the fall of 1976, the 100 Club on Oxford Street in London hosted a two-night music festival that was central to the nascent UK punk scene. The venue, with an official capacity of 350, reportedly had plenty of room to move, indicating the small, tightly-knit nature of the early punk community. "Pretty much every London punk fit into a single club on Oxford Street for a two-night music festival. Capacity at the 100 Club was officially 350, but there was plenty of room to move around." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lip0bZX7hBE
