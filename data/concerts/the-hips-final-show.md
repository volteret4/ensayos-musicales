# concert - The Hip's Final Show

## curiosities
**Emotional Farewell in Kingston (August 20, 2016)** : The Tragically Hip played their last concert on August 20, 2016, in Kingston, their hometown. During this emotionally charged performance, Gord Downie directly called upon the Prime Minister, who was present, to address the long-standing issues facing Canada's Indigenous people. The event garnered national attention, with the best tweet seen that day reading, "Canada closed, death in the family," encapsulating the nation's grief.
