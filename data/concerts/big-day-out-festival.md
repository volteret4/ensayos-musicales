# concert - Big Day Out Festival

## curiosities
**Fatal Crushing Incident (Australia, 2001)** : At the Big Day Out Festival in Australia in 2001, 16-year-old Jessica Michalik died after being crushed in a mosh pit during a Limp Bizkit performance. The coroner attributed her death to a heart attack related to asphyxiation and criticized Limp Bizkit's singer, Fred Durst, for his inflammatory comments during rescue efforts. **Jessica Mitchellick's Death (Sydney, Australia)** : During Limp Bizkit's set at the Big Day Out Festival in Sydney, Australia, a massive mosh pit formed in front of the stage, leading to a woman named Jessica Mitchellick being crushed. She was extracted by security and taken to the hospital but died five days later from a heart attack. "During their set, a massive mosh pit formed in front of the stage.
