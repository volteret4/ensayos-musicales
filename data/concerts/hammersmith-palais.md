# concert - Hammersmith Palais

## curiosities
**Site of Bauhaus's Final Gig (1983)** : The Hammersmith Palais in London was the venue for Bauhaus's last concert on July 5, 1983, marking the end of their initial run. "It was one final gig. July 5th, 1983, at the Hammersmith Pele in London." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A89ipsMeMAg
**Venue of Bauhaus's Final Gig** : The Hammersmith Palais in London was the location of Bauhaus's final live performance on July 5, 1983, where David J famously declared "rest in peace" at the close of the show. "It was one final gig. July 5th, 1983, at the Hammersmith Pele in London." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
