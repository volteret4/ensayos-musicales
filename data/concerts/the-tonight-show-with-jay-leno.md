## curiosities
**The Vines' Trashed Set (December 2002)** : In December 2002, The Vines were scheduled for a major appearance on "The Tonight Show with Jay Leno," which was a significant break for the band. However, during the rehearsal, lead singer Craig Nicholls trashed the set, resulting in the band being kicked off the show and losing a crucial opportunity to reach a broader audience.
