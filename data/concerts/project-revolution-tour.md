# concert - Project Revolution Tour

## curiosities
**Chris Cornell and Chester Bennington Performance (2008)** : During the 2008 Project Revolution Tour, Chris Cornell was on the bill, and he was joined onstage by Chester Bennington to perform Temple of the Dog's song "Hunger Strike." This surprise duet was later released twice to Linkin Park's fan club. "If we go back to the 2008 project revolution tour, Chris was on the bill. And when he stepped forward to sing the Temple of Dog Song, Hunger Strike, a song written for and dedicated to Chris's late roommate Andrew Wood of Mother Lovevont, Chester surprised everybody by coming in to sing with him." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qQm7u3W13eM
