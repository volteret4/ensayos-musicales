# concert - Blue Rodeo Massey Hall Show (Feb 2, 2017)

## curiosities
**Gord Downie's Last Public Performance** : On February 2, 2017, Gord Downie was in the audience when Blue Rodeo played Massey Hall in Toronto. For the final song of the night, he joined Blue Rodeo and opening act The Sadies on stage to sing "Lost Together," marking his last public singing appearance. "And that would be the last time Gord ever sang in public." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
