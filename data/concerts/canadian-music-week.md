# concert - Canadian Music Week

## curiosities
**Critical Acclaim** : Pezz achieved significant recognition at Canadian Music Week, receiving "amazing report cards" with scores of 93% in 1999 (placing them in the top 10) and 94% in 2000. These strong reviews led to increased attention from industry professionals. "So like we, so in 1999, we had a 93% report card. So we made it into their like top 10 thing. And then 2000, we made it again. We had a 94. And all of a sudden, all these industry people started paying attention to us." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
**Wattouche Promotion** : After self-producing "Wattouche," Pezz attempted to promote the album by trying to get into circuits like Canadian Music Week. "And then, you know, we would try to get on to like Canadian music week and all those, that kind of circuit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZlLH9J_lJw
