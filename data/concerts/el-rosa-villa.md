# concert - El Rosa Villa

## curiosities
**Site of Dimebag Darrell's Assassination** : The El Rosa Villa was a venue in Columbus, Ohio, where Dimebag Darrell was assassinated on December 8, 2004. Approximately 400 people were in attendance at the Damageplan gig when the mass shooting event occurred.
