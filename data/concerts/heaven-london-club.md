# concert - Heaven (London club)

## curiosities
**Filming Location for "The Hunger"** : This London club was the ironically named setting for a scene in the vampire movie "The Hunger," where Bauhaus performed their song "Bela Lugosi's Dead." "And there's a scene in the movie shot in a London club, ironically called Heaven, and the band singing, Bella Legosi's dead, is of course Bajaus." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=seLx5Ud4v7Y
