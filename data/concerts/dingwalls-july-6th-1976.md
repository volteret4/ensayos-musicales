# concert - Dingwalls (July 6th, 1976)

## curiosities
**Ramones' Impact on British Punk Scene** : This London club hosted the Ramones two nights after their Roundhouse gig. These two shows combined served as a pivotal "lesson" for "just about every single person who would become a major player in the British punk rock explosion," including future members of the Sex Pistols, The Clash, The Pretenders, The Damned, and Generation X. "Two nights later, the Ramones played a show at a club called Dingwalls." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
