## curiosities
**Showcase of Gospel and Soul** : The 1969 Harlem Culture Festival was a significant cultural event, documented in the Questlove documentary *Summer of Soul*. It featured performances by artists such as The Fifth Dimension and the Edwin Hawkins Singers, highlighting the intersection of gospel and popular music.
