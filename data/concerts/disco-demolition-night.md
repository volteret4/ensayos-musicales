# concert - Disco Demolition Night

## curiosities
**Anti-Disco Riot (Chicago 1979)** : In the summer of 1979, Chicago was the site of the infamous "Disco Demolition Night," an anti-disco riot. This event symbolized the intense backlash against disco music and culture, contributing to its eventual crash and burn by 1980.
