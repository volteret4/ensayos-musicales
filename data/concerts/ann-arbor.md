## curiosities
**Iggy and the Psychedelic Stooges Debut (October 31, 1967)** : The band, then known as Iggy and the Psychedelic Stooges, made their debut performance on October 31, 1967, in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Their debut was described as loud, spontaneous, sloppy, fuzzy, arrogant, violent, angry, and powerful.
