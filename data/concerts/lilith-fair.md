**Female-Centric Touring Festival** : Lilith Fair, founded by Sarah McLachlan, was a female-centric festival that toured North America for three consecutive summers, starting in 1997. It was created during a boom in the music industry, where the success of Lollapalooza encouraged other touring festival concepts.
