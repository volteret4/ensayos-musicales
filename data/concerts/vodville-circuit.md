## curiosities
**Early All-Female Bands as Novelties** : Starting around 1910, all-female bands began appearing on the Vodville circuit. Despite many musicians being highly skilled, these groups were often presented as novelties, with promoters and audiences focusing more on their appearance than their musical talent. "Starting around 1910, all female bands started showing up on the Vodville circuit. They weren't really taking all that seriously.
