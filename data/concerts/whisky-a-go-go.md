# concert - Whisky a Go-Go

## curiosities
**Scene of On-Stage Tambourine Attack (September 29, 1994)** : On their first North American tour, Oasis played the Whisky a Go-Go on the Sunset Strip. The performance saw multiple on-stage altercations between Liam and Noel, culminating in Liam hitting Noel in the head with a tambourine at the end of the set and refusing to return for the encore.
