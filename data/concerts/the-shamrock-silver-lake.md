# concert - The Shamrock (Silver Lake)

## curiosities
**Location of Stone Temple Pilots' Record Offer** : An offer from Atlantic Records to sign a major label deal was made to Mighty Joe Young (later Stone Temple Pilots) after a show they played at a venue called The Shamrock in Silver Lake. "And after a show at a place called The Shamrock and Silver Lake, an offer was made." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r6vVw66-gmI
