# concert - Plan K

## curiosities
**Ian Curtis Meets Annik Honoré** : On October 16, 1979, Joy Division played a gig at Plan K in Brussels, Belgium. This concert marked the pivotal moment when Ian Curtis met Annik Honoré, an encounter that led to their affair. "On October 16, 1979, the band played a gig at a place called Plan K in Brussels, Belgium. This is where Ian met a woman named Anik on a ray." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1VWJ0uCQx8w
