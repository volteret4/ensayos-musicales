# concert - MTV Debut

## curiosities
**Defining Media Moment (August 1, 1981)** : MTV launched at 12:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time on August 1, 1981, becoming a critical defining media moment for new wave music. Initially, it struggled to gain cable company pick-ups and had a limited library of 250 music videos, 30 by Rod Stewart. "The debut of MTV, 12.01 AM Eastern Daylight Time, August 1, 1981." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
**Impact on New Wave Success** : As record labels observed sales spikes for "MTV bands" in cities with cable access to the channel, the music industry recognized its power. The second wave of new wave bands, with their visual appeal (makeup, clothes, personas), were perfectly suited for television, turning music into a visual medium and fueling a "second British invasion" as MTV acquired more British promotional films. "Music morphed from an audio only thing into a very, very visual one." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
