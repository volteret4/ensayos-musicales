## curiosities
**Founding and Role in Manchester Transition (1982)** : Hacienda was a nightclub founded by Tony Wilson in Manchester in 1982. Although it didn't directly initiate the Manchester movement, it was a central protagonist and likely spearheaded the transition from Post-Punk to the Manchester sound, playing a key role in the local band scene and early House music adoption. **Pivotal for House Music in Manchester** : Hacienda was the venue where the first House music vinyls from Chicago began to play in Manchester, thanks to Tony Wilson's vision and DJs like John da Silva, who established the House sound with "Ibiza nights." It was key in introducing these sounds to the city before the rest of the UK.
