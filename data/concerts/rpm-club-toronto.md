# concert - RPM Club (Toronto)

## curiosities
**Nine Inch Nails Performance (April 17, 1990)** : Nine Inch Nails performed at this Toronto club on April 17, 1990, opening for Peter Murphy. They captivated the audience with a heavy cover of Queen's "Get Down Make Love," along with their own songs "Down In It" and "Head Like A Hole," concluding their set by smashing their gear. "It was April 17th, 1990 at the old RPM club in Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DKdF8NgF5Bw
