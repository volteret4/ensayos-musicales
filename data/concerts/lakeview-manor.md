# concert - Lakeview Manor

## curiosities
**Challenging Early Venue** : The Lakeview Manor in Kingston was described as "not a great spot" where The Tragically Hip performed, known for hosting strippers during the day and touring bands at night. Despite the venue's nature, the band gained experience there before expanding their reach beyond Kingston.
