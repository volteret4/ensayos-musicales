# concert - Lesser Free Trade Hall Show

## curiosities
**Punk's Inspiring Event** : This infamous concert on June 4th, 1976, in Manchester, featured the Sex Pistols playing to a tiny audience, but it was pivotal as it inspired the formation of Joy Division (then called Jordy Vision by the speaker) and influenced their future manager and label boss. "It was the infamous Lesser Free Trade Hall Show, June 4th, 1976." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
