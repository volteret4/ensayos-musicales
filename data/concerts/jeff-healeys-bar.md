# concert - Jeff Healey's bar

## curiosities
**The Trews' House Band Residency** : Jeff Healey's bar, located on Bathurst and Queen in Toronto, hosted The Trews as its house band every Thursday for the entire summer and fall of 2001. This residency was offered after Jeff Healey saw and liked their opening performance for him in Halifax. "we ended up getting a weekly residency gig at Jeff Healey's bar on Bathurst and Queen in Toronto." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zbKOwkpkAM
