**Standoff Over Corporate Branding (c. 1997)** : At a large festival in Manitoba where 5440 was opening for Pearl Jam, the band refused to go on stage due to a large Labatt's balloon that violated a clause in their contract regarding stage visibility. This led to a "big standoff" with promoters until Raine Maida of Our Lady Peace intervened to resolve the situation. "We paid a big festival where we opened for Pearl Jam.
