# concert - Woodstock 94

## curiosities
**25th Anniversary Event** : Woodstock 94 was held in Saugerties, New York, on the 25th anniversary of the original 1969 event, which took place on Max Yasgur's farm. "The first of the 90s was 1994 in Socrates, New York, held on the 25th anniversary of the event on Maxiazgur's farm in 1969." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The 90s Part 9： The Festivals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Career Boosts for Alt Acts** : Despite its organizational flaws, Woodstock 94 garnered significant media coverage and provided substantial career boosts for many alternative acts who performed there, including Nine Inch Nails and Green Day. "But it got a lot of coverage and a lot of alternative acts got big career boosts from performing." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The 90s Part 9： The Festivals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Cypress Hill Performance** : Cypress Hill performed at Woodstock 94, showcasing their growing popularity and ability to reach diverse audiences. "They were Woodstock 94." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The 90s Part 5b： Hip-Hop's Effects Part 2 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bQz8R0gbXc&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Negative Experience for Attendees** : The festival, attended by approximately 550,000 people, was described as "awful" due to terrible organization, overpriced food, and severe weather conditions including rain, mud, and thunderstorms. "I was there, amongst the 550,000-ish people, and boy, it was awful.
