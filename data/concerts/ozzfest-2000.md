# concert - Ozzfest 2000

## curiosities
**Christian Band Participation** : In 2000, the born-again Christian band P.O.D. performed on Ozzfest, an event organized by Ozzy Osbourne. This was considered an unexpected pairing given the "Prince of Darkness'" image. "And by the summer of 2000, this band of born again was on the road with the Prince of Darkness, Ozzy Osborne, and that year's version of Osfest." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Jay Weinberg's Musical Turning Point** : Jay Weinberg's attendance at Ozzfest in 2000, where he witnessed Slipknot perform, profoundly influenced his decision to pursue metal music and drumming. "Way back in 2000, Dad took Joey to an Osfest show, an event that included a new band called Slipknot." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dY7uB_QQWBc
