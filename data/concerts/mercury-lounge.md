# concert - Mercury Lounge

## curiosities
**Key Venue for The Strokes' Rise** : The Mercury Lounge, a famous venue in New York City's Lower East Side with a capacity of about 250, was a crucial location for The Strokes' early career. Their impressive performances there led the venue's booker to quit his job and become their manager, marking a significant turning point for the band. "These small venue gigs continued until they were able to work up to the Mercury Lounge, a famous place in the lower east side." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jEfofyobGiI
**The Strokes' Early Performance Venue** : The Mercury Lounge was another specific venue on the Lower East Side of Manhattan where The Strokes performed during their early career, alongside the Luna Lounge. "Specifically at a place called the Luna Lounge, and at another venue called the Mercury Lounge." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY
