# concert - Travis Strait Electric Company

## curiosities
**Rush Concert Details (1975)** : On a Thursday night in 1975, Rush performed at the Travis Strait Electric Company located at 4527 Travis, just south of Knox in Dallas. Admission was $3, and the event uniquely offered free beer from 8:30 PM to 10:00 PM, creating an environment with "lots of liquid refreshments" and "two big game rooms."
