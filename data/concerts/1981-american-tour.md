# concert - 1981 American Tour

## curiosities
**Rolling Stones / Jovan Musk Sponsorship** : The Rolling Stones' 1981 American tour was exclusively sponsored by Jovan Musk for $3 million, a pioneering corporate deal in rock music. This arrangement included Jovan's name on over two million tickets and extensive promotional merchandise, leading to $50 million in gross earnings for the band and a significant sales boost for Jovan, despite public criticism of rock being "co-opted by advertisers."
