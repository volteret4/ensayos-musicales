# concert - Great Western Forum

## curiosities
**Depeche Mode's Five-Night Stand (November 1993)** : In November 1993, Depeche Mode was scheduled for a five-night residency at the Great Western Forum in Los Angeles. This engagement was preceded by Martin Gore suffering a grand mal seizure at the Sunset Marquis Hotel, brought on by stress, alcohol, and drugs. "Then came a grand mall seizure in the middle of a business meeting at the Sunset Marquis Hotel in Los Angeles in November 1993, a head of five nights at the Great Western Forum." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jvh8SP2QKXQ
