# concert - Dingwalls

## curiosities
**Meeting Point for Future Punk Bands** : During their first visit to England in 1976, the Ramones played at this club. The audience consisted of "kids that would later form these kind of groundbreaking bands of their own," indicating its significance as a breeding ground for the emerging British punk scene. "And then we did this club Dingwalls. And basically the whole makeup of Dingwalls were all these kids that would later form these kind of groundbreaking bands of their own." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
**Ramones' Second UK Show (July 6, 1976)** : Two nights after their influential Roundhouse performance, The Ramones played at Dingwalls, another London club. These two gigs combined are credited with teaching a lesson to virtually every major player in the nascent British punk rock scene. "Two nights later, the Ramones played a show at a club called Dingwalls." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huAvyInzz3Q
