## curiosities
**Mass Casualties in Stampede** : The stampede at the Minsk metro station on May 30, 1991, resulted in a death toll of at least 53 people, with approximately 150 others injured, some very badly. Emergency responders discovered two layers of bodies on the stairs, with everyone in the bottom layer having died from being crushed by those on top. **Underground Station Stampede (May 30, 1991)** : On May 30, 1991, during a music and beer festival in Minsk, Belarus, a storm prompted hundreds of inebriated attendees to rush a nearby underground metro station for shelter. The rain made the station steps very slippery, leading to pushing and shoving. Many women wearing high heels were among the first to slip and fall, causing bodies to pile up. "On May 30, 1991, thousands of people were at a music and beer festival in the Belarusian city of Minsk when a storm hit." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
