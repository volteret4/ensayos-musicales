# concert - Coventry

## curiosities
**Early Venue for Rock Bands** : Coventry was a club where Joey Ramone's first band, Sniper, regularly played from 1972 to 1974. It was also an early venue for Kiss, highlighting its role in the formative years of future rock stars. "One of the places that they used to play was a club called Coventry, a venue that also saw early gigs by a band called Kiss." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j8uk7BnbXwU
