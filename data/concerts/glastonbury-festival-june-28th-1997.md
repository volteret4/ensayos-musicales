# concert - Glastonbury Festival (June 28th, 1997)

## curiosities
**Radiohead's Masterpiece-Defining Performance** : Radiohead's set at Glastonbury, shortly after the release of their "masterpiece" *OK Computer*, is maintained by some as "simply the best show of all time ever." Despite starting slowly and encountering significant mid-set technical problems (stage monitors, blinding spotlight on Thom Yorke), the band's confidence grew. Thom Yorke's decision to have the light turned on the crowd seemed to resolve the monitor issues and create a "magic" moment, solidifying the gig's legendary status.
