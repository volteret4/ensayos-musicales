# concert - Rock am Ring Festival

## curiosities
**2016 Weather Incident** : In 2016, a Rock am Ring performance was disrupted by severe weather, including lightning strikes, leading to injuries and the evacuation of attendees. Billy Talent, initially uncertain if they would play, ended up headlining unexpectedly after the Red Hot Chili Peppers requested them to go on after their set. The festival was ultimately canceled the following day due to the weather. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ocMHRLQMQY0
**Location and Scale** : The Rock am Ring Festival is a large music event held at the Nürburgring in Germany, attracting over 50,000 attendees. It has been a venue for Billy Talent's live album recordings. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ocMHRLQMQY0
