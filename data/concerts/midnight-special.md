# concert - Midnight Special

## curiosities
**ABBA's TV Taping (1976)** : ABBA participated in TV tapings for shows including "Midnight Special" in Los Angeles in October 1976, as part of their strategy to gain traction in the American market without extensive live touring. "When Abba was in Los Angeles in October 1976 to do some TV tapings like Dina's show midnight special Don Kirschner's rock concert." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 56 - Talk Shows of the 1970s | https://www.ftr70.com/
**TV Exposure for Artists** : The "Midnight Special" was a television program that offered a platform for musical acts like Wild Cherry and ABBA, providing important exposure to their music.
