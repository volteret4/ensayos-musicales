# concert - Terminal 1

## curiosities
**Nirvana's Last Performance (March 1, 1994)** : Terminal 1, an old airplane hangar in Munich, Germany, served as the venue for Nirvana's final concert appearance on March 1, 1994. Approximately 3,000 people attended the gig, during which Kurt Cobain, suffering from bronchitis and a sore throat, performed despite a power failure that necessitated an impromptu acoustic set. "On March 1, 1994, Nirvana played a venue called Terminal 1, an old airplane hanger in Munich, about 3,000 people were there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1Jt2fUBDusk
