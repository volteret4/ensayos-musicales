**Ancient Festival Origin** : Fiera de la Frequette No La is an ancient music festival that originated in the village of Ken Al Monga in the mountains of southern Italy, with its first recorded event dating back to 1450. The festival continues to take place today. "Every year or something called Fiera de la Frequette No La, it started in the village of Ken Al Monga, up in the mountains in the south of Italy. And as far as we've been able to tell, the first gig for this festival was in 1450, and it's still happening today." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
