# concert - Longhorn Ballroom, Dallas

## curiosities
**Connection to Jack Ruby and JFK Assassination** : The Longhorn Ballroom, which hosted the Sex Pistols in 1978 under manager Dewy Grume, was previously known as the Carousel Club. This earlier incarnation of the venue was owned by Jack Ruby, the individual who assassinated Lee Harvey Oswald, creating a unique historical link. "When the Texas ballroom hosted the Sex Pistols, it was run by a guy named Dewy Grume. 15 years earlier, the place was known as the Carousel Club. Grume worked for Jack Ruby, the owner of the club. He's the guy who assassinated Lee Harvey Oswald." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7kH_AfT3i4k
**Hostile Audience to Sex Pistols (1978)** : On January 10, 1978, during the Sex Pistols' only American tour, a concert at the Longhorn Ballroom in Dallas was met with extreme hostility, with the band being pelted by cans, bottles, rotten tomatoes, and even pig snouts from the crowd. "The band was pelted with cans and bottles, and not just cans and bottles, because the crowd came prepared with rotten tomatoes and get this pig snouts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7kH_AfT3i4k
**Sex Pistols Gig with Sid Vicious – January 10, 1978** : The Longhorn Ballroom in Dallas hosted a Sex Pistols show on January 10, 1978. Sid Vicious was reportedly "in fine form" that night, despite three of his bass strings being broken, likely because a roadie was playing his parts backstage. "Here's a radio commercial for the January 10th 1978 show at the Longhorn Ballroom in Dallas. ... Oh, Sid was in fine form that night in Dallas." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OcylgiXkDP8
