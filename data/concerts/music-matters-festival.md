# concert - Music Matters festival

## curiosities
**Singapore Gear Malfunction (2000s)** : During a performance at this festival in Clark Quay, Singapore, USS experienced a major technical failure due to voltage incompatibilities. Their electronic instruments' fuses blew, and they tripped breakers at two different clubs, which operated on 220 volts compared to their 120-volt North American gear. This forced them into an impromptu acoustic performance, with Ash on acoustic guitar and Jay attempting to scratch on a non-spinning record player, while a drummer supplied beats typically handled by a laptop. "Not only did we blow the fuses in our main electronic instruments, we also blew the breaker at two different clubs and so we had to DIY it and go back to our roots." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ayPJfDAF_to
