# concert - Tiananmen Square Protests

## curiosities
**Use of Beethoven's 9th Symphony** : During the Tiananmen Square protests, Chinese students famously played the last movement of Beethoven's 9th Symphony, "Ode to Joy," as a powerful protest anthem. They continued to play it right up until the point when tanks rolled in. "For example, the Chinese students in Tiananmen Square played it right up until the tanks rolled in." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0ARYt4sYT90
