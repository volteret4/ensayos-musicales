# concert - Sonic City (Japan)

## curiosities
**Site of Frusciante's First Quitting (May 7, 1992)** : Sonic City, a venue in Japan, was the location where John Frusciante refused to go on stage on May 7, 1992, just minutes before a Red Hot Chili Peppers show was scheduled to start. He declared, "I quit," citing his inability to perform and hearing voices, marking his first departure from the band. "On May 7, 1992, after just four shows in Japan, John refused to go on stage at a venue called Sonic City just minutes before the show was supposed to start." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
