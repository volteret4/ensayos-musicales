## curiosities
**Birthplace of New Romanticism** : The Blitz Club, along with Billies, was a pivotal British club that cultivated the New Romantic genre. It provided a dedicated space for new music, leading to the formation of influential synth-based groups such as Spandau Ballet, Visage, and Culture Club. "One such offshoot was known as New Romantic, which had its roots in a couple of British clubs dedicated to new music. One was called Billies, and the other was the Blitz Club." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
