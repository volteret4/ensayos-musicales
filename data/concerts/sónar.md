# concert - Sónar

## curiosities
**Aphex Twin's Mannequin Performance** : Sónar hosted a memorable performance by Aphex Twin where mannequins were placed on stage instead of the artists. This created an ambiguous experience for the audience, who could not determine if the performers were present or if the show was recorded, serving as an example of artists challenging traditional notions of live presence. "cuando actuaron aquí de nadie se en el sonar hace un montón de años pusieron unos manicis en el escenario y ellos no estaban el escenario no sabías ni si estaban no sabías ni si era grabado el directo no sabías nada había uno manici esquitos ahí en los instrumentos." ← Music Radar Clan > MF DOOM. **Magenta Presentation** : Project Magenta was presented at the Sónar festival, which served as an occasion for renewed attention to Google's AI-based music initiative. The physical demonstration of the system in operation at Sónar allowed observers to witness its advanced capabilities firsthand.
