# concert - The Gathering of the Tribes

## curiosities
**Fixed Location Predecessor** : The Gathering of the Tribes was noted as a previous festival in the US that was "rooted in one place," distinguishing it from the traveling format of Lollapalooza. "There had been other festivals in the US. Of course, there was Monterey, there was Woodstock, there was Ultimonts, the Us Festivals, there was something called the Gathering of the Tribes. But all of them were rooted in one place." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
