## curiosities
**Foo Fighters' Ability to Sell Out Wembley** : Dave Grohl noted the "poetry" in the Foo Fighters being a band capable of selling out a massive venue like Wembley Stadium, yet simultaneously choosing to record an album in a garage. This decision reflected a desire to innovate and make records in a primal, old-school fashion.
