# concert - Rock in 100 (likely Rock en Seine)

## curiosities
**Venue of Oasis's Breakup (August 29, 2009)** : The Parisian festival, Rock in 100, was the location of Oasis's final, highly controversial concert on August 29, 2009. Minutes before they were scheduled to go on stage, organizers announced the band's dissolution, following a violent physical altercation between a reportedly drunk Liam Gallagher and Noel Gallagher.
