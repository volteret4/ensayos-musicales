# concert - London Hammersmith Apollo

## curiosities
**Kate Bush's 2014 Residency** : The London Hammersmith Apollo was the venue for Kate Bush's 22-night residency, which began in late August 2014, marking her return to the stage 35 years after her first tour. "It wasn't until she agreed to perform a 22-night residency at the London Hammersmith Apollo, starting in late August 2014, 35 years after that first tour, that she returned to the stage." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oGscVtrdER0
