## curiosities
**Debauched Reputation (1994, Depeche Mode)** : Depeche Mode's 1994 Exotic Tour, supporting "Songs of Faith and Devotion," is historically noted for its debauched nature, characterized by rampant drug and alcohol use and bad behavior. "This road trip has gone down in history as one of the most debauched in the history of rock. Drugs, alcohol, bad behavior, it was not good." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
**Pizza Incident in Detroit (March 27, 1992)** : During U2's Zoo TV tour in the early 1990s, at a concert in Detroit on March 27, 1992, Bono famously used a cell phone on stage to call a local pizza place (Speedy Pizza). He attempted to order 10,000 pizzas for the 21,064-person audience. Though only 100 pepperoni pizzas were delivered by three employees, they were thrown into the chanting crowd, becoming a memorable anecdote of the tour. "You too, and the time they ordered pizza for the 21,064 people who came to see them in Detroit on March 27, 1992." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Somq2MoRi5Q
**U2 Stage Design by Willie Williams** : Willie Williams was responsible for the innovative stage design for U2's Zoo TV Tour, among other major tours designed by him since 1983. "Willie has designed all of you two's tours since 1983, the Joshua Tree stage, Zoo TV, Popmart 360, all of them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZVGnSVfed9s
