## curiosities
**Overcrowding and Faulty Wiring Fire (South of Cincinnati, May 28, 1977)** : The Beverly Hills Supper Club, located south of Cincinnati, experienced a devastating fire on May 28, 1977, that killed 165 people. The club was significantly overcrowded, hosting 3,000 patrons against a legal fire code capacity of 1,500, with faulty wiring and inadequate fire exits identified as the primary causes of the blaze.
