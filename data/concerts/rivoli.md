# concert - Rivoli

## curiosities
**Carney Lake Road Performance** : The Rivoli was a venue where Carney Lake Road, Chris Murphy and Jay Ferguson's pre-Sloan band, performed to a minimal audience. Despite the small crowd, the experience was part of their early musical journey. "We came up and played the Rivoli to no one, mind you." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mbic-70ZhpE
