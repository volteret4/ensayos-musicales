# concert - Leningrad Rock Club (1981-1991)

## curiosities
**Center of Soviet Rock** : Despite strict KGB supervision and audition requirements, the Leningrad rock club, opened in 1981, became the vibrant center of Soviet rock music. It served as a highly influential venue for underground bands until its closure in 1991, fostering a clandestine network of music distribution. **KGB-Run Rock Club** : The most famous of the KGB-supervised rock clubs opened in Leningrad in 1981. Bands wishing to perform there had to audition before grim-faced men in suits, who also monitored every performance from a balcony. Despite this stringent state control, the club became the central hub of Soviet rock and remained highly influential until its closure in 1991.
