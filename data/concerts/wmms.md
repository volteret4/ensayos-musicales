## curiosities
**Donna Halper and Rush's Breakthrough** : Cleveland rock station WMMS, through DJ Donna Halper, played a pivotal role in Rush's breakthrough in the United States. Halper, known for her open-mindedness, began playing "Working Man," leading to listener demand and the band's eventual success.
