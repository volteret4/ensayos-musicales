# venue - King Tut's Wah Wah Hut

## curiosities
**Oasis's Breakthrough Performance (1993)** : On Sunday, May 31st, 1993, Oasis, not on the official bill, bullied their way onto the stage at this Glasgow, Scotland venue. Their aggressive demand to play, under threat of burning the club down, led to their being seen by Alan McGee of Creation Records, who was so impressed he signed them on the spot, launching their career. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
