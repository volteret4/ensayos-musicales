## curiosities
**Sex Pistols' First Gig (November 6, 1975)** : This London art school hosted the Sex Pistols' first ever live gig in a small upstairs room on a Thursday night. The Sex Pistols were the opening act, playing only two songs before being shut down by the school's social programmer. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
