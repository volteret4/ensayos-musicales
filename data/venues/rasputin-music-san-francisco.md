# venue - Rasputin Music, San Francisco

## curiosities
**First Record Store Day Headline Event (April 19, 2008)** : This San Francisco record store hosted Metallica for an autograph session on April 19, 2008, drawing fans who waited up to three days. This endorsement by a "big artist" helped give the inaugural Record Store Day legitimacy. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
