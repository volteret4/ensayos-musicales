# venue - Glastonbury

## curiosities
**1990s Gold Standard** : By the 1990s, Glastonbury was considered the gold standard against which all other festivals were judged. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Annual Greenfield Festival** : Known as the largest annual Greenfield Festival in the world, Glastonbury takes place for five days almost every June. It has been held annually since 1981, with "fallow years" occurring approximately every half-decade or so, the idea being to give the land and the people who live in the area a break from the hordes. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Cultural Significance** : The festival has become a rite of passage for generations of music fans, a place where legends are born, careers are made and occasionally sidelined there, based on a single performance. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Founding and History (1970)** : Glastonbury Festival was first staged in the fall of 1970 by Michael Evis, who was inspired to create the event after seeing Led Zeppelin play outdoors. Evis drew upon an earlier Glastonbury tradition, a classical music and arts event held between 1914 and 1926. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Notable Performers** : The list of acts who have played Glastonbury is extensive, including U2, Radiohead, Foo Fighters, David Bowie, The Cure, Coldplay, Paul McCartney, The Rolling Stones, Neil Young, Bruce Springsteen, and Muse. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Original Name and Location** : Michael Evis initially named his festival the Piltdown Festival, though this name only lasted for one year. The festival is held on Worthy Farm, which is a working dairy farm. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Planned Return in 2021** : The festival announced its intention to return in June 2021, contingent on the successful distribution and efficacy of vaccines. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nYPGQiDL5bw
**Scale and Attendance** : Glastonbury attracts somewhere in the neighborhood of 175,000 people per event, who attend regardless of weather conditions. The festival is so popular that all tickets sell out in less than an hour, months before any acts are even announced. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
