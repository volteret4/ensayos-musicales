# venue - The Factory

## curiosities
**Andy Warhol's Performance Art Headquarters** : Located on East 47th Street in New York City, The Factory became the official home for The Velvet Underground after Andy Warhol discovered them at Cafe Bizarre in 1965. Warhol served as their patron, producer, and promoter from this location. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
