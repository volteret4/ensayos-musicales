# venue - RCA Studios (Hollywood)

## curiosities
**Recording of "(I Can't Get No) Satisfaction"** : The final session for The Rolling Stones' seminal song "(I Can't Get No) Satisfaction" took place at RCA Studios in Hollywood. The song was released on May 12, 1965, just four weeks after this final recording session. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
