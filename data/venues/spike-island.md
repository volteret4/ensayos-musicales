# venue - Spike Island

## curiosities
**Host of The Stone Roses' Legendary Gig (May 27, 1990)** : This location hosted The Stone Roses' largest concert to date on May 27, 1990, which became a legendary event for bringing together Madchester and rave cultures despite an average performance. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
**Unconventional Festival Location (May 27, 1990)** : This site, a peninsula into the Mersey River, was a reclaimed chemical dump surrounded by chemical factories and power plants, leading to attendees experiencing "chemical burns" in addition to sunburns during The Stone Roses' concert. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
