# venue - Coachella

## curiosities
**Financial Losses and Debt** : The promoter lost close to a million dollars on the first Coachella, requiring them to ask bands like Tool and Rage Against the Machine to delay payment. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Inaugural Lineup (1999)** : The first Coachella lineup in 1999 included Beck, Morrissey, Underworld, Rage Against the Machine, Pavement, and Modest Mouse. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Initial Struggles (1999)** : The first event only drew 10,000 people, despite a strong lineup. Factors contributing to low attendance included the perceived unpleasantness of the drive from Los Angeles into the desert, the recent disaster of Woodstock '99, the lack of on-site camping, and intensely hot weather. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Location and Schedule** : The festival is held over two weekends every April at the Empire Polo Grounds in Indio, California, attracting attendees from all over the world. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Near Demise and Revival** : The inaugural Coachella was so difficult that it nearly "killed the entire concept." There was no event in 2000, but it successfully reappeared in 2001 and has been a continuously growing concern ever since. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Origin (1999)** : Coachella, a "monster" festival, is a significant descendant of Lollapalooza and is considered the North American equivalent to Glastonbury. It would not exist without the alt-rock atmosphere of the 1990s. While often perceived as a 21st-century event, it actually began on October 9-10, 1999. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Pop Punk Headliner Decline (2010s)** : pop punk bands no longer headlined this festival in the 2010s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
