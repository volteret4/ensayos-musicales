## curiosities
**First Modern Music Festival (1967)** : Monterey Pop, held in San Francisco in the summer of 1967, is considered the first modern music festival, recognizable by today's standards. It led to the creation of other major festivals like Woodstock, Glastonbury, and Roskilde. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
