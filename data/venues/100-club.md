# venue - 100 Club

## curiosities
**Britain's First Official Punk Rock Festival (September 20 & 21, 1976)** : The 100 Club was the site of Britain's first official punk rock festival, featuring bands like Sex Pistols, The Clash, and The Damned, attracting lines around the block and drawing record company types and music press, despite performances not being "very good."
**Former Jazz Venue** : Located on Oxford Street in London, this club was primarily known as a jazz venue until September 1976.
**Punk Rock's Public Debut (September 1976)** : After the 100 Club festival, punk rock was no longer a secret, marking a major turning point for the genre in the UK.
