# venue - Chateau Marmont Hotel

## curiosities
**Scott Weiland's Demo Recording (May 1995)** : Scott Weiland recorded his demo "Chateau Mars" on a cheap four-track recorder at the hotel.
**Scott Weiland's Stay After Arrest (May 1995)** : Scott Weiland stayed at the hotel in Courtney Love's room after his May 1995 drug arrest and his wife's refusal to let him come home.
