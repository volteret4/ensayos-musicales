# venue - Chateau Marmont Hotel

## curiosities
**After-Party Venue for Arthur Fogel Documentary** : The bar at the Chateau Marmont hosted the after-party for the Hollywood premiere of the "Who the F Is Arthur Fogel?" documentary, where Alan Cross interacted with Lady Gaga and Stewart Copeland. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Courtney Love's Residence (Temporarily)** : Courtney Love was residing at the Chateau Marmont Hotel on Sunset Boulevard in Los Angeles when she invited Alan Cross to her room after her court case. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Scott Weiland's Demo Recording (May 1995)** : Scott Weiland recorded his demo "Chateau Mars" on a cheap four-track recorder at the hotel. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TDwagoyMvck
**Scott Weiland's Stay After Arrest (May 1995)** : Scott Weiland stayed at the hotel in Courtney Love's room after his May 1995 drug arrest and his wife's refusal to let him come home. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TDwagoyMvck
