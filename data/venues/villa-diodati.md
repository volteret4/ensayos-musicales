# venue - Villa Diodati

## curiosities
**Birthplace of Gothic Fiction (1816)** : Villa Diodati, a mansion near Geneva, Switzerland, served as the gathering place for four artsy individuals, including John William Polidori and Mary Shelley, during "the year without a summer" in 1816. It was here, amidst dark and rainy days, that they conceived "The Vampyre" and "Frankenstein," laying the foundation for gothic fiction. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XaLsZFLrMnI
