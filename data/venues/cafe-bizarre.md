# venue - Cafe Bizarre

## curiosities
**The Velvet Underground's Early Residency (1965)** : In December 1965, this Greenwich Village, New York City club was the site of The Velvet Underground's first regular gig. Despite playing six sets a night, six nights a week, for five dollars per set, their avant-garde music often drove away patrons, leading to their being fired multiple times before their eventual discovery by Andy Warhol. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
