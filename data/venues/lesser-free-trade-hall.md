# venue - Lesser Free Trade Hall

## curiosities
**Legendary Sex Pistols Gig (June 4, 1976)** : Hosted a pivotal and legendary performance by the Sex Pistols on June 4, 1976, a show arranged by the Buzzcocks. This event, despite a small audience, profoundly inspired many attendees to form influential bands and labels, including Georgia Vision, Pet Shop Boys, Simply Red, The Smiths, and Factory Records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
