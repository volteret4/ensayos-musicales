# venue - Père Lachaise Cemetery (Paris)

## curiosities
**Final Resting Place of Music Icons** : This historic cemetery is the burial site of Jim Morrison of The Doors (1971), and the location where the ashes of Stiv Bators of The Dead Boys were sprinkled on Morrison's grave (1990). In 2025, due to its full capacity, officials launched a lottery offering new burial plots. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
