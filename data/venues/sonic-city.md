# venue - Sonic City

## curiosities
**Site of John Frusciante's First Departure (May 7, 1992)** : John Frusciante refused to go on stage at this venue in Japan on May 7, 1992, minutes before a scheduled show, announcing his first departure from the Red Hot Chili Peppers. He was eventually convinced to play one final, "bad" 15-song set. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
