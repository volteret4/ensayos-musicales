## curiosities
**25th Anniversary Festival (1994)** : Green Day performed at the 25th anniversary of Woodstock in Saugerties, New York, in August 1994. The set was famous for descending into a mud fight with the crowd, during which bass player Mike Dirnt lost two teeth after running into a security guard and hitting his face on a speaker cabinet. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nyfdGYWelic
