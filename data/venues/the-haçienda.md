# venue - The Haçienda

## curiosities
**Eventual Demise** : The club eventually succumbed to problems related to drugs, gangs, and debt. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Initial Failure and Later Success** : The club was initially a "miserable failure," but it became "the center of the universe" once UK club culture took off. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Manchester Landmark (1980s)** : The Haçienda was an infamous club in Manchester, co-owned by New Order and Factory Records. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**New Order's Manchester Nightclub (1982)** : The Haçienda was a nightclub in Manchester, an idea conceived by New Order (among others, including manager Rob Gretton). It was built in a former yacht showroom and became a central hub of the Manchester music scene and a significant part of 1980s DJ culture. Despite its cultural impact, it was a "money pit" and a "complete and utter financial disaster," costing New Order millions of pounds from its opening in 1982. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
