## curiosities
**Restoration and Preservation of Tonto** : In 2013, the Tonto synthesizer was put up for sale and subsequently purchased and restored by the National Music Center in Calgary. It is now a major and important part of the Center's keyboard collection, ensuring its preservation and continued recognition for its historical significance. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
