# venue - Joshua Tree (studio)

## curiosities
**Foo Fighters Recording** : The Foo Fighters recorded their song "Outside" (2014) from the "Sonic Highways" album here.
**Location** : Located in the desert of Joshua Tree, California.
**Notable Clients** : Has hosted Queens of the Stone Age, Arctic Monkeys, and Daniel N. Waugh.
**Unique Setup** : The studio doubles as the owner's house, with clients typically bunking and cooking there during their sessions.
