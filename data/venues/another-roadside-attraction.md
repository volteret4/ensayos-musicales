# venue - Another Roadside Attraction

## curiosities
**Founding and Concept (1993)** : "Another Roadside Attraction" was a traveling festival created by The Tragically Hip, who invited "a bunch of friends" to tour with them for portions of the summer. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Tour Dates** : The festival was staged three times: in 1993, 1995, and 1997. A recording from the original 1993 tour was featured. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
