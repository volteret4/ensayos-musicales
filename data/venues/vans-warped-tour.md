# venue - Vans Warped Tour

## curiosities
**"Punk Purist" Backlash** : The corporate sponsorship caused punk purists to "bristle."
**Corporate Sponsorship (1996)** : Took on corporate sponsor Vans, a skate shoe maker, in 1996.
**Duration** : Ran for 24 summers.
**Featured Acts** : In its first few years, featured dozens of acts including Bad Religion, Dancehall Crashers, Face to Face, Buck-O-Nine, Less Than Jake, Mighty Mighty Bosstones, No Doubt, Sublime, and Goldfinger.
**Focus on Pop Punk & Ska Punk** : Embraced pop punk and ska punk from its inception due to audience demand.
**Inception (1995)** : Began in 1995.
