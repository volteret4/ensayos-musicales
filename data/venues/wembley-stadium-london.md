# venue - Wembley Stadium (London)

## curiosities
**Oasis Sets Beer Consumption Record (2025)** : During their 2025 reunion gigs, Oasis fans set a record for the most beer consumed at Wembley Stadium, with 250,000 pints (4,500 KG) consumed over the first three nights (90,000 people per gig), averaging 2.5 pints per person. This significantly surpassed consumption at Coldplay (120,000 pints/night) and Taylor Swift (40,000 pints/night) concerts. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0DFmS6MPbFI
