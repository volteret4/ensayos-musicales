# venue - Inner Ear Studios

## curiosities
**Dave Grohl's Childhood Heroes** : Many of Dave Grohl's heroes and favorite bands from his childhood made records at this studio.
**Foo Fighters Recording** : The Foo Fighters recorded their song "Feast or Famine" (2014) from the "Sonic Highways" album here. Bad Brains also recorded here.
**Location** : Arlington, Virginia.
**Longevity** : Has been in operation for over three decades.
