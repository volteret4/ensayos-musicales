# venue - Woodstock '99

## curiosities
**Disastrous Outcome** : The event was marred by widespread "vandalism, sexual assault, fires, [and] proper damage." It is unfavorably compared to the Altamont Free Concert of the 1960s, described as "Woodstock '99 was to the 90s, where Altamont was to the 1960s, and that is not a good comparison." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
