# venue - Queen's University (Kingston)

## curiosities
**Honorary Degrees (2016)** : The Tragically Hip received honorary degrees from their alma mater. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**Road Apples Writing Location (c. 1991)** : The bulk of The Tragically Hip's second album, "Road Apples," was written on a stage at Queen's University approximately 25 years prior to 2016. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
**The Tragically Hip's Origins** : The Tragically Hip formed in Kingston, in the "shadows" of this institution, where three of its members were students. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um_KEEcCeT4
