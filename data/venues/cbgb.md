# venue - CBGB

## curiosities
**Birthplace of American New Wave – Blondie Origins** : CBGB served as the launching ground for Blondie and many other artsy bands, becoming a central hub for the emergence of New Wave and punk scenes in New York. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZPlsR7_WiZQ
**Full Name and "Gorman Dysers"** : The full and proper name of the club is CBGB OMFUG, which stands for "Country, Bluegrass, Blues, and Other Music For Uplifting Gorman Dysers," a phrase speculated to be an unlikely anagrammatic connection to Imagine Dragons' name. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GmYlTwI_TQQ
**Living Colour Performances** : Living Colour played at CBGB in New York City early in their career. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**The Ramones Debut (1974)** : This New York club hosted The Ramones' first-ever show on August 14th, 1974, with only a handful of attendees. By the end of 1974, The Ramones had played there 74 times, with each gig increasing their buzz and leading to the club being packed with a diverse audience, marking the start of the New York punk rock revolution. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
