# venue - Record Stores

## curiosities
**Community Hubs** : Independent record stores were seen as places for like-minded individuals to meet and connect over music, providing a social experience beyond just purchasing. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
**Decline in Influence** : Traditional record stores are noted as having declined significantly in influence and operations due to the rise of streaming and digital music consumption. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
**Diminished Power in Streaming Era** : "started to close" as selling physical media went by the wayside, losing power as "cultural gatekeepers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Impact of COVID-19** : the pandemic "killed off a lot of record stores," both those trading in new and used vinyl. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Origin of Record Store Day** : independent record store owners in Baltimore came up with the idea for Record Store Day to revive their fortunes amidst declining sales and piracy. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Role in 80s Alt-Rock** : were part of the "outsider" scene in the 80s, reveling in their non-mainstream status. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Symbiotic Relationship with Labels** : Record labels maintained a symbiotic relationship with thousands of record stores for over 100 years, relying on them for product sales. This relationship was threatened by the labels' potential move into direct digital sales. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
