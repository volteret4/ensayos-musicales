# venue - Record Stores

## curiosities
**Diminished Power in Streaming Era** : "started to close" as selling physical media went by the wayside, losing power as "cultural gatekeepers."
**Impact of COVID-19** : the pandemic "killed off a lot of record stores," both those trading in new and used vinyl.
**Origin of Record Store Day** : independent record store owners in Baltimore came up with the idea for Record Store Day to revive their fortunes amidst declining sales and piracy.
**Role in 80s Alt-Rock** : were part of the "outsider" scene in the 80s, reveling in their non-mainstream status.
