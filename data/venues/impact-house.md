# venue - Impact House

## curiosities
**Scott Weiland Dropped from Program (April 16, 1999)** : The facility informed Judge Fiddler that Scott Weiland had been dropped from treatment for failing to comply with the program's terms, leading to a court order for him to enter another rehab facility.
**Scott Weiland's Disappearance (June 26, 1996)** : Weiland disappeared from the facility, leading to an arrest warrant.
**Scott Weiland's Six-Month Stay (April 1996)** : Scott Weiland was transferred to this residential drug treatment facility for what was intended to be a six-month stay.
