# venue - Bob Roberts Tattoo

## curiosities
**Early and Famous Los Angeles Tattoo Shop** : Bob Roberts Tattoo was one of the earliest and highly renowned tattoo shops in Los Angeles. It was known for being extremely difficult to secure an appointment, often booked up years in advance, and was frequented by artists like Flea of the Red Hot Chili Peppers. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZeMmPekKMQ
