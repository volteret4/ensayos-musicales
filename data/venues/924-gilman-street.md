# venue - 924 Gilman Street

## curiosities
**Major Label Deal Backlash** : Following Green Day's signing with Warner Brothers, old school fans picketed some of their shows at Gilman Street, angered by the band's perceived betrayal of their indie roots. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nyfdGYWelic
**Oakland Teen Punk Scene Hub** : This club catered to Oakland's teen punk scene and was a regular haunt for Tré Cool and other Green Day members in their early days. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nyfdGYWelic
**Still Exists (Modern Day)** : The club is still located on Gilman Street in Oakland, not far from San Francisco, and can be found near a donut shop. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nyfdGYWelic
