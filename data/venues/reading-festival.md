# venue - Reading Festival

## curiosities
**Embrace of Genres** : It became the first major festival to significantly embrace British punk, British new wave, and British metal. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Notable Performers** : Over the years, Reading has hosted major acts including Green Day, Oasis, Blur, Muse, The Cure, The Rolling Stones, Pink Floyd, The Who, Black Sabbath, Metallica, Guns N' Roses, and AC/DC. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Origin and Transition** : The Reading Festival, the older of the two twin festivals, originated as a jazz festival in the 1950s that ran until the 1970s before transitioning into a rock event. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Shared Lineup with Leeds** : Along with Leeds Festival, Reading shares a lineup with gigs held on the same days every August. Acts perform at both locations, requiring them to make a 300-kilometer journey between the cities as schedules are juggled. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
