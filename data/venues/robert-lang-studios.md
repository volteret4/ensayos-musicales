# venue - Robert Lang Studios

## curiosities
**Alice in Chains' Favorite Spot** : A favorite recording location for Alice in Chains.
**First Foo Fighters Recording** : The very first Foo Fighters record was made here.
**Foo Fighters Recording** : The Foo Fighters recorded their song "Subterranean" (2014) from the "Sonic Highways" album here.
**Location** : North of Seattle.
**Nirvana's Last Recording** : Nirvana recorded their last ever completed song at this studio.
