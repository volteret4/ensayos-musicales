# venue - Warped Tour

## curiosities
**Extensive Lineup History** : The list of artists who participated over the years is staggering and so extensive that even the organizers reportedly do not have a clear record of every performer. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Founder** : Kevin Lyman, a California-based event producer, created the Warped Tour. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Inception and Concept (1995)** : The Warped Tour began in 1995, taking inspiration from Lollapalooza's traveling festival concept. It crisscrossed North America, occasionally veering into Canada, with a "super efficient self-contained operation" that offered "maximum music for a low, low price." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Longevity** : The Warped Tour eventually became the longest-running North American traveling rock show. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
**Scale and Genre Diversity** : Each stop of the Warped Tour featured dozens of bands. Initially focused on punk, it eventually branched out to include other genres like hip hop. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7JP2tjJibTQ
