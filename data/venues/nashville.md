# venue - Nashville

## curiosities
**Johnny Cash Museum** : The Johnny Cash Museum in Nashville is where the chair used by Johnny Cash in his "Hurt" music video is on permanent display behind glass. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
**Sex Pistols Performance** : This club was the venue for a gig where The 101ers played and the Sex Pistols were the opening act, notably influencing Joe Strummer. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oPgeSCy93bo
