# venue - Abbey Road Studios

## curiosities
**"Lady Madonna Piano" in Studio 2** : A beat-up upright piano in Studio 2, famously used by The Beatles, bears a "Lady Madonna Piano. Do not touch." sign. Alan Cross admitted to touching it despite the warning. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Daily Operations** : Abbey Road Studios have been run in a structured manner since the 1930s, with a bell ringing at 9 AM to signal the start of the workday. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Equipment Diversity** : The studios house a wide array of equipment, ranging from vintage gear from the 1940s and 1950s to brand new installations. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Historical Client Roster** : Numerous renowned artists and bands have recorded albums at Abbey Road Studios, including The Beatles, Pink Floyd, Oasis, Blur, Muse, Kate Bush, Depeche Mode, Iron Maiden, Queen, U2, Michael Jackson, and Glenn Miller. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Paul McCartney Crosswalk Recreation** : For a documentary about Abbey Road Studios by his daughter Mary McCartney, Paul McCartney recreated his famous walk across the nearby crosswalk, during which he was almost hit by a car. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qtdetc5-MQo
**Private Tour (Early Morning)** : Alan Cross secured a private tour of Abbey Road Studios through EMI connections, attending at 8 AM before normal work hours began. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Studio 1 Features and Activity** : Studio 1 is described as the smallest of the studios and was "jammed with gear." Florence + The Machine were "midway through recording their 'Ceremonials' album" there during Alan Cross's visit. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Studio 2 Historical Significance** : Studio 2 is historically significant as the primary recording location for The Beatles (with George Martin) and where Pink Floyd's "Dark Side of the Moon" was created. Its control room overlooks the studio space from one floor up. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Studio 3 Features and Activity** : Studio 3 is described as being the size of a couple of basketball courts with a very high ceiling. During Alan Cross's visit, a 65-piece orchestra was recording the soundtrack for "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
