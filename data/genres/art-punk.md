# genre - Art Punk

## curiosities
**Art Punk – British Art School Tradition; Fall, Wire, XTC, Television, Patti Smith, Art Brut** : Art Punk is rooted in the British art school tradition — institutions that parked creative kids who weren't going to make it in university, focused on graphic design, visual arts, theater, and fine art. Art Punk is like Garage Rock (raw, rugged, intense) but with a more sophisticated twist: more educated cleverness, sometimes incorporating theater and performance art, less working class. Key bands include the Fall, Wire, XTC, Television, Yeah Yeah Yeahs, Suicide, and Patti Smith. Art Brut (part British, part German) took their name from a form of visual art made by outsiders like prisoners and the mentally ill.
