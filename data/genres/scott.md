# genre - Scott

## curiosities
**More Granular Classification** : In 1955, "Scott" was listed as one of the thinner slices into which genres could be categorized, providing a more detailed classification than the four main buckets. This indicates an early tendency towards genre specificity. "You could slice certain genres into thinner slices to include big band, Dixie Land, Scott, Hillbilly, and Jazz, gospel, Broadway show music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
