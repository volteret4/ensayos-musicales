# genre - Waltzing

## curiosities
**Historical Scandalousness** : The waltz, now considered "civilized dancing," was once deemed scandalous. Its controversy stemmed from the requirement for a man and a woman to dance in close proximity and touch each other, which was considered inappropriate at the time of its introduction.
