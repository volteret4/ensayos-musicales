**Finite Musical Combinations** : The Western musical scale consists of just 12 notes, and while the ways they can be combined into pleasing sounds or chords are numerous, the number is ultimately finite. This foundational principle explains why many songs share similar chord progressions and structures without necessarily implying plagiarism. "There are just 12 notes in the Western musical scale.
