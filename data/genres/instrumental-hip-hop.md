# genre - Instrumental Hip Hop

## curiosities
**Culmination with DJ Shadow's Endtroducing.....** : DJ Shadow's 1996 album, *Endtroducing.....*, is seen as a culminating point in the musical development process of instrumental hip hop. From a conceptual standpoint, the genre reached a climax with this album, with its evolution becoming notably less pronounced afterward, almost marking an end to a particular path rather than a beginning. **Influence of Manual Desynchronization in the 90s** : The distinct sound of sample-based instrumental hip hop in the 1990s was heavily influenced by the manual timing imperfections, or "desacompasamientos," that resulted from artists manually triggering samples. This characteristic, due to the hands-on nature of the technology and the need to adjust timings by ear, became a defining feature of the genre during that decade, exemplified by artists like J Dilla and DJ Shadow.
