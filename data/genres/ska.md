# genre - Ska

## curiosities
**Potential for Strong Rhythm Sections** : Skabands are mentioned as potentially having strong rhythm sections.
**Third Wave Revival** : Jerry Dammers of The Specials committed to bringing ska to a new generation, thereby spearheading the "Third Wave Ska" revival.
**West Indian Influence in UK** : Its sounds, along with Reggae, were introduced to industrial cities like Coventry by West Indian immigrants.
