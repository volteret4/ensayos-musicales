# genre - Punk funk

## curiosities
**Hybrid Post-Punk Genre** : Punk funk was a popular name for a danceable post-punk sound at the dawn of the 1980s, incorporating a funky rhythm section and other elements from new wave, Kraut Rock, funk, rap, and Jamaican dub, sometimes co-opting the "four on the floor" disco beat.
