# genre - Punk funk

## curiosities
**Hybrid Post-Punk Genre** : Punk funk was a popular name for a danceable post-punk sound at the dawn of the 1980s, incorporating a funky rhythm section and other elements from new wave, Kraut Rock, funk, rap, and Jamaican dub, sometimes co-opting the "four on the floor" disco beat. "This post-punk genre was known by a number of names. Disco punk, techno punk, dance punk, and punk punk." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
**Influence of Public Image Limited** : Public Image Limited significantly influenced the punk funk genre, and many bands recognized their pioneering role.
