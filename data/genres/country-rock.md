# genre - Country rock

## curiosities
**Artist Dislike for Label** : Many 1970s singers and bands labeled as country rock actively disliked the term, preferring alternative descriptors for their blended sound. This aversion reflects the often-unwanted imposition of genre classifications on evolving music. **Commercial Struggles** : Country rock faced significant commercial challenges in the 1970s and 1980s, struggling to achieve big record sales and secure radio airplay. It was often characterized as being "too rock for country" and "too country for rock," highlighting its difficulty fitting into established genre formats.
