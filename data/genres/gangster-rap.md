**Rise and Fragmentation of Music Video Culture** : The rise of gangster rap contributed significantly to the fragmentation of music video culture. Alongside alt-rock, it helped divide everything into distinct "scenes," leading to the demise of the music video monoculture and prompting video channels to develop genre-specific programming like "Yo MTV Raps" and "Raps City."
