# genre - Indy Rock

## curiosities
**Early 2000s Revival** : The early 2000s saw a significant Indy Rock revival, marked by the emergence of exciting and powerful new bands. This movement included groups such as The White Stripes, The Strokes, Interpol, the Yeah Yeah Yeahs, Franz Ferdinand, Arctic Monkeys, Arcade Fire, The Killers, and The Hives.
