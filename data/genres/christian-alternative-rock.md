**Growing Area of Popular Music** : This genre is highlighted as a very large and expanding area within popular music, garnering significant attention from fans. "They quite rightly point out that this is one area of popular music that's very big and getting bigger." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Limited Appeal Perception** : Despite its growth, the genre is often perceived by the mainstream music industry as having "built-in, limited appeal" due to its religious connotations. "But because of the scene's religious connotations, it's considered to have built-in, limited appeal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
**Mainstream Industry Misunderstanding** : The mainstream music industry often struggles to understand how to handle these bands and their values, being more accustomed to promoting themes of rebellion, sex, and violence rather than "God business." "Most of the mainstream music industry doesn't know what to do with these bands and their values." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g-tBn8dsSqw
