# genre - Death Metal

## curiosities
**Corpse Paint Link** : Peter Ebling, singer of The Undead in *Phantom of the Paradise* (1974), wore corpse paint similar to modern death metal or black metal.
**Emergence in Mid-1980s** : did not appear until the mid-1980s.
**Possible Proto-Origins in 1974** : Alan Cross theorizes that *Phantom of the Paradise* (1974) and its fictional band The Undead, with corpse paint and macabre themes, could be considered a proto-death metal group.
