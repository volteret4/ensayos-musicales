# genre - Intimist music

## curiosities
**Jeff Buckley's Pioneering Style** : Jeff Buckley's music is characterized as "intimista," "tan cantado toda tan triste tan melancólica" (so sung, so sad, so melancholic). His style was ahead of its time in the early 1990s when grunge dominated, but it later gained significant recognition and influence among a new generation of artists like Thom Yorke, Rufus Wainwright, and Anthony and the Johnsons, who were inspired by his sound. **Post-Grunge Resurgence** : After the decline of grunge, there was a resurgence of "musica más intimista" (more intimate music), with many artists drawing inspiration from Jeff Buckley's pioneering approach. This new wave of introspective sound helped solidify Buckley's posthumous legacy and increased his public recognition, creating a need for the kind of music he produced.
