# genre - punk rock

## curiosities
**The Cramps' Counter-Narrative** : While often associated with the punk movement, The Cramps publicly stated they did not identify with it. They argued that punk "disavowed the origins of rock and roll and wanted to destroy everything," whereas The Cramps embraced and celebrated rock's historical roots as "mythomaniacs." "Ellos decían no sentirse identificados con el movimiento, ya que el punk renego a los orígenes del rock and roll y quería acabar con todo, en cambio de Crams eran unos mitómanos." ← Amor, Terror y Psychobilly： La Historia de The Cramps | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tBVTzVQDQeQ
