# genre - Punk rock

## curiosities
**Beyond Music** : Punk evolved beyond a mere musical form, becoming "a way of thinking and acting and creating and presenting" that permeated music, film, visual art, literature, dance, and politics, significantly altering Western thought with its "screw you, I'm going to do it anyway" ethos. "And punk became more than just a form of music. It became a way of thinking and acting and creating and presenting." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YaYaFW2q6qI
**British Punk Ethos** : British punk became a vehicle for rebellion, aiming to "kick against the upper classes and the monarchy," shock older generations, challenge established institutions, and destroy the status quo, effectively giving power to the powerless. "British punk went beyond just mere music. It involved kicking against the upper classes in the monarchy. It was about shocking the older generations. It was about challenging all manner of established institutions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YaYaFW2q6qI
**Counterculture Development (1978-79)** : During the "winter of discontent" (1978-79), exacerbated by widespread strikes and extreme cold, punk rock intensified, transforming "from being this innocuous sort of thing into a genuine counterculture." "All this simply increased the heat of punk. It went from being this innocuous sort of thing into a genuine counterculture." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YaYaFW2q6qI

This highlights their enduring foundational role in the evolution of contemporary rock genres. **Los Angeles Adoption** : Los Angeles, a natural home for the alienated and unconventional, quickly adopted punk rock once records from New York bands like the Ramones and singles from the Sex Pistols began circulating.
