# genre - Glam Rock

## curiosities
**London Scene and Public Perception** : Glam rock was a prominent musical and fashion movement in London. While it was immensely popular among "teeny-boppers," many perceived it as having the "trappings of disposable bubblegum pop," indicating a divide in its cultural reception. "While glam rock was all the rage among teeny-boppers and had the trappings of disposable bubblegum popped to many people, Bowie was ready to take things to the next level." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4
**New York Dolls' Extension of Style** : The New York Dolls significantly extended the glam rock aesthetic, pushing the look "miles further" with their makeup, spandex, and fishnets, creating a highly shocking and provocative image in Manhattan. "And yeah, people had heard about this glam rock thing that was happening in London, but it was nothing like this. The dolls took that look miles further." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0xd0Hf5REg4
