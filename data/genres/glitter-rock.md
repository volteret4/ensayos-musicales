# genre - Glitter Rock

## curiosities
**Heavier American Variant** : In the United States, a heavier variant of glam, characterized by riffy guitars, was known as Glitter Rock, influencing bands like KISS, Cheap Trick, Quiet Riot, and Def Leppard. "This heavier variant was known as Glitter Rock in the United States." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
**Second Wave of Glam** : Glitter rock emerged as a second wave of glam, finding a significant home in America alongside British adherents like Gary Glitter. It was characterized by spandex and makeup, visible in artists like the New York Dolls, and the evolving careers of Lou Reed and Iggy Pop. "Meanwhile, there was a second wave of glam known as glitter rock, although it had its British adherence, convicted pedophile Gary Glitter being one of them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=blDNqVFheAw
