# genre - Neo-Psych

## curiosities
**Updating Psychedelic Sounds** : Neo-Psych emerged in the post-punk world as bands mined the psychedelic sounds of the late 1960s and early 1970s, updating them with new gear and a punk-influenced attitude. It manifested in both British and American forms, the latter known as the Paisley Underground.
