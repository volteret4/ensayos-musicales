# genre - Hot Cha-Cha

## curiosities
**Historical Racist Criticism** : Hot Cha-Cha was targeted in a British newspaper review which described it as deplorable and tribal, originating from America and stemming from the "jungle," suggesting it might be "the Negro's revenge." This criticism also linked hot cha-cha with other genres like ragtime, blues, jazz, and boogie-woogie under the same racist condemnation in 1956. "It follows ragtime, blues, jazz, hot cha-cha, and the boogie-woogie, which surely originated in the jungle. We sometimes wonder whether this is the Negro's revenge." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L0j9iDuHQVA
