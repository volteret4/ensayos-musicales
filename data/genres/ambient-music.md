# genre - Ambient Music

## curiosities
**Brian Eno's Invention** : Brian Eno is credited with the invention of the entire concept and genre of ambient music through several of his albums, thereby establishing a foundation for many other electronic music genres. "Several of these albums invented the whole concept and genre of ambient music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU ← ambient-music
**Distinction from Post-Rock** : Ambient music aims to create a musical environment that soothes listeners but differs from post-rock in its lack of climax or defined end. While ambient music drifts, post-rock typically takes the listener on a journey with a specific destination. "Ambient music drifts. There's almost no climax or defined end. Post-rock takes you on a journey with a destination." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY ← ambient-music
**Foundation for Electronic Music** : Ambient music, as pioneered by Brian Eno, laid the groundwork and created the foundations for a multitude of different electronic music genres. **Offshoot of Synthesizer Revolution** : This genre grew directly out of the synthesizer revolution, characterized by its focus on atmospherics and sonic textures, often featuring a complete absence of a beat. "Others went in the completely opposite direction, ambient music, with its focus on atmospherics and sonic textures and often a complete lack of beat grew out of the synthesizer revolution as well." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng ← ambient-music

**Origin Story (Car Accident)** : The genre's conception by Brian Eno was directly influenced by a 1975 car accident that left him bedridden for months. During this time, being unable to adjust the volume of very quiet "heart music," he had an epiphany about music's potential to create a harmonious sensory experience akin to color or light. "In 1975, he got into a car accident, and this led him bedridden for several months." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU ← ambient-music
**Soundscape Concept** : After his accident, Eno began experimenting with synthesizers to develop a "soft mesmerizing sound that gently went through various repetitions," which he termed "soundscapes" rather than mere sounds, a concept he fully mastered by 1979. "These weren't so much sounds as they were sound scapes, and he called it ambient music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU ← ambient-music
