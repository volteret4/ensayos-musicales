# genre - Ambient Music

## curiosities
**Brian Eno's Invention and Foundation of Genre** : Brian Eno is credited with inventing the concept and genre of ambient music, which subsequently laid the foundations for many different genres of electronic music. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
**Car Accident Inspiration for Ambient Music** : In 1975, a car accident that left Brian Eno bedridden for several months led him to conceive of music as having properties similar to color or light, inspiring his experimental development of "sound scapes" and the ambient music genre using synthesizers. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
