# genre - Ambient Music

## curiosities
**Distinction from Post-Rock** : Ambient music aims to create a musical environment that soothes listeners but differs from post-rock in its lack of climax or defined end. While ambient music drifts, post-rock typically takes the listener on a journey with a specific destination. "Ambient music drifts. There's almost no climax or defined end. Post-rock takes you on a journey with a destination." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-v4qnOO8wY
