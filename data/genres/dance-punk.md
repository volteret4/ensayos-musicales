# genre - Dance punk

## curiosities
**Hybrid Post-Punk Genre** : Dance punk was a term for a post-punk genre, interchangeable with others like disco punk and punk funk around 1979-1980. It integrated elements from New Wave, Kraut Rock, funk, rap, Jamaican dub, and disco, often employing disco's "four on the floor" rhythm with slithering bass lines and punk/New Wave instrumentation.
