# genre - Military marches

## curiosities
**Alternative to "Exciting" Music for Elders** : In the early 20th century, as a response to the perceived "cacophony" of new music like jazz, elders suggested military marches as a suitable alternative for "exciting music," praising them for promoting "good discipline" and suitability for marching. "The elders said, look, if you want exciting music, there are military marches, good discipline music for marching up and down the square." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m7MbV-eCLyE
