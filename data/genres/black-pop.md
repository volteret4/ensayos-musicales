# genre - Black Pop

## curiosities
**Redefinition by TONTO Synthesizer** : The TONTO synthesizer, through its use in Stevie Wonder's seminal 1970s albums, significantly redefined what Black Pop music could be. It introduced advanced electronic textures and multi-layered arrangements that influenced future productions in the genre, including those by Quincy Jones for Michael Jackson. "Tonto changed the direction of Stevie Wonder's career. And he changed what Black Pop music could be." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6SdPPsmpgJg
