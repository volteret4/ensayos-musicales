# genre - Alternative Rap

## curiosities
**Experimental Counterpart to Mainstream Rap** : Alternative Rap is defined not as a direct fusion of alt rock and rap, but rather as an experimental approach to hip hop that stands as an alternative to mainstream rap, mirroring how alt rock challenged mainstream rock. "This isn't exactly a melting of Alt Rock and Rap, but instead it's Rap that's an alternative to the mainstream of rap, just like Alt Rock was once an alternative to the mainstream of rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
