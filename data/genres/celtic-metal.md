# genre - Celtic Metal

## curiosities
**Origin and Influences** : Celtic metal developed from the idea that if traditional Celtic influences could merge with punk, they could also combine with metal. Cruachan, formed in Dublin in 1992, is often cited as the first Celtic metal band, drawing inspiration from the British metal band Skyclad and the folk stylings of Horslips, and adding death metal elements. "Listen, if these traditional influences and themes could go punk, why not metal? If we must point to the first Celtic metal band, we should probably go with Cruahan. They were formed in Dublin in 1992, taking cues not only from a British metal band called Skyclad, but also the folk styleings of horse-lips.
