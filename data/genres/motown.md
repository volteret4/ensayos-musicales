**Local Musicians for Live Gigs – Iggy Pop's Role** : In the mid-sixties, many Motown acts such as The Shangri-La's, The Temptations, and The Four Tops toured with only a couple of core musicians (typically a guitar and piano player). They would then hire local musicians, including bass players and drummers, to fill out the live band, rehearsing briefly before each show. Iggy Pop was one such local drummer, highly skilled at picking up songs quickly. "When a lot of the Motown acts at the time You know the Shangri-La's and the temptations and the four tops and others played live gigs They toured just with a couple of musicians usually guitar player and maybe a piano player The rest of the band including the bass player and the drummer would be hired locally." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo ← motown
