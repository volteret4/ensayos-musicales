# genre - Rave Culture

## curiosities
**Meeting of Minds at Spike Island (1990)** : Rave culture, which was popular in the late 1980s, converged significantly with the Madchester scene at The Stone Roses' Spike Island concert in May 1990. Attendees from both subcultures discovered commonalities, with "a little ecstasy" facilitating their interaction. "The mad Chester kids and the rave kids went face to face and with the help of a little ecstasy found that they had a lot in common." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI
