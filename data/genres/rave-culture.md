**Decline of Rave Culture** : The club and rave culture that was prominent in the UK during the 1980s had waned by the early 1990s, contributing to a void in homegrown British music. **Meeting of Minds at Spike Island (1990)** : Rave culture, which was popular in the late 1980s, converged significantly with the Madchester scene at The Stone Roses' Spike Island concert in May 1990. Attendees from both subcultures discovered commonalities, with "a little ecstasy" facilitating their interaction. "The mad Chester kids and the rave kids went face to face and with the help of a little ecstasy found that they had a lot in common." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qC0Ee4-dpnI ← rave-culture
