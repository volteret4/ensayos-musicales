# genre - Skiffle

## curiosities
**Mistakenly Predicted as New Craze (1956)** : In 1956, the *Daily Mirror* newspaper mistakenly predicted that "think-a-fold music" (skiffle) would be the new coming craze, supplanting rock and roll, which they declared "out of date, old-fashioned." They observed London's espresso bars turning into modern versions of old-time music halls, indicating a shift they believed would lead to skiffle's dominance.
