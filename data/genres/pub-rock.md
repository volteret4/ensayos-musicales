# genre - Pub Rock

## curiosities
**Characteristics and Scene** : Pub rock comprised a tight network of British bands that wore denim and other "glam-free outfits," performing home-spun rock and roll in small clubs around the London area. These bands explicitly rejected album-oriented rock, favoring songs typically two to three minutes in length, and played with a "proto-punk economy." This scene provided the foundation for his distinct sound and songwriting approach.
