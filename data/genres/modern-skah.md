# genre - Modern Skah

## curiosities
**British Invention and Rapid Spread** : Modern Skah was a British invention that rapidly emerged in the wake of punk. Along with Goth, Technopop, and Industrial, these styles spread quickly and often had short lifespans, largely due to the dense, interconnected nature of the British Isles. "There was Goth and Tecdopop and Industrial, Modern Skah, which were all basically British inventions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zB5zxycrbnY
**Post-Punk Revival and Decline** : Modern Skah was a significant post-punk sound, heavily promoted by the British music press through bands like The Specials, The Selector, and Madness. Its popularity with the press declined when rockabilly was favored, and the scene's racial harmony was undermined by an invasion of racist skinheads, causing it to "lay foul" for a while.
