**Grimbey's "Sell-Out" Status** : Grimbey faced severe backlash and was labeled as "sell-outs" by the punk community after signing with Reprise Records, a label owned by Warner Brothers. This decision to move from an underground discography to a more professional, corporate structure was seen as a betrayal of punk ideals, despite the band initially rejecting even larger multinationals.
