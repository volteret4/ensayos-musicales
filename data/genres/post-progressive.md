# genre - Post-Progressive

## curiosities
**Alternative Term for Complex Rock** : Post-Progressive was offered as an alternative term for the complex and stretching styles of rock that emerged during the 2010s progressive rock comeback. This genre, which included elements of math rock and Djent, was defined more by an "attitude and an approach to instruments and arrangements" than a fixed sound.
