# genre - Ballroom dancing

## curiosities
**Backlash Against Jazz** : Ballroom dancing, particularly waltzes, was promoted as an answer to the "cacophony" of jazz as the genre spread in the early 20th century. This suggests a societal effort to counter the perceived disorder of jazz with more traditional and structured dance forms. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m7MbV-eCLyE
