**Popularity in Turn of the 20th Century (Turn of 1900s)** : At the turn of the 1900s, military music was "all the rage." It was characterized by "big brass instruments and two four rhythms pounded out on multiple snare and bass drums." This genre was considered "powerful, rousing music," drawing comparisons to the "heavy metal of its era."
