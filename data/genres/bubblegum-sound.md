# genre - Bubblegum Sound

## curiosities
**Influence on Powerpop (1960s-1970s)** : The "bubblegum sound" of the 1960s and early 1970s is recognized as a significant precursor to Powerpop. It laid the groundwork with its emphasis on big hooks, sing-along choruses, and themes of cars, girls, and parties, which Powerpop then amplified with chunky guitars and drums.
