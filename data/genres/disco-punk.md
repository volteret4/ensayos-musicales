# genre - Disco punk

## curiosities
**Hybrid Post-Punk Genre** : Disco punk was one of several names for a danceable post-punk genre that emerged around 1979-1980. It fused elements of New Wave, Kraut Rock, artsy punk from CBGB, funk, rap, Jamaican dub, and disco, creating grooves that encouraged movement. "This post-punk genre was known by a number of names.
