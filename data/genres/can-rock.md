# genre - Can-rock

## curiosities
**Prominence in 1990s Canada** : Can-rock was a dominant musical genre in Canada during the 1990s, characterized by a mass embracing of homegrown music driven by nationalism and patriotism. This period saw increased record deals, radio play, and the emergence of a new star system.
