# genre - Acid House

## curiosities
**Avant-Garde Sound Introduced at ACTV, Spreading to Puzzle** : Acid House was an avant-garde sound introduced by DJ Arturo Roger at ACTV, which subsequently spread to Puzzle. This genre played a role in the increasing electronic influence on the Ruta's music, contributing to the rising BPMs and "maquinero" (harder electronic) sound of the 90s. **Distinctive Sound and Instruments** : Acid House is characterized by its unique bass lines produced using Roland TB-303 synthesizers and rhythms created with TR-808 drum machines. This distinctive sound emerged in the mid-80s, playing a role in the further fracturing of dance music into numerous subgenres. "Acid House with its bass lines from Roland TV 303 synthesizers and TR808 drum machines." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0FYq5MBdHw4 ← acid-house ← acid-house
**Emergence in Megamixes (Post-Italo Disco)** : Following the cultural shift brought by "La Ruta y el Sonido Valencia," the Megamix phenomenon started incorporating faster and stronger musical styles. Acid House, along with New Beat and EBM, were among the first new genres to dominate these compilations, moving beyond the traditional Italo Disco sound. "Asid House, New It y EBM al principio y máquina y OroDens más adelante pasaron a dominar esos recopilatorios." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Explosion in the 1980s** : Acid House experienced an explosion in popularity during the 1980s, marking it as a significant youth culture with its own distinct sound, alongside Goth, Blitzkids, and Hip Hop. **Foundational Rave Music (UK)** : Acid House from Chicago, alongside House music, was a key genre that defined the sound of the first raves in the United Kingdom during the "Second Summer of Love" in the late 1980s. It was often blended with early local productions. **Influence on Big Beat Bass Lines** : Big Beat bass lines often recalled those of acid house, partly because the genre frequently used the Roland TB-303 synthesizer, which acid house popularized for its distinctive bass sounds. "Las líneas de bajo por lo general suelen ser también contundentes, y recuerdan a las del drama bass a las del hit house, ya que en muchos casos se empleaba al mismo sintetizador de bajos que popularizó a este genero, el Roland B303." ← Por qué el cine de los 2000 sonaba así？ Big Beat | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UOFTiKXruFQ
**Origin in Ibiza and England** : Between 1988 and 1989, Acid House developed in England, drawing inspiration from the vibrant club scene of Ibiza. This emergence played a pivotal role in giving rise to the broader Rave culture.
