# genre - Acid House

## curiosities
**Distinctive Sound and Instruments** : Acid House is characterized by its unique bass lines produced using Roland TB-303 synthesizers and rhythms created with TR-808 drum machines. This distinctive sound emerged in the mid-80s, playing a role in the further fracturing of dance music into numerous subgenres.
