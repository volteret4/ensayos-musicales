## curiosities
**Pinkerton's Ahead-of-Its-Time Sound** : "Pinkerton" was described as a "proto-emal record," a sound that people were not quite ready for in 1996, placing it six or seven years ahead of its time. It is now considered very well-regarded and influential, particularly within the emo scene.
