# genre - Cosmic American Music

## curiosities
**Gram Parsons' Definition** : Gram Parsons coined the term "cosmic American music" to describe his unique sound, which he considered a "stew of all types of American music" that intentionally crossed genre and cultural boundaries. This term was his preferred alternative to "country rock."
