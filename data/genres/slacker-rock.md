# genre - Slacker Rock

## curiosities
**Characteristics and Lo-Fi Connection (Early 1990s)** : Slacker Rock emerged in the early 1990s as a style of music that was laid back while maintaining a ragged-around-the-edges sound. It often overlapped with the low-fidelity aesthetic, reflecting a broader trend in alternative music following the grunge explosion.
