## curiosities
**Emergence from Early Rock Bands** : Psicodelia, along with heavy metal, emerged directly from the "remains" or influence of early rock bands, exemplified by groups like The Jarpers. This highlights how initial, sometimes unstable, rock formations served as a foundational "seed" for future genre developments.
