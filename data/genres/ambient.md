# genre - Ambient

## curiosities
**Influence on David Botrell's Production Approach** : David Botrell's early experiences as an assistant at Grant Avenue Studios in 1983-84 involved "early ambient sessions," which was an unusual and creative environment not bound by typical pop song structures. This lack of rules allowed him to develop a foundational approach to recording music based on making music that "made you feel good." "my beginnings in recording and making music stemmed from those early ambient sessions." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vWvYe1a-2Aw
**Kraftwerk's Influence** : Ambient and chillout music genres are noted to have been significantly influenced by Kraftwerk, underscoring the German group's broad impact across various electronic and mood-driven musical styles. "Today's EDM and all techno from groups like Underworld and anything that came out of Detroit to Daff Punk, Ambient and Chillout music, Craftwork influence." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
