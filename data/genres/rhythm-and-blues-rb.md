# genre - Rhythm and Blues (R&B)

## curiosities
**Origins from Big Band Downsizing** : R&B emerged in the late 1940s when big bands were forced to downsize due to post-war economic pressures and the 1942-1944 recording strike. These smaller groups emphasized a heavier rhythm and beat, leading directly to the birth of rock and roll. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
