# genre - psychobilly

## curiosities
**Term Coined by The Cramps** : The Cramps coined the term "Psychobilly" to promote their concerts on posters and flyers, and it rapidly evolved into a recognized musical genre. This term encapsulated their unique blend of rockabilly, protopunk, and a dark, campy aesthetic. "El término Psycho Bill Lilloy con Yoron para promocionar sus conciertos en carteles y flyers y rápidamente se convirtió en un género musical." ← Amor, Terror y Psychobilly： La Historia de The Cramps | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tBVTzVQDQeQ
**The Cramps' Distinction from Other Psychobilly Bands** : When The Cramps toured the UK, they encountered other bands categorizing themselves as psychobilly. However, The Cramps emphasized that their only common ground with these bands was shared conceptual references, and they did not share the fast and violent sound that characterized many of these other groups. "Cuando viajaron al reino unido de gira, para su sorpresa, había varias bandas que se englobaban dentro del género, pero ellos defendían que lo único que tenían en común con esas bandas eran las referencias, no compartían su sonido rápido y violento." ← Amor, Terror y Psychobilly： La Historia de The Cramps | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tBVTzVQDQeQ
