# genre - Emo Rap

## curiosities
**Emotional Hip Hop Themes** : Emo Rap fuses the thematic elements and emotional depth of Emo with hip hop, resulting in highly personal and vulnerable lyrical content that explores themes like heartbreak, fear, anxiety, depression, and loneliness. "Basically, we get all of Emo's hits when it comes to themes and feels, made it with hip hop, very personal, very vulnerable stuff, heartbreak, fear, anxiety, depression, loneliness." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**Offshoot of Emo** : Emo Rap is described as a combination of emo and hip hop, starting to appear at the end of the 2000s decade. Its name directly reflects its blended sound. "There was Emo Wrap, which is just like it sounds, a combination of emo and hip hop. It started popping up at the end of the decade." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo
**SoundCloud Birthplace** : Emo Rap primarily originated and gained prominence on SoundCloud, serving as a key platform for its development and dissemination. "This genre was birthed on SoundCloud more than anywhere else." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
