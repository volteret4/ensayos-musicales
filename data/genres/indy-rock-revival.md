# genre - Indy Rock Revival

## curiosities
**Rise in Early 2000s** : The Indy Rock Revival began at the very tail end of the 1990s and gained significant momentum over the next couple of years, with The Strokes identified as one of its very first new bands. By late 2003/early 2004, The Strokes, along with bands like The White Stripes, the Yeah Yeah Yeahs, Interpol, The Vines, The Hives, and Arcade Fire, were firmly established within this movement, leading to cries of "Rock is Back" after a period dominated by boy bands and other pop stars.
