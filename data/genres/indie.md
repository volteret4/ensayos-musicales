# genre - Indie

## curiosities
**"Selling Out" Perception** : Within certain segments of the indie world, profiting from music is often viewed as "wrong" and a betrayal of artistic ideals and principles. This perspective suggests that money compromises the purity of the relationship between the artist and their music, as well as with their fans. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfie4YYncxk
**Growth of Independent Labels** : The independent label scene became crucial for bands like Bauhaus in post-punk England, with "small nimble indie labels" appearing almost daily, capable of quickly signing and promoting new sounds and trends. "Bauhaus and Bella Logosi's dead, at least on beggars banquet, another one of those small nimble indie labels that popped up almost daily in post-punk England." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
**Later Terminology** : The word "indie" emerged as a term slightly later than "post punk" to describe the music evolving from the original punk scene. "The word that came a little later was indie." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
