**Anglo-Saxon Influence in Spain and Latin America** : Classic Anglo-Saxon guitar jangle movements were adopted and reinterpreted by bands like Los Planetas in Spain and Soda Stereo in Latin America. While these artists created distinct interpretations, these Anglo-Saxon-inspired styles never fully caught on in Spanish-speaking countries, despite their quality.
