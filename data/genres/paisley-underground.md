# genre - Paisley Underground

## curiosities
**American Neo-Psych Scene** : The Paisley Underground was an American Neo-Psych scene that began on the US West Coast around 1982. These groups delved into California-style psych from the 1960s, building upon the sounds pioneered by bands like The Byrds and Love. **Paisley Underground – LA Early 1980s; Beatles Pop + California Psych; Named After Red Paisley Dress; Bangles Biggest Group** : The Paisley Underground came out of California in the early 1980s, taking Beatles-like pop and mixing it with 1960s California psychedelia. The name came from a comment by a member of a band called The Three O'Clock who found a red paisley dress in a thrift store. The scene featured breezy pop songs with lots of melody, and the name helped record labels discover these bands. The biggest group to emerge from the Paisley Underground was the Bangles, who before their top-40 success were a highly regarded garage pop band. "The biggest group to come out of the whole Paisley Underground was the bangles." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9cAeeExXKwU
