# genre - Paisley Underground

## curiosities
**American Neo-Psych Scene** : The Paisley Underground was an American Neo-Psych scene that began on the US West Coast around 1982. These groups delved into California-style psych from the 1960s, building upon the sounds pioneered by bands like The Byrds and Love.
