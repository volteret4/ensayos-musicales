# genre - Madchester

## curiosities
**"Holy Trinity"** : The Happy Mondays are recognized as part of the "holy trinity of Madchester." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Foundations from The Smiths** : The Smiths established the foundations for the Madchester scene, which flourished in the late 1980s and early 1990s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Historical Context (90s)** : The Madchester scene in the 1990s was widely known for its association with extensive drug and alcohol use, including pot, coke, heroin, PCP, and crack, which led to "wrecked brains, wrecked bodies, wrecked cars, [and] wrecked careers" among its participants. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
