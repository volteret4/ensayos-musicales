# genre - Madchester

## curiosities
**Cultural Hub at The Haçienda** : The Haçienda nightclub in Manchester was a focal point for the Madchester scene, with its balconies and stairwells offering a unique vantage point for observing the rise of this genre and the emergence of DJs as prominent figures. "The balconies and stairwells were ringside seats to the explosion in rave music and mad chester in the rise of the DJ as star." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2RKchFW42Jc
**Era of Drugs and Debauchery (90s)** : The Madchester scene of the 1990s was notoriously associated with extensive drug and alcohol use, characterized by "stories of drugs and alcohol and debauchery," including substances like "Pot Coke, heroin, PCP crack," which led to "wreck brains, wrecked bodies, wrecked cars, wrecked careers." "Now, if you're a fan of the whole madchester thing back in the 90s, you know all about the stories of drugs and alcohol and debauchery." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Rise in UK (1990)** : The rise of Madchester in the UK in the spring of 1990 contributed to rock's quick resurgence, alongside the beginnings of Grunge in the US. "There was the rise of Madchester in the UK..." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MK7L0eCpDHc
