## curiosities
**Sound Profile with Beats/Skullcandy** : Beats and Skullcandy headphones are considered inappropriate for acoustic music due to their very specific equalization. This sound profile, which includes a strong, saturated bass (a "subwoofer effect"), distorts the natural and clear qualities essential for acoustic genres. The emphasis on exaggerated low frequencies detracts from the fidelity and balance typically desired for acoustic instruments and vocals.
