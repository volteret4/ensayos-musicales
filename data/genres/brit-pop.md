# genre - Brit Pop

## curiosities
**Absorption of "New Wave of New Wave"** : The "new wave of new wave" movement of the early 1990s was short-lived, with many of its bands soon being subsumed into Brit Pop. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9CNNsqXiL6U
**Height of the Movement (1995)** : The "record war" between Blur and Oasis in August 1995, involving the simultaneous release and chart battle of their singles "Country House" and "Roll With It," is noted as the "height of Brit Pop." This event captured national attention and demonstrated the genre's significant cultural impact in the UK. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UIBWyUt38g
