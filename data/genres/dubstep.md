# genre - Dubstep

## curiosities
**Genre Transformation Through Remixes** : Remixes can transform a song originally written for one genre into another, with dubstep serving as an example of a genre into which many familiar songs have been remixed, turning them into "bangers." **Integration into Rock Music** : Dubstep influences were purposefully integrated into USS's production, particularly evident in the song "This Is The Best." This coincided with a period when the success of bands like Imagine Dragons was opening a "lane" for electronic-infused rock. "Ash night or both like huge dubstep fans and so that made it into the production of it." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N-3eddy4J7g ← dubstep
**Reinterpretation by James Blake** : James Blake has uniquely reinterpreted Dubstep, taking its rhythmic bases from the early 2000s UK scene and slowing them down significantly to create entirely new and distinct sounds. This approach is seen as a novel interpretation of a genre that was once considered exhausted. "Cuando uno vise el Duffstep, piensan esto.
