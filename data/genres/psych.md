# genre - Psych

## curiosities
**Influence on Vaporwave** : Psych music is identified as one of the source genres from which Vaporwave drew elements, contributing to its unique sound. "Vaporware took bits from Psych, Lounge Music, R&B, and the Chill Wave of the Outs, and then slowed everything down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**Tim Buckley's Exploration** : Tim Buckley explored the psych genre as part of his musical evolution. "cycling through folk, psych, bits of jazz, fun, and soul." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MmXDbXI6Sqk
