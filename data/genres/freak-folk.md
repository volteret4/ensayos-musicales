# genre - Freak Folk

## curiosities
**Tweetpop Spin-off** : Freak Folk emerged as a spin-off from the Tweetpop sensibility during the 1990s. It is characterized as a "mind-expanding psychedelic folk music" that deliberately avoids loud guitars and amplifiers. This experimental sound can be traced back to the late 1960s and early 1970s, with early Beck being cited as a prime example of Freak Folk infused with a Tweetpop aesthetic. "There were some spin-offs and one of them was known as Freak Folk." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
