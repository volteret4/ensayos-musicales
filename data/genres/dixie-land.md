# genre - Dixie Land

## curiosities
**More Granular Classification** : Dixie Land was one of the more granular genre classifications available in 1955, alongside broader categories like rock, pop, country, and R&B. This early level of detail foreshadowed the extensive fragmentation of music genres in later decades. "You could slice certain genres into thinner slices to include big band, Dixie Land, Scott, Hillbilly, and Jazz, gospel, Broadway show music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstJ8M2laho
