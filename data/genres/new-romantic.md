# genre - New Romantic

## curiosities
**Synth-Based Offshoot of Technopop** : New Romantic was a specific offshoot of synth-based music, originating in British clubs like Billies and The Blitz Club. This movement was instrumental in breeding groups such as Spandau Ballet, Visage, and Culture Club, characterized by their unique style and electronic sounds. "One such offshoot was known as New Romantic, which had its roots in a couple of British clubs dedicated to new music. One was called Billies, and the other was the Blitz Club.
