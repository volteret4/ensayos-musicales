# genre - Blue Beat

## curiosities
**Alternative Name for Ska** : Blue Beat is an alternative name for Ska, a music genre that emerged in Jamaica in the late 1950s and gained popularity internationally, particularly in Britain. The term was used when discussing its period of receding popularity and subsequent rediscovery. "After a brief burst of popularity, SCOP, also known as Blue Beat, receded for a couple of decades, until it was discovered by some white kids from Coventry, who gave this working class music from Jamaica a new twist by adding working class punk from England." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk
