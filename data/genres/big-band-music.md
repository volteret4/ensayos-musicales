# genre - Big Band Music

## curiosities
**Neil Peart's Passion** : Neil Peart, the drummer and lyricist for Rush, held a deep passion for big band music. This interest was evident in his admiration for legendary big band drummers like Gene Krupa and Buddy Rich, whom he found fascinating. "He was also fascinated by big band drummers like Jean Crouppa and Buddy Rich." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B0CZ1deEgic
**Size Expansion for Volume** : As big band music grew in popularity, the bands themselves grew larger, partly to naturally "pump up the volume" for increasing audiences. However, after World War II, the expense of maintaining such large ensembles became prohibitive, leading to a shift towards smaller combos. "As more people got into big band music, the band obviously got bigger, partly to pump up the volume for all the people that came to see them." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrrwqdpCjpQ
