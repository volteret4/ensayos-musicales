# genre - Celtic Punk

## curiosities
**Emergence from Irish Punk** : Celtic punk emerged in the wake of punk rock's popularity in Ireland during the 1970s. The inherent boisterousness, dark themes, and drinking song tradition of Irish music naturally lent themselves to the punk rock attitude, leading to the creation of this subgenre. "Traditional Irish music can be boisterous, have dark and sad themes, and feature great drinking songs. And when you think about it, at a bit of punk rock attitude, really wasn't that much of a stretch." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
