# genre - Traditional Celtic Music

## curiosities
**19th Century Revival** : The first significant recognition of traditional Celtic music occurred in the 19th century during a revival of old customs and practices. This resurgence was partly a response to the growing power of England, as Celts sought to maintain their distinct identity. "The first recognition of traditional Celtic music began in the 19th century, when there was a revival of the old ways, partly in response to the power of England. The Celts were determined to remain a distinct people." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
**Cross-Pollination and Identity** : This revival involved considerable cross-pollination, resulting in various branches of Celtic music. Each of these branches served to affirm the identity of Celtic people. "There was much cross-pollination too. And the result was slightly different branches of this music, but each of these branches was used to identify these people as Celts." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuUF4MByxyo
