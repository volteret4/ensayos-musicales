## curiosities
**Instrumental Pillar of Hip Hop** : Turnabilism is identified as a crucial instrumental movement and one of the four foundational pillars of hip hop culture. It involves DJs innovatively using vinyl turntables as musical instruments for performance, rather than simply for playing records, which led to the development of techniques like scratch and breakbeat.
