**Origin of the Bo Diddley Beat** : The rhythmic pattern popularized by Bo Diddley in 1955, known as the "Bo Diddley beat," can be traced back over 400 years to Yoruba Drumming, which originated in West Africa. This rhythm is considered "completely non-copyrightable and fair game for anyone who wants to use that rhythmic pattern."
