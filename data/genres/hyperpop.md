# genre - Hyperpop

## curiosities
**Accelerated and Experimental Characteristics** : It is an accelerated and experimental pop subgenre with auto-tuned vocals, simple melodies, many rhythm changes, and an exaggerated bass inherited from Bavarian Bass. "Es un sub-género del pop acelerado y experimental. **Blurring Pop Boundaries** : This movement has blurred the boundaries of traditional pop, drawing it into an experimental, chaotic, and deeply emotional territory. "Este movimiento ha desdibujado en los límites del pop tradicional, llamándola a un terreno experimental, caótico y profundamente motivo." ← 5 Discos Para Entrarle al HYPERPOP | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wP8MaC-2D8A
**Designed for Digital Attention Spans** : This genre is designed to capture the attention of an audience accustomed to endless scrolling, where every beat, glitch, and rhythm change is another stimulus in the organized chaos of the digital era. "Es un genero diseñado para captar la atención de una diencia acostumbrada al escuro del infinito, donde cada beat, glitch y cambio de ritmo es un estímulo más en el caos organizado de la era digital." ← 5 Discos Para Entrarle al HYPERPOP | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wP8MaC-2D8A
**Evolution and Commercialization Impact** : This movement has evolved from its experimental and niche roots to become a global phenomenon, redefining what pop can be. As the genre has gained recognition and become more commercial, it may have also lost its quality as a genre for "weirdos and misunderstood," leaving behind some of the essence that defined its beginnings. "Tal vez, ahora que el género ha ganado reconocimiento y se ha vuelto más comercial, ha perdido también su calidad de género para raros y incontrendidos, dejando atrás parte de la sencilla que lo definían sus inicios." ← 5 Discos Para Entrarle al HYPERPOP | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wP8MaC-2D8A
**Exaggerated Pop with EDM Elements** : Hyperpop is an extreme, exaggerated interpretation of a pop song, incorporating earwormy hooks, dance beats, EDM keyboards, ample auto-tune, and a cartoonish feel reminiscent of 1960s bubblegum pop. "And it's just like it sounds, an exaggerated interpretation on what it means to write a pop song. So that means a lot of earwormy hooks, lots of dance beats, EDM keyboards, plenty of auto tune, and even a cartoonish feel, not unlike some of the stupidly humbable bubblegum songs from the 1960s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c ← hyperpop
**Generational Sound of Gen Z** : Hyperpop is a genre that encapsulates the immediacy, constant stimuli, and "internet-infused" adolescent nostalgia so characteristic of Generation Z. "Si hay un genero que encapsula la inmediatez, nos estímulos constantes y la nostalgia interneterada lescente tan propios de la generación Z. SES el hyperpop." ← 5 Discos Para Entrarle al HYPERPOP | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wP8MaC-2D8A
**Nostalgic and Distorted Influences** : It is laden with a particular nostalgia for genres that defined this generation's adolescence, including Euro Trance, Happy Hardcore, emo music, video games, and even Dance Metal. Hyperpop takes these intense sounds, distorts and exaggerates them, passing them through an internet filter, transforming them into something deeply connected with a culture that blends nostalgia, irony, the queer movement, and excess as a lifestyle. "Todo ello cargado de una nostalgia particular de los géneros que definieron la adolescencia de esta generación. Euro Trends, Happy Hardcore, música emo, videojuegos y hasta Dance Metal." ← 5 Discos Para Entrarle al HYPERPOP | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wP8MaC-2D8A
**Origination from Eurodance Influence** : Hyperpop, a genre defined and originated by A. G. Cook, explicitly acknowledged and adapted the hyper-commercial influence of Eurodance. It transformed the artificial aesthetics and unique vocal characteristics of Eurodance into a novel and distinct musical vision. "EYCOOC reconoció la influencia hyper comercial de Eurodance y la adaptó a su propia visión, marcando y dando origen al hyperpop." ← La gran mentira detrás del Eurodance | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_F34L8wfrdo

Cook, and popularized by artists like Sophie and Charli XCX. **Polarizing and Extreme** : This genre is intentionally polarizing and designed to elicit extreme responses due to its extreme form of pop, making it a style that is not universally appealing.
