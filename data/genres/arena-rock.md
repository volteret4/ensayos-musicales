# genre - Arena Rock

## curiosities
**Corporate Perception** : At the end of the 1970s, much of the arena rock scene was perceived as overly corporate, leading audiences to seek out more authentic alternatives like New Wave. "The Roxanne featured a lot of mostly arena rock bands that some felt were too corporate." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYsV3ozbk4M ← arena-rock
**Late 1970s / 1980s Explosion** : Arena rock began to gain momentum in the late 1970s and "totally blew up" in the 1980s. This genre, characterized by power ballads, attracted a new breed of rock fans, predominantly younger women and their mothers, filling large venues. "Arena Rock and the power ballad that filled up those arenas with a brand new breed of rock fans, the younger women and probably their moms too, took hold of rock." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 7 - Women Who Rocked the 70s | http://www.ftr70.com
