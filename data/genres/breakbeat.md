**Evolution into a Key Composing Method** : Breakbeat, initially dismissed as uncreative and not an instrument for using pre-existing song fragments, has evolved into one of the most important contemporary methods of composition. This involves taking pieces of other songs, changing and modifying them to create new combinations. **Initial Dismissal as Uncreative** : When breakbeat emerged, or the creation of rhythmic bases from fragments of other songs, it was criticized as uncreative and not a genuine instrument, simply because it involved borrowing something already made. Despite this initial dismissal, breakbeat is now recognized as one of the most important methods of composition.
