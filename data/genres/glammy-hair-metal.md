# genre - Glammy Hair Metal

## curiosities
**Alice in Chains' Early Sound (1991)** : In early 1991, during the filming of *Singles*, Alice in Chains was characterized as "still trying to be a glimy hair metal band." This description indicates the band's initial musical direction before fully embracing the grunge sound they would become known for.
