# genre - Techno

## curiosities
**Definition and Characteristics** : Techno is generally defined as percussion-based electronic dance music, stripped down to emphasize bass lines and drum beats. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Detroit Origins** : A small scene in Detroit, spearheaded by three friends—Kevin Saunderson, Juan Atkins, and Derrick May—took these diverse elements and combined them in a new and highly influential way, aiming to make funky dance music using cheap synthesizers. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Roots and Influences** : Its roots lie in the earliest synthesizer pioneers, avant-garde electronic composers, Kraftwerk, David Bowie, the English techno pop scene, and Afrika Bambaataa, as well as the quick-handed DJs who powered their mixers and turntables off streetlights. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**UK Embrace (1988)** : In 1988, English music fans widely embraced Detroit Techno, along with Chicago House and New York dance. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
