**Musical Definition - Core Elements** : The fundamental elements of techno include a strong and deep kick drum, complemented by a snare, a clap, and a bass line. The bass line may not always be a traditional bass but can be a repeated low sound, often manipulated with pitch effects. Techno frequently incorporates many effects, and typically features one or two simple, looping melodic lines, as its true essence lies in its rhythmic base.
