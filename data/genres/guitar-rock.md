# genre - Guitar Rock

## curiosities
**Current State of Alt-Rock** : some argue that there are "too few big guitar bands" in alt-rock today, indicating a shift away from its post-grunge dominance.
**Dominance Post-Grunge** : after grunge's rise in the early 90s, alternative music became primarily focused on guitar rock, with most bands being grunge or "grunge-ish" and often using drop-tuning.
**Minor Presence in 80s Alt-Rock** : only a "smattering of guitar rock" was present on diverse alt-rock radio playlists in the late 80s.
