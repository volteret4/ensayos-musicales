# genre - Rocker-billy

## curiosities
**Minimalist Drum Kits in the Early 1980s** : In the early 1980s, some rocker-billy bands featured drummers who utilized very minimalist kits, sometimes consisting of little more than a stand-up snare drum and a single cymbal. This contrasts sharply with the large kits seen at the other end of the spectrum.
