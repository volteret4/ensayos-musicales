# genre - drum and bass

## curiosities
**David Bowie's Incorporation (1997)** : Drum and bass was a new dance genre that David Bowie actively explored and integrated into his 1997 album, *Earthling*. Although it was already an established genre, Bowie's use of it was seen by many as providing official recognition and validation. "He'd become interested in various new genres of dance music, including industrial, techno, jungle, and drum and bass." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yq2A_o9-ulM
**Influence on USS Song** : A drum and bass beat was used in the demo of USS's song "Pasturella Pestus," providing the musical foundation for Ash's long-held lyrical part. "That went over a little drum and bass beat and the part I could never get out of my head like it was a brilliant part." ← Ongoing History of New Music > USS In Their Own Words： Part 2 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N-3eddy4J7g&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
