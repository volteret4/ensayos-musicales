# genre - drum and bass

## curiosities
**David Bowie's Incorporation (1997)** : Drum and bass was a new dance genre that David Bowie actively explored and integrated into his 1997 album, *Earthling*. Although it was already an established genre, Bowie's use of it was seen by many as providing official recognition and validation.
