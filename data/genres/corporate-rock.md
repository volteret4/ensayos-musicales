# genre - Corporate Rock

## curiosities
**Mainstream-Oriented Band Type** : "Corporate rock band" was a term used to describe a project specifically put together with the explicit goal of being mainstream and maximizing record sales. Bands fitting this description, like Toto, were often considered "uncool" by certain segments of the rock audience, particularly the alt-rock crowd. "Toto was what we used to call a corporate rock band. The kind of project put together with the express purpose of being mainstream and selling as many records as possible." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yuA9Xor8vfw
