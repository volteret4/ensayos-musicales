# genre - Über Rock

## curiosities
**Alternative Name for Krautrock** : Along with "Deutsch German Rock," "Teutonic Rock," and "Götterdämmerung Rock," "Über Rock" was one of the names initially used to refer to the new German music before the British term "Krautrock" became widely adopted. "While most music people initially refer to this music as Doitrach German Rock, it was also called Uber Rock, Tutonic Rock, and Gaudedemmer Rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
