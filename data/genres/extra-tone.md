# genre - Extra-Tone

## curiosities
**Definition and BPM Threshold** : Extra-Tone is a term used to describe music that reaches an extremely fast tempo, specifically 1000 beats per minute (BPM). While technology has allowed composers to create songs with BPM counts exceeding 15,000 since 1992, Moby's "Thousand" remains notable for achieving this speed as a hit single.
