# genre - Gothic music

## curiosities
**Architectural Influence on Development** : The evolution of gothic music is directly linked to the acoustic properties of medieval stone cathedrals. Hildegard von Bingen's slow, somber sacred monophony, designed to be harmonious with the long echoes and reverb of these cavernous spaces, established a style of chanting and composition that adapted to and utilized the architectural technology. This early form twisted and evolved through centuries, leading directly to modern gothic music culture.
