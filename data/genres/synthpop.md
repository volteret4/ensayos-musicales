# genre - Synthpop

## curiosities
**Synthpop / Technopop – Guitars Out, Electronics In; Filled the Post-Disco Dance Void** : Synthpop (also called Technopop) emerged as one of the biggest offshoots of New Wave, based on the idea that guitars were out and electronics were the future. It was also extremely danceable, filling the post-disco dance void that had been created when disco was deemed uncool. Devo (from Akron, Ohio, supported by David Bowie) were among the original synthpop pioneers, and keyboard-focused UK acts like Depeche Mode, Orchestral Manoeuvres in the Dark, and Soft Cell pushed the sound further.
