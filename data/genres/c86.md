# genre - C86

## curiosities
**C86 – NME Cassette, May 1986; Young Raw Indie UK Bands; "C" from Cassette Capacity Designations** : In May 1986, NME included a special cassette with one of their issues featuring young, recently signed bands from various independent British labels. Because blank cassettes had numerical designations (C60 = 60 min, C90 = 90 min), the NME called their 1986 cassette C86. All bands on the tape were young, the music was raw and sometimes primitive, and nothing came from a major label. C86 became shorthand for young, raw, primitive indie rock in the UK.
