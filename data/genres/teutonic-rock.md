# genre - Teutonic Rock

## curiosities
**Alternative Name for Krautrock** : "Teutonic Rock" was among several names, including "Deutsch German Rock," "Über Rock," and "Götterdämmerung Rock," used to describe the emerging German music scene before the British-coined "Krautrock" became the standard term. "While most music people initially refer to this music as Doitrach German Rock, it was also called Uber Rock, Tutonic Rock, and Gaudedemmer Rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
