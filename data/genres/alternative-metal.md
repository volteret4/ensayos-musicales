# genre - Alternative Metal

## curiosities
**Prominence in the 1990s and 2010s Comebacks** : Alternative Metal, also known as Alternative Hard Rock, was a prominent genre in the 1990s. In the 2010s, it saw comebacks from older groups like Jane's Addiction, Biohazard, and Faith No More, as well as the emergence of new young bands such as Greta Van Fleet, Turnstile, and The Struts, who were inspired by classic rock.
