## curiosities
**Early 2000s Revival (2000-2001)** : The "Post-Punk Revival," also known as the Indie Rock Revival, occurred around 2000-2001. This movement presented a new take on earlier alt-rock, garage rock, and punk styles, bringing sharp and clever new bands to the forefront.
