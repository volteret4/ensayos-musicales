**Intentional Guitar Distortion** : A defining characteristic of Surf Rock was the intentional distortion of electric guitars, achieved through heavy reverb and by physically modifying guitars and amplifiers in ways that voided warranties. This practice was necessary because dedicated guitar pedals and other outboard effects were not yet available. "What we had here was electric guitar, as with lots of reverb. And unlike just about everyone else, the guitars were intentionally distorted." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UpWz3X33vSY ← surf-rock
**West Coast Emergence** : Surf Rock developed on the American West Coast, becoming a significant musical trend in certain circles within Southern California. It was largely pioneered by Dick Dale and the Deltones and initially began as an instrumental genre.
