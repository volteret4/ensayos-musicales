# genre - Japanese noise

## curiosities
**Extreme Distortion Use** : Japanese noise bands take the use of distortion and feedback to extreme levels, pushing sonic boundaries. Acts such as The Boredoms and Merzbow are examples of artists operating within this genre, exploring the outer limits of aggressive, unrefined sound.
