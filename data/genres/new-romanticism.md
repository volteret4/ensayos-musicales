# genre - New Romanticism

## curiosities
**Importance of Style and Music** : Groups within this movement created highly stylish music that was considered just as important as the elaborate visual looks they adopted. "These groups made very stylish stuff and combined it with a series of looks that were just as important as the music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng
**London Nightclub Origins** : This significant offshoot of Technopop originated in London nightclubs where a lot of Technopop music was played, drawing inspiration from artists like David Bowie, Roxy Music, and Brian Eno. "One of the biggest offshoots was something called New Romanticism. This rose from some of the nightclubs in London where they played a lot of Tecno Pop music. Taking cues from David Bowie and Roxy Music and Brian Eno." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KF5MwJVYng
**Subgenre of Techno Pop** : New Romanticism emerged as a microgenre within Techno Pop, influencing bands such as Duran Duran, Spandau Ballet, and ABC. "Technopop began several microgenres of its own, including something called New Romanticism, which ended up giving us Durand-Duran and Spando Balais and ABC." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uTORLOm5Yg
