# genre - Riot Grrrl

## curiosities
**Hardcore Punk with Female Perspective** : A subgenre of hardcore punk bands that expressed themselves with the same anger as male bands but from a distinctly female perspective.
**Peak Popularity** : Was at its peak in the early 1990s, with Bikini Kill receiving the most coverage.
**Thematic Focus** : Song topics included female empowerment, domestic abuse, rape, sexuality, racism, the patriarchal nature of society, and various feminist thinking.
