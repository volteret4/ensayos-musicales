# genre - Noisy Pop

## curiosities
**British Scene and Sloan Influence** : "Noisy pop" was a prevalent genre in England during the late 1980s/early 1990s, exemplified by bands on Creation Records such as My Bloody Valentine and Ride. This sound significantly influenced Sloan's album "Smeared." "like my bloody Valentine and ride and all those sort of noisy pop bands that were on creation records and everything." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mbic-70ZhpE
