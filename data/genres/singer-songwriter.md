# genre - Singer-songwriter

## curiosities
**ABBA's Non-Affiliation** : ABBA was not considered part of the singer-songwriter movement that was active in the mid-1970s, further distinguishing their musical style. "they were not part of the singer songwriter movement." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 56 - Talk Shows of the 1970s | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Ben Howard's Style** : Ben Howard is described as a typical "singer with a guitar" in the singer-songwriter tradition, who has achieved considerable success in the UK, with a style comparable to artists like Bon Iver (referred to as Curnivar net in the transcript). "es el típico chico un tanto cantador con la guitarra." ← Music Radar Clan > LANZAMIENTOS JUNIO 2018 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AvdFC5Q0nKk&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Part of Alt Rock Umbrella** : Singer-songwriters whose style didn't conform to the mainstream view of rock were among the "new fresh, strange and fascinating sounds" discovered under the umbrella of Alternative Rock in the 1990s. "Things like Goth, Industrial, Shoegays, Lofi, even singer songwriters that didn't fit the mainstream's view of Rock." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The 90s Part 6： The Punk Revival | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QtMI00T5jw&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
