## curiosities
**Cultural Phenomenon of Late 1980s/Early 1990s** : The Madchester sound, characterized by bands like The Stone Roses, Happy Mondays, The Charlatans, and Inspiral Carpets, was described as "all the rage" in the early 1990s, culminating in major events like the Spike Island concert. **Popularity in Early 1990s UK** : At the beginning of the 1990s, the Madchester Sound, featuring bands like The Stone Roses, The Happy Mondays, The Charlatans, and Inspiral Carpets, was "all the rage" in the UK. This scene culminated in The Stone Roses' legendary Spike Island gig in May 1990.
