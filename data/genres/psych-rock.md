# genre - Psych Rock

## curiosities
**Birth, Decline, and 2010s Resurgence** : Psychedelic or Psych Rock was born in the 1960s and was popular until the 1970s when it fell out of favor. Despite this, elements of the genre persisted in scenes like the Paisley Underground, Madchester, and rave rock. In the 2010s, it experienced a resurgence with artists such as Ariel Pink, King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard, Temples, The Black Angels, Unknown Mortal Orchestra, Animal Collective, War on Drugs, and Mac DeMarco, with Tame Impala being one of the most successful. "The trippy sounds of Psychedelic or Psych Rock was born in the 1960s and was quite the thing until sometime in the 70s when it fell out of favor."
