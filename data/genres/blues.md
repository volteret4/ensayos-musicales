# genre - blues

## curiosities
**Source of Floating Lyrics** : Carl Perkins' "Matchbox" incorporates "floating lyrics" found in various traditional blues songs, such as the verse "I'm a poor boy and I'm a long way from home" from the song "Poor Boy, Long Way From Home." This demonstrates the influence of traditional blues on early rockabilly and rock and roll.
