# genre - Dream Pop

## curiosities
**Early 90s Mention** : Dream pop was mentioned as one of the many forms of Alt Rock that enjoyed moments in the sun during the 90s, highlighting the breadth of alternative sounds beyond the mainstream. "We could have just as well mentioned, early emo, dream pop, math rock, post-hardcore, alt metal, alt country, Tweet Rock, contemporary Christian alternative music, and so many more." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The 90s Part 7： The Other Genres | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=amHre9ZZFkU&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Influence on The Cranberries** : The Cranberries' guitar sound occasionally showed influences reminiscent of dream pop, linking them to a specific stylistic lineage. "aquella influencia que a veces se recordaban esas guitarras a mi bloody ballanta igual de mi pop." ← Music Radar Clan > Recordar a THE CRANBERRIES | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9HTrbETl_Pg&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Integration with Electronic Music** : Boards of Canada's "The Campfire Headphase" is described as sounding like a dream pop band integrated into electronic music, rather than an electronic band influenced by dream pop, contributing to the evolution of the genre. "a veces parece más una banda de Drimpop integrada en la electrónica que una banda de electrónica influenciada... es un viaje muy romántico por lo que es la evolución del Drimpop." ← Music Radar Clan > BOARDS OF CANADA. La visión romántica del IDM | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=llE5K5hjwVg&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Renewed Dream Pop** : Lowtide's debut album is praised for its contribution to a "renewed dream pop" sound, with comparisons drawn to Cocteau Twins. "este nuevo dream pop renovado que hacen que más interesante de pareció, es como me recuerda muchas veces a los coctotunes." ← Music Radar Clan > Lanzamientos Febrero  2018 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pO_KgN9SRK4&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Romantic and Nostalgic Sound** : Dream pop is characterized by a nostalgic and romantic sound, which is present in Boards of Canada's music, particularly in "Music Has the Right to Children" and "The Campfire Headphase." "este disco tiene muchísimas emociones contenidas de tras el sonido instrumental, desde inspiraciones en la naturaleza tiene un sonido muy nostálgico, muy romántico, a veces incluso recuerda el dream pop." ← Music Radar Clan > BOARDS OF CANADA. La visión romántica del IDM | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=llE5K5hjwVg&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Subgenre of Post-Punk** : Dream pop is identified as one of the styles that falls under the post-punk umbrella, characterized by immersive music with heavy atmospherics, texture, and effects. "Goth, dark wave, dream pop, the jangly guitars, bands like R.E.M., the spiky stuff of groups like Wire and Shoegay's were, and in some cases still are, artists in those buckets that we can consider to be post-punk." ← Ongoing History of New Music > What Exactly is Post-Rock？ Part 1 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pLf3kUONewg&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
