# genre - Electronic Dance Music

## curiosities
**Love Parade Disaster (Duisburg, Germany, July 24, 2010)** : The annual free Love Parade electronic dance music festival in Duisburg, Germany, on July 24, 2010, resulted in 21 deaths and over 700 injuries. The single, narrow tunnel entrance to the festival grounds became dangerously overcrowded; despite police attempts to seal it off and instruct people to turn back, continuous pushing from behind caused an immense crush, with autopsies revealing all victims had suffered broken ribs from the pressure. "It was the annual free, love parade electronic dance music festival." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
**Time Warp Festival Tragedy (Buenos Aires, 2016)** : At the Time Warp Festival in Buenos Aires in 2016, a significant number of people died on the first night due to "bad drugs," with four others hospitalized in critical condition from poisoning. The severity of the incident led to the immediate cancellation of the festival's second day. "Most people died on the first night from bad drugs." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
**Time Warp Organizers Arrested, Festivals Banned** : Following the drug-related deaths at the Time Warp Festival in Buenos Aires, the festival organizers were arrested and charged with facilitating a location where individuals could purchase illegal drugs. As a direct consequence of this tragedy, Argentina subsequently banned electronic music festivals. "The festival organizers were arrested and charged with facilitating a place where people could purchase drugs. Argentina banned electronic music festivals after that." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pgchw2h87aw
