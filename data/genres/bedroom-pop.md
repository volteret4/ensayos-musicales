# genre - Bedroom Pop

## curiosities
**DIY Home Recording Ethos** : Bedroom Pop emerged as a genre fueled by the increased accessibility of home recording software and equipment like GarageBand, enabling artists to create professional-sounding music in their own homes and distribute it online for free. "The rise in the availability of home recording software and equipment made it possible for almost anyone to create professional sounding recordings in their bedroom, hence the name." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**Historical Antecedents** : While prominent in the 2010s, Bedroom Pop has roots dating back to the late 1960s with acts like The Beach Boys, and also connects to 1980s Dreampop and 1990s lo-fi slacker sounds and attitudes. "Heck an argument can be made that the Beach Boys were making this kind of music in the late 1960s. And there's a definite connection to both Dreampop, which was a product of the 80s, and the low-fi slacker sounds in attitude of the 1990s." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**LGBTQ+ Community Connection** : The intimate and personal nature of Bedroom Pop makes it a significant medium for artists, particularly within the LGBTQ+ community, to explore personal themes and experiences. "Another thing we should say about bedroom pop is it ties to the LGBTQ plus community. Because the style is so intimate and so personal, some artists use it to explore these particular themes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**Synthetic and Vulnerable Sound** : Generally, Bedroom Pop features synthetic sounds from electronic keyboards, drums, and loops, occasionally with acoustic guitar, and vocals/lyrics often convey vulnerability, insecurity, and naiveness. "Generally, the sounds are synthetic, electronic keyboards, drums, loops, maybe an acoustic guitar here and there. You may also pick up elements of vulnerability, insecurity, and naiveness with the vocals and the lyrics." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**Unfiltered and Intimate Production** : A defining characteristic of Bedroom Pop is its unfiltered nature, lacking intermediaries like producers or record labels, which allows for extreme do-it-yourself music making with an emphasis on intimacy, raw emotion, and sparse production. "One of the attractive things about making music in this style is that it can be completely unfiltered. There are no intermediaries like a producer or a record label that might want to offer suggestions or edits or notes." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
