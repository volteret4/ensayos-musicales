# genre - Time Lord Rock

## curiosities
**Doctor Who Inspiration** : Time Lord Rock is a subgenre predominantly found in Britain and Australia, largely due to the popularity of the TV series "Doctor Who" in those regions, which serves as its primary source of inspiration. "This seemed to be mostly a British and Australian thing for some reason, maybe because that's where the TV series Doctor Who was the biggest." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6snk0m4200
