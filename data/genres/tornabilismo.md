**Birth in Late 1970s** : The culture of turntablism emerged in the late 1970s, specifically around 1978-1979, closely associated with hip-hop culture. It represented a crucial leap in DJing, transforming the turntable from a playback device into a musical instrument. **Platter as an Instrument** : Turntablism revolutionized DJing by shifting the use of the turntable from a mere apparatus for playing records to a musical instrument. This was made possible by direct-drive turntables like the Technics SL-1200, which prevented damage to records during manipulation.
