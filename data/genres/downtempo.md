**Influence on Chill Out Diversification** : In the first half of the 1990s, as the Chill Out genre expanded and incorporated influences from outside rave culture, "Down Tempo" music was identified as a key contributor. This style helped broaden the sonic palette of Chill Out, moving it beyond its initial dance-music roots.
