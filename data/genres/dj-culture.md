## curiosities
**Beats Per Minute (BPM) Measurement** : In DJ culture, BPM, or beats per minute, is a crucial measurement of tempo used by DJs for music selection on the dance floor. It helps categorize music and can also be used for personal activities like pacing a jog, where 170 BPM is suitable for a fast stride. "It means beats per minute.
