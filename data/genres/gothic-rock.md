# genre - Gothic Rock

## curiosities
**Post-Punk Development** : As UK punk rock gained momentum in 1976, artists facing dire economic conditions gravitating toward "dark thoughts" about their life prospects. This led to a theatrical style featuring Victorian-era fashion and dark musical themes, eventually categorized as "gothic" by music writers. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
**Volcanic Roots** : The 1815 eruption of Mount Tambora in Indonesia led to "the year without a summer" in 1816, which inspired Mary Shelley to write _Frankenstein_. This book became a foundational text for gothic literature, which later influenced the dark themes and "ultra-noir" look of gothic rock in the late 1970s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2KA97T4u08
