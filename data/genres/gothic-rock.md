# genre - Gothic Rock

## curiosities
**Gothic Rock Lineage – From Tambora's Eruption to Mary Shelley to Bauhaus** : The genealogy of gothic rock traces back to the 1815 eruption of the Tambora volcano in Indonesia — the largest in recorded history — which caused the "Year Without a Summer" in 1816. The resulting gloom and cold inspired Mary Shelley to conceive *Frankenstein*, a foundational text of Gothic literature. That Gothic tradition, running through horror cinema and theatrical glamour of the 1960s, was eventually absorbed into rock music by bands like Bauhaus, Joy Division, and The Cure in the late 1970s.
