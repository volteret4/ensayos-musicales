# genre - Contemporary Hit Radio

## curiosities
**"Power" Song Rotation** : Contemporary Hit Radio stations (formerly Top 40) categorize their most popular songs as "power" songs. There are typically two to five such songs, optimized for rotation based on audience demands. In extreme cases, a single power song might be played up to 120 times a week, averaging once every hour and 24 minutes. "The most popular songs of the moment are placed in a special category that's called power or something similar." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjwRApoJhA
