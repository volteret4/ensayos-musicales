**Growing Trend in Music** : Retro-futurism, drawing influences from artists like Gainsbourg and Bowie, and cinematic works by James Bond and Kubrick, is a growing musical style. Artists like Faber and John Meniswick are having significant success in this direction, and even mainstream artists like Adele and Daft Punk are incorporating elements of these sounds.
