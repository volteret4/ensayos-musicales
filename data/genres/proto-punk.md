# genre - Proto-punk

## curiosities
**Definition - Precursor to Punk Rock** : Proto-punk is a classification for music that was essential in laying the groundwork for punk rock, representing the stylistic and attitudinal developments that had to occur before punk rock could fully emerge as a defined genre.
