# genre - Techno pop

## curiosities
**80s Alt-Rock Radio Inclusion** : Techno pop was among the diverse genres featured on alt-rock radio playlists in the 1980s, demonstrating the broad range of music played before the rise of grunge. ← Ongoing History of New Music > Theories, Thoughts, and Half-Baked Ideas | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Emergence as a Genre (Around 1980)** : Technopop is described as a new post-punk, all-electronic music genre that began to "crest" around 1980, marking a significant shift in popular music. "Just as this new post-punk, all-electronic music called Technopop was starting to crest." ← Ongoing History of New Music > Golden Age of Synths as told by OMD | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0fQQubt9UTI&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Rise in the 1980s** : The early 1980s saw the widespread adoption of synthesizers, leading to the emergence of techno pop. This genre brought synthesizers into popular music in meaningful ways, influenced heavily by pioneers like Kraftwerk. "And this brings us to the techno pop sounds of the 1980s, to Pesh Mode, Human League, Yazoo, Visage" ← Ongoing History of New Music > The History of Alt-Rock： Chapter 10 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
