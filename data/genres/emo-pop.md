# genre - Emo Pop

## curiosities
**Offshoot of Emo** : Emo Pop emerged as a subgenre from the broader emo movement, retaining the sensitive lyrical themes but incorporating more pop hooks and single melodies, exemplified by bands like Fall Out Boy and Panic! At The Disco. "There was Emo Pop, same grounding and sensitive matters, but with more pop hooks and single melodies, this is where followed boy and panic at the disco would end up." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_yslM5oanRo
