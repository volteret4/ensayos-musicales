# genre - Música Ambiental

## curiosities
**Designed for Flexible Attention** : Coined by Brian Eno, "música ambiental" (ambient music) is characterized by its ability to adapt to multiple levels of auditory attention. It is designed to be simultaneously ignorable and interesting, providing a sonic backdrop that does not impose itself on the listener but rather allows for varied engagement depending on their focus. "La música ambiental debe poder adaptarse a muchos niveles de atención auditiva, sin imponer o en particular. Debe ser tan ignorable como interesante." ← Muzak： Música para ser ignorada | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7iN6WiB5Y1g
