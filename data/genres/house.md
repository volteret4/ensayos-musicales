# genre - House

There, it experienced a significant explosion in popularity and evolved into a completely new form within the European context. **Chicago Origins and Sound** : House music emerged from Chicago with roots in gospel and as an evolution of disco, which was resisting decline in the city. Unlike techno, it originated from the city's ghettos and utilized rhythm machines and samples instead of traditional bands. **Old School House Characteristics** : Old School House, particularly from the late 1980s and early 1990s, is characterized by a classic 4x4 beat at around 120 bpm and the iconic "piano stab." This piano stab is a short, dry chord that, despite being melodic, functions primarily as a rhythmic rather than harmonic element. Key labels from this era included Tracks Records from Chicago and Strilly Riding and Lyrgos Records from New York. "Hablamos del clásico beat 4x4 en un tempo alrededor de 120 bpm y el mitico pianito del House, el llamado piano stab. Ese acorde corto y seco, que aunque sea melodico, se utiliza más como un recorso ritmico que armonico." ← ¿Qué es el RALLY HOUSE？ El género que suena como un juego de PS1 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKCDTQlz0uw
