# genre - New Wave of British Heavy Metal

## curiosities
**Key Bands of the Movement** : This movement, active in the late 1970s and early 1980s, encompassed edgy bands such as Judas Priest, Iron Maiden, and initially, Def Leppard, before Mut Lang transformed Def Leppard's sound.
