# genre - High-resolution audio

## curiosities
**Evolution and Accessibility** : The concept of high-resolution audio emerged as record labels, digital storefronts, hardware manufacturers, and streaming platforms began to improve audio quality over the last decade. Initially, specialized and expensive playback hardware was required, but now, major streamers like Apple Music, Amazon Music, and Tidal offer high-resolution audio. These files are uncompressed, retaining all the musical information, often containing more data than a standard CD. "It began with the introduction of the concept of high-resolution audio, digital music files that were not compressed, did not have anything stripped out, and in fact contained more information than what we got from a CD." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GlcOoINI7EQ
