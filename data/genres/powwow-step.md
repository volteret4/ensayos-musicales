# genre - Powwow Step

## curiosities
**Blend of Traditional and Modern** : Powwow Step is a genre that blends traditional First Nations music with modern electronic dance, hip-hop, and dubstep elements. This fusion creates a unique sound that represents Indigenous culture in a contemporary context. "They blend dance and hip-hop and dubstep with First Nations music into a sound that's been called Powwow Step." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YFv6Ln00XjA
