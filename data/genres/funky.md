**Early Influence on Mixing Techniques (Late 1970s)** : In the late 1970s, some American funky and disco DJs began creating new songs by mixing rhythmic parts from various tracks. These experimental mixes were played in discotheques and became highly successful, leading to the circulation of pirated cassettes and eventually the commercialization of mixed records. "A finales de los años 70, algunos diges norteamericanos de fanque y disco empezaron a crear canciones mezclando partes rímpicas de varios temas para reproducirlos en discotecas, donde eran todo un éxito." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
