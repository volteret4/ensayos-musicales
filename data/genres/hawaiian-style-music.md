# genre - Hawaiian style music

## curiosities
**19th Century Origins** : When Portuguese explorers introduced the Spanish guitar to native Hawaiians in the 19th century, Hawaiians developed a unique playing style: laying the guitar horizontally on their laps and using a steel bar against the strings while plucking notes with the other hand.
