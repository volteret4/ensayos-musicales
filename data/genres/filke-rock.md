# genre - Filke Rock

## curiosities
**Cultural Impact** : Filke music has demonstrated enduring cultural significance, evidenced by its dedicated awards program, the Pegasus Awards, and the existence of a Filke Hall of Fame. "Filke music is so enduring that it has its own awards program called the Pegasus Awards, and there's a Filke Hall of Fame." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8lQv8eMJ868
**Origin and Characteristics (1950s)** : Filke rock, a precursor to nerd rock, emerged in the 1950s as a form of folk music. Its name originated from a typo (I next to O on the keyboard). It was favored by fans of science fiction, horror movies, and fantasy, and typically involved musicians playing acoustic guitars at sci-fi conventions in gatherings known as "House Filks" or "Filke circles." "The name itself is a typo... These were folk songs, favored by certain fans of science fiction and horror movies and fantasy." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8lQv8eMJ868
