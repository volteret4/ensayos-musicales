# genre - Surf Music

## curiosities
**Essential Role of Fender Stratocaster** : With the consultation and influence of Dick Dale, the Fender Stratocaster became an indispensable component of the surf music sound. This collaboration helped define the genre's distinct sonic characteristics. **Southern California Craze (Early 1960s)** : The surf music craze originated in Southern California in the early 1960s. This genre predated the popular songs by bands like The Beach Boys, with artists like Dick Dale already innovating within the style. "By the early 1960s, the surf music craze had begun in Southern California." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oUkMOkEr9U
