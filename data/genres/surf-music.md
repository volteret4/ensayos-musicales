# genre - Surf Music

## curiosities
**Essential Role of Fender Stratocaster** : With the consultation and influence of Dick Dale, the Fender Stratocaster became an indispensable component of the surf music sound. This collaboration helped define the genre's distinct sonic characteristics. **Origin Point: "Moon Dog"** : Despite the general rule that there is never truly a "first" in music, historians of the Surf Music genre commonly point to The Gamblers' "Moon Dog" as the first surf music record. While twangy guitar instrumentals existed before it, "Moon Dog" is considered a strong candidate for marking the genre's beginning. "Historians of the Surf Music genre now generally point to it as the first Surf Music record ever, and it's as good a choice as any." ← Episode 86： ＂LSD-25＂ by the Gamblers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UNd6UwbL1rA
**Southern California Craze (Early 1960s)** : The surf music craze originated in Southern California in the early 1960s. This genre predated the popular songs by bands like The Beach Boys, with artists like Dick Dale already innovating within the style.
