# genre - Mental Music

## curiosities
**Jamaican Folk Roots** : Mental music is a form of Jamaican folk music based on traditional African sounds, brought over from Africa with the slave trade. It contributed to the mix of indigenous Caribbean music that influenced the birth of Ska.
