# genre - Country and Western

## curiosities
**Billboard Magazine Origin** : The name "Country and Western" was coined by Billboard magazine, initially not as a descriptor for a single genre but as a title for a collection of different genres on their charts. It combined "Country music" from the Eastern US and "Western music" from the West. **Early Target Market for Fender** : Leo Fender initially targeted country and western musicians, particularly those who were already using lap steel guitars, as the primary audience for his solid-body Spanish-style guitars.
