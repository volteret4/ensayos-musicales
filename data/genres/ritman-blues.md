## curiosities
**British Absorption of American R&B** : In the early 1960s, there was a significant fervor in the United Kingdom for absorbing American Ritman Blues records, which were not widely available in Europe. This led to a unique, sometimes "weird" and "not fully understood," reinterpretation of American R&B by British bands. **Struggles Against Beat Music Dominance** : While Ritman Blues was a foundational inspiration for many early 60s British bands, its sound often struggled to gain widespread popularity against the dominant "Beatle sound." Bands dedicated to Ritman Blues, like The Jarpers, found it challenging to resonate with a public preferring the lighter, more popular beat music.
