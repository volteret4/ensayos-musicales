# genre - Indie Rock

## curiosities
**Engine of Change for Rock and Roll** : The indie music universe is identified as the engine of change for rock and roll, where upcoming sounds, trends, and fads originate and propel the genre into the future.
**Modern Era Beginning (1986)** : The release and subsequent success of the C86 cassette in 1986 is considered by some to mark the beginning of the modern era of indie music in the UK.
**Part Two History** : The podcast episode details part two of the history of indie rock.
**Post-Grunge Explosion** : things "loosened up" after the grunge era, and Indie Rock "exploded," contributing to a greater variety of sounds in alt-rock radio.
