# genre - Flamenco

## curiosities
**Origin of Guitar Solos (1890s)** : The rise of Flamenco guitar in the 1890s is suggested as the period when the guitar began to be showcased as a solo instrument, leading to the development of intricate fretboard playing for solos. "The guitar solo, the idea of playing something crazy on the fretboard with your left hand, is something that maybe dates back to the 1890s with the rise of the Flamenco guitar, which was among the very first to showcase the guitar as a solo instrument." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnQ1pNbIUjw
