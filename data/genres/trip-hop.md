# genre - Trip Hop

## curiosities
**British Crossbreeding** : Trip Hop was described as a "British crossbreeding of hip hop and electronica," producing acts like Portus Head, Machiba, Sneakopimps, Estero, and Massive Attack. "And then there was Trip Hop, a British crossbreeding of hip hop and electronica that gave us acts like Portus Head, Machiba, Sneakopimps, Estero and probably the greatest of them all. **Influence from AR Kane** : AR Kane was tremendously influential in the development of Trip Hop, among other genres, demonstrating their broad impact on new musical approaches. ← Ongoing History of New Music > Dreampop - A History | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Sound Definition** : Trip Hop is fundamentally a form of down-tempo music that ingeniously integrates elements of electronic and hip hop in a manner that makes their primary ingredients subtle. It also incorporates aspects of funk, soul, jazz, various samples, and a range of sonic experimentation. "It's basically a form of down-tempo music that incorporated elements of both electronic and hip hop, but it's a way that you really can't tell that those are the main ingredients." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The 90's Part 2B： Front Women | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eo32mAZd8DI&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
