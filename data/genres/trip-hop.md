# genre - Trip Hop

## curiosities
**Bristol Origin and Definition** : Defined in 1994 by Portishead, originating from Bristol.
**Musical Elements** : Described as a form of down tempo music that incorporates elements of electronic and hip hop in a subtle way, along with dashes of funk, soul, jazz samples, and various bits of sonic experimentation.
**UK Dominance** : Became one of the most dominant sounds in the UK in the mid-1990s.
