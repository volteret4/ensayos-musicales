# genre - Reggae

## curiosities
**Bob Marley as "King"** : Bob Marley is referred to as "The King of All Things reggae."
**Dominant Scene in Laguna Beach** : Reggae music was the dominant genre in Laguna Beach, California, where Taylor Hawkins grew up, with hard rock being "frowned upon" and associated with "heshers."
**West Indian Influence in UK** : Its sounds, along with Ska, were introduced to industrial cities like Coventry by West Indian immigrants.
