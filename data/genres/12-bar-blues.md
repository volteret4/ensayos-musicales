# genre - 12 bar blues

## curiosities
**Codification by W.C. Handy** : The 12 bar blues features one of the most common chord progressions in Western music. Its style of lyrics, phrasing, structure, and duration have been largely standardized since the days of gospel, spirituals, and African-based oral traditions. An Alabama musician named W.C. Handy is credited with probably being the first to codify 12 bar blues playing around 1905. "An Alabama musician named W.C.
