# genre - Trad Jazz

## curiosities
**1960s UK Revival** : Around the mid-1960s in the UK, Trad Jazz experienced a revival. English "hipsters" who were unimpressed with the emerging rock and roll movement chose to embrace traditional jazz, which had been popular approximately 60 years prior. This led to new artists like Acker Bilk (mentioned as Acre Milk), Kenny Ball, and Monty Sunshine bringing New Orleans Dixie Land back into vogue, a trend that lasted until about 1965. "English hipsters who were unimpressed with this new rock and roll thing, decided that traditional jazz that was originally big 60 years previous was actually where it was at." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gkLMwrthZ9s
