**924 Gilman Street Club – All-Ages Punk Hub** : Located at 924 Gilman Street in West Berkeley, California, this venue is recognized as arguably the most important all-ages punk club globally. Founded in 1986, it operates as a member's only establishment, requiring a $2 annual fee for membership, which includes voting rights at bi-monthly meetings where club business is discussed. The club enforces strict rules: no drugs, alcohol, violence, racism, homophobia, or major label bands. Due to the major label ban, successful alumni such as Green Day, The Offspring, AFI, Primus, and Bad Religion are no longer welcome to perform there. "Probably the most important, all-ages punk club in the world." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKHdTSfDd6E
