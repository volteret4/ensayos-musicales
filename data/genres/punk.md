# genre - Punk

## curiosities
**DIY Ethic** : Demonstrated that anyone with attitude, regardless of professional musicianship or serious skills, could make music, directly contrasting with pre-punk industry norms.
**Evolution of Music** : Following punk's emergence, a wide array of music began to appear that, while not necessarily sounding like traditional punk, clearly bore the influence of its breakthrough, thereby opening up new musical possibilities.
**Inspiration for Indie Labels** : Its spirit and do-it-yourself mentality served as a significant inspiration for the formation of numerous independent record labels in both the UK and the US.
**Part of Diverse 80s Alt-Rock** : "every conceivable flavor of punk" was a genre played on alt-rock radio stations in the late 80s, contributing to their breadth of music.
