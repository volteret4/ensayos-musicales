# genre - Canción Narrada

## curiosities
**Distinction from Rap** : The fundamental difference between "canción narrada" and rap lies in the former's lack of a consistent rhythmic following, which is a defining characteristic of rap. Despite this, the vocal delivery styles are often quite similar, making it challenging to distinguish between them at certain points. **Precursor to Rap** : "Canción narrada" (narrated song) is described as a vocal resource or singing technique that significantly predates modern rap and is considered one of its primary precursors. Artists such as James Brown and Grace Jones widely employed this style in their music.
