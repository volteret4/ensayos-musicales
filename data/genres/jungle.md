**Emergence and Characteristics (1992)** : Jungle emerged around 1992 as a distinct genre, characterized by tempos oscillating between 160 and 170 BPMs, deep bass lines, and strong influences from dancehall and ragga. It was deeply connected to sound system culture and gained popularity in the British scene through pirate radio and illegal parties. "Hacia 1992, de ese caldo de cultivo surgió el jungle." ← Por qué los videjuegos de los 2000 sonaban así？ Jungle⧸Drum and Bass | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3_cr6vOW3KQ
**Evolution from Breakbeat Hardcore** : Jungle emerged in the United Kingdom as a direct evolution from Breakbeat Hardcore, following the fragmentation of the larger rave scene into more specialized genres. "En el rey unido, por ejemplo, el Briguit hardcore sería algermente el jungle." ← Qué fue el GABBER y por qué asustaba a Europa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0mnM_wO4eFY
**Influence on Second Wave IDM** : During the "second wave" of IDM, the prominence of percussion in the genre significantly increased. This shift was partly influenced by the energetic and complex rhythmic structures found in jungle and drama en bass (drum and bass) music. "durante esta segunda ola, la percusión tomó más protagonismo, en parte influenciada por el jungle y el drama en bass." ← Qué fue el IDM y por qué cambió la música electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RTwn3hP1XTg
**Key Differences from Drama Bass** : Jungle is described as more raw and chaotic compared to drum and bass. It features profound bass, percussions with numerous breaks, dark ambient sounds, and a strong influence from dancehall and ragga, remaining heavily tied to rave culture. "El jungle es más crudo y caótico, bajos profundos, percusiones con muchos breaks, ambientadas con sonidos escuros y una fuerte influencia del dexhol y el raga." ← Por qué los videjuegos de los 2000 sonaban así？ Jungle⧸Drum and Bass | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3_cr6vOW3KQ
**Scene Reputation and Evolution to Drama Bass (1995)** : Despite its growing popularity, jungle's reputation suffered by 1995 as the scene became associated with increased physicality, harder drugs, fights, and conflicts. This negative perception led several musicians to distance themselves and refine the jungle sound, which eventually gave birth to drum and bass. "A la aumentar la velocidad de la intensidad, la escena se volvió mucho más física, entraron drogas más duras y fue común ver peleas y conflictos." ← Por qué los videjuegos de los 2000 sonaban así？ Jungle⧸Drum and Bass | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3_cr6vOW3KQ
