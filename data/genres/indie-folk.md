# genre - Indie Folk

## curiosities
**2010s Resurgence with Roots in 1950s/60s** : While not a new genre of the 2010s, Indie Folk experienced a significant resurgence in that decade, with its foundational roots tracing back to the late 1950s or 1960s. "Then we have Indy Folk. It's not exactly a new genre from the 2010s. I mean, it's roots go back to the 60s or even the late 1950s, but it did have a new major coming out that decade." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**Alt Rock Fusion of Acoustic Genres** : Indie Folk is described as a subgenre within alt rock where folk, blues, gospel, and even country music converge, emphasizing acoustic instrumentation, rootsiness, and authenticity. "This is a corner of alt rock where folk, blues, gospel, and even a little country came together." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
**Authenticity and Acoustic Vibe** : A core aspect of Indie Folk is its emphasis on rootsiness and authenticity, typically featuring largely acoustic arrangements and cultivating a generally relaxed and quiet atmosphere. "An emphasis is placed on rootceness and authenticity. Everything could be largely acoustic, and the vibe generally relaxed and quiet." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c
