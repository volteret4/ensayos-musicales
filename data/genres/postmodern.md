# genre - Postmodern

## curiosities
**Depeche Mode's Categorization** : Depeche Mode was referred to as "the latest postmodern group" at the time of their infamous *Violator* autograph session in Los Angeles in 1990, indicating how the band was categorized in the media. "The band is called Depeche Mode, the latest postmodern group to retake the United States by storm." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jD1dWxNLvfE
**UK Term Evolution** : "Postmodern" was a term that evolved in the UK to describe new music, used alongside "New Pop" and "New Music" as alternatives or successors to "New Wave." "But in the UK, other terms evolved. Remember that they got to New Wave first. So they were moving on. There was New Pop as a term postmodern was another, like I mentioned earlier, New Music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYsV3ozbk4M
