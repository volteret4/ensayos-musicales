# genre - Minto Music

## curiosities
**African Roots** : Minto music's traditional sounds originated with "the slaves from Africa." This directly links the genre to the cultural heritage and musical traditions of enslaved African peoples. **Ancient Roots** : Minto music is noted to have roots dating back "hundreds of years." This emphasizes its long-standing presence and evolution as a traditional musical form. "Both sounds date back hundreds of years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
**Jamaican Folk Music** : Minto music is described as a form of Jamaican folk music that emerged from Kingston. It is based on traditional sounds brought by slaves from Africa. "Kingston was Minto music, a form of Jamaican folk music based on traditional sounds that came with the slaves from Africa." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNIVqKqAlnk
