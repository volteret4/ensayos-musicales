# genre - New Pop

## curiosities
**MTV-Driven Visual Genre** : New Pop was an umbrella term for early 1980s artists whose emergence was propelled by MTV and the explosion of music videos. It encompassed diverse pop styles, often experimental but always tempered with a slick pop sheen for wider appeal. A defining characteristic was the artists' unique visual looks, which, through music videos, transformed them into brief "cultural phenomenons."
