# genre - Hot Rod Rock

## curiosities
**Shift in Focus** : Hot Rod Rock emerged as a subset of surf music where the lyrical and musical focus shifted from the beach to the street, emphasizing souped-up cars and hot rods rather than surfboards, while still drawing inspiration from the California lifestyle. "The focus on the music moved from the beach to the street. And it was still largely inspired by the California lifestyle, but souped up cars and hot rods replaced surfboards." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOeChe_D1Ag
