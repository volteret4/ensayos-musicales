# genre - Electrónica

## curiosities
**Dominance in Spanish Sales Charts (1980s-1990s)** : During the 1980s and 1990s, unique electronic music compilation albums, known as Megamixes, frenetically and skillfully blended with scratches, brakes, and sound effects, topped the sales charts in Spain. This was a particular and unprecedented phenomenon in the country. "Unos peculiares discos recopilatorios de música electrónica mezclados frenética y habilidos a mente con Scratches, Brakes y efectos de sonido encabezaban las listas de ventas." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Evolution in Megamixes** : Following the cultural and commercial explosion of "La Ruta y el Sonido Valencia," the Megamix phenomenon in Spain began to incorporate faster and more forceful electronic music styles, moving beyond its initial focus on Italo Disco. **Practice of Pressing Own Vinyls** : It is a common practice among many electronic artists, such as Portis Gato and Fadwou y Eslín, to press their own vinyl records containing their unique sounds. These custom pressings are often used for personal remixes or for integrating into real-time music creation during live performances, explaining why some unreleased material might exist in physical formats without a public release. **Sound Processing Evolution** : While much of electronic music increasingly leans towards artificial and synthetic sounds, there is a developing trend, notably championed by James Blake, that seeks to process sound to make it appear more natural and organic. This perspective might become a more common direction in the future as a reaction against overly artificial sounds. "Cada vez procesamos más el sonido y estamos yendo a sonidos que son cada vez más artificiales. **Sound Profile with Beats/Skullcandy** : Beats and Skullcandy headphones are characterized by a unique and specialized equalization that strongly accentuates bass frequencies, creating a powerful "subwoofer effect." This sound profile is particularly well-suited for electronic music, as it enhances the deep bass and rhythmic elements often central to the genre, making them popular among listeners of this style. "Tiene un efecto subvofer que se atura mucho los bajos y que daba un efecto de como hipotente, que hace que le guste mucho a la gente de la que le gusta el electrónica y el hip hop." ← Music Radar Clan > Auriculares recomendados para cada presupuesto | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WrJxQz0M2lI&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← electrónica ← electrónica
**Transformative Impact of Sampling** : The emergence of albums like DJ Shadow's "Introducing" and "Diabalanches'" "Sin-Sail-Few" fundamentally transformed electronic music. DJ Shadow pioneered the idea of an entire album made from samples, while "Diabalanches" pushed this to an extreme with over 3000 fragments, establishing new conceptual perspectives and becoming a landmark in the genre's history. ← Music Radar Clan > THE AVALANCHES, recods y un disco con 3.000 samples | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bV-Zn0GHssM&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← electrónica ← electrónica
