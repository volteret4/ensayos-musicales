# genre - Hard Rock

## curiosities
**"Satanist" Branding due to Association with Crime** : Hard rock, along with metal, was a genre often viewed with suspicion and branded as "Satanist" by those who distrusted it, particularly after its association with serial killers like Richard Ramirez. The public reaction was fueled by the perception that the music could inspire evil. **Alice in Chains Vocal Harmonies** : Alice in Chains was noted for having the best hard rock harmonies since the first version of Van Halen, specifically those between David Lee Roth and Michael Anthony. "Best hard rock harmony since, I don't know, David Lee Roth and Michael Anthony in the first version of Van Halen." ← Ongoing History of New Music > Groups That Changed Their Front-Person and Survived | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vLZla5h4xKQ&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Frowned Upon in Orange County** : In Taylor Hawkins' early years in Orange County, California, hard rock was generally "frowned upon" by the local scene, which favored reggae. Those who listened to hard rock were often labeled "heshers" and were not considered "cool" or "surfers." "So hard rock was really frowned upon. You were a hesher. You were from the valley. You're not cool. You weren't a surfer." ← Ongoing History of New Music > Remembering Taylor Hawkins | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dD-VEcFpIFA&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Grammy Award for Linkin Park** : Linkin Park's debut album, Hybrid Theory, was nominated for three Grammys, winning one in the category of Best Hard Rock Performance for their song "Crawling." **Influence on Alt Hip Hop** : The band Oxymorrons from Queens, New York City, are noted for their sound which incorporates a "Hard Rock Twist" into Alternative Hip Hop. "and you can call them Alternative Hip Hop with a Hard Rock Twist." ← Ongoing History of New Music > Black History Month 2023 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1NNvxgx8uA&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Long Hair as a Standard** : By the mid-1970s, long hair became a standard requirement in some quarters of music, particularly within hard rock and metal. This tradition continues today, cementing long hair as an expected aesthetic for these genres. "Long hair became standard in some quarters. Hard rock and metal often required it. That's a tradition that continues today." ← Ongoing History of New Music > Rock Explainer 2 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bferkEWvJg0&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**Shift in UK Market Dominance** : This increased chart presence marked a shift from a long period where American metal and hard rock bands, with few exceptions like Accept and Scorpions, largely dominated the British market. "And this was a nice change from a long stretch where American metal and hard rock bands pretty much had the British market to themselves, with a couple of bands like Accept and Scorpions thrown in." ← Ongoing History of New Music > The History of the 2010s Part 2： The Role of Indie Rock | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
**The Runaways as Pioneers** : The Runaways are considered the first all-female hard rock band in history to gain international attention. Their presence, led by Sandy West on drums, challenged gender norms in the male-dominated hard rock genre. "We can safely call them the first all-female hard rock band in history to receive international attention." ← Ongoing History of New Music > Driven By Her： History of Female Drummers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9GHpC0kCgHY&list=PLM8bxV0E1Gse5C3n1u-mkVSTZLL5AJICG
