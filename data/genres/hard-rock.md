# genre - Hard Rock

## curiosities
**"Satanist" Branding due to Association with Crime** : Hard rock, along with metal, was a genre often viewed with suspicion and branded as "Satanist" by those who distrusted it, particularly after its association with serial killers like Richard Ramirez. The public reaction was fueled by the perception that the music could inspire evil.
