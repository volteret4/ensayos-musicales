# genre - Hard Rock

## curiosities
**"Satanist" Branding due to Association with Crime** : Hard rock, along with metal, was a genre often viewed with suspicion and branded as "Satanist" by those who distrusted it, particularly after its association with serial killers like Richard Ramirez. The public reaction was fueled by the perception that the music could inspire evil. Those who listened to hard rock were often labeled "heshers" and were not considered "cool" or "surfers." "So hard rock was really frowned upon. You were a hesher. You were from the valley. **Long Hair as a Standard** : By the mid-1970s, long hair became a standard requirement in some quarters of music, particularly within hard rock and metal. This tradition continues today, cementing long hair as an expected aesthetic for these genres. "Long hair became standard in some quarters. Hard rock and metal often required it. **Origins in the Blues** : Hard rock is characterized by its foundational roots, being "born out of the blues," a clear distinction from soft rock's origins in pop and folk music. **Precursor to Heavy Metal** : Hard rock was the prevailing term used to describe the musical style that later became known as heavy metal during its formative years. Bands like Cream, categorized as hard rock, contributed elements that were foundational to the heavy metal genre before its distinct classification.
