# genre - Rara music

## curiosities
**Haitian Parade and Procession Music** : Rara music is a vibrant form of Haitian music primarily associated with parades and processions. Its distinctive sound comes from instruments often improvised from everyday objects like pipes, empty coffee cans, and homemade drums, complemented by other percussion instruments such as maracas, and sometimes trumpets and saxophones when available. This genre served as a significant influence on Arcade Fire's "Reflektor" album.
