# genre - Dreampop

## curiosities
**Characteristics and Influence** : Dreampop emerged by blending ideas from psychedelic music with intricate textures and atmospheric sounds, often achieved through electronics or studio techniques. The genre aimed for "total immersion in music," with acts like the Cocteau Twins pioneering a "dreamy, slightly stone sort of sound" and unique vocal approaches. "These groups copped ideas from psychedelic music and blended them with interesting textures and atmosphere, often using electronics or studio tricks." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LgJ7ww2AgF4
**Dreampop – Coined by Alex Elluley of AR Kane; Atmospheric, Textured; Lana Del Rey as Minimalist Example** : Dreampop is a very atmospheric and highly textured form of alt rock with DNA from certain corners of Goth and psychedelic music. The term was apparently coined by Alex Elluley, part of an experimental British band from the mid-1980s called AR Kane. The sound is meant to immerse the listener in mood and vibe — often highly produced with effects-laden guitars and synths, or achieved through a special kind of minimalism. Dreampop often features breathy vocals, male or female. Lana Del Rey's 2012 work is cited as minimalist Dreampop. "As far as we can tell, the term Dreampop was coined by a guy named Alex Elluley." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9cAeeExXKwU
