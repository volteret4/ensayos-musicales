**Accidental Birth from Injury** : Doom Metal did not originate from a happy idea but from an accident and a wound. This paradoxical origin gave rise to the heaviest and darkest sound in music, a direct result of Tony Iommi's injury and his innovative solution to continue playing guitar. "El Doom Metal funciona de la misma manera, un genero que no nació de una idea feliz, sino de un accidente, de una herida que, paradogicamente, dió luz al sonido más pesado y oscuro de la música." ← El DOOM METAL es el DARK SOULS de la música | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v0eaod_repg

**Defining Musical Characteristics** : Doom Metal is characterized by slow, heavy tempos, with riffs becoming longer and repetitive, and sustain stretching almost infinitely, multiplying the inherent heaviness of metal. These bands aimed not to dazzle with speed, but to trap the listener in an oppressive atmosphere where each beat feels like a ton. "Los riffs se volvieron más largos y repetitivos, el susto en se estiraba hasta el infinito, la pesadier del metal se multiplicó. Era como si cada compás, es ser una tonelada." ← El DOOM METAL es el DARK SOULS de la música | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v0eaod_repg
**Emergence in the 1980s** : While its roots trace back to Black Sabbath's early sound, Doom Metal began to truly take shape in the 1980s. This was a reaction against the prevailing trend in metal which favored faster, more technical, and virtuosic sounds, as some bands consciously chose slowness and atmosphere. "Pero en Paláleo ese éxito, empezás a surgir una reacción opuesta. **Journey Without Destination** : The prolonged riffs, slow tempos, and dense atmospheres of Doom Metal create a space suspended in time that invites reflection. Feeling the electric guitars resonate through the body becomes an almost primitive experience, where for a moment, all notion of progress and the frantic rhythm of daily life is lost. The genre is described as a journey without a destination, with its objective being to inhabit the sound itself. "El Doom Metal es un viaje que no tiene destino, porque es objetivo, no es llegar a ningún sitio, es evitar el sonido." ← El DOOM METAL es el DARK SOULS de la música | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v0eaod_repg

**Structure as Prolonged Sonic Ritual** : Unlike faster metal subgenres, Doom Metal is structured as a prolonged sonic ritual where time dilates, notes are suspended, and the music becomes a state of mind. The use of drone, repetition, and silences creates a trance-like effect, similar to religious chants or shamanic meditations, enveloping the listener rather than progressing. "A diferencia de otros subgéneros del Metal más acelerados, el Doom se estructura como ritual sonoro prolongado.
