## curiosities
**Key Component of 90s Alt-Rock** : The Riot Girl movement played a significant role in the alt-rock explosion of the 1990s, comprising all-female or predominantly female groups primarily from the Pacific Northwest. This subculture was distinctly political, feminist, queer-positive, anti-racist, anti-sexist, and opposed to violence against women, actively promoting activism and support networks through zines and chapters. "The Riot Girl movement was a very important component to the whole alt rock explosion of the 1990s. These were all female, or at least mostly female groups, from the Pacific Northwest.
