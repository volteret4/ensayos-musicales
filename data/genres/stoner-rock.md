# genre - Stoner Rock

## curiosities
**Foundations from Josh Homme** : Josh Homme's signature "chunky, riffy sound" is recognized as a significant foundational element of the genre now known as Stoner Rock. "His signature chunky riffy sound is a big part of the foundations of what we now call Stoner Rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVIswnZYFkU
**Popular Term for Kyuss's Genre** : This genre name became widely associated with the fuzzy guitar music pioneered by Kyuss, despite Josh Homme's personal dislike for the term. "But the one that really stuck was Stoner Rock. Josh grew to hate that term." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mG1TQuhcf1o
