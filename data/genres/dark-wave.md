# genre - Dark Wave

## curiosities
**Proposed Label for Joy Division** : "Dark Wave" was suggested as a potential descriptor for Joy Division's unique post-punk sound, which was characterized by brooding lyrics and low-voiced singers, as attempts were made to categorize their uncategorizable style.
