# genre - Britpop

## curiosities
**Decline** : The scene was considered "over" by the time Elastica released its second album, five years after its debut.
**Female-Fronted Bands** : A scene that included many female-fronted groups such as Lush, Catatonia, Echobelly, Sleeper, Saint Etienne, and Elastica.
**Foundations from The Smiths** : The Smiths laid the foundations for Britpop, a genre that would dominate the remainder of the 1990s.
