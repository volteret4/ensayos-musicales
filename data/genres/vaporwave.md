# genre - Vaporwave

## curiosities
**Core Musical Principles** : The musical foundation of vaporwave is based on sampling extracts of pop, funk, new age, or city pop songs from the 1980s and 1990s, which are then slowed down, looped, and heavily drenched in reverb to create a hypnotic atmosphere. "El no musical, la base del género, se basa en el sample de extractos de canciones de pop, funk, new age, o city pop de los años 80s y 90s, reducidos y pegados en Lop. A los cuales se le aplica un pesado river, lo cual genera un aire innotico." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Core Technique and Emotional Impact** : At its core, vaporwave involves taking popular songs, slowing them down, and applying a reverb effect, which also lowers the pitch and expands the chords. This simple manipulation profoundly changes the song's meaning, transforming music into something new and often evoking nostalgia or a sense of distortion. "Una corriente nación de los márgenes del vaporwave, que consiste básicamente en tomar canciones populares, ralentizarlas, y aplicarles un efecto de riverba." ← ¿Por qué el Slowed & Reverb y los Speed Up están rompiendo YT y TikTok？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0WWra85DsCo
**Cultural Deconstruction and Nostalgia** : A key aspect of vaporwave is its ability to deconstruct pop culture, turning it into a distorted mirror of itself. It creates a feeling that everything once brilliant about the 1980s and 1990s is now shrouded in a mist, perceivable only through the filter of memory, evoking a wistful dream-like state. "No se trata solo de ralentizar, de construir la cultura pop, para convertirla en un espejo distorsionado de sí mismo." ← ¿Por qué el Slowed & Reverb y los Speed Up están rompiendo YT y TikTok？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0WWra85DsCo
**Declared Dead in 2013, Leading to Subgenre Explosion** : In 2013, merely four years after its inception, the vaporwave community itself declared the genre "dead" due to the saturation and repetitiveness of its visual tropes (sculptures, pastel colors, old operating system windows). However, this "death" ironically marked the beginning of its branching into numerous subgenres. "En 2013, con apenas cuatro años de vida, declararon al vaporwave muerto." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Decline Due to Inflexibility** : Vaporwave is presented as an example of a genre that disappeared because it "didn't know how to adapt" to internet trends. Its artistic foundation, along with interesting subgenres like Mallsoft, Utopian Virtual, and Slushwave, failed to connect with audiences beyond niche communities, unlike Phonk's adaptability. "El vapor wave desapareció porque no supo hacer eso." ← ¿Qué pasó con el PHONK？ (La historia real) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ehfWyUHFFT8
**Definition as Musical Recycling** : Vaporwave is defined as a musical genre that involves taking "completely exhausted, outdated, and antiquated melodies," then giving them a "facelift" to renew and bring their essence to the present. This process is likened to the "recycling of music," representing a modern approach to composition by reinterpreting existing sounds. ← Music Radar Clan > THE AVALANCHES, recods y un disco con 3.000 samples | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bV-Zn0GHssM&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← vaporwave ← vaporwave
**Distinctive Visual Aesthetics (A E S T H E T I C S)** : Vaporwave adopts a unique visual aesthetic rooted in digital nostalgia and internet culture, incorporating elements such as low-resolution graphics, glitch art, Greco-Roman iconography, pastel and neon colors, and design styles from 1990s websites. This aesthetic, known as "A E S T H E T I C S," aims to evoke a sense of fascination with an idealized digital past where consumerism, technology, and pop culture merge into an evocative and unsettling visual collage. "Esta estética, conocida como a estética, busca evocar una sensación de fascinación por un pasado digital y idealizado." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Electronic Micro-Genre** : Vaporwave is classified as a micro-genre of electronic music, meaning it possesses a very specific sound targeted at a small, niche audience, and often functions as mood music. "This is a subgenre of electronic music. No, wait, it's more like a micro-genre, which means that it's very, very specific in the way it sounds, and it's aimed at a small and specific audience." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c ← vaporwave
**Enduring Importance Beyond Meme Status** : Despite often being perceived as just a meme, vaporwave has proven to be profoundly significant, altering how music scenes emerge and spread (moving from clubs to the internet), expanding its boundaries through diverse subgenres, offering a critique of consumerism and digital alienation, forging an unprecedented link between music and visuals, and fostering a DIY spirit in the digital realm. "Hoy en día el vaporwave, aunque sigue restrando esa imagen de Meme, ha demostrado ser mucho más importante de los que muchos creyeron." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**First Internet-Born Music Genre** : Vaporwave holds the distinction of being the first musical genre to be entirely conceived and developed on the internet, demonstrating a new paradigm for the emergence of music scenes beyond physical clubs and streets. "Fue el primer género musical en la serie y desarrollarse en internet, demostrando que las escenas podían surgir más allá de los clubs y las calles." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Influences and Slowed-Down Aesthetic** : Vaporwave incorporates elements from Psych, Lounge Music, R&B, and Chillwave, characterized by significantly slowed-down tempos. "Vaporware took bits from Psych, Lounge Music, R&B, and the Chill Wave of the Outs, and then slowed everything down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c ← vaporwave
**Internet-Era Hauntology** : Vaporwave is a genre identified as an internet-era application of hauntology, specifically evoking nostalgia for the 1980s. It leverages elements of past aesthetics to create a sense of longing for a period that many listeners may not have directly experienced. "aplicado a géneros como el vapor wavycis en hostarcia por los años 80." ← El Género Musical Más Escalofriante de Internet. Hauntología | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2jcvyJLgYcg
**Internet-Native with Visuals and Anonymity** : Emerging in the early 2010s, Vaporwave is an internet-native music form that extends beyond sound to include memes, art, graphic design, typography, Reddit threads, and often anonymous social and political commentary. "Vaporware is a very internet-former music that comes with memes and art and graphic design and topography and long threads on reddits and bits of social and political commentary, all often cloaked by anonymity." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c ← vaporwave
**Meme Subgenres' Resonance** : Curiously, the Vaporwave subgenres that achieved the most resonance on the internet were the "most meme-centric ones," such as Simpsons Wave, highlighting how non-artistic adaptability can sometimes dictate online popularity. "Creosamente los sugéneros del vapor wave que tuvieron más reeson a la internet fueron los más memes como el Simpsons Wave." ← ¿Qué pasó con el PHONK？ (La historia real) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ehfWyUHFFT8
**Origin on the Internet (Early 2010s)** : Vaporwave emerged in the early 2010s, with its origins rooted in the internet rather than in physical streets or neighborhoods. Its initial references showed strong similarities to DJ Screw's "Chopped and Screwed" mixtapes that circulated in Houston during the 1990s. "Un genero que surgió a principios de la década de 2010 en Internet y no en las calles bondos barrios." ← ¿Por qué el Slowed & Reverb y los Speed Up están rompiendo YT y TikTok？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0WWra85DsCo
**Parallel to Witchhouse in Press Reception** : Vaporwave, much like Witchhouse, faced similar challenges with the music press in its early stages. Albums such as "Floral Shoppe" and "Chuck Person's Eccojams Vol. 1" were often disregarded by critics who perceived them as mere "memes," highlighting a broader paradigm shift in how internet-born genres were initially received. "Esto también ocurrió con la primera etapa del Bapar Way, discos como Floral Chopo, Chuck Person Secochán, Volumen 1, fueron dejados de lado por gran parte de la prensa musical, quienes lo veían como memes." ← Witch House： Cuando internet inventó su género maldito | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SLlzXA0eyu0
**Postmodern Reinterpretation** : With the popularization of the internet, vaporwave emerged as a postmodern reinterpretation of Muzak, leveraging its "septic and consumerist" qualities. Artists in this genre often apply deep reverbs and ambient sounds to existing elevator music, creating a distinct aesthetic that references commercialism and often evokes a sense of nostalgia. "Su calidad septica y consumista fue aprovechada por el vaporwave. Nonde los artistas que crean dentro del género aplican profundos reverbs y sonido ambiente a la música de ascensor." ← Muzak： Música para ser ignorada | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7iN6WiB5Y1g
**Screwed and Chopped Technique** : A key technique in Vaporwave is "Screwed and Chopped," where samples are not only added but also specifically slowed down, contributing to the genre's distinct sonic atmosphere. "Vaporware also uses a technique called Screwed and Chopped, in which samples are added, but also slowed down." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdTs-4Irv8c ← vaporwave
**Virtual Counterculture and Resilient Genre** : Vaporwave has evolved into a virtual counterculture that resists easy categorization, and notably, it has "died" and "resurrected" multiple times, continually taking on new forms through its subgenres. "Un género que se ha convertido en una especie de contragultura virtual, que se reúsa a ser encasillado y que ha muerto y resucitado varias veces, tomando nuevas formas en subgeneros como el malls off, el dream-pang y el slash-wave." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Virtual Utopia Subgenre** : Virtual Utopia is identified as a particularly interesting branch of vaporwave. This subgenre is characterized by its soundtracking of the melancholy associated with a promised, but ultimately unfulfilled, future. It achieves this by recontextualizing old startup sounds, Messenger notifications, CD-ROM aesthetics, and Wii menus, transforming them into corporate muzak that captures the obsessive, and at times cheesy, essence of that bygone era. "En Lutopía Virtual, una de las ramas más interesantes al mi parecer del vaporwave, pone banda sonora a ese futuro. Recuperas a sonidos de arranque, las notificaciones de Messenger, la estética de Ceroom, los menos de la Wii y la traslada un muza corporativo que retrata la obsesiva, me llaveces cursi que fuese a época." ← Por Qué Tu Cerebro Nunca Olvidará Este Sonido | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=86tqZ33gn_4
