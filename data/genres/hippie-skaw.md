## curiosities
**Fourth Wave California Sound** : "Hippie-skaw," interchangeable with "Beatska," characterizes a laid-back, chilled, and slightly stoned sound from California that incorporates reggae, hip hop, and punk influences, part of the fourth wave of Ska.
