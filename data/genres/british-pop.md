# genre - British pop

## curiosities
**The Smiths' Contribution** : The Smiths contributed to British pop music with their short, sharp, and danceable singles, such as "This Charming Man." Their musical style had a profound impact, laying the groundwork for Britpop which would later dominate the British pop scene for nearly a decade.
