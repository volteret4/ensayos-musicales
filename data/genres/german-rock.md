# genre - German Rock

## curiosities
**Self-Reference and British Naming** : German musicians and fans initially referred to their distinct new sound as "German rock" or "Doitsch German Rock." However, the British bestowed the name "Krautrock," initially as a pejorative, which ultimately stuck despite German disapproval.
