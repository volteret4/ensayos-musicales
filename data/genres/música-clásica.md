**Sound Profile with Beats/Skullcandy** : Beats and Skullcandy headphones are not advised for classical music listeners. The headphones' unique and strong bass-heavy equalization, with its "subwoofer effect," significantly distorts the intricate and nuanced soundscapes of classical compositions. This sonic characteristic is detrimental to the faithful reproduction required for classical music, which emphasizes clarity, balance, and detail across all frequencies.
