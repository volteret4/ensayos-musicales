# genre - Ethereal Pop

## curiosities
**Predecessor Term for Dreampop (Mid-80s)** : Until the mid-1980s, the emerging genre that would become Dreampop was often referred to as "Ethereal Pop." This designation, though evocative, was considered less catchy or "roll off the tongue," leading to the adoption of "Dreampop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
