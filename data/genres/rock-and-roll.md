# genre - rock and roll

## curiosities
**Dominant Genre Emergence (Post-1956)** : December 4, 1956, is identified as a significant date marking the transition where rock and roll began to solidify as a dominant genre, distinct from rockabilly, gospel, and country music, which had previously been considered "the same kind of thing." Artists like Jerry Lee Lewis, who began their careers after this establishment, exemplify this shift.
