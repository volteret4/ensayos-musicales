# genre - Ragtime

## curiosities
**The "Malarial" Scandal of 1900** : In 1900, a music magazine condemned Ragtime as "virulent poison" and a "malarial epidemic" that threatened the sanity of the youth. This reflected the intense cultural and racist anxieties triggered by its "ragged" or syncopated rhythms. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Pi7JLwAeOI
