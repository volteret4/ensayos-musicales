# genre - Motorikbeat

## curiosities
**Pioneered by Neu!** : The Motorikbeat is a distinctive, straight 4/4 rhythm pioneered by the band Neu!, designed to convey a feeling of continuous movement, evocative of driving a motorcar on Germany's vast motorways. This rhythm became popular within the German underground and influenced artists internationally. "They were pioneers of something known as the MotorRickbeat. This is a straight, fore-for rhythm that conveys the feeling of movement, perhaps in a motorcar on the motorway." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePZDkqLM_gw
