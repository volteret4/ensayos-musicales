**Rise via Ringtones - Mid-2000s** : The polyphonic ringtone industry contributed significantly to the rise of reggaeton, a genre that was emerging in Panama and Puerto Rico during the mid-2000s. Traditional radio chains initially refused to program reggaeton songs, but the genre found widespread popularity through reality shows like Gran Hermano and, especially, through polyphonic ringtones, as its sound was perceived as more natural for this format than rock music.
