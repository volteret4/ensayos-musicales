# genre - Jump Blues

## curiosities
**Dissemination via Radio (WWII Era)** : Jump Blues emerged as part of a new "melting pot" of sounds, which gained widespread dissemination through radio during World War II. The challenges faced by Big Bands and the American Federation of Musicians strike created an opportunity for genres like Jump Blues to be heard by a wider audience for the first time. "Boogie-Wooogie, Jump Blues, Swing, Gospel, Spear Trolls, a whole new melting pot of sounds was created and disseminated through the radio as a result of the big band problems and the musician's strike." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk
