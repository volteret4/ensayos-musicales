# genre - Jump Blues

## curiosities
**Dissemination via Radio (WWII Era)** : Jump Blues emerged as part of a new "melting pot" of sounds, which gained widespread dissemination through radio during World War II. The challenges faced by Big Bands and the American Federation of Musicians strike created an opportunity for genres like Jump Blues to be heard by a wider audience for the first time. "Boogie-Wooogie, Jump Blues, Swing, Gospel, Spear Trolls, a whole new melting pot of sounds was created and disseminated through the radio as a result of the big band problems and the musician's strike." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7WjNR1ujhk ← jump-blues
**Foundation of Rock and Roll** : Jump blues is one of the primary Black Music genres from which rock and roll, and subsequently alt rock, substantially descended. "It's only right, given that rock and roll is descended substantially from Black Music, the blues, rhythm and blues, jump blues, jazz, gospel, and so many other genres." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1NNvxgx8uA ← jump-blues ← jump-blues
**The Kansas City 5's Primary Style** : The Kansas City 5, an early incarnation of The Animals, predominantly played jump blues, a musical area where the tastes of Eric Burden and John Steele converged, covering artists like Ray Charles, Louis Jordan, and Big Joe Turner.
