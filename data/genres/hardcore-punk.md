# genre - Hardcore Punk

## curiosities
**American Invention** : Described as an "American invention" that directly descended from the hard, fast, and loud punk of the 1970s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Core Ideology** : Characterized by being anti-establishment, anti-hippie, deliberately anti-commercial, and positioned against what its proponents viewed as the corrupt music industry. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Driver of Indie Label Creation** : Due to its anti-commercial nature and rejection by existing record labels, hardcore acts were compelled to establish their own labels, such as SST, Epitaph, and Alternative Tentacles. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Establishing Indie Music Universe** : The hardcore scene was crucially important in establishing the entire universe of indie music in the early 1980s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Growth with Reagan's Presidency** : The genre's popularity and widespread appeal significantly increased during Ronald Reagan's time in office, as young people reacted to his conservative political policies. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Incorporation of Pop Sensibilities** : Despite its intensity, bands like Black Flag, Bad Religion, and The Descendents began to incorporate melodic elements and "pop sensibilities." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Intensified Punk** : Aimed to amplify the existing punk sound, making the music even harder, faster, and louder. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Musical Characteristics** : Known for being short, sharp, and fast, tailored to deliver its message efficiently. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**North American Non-Mainstream Rock (Late 1980s)** : By the very end of the 1980s, hardcore punk was popular among North American fans interested in non-mainstream rock. These fans often spent time in small, dark clubs or record stores, seeking out music outside mainstream channels. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
**Origins (Late 1970s, California)** : Its roots are traced to the late 1970s in California, emerging as the fast, hard, radical, and angry extreme of the punk rock spectrum. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VOv5mT1dJd8
**Pioneering by Bad Brains** : Bad Brains is considered one of the pioneers of the entire hardcore punk scene and laid foundations for all American hardcore in the 1980s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Racial Harmony within Scene** : Due to Bad Brains' influence, rules of racial harmony were established in many areas of the hardcore scene. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PdCAjFW04Oc
**Rigid Dogma Challenge** : Had a "rigid, immovable hardcore dogma" that bands like Hüsker Dü broke by introducing nuanced concept albums and strong melodic elements. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
**Subgenres** : Includes bandage, death core, pornogrind, screamo, power violence, positive hardcore, nard core, and Nintendo core. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZV1lz-obQE
