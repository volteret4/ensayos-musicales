**Nebulous Umbrella Term of the 1990s** : "Alternative" emerged in the early 1990s as a "nebulous umbrella term" used to classify bands that were so unique they "defied description." It came to broadly signify "difference" and served as a category for artists like Tool, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Beastie Boys, and Rage Against the Machine, causing confusion due to its broadness. "Alternative came to mean difference. It was a basket in which to put bands that defied description." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t2uXHP9cBdM
