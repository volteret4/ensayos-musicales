# genre - Alternative

## curiosities
**Chinese Scene Observation (Early 2000s/2010s)** : Seymour Stein observed the developing Chinese punk and alternative scene at Beijing's D22 club, predicting that "something's happening here" and that it would "be big everywhere" globally. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjProJd_JrM
**Early Origins with The Velvet Underground** : The Velvet Underground, active in the 1960s, is recognized as potentially the first-ever alternative band. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
**Impact on InXS Album Sales (1992 onwards)** : The public's increasing fascination with alternative music, alongside grunge, was cited as a factor in the underperformance of InXS albums like "Welcome to Wherever You Are" (1992) and "Full Moon, Dirty Hearts" (1993). ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=23OgreyK66o
**Lollapalooza Focus (1990s)** : Lollapalooza became the touring music festival for the alternative generation for several years in the 1990s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Ramones as Pioneers (1990s)** : The alternative music scene of the 1990s recognized the Ramones as pioneers, tracing much of their own music back to them. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Epz5Sn151bk
**Red Hot Chili Peppers' Rise to Prominence (1991)** : The release of *Blood Sugar Sex Magic* in 1991 elevated the Red Hot Chili Peppers to one of the biggest groups of the alternative era. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TESrEzVHLCc
**Rock Comeback (1990s)** : Alternative music contributed to a rock comeback in 1990, continuing through to about 1996. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
**The Velvet Underground as Pioneers** : The Velvet Underground is considered by many to be the "first true alternative band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Warped Tour Initial Focus (1995)** : The first Warped Tour in 1995 was designed as a "baby brother" to Lollapalooza, featuring a collection of up-and-coming alternative bands. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
**Warped Tour Pivot (1996)** : The Warped Tour pivoted from a broad range alternative focus to punk and other music compatible with skate culture in 1996. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jfQaUV6Bq4
