# genre - Synth Pop

## curiosities
**Birth of a Subgenre (Early 1980s)** : A form of New Wave that emerged in the early 1980s, characterized by the explicit rejection of guitars in favor of synthesizers, sequencers, and drum machines.
**Experimental Approach to Technology** : Musicians often had little to no formal musical training, approaching synthesizers in a "punk" way by experimenting with sounds until they created novel, sometimes "never been heard anywhere in the universe before," sounds.
