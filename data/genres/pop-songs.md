# genre - Pop songs

## curiosities
**Simple Structures** : Pop songs are characterized by their inherently simple structures, which often involve basic melodic patterns like choruses based on two alternating notes punctuated by a few others. This simplicity means that two different composers could independently arrive at very similar melodies, leading to "unfortunate sonic coincidences" rather than deliberate plagiarism. "Pop songs by their very nature have very, very simple structures." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L09R2evjyMc
