# genre - Cuddlecore

## curiosities
**CUB's Self-Created Genre** : The Vancouver band CUB coined and named their own genre, "cuddlecore," to specifically describe their music. This genre was characterized by melodic compositions, the use of jangly guitars, and a pronounced childlike quality that the band intentionally cultivated. This aesthetic extended to their stage presence, which often included performing in pajamas and giving gifts to fans. "Their music was melodic, it had the jangly guitars, and it had such a childlike quality to it that the band created a new genre for themselves." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jhgZrwtYE2g
