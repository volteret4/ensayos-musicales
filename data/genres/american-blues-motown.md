# genre - American blues / Motown

## curiosities
**Influence on U2** : The sounds of American blues and Motown from Detroit significantly influenced U2, as they, like many Canadians, grew up hearing these genres. This exposure fostered a high regard for American innovation in music and contributed to the sonic palette U2 explored, particularly evident in albums like "The Joshua Tree." "we all, even here in Canada, we all grew up with American blues ringing in our ears, the sounds of Detroit from Motown and there’s always been a very high regard for American innovation." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L03Yk1SHRNM
