**Judgment Night Soundtrack as Catalyst (1993)** : The 1993 *Judgment Night* soundtrack, which radically paired rock bands with hip-hop outfits for 11 collaborations, was a major catalyst for the creation of rap rock. This groundbreaking project helped bridge the divide between rock and hip-hop worlds, bringing fans from both sides into new genres that would become very big later in the decade. "In retrospect, this was a major catalyst for the creation of rap rock and new metal, two genres that would be very, very big later in the decade." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIkjd1OjCXM
