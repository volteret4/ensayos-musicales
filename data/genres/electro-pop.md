# genre - Electro Pop

## curiosities
**Alternative Term for Technopop** : "Electro pop" is an alternative term, alongside synth pop, used to categorize the synthesizer-driven music that emerged during the later phase of the New Wave era, commonly known as Technopop. "And we're going to use the term Technopop, although some people prefer calling it synth pop or electro pop." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PJRCC6EwUY
**Dream as an Example** : The band Dream, in which Brian Cox played keyboards, is identified as an electro pop group from the early 1990s. "Back in the early 90s, Brian played keyboards in an electro pop band called Dream." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QfiRRtsZoYU
