# genre - Bluebeat

## curiosities
**Second Wave Ska in the UK** : Bluebeat was the name given to the slightly evolved form of Ska that arrived in the UK with Jamaican immigrants following World War II. It lived alongside R&B, Soul, and Reggae and was embraced by various British youth subcultures and early punks. "And back then, it became known as Bluebeat." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WX2GlpMNg_8
