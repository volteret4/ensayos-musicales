# genre - Drone bands

## curiosities
**Fuzz and Distortion as Core Elements** : Drone bands are characterized by their minimalist, sustained sounds, which can often be comprised of nothing but fuzz and distortion. Sunn O))) is cited as an example of a band that embodies this genre through its heavy reliance on these effects.
