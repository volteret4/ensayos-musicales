# genre - Electro

## curiosities
**Belgium Origin and Chemical Brothers Influence** : A new electro sound emerged in Belgium, which found a very important base in The Chemical Brothers' pioneering work in blending electronic and rock elements. This sound gave rise to artists like Soulwax and 2manydjs. **Dance Sound in New York and Chicago** : Electro was one of the various types of dance music that emerged from clubs in New York and Chicago, intriguing artists like New Order. It contributed to the evolving electronic sound that captivated the alternative dance scene. "And this is where they learned about post-disco, the growing house music movement, and various types of electro." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0FYq5MBdHw4 ← electro ← electro
**Foundation of Early Techno** : The electro sound was central to early techno productions, notably "Alleys of Your Mind" by Cybotron, which laid the groundwork for the genre. **Planet Rock as Foundational Track** : Afrika Bambaataa's "Planet Rock," created by combining Kraftwerk melodies with a Roland TR-808, is recognized as the foundational track of the electro genre.
