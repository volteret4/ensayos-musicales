# genre - Indy

## curiosities
**British Term for Independent Labels** : In Britain, the equivalent of "alternative labels" were referred to as "Indy," a shorthand for independent. This category encompassed labels such as Mute, Factory, Rough Trade, and Creation, all of which operated as standalone entities distinct from the major multinational record labels of the era. "The British had their alternative labels too, but they didn't call them that. They refer to their version as Indy as an independent." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
**Catch-All Term for New Rock Segment** : "Indy" has largely solidified its position as a catch-all umbrella term for a specific segment of "New Rock." The meaning of "Indy" has become relatively consistent across both the Atlantic, reflecting a shared understanding of its independent ethos. "And now Indy has pretty much taken its place as a catch-all umbrella term for a certain segment of the New Rock that comes out." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
**Distribution Challenges and Major Label Deals** : A significant hurdle for Indy labels is distribution, encompassing the manufacturing and delivery of CDs to stores, as well as the collection of sales revenue to compensate artists. Major labels are highly efficient in these areas. Consequently, many Indies "cut a deal with the major to take care of that into the business," subcontracting distribution while retaining their creative independence. "The biggest challenge an Indy label faces is distribution." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
**Equivalence with "Alternative"** : The term "Indy" eventually came to mean precisely the same thing as "alternative." Over time, "Indy" gained greater prominence across both sides of the Atlantic and has outlasted "alternative" as a preferred descriptor, being considered a "better word." "The bottom line is that alternative and Indy came to mean exactly the same thing. In fact, Indy as a term has gained strength on both sides of the Atlantic and has outlasted alternative." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
**Reassigned Meaning in Digital Era** : The word "Indy" has experienced a resurgence and taken on enhanced significance in the early 21st century, largely due to the rapid shifts in the music industry brought about by the digital era. It continues to signify independence, specifically referring to groups that record for labels not associated with the majors in terms of artist signings and the music they create. "Meanwhile, however, the word Indy has been reassigned and has taken on greater resonance than ever before. Thanks to the sudden shift in the music industry, the came with the advent of the digital era in the early 21st century." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
