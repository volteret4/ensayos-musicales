# genre - Country music

## curiosities
**Authenticity and Struggle** : For country music, the concept of authenticity is deeply tied to the "music of struggle," almost reveling in hardship, which contrasts with rock's focus on escaping struggle. This emphasis on lived experience often led to skepticism towards privileged artists or those perceived as insincere. "Some genres do not permit veering too far away from this elusive idea of authenticity. **Skepticism Towards Rock Singers** : Some country singers and their fans were concerned that rock singers entering the country genre were insincere in their appreciation or were making fun of country music. The differing approaches to music-making, such as a rock artist's desire to "play what you feel" versus a country session player's preference for specific instructions, fueled this suspicion. "They were worried that the rock singers were not sincere about their appreciation for country.
