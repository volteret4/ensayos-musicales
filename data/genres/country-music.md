# genre - Country Music

## curiosities
**Influence of Blues Slide Players** : The slide guitar techniques developed by early 20th-century Mississippi Delta blues players, who improvised with knives or beer bottle necks, influenced countless country musicians. "Like that, interned influence not only a billion country players, but rockers like Rye Kooter, Brian Jones and Keith Richards of the Rolling Stones and Duane Almond." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8GU8ZpSgY8
**Prevalence of Alcohol References** : A notable characteristic of country music is its frequent mention of alcohol; statistics show that one in five country music songs contains some reference to alcohol. "One in five country music songs have some reference to alcohol." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GO0K_N7Ys7I
**Rise of Steel Guitar** : The steel guitar, an evolution of the Hawaiian lap-style guitar where a metal slide (the "steel") is used, became a significant instrument and sound in country music. "Follow that forward a number of decades and you end up with the steel guitar, which became a big thing in country music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8GU8ZpSgY8
