# genre - Mainstream Rock

## curiosities
**Dominant Camp in 70s-90s Rock** : From the 1970s to the 1990s, Mainstream Rock represented one of two dominant "tribes" in rock music. Fans of these artists occupied most of the public's attention, garnered the majority of radio airplay, achieved the highest record sales, and sold the most concert tickets, establishing themselves at the forefront of the rock and roll zeitgeist. "On one side were the mainstream rockers, fans of the artists that occupied most of the public's attention. They also got most of the radio, airplay, most of the record sales, and sold most of the concert tickets." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2EerEOuP52I
**Historical Domain of Concept Albums** : Alongside progressive rock, mainstream rock bands historically dominated the domain of concept albums for many years. "Although such records were mostly the domain of Prague Rock and mainstream rock bands through the years, there's been an alt rock component to the concept album through the decades." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1KyNY3zv6uc
