# genre - Música Disco

## curiosities
**Dominant Sound Before "Música Blanca"** : In the early 1980s, disco music was a dominant genre in discotheques, often played for dancing and casual entertainment. The emergence of "música blanca" at Barraca, spearheaded by DJs Juan Santamaría and Carlos Simó, marked a conscious effort to differentiate from and move beyond these popular "black sounds."
