# genre - Progressive Rock

## curiosities
**Birth, Decline, and 2010s Comeback** : Progressive Rock was born in the late 1960s and early 1970s, flourishing as rock musicians became more virtuosic and recording technology advanced, allowing for complex sounds. It fell out of favor when punk and new wave movements criticized it as "too bloated, too inaccessible, too elitist, too complicated." However, it saw a significant comeback in the 2010s, evolving to include styles like Math Rock and Djent, and attracting bands from Biffy Clyro to The Mars Volta and Radiohead. "Prague rock was born in the late 1960s and early 70s, and was a big deal until punk came along and tried to wipe it out." "But in the 2010s, that style of rock saw a comeback." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HSvgRyGiOpE
**Characteristics (Early 1970s)** : The early 1970s was identified as the "age of Prague Rock," characterized by ultra-complex musical constructions created by "virtual musicians" in sophisticated recording studios. It fused rock and classical music, often being escapist, fantastical, bombastic, and "completely over the top." "The early 70s was the age of Prague Rock, ultra complex constructions made by virtual musicians and super sophisticated recording studios." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VYi3r0G-ne4
**Criticism and Indulgence (Early 1970s)** : Despite its complexity, Progressive Rock was not universally accepted and gained a reputation in some circles as "self-indulgent rubbish." This perception, alongside the "cancer of Mellow" and bad AM radio, contributed to a growing frustration that fueled the punk movement. "But Prague Rock was not for everyone in some circles and had a rep of being a well self-indulgent rubbish." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VYi3r0G-ne4
**Fantasy Landscape Artwork** : Progressive Rock bands like Yes frequently featured illustrations of fantasy landscapes, often painted by artists like Roger Dean, as a distinctive element of their album artwork. "Think back to pretty much any album from Yes, the Prague Rock Band." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pwebM3TfSdg
