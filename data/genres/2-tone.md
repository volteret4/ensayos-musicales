# genre - 2 Tone

## curiosities
**Subgenre of New Wave** : The "new Scott Seen" was mentioned as a subgenre that fit under the expansive New Wave umbrella, encompassing bands like The Specials and English Beat. This classification highlights the integration of the 2 Tone movement's unique blend of ska, punk, and reggae into the broader New Wave categorization. "There was Power Pop, which could include everyone from Elvis Costello to Tom Petty, a new Scott Seen, a specials English beat and so on, and all of the flavors of Technopop, all those groups who threw away their guitars and favor of synthesizers." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z8BTWo66SDI
