**Target of New York City Cabaret Law (1980s)** : During the 1980s, New York City's cabaret law was invoked by Rudy Giuliani to target rave events. This led to dance parties being pushed into unsafe, unlicensed underground warehouses and basements, as the law prohibited dancing in establishments serving alcohol without a special license.
