# genre - Rave

## curiosities
**Prominence in the UK** : Rave culture was highly popular and significant in the UK during the late 1980s, providing a receptive environment for bands whose sound incorporated elements of modern dance, like The Stone Roses. "It was the perfect combination for the times, especially since Rave and House were so big in the UK." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CVPPymc_qrk
**Target of New York City Cabaret Law (1980s)** : During the 1980s, New York City's cabaret law was invoked by Rudy Giuliani to target rave events. This led to dance parties being pushed into unsafe, unlicensed underground warehouses and basements, as the law prohibited dancing in establishments serving alcohol without a special license. "In the 1980s Rudy Giuliani used this law to crack down on hip-hop venues and raves, forcing dance parties into dangerous underground unlicensed warehouse and basements." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aSk1XQHNkd8
