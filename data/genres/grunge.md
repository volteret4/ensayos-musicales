# genre - Grunge

## curiosities
**"Alt Rock with Training Wheels"** : described as a genre that made great music and lured millions into the alternative nation with a "different yet familiar approach to traditional guitar rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**"Gold Rush" Phenomenon** : Its sudden popularity, particularly after Nirvana's Nevermind sales figures were revealed by SoundScan, sparked a "Grunge Gold Rush" where record labels sought out similar acts. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**"Well Ran Dry" in Late 90s** : the original grunge bands died off, and for a while in the late 90s, there was "nothing concrete to replace it," leading to "Dreck-like New Metal." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Alt-Rock's Addiction to 90s Music** : the popularity of grunge and other 90s music led to audiences continually demanding it, and many acts from that era remained popular, making it harder for new artists to break through in the 21st century. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Cultural Storm (1990-1996)** : Cited as an example of a "perfect cultural storm" in music, lasting about six years, from 1990 to 1996. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg
**Decline by 1994** : Began "slowly losing momentum" by 1994, with its "heaviness and bleakness" seeming "a little too much" after Kurt Cobain's death, and remaining bands sounding "more and more derivative." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Dock Martens as Grunge Footwear** : The Seattle shoe store managed by Susan Silver became known as the place where many grunge bands purchased their Doc Martens boots. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Dominant Era (Early 1990s)** : Defined the early 1990s era, featuring bands like Nirvana, Pearl Jam, and Soundgarden, and Alice in Chains. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Emergence in 1990s** : began after the 1990 recession, ushering in the intense alt rock seen early in that decade. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sOU54d8mr5k
**Ephemeral Sub-culture** : Described as a musical trend and sub-culture that has "gone," though its "echoes remain." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=evlGhfUe6QQ
**Evolution in STP's Sound** : Stone Temple Pilots' debut album *Core* was characterized as grunge, while their second album *Purple* was described as less grunge and more "straight ahead alt rock." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TDwagoyMvck
**Foundation of Commercial Alt-Rock Radio** : became the foundation of alt-rock radio's sound for many years, with stations playing grunge or grunge-ish (guitar-based, often drop-tuned) music as a "safer from a ratings point of view" option. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Impact on Alt-Rock Homogenization** : its popularity led alt-rock to coalesce around hard guitar rock, reducing the variety of sounds on alt-rock radio and leading to an "unstoppable march towards a homogeneity in sound." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Impact on InXS Album Sales (1992 onwards)** : The public's increasing fascination with grunge music, alongside all things alternative, was cited as a factor in the underperformance of InXS albums like "Welcome to Wherever You Are" (1992) and "Full Moon, Dirty Hearts" (1993). ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=23OgreyK66o
**Influence on Canadian Generation Xers (1990s)** : Canadian Generation Xers embraced grunge coming from the United States in the 1990s. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0pOgz3ZobE
**Invasion of the UK (Early 1990s)** : The genre "invaded the kingdom and was slowly conquering her Majesty's lands," for a time supplanting the "proud tradition of British rock and wall" with bands like Pearl Jam, Nirvana, and Soundgarden. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg
**Musical Style** : Characterized by music that was "slow and low," sometimes employing a quiet-loud dynamic. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pE7TaWSUa-o
**Premature Demise (1994)** : Kurt Cobain's suicide "prematurely killed any momentum Grunge had," creating a vacuum that "opened the door for everything that happened in British rock for the next several years." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vzyMSSlD7Yg
**Rise in Early 1990s** : became a dominant force in alternative music around 1991-1992, supplanting the mainstream. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqTFinLk3oU
**Rock Comeback (1990s)** : Grunge music contributed to a rock comeback in 1990, continuing through to about 1996. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nX8uAFDzDYM
**Shaped by Butch Vig** : The entire grunge revolution of the early 1990s was significantly shaped by producer and drummer Butch Vig. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gsSTJWN6cUo
**Susan Silver's Impact** : Susan Silver is recognized as one of the most important women of the Grunge era, managing several key bands in the Seattle scene starting in 1983. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PzyvXmDKFUc
**Transience (N/A)** : Though its echoes remain, grunge as a sub-culture is considered to have run its course. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RvzvcACkYqk
