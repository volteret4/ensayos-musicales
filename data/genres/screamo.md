# genre - Screamo

## curiosities
**Aggressive Music Progression** : Screamo was described as the further progression of punk, hardcore, and post-hardcore. Dallas Green specifically cited bands from the "deep head early first-screamo stuff" as influences for Alexis on Fire's sound, highlighting its role in shaping their distinct style. "You have punk, which turns into hardcore, which turns into post-hardcore, which becomes screamo and all that sort of weirdness, right?" ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEPT8ZVZ0so
**Subgenre of Third Wave Emo** : Screamo emerged as one of the fragmented variations and subgenres within the third wave of emo. "Emo-pop, screamo bands, just a couple of examples." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_am7ifh7Pk
