# genre - Hardcore

## curiosities
**Emergence from Punk (Late 1970s)** : Emerged from the fractured original punk rock sounds at the end of the 1970s, characterized as among the most aggressive forms of music ever invented.
**Nate Mendel's Understanding** : Nate Mendel's understanding of hardcore "clicked" with the energy, aggression, and weirdness of Black Flag's "Damaged" album. His formative musical years were almost exclusively dedicated to hardcore.
**Slam Dancing Origins** : The genre promoted "slam dancing," where participants would deliberately crash or slam into other dancers with enthusiasm, leading to the formation of circle pits and "walls of death."
