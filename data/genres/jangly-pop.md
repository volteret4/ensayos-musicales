# genre - Jangly pop

## curiosities
**The Smiths' Signature Style** : This genre describes The Smiths' distinct musical style, which had clear roots in 1950s and 60s music. Their arrangements were simple and stark, avoiding big solos or layers of overdubs, creating a refreshing sound in the 1980s landscape.
