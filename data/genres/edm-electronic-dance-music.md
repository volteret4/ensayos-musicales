**Emergence as a Major Genre** : In the 2010s, EDM emerged as a fourth genre competing for attention alongside rock, pop, and hip hop, characterized by superstar DJs and massive public events. For many, electronic dance music was considered "the new rock and roll," with "people with laptops" replacing guitarists as the big heroes.
