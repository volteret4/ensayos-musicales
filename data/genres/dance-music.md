# genre - Dance Music

## curiosities
**Hardwired for Dance** : Scientists in Finland utilized FMRI machines to observe brain activity in volunteers listening to happy dance music. This research revealed distinct connections between the auditory cortex and the motor cortex, leading to the conclusion that humans are inherently hardwired to dance to certain types of music. "Happy dance music showed clear connections between the auditory cortex and the motor cortex. So they concluded that we are, for some reason, hardwired to dance to certain music." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mTbagQE7akQ
**Integration with Hip Hop by MARRS** : The track "Pump Up the Volume" by MARRS was highly influential in the late 1980s for its role in mashing up hip hop and dance music, leveraging the new art form of sampling to create a number one hit. ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLMkvJAaUY
