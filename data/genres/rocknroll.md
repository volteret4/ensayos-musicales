# genre - Rock'n'Roll

## curiosities
**Birth of Rock'n'Roll (1950s)** : Rock'n'Roll emerged in the 1950s from a confluence of musical developments, including the evolution of rhythm and blues from jazz, and its fusion with country, western, hillbilly, and traditional blues music. This new genre was a direct descendant of the blues, its genesis ultimately linked to the Great Migration and the abolition of slavery.
