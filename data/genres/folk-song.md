## curiosities
**Original Form of "The Ghost of Tom Joad"** : Bruce Springsteen's 1995 song "The Ghost of Tom Joad" was originally conceived and performed as a quiet folk song, telling the story of the main character from John Steinbeck's novel "The Grapes of Wrath."
