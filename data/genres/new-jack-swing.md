# genre - New Jack Swing

## curiosities
**New Jack City Talent Show Reference** : A high school talent show where Pezz (then To Each His Own) and Dragonflower performed was named "New Jack City talent show," referencing the movie "New Jack City," which reflected the "New Jack Swing" cultural trend of the time.
