# genre - Hot Jazz

## curiosities
**Blamed for Immorality and Crime (1920s)** : In the 1920s, hot jazz was blamed for encouraging illicit sex and turning people onto illegal alcohol, particularly during the Prohibition era. This was an early instance of music being scapegoated for social problems, a pattern that would recur with rhythm and blues and rock and roll.
