# genre - Classic rock

## curiosities
**Definition and Lineage** : "Classic rock" is a term used to describe bands such as Van Halen, Aerosmith, The Rolling Stones, and Led Zeppelin. Their musical traditions are deeply rooted in the blues, with spiritual ancestors identified as figures like Robert Johnson and Howlin' Wolf, distinguishing them from genres grounded in punk. "At the other end of the spectrum, Van Halen and Eros Smith and the Rolling Stones and Led Zeppelin are now described as classic rock. Their sonic traditions are grounded in the blues, not punk." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ft8nruhWCas
**Mainstream Rejection (Late 1980s)** : By the late 1980s, classic rock was among the mainstream genres that fans of alternative and non-mainstream music deliberately avoided. This audience sought out unique sounds in indie clubs and record stores, contrasting with the music widely available on Top 40 radio. "You want nothing to do with hair metal or classic rock or new kids on the block or anything on top 40 radio." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OiqdZfxdBP0
