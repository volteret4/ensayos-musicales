# genre - Classic Rock

## curiosities
**Defined by Older Acts in Early 1990s** : The term "classic rock" began to be defined in the early 1990s by established acts like the Rolling Stones, Pink Floyd, and Elton John, as hair metal was losing its appeal. This categorization emerged as a way to describe older, enduring rock artists.
