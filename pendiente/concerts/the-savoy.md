# concert - The Savoy

## curiosities
**Managed by Mo Gale** : The Savoy Club was run by Mo Gale, who was also a prominent manager for numerous significant Black acts in New York during the late 1930s and early 1940s. "as well as running the Savoy Club, which we talked about in several of the earliest episodes of the podcast." ← Episode 50： ＂Honky Tonk＂, by Bill Doggett | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H2rYnKmeZF0
**Residency for Lucky Millinder Orchestra** : The Lucky Millinder Orchestra, specifically the incarnation initially formed by Bill Doggett, secured a residency at The Savoy after Chick Webb's band ceased playing there. "This incarnation of the Lucky Melinda Orchestra, the one that was put together by Doggert before Melinda took the band over, is the one that got a residency at the Savoy, after Chick Webb's band stopped playing there." ← Episode 50： ＂Honky Tonk＂, by Bill Doggett | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H2rYnKmeZF0
