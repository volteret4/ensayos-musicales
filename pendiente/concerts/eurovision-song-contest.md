## curiosities
**Olivia Newton-John's 1974 Participation** : In 1974, Olivia Newton-John represented Britain in the international song competition, but she was reportedly forced to sing "Boom Bang" while wearing a "very matronly light blue dress" that resembled a nightgown. She later commented that "no dress could have saved that horrific song" and ultimately lost to Abba's "Waterloo."
