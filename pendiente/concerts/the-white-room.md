# concert - The White Room

## curiosities
**Pioneering Rave Rest Area** : "The White Room" emerged in 1989 at Club Heaven in London as the first designated refuge for people to rest away from the intense ecstasy of the dance floors during rave parties. It was founded by DJs Jimmy Cauty and Alex Paterson of The Orb, who mixed soft ambient music, Kraut Rock, and "Dap Hamaycano" to create an atmosphere of harmony and tranquility.
