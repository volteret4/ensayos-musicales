# concert - CMA Awards (1974)

## curiosities
**"If You Love Me, Let Me Know" Nomination** : Olivia Newton-John's song "If You Love Me, Let Me Know" was nominated for Single of the Year at the 1974 CMAs. **Olivia Newton-John's Controversial Win** : Olivia Newton-John's win of Female Vocalist of the Year at the 1974 CMAs, alongside her Grammy for "Let Me Be There," caused significant controversy among country purists and led to George Jones and Tammy Wynette leaving the Country Music Association in protest.
