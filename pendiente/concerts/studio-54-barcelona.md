# concert - Studio 54 Barcelona

## curiosities
**Source for First Spanish Mix Record** : The iconic discotheque Studio 54 in Barcelona was the setting whose most popular tracks were compiled into "Studio 54 Connection," the first mix record ever published in Spain. Raúl Orellana, the club's resident DJ, was responsible for mixing the album.
