## curiosities
**Studio Reunion Album (1985)** : Four years after "The Survivors" concert, in 1985, Carl Perkins, Johnny Cash, and Jerry Lee Lewis reunited for a studio album at Sun Studios. Roy Orbison filled in for Elvis Presley. The album is acknowledged as having its moments but is not considered a discography highlight for any of the participating artists.
