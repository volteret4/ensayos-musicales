# concert - Ozark Jubilee

## curiosities
**Carl Perkins' First TV Appearance** : Carl Perkins made his inaugural television appearance on the "Ozark Jubilee," where he performed his hit song "Blue Suede Shoes." This performance remarkably coincided with Elvis Presley's cover of the same song being broadcast on the "Stage Show" on a competing network.
