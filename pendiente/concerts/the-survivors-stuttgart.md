# concert - The Survivors (Stuttgart)

## curiosities
**First Million Dollar Quartet Reunion (April 23, 1981)** : "The Survivors" was the first reunion performance in the spirit of the original Million Dollar Quartet session, taking place on April 23, 1981, in Stuttgart. It featured Carl Perkins, Jerry Lee Lewis, and Johnny Cash on stage for an impromptu performance, notably without Elvis Presley who had passed away. "The first, and the one that was more in the spirit of the original sessions, was a live performance released as The Survivors, when on April 23, 1981, Perkins and Lewis joined Cash on stage in Stuttgart for an infomptu performance." ← Episode 51： ＂Matchbox＂ by Carl Perkins | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Nrjr0AGd3U
**Musical Content of Reunion** : During this reunion, the artists performed their biggest hits, but a substantial portion of the show was dedicated to old gospel and country songs they all knew. They traded off vocals on songs like "Al Flair Way," "I Saw The Light," "Will The Circle Be Unbroken," and "Peace In The Valley" (dedicated to Elvis).
