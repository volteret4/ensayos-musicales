# concert - Australian Tour (October 1957)

## curiosities
**Gene Vincent's Visa Delays** : Gene Vincent and his band, the Blue Caps, were unfortunately held up in Honolulu due to visa issues, causing them to miss the initial two shows of Little Richard's Australian tour. Local Australian group Johnny O'Keefe and the DJs stepped in to perform in their absence. "Vincent actually had to miss the first couple of shows, on the tour, as he and the blue cap got held up in Honolulu, apparently due to visa issues, and couldn't continue on to Australia with the rest of the tour until that was sorted out." ← Episode 54： Keep A Knockin | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oA4Y_K9E5Y4
**Little Richard's Profound Conversion Experience** : Little Richard's flight to Australia was unusually challenging, during which he saw the plane's engine glowing red and believed it was on fire and held up by angels. This experience, compounded by seeing Russia's Sputnik satellite pass low over Australia like a "fireball" days later, deeply convinced him it was a divine sign to abandon his sinful life and commit to religion. "The flight to Australia was longer and more difficult than any Richard had experienced before, and at one point he looked out of the window and saw the engine's glowing red." ← Episode 54： Keep A Knockin | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oA4Y_K9E5Y4
