# concert - Houston

## curiosities
**Segregation and Dance Prohibition Incident (Undated)** : At a Fats Domino concert in Houston, white audience members from the balcony moved to join black dancers on the floor. A black policeman, attempting to avert a race-mixing riot, ordered everyone to sit down and stop dancing. However, a white police officer overruled him, declaring that only white people were permitted to dance. Domino refused to continue playing if black people were not also allowed to dance, and while the show avoided violence, a dozen people were arrested for threatening the police.
