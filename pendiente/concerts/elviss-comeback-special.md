# concert - Elvis's comeback special

## curiosities
**"Trouble" as Opening Song (1968)** : Elvis Presley chose the song "Trouble" as the very first song performed during his renowned 1968 "comeback special." This decision highlighted his personal esteem for the track, despite one of its writers, Jerry Leiber, having initially felt it was not "right" for Elvis's vocal style.
