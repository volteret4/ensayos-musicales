# concert - Memorial Colosseum (Charlotte, NC)

## curiosities
**Banning of Outdoor Concerts** : Following a loud outdoor concert by Ted Nugent and his band at the Memorial Colosseum, the Charlotte City Council responded by banning all subsequent outdoor concerts, viewing Nugent and his music as a menace. "Nugent was loud to the point that after he and his band played an outdoor concert at the Memorial Colosseum in Charlotte, North Carolina, the city council banned outdoor concerts." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 58 - WKRP in Cincinnati | https://www.ftr70.com
