# concert - 1958 Chuck Berry and Jerry Lee Lewis Tour

## curiosities
**Boston Police Intervention and Rock and Roll Ban** : During the Boston leg of the 1958 tour, police became upset with the dancing audience during Jerry Lee Lewis's set and forced Alan Fried to make them sit down. In a later show headlined by Chuck Berry, police refused to dim the lights, prompting Fried to publicly challenge them. This led to police falsely attributing numerous crimes to the concert and Boston's decision to ban rock and roll concerts altogether, resulting in the cancellation of many remaining tour dates. "The early show, which had been headline by Lewis, had had the audience dancing, and the police were not at all impressed with this.
