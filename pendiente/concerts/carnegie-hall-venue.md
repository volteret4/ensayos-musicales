# concert - Carnegie Hall (Venue)

## curiosities
**Benny Goodman's Integrated Performance (1938)** : At the beginning of 1938, John Hammond promoted a concert by the Benny Goodman band at Carnegie Hall. This event was groundbreaking as it was the first time an integrated band had played at the venue and the first time popular music was treated with the same seriousness as classical music. **Spirituals to Swing Venue (1938, 1939)** : Carnegie Hall also served as the venue for John Hammond's "Spirituals to Swing" concerts in 1938 and 1939, pivotal events that aimed to showcase a history of black music to an integrated audience and played a crucial role in the revival of Boogie Woogie.
