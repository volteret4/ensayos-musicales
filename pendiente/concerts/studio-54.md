# concert - Studio 54

## curiosities
**Site of Mick Jagger's Apology to Liza Minnelli** : In 1979, Mick Jagger publicly apologized to Liza Minnelli at Studio 54. This apology was for the controversial inclusion of Liza's mother's image on the original "Some Girls" album cover, which had caused significant offense. **Symbol of 1970s Mainstream Music Scene** : Studio 54 in New York City was a prominent symbol of the mainstream music scene in the mid-1970s, particularly the disco era. Record executives were "too busy going to big stadium shows and getting down at Studio 54" to pay attention to the emerging punk scene.
