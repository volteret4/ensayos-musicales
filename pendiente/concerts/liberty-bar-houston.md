# concert - Liberty Bar (Houston)

## curiosities
**Meeting of Emmylou Harris and Linda Ronstadt (1973)** : Emmylou Harris and Linda Ronstadt met at this honky-tonk in Houston in 1973. Linda described it as a "loud, rowdy place" that would become "really quiet when Emileu sang," leading to their friendship and collaboration.
