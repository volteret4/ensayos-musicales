## curiosities
**Platform for Reggaeton Popularity** : Gran Hermano (Big Brother) reality show played a significant role in popularizing reggaeton music. Alongside polyphonic ringtones, it provided an alternative platform for songs like Lorna's "Papichulo" and Daddy Yankee's "Gasolina," which traditional radio stations initially refused to program.
