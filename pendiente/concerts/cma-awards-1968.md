## curiosities
**Genie C. Riley's Dress Controversy** : At the 1968 Country Music Association Awards, Genie C. Riley attempted to wear a floor-length gown, but her producer, Shelby Singleton, intervened by calling the tailor to have the dress shortened to mid-thigh, further demonstrating his control over her image.
