# concert - Bing Crosby Christmas Special

## curiosities
**Bowie's Appearance and "Little Drummer Boy" Refusal (Late 1977)** : In late 1977, David Bowie appeared on this Christmas special, recorded in London for broadcast in the US and UK. Ostensibly there to promote his album "Heroes" (which included a video), Bowie famously balked when producers proposed a holiday duet with Bing Crosby of "The Little Drummer Boy," thinking the song "unworthy."
