# concert - Club a Go-Go

## curiosities
**Newcastle Music Hub** : The Club a Go-Go served as a significant local venue in Newcastle, where The Alan Price Rhythm and Blues Combo (later The Animals) were the resident band. It regularly hosted visiting London jazzmen and touring American blues players. **Sonny Boy Williamson II Performance** : The Animals performed backing the American blues musician Sonny Boy Williamson II at the Club a Go-Go, a performance that highlighted their electric organ sound and garnered admiration from Graham Bond.
