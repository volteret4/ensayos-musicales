# concert - Carnegie Hall (1971)

## curiosities
**Chicago's Political Performance** : During their 1971 performance at Carnegie Hall, Chicago played "A Song for Richard and His Friends," a direct plea for then-President Richard Nixon to "just go away," three years before his actual resignation. "When they played Carnegie Hall in 1971, they played a song for Richard and his friends. Yes, that Richard.
