# concert - 1937 Savoy Cutting Contest

## curiosities
**Epic Battle of Big Bands** : This major event in 1937 at the Savoy Ballroom pitted Chick Webb's Savoy Orchestra against Benny Goodman's band. The ballroom, equipped with two bandstands for continuous music, hosted 4,000 dancers, with another 5,000 people trying to hear outside despite a police cordon. Chick Webb's band decisively won.
