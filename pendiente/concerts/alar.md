# concert - Alar

## curiosities
**Recording Location for Norah Jones' Second Album** : Norah Jones recorded her second album upstate at Alar with producer Arif Mardin and her band. Despite Arif Mardin's usual strict 6 PM departure rule, he stayed overnight and participated in late-night martini drinking sessions, humorously coming down in his pajamas for water.
