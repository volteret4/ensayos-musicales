# concert - The Sanctuary

## curiosities
**Converted Church Nightclub** : The Sanctuary was a church that had been converted into a nightclub, and it was featured in a scene from the 1971 Jane Fonda movie "Klute," showing DJ Francis Grasso at work. "you saw a scene where Francis, DJ Francis is in the sanctuary, a church that was converted into a nightclub." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 1 - Disco Doesn't Suck (Encore) | https://www.ftr70.com/blog/tag/70s+podcast
**Inclusive Sanctuary for Queer Community** : The Sanctuary was one of the members-only sanctuaries, alongside The Loft and Paradise Garage, that welcomed and legitimized the presence of the queer community, providing a racially inclusive space open to men and women. "places like the loft, the sanctuary, paradise garage that welcomed even legitimized the presence of the queer community." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 1 - Disco Doesn't Suck (Encore) | https://www.ftr70.com/blog/tag/70s+podcast
