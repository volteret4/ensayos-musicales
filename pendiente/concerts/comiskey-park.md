# concert - Comiskey Park

## curiosities
**Disco Demolition Night (1979)** : This event was provoked by a Chicago DJ who encouraged rock fans to bring disco records to Comiskey Park to be blown up between games of a double-header, resulting in a riot where the outfield was set on fire. An African American usher, Vince Lawrence, reported that non-disco records by black artists like Stevie Wonder and Marvin Gaye were also destroyed, and white protestors yelled "disco sucks" at him. Rich Wortham, a white Chicago White Sox pitcher, noted this event likely wouldn't have occurred on "country and western night," highlighting racism as a factor.
