# concert - Dick Clark's Caravan of Stars

## curiosities
**The Ronettes' Tour with Substitute** : Following the success of "Be My Baby," The Ronettes were invited to join Dick Clark's Caravan of Stars tour. However, Phil Spector insisted Ronnie remain in California to record a follow-up, so her cousin Elaine filled in for the first couple of weeks of the tour.
