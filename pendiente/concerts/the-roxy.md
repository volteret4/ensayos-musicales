# concert - The Roxy

## curiosities
**Competition for The Troubadour** : The Roxy was opened by Lou Adler and David Geffen in 1973, located not far from The Troubadour. Its establishment created significant competition that contributed to the decline of The Troubadour's prominence in the music scene.
