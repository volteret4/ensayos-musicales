# concert - Tupelo Mississippi Fair

## curiosities
**Elvis's Performance with New Musicians (1958)** : Elvis Presley performed at the fair in his old hometown of Tupelo, Mississippi. This particular engagement featured his regular band members DJ Fontana and The Jordanaires, but also included temporary new musicians: Hank Garland on guitar and Chuck Wigginson on bass, filling in after Scotty Moore and Bill Black's initial resignation.
