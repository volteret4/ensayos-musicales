## curiosities
**Commercial Success and Icon Status** : Many Thunderdome events were recorded and commercialized, and compilation CDs of their parties were widely distributed across Europe. The Thunderdome logo eventually achieved recognition comparable to any major 1990s pop icon. **Contemporary Revival Events (2017, 2022-Present)** : After several years of inactivity, Thunderdome returned in 2017 to celebrate its 25th anniversary. In 2022, it celebrated its "teneta aniversario Nutrek" with all tickets sold out. Due to growing demand, there is now an annual Thunderdome event, demonstrating a strong revival. **Foundational Gabber and Hardcore Macro-Party (1992)** : Thunderdome originated in 1992 as a series of large-scale parties held in pavilions and stadiums across the Netherlands. It became a focal point for the most extreme Gabber and hardcore sounds in the country, defining the early scene.
