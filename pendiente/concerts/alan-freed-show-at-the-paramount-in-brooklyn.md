## curiosities
**Carole King's Musical Awakening (Age 13)** : At 13, Carole King attended an Alan Freed show at the Paramount in Brooklyn with Jules Zwick. She witnessed R&B stars like LaVern Baker, B.B. King, Mickey Baker, and The Moonglows, which introduced her to a world of music beyond "white bread pop." This experience, particularly going backstage, solidified her desire to be a part of the music industry.
