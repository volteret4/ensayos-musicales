# concert - World Series (1968)

## curiosities
**Jose Feliciano's Controversial Star-Spangled Banner** : At Game 5 of the 1968 World Series in Tiger Stadium, Detroit, Jose Feliciano performed a highly unconventional version of the Star-Spangled Banner. This rendition sparked widespread controversy, with many finding it "not traditional enough" and even "too black" according to Carlos Santana, leading to calls for legal regulation of anthem performances and accusations of communism against Feliciano.
