# concert - Manchester 1964 TV special

## curiosities
**Featured Artists** : The special included performances by Sister Rosetta Tharp, Muddy Waters, Otis Spann, Reverend Gary Davis, Sonny Terry, and Varnie McGee, performing outdoors for an audience of young British white people. **Historic Blues Program** : Filmed in 1964 at the abandoned World War Hymn Road railway station in Manchester for a TV program, this event is considered one of the greatest blues TV programs of all time. It featured a gathering of black American music greats. "That piece of track, which is now part of the Fallafield cycle loop, was the place where some of the greats of black American music were filmed for what may have been the greatest blues TV programme of all time." ← Episode 5： ＂This Train＂ by Sister Rosetta Tharpe | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=II-hDnwUgT8
