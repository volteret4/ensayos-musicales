## curiosities
**Central Setting in Saturday Night Fever** : This disco club served as the pivotal setting for much of the drama in *Saturday Night Fever*. For characters like Tony Manero, it represented an essential escape from the realities of working-class life in the 1970s, offering a space where dancing allowed them to feel part of a "ruling class."
