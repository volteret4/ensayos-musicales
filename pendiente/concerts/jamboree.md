# concert - Jamboree

## curiosities
**Exploitation Film (1957)** : "Jamboree" was one of many exploitation films produced in the late 1950s, starring popular DJs like Dick Clark (who starred in this one, rather than Alan Freed who appeared in most others). These films featured interchangeable, simplistic plots, often about teenagers putting on a dance. Their primary appeal was not the plot but the live performances by rock and roll musicians, including Jerry Lee Lewis, Karl Perkins, Fat Stumino, Slim Whitman, and Count Basie with Joe Williams. The film also contains the only known footage of Lewis Lyman and the Teen Courts.
