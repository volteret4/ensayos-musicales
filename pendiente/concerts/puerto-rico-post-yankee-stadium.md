# concert - Puerto Rico (post-Yankee Stadium)

## curiosities
**Completion of Yankee Stadium Film and Album** : The Fania All-Stars performed in Puerto Rico the year after the disastrous Yankee Stadium concert. This live performance was utilized to complete the album and film that had been initiated at the Yankee Stadium event, salvaging the recording project. "Al año siguiente, la fanía oflestar se actuó en Puerto Rico, y con ese directo completaron el disco y la película que había iniciado en el Yankee Stadium." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
