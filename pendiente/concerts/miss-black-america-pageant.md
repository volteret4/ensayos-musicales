# concert - Miss Black America pageant

## curiosities
**Jackson 5's First TV Appearance (September 1969)** : The Jackson 5 made their very first television appearance on the Miss Black America pageant in September 1969. This debut predated their more widely seen performances on the Ed Sullivan Show and American Bandstand.
