# concert - Blue Note Anniversary

## curiosities
**All-Star Performance** : The Blue Note anniversary concert featured an all-star lineup including Norah Jones, Wayne Shorter, Brian Blade, John Patitucci, and Jason Moran (who organized the event). Norah described the experience of watching the performance from the side of the stage as "experiencing some kind of cosmic truth" due to the hyper-real musicality.
