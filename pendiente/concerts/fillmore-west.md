## curiosities
**B.B. King's Crossover Performance (1967)** : B.B. King's 1967 performance at the Fillmore West in San Francisco was a pivotal event that marked his transition from a declining blues career to a successful crossover artist. Playing for a largely white, "hippie" audience for the first time, he received a standing ovation before he even began to play.
