# concert - Buda Bar

## curiosities
**Contributor to Chill Out Compilation Fever** : The compilations released by Buda Bar in Paris, alongside the *Café del Mar* series by José Padilla, played a crucial role in initiating a global "fiebre" (fever) for Chill Out compilations. These collections contributed to saturating the market with Chill Out CDs. "Este álbum, junto a las recopelaciones del Buda Bar de París, dieron inicio a una fiebre global por los recopelatorios de Chill Out." ← Chill Out： La pausa que necesitaba la electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VPOAA3ZaA9s
