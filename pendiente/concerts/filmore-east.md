# concert - Filmore East

## curiosities
**Bill Graham's Post-Troubadour Offer** : After Elton John's American debut at The Troubadour, Bill Graham, the owner of the Filmore East in New York, offered John $5,000 to perform there. This sum was an unprecedented amount for a new artist, highlighting the immense impact of John's Troubadour performance.
