## curiosities
**Oasis Performance (1996)** : Oasis was invited to perform at MTV Amplac in 1996, an honor extended to "all the great important groups of the 90s." The concert became a pivotal moment in the band's history when Liam Gallagher refused to participate, leading Noel Gallagher to perform all vocals, marking an early sign of the brothers' strained relationship.
