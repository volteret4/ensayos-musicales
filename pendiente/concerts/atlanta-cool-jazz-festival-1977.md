# concert - Atlanta Cool Jazz Festival (1977)

## curiosities
**Exclusion of Jazz Artists** : In 1977, the Atlanta Cool Jazz Festival notably featured no jazz artists, instead showcasing soul music. The promoter explained this decision by stating that "the economics of jazz are limited," and a genuine jazz festival would likely play to a "mostly empty stadium."
