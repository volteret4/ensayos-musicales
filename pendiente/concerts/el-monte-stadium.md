# concert - El Monte Stadium

## curiosities
**Art Lebo's Regular Shows** : El Monte Stadium hosted regular shows organized by Art Lebo. Kip Tyler and The Flips served as the house band for these events, performing themselves and backing other musicians like Richie Valens. The stadium's shows were later immortalized by The Penguins in their song "Memories of El Monte."
