# concert - Boy Meets Girls

## curiosities
**British TV Showcase for Rock and Roll** : "Boy Meets Girls" was one of the inaugural rock and roll TV shows produced by Jack Good for ITV. It was on this program that Gene Vincent famously adopted his black leather, hunched-over stage persona, which, in contrast to other acts like Cliff Richard or Adam Faith, captivated British teenagers and defined the visual image of a rock and roll star. "Vincent's appearances on Boy Meets Girls, dressed in black leather, hunched over, in pain because of his leg, defined for British teenagers of the 1950s what a rock and roll it was meant to look like." ← Episode 41： ＂Be-Bop-A-Lula＂ by Gene Vincent and the Bluecaps | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tYBQ5F7O3G0
