**Grimbey's Expulsion** : After signing with Reprise Records, a major label, Grimbey was expelled from 1924 Gilman Street. This expulsion underscored the strict anti-corporate ethos of the punk club and reflected the "sell-out" sentiment directed at the band by parts of the punk community.
