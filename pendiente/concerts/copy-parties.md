# concert - Copy Parties

## curiosities
**Secret Gatherings for Digital Exchange** : Copy Parties were secret gatherings organized within the cracker and demoscene culture, predominantly in Northern Europe. Hundreds of young people would travel with their monitors to these events to copy games, share tricks, and release code, effectively functioning as "data raves." "Incluso organizaban fiestas secretas, las llamadas Copi Partis. Cientos de jóvenes del norte de Europa viajaban cosas moritores para copiar juegos, compartir trucos y liberar coal. Eran literalmente, riz de datos." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
