## curiosities
**Early British Rock and Roll TV Show** : "Oh Boy" was one of the first two rock and roll television shows produced by Jack Good for ITV. Good, a pioneering TV producer, moved to ITV after his work on the BBC's "6-5 Special" to create these new music programs.
