# concert - Star Club

## curiosities
**"Needles and Pins" Learned There** : The Searchers learned the song "Needles and Pins" in Hamburg from Cliff Bennett and the Rebel Rouses, who were covering it live. "The song chosen for that third single was one they learned in Hamburg, from Cliff Bennett and the Rebel Rouses." ← Episode 113： ＂Needles and Pins＂ by The Searchers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=izC9ZBI0UHM
**The Searchers' Hamburg Performances (1962)** : Like The Beatles, The Searchers played at the Star Club in Hamburg in 1962. Recordings of their performances there, including a live version of Brenda Lee's "Sweet Nothings," were later released as singles. "Like the Beatles, the searchers went off to play at the Star Club in Hamburg in 1962, recordings were made of their performances there, and their live version of Brenda Lees Sweet Nothings later got released as a single after they became successful." ← Episode 113： ＂Needles and Pins＂ by The Searchers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=izC9ZBI0UHM
