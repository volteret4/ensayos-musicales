# concert - Dolly Parton's solo TV show (1976)

## curiosities
**Country and Pop Mix** : Dolly Parton launched her first solo TV show in 1976, which, like the music industry itself, featured a mix of country and pop. A notable episode showcased performances by Emmylou Harris and Linda Ronstadt.
