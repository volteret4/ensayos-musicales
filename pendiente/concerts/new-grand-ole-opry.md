# concert - New Grand Ole Opry

## curiosities
**"Nashville" Film Setting (1975)** : In Robert Altman's 1975 film "Nashville," Timothy Brown's character, Tommy Brown, performs at the "new Grand Ole Opry," which had reportedly only been open for about a year at the time the film was set.
