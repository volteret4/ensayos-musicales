# concert - London Planetarium

## curiosities
**Venue for The Dark Side of the Moon's Presentation** : The London Planetarium was chosen as the site for the most important official presentation of Pink Floyd's album, *The Dark Side of the Moon*. This location was selected because the album's cover art and overall thematic content aligned well with the venue. Despite the significance of the location, the band themselves boycotted the event due to their displeasure with the album being released prematurely.
