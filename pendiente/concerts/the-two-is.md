# concert - The Two I's

## curiosities
**Famous Coffee Bar Venue** : This establishment was a celebrated coffee bar where The Vipers Skiffle Group regularly performed. It also served as the discovery location for prominent artists like Tommy Steele and Terry Dean. John Foster successfully secured a two-week residency for Cliff Richard and The Drifters at this venue. "The famous coffee bar where the Viper's used to play, and where both Tommy Steel and Terry Dean had been discovered, and he was pretty sure he could get them a gig there." ← Episode 70： ＂Move It＂ by Cliff Richard and the Drifters | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uoEWP94f6S4
