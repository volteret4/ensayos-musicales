# concert - Club Heaven

## curiosities
**Venue for Early Chill Out Spaces** : Club Heaven in London was the site where "The White Room," the first designated chill-out space, emerged in 1989. This club provided the setting for pioneering DJs Jimmy Cauty and Alex Paterson to create an environment of harmony and tranquility for rave attendees to rest.
