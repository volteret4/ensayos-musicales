# concert - Roanoke, Virginia

## curiosities
**Segregation and Riot (May 1956)** : A Fats Domino concert in Roanoke in May 1956 featured strict racial segregation, with white attendees in the balcony and black attendees on the main floor. When white teenagers descended to dance with black people, a white individual in the balcony initiated a riot by throwing a whiskey bottle, which then escalated into street violence. The New York Times controversially blamed the black audience, leading the venue owner to ban mixed-race audiences thereafter.
