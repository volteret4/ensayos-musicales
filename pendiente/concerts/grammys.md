## curiosities
**Impact on Norah Jones' Career and Life** : The Grammys had an "insane" impact on Norah Jones' career, leading to a huge post-award tour and overwhelming media attention. This included her photo being published on the New York Post cover next to Saddam Hussein's, and details about her private life (like apartment rent) being exposed, making her feel unable to return home. "Yeah, but then the Grammys just like was insane. Yeah. So and the next day my picture was on the cover of the New York Post, Nexus Saddam Hussein's picture.
