# concert - Mother Ship Tour

## curiosities
**Legendary Stage Production** : The Mother Ship Tour of 1976, propelled by the imagery and strong sales of the *Mother Ship Connection* album, was a legendary P-Funk event. It featured a full-scale spaceship on stage and a massive band of expert funk players, boasting a stage show even more extravagant than Earth Wind & Fire's, attracting a diverse audience who might not typically attend a funk concert.
