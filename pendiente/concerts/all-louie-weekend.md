# concert - All Louie Weekend

## curiosities
**Radio Station Event (1980s)** : In the 1980s, certain radio stations initiated "All Louie Weekend" events, dedicating an entire weekend to playing different versions of "Louie Louie" without repetition. **Richard Berry and Jack Ely Performance** : One such radio station invited Richard Berry to perform "Louie Louie" live with Jack Ely, backed by Berry's former rhythm section, Lady Bow and Herb And. This occasion marked the first time Berry met the man who made his song famous.
