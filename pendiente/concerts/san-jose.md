## curiosities
**Riot Incident (July 1956)** : A Fats Domino concert in San Jose in July 1956 erupted into a riot after someone threw a string of lit firecrackers into the audience. The ensuing disturbance resulted in 1,000 broken beer bottles, 12 arrests, and 12 people requiring medical attention, highlighting the volatile atmosphere surrounding his performances.
