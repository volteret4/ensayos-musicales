# concert - Monterey Jazz Festival

## curiosities
**Johnny Otis's 1970 Performance** : A highlight of Johnny Otis's later career was his 1970 performance at the Monterey Jazz Festival. His band for this event featured an impressive lineup of veteran musicians, including Esther Phillips, Big Joe Turner, Eddie "Cleanhead" Vincent, Roy Milton, Peewee Crayton, Ivory Joe Hunter, and Roy Brown. This performance was released as a live album and footage of the band performing "Willie and the Hand Jive" was used in Clint Eastwood's film "Play Misty for Me."
