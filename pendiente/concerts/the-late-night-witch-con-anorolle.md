# concert - The Late Night Witch, con Anorolle

## curiosities
**Last White Stripes Performance (2009)** : The White Stripes made their final joint appearance on stage in 2009, reuniting for the last episode of The Late Night Witch, con Anorolle (likely "The Late Night With Conan O'Brien"). This performance marked the ultimate occasion the duo was seen publicly performing together before their official separation.
