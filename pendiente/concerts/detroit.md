# concert - Detroit

## curiosities
**Proposed Aretha Franklin Recording Location** : Detroit was chosen as the location for a proposed Blue Note recording project with Aretha Franklin, intended to be a "real basic" session centered on her piano playing, with Jerry Jemmott on bass and Bernard Purdie on drums. The city was selected so Aretha wouldn't have to travel due to her health.
