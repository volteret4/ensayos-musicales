# concert - Alan Freed Tour

## curiosities
**Legendary for Disputes** : This tour, featuring Jerry Lee Lewis, Chuck Berry, Buddy Holly, Larry Williams, The Chantels, and eleven other acts, became legendary not primarily for the music, which was excellent, but for the intense personal disputes between Jerry Lee Lewis and Chuck Berry. These included a knife-waving confrontation involving Lewis's father, Elmo, and ongoing rivalry over headlining status, which pushed Lewis to perform increasingly outrageous onstage stunts.
