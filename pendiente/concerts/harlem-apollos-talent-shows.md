# concert - Harlem Apollo's talent shows

## curiosities
**The Ronettes' Debut** : The group, then known as Ronnie and the Relatives, made their first appearance at a Harlem Apollo talent show. Their male lead singer experienced stage fright, prompting Ronnie to take over lead vocals, which was well-received by the notoriously hostile audience and led to the group performing regularly.
