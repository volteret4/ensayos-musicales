# concert - Dick Clark's Caravan of Stars Tour

## curiosities
**Platform for "Where Did Our Love Go" Success** : Dick Clark's Caravan of Stars tour provided a crucial platform for The Supremes' "Where Did Our Love Go." Initially performing as the opening act at the bottom of a bill featuring over a dozen groups, the tour coincided with the song's rapid ascent to number one on the charts.
