# concert - Saturday Night Fever (soundtrack)

## curiosities
**Inclusion of Kool & the Gang's "Open Sesame"** : Kool & the Gang's disco-funk track "Open Sesame" appeared on the soundtrack to the 1977 film *Saturday Night Fever*. However, this inclusion did not significantly boost the band's album sales, as their *Open Sesame* LP only reached number 110 on the album chart.
