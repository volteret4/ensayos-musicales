# concert - Yankee Stadium

## curiosities
**Disastrous Overcrowding and Cancellation** : The event did not go as planned, not due to a lack of public, but the opposite: it was so massively attended that controlling the crowd became impossible. Parts of the audience ended up rioting before the concert was halfway through, with thefts and damage to sound equipment, leading to its cancellation even before Héctor Lavoe could perform. "Las cosas no salieron como esperaba... El evento fue tan multitudinario que resultó imposible controlar a la gente. Parte del público acabó se apotiando el concierto antes de que llegara a la mitad, actor la boen y siquiera había salido a cantar. Hubo robos distrumientos y de parte del equipo de sonido, lo convidió a cancelarlo." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Filming Issues** : The Yankee Stadium concert was being recorded to produce a new film, similar to the Cheetah Club event. Its premature cancellation created significant problems for the documentary project, which ultimately had to be completed with footage from a subsequent concert. "Eso fue un problema, porque al igual que el chita, estaba siendo grabado para editar una película nueva." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Jerry Masucci's Ambitious Consolidation Attempt** : Jerry Masucci rented Yankee Stadium to host a concert intended to commemorate the second anniversary of the Fania All-Stars' breakthrough performance and definitively consolidate the Fania phenomenon. This ambitious plan reflected the label's aspiration for mainstream recognition. "Jerry Masuichi quisiera un más lejos, alquilo el Yankee Stadium para organizar el concierto que debía consolidar definitivamente el fenómeno fanía." ← LA FANIA.
