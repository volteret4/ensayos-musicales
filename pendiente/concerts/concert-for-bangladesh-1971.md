# concert - Concert for Bangladesh (1971)

## curiosities
**Boost to Billy Preston's Solo Career** : Billy Preston's participation in the Concert for Bangladesh in 1971, where he played alongside George Harrison, Eric Clapton, and Bob Dylan, significantly raised his public profile. This exposure helped to make his name "more known to record buyers" and consequently benefited his solo career.
