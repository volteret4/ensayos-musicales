## curiosities
**Venue for Iconic Live Album** : The Fillmore East served as the recording location for The Allman Brothers Band's iconic "Live at Fillmore East" album. The album, recorded in 1970 and released in 1971, captured extended live performances, including a 22-minute version of "Whipping Post," which helped propel the band to widespread attention.
