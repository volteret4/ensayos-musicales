# concert - Don Kirshner's In Concert

## curiosities
**Acquisition by Dick Clark (1974)** : This show was acquired by Dick Clark in 1974 and subsequently integrated into his burgeoning media empire. Don Kirshner, having been "kicked out," proceeded to develop his own syndicated rock music program.
