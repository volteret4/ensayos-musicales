## curiosities
**Bob Marley's 1975 Oakland Show** : In 1975, the Paramount Theatre in Oakland, California, hosted a Bob Marley concert promoted by the legendary Bill Graham, where attendees were notably struck by Marley's "bizarre appearance" in his street clothes and dreadlocks.
