# concert - 6-5 Special

## curiosities
**Pivotal British TV Rock and Roll Show** : "6-5 Special" was a BBC TV show that played a significant role in introducing rock and roll and skiffle to British television audiences. It was produced by Jack Good, who was highly influential in bringing these new music genres to British screens.
