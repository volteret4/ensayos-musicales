## curiosities
**Dissemination of Manchester Movement (Spring 1988)** : The Peel Sessions, hosted by the "grand visionary" John Peel, were crucial for spreading the Manchester movement throughout the United Kingdom. Appearing on a Peel Session automatically made artists fashionable. In the spring of 1988, Peel Sessions featured Happy Mondays, The Stone Roses, and even Guy Called Gerald (a rare inclusion for House music at the time), significantly boosting their reach.
