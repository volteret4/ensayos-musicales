# concert - Sinatra at the Sands (1966)

## curiosities
**Highest Charting Live Album for Frank Sinatra** : *Sinatra at the Sands*, recorded in 1966 at the Sands Hotel in Las Vegas with Count Basie's orchestra, was conducted and arranged by Quincy Jones. This acclaimed performance resulted in Frank Sinatra's top-selling and highest-charting live album ever, reaching number nine on the Top LPs chart in October 1966.
