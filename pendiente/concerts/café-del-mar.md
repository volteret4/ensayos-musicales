# concert - Café del Mar

## curiosities
**Ibiza's Iconic Sunset Bar** : Café del Mar, a bar located directly on the seafront in Ibiza, has been open since 1980 and became especially popular in the mid-1990s for its sunset views. It gained international fame through the musical sessions of its resident DJ, José Padilla, whose selections profoundly influenced the global perception of Chill Out music. **Spiritual Experience for Tourists** : The sunsets at Café del Mar, combined with the distinctive music played there, created an almost spiritual experience for international tourists. This unique atmosphere fostered a cult-like devotion, with visitors spreading the wonders of the place and the Chill Out genre worldwide, significantly contributing to its global expansion.
