# concert - Jerry Lee Lewis UK Tour (1958)

## curiosities
**Cancelled Due to Scandal** : Jerry Lee Lewis's UK tour, intended as a triumphant six-week engagement to solidify his status as a top rock and roll star, was abruptly cancelled after only three days. The tour ended when the British press discovered Lewis had married his 13-year-old cousin, Myra Gale Brown, making his scandalous private life public and leading to immediate public outcry.
