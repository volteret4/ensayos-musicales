**The Byrds' Controversial Performance** : In 1968, The Byrds were booed off the stage at the Grand Ole Opry. This reaction was a direct response from the country music establishment, who were greatly upset by their album "Sweetheart of the Rodeo," which incorporated rock elements into traditional country.
