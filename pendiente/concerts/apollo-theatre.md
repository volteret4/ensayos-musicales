# concert - Apollo Theatre

## curiosities
**Demanding Chitlin Circuit Venue** : The Apollo Theatre in Harlem was a highly prestigious yet extremely demanding venue on the Chitlin Circuit, particularly known for its "Amateurs Night." Audiences there were notoriously critical, coming "prepared with baskets full of rotten fruit and eggs to throw at the stage," but would also show fierce loyalty to acts that exceeded expectations. **James Brown's First Residency** : James Brown's first residency at the Apollo Theatre marked a significant turning point in his career. Despite initial worries about his new group and headliners like Little Willie John, Brown's performance was so compelling that his group was promoted to co-headline status, laying the groundwork for the venue to become synonymous with him in later years.
