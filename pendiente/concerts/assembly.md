**Massive Demoscene Fair in Finland** : Assembly is a massive demoscene fair held in Finland, serving as a key event for the demoscene movement. Participants, often with ties to the cracker culture, would gather with their computers to compete in various categories, projecting their demos onto giant screens in sports pavilions. "se empezaron a organizar ferias masivas, como la Amítica Assembly, En Finlandia..." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
**Site of Second Reality Debut (1993)** : The 1993 Assembly event in Finland was where The Future Crew debuted their "Second Reality" demo, a piece so technically impressive that it received a standing ovation and demonstrated the power of the PC beyond commercial expectations.
