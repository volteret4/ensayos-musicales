# concert - Doreen O'Brothers / Love Me Tender (Film)

## curiosities
**Elvis's First Film Role** : This Western film, initially titled "Doreen O'Brothers" and later renamed "Love Me Tender," was Elvis Presley's debut in cinema. He had expected a straight acting role without songs but was annoyed by the addition of musical numbers and the studio's decision to replace his band with Hollywood session musicians.
