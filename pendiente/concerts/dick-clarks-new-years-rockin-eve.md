# concert - Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve

## curiosities
**1974 Edition Details (Host and Guests)** : The 1974 broadcast of Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve was hosted by renowned comedian George Carlin. The musical lineup included Billy Preston, making a return appearance, along with Tower of Power, The Pointer Sisters, and Linda Ronstadt. "New Year's Rock and Eve 1974 was hosted by the legendary comedian George Carlin and musical guests included Billy Preston who was back again he was on the previous year." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 47 - Dick Clark's Entertainment Empire | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Debut Production (1972)** : The show first aired in 1972, with Three Dog Night co-hosting the event. Three Dog Night also performed "Celebrate" as the inaugural song played during the New Year's special. "Dick Clark's New Year's Rock and Eve was first produced in 1972 with co-hosts Three Dog Night." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 47 - Dick Clark's Entertainment Empire | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Rivalry with Guy Lombardo** : Dick Clark purposefully launched this show to directly challenge Guy Lombardo's long-established New Year's Eve broadcasts, which traditionally featured big band music from venues like the Roosevelt Grill and Waldorf Astoria. Clark viewed Lombardo's format as outdated. "Dick Clark was definitely taking aim at the King of New Year's Eve traditions with Guy Lombardo who had played big band music on his New Year's Eve special." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 47 - Dick Clark's Entertainment Empire | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Succession of New Year's Eve Hosting (1977)** : Following Guy Lombardo's death in 1977, the unofficial mantle of New Year's Eve television hosting passed to Dick Clark. His show became the preferred choice not only for younger audiences but for anyone preferring to avoid "amateur night" outings. "when Lombardo died in 1977 the New Year's Eve mantle was kind of unofficially passed to him." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 47 - Dick Clark's Entertainment Empire | https://www.ftr70.com/
