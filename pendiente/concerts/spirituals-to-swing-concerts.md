# concert - Spirituals to Swing concerts

## curiosities
**Hammond's Legendary Series** : These concerts, organized by John Hammond, were legendary events at Carnegie Hall, aiming to present a linear narrative from "spirituals to swing." **Problematic Narrative** : Despite their importance, the concerts were problematic due to their underlying narrative that portrayed black musical forms, particularly religious music like that performed by Sister Rosetta Tharp, as less sophisticated or "primitive" compared to big band music.
