# concert - Wimbledon (1974)

## curiosities
**Meeting of Elton John and Billie Jean King** : The prestigious Wimbledon tennis tournament in 1974 was the setting for the first meeting between musician Elton John and tennis star Billie Jean King. This encounter sparked a close friendship, with the two becoming "each other's groupies" and bonding over shared experiences, including playing tennis together and possibly their personal challenges regarding public sexual orientation.
