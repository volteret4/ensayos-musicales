# concert - National Library of New York

## curiosities
**Charity Event Location (2010)** : In 2010, the National Library of New York served as the venue for a charity concert where Björk and Dirty Projectors first deepened their collaboration. The event was organized to promote marine protected areas and raise awareness for whale conservation, which ultimately inspired their collaborative album.
