# concert - David Mancuso's Loft

## curiosities
**Pre-Disco Birthplace (1970)** : David Mancuso's loft at 647 Broadway in New York is recognized as the birthplace of the pre-disco era. On Valentine's Day, 1970, Mancuso opened his home to all people, regardless of sexual orientation or race, creating a safe space to dance and enjoy company.
