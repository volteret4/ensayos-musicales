# concert - Rock and Roll Hall of Fame

## curiosities
**Clash Induction and Failed Reunion Prospect (March 10, 2003)** : The induction ceremony for The Clash into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in New York on March 10, 2003, was seen as "the best ever prospect for a clash reunion." There were strong indications that the band would perform up to three songs, but Joe Strummer's death in December 2002 meant he never attended the ceremony. **Nirvana's Induction Performance** : Nirvana's induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame featured a performance with female vocalists Lorde, St. Vincent, Kim Gordon, and Joan Jett. This event was referenced by The Tragically Hip as a precedent for their own decision to perform with Feist at the Juno Awards. "Nirvana did it with Lord St. Vincent, Kim Gordon and Joan Jett when they were inducted to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
