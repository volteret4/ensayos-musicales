**Amplifier Modification for Distortion** : Frustrated with the sound of his amplifier, Dave Davis took a knife and "slashed the cone." He then fed the sound from this modified, "slashed amp" through his "new larger amp" to achieve a distinctively "distorted, fuzzy sound." This innovative technique was "almost unknown in Britain at the time" and is considered a pivotal moment in standardizing distorted guitar as a rock element.
