## curiosities
**Talent Scout and Producer** : At the time he discovered The Teenagers, Richie Barrett was the lead singer of The Valentines and also worked as a talent scout and producer for George Goldner at Rama Records. His role involved finding acts, arranging their music, and developing young talent, similar to the work of Ike Turner for Chess and Modern, or Jesse Stone for Atlantic.
