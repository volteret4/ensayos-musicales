## curiosities
**Ape Escape Soundtrack Composer** : Soichi Terada composed the acclaimed, three-and-a-half-hour drum and bass soundtrack for the PlayStation game Ape Escape. His work blended sharp and nervous synthesizer melodies with marimbas, maracas, and vibrant colors, designed to evoke a range of different emotions in the player without becoming repetitive.
