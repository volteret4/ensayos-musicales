# artist - Mary Wilson

## member of

## curiosities
**Longest-Serving Member of The Supremes** : Mary Wilson was the sole member who remained with The Supremes from their formation until their dissolution in 1977, experiencing their entire trajectory from "No Hit Supremes" to global superstars.
