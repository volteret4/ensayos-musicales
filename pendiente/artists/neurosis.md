**Times of Grace (1999) - New Album** : In 1999, after their acclaimed 1996 album *Through Silver in Blood*, Neurosis sought Steve Albini to record their new album, *Times of Grace*, aiming for an even heavier and drier sound, which Albini successfully achieved. This album marked a turning point, establishing a more powerful sound that would define their future works. The band was so satisfied with the outcome that Albini became their regular producer.
