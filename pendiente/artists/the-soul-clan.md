## curiosities
**Supergroup Formation** : Benny King teamed up with Solomon Burke, Don Cove, Joe Tex, and Arthur Cunley to form a supergroup called The Soul Clan in the later 1960s. This collaboration, however, did not succeed in kickstarting King's recording career despite the collective talent.
