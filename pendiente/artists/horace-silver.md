# artist - Horace Silver

## songs
**Peace (Year Unknown)** : Horace Silver composed "Peace," a "heavy song" that Norah Jones recorded with Wayne Shorter, Brian Blade, and John Patitucci. Wayne Shorter, who had played with Horace Silver at some point, notably waited until the solo section to play during the recording, demonstrating his philosophy of only entering when he had something profound to contribute.
