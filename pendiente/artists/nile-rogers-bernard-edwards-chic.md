# artist - Nile Rogers & Bernard Edwards (Chic)

## curiosities
**Producer Transition from Band Leaders** : Nile Rogers and Bernard Edwards, leaders of the disco band Chic, successfully transitioned from scoring their own hits with Chic to producing a wide range of artists, including Madonna, Robert Palmer, Diana Ross, and David Bowie.
