# artist - DJ Escriu

## curiosities
**Chop 'N' Screw Pioneer** : DJ Escriu cultivated a cult scene in Houston in the early 90s by experimenting with radically slowing down music to "narcotic levels." This sound, known as Chop 'N' Screw, transformed G-Funk into a dragged-out, immersive experience.
