# artist - Lésepirín

## members
- Jimmy Page
- John Paul Jones
- Robert Plan

## curiosities
**Formation from The Jarpers' Remains** : Lésepirín was formed from the remnants of The Jarpers' dissolution, spearheaded by Jimmy Page and John Paul Jones who sought out Robert Plan. This group became a central force in the music revolution, emerging from the ashes of a less stable, earlier band.
