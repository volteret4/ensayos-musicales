# artist - Sounds of Blue

## members
- Christine Perfect (bass)

## curiosities
**Early Band for Christine Perfect - Playing Bass** : Christine Perfect, who would later become Christine McVie, played bass with this local band near Birmingham. This was before she moved to London and joined Chicken Shack, a spin-off of Sounds of Blue.
