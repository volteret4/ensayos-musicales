## songs
**No Matter What Shape Your Stomach's In (1966)** : This instrumental track was a polished version of an Alka Seltzer ad melody, recorded by The T-bones, a group formed by Dave Pell using members of the celebrated Wrecking Crew. It became a one-hit wonder, charting all the way to number 3 in early 1966.
