# artist - The Lower Third

## genres
- Mod Rock

## songs
**Can't Help Thinking About Me (1966)** : This song, released by David Bowie under his new name, featured his emerging "alien tone of voice" but was played over a track by The Lower Third that still bore a strong resemblance to The Who's sound. Bowie's manager attempted to force it onto the charts, and it reached number 34 on the Melody Maker chart, though it missed the official British chart. **You've Got A Habit of Leaving (1965)** : Issued in the summer of 1965, this was the debut single by Davy Jones and The Lower Third, written by Jones himself. The band's sound was strongly influenced by the mod rock style of The Who.
