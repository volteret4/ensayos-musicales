# artist - East Street Band

## curiosities
**Nebraska Album Origins** : Bruce Springsteen's 1982 album "Nebraska" was notably recorded without the E Street Band, as it originated from demos that Stevie Van Zandt convinced Springsteen to release as a standalone album. This decision resulted in a stripped-down, introspective sound distinct from their usual collaborations.
