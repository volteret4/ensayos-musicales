## curiosities
**Encouraging Norah Jones' Collaboration with Wayne Shorter** : After the Blue Note anniversary performance, Jason Moran emailed Norah Jones, strongly encouraging her to record with Wayne Shorter. Norah credits this "little push" as essential for her to approach Wayne about the collaboration. "He emailed me after that. He's like, you know, you should make a record with Wayne or you should like record with Wayne.
