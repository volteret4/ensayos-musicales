# artist - Betty Friedan

## albums
**The Feminine Mystique (1963)** : Published in 1963, Betty Friedan's "The Feminine Mystique" is widely credited by historians and sociologists as initiating the second wave of the women's rights movement, or even the feminist movement. Her thesis challenged the notion that housewives were genuinely fulfilled with their lives, sparking a vital conversation and forming a foundation for the movement that took off in the 1970s.
