# artist - Lisa Gray

## curiosities
**Ghostly Vocal Effect for "Johnny's Remember Me"** : Soprano Lisa Gray contributed her vocals to John Leyton's "Johnny's Remember Me" in an unconventional manner: she recorded her fantastical, premonitory backing vocals from the bathroom of Joe Meek's studio. This unusual recording technique was specifically employed to achieve the song's signature phantasmagorical and haunting effect.
