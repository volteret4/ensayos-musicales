# artist - Billy Ward and the Domino's

## members
- Jackie Wilson (lead singer)

## songs
**Don't Be Cruel (1956)** : Elvis Presley observed Billy Ward and the Domino's, with Jackie Wilson as their lead singer, perform Elvis's own hit "Don't Be Cruel" in Las Vegas. Elvis was highly influenced by Wilson's rendition, particularly a specific "Yankee pronunciation" of "Telephone."
