# artist - Ronnie Milsap

## curiosities
**1974 CMA Male Vocalist of the Year Win** : Ronnie Milsap won the Male Vocalist of the Year award in 1974, an award that did not make country music purists happy. "Country music purists were not really that happy about Rich getting the award in 74 or Ronnie Mills up winning male vocalist of the year that same year." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 2 - Countryish Music of the 1970s (Encore) | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Rhythm and Blues Background** : Milsap was considered an "outsider" by some purists because he started his career trying to make it in rhythm and blues.
