**The Carol Burnett Show (1967-1978)** : This variety show aired from 1967 to 1978, ending by Burnett's own decision before she could be asked to conclude it. It was celebrated as an "incredible run" and a "weekly showcase of comedic genius," featuring a strong supporting cast. The show relied on TV-designed comedy sketches, similar to "The Milton Berle Show," often satirizing the entertainment industry in a style akin to a "prelude to Saturday Night Live."
