# artist - Crazy Frog

## songs
**Axel F (2005)** : This instrumental theme, originally from the "Beverly Hills Cop" soundtrack, was reinterpreted by the animated character Crazy Frog, created by Hamba. This polyphonic ringtone became the best-selling ringtone of all time, reaching number one on charts worldwide. It marked a shift in ringtones from personal expression to a commercial spectacle, with accelerated remixes and supposedly humorous audio clips dominating the market.
