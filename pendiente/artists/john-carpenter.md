## curiosities
**Budgetary Innovation (1974)** : As a young film student, Carpenter faced budget limitations for his first film, *Dark Star*, which forced him to use an unconventional tool: a synthesizer. This decision not only saved his film but also inadvertently defined the sound of 1980s horror cinema, and by extension, contemporary horror. "la solución este problema, aparte de salvar a su película, va a definir el sonido del cine de terror de los años 80 y, por extensión, el actual." ← Por qué el cine de terror de los 80 suena así？ Horror Synth | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hd23tCTRk8E
**Renowned Soundtrack Composer** : John Carpenter is highly respected for his work on numerous film soundtracks, which have achieved strong sales. "es una persona muy consolidada por todos los trabajos que hizo con las bandas sonoras, se vendieron muy bien." ← Music Radar Clan > Lanzamientos de Octubre 2017 | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q1_bxi16osQ&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc
**Signature Sound Forged** : After his initial experiment with *Dark Star*, Carpenter truly forged his signature style in *Assault on Precinct 13*. This style, characterized by synthetic and dark minimalism, would become a hallmark of his entire body of work and be widely imitated by almost all videoclippish horror cinema of the 1980s and many current works.
