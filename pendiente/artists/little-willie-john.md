## curiosities
**Apollo Residency Headliner** : Little Willie John, a prominent blues star, headlined James Brown's first residency at the Apollo Theatre. However, Brown and the Famous Flames proved so popular that they were quickly promoted to co-headline status, much to John's annoyance. "But he was still worried about his new group and how they would fare on this residency which also featured Little Richard's old group The Upsetters and was headlined by the blue star Little Willie John." ← Episode 39： ＂Please Please Please＂ by James Brown and the Famous Flames | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSkNVh93tAs
**Remembered by James Brown** : Little Willie John was one of the King Records artists whom James Brown remembered and thought about when Brown was producing records for other King artists like Hank Ballard and Bill Doggett in 1969, as part of his effort to pay back dues. "thinking about Little Willie John and other nice things." ← Episode 50： ＂Honky Tonk＂, by Bill Doggett | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H2rYnKmeZF0
