**Theory on Linda Martell's Visibility (2009)** : In a 2009 article, Alice Randall proposed a theory about Linda Martell's career, questioning if it might have been more successful if her race had not been immediately apparent. She pondered whether putting Martell's face on her album cover and her TV appearances hindered her career, suggesting that some performances might have been canceled when it was revealed she was not white.
