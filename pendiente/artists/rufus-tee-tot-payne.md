# artist - Rufus "Tee Tot" Payne

## genres

## curiosities
**Mentor to Hank Williams** : Rufus "Tee Tot" Payne was an African-American Blues musician whom a young Hank Williams met on the street. Payne was instrumental in teaching Williams that music was not just about sheet music but about lived experience and feeling in every beat. He emphasized the importance of honesty and an unshakeable rhythm, which became fundamental to Williams' style. "Ayer conoció a Rufus, Pitot Pain, un músico afraamericano de Blues que vivían la calle. El le mostró que la música no se limitaba tan solo a las partituras, sino que se vivía y se sentía en cada compás." ← El genio roto del country： La leyenda de Hank Williams | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qczx7qWg4xM
