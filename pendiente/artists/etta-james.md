## curiosities
**Recording at Fame** : Etta James recorded at Fame Recording Studios, a facility recognized for offering black artists a place to work without disturbance during a time of widespread racism and discrimination. "Wilson Pickett, Aretha Franklin, Edda James. They all recorded there." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk ← etta-james
