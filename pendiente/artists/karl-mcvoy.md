# artist - Karl McVoy

## curiosities
**Early Piano Influence on Jerry Lee Lewis** : Karl McVoy, Jerry Lee Lewis's uncle, was a major influence on his piano playing. Although he never became famous, he made a couple of records after his nephew's success, with one from 1957 featuring musicians who also worked on Elvis's hits, including Chet Atkins and The Jordanaires, sounding remarkably like Jerry Lee himself.
