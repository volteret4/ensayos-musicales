## curiosities
**Sega Megadrive Startup Integration** : The iconic "Sega!" boot-up audio, commonly associated with the Megadrive console, was cleverly embedded within the initial "Sonic the Hedgehog" game rather than the console's firmware. This proved to be a highly effective branding strategy, as the widespread ownership of the first Sonic game meant almost all Megadrive users experienced this distinctive, compressed choral sound.
