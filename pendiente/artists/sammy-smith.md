**Help Me Make It Through the Night (1970)** : Sammy Smith released her cover of this Chris Kristofferson song in November 1970, which became a significant crossover hit. It reached number one on the country charts, number eight on the Billboard Hot 100, and number three on the easy listening chart, defining a "sweet spot" of country and pop for that era.
