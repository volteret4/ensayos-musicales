## genres

## songs
**Superfly Meets Shaft (1973)** : This novelty song, released by John Free and Ernest Smith recording as John and Ernest, was a mashup about the popular blaxploitation movie characters Superfly and John Shaft. It peaked at number 31 on the Billboard Hot 100 nationally, but achieved greater popularity in regional markets, reaching number 9 in Murphy's Borough, Tennessee, in April 1973 and in Molene, Illinois, in June. The song was produced by Dickie Goodman, and sampled popular tunes from the iconic soundtracks of "Superfly" and "Shaft."
