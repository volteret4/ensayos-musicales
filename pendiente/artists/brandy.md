# artist - Brandy

## songs
**The Boy Is Mine (1998)** : This duet with Monica was the winner of the 1998 Billboard Song of the Summer and was the most dominant Song of the Summer of the 90s, spending 13 weeks at number one between June and August 1998. The song "maxed out on Sass" and, thanks to producer Rodney Jerkins, "lilted and fluttered like a butterfly circling over a hammock."
