## albums
**Goat (1991)** : In 1991, Steve Albini collaborated with The Jesus Lizard, producing their album *Goat*, which is widely considered one of the pinnacles of noise rock. Albini, having a deep understanding of the band, knew he needed to capture the primitive energy of David Yow and his bandmates, resulting in a raw album with "oxidized guitars" that sounded like radial saws, very similar in style to Big Black.
