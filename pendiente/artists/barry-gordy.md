## curiosities
**3 AM Inspiration for "Shop Around"** : After two unsuccessful versions, Barry Gordy had a sudden realization at 3 AM that The Miracles' "Shop Around" needed a new, faster arrangement. His immediate action to call Smokey Robinson and get the group into the studio led to the song becoming a number two pop hit and a million-seller. **Evolution of Motown's Slogan** : Barry Gordy initially coined the Motown slogan "create, make, and sell" to reflect his goal of producing great records that sold widely. However, recognizing the financial challenges of unpaid distributors, he revised it to "create, sell, and collect," emphasizing the crucial importance of securing payment. **Production of The Fidelitones** : In the late 1950s, before Motown's major success, Barry Gordy produced several unreleased tracks for The Fidelitones on the Aladdin label, featuring future Motown talents Brian Holland and Freddie Gorman. **Renaming The Marvelettes** : Barry Gordy is credited with giving The Marvelettes their definitive name. After hearing the group, then known as The Cassin Yet, he supposedly remarked, "those girls are marvels," leading to their new identity. **Signing The Primettes (The Supremes)** : Motown staff widely believed that Barry Gordy signed The Primettes (later The Supremes) more due to his attraction to member Diane Ross than for the group's talent, despite their initial lack of success. "Motown had already signed one girl group, a group called The Primates, who had been renamed and who had so far released two singles.
