## songs
**Don't Mess With Bill (1966) - Writer and Producer** : Smokey Robinson wrote and produced this song, which became The Marvelettes' first top ten pop hit since "Playboy" (1962). For this record, Robinson notably shifted lead vocal duties from Gladys Horton to Wonder Young. "Smokey Robinson took over their production and decided to start using Wander as the lead vocalist, rather than Gladys, who had sung lead on their hits up to that point.
