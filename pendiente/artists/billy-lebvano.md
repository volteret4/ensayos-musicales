## curiosities
**Post-Lymon Lead Singer for The Teenagers** : After Frankie Lymon's departure, Billy LeBvano, a white kid, became one of the new lead singers for The Teenagers. According to Jimmy Merchant, LeBvano's vocal style was reminiscent of Freddie Houston, who would later become a lead singer for The Ink Spots.
