# artist - Cornel Gunter of The Coasters

## curiosities
**Life-Saving Intervention for Jackie Wilson** : Cornel Gunter was present when Jackie Wilson collapsed on stage in 1975 during a performance of "Lonely Teardrops" and administered mouth-to-mouth resuscitation, possibly saving Wilson's life. "Cornel Gunter of the coasters gave him mouth to mouth and possibly saved his life." ← Episode Sixty-Four： ＂Reet Petite＂ by Jackie Wilson | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BYU-H4NvnmE
**Suspicious Death** : Gunter was murdered in Las Vegas in 1990 by an unknown gunman, and some close to Jackie Wilson considered it no coincidence, believing there was something strange about Wilson's collapse that Gunter had observed. "Some of those close to Wilson think it's no coincidence that Cornel Gunter, who had been there when Wilson collapsed, and had always thought there was something strange about it, was murdered himself in 1990, in Las Vegas, by an unknown gunman." ← Episode Sixty-Four： ＂Reet Petite＂ by Jackie Wilson | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BYU-H4NvnmE
