# artist - The Thunderbirds

## members
- Carlos Ward (saxophone player, arranger)
- Run Holden (leader)

## songs
**Louie Louie (Cover)** : The Thunderbirds developed an influential arrangement of "Louie Louie," with saxophone player Carlos Ward simplifying its chord structure for guitar. This version, learned by many Pacific Northwest bands from their live shows, became the template for subsequent covers.
