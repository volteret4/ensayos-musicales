# artist - Betty White

## curiosities
**Attending Steve Martin's Troubadour Show (1974)** : In 1974, Betty White and her husband Alan Ludden attended The Troubadour specifically to see Steve Martin, who was then an obscure opening act for Linda Ronstadt. Martin expressed his honor at meeting them, and White told him they came because they heard he was funny. "Passing through the lobby before the show, I saw Betty White and her husband Alan Luddon waiting in line. I loved Betty White, so I went up to them. I'm so honored to meet you both. And then I said, isn't Linda great?
