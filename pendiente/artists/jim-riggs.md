# artist - Jim Riggs

## curiosities
**Mac 4 Boot Sound (Early 90s)** : As a sound designer for Apple, Jim Riggs was responsible for creating the welcome sound for the Mac 4, released in the early 90s. He drew inspiration from the distinctive final chord of The Beatles' "A Day in the Life," resulting in a sound that, with slight variations, became a staple for most subsequent Mac models. "Eso fue lo que pensó Jim Riggs, diseñador de sonido de Apple.
