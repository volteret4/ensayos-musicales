# artist - The Honeydrippers

## members
- (High school students recruited by Roy Hammond)
- Roy Hammond

## songs
**Impeached the President (June 1973)** : This political funk song, released on June 1st, 1973, was credited to The Honeydrippers but primarily consisted of Roy Hammond and high school students he recruited. It was released as calls for Richard Nixon's impeachment intensified due to the Watergate scandal and has become one of the most sampled songs ever.
