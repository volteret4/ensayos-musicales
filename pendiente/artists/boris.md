## genres

## curiosities
**Japanese Drone Metal Development** : The Japanese band Boris further developed Drone Metal into a ritualistic territory. Their work often eliminates vocals and in many cases even the drums, focusing on minimalist soundscapes where notes are sustained for minutes, intertwining to produce tectonic vibrations that virtually halt musical time, reducing Doom to its maximum simplicity.
