# artist - Alan Ludden

## curiosities
**Attending Steve Martin's Troubadour Show (1974)** : Alan Ludden and his wife Betty White attended The Troubadour in 1974 to see Steve Martin perform. Martin, then an opening act, was honored by their presence, and Ludden confirmed they came because they heard Martin was funny.
