# artist - Bill Hall

## songs
**Could I Have This Dance (1980)** : Hall co-wrote this hit song with Wayland Holyfield, having initially played a song that contained the line "Could I have this dance for the rest of my life." This phrase sparked the idea that developed into the popular wedding song featured in *Urban Cowboy*.
