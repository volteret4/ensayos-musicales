## albums
**Café del Mar (1994) - First Compilation in Series** : José Padilla compiled and released the first album in the iconic *Café del Mar* series in 1994. This collection successfully captured the unique sonic experience of the sunsets at the Café del Mar bar in Ibiza, allowing visitors to take that atmosphere home with them. Its release, alongside compilations from Buda Bar, initiated a global craze for chill-out compilations. ## curiosities
**Defining the Ibiza Sound at Café del Mar** : As the resident DJ at Café del Mar in Ibiza, José Padilla became renowned for his sessions that perfectly encapsulated the essence of the island's sunsets. His musical selections blended ambient sounds, World Music, "Jazz Swab" (likely Jazz Suave), and "Rindmoss Electrónicos" (likely Ritmos Electrónicos), significantly contributing to the relaxed and contemplative spirit of Ibiza. His work redefined Chill Out for a wider audience, turning the sunset experience at the bar into a near-spiritual event for tourists.
