# artist - Bernie Taupin

## songs
**Philadelphia Freedom (1975)** : Bernie Taupin co-wrote this song with Elton John, inspired by Billie Jean King and her Philadelphia Freedoms tennis team. The song is a tribute to the city of Philadelphia, adopting the "Philadelphia sound" with its R&B feel and lush strings, and Elton John intended it to sound like a "funky black record."
