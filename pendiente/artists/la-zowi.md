## curiosities
**"Queen of Trap"** : La Zowi became known as the "Queen of Trap" due to her influence and popularity in the Spanish trap scene. "y se convirtió en la reina del trap." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Key Figure in Trap Ecosystem** : La Zowi was one of the key figures orbiting the PXXR GANG, collaborating with other artists and uploading videos. "o figuras clave como Elmini, Hakim, Misnina, Secol Real o La Zooey, empezaron a colaborar entre ellos de a subir vídeos." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Popularizer of Ratchet Aesthetic** : La Zowi played a significant role in popularizing the "ratchet" aesthetic, which was later adopted and further popularized by artists like Rosalía and Bad Gyal. "La Zoe puso de moda todo el roguito del rachet que popularizaría más tarde, Rosalía o Batguial." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
