## curiosities
**Musical Influence** : Johnny Cash, Luther Perkins, and Marshall Grant admired The Louvin Brothers and particularly wanted their early musical efforts to sound like them during their jam sessions. **Performance at Influential Show (1955)** : The Louvin Brothers were on the bill at a country music show in September 1955, where Gene Vincent saw Elvis Presley perform. While not the headliners, their presence contributed to the country music context of the event that shaped Vincent's path. "The headliner was Hank Snow, and the Louvin brothers were also on the bell, but the act that changed Jean's life was lower down the bell.
