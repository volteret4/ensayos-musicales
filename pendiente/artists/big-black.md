## albums
**Atomizer (1986) - Debut Album** : In 1986, Steve Albini produced *Atomizer*, the debut album of his band Big Black. This groundbreaking post-hardcore record notably eschewed conventional drums, instead utilizing a Roland TR-606 drum machine, which imparted a distinctive industrial sound to the music. The abrasive intensity of the guitars garnered significant attention from specialized critics and respected musicians, including Robert Plant, who became a fan upon hearing it.
