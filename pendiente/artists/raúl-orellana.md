# artist - Raúl Orellana

## curiosities
**Principal Megamixer for Blanco y Negro Music** : Following the success of the Max Mix series, Blanco y Negro Music decided to launch their own series, "Bolero Mix," and definitively signed Raúl Orellana, establishing him as their primary Megamixer. He played a key role in the long-running success of the Bolero Mix series. "fichando ya definitivamente Raúl Erellana, quien se convirtió en su principal Megamixer." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Resident DJ of Studio 54 Barcelona** : Raúl Orellana was the resident DJ at the iconic Studio 54 discotheque in Barcelona. He was responsible for the precise and polished mix of the "Studio 54 Connection" album, which was the first mix record published in Spain.
