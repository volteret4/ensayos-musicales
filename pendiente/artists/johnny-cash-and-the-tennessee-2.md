## curiosities
**Development of the "Boom-Chick-a-Boom" Rhythm** : Since the group did not have a drummer, Johnny Cash innovated by placing paper between the strings and the fretboard of his acoustic guitar. This deadened the sound, approximating a snare drum, and resulted in the "boom chick" sound, which became a signature of Cash's recordings for decades. This unique country music take on the two-beat rhythm was largely born out of instrumental necessity.
