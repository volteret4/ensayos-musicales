## curiosities
**Early Group for Tommy Steele** : The Cavemen were an early group centered around Thomas Hicks and Mike Pratt, with Lionel Bart initially playing washboard before focusing on songwriting. The group also included various Vipers and other skifflers. "The Cavemen became a floating group of musicians, centered around Hicks and Pratt, and with various vipers and other skifflers pulled in as and when they were available." ← Episode 48： ＂Rock With the Caveman＂ by Tommy Steele | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=scDLm48u2yc
**Skiffle Musicians' Disdain** : Other skiffle musicians in The Cavemen looked down on Hicks for wanting to play rock and roll songs like "Heartbreak Hotel" or "Blue Suede Shoes" instead of traditional skiffle tunes like "Drink a Little Water-Solvey" or "Rock Island Line."
