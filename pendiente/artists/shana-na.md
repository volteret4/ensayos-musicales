# artist - Shana Na

## genres
- Rock and Roll (50s revival)

## curiosities
**Grease Soundtrack Contribution** : Shana Na, a 50s revival group, performed a significant portion of the pre-Beatles rock and roll featured on the *Grease* movie soundtrack. Their contributions helped establish the nostalgic 1950s soundscape of the film.
