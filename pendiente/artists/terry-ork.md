# artist - Terry Ork

## curiosities
**Advocate for Television** : Terry Ork, manager for Television, was crucial in their early days at CBGB, persistently "badgering" Hilly Crystal to book the band despite Crystal's initial negative impressions. Ork was primarily interested in helping Television realize their artistic vision rather than purely commercial success. "Terry Ork badgered him. He said, it was Terry Ork who badgered me into having television back time and time again because they were so god awful when they started." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 57 - Punk and New Wave at New York's CBGB | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Creator of Avant-Garde Vibe** : Ork played a pivotal role in shaping the avant-garde atmosphere at CBGB, bringing his "art see friends" and leveraging his knowledge of theater, film, and art to excite Hilly Crystal about the emerging scene. Crystal credited him with the entire scene's existence. "He brought his art see friends to CBGB and he helped create this avant-garde art vibe." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 57 - Punk and New Wave at New York's CBGB | https://www.ftr70.com/
