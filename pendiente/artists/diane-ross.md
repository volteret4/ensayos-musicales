## curiosities
**Barry Gordy's Attraction and Group Signing** : Motown staff widely believed that The Primettes (later The Supremes) were signed primarily because of Barry Gordy's attraction to Diane Ross, rather than the group's inherent talent, despite their initial lack of success. "Motown had already signed one girl group, a group called The Primates, who had been renamed and who had so far released two singles. But the Supremes, as they were renamed, wouldn't become successful for several years, and were generally regarded as a joke among the Motown staff, who thought, not entirely without reason, that they had been signed more because Barry Gordy was attracted to Diane Ross, one of the members of the group, than because of any talent they had." ← Episode 93： ＂Please Mr. Postman＂ by the Marvelettes | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EPg8Wx30-uM
