## curiosities
**Rock Music Definition (1970)** : As the 1970s began, the renowned rock critic Lester Bangs, later immortalized in film by Philip Seymour Hoffman in "Almost Famous" (2000), wrote that rock music is fundamentally an adolescent form, reflecting the specific rhythms, concerns, and aspirations of a specialized age group. He argued it couldn't grow up without transforming into something distinctly different, though potentially equally valid. This observation was made in 1970 as part of a review for The Stooges' album *Fun House*. "rock music is, and I quote, basically an adolescent music reflecting the rhythms, concerns, and aspirations of a very specialized age group.
