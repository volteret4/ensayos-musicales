# artist - Puff Daddy (P. Diddy)

## curiosities
**Highest Earner of the Year (2016)** : Puff Daddy was the top earner on the Forbes list for the year, taking in approximately 130 million euros (over 100 million dollars). This significant income was largely attributed to a massive tour commemorating the 25th anniversary of his first album, Bad Boy. Despite being a very important figure in the industry for his production work, he did not typically rank so high in previous years, but the tour propelled him to the top.
