## curiosities
**Bakersfield Sound Figure** : Buck Owens was a prominent musician in Bakersfield in the 1950s, hailing from Dust Bowl migrant families. He was instrumental in developing a "tough form of honky tongue country in western" that drew influences from Hillbilly Boogie and western swing.
