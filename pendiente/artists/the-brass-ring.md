# artist - The Brass Ring

## members
- Phil Bodner (leader)

## songs
**The Disadvantages of You (1967)** : This song was a reboot of a catchy melody derived from a Benson and Hedges ad, which promoted a new, extra-long cigarette. The studio group, led by producer and arranger Phil Bodner, successfully charted with this adaptation, reaching number 36 in March of 1967.
