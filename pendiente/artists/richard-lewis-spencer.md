# artist - Richard Lewis Spencer

## songs
**Color Him Father (1969)** : Richard Lewis Spencer wrote the song "Color Him Father," which tells the story of a dedicated stepfather caring for a woman and her seven children after their father's death. The song was first recorded by The Winstons in R&B style and earned Spencer a Grammy for R&B Song of the Year in 1970.
