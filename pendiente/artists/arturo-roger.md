# artist - Arturo Roger

## curiosities
**Most Popular DJ at ACTV - Introducing Avant-Garde Sounds** : Arturo Roger was the most popular DJ at ACTV, known for introducing cutting-edge, avant-garde sounds like New Beat and Acid House. His influence was so significant that New Beat spread to other clubs such as Spook and Espiral, while Acid House became prominent at Puzzle, further diversifying the Ruta's musical landscape.
