# artist - Art Reynolds

## members
- The Art Reynolds Singers

## genres
- Gospel

## songs
**Jesus Is Just Alright (1966)** : This gospel song was written by Art Reynolds and first performed by The Art Reynolds Singers in 1966. It was later covered by The Byrds in 1969 and then by The Doobie Brothers in 1972.
