## curiosities
**Unsuccessful Marvelettes Side Project** : Motown released a single under the name "The Darnells" featuring Gladys Horton and Wonder Young sharing lead vocals, with The Andantes providing backing. This experiment to change things up for The Marvelettes was commercially unsuccessful.
