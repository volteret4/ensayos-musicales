# artist - Stan Kenton's band

## curiosities
**UK Tour as Union Exchange (1955)** : Stan Kenton's band toured the UK as part of the "one-in, one-out" exchange agreement established between American and British musicians' unions in 1955. This tour facilitated a reciprocal arrangement where the Ted Heath Orchestra toured the US.
