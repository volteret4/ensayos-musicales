# artist - Illinois Jacket

## instruments
- Tenor saxophone

## songs
**Flying Home (1942)** : Illinois Jacket's tenor saxophone solo on Lionel Hampton's 1942 recording of "Flying Home" introduced a completely new sound with its "hunk and scrunk" quality, which subsequently defined a whole new genre called rhythm and blues. This solo was so exceptional that successive tenor sax players who replaced him in Hampton's band copied it note for note rather than improvising their own versions.
