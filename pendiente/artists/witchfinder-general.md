## genres

## curiosities
**European Doom Pioneer** : During the 1980s, as a reaction against the constant acceleration prevalent in metal, Witchfinder General emerged in Europe as one of the bands that began to explore the possibilities of slow, heavy tempos. They prioritized weight, slowness, and atmosphere, contributing to the nascent form of Doom Metal.
