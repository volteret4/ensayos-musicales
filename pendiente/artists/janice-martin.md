## songs
**Drugstore Rock 'n' Roll (1956)** : Janice Martin's first attempt at songwriting, written in 10-15 minutes, became the B-side to "Will You, Will You." This song is notable as one of the first records to specifically address the experience of white teenagers from their own perspective, a turning point in rock and roll history. "Her very first attempt at writing a song took her by her own accounts 10 or 15 minutes to write, and ended up as the B side." ← Episode 40： ＂Drugstore Rock 'n' Roll＂ by Janis Martin | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0i2p9TelN0
