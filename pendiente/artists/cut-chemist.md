# artist - Cut Chemist

## curiosities
**Artisanal Approach to Sampling** : Cut Chemist's music is grouped with that of J Dilla and DJ Shadow as exemplifying an artisanal, pure craftsmanship approach to the sampling process. This refers to the manual, ear-driven method of creating tracks with limited technology, which gave their music a distinctive, raw quality marked by subtle imperfections.
