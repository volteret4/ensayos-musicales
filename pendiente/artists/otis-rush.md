## curiosities
**Tour Headliner (1957)** : Otis Rush co-headlined an otherwise all-Black tour that The Crickets were booked on, due to the mistaken belief by promoters that The Crickets were a Black doo-wop group. "The group even got booked on an otherwise all Black tour, headlined by Clyde McFatter and Otis Rush, booked by people who hadn't realised they were white." ← Episode 74： ＂It Doesn't Matter Any More＂ by Buddy Holly | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6unib5T2mDQ
