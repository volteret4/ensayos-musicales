## curiosities
**Early Phonk Artist** : Lila Glimain was among the new global artists who emerged on SoundCloud after 2011, generating a new Phonk scene. These artists paid homage to the Memphis and Houston pioneers while consciously adding a new aesthetic filter to the sound.
