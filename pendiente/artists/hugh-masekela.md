# artist - Hugh Masekela

## curiosities
**Festival Co-Creator Role** : Hugh Masekela, an exiled jazz musician from South Africa, co-conceived the Zaire 74 festival with Stewart Levine. They had met about 10 years prior at the Manhattan School of Music and developed a lifelong friendship, envisioning the festival as an opportunity to unite African American and African performers.
