# artist - Cassandra Wilson

## albums
**New Moon Daughter (1995)** : This album made a profound impact on Nora Jones, who was "obsessed" with it. It stood out significantly from other music she listened to and was deeply emotional for her, as she listened to it constantly while her aunt was dying of cancer in Florida. The album was unique in her experience as a singer's record composed entirely of acoustic guitars with no keyboards, contributing to its earthy, slightly twangy sound.
