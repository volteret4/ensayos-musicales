**Impact on Workflow - Melody Generation** : The AI in Project Magenta learns to develop and compose melodies by analyzing the user's compositional style. It creates behavioral patterns from user-inputted melodies to predict and compose new ones. This allows a user to provide just a few tonal references (e.g., three or four notes), and the AI can then generate hundreds of prepared melodies or directly develop a melody based on those guidelines, radically changing the way melodies are created with synthesizers.
