# artist - Glenn Reeves

## genres
- Rockabilly

## songs
**Heartbreak Hotel (1956) - Demo Recording** : Contributed to the completion of "Heartbreak Hotel" and recorded the demo for Mae Axton and Tommy Durden. He impersonated Elvis on the demo, but refused to take official songwriting credit, deeming the song "ridiculous." "Reeves did, though, record the demo for them.
