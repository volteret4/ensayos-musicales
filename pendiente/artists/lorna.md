## songs
**Papichulo (2003)** : This reggaeton song was initially rejected by traditional radio stations like Los 40 Principales. However, it found significant popularity through reality shows like Gran Hermano and, crucially, as a polyphonic ringtone. Reggaeton, which sounded more natural in the multi-channel sound of polytones than rock, gained ground through these alternative platforms.
