## curiosities
**Influential Rastafari Drummer** : Count Ossie was a prominent Rasta Nyabinghi drummer, who Prince Buster brought in, along with four other drummers from the tradition, to record on his single "O'Carolina." He became recognized as the single most influential drummer in Jamaica, integrating Rasta sacred rhythms into popular music.
