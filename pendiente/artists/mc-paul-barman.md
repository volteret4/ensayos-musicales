## albums
**Chamberland (June 22nd) - Experimental Hip Hop Featuring Major Collaborators** : This album from MC Paul Barman, referred to in the transcript as "Cochamber," is described as one of the most interesting releases of the year, though it might be overlooked due to its niche nature. It features unique, experimental hip hop with jazz and soul foundations, and highly artistic lyrics. The album boasts significant collaborations, including The Roots, Prince Paul, and MF Doom (referred to as Emeth Dumb in the transcript).
