# artist - Greg Shaw

## curiosities
**"In Defense of Rock Theory" (1977)** : Greg Shaw wrote this essay in 1977, where he expressed his view on the meaning in rock and roll, stating that his love for rock stemmed from the experience and feeling it invoked rather than profound meaning. He also founded a zine that was a precursor to major music publications like Rolling Stone and Cream Magazine. "I don't think there's any great meaning capitalized intentionally in rock and roll.
