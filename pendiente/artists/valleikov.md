**Refusal of Tom Parker as Manager** : Valleikov initially considered Tom Parker as his manager but was put off after a couple of dates due to what he perceived as Parker's "un-Christian attitude to money." Parker insisted on a percentage of the gross in addition to fixed fees, which Valleikov found too grasping. "Aikov was playing dates for fixed fees, and Parker started insisting that as well as the fixed fee, Aikov should get a percentage of the gross.
