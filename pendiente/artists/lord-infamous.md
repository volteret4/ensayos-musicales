**Part of Three 6 Mafia Mythology** : As a member of Three 6 Mafia, Lord Infamous was an integral part of the band's self-created mythology. Each member, including Lord Infamous, was depicted as if emerging from a secret horror lodge, forming a nightmarish rock'n'roll coven from the margins of Memphis.
