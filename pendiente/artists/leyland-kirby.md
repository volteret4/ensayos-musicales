# artist - Leyland Kirby

## albums
**Bleaklow (2006) - The Stranger Project** : Released in 2006 under the project name The Stranger, this album is notable for its extensive duration of over 10 hours. Instead of merely re-forming a few rave anthems, it reduces them to "sonic ruins," transforming them into a tapestry of noise and ghostly reverberations. The album is described as the "death certificate of rave culture," presenting a form of Dark Ambient music imbued with the melancholy of a scene that no longer exists, where the genre itself transforms into its own ghost.
