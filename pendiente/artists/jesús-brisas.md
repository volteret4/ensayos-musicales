# artist - Jesús Brisas

## curiosities
**DJ Contributing to Espiral's Fame (From 1976)** : Jesús Brisas was a DJ at Espiral who helped the club gain widespread fame outside of Valencia starting in 1976. Espiral quickly became known as "la discoteca más cañera de levante" (the toughest club in the east), with a hard sound that incorporated genres like EBM, Dark Wave, and Industrial, which collectively defined the "música máquina" sound.
