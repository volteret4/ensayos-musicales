## songs
**Rock the Boat (1971)** : Initially released in 1971 as an album cut, this song was discovered by New York DJs who gave it regular club play. RCA executives, upon realizing its popularity, released a remixed dance version as a single in 1974, which then reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100 on July 6, 1974, after just four weeks in the top 40.
