## curiosities
**DJing and Chicago Connections** : Juan Atkins and Terry McMillan began DJing together under this name, mixing their initial productions with emerging House music from Chicago. They made regular weekend trips to Chicago, forging relationships with influential DJs such such as Ron Hardy and Frankie Knuckles, who later became legendary figures. "Empezaron a pinchar bajo el nombre de Deep Space on Works.
