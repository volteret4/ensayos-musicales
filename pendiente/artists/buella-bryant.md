# artist - Buella Bryant

## genres
- Blues

## curiosities
**Real Mentor to The Isley Brothers** : Buella Bryant was a less well-known professional blues shouter who served as the "real mentor" for the three eldest Isley Brothers (O'Kelly, Rudolph, and Ronald). They met her while traveling from Boston to New York. Bryant had a moderately successful career from the 1950s to the 1980s, primarily through live performances, and made a handful of "very listenable records." She used her extensive contacts in the music industry to help the young Isley Brothers secure bookings when they were robbed and stranded in New York.
