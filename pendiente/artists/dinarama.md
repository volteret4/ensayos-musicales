## curiosities
**Evolution into Alaska y Dinarama** : Dinarama later saw Alaska and Nacho Canut join its ranks, leading to the band being renamed Alaska y Dinarama. This new configuration became one of the most popular bands of the 1980s. **Formation by Carlos Berlanga (Undated)** : Dinarama was formed by Carlos Berlanga after his departure from Alaska y los Pegamoides, prompted by his disenchantment with the band's darker musical direction. He sought a new creative outlet with this project.
