## curiosities
**"House of the Rising Sun" Guitar Part** : Hilton Valentine is credited with creating the distinctive arpeggiated guitar part for The Animals' arrangement of "House of the Rising Sun." **Post-Animals Career and Death** : After leaving the reformed Animals group in 2001, Hilton Valentine played briefly with Eric Burdon's Animals in the US before returning to his "first love," skiffle. He passed away four weeks prior to the podcast's recording. "Hilton Valentine moved to the US and played briefly with Birdon's animals after quitting Steele's, before returning to his first love, Skiffle. He died exactly four weeks ago today, and will be missed." ← Episode 115： ＂House of the Rising Sun＂ by the Animals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=34aIGCnb4X4
**Prior Band, The Wildcats** : Hilton Valentine was more experienced than other early Animals members, having previously performed in a rock and roll group called The Wildcats and even recorded an album with them, though copies are now difficult to locate. "Valentine was notably more experienced than the other members, and had previously performed in a rock and roll group called The Wildcats, not the same band who backed to Marty Wilde, and had even recorded an album with them" ← Episode 115： ＂House of the Rising Sun＂ by the Animals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=34aIGCnb4X4
