## curiosities
**Early Collaboration with Carole King** : Jerry Landis met Carole King while in college. They recorded demos for other writers together, although they never co-wrote songs. For some of these demos, they reused the name "The Co-Sounds." "Landis and King started working together, recording demos for other writers, they're never writing together." ← Episode 89： ＂Will You Love Me Tomorrow？＂ by the Shirelles | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Kns2HGcuSg
**Multiple Aliases and Real Identity** : After his brief collaboration with Carole King, Jerry Landis recorded under other names, including True Taylor and Paul Kane. He eventually reunited with his partner "Tom" and decided to work under their real names, revealing Jerry Landis to be Paul Simon, and "Tom" to be Art Garfunkel.
