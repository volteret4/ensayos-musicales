## curiosities
**Appearance in "Go Johnny Go" Film** : The Flamingos were featured in the Alan Freed film "Go Johnny Go," a production that also included performances by Richie Valens and other notable rock and roll artists. **Changing Lineup and Successors** : The group's membership was fluid; Curtis Williams left for the Hollywood Flames and then The Penguins, while Cornell Gunter and Richard Berry also departed, with the remaining members subsequently renaming themselves The Platters.
