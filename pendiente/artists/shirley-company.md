# artist - Shirley & Company

## songs
**Shame Shame Shame** : This proto-disco hit was written and produced by Sylvia Robinson for Shirley & Company in the late 1960s or early 1970s. It notably inspired David Bowie, John Lennon, and Carlos Alomar to rework their song "Footstump" into "Fame."
