# artist - Wayland Holyfield

## songs
**Could I Have This Dance (1980)** : Holyfield co-wrote this popular wedding song with Bill Hall. The inspiration came from Hall playing a song that included the line "Could I have this dance for the rest of my life," which Holyfield recognized as having potential. "Whalen Holyfield got one.
