# artist - Fred Neil

## genres
- Blues (early)
- Singer-songwriter (later)

## curiosities
**Early Folk Scene Organizer** : Fred Neil was a musician who, in the early 1960s, mostly played blues songs. He was responsible for organizing the bills for Bob Dylan's first gigs on the Greenwich Village "basket house" circuit.
