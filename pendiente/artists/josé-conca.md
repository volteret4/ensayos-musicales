# artist - José Conca

## curiosities
**DJ at Chocolate - Second Golden Age** : José Conca took over the DJ booth at Chocolate after Tony Vitale's departure in 1986. Under his guidance, Chocolate entered a "second golden age," characterized by a shift towards more electronic music and a refined mixing technique inspired by Fran Lenaers, who was revolutionizing the sound of Valencia at Spook Factory.
