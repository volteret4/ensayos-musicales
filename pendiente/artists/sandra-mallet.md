## songs
**Camel Walk (Year unknown)** : This was the first record on which The Van Dellers sang backing vocals, though it was credited to "Sandra Mallet and the Van Dellers." Mallet was a minor Motown singer, and the song, written by Barry Gordy, was an attempt to clone the hit "Locomotion."
