# artist - Barbara Gay

## songs
**My Boy Lollipop (1956)** : This original version of the song was recorded by Barbara Gay, a teenage girl managed by mobster Guy Tano Vastola, in 1956. The backing was provided by standard New York R&B session players. Although not a hit upon its initial release, it was later embraced by Jamaican soundmen due to its resemblance to the developing Ska style. ## curiosities
**Ellie Greenwich Connection** : Barbara Gay's "My Boy Lollipop" was significant enough that songwriter Ellie Greenwich briefly adopted the stage name Ellie Gay out of admiration for the record, before eventually deciding against it.
