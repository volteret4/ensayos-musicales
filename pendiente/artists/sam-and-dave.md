**I Can't Stand Up for Falling Down (unknown year)** : A previously obscure B-side from the 1960s that Elvis Costello and The Attractions covered and reinvented as an "ebullient rave-up." Their version became a number four UK hit and generated royalties for its black songwriters, Homer Banks and Alan Jones.
