# artist - Clams Casino

## curiosities
**Pioneer Producer for Triangle** : Clams Casino was one of the first producers to release music through the Triangle label. He later became a head producer for Saproki (A$AP Rocky), incorporating the dark, slow, and textured sounds of Witchhouse into mainstream success.
