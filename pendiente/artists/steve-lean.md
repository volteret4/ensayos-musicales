# artist - Steve Lean

## member of
- PXXR GANG

## instruments
- Autotune (used for vocal deformation)
- Cheap computers (for production)

## curiosities
**Co-founder of PXXR GANG** : Steve Lean, along with John Biff, Khaled, and K.D.K.in, founded PXXR GANG in El Raval, Barcelona. His production skills with cheap computers and heavy Auto-Tune were central to the group's sound. "Y una vez allí, junto a Steve Lin, fundan la banda que lo cambiará todo, la pur gang." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Early Producer for John Biff** : At the age of 16, Steve Lean, a young man from Barcelona, began producing tracks for John Biff. This early collaboration was a precursor to their joint work in PXXR GANG. "uno de ellos era un chaval de 16 años de Barcelona que se hacía llamar a Steve Lin, que no empezó a producir temas para John Biff." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Production Style** : Steve Lean was known for producing music on cheap computers with a significant use of Auto-Tune. This DIY approach and distinctive sound became a hallmark of the early Spanish trap scene. "hacían musicales de ordenadores baratos con mucha autotune." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
