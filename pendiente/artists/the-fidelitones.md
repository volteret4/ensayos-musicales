## curiosities
**Early Recordings with Barry Gordy** : Before joining Motown, Brian Holland and Freddie Gorman were members of The Fidelitones, who recorded several tracks for Aladdin in the late 1950s. These recordings were produced by Barry Gordy but were never officially released.
