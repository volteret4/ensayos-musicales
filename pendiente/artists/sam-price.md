## curiosities
**Strained Collaboration with Tharp** : Sam Price, a pianist, had a difficult working relationship with Sister Rosetta Tharp. He disliked her open tuning and non-jazz keys, insisting on a capo, and was initially against mixing gospel with his boogie-woogie style. Despite the success of "Strange Things Happening Every Day," he resented only receiving a flat fee.
