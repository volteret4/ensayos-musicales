# artist - Andy Scott

## curiosities
**Resentment of "Little Willie" Success** : As the guitarist for Sweet, Andy Scott stated in a 1978 interview that the band deliberately avoided touring the United States to support their hit "Little Willie." This was because the song became a hit in the US 18 months after its European release, and by then, the band was actively trying to evolve its image away from bubblegum.
