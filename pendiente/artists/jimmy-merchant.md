# artist - Jimmy Merchant

## member of
- The Teenagers

## curiosities
**Co-writer of "Why Do Fools Fall in Love"** : Jimmy Merchant helped Herman Santiago write "Why Do Fools Fall in Love" but was not credited on the song's original release. "Why the fools fall in love, was written by Herman Santiago, with the help of Jimmy Merchant." ← Episode 35： ＂Why Do Fools Fall In Love？＂ by Frankie Lymon and the Teenagers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AtZf9PJEttM
**Continued Performance** : Jimmy Merchant continued to perform with Herman Santiago in various lineups of The Teenagers until his retirement a few years prior to the podcast's recording. "But Santiago and Merchant continued, often done, with a lineup of the Teenagers." ← Episode 35： ＂Why Do Fools Fall In Love？＂ by Frankie Lymon and the Teenagers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AtZf9PJEttM
**Legal Battle for Song Credit** : In 1992, Merchant and Herman Santiago finally won legal credit for having written "Why Do Fools Fall in Love." However, this ruling was tragically overturned in 1996 because they were deemed to have waited too long to initiate legal action, causing the rights to revert to Frankie Lymon's estate and Morris Levy.
