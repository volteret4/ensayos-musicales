# artist - Stewart Levine

## curiosities
**Festival Co-Creator & Producer Role** : Stewart Levine, a native New Yorker, co-conceived Zaire 74 with Hugh Masekela, aiming to bring together African American and African performers in a "return to the beat." He later became a prominent producer, notably producing "Up Where We Belong" by Joe Cocker and Jennifer Warnes and Simply Red's 1982 hit "Holding Back the Years."
