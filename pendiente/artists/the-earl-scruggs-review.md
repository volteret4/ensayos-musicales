## albums

## songs
**Everybody Wants to Go to Heaven (1971)** : Performed at the Ozark Music Festival, this song was originally written by Don Nix and first recorded by blues artist Albert King in 1971. Although not traditional bluegrass, its performance by the Review demonstrated Earl's belief that the banjo could be used in all types of music.
