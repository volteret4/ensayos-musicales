## curiosities
**Accidental Creator of "Honky Tonk" Riff** : Billy Butler, Bill Doggett's guitarist, inadvertently initiated the creation of the hit song "Honky Tonk" when he started idly noodling an instrumental line on his bass strings between songs during a dance in Lima, Ohio. This unplanned riff sparked the band's improvisation and the audience's immediate response.
