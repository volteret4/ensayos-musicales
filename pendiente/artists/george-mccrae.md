# artist - George McCrae

## songs
**Rock Your Baby (1974)** : Inspired by "Rock the Boat" being played in Miami dance clubs, Harry Casey and Rick Finch wrote this song. They laid down the music tracks, then found George McCrae, a West Palm Beach native, to sing. McCrae, who had paused his singing career to manage his wife Gwen's, became known for his distinctive falsetto on the track. It reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100 the week after "Rock the Boat" on July 13, 1974, and later inspired John Lennon's "Whatever Gets You Thru the Night" and Abba's "Dancing Queen."
