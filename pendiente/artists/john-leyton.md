# artist - John Leyton

## songs
**Johnny's Remember Me (1961)** : Sung by John Leyton, this song became Joe Meek's first number one hit in 1961. The lyrics recount a chilling narrative of a young man being relentlessly pursued by the ghost of his deceased lover, contributing to its haunting and characteristic sound.
