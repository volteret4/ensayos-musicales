# artist - Mortiis

## members
- Håvard Ellefsen (Aar)

## genres
- Black Metal (abandoned)
- Dungeon Chiptune (laid foundations for)
- Dungeon Synth

## albums
**First Album (1993)** : This was the first album to incorporate the aesthetic and imagery of Black Metal but was entirely composed using synthesizers. It marked a turning point in the genre. "En 1993 lanzó su primer disco. ## curiosities
**Berlin School Influence** : Mortiis cited the Berlin School of electronic music and artists such as Tangerine Dream and Klaus Schulze as his main references, which is logical given their creation of layered, synthesizer-based textures to evoke spatial and futuristic journeys. "mortiscito a la escuela de Berlin y artistas como Tangier Indry, Mikraus Shullz, como sus principales referencias." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
**Departure from Black Metal** : After recording the first album with Emperor, Håvard Ellefsen abandoned Black Metal, expressing weariness with the genre's direction, to explore a more atmospheric and introspective musical path with his solo project, Mortiis. "Tras grabaron el primer disco con la banda, abandonando el Black Metal, cansado del rumbo que estaba tomando el genero, para explorar un camino más atmosférico e introspectivo." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
