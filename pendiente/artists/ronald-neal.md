# artist - Ronald Neal

## curiosities
**Classically Trained Actor** : Ronald Neal was a stage actor, performing in plays such as "Kiss Me Kate" and "A Streetcar Named Desire," and was considered "classically trained" before taking on the role of a cocaine dealer in the film "Superfly."
