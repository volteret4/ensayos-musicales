**Honesty at MTV Unplugged** : The MTV Unplugged session was possibly one of the most honest concerts of his career. Unlike many other concerts in 1993 where he needed to be drugged to perform, during Unplugged, he was suffering from severe withdrawal symptoms. His "miradas al infinito" throughout the performance were profoundly revealing of his true state, which the public was largely unaware of, as magazines continued to portray a screaming, smiling, energetic image.
