## curiosities
**Harmonic Influence on Brian Wilson (1963-1964)** : The sophisticated modern jazz harmonies of The Four Freshmen were a significant influence on Brian Wilson's musical development and vocal arrangements for The Beach Boys. This influence is particularly noticeable in the expanded vocal complexities on the "Surfer Girl" album and the advanced harmonic structure of songs like "The Warmth of the Sun," which transcended typical teen pop conventions. "This is harmonic sophistication on a totally different order from anything else that was being done in team pop music at the time.
