## curiosities
**Assessment of African American Country Stars (1975)** : In a 1975 article published in the Terahot Tribune, Regina Taylor identified three African Americans who she believed could be categorized as country music stars: Charlie Pride, Stoney Edwards, and O.B. McClinton.
