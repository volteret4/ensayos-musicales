**Baby Let Me Hold Your Hand (1964) - Influential B-Side** : This B-side single, produced by Bert Berns for Atlantic, featured a new and radically reworked arrangement of "Don't Turn My Clothes," adapted by Berns and Wes Farrell. It included an intro imitating Sam Cooke, followed by an upbeat, twist-flavored song with a distinctive guitar riff and Latin feel, serving as a musical blueprint for The Animals' "House of the Rising Sun."
