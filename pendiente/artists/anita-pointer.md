## curiosities
**"Country People" Self-Identification** : Anita Pointer, a member of the Pointer Sisters, stated, "We were country people," reflecting their upbringing in Arkansas where country music was prevalent in their home, on the radio, and in church. **Inspiration for "Fairy Tale"** : Anita Pointer's personal heartbreak over a married DJ inspired her and her sister Bonnie to write the country song "Fairy Tale," marking their entry into the country music industry. "Anita was dating a DJ and he neglected to tell her that he was married.
