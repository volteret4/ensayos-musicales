# artist - David Bellamy

## songs
**Spiders and Snakes (1974)** : David Bellamy is credited as co-writer of this Jim Stafford hit. He specifically wrote the lyrical phrase "I don't like spiders and snakes and that ain't what it takes to love me," which was inspired by a house with spiders. Stafford then wrote the accompanying story about Mary Lou.
