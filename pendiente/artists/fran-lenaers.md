# artist - Fran Lenaers

## curiosities
**Revolutionizing DJ Mixing in Valencia** : Fran Lenaers was instrumental in revolutionizing the sound of Valencia at Spook Factory, particularly through his innovative mixing technique. Before Lenaers, it was rare for DJs in discotheques to seamlessly mix songs, typically letting one track finish before starting the next; Lenaers changed this by pioneering continuous mixing. He profoundly influenced the musical direction of Spook Factory and, by extension, the Ruta, pushing towards more techno and EBM sounds.
