## albums
**Master Jam (1979)** : An LP by Rufus, produced by Quincy Jones, featuring Chaka Khan on vocals. Its lead single, "Do You Love What You Feel," became a major hit as 1979 turned into 1980. ## songs
**Do You Love What You Feel (1979/1980)** : The lead single from Rufus's LP *Master Jam*, which was produced by Quincy Jones and featured Chaka Khan. This "Yacht Soul adjacent" track achieved significant chart success, reaching number one on the R&B chart and number 30 on the Hot 100 as 1979 turned into 1980. ## curiosities
**Evolution from The American Breed** : The band Rufus evolved from The American Breed, who had recorded Larry Weiss's song "Bend Me, Shape Me." Rufus later became Rufus and Chaka Khan. "That was recorded by the American breed in 1968, which became Rufus, which then, yes, becomes Rufus of Rufus and Chaka Khan." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 2 - Countryish Music of the 1970s (Encore) | https://www.ftr70.com/
