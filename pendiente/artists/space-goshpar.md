## songs
**Filda Funk 1990 (2011)** : Released in 2011, this track marked Space Goshpar's definitive entry into the Phonk genre. It was part of his effort to create "authentic Miami funk" by blending the dirty, lo-fi aesthetics of Memphis with the "narcotic slowness" of Houston, while incorporating a modern, digital sensibility influenced by West Coast G-Funk. ## curiosities
**Phonk Co-Creator** : In 2011, this Miami-based producer and rapper, along with his collective Raider Clan, developed a new sound by combining the "dirty and lo-fi aesthetics" of Memphis rap with the "narcotic slowness" of Houston's Chop 'N' Screw. He infused this blend with a modern, digital sensibility, influenced by West Coast G-Funk, naming it "authentic Miami funk."
