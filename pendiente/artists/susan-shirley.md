# artist - Susan Shirley

## songs
**True Love and Apple Pie (Pre-1971)** : This was the early version of the melody that songwriters Roger Cook and Roger Greenaway initially composed for singer Susan Shirley. This melody would later be adapted with new lyrics to become "I'd Like to Teach the World to Sing" for the iconic Coca-Cola commercial.
