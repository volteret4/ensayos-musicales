## curiosities
**Groundbreaking Style and Confidence** : LaBelle was recognized as a groundbreaking group that pushed boundaries with their innovative costumes, dynamic stage shows, and assertive, confident presence. Their style represented a significant departure from earlier, more constrained female groups like Diana Ross and The Supremes.
