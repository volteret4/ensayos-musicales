# artist - Johnny Pacheco

## curiosities
**Creator of Fania All-Stars (1971)** : In 1971, Johnny Pacheco formed a supergroup called Fania All-Stars, bringing together the leading stars of the Fania label. This collective became instrumental in popularizing salsa worldwide. "En 1971, Johnny Pachico creó un super grupo con las estrellas del sillo al que llamó fanía Oles Tars." ← LA FANIA. **Focus on Artistic Direction** : Pacheco eventually sold his share of Fania Records to Jerry Masucci, opting to dedicate himself exclusively to artistic direction. This decision allowed Masucci to become the sole owner of the label. "Masuichi, quien para entonces, llega el único propietario de la fans y a transcomprarles su parte a Johnny Pacheco, que prefería dedicarse exclusivamente a la dirección artística." ← LA FANIA. **Founding Pacheco y su charanga (1960)** : In 1960, Pacheco established his own group, Pacheco y su charanga. This ensemble achieved immediate success with its first album, leading to international tours. "En 1960 fundó su propio grupo, Pacheco y su charanga, con el que logró un éxito mediato entre su primer disco y puedo realizar giras por todo el mundo." ← LA FANIA. **Motivation to Found Fania Records** : Pacheco became dissatisfied with the minimal royalties he received from existing record labels. Additionally, he wasn't particularly interested in the then-fashionable boogaloo genre. This led him to decide to create his own label to release his music, despite lacking the necessary capital. "Pronto se dio cuenta que las regalías que recibía eran mínimas y, además, tampoco sentía mucha tracción por las bandas de búgalo, el género de moda. Así que decidió crear su propiesa y yo para poder editar su música." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Partnership with Jerry Masucci** : Lacking capital for his label, Pacheco proposed a partnership to his lawyer, Jerry Masucci, an Italian-American ex-policeman. This collaboration led to the founding of Fania Records. "Por eso, le propuso su abogado Jerry Maswichi, un expolicía ítolo americano que se asociaran para montar el negocio." ← LA FANIA. This background gave him a strong foundation for his later endeavors. "Johnny Pacheco ya era un veterano de las orquestas nollorquinas de los años 50." ← LA FANIA.
