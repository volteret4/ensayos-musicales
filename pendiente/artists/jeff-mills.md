**Integration of Roland TR-909** : Jeff Mills pioneered the use of a Roland TR-909 drum machine in his live sets. This introduced a new dimension to DJing, as he began generating sounds with his own equipment concurrently while spinning records, moving beyond just playing music or turntablism. **Pioneering Three and Four Deck DJing** : Jeff Mills made a significant innovation by being the first DJ to transition from using two turntables to three and even four turntables in his sets. This marked a complex process where two turntables would simultaneously play sound, with another waiting.
