# artist - Purple Motion

## member of
- The Future Crew

## curiosities
**Orchestral Arranger** : Purple Motion gained a reputation for achieving orchestral density using only 8 channels in his compositions. Today, he is a respected symphonic composer often called upon to create orchestral arrangements for major game franchises like Final Fantasy and Metal Gear. "Por otro lado, Parpe el Motion, que tenía fama de lograr una densidador que está al condensolo de 8 canales, hoy en día se ha convertido en un respeto de compositor sinfónico a que suelen llamar para hacer arreglos orquestales de franquicias como Final Fantasy o Metal Gear." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
**Second Reality Music Composer** : Purple Motion composed music for the "Second Reality" demo by the Finnish group Future Crew, presented at the 1993 Assembly. This demo pushed the boundaries of what was believed possible on a PC, featuring morphing faces and rotating vectorial environments, earning a standing ovation. "La música, compuesta por Escaven y Parpele Motion, sonadas intronizadas con efectos que son las que creían posible en un PC." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
