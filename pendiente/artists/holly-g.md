## curiosities
**Black Opry Founder on Country Music's Origins (2022)** : Holly G, founder of Black Opry, an organization dedicated to promoting Black Country artists, stated in 2022 that black people invented country music and are consistently told it is not for them. She views this as an "American problem," where black people create an art form or industry and are subsequently pushed out of it. "I get told all the time that country music isn't for black people, but the problem is that we invented it." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 53 - More than Charley Pride - African Americans in 70s Country | https://www.ftr70.com/
