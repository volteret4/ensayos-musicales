# artist - Eddie Floyd

## songs
**Knock on Wood (1960s)** : David Bowie covered this 60s Stax classic by Eddie Floyd on his 1974 concert album, 'David Live'. This cover was part of Bowie's embrace of a "Soulman" persona, with his band rearranging songs and vocalizing like an R&B crooner.
