## songs
**Needles and Pins (1963) - Original Recording, Canada #1** : Jackie DeShannon recorded this song in early 1963, backed by members of The Wrecking Crew. It reached the lower reaches of the US Hot 100 but made number one in Canada. She insists she co-wrote the song with Sonny Bono and Jack Nitzsche, though she was uncredited. ## curiosities
**Original "Needles and Pins" Structure Flaw** : DeShannon's original version of "Needles and Pins" contained only about 90 seconds of actual song, ending with a minute of vamping with placeholder lyrics that didn't work well for her record. "Dishanan's original, was really only about 90 seconds worth of actual song in Needles and pins, and Dishanan's version ends with a minute or so of vamping." ← Episode 113： ＂Needles and Pins＂ by The Searchers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=izC9ZBI0UHM
**Songwriter Partnership with Sharon Sheely** : Jackie DeShannon formed a writing partnership with Sharon Sheely, who was Eddie Cochran's girlfriend and was with him in the fatal car crash. "A singer-songwriter who had started up a writing partnership with Sharon Sheely, the writer who had been Eddie Cockburn's girlfriend, and in the fatal car crash with him." ← Episode 113： ＂Needles and Pins＂ by The Searchers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=izC9ZBI0UHM
**Uncredited Authorship of "Needles and Pins"** : DeShannon maintains she co-wrote "Needles and Pins" with Sonny Bono and Jack Nitzsche, believing her name was omitted from the credits due to their alleged abusive and misogynistic nature.
