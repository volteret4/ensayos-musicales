## curiosities
**Meeting with Kefta Boyz (2013)** : In April 2013, Los Corredores del Bloque, a Madrid-based rap collective, performed a concert with Malakay Split in Las Áreas Las Matas de Barcelona. Among the attendees were Kefta Boyz, who had traveled to Barcelona specifically to meet them. This meeting facilitated crucial connections between the two scenes. "En abril de 2013, los corredores del bloque, el colectivo de rap, Madrid leño del cual saldrían que y D.K. no es Rael B, entre otros, tienen un concierto junto a Malacayesplit, otro grupo o Barcelona es afín, en las áreas las matas de Barcelona." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Members as Pioneers** : This Madrid collective included future trap pioneers like K.D.K.in and Raël B, who would go on to have significant impact on the Spanish trap scene.
