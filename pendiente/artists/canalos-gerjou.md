# artist - Canalos Gerjou

## curiosities
**Creator of the First Viral Soundfont Song** : Canalos Gerjou is credited with creating "Never Mint 64," a soundfont version of American Football's "Never Meant," which achieved viral status and became the first widely popular soundfont song. This track significantly inspired other creators, including Un For World. "La primera canción volverse viral fue Deber Mint 64, una versión de Neber Mint de la banda M a Medican Football.
