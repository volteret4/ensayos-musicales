## songs
**Suavecito (1972)** : Released in April 1972, this song was Molo's only hit, reaching number 18 on the Billboard Pop Chart. Felix Contreras suggested that mainstream radio at the time only had room for one Latin rock band, a slot already occupied by Jorge's brother Carlos Santana.
