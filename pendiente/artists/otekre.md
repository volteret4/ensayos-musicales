# artist - Otekre

## labels
- Warp Records

## albums
**Artificial Intelligence (1992)** : Featured on the groundbreaking Warp Records compilation, which aimed to bring the spirit of rave music into homes and establish a new, more contemplative understanding of electronic music, giving birth to the IDM genre. Otekre contributed to this compilation with their distinct, experimental sound. "Otekre llevará la música territorios más experimentales y abstractos, obsesionados con la geometría tanto en el diseño como en el sonido." ← Qué fue el IDM y por qué cambió la música electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RTwn3hP1XTg

## curiosities
**Experimental and Abstract Approach** : Their musical output delves into experimental and abstract territories, characterized by an intense obsession with geometry evident in both their sound design and overall aesthetic. Their work is described as one of the most radical and least "human" propositions within Warp Records' catalog.
