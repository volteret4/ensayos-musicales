# artist - Thom Yorke (as "Tom York")

## albums
**Tomorrow's Modern Boxes (Economic Re-edition) (December 8, 2017)** : An economic re-edition of Thom Yorke's album "Tomorrow's Modern Boxes," referred to as "tumoros modern boxes" in the transcript, is being released on December 8th. While the album is still available, its previous price was high. The new version, expected to cost around 25-30 euros, will be identical in terms of the white vinyl, its weight, and quality, but will omit the plastic sleeve with text and other "parafernalia," offering a more accessible option without compromising the disc's quality.
