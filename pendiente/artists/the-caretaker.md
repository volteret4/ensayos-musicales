# artist - The Caretaker

## albums
**Everywhere at the End of Time (2016-2019) - Dementia Simulation Series** : This project, released under the alias Kirby Kear, is a series of six albums. Across these discs, the artist collects blurry old orchestral songs and progressively deteriorates them through layers of noise, echoes, and other effects, simulating the effects of dementia. While the melodies on the first disc are still recognizable despite being blurry, by the sixth and final album, melody is completely absent, as everything has become blurred, symbolizing the patient's approach to death. "En ellos recogen unas viejas canciones orquestales antiguas borrosas que paulatinamente, disco a disco, se van deteriorando, mediante capas de ruido, ecos y otros efectos para simular los efectos de la demencia." ← El Género Musical Más Escalofriante de Internet. **Selected Memories for the Haunted Ballroom (1999) - Debut Album** : This was The Caretaker's first album, inspired by the ballroom scene from Stanley Kubrick's "El Resplandor" (The Shining). It reinterprets old, ghostly melodies from a ballroom where everyone has died, and only a photograph remains as a memory. The album is an early example of hauntology, aiming to evoke a nostalgia for something the listener has never truly experienced. "Selected Memories for the Haunted Ballroom, un disco que reinterpreta cómo sonaban esas viejas melodías fantasmales de ese salón, donde ya todo el mundo ha muerto y tan solo queda una foto como recuerdo." ← El Género Musical Más Escalofriante de Internet. ## curiosities
**Alter Ego's Metaphorical Death** : Following the completion of the *Everywhere at the End of Time* series, Leland Kirby metaphorically "killed" his most well-known alter ego, The Caretaker. This act coincided with a significant surge in popularity for The Caretaker's music, particularly online, leading to numerous analyses, reflections, and sensationalist videos on YouTube. "Tras eso, Leland mató a su más conocido alter ego de Kirteiker." ← El Género Musical Más Escalofriante de Internet. Hauntología | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2jcvyJLgYcg
**TikTok Challenge Phenomenon** : The *Everywhere at the End of Time* series spawned a viral trend on TikTok known as the "Kirteiker Challenge." This challenge involved users listening to all the albums in the series consecutively and posting their reactions. As a result, the artist's work inadvertently adopted a creepypasta aesthetic, with his music frequently used to accompany short videos on platforms like TikTok featuring liminal aesthetics, dreamcore, weirdcore, and analog horror, which introduced his work to a broad young audience. "En TikTok suyo al Kirteiker Challenge, que consistían escucharlosis, discos del tirón y postear tu reacción." ← El Género Musical Más Escalofriante de Internet.
