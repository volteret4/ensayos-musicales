# artist - Flatt and Scruggs

## songs
**The Ballad of Jed Clampett (1962)** : This song served as the theme to the popular CBS television show "The Beverly Hillbillies," which ran from 1962 until 1971. Earl Scruggs noted in a 1979 interview that he believed this theme song introduced bluegrass music to many people not just across the country, but around the world.
