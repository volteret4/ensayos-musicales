# artist - Edward Seaga

## genres
- Mento (incorporation into R&B)
- R&B (producer)

## songs
**Come on Manioe** : Edward Seaga produced this R&B record by Higgs & Wilson, which is considered the first milestone in Ska's development. He conceived the idea of incorporating a Mento rhythm by instructing the guitarist to stress the off-beats, making the record sound uniquely Jamaican.
