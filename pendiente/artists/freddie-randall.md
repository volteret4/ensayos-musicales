## instruments

## curiosities
**US Tour as Union Exchange (1955)** : Freddie Randall, a trumpet player from Devon, was allowed to tour the US as part of a "one-in, one-out" exchange agreement between the American and British musicians' unions established in 1955. His US tour was a direct condition for Louis Armstrong being permitted to tour the UK.
