# artist - Bernadjema

## curiosities
**Psycho Shower Scene Score - Avant-Garde Strings** : Bernadjema (Bernard Herrmann) composed the score for the iconic shower scene in *Psycho*, employing all violins to create a screeching and piercing sonic movement. This innovative approach positioned the score closer to contemporary musical avant-garde than the conventional film music of its era, drawing comparisons to John Cage rather than Max Steiner. It is widely considered one of the most famous and imitated film scores in history.
