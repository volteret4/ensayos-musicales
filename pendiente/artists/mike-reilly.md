**Advocating "Midwestern Sound"** : Mike Reilly, as of June 2021, continues to resist the "country rock" label for Pure Prairie League, preferring to define their music as the "midwestern sound." This label reflects their diverse musical background in Cincinnati, which included jazz, blues, country, bluegrass, R&B, and soul, and their wide-ranging influences from The Beatles to Lonnie Mack, as well as The Byrds and Poco.
