# artist - Johnny Little John

## members
- Jerry Lee Lewis (piano, drums)
- Johnny Little John (bass)
- Paul Whitehead (violin, accordion, piano)

## songs
**Whole Lotta Shakin' Going On (1957 - early version)** : Johnny Little John sang lead on a version of "Whole Lotta Shakin' Going On" that was significantly different from earlier recordings, playing it faster and incorporating a spoken section where he encouraged the audience to "stand in one spot and wiggle around just a little bit." This performance was a direct inspiration for Jerry Lee Lewis's hit version. ## curiosities
**Elvis-like Style** : Johnny Little John, a bassist who toured with Jerry Lee Lewis and Paul Whitehead, had a style that reminded Jerry Lee of Elvis, which made Lewis jealous.
