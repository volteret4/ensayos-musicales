# artist - Eiffel 65

## curiosities
**Italian Eurodance Act (Late 1990s)** : Eiffel 65, an Italian band, was among the Eurodance projects that emerged during the genre's final, self-parodying phase. Their work became memorable for its exaggerated style, contributing to the "meme fodder" in the quest for the "cheesiest" sound.
