## genres

## curiosities
**Free Jazz and Funk Infusion** : The Pop Group introduced free jazz improvisation into punk, maintaining a constant funk presence in their music, similar to The Slits. They explored the more danceable aspect of post-punk, emphasizing bass and rhythm.
