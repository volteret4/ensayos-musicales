## curiosities
**Joining and Rejoining The Famous Flames** : Bobby Bird was a key member of James Brown's early groups, starting with The Gospel Starlighters and evolving into The Famous Flames. He quit along with the rest of the band after the initial success of "Please Please Please" due to credit issues but rejoined "fairly shortly afterwards" and stayed with Brown for many more years, also rejoining prior to the Apollo residency after another period away. **Meeting James Brown in Detention** : Bobby Bird met James Brown during a baseball game between inmates and outsiders at the juvenile detention center where Brown was incarcerated. Discovering Brown's singing talent and reputation as "Music Box," Bird was impressed enough to help Brown secure an early release from his sentence by speaking on his behalf at a parole hearing.
