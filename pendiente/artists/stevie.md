# artist - Stevie

## instruments

## curiosities
**Unique Drumming Style** : Stevie possesses a drumming style that is highly unique and instantly recognizable. Her rhythm in playing drums perfectly matches the way she sings, described as having a strong "pocket" that cannot be imitated, and a "churchy" and full sound.
