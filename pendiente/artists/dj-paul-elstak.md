## curiosities
**Gabber Pioneer and Label Head** : DJ Paul Elstak was fundamental in defining and popularizing the Gabber sound to the young Dutch audience in the early 1990s. He achieved this through his productions and his influential label, Rotterdam Records, which played a crucial role in the genre's establishment and dissemination.
