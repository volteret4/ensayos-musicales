# artist - Winony Harris

## songs
**Round the Clock Blues (Pre-1958)** : This old song by Winony Harris served as the inspiration for "Rock Around the Clock" and was later slightly reworked by Chuck Berry for his song "Real in a Rockin'." It was also recorded by Big Joe Turner with Johnny Otis's band.
