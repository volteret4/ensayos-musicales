# artist - The Duces of Rhythm

## curiosities
**Recording with Little Richard** : The Duces of Rhythm were a group that recorded alongside Little Richard and The Tempo Toppers for Peacock Records in early 1953. They recorded four tracks, issued as two singles, which did not achieve success.
