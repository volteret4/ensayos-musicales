# artist - Don Cornelius

## concerts
- **Soul Train (November 1975) - Host** : Don Cornelius hosted Soul Train, the televised dance show, where David Bowie was invited to appear in November 1975. Cornelius interviewed Bowie, who was among the first white performers on the show, just months after Elton John's appearance. "In November of 75, Boey was invited on Soul Train, the televised dance show hosted by Don Cornelius."
