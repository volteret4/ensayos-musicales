**The Power (1990)** : This song was one of Snap!'s breakthrough hits, released after "Pump Up The Jam," and was key in emulating and popularizing the Eurodance formula. The vocals for the track were notably provided by rapper Otur Bobby, an ex-military individual. ## curiosities
**Frankfurt Production Duo** : Snap! comprised two producers from Frankfurt, Germany, who originated from the city's EBM and Newbeat scenes. They applied their background in electronic music to develop the Eurodance sound. "Con un lado Snap eran dos productores de Frankfurt que venían de la escena Ebime y el Newbeet." ← La gran mentira detrás del Eurodance | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_F34L8wfrdo
**Producer-Rapper Formula** : Snap! was a pioneer in establishing a standard Eurodance formula where producers, needing charisma for television, recruited rappers. These rappers, often American ex-military personnel found in clubs, were signed for low fees to front the act, providing a touch of "American credibility" to the music for broader appeal. "Snap eran dos productores de Frankfurt que venían de la escena Ebime y el Newbeet. [...] necesitaban el carisma de una estrella, así que las crearon." ← La gran mentira detrás del Eurodance | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_F34L8wfrdo
