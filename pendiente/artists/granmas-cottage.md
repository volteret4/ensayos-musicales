# artist - Granmas Cottage

## genres
- Coldysynth (Cowsysynth)

## curiosities
**Recreation of Grandmother's House Atmosphere** : Granmas Cottage's music aims to evoke the cozy feeling of being at a grandmother's house, sitting by the fireplace, eating freshly baked cookies, and drinking hot tea while it rains outside. This artistic goal is closely related to the internet trend "Kotashkor." "Su música, busca recrear esa sensación de estar en casa de tabuelas sentado junto a la chimenea, comiendo galletas recién orneadas y tomándote caliente mientras llueve afuera." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
