# artist - Monica

## songs
**The Boy Is Mine (1998)** : This duet with Brandy was the winner of the 1998 Billboard Song of the Summer and was the most dominant Song of the Summer of the 90s, achieving 13 weeks at number one between June and August 1998. The song "maxed out on Sass" and, with production by Rodney Jerkins, "lilted and fluttered like a butterfly circling over a hammock."
