## curiosities
**Pioneer of Bass Line Style** : Jimmy Yancey was a Boogie Woogie pianist who specialized in a particular bass line style that became a foundational element of R&B, especially in New Orleans. This bass line directly influenced Fats Domino's piano bass lines and was later named in Meade Lux Lewis's famous "Yancey Special." **Testimony in Copyright Case** : In the copyright infringement lawsuit between Meade Lux Lewis and Sunni Thompson regarding a similar bass line, Jimmy Yancey testified that he had originated and developed the bass line style long before Lewis. His testimony contributed to the dismissal of Lewis's claim.
