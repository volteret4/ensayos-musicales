# artist - Kike Jaén

## curiosities
**DJ and Architect of "Cultura de Parking" at N.O.D.** : Kike Jaén was the DJ at N.O.D. and the primary figure responsible for establishing the "Cultura de Parking" phenomenon, which involved large gatherings and parties in the club's parking lot. His role was crucial in making N.O.D. a hub for this unique social trend, where attendees would invest heavily in car sound systems and create their own parallel parties. "Rageantaran los platos por Kikaien, con la principal responsable de la cultura de Parking, algo similar a lo que se conoce hoy en día como un botellón." ← Cuando Valencia fue la capital mundial del Clubbing | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ckJgog8P_og
