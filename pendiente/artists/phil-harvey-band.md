## curiosities
**Composed of Former Moon Dogs Members** : The band was assembled by Phil Specter from members of The Moon Dogs, a band Marshall Leib (Specter's best friend) had been in previously. Members included guitarist Elliott Ingber, bass player Larry Taylor, keyboard player Howard Hirsch, and drummer Rudd Schaefer. Kim Fowley, observing their single performance, noted some of these musicians for future collaborations. **One-Gig Band Concept** : Formed by Phil Specter (under the pseudonym Phil Harvey) to promote his solo instrumental single, this band played only one gig. Its concept was a unique mix of Dwayne Eddy's style rock guitar instrumentals and complex jazz, with the members all dressed as mobsters.
