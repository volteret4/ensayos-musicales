## songs
**Fancy (1991)** : Reba McIntyre's cover of Bobby Gentry's "Fancy" in 1991 is widely known. She frequently performs it live, including a notable performance at the CMA Awards in 2019. Her signature stage move involves starting the song in a raggedy coat and dramatically shedding it to reveal a sparkling red gown, symbolizing the character's journey of survival and empowerment.
