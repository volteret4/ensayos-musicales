# artist - Barry Mann

## songs
**We Gotta Get Out of This Place (Own Version Shelved)** : Barry Mann co-wrote "We Gotta Get Out of This Place" with Cynthia Weil. Although originally for The Righteous Brothers, Mann decided to record his own version. However, The Animals heard his piano demo and released their version first, leading to Mann's version being shelved. ## curiosities
**Spector's Contribution to "You've Lost That Lovin' Feeling"** : Barry Mann asserted that Phil Spector contributed the middle section to the song "You've Lost That Lovin' Feeling." This statement highlights a specific, and potentially more substantial, songwriting contribution by Spector than some of his other co-writers often acknowledged.
