# artist - Thelma Blackmon

## songs
**I Gotta Know (1956)** : Thelma Blackmon, the mother of one of Wanda Jackson's school friends, wrote this song after Jackson discussed her desire to move into rockabilly. Jackson found the song "perfect" for restarting her career, and it became a significant country hit for her.
