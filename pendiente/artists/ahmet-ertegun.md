## curiosities
**Atlantic Records Owner and "Mess Around" Contribution** : Ahmet Ertegun, the owner of Atlantic Records, is credited as a writer for "Mess Around" and genuinely contributed to its songwriting, which was a rarity for record executives who often took credit for others' work. He initiated the song's creation by asking Ray Charles to play piano in Pete Johnson's style, then contributed new words based on traditional floating lyrics to Charles's rendition of "Cow Cow Blues."
