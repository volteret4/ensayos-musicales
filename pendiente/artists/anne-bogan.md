## curiosities
**"General Utility Singer" at Motown** : Anne Bogan was a versatile vocalist known as a "general utility singer" around Motown. She sang with The Andantes and The Changes 3, performed duets with Harvey Fuqua, and even toured as a substitute for Tammi Terrell with Marvin Gaye.
