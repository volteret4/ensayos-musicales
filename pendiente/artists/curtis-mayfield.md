## curiosities
**Black Music for Black People** : Curtis Mayfield articulated a clear philosophy regarding his music's intended audience and purpose. He stated that black musicians were primarily creating "black music for black people," implying that commercial crossover to white audiences, while sometimes occurring, was not the main objective. This perspective reflected the Black Power movement's emphasis on self-determination and creating art that served the black community first. "Curtis Mayfield said that black musicians were making black music for black people and if it crossed over it did.
