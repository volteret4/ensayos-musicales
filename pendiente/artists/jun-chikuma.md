# artist - Jun Chikuma

## albums
**Bomberman Hero Soundtrack (1995)** : Jun Chikuma, known for composing music for Bomberman games since their NES debut in 1995, created the soundtrack for Bomberman Hero on Nintendo 64. Leveraging the improved sound quality and fewer technical limitations of the N64, the album primarily featured drum and bass but also explored elements of psychedelia, jazz, and lounge music.
