# artist - Ross McManus

## curiosities
**Ambition for Hit Parade Success** : In Elvis Costello's own recollection, his father Ross "would try anything to get over on the hit parade," exemplified by a "delightfully cheesy, televised ring-a-ding-ding performance" in 1963 of the Pete Seeger folk-standard "If I Had a Hammer," and his work recording jingles for TV adverts.
