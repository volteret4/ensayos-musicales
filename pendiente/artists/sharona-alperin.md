# artist - Sharona Alperin

## curiosities
**Inspiration for "My Sharona" (1979)** : Sharona Alperin was the 17-year-old inspiration for The Knack's hit song "My Sharona," when Doug Fieger, then 25, first saw her. She later dated Fieger from the age of 19 to 21, describing the period touring with The Knack as "magical." "Sharona is a real person.
