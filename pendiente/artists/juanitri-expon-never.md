# artist - Juanitri Expon-Never

## curiosities
**Good Time Soundtrack - Unconventional and Intense Score** : Juanitri Expon-Never (Oneohtrix Point Never) elevated the use of ambient and textural music to new levels for the soundtrack of *Good Time*, the film by the Safdie brothers. Influenced by figures like Tangier Indrimi (Tangerine Dream) and Vanjelly (Vangelis), this score was shaped through "Lopatin's anagogic filter," delivering moments of serene beauty alongside disquieting intensity. The soundtrack demonstrates the necessity of breaking with conventional approaches to create a truly unique experience. **Lopatin's Anagogic Filter - Unique Artistic Process** : Juanitri Expon-Never's soundtrack for *Good Time* was notably "molded through Lopatin's anagogic filter." This artistic process, drawing inspiration from artists such as Tangier Indrimi and Vanjelly, enabled the creation of a score that features both serene beauty and unsettling intensity, contributing to its unique and unconventional character.
