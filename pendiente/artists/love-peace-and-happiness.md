## curiosities
**Formation After Marvelettes Split** : Anne Bogan, who had been a utility singer at Motown and replaced Gladys Horton in The Marvelettes, joined Love Peace and Happiness after The Marvelettes dissolved. The group later merged with Harvey Fuqua to form the 17-piece funk band New Birth.
