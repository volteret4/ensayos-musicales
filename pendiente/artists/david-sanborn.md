# artist - David Sanborn

## albums
**Young Americans (1975) - Saxophonist** : David Sanborn was one of the new collaborators on David Bowie's 1975 album 'Young Americans', contributing saxophone to the album's "Plastic Soul" sound. His involvement was part of Bowie's deep dive into American R&B for the first time since the mid-60s.
