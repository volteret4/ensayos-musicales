**Influence on Chuck Berry's Guitar Style** : T-Bone Walker, a renowned blues guitarist, directly influenced Chuck Berry's innovative guitar playing. Berry learned from Walker the technique of playing solo lines on two strings simultaneously, which created a thicker sound with parallel harmonies and became a defining characteristic of Berry's "Johnny B.
