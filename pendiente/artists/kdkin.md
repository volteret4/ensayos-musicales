# artist - K.D.K.in

## member of
- Los Corredores del Bloque
- PXXR GANG

## songs
**No me identifico (2011)** : This YouTube track was published on April 14, 2011, by the rapper from Comet. With this song, K.D.K.in made it clear that he did not identify with the existing Spanish hip-hop scene and definitively detached himself from it. "Con ello, ponía de manifiesto que no se se se enciera identificado con esa escena y se deslegaba definitivamente de ella." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Perdedores del barrio** : This song was K.D.K.in's swift response to C. Tangana's track "No te pegarás" from the album "Ídolo." K.D.K.in uploaded the video just a few days after C. Tangana's diss, delivering a famous line and reminding C. Tangana that he had no style before becoming famous. "Caíde caí, tardó tan solo unos días en subir el videoperdedores del barrio, donde les soltó la frase mítica. Y le recordó que antes de ser famoso, no tenía estiva." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s

## curiosities
**"No te pegarás" Diss from C. Tangana** : C. Tangana indirectly challenged PXXR GANG and K.D.K.in in his song "No te pegarás" from the album "Ídolo," criticizing them for repetitive ideas. K.D.K.in quickly responded with his own video, "Perdedores del barrio." "C tangana tiraba una pollita a la purga en cuando dice, o si vais a quedar con todo, lo repetíáis como los horos, todo lo mismo como bobos." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Artistic and Commercial Differences at Primavera Sound 2018** : At the Primavera Sound festival in 2018, K.D.K.in participated in a discussion with C. Tangana and Bad Gyal. This debate highlighted significant artistic, commercial, and ideological differences between K.D.K.in (representing Granada's street-oriented trap) and C. Tangana (representing Madrid's more business-minded approach). "Y se dejaron patentes las diferencias de criterio artístico, comercial y diológico ante el madrileño y el gran adino." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Confrontation with Enry-K** : During a concert in 2014, K.D.K.in slapped Enry-K, a member of the group N.E.N.O. DE CLICCO. The tension arose because Enry-K reportedly stopped speaking to K.D.K.in after the latter gained fame, despite their previous collaborations. "La atención estalló cuando Caíde Caín les soltó un guantazo a Enwise, uno de sus miembros." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Move to El Raval** : After friendly encounters between Los Corredores del Bloque and Kefta Boyz in Barcelona, K.D.K.in left Madrid and settled in El Raval. This neighborhood functioned as the "true Spanish trap house" and was where he co-founded the band PXXR GANG with John Biff, Khaled, and Steve Lean. "K.D.K.in de jamas, R.I.T. y se instalan todos en el rival, que funcionará como la verdadera trap house española." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Spaghetti Video After Confrontation** : The day after the confrontation with Enry-K, K.D.K.in and John Biff uploaded a video to YouTube calmly explaining the slap while eating a plate of spaghetti. This incident became a legendary moment in Spanish trap history. "Al día siguiente, Caíde y Unbef subieron un video explicando el tortazo con total calma mientras se comían un plato de espaguetis." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
