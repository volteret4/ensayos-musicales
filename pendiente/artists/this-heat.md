## curiosities
**Extreme Experimentation** : Described as potentially the "best band in history," This Heat (likely "Deshid" in the transcript) manipulated magnetic tapes and mechanical noises, employing techniques from concrete music. Their experimentation was so extreme that they almost invented several genres along the way, as evidenced by a song from 1979. "Deshid, la futisiva mejor banda de la historia, manipolevan cintas magnéticas y ruidos mecánicos, técnicas de la música concreta, pero llevaron esa experimentación tan al extremo que casi inventan varios géneros por el camino." ← La traición que salvó al punk.
