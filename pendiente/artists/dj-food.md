# artist - DJ Food

## curiosities
**Sampling 60s Soundtracks and Jazz** : As a producer, DJ Food, along with Nightmares on Wax and Funky Porcini, creatively sampled "pizzaz" (coolest/most stylish) 60s soundtracks and jazz, layering them over looped beats, showcasing a brilliant and revolutionary use of the sampler.
