# artist - Verdean White

## member of

## curiosities
**Bassist and Founding Member** : Verdean White is the brother of Maurice White and was retained as the bassist when Maurice disbanded the initial Earth Wind & Fire lineup in 1973, before signing a new deal with Columbia Records.
