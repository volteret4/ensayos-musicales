# artist - Berry Gordy

## labels
- Motown

## curiosities
**Granting Stevie Wonder Unprecedented Freedom** : Berry Gordy, whose Motown label was known as a "hit factory" rather than a "think tank," gave Stevie Wonder unprecedented freedom and a "truckload of cash" to create "Songs in the Key of Life," allowing Wonder to put his life experience into the music. **Renaming Musical Groups** : Berry Gordy, president of Motown, renamed The Supremes to "Diana Ross and The Supremes" to highlight Diana Ross as the star, a practice he also applied to The Miracles when they became "Smokey Robinson and the Miracles."
