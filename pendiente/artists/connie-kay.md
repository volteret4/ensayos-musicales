# artist - Connie Kay

## instruments

## curiosities
**Influential Backbeat Drumming** : As the drummer from the Modern Jazz Quartet, Connie Kay played a distinct "BAP, BAP" snare drum part on Big Joe Turner's "Shake, Rattle and Roll." This drumming, while not subtle, is considered highly influential for early rock and roll, providing a significant forward momentum to the track.
