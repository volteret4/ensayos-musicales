# artist - Cass Elliott

## concerts
- **George McGovern Benefit Concert (1972)** : Cass Elliott was one of the famous people who served as an usher at Warren Beatty's benefit concert for George McGovern in Los Angeles on April 5th, 1972. "He masterminded everything from the invitations to getting famous people as ushers like Jack Nicholson, Bert Lancaster, Cass Elliott I remember."
- **The Midnight Special Pilot (1972)** : Cass Elliott was a guest on the 90-minute pilot of *The Midnight Special* in August 1972, where she discussed the importance of voter registration with host John Denver and performed "Leaving on a Jet Plane" with him. "Here's Denver and Cass Elliott. You know how it is. You know I'm really glad you're here tonight because this is about voter registration."
