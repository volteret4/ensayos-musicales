# artist - Silver Bullet Band

## curiosities
**Early Collaborations** : Members of the Silver Bullet Band provided assistance on Bob Seger's eighth studio album, "Beautiful Loser," released in 1975, specifically contributing to a cover of Ike and Tina Turner's "Nutbush City Limits." This collaboration occurred before the band gained widespread recognition outside of Michigan.
