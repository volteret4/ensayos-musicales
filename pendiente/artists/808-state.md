## curiosities
**Early Local House Music (1987-1988)** : 808 State (referred to phonetically as "886") is mentioned as one of the first groups from Manchester to produce House music, with publications appearing between 1987 and 1988. This marked the emergence of a local generation of House music within the city. "al final en 1988, 87, 88 salen los primeros discos o los primeros grupos de música House de Manchester que hacían música House, tenemos aquí el 886" ← Music Radar Clan > Sonido MADCHESTER | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SeClcq_45LE&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← 808-state
**Named After TR-808** : The electronic music band 808 State is explicitly named after the Roland TR-808 Rhythm Composer, underscoring the drum machine's profound influence on their identity and sound.
