**Discovery of "Only You" Sheet Music** : Jean Bennett, Book Ram's assistant, discovered the sheet music for "Only You" when a box of Ram's belongings fell apart. She found it interesting, despite Ram's assessment that it was "rubbish," and placed it on his piano, leading to Tony Williams' insistence on recording it.
