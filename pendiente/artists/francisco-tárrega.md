# artist - Francisco Tárrega

## songs
**Gran Vals (1902)** : An extract from this classical composition by the Spanish composer Francisco Tárrega was included in the Nokia 2110 phone in 1994 as one of its selectable melodies. This melody, initially called "Type 7," would later become known as the iconic "Nokia Tune," marking a significant moment in the personalization of mobile phones.
