# artist - Clive Davis

## curiosities
**Rejection of "Bat Out of Hell"** : As the legendary head of CBS Records, Clive Davis flatly rejected Meat Loaf's *Bat Out of Hell* album, famously telling songwriter Jim Steinman, "clearly you have no idea how to write a rock song." This decision by Davis highlighted his skepticism towards the album's unconventional rock opera format. "CBS Records, which was run by the legendary Clive Davis, said via Clive Davis that, no way, be making this.
