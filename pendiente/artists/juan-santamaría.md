# artist - Juan Santamaría

## curiosities
**Pioneering DJ at Barraca (1981)** : Juan Santamaría, alongside Carlos Simó, began spinning what they termed "música blanca" at Barraca in 1981. This sound was a distinct departure from the prevailing funk and disco, focusing on groups like Depeche Mode, The Cure, Joy Division, and The Fall, and featured exotic, unfamiliar rhythms.
