# artist - Wilbur Sweatman's Jazz Orchestra

## songs
**Blue In The Blues (1918)** : This 1918 recording contains the earliest known instance of the riff that Chuck Berry would later adapt for the intro to "Johnny B. Good." Wilbur Sweatman's band was one of the very first black jazz groups to ever record. ## curiosities
**Pioneering Black Jazz Recording Group** : Wilbur Sweatman's Jazz Orchestra was one of the very first black jazz groups in history to make recordings. Sweatman himself was a friend and colleague of Scott Joplin.
