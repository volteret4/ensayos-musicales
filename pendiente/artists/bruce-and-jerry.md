## songs
**Take This Pearl (Year Unknown)** : A ballad released by Bruce and Jerry on Our Bowen Records, featuring vocals by Bruce Johnston and Jerry Cooper, with backing from Johnston, Dave Shostack, Mike Dizzy, Sandy Nelson, and bass player Harper Cosby. The single was commercially unsuccessful. The release came about after Terry Melcher, son of the label's owner, recalled seeing The Sleepwalkers and sought rock and roll success for the label, hoping to replicate the success of Jan and Arnie.
