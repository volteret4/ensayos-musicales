# artist - Pimp Flaco

## member of
- Los Santos

## curiosities
**Beaten by Los Sugus** : Pimp Flaco was physically assaulted by members of Los Sugus, a younger band from PXXR GANG's circle, when Los Sugus were minors. The altercation occurred because Pimp Flaco had crossed paths with the PXXR GANG circle. "Cuando eran menores de edad, dieron una paliza a pinflaco, quien había cruzado vez con el círculo de la purga." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Collaboration with Kinder Malo** : Pimp Flaco collaborated with Kinder Malo, bringing an ironic proposal to the trap scene. They were capable of turning a song about conspiracies into a generational anthem. "Los hermanos Marcelo Nese, Skindermalo y Pinflaco, trajeron una propuesta masironica, asociándose en sus inicios con Cecilio G." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Conflict with Cecilio G: The Yellow Car Incident** : Pimp Flaco had a significant beef with Cecilio G, which culminated in the famous "yellow car incident." This event was a widely discussed confrontation between the two artists. "Por nombrar otro, también acabó mal con Pinflaco y quinder malodando pie al famoso incidente del coche amarillo." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Response to Assault: "Los buenos siempre hablan"** : After being beaten by Los Sugus and appearing with a black eye, Pimp Flaco responded with the phrase "Los buenos siempre hablan" (The good guys always talk). This statement became a memorable part of the beef. "El, con un ojo morado, respondió con los buenos siempre hablan." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
