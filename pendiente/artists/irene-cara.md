# artist - Irene Cara

## songs
**What a Feeling (1983)** : Irene Cara co-wrote and sang "What a Feeling" from the 1983 movie "Flashdance." This highly successful song earned her both a Grammy Award and an Academy Award. ## curiosities
**Role in "Sparkle" and Soundtrack Controversy (1976)** : Irene Cara starred in the 1976 movie "Sparkle," portraying a singer in the film. Although she sang the songs within the movie, Aretha Franklin performed them on the official soundtrack album, a decision that reportedly disappointed Cara, who had hoped it would be her own record.
