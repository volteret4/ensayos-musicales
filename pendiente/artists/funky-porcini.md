## curiosities
**Definition of Trip Hop** : James Bradell, who released Trip Hop under the alias Funky Porcini on the Ninja Tune label, famously defined the genre as "the perfect mix between computers and cannabis." **Sampling 60s Soundtracks and Jazz** : As a producer, Funky Porcini, along with DJ Food and Nightmares on Wax, creatively sampled "pizzaz" (coolest/most stylish) 60s soundtracks and jazz, layering them over looped beats, showcasing a brilliant and revolutionary use of the sampler.
