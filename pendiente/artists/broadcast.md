## curiosities
**Collaborator with Ghost Box Artists** : Broadcast collaborated with artists associated with Ghost Box Records, contributing to the label's aesthetic of failed futures and its fictional sound archive. This archive drew heavily from library music, public service jingles, educational documentaries, and television iconography from past decades, especially the BBC Radiophonic Workshop.
