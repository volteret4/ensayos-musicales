**Glam Predecessor (1969-1970)** : Marc Bolan, as the leader of T-Rex, is considered a significant predecessor of glam music. By 1969-1970, he was already exhibiting theatrical behavior in his compositions and videos, defining much of the glam aesthetic, including an androgyne style. **Glam Rock Pioneer (1971)** : Marc Bolan, a former rival who became friends with David Bowie, was a key figure in the emergence of glam rock. In March 1971, he appeared on "Top of the Pops" wearing satin clothes and covered with glitter dust, an appearance that profoundly inspired David Bowie.
