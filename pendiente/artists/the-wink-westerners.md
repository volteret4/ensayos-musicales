## curiosities
**Band Formation and Early Lineup** : Formed by Roy Orbison and a group of friends in his teens, the band was named after their small town of Wink. It had various lineups before settling on a two-guitar, bass, drums, and electric mandolin configuration with Orbison, Billy Pat Ellis, Jack Caneeley, Johnny Wilson, and James Morrow. "In his teens he and a group of his friends formed a country in western band, the Wink Westerners, named after the small town they lived in. **Local Radio Show and Backing Slim Whitman** : While Roy Orbison was still in school, The Wink Westerners secured their own weekly radio show. Their local popularity led to them being chosen as the backing group for country star Slim Whitman when he came to town.
