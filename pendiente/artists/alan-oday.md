# artist - Alan O'Day

## songs
**Angie Baby (1974) - Songwriter** : Alan O'Day wrote "Angie Baby." He stated that he was inspired by The Beatles' "Lady Madonna" and the idea of crafting a song about a person whose life unfolds with the radio playing constantly in the background, recognizing that "There are songs for all our emotions and the radio really speaks for us in a way that nothing else does."
