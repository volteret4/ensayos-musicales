## genres

## curiosities
**Comparison to John Frusciante's Early Solo Work** : Syd Barrett is used as a point of comparison for John Frusciante's early solo album, "Niandra LaDes and Usually Just a T-Shirt." The comparison stems from similarities in the unpolished nature of the ideas, low recording quality, strange and critical lyrics, and the shared history of severe drug abuse and eccentric behavior. However, it is noted that Barrett's issues were rooted in real mental health problems that plagued him his entire life, while Frusciante's similar behaviors were largely attributed to drug use.
