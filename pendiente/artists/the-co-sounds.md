## curiosities
**Carole King's First Group** : The Co-Sounds was Carole King's first group, formed with Jules Zwick after she left stage school and returned to a normal high school. The group's name was later reused for some demos recorded by Jerry Landis (Paul Simon) and Carole King. "She started attending a stage school, where one of her classmates was Al Pacino, but after a short while she left, deciding that she wasn't cut out for the non-musical aspects of the school, and went back to a normal high school where she formed her first group, The Co-Sounds, along with Swick." ← Episode 89： ＂Will You Love Me Tomorrow？＂ by the Shirelles | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Kns2HGcuSg
