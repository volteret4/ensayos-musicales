## curiosities
**Popularity and Support at Motown** : Florence Ballard was highly regarded by Motown staff, known for being "very popular," "helpful," and "friendly." She actively assisted Gladys Horton in crafting her lead vocal part for "Please Mr. Postman," showcasing her supportive nature despite The Supremes' then-struggling status. "One of the girls, though, Florence Ballard, was very popular at Motown, and was generally regarded as being helpful and friendly.
