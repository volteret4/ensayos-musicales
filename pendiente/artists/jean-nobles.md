## curiosities

His wife, Delna Roadwater, received songwriting credit for Dale Hawkins' "See You Soon, Baboon" and "Suzy Q," and Nobles himself provided the distinctive Tarzan-style yodeling call for the "See You Soon, Baboon" record.
