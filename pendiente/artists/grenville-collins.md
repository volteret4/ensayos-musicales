## curiosities
**Manager of The Kinks (Early)** : Grenville Collins, best friend of Robert Wace, partnered with Wace to manage The Bull Weevils (later The Kinks) after Wace's pop star ambitions failed. As part of their management deal, Collins committed "half his wages from his job as a stockbroker" to fund the group, and he and Wace would receive "50% of the group's earnings."
