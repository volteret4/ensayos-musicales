# artist - Chaka Khan

## songs
**Do You Love What You Feel (1979/1980)** : The lead single from Rufus's LP *Master Jam*, featuring Chaka Khan's vocals. This "Yacht Soul adjacent" track brought Rufus and Chaka to number one on the R&B chart and number 30 on the Hot 100 as 1979 transitioned into 1980. **Stuff Like That (1978)** : Chaka Khan provided vocal work on this buttery smooth track by Quincy Jones, which he recorded with Nick Ashford and Valerie Simpson. It topped the R&B chart in the summer of 1978 and peaked at number 21 pop, helping to establish Jones as a pivotal figure in "Yacht Soul."
