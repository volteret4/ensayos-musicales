## curiosities
**Renaming to The Fliers** : After recording one single for John Dolphin's record label, the band renamed themselves The Fliers and subsequently moved to Fliers Records. "before renaming themselves the fliers, and moving over to flier records." ← Episode 31： ＂Only You＂ by the Platters | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ojUNoetEX-c
**Uncredited Debenares Recording** : The Debenares' first record was released under the name The Hollywood Blue Jays by John Dolphin, a name he used for various groups. This was a disappointment for the actual band members who expected their own name to be credited.
