## curiosities
**Formation and Lack of Commercial Success (Post-October 1963)** : The Marksmen were formed by David Marks after his departure from The Beach Boys on October 5, 1963, and they subsequently signed with A&M Records. Despite rumors suggesting manager Murray Wilson threatened DJs to prevent airplay, the band's lack of commercial success was primarily attributed to the perceived poor quality of their "shoddy sounding, garage rock records," which had minimal commercial potential in the 1963 market. Their "flatter and more affectless vocals" are noted as prefiguring certain punk singers, though this style lacked commercial viability at the time.
