# artist - DJ Dickie Ronny

## curiosities
**Origin of New Beat in Antwerp** : At the Club Hansen Beljick in Antwerp, Belgium, DJ Dickie Ronny accidentally sparked the New Beat genre. He played the EBM track "Flesh" by A Split Second at 33 revolutions per minute instead of its intended 45 RPM, with the pitch control set to maximum. This resulted in a denser, darker sound that captivated the clubgoers.
