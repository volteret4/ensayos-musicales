# artist - Bunny Robin

## curiosities
**Engineer for Blueberry Hill** : Bunny Robin was the engineer at the "Blueberry Hill" recording session for Fats Domino. Despite the band's inability to complete a full take, Robin believed the material was salvageable and skillfully edited together a complete version from various half-finished takes, making the join imperceptible due to Earl Palmer's precise timing.
