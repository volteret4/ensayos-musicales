**Second Wave IDM Artist** : Was a prominent artist during the "second wave" of IDM, with their music being released on key labels such as Me le plato, Planet Moo, Mor, and El Scam. This era saw a greater emphasis on percussion, drawing influence from genres like jungle and drum and bass, differentiating it from the first wave's focus on melody and textures.
