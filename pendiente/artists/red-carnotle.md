## curiosities
**Departure from The Tennessee 3** : Red Carnotle was initially part of Johnny Cash's group, playing steel guitar. During an audition for Sam Phillips, he became so nervous that his hands shook, preventing him from tuning his guitar. He eventually decided he was holding the other three back and left the studio, reducing the group to The Tennessee 3.
