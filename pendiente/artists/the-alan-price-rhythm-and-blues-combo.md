# artist - The Alan Price Rhythm and Blues Combo

## members
- Alan Price (piano)
- Charles Chandler (bass)
- Eric Burden (vocals)
- Hilton Valentine (guitar)
- John Steele (drums)

## genres
- R&B

## curiosities
**Club a Go-Go Residency** : This group formed as a side project by members of The Contours and became the resident band at Newcastle's Club a Go-Go, a notable venue for touring American blues players and London jazzmen.
