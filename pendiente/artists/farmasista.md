## genres

## songs
**Norman Memphis (2019)** : Released in 2019, this track by Russian producer Farmasista was the "trigger" for the Drift Phonk phenomenon. It heavily samples Memphis classics from artists like Richard Cresquilat, The DJ Sound, and producers from "Cresix Mafia" (specifically DJ Poly and UCJ), processing them through an "industrial shredder." The track features "hyper-saturated production" and an "almost EDM sound," with the 808 cowbells as the main protagonist.
