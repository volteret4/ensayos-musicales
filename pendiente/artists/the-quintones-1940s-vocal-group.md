## songs

## curiosities
**Influence on Book Ram's Ideal Sound** : Book Ram managed and wrote for The Quintones in the 1940s. Their lineup of four men and one woman gave Ram a preference for this particular vocal blend, which he later sought to replicate and perfect with The Platters.
