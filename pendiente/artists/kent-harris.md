# artist - Kent Harris

## curiosities
**Sued and Awarded Joint Credit** : Kent Harris sued Lieber and Stoller regarding The Coasters' recording of "Shopping for Clothes," which was a reworked version of his original song "Close Line, Wrapped Up." He was subsequently awarded joint writing credit with Lieber and Stoller for the track. **Writer of "Close Line, Wrapped Up"** : Kent Harris was the singer and writer of the comedy blues song originally titled "Close Line, Wrapped Up," which was performed under the name Bougaloo and his solid crew. This song served as the basis for The Coasters' "Shopping for Clothes."
