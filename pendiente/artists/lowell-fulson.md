# artist - Lowell Fulson

## genres
- Smooth West Coast blues

## songs
**Reconsider Baby (1954)** : "Reconsider Baby" was Lowell Fulson's biggest record, released shortly after Ray Charles's tenure as his pianist and musical director. It serves as an example of the kind of smooth West Coast blues style that Charles would have been playing with Fulson.
