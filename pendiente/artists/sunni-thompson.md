## curiosities
**Copyright Infringement Lawsuit (vs. Meade Lux Lewis)** : Sunni Thompson successfully defended against a copyright infringement lawsuit brought by Meade Lux Lewis, who claimed Thompson's instrumental copied a bass line from "Yancey Special." The case was dismissed on the grounds that the bass line was too simple to be copyrightable, that Jimmy Yancey had developed it earlier, and that Lewis had dedicated the composition to the public domain by releasing it without prior copyright registration.
