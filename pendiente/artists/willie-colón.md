# artist - Willie Colón

## member of
- Fania All-Stars

## members
- Héctor Lavoe (vocals)
- Willie Colón (trombone)

## instruments
- Trombone

## albums
**Metiendo Mano (1977)** : This album marked the first collaboration between Willie Colón and Rubén Blades, signifying a new creative partnership for Colón after his work with Héctor Lavoe. It was released in 1977 and helped set the stage for their subsequent, highly influential work. "En 1977 viola la lucha el de buen de Rubén Blades, metiendo mano." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Siembra (1978)** : Released in 1978 as his second collaboration with Rubén Blades, *Siembra* was a pivotal album that steered salsa in a new direction. It became Fania Records' best-selling album and was crucial in propelling salsa to global recognition. "Pero fue su siguiente trabajo, siempre, lanzado en 1978, el que llevó a la salsa hacia un nuevo rumbo." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k

## curiosities
**"Bad Boys of Salsa" Image** : Willie Colón and Héctor Lavoe became known as the "bad boys of salsa." Their album covers often depicted scenes from gangster movies, and they adopted a corresponding style of dress and behavior, predating similar aesthetics in genres like reggaeton. "Se convertieron en los chicos malos de la salsa. Sus portadas se requerían escenas de películas de cancers y empezaron a vestir de comportarse como ellos, mucho antes que el jicó vuelve requetón." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Bronx-Born Trombonist with Puerto Rican Roots** : Willie Colón was a trombonist born in the Bronx with deep Puerto Rican roots, embodying the emerging Nuyorican identity that Fania Records began to champion. His background allowed him to connect authentically with the youth of the time. "Willy Colom, un trombonista con raíces portorriqueñas, nacido en el Bronx." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Connection with Street Life** : Colón and Lavoe's music, unlike the polished sound of big orchestras, was "dirtier, realer, and more dangerous." They understood the realities of street life, which allowed them to immediately connect with the Latin youth of their era. "Sabían lo que se vivían las calles y por eso lograron conectar de inmediato con la juventud latina de la época." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Partnership with Héctor Lavoe** : For his first album, Fania Records paired Colón with Héctor Lavoe, a recent arrival from Puerto Rico, as his singer. Despite Colón speaking little Spanish and Lavoe little English, they understood each other perfectly and formed a highly successful duo. "La compañía limpuso como cantante a un recién llegado desde Puerto Rico. Su nombre era Héctor Laboe... Colom hablaba muy poco español, y Laboe apenas unas palabras de inglés, pero aún así se entendieron a la perfección." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Signed by Fania at 15, Recorded First Album at 17** : Colón signed with Fania Records at the remarkably young age of 15. By 17, he had the opportunity to record his debut album, showcasing his prodigious talent early in his career. "Firmó por Fania con tan solo 15 años. A los 17, tuvo al fin la oportunidad de grabar su primer disco." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
**Strained Off-Stage Relationship with Rubén Blades** : Despite their excellent stage chemistry, Willie Colón and Rubén Blades had a very different relationship off-stage. Their realities were contrasting: Colón was born in the streets and knew the harshness of barrio life, while Blades had family protection and left a law career for music. "Buenicoloni Rubén Blades... se entendían muy bien sobre el escenario, pero fuera de él la relación era muy distinta. Sus realidades no podían ser más opuestas, mientras cuando nabían ha sido en las calles y conocía la dureza de la vida de barrio." ← LA FANIA. El imperio que creó la salsa | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UXx3AvorG-k
