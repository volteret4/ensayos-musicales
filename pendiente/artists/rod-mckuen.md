# artist - Rod McKuen

## curiosities
**English Adaptation of "Seasons in the Sun"** : Rod McKuen was responsible for adapting Jacques Brel's "Seasons in the Sun" into English. Notably, his adapted version retained the original lyrics pertaining to a cheating wife.
