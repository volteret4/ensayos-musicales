# artist - John Start

## members
- The Bull Weevils
- The Ram Rods
- The Ray Davis Quartet

## curiosities
**Departure from Band** : Shortly after Ray Davis committed seriously to The Ram Rods (who became The Bull Weevils), John Start decided to leave the group. He chose to "grow up, get serious, give up the drums, and become a quantity surveyor." A significant factor in his decision was his strong dislike for Ray Davis, whom he considered an "unpleasant troubled person."
