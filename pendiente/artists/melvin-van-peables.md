# artist - Melvin Van Peables

## curiosities
**Director of *Sweet Sweet Back's Badass Song*** : Melvin Van Peables directed the seminal 1971 film *Sweet Sweet Back's Badass Song*, which is considered ground zero for black exploitation cinema. Earth Wind & Fire served as the uncredited backing band for its soundtrack.
