## curiosities
**"Not-So-Secret Weapon" of George Clinton** : Bootsy Collins was recognized as George Clinton's essential collaborator and "not-so-secret weapon," joining the P-Funk collective permanently in 1972 after being a veteran of James Brown's JB's at their peak. **Solo Album "Bootszilla"** : In March 1978, George Clinton produced a track for bassist Bootsy Collins called "Bootszilla," which successfully replaced Parliament's "Flashlight" at number 1 on the R&B chart three weeks after "Flashlight" first topped it.
