## curiosities
**Kid Congo Powers Returns to Gun Club (Post-1983)** : Following the release of The Cramps' 1983 album *Smell of Female*, Kid Congo Powers amicably left The Cramps to rejoin Gun Club. This guitarist was Kid Congo Powers.
