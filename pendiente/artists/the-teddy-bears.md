**To Know Him Is To Love Him (Year Unknown)** : The first single by The Teddy Bears, inspired by the epitaph on Phil Specter's father's grave. Sandy Nelson played drums on the track, which went to number one. Although Bruce Johnston has sometimes been credited with playing bass on it, this is considered unlikely as Johnston was primarily a bass player at the time. Nelson's playing, though described as rudimentary, significantly boosted his session career.
