# artist - Bruce Sussman

## curiosities
**Co-writer of "Bandstand Boogie" Lyrics** : Bruce Sussman collaborated with Barry Manilow to write lyrics for the original "Bandstand Boogie" theme song of American Bandstand. Manilow's lyrical rendition was later recorded and adopted as the show's new theme. "So I got my friend Bruce Susman and I and we sat down and we wrote words to the thing so I could actually sing it." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 47 - Dick Clark's Entertainment Empire | https://www.ftr70.com/
