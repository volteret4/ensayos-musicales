## albums
**Fischmonger (2021) - Debut Album** : "Fischmonger" is Anders' debut album, recorded in the midst of the pandemic at his parents' house and released in 2021. The album leans more towards the indie sound of 100 Gecs and Charli XCX than Sophie's deconstructed electronica. Deeply emo and with a markedly adolescent character, "Fischmonger" moves from brosstep, close to Skrillex, to indie rock, without any hang-ups. "Fischmonger es el álbum de debut de Anders Cors. Grabado en plena pandemia en casa de sus padres y lanzado en 2021, el disco se acerca más a la propuesta indie de Ho Han, Dren, que es el electrónica de construida de Sophie." ← 5 Discos Para Entrarle al HYPERPOP | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wP8MaC-2D8A
