## curiosities
**"Glue" of The Marvelettes** : Juanita (Winnie Cowatt) was considered the "glue" that held the original Marvelettes group together, being a friend to all the other members. Her departure significantly impacted the group's internal cohesion. "Juanita and Georgiana had been the glue holding the group together. **American Bandstand Incident and Departure** : Winnie Cowatt, also known as Juanita, experienced a severe breakdown and subsequently left The Marvelettes after a humiliating incident on "American Bandstand." She mistakenly stated that Detroit was a suburb of Inkster, leading to mockery that deeply affected her. "She felt so bad about this slip-up and the way she was mocked for it, so she had a breakdown and ended up leaving the group." ← Episode 93： ＂Please Mr. Postman＂ by the Marvelettes | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EPg8Wx30-uM
**Exclusion from Motown Anniversaries** : Winnie Cowatt was not invited to any of Motown's 25th, 40th, 50th, or 60th-anniversary celebrations, mirroring the lack of recognition for other original Marvelettes members.
