**Health Struggles and Early Retirement** : Georgiana Tillman suffered from sickle cell anemia since childhood, and the relentless touring schedule severely exacerbated her health, leading to a later diagnosis of lupus. The toll on her body forced her to retire from music, after which she worked as a secretary for Motown.
