## songs
**House of the Rising Sun (Arrangement)** : Van Ronk learned this song from a 1953 recording by Halley Wood. He fundamentally rearranged it, changing chords and introducing a descending bass line, mostly in semitones, while maintaining the same chord shape. This "single trick," common in jazz and Baroque but unusual in folk, created interesting chords (e.g., A minor, A minor over G, A minor over F sharp, F) and later influenced hundreds of songs.
