# artist - Zager and Evans

## songs
**In the Year 2525 (1969)** : This song was the winner of the 1969 Billboard Song of the Summer. It is described as a "dauer dystopian summer screed" and was a total curio by a one-hit wonder duo. After dominating the summer of 1969, a period that included the moon landing and Woodstock, the duo never appeared on the Hot 100 chart again.
