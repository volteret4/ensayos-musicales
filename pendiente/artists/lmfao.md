## songs
**Party Rock Anthem (2011)** : This song was the winner of the 2011 Billboard Song of the Summer. Described as a "very silly song," it became a "pretty serious hit" with six weeks at number one in July and August. The "goof ball duo" released a "hot weather banger" that included its own dance ("every day I'm shuffling") and represented early 2010s electronic dance music reaching a "new pop apex."
