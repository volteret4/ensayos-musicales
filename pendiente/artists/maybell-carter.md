## instruments

## curiosities
**Career Tied to Men (Pre-1950s)** : Before Kitty Wells in the 1950s, Maybell Carter's career, like those of other women in country music, was not independent from that of a man. She was primarily known as "Mother Maybell," Johnny Cash's mother-in-law, and the matriarch of the singing Carter Family, rather than being recognized first and foremost as one of country music's most influential guitarists.
