## curiosities
**Band Leader's Adaptation Strategy** : As band leader of Kool & the Gang, Robert "Kool" Bell recognized the imperative to adapt to the rising disco trend by 1976. He initiated a total overhaul of the group, bringing in new personnel and steering their sound towards slick balladry and gently funky pop, ultimately leading to significant 80s crossover success.
