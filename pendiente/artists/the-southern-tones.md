## curiosities
**Source of "I Got a Woman" Melody** : The Southern Tones were a gospel act recording for Duke Records whose gospel tune was heard by Ray Charles and used as the melody for his groundbreaking song "I Got a Woman." They had little commercial success and would be largely forgotten were it not for this particular record.
