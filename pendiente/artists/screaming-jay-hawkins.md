# artist - Screaming Jay Hawkins

## curiosities
**Scandalous Stage Presence** : Screaming Jay Hawkins captivated audiences with his possessed singing style and highly theatrical, scandalous stage performances. He often appeared in a coffin, and incorporated props like skulls and fire, which deeply shocked and scandalized audiences of his time.
