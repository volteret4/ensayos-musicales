## curiosities
**Phonk Era Artist** : Lil Ugly Mane gained a substantial following in the 2010s, during the resurgence of Memphis-inspired music. He attracted fans particularly through music vlogs, forums, and SoundCloud, which served as a crucial archive for beats and loops from the 90s Memphis scene, contributing to the birth of Phonk. "Artistas como Lil Agdemain... ganaron muchos seguidores, especialmente a través de vlogs de música, foros y sobre todo SoundCloud que se convirtió en un archivo local, Parabits y Loops inspirados en la escena de 2090 era." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
