## albums
**Cold Shot (Late 1960s)** : At the age of 15, Sugar played all the guitar and bass parts on his father Johnny Otis's "Cold Shot" album. This record, credited to "the new Johnny Otis show," was a core band effort with Johnny Otis on piano and drums, and Delmar "Mighty Mouth" Evans on vocals. "Cold Shot, by the new Johnny Otis show, featured a core band of just three people. **For Adults Only (Late 1960s)** : Sugar, playing guitar and bass, was part of the trio that recorded the album "For Adults Only" under the name Snatch and the Poon Tangs. This album, also featuring his father Johnny Otis and Delmar "Mighty Mouth" Evans, aimed to preserve black folklore through songs with obscene humor. ## curiosities
**Early Start to Music Career** : Sugar began playing with his father Johnny Otis's band at the exceptionally young age of 11. To circumvent venues serving alcohol, he often wore false moustaches and sunglasses during performances.
