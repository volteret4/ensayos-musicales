# artist - The Barcelona's Esprison Afer

## curiosities
**Distinctive Sound** : The Barcelona's Esprison Afer is known for one of the most characteristic sounds in the Eckpank genre, described as "edante, sucido y mutante" (edgy, suicidal, and mutant). They are considered among the strangest, most punk, and most addictive acts in the current panorama.
