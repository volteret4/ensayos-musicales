## curiosities
**Phonk Collective** : This collective, led by Space Goshpar in Miami, was instrumental in developing the early Phonk sound in 2011. They integrated the "dirty and lo-fi techniques" of Memphis rap with the "narcotic slowness" of Houston, adding a modern and digital feel, heavily influenced by West Coast G-Funk.
