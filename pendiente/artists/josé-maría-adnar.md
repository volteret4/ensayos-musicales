# artist - José María Adnar

## curiosities
**Car Bomb Attempt (1995)** : On April 19, 1995, José María Adnar (likely José María Aznar, then leader of the opposition in Spain, who later became Prime Minister) was the victim of a failed car bomb attack by the terrorist group ETA. This event was controversially exploited by Max Music weeks later with the release of their "Bomba Zomix" album.
