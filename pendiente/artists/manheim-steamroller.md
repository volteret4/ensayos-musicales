## albums
**Holiday Albums (1980s-Present)** : Beginning in the 1980s, Manheim Steamroller started issuing Christmas music, which proved exponentially more popular than any of Chip Davis's other seasonal releases. Their holiday albums have collectively achieved platinum or multi-platinum status, establishing Manheim Steamroller as "the sound of Christmas" for generations of Middle Americans. ## curiosities
**American Gramophone Label Origin** : Chip Davis founded American Gramophone in 1974 as an Omaha-based independent label, initially to release his own instrumental music project, Manheim Steamroller, a synthesizer classical fusion venture. The label later also released C. W. McCall's material. **Launch Through C.W. McCall's Success** : The unexpected chart success of C. W. McCall, which originated from an ad campaign for sliced bread, played a crucial role in launching the musical career of Chip Davis. Davis, who created the music for McCall, ultimately became far better known for his Manheim Steamroller project.
