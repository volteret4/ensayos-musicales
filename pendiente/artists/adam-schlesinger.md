## curiosities
**Early COVID-19 Infection and Death** : Adam Schlesinger, the leader of Fountains of Wayne, contracted COVID-19 early in the pandemic, and his condition rapidly deteriorated. He was hospitalized, and his health worsened to the point where he was placed on a ventilator. Tragically, he died just a week later, on April 1, 2020. "He contracted COVID early in the pandemic. And it got bad real quick and he was hospitalized." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u5NLt6mkmrk ← adam-schlesinger
