# artist - The Tempo Toppers

## curiosities
**Little Richard's Band** : The Tempo Toppers were Little Richard's band with whom he recorded for Peacock Records in early 1953, alongside another group, The Duces of Rhythm. They later split up around the time Richard was dropped by Peacock and faced legal troubles.
