# artist - The Allman Brothers

## curiosities
**Influence from Delaney & Bonnie** : The Allman Brothers were influenced by the American roots traditions, including the fusion of country, gospel harmonies, and blues, that Delaney & Bonnie helped establish as central to 70s music. "A direction that became central to 70's music through artists like the Almond brothers." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 59 - Bands and Booze on the Festival Express of 1970 | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Pilgrimage to Fame** : The Allman Brothers made a "pilgrimage" to record at Fame Recording Studios, seeking to absorb the renowned musical atmosphere of the studio. "Then later, the Rolling Stones and the Almond Brothers made pilgrimages to record at Fame." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QEkGSpxeuMk ← the-allman-brothers
