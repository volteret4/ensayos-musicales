## curiosities
**Continued Activity** : Aviador Dro continues to tour and release new material to this day, demonstrating their enduring presence in the electronic music scene. "Ambas bandas siguen haciendo giras y editando material a día de hoy." ← La España Siniestra ｜ La edad de oro del Postpunk y el Dark wave | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN_buvQC69c
**Departure of Members to Form Esplendor Geométrico** : The majority of the band, excluding Servando Carballar, became dissatisfied with the evolving sound and decided to leave Aviador Dro. These members then went on to form the experimental electronic group Esplendor Geométrico. "pero el grueso de la banda, salvo Servando, descontenta con el sonido que estaba tomando, decidió abandonar el grupo para formar esplendorgeó métrico." ← La España Siniestra ｜ La edad de oro del Postpunk y el Dark wave | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN_buvQC69c
**Formation of DRO (1982)** : Discontent with the musical direction and the lack of a label willing to release his album after most band members left, Servando Carballar founded his own label, DRO (Discos Radioactivos Organizados), in 1982. This label would become highly influential. "Miendo que ninguna discográfica quería editarle ese disco, Servando Creon, 1982, es el sello drogo." ← La España Siniestra ｜ La edad de oro del Postpunk y el Dark wave | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN_buvQC69c
**Initial Singles with Movieplay** : Movieplay released two singles for Aviador Dro, marking their initial foray into the music scene. These releases preceded the internal split that would lead to the formation of Esplendor Geométrico. **Pioneers of Electronic Pop (1979)** : Aviador Dro formed in 1979 and were the pioneers of electronic pop in Spain. Their music drew influences from influential international bands like The Residents, Devo, and Kraftwerk. "A viador drogo fueron los pioneros del pop electrónico en el país.
