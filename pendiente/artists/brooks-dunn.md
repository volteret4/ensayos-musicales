## songs
**My Maria (1996)** : Brooks & Dunn covered B.W. Stevenson's "My Maria" in 1996. Ronnie Dunn initially hesitated to cover the song, viewing it as a rock song and feeling "leery about this falsetto," highlighting the genre-bending nature of the original track.
