## curiosities
**Name and Song Connection to The Gamblers** : The Moon Dogs were a band that included Derry Weaver and Larry Taylor, both of whom would later be associated with The Gamblers. The Moon Dogs themselves used to play a song called "Moon Dog," which is the same title as The Gamblers' A-side, written by Weaver. This connection is a key piece of evidence in determining the likely lineup for The Gamblers' record. "Given that the A side is called Moon Dog, that Weaver and Taylor were in a band called The Moon Dogs, that used to play a song called Moon Dog, and that Weaver is credited as the writer, I think we can assume that the lead guitar is Derri Weaver, and that Elliot Inber's list of credits is mostly correct." ← Episode 86： ＂LSD-25＂ by the Gamblers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UNd6UwbL1rA
