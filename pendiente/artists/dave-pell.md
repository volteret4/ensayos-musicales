# artist - Dave Pell

## curiosities
**Alka Seltzer Melody Idea (1965)** : In 1965, jazz band leader Dave Pell identified a catchy melody in an Alka Seltzer commercial and believed it possessed enough appeal to be transformed into a pop hit. This insight led him to assemble a studio group to record an instrumental version.
