# artist - The Andantes

## curiosities
**Anne Bogan's Membership** : Anne Bogan, a versatile singer who later joined The Marvelettes, had previously sung with The Andantes, indicating her established role within Motown's session musician community. **Motown's In-House Backing Vocal Group** : The Andantes served as Motown's prominent in-house backing vocalists, contributing to numerous recordings. They sang backing vocals on The Marvelettes' single "The Darnells" and frequently provided backing for later Marvelettes records when the main group was on tour. "Motown's in-house backing vocal group The Andantees." ← Episode 93： ＂Please Mr. Postman＂ by the Marvelettes | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EPg8Wx30-uM
**Motown's Normal Female Backing Group** : The Andantes were Motown's regular female backing vocal group. Their unavailability for one session led Martha Reeves to call The Delphiys to fill in, which eventually led to The Delphiys being signed as Martha and the Van Dellers.
