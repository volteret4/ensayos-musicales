## curiosities
**Bandwagon Jumper** : Linda Hayes was known for her tendency to "jump on bandwagons," frequently recording tribute records or answer songs to cash in on current trends and successful artists, such as Johnny Ace's death or Willie Maibon's hit. "We'd find her popping up all over the place when there was a bandwagon to jump on." ← Episode 31： ＂Only You＂ by the Platters | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ojUNoetEX-c
**Brother-Sister Duet** : A duet was recorded and credited to "Linda Hayes and Tony Williams of the Platters," showcasing their familial and musical connection, though it didn't achieve commercial success. **Sister of Tony Williams** : Linda Hayes was the sister of Tony Williams, who would become the lead singer of The Platters. She introduced her brother to producer Ralph Bass, an introduction that ultimately led to Williams joining The Flamingos (later The Platters).
