# artist - Kitty Wells

## curiosities
**Pioneer for Female Independence (1950s)** : Kitty Wells was the first woman in country music, starting in the 1950s, to establish a career that was independent from that of a man. She, along with Patsy Cline, "opened the door" for subsequent female artists.
