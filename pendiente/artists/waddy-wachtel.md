## songs
**Werewolves of London (Opening Lyrics)** : Wachtel was instrumental in the creation of Warren Zevon's "Werewolves of London," specifically credited with originating the song's distinctive opening lyrics. These lines ("I saw werewolf with a Chinese menu in his hand walking through the streets of Soho in the rain he was looking for the place called Li Ho Fux going to get a big dish of beef Chowmain") were directly inspired by Wachtel's recent trip to London and a real Chinese restaurant he encountered there.
