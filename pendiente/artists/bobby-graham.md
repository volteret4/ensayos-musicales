# artist - Bobby Graham

## members
- Mike Barry and the Outlaws
- The Kinks (session drummer)

## curiosities
**Prominent Session Drummer** : Bobby Graham served as a session drummer for The Kinks on their debut single "Long Tall Sally" and several subsequent early recordings, as the group lacked a permanent drummer at the time. He was one of the two main "uncredited session drummers in the UK," alongside Clem Cattini, and had previously played for Joe Meek as part of Mike Barry and the Outlaws.
