# artist - Shuggie Otis

## songs
**Strawberry Letter 23** : An obscure track by acclaimed multi-instrumentalist Shuggie Otis, described as a romantic but lyrically elliptical song. Quincy Jones later agreed to reinvent this song in The Brothers Johnson's style, leading to it becoming a number one R&B and top five pop hit for them in the summer of 1977.
