## curiosities
**US Doom Pioneer** : Trouble, hailing from the United States in the 1980s, played a significant role in developing the early Doom Metal sound. They consciously diverged from the faster, more virtuosic metal styles of the era, instead centering their music on weight, slowness, and a heavy atmosphere, characterized by extended sustain and magnified heaviness.
