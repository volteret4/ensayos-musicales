## songs
**Why Do Fools Fall in Love (1956) - Original Intended Lead** : Herman Santiago was originally slated to be the lead singer for "Why Do Fools Fall in Love." The distinctive falsetto, which became a hallmark of the record, was a late addition when the song was rearranged in the studio to instead feature Frankie Lymon. ## curiosities

**Legal Battle for Song Credit** : In 1992, Santiago and Jimmy Merchant finally won legal credit for having written "Why Do Fools Fall in Love." However, this victory was overturned in 1996 due to the lapse of time in taking legal action, causing the song rights to revert to Frankie Lymon's estate and Morris Levy.
