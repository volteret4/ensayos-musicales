## albums
**Flamboyant (Year not specified)** : With this album, Dorian Electra establishes themselves as a key creative figure in hyperpop. This album invites exploration of gender, masculinity, and queer identity, blending humor and provocation. Musically, "Flamboyant" is the album that strays least from the margins of hyperpop, being very close to the early "chiclosas" (chewing gum-like) and 2000s productions of PC Music. The album is an explosion of baroque synthesizers, glitchy beats, and arrangements that leap from melodic to chaotic in seconds. It is arguably the most pop and accessible album on this list, full of adolescent hits, and had a considerable impact on the less experimental and more theatrical side of the genre.
