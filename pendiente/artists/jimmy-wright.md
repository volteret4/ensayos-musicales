## instruments

## curiosities
**Arrangements for The Teenagers** : Jimmy Wright, a session saxophone player, was instrumental in shaping the sound of The Teenagers' records. He handled the arrangements for all their recordings, and it was his specific idea to incorporate Sherman Garns' distinctive bass intros, which became a signature element of their music.
