He was also known for using "pancake 31" make-up on stage, a practice Richard adopted and continued throughout his career. **Major Blues Star and Influence** : Billy Wright was a significant blues star between 1949 and 1951, known for being one of the first blues singers to incorporate gospel-inspired "panorasms" into his vocals. He served as one of Little Richard's two biggest influences, with Richard becoming a "wannabe" and incorporating parts of Wright's style.
