# artist - Barry White's Love Unlimited Orchestra

## songs
**1984 String Arrangement Emulation (1974)** : David Bowie admitted that the string arrangement on his 1974 track "1984" was an attempt to emulate the lush proto-disco soul sound of Barry White's Love Unlimited Orchestra. This detail demonstrates Bowie's conscious integration of R&B and early disco influences into his music.
