## curiosities
**Pioneers of Ambient House** : This artist (likely KLF), along with The Orp (The Orb), was instrumental in initiating Ambient House and the concept of chill-out rooms, which offered atmospheric passages in electronic music. However, these spaces were still largely considered extensions of the dance floor, serving as temporary respites for conversation and recharging rather than dedicated deep listening.
