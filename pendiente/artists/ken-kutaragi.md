# artist - Ken Kutaragi

## curiosities
**Vision for PlayStation 2 (2000)** : As the head of PlayStation during the development of the PlayStation 2, Ken Kutaragi articulated a grand vision to his team, describing the player's anticipated experience as "a floating world with a vast space and Earth in the background." This description profoundly influenced Takafumi Fujisawa and his team in their design of the console's startup sound.
