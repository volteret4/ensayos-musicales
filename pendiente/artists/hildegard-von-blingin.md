## curiosities
**Prominent Bardcore Artist** : Hildegard von Blingin' is one of the Bardcore artists who have reinterpreted contemporary songs using medieval sounds and instruments. This phenomenon began as a musical meme during the pandemic and rapidly gained popularity on social media, blending humor and anachronism.
