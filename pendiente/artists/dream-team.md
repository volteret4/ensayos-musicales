# artist - Dream Team

## members
- José María Castells
- Quique Tejada
- Tony Peret

## curiosities
**Formation and Success at Max Music** : The Dream Team was formed by Quique Tejada, Tony Peret, and José María Castells after Tejada's high-profile move from Blanco y Negro Music to Max Music. This trio became the official Megamixers for Max Music, producing virtually all of the company's megamixes during their tenure. Their mixes served as the soundtrack for a generation's parties, road trips, and summers. **Move to Vale Music with Ricardo Campoy** : When Ricardo Campoy decided to leave Max Music and establish his own label, Vale Music, he reportedly took The Dream Team and other employees with him. This move significantly weakened Max Music and boosted the new Vale Music label.
