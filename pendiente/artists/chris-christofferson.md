# artist - Chris Christofferson

## curiosities
**Country Music as White Man's Soul Music (1991)** : Chris Christofferson stated in 1991 that country music was "white man's soul music," emphasizing its themes of drinking, cheating, and sex, which he noted were not typically discussed in pop music. This viewpoint, however, is considered problematic as it inherently excludes black people from participating in country music, either as performers or fans. "country is white man's soul music.
