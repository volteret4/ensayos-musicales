## curiosities
**Claim Regarding Little Richard's Conversion** : John Mariscalco frequently asserted that Little Richard's decision to convert to religion was not solely spiritual but a strategic move motivated by "worldly considerations," specifically to break an unfavorable contract with Specialty Records which paid him only half a cent per record and could only be ended by his death or an "act of God."
