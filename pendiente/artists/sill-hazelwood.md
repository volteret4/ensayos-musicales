# artist - Sill & Hazelwood

## curiosities
**Coasters Producers** : Sill and Hazelwood were credited as producers for several Coasters recordings during a period when Jerry Lieber and Mike Stoller stopped exclusively producing the group. They had previously achieved hits producing records like "Vappel Rouse" and "Byteway and Addy," but their work for The Coasters did not achieve significant commercial success.
