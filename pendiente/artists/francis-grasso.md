# artist - Francis Grasso

## curiosities
**Club Mix for Sexual Experience** : He created an early example of a club mix by matching the drum solo from Chicago's "I'm a Man" with Robert Plant's moaning and groaning in Led Zeppelin's "Whole Lotta Love," which resulted in a powerful, sexual experience. The scene was set in The Sanctuary, a former church that had been converted into a nightclub.
