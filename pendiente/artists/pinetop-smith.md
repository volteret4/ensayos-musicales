# artist - Pinetop Smith

## albums
**Pinetops Boogie Woogie (1928) - First Real Boogie Record** : Released in 1928, this record is cited as the first "real" Boogie record. The rhythm heard in this song, even a decade before the Glenn Miller Orchestra's "In the Mood," is noted as not being significantly different, demonstrating the shuffle rhythm that largely characterized early Boogie Woogie.
