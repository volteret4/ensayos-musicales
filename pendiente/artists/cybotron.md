## albums
**Alleys of Your Mind (1971) - Self-Released Electro** : This track, self-released, featured an electro sound that laid the foundations for what would become techno. It achieved relative success within Detroit, leading to a record deal with the Fantasy label. ## curiosities
**Afrofuturist Sound Impact** : The Afrofuturist sound developed by Cybotron gained significant traction among Detroit's youth, contributing to the emergence of a new musical scene in the city. "El sonido de Afrofuturista, DitiBotron estaba llegando fuerte a la juventud de Detroit, y se estaba creando una escena." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw
**Detroit Techno Influence** : Cybotron's music, specifically the track "Zohyfield of Demoroder" (as transcribed, likely a mis-transcription for "Clear"), was a significant influence in Detroit during the genesis of techno, alongside Kraftwerk. "lo que más escuchaba era Crafwerk, junto al tema Zohyfield of Demoroder." ← Por qué KRAFTWERK es la banda más influyente de la historia？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wWOBez9Cqhs
**Formation and Shared Interests** : Formed in 1980 by Juan Atkins and Richard Davis, whom Atkins met while studying music. Davis, a Vietnam veteran, was considerably older than Atkins. They connected through mutual interests in science fiction, futurism, and electronic music. "En 1980 forma a Cibotron, junto a Richard Davis... Ambos eran la ficionadas a la fíjana ficción, a los futuros logos y a la música electrónica." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw
**Musical Divergence and Split** : Although they shared common ground, their individual musical preferences led to the group's separation in the mid-1980s. Richard Davis desired a shift towards rock music, while Atkins was committed to continuing the electronic path that was proving successful. "Mediados de los 80, Cibo Tron se separó, y a que Richard Davis quería dar un giro hacia el rock, y Atkins quería seguir el camino electrónico que tanto les estaba funcionando." ← Cómo Detroit Reinventó la Música Electrónica ｜ Pioneros del Techno | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H4AtVFOVtUw
**Seminal Techno Project** : Early tracks from Cybotron, a seminal techno project, are characterized as a blend of American funk and the technological minimalism of Kraftwerk, showcasing the foundational influences of the genre. "Las primeros tracks de Cibo Tron o model 500, proyectos seminales del tecno, son como un hijo bastardo de ese fancaamericano y del minimalismo tecnológico de Crafwerk." ← Por qué KRAFTWERK es la banda más influyente de la historia？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wWOBez9Cqhs
