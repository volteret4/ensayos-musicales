# artist - Don Arden

## curiosities
**Promoter of Controversial 1962 UK Tour** : Don Arden promoted Little Richard's October 1962 UK tour as his "first rock and roll tour in five years," despite Richard's intention to perform only gospel music, leading to a clash with audience expectations and Arden's "apoplectic" reaction to Richard's gospel sets.
