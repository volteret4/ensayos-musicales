## songs
**December 1963 (Oh, What a Night) (1975)** : Released in December 1975, this song hit number one on the Hot 100 in March 1976 and stayed there for almost a month. Lead vocals on the "Oh, what a night" part were sung by drummer Greg Polci. The song is explicitly about a guy losing his virginity but does so subtly compared to more overtly sexual hits of the era like Rod Stewart's "Tonight's the Night."
