## curiosities
**Introducing "Love Is Strange" to Sylvia** : While performing on the same bill as Bo Diddley, Jody Williams, who was attracted to Sylvia, played his unreleased version of "Love Is Strange" for her, which subsequently led to Mickey & Sylvia recording it and turning it into a hit. **Uncredited Co-writer of "Love Is Strange"** : Williams played lead guitar on Bo Diddley's unreleased recording of "Love Is Strange" and was claimed by Diddley to have written the music for the song. However, Williams was not credited and never received any money from the hit song, leading to a decades-long estrangement with Diddley.
