## curiosities
**Daily Motown Visits as a Postman** : After his work as a postman, Freddie Gorman, a former bandmate of Brian Holland in The Fidelitones, would regularly "pop into Motown" due to his continued musical ambitions. This routine led to his involvement in writing "Please Mr. Postman." "Gorman still had musical ambitions, and he would pop into Motown every day after he finished work as a postman." ← Episode 93： ＂Please Mr. Postman＂ by the Marvelettes | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EPg8Wx30-uM
**End of Songwriting Partnership with Brian Holland** : Freddie Gorman's songwriting collaboration with Brian Holland ended when Holland began completing song ideas with his brother Eddie and Lamont Dozier before Freddie could contribute. This marked a shift in Holland's creative partnerships.
