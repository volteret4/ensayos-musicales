# artist - Jean Austin

## curiosities
**Association with Tom Parker** : Parker helped Austin organize shows, and some sources claim Parker managed him during this period, though others disagree. "Parker helped him organize some shows. According to some claims, Parker was his manager, though other sources disagree." ← Episode 33： ＂Mystery Train＂, by Elvis Presley | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSHTmHy3Jo0
**Decline in Popularity (Late 1930s)** : By the late 1930s, when Tom Parker met him, Austin's popularity had significantly diminished. "Austin had been a huge star, but by the time Parker got to know him in the late 30s, he was much less popular." ← Episode 33： ＂Mystery Train＂, by Elvis Presley | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSHTmHy3Jo0
**Financial Hardship** : Austin had fallen on such hard times that he would attempt to pay for petrol with a check and suggest the attendant frame his autograph instead of cashing it, claiming the autograph was worth more. "Austin had fallen on such hard times that he would fill his car with a petrol station, pay by check and then tell them that his autograph was probably worth more than the money, so why not just leave that check uncashed and frame it?" ← Episode 33： ＂Mystery Train＂, by Elvis Presley | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSHTmHy3Jo0
**Influence on Tom Parker's Philosophy** : Austin taught Tom Parker the valuable lesson that while stars' popularity rises and falls, managers can consistently make money regardless of their age or the artist's success. "That lesson was that the stars come and go and rise and fall in popularity, but managers can keep making money no matter how old they are." ← Episode 33： ＂Mystery Train＂, by Elvis Presley | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSHTmHy3Jo0
