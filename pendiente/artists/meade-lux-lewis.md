## curiosities
**Copyright Infringement Lawsuit (vs. Sunni Thompson)** : Meade Lux Lewis initiated a copyright infringement lawsuit against Sunni Thompson, alleging that Thompson's instrumental had a bass line too similar to his "Yancey Special." The case was ultimately dismissed due to several factors, including Jimmy Yancey's testimony that he created the bass line earlier, the court's view that the bass line was too simple to be copyrighted independently, and Lewis's failure to register copyright before the song's release.
