**Financial Support for Buddy Holly (1955)** : Larry Wellborn, a bass player, demonstrated his belief in Buddy Holly's talent by lending him $1000 in 1955, a substantial amount of money, which enabled Holly to purchase his first Fender Stratocaster, an amplifier, and a stage suit.
