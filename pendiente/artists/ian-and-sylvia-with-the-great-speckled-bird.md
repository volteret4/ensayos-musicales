# artist - Ian and Sylvia with The Great Speckled Bird

## members
- Sylvia Tyson

## curiosities
**Canadian Representation** : Ian and Sylvia with The Great Speckled Bird provided Canadian representation on the Festival Express tour. "Certainly, you cannot have a tour like this without some Canadian representation. And it had that. We had Ian and Sylvia with the great speckled bird." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 59 - Bands and Booze on the Festival Express of 1970 | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Ego-less Collaboration** : Sylvia Tyson, who sang with The Great Speckled Bird, famously commented on the collaborative and communal atmosphere aboard the Festival Express, stating, "we left our egos at the station."
