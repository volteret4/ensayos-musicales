# artist - Valerie Simpson

## songs
**Stuff Like That (1978)** : Valerie Simpson contributed to the lyrics and melodic hooks for this Quincy Jones track, performing vocal work alongside Nick Ashford and Chaka Khan. This collaboration resulted in an R&B chart-topper and a Top 40 pop hit for Jones.
