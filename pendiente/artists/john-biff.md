## curiosities
**Artistic and Commercial Differences at Primavera Sound 2018** : At the Primavera Sound festival in 2018, John Biff participated in a discussion with C. Tangana and Bad Gyal. This debate highlighted significant artistic, commercial, and ideological differences between John Biff (representing Granada's street-oriented trap) and C. Tangana (representing Madrid's more business-minded approach). "se sentaron en una charla John Bif, se tangana y batial. Y se dejaron patentes las diferencias de criterio artístico, comercial y diológico ante el madrileño y el gran adino." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Co-founder of Kefta Boyz** : John Biff co-founded the influential collective Kefta Boyz in Granada, which was doing something radically new in the country, inspired by American trap and Memphis rap. "La banda formada en granada por John Biff, Calet, Wormini entre muchos otros, hacía algo radicalmente nuevo en el país." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
**Co-founder of PXXR GANG** : John Biff, along with Khaled, K.D.K.in, and Steve Lean, founded PXXR GANG in El Raval, a band that would change the Spanish music scene. "fundan la banda que lo cambiará todo, la pur gang." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
