**Early Musical Exposure (1959)** : Robert Zimmerman, later known as Bob Dylan, saw one of the "Winter Dance Party" shows in Duluth, Minnesota, where Buddy Holly, Dion, and Richie Valens were filling in on drums. This early experience occurred before Zimmerman moved to Greenwich Village.
