# artist - Freddie Allen

## songs
**We've Only Just Begun (Pre-1970)** : Freddie Allen recorded an early version of "We've Only Just Begun," which included a bridge and a third verse penned by Paul Williams and his songwriting partner. Despite these additional lyrical components, Allen's recording of the song did not achieve commercial success as a hit.
