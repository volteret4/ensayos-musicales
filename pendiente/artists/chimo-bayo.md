# artist - Chimo Bayo

## curiosities
**Most Representative Figure of the Ruta Movement** : Chimo Bayo is arguably the most representative figure of the Ruta del Bacalao movement, commanding a legion of both fans and detractors, a unique position within the scene. He was one of the prominent names to emerge in the 90s, developing his own vision of the "sonido Valencia" during a period when the Ruta's music evolved towards more commercial and trance-influenced styles.
