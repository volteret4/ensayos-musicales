# artist - Tony Peret

## member of
- Dream Team

## curiosities
**Member of The Dream Team** : Tony Peret formed "The Dream Team" with Quique Tejada and José María Castells at Max Music. This group was responsible for producing almost all of the company's megamixes, achieving star status in Spain and providing the soundtrack for parties, road trips, and summers for an entire generation. "Junta Toni Peret, hijo de María Castéis, formaron el Dream Team, quienes pasaron a realizar prácticamente todos los Megamixes de la compañía." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**New Mixer for Max Music** : Tony Peret, a young local DJ, was recruited by Max Music alongside José María Castells to replace Mike Platinas and Javier Usia. They became the official Megamixers for the label following the success and innovation of Max Mix 3.
