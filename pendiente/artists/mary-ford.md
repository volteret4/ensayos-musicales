# artist - Mary Ford

## curiosities
**Pioneer of Overdubbing (Late 1940s)** : Mary Ford was a pioneer in overdubbing techniques during the late 1940s, known for her sophisticated vocal harmonies created through this method, which were later crudely copied by Norman Petty.
