## curiosities
**Backing Vocals for Elvis (Initial RCA Sessions)** : Ben and Brock Speer, members of The Speer Family (who were signed with RCA), were hired by Steve Sholes for Elvis Presley's initial RCA sessions to provide backing vocals alongside Gordon Stoker. This created an "unbalanced gospel quartet" with "too many tenors and no baritone" as a cost-cutting measure.
