## curiosities
**Initial Exclusion and Elvis's Demand** : Only Gordon Stoker, one member of The Jordanaires, was present at Elvis's initial RCA sessions. The full group was not hired due to Steve Sholes' cost-cutting measure of using RCA-signed artists (The Speer Family). Elvis later insisted that all The Jordanaires be employed for his future sessions. "When Elvis found out at a later session that this had happened as a cost-cutting measure, he insisted that all the Jordanaires be employed at his future sessions." ← Episode 38： ＂Heartbreak Hotel＂ by Elvis Presley | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ES35PYeAwfs
**Primary Backing Group for Elvis** : The Jordanaires were the group that backed Elvis Presley throughout the 1950s and 1960s. "the group who would back Elvis throughout the 50s and 60s." ← Episode 38： ＂Heartbreak Hotel＂ by Elvis Presley | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ES35PYeAwfs
