**Night of the Vampire (Year Not Specified)** : This song, produced by Joe Meek, is highlighted as an early and significant example reflecting Meek's deep fascination with dark and esoteric themes. It is recognized as one of the earliest precursors to what would later become known as Gothic Rock.
