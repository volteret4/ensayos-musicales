# artist - Tony Vitale

## curiosities
**DJ at Chocolate - The Gitano (Until 1986)** : Tony Vitale, known as "El Gitano," was the resident DJ at Chocolate until 1986, where he cultivated a dark and gothic atmosphere. He was renowned for spinning "sinister" bands like Bauhaus, Cabaret Voltaire, and Throbbing Gristle, alongside more guitar-driven groups such as The Cramps and The Meteors.
