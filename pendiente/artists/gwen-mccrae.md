# artist - Gwen McCrae

## songs
**Rockin' Chair (1973)** : This song, originally intended for another singer on her label, was recorded by Gwen McCrae. It became an answer record to "Rock Your Baby" and featured her husband George McCrae on background vocals, reaching number nine in 1973 and entering the top 10 in 1975. "Rock and Share was not meant necessarily for Gwen either. ## curiosities
**Husband as Manager** : Her husband, George McCrae, managed her singing career before he unexpectedly achieved fame with "Rock Your Baby."
