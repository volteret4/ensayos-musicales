## genres

## albums
**Heat Treatment (1976)** : This LP was the first album of renown produced by Mut Lang. It won critical acclaim and charted for both Parker and Lang, cracking the Billboard album chart at number 169. Graham Parker, however, disliked Lang's meticulous "stiff instrument by instrument recording approach." "The first album of any renown that Lang produced was Heat Treatment.
