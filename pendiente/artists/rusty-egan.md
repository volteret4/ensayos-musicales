## curiosities
**Co-Founder of The Blitz Club** : Rusty Egan, who did not fully fit with the punk scene, co-founded The Blitz Club in a small London basement in the late 1970s with Steve Strange. The club paid homage to David Bowie's glam aesthetic and played new music, with Kraftwerk being a constant presence.
