# artist - Teresa Brewer

## songs
**I Love Mickey (1956)** : Teresa Brewer, along with New York Yankees great Mickey Mantle, recorded this single as an ode to the Yankee Hall of Famer. The catchy tune includes lyrics such as, "Mickey who? You know who the fellow with the celebrated swing. I love Mickey. Mickey who? You know who the one who drives me baddy every spring." **You Send Me (1957) - Cover Version** : Teresa Brewer, a white singer, released a cover version of Sam Cooke's "You Send Me," which deeply offended Sam and Bumps Blackwell. They accused her of being a "thief" for copying Sam's distinctive "woe-o's," especially as her version charted in the top ten. "What they did object to was the white cover version by Terry Zibroo.
