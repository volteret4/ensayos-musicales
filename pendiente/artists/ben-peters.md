# artist - Ben Peters

## curiosities
**Songwriter of "Kiss An Angel Good Morning"** : Ben Peters wrote "Kiss An Angel Good Morning," a significant pop crossover hit for Charlie Pride. His songwriting was noted for its catchiness, comparable to another country pop crossover, Freddie Fender's "Before the Next Teardrop Falls."
