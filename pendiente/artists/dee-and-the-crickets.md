# artist - Dee and The Crickets

## genres

## curiosities
**Mistaken Identity (Year not specified)** : This Black doo-wop group, which specialized in smooth Ink Spots-style ballads, existed about five years before Buddy Holly's group, The Crickets. Black radio stations mistook Buddy Holly's Crickets for them, leading to unexpected airplay and bookings for the white group. "It turned out that there had been another group called the Crickets.
