**Recording and Touring with His Group** : Cornel Gunter's version of The Coasters was not permitted to record under "The Coasters" name, so they made records credited as "Cornel Gunter and The Coasters." However, his group was able to tour under "The Coasters" name and was a cheaper option than the original studio group. Dick Clark, a friend of Gunter, began booking Gunter's version of the group for tours.
