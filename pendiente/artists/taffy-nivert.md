## songs
**Afternoon Delight (1976)** : Taffy Nivert, along with Bill Danoff, John Carroll, and Margot Chapman, formed the four-part harmony for "Afternoon Delight." She recognized that while she and Bill were good singers, they weren't great, so they brought in other talented vocalists.
