# artist - The Slades

## songs
**You Cheated** : An original track recorded by The Slades, a white group from Texas. This song had potential to be a big hit, but its original label faced distribution issues with major distributors, hindering its commercial reach. This ultimately led George Matola to seek a sound-alike version, which became the hit by The Shields.
