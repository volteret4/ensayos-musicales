## curiosities
**Representative of Traditional Spanish Hip Hop** : ToteKing was one of the artists who defined the canons of Spanish hip-hop in the 90s and 2000s, focusing on poetry, social messaging, and boom-bap production. This style rejected the "swag" and ostentatious attitude of the West Coast.
