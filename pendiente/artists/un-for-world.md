# artist - Un For World

## curiosities
**Creator of Elaborate Soundfont Versions** : Un For World was inspired by Canalos Gerjou's viral track and is known for creating "very worked versions" of songs, such as Boards of Canada's "Roygbiv." This artist is praised for a creative approach that involves not just compiling MIDI tracks but also crafting their own and innovatively applying sound samples. "inspiró a la cuenta Un For World, que subió una versión muy trabajada de Roy Vibh de Borsav Canada. Este, sin dudas, mi artista es un fonte preferido. Ha reinterpretado FX Twin, PortxG, Tomas y Fatad, y no se vivica solo recopilar las pistas Mibi, crea las soyas propias y aplica a los sonidos de forma muy creativa." ← El fenómeno de Soundfont Album. Discos reinterpretados en videojuegos | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ekozTKngeAw
**Influence on Soundfink** : Un For World's version of "In Rainbows" served as an inspiration for Soundfink, who subsequently became one of the most listened-to creators in the soundfont genre, building on the foundation set by Un For World's creative work. "Esta versión del disco inspiro al que posiblemente sea el creador que más escuchas acumula. Soundfink es real..." ← El fenómeno de Soundfont Album. Discos reinterpretados en videojuegos | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ekozTKngeAw
**Radiohead "In Rainbows" Reinterpretation** : Un For World's soundfont reinterpretation of Radiohead's "In Rainbows" album achieved notable recognition, gaining the attention and retweet of Johnny Greenwood, a member of Radiohead and a known video game enthusiast. "Su reinterpretación del disco en Rimbauds llegó hasta Johnny Greenwood, integrante de Radiohead y gran amante de los videojuegos, que han retweetado el album." ← El fenómeno de Soundfont Album.
