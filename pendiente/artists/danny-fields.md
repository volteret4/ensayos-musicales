# artist - Danny Fields

## curiosities
**Early Believer in The Ramones** : Fields was a steadfast supporter of The Ramones "right from the beginning," recognizing their potential even when Hilly Crystal and others initially dismissed them. "Danny Fields always believed in them right from the beginning." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 57 - Punk and New Wave at New York's CBGB | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Manager for Ramones' Europe Tour** : Danny Fields managed The Ramones during their European tour with The Talking Heads, and famously described the experience as "taking Archie Bunker to the Vatican," highlighting the band's populist sensibilities and their aversion to European culture. "Danny Fields, who was the manager of the talking heads, said that taking the Ramones to Europe Was like taking Archie Bunker to the Vatican, they were populists, not artists." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 57 - Punk and New Wave at New York's CBGB | https://www.ftr70.com/
