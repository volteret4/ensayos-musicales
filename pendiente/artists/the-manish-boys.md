# artist - The Manish Boys

## members
- Davy Jones (frontman)

## songs
**I Pity the Fool (1965)** : This single, a cover of Bobby Bland, featured Davy Jones as frontman. A notable aspect of this recording is that the guitar solo was played by a young, hot-shot session guitarist named Jimmy Page.
