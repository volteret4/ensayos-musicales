# artist - Whitey's Lindy Hoppers

## curiosities
**Pioneers of Lindy-Hop** : Whitey's Lindy Hoppers were the dancers who invented the Lindy-Hop at the Savoy Ballroom and had a dedicated section of the floor for their performances. "The Savoy was where the Lindy-Huppers invented, and it was the place to dance." ← Episode 4： ＂Choo Choo Ch'Boogie＂ by Louis Jordan | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DFZnwvsXGJM
**Strike at Basie/Webb Cutting Contest** : During the after-hours cutting contest, Whitey's Lindy Hoppers, led by "Whitey," went on strike and danced only to Count Basie's music, refusing to perform for Chick Webb's band after Webb insulted them following a misunderstanding.
