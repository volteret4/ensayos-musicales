# artist - Honor Blackman & Patrick Macnee

## songs
**Kinky Boots (1964)** : In February 1964, Honor Blackman and her co-star Patrick Macnee released this novelty single. The song capitalized on the "kinky" buzzword of the day, which had gained prominence in the wake of the Profumo sex scandal and the popularization of "Kinky Boots" as fashionable attire, particularly those worn by Blackman's character in "The Avengers."
