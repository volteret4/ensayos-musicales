**Great Blues Guitarist and Showman** : Guitar Slim was one of the great blues guitarists of the 1950s and an exceptional showman, famous for his performance style which included using a guitar cord allegedly 350 feet long. This allowed him to remain plugged into his amplifier while walking through the crowd and even out into the street during his performances.
