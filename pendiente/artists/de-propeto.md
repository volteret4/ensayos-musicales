# artist - De Propeto

## curiosities
**Early Gabber Producer** : De Propeto was instrumental in defining and popularizing the sound of Gabber to the young Dutch audience. He worked alongside DJ Paul Elstak and his Rotterdam Records label to establish the genre's brutal sound and energy.
