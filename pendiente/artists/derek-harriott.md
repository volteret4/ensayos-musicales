## albums
**Lollipop Girl (Full Band Version)** : After Duke Reid wore out his exclusive copy of "Lollipop Girl," he brought Derek Harriott into the studio along with musicians from a local Big Band to record a new version. This updated recording featured a full band backing Harriott and was subsequently released as a record by Reid, in addition to being played on his sound system. ## songs
**Lollipop Girl** : Derek Harriott and Claudette Sang initially recorded this song with just a piano backing. They sold their single copy to a small sound system owner, where its success led to it being traded to Coxsone Dodd for an American record. The song became so popular that Duke Reid eventually acquired a copy for himself, leading to a tense confrontation with Dodd. "One of the big examples of this was Lollipop Girl, a song by Derek Harriet and Claudix Sang.
