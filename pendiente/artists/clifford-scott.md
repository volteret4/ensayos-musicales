## curiosities
**Lead Saxophonist on "Honky Tonk"** : Clifford Scott, Bill Doggett's saxophone player, took over the main instrumental line on "Honky Tonk" after Billy Butler's initial guitar noodling. Scott played a "honking saxophone version" of the riff, becoming the lead instrumentalist for the spontaneous jam that became a hit.
