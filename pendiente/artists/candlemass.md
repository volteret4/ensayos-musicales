# artist - Candlemass

## genres

## curiosities
**European Doom Pioneer** : Candlemass was among the European bands in the 1980s that chose to deviate from the rapid pace of contemporary metal. They placed slowness, heaviness, and atmosphere at the core of their musical proposal, effectively exploring the potential of these elements to shape the emerging Doom Metal sound.
