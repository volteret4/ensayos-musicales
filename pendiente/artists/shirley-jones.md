## curiosities
**Singing Ability in The Partridge Family** : When *The Partridge Family* sitcom was being cast, Shirley Jones, David Cassidy's real stepmother, was initially believed to be the only cast member who could sing, before producers discovered David Cassidy's vocal talents.
