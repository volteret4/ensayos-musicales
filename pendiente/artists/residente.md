## albums
**Residente (2017) - Global Transcending Rap** : This self-titled album by Residente, a former member of Calle 13, goes far beyond a typical rap record. It incorporates numerous influences from world music, features multiple languages, and includes collaborations with many important artists. It is a truly complete album, considered the best and most interesting album made in Spanish, marking Residente's transcendence from rap and urban culture into a new, more comprehensive musical territory.
