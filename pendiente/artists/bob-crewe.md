# artist - Bob Crewe

## curiosities
**Diet Pepsi Melody Inspiration (1966)** : In 1966, Bob Crewe, who had a track record of writing and producing hits for The Four Seasons, identified the catchy melody in a Diet Pepsi ad titled "Music to Watch Girls By." He subsequently decided to adapt this jingle into an instrumental pop single.
