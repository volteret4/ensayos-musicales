## curiosities
**Following Hawkins' Musical Path** : Canned Heat is identified as one of the 1960s white rock acts that followed in Dale Hawkins' musical footsteps. Their sound incorporated elements such as electric bass, Chess-style guitar riffs, and country-inflected vocals, which were pioneered and featured in Hawkins' "Suzy Q."
