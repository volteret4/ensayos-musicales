**"Scary Monsters" Video Extra** : Steve Strange, who later became the frontman of the New Romantic band Visage, was plucked from London's avant-garde Blitz Club to be an extra in David Bowie's groundbreaking 1980 music video for his "Scary Monsters" album. This early exposure was part of the video's significant cultural impact that helped launch the New Romantic movement.
