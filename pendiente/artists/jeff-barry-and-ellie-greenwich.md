# artist - Jeff Barry and Ellie Greenwich

## songs
**Doo Wah Diddy (1964)** : This song, originally titled "Doo Warr Diddy," was written by Jeff Barry and Ellie Greenwich. Like many of their songs, it was based around a nonsense phrase, possibly tapping into the longer history of "Didiwad Didi" in African American folklore. Greenwich's demo version is noted as an early example of bubblegum pop and a very early use of fuzz guitar, a stylistic hallmark of the genre.
