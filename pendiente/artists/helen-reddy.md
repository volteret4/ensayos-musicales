## songs
**Angie Baby (1974) - Mysterious #1 Hit** : This song was the last number one hit of 1974, described as "unusual," "mysterious dark," and enigmatic. Its lyrics portray Angie as a loner, pulled out of school, who finds escape by listening to songs on her radio. A "neighbor boy with evil on his mind" enters her room intending to dance, but as Angie lowers the radio's volume, he "gets smaller with the sound" and appears to be absorbed into the radio, "never to be found." Helen Reddy herself preferred not to interpret the song, stating she would "rather hear how others interpreted" it. The songwriter, Alan O'Day, was inspired by The Beatles' "Lady Madonna" and the concept of someone growing up with radio as a constant soundtrack to their life, believing "the radio really speaks for us." The name "Angie" may also have been influenced by The Rolling Stones' song of the same name. Prior to Reddy recording it, the song was offered to Cher.
