**Visionary A&R Man** : Ralph Bass was a visionary A&R man who signed The Famous Flames to Federal Records. He was exceptionally confident in "Please Please Please," championing the song against strong opposition from his boss, Sid Nathan. Brown's autobiography even suggests Bass was fired and rehired due to the song's success, highlighting his conviction.
