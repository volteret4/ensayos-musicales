## curiosities
**Reluctance to Adopt "Ripping" Style** : Johnny Johnson, Chuck Berry's regular pianist, initially disliked the "ripping" piano technique popularized by Jerry Lee Lewis, considering it "all flash and no technique." Despite his reluctance, Leonard Chess insisted he adopt the style for Berry's new records, even demonstrating it himself. Johnson later acknowledged that the simple technique did make the records more exciting and appealed to kids.
