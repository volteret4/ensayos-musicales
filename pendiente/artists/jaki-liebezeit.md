**Free Jazz Background and Collaborations** : Jaki Liebezeit was a free jazz drummer who had previously played with Chet Baker and lived in Spain in the early 1960s, where he notably shared the stage with the renowned Tete Montoliu at the Jamboreé de Barcelona. **Pivotal Role in Can's Rhythmic Sound** : Liebezeit's drumming was a crucial and distinguishing element of Can's sound, emphasizing percussions over melodies. His powerful contribution was evident in tracks like "Yoo Doo Right," where his drums created a tribal mantra, and on "Tago Mago," where his drumming was described as being "in a state of grace." His funky, precise, and polished rhythms were later highlighted on "Ege Bamyasi." "Un mantra tribal que genera la batería de Liberty Side, a cual se repita lo largo del tema con sus tiles variaciones, hypnotica y tensa. ...
