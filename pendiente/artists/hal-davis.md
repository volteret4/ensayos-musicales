# artist - Hal Davis

## curiosities
**Producer for Diana Ross** : Hal Davis produced Diana Ross's "Love Hangover." When Ross initially disliked the music, Davis transformed the studio into a club setting with red lights and a strobe light to help her feel the mood, which led to her genuine laughter being captured on the track.
