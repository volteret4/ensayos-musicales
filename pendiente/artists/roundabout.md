## curiosities
**Formed by Chris Curtis** : After his solo single failed, Chris Curtis attempted to form a band named Roundabout based on a rotating musician concept. "Curtis then tried to form a band, which he named Roundabout, based on the concept that musicians could hop on or hop off at any point, with Curtis as the only constant member." ← Episode 113： ＂Needles and Pins＂ by The Searchers | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=izC9ZBI0UHM
**Precursor to Deep Purple** : The guitarist (Richie Blackmore) and keyboard player (John Lord) quickly decided they would prefer Curtis to leave, and subsequently formed Deep Purple.
