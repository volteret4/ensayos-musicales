# artist - Jack Neal

## curiosities
**Buddy and Jack Duo** : Jack Neal performed with Buddy Holly as "Buddy and Jack," with Neal singing while Holly played the music, and some early recordings of this duo survive. "He would also appear on the radio with another friend Jack Neal, as Buddy and Jack." ← Episode Sixty-One： ＂That'll Be the Day＂, by the Crickets | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KVJMKLc54O0
**Departure from Radio (1950s)** : Jack Neal, who was a few years older than Buddy Holly, decided to leave the "Buddy and Jack" radio show after getting married, citing a lack of time for radio commitments. "When Jack Neal, who was a few years older than Buddy, got married and decided he didn't have time for the radio anymore, the Buddy and Jack show became the Buddy and Bob show." ← Episode Sixty-One： ＂That'll Be the Day＂, by the Crickets | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KVJMKLc54O0
