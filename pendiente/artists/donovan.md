## genres

## curiosities
**Influence on Space Oddity Sound** : Donovan was a pioneer of psychedelic folk and folk rock whose "trippy grooves" influenced David Bowie's "Space Oddity." This new sound, part of Bowie's move away from Mod Rock, helped usher him into his "Glam Starman" phase.
