## songs
**Al Yoop (Year Unknown)** : A novelty record put together by Kim Fowley and Gary Paxton. It became a hit, leading to the formation of a touring band for The Hollywood Argales that included Derry Weaver and Marshall Leib. Gaynell Hodge played piano and Sandy Nelson played drums on the record.
