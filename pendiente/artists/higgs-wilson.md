## songs
**Come on Manioe** : This R&B track is considered the first milestone in the development of Ska. Produced by Edward Seaga, it incorporated a Mento rhythm by having the guitarist stress the off-beats, creating a uniquely Jamaican sound despite an otherwise straightforward New Orleans-style R&B composition with a Trisio bass line.
