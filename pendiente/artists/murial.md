# artist - Murial

## genres
- Electronic Dance Music

## curiosities
**Atmosphere of Distant Memory** : In addition to vocal manipulation, Murial incorporates deep textures and ambient sound effects, which collectively imbue her music with an atmosphere of distant memory, as if recalling an experience like walking in the rain. While her tracks can be danced to, they are not characterized as cheerful music, highlighting the complex emotional impact achieved through her sonic choices. "Además, le mete texturas graves y efectos de sonido ambiente. **Manipulation of Pitch and Textures** : Murial's music features fast rhythms and structures typical of electronic dance music, but she manipulates the pitch of vocal samples by lowering or distorting them. This technique gives the vocals a ghostly and ethereal quality, distinguishing her sound from conventional dance music.
