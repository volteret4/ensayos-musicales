## songs
**Only in America (1963)** : This song was originally written by Libra and Stuller for The Drifters as a subtle satire with black performers singing about the American dream. However, Atlantic Records, fearing it would be tasteless in the 1963 racial climate, used The Drifters' backing track and had Jay and the Americans, a white group, record new vocals, transforming it into a straightforward piece of boosterism.
