# artist - William Duke Rayner

## curiosities
**Discovery of Linda Martell (1969)** : William Duke Rayner, a furniture store owner from Nashville, discovered Linda Martell in 1969 after hearing her cover country songs at an Air Force Base in South Carolina. Impressed by the audience's reaction, he convinced her to let him fund a demo record and become her manager. **Lawsuit against Linda Martell (1970)** : In May 1970, Rayner filed a lawsuit against Linda Martell, asserting that he was due royalties and claiming she lacked the business ability to manage her money. He contended that she would "dissipate said money unless the court orders her to set aside and deposit with the court at least 25% of all that she earns."
