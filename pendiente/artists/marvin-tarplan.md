## curiosities
**Long-Term Collaboration with Smokey Robinson** : After being "borrowed" by Smokey Robinson for a Miracles tour following The Primettes' audition, Marvin Tarplan's temporary assignment extended into a 50-year collaboration. He continued to work with Robinson, both in The Miracles and on his solo records until 2008, and co-wrote many of Robinson's most successful hits.
