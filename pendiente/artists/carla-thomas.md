# artist - Carla Thomas

## labels
- Satellite Records
- Stax Records

## songs
**Because I Love You (Year Unknown)** : Carla Thomas sang this song with her father, Rufus Thomas, for the first session in the new Stax studio. The track became a local hit and led to a distribution deal with Atlantic Records. "For the first session in the new studio, they recorded a song Rufus had written, because I love you, with a few musicians that he knew" ← Episode 105： ＂Green Onions＂ by Booker T.and the MGs | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cw6xJG8E_w0
**GWARE's look at his eyes (Year Unknown)** : This solo record by Carla Thomas, an abbreviation for "Gee Whiz, Look at His Eyes," became a significant hit for Satellite Records, reaching number five on the R&B chart and ten on the pop charts. Despite a difficult recording session involving an impromptu string arrangement, it proved Satellite could achieve real success. "Despite this, GWARE's went to number five on the R&B chart, and ten on the pop charts. Satellite records had a real hit." ← Episode 105： ＂Green Onions＂ by Booker T.and the MGs | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cw6xJG8E_w0

## curiosities
**Astonishing Voice** : Carla Thomas, daughter of Rufus Thomas, possessed an "astonishing voice" that impressed those at Stax Records. "Thomas also brought in his daughter, Kala, who happened to have an astonishing voice." ← Episode 105： ＂Green Onions＂ by Booker T.and the MGs | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cw6xJG8E_w0
**Head Arrangement for Strings** : For her song "GWARE's," a string section was booked, but the trumpet player Bob Talley failed to write an arrangement. Jim Stewart had to direct the string players to perform a "head arrangement," giving them simplified instructions like "just play doughnuts" (meaning semi-breves or whole notes). "Stewart had to make the string players play from a head arrangement, something string players normally never do, and ended up giving them directions like just play doughnuts" ← Episode 105： ＂Green Onions＂ by Booker T.and the MGs | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cw6xJG8E_w0
**Solo Success** : Following the local hit "Because I Love You" with her father, Carla Thomas released a solo record, "GWARE's look at his eyes" (Gee Whiz, Look at His Eyes), which achieved significant chart success, reaching number five on the R&B chart and ten on the pop charts.
