# artist - Ricardo Campoy

## curiosities
**Co-founder of Max Music** : Ricardo Campoy, a salesman who previously sold Italo Disco maxis in discotheques, decided with Miguel DeGa to form their own company, Max Music, in 1964. They were central figures in the Spanish Megamix phenomenon and were known for their unscrupulous business practices. "Miguel de Gaid Ricardo Campoy, dos comerciales 20 a.C. que se ganaban la vida vendiendo Maxis de Italo Disco en discotecas, deciden que ha llegado el momento de formar su propia empresa y crean el sillo Max Music en 1964." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Departure from Max Music and Founding of Vale Music** : After years of tension and internal disputes, Ricardo Campoy decided to leave Max Music and establish his own record label, Vale Music. Accounts vary, with Campoy stating he took a self-loan of 75 million pesetas and brought the Dream Team and other workers with him, while sources closer to DeGa claimed he illegally emptied Max Music's account. This move effectively ended Max Music and marked the beginning of Vale Music. "Ricardo Campoy decidió abandonar Max Music y fundar su propia sello." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Kidnapping Attempt Target** : On September 3, 1998, Miguel DeGa hired 13 hitmen to kidnap Ricardo Campoy as retaliation for his departure and the founding of Vale Music. The kidnappers, however, mistakenly abducted José María Castells due to him driving the same car model as Campoy.
