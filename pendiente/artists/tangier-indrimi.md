# artist - Tangier Indrimi

## curiosities
**Influence on Juanitri Expon-Never** : Tangier Indrimi (Tangerine Dream) is explicitly mentioned as an influence on Juanitri Expon-Never's soundtrack for *Good Time*. This influence contributed to the unique blend of electronic textures and atmospheric intensity characteristic of the film's score.
