## curiosities
**Conceptual DJ and Discography** : Tien de Crecy is a famous DJ known for creating conceptual music and boasts a considerably long discography. His musical style, characterized as "discotecquero" electronic, was highly representative of the French electronic scene during the mid-1990s, distinguishing him from the more ambient sound preferred by the future members of Air.
