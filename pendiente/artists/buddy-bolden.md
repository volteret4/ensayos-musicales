## curiosities
**Hospitalized with Schizophrenia** : From 1907 onwards, Buddy Bolden was admitted to a psychiatric hospital due to schizophrenia, which helps establish the latest possible date (1906) for his purported playing of "My Book It's Got A Whole in It" before his institutionalization. **Pioneering Jazz Trumpeter** : Buddy Bolden is remembered as a great jazz trumpeter, with claims that he originally played the jazz standard "My Book It's Got A Whole in It" around the turn of the 20th century, potentially dating the melody to 1906. However, no recordings of him performing the song survive.
