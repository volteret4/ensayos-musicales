**House Band at El Monte Stadium** : Kip Tyler and The Flips were considered a "hot band" in Los Angeles, particularly serving as the house band for Art Lebo's regular shows at El Monte Stadium. These shows were later immortalized by The Penguins in their song "Memories of El Monte."
