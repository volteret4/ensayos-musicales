## curiosities
**Early Career - Gospel to R&B** : Stewart began his career in the gospel field before transitioning to vocal group R&B. He occasionally filled in for a group called The Rainbows, which featured Don Covay, who later became a well-known soul singer. **Minor Bluesman Status** : Billy Stewart is described as a largely unknown figure and a minor bluesman on Chess Records. His style was considered "too close to soul music" for the traditional Chicago blues fans of Chess to fully embrace him.
