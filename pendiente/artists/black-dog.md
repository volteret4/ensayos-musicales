## members

## labels

## albums
**Artificial Intelligence (1992)** : This trio was featured on the Warp Records compilation, which was pivotal in recontextualizing electronic music from the rave scene for home listening, giving rise to the IDM genre. Black Dog had already been pushing the boundaries of the genre with their early, critically acclaimed EPs.
