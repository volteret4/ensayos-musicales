# artist - The Maccabees

## albums
**Complete Works (Box Set) (October 20)** : This is a large and expensive (85 euros) box set on vinyl that compiles all of The Maccabees' material, along with a vinyl disc of "unreleased" content. This "unreleased" material consists of previously available B-sides and singles rather than truly new tracks. Individual reissues of their albums are not planned for now, only this complete box set.
