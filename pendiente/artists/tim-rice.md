# artist - Tim Rice

## curiosities
**Co-Creator of Jesus Christ Superstar** : Tim Rice co-created the musical *Jesus Christ Superstar* with Andrew Lloyd Webber. Due to a lack of funds for a stage show, it was first released as an album in 1970, with the first stage performance not occurring until October 1971.
