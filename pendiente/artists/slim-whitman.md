## songs
**Rose Marie (1955)** : This hit set a chart benchmark in England, spending a record 11 weeks at number one on the UK singles chart, a record that would not be beaten for 36 years until a Brian Adams song produced by Mut Lang in 1991. **International Yodeling Star** : Slim Whitman was an American country and western star popular in the 1950s, known worldwide for his yodeling vocals. His popularity extended across America, Europe, and even parts of Africa.
