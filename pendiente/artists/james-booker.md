# artist - James Booker

## instruments

## curiosities
**New Orleans Piano Player** : James Booker was one of two prominent New Orleans piano players brought in for Little Richard's first Specialty Records session. He played in the same style as Fats Domino and was a veteran of sessions for Domino or artists who worked in Domino's style.
