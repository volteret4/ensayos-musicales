## curiosities
**Co-Writer of "I Want Elvis for Christmas" (as Walden Kosoto)** : Bobby Darin, then known as Walden Kosoto, co-wrote the novelty song "I Want Elvis for Christmas." He later changed his name and became a significant figure in music. "a young man named Walden Kosoto, who would soon change his name to Bobby DARREN." ← Episode 40： ＂Drugstore Rock 'n' Roll＂ by Janis Martin | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l0i2p9TelN0
**Pioneering Apollo Performance (Pre-1957)** : Bobby Darin played the Harlem Apollo before The Crickets, disproving the claim that The Crickets were the first white act to perform there. "Bobby Daring had played there before them, and I think so had the jazz drummer Buddy Rich and maybe one or two others." ← Episode 74： ＂It Doesn't Matter Any More＂ by Buddy Holly | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6unib5T2mDQ
