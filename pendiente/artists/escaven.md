## curiosities
**Assembly Music Contest Winner** : Escaven won the music contest at the Assembly festival in 1993, 1995, and 2002. His atmospheric and catchy sounds have allowed him to continue working in the industry today, specifically in sound design for video games. "Les caven que ganó el concurso de música de la Asemble y de 1993, 1995-2002." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
**Second Reality Music Composer** : Escaven composed music for the "Second Reality" demo by the Finnish group Future Crew, presented at the 1993 Assembly. This demo pushed the boundaries of what was believed possible on a PC, featuring morphing faces and rotating vectorial environments, earning a standing ovation. "La música, compuesta por Escaven y Parpele Motion, sonadas intronizadas con efectos que son las que creían posible en un PC." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
**Video Game Sound Designer** : Thanks to his atmospheric and catchy sounds, Escaven continues to work in the video game industry, specializing in sound design for games such as Alan Wake and Control. "Pues gracias a sus sonidos ambos féricos y pegaditos, había de hoy siguen la industria, trabajando en el diseño de sonido de juegos como Alamwayco Control." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
