## songs
**Gotta Travel On (1959)** : This song, originally titled "Done Laid Around," was Billy Grammar's first single for Monument Records. Despite a copyright issue where the label initially believed it to be in the public domain and had to reissue it with Paul Clayton credited, the record was a success, reaching number four on the pop charts.
