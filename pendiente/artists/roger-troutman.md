## curiosities
**Dayton, Ohio Funk Pioneer** : Roger Troutman, along with his band Zapp, originated from Dayton, Ohio, a city known as the "birthplace of funk." He was a recognizable figure in the local community, often seen in everyday places like Kroger. His contributions, alongside other Dayton funk bands, helped define the sound of the 70s and 80s.
