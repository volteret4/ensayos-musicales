## albums
**14-year-old high school PC-fascistite, Lord's Republic (2014)** : Initially released as a pair of cassettes in 2014, these recordings were later re-edited and released on vinyl under this title. The band's sound is described as ultra-fast, lo-fi punk rock with "martian" vocals, combining hardcore punk with elements of power pop and new wave. ## curiosities
**Distinctive Sound** : The band's sound is characterized as resembling "gods reproduced at 45 revolutions per minute, on a record player that urgently needs a needle change," conveying a raw, high-speed, and somewhat distorted quality. "la banda suena como dios reproducidos a 45 revoluciones por minuto, en un tocalisco es que necesita un cambio de hoja urgentemente." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Formation and Style (2013)** : The Conjets formed in 2013 in Indiana, playing an ultra-accelerated, lo-fi punk rock with "martian" vocals. Their sound blended the energy of hardcore punk with influences from Power Pop and New Wave. "Una de estas bandas fue de conjets, formada en 2013 en Indiana, una banda que tocaba a Punx Rock Low Fy, ultra acelerado, con voces marcianas, que tenía mucho de ese hardcore punk, pero también de Power Pop y New Wave." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Foundational Role in Eckpank** : The Conjets are considered a foundational band of the Eckpank genre, with their first LP, released by Tequeton Traejer, seen as a cornerstone of the movement. They were also positioned at the extreme end of the Eckpank spectrum in an influential 2018 Instagram diagram.
