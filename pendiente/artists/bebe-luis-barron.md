## curiosities
**Forbidden Planet (1956) Score - First Electronic and Magnetic Tape Soundtrack** : For the 1956 film *Forbidden Planet*, the musical duo Bebe, Luis Barron created the first entirely electronic film score. This work also marked a historical milestone as the first score to be recorded on magnetic tape. Their avant-garde composition was ahead of its time, serving as an inspiration for numerous musicians seeking to create sounds for unknown worlds and extraterrestrial spacecraft, thereby immersing audiences in a fascinating and pioneering sonic universe. "Para la película de 1956, Forbidden Planet, la pareja de músicos, Bebe, Luis Barron, crearon la primera banda sonora totalmente electrónica y marcaron un hído en ser también la primera partito de la realizada en Cinta Magnética." ← Las Bandas Sonoras Más Atípicas y Revolucionarias del Cine | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fiSrdUP3YXY
