## curiosities
**Formation and Sound (1981)** : Grimorio Oscuro was formed in Donostia in 1981 and possessed a characteristic sound of the scene, drawing comparisons between The Cure and Joy Division. They released two singles but primarily remained an underground act. "bandas como Grimen Sorka, formada en Don Austin, en 1981, tenían ese sonido tan característico de la escena, entre Vikyour y Jordi Vision." ← La España Siniestra ｜ La edad de oro del Postpunk y el Dark wave | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN_buvQC69c
**Transformation into La Dama se Esconde (1983)** : In 1983, Grimorio Oscuro evolved and transformed into the band La Dama se Esconde, which then adopted a pop sound closer to New Wave. This shift led them to achieve relative success outside the underground during the decade.
