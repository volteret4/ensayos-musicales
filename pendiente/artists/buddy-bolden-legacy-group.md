# artist - Buddy Bolden Legacy Group

## curiosities
**Facsimile Recording of My Book It's Got a Whole In It** : This group undertook the project of creating a "reasonable facsimile" of how the legendary jazz trumpeter Buddy Bolden would have played "My Book It's Got a Whole In It," as no original recordings of Bolden exist. "a group called the Buddy Bolden Legacy Group have put together what, other than the use of modern recording, seems a reasonable facsimile of how Bolden would have played the song." ← Episode 54： Keep A Knockin | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oA4Y_K9E5Y4
