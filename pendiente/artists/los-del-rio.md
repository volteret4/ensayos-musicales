# artist - Los Del Rio

## songs
**Macarena (1996)** : This song was the winner of the 1996 Billboard Song of the Summer. It is described as a "bilingual novelty hit" that spent a staggering 14 weeks at number one, with approximately half of those weeks accumulated between late July and Labor Day.
