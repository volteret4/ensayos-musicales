# artist - Stoney Edwards

## genres
- Country

## songs
**She's My Rock (1973)** : Stoney Edwards was the first artist to record the song "She's My Rock" in 1973, a song that George Jones later had a hit with in 1984. "that version was by the first artist to record at Stony Edwards in 1973." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 53 - More than Charley Pride - African Americans in 70s Country | https://www.ftr70.com/

## curiosities
**Industrial Accident and Delayed Career** : Stoney Edwards suffered a horrific industrial accident where he was accidentally sealed into an airtight tank, taking two years to recover. This health issue and a later start in his career (in his 40s rather than 20s) may have prevented him from achieving a larger following despite his "wonderful" voice. "He had a horrific industrial accident when he was accidentally sealed into an airtight tank and it took two years for him to recover from that." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 53 - More than Charley Pride - African Americans in 70s Country | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Regina Taylor's "Star Category" (1975)** : In a 1975 article in the Terahot Tribune, Regina Taylor identified Stoney Edwards as one of three African Americans deserving of "star category" status in country music, alongside Charlie Pride and O.B.
