## curiosities
**Championing "Suzy Q" at Atlantic** : Jerry Wexler, an executive at Atlantic Records, became enthusiastic about Dale Hawkins' "Suzy Q" after hearing it. He informed Leonard Chess that if Chess Records did not release the single, Atlantic would, which prompted Chess to finally put the record out.
