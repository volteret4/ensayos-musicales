# artist - City Boy

## genres
- Pop Rock (proto-hair metal)

## albums
**City Boy (1977) - Debut Album** : Mut Lang's production on this debut album by the Birmingham band contributed to his growing reputation as a fastidious producer with a rich production sound. His work here also secured him a job with The Boomtown Rats. ## songs
**5705 (1978)** : This single, titled after the last four digits of a phone number for a would-be hookup, reached number 27 on the Hot 100. It served as a prototype for Tommy Tutone's 1982 hit "867-5309/Jenny" and foreshadowed the distinctive Mut Lang sound: an intricate, high-pitched vocal arrangement paired with an explosive, fist-pumping chorus.
