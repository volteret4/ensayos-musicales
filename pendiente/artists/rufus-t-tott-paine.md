# artist - Rufus T. Tott Paine

## curiosities
**Influence on Hank Williams** : Rufus T. Tott Paine, an African American street musician, was instrumental in teaching Hank Williams how to play guitar chords and how to incorporate blues into country western music. This demonstrates the profound influence of black musicians on the foundational figures of country music.
