## curiosities
**Avatar of Roots-Oriented Rock** : Edmonds quickly became recognized as the "avatar" of the roots-oriented approach to rock music. He was a central figure at the "crux" of the pub rock scene and went on to produce albums for several bands within this scene, including Ducks Deluxe and the American band Eggs Over Easy. **Formation of Rockpile** : He formed an on-again, off-again band called Rockpile with Nick Lowe, named after Edmonds' 1972 LP. While the band played gigs more frequently than they recorded, they were instrumental in infusing retro rock elements into what would eventually become the New Wave sound.
