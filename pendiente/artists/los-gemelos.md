# artist - Los Gemelos

## curiosities
**Resident DJs at Puzzle (1980s)** : "Los Gemelos" were the resident DJs at Puzzle, renowned for their legendary Sunday sessions that began at 6 AM. These sessions were particularly famous for attracting crowds from other major Ruta clubs like Spook, Barraca, and Chocolate, making Puzzle a pivotal after-party destination. "Míticas eran sus sesiones de los domingos a la 6 de la mañana cuando se llenaba de gente procedente de Spunk, perraca o chocolate, obra de los gemelos, los DJes residentes." ← Cuando Valencia fue la capital mundial del Clubbing | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ckJgog8P_og
