**Early Girl Group** : The Chantels are mentioned as one of the early girl groups, with their phenomenon largely based in New York. The Shirelles' eventual name was chosen to be reminiscent of The Chantels with its "-elles" ending. "But if you remember the episodes on the bobbets and the shantels, girl groups in the early years were largely phenomenon based in New York and that's more or less the case with the Chirrells who didn't come from New York itself but from Pasey, New Jersey about 16 miles away." ← Episode 89： ＂Will You Love Me Tomorrow？＂ by the Shirelles | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Kns2HGcuSg
