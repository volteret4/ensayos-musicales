# artist - Pye Corner Audio

## curiosities
**Contributor to Ghost Box Mythology** : Pye Corner Audio is identified as one of the artists who helped establish the aesthetic and sonic mythology of Ghost Box Records. The label focused on themes of failed futures by creating a fictional sound archive based on library music, public service jingles, educational documentaries, and past television iconography, particularly drawing inspiration from the BBC Radiophonic Workshop.
