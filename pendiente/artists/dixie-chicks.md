## curiosities
**The "Dixie Chick Theory" for Decline of Protest Songs** : In March 2003, at a concert in London, Natalie Maines, the lead singer, stated that the band was ashamed that President George Bush was from Texas, in response to the invasion of Iraq. Within a week, country radio stations banned their music, and nearly 20 years later, many people have not forgiven Maines for her comments, contributing to a theory about why protest songs have ceased to be hits.
