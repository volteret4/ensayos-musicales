**Prophecy of Hair Metal's End** : Kip Winger, of the band Winger, recognized the impending demise of the hair metal era after watching Nirvana's music video for "Smells Like Teen Spirit" in 1991. His immediate reaction was, "We are finished. We all knew it. It was obvious," accurately predicting the abrupt end of their genre with the rise of grunge and alternative rock.
