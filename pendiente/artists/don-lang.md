# artist - Don Lang

## songs
**6-5 Hand Jive (March 1958)** : Don Lang, a prominent figure associated with the British TV show "6-5 Special," released this song in March 1958. It was directly inspired by the "Hand Jive" dance popularized on the show. Despite the "6-5 Special" not being shown in Sweden, the song spurred at least three sound-alike cover versions in that country by Inga Berggren, Toa Carson, and The Monkeys.
