# artist - Sean Combs

## songs
**I'll Be Missing You (1997)** : This song, released by Sean Combs (aka Puff Daddy), was the winner of the 1997 Billboard Song of the Summer. It was "built out of" The Police's "Every Breath You Take" and functioned as an elegy for Combs' deceased friend, rapper Biggie Smalls (aka The Notorious B.I.G.). Neither the song nor Combs's career is noted as having aged particularly well.
