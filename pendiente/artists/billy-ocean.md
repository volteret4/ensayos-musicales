## songs
**Caribbean Queen (1984)** : A number one pop and R&B smash hit for Billy Ocean, which was helmed by R&B producer Keith Diamond. This success prompted the Drive team to seek a single that could follow up on its chart performance. **Get Out of My Dreams, Get Into My Car (1988)** : A danceable, exuberant, and kitschy song produced and co-written by Mut Lang. It was a comeback hit for Billy Ocean after a string of mid-80s hits, rising to number five on the pop chart and just weeks away from scoring another number one. The sound was characterized as "Clattery Dance Pop with Husky Soul Vocals." **Loverboy (1985)** : Produced by Mut Lang, teaming with Keith Diamond and Ocean, this single took Billy Ocean in an entirely different direction with a sleek, space-age sound. It fused the dance pop Billy Ocean had been doing with the synth rock Mut Lang had developed for other artists like Def Leppard and The Cars. The accompanying video fittingly looked like an extension of the George Lucas's Star Wars universe, and the song reached number two on the Hot 100 in early 1985.
