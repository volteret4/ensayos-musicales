**Copyright Renewal Dispute with Richmond Organization** : In 1989, when the 28-year copyright term for "The Lion Sleeps Tonight" expired, George Weiss filed to renew the copyright and intended to prevent the Richmond Organization from publishing the song. This led to a court battle where Weiss successfully argued that the Richmond Organization had acknowledged him as a writer since 1961.
