## curiosities
**Seminal Hardcore Act** : Brian McMahan and Britt Walford were part of Squirrel Bait when they were 14 years old. The band is described as a seminal hardcore act and a precursor to the "mocor" (grunge) sound, with its members growing up in proximity to and interacting with other significant bands of the era. "Con 14 formaron parte de Squire-El-Bate, la seminal de hardcore y el sonido de mocor. Estos niñas programistas, con pintas de net, literalmente crecieron y secodeado en con Gisquier-Dew, Black Flug o Big Black." ← Spiderland： La Revolución del Rock Hecha por Adolescentes | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fLzXxQMqR78
