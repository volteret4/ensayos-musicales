## curiosities
**Shared Name Confusion** : The Capris was one of the names used by Jesse Belvin's group (which was also known as The Saxons, with Alex Hodge in this particular lineup instead of Gaynell). Confusingly, there was also another distinct group on the same label, also called The Saxons, who also recorded as The Capris, leading to at least one single being released with one group on each side, causing significant confusion for collectors.
