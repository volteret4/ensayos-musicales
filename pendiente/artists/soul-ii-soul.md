**Formed by Wild Bunch Hip Hop Purists** : The members within The Wild Bunch who were more purist to hip hop culture eventually formed Soul II Soul. This group later achieved significant recognition and, while not widely known, remains an important reference in 1990s music. "Los miembros más puristas de la cultura hip hop que estaba en The Wild Bunch acabaron formando un numeroso grupo que se ababa sol tu sol, que después acabó teniendo un montón de grame y sigue bueno, no es muy conocido, pero es un importante referente de la musiliano 90." ← Music Radar Clan > Massive Attack y El Origen del Trip Hop en Bristol. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bpOr4KKm5rM&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← soul-ii-soul
**Pioneer of Electronic Indie Fusion** : Soul II Soul (phonetically referred to as "Saletien") is cited as a foundational group for the birth of Trip Hop. They embody the "curious love story" between mass-market electronic music and indie music, creating a highly danceable indie sound with electronic foundations.
