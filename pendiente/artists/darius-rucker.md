# artist - Darius Rucker

## concerts

## curiosities
**Grand Ole Opry Induction (2012)** : Darius Rucker was inducted into the Grand Ole Opry in 2012. He is the only African American musician to be permitted membership in the Opry since Charlie Pride in 1967.
