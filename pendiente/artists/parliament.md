## albums
**Mother Ship Connection (1975)** : Released at the very end of 1975, this album became the first platinum P-Funk album in 1976 and represented their biggest crossover moment. Bootsy Collins was a highlight, especially on the second single, "Give Up the Funk (Tear the Roof off the Sucker)." The album cover famously depicted George Clinton, a Star Trek fan, emerging from a flying saucer.
