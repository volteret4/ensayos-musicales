# artist - Keith Carradine

## songs
**I'm Easy (1975)** : Keith Carradine achieved a hit with his song "I'm Easy," which he sang in Robert Altman's 1975 film "Nashville." Altman's directorial approach allowed actors to contribute their own songs to the movie.
