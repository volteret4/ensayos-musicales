## curiosities
**Drifters-Sounding Name (Lover Patterson Management)** : The Drapers were a group managed by Lover Patterson, who was also Benny King's manager. The group was intentionally given a name that sounded as similar to "The Drifters" as possible, suggesting an attempt to capitalize on their recognition.
