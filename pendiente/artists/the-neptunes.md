## curiosities
**Production of "Hot in Here"** : Production wizards Pharrell Williams and Chad Hugo, known as The Neptunes, were responsible for producing Nelly's 2002 Song of the Summer, "Hot in Here." They achieved this by taking an old Chuck Brown go-go record, amplifying its heat "high enough to make you quote take off all your clothes," and integrating St. Louis rapper Nelly's "Dirty South Style."
