## genres

## songs
**Car Wash (1977)** : This song, by the LA group Rose Royce, was a Hot 100 number one hit in early 1977. Similar to "Jive Talkin'," it was constructed around a popping funk bass line and was featured on the soundtrack of a black exploitation film.
