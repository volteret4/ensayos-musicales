# artist - The Kinshasa Band

## curiosities
**Collaboration with Johnny Wakelin (1974)** : The Kinshasa Band collaborated with Johnny Wakelin to record and release the reggae-influenced song "Black Superman, Muhammad Ali" in 1974, which went on to become a number one pop hit in 1975.
