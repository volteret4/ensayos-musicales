## curiosities
**Demoscene Group Innovation** : The Future Crew was a demoscene group known for its fierce competition in creating the best intros and demos. Their drive led to significant innovations in global technology, particularly in animation and video game development. They are considered possibly the most legendary group in demoscene history. "Grupos como de Black Lotus, Parlight, The Future Crew, se picaven tanto por lograr los mejores síntricos que acabaran aportando muchas innovaciones a la tecnología mundial, tanto en animación como para los videojuegos." ← El oscuro origen de la música de los KEYGEN | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_dccHK0JW5w
**Remedy Entertainment Origin** : The Finnish game development company Remedy Entertainment, creators of Max Payne and Alan Wake, emerged from the ashes of Future Crew, arguably the most legendary group in demoscene history. **Second Reality (1993) - Landmark Demo** : The Finnish group Future Crew created "Second Reality," an acknowledged masterpiece demo for the 1993 Assembly. The music, composed by Escaven and Purple Motion, featured synchronized effects thought impossible on a PC, showcasing morphing faces and rotating vectorial environments. This demo stunned the audience, who applauded it with a standing ovation, demonstrating that a group of hobbyists could push PC hardware beyond what multimillion-dollar companies achieved.
