# artist - José María Castells

## member of

## curiosities
**Member of The Dream Team** : José María Castells formed "The Dream Team" with Quique Tejada and Tony Peret at Max Music. This group was responsible for producing almost all of the company's megamixes, achieving star status in Spain and providing the soundtrack for parties, road trips, and summers for an entire generation. **New Mixer for Max Music** : José María Castells, a young local DJ, was recruited by Max Music alongside Tony Peret to replace Mike Platinas and Javier Usia. They became the official Megamixers for the label following the success and innovation of Max Mix 3. "En Max Music no perdieron el tiempo, y encontraron los institutos perfectos de Mike Platinas y Javier Usia en Tony Peretta y José María Castéis, unos jóvenes digis locales." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Victim of Mistaken Identity Kidnapping** : On September 3, 1998, José María Castells was mistakenly kidnapped by 13 Mexican hitmen hired by Miguel DeGa, who intended to abduct Ricardo Campoy. Castells was targeted because he drove the same car model as Campoy. He was gagged and beaten but managed to escape his captors during a lapse in their vigilance, reaching a police station in Berga.
