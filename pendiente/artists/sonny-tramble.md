## curiosities
**Pioneering Electric Bass in Shreveport** : Sonny Tramble, a country musician who played with Jim Reeves, played the electric bass on Dale Hawkins' "Suzy Q." He was the only person in Shreveport at the time to own an electric bass, a deliberate choice by Hawkins to emulate electric blues sounds.
