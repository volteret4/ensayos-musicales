# artist - Al Lewis

## curiosities
**Career Revival** : Al Lewis's songwriting career, which had been in decline since his hits with Eddie Canter in the 1930s, was revitalized by the immense success of Fats Domino's "Blueberry Hill." This resurgence enabled him to re-establish himself as an R&B songwriter, contributing to songs for Domino himself and for Little Anthony and the Imperials.
