**Recording Advice for "The Nearness of You"** : During the recording of "The Nearness of You," Arif Mardin advised Norah Jones to hit the actual melody of the classic song at least for the first time, even though he liked her looser phrasing. This subtle guidance was typical of his approach, where he didn't try to "put [her] in a box or change anything," but focused on melodic clarity and essential production tasks like picking takes and comping vocals. "And he said, no, I know I like how you're phrasing it, but at least the first time, can you just hit the actual melody on the actual...
