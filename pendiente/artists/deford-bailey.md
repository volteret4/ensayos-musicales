# artist - DeFord Bailey

## concerts
- Grand Ole Opry

## instruments
- harmonica

## curiosities
**First African American Grand Ole Opry Member** : DeFord Bailey was the first African American to be allowed membership in the Grand Ole Opry, with his last performance there occurring in 1941. Charlie Pride became the second African American member in 1967.
