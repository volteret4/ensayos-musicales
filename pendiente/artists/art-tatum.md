## curiosities
**Influence on Ray Charles and "The Tatum" Term** : Art Tatum was a highly proficient blind jazz pianist who profoundly inspired Ray Charles with his musicality. His exceptional skill led to the coining of "the Tatum" in computational musicology, a term signifying the smallest time interval between successive notes in a rhythmic phrase.
