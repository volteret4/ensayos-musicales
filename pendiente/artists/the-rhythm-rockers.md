## members

## songs
**L-Loco Cha-Cha-Cha** : Richard Berry first heard this instrumental, originally by René Touzet, played by The Rhythm Rockers at a gig. He loved its intro and decided to "rip it off," which became the foundational element for "Louie Louie." "The song had its genesis in a piece that Barry heard played as an instrumental by a group he was singing with at a gig one night, The Rhythm Rockers.
