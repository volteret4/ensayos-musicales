## curiosities
**Formation and Pioneering Western Swing (1932)** : Formed in 1932 by Milton Brown after his departure from the Light Crust Doughboys due to low pay and creative differences. The Musical Brownies are widely recognized as the first band to play "proper Western Swing," setting many of the genre's defining characteristics.
