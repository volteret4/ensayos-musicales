# artist - Guy Tano Vastola

## songs
**My Boy Lollipop (1956) - Mobster Connection** : Guy Tano Vastola, a mobster, acquired the rights to "My Boy Lollipop" from Maurice Levy. He then managed teenage singer Barbara Gay and arranged for her to record the original 1956 version of the song with New York R&B session players.
