# artist - Carlos Alomar

## member of
- David Bowie

## instruments

## albums
**Young Americans (1975) - Guitarist** : Carlos Alomar was among the new collaborators on David Bowie's 1975 album 'Young Americans', contributing guitar to the album's "Plastic Soul" sound. He also co-wrote the album's biggest hit, "Fame," with Bowie and John Lennon. ## songs
**Fame (1975) - Funk Riff Creator & Co-writer** : Carlos Alomar contributed a "wicked funk riff" to the song "Fame" and co-wrote it with David Bowie and John Lennon. This funky riff was instrumental in making "Fame" a hit on Black Radio and contributing to its de facto disco sound on Top 40 radio, helping it become Bowie's first US number one single. **Bowie's Core Band Member** : Carlos Alomar was the guitarist in David Bowie's core band during the recording of *Low* and *Heroes*.
