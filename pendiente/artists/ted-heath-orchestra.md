# artist - Ted Heath Orchestra

## curiosities
**US Tour as Union Exchange (1955)** : The Ted Heath Orchestra toured the US as part of the "one-in, one-out" exchange agreement established between American and British musicians' unions in 1955. This tour was reciprocated by Stan Kenton's band touring the UK. The orchestra was not led by the former prime minister of the same name.
