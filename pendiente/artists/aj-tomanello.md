## curiosities
**"Suzy Q" Demo Drummer and Cowbell Origin** : AJ Tomanello played the drum part with its distinctive cowbell on the demo version of "Suzy Q." This part, which emerged from Hawkins hearing Tomanello play his drum and cowbell simultaneously during a soundcheck, was later closely copied by Ronnie Lewis for the final released version when Tomanello was unavailable for the session.
