## curiosities
**Admiration for Animals' Organ Sound** : Graham Bond, a renowned Hammond organ player, was greatly impressed by The Animals' electric organ sound after hearing them perform at the Club a Go-Go, leading him to facilitate their London gigs. "soon Bond was raving back in London about this group from Newcastle he'd heard." ← Episode 115： ＂House of the Rising Sun＂ by the Animals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=34aIGCnb4X4
**Origins from Alex Korner's Band** : This group was formed by Graham Bond and other members who had departed from Alex Korner's band. "Bond and a few other members of the corner group had quit, and had formed their own group, the Graham Bond organisation" ← Episode 115： ＂House of the Rising Sun＂ by the Animals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=34aIGCnb4X4
