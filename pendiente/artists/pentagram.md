**Early Deviation from Metal Trends** : In the early days of metal, as the genre began to lean towards faster, more technical, and virtuosic sounds, bands like Pentagram stood apart from this trend. They chose instead to recover and emphasize the darker roots that were emerging, predating the formalization of Doom Metal in the 1980s.
