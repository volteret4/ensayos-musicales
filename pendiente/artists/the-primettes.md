## curiosities
**Controversial Signing Reason** : The Primettes' signing was controversial among Motown staff, who widely believed they were brought on more due to Barry Gordy's attraction to member Diane Ross than for their talent. Florence Ballard, however, was well-regarded for her helpful and friendly demeanor. **Motown's First Girl Group** : The Primettes were the first girl group signed to Motown, preceding The Marvelettes. They were later renamed The Supremes but struggled to achieve success for several years after releasing only two singles.
