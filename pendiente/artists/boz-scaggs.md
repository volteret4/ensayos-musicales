# artist - Boz Scaggs

## songs
**Look What You've Done To Me (1980)** : This song was written by Boz Scaggs with lyrics inspired by watching the film footage of a slow dance scene between Bud and Pam in "Urban Cowboy." It became integral to the movie's narrative, playing during that specific moment.
