# artist - James Ferraro

## albums
**Far Side Virtual (Year not specified)** : This album was another important early release in the vaporwave scene. It differentiated itself by using fewer samplers and having a sound closer to Muzak and predefined digital timbres often associated with MIDI. The album was seen as visionary for evoking a world where technology and marketing had infiltrated and colonized personal space.
