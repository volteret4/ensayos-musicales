## curiosities
**Hypnotic Sound Technique** : His signature technique involved drastically slowing down beats, repeating song fragments, and rapping in a stretched, almost somnambulant manner to achieve a hypnotic and nearly narcotic effect. Witchhouse producers later adapted this exact technique to invoke a ghostly atmosphere. **Originator of Chopped & Screwed** : DJ e-Screw is a legendary figure who originated the Chopped & Screwed G-Hop scene in Houston during the 1990s. His celebrated "screwtapes" were influential and collected by musicians like Robert Disarro.
