## curiosities
**Travel to US with Ray Noble (Mid-1930s)** : As Ray Noble's vocalist, Al Bowley was permitted to travel to the US during the mid-1930s. This was allowed under a musicians' union agreement that restricted entire bands but sometimes permitted individual "non-instrumental performers" to cross borders if they employed local musicians.
