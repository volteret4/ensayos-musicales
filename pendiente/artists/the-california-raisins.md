# artist - The California Raisins

## genres
- R&B (covers)

## albums
**R&B Covers Albums (1988)** : Following the unexpected popularity of their animated ads, The California Raisins recorded a series of albums consisting of R&B covers. One of these albums achieved platinum status in 1988, demonstrating the commercial success of characters born from advertising. ## songs
**I Heard It Through the Grapevine (Year Unspecified)** : This cover of the Motown classic by Marvin Gaye was notably featured in a series of "weirdly popular" animated ads for the Raisin Growers Association. The immense popularity of these ads propelled The California Raisins' cover version onto the Hot 100 chart.
