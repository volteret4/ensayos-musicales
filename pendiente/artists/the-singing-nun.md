# artist - The Singing Nun

## songs
**Dominique (1963)** : This French-language song by a Belgian nun was the number 1 hit on December 14, 1963, placing it directly above The Kingsmen's "Louie Louie" on the charts. Its popularity in France was partially attributed to the word "Nika" in its chorus, which was a French swear word meaning "to fornicate."
