## curiosities
**Bluffing His Way into Manfred Mann** : When Manfred Mann was looking for a bass player, McGuinness, who was primarily a guitarist, bluffed to Paul Jones that he had switched to bass and got the job. He had never played bass until his first gig with the group, and the only question he was asked during his interview was if he was willing to play simple parts. "McGuinness was a guitarist, but bluffed to Paul Jones that he'd switched to bass, and got the job.
