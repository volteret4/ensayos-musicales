# artist - Javier Usia

## curiosities
**Co-mixer of the First Max Mix** : Javier Usia collaborated with Mike Platinas, the winner of Max Music's mixing contest, to record the first "Max Mix" album. This seminal release introduced advanced mixing techniques to Spain with unprecedented precision and imagination. "El concurso lo ganó Mike Platinas, quien junto a Javier Usia, grabaron el primer Max Mix." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Unpaid for Max Mix 2 and Departure from Max Music** : According to Mike Platinas, Javier Usia, along with Platinas himself, did not receive payment for their work on the second "Max Mix" album. This dispute over compensation led both mixers to abandon the project, leaving Max Music to find new talent for subsequent releases.
