## curiosities
**Genre Bridging Group** : This sibling group is recognized for bridging the gap between western swing and the emerging Bakersfield sound. They were poor migrants who moved to California before the main influx of "Oak Ease." They are often given credit for making the first rock and roll record, though this claim is considered largely meaningless. They were a significant influence on both the Bakersfield sound and the music that became rockabilly.
