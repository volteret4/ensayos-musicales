**Rock and Roll Waltz (1950s)** : Kay Starr's "Rock and Roll Waltz" is presented as an example of a female performer successfully combining genres, similar to Wanda Jackson's approach. Starr employed ambiguity in her recording, allowing it to be interpreted as either a parody of older styles from a youth perspective or a mockery of new styles from an older perspective.
