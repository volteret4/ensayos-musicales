**Little Richard's Piano Teacher and Clone** : Escarito, born Stephen Quincy Reader (also known as Escaucreader), was a gay, black R&B singer, known for his massive pompadour, mustache, and flamboyant, loud screaming vocal style. Little Richard consistently credited Escarito with teaching him to play the piano, claiming his own piano style was a copy of Escarito's and that Escarito was a superior player. Escarito later made records as a "Little Richard clone."
