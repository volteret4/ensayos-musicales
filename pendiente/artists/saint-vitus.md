## curiosities
**US Doom Pioneer** : In the United States during the 1980s, Saint Vitus was a key band that consciously moved away from the accelerating trend in metal. They began to explore the possibilities offered by slow and heavy tempos, focusing on creating a massive, oppressive atmosphere with prolonged and repetitive riffs.
