# artist - Nintendo

## curiosities
**GameCube Philosophy (2001)** : Nintendo deliberately distanced itself from the intense console war between Xbox and PlayStation 2, which had already claimed the Dreamcast as its first victim. Instead of focusing on technical advancement, Nintendo prioritized "fun over technical advancement," aiming for the GameCube to be perceived as "a box full of toys." "Nintendo se posicionó la favor de la diversión más cala de la avance técnico. Entender que en Cube era de una caja llena de juguetes." ← Por Qué Tu Cerebro Nunca Olvidará Este Sonido | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=86tqZ33gn_4
**Wii and Wii U Marketing Shift (2006, 2012)** : With the advent of the Wii and Wii U, Nintendo shifted its strategy to target the "casual" or "uninitiated" player. This involved designing a profoundly clean and friendly user experience, transforming the console's startup into a comfortable and welcoming space with a white environment, soft sounds, and muzak-like music, akin to entering a digital spa. "Pero con la llegada de Wii y Wii U, ya el objetivo cambió. Nintendo quería conquistar al jugador casual. Al no iniciado. Y para ello, diseñó una experiencia completamente distinta, profundamente polcra llamable. La arranque dejó de ser un espectaculo para convertirse en un espacio confortable." ← Por Qué Tu Cerebro Nunca Olvidará Este Sonido | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=86tqZ33gn_4
