## songs
**Burnt Biscuit (Year Unknown)** : This instrumental, written by Chips Moman, was the first single released on Bolt Records, a subsidiary of Stax. Despite Moman's belief that it could be as big as "Last Night," it did not become a hit. ## curiosities
**Named After Moman's Car** : The group The Triumphs was named after the Triumph sports car driven by Chips Moman, who wrote their single "Burnt Biscuit." This naming convention mirrored the initial idea for Booker T and the M.G.'s.
