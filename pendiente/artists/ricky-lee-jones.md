## songs
**Chuck E's in Love (1979)** : This song became Ricky Lee Jones' first single from her debut album, reaching number four on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1979. Although many perceive it as a happy song, Jones herself described it as a "desperate lament" about Chuck Weiss, whom she met working in the kitchen of The Troubadour. It lost the Song of the Year Grammy to "What a Fool Believes." "She said a lot of people think that it's a happy song, but it's really kind of a desperate lament. ## curiosities
**First Troubadour Performance and Key Meetings** : Jones made her debut at The Troubadour when a friend who was performing there asked her to sing a couple of songs. It was there that she met Chuck Weiss, who was working in the club's kitchen, and later Tom Waits, leading to the three of them hanging out and forming a significant, though turbulent, relationship with Waits.
