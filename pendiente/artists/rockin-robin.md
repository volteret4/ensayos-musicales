# artist - Rockin' Robin

## songs
**Louie Louie (Cover)** : Contractually released as a solo record, this was the Whalers' version, with Rockin' Robin as lead vocalist. He was notable for learning the song's lyrics directly from Richard Berry's original record, a fact that became ironic given the later widespread misinterpretation of the lyrics. His ad-libbed shout before the guitar solo was widely imitated.
