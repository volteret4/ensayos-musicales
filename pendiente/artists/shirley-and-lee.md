## curiosities
**Jamaican Sound System Popularity** : Like much New Orleans R&B, Shirley and Lee's music was hugely popular among Jamaican sound system audiences. This popularity influenced Coxsone Dodd's decision to seek out a singer with a similar voice to Shirley Goodman, leading to his discovery of Millie Small. "Like much new Orleans R&B, Shirley and Lee were hugely popular among the sound system listeners." ← Episode 114： ＂My Boy Lollipop＂ by Millie | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kf-DtqoOsRI
**Minor US Chart Success & Imitators** : Despite their popularity among sound system listeners in Jamaica, Shirley and Lee only achieved minor chart success in the United States. However, their distinctive sound spawned numerous imitators, including Jean and Eunice and Mickey and Sylvia.
