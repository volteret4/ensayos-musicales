## curiosities
**Lead Vocalist Role in The Coasters** : Billy Guy, along with Carl Gardner, often took the lead vocal parts in The Coasters' songs, particularly in the new style introduced with "Yakati Yak," where they sang in unison. "Here the lead vocal was taken in unison by the two longest serving members of the group, Gardner and Billy Guy." ← Episode 68： ＂Yakety Yak＂ by the Coasters | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dtMHDD0-oOo
**Pursued Solo Career** : Billy Guy decided to pursue a solo career but, unlike Cornel Gunter, he did not formally quit The Coasters. Instead, he would send a replacement to go out on the road for him while he continued to sing with the group in the studio. His solo records did not perform well, with several of them eventually being reissued under The Coasters' name, even though no other members of the group were involved. "Billy Guy decided he wanted to try a solo career but unlike Gunter, he didn't quit the group." ← Episode 68： ＂Yakety Yak＂ by the Coasters | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dtMHDD0-oOo
**Role in "Shopping for Clothes"** : In The Coasters' reworked version of "Shopping for Clothes," Billy Guy played the role of the shopper in the song's dialogue. He vaguely remembered about half of the original song's lyrics, which allowed Lieber and Stoller to reconstruct the track.
