# artist - Stevie Lang

## songs
**Hot Summer Nights (1979)** : As the lead singer for the band Knight, Stevie Lang's vocals on this single helped it reach number 18 on the Hot 100 in the summer of 1979, marking the last time she would have greater chart success than Mut Lang. ## curiosities
**Early Career and Marriage to Mut Lang** : Stevie Van Kirkken, also known as Stevie Van, knew Mut Lang since middle school and became the female harmony vocalist in his band Hocus. They married not long after Hocus broke up and briefly attempted to record as a duo.
