# artist - Gorgeous George

## curiosities
**Inspiration for James Brown's Cape Routine** : The caped wrestler Gorgeous George served as the inspiration for James Brown's elaborate live performance routine for "Please Please Please." During this routine, band members would drape Brown in a cape as he feigned distress and tried to continue singing.
