# artist - Mary Limmanson

## curiosities
**Life Saved by Exit Music (For a Film)** : Mary Limmanson, who was institutionalized for severe depression and a mental disorder, recounted in a *Rolling Stone* interview how Radiohead's *Exit Music (For a Film)* saved his life. During an escape from the institution, he planned to commit suicide by jumping from a cliff. However, just as he was on the verge of jumping, he heard the song, which prompted him to reflect for hours, ultimately preventing him from carrying out the act and preserving his life. He explicitly states that he is alive thanks to this song.
