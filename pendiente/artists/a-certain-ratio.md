# artist - A Certain Ratio

## albums
**To Each... (1981) - Re-edition** : This is a re-edition of A Certain Ratio's 1981 album, phonetically referred to as "Tugee" or "tu H" in the transcript. The original 1981 release was considered a standout album in the Post-Punk genre, which experienced an "incredible year" in 1981. Although perhaps not as widely remembered today as Joy Division, it was a very significant contribution to the Post-Punk scene.
