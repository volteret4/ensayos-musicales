# artist - Tom Wilson

## curiosities
**Animals' Post-Most Producer** : After The Animals parted ways with Mickey Most, they signed with Decca and engaged Tom Wilson as their new producer. He controversially produced their album "Animalism," farming out two tracks to a protégé using session musicians rather than the band itself. **Dylan's Producer and Experimenter** : Tom Wilson was Bob Dylan's new producer in late 1964. He conducted an experiment by overdubbing electric backing onto an outtake of Dylan's "House of the Rising Sun" from his first album sessions, an experiment with lasting ramifications for music.
