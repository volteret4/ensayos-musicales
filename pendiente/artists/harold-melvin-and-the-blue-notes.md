# artist - Harold Melvin and the Blue Notes

## genres
- Disco

## songs
**The Love I Lost (1973)** : This song was originally written as a ballad by Danny Gamble and Leon Huff. Session drummer Earl Young, working with MFSB, felt the song "lagged" and conceived the idea of "jazzing it up" by creating a driving groove with his kick drum and hi-hat, which became the pioneering "four on the floor" disco beat.
