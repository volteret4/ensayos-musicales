## curiosities
**Notable Members** : Several musicians who later became prominent with Cliff Richard and The Shadows, including Hank Marvin, Jett Harris, and Tony Meahin, had previously played with The Vipers. Additionally, The Two I's coffee bar, where The Vipers regularly performed, was the discovery place for artists like Tommy Steele and Terry Dean.
