**Bridge Between Genres** : Disarro Records acted as a crucial bridge, connecting Southern Kinghops with the emerging gothic scene that would define Witchhouse. **Founder of Disarro Records** : A musician from Houston, Robert Disarro founded Disarro Records in 2007. He grew up listening to DJ e-Screw and collecting his famous "screwtapes," which deeply influenced his musical sensibility. "Robert Dizarro, un músico de Houston que creció escuchando a DJ e-Screw, coleccionando sus celebres Greatapes, fundó en 2007 de Disarro Records, el seno que dictó los primeros trabajos de Salem, la banda afundacional del genero." ← Witch House： Cuando internet inventó su género maldito | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SLlzXA0eyu0
