# artist - Floyd Newman

## instruments
- Piano
- Saxophone

## curiosities
**Discovering Isaac Hayes** : Newman hired a young Isaac Hayes for his bar band, even though Hayes could initially only play piano with one hand. Newman recognized Hayes's innate "feel for the music," leading Hayes to eventually learn to play with both hands and become a key Stax musician. "Floyd Newman, who had hired a young man for his bar band when the young man could only play piano with one hand, just because he seemed to have a feel for the music." ← Episode 105： ＂Green Onions＂ by Booker T.and the MGs | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cw6xJG8E_w0
**Recommending Booker T. Jones** : Newman recommended Booker T. Jones as a reliable piano player for Stax sessions, with a strategic motive: he hoped that if Jones was occupied on keyboards, he would not be playing baritone sax, allowing Newman to secure more sax gigs. "As Floyd Newman recommended him as a reliable piano player, in the hopes that if Jones was on keyboards, he wouldn't be playing Bavito and Sax, so Newman would get more of those gigs." ← Episode 105： ＂Green Onions＂ by Booker T.and the MGs | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cw6xJG8E_w0
**Spoken Word on "Last Night"** : Floyd Newman, a session sax player for The Mar-Keys, provided the spoken "ooh last night" interjection that punctuated the hit instrumental "Last Night."
