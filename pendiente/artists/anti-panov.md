# artist - Anti-Panov

## curiosities
**"First Punk of the USSR"** : Anti-Panov, known as Zbind, was the leader of a band considered "too extreme for a KGB-monitored club" like the Leningrad Rock Club. He is widely regarded as the first punk of the USSR, pioneering a more radical sound. "Su líder, Antraipanov, conocido como Zbind, es considerado el primer punk de la URSS." ← Viktor Tsoi.
