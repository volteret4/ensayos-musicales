**Uncredited First Record (1952)** : In 1952, The Debenares, including Richard Berry and Cornell Gunter, recorded their first record for John Dolphin's Dolphins of Hollywood. They were disappointed when it was released credited to The Hollywood Blue Jays, another name Dolphin used, and failed to achieve any success.
