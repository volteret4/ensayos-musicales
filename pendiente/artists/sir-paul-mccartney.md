# artist - Sir Paul McCartney

## songs
**Live and Let Die (1973)** : Sir Paul McCartney read an "e-inflaming novel" to better understand James Bond the night before he wrote this song, which served as the theme for the James Bond film "Live and Let Die." The song was a significant hit independently of the movie.
