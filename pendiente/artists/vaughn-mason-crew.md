# artist - Vaughn Mason & Crew

## songs
**Bounce, Rock, Skate, Roll (Year not specified)** : This song was specifically written for roller skating, with a beat perfectly suited for skating. It became a recognized anthem among skaters, crossing all lines and remaining popular through the 70s, 80s, 90s, and 2000s. At skating rinks in the 70s, it was often played at the end of the night as a signal that it was almost time to leave. ## curiosities
**Last Live Performance** : Vaughn Mason gave what is believed to be his last live performance for roller skaters in November 2019, before the widespread news of the COVID virus. This event, held in Virginia (Reston, right on the Potomac) on the 31st or 32nd floor of a building, featured a "train" of skaters dancing around him and his son, showing appreciation for his contribution to their community. Mason passed away in April 2020.
