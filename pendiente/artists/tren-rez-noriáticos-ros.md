**The Social Network (2010) - Best Original Score Oscar** : In 2010, the film *The Social Network* won the Oscar for Best Original Score, composed by Tren, Rez, Noriáticos, Ros (Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross). Their work was instrumental in popularizing the use of ambient and textural music in modern dramas and thrillers, expanding the application of electronic music beyond its traditional use in science fiction.
