# artist - The Steve Miller Band

## genres
- Blues
- Psychedelic rock

## albums
**Brave New World (1969)** : This album featured a collection of psychedelic rock songs, notably including "space cowboys." It was one of two albums released by the band in 1969. ## songs
**Jackson Kent State Blues (1970s)** : Steve Miller wrote this song expressing outrage and frustration over the Jackson State and Kent State shootings. The lyrics criticize politicians for being unresponsive and preoccupied with voters while students are killed, and lament the "silent majority" glued to television screens, suggesting government agencies like the CIA and FBI are coming "unglued."
