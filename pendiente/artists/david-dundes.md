## songs
**Jeans On (Year Unspecified)** : David Dundes, a British singer, songwriter, and jingle writer, took a full-length version of his jingle, "Jeans On," to number 17 on the Hot 100. The song achieved pop success even though many American listeners were unfamiliar with the British brand, Brutus Jeans, that it was originally promoting.
