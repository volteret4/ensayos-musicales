# artist - Carlos Simó

## curiosities
**Pioneering DJ at Barraca (1981)** : Carlos Simó, with Juan Santamaría, initiated the "música blanca" movement at Barraca in 1981. Their selections, including bands like Depeche Mode, The Cure, Joy Division, and The Fall, brought a new, avant-garde sound to Valencia, differentiating it from the popular funk and disco genres of the era.
