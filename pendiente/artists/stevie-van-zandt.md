# artist - Stevie Van Zandt

## curiosities
**Convincing Bruce Springsteen** : Stevie Van Zandt played a pivotal role in the release of Bruce Springsteen's 1982 album "Nebraska." Originally, the songs were merely demos intended for the E Street Band, but Van Zandt convinced Springsteen to release them as their own album, recognizing their unique artistic merit.
