# artist - Warren Beatty

## curiosities
**Rock and Rhetoric Rallies (1972)** : Warren Beatty, an A-list actor and "70s heartthrob," organized a series of "rock and rhetoric rallies" in 1972 to support George McGovern and oppose Richard Nixon's re-election. His motivation was a strong personal dislike for Nixon. These rallies raised hundreds of thousands of dollars for McGovern's campaign and notably featured prominent celebrities, including Jack Nicholson, Burt Lancaster, and Cass Elliott, serving as ushers.
