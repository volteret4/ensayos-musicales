# instrument - Rubato (Free Time)

## curiosities
**Nashville A-Team Resistance** : The use of rubato, or free time, where musicians slightly speed up or slow down for expressive effect, met significant resistance from the Nashville A-team during the recording of Roy Orbison's "Running Scared." Bassist Bob Moore, in particular, was uncomfortable with it, believing pop music for dancing required a steady beat. "A chunk of the song is in Rubato, or Free Time, where the musicians speed or pass low down slightly to make the music more expressive.
