# instrument - Drum Loop

## curiosities
**Pioneering Music Production Technique** : The Bee Gees innovated a new music production technique when creating "Stayin' Alive." Due to their drummer's absence, they took a two-bar phrase from their song "Night Fever," slowed it down slightly, and spliced it into a half-inch four-track tape loop. This was considered groundbreaking at the time, marking the first instance of a song being constructed piece by piece from a drum loop. "No one had taken a drum beat before and created a two bar phrase. We were breaking new ground." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 50 - The Travolta Trilogy | https://www.ftr70.com/
