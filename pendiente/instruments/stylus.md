# instrument - stylus

## curiosities
**Vibrating Element in Early Recording** : In early recording systems, a stylus was directly connected to a vibrating diaphragm. As sound caused the diaphragm to move, the stylus would vibrate accordingly, transferring these movements to a continuous groove cut into a cylinder or rotating disc.
