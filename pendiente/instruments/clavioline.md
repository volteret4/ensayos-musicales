# instrument - Clavioline

## curiosities
**Key Instrument in "Telstar"** : The main melody of The Tornados' massively successful hit song "Telstar," which was produced by Joe Meek, was notably performed on a clavioline. This instrument is specifically described as a primitive synthesizer, highlighting Meek's early adoption of electronic sounds. "La melodía principal está tocada con un clave o lin, un permitivo sintetizador." ← El productor que escuchó el nuevo mundo. Joe Meek. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GEI5dpG5pxQ
