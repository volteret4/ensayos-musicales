## curiosities
**PC Tracker Evolution** : FastTracker 2 was a software that took tracker music on the PC scene to another level, expanding the capabilities beyond earlier trackers. It allowed for up to 32 audio channels and supported new formats like XM, furthering the complexity and richness of tracker compositions.
