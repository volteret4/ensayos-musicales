# instrument - MIDI sounds

## curiosities
**Characteristic of Utopian Virtual** : MIDI sounds are especially characteristic of the Utopian Virtual style, used to evoke the "kitschy" and obsolete technological era of the 80s and 90s. "especialmente sonidos MIDI que evoca el locúrsi de esa era tecnológica y a obsoleta." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Predominant in Far Side Virtual** : James Ferraro's *Far Side Virtual* utilized predefined digital timbres typically associated with MIDI, contributing to its unique sound.
