# instrument - Calimba

## curiosities
**Maurice White as Most Famous Player** : Maurice White of Earth Wind & Fire was probably the most famous player of the kalimba, an African-style thumb piano, incorporating it into the band's sound, notably on the *Sweet Sweet Back's Badass Song* soundtrack.
