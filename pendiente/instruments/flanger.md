# instrument - Flanger

## curiosities
**Vocals and Sound Effects (Late 1990s)** : The flanger effect was frequently employed in Eurodance songs during the late 1990s. It was often used in conjunction with vocoders to create dramatic buildups and to produce the characteristic "smurf" (pitch-shifted) vocal sound.
