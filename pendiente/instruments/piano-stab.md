# instrument - Piano stab

## curiosities
**Classic House Element** : The piano stab is a mythical and classic element of House music, described as a short, dry chord. Despite its melodic potential, it is primarily utilized as a rhythmic resource rather than a harmonic one in traditional House. "el mitico pianito del House, el llamado piano stab.
