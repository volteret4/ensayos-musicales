# instrument - Tenor saxophone

## curiosities
**Fats Domino's Signature - Tenor Saxophone Solo** : A tenor saxophone solo, typically placed two-thirds into the track, became a hallmark of Fats Domino's later recordings, with his own voice fulfilling this role on "The Fat Man." "Domino's later records almost all featured a tenisex solo, roughly two thirds of the way through the record.
