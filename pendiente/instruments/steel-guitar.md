# instrument - Steel guitar

## curiosities
**Melancholic Atmosphere** : The steel guitar, an instrument adopted from Hawaiian folklore, is played by sliding a slide over its strings. In Honky Tonk music, it was used to intensify the characteristic melancholic atmosphere, contributing to the genre's unmistakable sound. "el estirditar, un instrumento adoptado del folclo de Javallano, que se toca deslizando un slide sobre las cuerdas, intensificaba esa atmósfera melancólica tan inconfundible." ← El genio roto del country： La leyenda de Hank Williams | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qczx7qWg4xM
