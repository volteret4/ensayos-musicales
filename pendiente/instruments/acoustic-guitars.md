# instrument - Acoustic guitars

## curiosities
**Contribution to Kino's Early Sound** : Acoustic guitars were a key component of Kino's early sound, notably on their debut album "45." When combined with electronic drums (due to the lack of a drummer), they created a "curious primitive sound" that defined the band's initial aesthetic. "Como no tenían baterista a usar una batería electrónica, lo que en combinación con las guitarras acústicas aportaron un curioso sonido primitivo." ← Viktor Tsoi.
