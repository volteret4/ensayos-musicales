# instrument - High hat cymbal

## curiosities
**Disco Beat Contribution** : Earl Young's innovative use of the high-hat cymbal, struck to drive the beat in conjunction with the "four-on-the-floor" bass drum, was a crucial element in the creation of the disco beat on Harold Melvin & The Blue Notes' "The Love I Lost."
