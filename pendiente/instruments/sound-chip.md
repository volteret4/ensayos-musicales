## curiosities
**Impact on Dungeon Synth Aesthetics** : The aesthetic born from the necessity to work with limited sound technology in early RPGs, where themes ended up sounding almost like game soundtracks, directly influenced the Dungeon Synth genre. This necessity-driven style, exemplified by Burzum's prison albums, marked a significant wave in the genre's development. **Limitations of 8-bit and 16-bit Consoles** : Early video game composers faced significant limitations with the sound chips of 8-bit and 16-bit consoles, which made it impossible to faithfully recreate medieval instruments. This technical constraint forced them to imitate timbres and adapt melodies, inadvertently leading to the creation of a distinct and recognizable musical identity for early RPGs.
