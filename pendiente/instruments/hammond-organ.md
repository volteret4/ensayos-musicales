**Essential in Jazz/R&B Intersection** : Despite its general public image, the Hammond organ has become an "essential instrument" in the intersection of jazz and R&B, played in a distinct manner by artists such as Jimmy Smith, Bucketee Jones, Ray Charles, Georgie Fame, and Billy Preston, almost akin to how a violin is referred to as a fiddle in folk/country music. "But in that part of music, which is the intersection of jazz and R&B...
