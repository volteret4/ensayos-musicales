# instrument - Magnetic tape recorder

## curiosities
**Tony Visconti's Recording Method** : During the recording of David Bowie's *Heroes*, producer Tony Visconti kept a magnetic tape recorder running continuously throughout the entire process. This was done to ensure that all the constantly flowing ideas, which often risked being lost, were captured. "Visconti tenía una grabadora de cinta magnética y ciencia en día durante todo el proceso para que no se perdiese nada." ← El proyecto que salvó a David Bowie.
