# instrument - Voice

## curiosities
**Complex Processing as an Instrument** : The human voice, particularly in James Blake's music, is subjected to complex processing, often layered multiple times with varying treatments. This technique transforms the voice into something resembling other instruments, female choirs, or sounds manipulated by different playback speeds, effectively making the voice a highly versatile and processed instrument. **Natural Vocal Pitch Nuance** : For a human voice to sound natural and not artificial, it should not consistently hit perfect pitch. Instead, slight deviations, often described as being "a bit below the perfect pitch," are preferred, as perfect pitch can make a voice sound like a machine or a musical instrument rather than a human voice. "you don't want to be hammering it out into the perfect pitch, because then it's going to sound like a machine, right? Like a musical instrument, not a voice anymore." ← https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mjQBaX_3oL4 ← voice
