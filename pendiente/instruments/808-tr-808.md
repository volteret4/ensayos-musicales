# instrument - 808 (TR-808)

## curiosities
**Foundational Beat Machine** : The Roland TR-808 drum machine was a key instrument for Memphis rap producers, who preferred to create their own loops with it. Its distinctive sound, particularly the frantic hi-hats and iconic cowbell, helped define the Memphis style, as notably utilized by Tommy Wright III on his influential album "On The Run." "Preferían hacer sus flipos loops con la Roland TR808... por ese uso de la 8-08, con esos hi-hats frenéticos y en mi toco acencerroca habría creando un estilo." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
**Resurrection by Phonk Producers** : Global phonk producers, in their homage to Memphis rap, specifically rescued and incorporated the crude 808 sound, mixing it with elements from other Southern rap scenes to create the new genre. "rescataron el sonido crudo del 808 y lo mezclaron con elementos de otras escenas del rap soreño." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
