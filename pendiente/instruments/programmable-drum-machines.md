## curiosities
**Affordable Innovation for Techno (1980s)** : Programmable drum machines were incredibly affordable instruments that early techno artists, including Juan Atkins, Derrick May, and Kevin Saunderson, could acquire and use to create their music. These machines, though now expensive collector's items, were accessible to young artists without resources.
