# instrument - Cassette

## curiosities
**Analog Sound of Debut Album** : The Wu Tang Clan's debut album *Enter the Wu-Tang (36 Chambers)* had a distinctive sound that resembled an "underground demo tape from a cassette." This was due to its production without means or resources, resulting in a raw, impure, unclean, and "contaminated" sound, which ultimately made it very interesting and characteristic of the group's best work. "sin embargo el disco en el butanclan sonaba a maqueta andernal grauma de un cassette. No tenía que ser deshonido, no tenía pureza, no estaba limpio, había contaminaciones, estaba mal echa, había tenia ese sonido que tenían las maquetas del disco Andernal y es lo que lo hizo tan interesante al final." ← Music Radar Clan > Wu Tang Clan. **Lack of Professional Promotional Material** : Cassettes did not feature the same abundance of professional promotional material that was never released commercially, a factor that makes 1990s promotional CDs particularly valuable to collectors. "No pasaba con el caset y es que hubo un montón de material promocional que se hizo en disco compacto que no llegó a salir a la venta pero que se hizo de manera profesionalizada." ← Music Radar Clan > ESPECIAL FORMATOS： El CD | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7iRO7Hwg9V0&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← cassette
**Limited Collector Value for Singles** : Unlike Compact Disc singles, cassette singles never gained significant collector's value. This highlights a difference in how the physical formats' rarities are perceived and valued by collectors. "en el caset nunca lo tuvieron [un gran valor los singles]." ← Music Radar Clan > ESPECIAL FORMATOS： El CD | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7iRO7Hwg9V0&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← cassette
**Rapid Growth Post-Walkman** : The cassette format saw an almost explosive development after the introduction of the Walkman. This contrasted with the Compact Disc's initially slow and progressive evolution. "El cassette que tuvo un desarrollo prácticamente explosivo después del nacimiento del Wormung." ← Music Radar Clan > ESPECIAL FORMATOS： El CD | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7iRO7Hwg9V0&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← cassette
**Slow Evolution Due to Patent Issues** : The audio cassette experienced a very slow evolution and nearly failed due to Philips' rigorous patent on the system. This served as a cautionary tale for Sony and Philips when designing the licensing model for the Compact Disc. "una de las razones porque el cassette evolucionó muy despacio y casi fracaso fue precisamente por la rigorosa patente que tenía Philips del sistema de cassette." ← Music Radar Clan > ESPECIAL FORMATOS： El CD | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7iRO7Hwg9V0&list=PLBFijugu0fhAAIR-Ibh59aaU8VWg-SkLc ← cassette
