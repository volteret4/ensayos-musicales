## curiosities
**Iconic Early Techno Gear** : The TR-808 (Roland TR-808) was one of the programmable drum machines utilized by early techno artists. While now a highly prized and expensive fetish item, it was originally a cheap machine, making it accessible to young, resource-limited musicians in Detroit.
