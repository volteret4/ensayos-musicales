## curiosities
**Tony Visconti's Innovative Setup for "Heroes"** : Producer Tony Visconti developed an innovative microphone system to capture David Bowie's vocal on "Heroes." Mics were spaced across the room and gated to turn on one by one as Bowie's vocal became louder, making the song sound "that much more thunderous and anthemic."
