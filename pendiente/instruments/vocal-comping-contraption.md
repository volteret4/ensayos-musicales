# instrument - Vocal Comping Contraption

## curiosities
**Arif Mardin's Vintage Device** : Arif Mardin used a "crazy contraption" from the 1970s for comping vocals. This "very old school" device had two faders and a box, allowing for comparisons between different takes and crossfades. "He had this crazy contraption he used to comp vocals from the 70s.
