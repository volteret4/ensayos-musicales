## curiosities
**Core of Kraftwerk's Later Sound** : From "Trans-Europe Express" and "The Man-Machine" onwards, sequencers became a fundamental component of Kraftwerk's sound, creating the cold, precise, yet deeply physical and danceable music they were known for.
