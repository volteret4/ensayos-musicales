## curiosities
**Moving Magnet (MM) and Moving Coil (MC) Types** : There are two principal types of turntable cartridges: Moving Magnet (MM) and Moving Coil (MC). These types differ significantly in their design and the power of the sound signal they produce. In MM cartridges, the magnet vibrates, while in MC cartridges, the coil vibrates, resulting in varying output levels. **Preamplifier Compatibility and Cost** : The quality and power of a preamplifier are crucial for its compatibility with different cartridge types. Moving Coil (MC) cartridges, where the coil vibrates, are generally more complex, more expensive, and less common (minority) compared to Moving Magnet (MM) cartridges, where the magnet vibrates. Less powerful preamplifiers may struggle to provide adequate amplification for the lower output of MC cartridges, whereas MM cartridges are usually problem-free with most preamplifiers.
