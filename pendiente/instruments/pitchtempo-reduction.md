# instrument - Pitch/Tempo reduction

## curiosities
**Eccojams Technique** : This technique is also fundamental to Eccojams, which involves slowing down and fragmenting tracks to give them a glitch effect, drawing inspiration from Chop 'n' Screw and Plunderphonics. "ralentizando y fragmentando las pistas para darle un toque glitch." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Fundamental for Early Vaporwave** : A key technique in early vaporwave, as demonstrated by SunsetCorp (Daniel Lopatin), involved reducing the tempo and pitch of popular song extracts. "reducidos de tempo y pitch y pegados en bucle" ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Used in Broken Transmission** : Slowdowns (ralentizaciones) are explicitly mentioned as a manipulation technique in Broken Transmission to create fragmented and ethereal soundscapes. "A través de bucles, ralentizaciones y distorsiones" ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
