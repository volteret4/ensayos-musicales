# instrument - Vocoder

## curiosities
**First Use by Kraftwerk on Autobahn** : The vocoder was included for the first time by Kraftwerk on their album "Autobahn," specifically for processing voices. This marked a significant step in their evolution towards a fully electronic sound and a departure from traditional vocal techniques. "incluyeron por primera vez un poco de er para procesar las voces." ← Por qué KRAFTWERK es la banda más influyente de la historia？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wWOBez9Cqhs
**Vocals and Sound Effects (Late 1990s)** : The vocoder was a common studio effect utilized in late 1990s Eurodance. It served to "smurf" (pitch-shift) vocals, generating distinctive sonic textures, and was often paired with flanger effects for added impact. "Y las canciones se llenaron de subidones y efectos de estudio como el flanger o el bocoder para pitufar las voces." ← La gran mentira detrás del Eurodance | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_F34L8wfrdo
