# instrument - Cencerros (cowbells)

## curiosities
**Signature Memphis Rap Sound** : Cowbells were a characteristic element of the Memphis rap sound, contributing to its dirty, raw, and lo-fi texture, a trait that continues to pulse in modern phonk. "Cencerlos, ritmos incopados... En sus cencerros." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
**Tommy Wright III's Iconic Use** : Tommy Wright III famously employed a "mythical cowbell" on his 1994 album "On The Run," which, alongside frantic hi-hats, helped create a distinctive and influential style within the Memphis scene.
