# instrument - Roland TR-606

## curiosities
**Big Black's Industrial Sound Element** : The Roland TR-606 drum machine was a key element in Big Black's debut album *Atomizer*. It was used to replace conventional drums, providing a distinct industrial sound that contributed significantly to the album's groundbreaking post-hardcore aesthetic.
