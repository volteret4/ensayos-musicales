**Pioneering P2P File Sharing (2000)** : Napster, launched in 2000, was the first peer-to-peer (P2P) system that allowed users to download audio files and share music. Its emergence was one of the two major "hammer blows" that significantly impacted Compact Disc sales starting in 2001, marking a shift in music consumption.
