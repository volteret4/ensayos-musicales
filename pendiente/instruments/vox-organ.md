## curiosities
**Graham Bond's Appreciation** : Graham Bond, a respected Hammond organ player, notably admired The Animals' electric organ sound, especially after hearing them live at the Club a Go-Go. "One person who definitely didn't dislike the sound of the electric organ was Graham Bond" ← Episode 115： ＂House of the Rising Sun＂ by the Animals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=34aIGCnb4X4
**Signature Animals Sound** : The Vox organ, acquired by Alan Price, became one of the defining sonic elements of The Animals, despite Eric Burden's personal aversion to its sound. "Price would get a Vox organ rather than Cartopiano between gigs, while burdened dislike the sound of the organ, even though it became one of the defining sounds of the group." ← Episode 115： ＂House of the Rising Sun＂ by the Animals | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=34aIGCnb4X4
