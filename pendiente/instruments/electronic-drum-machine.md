## curiosities
**Kraftwerk's Self-Made Innovation** : Kraftwerk famously manufactured their own electronic drum machine, which they used to establish the metronomic pulse for the title track of their 1974 album "Autobahn." This self-made instrument was a key component in their early electronic sound.
