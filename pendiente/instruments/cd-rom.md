## curiosities
**Not the Origin of Digital Audio CD** : Contrary to common belief, the audio Compact Disc did not become digital because it inherited the data storage system of the CD-ROM. The audio CD was developed specifically for music reproduction, and the CD-ROM for computers came later. **Post-Audio CD Development (1984)** : The CD-ROM, a Compact Disc designed for data storage applications in computers, was introduced in 1984. This was almost four years after the commercial launch of the audio Compact Disc, clarifying that the music CD was not an inheritance of the CD-ROM system.
