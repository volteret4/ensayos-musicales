**Enabling Advanced Home Production** : Increasingly sophisticated VST (Virtual Studio Technology) programs, used in conjunction with home studio software like FL Studio, Ableton, and Reason, revolutionized music production. They allowed musicians to virtually recreate any synthesizer, drum machine, bass, or guitar, and apply professional-grade mixing and mastering effects from a home computer, empowering unprecedented creative freedom and the rise of unconventional genres.
