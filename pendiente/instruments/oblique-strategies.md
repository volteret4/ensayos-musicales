# instrument - Oblique Strategies

## curiosities
**Creative Tool for Brian Eno** : Oblique Strategies, a card deck created by Brian Eno, were used by him to promote creativity and generate original ideas during the recording of David Bowie's *Heroes* and were crucial for *Lodger*. "si usamos sus oblices, una carta creada por el para promover la creatividad y generar ideas originales." ← El proyecto que salvó a David Bowie. La trilogía de Berlín | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CHJwoQRxrKg
**Inspiration for *Lodger* Recording Techniques** : For David Bowie's *Lodger*, crucial ideas were drawn from Brian Eno's Oblique Strategies. These included techniques like playing old Bowie melodies backward or having musicians swap instruments to capture spontaneity. "Algunas de las ideas que sacaron de ellas fue tocar antiguas melodías de Bowie al revés, o intercambiasse los instrumentos entre los músicos para captar la espantaneidad del momento." ← El proyecto que salvó a David Bowie.
