## curiosities
**ADT for Vocal Enhancement** : Automatic Double Tracking (ADT) was developed at John Lennon's request to enhance his vocals, as he did not have a very powerful voice. The system automatically creates two identical vocal takes with a minuscule delay, adding power, presence, and a specific effect, and is still widely used today for similar purposes.
