# instrument - DX7

## curiosities
**High School Synthesis Class** : The DX7 was one of the synthesizers available in the synthesis ensemble class Nora Jones took at her performing arts high school in Dallas. Students were encouraged to write songs using instruments like the DX7 and Moog.
