## curiosities
**Catalyst for Internet-Born Genres** : Reason, along with FL Studio and Ableton, represented a new generation of home studio programs. These tools, combined with increasingly sophisticated VST plugins, fundamentally changed music production by allowing musicians to recreate any instrument and apply professional mixing and mastering effects from a domestic computer. This total control fostered immense experimentation and the flourishing of unconventional, internet-born genres like Witchhouse.
