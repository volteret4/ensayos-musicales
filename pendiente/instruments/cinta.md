# instrument - Cinta

## curiosities
**Manual Production Medium for Early Megamixes** : For the first "Max Mix" albums, the recording process was entirely manual, relying solely on cuts and splices of magnetic tape ("cinta"). This hands-on method was used in the absence of more advanced digital tools like samplers and sequencers, demonstrating a unique precision and imagination.
