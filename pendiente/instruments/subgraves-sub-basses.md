# instrument - Subgraves (sub-basses)

## curiosities
**Signature Memphis Rap Sound** : Potent sub-basses were a characteristic element of the Memphis rap sound, contributing to its dirty, raw, and lo-fi texture. This emphasis on heavy sub-basses continued to be a defining feature of phonk, maintaining Memphis rap's DNA. "subgraves potentes... subgraves pesados...
