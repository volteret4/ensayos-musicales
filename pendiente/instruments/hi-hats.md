# instrument - Hi-hats

## curiosities
**Characteristic Trap and Memphis Sound** : Hi-hats were a distinctive element in Memphis rap and later trap music, often described as sounding like a machine gun, contributing to the genre's raw and direct sound. Tommy Wright III notably used "frenetic hi-hats" in his influential 1994 album, which defined a style.
