**Impact on Muzak's Perception** : The arrival of synthesizers and electronic music significantly influenced the popularization and perception of Muzak. The "cold" and often impersonal element introduced by computer-generated music, combined with the lack of a human interpreter, uniquely aligned with Muzak's inherent standardized and predictable nature, reinforcing its image as an artificial and uniform sound.
