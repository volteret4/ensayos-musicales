# label - All Platinum Records

## curiosities
**Founded by Sylvia Robinson** : After a hiatus to have children, Sylvia Robinson founded her own record label, All Platinum. For this label, she wrote and produced numerous proto-disco hits for other artists in the late 1960s and early 1970s, including "Shame Shame Shame" for Shirley & Company.
