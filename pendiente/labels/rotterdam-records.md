# label - Rotterdam Records

## curiosities
**Foundational Gabber Label** : Rotterdam Records, under the direction of DJ Paul Elstak, played a fundamental role in defining and popularizing the brutal sound of Gabber for the young Dutch audience in the early 1990s. Its releases were crucial in establishing the genre.
