# label - Interfisch Records

## curiosities
**Connection to Tresor Records** : Interfisch Records (transcribed as Interfix Records) was the label from which Tresor Records would emerge. In 1989, Jeff Mills went to Berlin to DJ at a festival organized by Interfisch, quickly establishing a fruitful relationship that would later lead to his work with Tresor.
