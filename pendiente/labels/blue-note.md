# label - Blue Note

## curiosities
**Almost Signed Aretha Franklin** : Blue Note Records was very close to signing Aretha Franklin. Plans were made for a recording project in Detroit centered around her piano playing, with Jerry Jemmott and Bernard Purdie, but it was thwarted by Aretha's illness.
