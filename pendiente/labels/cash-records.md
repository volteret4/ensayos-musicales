# label - Cash Records

## curiosities
**Part of John Dolphin's Empire** : Cash Records was one of a series of small labels owned by John Dolphin, who also owned Dolphins of Hollywood, the largest Black-owned record store in LA. These labels typically released one or two singles by artists before they were picked up by larger labels.
