# label - Lumpin Records

## curiosities
**Founding and Role in Eckpank Scene** : Lumpin Records was founded by Martin Meyer, leader of the band lumpiante de Numper. The label was established to release his own music and became a key platform for publishing many bands from the nascent Eckpank scene in the US Midwest. "Martin Meyer, su líder, abrió el sello lumpi record para publicar sus discos, y acabó editando a muchas de las bandas de esta escena que se estaba gestando." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
