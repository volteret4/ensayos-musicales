# label - Kent Records

## curiosities
**Ownership and Johnny Otis Signing (Late 1960s)** : Kent Records was a small blues label owned by the Bihari Brothers, who were veterans of the 1950s music industry. Frank Zappa successfully persuaded the Bihari Brothers to sign Johnny Otis, leading to the release of Otis's "Cold Shot" album.
