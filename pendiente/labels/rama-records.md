# label - Rama Records

## curiosities
**Focus on R&B Singles** : George Goldner established Rama Records as a label specifically for R&B singles. This move was a direct result of the Mambo craze, which led many Black teenagers to his Latin music clubs and subsequently sparked his interest in their preferred music.
