# label - Colpix Records

## curiosities
**Early Ronettes Label** : Colpix Records, a label owned by film studio Columbia Pictures, was the first label to sign The Ronettes (then Ronnie and the Relatives). However, their initial singles, "I Want a Boy" and "I'm Going to Quit While I'm Ahead," released in 1961, were commercially unsuccessful and failed to chart.
