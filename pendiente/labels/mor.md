# label - Mor

## curiosities
**Key Label for Second Wave IDM** : This label became a crucial platform for editing and releasing music by "second wave" IDM artists, including Yucic, Bola, Alvanoto, Fortead, Squid Pusher, Borsafcanada, and Benetan Snaers. This era was characterized by an increased emphasis on percussion, influenced by jungle and drum and bass. "Me le plato, Planet Moo, Mor, o El Scam, se convirtieron en espacios clave para editar a músicos como Yucic, Bola, Alvanoto, Fortead, Squid Pusher, Borsafcanada o Benetan Snaers." ← Qué fue el IDM y por qué cambió la música electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RTwn3hP1XTg
