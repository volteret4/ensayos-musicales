# label - Mills Music

## curiosities
**Publisher for Duke Ellington and Book Ram** : Mills Music served as Duke Ellington's publisher, and Book Ram was introduced to them by Ellington. They later acted as Ram's publishers, notably suing on his behalf during the "I'll Be Home for Christmas" copyright dispute.
