# label - Ghost Box Records

## curiosities
**Embodiment of Hauntology** : Ghost Box Records is credited with perfectly encapsulating the idea of hauntology. The term "hauntology" was first explicitly used in a musical context in 2006 to refer to this record label, highlighting its foundational role in popularizing the concept within music. **Founding and Aesthetic Mission (2004)** : Ghost Box Records was founded in 2004 and quickly established itself as an aesthetic and sonic laboratory. The label built a mythology centered around library music, public service jingles, educational documentaries, and television iconography from past decades, particularly drawing influence from the BBC Radiophonic Workshop. Its artists embraced the feeling of failed futures and transformed it into a fictional sound archive. "Fundado en 2004, Gwosbox actúa como un laboratorio estético y sonoro, construyó una mitología basada en la música de biblioteca, las intonías de servicio público, los documentales educativos y la conografía televisiva de décadas pasadas, especialmente del taller de fónico de la BBC." ← El Género Musical Más Escalofriante de Internet. **Influence of Boards of Canada** : The artists on Ghost Box Records drew significant inspiration from the works of Boards of Canada, especially their album *Geogaddi*, in developing their distinctive sound and aesthetic. "Además de la tallierra de fónico de la BBC, los artistas de Gwosbox también se basaron las trabajos de Wors off Canadá, especialmente en Geogadi." ← El Género Musical Más Escalofriante de Internet.
