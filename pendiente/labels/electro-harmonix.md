# label - Electro Harmonix

## curiosities
**Rediscovery of Los Saicos** : In the 2000s, Electro Harmonix was one of the labels, along with Munster Records, that rediscovered and reissued the music of Los Saicos. This effort was crucial in positioning the Peruvian band as a key piece within the archaeology of protopunk, bringing their previously unknown legacy to a wider audience. "hasta que en los 2000 fueron la descubiertos por coleccionistas y sellos como el electroarmónix y múster recos, que comenzaron a reditarlo o situarlos como una pieza clave dentro del arcoología del protopunk." ← El punk existía antes de Sex Pistols (y era algo salvaje) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ilXpBujkWZE
