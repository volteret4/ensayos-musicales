# label - Jewel

## curiosities
**Contract Voided by Sam Phillips** : Roy Orbison and The Teen Kings recorded "Ooby Dooby" for the independent label Jewel. Sam Phillips later discovered Orbison was under 21, rendering the contract void, and subsequently obtained an injunction preventing Jewel from distributing any more copies of the single, of which only a few hundred had been released. "He discovered that Orbison wasn't yet 21 and so the contract he'd signed with Jewel was void. Phillips signed Orbison, got an injunction taken out against Jewel, preventing them from putting out any more copies of the single, only a few hundred ever got released, and quickly went into the studio to record a new version of the song." ← Episode 42： ＂Ooby Dooby＂ by Roy Orbison and the Teen Kings | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1zvzpXqr-k8
