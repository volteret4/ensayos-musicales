# label - Manhattan Records

## curiosities
**Sister Label to Blue Note** : Manhattan Records was established or revamped by Arif Mardin as a sister label to Blue Note Records, overseen by Bruce Lundvall. Its focus was on genres like classical, musical theater, and adult contemporary.
