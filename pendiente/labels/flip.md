# label - Flip

## curiosities
**Audition Label for Sun Records** : Flip was a new record label established by Sam Phillips as a cost-cutting measure while he faced financial difficulties. Its purpose was to release records, like Carl Perkins's first single "Movie Mag," that did not pay union scale. Distributed only locally around Tennessee, these releases served as "audition records," allowing Phillips to gauge an artist's local performance without incurring full royalty payments, expensive shipping costs, or large print runs.
