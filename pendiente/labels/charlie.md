# label - Charlie

## curiosities
**British Reissue Label** : Charlie was a British reissue label that played a crucial role in bringing Sun Records' extensive back catalog to a wider audience. They employed Martin Hawkins and Colin Escott, two "young music obsessives," to systematically release Sun's rockabilly and blues archives.
