## curiosities
**Discovery and Marketing of LSD-25 Derivatives** : Sandos was the pharmaceutical company where Albert Hoffman discovered LSD-25. While they were delighted with the chemical's unusual effects, they developed and marketed variants of the molecule that affected the brain without the hallucinogenic properties, selling them as migraine treatments. They made LSD-25 itself available as an experimental drug for psychiatrists and psychologists to study states of consciousness and potential treatments for alcoholism and psychiatric illnesses, marketing it under the trade name Dallicid.
