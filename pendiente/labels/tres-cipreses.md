# label - Tres Cipreses

## curiosities
**Absorption by DRO** : Tres Cipreses was eventually absorbed by the independent label DRO (Discos Radioactivos Organizados), which was also acquiring other labels in the scene, consolidating independent music distribution. "Duro, quienes también acababan de absorber tres cipreses." ← La España Siniestra ｜ La edad de oro del Postpunk y el Dark wave | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN_buvQC69c
**Founded by Parálisis Permanente** : Parálisis Permanente established Tres Cipreses as their own independent label, inspired by British bands who self-released their material. This was a direct response to Hispavox's reluctance to publish their dark music.
