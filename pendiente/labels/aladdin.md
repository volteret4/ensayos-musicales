# label - Aladdin

## curiosities
**Early Barry Gordy Production for The Fidelitones** : In the late 1950s, Barry Gordy produced tracks for The Fidelitones on the Aladdin label. These recordings, featuring future Motown talents Brian Holland and Freddie Gorman, were never released.
