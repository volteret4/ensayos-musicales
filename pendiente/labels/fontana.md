# label - Fontana

## curiosities
**Mid-sized British Label** : Fontana was a mid-sized British record label to which Chris Blackwell licensed Millie Small's early British singles, including "Don't You Know" and "My Boy Lollipop." This arrangement was chosen because Island Records lacked wide distribution at the time.
