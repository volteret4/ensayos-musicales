# label - Checker

## curiosities
**Precedent for White Artists** : Checker, a subsidiary of Chess Records, set a precedent for releasing music by white artists when it put out Bobby Charles's single "Later Alligator." This made Stan Lewis believe Checker might be receptive to another record by a white act, specifically Dale Hawkins.
