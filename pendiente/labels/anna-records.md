# label - Anna Records

## curiosities
**First Single** : The label's initial release was "Hope and Pray," a song written by Davis and Gwen Gordy, performed by The Voice Masters. "With its first single being a Davis and Gwen Gordy song, Hope and Pray, performed by The Voice Masters." ← Episode Sixty-Four： ＂Reet Petite＂ by Jackie Wilson | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BYU-H4NvnmE
**Founding by Gordy Sisters and Billy Davis** : Anna Records was founded by Gwen Gordy, Anna Gordy (sisters of Barry Gordy Jr.), and Billy Davis, following their professional fallout with Nat Tarnapol. "Davis and Gwen Gordy went off to found their own record label, along with Gwen and Barry's sister Anna. Anna Records, as it was called, didn't have the most propitious start." ← Episode Sixty-Four： ＂Reet Petite＂ by Jackie Wilson | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BYU-H4NvnmE
**Gordy Family Label** : Anna Records was identified as a "Gordy family label." The Voice Masters were the first group signed to this label, and their record "Hope and Play" was "the very first record ever put out by a Gordy family label."
