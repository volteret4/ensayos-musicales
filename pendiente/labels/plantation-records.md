# label - Plantation Records

## curiosities
**Controversial Name** : Plantation Records, owned by Shelby Singleton, was a record label with a highly controversial name due to its clear racial connotations. Linda Martell directly challenged Singleton on this, stating that it implied black people belonged on a plantation, despite Singleton's attempts to deny any racial meaning.
