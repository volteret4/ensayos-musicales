# label - Vic Records

## curiosities
**Dropping Artists** : Mickey & Sylvia were among the artists dropped by Vic Records after their commercial success continued to diminish following the closure of Groove Records. "After Mickey and Sylvia were dropped along with the rest of the Vic artists." ← Episode 49： ＂Love is Strange＂ by Mickey and Sylvia | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FkyLPVvPbcI
**Inherited Struggling Talent** : Vic Records was described by producer Valence as having "probably the worst collection of talent in the history of the world" and being "severely in debt" when artists like Mickey & Sylvia were transferred there after Groove Records was closed by RCA. This move led to a loss of career momentum for the transferred artists.
