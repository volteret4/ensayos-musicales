# label - Booji Boy Records

## curiosities
**Devo's Self-Funded Debut (1970s)** : Booji Boy Records was Devo's own label, through which they self-funded their first recording with $2,000. They also produced a film to accompany this recording, demonstrating their DIY ethos and desire for creative control.
