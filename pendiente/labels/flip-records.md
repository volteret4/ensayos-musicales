# label - Flip Records

## curiosities
**Pressured Berry for Follow-ups** : After "Louie Louie" became a local hit, Flip Records pressured Richard Berry to produce follow-ups specifically to that song, rather than allowing him to explore new material. **Purchased "Louie Louie" Songwriting Rights (1959)** : In 1959, the owner of Flip Records acquired the songwriting rights to "Louie Louie" and several other songs from Richard Berry for $750. **Richard Berry's Label for "Louie Louie"** : Richard Berry's original 1957 recording of "Louie Louie" was released on Flip Records, a small label.
