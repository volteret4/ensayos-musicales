# label - Decker

## curiosities
**Lionel Hampton's "Flying Home" (1942)** : Lionel Hampton recorded his most famous version of "Flying Home" for Decker in 1942. The label description for this instrumental featured the term "Fox Strott," and the recording itself became famous for Illinois Jacket's groundbreaking saxophone solo.
