# label - Ryes y Records

## curiosities
**Influential Belgian Label** : This super influential Belgian label (R&S Records) was crucial in shaping the IDM genre. Its ambient division, Apollo, released pivotal works like Aphex Twin's "Selected Ambient Works 85-92," and R&S itself released early material from artists such as Villasfer and Aphex Twin, embodying the same innovative spirit.
