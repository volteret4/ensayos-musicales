# label - Triumph Records

## curiosities
**Founded and Closed by Joe Meek** : Joe Meek established Triumph Records in 1960, following his experiences at various radio stations and studios. The label came very close to securing a number one hit with "Angela Jones," but was ultimately forced to close after only a few months due to insurmountable distribution challenges and Meek's notoriously difficult temperament.
