# label - Tico Records

## curiosities
**Latin Music Specialization** : George Goldner launched Tico Records as a label dedicated to Latin records, following the popularity of his chain of nightclubs that featured Latin music. The label became notable for releasing early recordings by prominent artists such as Tito Puente.
