## curiosities
**Initial Positive Reaction to In Utero** : Nirvana's record label in Urbana initially reacted positively to *In Utero* upon its first listen, even contemplating re-recording it with a different producer. This early satisfaction contrasted with the band's later specific requests for sonic adjustments. **Major Labels' Resistance to Albini** : After the release of *In Utero*, major record labels collectively refused to work with Steve Albini for approximately a year. This widespread resistance significantly hampered his ability to secure new production projects during that period, as a direct consequence of the album's controversial production process.
