# label - American Gramophone

## curiosities
**Founding by Chip Davis (1974)** : American Gramophone was established in 1974 by Chip Davis as an Omaha-based independent label. Its primary purpose was to release Davis's own instrumental music project, Manheim Steamroller, before it also became the initial distributor for C. W. McCall's debut single.
