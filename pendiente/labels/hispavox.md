# label - Hispavox

## curiosities
**Release of Alaska y los Pegamoides' Album** : Hispavox was the label that released Alaska y los Pegamoides' only studio album, "Grandes Éxitos," in 1982. This release was pivotal in the band's transition from the underground to the mainstream. "sacaron su único disco de estudio en 1982 para el Sillo Ispavox." ← La España Siniestra ｜ La edad de oro del Postpunk y el Dark wave | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN_buvQC69c
**Reluctance to Release Dark Music** : Hispavox showed significant reluctance to release an album from Parálisis Permanente due to the band's dark sound. This hesitation led the band to establish their own independent label, Tres Cipreses.
