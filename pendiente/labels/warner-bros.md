## curiosities
**Fine for Non-Compliance** : The Beach Boys failed to meet the May 1973 deadline imposed by Warner Bros for the release of Smile. Consequently, they had to pay a $50,000 fine for not fulfilling this contractual obligation, further adding to the legend of the lost album.
