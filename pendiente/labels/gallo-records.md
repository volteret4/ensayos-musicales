# label - Gallo Records

## curiosities
**Dominant South African Record Label** : At the time Solomon Linda was active, Gallo Records was the sole record label in South Africa and owned the only recording studio in Sub-Saharan Africa, giving it a monopolistic position in the region's music industry. **Mismanagement of "In Boube" Copyright** : After Folkways copyrighted "Wimoweh" in the USA, Gallo realized its mistake in not registering "Ember Bay" (In Boube) under Solomon Linda's name there. They were later pressured into a hugely disadvantageous agreement with Folkways, effectively surrendering global copyright to the song in exchange for a modest one-off fee and rights in only a few African territories.
