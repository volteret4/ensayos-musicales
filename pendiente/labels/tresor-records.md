# label - Tresor Records

## curiosities
**First Release for Jeff Mills (as X-X-Q1)** : Tresor Records made an offer to Jeff Mills that he couldn't refuse after publishing EPs recorded by Mills under the alias X-X-Q1, which originally came from Underground Resistance contacts in Berlin and became the label's first reference. "tras publicar el disco, harían una oferta a Jazz Mills que no pudo rechazar." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
**Launch of "Wweithfunk Transmission volumen 1"** : In 1992, Berlin-based Tresor Records (referred to as a "senyor y discoteca") launched "Wweithfunk Transmission volumen 1," an album that defined modern techno. This release was pivotal in establishing techno's direct connection to the body, stripped of previous influences, and solidified the Detroit-Berlin axis in electronic music.
