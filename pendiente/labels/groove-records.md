# label - Groove Records

## curiosities
**Closure After Breakthrough Hit** : Despite Mickey & Sylvia's "Love Is Strange" becoming a breakthrough hit for the label, RCA controversially decided to close Groove Records. Its acts and producer Valence were transferred to another RCA subsidiary, Vic Records. **RCA Subsidiary Founded (1953)** : Groove Records was established in 1953 as a subsidiary of RCA. It was one of the "fake indie labels" created by major record labels to gain access to the independent R&B distribution networks they had previously been excluded from.
