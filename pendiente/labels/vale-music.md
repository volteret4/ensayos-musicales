# label - Vale Music

## curiosities
**Founding by Ricardo Campoy** : Vale Music was created following Ricardo Campoy's departure from Max Music, where he had been a co-founder. Campoy reportedly took a self-loan of 75 million pesetas and brought The Dream Team and other staff from Max Music to establish his new label. **Partnership with Operación Triunfo** : Vale Music secured a contract to release weekly albums for "Operación Triunfo," which became the biggest musical television success in Spanish history. Beyond these weekly releases, the label also took on the responsibility of managing the careers of the show's contestants. This partnership represented the last major commercial success for the Spanish record industry before the widespread impact of piracy. "Operación triunfo se convirtió en el mayor éxito televisivo musical de la historia del país." ← Ritmo y Extorsión： Así Dominaron los Megamix los 90s | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-qekjKky5bE
**Post-Max Music Success and TV Collaborations** : After the tumultuous end of Max Music, Vale Music continued to release successful compilations such as "Disco Estrella" and "Dance Mix." The label innovated by collaborating with popular television programs like "Gran Hermano" and "Crónicas Marcianas" to release albums featuring music from those shows.
