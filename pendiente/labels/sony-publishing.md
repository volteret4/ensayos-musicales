# label - Sony Publishing

## curiosities
**Jesse Harris's Deal** : Songwriter Jesse Harris had a publishing deal with Sony, which allowed him to fund demos that Nora Jones sang on. This collaboration was an early step in Nora's career, leading to her first public performances of original material. "My friend, Jesse Harris, who's the songwriter, asked if I would do some demos for him.
