# label - Motor City Records

## curiosities
**Ian Levine's Motown Artist Revival Label** : Motor City Records was a British label owned and operated by DJ Ian Levine. Its mission was to release new recordings by artists who had previously recorded for Motown in the 1960s, successfully attracting over a hundred former Motown artists.
