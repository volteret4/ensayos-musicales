**"Sham Corporation" Ruling (1974)** : A court ruled in 1974 that Five Platters Inc. was a "sham corporation" designed to unlawfully strip band members of their ownership in the band name, and that Book Ram had unfairly benefited. Subsequently, its trademark in "The Platters" name was declared void. **Assignment of Name Rights by Members** : At the time of its formation, the members of The Platters assigned any rights they had to the band's name to Five Platters Inc., a decision that led to decades of litigation. **Controlled by Book Ram** : This corporation was established by Book Ram soon after The Platters signed to Mercury Records, with Ram maintaining control despite band members being shareholders. The purpose was allegedly to manage the band's rights, but it became a source of major legal disputes.
