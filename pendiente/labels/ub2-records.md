# label - UB2 Records

## curiosities
**International Market Access** : In 2002, UB2 Records facilitated the re-release of The White Stripes' "White Blood Cells" album, which had initially been issued by an indie label. As a label with greater reach, this strategic move significantly aided the album in penetrating international markets and greatly enhanced the band's global recognition.
