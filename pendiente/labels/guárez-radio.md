## curiosities
**Precursor to Muzak Company (1934)** : Founded by Yotchow Goodesquayer, Guárez Radio was an American company established in 1934 that initially focused on distributing music through electrical wiring to homes. In the same year, fascinated by the name Kodak, Goodesquayer decided to rename the company to Muzak, a portmanteau of "música" and "Kodak."
