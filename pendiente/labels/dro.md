# label - DRO

## curiosities
**Absorption of Other Independent Labels** : DRO became a powerful independent label, known for absorbing other labels in the scene, including Tres Cipreses and Grabaciones Accidentales (GASA). This move consolidated its influence within the Spanish underground. "Duro, quienes también acababan de absorber tres cipreses." ← La España Siniestra ｜ La edad de oro del Postpunk y el Dark wave | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN_buvQC69c
**Founded by Servando Carballar (1982)** : Servando Carballar founded the label DRO (Discos Radioactivos Organizados) in 1982, after most of Aviador Dro's members left and no other label would release his album. The single "Nuclear, Sí" was its first release.
