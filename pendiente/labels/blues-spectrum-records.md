# label - Blues Spectrum Records

## curiosities
**Founded and Operated by Johnny Otis (1970s)** : Johnny Otis founded and managed Blues Spectrum Records during the 1970s. The label focused on releasing late-career recordings by veteran artists like Charles Brown, Big Joe Turner, and Louis Jordan, with some of these being their final recordings.
