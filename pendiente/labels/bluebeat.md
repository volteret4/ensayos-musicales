# label - Bluebeat

## curiosities
**Dominant Jamaican Music Label in UK (1960s)** : Bluebeat was the largest label distributing Jamaican music in Britain during the early 1960s, notably surpassing Island Records in market share. **Genre Terminology in Britain** : The label's popularity was so immense that its name, Bluebeat, became a common colloquial term in Britain for the entire Ska genre, similar to how "Motown" referred to a specific sound.
