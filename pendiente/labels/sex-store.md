# label - Sex (store)

## curiosities
**Punk Fashion Catalyst** : "Sex" was a clothing store in London opened by Malcolm McLaren, inspired by the distinctive style of Richard Hell. This establishment played a crucial role in defining the canonical aesthetic of punk, serving as the foundation for the visual image of bands like the Sex Pistols and widely influencing punk fashion.
