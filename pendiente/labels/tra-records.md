# label - TRA Records

## curiosities
**Florence Greenberg's First Label** : TRA Records was the first record label started by Florence Greenberg, a suburban housewife with no prior experience in the music business. The Shirelles were the first group signed to this label, and they recorded "I Met Him On A Sunday" there.
