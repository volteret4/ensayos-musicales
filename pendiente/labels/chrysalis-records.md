# label - Chrysalis Records

## curiosities
**Blondie's Record Label** : Chrysalis Records was Blondie's record label during the period of their first two albums, *Blondie* and *Plastic Letters*. "They were with Chrysalis records and they wanted Blondie to get a hit so they hooked them up with producer Mike Chapman." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 57 - Punk and New Wave at New York's CBGB | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Push for a Hit** : After the first two albums failed to gain commercial traction, Chrysalis Records brought in producer Mike Chapman to help Blondie achieve a hit, demonstrating the label's investment in the band's commercial success.
