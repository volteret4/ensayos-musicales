# label - Shout Factory

## curiosities
**Preservation of Original WKRP Music** : Shout Factory notably preserved the original music in the complete DVD series of *WKRP in Cincinnati* by actively purchasing the specific rights for DVD distribution, a task that 20th Century Fox was unwilling to undertake, highlighting Shout Factory's dedication as "music guys." "So if you want to revisit or introduce yourself to WKRP and Cincinnati, you want the DVDs from Shout Factory. They were able to keep the original music because they were buying the rights for DVDs, not for streaming and they were music guys." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 58 - WKRP in Cincinnati | https://www.ftr70.com
