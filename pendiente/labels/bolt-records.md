# label - Bolt Records

## curiosities
**Stax Subsidiary for Airplay** : Bolt Records was established as a subsidiary label by Stax. The strategy was to release records under two different label names to increase the likelihood of radio stations playing more of their music, without appearing to favor one label too much. "They started a subsidiary label, Bolt. If you put records out with two different label names, it was more likely that radio stations would play more of your records" ← Episode 105： ＂Green Onions＂ by Booker T.and the MGs | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cw6xJG8E_w0
