# label - P-Cock Records

## curiosities
**Don Robey's Ownership and Artist Management** : P-Cock Records was owned by Don Robey. He signed Marie Adams, hoping she would become a success akin to Big Mama Thornton. Robey's business practices influenced Marie Adams's career, notably releasing her version of Johnny Ace's "My Song" partly because he owned the publishing rights. When artists like Adams were not selling well, Robey would hand them over to Johnny Otis to produce, hoping for commercial success. "Roby had challenged her to sing along with Diana Washington's record, Harbour Light.
