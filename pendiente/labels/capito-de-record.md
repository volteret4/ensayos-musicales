# label - Capito de Record

## curiosities
**Contentious Relationship with Pink Floyd** : Capito de Record, a record label, had a long-standing poor relationship with Pink Floyd, particularly with Roger Waters, the band's leader. This tension culminated with *The Dark Side of the Moon*, which was the last album Pink Floyd recorded with them. The label released the album prematurely due to high anticipation, before it was fully finished and out of post-production, deeply annoying the band and leading to their boycott of the official launch.
