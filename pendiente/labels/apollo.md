# label - Apollo

## curiosities
**Ambient Division of Ryes y Records** : Apollo was the ambient division of the highly influential Belgian label Ryes y Records (R&S Records). It gained prominence by releasing seminal IDM works, including Aphex Twin's "Selected Ambient Works 85-92."
