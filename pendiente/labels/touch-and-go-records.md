# label - Touch and Go Records

## curiosities
**Initial Rejection, Later Endorsement** : Slint initially wrote to Touch and Go Records in 1987 hoping they would release their album "Fritz" but received no response, leading to self-release. Years later, the label's founder discovered Slint and declared himself a fan, subsequently agreeing to release their 1990 album *Spiderland*. "Fritz se grabó en 1987 con Alvinie y pese a que escribieron a la Tao Chango Records, para que lanzaran el disco, no recibieron respuesta de ellos, por lo que se le autoaditaron." ← Spiderland： La Revolución del Rock Hecha por Adolescentes | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fLzXxQMqR78
**Singular Work in Catalog** : In 1991, Touch and Go Records released *Spiderland*, which is described as the most singular work in their catalog, highlighting its unique artistic merit.
