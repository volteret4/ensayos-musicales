# label - Mercury

## curiosities
**Label Stance on Political Music** : The president of Mercury Records advised Roy Hammond that releasing political songs, such as "Open Letter to the President" and "I Wasn't There But I Can Feel the Pain" from his "Sex and Soul" album, was "holding himself back." This pressure led Hammond to form his own record label to maintain artistic control over his politically conscious music.
