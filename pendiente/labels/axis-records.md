# label - Axis Records

## curiosities
**Founded by Jeff Mills in New York** : Axis Records was established by Jeff Mills in New York, serving as the first core of what would become the label through which he would release all his subsequent work. "establecer el primer núcleo de lo que pronto sería Axis Records, se lló donde editará todo su trabajo posterior." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
**Headquarters in Chicago with Robert Hood** : After his time in Berlin, Jeff Mills moved to Chicago, where he set up the headquarters for his label Axis, which he built alongside his friend Robert Hood (transcribed as Robert Hutt) from Underground Resistance. "Tras acabar su etapa en Verlín, Mills se mudó a Chicago, donde montó la sede de su sello Axis, el cual levantó junto a su amigo de underground resistance Robert Hutt." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
**Matrix Label with Sub-labels** : Axis Records functions as a matrix label comprising several sub-labels, each with its own identity. These include Purpose Maker, where Mills launched his most radical and conceptual EPs; Tomorrow (transcribed as Tu morro), for his more melodic and spatial material; and 6-7-7, dedicated to other affiliated artists and producers. "Axis funcionaba como un sello matriz de varios obceños con identidades propias, como Purpose Maker, donde lanzó su supes más radicales y conceptuales, y exploró ese techno del truco de la cultura. Tu morro, donde lanzó su material más me lo digo y es facial, o 6-7-7, se dio dedicado a otros artistas y productores afines." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
