## curiosities
**Ellie Greenwich and Tony Powers' Publisher** : Trio Music, owned by Lester Sill, served as the publishing company for Ellie Greenwich and Tony Powers, holding first refusal rights on their compositions. Many of Greenwich and Powers' most successful songs were those rejected by Trio and subsequently sold elsewhere. "trio had first refusal on anything that wrote.
