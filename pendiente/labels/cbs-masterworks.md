# label - CBS Masterworks

## curiosities
**Initial Reservations about Recording Baroque Music** : Following Glenn Gold's concert in New York in 1955, CBS Masterworks extended a recording contract to him. The label initially expressed hesitations about recording Johann Sebastian Bach's "Goldberg Variations," primarily because Baroque music was generally perceived as being overlooked and less popular with the public at that time. Despite these concerns, Gold's recording unexpectedly achieved record-breaking success. "La discográfica tenía reticencias en grabar esa obra, ya que la música barroca solía pasar de esa percebida para el público." ← El Pianista que Llevó a Bach al Cosmos ｜ Glenn Gould | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WpwjSQr8Qco
