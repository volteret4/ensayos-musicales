# label - Private Stock Records

## curiosities
**Small Label with 70s Hits** : Private Stock Records was described as a small label that distributed several 1970s hits, including David Soul's "Don't Give Up on Us," Starbuck's "Moonlight Feels Right," and Samantha Sang's "Emotion." It also made a deal with Dickie Goodman to release "Mr. Jaws."
