# label - Purpose Maker

## curiosities
**Jeff Mills' New York Residency** : The Limelight (transcribed as Lamly Light) was a prestigious club in New York where Jeff Mills secured a coveted residency after leaving Underground Resistance. This period, coinciding with the rise of the rave and Club Kid scene, allowed Mills to refine his minimalist and forceful style and establish the beginnings of Axis Records. "donde consiguió una residencia muy codiciada en el prestigioso Club, Lamly Light, justo cuando la escena Reyfi ClubKid se empezaba a formar." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
**Key Venue for Detroit-Berlin Axis** : Tresor, a Berlin club, was crucial in consolidating the Detroit-Berlin connection in techno. It opened in 1992, and Jeff Mills became a resident DJ there for several months, refining his brutalist and minimalist style in its cavernous, industrial setting. "Dimitrija Garman lo fichó para el recén abierto 3-or, donde actuó unos meses como residente del mítico Club Verlínés. En ese espacio cavernoso, de paredes del adrillo y ambiente industrial, Mills afinó sus estilos más minimalistas y brutal..." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
**Platform for Radical Techno** : Purpose Maker is a sub-label of Axis Records where Jeff Mills launched his most radical and conceptual EPs. It's known for exploring "tricky techno" (techno del truco de la cultura) and releasing his most popular dance floor content: raw techno without typical melodies, characterized by obsessive, hypnotic, and mechanical percussions. "Fue un Purpose Maker donde Mills lanzó su contenido más popular dentro de las pistas de baile, un techno bruto sin melodías al uso y con percusiones obsesivas, imnoticas y mecánicas." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
**Release of "The Bells"** : The second reference from the Purpose Maker label (referred to as "Catmoda") gave rise to "The Bells," a track that went global and is widely regarded as "the most important track in the history of techno." "La segunda referencia del sello Catmoda acabó dando la vuelta al mundo gracias a un track que seguramente sea el más importante de la historia del techno. Debels." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
**Setting for Jeff Mills' First Commercial DJ Set** : The Liquid Room in Tokyo is the legendary club where Jeff Mills' first officially commercialized DJ set, "Live at Liquid Room" (transcribed as Aifant de Liquid Room), was recorded in 1996. This set is celebrated for its intensity and for transforming dance music into a deeply physical experience. "La primera asisión comercializada oficialmente de Jeff Mills fue el Aifant de Liquid Room, de 1996. Un set en el legendario Club de Tokio, que encarna a la perfección su intensidad tras los platos." ← Cómo Jeff Mills transformó el techno para siempre | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=omE3zOiZAXs
