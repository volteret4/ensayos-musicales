# label - Fall Records

## curiosities
**Early Pulp Management and Exploitation** : Fall Records was an independent label that managed Pulp for nearly ten years, releasing their first three albums (roughly 1981-1992). Despite this long association, the label never supported the band, providing no resources or backing. Upon seeing Pulp's later success, Fall Records controversially released the "Countdown" compilation in 1996 without the band's consent, purely for profit, much to Jarvis Cocker's dismay.
