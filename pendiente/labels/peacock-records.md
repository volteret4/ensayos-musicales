# label - Peacock Records

## curiosities
**Little Richard's Second Label** : Peacock Records signed Little Richard and his band, The Tempo Toppers, after Johnny Otis recommended him to Don Roby, the label owner. Their initial recordings in early 1953, issued as two singles, were unsuccessful. Richard later recorded one more session for Peacock, but was ultimately dropped due to creative and personal conflicts with Roby.
