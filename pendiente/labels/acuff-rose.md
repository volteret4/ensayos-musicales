# label - Acuff Rose

## curiosities
**Publishing Conflict with Sam Phillips** : Acuff Rose, a publishing company, had an agreement with The Everly Brothers requiring them to record only songs published by Acuff Rose. This directly conflicted with Roy Orbison's existing contract with Sam Phillips, which stipulated Phillips would publish all of Orbison's compositions, necessitating a significant deal for Orbison to leave Sun. **Wesley Rose's Control over RCA Material** : Despite Roy Orbison being signed to RCA Records largely based on his songwriting ability, the songs chosen for him to record at RCA were not his own choices and were instead all picked by Wesley Rose of Acuff Rose.
