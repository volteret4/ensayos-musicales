# label - UNI

## curiosities
**Elton John's Label Mate** : Neil Diamond referred to Elton John as a "UNI record label mate" when he introduced him for his American debut at The Troubadour in 1970. "He returned to the Trubidor to introduce a new British rock singer and a UNI record label mate who was making his American debut at the Trubidor." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 41 - The Impact of The Troubadour on 1970s Popular Culture | https://seventies.libsyn.com/ep-41-the-impact-of-the-troubadour-on-1970s-popular-culture
**Signing Elton John** : UNI was the record label that signed Elton John, and also provided Neil Diamond with more creative control over his songwriting. "At UNI, the label that also signed Elton John, he had a lot more control over what he wrote, which was almost kind of like this uncontrollable force in him." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 41 - The Impact of The Troubadour on 1970s Popular Culture | https://seventies.libsyn.com/ep-41-the-impact-of-the-troubadour-on-1970s-popular-culture
