# label - Hi Records

## curiosities
**Cinema Studio Inspiration** : Hi Records, a Memphis label that started around the same time as Satellite, notably used an old cinema as its studio. This approach inspired the Satellite team to move their studio to a similar abandoned cinema, which offered advantages like a lobby for a record shop.
