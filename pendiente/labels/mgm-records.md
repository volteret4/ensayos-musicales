# label - MGM Records

## curiosities
**Million-Dollar Advance (1965)** : MGM Records offered Roy Orbison a million-dollar advance to sign with them, a deal brokered by his manager Wesley Rose who was in conflict with Monument's Fred Foster. However, immediately after Orbison signed with MGM, his string of hit records dried up.
