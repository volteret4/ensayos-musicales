# label - Deram

## curiosities
**Bowie's First Solo Contract & Album Failure** : David Bowie was signed to a solo contract with the Deram label after his manager's efforts to chart "Can't Help Thinking About Me" on the Melody Maker chart. However, his self-titled 1967 debut LP and novelty single "The Laughing Gnome" both failed to chart, leading to him being dropped by the label in 1968 and considering quitting music.
