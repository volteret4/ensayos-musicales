# label - American Sound Studio

## curiosities
**Chips Moman's Post-Stax Venture** : After departing from Stax Records due to conflicts, Chips Moman went on to found his own studio, American Sound Studio, in another part of Memphis. This studio would become responsible for producing a string of classic soul, country, and pop records.
