## curiosities
**Chocolate's Defining Sound** : Gothic music was a core element of Chocolate's identity, which was known as "the darkest, the most gothic club on the Ruta." DJ Tony Vitale "El Gitano" specifically played "sinister" bands like Bauhaus, Cabaret Voltaire, and Throbbing Gristle, attracting clientele from the punk and sinister subcultures.
