# genre - Doomgaze

## curiosities
**Shoegaze Sound Wall and Doom Density** : Doomgaze is a subgenre that blends the massive, layered "sound wall" aesthetic typical of shoegaze with the oppressive density of Doom Metal. This combination results in a dense, atmospheric sound that maintains the heavy characteristics of Doom while incorporating the textural and often ethereal elements of shoegaze.
