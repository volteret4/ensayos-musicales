# genre - Música Gótica Española

## curiosities
**Alternative Name: Afterpunk or Música Siniestra** : Spanish gothic music was also known as Afterpunk or "música siniestra" (sinister music) in the peninsula. This genre, with its dark and radical sounds, provided a tenebrous contrast to the Movida Madrileña.
