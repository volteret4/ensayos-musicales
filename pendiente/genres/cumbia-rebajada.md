# genre - Cumbia Rebajada

## curiosities
**Monterrey, Mexico Origin (Early 1990s)** : This subgenre emerged in Monterrey, Mexico, around the same time DJ Screw was creating his sound in Houston, though without any physical or cultural connection. It originated accidentally when "solidero" Gabriel Dueñez's sound equipment malfunctioned, causing Colombian cumbia and vallenato records to play slower and heavier, which delighted partygoers.
