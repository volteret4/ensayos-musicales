# genre - Memphis Rap

## curiosities
**Early Sound and Themes** : In Memphis during the early 90s, artists like Kim Ping Skinny Pimp, Rixx Macia, Tommy Ritter Zero, and Bodily Esquikki created a hip hop style characterized by "saturated, oppressive atmospheres" and "horror-themed lyrics." This sound heavily utilized the Roland TR-808 drum machine, featuring prominent cowbells, numerous high hats, and deep sub-bass to mark the rhythm.
