# genre - Saba de Ishown

## curiosities
**Spanish Version of Italo Disco** : The "Saba de Ishown" was presented as the Spanish version of Italo Disco, featured prominently in early Megamix compilations like Max Mix 2. It represented a localized interpretation of the popular Italian dance sound.
