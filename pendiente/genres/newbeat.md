# genre - Newbeat

## curiosities
**Development in Belgium (Late 1980s)** : The Newbeat genre was actively developing in Belgium during the late 1980s. This local electronic music scene, alongside EBM in Germany and Belgium, contributed to Europe's rich electronic music landscape before the arrival of US techno and house.
