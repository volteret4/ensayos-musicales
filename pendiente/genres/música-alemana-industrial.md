## curiosities
**Influence on Depeche Mode's Ultra** : German industrial music, alongside Krautrock, had significant influences on Depeche Mode's Ultra album. This genre contributed to the album's powerful rhythmic foundations and profound electronic textures, shifting the band's sound towards more complex, computerized structures rather than solely relying on synthesizers. This reflected a broader exploration of new musical territories for the band.
