# genre - voodoo rockabilly

## curiosities
**Term Coined by The Cramps** : The Cramps utilized the term "Voodoo Rockabilly" as a promotional descriptor for their concerts, alongside "Psychobilly" and "Gothabilly," reflecting the eclectic and dark influences within their sound. "Así como searon ese término para promocionar sin carteles, también usaron otros como Rockabilly Budu o Gozabilly." ← Amor, Terror y Psychobilly： La Historia de The Cramps | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tBVTzVQDQeQ
