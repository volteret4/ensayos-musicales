## curiosities
**José Padilla's Use in Ibiza** : José Padilla, the resident DJ at Café del Mar in Ibiza, incorporated "Rindmoss Electrónicos" (likely Ritmos Electrónicos) into his musical selections for the sunset sessions. This blend, alongside ambient sounds, World Music, and Jazz Swab, was instrumental in capturing the unique, relaxed, and contemplative essence of Ibiza, thereby redefining Chill Out for a wider audience.
