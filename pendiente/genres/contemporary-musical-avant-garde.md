# genre - Contemporary Musical Avant-Garde

## curiosities
**Influence on Psycho Score** : Bernadjema's score for the *Psycho* shower scene, with its screeching violins, was considered more aligned with contemporary musical avant-garde than the conventional film music of its era. This innovative approach drew comparisons to figures like John Cage.
