**Pictorial Pillar of Hip Hop** : Graffiti is defined as a pictorial or plastic art movement and constitutes one of the four essential pillars of hip hop culture. It developed alongside breakdance, turnabilism, and rap in the cultural hubs of New York's minority neighborhoods during the formative period of hip hop.
