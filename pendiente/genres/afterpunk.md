# genre - Afterpunk

## curiosities
**Melancholic vs. Comedic Extremes** : Within the Afterpunk scene, bands like Décima Víctima represented the melancholic extreme, characterized by their cold and dark sound, while Derribos Arias occupied the comedic and absurd extreme, offering a unique blend of chaos and genius. **Spanish Term for Post-punk/Gothic** : Afterpunk was a term used in Spain, alongside "música siniestra," to describe the Spanish gothic and post-punk movement. It highlighted the genre's distinct evolution and themes.
