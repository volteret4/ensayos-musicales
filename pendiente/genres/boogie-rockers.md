# genre - Boogie Rockers

## curiosities
**Cecil Gant's B-sides** : Boogie Rockers were the typical style for Cecil Gant's B-side record releases, setting them apart from his ballad A-sides, and were stylistically very similar to Roy Brown's "Good Rockin' Tonight."
