# genre - New It

## curiosities
**Emergence in Megamixes (Post-Italo Disco)** : Following the cultural shift brought by "La Ruta y el Sonido Valencia," the Megamix phenomenon started incorporating faster and stronger musical styles. New It (likely New Beat), along with Acid House and EBM, were among the first new genres to dominate these compilations, moving beyond the traditional Italo Disco sound.
