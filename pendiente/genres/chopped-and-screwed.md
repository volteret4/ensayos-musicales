# genre - Chopped and Screwed

## curiosities
**Origin and Naming Convention** : This subgenre of hip hop originated in Houston, Texas, through the experiments of DJ Screw in the early 1990s. The name "Chopin Escriu" (Chopped and Screwed) reflects its core techniques: "Chop" refers to the cuts, repetitions, and jumps in phrases, while "Screw" denotes the dragged, low-pitched, and slowed tempo.
