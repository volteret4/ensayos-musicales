## curiosities
**Mambo Craze of the 1950s** : Mambo was a significant musical trend that gained popularity in the pop markets during the 1950s, particularly around 1953-1954. Named after Perez Prado's song "Mambo Jambo," it was part of a broader category of "Exotica" music from various Latin American and Caribbean cultures, and for a period, it was considered a strong contender to shape the future of music alongside rock and roll.
