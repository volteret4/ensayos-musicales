## curiosities
**Fusion of Doom and Death Metal** : Death Doom Metal is a hybrid subgenre born from the union of the oppressive density of Doom Metal and the brutal aggression of Death Metal. It combines the slow, heavy atmospheres of Doom with the harsh vocals and extreme instrumentation often found in Death Metal.
