## curiosities
**Decline in US Charts (Late 1956)** : The initial wave of white rockabilly acts that achieved prominence between 1955 and 1956, including artists like Carl Perkins, Gene Vincent, and Bill Haley, experienced a significant decline by late 1956, failing to secure further major hits in the US.
