**Dominance of Gregorian Chant in the Middle Ages** : During the almost ten centuries of the Middle Ages (mid-400s to mid-1400s), the Church's influence dominated all aspects of life. Consequently, the majority of music during this period was liturgical, primarily destined to praise God, with Gregorian chant being a prime and widespread example of this sacred music.
