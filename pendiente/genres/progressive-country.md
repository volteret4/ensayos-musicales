# genre - Progressive Country

## curiosities
**Arrival in 1974** : Progressive country music emerged in 1974, coinciding with the inaugural year of the PBS television program "Austin City Limits." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 48 - The Worst of the 70s The Music of 1974 | https://www.ftr70.com/
**Roots in The Beatles' Arrival (1964)** : Progressive country is believed to coincide with, or be a result of, The Beatles' arrival at JFK Airport in New York on February 7, 1964. Their influence encouraged baby boomers to challenge norms, and progressive country is seen as part of that cultural pushback against established styles, particularly Nashville's polished country sound.
