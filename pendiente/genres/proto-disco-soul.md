# genre - Proto-Disco Soul

## curiosities
**Influence on "1984"** : David Bowie admitted that the string arrangement on his 1974 track "1984" was an attempt to emulate the lush proto-disco soul of Barry White's Love Unlimited Orchestra. This early flirtation with the genre foreshadowed his "Plastic Soul" album, 'Young Americans'.
