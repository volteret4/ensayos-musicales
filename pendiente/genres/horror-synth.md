# genre - Horror Synth

## curiosities
**Diverse Influences** : Horror Synth is not a closed genre; its new musicians draw from a variety of scenes, including extreme metal, house, progressive rock, and even Dungeon Synth, all united by a profound passion for 1980s horror cinema. "estos nuevos músicos no vienen de una escena concreta, sino de varias, del metal extremo, del house, pero todo le suele la misma religión, una pasión sagrada por el cine de terror de los 80." ← Por qué el cine de terror de los 80 suena así？ Horror Synth | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hd23tCTRk8E
**Genre Naming** : "Horror Synth" was the chosen name to encompass both the pioneering artists and the new wave of musicians creating dark, synthetic sounds, recognizing a unified aesthetic and passion.
