# genre - Música de Mobiliario

## curiosities
**Functional Background Sound** : More than a century ago, Erik Satie conceptualized "música de mobiliario" (furniture music) as a functional soundscape. Its purpose was to subtly integrate into daily environments, masking everyday noises like the clinking of cutlery at a dinner, filling uncomfortable silences, and generally complementing the atmosphere without drawing explicit attention. "Creó el término música de móvil yario para mascarar escenas diarias, preyanar silencios incómodos y enmascarar sonidos cotidianos como el ruido de los cubiertos en una cena." ← Muzak： Música para ser ignorada | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7iN6WiB5Y1g
