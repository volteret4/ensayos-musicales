# genre - Máquina

## curiosities
**Influence on Eurodance (Mid-1990s)** : Máquina, a genre predominantly from Southern Europe, exerted an influence on Eurodance during the mid-1990s. This contribution helped to accelerate Eurodance's tempos and shape its sonic evolution. "...o la máquina." ← La gran mentira detrás del Eurodance | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_F34L8wfrdo
**Later Dominance in Megamixes** : Máquina (Makina) became one of the dominant genres in Megamix compilations later in their evolution, after the initial shift from Italo Disco towards Acid House, New Beat, and EBM. This genre represented a faster and more forceful style of electronic music embraced by the series like "Maquina Total" and "Lo Más Duro."
