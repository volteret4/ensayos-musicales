# genre - Proto-rap

## curiosities
**1970s Development** : The major evolution of narrative music that eventually led to early rap largely took place during the 1970s. This period saw the combination of jazz and Afro-jazz with storytelling, sometimes influenced by Jamaican and funky music, where the voice narrated without strictly adhering to rhythm. **Early Forms (Pre-1970)** : It is difficult to find recordings of narrative music that clearly fit the proto-rap style from before 1970, although some examples exist from the late 1960s, such as those by Melvin Van Peebles.
