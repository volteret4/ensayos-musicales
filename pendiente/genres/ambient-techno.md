# genre - Ambient Techno

## curiosities
**Derivation from Ambient House** : Ambient Techno emerged as a direct derivative of Ambient House, originating from dance music during the mid-1990s. This genre represented a further diversification of electronic music, blending the atmospheric qualities of ambient sounds with the rhythmic structures of techno.
