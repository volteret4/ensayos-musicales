# genre - Country-politan

## curiosities
**Country Music Association's Initial Goal** : The Country Music Association, formed in 1958, aimed to promote country music, specifically the "country-politan" style, which was a smoother version incorporating strings instead of traditional banjos and fiddles. This approach was driven by the desire to make country music more broadly appealing and profitable, attracting pop and rock fans. **Intentional Crossover Sound (1970s)** : The "country-politan" sound, also known as country-pop, featured big backing orchestral arrangements and was intentionally designed in the 1970s to increase country music's radio airplay and record sales. This strategy proved effective and created more exposure for artists.
