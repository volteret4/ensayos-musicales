# genre - Nightcore

## curiosities
**Accelerated Music Phenomenon** : Nightcore is a genre characterized by accelerated music and typically features high-pitched vocals. It gained popularity on platforms like YouTube years ago and, far from dying out, has spread significantly on TikTok. "El Nightcore se popularizó en la plataforma hace años y lejos de morir se está extendiendo en TikTok." ← ¿Por qué el Slowed & Reverb y los Speed Up están rompiendo YT y TikTok？ | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0WWra85DsCo
**Microaesthetic of Idealized Medieval Life** : Nightcore, alongside other micro-aesthetics like Cottagecore, Goblincore, and Dark Academia, is actively rescuing and promoting an idealized vision of medieval life. This trend acts as a refuge in the current era of screens and uncertainty, offering an escape into a fast-paced, high-pitched electronic aesthetic. "el Nightcore (...) están rescatando una visión idealizada de la vida medieval." ← Por qué todo suena medieval en 2025？ (Dungeon Synth, Bardcore...) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FDNQdBRktSA
**Visuals and Evolution to "Speed Up"** : Virtually any song searched online can be found in a Nightcore version, often accompanied by images of anime-style drawn girls. Today, the term "Nightcore" is gradually being superseded by the more general "Speed Up" as the preferred label for these new accelerated versions, reflecting a shift in terminology on platforms like TikTok.
