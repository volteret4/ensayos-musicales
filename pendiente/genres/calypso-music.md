**Brief US Popularity in the 1950s** : Calypso music experienced a period of immense popularity in the United States around 1953-1954. During this time, it was one of several non-rock and roll genres that an impartial observer might have expected to become the dominant musical trend, challenging the emerging rock and roll scene.
