# genre - Synth-driven Pop

## curiosities
**Emergence in the 1980s** : Synth-driven pop defined the musical sound of the 1980s, marking a significant transition from the preceding decade's sounds. Songs like Devo's "Whip It" are noted as serving as a bridge between the music of the 1970s and this new, synthesiser-focused pop style.
