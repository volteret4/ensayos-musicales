# genre - Electric Rock

## curiosities
**Peak During 1960s Societal Upheaval** : Electric rock, alongside acid rock, was at its "peaking" during the period of "general upheaval of society in the 1960s." Its diminished presence in the 1970s reflected the decade's more mellow societal tone.
