# genre - Electro-funk

## curiosities
**Dominant in Early Memphis Clubs** : In the early Memphis club scene, electro-funk from artists like Nucleus or Africa Bambaataa was the primary music for dancing before the emergence of New York hip-hop and the fusion efforts of DJ Spanish Fly.
