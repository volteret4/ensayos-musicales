# genre - Drone Metal

## curiosities
**Purest Essence of Doom** : Drone Metal distills the Doom genre to its purest essence, stripping away any superfluous elements. This subgenre is characterized by its minimalist soundscape, often eliminating vocals and sometimes even drums, leaving only sustained notes that intertwine and resonate for minutes. "Pero hay un estilo que distila al género hasta asociencia más pura, despojándolo de cualquier elemento superfluo, ese es el Drone Metal." ← El DOOM METAL es el DARK SOULS de la música | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v0eaod_repg
**Tectonic Vibrations and Stilled Time** : In Drone Metal, notes are held for minutes and interweave, creating vibrations that are described as tectonic. The musical time practically stops, reducing Doom to its maximum simplicity by focusing on extended sonic textures rather than conventional rhythm or melody. "Las notas se sostienen durante minutos y se entrelazan, provocando liberaciones que parecen tectónicas. Aquí el tiempo musical prácticamente se detiene, es el Doom reducido a su máxima sencilla." ← El DOOM METAL es el DARK SOULS de la música | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v0eaod_repg
