# genre - Fudge Wave

## curiosities
**Visual-First, Meme-Driven Subgenre** : Fudge Wave is listed among the subgenres that frequently developed their visual identity before their musical sound. It, along with Simpsons Wave, often did not evolve beyond meme status, contributing to a superficial understanding of vaporwave among the general public.
