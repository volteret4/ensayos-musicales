# genre - Brick Bichar corei

## curiosities
**Popularity in Late Rave Scene** : As the UK rave scene evolved and its initial spirit diluted, attendees, including various new groups, increasingly demanded higher BPMs and more aggressive sounds. Break Bichar corei (Breakbeat hardcore) became highly popular among the public during this period.
