# genre - Psychedelic Folk

## curiosities
**Bowie's Transitional Sound** : David Bowie's "big break" involved a shift from Mod Rock to psychedelic folk, a sound that echoed the "trippy grooves" of folk rock pioneers like The Byrds and Donovan. This new direction was pivotal in the creation of "Space Oddity," which ushered in his "Glam Starman" phase.
