# genre - Rare Phonk

## curiosities
**Evolution from Early Phonk** : Rare Phonk emerged as a lighter evolution from the "oppressive and terrifying darkness" of early Phonk. Pioneers like DJ Smokey, DJ Jung Bump, and collectives such as Jolimopo por Pell Pose shifted towards a more "chill" approach, embracing psychedelia, and incorporating references to the internet, video games, marijuana, and anime. "Poco a poco la oscuridad impresiva y terrorífica, empezamos a dejar paso a un enfoque más ligero, dando lugar al rar Funk." ← ¿Qué pasó con el PHONK？ (La historia real) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ehfWyUHFFT8
