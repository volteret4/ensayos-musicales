# genre - Nashville Sound / Countrypolitan

## curiosities
**Architects and Characteristics** : Chet Atkins and Floyd Kramer are identified as the "principal architects" of the Nashville Sound (also known as Countrypolitan). This style of country music moved away from its traditional roots, leaning towards a "pre-rock pop sound" characterized by vocal groups and strings, with a notable absence of traditional country instrumentation such as fiddles, mandolins, banjos, and steel guitars. It aimed to smooth out rough edges and make the music more "manoured."
