# genre - Hardcore Breakbeat

## curiosities
**Origin from Breakbeat Experimentation** : Hardcore breakbeat originated from the intensive experimentation with cut and manipulated breakbeats by DJs and producers in the early 1990s. It was a fusion genre, blending the 4x4 rhythms of acid and techno with amplified breakbeat rhythms, often sourced from hip hop.
