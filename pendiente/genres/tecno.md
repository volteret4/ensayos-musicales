## curiosities
**"Intelligent Techno" as Precursor to IDM** : Before the term IDM became widely accepted and consolidated, some segments of the music press began referring to this new, more contemplative and intricate form of electronic music as "intelligent techno." **Detroit Techno as Core Influence** : The more futuristic strains of Detroit Techno, particularly the work of artists like Derry May and Carc Drake, were considered by the founders of Warp Records to be at the core of a new type of music and listening. This provided a fundamental aesthetic and conceptual basis for the emergence of IDM. "Esta gente creía que el Tegno de Detroit más futurista, el de Derry May y o Carc Drake, estaba en el núcleo de un nuevo tipo de música y de escucha." ← Qué fue el IDM y por qué cambió la música electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RTwn3hP1XTg
**Shift from Functional Dance Music** : Before 1992, electronic dance music was almost exclusively experienced in clubs, primarily for dancing. The "Artificial Intelligence" compilation and the subsequent IDM movement fundamentally challenged this paradigm, proposing that techno could be listened to while seated, occupying a cultural space comparable to progressive rock, spiritual jazz, or contemporary classical music.
