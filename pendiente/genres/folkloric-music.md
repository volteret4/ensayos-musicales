# genre - Folkloric Music

## curiosities
**Government-Backed and Chart-Dominating in Late 70s USSR** : In the late 1970s, Folkloric music, alongside Pop Moscovita, dominated the Soviet charts. This genre was backed by the government and considered more controllable, providing an official musical landscape distinct from the burgeoning underground rock movement. "La música que dominaba las listas de éxito es en el pop-moscovita y la música folculórica. Vamos, las que estaban respaldadas por el gobierno y eran más controlables." ← Viktor Tsoi.
