## curiosities
**Boxing Accessibility in 1970s** : In the 1970s, boxing was widely available on television, predating the pay-per-view era, allowing audiences to "simply turned on your TV to watch." This broad accessibility played a significant role in creating fans for the sport and its stars like Muhammad Ali.
