# genre - Teen Tragedy Records

## curiosities
**Common Narrative Structure (1960s Pop Music)** : Teen tragedy records prevalent in the 1960s frequently featured dramatic narratives, often centered around car races or other perilous situations, culminating in the protagonist's untimely death, as exemplified by songs like "Dead Man's Curve" or "Tell Laura I Love Her." The Beach Boys' "Don't Worry Baby" initially sets up this familiar scenario but then subverts it by focusing on the protagonist's intense emotional anxiety rather than a physical catastrophe.
