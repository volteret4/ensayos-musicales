# genre - Ambient Sound

## curiosities
**Brian Eno's Influence on Low** : Brian Eno's ambient sound bore a heavy influence on David Bowie's 1977 album 'Low', the first in his Berlin trilogy. This atmospheric approach, combined with electronic music and post-punk guitars, characterized Bowie's experimental turn away from his mid-70s imperial phase.
