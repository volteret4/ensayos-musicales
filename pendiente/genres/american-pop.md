## curiosities
**Influence on Hunky Dory** : After his 1971 US tour, David Bowie began composing on piano, writing songs for 'Hunky Dory' with "the poignancy of traditional American pop," alongside the pomp of glam rock. This blend showcased his emerging commercial potential and his ability to synthesize diverse influences.
