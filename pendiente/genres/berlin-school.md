## curiosities
**Influence on Dungeon Synth** : The Berlin School, with artists like Tangerine Dream and Klaus Schulze, significantly influenced Dungeon Synth, particularly artists such as Mortiis. These pioneers created layered, synthesizer-based textures designed to evoke futuristic and spatial journeys, a concept translated into the medieval and dungeon imagery of Dungeon Synth.
