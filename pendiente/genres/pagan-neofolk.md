## curiosities
**Early 2000s Popularization and Recent Peak** : Bands like Faun and Omnia were instrumental in popularizing Pagan Neofolk in the early 2000s. The genre is currently experiencing its peak in the last two years, driven by the success of bands like Wardruna and Heilung, demonstrating its growing appeal. **Emergence and Characteristics** : Pagan Neofolk is a genre that gained prominence, particularly following the rise of popular culture phenomena like the TV series "Vikings," which cultivated a cult for pagan and Viking themes. This genre utilizes instruments from pre-Christian Europe and evokes an atmosphere of rituals, ceremonies, and pagan celebrations.
