# genre - Ambient Trance

## curiosities
**Derivation from Ambient House** : Ambient Trance developed as a derivative of Ambient House in the mid-1990s, also stemming from the broader dance music scene. It combined the atmospheric and relaxing elements of ambient music with the hypnotic and evolving characteristics of trance.
