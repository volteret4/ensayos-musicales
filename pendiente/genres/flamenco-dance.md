# genre - Flamenco dance

## curiosities
**Major Inspiration for Contemporary Dance** : Flamenco dance serves as one of the great inspirations for a significant portion of contemporary dance globally. Many prominent contemporary dancers travel to Spain specifically to learn flamenco, subsequently incorporating its influences into their own contemporary work, which may otherwise be unrelated to traditional flamenco.
