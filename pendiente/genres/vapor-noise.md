# genre - Vapor Noise

## curiosities
**Experimental, Rhythm-less Variant** : Vapor Noise is described as an experimental variant of vaporwave that lacks rhythm and blends elements from both Broken Transmission and Hardvapour. It is considered a minor subgenre due to few releases or lack of full definition.
