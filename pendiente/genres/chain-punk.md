# genre - Chain Punk

## curiosities
**Aesthetic and Attitude** : Chain Punk refers to a more aggressive, traditional punk style, characterized by a "jargoleta" aesthetic including leather jackets, mohawks, and safety pins, and a generally more violent and serious tone. It serves as a contrast to the colorful and ironic Ekpank.
