# genre - Stand-Up Comedy

## curiosities
**Evolution in the 70s** : While stand-up comedy was not a 70s invention, having been a staple of early 20th-century radio and TV shows like "Milton Berle" and "Ed Sullivan," the decade saw its "rock and rollization." This evolution involved becoming "smarter and more current and edgier," often complete with drugs and alcohol, and led to an explosion of comedy clubs and mega-stars.
