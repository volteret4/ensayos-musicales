# genre - Akron Sound

## curiosities
**Akron Sound - Working Class Intellectualism (Late 1970s)** : Developing in the late 1970s, the Akron Sound, originating from Akron, Ohio, was described in a 1999 Akron Beacon Journal article as "working class" yet possessing an element of "detached physics club intellectualism." Unlike typical "get drunk and get in a fight songs," these tracks reflected the city's industrial backdrop and its population of chemists and engineers, appealing to "nerds."
