# genre - G-Funk

## curiosities
**Influence on Chop 'N' Screw** : G-Funk was the base music that DJ Escriu slowed down and transformed in his Chop 'N' Screw experiments in early 90s Houston, turning it into a "dragged-out" experience. **Influence on Early Phonk** : Space Goshpar's initial vision for authentic Miami funk was influenced by West Coast G-Funk, integrating its sound into a modern, digital sensibility.
