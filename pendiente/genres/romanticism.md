**Medieval Inspiration in 19th Century Romanticism** : The obsession with the medieval is not new; since the Romanticism of the 19th century, artists and musicians already looked to the Middle Ages as a source of mystery, epic, and lost spirituality. This era saw the emergence of operas evoking knights and crusades, and a neo-Gothic architecture that restored the grandeur of castles and cathedrals to the collective imagination.
