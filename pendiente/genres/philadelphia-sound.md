# genre - Philadelphia Sound

## curiosities
**Influence on Elton John's "Philadelphia Freedom"** : The distinctive "Philadelphia sound," characterized by its R&B feel and lush strings, heavily influenced Elton John's 1975 song "Philadelphia Freedom." Elton John explicitly stated his intention for the record to be a tribute to this "black music," aiming for it to sound like a "funky black record" that would cross over into the black market.
