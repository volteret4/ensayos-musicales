# genre - Música Blanca

## curiosities
**Pioneering Avant-Garde Sound at Barraca (1981)** : "Música blanca" was a revolutionary sound introduced by DJs Juan Santamaría and Carlos Simó at Barraca in 1981, specifically designed to distinguish itself from the prevalent funk and disco of the era. This genre was defined by groups such as Depeche Mode, The Cure, Joy Division, and The Fall, featuring exotic and largely unknown rhythms that attracted diverse urban tribes and fostered a spirit of brotherhood.
