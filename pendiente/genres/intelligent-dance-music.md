# genre - Intelligent Dance Music

## curiosities
**Contemporary Diffuseness** : Today, the IDM label has become increasingly vague and diffuse as its styles have blended with alternative pop, hip-hop, and other contemporary musical currents. Despite this, many of the original producers continue to release new material, and fresh artists still emerge carrying the spirit of IDM. **Controversial Label** : Since its emergence, the "IDM" label has been contentious, often perceived as pretentious because it implicitly suggests that other forms of electronic music are not intelligent. Consequently, many musicians associated with the scene have openly disavowed this tag. "Desde entonces, la tiqueta ha sido polémica. Claramente suenas no. Es como si sugeriese que el resto de música electrónica no fue inteligente." ← Qué fue el IDM y por qué cambió la música electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RTwn3hP1XTg
**Evolution in the 1990s** : Throughout the 1990s, IDM continuously mutated. A "second wave" of artists and labels emerged, distinguishing itself from the first wave (which focused more on melody and textures) by placing a greater emphasis on percussion, influenced by the sounds of jungle and drum and bass. "Durante los años 90 lide, me no dejó de imutar, y varios generos de artistas, como la misma Bjork, empezaron a incorporar esas sensibilidades en sus trabajos." ← Qué fue el IDM y por qué cambió la música electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RTwn3hP1XTg
**Origin of the Name (1993)** : The terms "Intelligent Music" and "EDM" (IDM) became consolidated in 1993 through the IBM list, a dedicated mailing list for fans of Warp Records and Aphex Twin. This occurred after some segments of the press had initially referred to this new sound as "intelligent techno." "hasta que en 1993, a través de una lista de correo de fans de Warp y FX Twin, la IBM list, el término intelligent music y EDM acabó consolidándose." ← Qué fue el IDM y por qué cambió la música electrónica | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RTwn3hP1XTg
**Reflecting a Deeper Intent** : Despite its controversial and pretentious nature, the term "IDM" managed to reflect a genuine sentiment within a segment of the electronic community. This group sought a deeper, more profound layer in techno music beyond its purely functional role for dancing, aiming for a more conscious, attentive, and thoughtful listening experience.
