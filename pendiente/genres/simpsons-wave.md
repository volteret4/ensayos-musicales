# genre - Simpsons Wave

## curiosities
**Meme-Centric Vaporwave Subgenre** : Simpsons Wave is highlighted as one of the "most meme-centric" subgenres of Vaporwave that, ironically, achieved the most resonance on the internet, showcasing how meme culture can sometimes drive online popularity more effectively than artistic depth for certain genres. "Creosamente los sugéneros del vapor wave que tuvieron más reeson a la internet fueron los más memes como el Simpsons Wave." ← ¿Qué pasó con el PHONK？ (La historia real) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ehfWyUHFFT8
**Visual-First, Meme-Driven Subgenre** : Simpsons Wave is identified as one of the subgenres where the visual imaginary was often created before the sonic component. Along with Fudge Wave, it largely remained meme-driven and contributed to a superficial public perception of the broader vaporwave genre.
