# genre - New Orleans R&B

## curiosities
**Caribbean Influence** : Records from the New Orleans area, including some by Shirley and Lee like "Feel So Good," often showed a noticeable Caribbean influence in their style. **Influence of Fats Domino** : The entire genre of New Orleans music owes a profound debt to Fats Domino for his foundational contributions and unique sound, which shaped its development and influence. **Popularity in Jamaican Sound Systems** : New Orleans R&B was immensely popular among Jamaican sound system listeners. Artists like Shirley and Lee, Fats Domino, Lloyd Price, and Professor Longhair, whose music was in this style, were heavily featured.
