# genre - Ballad

## curiosities
**1970s Dominance** : The 1970s was a ballad-heavy decade, particularly its first half before disco gained prominence, which significantly influenced the types of songs that became Songs of the Summer. "The 70's was a ballad heavy decade, especially its first half before disco took over." ← Hit Parade Music History and Music Trivia > Song(s) of the Summer Edition Part 2 | https://shows.acast.com/hit-parade/episodes/695d4ef50c30a1408dc292e4
**Cecil Gant's A-sides** : Ballads were a characteristic feature of Cecil Gant's A-side record releases, contrasting with his boogie rocker B-sides. His major hit, "I Wonder," was a prominent example of his ballad style. "But while Gant A-sides were ballad, his B-sides were Boogie Rockers." ← Episode 7： ＂Good Rockin' Tonight＂ by Wynonie Harris | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IgqSPoYRUCM
