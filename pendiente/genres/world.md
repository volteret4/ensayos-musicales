## curiosities
**Influence on Chill Out Diversification** : In the first half of the 1990s, as the Chill Out genre began to diversify, "World" music became a significant external influence. This, alongside instrumental hip-hop and Dub, contributed to the genre's expansion beyond its rave origins. **José Padilla's Use in Ibiza** : José Padilla, the resident DJ at Café del Mar in Ibiza, integrated World Music into his sunset sessions. This blend, along with ambient sounds, Jazz Swab, and electronic rhythms, helped define the relaxed and contemplative spirit of Ibiza and redefined Chill Out for a broader public.
