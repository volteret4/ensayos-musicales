**Influence on Eurodance (Mid-1990s)** : Happy Hardcore, an accelerated and energetic genre developing in European clubs, became a significant influence on Eurodance in the mid-1990s. As classic Eurodance showed signs of weakness, elements from Happy Hardcore helped to evolve the genre.
