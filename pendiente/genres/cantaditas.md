# genre - Cantaditas

## curiosities
**Later Inclusion in Commercial Megamixes** : Towards the end of their commercial lifespan, successful Megamix series like "Maquina Total" and "Lo Más Duro," which initially leaned towards techno or EDM, began to include "cantaditas" (vocal trance/commercial dance with vocals). This represented a shift towards more commercial and accelerated styles, alongside pachanga and sped-up cover versions.
