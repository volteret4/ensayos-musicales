# genre - Gospel Music

## curiosities
**Community's Open Secret** : The gospel music community had a more relaxed attitude towards homosexuality and bisexuality compared to other parts of the church, allowing relationships like that between Sister Rosetta Tharp and Marie Knight to be an "open secret." **Origin and Creator** : Modern black gospel music largely originated around the same time as big bands and was primarily created by Thomas Dorsey around 1930. He integrated popular music idioms, such as blues and jazz, with traditional Christian hymns, making it distinct from earlier forms of religious singing.
