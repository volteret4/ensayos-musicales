# genre - Ekpank

## curiosities
**Aesthetic Evolution** : The aesthetic of Ekpank became markedly more absurd, featuring surreal collages, deliberately ridiculous lyrics, and an attitude that celebrates the bizarre above all else. This was molded through internet memes, forums, and platforms like Bandcamp and YouTube. "Mientras que su estética se volvió más marcadamente absurda, compartadas sus realistas, letras deliberadamente ridículas y un actitud que celebra valorizarro por encima de todo." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Global Expansion and New Generation** : Although similar groups existed worldwide, the coining of the term Ekpank sparked a new generation of bands that identified with the label. The scene moved beyond its Midwest origins to become a global micro-genre, with notable vibrant scenes emerging in Australia and Spain. "El ekpank se había convertido en la etiqueta de moda de la underground de internet y pasó de ser un fenómeno local a un global." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Internet and Meme-Driven Naming (2017-2018)** : The subgenre gained its name from Instagram memes, starting in 2017. A 2018 diagram by "meme le escribo," positioning bands on a spectrum from Chain Punk to Ekpank, inadvertently gave the movement a definitive name and set it against more serious punk. This turned Ekpank into a trendy internet underground label, transforming it from a local to a global phenomenon. "esta publicación, para bien o para mal, había puesto un nombre definitivo al movimiento y la habían enfrentado con el panc más serio y distorsionado." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Origin and Early Characteristics (Mid-2010s)** : Ekpank originated in the US Midwest in the mid-2010s, initially as bands playing fast, hard-hitting punk with a "clownish" attitude and "nerd" aesthetic. These musicians enjoyed hardcore punk but didn't identify with the serious, aggressive image of traditional punk. They embraced insecurity, introversion, and fun, playing in basements and releasing ultra-limited music on cassettes. "A mediados de la década de 2010, empezaron a surgir en el medio huesté de Estados Unidos una serie de bandas con ese sonido rápido y contundente, pero con una actitud, digamos, más payazá y con una estética más nerd." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Pandemic's Role in Development (Late 2010s)** : Towards the end of the 2010s, particularly during the pandemic, many rising punk artists leveraged isolation and the "duit dueltzerve" (do-it-yourself) nature of the genre. They recorded at home with simple equipment like a drum machine and a poorly tuned guitar, allowing for increasingly experimental and interesting sonic explorations. "muchos panks en auge aprovecharon la reclusión y la naturaleza, duit dueltzerve del género, para grabarse en sus casas y experimentar con ese sonido." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Revolutionary Impact** : Ekpank, without explicitly aiming to save or reinvent punk, has become an almost accidental revolution within the genre. It has revitalized the primitive, visceral, and energetic spirit of rock and roll through the language of the internet, connecting with new generations in an unprecedented way. "El Eckpank no pretende salvar al punk de reinventarlo, pero de forma casi accidental se ha convertido en una pequeña revolución dentro del genero." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
**Sound Evolution** : The sound of Ekpank evolved to become even more playful, incorporating frenetic rhythms, spasmodic and playful guitars, and more nasal or "martian" vocals. This contrasts sharply with the seriousness of hardcore punk. "El sonido evoluciono hacia un estilo aún más juguetón, abrazando ritmos frenéticos, tarras espasmódicas y juguetonas y voces más nasalísimas cianas." ← Egg Punk： Explorando el lado más raro del punk | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZvQCffRFhHQ
