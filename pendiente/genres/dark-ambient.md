# genre - Dark Ambient

## curiosities
**Hauntological Subgenre** : Dark Ambient music can be utilized to convey hauntological themes, particularly when imbued with melancholy. The project The Stranger (Leyland Kirby) exemplified this by transforming rave culture into a Dark Ambient soundscape, conveying the sadness of a vanished scene.
