# genre - DasKpop

## curiosities
**Term Coined by Lizard King** : The term "DasKpop" was coined by musician Lizard King to define the more spatial and progressive aspect of the demoscene and cracktro music he created and was part of. This genre represented an avant-garde sub-genre within the broader chiptune and tracker music scenes.
