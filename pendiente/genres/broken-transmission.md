# genre - Broken Transmission

## curiosities
**Nostalgic and Chaotic Soundscapes** : It utilizes lo-fi fragments from television transmissions, advertisements, movies, and radio, manipulating them to construct a nostalgic and chaotic soundscape. Through loops, slowdowns, and distortions, it achieves a collage effect that evokes a fragmented and ethereal atmosphere. "Utilizamos tras Low-Fight de transmisiones de televisión, anuncios, películas y radio, manipulándolas para crear un paisaje sonoro nostálgico y caótico." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA

**Simulating Channel Surfing (Also known as Signalwave)** : Broken Transmission, also known as Signalwave, employs short sound fragments to simulate the experience of changing channels or radio stations.
