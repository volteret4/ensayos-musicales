# genre - Música Urbana

## curiosities
**Critique of Authenticity** : Despite its widespread adoption, some purists maintain that if one is not "inside" or "in the street," what they are making is not truly trap. **Industry's Neutral Label for Blended Genres** : As trap became integrated into the industry, it was rebranded with the less conflictive label "Música Urbana." This "white" and commercial tag encompassed the blend of reggaeton, flamenco, and dembow that was already emerging underground, but presented with a cleaner, more pop vision. "Música urbana. Una tiqueta blanca e inconmercial, donde había todo lo que ya se estaba mezclando en el Andes Grounds de 2013." ← La salvaje historia del TRAP ESPAÑOL (2013-2026) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hBhiU5jj6s
