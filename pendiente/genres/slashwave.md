# genre - Slashwave

## curiosities
**Echoey, Atmospheric, and Emotive Sound** : Slashwave is characterized by its echoey and atmospheric sound, achieved through manipulated samples and generous application of effects like reverb, delay, and especially phaser. Unlike other vaporwave branches focused on nostalgia and consumerism, Slashwave evokes themes of love, melancholy, and dreams, making it the most romantic and emotional subgenre. "El Slash Wives se caracteriza por su sonido de encho y atmosférico, logrado a través de la manipulación de Samples y el uso generoso de efectos como el River, el delay y especialmente el Faser." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Popularized in 2013** : The genre gained significant popularity in 2013, notably aided by artists such as Telepath. "Se popularizó en 2013 gracias a Telepath." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
**Shift Towards Original Compositions** : While primarily using samples from Asian genres like Japanese and Chinese pop, Slashwave has recently experienced a surge in popularity by beginning to incorporate original compositions without samples, similar to Utopian Virtual, thereby gaining increased presence online. "el género experimentado en pico de popularidad recientemente gracias a empezar a incluir composiciones originales sin Samples. Ganando cada vez más presencia en la red, gracias artistas como Desert Sandfield's Warman Night." ← Vaporwave： Mucho más que un Meme | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhOnKc0SxsA
