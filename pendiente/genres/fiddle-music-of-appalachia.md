# genre - Fiddle Music of Appalachia

## curiosities
**Beginning of Country Music (Early 20th Century)** : Fiddle music from Appalachia near the turn of the 20th century is considered a fundamental part, and arguably the actual beginning, of country music's history. This historical significance makes a country rock band celebrating the fiddle, like The Charlie Daniels Band, a natural fit for Austin City Limits. "And to be sure, fiddle music of Appalachia near the turn of the 20th century is very much a part of country music's history. If not the actual beginning.
