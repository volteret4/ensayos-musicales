## curiosities
**Mid-1990s Underground Style** : In the mid-1990s, drum and bass was an emerging electronic subgenre that, despite its influence, typically remained in nightclubs. It was characterized as an experimental and raw sound, not primarily intended for a mass audience but rather for urban subcultures and raves. **Origin from "Amen, Brother" Break (Early 1990s)** : In the early 1990s in the United Kingdom, various musicians collectively agreed to accelerate and chop the same "Amen, Brother" drum solo by The Winstons that Dr. Dre had slowed for N.W.A. This creative manipulation gave rise to Drum & Bass, one of the most exciting genres of that decade.
