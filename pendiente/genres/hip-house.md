# genre - Hip House

## curiosities
**Influence on Eurodance Vocal Structure** : Hip House, a subgenre originating from Chicago, directly influenced the "beauty and the beast" vocal structure prevalent in Eurodance. It pioneered the innovative blend of female soul vocals with male rap phrasing, a formula subsequently adopted and popularized by Eurodance.
