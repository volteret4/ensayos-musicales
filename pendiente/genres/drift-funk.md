# genre - Drift Funk

## curiosities
**Characteristics and Aesthetic** : Drift Funk is one of the most popular sub-genres of Funk, characterized by an aesthetic inspired by drift culture and a more rhythmic sound. It serves as a precursor to Funk House, linking the broader Funk movement to the emergence of Rally House.
