# genre - IDM (Intelligent Dance Music)

## curiosities
**Critical Preference in Early 1990s** : In the early 1990s, electronic music critics showed a strong preference for IDM, which they regarded as a more "intellectual" and sophisticated version of techno. This preference led them to dismiss or ignore hardcore techno albums, such as The Prodigy's debut "Experience," often viewing them as sacrilegious or not worthy of serious critical attention.
