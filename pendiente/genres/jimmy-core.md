# genre - Jimmy-Core

## curiosities
**Alternative Name for Eckpank (YouTube Channel Honor)** : The emerging Eckpank scene was sometimes called Jimmy-Core in internet forums, in honor of the YouTube channel "Jimmy de Dock" which was instrumental in disseminating and popularizing the scene. This name was an early attempt to label the genre.
