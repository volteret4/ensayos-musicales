# genre - Brazilian Funk

## curiosities
**Distinct from Brazilian Phonk** : Brazilian Funk (Fonc Brasileno) is a genre that, while potentially confused with Brazilian Phonk, takes the "percussion of funk" and "crazy voices of Brazilian MCs," processing them through an "increasingly saturated filter." Visually, it is described as lacking much originality, often featuring characters and heroes transformed into Super Saiyans, though it maintains the original Phonk's purple hue.
