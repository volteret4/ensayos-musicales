# genre - Protóic Pank

## curiosities
**Historical Presence in Spain (1980s)** : Elements of "Protóic Pank" (Proto-Punk) can be historically traced back to Spain in the 1980s, particularly with bands like Los Niñatos, who exhibited many characteristics later associated with the Eckpank genre. This suggests a precedent for the unique Spanish punk sound.
