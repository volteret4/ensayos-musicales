# genre - Horrorcore

## curiosities
**Memphis Rap as Foundation** : The raw and dark sound of Memphis rap, characterized by cowbells, syncopated rhythms, and heavy sub-basses, ultimately laid the groundwork for genres such as horrorcore. "acabaría sembrando las bases, ojo, del horror core, el crank." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
**Phonk's Aesthetic Adoption** : Global phonk producers adopted the horrorcore aesthetic of Memphis rap, imitating album covers and incorporating its raw 808 sound, blending it with other Southern rap elements. "adoptaron la estética horror core de Memphis, imitaron sus portadas, rescataron el sonido crudo del 808." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
**Three 6 Mafia's Creation** : Three 6 Mafia is credited with creating "southern horrorcore," disseminating its distinctive, dark, and violent sound throughout Memphis and beyond. "Habían creado el horror corra a la soreña, y lo difundieron por toda la ciudad." ← Memphis Rap： Cuando el rap era puro terror | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lWBpNa-xSt4
