# genre - Coldysynth

## curiosities
**Childlike Melodies and Inspirations** : The sound of Coldysynth is built upon soft and emotional melodies, frequently reminiscent of those created with a child's keyboard or toys. Its aesthetic draws heavily from illustrations by Beatrix Potter and Arthur Rackham, featuring enchanting fables of bunnies, goblins, and hares. "Su sonido se construye a partir de melodías suaves y emocionantes, que a menudo parecen sacadas de un teclado infantil o hechas con juguetes." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
**Comfortable Nostalgia and Peaceful Aesthetics** : Also known as Cowsysynth, this subgenre seeks to evoke comfortable nostalgia, deliberately moving away from the dark and somber tones of classic Dungeon Synth. Its goal is to transmit calm, warmth, and peace. "Este Super Stilock busca abocar una nostalgia confortable, alejándose por completo de los tonos oscuros y sombridos del dan Jon Synth clásico para transmitir calma, calidad y paz." ← Dungeon Synth： El género más misterioso de internet | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CBIHO0ihIxQ
**Connection to Kotashkor** : This microgenre is strongly associated with an internet trend known as "Kotashkor," which, despite its potentially whimsical sound, effectively delivers on its promise of cozy comfort.
