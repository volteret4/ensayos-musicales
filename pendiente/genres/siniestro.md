# genre - Siniestro

## curiosities
**Chocolate's Core Identity and Clientele** : The "siniestro" (sinister) aesthetic and music were fundamental to Chocolate's identity, drawing a specific audience that was primarily from the punk and sinister subcultures. DJ Tony Vitale "El Gitano" curated this atmosphere by playing bands like Bauhaus, Cabaret Voltaire, and Throbbing Gristle, solidifying Chocolate as the "most gothic" club on the Ruta. "Por lo que el catalogo del gente que visitaba el club era sobretodo proviniente en el punk y la ula siniestra." ← Cuando Valencia fue la capital mundial del Clubbing | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ckJgog8P_og
