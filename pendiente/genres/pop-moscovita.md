# genre - Pop Moscovita

## curiosities
**Government-Backed and Chart-Dominating in Late 70s USSR** : In the late 1970s, Pop Moscovita was the dominant musical genre on Soviet hit lists. This type of music, along with Folkloric music, was backed by the government and considered more controllable, contrasting with the underground rock scene. "La música que dominaba las listas de éxito es en el pop-moscovita y la música folculórica. Vamos, las que estaban respaldadas por el gobierno y eran más controlables." ← Viktor Tsoi.
