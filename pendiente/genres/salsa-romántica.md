# genre - Salsa Romántica

## curiosities
**Emergence in 80s and 90s** : During the 1980s and especially the 1990s, "salsa romántica" emerged as a new trend. This genre was notably more polished and danceable than earlier salsa styles, focusing its lyrics on themes of heartbreak and nostalgia, thereby moving away from the combative, street-oriented tone of Fania's "salsa dura." "Durante los 80 y especialmente los 90 llegó la salsa romántica, un generó mucho más polido y raveable, con letras centradas en el desamor y la nostalgia, pero muy alejadas del tono combativo y cayerjero de la salsa dura de la fansa." ← LA FANIA.
