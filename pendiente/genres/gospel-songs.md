# genre - Gospel Songs

## curiosities
**BMI's Role** : BMI, established as a rival to ASCAP, specifically dealt with "low-class music," which included gospel songs. During the ASCAP Boycott of 1941, this made gospel music, along with blues and country, suddenly very important as it was one of the few types of music that could be played on the radio, significantly boosting its audience.
