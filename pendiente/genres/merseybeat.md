**"You Still Want Me" - Merseybeat Pastiche** : The Kinks' second single, "You Still Want Me," was a "Merseybeat pastiche." This stylistic choice, despite songwriting advice from Larry Page, did not lead to chart success, as the song performed even worse than their debut single.
