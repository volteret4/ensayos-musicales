# genre - G-Hop

## curiosities
**Influence on Witchhouse** : The Southern G-Hop scene, particularly Houston's Chopped & Screwed style from the 90s, was a significant influence on Witchhouse. Producers with a dark sensibility adopted its technique of slowing sounds to create a narcotic effect, re-purposing it to invoke ghostly atmospheres.
