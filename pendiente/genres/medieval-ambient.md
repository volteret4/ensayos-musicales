# genre - Medieval Ambient

## curiosities
**Subgenre Development from Dungeon Synth** : Medieval Ambient is a subgenre that has emerged from Dungeon Synth, developing an identity almost distinct from its parent genre. Artists like Fief, Dim, Sûredian Miz, and Murgrind are key figures in this branch, which contributes to the broader diversification and popularity of medieval-inspired electronic music.
