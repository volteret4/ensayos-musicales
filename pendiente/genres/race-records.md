# genre - Race records

## curiosities
**Segregated Music Category** : After World War I, as selling records and radio became profitable, a genre specifically for records by black artists, called "race records," was created. This segregation was implemented to control what music white children, and more precisely their parents, were exposed to, aiming to prevent them from listening to "the wrong type of music." "a genre called race records, records by black artists, was created to help make sure that white children and more accurately their parents could see or somehow put up a gate to make sure that they were not buying or listening to the so-called wrong type of music." ← For the Record - The 70s > Ep. 53 - More than Charley Pride - African Americans in 70s Country | https://www.ftr70.com/
