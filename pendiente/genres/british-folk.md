# genre - British Folk

## curiosities
**Influence on Space Oddity** : British Folk was one of the elements David Bowie combined with his fascination for Stanley Kubrick's "2001: A Space Odyssey" and Bee Gees-style storytelling to create "Space Oddity." This eclectic mix contributed to the song's "intoxicating diddy" quality and its unique narrative.
