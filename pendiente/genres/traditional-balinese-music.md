# genre - Traditional Balinese Music

## curiosities
**Influence on Akira Soundtrack** : This traditional music genre, specifically Gamelan, served as a direct influence for some of the unique percussive and mysterious sounds found in the soundtrack for the film *Akira*, composed by the collective Genio y Yamashiro Bumi.
