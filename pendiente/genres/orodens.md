# genre - OroDens

## curiosities
**Later Dominance in Megamixes** : OroDens (likely Eurodance) became one of the dominant genres in Megamix compilations later in their evolution, after the initial shift from Italo Disco towards Acid House, New Beat, and EBM. This genre represented a faster and more forceful style of electronic music embraced by the series like "Maquina Total" and "Lo Más Duro."
