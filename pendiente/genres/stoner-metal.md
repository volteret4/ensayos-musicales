# genre - Stoner Metal

## curiosities
**Integration of Psychedelic Rock and Blues** : Stoner Metal incorporates elements from psychedelic rock and blues to forge a more melodic and groovy sound. While retaining the weight and density characteristic of Doom Metal, it infuses an air that is more hypnotic and lysergic, creating a distinct, trance-inducing experience. "Por otro lado el esto en el metal incluyó elementos del róxico delico y el blues para crear un sonido más melodico y gruby.
