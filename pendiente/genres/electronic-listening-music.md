# genre - Electronic Listening Music

## curiosities
**Proposed Name for IDM (1992)** : This phrase appeared on the cover of Warp Records' "Artificial Intelligence" compilation in 1992, intending to condense the revolution that was about to unfold in electronic music. It signaled a new vision for the genre, moving it towards contemplative home listening. Despite its clear declaration, the name never fully caught on to define the broader genre.
